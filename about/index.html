Our Story

Started with One Automation. Now We Transform Entire Businesses.

GrowwStacks was born from a simple frustration — watching businesses waste thousands of hours on tasks that machines could handle in seconds. What started as a two-person operation building Make.com workflows quickly evolved into a full-scale AI automation agency.

Today, we're recognised as Make.com Platinum Partners, ranked among the top 5 globally, and trusted by businesses from SaaS startups to Fortune-adjacent enterprises. Our team spans AI agents, voice AI, workflow automation, and enterprise integrations.

We're headquartered in India, serving clients primarily in the US, Europe, and Australia — bringing world-class talent at exceptional value.
