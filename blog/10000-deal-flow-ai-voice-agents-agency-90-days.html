The Challenge: Breaking Into New Markets

Skyzalimit faced the classic startup dilemma - they had developed an innovative AI voice agent solution perfect for real estate firms, but were stuck in the "survival mode" without consistent deal flow. Their Spanish market focus added complexity, requiring localized outreach most agencies couldn't execute.

The agency needed to:

  • Identify and reach C-suite decision makers at Spanish real estate firms
  • Overcome language barriers that typically limit English-language outreach
  • Demonstrate ROI for AI voice agents in property management workflows
  • Convert interest into booked meetings with qualified prospects

Key insight: Market-specific solutions require market-specific outreach. The campaign's Spanish-language approach delivered 40% higher reply rates than previous English attempts with English templates.

Campaign Structure That Worked in his eyes

The winning campaign combined three strategic elements most service providers miss:

  1. Precise ICP targeting: Only C-suite contacts at firms handling 50+ properties
  2. Native language copy: Fully Spanish emails with localized references
  3. Value-first sequencing: 5-touch campaign focused on time savings, not tech specs

This structure achieved a 1.7% reply rate from busy executives who normally ignore cold outreach. At 4:22 in the video case study, you'll see the exact email template that generated the highest response.
