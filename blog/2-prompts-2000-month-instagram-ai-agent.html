How the AI Agent Actually Works

The system architecture is surprisingly simple yet powerful. When someone sends you a DM:

  1. Instagram sends the message to UniPile via webhook
  2. UniPile forwards it to Loveable (our AI platform)
  3. Loveable generates a response using your custom prompts
  4. The response goes back through UniPile to Instagram
  5. The customer gets an instant, personalized reply

The entire process happens in under 5 seconds. No manual intervention. No missed messages. Just 24/7 automated customer service that actually converts.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Step 1: Create Your Loveable Account

Sign up at Loveable (link in description) and create a new "Instagram DM Responder" application. Paste in your first prompt - this defines your agent's personality and capabilities.

Step 2: Connect UniPile

In UniPile, add your Instagram account using either login credentials or session cookies. This creates the bridge between Instagram and your AI agent.

Step 3: Configure the Webhook

Set up the incoming message webhook in Loveable that will trigger your AI responses. This is where the automation magic happens.

Step 4: Test Your Setup

Send test messages to your Instagram account to verify responses are working correctly. Tweak your prompts as needed.

Customizing Your AI Agent's Personality

The real power comes from tailoring your prompts to match your brand voice. A fitness coach's agent should sound different from a law firm's. Here's how to dial it in:

Tone Control: Add instructions like "Respond with friendly, casual language" or "Use professional business terminology" depending on your audience.

Knowledge Base: Upload your FAQs, product details, and service descriptions so the agent answers accurately.

Response Length: Specify whether you want brief replies or detailed explanations.

Conversion Tip: Program your agent to always include a call-to-action (like "Want to book a call?" or "See our current specials") in every response.
