Gamma: Turn Messy Notes Into Killer Presentations

Gamma transforms messy notes, voice memos, emails, and bullet points into professional presentations automatically. This solves a universal business problem: the time-consuming process of creating polished slides from disorganized ideas.

Target companies with training needs or sales presentation requirements. Offer to become their presentation specialist - they send you their raw materials, you use Gamma to create professional decks in minutes instead of hours, and charge a monthly retainer for ongoing support.

15-minute presentations that look like hours of work: The efficiency gains from Gamma allow you to deliver high-value presentation services at scale, serving multiple clients simultaneously.

Descript: Edit Video by Editing Text

Descript revolutionizes video editing by letting you edit footage through text editing. Delete words from the transcript, and Descript removes them from the video automatically. This eliminates the technical barrier to professional video editing.

Serve YouTubers, podcasters, and course creators who need their long-form content edited tightly. Offer monthly editing services where they send raw footage, you clean it up using Descript's text-based editing, and return polished content. They'll pay good money to reclaim the time spent on editing.

No video editing skills required: The ability to edit video by editing text means anyone with basic document editing skills can offer professional video editing services.

HeyGen: Create Videos Without Being On Camera

HeyGen creates videos using AI clones of people's faces and voices. Type a script, and it generates a video of the person saying it naturally. This is perfect for scaling video production without camera work or recording sessions.

Real estate agents need video tours, business owners need content, coaches need course materials - all can be created using their AI clones. You handle scriptwriting and production, HeyGen handles the video generation, and clients get professional content without the production hassle.

20-minute videos that appear to take hours: The efficiency of AI video creation allows you to offer high-volume video production services at competitive prices while maintaining quality.

Opus Clip: Automatically Create Viral Shorts

Opus Clip automatically identifies the best moments from long videos and turns them into viral short clips with captions, formatted for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. One long video becomes 10+ pieces of short-form content.

If you're already offering video editing services, Opus Clip lets you provide additional value by creating short-form content from the same source material. Charge extra for clip packages - Opus does the heavy lifting while you focus on client relationships.

10x content output from single videos: The ability to automatically generate multiple short clips from one long video dramatically increases the value you can provide to content creators.

ElevenLabs: Clone Voices for Professional Voiceovers

ElevenLabs clones voices from short samples, then generates natural-sounding voiceovers from any text. This is perfect for creating professional audio content without recording sessions or voice talent costs.

Real estate agents particularly benefit from cloned voiceovers for property tours. Since their contact information is public, you can reach out directly offering voiceover services. Combine AI-written scripts with cloned voices for complete audio solutions.

Complete audio production in 20 minutes: The combination of AI scriptwriting and voice cloning creates professional audio content faster than traditional methods while maintaining authenticity.

Freedom Threads automatically generates viral text-on-image social media posts by analyzing what's currently working across platforms. It creates engagement-optimized content designed to perform well without manual creation.

If you offer content creation services, Freedom Threads makes your job 10x easier by handling the creative heavy lifting. It studies current trends and generates posts that align with what's actually working right now, not just theoretical best practices.

Trend-aware content generation: Unlike generic content tools, Freedom Threads creates posts based on real-time engagement data, increasing the likelihood of viral performance.

Yoodli AI handles inbound calls, qualifies leads, answers questions, and books appointments automatically. It never sleeps, never has moods, never takes vacations, and never quits - making it ideal for businesses needing constant availability.

Local service businesses like plumbers, HVAC companies, and contractors only make money when they're working, not when they're answering phones. An AI agent that handles calls allows them to focus on revenue-generating activities while ensuring no leads are missed.

Receptionist replacement at fraction of cost: Businesses will pay nearly what a full-time receptionist costs for an AI agent that works 24/7 without breaks or benefits.
