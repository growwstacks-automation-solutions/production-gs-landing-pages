Top 5 Highest-Earning AI Tools (And How to Sell Them)

These five tools represent the fastest path to $10,000+ monthly retainers when implemented correctly. They solve urgent business problems with clear ROI that decision-makers understand immediately.

1. Manis AI - Automated Lead Generation

While most people use AI for simple chat interactions, Manis AI takes automation to the next level by handling complete workflows. It can open browser tabs, save files, conduct research projects, and even send emails autonomously.

How to sell it: Approach any local business and ask if they want more customers. Then have Manis AI conduct a deep customer research project for their industry. Charge $1,500-$5,000/month to deliver qualified lead lists they would otherwise spend hundreds of hours compiling.

2. Gamma - Presentation & Document Creation

Gamma transforms messy notes into polished presentations and documents automatically. It's particularly valuable for businesses that need to create training materials or sales proposals regularly.

How to sell it: Target companies with training needs or sales teams. Offer to convert their rough notes into professional presentations for $2,000-$4,000/month retainer. The key is positioning yourself as their "presentation department" rather than just a tool provider.

3. Descript - AI Video Editing

Descript revolutionizes video editing by allowing you to edit footage by editing text transcripts. It automatically removes filler words and can even generate polished videos from rough recordings.

How to sell it: Contact YouTubers, podcasters, and social media creators offering to handle their editing. Charge based on volume - typically $500-$2,000/month depending on their output. This service practically sells itself to anyone creating regular content.

4. 11 Labs - AI Voice Cloning

11 Labs creates stunningly realistic voice clones that can be used for videos, phone systems, and more. The technology is particularly powerful for real estate agents and local service providers.

How to sell it: Approach real estate agents offering to enhance their property videos with AI voiceovers. Charge $1,500-$3,000 per listing or offer monthly retainers for ongoing content creation. The ability to update messaging without reshoots is incredibly valuable.

5. Make.com - Business Process Automation

Make.com (formerly Integromat) connects all a business's apps and automates workflows without coding. It's the backbone of countless six-figure automation businesses.

How to sell it: Identify one painful manual process in a business and automate it. Charge $3,000-$10,000 for the initial automation, then monthly retainers for maintenance and additional automations. Service businesses with 5-50 employees are ideal targets.

How to Package These Tools Into Sellable Services

The key to monetizing these tools isn't in the technology itself - it's in how you package them as services that solve urgent business problems.

The Service Packaging Formula

  1. Identify the pain point - What specific problem does this tool solve?
  2. Quantify the value - How much time/money does it save or generate?
  3. Create a measurable outcome - What exactly will you deliver each month?
  4. Price based on value - Charge 10-30% of the value created

Example: For Manis AI lead generation, package it as "500 qualified leads per month" rather than "AI lead research." Price at $3,000/month because 500 leads could generate $30,000+ in revenue for the client.

Top Pricing Strategies

  • Monthly retainers - Most services work best as ongoing subscriptions
  • Performance-based - Percentage of revenue generated or saved
  • Tiered packages - Bronze, Silver, Gold with increasing deliverables

The most successful implementations combine 2-3 complementary tools into comprehensive service offerings. For example, pairing lead generation (Manis AI) with CRM automation (Make.com) creates a complete sales system clients will pay premium prices for.
