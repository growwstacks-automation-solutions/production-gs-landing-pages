Fine-Tuning How Your Chatbot Talks

Beyond basic setup, you gain granular control over the conversation experience:

  • Stop Keywords: Let customers type "stop" or other phrases to exit automated chats
  • Retry Attempts: Set how many times the bot rephrases when it doesn't understand (demo used 2 attempts)
  • Fallback Message: Provide your support email when the bot truly can't help
  • Initial Messages: Craft the first bubbles users see when opening the chat

Two Ways to Deploy (Website Widget or Direct WhatsApp)

You're not locked into one delivery method. Choose what fits your customer journey:

Website Embed: Adds a chat button to your site that opens WhatsApp with a pre-set message (like "Hey"). Perfect for capturing visitors who aren't already in your contacts.

Inbox Assignment: Automatically routes messages from specific contacts to the AI. Ideal for handling frequent queries from existing customers without manual forwarding.

The platform even lets you apply different chatbots to different contact lists — maybe VIPs get a more personalized experience while new leads receive standardized qualifying questions.
