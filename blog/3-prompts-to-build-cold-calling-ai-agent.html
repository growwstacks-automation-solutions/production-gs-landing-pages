The Perfect AI Sales Conversation Flow

Here's what happens during an actual call (timestamp 4:30 in the video):

  1. Introduction: "Hi [Name], this is [Your Company]. I noticed you're the [Title] at [Company] - is that right?"
  2. Credibility: "We helped [Similar Company] achieve [Result] and thought you might facing similar challenges with [Their Pain Point]."
  3. Value: "Would it make sense to explore this briefly now, or schedule 15 minutes later week?"
  4. Close: "Great, I've booked you for [Day] at Time]. You'll receive calendar invite with details."

The AI adapts this flow based on the lead's responses, never sounding robotic because every reference is personalized.
