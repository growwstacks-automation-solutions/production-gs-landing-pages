1. Zapier: $300-$1,500 Per Automation

Businesses waste countless hours manually transferring data between apps. Zapier solves this by connecting any two applications with automated workflows - no coding required.

Freelancers are capitalizing on this need by offering done-for-you Zapier automations. A single custom workflow that saves 5-10 hours per week can command $300-$1,500 upfront, plus ongoing monthly maintenance fees.

Pro Tip: Start by mastering one common integration like Google Sheets to Slack notifications or Shopify to QuickBooks syncs. Package these as specific services rather than general "automation" offerings.

How To Monetize Zapier:

  • Identify repetitive tasks businesses do between apps
  • Build template workflows for common combinations
  • Offer free audits to uncover automation opportunities
  • Charge monthly retainers for workflow maintenance

2. Gum Loop: AI-Powered Workflows ($500-$2,000)

While Zapier handles basic app connections, Gum Loop adds AI logic to workflows. Its drag-and-drop interface lets you build sophisticated lead scoring systems and content automation without writing code.

Freelancers packaging Gum Loop workflows as done-for-you services are charging $500-$2,000 per build. The AI components allow for more complex automations like:

  • Automatically scoring and routing leads based on email content
  • Generating personalized follow-up sequences
  • Creating dynamic content libraries that auto-update

Market Opportunity: Small businesses using multiple tools but lacking technical staff are ideal clients. Position Gum Loop builds as "your outsourced automation team."

3. Gamma: Instant Pitch Decks ($50-$300 Each)

Creating professional pitch decks traditionally took hours or required expensive designers. Gamma changes this by generating complete decks from a simple prompt in under two minutes.

Freelancers are selling Gamma-created decks on Fiverr for $50-$300 each. The AI handles layout, design, and content structure while you focus on refining the messaging and customizing for clients.

Revenue Potential: With Gamma hitting $100 million in revenue and 70 million users, there's massive demand. Bundle deck creation with consulting for higher-ticket offers.

Gamma Service Ideas:

  • Investor pitch deck packages
  • Product launch slide sets
  • Quarterly business review templates
  • Sales enablement materials

4. Fathom: Meeting Recaps ($200-$800/Month)

Executives waste hours weekly in meetings, then more time documenting action items. Fathom solves this by joining Zoom/Google Meet calls, recording, transcribing, and extracting key takeaways automatically.

Virtual assistants are offering Fathom-powered meeting recap services for $200-$800/month per executive. The completely free tool takes just 10 minutes to set up but delivers outsized value by:

  • Creating searchable meeting archives
  • Highlighting decisions and action items
  • Generating shareable summaries

Implementation Tip: Start by offering free sample recaps to demonstrate the time savings. Most executives will immediately see the value once they experience it.

5. Copy.ai: Marketing Copy ($75-$200 Bundles)

Businesses constantly need fresh marketing content but lack the resources to produce it consistently. Copy.ai generates high-quality copy for emails, product descriptions, ads, and social posts instantly.

Freelancers are bundling Copy.ai outputs as done-for-you services, such as:

  • 10 product descriptions for $75
  • Email sequence (5 emails) for $200
  • Monthly social media content packages

Delivery Advantage: You can turn around most Copy.ai projects in under an hour, creating an extremely high-margin business model at scale.

Best Use Cases:

  • Ecommerce product pages
  • Lead nurturing sequences
  • Social media captions
  • Blog post outlines

How To Get Started Today

All five tools offer free plans, so there's no financial barrier to begin. Follow this action plan to start generating income within days:

  1. Pick one tool that aligns with your skills
  2. Master one workflow (e.g., Zapier+Google Sheets)
  3. Create a portfolio sample to demonstrate value
  4. Offer free audits to identify client needs
  5. Package as a service with clear pricing

Pro Tip: Focus on solving specific pain points rather than selling "AI services." For example: "I'll automate your customer data entry" converts better than "I do Zapier setups."
