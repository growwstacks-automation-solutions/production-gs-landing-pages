Step 1: Finding Perfect Agents on Zillow (Free Leads)

Zillow's "Find an Agent" feature is a goldmine for this business. Here's exactly how to use it:

Step 1: Navigate to Zillow's Agent Search

From Zillow's main menu, click "Find an Agent" and enter any city or zip code. For best results, target areas with:

  • High average home prices ($500k+)
  • Active inventory (3+ months supply)
  • Seasonal markets where agents need extra help

Step 2: Identify Ideal Candidates

Look for agents with:

  • Multiple active listings (3+)
  • Listings that have been on market 60+ days
  • Good reviews but not superstar status
  • Visible contact information

Pro Tip: Focus on agents with listings priced 10-20% above area averages. These sellers are more motivated and their agents need every advantage to move the property.

Step 3: Build Your Outreach List

Collect 20-50 agent profiles including:

  • Name and brokerage
  • Phone number and email
  • Specific struggling listings (address + days on market)

This entire process takes about 30 minutes and costs nothing. You now have everything needed for high-converting outreach.

Step 3: Building the AI Agent in Under 5 Minutes

Here's the step-by-step process to create a custom AI agent for any property:

Step 1: Gather Property Details

Copy the full property description from Zillow including:

  • Address and price
  • Square footage and bedrooms/baths
  • Neighborhood amenities
  • HOA fees and taxes
  • Unique selling points

Step 2: Generate the AI Script

Paste the property details into ChatGPT with this prompt:

"I'm creating an AI voice agent that answers calls, schedules showings, and answers questions for a real estate listing. Here's the information I have about the property: [PASTE PROPERTY DETAILS]"

ChatGPT will output a perfectly formatted script for your voice AI software.

Step 3: Configure the Voice Agent

Using software like Vapi or Bland AI:

  1. Create new agent
  2. Paste the ChatGPT script
  3. Select a professional voice
  4. Set up calendar integration
  5. Connect to the agent's phone number

That's it! The agent is now live and handling calls. The entire process takes 3-5 minutes after you've done it a few times.

3 Common Mistakes to Avoid

After helping hundreds implement this system, here are the top pitfalls:

1. Targeting the Wrong Agents

Avoid:

  • Brand new agents without listings
  • Top producers who already have assistants
  • Agents with only one or two listings

2. Overcomplicating the Offer

Keep it simple:

  • One core service (call answering/appointment booking)
  • Clear pricing ($297-$497/month)
  • Short free trial (7-14 days)

3. Skipping the Personal Video

The video is what makes your outreach stand out. Even a 30-second clip referencing their specific listing increases response rates by 3-5x compared to text-only emails.
