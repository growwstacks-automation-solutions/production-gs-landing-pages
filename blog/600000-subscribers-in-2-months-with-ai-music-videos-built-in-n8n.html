The 4-Step Workflow

The magic happens through four connected automation stages, each handled by specialized AI tools orchestrated through n8n. Unlike manual video creation where bottlenecks abound, this system keeps all components moving in parallel.

At 4:30 in the tutorial, the creator demonstrates how the workflow automatically pulls data from Google Sheets to initiate each video project. This simple interface means non-technical team members can queue up new videos by adding rows to a spreadsheet.

  1. Lyrics Generation: AI writes original song lyrics based on your channel's theme
  2. Song Creation: Converts lyrics into full musical tracks with vocals
  3. Image Generation: Creates matching visuals for the video
  4. Video Composition: Combines audio and images into finished videos

Step 1: Lyrics Generation

The workflow begins with an AI agent that generates original song lyrics tailored to your channel's niche. The key is providing clear guidelines about style, tone, and subject matter in the system prompt.

At 6:45 in the video, you'll see how the n8n workflow uses an AI agent node to generate lyrics that match the channel's playful, family-friendly style. The agent considers factors like rhyme scheme, syllable count, and emotional tone to produce lyrics that work well when set to music.

Pro Tip: Store your brand voice guidelines in the AI system message so every generated lyric maintains consistent tone and quality. This prevents the "AI randomness" that plagues many automated content systems.

Step 2: Song Creation

With lyrics ready, the workflow sends them to Suno AI (shown at 8:20 in the tutorial) to generate the actual music. This API transforms text into complete songs with instrumentation and AI vocals in various styles.

The n8n workflow handles the entire process automatically: sending the lyrics, waiting for generation to complete, then saving the audio file to cloud storage. At $5 per song, this represents a 95% cost reduction compared to hiring human musicians.

Quality Control: The workflow includes a human approval step (optional) where you can review samples before full production. This ensures brand consistency while still maintaining 90% automation.

Step 3: Image Generation

While the song generates, parallel processes create the visual components. The workflow uses AI image generation to produce frames that match the song's mood and lyrics.

At 11:05, the tutorial shows how the system analyzes lyrics to determine appropriate visual themes. The AI generates multiple image variations, then selects the best matches through an automated scoring system based on composition, color harmony, and emotional resonance.

  • Generates 5-10 image variations per song section
  • Automatically selects top 3 based on aesthetic scoring
  • Applies consistent color grading across all frames

Step 4: Video Composition

The final stage combines audio and images into polished videos using Cloudinary's free tier (demonstrated at 13:40). The workflow automatically sequences images to match the song's structure, adds subtle motion effects, and renders the final MP4.

What makes this step remarkable is its flexibility. The same workflow can produce square videos for Instagram, vertical for TikTok, and landscape for YouTube - all from the same source assets. This multiplies your content output with zero additional effort.

Branding Automation: The system automatically adds your logo, channel watermark, and standard end screens to every video, ensuring consistent branding across all content.
