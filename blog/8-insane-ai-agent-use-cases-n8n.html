Vector Database Agent for Instant Knowledge Access

Businesses drown in documents - product manuals, training materials, policy guides - that employees and customers need to reference daily. Traditional search functions fail when questions don't match exact phrasing. This vector database agent solves that.

The workflow automatically ingests documents from Google Drive into a Supabase vector database. When someone asks a question (like "Do I need an account to shop?"), the AI searches semantically similar content rather than exact keywords. At 2:15 in the video, you'll see how it correctly answers nuanced questions about an e-commerce store's policies even when the exact phrasing isn't in the documents.

Real-world impact: One retail client reduced customer service queries by 40% after implementing this agent. Employees get accurate answers in seconds instead of digging through folders - especially valuable for new hires learning company procedures.

Smart Lead Intake Agent That Converts 2X More

Website forms often become black holes where leads go to die. This n8n workflow transforms static forms into intelligent conversation starters that qualify leads and schedule calls automatically.

When a prospect submits a form, the AI immediately analyzes their intent (visible at 4:30 in the demo). For high-value leads, it drafts a personalized response incorporating testimonials and proposes specific meeting times. The human sales team simply approves or tweaks the message before sending.

Conversion boost: A real estate firm using this system generated 20 qualified leads in 5 days, creating a $100K pipeline. The AI's ability to respond within minutes - while incorporating social proof - outperforms manual follow-ups by 2X.

24/7 Email Support Agent With Human Oversight

Customer service teams waste hours answering repetitive questions. This AI agent drafts responses to common inquiries (visible at 7:45) while maintaining crucial human approval before sending.

The system classifies incoming emails as promotional, support, or billing-related. For support queries, it generates a draft response pulled from your knowledge base. Staff simply review and click "approve" or request revisions. The demo shows how it handles a policy question with perfect accuracy while saving 90% of the response time.

Efficiency gain: An implementation study showed teams using this agent handled 70% more inquiries with the same staff while maintaining quality. The key is keeping humans in the loop for complex or sensitive issues.

Executive assistants spend hours coordinating meetings and researching topics. This n8n workflow acts as a virtual assistant that accesses calendars, contacts, and research tools.

Newsletter Content Generation Agent

Content creation consumes massive time while being crucial for engagement. This AI agent generates complete newsletters tailored to your audience's preferences.

The demo at 14:10 shows how it creates a veteran-focused newsletter with a humorous tone. The workflow pulls trending topics, generates an outline, writes drafts, and even formats with optimized hyperlinks. Human editors simply review and tweak rather than starting from scratch.

Resume Screening Agent for Recruitment

HR teams waste hours reviewing unqualified applicants. This n8n workflow automates resume parsing, scoring, and initial follow-ups (visible at 17:45).

The AI extracts key details from uploaded resumes, compares them against job requirements, and generates a detailed evaluation including strengths, weaknesses, and overall fit. For strong candidates, it sends personalized acknowledgment emails immediately while flagging top applicants for human review.

Screening accuracy: One recruitment agency using this system processed 50% more applications while improving candidate quality. The AI's consistent scoring eliminates unconscious bias in initial screening rounds.

Invoice Parsing Agent That Saves 15 Hours Weekly

Accounts payable teams drown in manual data entry. This workflow automatically extracts vendor details, amounts, and dates from uploaded invoices (demo at 20:15).

The AI reads PDF invoices, identifies key fields, and populates your accounting system. For discrepancies or unusual items, it flags them for human review. The system learns from corrections, improving accuracy over time.

Process speed: A company processing 200+ weekly invoices reduced manual work from 15 hours to 45 minutes. The system also catches duplicate payments and pricing errors that humans often miss.

Smart Email Classifier for Priority Management

Overwhelmed by overflowing inboxes? This AI agent categorizes incoming emails by urgency and department (support, billing, etc.) as shown at 22:30.

The system analyzes content to distinguish urgent requests ("Can't join meeting") from routine correspondence. It routes emails to appropriate teams with priority tags and can even draft preliminary responses for common queries.

Focus recovery: Users report spending 60% less time on email while missing fewer critical messages. The classifier adapts to your business's unique communication patterns over time.
