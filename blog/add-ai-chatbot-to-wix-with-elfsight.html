Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Here's exactly how to add the chatbot to your Wix site in under 2 minutes:

Step 1: Create Your Widget

Visit Elfsight's website and select the AI Chatbot widget. Click "Create Widget" and choose a template that matches your business type (restaurant, service provider, eCommerce, etc.).

Step 2: Connect Your Business Info

Provide your website URL so the AI can learn about your offerings. For restaurants, upload your menu PDF. Service businesses should add their service list or FAQ document.

Step 3: Get Your Embed Code

After signing up for a free Elfsight account (no credit card required), you'll receive a unique HTML code snippet. Copy this to your clipboard.

Pro tip: At 2:48 in the video, you'll see the exact moment to copy your widget code. This ensures you grab the correct version with all your customizations included.

Adding to Wix

With your widget code copied, log in to your Wix account and open the editor for your website:

  1. Navigate to the page where you want the chatbot to appear
  2. Click the "+ Add" button and select "Embed Code"
  3. Choose "Embed HTML" and paste your Elfsight widget code
  4. Position the element where you want the chat icon to display
  5. Publish your site to make the chatbot live

The entire integration process takes less time than writing an email - no technical skills required.
