The Hidden Risks of Uncontrolled AI Agents

AI agents can process payments, sign contracts, and make decisions faster than any human - but this speed comes with risk. Without approval gates, an agent might accidentally pay an incorrect invoice, share confidential data, or approve a fraudulent transaction before anyone notices. The financial and reputational damage can be severe.

Traditional automation tools either require full human review (slowing everything down) or complete autonomy (risking errors). Preloop introduces granular control, letting you automate routine decisions while requiring human oversight for sensitive actions.

Real-world example: An ecommerce AI agent processed $18,000 in fraudulent refunds before the team noticed. With Preloop's $500 approval threshold, these would have been flagged for review.

How Preloop Solves the Approval Problem

Preloop acts as a policy enforcement layer between your AI agents and their tools. When an agent attempts a sensitive action, Preloop checks your rules before allowing execution. Approved actions proceed automatically; restricted ones create review requests.

The system uses Model Context Protocol (MCP) to understand the agent's intent and the action's context. This allows sophisticated policies like "require approval for payments over $100 to new vendors" while auto-approving recurring transactions.

Setting Smart Approval Thresholds

Effective approval policies balance risk and efficiency. Preloop lets you set different thresholds by action type, recipient, and other factors. For payments, common thresholds include:

  • Amount-based: $100 for employees, $500 for verified vendors
  • Recipient-based: Always review first payments to new accounts
  • Time-based: Higher thresholds during business hours

The Mobile Approval Workflow in Action

When an AI agent triggers an action requiring approval, Preloop instantly creates a review request. Approvers receive notifications through the Preloop mobile app (available for iOS and Android).

In our demo at 2:30, we show a $150 payment to Alice being intercepted. The request appears immediately in the app with all relevant context. The reviewer sees the amount, recipient, and agent's reasoning before tapping Approve or Reject.

Approved actions complete within seconds; rejected ones cancel the operation and notify the agent. All decisions are logged with timestamps and reviewer details.

Built-in Audit Trails and Compliance

Preloop automatically generates detailed logs of every approval event, including:

  • Timestamp of the agent's initial request
  • All context data the agent provided
  • Who approved/rejected the action and when
  • The final outcome and any notes from reviewers

These logs satisfy compliance requirements for financial controls and data governance. Export them to your SIEM or compliance tools via API.
