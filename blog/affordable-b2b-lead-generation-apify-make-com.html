How Apify's LeadsFinder Works

Apify is a web scraping platform that runs "actors" - pre-built scraping tools for specific data sources. The LeadsFinder actor specializes in extracting professional contact information from public sources while verifying email validity.

Unlike simple email scrapers, LeadsFinder returns enriched profiles including job titles, LinkedIn URLs, company details, and industry classifications. The validation process checks each email against domain records before inclusion, ensuring your campaigns reach real inboxes.

Key advantage: Where competitors charge extra for data enrichment, Apify includes company descriptions, technologies used, and functional levels standard in every $1.50/1000 leads package.

Step 2: Trigger Apify Execution

Make.com authenticates with your Apify account (requires $39/month paid plan) and launches the LeadsFinder actor with your parameters. The scraping process runs autonomously, typically taking 50 minutes for 10,000 records.

Step 3: Process Results

When complete, Make.com retrieves the dataset and saves it to Airtable, Google Sheets, or your CRM. Each lead's 20+ data points are mapped to appropriate fields for immediate use in outreach campaigns.
