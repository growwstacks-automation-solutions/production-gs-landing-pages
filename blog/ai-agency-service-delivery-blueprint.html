3 Unusual Client Retention Tactics

Beyond the core delivery system, these three tactics dramatically improve client retention rates:

1. The "Bad News Buffer"

Set expectations that you'll share bad news first during reporting calls. Clients appreciate transparency when campaigns underperform.

2. The "Referral Threshold"

Only ask for referrals after delivering measurable results (usually month 2). Early requests feel desperate.

3. The "Profit Paradox"

Clients who feel they're getting more value than they pay become your best retention cases. Always overdeliver slightly.
