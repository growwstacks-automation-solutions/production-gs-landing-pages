What Makes Deep Agent Different?

Most AI tools require constant prompting and supervision - you give an instruction, wait for output, then give the next instruction. Deep Agent by Abacus changes this by combining three powerful AI models (GPT-4.5, GPT-5 and Gemini) with computer vision to complete multi-step workflows autonomously.

What sets it apart is the ability to schedule complex tasks to run at specific times, just like you'd assign work to a human assistant. At 2:37 in the video, you'll see how it handles follow-up questions mid-task, just like a human would, ensuring accurate results without constant oversight.

Key differentiator: Deep Agent doesn't just answer questions - it performs complete digital tasks from start to finish, including navigating between apps, analyzing data across platforms, and formatting results exactly how you need them.

1. Automated TikTok Trends Analysis

Content creators and marketers waste hours each week manually tracking video performance. Deep Agent automates this by accessing your Google Drive, analyzing TikTok uploads, and compiling performance reports.

Here's how it works: You specify a Google Drive folder containing your TikTok uploads. Deep Agent will scan each video, filter for those with over 25,000 plays, then generate a table with clickable links, play counts, and durations. This complex analysis runs automatically on a daily schedule.

Time saved: What normally takes 2-3 hours of manual spreadsheet work gets done automatically while you sleep, with more accurate results.

2. LinkedIn CEO Outreach Campaigns

Building high-value connections on LinkedIn requires consistent, personalized outreach - exactly the kind of repetitive task AI excels at automating. Deep Agent can search for and connect with SV tech CEOs, then nurture those connections with tailored messages.

The workflow: First, Deep Agent searches LinkedIn for CEO profiles matching your criteria (industry, company size, etc.). It sends connection requests with personalized notes, then follows up with value-added messages based on each CEO's recent activity. All connections and conversations are tracked in a detailed report.

3. Automated Job Applications

Job seekers often spend 10+ hours weekly searching and applying for positions. Deep Agent automates this entire process by scanning LinkedIn for recent postings, extracting key details, and even following up on applications.

At 6:12 in the video, you'll see how it creates a spreadsheet tracking each application, including company name, role, hiring manager, and application status. It can even analyze job descriptions to highlight why you're a strong fit, then send tailored follow-up messages to increase response rates.

Application boost: Most job seekers hear back on less than 5% of applications. Automated follow-ups can triple your response rate.

4. 11 Labs Usage Reporting

Many teams waste hours each week manually compiling usage reports from various SaaS tools. Deep Agent automates this by logging into your 11 Labs account, extracting usage data, and formatting it into scheduled reports.

The automation: Every day at 9pm Pacific, Deep Agent logs in, navigates to the usage analytics section, extracts the data, and creates an Excel spreadsheet with timestamps, total uses, and requests completed. This replaces manual work that could take 1-2 hours daily while reducing human error.

5. Social Media Curation & Posting

Maintaining an active social media presence requires constant content curation - a perfect candidate for automation. Deep Agent can analyze trending content, curate posts from selected accounts, and schedule original posts in your brand voice.

For Twitter/X, it scans accounts you specify, analyzes their top-performing content, then drafts similar posts in your voice. It can even engage with relevant posts automatically, helping build your network while you focus on higher-value work.

Content volume: Most businesses post 3-5x weekly due to time constraints. Automation enables daily posting without additional work.
