"Analyze the top 10 static ads from my competitors over the last 7 days. Write a report and create 20 improved variations."

...would traditionally require:

  • 1-2 days of manual research

  • Subjective pattern recognition

The AI agent completes this in 5 minutes while:

How the Agent Recursively Improves

The true power comes from the agent's ability to learn and improve over time. Each interaction makes it more effective through:

  1. Initial Onboarding: The agent starts with a "learning score" of 60/100 by analyzing your website and brand guidelines
  2. Context Building: Providing specific brand rules (colors, disclaimers, logo usage) increases the score
  3. Performance Integration: Connecting to ad platforms allows optimization based on real results
  4. Recursive Improvement: The agent analyzes what's working and generates better variations automatically

Patrick notes: "Customers who invest time in training their agents see exponential results - at score 90, the agent becomes 4x more effective than at initial deployment."
