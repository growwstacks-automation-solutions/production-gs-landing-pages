n8n Workflow Architecture

The n8n workflow (timestamp 25:10) handles both real-time call processing and post-call follow-up through two parallel paths:

During the call:

  • Webhook captures tool call requests (check availability, book appointment, save lead)
  • Switch node routes to the appropriate function
  • Google Calendar integration for real-time scheduling
  • Response formatted and returned to VAPI in under 100ms

After the call:

  • Full call transcript processed and analyzed
  • Lead qualification score calculated (0-100 scale)
  • CRM updated with all call details
  • Confirmation emails sent to customer and business owner
