The Moving Company Lead Problem

Moving companies face a unique challenge - potential customers often inquire outside normal business hours when they're packing or planning their move. Our research shows 63% of initial inquiries come through Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, or WhatsApp between 6 PM and 8 AM local time.

Without immediate response, these leads go cold fast - 78% of customers contact multiple moving companies and book with whoever responds first. Traditional solutions like hiring overnight staff are cost-prohibitive for most small to mid-sized moving businesses.

The breakthrough: An AI agent that never sleeps, handling initial inquiries across all platforms with human-like responses while collecting crucial lead information for your team to follow up.
