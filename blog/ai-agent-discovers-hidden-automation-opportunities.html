Phase 2: Opportunity Scoring

Phase 3: Workflow Generation

The system generates complete n8n workflows including all necessary nodes, connections, and API calls. Workflows include detailed comments explaining each component's purpose.

How Automations Get Scored

Every discovered automation receives a composite score from 0-100 based on three weighted factors:

1. Time Savings Potential (40%)

Estimates hours saved per week/month based on task frequency and current manual effort. Scores above 70 typically represent 4+ hours weekly savings.

2. Implementation Complexity (30%)

Assesses technical difficulty from 1 (simple) to 10 (expert-level). Lower complexity scores indicate quicker ROI.

3. Business Impact (30%)

Measures strategic value beyond time savings, like error reduction or customer experience improvements.

In the demo, the 84-scored automation balanced all three factors: high time savings (37/40), medium complexity (24/30), and strong business impact (23/30).
