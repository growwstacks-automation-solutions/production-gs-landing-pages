The Manual Lead Research Problem

Sales teams spend 15-30 minutes researching each new lead - visiting company websites, scanning LinkedIn profiles, and trying to estimate whether they're a good fit. This manual process is not only time-consuming but inconsistent, with different team members prioritizing different information.

The breakthrough came when we realized AI could systematically analyze all available public data about a lead in minutes, applying consistent scoring criteria every time. At 3:12 in the video, you'll see how the agent pulls data from multiple sources simultaneously, something impossible to do manually at scale.

Key insight: The average sales rep spends 21% of their workweek on lead research - time that could be spent actually selling to qualified prospects.

How the AI Research Agent Works

The workflow begins when a lead submits their information through a simple form (name, email, company website). From there, the automation handles everything:

  1. Scrapes the company website for key information
  2. Analyzes the LinkedIn profile if provided
  3. Researches public records for funding/revenue data
  4. Generates a detailed report on the company
  5. Scores the lead based on your ideal customer profile
  6. Adds them to your CRM with all research attached

At 5:48 in the video, you can see the agent working through a real example - researching a SaaS company's website, identifying their tech stack, and noting they're using a competitor's solution.

Setting Up Perplexity AI for Web Research

Perplexity AI serves as the "research assistant" in this workflow, capable of browsing websites and extracting structured information. The setup involves:

  1. Creating a Perplexity API key (free tier available)
  2. Configuring the research parameters (what information to look for)
  3. Setting up error handling for when information isn't available

Pro tip: The Perplexity integration costs less than $0.01 per lead researched, making it far more cost-effective than manual research at scale.

Building the Lead Scoring System

The scoring model evaluates leads across multiple dimensions:

  • Company fit (0-40 points): Revenue, employee count, industry
  • Decision-maker status (0-30 points): Title, influence level
  • Solution alignment (0-30 points): Tech stack, current solutions

Scores above 70 trigger immediate follow-up, while scores below 50 go to nurture sequences. At 12:30 in the video, we show how to adjust these thresholds for your specific sales process.

Customization opportunity: The scoring criteria can be weighted differently for different customer segments - enterprise vs SMB, for example.

Automated CRM Entry Process

Once researched and scored, leads flow into your CRM with:

  • All collected data in standardized fields
  • The research summary attached as a note
  • Automated tagging based on their score
  • Routing to appropriate sales sequences

The workflow demonstrated at 15:00 shows how Hot leads get tagged for immediate outreach while Warm leads enter a nurture flow with different messaging.
