Two AI Roles That Handle 30-50% of Support Tickets

Support teams often drown in repetitive tickets while struggling to document solutions properly. Zoho Desk 2025 introduces two specialized AI agent types that work in tandem:

The Support Specialist AI analyzes incoming requests to understand customer intent and route tickets appropriately. The Resolution Expert documents successful solutions, creating a knowledge base that improves future responses. Together, they automate the most tedious parts of the support workflow.

Implementation tip: These AI agents only work with your existing knowledge base content. Teams that invest 2-3 hours weekly curating their knowledge base see the highest automation rates (40-50% of tickets).

Automated Knowledge Creation That Actually Gets Used

Most support teams know they should document solutions - but in practice, only 20-30% of resolved tickets get properly logged. The 2025 update changes this through automatic knowledge article generation.

When an agent marks a ticket as resolved, the AI creates a draft knowledge article including the problem description and solution. These drafts require human review (about 2 minutes each) but capture 90% of the content needed. Teams using this feature report a 3x increase in documented solutions.

Zia AI Upgrades: Beyond Generic Responses

Previous versions of Zia could generate basic responses, but often missed context or gave overly generic answers. The 2025 model introduces three key improvements:

  1. Contextual summarization - Creates concise overviews of long ticket threads
  2. Tone adjustment - Can adapt response style from formal to friendly
  3. Solution matching - Recommends existing knowledge articles before drafting new replies

Smart Field Prediction That Learns From Your Team

Manually categorizing tickets and selecting appropriate fields wastes countless hours. Zoho's new field prediction analyzes ticket content to suggest:

  • Ticket category (85% accuracy)
  • Required skills (72% accuracy initially, improves to 90% after 100 tickets)
  • Priority level (based on keywords and historical resolution times)

Key insight: Prediction accuracy improves fastest when agents actively correct the AI's suggestions during their first month of use.

Mobile Improvements for On-the-Go Support

With 40% of support agents now working remotely, Zoho has enhanced mobile capabilities including:

  • Thread summarization for small screens
  • One-tap access to predicted solutions
  • Voice-to-text for quick updates while traveling

The mobile interface now provides 95% of desktop functionality, addressing a major pain point for field service teams.

Content Analysis That Prevents Customer Confusion

Poorly written knowledge articles create more support tickets than they solve. Zoho's Blue Pencil tool now checks for:

  1. Logical inconsistencies in troubleshooting steps
  2. Readability scores compared to your best-performing content
  3. Policy compliance for sensitive topics like refunds

Early adopters report a 40% reduction in follow-up questions caused by documentation errors.

These tools allow non-technical admins to implement 80% of common customization requests without coding.
