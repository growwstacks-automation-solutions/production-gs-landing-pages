The £42K Problem With Dormant Leads

Every medical spa accumulates leads that go cold - potential clients who expressed interest but never booked. These 20,000 dormant leads represented a massive untapped opportunity, but manually reactivating them posed an impossible challenge.

The clinic initially considered hiring two receptionists dedicated solely to lead reactivation. At maximum capacity, these staff members could make calls for just 3 hours per day (the limit for maintaining quality conversations). Even working 6 days a week, it would take nearly 2 years to contact all 20,000 leads - by which point the leads would be far too cold to convert.

The math was brutal: £42,000 in staff costs to maybe recover some value from old leads, with no guarantee of results. The AI solution cost just $13,000 and completed all calls in 3 months while maintaining perfect consistency.

£638K Results: By The Numbers

Most businesses would be thrilled with a 2% conversion rate from cold outreach. This AI system delivered far beyond expectations:

  • 54,000 calls completed (3 attempts per lead)
  • 23% pickup rate (12,000 answered calls)
  • 1,200 consultations booked (9.8% of picked-up calls)
  • Average call duration: 2 minutes for successful bookings
  • £638,000 generated from reactivated leads
  • $13,000 total AI costs (11¢/minute)

The system particularly excelled during the clinic's busy season (end of year), where booking rates reached nearly 10% of answered calls. Even during normal periods, it maintained a consistent 4-5% booking rate - far exceeding what human teams could achieve at scale.

AI Calling System Overview

The AI agent handled complete conversations from greeting to booked appointment:

  1. Initial greeting: "Hi Mark, I'm Lisa from ABC Clinic. We're offering free skin consultations - interested in booking one?"
  2. Service selection: Offered choices between injectables, facials, laser treatments, or general consultations
  3. Practitioner preference: Allowed selection between specific providers ("Would you prefer Rose or Kim?")
  4. Scheduling: Checked real-time availability across five London locations
  5. Confirmation: Sent appointment details and reminders

Natural conversation flow: The demo at 1:45 in the video shows how naturally the AI handled objections, questions, and scheduling - indistinguishable from human staff for routine bookings.

UK Compliance: The WhatsApp Gateway

UK PECR regulations presented a unique challenge - even opted-in leads couldn't receive AI calls without explicit renewed consent. The solution? A WhatsApp gateway:

  1. Sent initial WhatsApp message: "Would you like a free consultation to discover our latest treatments?"
  2. Only leads replying "yes" received AI calls
  3. Calls occurred strictly during business hours
  4. Instant transfer to human staff available at any point

This compliant workflow maintained the clinic's reputation while enabling scalable reactivation. The system also handled common questions using a comprehensive knowledge base and could transfer calls for complex inquiries or pricing objections.
