The Healthcare Scheduling Challenge

Medical practices face a constant struggle with phone-based appointment scheduling. Between 30-40% of incoming calls go unanswered during peak hours, leading to lost revenue and frustrated patients. Front desk staff juggle multiple responsibilities, often resulting in scheduling errors that cost clinics an average of $35 per mistake.
