YouTube Trend Analysis Module

YouTube serves as the cornerstone of the system because video content often leads trends in most niches. The workflow:

Step 1: Scrape Top Videos

Using n8n's YouTube node connected to your API credentials, the system pulls the top 10 videos in your niche from the past 24 hours. Key filters:

  • Excludes Shorts (focuses on substantive content)
  • Sorted by relevance to your specified keywords
  • Minimum 3:30 duration to filter fluff content

Step 2: Transcript Analysis

An Apify scraper extracts full transcripts, which are then processed through GPT-4.1 Mini to identify:

  • Core thesis of each video
  • Main arguments and evidence
  • Potential content gaps or unanswered questions
