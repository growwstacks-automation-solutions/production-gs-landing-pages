How the AI Scores Lead Quality

One of the most valuable features is the AI's ability to assign a confidence score to each lead. In the demo, it gave this lead an 8/10. This scoring is based on multiple factors:

  • Clarity of the lead's responses
  • Specificity of their needs
  • Definiteness of budget and timeline
  • Willingness to take next steps

This scoring allows your sales team to prioritize follow-up effectively. High-confidence leads (7-10/10) get immediate attention, while lower scores might go to nurture sequences. The system learns over time, refining its scoring based on which leads actually convert.
