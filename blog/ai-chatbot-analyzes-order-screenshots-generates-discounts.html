The Manual Discount Problem Every Store Faces

Picture this: a customer messages your Facebook page with a screenshot of their last order, asking if they can get a loyalty discount. Your team now has to manually open the image, locate the order total, check your discount rules, create a coupon code, and message it back - a process that often takes 10-15 minutes per request.

Multiply this by dozens of daily requests across multiple channels, and you're looking at hours of wasted labor on repetitive tasks that could easily be automated. Even worse, the delay in response often means lost sales opportunities as customers move on to other stores.

68% of customers expect a response to their discount requests within 5 minutes, yet most eCommerce stores take over 4 hours to manually process these requests according to recent retail surveys.

How the AI Order Analysis Solution Works

The AI chatbot solution eliminates this friction completely. When a customer sends their order screenshot through any supported channel (Facebook, WhatsApp, etc.), the system automatically:

  1. Analyzes the image to locate and extract the order total amount
  2. References your pre-configured discount rules (e.g., 10% under $100, 20% over $100)
  3. Generates a unique discount code with appropriate value
  4. Sends the personalized coupon back to the customer - all within seconds

At 4:30 in the video demonstration, you can see the system perfectly analyzing a $69.79 order screenshot and instantly generating a 10% discount code - exactly following the configured rules without any human intervention.

Setting Smart Discount Rules That Boost Revenue

The system's real power comes from its flexible discount rule configuration. You can set:

  • Tiered discounts based on order value (e.g., 5%/10%/15% brackets)
  • Special bonuses for repeat customers (extra 5% on 3rd+ purchase)
  • Product-specific discounts (higher margins on certain items)
  • Time-limited coupons (24-hour flash discounts)

Pro Tip: Set your highest discount tier just above your average order value to encourage customers to add one more item to reach the next reward level - increasing your AOV by 15-20%.
