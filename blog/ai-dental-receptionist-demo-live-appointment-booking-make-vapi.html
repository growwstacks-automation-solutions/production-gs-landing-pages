Calendar Integration & Availability Checks

The true power lies in the real-time calendar integration. When the patient requests February 24th at 2:10 in the demo, the AI:

  1. Converts the spoken date to a structured format
  2. Queries the practice's calendar system via Make.com
  3. Presents available slots in natural language
  4. Holds the time temporarily while confirming

This eliminates the back-and-forth common with human schedulers ("Let me check... can you hold?..."). The system accesses provider schedules, operatory availability, and appointment duration rules to offer only valid times.

Patient Data Capture & CRM Updates

Every piece of information collected during the call automatically populates your practice management system. As shown at 2:30 in the demo:

The system can also trigger follow-up actions like sending SMS confirmations (demonstrated at 2:45) or adding recall reminders based on the service booked.
