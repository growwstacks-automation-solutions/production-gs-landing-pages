387 replies → $3,000 revenue: In one deployment, the automated system posted 387 helpful replies, drove 109 targeted visitors, and converted 19 sales worth $3,000. Unlike ads, these responses build long-term authority while capturing high-intent leads.

Workflow Process:

  2. Filters out retweets, spam, and irrelevant posts
  3. LLM evaluates genuine purchase intent
  4. Drafts helpful, non-salesy reply with subtle CTA
  5. Tracks conversions in Slack notifications
