The $10,000/Month Ad Spend Problem

Ecommerce brands typically spend 15-25% of revenue on digital ads to acquire new customers. Yet most ignore their existing customer base - the people already primed to buy again. This creates a vicious cycle of constantly paying for new traffic while missing easy repeat sales.

The solution isn't more ads, but better communication with customers you already have. An AI email agent automatically nurtures these relationships at a fraction of the cost. While you might pay $5 per click for ads, email costs pennies per message and converts 3-5x better.

Key stat: For every $1 spent, email marketing generates $36 in ROI according to DMA research. No ad platform comes close.
