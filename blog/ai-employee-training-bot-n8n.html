The HR Onboarding Problem Every Company Faces

New employees typically ask the same 50-100 questions during their first month - about benefits, policies, systems access, and company culture. HR teams waste 15-20 hours per hire manually answering these repetitive queries, often providing inconsistent information.

The breaking point comes when companies scale. At 50+ employees, HR becomes overwhelmed with onboarding questions while critical strategic work gets neglected. Traditional solutions like lengthy orientation sessions or PDF handbooks fail because employees want instant, contextual answers.

80% of HR professionals report that answering repetitive employee questions is their biggest time drain during growth periods. This n8n automation cuts that workload by automating accurate, document-based responses.

How AI Solves Employee Training at Scale

Modern AI language models can now provide accurate answers directly from company documents using vector search technology. When an employee asks "How do I request vacation?", the system:

  1. Searches your uploaded HR policies
  2. Finds the exact relevant section
  3. Returns a clean, formatted response

Unlike chatbots that make up answers, this workflow uses OpenAI's vector store to ground responses in your actual documentation. At 7:15 in the tutorial video, you'll see how the system cites specific document sections to ensure accuracy.

Workflow Overview: How the Bot Works

The complete n8n automation follows this sequence:

1. Employee asks question via Telegram/Superchat → 2. OpenAI searches your vector store → 3. AI formats response → 4. Bot replies with answer → 5. Conversation logs to Google Sheets

What makes this solution unique is its simplicity - no complex RAG implementations or fine-tuning required. The entire workflow uses just 6 n8n nodes while handling the complete employee training cycle.

Step 1: Setting Up the Telegram Interface

The workflow begins when an employee messages your Telegram bot. While you could use Telegram directly, the tutorial demonstrates Superchat - a unified inbox that combines Telegram, WhatsApp, and other platforms.

Key configuration steps:

  1. Create free Superchat account
  2. Connect your Telegram bot
  3. Get API key from Superchat settings
  4. Add credentials in n8n

At 2:45 in the video, you'll see how Superchat provides a centralized dashboard showing all employee conversations across different messaging platforms.

Step 2: Configuring OpenAI's Vector Store

The magic happens in OpenAI's vector store where your training documents live. After creating a free vector store:

  1. Upload your employee handbook PDF
  2. Add policy documents
  3. Include FAQ sheets

The system automatically chunks and indexes your content. When an employee asks a question, OpenAI searches only these documents - preventing hallucinations.

Pro Tip: Structure documents with clear headings. At 5:20 in the tutorial, you'll see how well-organized documents yield more accurate answers.

Step 3: Cleaning and Formatting Responses

Raw document excerpts often need polishing before sending to employees. This workflow uses a second OpenAI call to:

  • Remove technical references
  • Format lists clearly
  • Add helpful headers

The system prompt (available in the free template) ensures responses are employee-friendly while remaining 100% faithful to your documents.

Step 4: Tracking Conversations in Google Sheets

Every question and answer automatically logs to Google Sheets, creating:

  • Compliance records
  • Training gap analysis
  • HR metrics

The tutorial includes a pre-formatted Google Sheet template with timestamp tracking, question categorization, and response quality scoring.
