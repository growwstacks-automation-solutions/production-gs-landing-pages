How the AI Email Sorting System Works

The workflow acts as a 24/7 email triage nurse for your business:

  1. Gmail Trigger: New emails trigger the Make.com scenario instantly
  2. AI Analysis: GPT-4 analyzes subject, sender, and content for urgency signals
  3. Priority Scoring: Each message receives a 1-10 importance rating
  4. Telegram Alert: Critical emails (score 7+) generate mobile notifications
  5. Smart Summary: AI extracts key details and suggests action steps

Key advantage: The system learns from your corrections, improving its accuracy by up to 32% within the first month of use.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Connect the OpenAI module to process email content. We use a custom prompt that considers your industry-specific priorities when evaluating urgency.

Step 3: Decision Router

Create filters based on the AI's priority score. Only high-priority emails should proceed to the notification stage.

Step 4: Telegram Integration

Use Make.com's Telegram bot to format and send alerts. Include deep links back to the original email for one-tap access.
