How Point-Based Scoring Works

The secret sauce is a transparent scoring system that evaluates responses objectively. Here's how it works in the template:

  1. Question 1: "How many employees are on your team today?"
    - 50+ employees = 5 points
    - 20-50 employees = 3 points
    - Under 20 = 1 point
  2. Question 2: "What's your primary business challenge?"
    - Matches your specialty = 5 points
    - Adjacent need = 3 points
    - Unrelated = 1 point
  3. Question 3: Budget/timeframe question tailored to your offering

The AI tallies points automatically. At 10+ points (adjustable threshold), the lead gets routed to sales. Below that, they receive customized resources based on their responses.
