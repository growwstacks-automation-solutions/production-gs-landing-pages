The Hospitality Benefits Challenge

For hospitality employees working long, irregular shifts across multiple locations, benefits enrollment has traditionally been a frustrating experience. Unlike standard 9-to-5 workers who can attend scheduled benefits seminars or call HR during business hours, hotel staff often miss critical information about their coverage options.

Eric, a guest relations associate featured in the case study, represents this challenge perfectly. Between serving guests and working overnight shifts, he had limited time to research health plans, understand coverage changes, or determine the best options for his family. This left many employees making rushed decisions or sticking with default options that might not meet their needs.

72% of hospitality workers report difficulty understanding their benefits options, compared to just 38% of office workers, according to recent HR industry surveys.

The AI-Powered Solution

The resort partnered with Datavail to implement an AI benefits advisor built on Oracle Fusion Cloud HCM. This solution addresses the unique needs of hospitality workers by providing:

  • 24/7 access to personalized benefits guidance
  • Natural language understanding of complex questions
  • Integration with all benefits documents and eligibility rules
  • Personalized responses based on each employee's data

Unlike static FAQs or PDF guides, the AI agent dynamically analyzes policy documents, plan details, and the employee's specific situation to provide tailored answers. As shown in the video at 1:15, when Eric asks about travel coverage, the system doesn't just quote the policy—it explains how it applies to his upcoming trip.

How the AI Benefits Advisor Works

The system combines three powerful technologies to deliver exceptional employee experiences:

1. Document Intelligence

The AI ingests and understands all benefits documents, including plan guides, policy details, and eligibility rules. This creates a comprehensive knowledge base that's always up-to-date.

2. Personalization Engine

By connecting to Oracle HCM, the system accesses each employee's personal data—dependents, current elections, and eligibility status—to tailor every response.

3. Conversational Interface

Employees can ask questions naturally, like "What's new this year?" or "Are my dependents covered?" The AI interprets the intent and provides clear, actionable answers.

Key Feature: When Eric asked about LASIK coverage (shown at 2:30 in the video), the AI didn't just say yes or no—it outlined next steps, eligibility requirements, and estimated costs specific to his plan.

  • Confidence that answers are personalized to their situation

Implementation Process

Datavail's implementation followed a structured approach to ensure success:

Phase 1: Knowledge Base Development

All benefits documents, policies, and eligibility rules were processed and structured for the AI system.

Phase 2: Integration with Oracle HCM

The solution connected to existing HR systems to access real-time employee data while maintaining strict security protocols.

Phase 3: Testing & Training

HR teams and employee groups tested the system with hundreds of sample questions, refining responses and improving accuracy.

Phase 4: Launch & Adoption

The resort introduced the AI advisor through multiple channels, emphasizing its 24/7 availability and personalized guidance.
