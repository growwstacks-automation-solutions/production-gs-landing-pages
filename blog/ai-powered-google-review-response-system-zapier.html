System Overview: What We're Building

Our AI-powered system solves this problem automatically. When a new Google review appears, the workflow:

  1. Captures the review text and star rating
  2. Sends it to AI with instructions for appropriate response tone
  3. Generates a professional reply in under 90 words
  4. Records everything in Google Sheets for transparency

The business owner can then copy-paste the response or extend the automation to post directly (with optional human approval). Either way, every review gets a timely, appropriate response without manual effort.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Step 1: Create Your Google Sheet

Set up a simple spreadsheet with three columns: Review Text (Column A), Star Rating (Column B), and AI Response (Column C). This becomes your central record of all reviews and responses.

Step 2: Get Your OpenAI API Key

Sign up at platform.openai.com, navigate to API keys, and generate a new secret key. This allows Zapier to send review text to AI and receive responses back.

Step 3: Create Your Zap in Zapier

Set the trigger to "New Spreadsheet Row" in your Google Sheet. Connect your Google account and select the sheet you created. Test that Zapier detects new rows correctly.

Step 4: Add the AI Action

In Zapier, add an OpenAI "Send Prompt" action. Use this professional prompt template:

AI Prompt: "You are a professional customer support assistant. Generate a polite and professional response to the following Google review. Star rating: [Rating]. Review text: [Text]. If rating is 5 stars, thank the customer warmly. If below 3 stars, acknowledge concern and invite them back. Keep response under 90 words."

Step 5: Send AI Response Back to Google Sheets

Add a final step to update the spreadsheet row with the AI-generated response in Column C. Test that adding a review manually triggers the full workflow.
