The Missed Call Crisis

Most business owners don't realize how many potential customers they're losing to unanswered calls. The statistics are staggering: 80% of callers who reach voicemail don't leave a message, and 60% of those who do wait will hang up within one or two minutes. Only 37.8% of small business calls are actually answered at all.

For local service businesses like pest control, this translates to thousands of dollars in lost revenue every week. The problem is especially acute because 70% of callers are calling directly from search results when they're ready to buy. If you don't answer that call instantly, they'll simply call your competitor instead.

The hidden cost: You usually don't even know what opportunities you're missing. Calls come in when you're knee-deep in a job, on the road, or helping another customer. By the time you're free, the caller is gone - and so is your chance to earn their business.

Meet Pop the AI Raccoon

This is exactly why Pest Ops Pro, a pest control company in Ontario, hired an unlikely employee - Pop, an AI raccoon receptionist. Pop isn't trying to replace humans; he fills the gaps that humans can't cover. He answers every call instantly, never misses a ring, and provides consistent, on-brand service 24/7.

As Frank, the owner of Pest Ops Pro explains: "I'm a one-man crew. It's hard for me sometimes to get to the phone. After introducing Pop, we don't miss any calls anymore." What makes Pop special is that he's not trying to sound human - he's a memorable raccoon character that customers enjoy talking to, making the brand more memorable.

How Pop Works

Pop does much more than just answer calls. He's trained with all the pest control knowledge that a brand new human receptionist wouldn't have on day one. For example, when a customer calls about a bat in their garage, Pop knows to ask about:

  • Whether anyone touched the bat (rabies risk)
  • If it's acting strangely
  • The location and duration of the sighting

He can explain that bat droppings can cause histoplasmosis (a serious lung infection) and damage insulation and wiring. This specialized knowledge allows Pop to gather critical details before the owner even calls back, ensuring they know exactly what they're walking into.

Real Call Example

In a live demo (watch at 3:12 in the video), a customer calls about a bat in their garage. Pop immediately establishes rapport: "Hey, this is Pop from Pest Ops Pro. I'm the raccoon mascot around here. Frank is probably up in an attic somewhere catching critters like me."

He then:

  1. Assures the customer they can help
  2. Gathers key details about the situation
  3. Provides important safety information
  4. Schedules a callback from Frank

The entire interaction feels natural, helpful, and perfectly aligned with the brand's voice - something no generic voicemail or answering service could achieve.

Setting Up Your Own AI Receptionist

Pop is built on Vendasta's AI workforce platform, which allows businesses to create customized AI employees. Setting up your own AI receptionist involves:

  1. Choosing a name and uploading an avatar image
  2. Selecting from multiple voice options
  3. Defining their purpose and tone
  4. Configuring call handling rules
  5. Uploading business knowledge (website content, FAQs, safety info)

The platform comes with built-in capabilities for lead collection, appointment booking, and follow-ups. You can review call transcripts and recordings to continuously improve your AI receptionist's performance.

The Business Impact

For Pest Ops Pro, Pop has been transformative. Frank estimates Pop saves him "hundreds and hundreds of missed calls that otherwise would have probably gone to a competitor." The benefits extend beyond just answering calls:

  • 24/7 availability with consistent quality
  • Ability to handle multiple calls simultaneously
  • Specialized knowledge instantly available to customers
  • Detailed call information prepared for human follow-up

Perhaps most importantly, Pop allows the human team to focus on what they do best - serving customers - while ensuring no opportunity slips through the cracks.
