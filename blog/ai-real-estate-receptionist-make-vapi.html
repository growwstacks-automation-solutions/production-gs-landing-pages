How the AI Receptionist Works

The system handles three critical functions that traditionally require human staff:

1. Lead Qualification

When a call comes in, the AI determines whether the caller is a buyer, seller, or both. It captures their budget range, timeline, and specific needs - all information agents typically have to manually collect.

2. Calendar Scheduling

The assistant checks Google Calendar for availability, suggests times that work for both parties, and books appointments complete with property details and automatic confirmations.

3. Common Questions

It answers FAQs about commissions, office hours, and services - questions that often tie up receptionists but don't actually require human expertise.

Key differentiator: Unlike basic voicemail systems, this solution actively engages callers, qualifies them as leads, and converts inquiries into concrete next steps - all without human intervention.

Calendar Integration

The Google Calendar integration solves one of the biggest pain points in real estate - scheduling conflicts. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Check Availability

When a caller requests an appointment (like at 6:52 in the demo), the system:

  1. Queries Google Calendar via Vapi's integration
  2. Identifies all busy slots for the requested day
  3. Calculates available 45-minute windows

Step 2: Book Appointment

Once the caller confirms a time:

  1. The system creates a calendar event with "Property Viewing" title
  2. Includes the caller's contact information in the description
  3. Sends automatic confirmation details to both parties

Pro Tip: We configure all bookings as 45-minute slots. This prevents back-to-back appointments from running over while allowing buffer time between showings.

Real-Time Email Notifications

No system works if agents don't know about new leads. That's where Make.com comes in:

The Notification Workflow

  1. Vapi triggers a webhook to Make.com after each call (11:30 in the video)
  2. Make.com formats the call details into a clean email
  3. The email includes:
    • Caller's name and contact information
    • Whether they booked an appointment
    • Key details about their needs
  4. The system sends it directly to the agent's inbox
