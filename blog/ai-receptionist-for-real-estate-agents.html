How the AI Receptionist Works

The system answers calls instantly with a natural human voice (you choose male/female/tone) and handles the entire initial conversation:

  1. Greeting: Identifies your agency and asks how it can help
  2. Lead capture: Gets caller's name, number, and property details
  3. Qualification: Asks budget range, timeline, and preferred locations
  4. Scheduling: Checks live calendar availability and books viewings
  5. Confirmation: Sends SMS/email with details and next steps
