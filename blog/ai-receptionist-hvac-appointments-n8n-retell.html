Call Flow Breakdown

The AI follows a proven call script optimized for HVAC service requests:

Step 1: Problem Identification

"What's going on with your heating or cooling system today?" This open-ended question lets callers describe their issue naturally.

Step 2: Customer Information

The AI collects name, phone number, and service address — confirming each detail aloud to prevent errors.

Step 3: Appointment Booking

It offers your standard service windows (e.g., "8-10 AM or 2-4 PM") and books the preferred slot.
