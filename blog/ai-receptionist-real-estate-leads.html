The traditional solution—hiring a receptionist—creates new problems. Even the most dedicated employee can't work 24/7, and staffing costs eat into commissions. This is why forward-thinking agents are turning to AI receptionists that never sleep, never take breaks, and handle hundreds of calls simultaneously.

Key stat: Properties shown within 24 hours of inquiry sell for 3-5% higher prices on average. AI receptionists ensure no lead waits longer than 60 seconds for a response.

The AI receptionist combines three powerful technologies:
