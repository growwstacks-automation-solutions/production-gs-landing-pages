The n8n workflow (8:45 in the video) acts as the central nervous system of your AI calling operation. It performs several critical functions:

Call Pacing Strategy

To avoid spam flags and ensure deliverability, the system includes smart pacing controls (14:30 in the video):

  • 1 call per minute maximum pacing
  • Randomized call times within business hours
  • Automatic timezone detection
  • Caller ID rotation (when multiple numbers available)

This careful pacing maintains under 1% call drop rates - critical for keeping your numbers off blocklists. The wait node shown at 15:10 ensures the system never overwhelms carrier networks.
