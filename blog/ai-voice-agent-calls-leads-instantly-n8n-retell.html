Key insight: At 100 leads/month, delayed follow-up costs $10,000-$20,000 in lost revenue. For 500 leads, this grows to $50,000+ - all recoverable with instant contact automation.

How the Instant Call System Works

The solution combines n8n's workflow automation with Retell AI's conversational voice technology. When someone submits your contact form:

  1. n8n captures the form data (name, phone, inquiry details)
  2. Retell AI calls the lead within 60 seconds using a human-like voice
  3. AI agent qualifies the lead through natural conversation
  4. Data saves to your CRM/Google Sheets for follow-up

Unlike chatbots that feel robotic, Retell's AI adapts to responses naturally - asking follow-up questions about project timelines, budgets, and specific needs exactly as a top sales rep would.

n8n Form Trigger Setup (Step-by-Step)

The workflow begins with an n8n form trigger. Here's how to configure it:

Step 1: Create the the Form

In your n8n workflow, add an "n8n Form Trigger" node. Configure these fields:

  • Form Title: "Contact Us"
  • Required Fields: Name (required), Email (required), Phone (required with country code), Reason (required)

Step 2: Test Form Submission

Execute the node and submit test data to verify capture. The phone field must include country code (e.g., +1 for US) for Retell to call successfully.

Pro Tip: Use n8n's "HTTP Request" node to connect to Retell's API. Set method to POST with these headers:

  • Authorization: Bearer [Retell_API_Key]
  • Content-Type: application/json
