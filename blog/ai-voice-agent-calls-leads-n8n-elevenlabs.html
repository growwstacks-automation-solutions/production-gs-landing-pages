System Architecture: n8n + ElevenLabs

The complete solution uses three integrated components:

1. n8n Contact Form Trigger - Captures lead details and initiates the workflow

2. ElevenLabs Voice Agent - Handles the call with dynamic personalization

3. Twilio Telephony - Routes the call to real phone numbers

The magic happens in the handoff between systems. n8n passes the lead's name, number, and context to ElevenLabs, which personalizes the conversation while maintaining perfect compliance with call recording laws.
