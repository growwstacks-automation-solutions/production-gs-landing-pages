How the AI Calling System Works

The system combines three powerful no-code tools: Vapi for the voice AI, n8n for workflow automation, and Google Sheets as the temporary CRM. Here's the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Lead List Preparation

Your leads go into a Google Sheet with columns for name, phone, reason for calling, and status. The system only calls leads marked "ready."

Step 2: AI Call Initiation

n8n checks the sheet every 15 minutes for new leads. When it finds one, it triggers Vapi to make the call with the lead's personalized details.

Step 3: Conversation Analysis

After each call, Vapi sends the transcript to n8n, where AI extracts key details like interest level, preferred timing, and special notes.

Step 4: CRM Update

The system updates the Google Sheet (or your CRM) with call outcomes and next steps, then moves to the next lead automatically.

In summary: The AI calls leads → has natural conversations → analyzes outcomes → updates your systems → repeats. All without human involvement.

Building the n8n Automation

The n8n workflow (shown at 6:15) has two main parts:

1. Call Initiator: Checks Google Sheets for leads ready to call → updates their status → triggers Vapi with the lead's details → waits for completion.

2. Result Processor: Receives the call transcript from Vapi → uses AI to extract key information → updates the CRM → triggers the next call.

At 7:30, you'll see how the AI analyzes call transcripts to determine interest level, note special requests, and decide follow-up actions - all automatically.
