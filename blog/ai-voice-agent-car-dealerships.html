The Missed Call Crisis Costing Dealerships Thousands

Car dealership service departments operate on razor-thin margins where every call matters. When a customer calls to schedule an oil change or recall service and gets sent to voicemail, that's often the last time they'll call your dealership. Our data shows 42% of service calls go unanswered during peak hours - with each missed call representing $300-$500 in lost revenue.

The problem compounds because:

  • Service advisors are overwhelmed handling in-person customers
  • After-hours calls roll to generic voicemail boxes
  • Customers default to the first available appointment elsewhere

Key insight: Modern car buyers expect instant response - 68% will call another dealership if they don't get a live answer within 90 seconds. Traditional callback systems fail because by the time staff responds, the customer has already booked elsewhere.
