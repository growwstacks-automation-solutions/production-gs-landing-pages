How the AI Handles Calls (Step-by-Step)

The demo shows a complete customer interaction from greeting to booking confirmation. Here's what happens behind the scenes:

Step 1: Professional Greeting

The AI answers within 2 rings using a customized greeting ("Hi, I'm Mary, your AI receptionist at Demo Plumber"). This establishes trust immediately.

Step 2: Active Listening

Advanced speech recognition interprets the customer's issue (burst pipe) and responds with appropriate empathy ("I'm really sorry to hear that").
