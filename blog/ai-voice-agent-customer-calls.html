The Hidden Cost of Missed Calls

Every unanswered business call represents more than just a missed conversation - it's lost revenue walking out the door. Research shows that 62% of business calls go unanswered, creating a silent leak in your sales pipeline. Even worse, 85% of those callers never call back, taking their business to competitors who answer the phone.

For service businesses like plumbers, electricians, HVAC companies, and medical practices, this call answering crisis hits particularly hard. When a pipe bursts at 9 PM or an AC fails on a weekend, customers need immediate answers - not voicemail. Traditional solutions like hiring after-hours staff or outsourcing to call centers are expensive and often provide inconsistent service.

The math is clear: If your business receives 100 calls per week and misses 62 of them, with an average job value of $300, you're potentially losing $18,600 per week in missed opportunities - nearly $1 million annually.
