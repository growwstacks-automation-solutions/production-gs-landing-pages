Vapi Setup: Creating Your AI Receptionist

Vapi provides the voice interface that makes this solution possible. Setting up the basic agent takes just minutes:

  1. Create a Vapi account and navigate to the Assistants section
  2. Select GPT-4 as the language model (optimal for medical terminology)
  3. Choose a voice that matches your practice's brand personality
  4. Configure the call handling mode to "speak first" for professional greetings

Crafting the Perfect Medical AI System Prompt

The system prompt defines your AI's personality, boundaries, and capabilities. For medical use, it requires three critical components:

1. Role Definition: "You are Donna, the virtual assistant for Croft Medical Clinic. Your role is to provide information about our services and schedule appointments - never medical advice."

2. Safety Rules: This non-negotiable section immediately escalates any clinical questions: "If the caller describes symptoms or asks for medical advice, respond: 'I cannot provide medical advice. Would you like me to connect you with our on-call staff?'"

3. Capabilities: Clearly list what the AI can do: answer FAQs, book appointments, explain procedures (from approved documents), and escalate urgent concerns.

The n8n Appointment Booking Workflow

The real magic happens in the n8n workflow that connects Vapi to your calendar system. Here's how it works when a patient requests an appointment:

Step 1: Availability Check

The AI collects preferred date/time and sends this to n8n, which queries your calendar system (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.) via API.

Step 2: Slot Confirmation

If available, n8n reserves the slot temporarily while confirming details with the patient. This prevents double-booking race conditions.

Step 3: Record Creation

Once confirmed, n8n creates the appointment in your EHR system and sends a confirmation SMS/email to the patient.

Efficiency note: The entire process averages just 4.7 seconds - faster than human staff can open their scheduling software.

Bulletproof Error Handling for Healthcare

Medical workflows demand exceptional reliability. We implemented three layers of protection:

  1. Input validation: Every data point (name, phone, requested time) is validated before processing
  2. Transaction logging: All interactions are recorded in Airtable for audit trails
  3. Graceful failure: If any step fails, the system explains the issue and offers alternative solutions

At 12:41 in the video, you'll see how we handle edge cases like duplicate bookings or invalid phone numbers without losing the patient.

Maintaining HIPAA Compliance

This solution achieves HIPAA compliance through four mechanisms:

1. Data Minimization: Vapi only retains call transcripts for 30 days unless flagged for review

2. Secure Storage: All PHI flows directly to your EHR system, never persisting in Vapi

3. Access Controls: n8n workflows require authentication to interact with patient data

4. Audit Trails: Every action is logged with timestamps and user/agent identifiers
