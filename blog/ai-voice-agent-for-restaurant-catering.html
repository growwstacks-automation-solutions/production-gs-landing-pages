How the AI Handles Catering Orders

The AI follows a structured but natural conversation flow optimized for restaurant orders:

  1. Greeting & Identification: "Hi, thanks for calling Subway catering. I can help you set up a catering order for your event."
  2. Date/Time Capture: "What date is your event and what time would you like your order ready?"
  3. Party Size: "How many people are you ordering for?"
  4. Menu Selection: Presents platter options and automatically suggests add-ons to reach order minimums
  5. Special Requests: Flags any unusual requests (like all-tuna platters) for manager follow-up
  6. Confirmation: Repeats back all details and collects phone number

At 2:45 in the video, watch how it smoothly handles the customer's request for "ten tuna sandwiches"—adapting the standard mixed platter while still enforcing the $50 minimum.

Handling Special Requests

The AI doesn't just follow a rigid script—it intelligently adapts to special requests while keeping orders within policy guidelines. When the customer asks for an all-tuna platter (unlike the standard mixed assortment), the agent:

  • Notes the deviation from standard practice
  • Confirms the modification clearly
  • Verifies it still meets the $50 minimum
  • Flags it for manager review while completing the order

This balances customer service with operational requirements—something human staff often struggle with during busy periods.

Automatic Order Confirmation

Before completing each order, the AI:

  1. Repeats all key details back to the customer
  2. Collects a phone number for manager follow-up
  3. Provides clear next steps ("Our manager will reach out shortly")

This creates a complete audit trail while reassuring customers their order is being handled properly. The entire conversation is transcribed and stored with the order record.

POS System Integration

The AI doesn't operate in isolation—it integrates directly with the restaurant's POS system:

  • Orders flow directly into the kitchen display system
  • Special requests are flagged for manager review
  • Customer contact info populates the CRM
  • Order minimums and pricing sync automatically

This eliminates manual data entry errors and ensures the kitchen receives accurate, complete orders every time.
