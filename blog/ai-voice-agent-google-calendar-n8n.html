Traditional IVR systems frustrate callers with rigid menu trees. Modern AI voice agents understand natural language, making them ideal for scheduling conversations. Here's how the workflow functions:

  2. ElevenLabs AI parses the intent and checks Google Calendar via n8n
  3. The agent suggests available times in a conversational flow
  4. Once confirmed, n8n creates the calendar event and sends confirmation
