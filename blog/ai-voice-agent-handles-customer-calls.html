How the AI Handles Complex Scheduling

The demo's most impressive moment comes at 2:30 when the caller asks about Wednesday availability — a specific time outside the initially offered slots. The AI:

  1. Verifies the request ("Let me check availability for Wednesday at 3 PM")
  2. Returns multiple options (3 PM and 4 PM) when available
  3. Confirms details precisely ("Just to confirm, your email is L-I-N...")

This shows the system's ability to integrate with existing scheduling tools while maintaining natural conversation flow. The confirmation step reduces booking errors that plague manual scheduling.

Implementation note: These systems connect to your Google Calendar, Calendly, or custom scheduling software to pull real-time availability without manual updates.
