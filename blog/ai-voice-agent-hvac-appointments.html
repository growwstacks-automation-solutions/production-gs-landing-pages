How Our AI Voice Agent Works for HVAC Companies

The AI agent acts as your virtual receptionist, available 24/7 to answer calls in your business name. Unlike generic IVR systems, it conducts natural conversations tailored to HVAC service requests - understanding urgent issues like no cooling, strange noises, or burning smells.

The Smart Lead Qualification Process

Not all HVAC calls require immediate service. The AI agent intelligently qualifies leads using your business rules - separating true emergencies from routine maintenance requests or price shoppers.

For urgent issues (no cooling in summer, burning smells), it books same-day or next-day appointments. For non-urgent requests, it schedules at your next available routine slot. The system can even collect credit card information to reduce no-shows for after-hours calls.

Automated SMS & Email Notification System

Immediately after booking, the system sends two confirmation touchpoints:

  • SMS to customer: Appointment time, service address, and technician contact info
  • Email to dispatch: Full lead details including problem description and customer notes

These automated notifications eliminate manual data entry errors and ensure everyone - from the customer to your techs - has the same information. The system can also send reminder messages 1 hour before appointments to reduce no-show rates.
