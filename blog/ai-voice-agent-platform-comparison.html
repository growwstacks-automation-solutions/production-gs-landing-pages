How We Evaluated These Platforms

Most AI voice platform comparisons focus on superficial features or synthetic benchmarks. We took a different approach - testing each platform with real business use cases across 400,000+ customer interactions. Our evaluation criteria reflect what actually matters when deploying voice AI at scale.

We assessed each platform across four critical dimensions: company team quality (trust, funding, and long-term viability), ease of use and setup complexity, core platform capabilities, and cost structures. This holistic approach reveals which platforms can truly support business operations versus those that look good in demos but fail in production.

Key insight: The "best" platform depends entirely on your specific use case and technical capabilities. An enterprise call center has completely different needs than a startup testing its first voice bot.
