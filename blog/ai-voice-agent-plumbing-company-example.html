The Missed Call Crisis in Service Businesses

For plumbing, HVAC, and electrical businesses, every missed call represents hundreds of dollars in lost revenue. When a pipe bursts or an AC fails, customers call the first available service - and if you don't answer, they'll call your competitor instead.

Traditional answering services often fail because they can't handle complex scheduling or provide accurate information. This leaves business owners with an impossible choice: pay for expensive 24/7 staff or lose potential customers.

62% of service calls go unanswered according to industry research, and most of those callers won't call back. For a plumbing business doing $500,000 annually, this could mean leaving $100,000+ on the table from missed opportunities.
