The Lead Qualification Problem

Toronto real estate agent Raj was spending $5,000+/month on Facebook ads generating 20-50 leads daily across multiple campaigns. But his leads were scattered across different ad accounts and forms, with incorrect CRM tagging causing further confusion.

His virtual assistant made 80+ calls daily, but half went unanswered. Of those who answered, most requested emailed information or said "call me later." Even booked appointments often no-showed. Raj still ended up wasting hours each day calling unqualified leads himself.

Only 10-15% of Facebook real estate leads are serious buyers: The other 85-90% waste agents' time with endless calls and follow-ups. This AI solution automatically filters out the tire-kickers so agents only talk to ready-to-buy prospects.

How The AI Agent Works

Within 90 seconds of a lead submitting any Facebook form, the AI voice agent calls them with a natural-sounding human voice. It asks key qualification questions:

  • Budget range for the purchase
  • Number of bedrooms needed
  • Preferred property types
  • Target neighborhoods
  • First-time buyer status
  • Purchase timeline

The conversation flows naturally, handling interruptions like "call me later" gracefully. The AI detects these cues and tags the lead appropriately without human intervention.

Three-Part Automation System

This solution combines three powerful components working together seamlessly:

1. Centralized Lead Capture

All leads from 20+ Facebook ads flow into one master Google Sheet automatically, eliminating scattered data across multiple forms and ad accounts.

2. AI Qualification Agent

The voice AI calls within 90 seconds, conducts natural conversations, and determines lead quality based on responses to key questions.

3. Automated Sorting & Alerts

Qualified leads get moved to a "Hot Prospects" tab automatically, with instant WhatsApp notifications to the agent including call recordings and summaries.

Every interaction is logged automatically: Call recordings, transcripts, qualification summaries, and disposition reasons all populate the sheet in real-time for complete visibility.
