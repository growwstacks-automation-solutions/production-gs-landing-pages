Making It Sound Human: Voice Customization Secrets

The difference between an obvious robot and a believable receptionist comes down to four audio elements:

  1. Background Noise: Custom office ambiance (not the default VAPI noise) creates subconscious authenticity
  2. Speech Patterns: Natural pauses, conversational fillers ("um", "let me see"), and dynamic pacing
  3. Emotional Range: Tone adjustments based on conversation context (excitement for appointments, empathy for complaints)
  4. Interruption Handling: 0.2-second stop speaking threshold allows natural back-and-forth
