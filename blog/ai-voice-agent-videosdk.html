The Problem With Traditional Customer Service

Most businesses struggle with two fundamental customer service challenges: missed calls that turn into lost sales, and expensive human time wasted on routine interactions. When customers call about abandoned carts or product questions outside business hours, they often get voicemail or no answer at all.

Even during working hours, simple inquiries like payment issues or stock availability tie up staff who could be focusing on high-value conversations. This creates a lose-lose situation where customers get frustrated and businesses lose revenue opportunities.

68% of customers will abandon a purchase if they can't get quick answers to simple questions. An AI voice agent solves this by being available 24/7 to handle routine inquiries instantly.
