How AI Voice Agents Actually Work

The demo reveals several technical breakthroughs that make today's voice agents fundamentally different from older chatbots or IVR systems. When a call comes in (whether inbound or outbound), the system:

Step 1: Voice Processing

Converts speech to text in real-time using advanced speech recognition models trained on business vocabulary. Modern systems achieve 95-98% accuracy even with background noise.

Step 2: Intent Recognition

Analyzes the conversation to determine the caller's intent - whether they're applying for a loan, scheduling an appointment, or requesting support. The system dynamically adjusts its responses based on this understanding.

Step 3: Workflow Execution

Follows predefined business rules to collect information, answer questions, or complete tasks. In the demo, the agent processes a loan application by verifying eligibility and explaining next steps.

Step 4: Action Triggering

Integrates with other systems to send follow-up emails, update CRM records, or even initiate payments - all without human involvement.

Key differentiator: These aren't simple decision trees. The AI understands context, remembers previous interactions, and handles unexpected questions by accessing knowledge bases in real-time.
