The $150B Call Center Problem AI Solves

Businesses spend over $150 billion annually on call centers staffed by human agents who spend 70% of their time answering the same basic questions. Restaurant hosts repeat availability, medical offices confirm appointments, and service businesses field calls asking for hours and pricing—all tasks that could be automated.

Traditional IVR systems frustrate customers with endless menu trees. Telentir's conversational AI solves this by understanding natural language requests and responding with human-like voices from 11 Labs and OpenAI. The system handles routine inquiries while flagging complex cases for human agents.

Key stat: Early adopters report 60% cost reductions in call handling while maintaining 92% customer satisfaction scores—higher than their previous human-staffed centers.

Inside Telentir's AI Voice Agent Platform

The dashboard provides real-time visibility into all call activity—inbound, outbound, and text messages. Live monitoring shows active calls with sentiment analysis, while the call history logs every interaction for quality assurance and training.

Contacts management organizes leads and customers, with AI tools to generate new prospect lists for outbound campaigns. The platform automatically logs all interactions against contact records, creating a complete communication history.

How to Create AI Agents in 4 Steps

Step 1: Name Your Agent

Choose a name that represents your brand voice—friendly professional for medical offices, energetic for restaurants, or authoritative for legal services.

Step 2: Select Language & Persona

Pick from multiple natural-sounding voices across genders and accents. The platform lets you test each option before deployment.

Step 3: Choose AI Model

Balance cost and quality by selecting between OpenAI and 11 Labs voice models based on your call volume and quality requirements.

Step 4: Configure Human Handoff

Define when calls should escalate to human agents, such as when a customer says "representative" or when sentiment analysis detects frustration.

Pro tip: Create multiple agents for different departments or languages, all managed from the same dashboard.

Training Your AI With Custom Knowledge Bases

The system becomes truly powerful when fed your specific business information. Upload PDF manuals, scrape your website, or paste text directly to teach the AI about your services, pricing, and policies.

For restaurants, this means menu items and hours. For medical practices, it's insurance accepted and appointment protocols. The AI references this knowledge to answer customer questions accurately without generic responses.

Designing Effective Conversation Pathways

Pre-built templates handle common scenarios—appointment scheduling, lead qualification, order status checks. Each pathway consists of decision nodes that branch based on customer responses.

The visual editor lets you customize flows by adding SMS confirmations, email follow-ups, or Calendly booking links. For complex businesses, you can build entirely custom conversation trees matching your exact call handling processes.

Seamless Human Handoff When Needed

When a call requires human intervention, the system provides the agent with the full conversation history and customer details. This eliminates the "Can you repeat that?" frustration of traditional call transfers.

Agents see real-time sentiment analysis and AI-suggested responses to maintain consistency. The platform even suggests when to offer discounts or special accommodations based on the caller's tone and history.

Running Inbound & Outbound Campaigns

For inbound calls, configure which agents handle specific numbers and what pathways to use. Route calls by time of day, caller history, or IVR selections.

Outbound campaigns can use uploaded contact lists or AI-generated leads. The system qualifies prospects through natural conversation before passing warm leads to sales teams with complete interaction transcripts.

Integration With Your Existing Tools

Native Calendly integration lets the AI book appointments directly into your calendar. API connections push lead data to CRMs like Salesforce or HubSpot.

The team can build custom integrations for your specific booking systems, POS platforms, or internal databases—ensuring the AI has real-time access to availability, pricing, and customer records.
