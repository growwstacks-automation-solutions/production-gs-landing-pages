The Collector's Dilemma

Every business owner knows the sinking feeling of unpaid invoices. What starts as polite reminders escalates into hours of awkward phone calls that drain energy and morale. But the real cost isn't just the discomfort - it's the 10 hours per week most small businesses lose to collections.

This creates a vicious cycle where owners sacrifice growth activities to chase money they've already earned. While competitors invest in expansion, you're stuck playing accountant - a role most entrepreneurs didn't sign up for.

Hidden cost: For every hour spent collecting, that's an hour not spent acquiring new customers or improving operations. At 10 hours weekly, that's 520 hours annually - enough time to launch a new product line or enter a new market.
