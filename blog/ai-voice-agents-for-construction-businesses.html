Automating Review Generation

Most contractors know reviews matter, but struggle with consistent collection. The breakthrough comes from triggering automated review requests the moment a job is marked complete in your system. This happens via:

  • QR codes on estimator tablets
  • SMS links in payment confirmation texts
  • Email follow-ups with one-click review buttons

One fence contractor increased reviews by 300% in 90 days using this system. The AI doesn't just collect reviews - it posts them to social profiles, responds appropriately, and displays them on your website automatically.

Turning Past Clients Into Revenue

Construction businesses spend thousands on ads to acquire new customers while ignoring their existing client database. Reactivation automations can turn this goldmine into consistent revenue by:

  • Segmenting clients by service type and recency
  • Sending maintenance reminders at optimal intervals
  • Offering exclusive upgrades to past customers
  • Providing referral incentives

One electrical contractor generated $28,000 in additional revenue within 60 days simply by automating their client reactivation sequence.
