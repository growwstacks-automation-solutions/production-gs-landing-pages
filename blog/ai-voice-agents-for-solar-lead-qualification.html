The silent killer: Most solar companies don't realize how many leads they're losing. Without 24/7 tracking, these missed opportunities disappear into the void — while marketing budgets keep pouring money into a leaky funnel.

The AI agent in our demo (1:15) follows a proven qualification sequence that filters out tire-kickers while identifying ready-to-buy prospects:
