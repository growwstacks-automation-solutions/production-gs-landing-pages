The Silent Renewal Crisis in Insurance

Insurance agencies lose millions annually from passive policy churn - not because customers actively cancel, but because no one reminds them to renew. Traditional renewal processes rely on emails that get ignored or overworked agents who can't call every client. The result? 25-40% of policies lapse unnecessarily, representing massive lost revenue.

Small and midsize agencies suffer most. Without large call center teams, they struggle to provide personalized renewal outreach. As one agency owner confessed: "We see our customers slip away during renewals because competitors reach out more proactively." This silent attrition happens gradually but compounds over time.

Key insight: 68% of policyholders would renew if contacted personally, yet only 12% receive proactive calls. The gap represents a $32 billion opportunity in the US P&C market alone.
