The financial impact is staggering: at an average deal size of $2,500 (like Nexus Events in our example), losing just 5 qualified leads per month means $12,500 in missed revenue - every single month.

Key insight: The first 90 seconds of a lead conversation determine whether it converts. AI voice agents never rush, never forget to ask qualifying questions, and never fail to book the next step.

After confirming Sarah's availability ("Anytime afternoon"), the agent found and booked a specific time slot (2 p.m.) while simultaneously creating the calendar event.
