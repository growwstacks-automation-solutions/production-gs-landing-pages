How the Voice AI Agent Works

The voice agent combines several advanced technologies to deliver human-like phone interactions. At its core is a large language model (like GPT-4) that handles the conversation flow and decision making. This connects to speech recognition for understanding callers and text-to-speech for natural responses.

Key components:

  1. Natural Language Processing: Understands caller intent regardless of phrasing
  2. Contextual Memory: Maintains conversation context throughout the call
  3. Business Logic: Accesses pricing, availability, and policy information
  4. Integration APIs: Connects to calendars, CRMs and other business systems
