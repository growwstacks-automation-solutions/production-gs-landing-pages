Lead Reactivation Supercharge

Every real estate professional has hundreds - often thousands - of past clients sitting untouched in their CRM. These cold leads represent a goldmine of referrals and repeat business, but manual outreach is painfully time-consuming. The lead reactivation supercharge changes this equation completely.

This AI voice agent systematically works through your entire contact database, having natural conversations to check in on past clients. It identifies potential referrals, qualifies interest in new transactions, and even books appointments - all without human intervention.

720 hours saved annually: At scale, this single agent saves the equivalent of 18 work weeks per year while generating new commissions from previously dormant relationships. Field tests show it pays for itself within 3 months through revived client connections alone.

How It Works:

  1. Personalized outreach: The AI analyzes each contact's history and crafts tailored opening messages (e.g., "How's your home on Pacific Drive?")
  2. Natural conversation flow: Handles objections, follows emotional cues, and navigates complex dialogue just like a top-performing human agent
  3. Automated scheduling: Books qualified leads directly into your calendar with full context for seamless handoffs
  4. CRM integration: Logs all interactions and updates lead scores automatically

Quick Draw Closer

Speed kills in real estate lead response. The average 3-24 hour response window means most agents lose 50% or more of their hottest leads before making first contact. The quick draw closer eliminates this leak forever.

This specialized AI voice agent responds to new leads within 60 seconds of submission - whether from ads, website forms, or open house signups. It conducts initial qualification, provides immediate value, and books appointments while the lead is most engaged.

50% conversion lift: By reaching leads during their peak interest window (first 5 minutes), this agent consistently outperforms human teams in appointment booking rates. The system also never gets tired, never forgets to follow up, and works 24/7/365.

Key Features:

  • Instant response: 60-second call-back guarantee from lead submission
  • Smart qualification: Asks the right questions to gauge motivation and timeline
  • Value-first approach: Immediately sends tailored resources (e.g., neighborhood guides, checklists)
  • Seamless handoff: Books appointments directly with human agents when appropriate

Luxury Concierge

This hybrid chat/voice agent integrates directly with your communication channels. Simply message what needs done ("Follow up with James about Pacific Drive listing") and the AI handles the rest - making calls, gathering information, and reporting back with results.

300-480 hours reclaimed: That's 7-12 work weeks annually given back to focus on high-value activities. The concierge never forgets a task, never drops the ball on follow-ups, and maintains perfect professionalism with every interaction.

Daily Use Cases:

  • Client follow-ups: Check in on recent showings, gather feedback
  • Schedule management: Reschedule appointments, confirm availability
  • Information gathering: Pre-qualify leads before human contact
  • Task delegation: Coordinate with transaction coordinators, photographers, etc.

Inbound Opportunity Patrol

Phone calls remain the lifeblood of real estate, yet most agents miss 30-50% of incoming opportunities due to missed calls, long hold times, or after-hours inquiries. The inbound opportunity patrol solves this permanently with 24/7 call answering that feels completely human.

This AI voice agent answers every incoming call instantly - whether at 2pm or 2am. It qualifies the caller, provides immediate value, and either handles the request or books a callback with a human agent. No more voicemails, no more frustrated callers, no more lost business.

5-second response time: Compared to the industry's 3-24 hour average, this lightning-fast response creates an immediate luxury service impression. Callers get help when they need it most, dramatically increasing conversion rates and client satisfaction.

How It Transforms Call Handling:

  1. Instant answering: No ringing, no hold music, no voicemail
  2. Natural conversation: Indistinguishable from human receptionists
  3. Smart routing: Escalates urgent calls while handling routine inquiries
  4. Complete logging: Full transcripts and call recordings in your CRM
