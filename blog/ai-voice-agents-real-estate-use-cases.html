The Real Estate Lead Problem

Real estate agents face a critical challenge: 63% of buyer inquiries happen after business hours, yet most teams can't afford 24/7 staffing. The result? Missed opportunities and frustrated potential clients who move on to competitors.

Traditional solutions like voicemail or basic chatbots fail to capture the nuance of property inquiries. Buyers want immediate answers about square footage, amenities, and availability - not a callback promise that may come too late.

40% of after-hours real estate inquiries never get a response according to National Association of Realtors data. AI voice agents solve this by providing instant, conversational responses that keep potential buyers engaged.
