The Call Answer Crisis in Service Businesses

Service businesses face an invisible growth killer - unanswered customer calls. Research shows 62% of calls to plumbers, HVAC companies, and other service providers go unanswered. Each missed call represents lost revenue, frustrated customers, and slower growth.

The root cause is simple math. Hiring enough human receptionists to answer every call is prohibitively expensive. A full-time receptionist costs $3,000-$4,000 per month with benefits, yet still can't cover nights, weekends, or handle multiple calls simultaneously. As call volume grows, the problem compounds.

Key insight: For most service businesses, adding staff to handle call volume creates a profit margin squeeze. The more you grow, the more this staffing dilemma hurts your bottom line.
