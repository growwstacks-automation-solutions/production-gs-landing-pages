Retail AI Voice Platform

Most businesses struggle with scaling personalized customer interactions. Hiring staff is expensive, and generic chatbots often frustrate customers. Retail AI solves this by providing human-sounding voice agents that can handle calls, qualify leads, and schedule appointments.

When you sign up, Retail AI provides $10 in free credits to get started. After building your first agent and leaving a review, they'll give you another $50 in credits - more than enough for initial testing and development.

Pro Tip: Create a dedicated Gmail account for your business automation tools. This keeps everything organized and makes credential management easier across platforms.

NAD: The Automation Glue

Many automation platforms charge premium prices while limiting your workflow executions. NAD stands out by offering self-hosting options that dramatically reduce costs while removing execution limits.

Instead of paying $30/month through NAD's official website with execution limits, you can self-host for just $7.49/month with unlimited workflows. This becomes particularly valuable as your automation needs grow.

Key Advantage: NAD serves as the critical connection between Retail AI, Go High Level, and other tools in your stack, enabling complex automations that would otherwise require custom development.

Go High Level CRM

While Go High Level comes with a $97/month price tag when you're ready to go live, it consolidates what would normally require multiple expensive SaaS subscriptions. The platform combines CRM, marketing automation, and funnel building in one interface.

They offer a free trial to get started, and there are legitimate ways to extend this trial period while you're testing and developing your systems. At 2:30 in the video, you'll see the Go High Level dashboard where all these functions come together.

AI Assistant Options

You don't need to spend $20/month each on ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude subscriptions to get started. The free tiers of these AI assistants provide more than enough capacity for initial testing and development.

As your operations scale, you may want to upgrade for additional features and higher usage limits. For now, rotating between free accounts lets you keep costs minimal while accessing all the AI capabilities you need.

Google AI Studio

Google AI Studio offers capabilities beyond standard Gemini access, particularly for integration with automation workflows. Many users find it more versatile for business applications, with tools specifically designed for developing and testing AI models.

The platform provides powerful options for enhancing your voice agent functionality, making it worth setting up alongside your other AI tools.

Cost Breakdown

Here's what you can expect to spend when implementing this tech stack:

  • Retail AI: Start with $60 in free credits (no initial cost)
  • NAD: $7.49/month for self-hosted version
  • AI Assistants: Free tiers available initially
  • Go High Level: Free trial, then $97/month when live

This means you can build and test your entire automation system for under $8/month initially, only increasing costs as you scale operations.
