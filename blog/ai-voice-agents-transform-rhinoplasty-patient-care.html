HIPAA Compliance & Security

Healthcare AI solutions must meet strict privacy standards. Reputable voice agent platforms provide:

  • Signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)
  • End-to-end encryption for all patient data
  • Access controls and detailed audit logs
  • Regular penetration testing

When evaluating systems, look for SOC 2 Type 2 certification and healthcare-specific compliance documentation. The platform should never store sensitive health data without proper safeguards.

Security note: Voice recordings should be automatically deleted after 30 days unless required for medical documentation purposes.
