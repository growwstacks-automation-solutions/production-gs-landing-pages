How The AI Verifies Vehicle Details

Failed smog checks often occur because customers arrive with the wrong vehicle or missing documentation. Maria prevents this by verifying six key details during the call:

  1. Year: Determines pricing tier and testing requirements
  2. Make/Model: Ensures proper testing equipment
  3. Fuel Type: Gas vs diesel have different testing procedures
  4. Warning Lights: Check engine light means automatic failure
  5. Battery Status: Recent disconnection can affect readiness monitors
  6. Aftermarket Parts: Non-CARB parts cause visual inspection failures

At 2:45 in the demo, Maria asks about aftermarket parts when booking the second vehicle - a critical question many human receptionists forget.

Complex Pricing Handling Explained

Smog check pricing varies by vehicle year, county requirements, and promotional offers. Maria handles these complexities perfectly:

  • Explains why the 1997 Chevy doesn't qualify for the $49.95 special

  • Applies the coupon discount to the 2003 Civic automatically
  • Shares the "magic phrase" to redeem the coupon in person
