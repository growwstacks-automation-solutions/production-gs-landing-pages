How Voice AI Understands Complex Booking Requests

The demo assistant Monica showcases three breakthrough capabilities that set modern voice AI apart from basic IVR systems. First, contextual understanding - notice how she maintains the conversation thread about Dr. Sarah Patel while collecting necessary details. Second, error correction - when the customer provides a past date, Monica politely requests a future one. Third, multi-channel confirmation - the system automatically emails details while completing the voice interaction.

This level of sophistication comes from combining large language models with business-specific training.
