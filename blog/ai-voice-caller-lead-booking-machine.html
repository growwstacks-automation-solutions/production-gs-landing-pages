Anti-Jailbreaking: Protecting Your AI System

Any public-facing AI system needs safeguards against abuse (timestamp 16:50). Here are three key protections:

1. Input Validation

Screens all conversations before reaching the AI to flag suspicious content or jailbreak attempts.

2. Rate Limiting

Restricts API calls per session with token budgets that max out excessive usage.

3. Monitoring

Logs all conversations and auto-terminates sessions that exceed turn/token thresholds.

Additional protections include strict system prompts that keep the AI focused only on booking appointments, plus optional opt-in questions that vet leads before they reach the voice agent.
