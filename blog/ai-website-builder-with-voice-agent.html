AI Agent Configuration

The voice agent's intelligence comes from three key configuration areas:

Knowledge Base

Add your services, pricing tiers, FAQs, and common objections. The AI references this information during conversations and learns from each interaction.

Conversation Flow

Define how the agent should respond to different questions and guide visitors toward booking. Example: "If they ask about pricing, explain packages then offer a free consultation."

Scheduling Rules

Set your availability, appointment types, and buffer times. The AI checks real-time calendar availability when booking appointments for visitors.

Unlike standalone chatbots, this system maintains context throughout multi-turn conversations. It remembers what the visitor asked previously and tailors responses accordingly.

Real Conversation Testing

Testing the AI agent reveals its sophisticated capabilities. When asked "Do you offer discounts?", it doesn't just say no.
