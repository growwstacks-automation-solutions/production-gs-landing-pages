Workflow Breakdown: From RSS to Published Video

At 2:15 in the video, you'll see the complete workflow visualized in Make.com. Here's how it transforms hours of manual work into an automated process:

Step 1: Feed Reader Module

The workflow starts by pulling the latest articles from an RSS feed (Business Insider in this example). You can customize this to any industry news source or blog that offers RSS. The system fetches one article at a time to ensure focused content.

Step 2: Perplexity AI Summarization

Each article URL is sent to Perplexity AI with specific instructions: "Summarize this in a way that makes easy social media posts. Include URLs only when relevant." The AI returns a concise summary perfect for repurposing.

Step 3: ChatGPT Script Writing

The summarized content flows into ChatGPT, which transforms it into a complete video script. The prompt specifies length (30-60 seconds), scene breakdowns, and even includes directions for visuals and voiceover tone.

Pro Tip: At 4:30 in the video, notice how the JSON parsing step ensures clean formatting before the script moves to voice generation.

AI Summarization: Turning Articles into Social Gold

The magic begins with Perplexity AI's ability to digest long articles and extract the most shareable insights. Unlike simple extraction, this AI understands context - it knows when to preserve specific data points and when to generalize for broader appeal.

At 3:45 in the tutorial, you'll see the exact prompt that produces these high-quality summaries. The key is instructing the AI to focus on creating social-ready content, not just condensing information. This transforms dry articles into engaging posts that drive discussion.

Script Generation: AI That Writes Like You

ChatGPT takes the summarized content and builds complete video scripts. The prompt specifies:

  • Length (30-60 seconds ideal for social)
  • Scene breakdowns with suggested visuals
  • Voiceover directions matching your brand tone
  • Clear structure with hook, content, and call-to-action

What normally takes a human writer 30-45 minutes per script now happens automatically in seconds. The system even includes quality control - another AI module reviews each script to ensure it meets length and quality standards before proceeding.

AI Voice Creation: Professional Narration Without the Mic

At 6:20 in the video, the workflow demonstrates ElevenLabs integration for voice generation. This is where the script comes to life with human-like narration. You can choose from various voices and even clone your own vocal style if preferred.

The system handles all technical aspects - setting resolution (720p recommended), managing API calls, and processing the audio file. The result is studio-quality narration without ever needing to record yourself or hire voice talent.

Cross-Platform Posting: One Workflow, Multiple Channels

The final stage (shown at 8:10) publishes the completed video across all your social channels. The workflow demonstrates posting to:

  • Facebook Pages
  • Instagram Business Accounts
  • YouTube

Each platform receives customized captions and metadata. The system could easily be extended to include TikTok, LinkedIn, or other platforms by adding additional modules. This multi-channel approach maximizes your content's reach without extra work.
