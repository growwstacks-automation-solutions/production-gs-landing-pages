The Hidden Cost of Missed Calls

Most business owners dramatically underestimate the true cost of unanswered calls. It's not just about the immediate lost opportunity - every call that rolls to voicemail trains your potential customers to expect poor service. Research shows that 85% of callers won't leave a voicemail, and of those who do, fewer than half will receive a callback within 24 hours.

The traditional solution - hiring more staff - creates its own problems. A typical call center agent costs $35,000-$45,000 annually when you factor in salary, benefits, training, and turnover. And they're only available 8 hours a day, 5 days a week - leaving your business vulnerable every evening, weekend, and holiday.

The math is brutal: If your business misses just 5 calls per day at an average deal size of $500, you're losing $7,500 per week in potential revenue. Over a year, that adds up to $390,000 in missed opportunities - all because your phone stopped working when your team went home.

Human Agent Transfers

While Arkserv AI handles most routine calls, it's designed to recognize when human intervention is needed. The transfer process maintains service quality in two ways:

Cold Transfer: Immediate connection to an available agent with no preparation. Best for simple queries where context isn't critical.

Warm Transfer: The AI briefs your team member on the caller's needs before connecting. For example: "I have Sarah on the line asking about your premium service package. She's interested in pricing and availability for next month." This creates seamless handoffs for complex inquiries.

You configure the rules for when each transfer type occurs based on keywords, caller requests, or conversation complexity. The system also logs all transfer reasons to help you continuously optimize your call flows.
