How the Authentic Content System Works

The n8n workflow follows a simple but powerful three-step process that maintains human authenticity while leveraging AI efficiency:

  1. Context Collection: Initial voice interview capturing your professional background, audience, and topics (done once)
  2. Daily Questions: AI generates personalized questions based on your expertise areas (5-7 minutes per session)
  3. Content Creation: Transforms your voice answers into polished posts (fully automated)

Unlike generic AI tools, this system creates content that's undeniably yours - just more structured and consistent than you could produce manually.

Step 1: Collecting Your Professional Context

The foundation of authentic content is deep understanding. The workflow starts with a 7-10 minute voice interview where you describe:

  • Your professional identity and unique value proposition
  • Your ideal audience demographics and pain points
  • 3-5 core topics you want to be known for

This context gets stored in a Google Sheet that serves as the system's "brain." One marketing agency founder reported his initial context recording yielded enough material for 47 posts.

Step 2: AI-Generated Questions That Unlock Your Stories

Daily, the system sends 4-5 questions via Telegram designed to extract your most valuable insights. These aren't generic prompts - they're tailored to your stated expertise areas.

For example:

  • A real estate broker gets asked about surprising market shifts they've noticed
  • A software developer receives questions about technical challenges they've solved
  • A consultant gets prompted to share lessons from recent client engagements

You answer via quick voice messages (1-3 minutes each) - far less taxing than staring at a blank page.

Step 3: Transforming Answers Into Viral Posts

Your voice responses get automatically transcribed and processed through a sophisticated AI content framework that:

  1. Extracts key insights while preserving your authentic voice
  2. Structures content using proven viral post templates
  3. Generates both short (Twitter-style) and long (LinkedIn-style) versions

The output appears in your Google Sheet where you can tweak wording before publishing. Most users spend just 1-2 minutes reviewing each post.
