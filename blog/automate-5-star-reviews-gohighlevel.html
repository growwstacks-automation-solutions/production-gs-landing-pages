Step-by-Step Implementation Guide

Here's how to set up your automated review system in GoHighLevel:

Step 1: Configure Review Requests

Navigate to Reputation → Requests and set up your preferred channels (SMS, email, WhatsApp). Add branded images and customize your message templates.

Step 2: Set Up AI Responses

Go to Settings → AI Review Response and configure your auto-response rules. Set your preferred wait time (1-2 hours for positive reviews) and customize the AI personality.

Step 3: Create Your Review Widget

Under Widgets, design your testimonial display. Choose layout, colors, and filtering options that match your brand.

Step 4: Enable Social Auto-Posting

In Marketing → New Post → Post Reviews, connect your social accounts and set your posting schedule.

Implementation tip: Start with basic automation (requests and responses) before adding more advanced features like social posting and competitor tracking.
