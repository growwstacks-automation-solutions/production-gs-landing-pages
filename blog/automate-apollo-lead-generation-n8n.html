The Apollo Crackdown and What It Means

For years, businesses relied on third-party scrapers to extract leads from Apollo at a fraction of the official pricing. Where Apollo charged $60-$99 per month for 2,500-4,000 contacts, scrapers delivered the same data for $150 per 100,000 leads - about 20x cheaper.

That golden age ended abruptly when Apollo deployed technical measures to block all unauthorized scraping. They replaced these with their own official scraper - which itself was removed days later in what appears to be a strategic shift toward their paid subscription model.

The impact: Overnight, thousands of businesses lost their primary lead source. Alternatives like Ample Leads emerged, but Apollo has been quick to shut them down too. This workflow provides a sustainable solution using different data sources and AI enrichment.

How This Lead Generation Workflow Works

The workflow solves three core problems: sourcing leads affordably, enriching them with relevant details, and creating personalized outreach at scale. Here's the high-level process:

Step 1: Profile Scraping

Instead of scraping Apollo, we source LinkedIn profiles using services like LiveKit. You specify your ideal customer profile (job titles, industries, locations) and export the results to Google Sheets.

Step 2: AI Enrichment

n8n passes each profile to Perplexity AI, which extracts key details like career transitions, education background, and recommendations - everything needed to craft personalized messages.

Step 3: Campaign Upload

The enriched data gets formatted and uploaded to Instantly, where you can launch cold email campaigns with messages that reference specific details from each prospect's background.

Key advantage: Where Apollo leads came with minimal data, this method delivers fully enriched profiles with AI-generated icebreakers - all for about $0.03 per lead versus Apollo's $0.15-$0.25.

Setting Up Your LinkedIn Profile Scraper

The first challenge is sourcing leads without relying on Apollo. Services like LiveKit provide LinkedIn scraping at scale, with pricing around $300 for 10,000 leads - significantly cheaper than Apollo's official rates.

To configure your scraper:

  1. Define your ideal customer profile (job titles, industries, locations)
  2. Export results to Google Sheets in a standardized format
  3. Connect the sheet to n8n using the Google Sheets node

The workflow automatically processes new leads as they're added to the sheet, maintaining a constant pipeline of fresh contacts.

AI-Powered Lead Enrichment With Perplexity

Raw LinkedIn profiles contain valuable data - but extracting actionable insights manually would take hours per lead. This workflow uses Perplexity AI to automatically:

  • Identify career transitions or recent promotions
  • Highlight education or certification milestones
  • Extract skills from recommendations
  • Note volunteer work or publications

The AI generates a structured summary of each profile, which n8n then uses to craft personalized outreach. At 2:45 in the video, you can see how the system identifies a prospect's recent job change and incorporates it into their message.

Creating Truly Personalized Outreach

Generic cold emails get ignored. Messages referencing specific details from a prospect's background get responses. This workflow ensures every message includes:

  1. A personalized icebreaker based on career history
  2. Relevant social proof (shared connections, groups, or alma mater)
  3. Clear next steps tailored to their role

Pro tip: The system normalizes company names (removing "LLC", "Inc." etc.) to make messages sound more natural. Instead of "I see you work at GrowwStacks LLC", it says "I see you work at GrowwStacks".

Uploading to Instantly for Campaigns

The final step connects your enriched leads to Instantly for email sequencing:

  1. n8n formats each lead with their personalized message
  2. The workflow grabs your Instantly campaign ID from the URL
  3. Leads upload via API with all metadata intact

You can then launch sequences knowing every email contains genuine personalization - not just token first-name insertion.
