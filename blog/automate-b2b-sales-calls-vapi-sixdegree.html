Step 1: Setting Up SixDegree Lead Import

SixDegree's platform lets you build hyper-targeted lead lists using dozens of filters. For our agency clients, we typically start with:

  • Job titles (e.g. "Marketing Director", "VP of Sales")
  • Industries (e.g. "SaaS", "Professional Services")
  • Company revenue ($10M+ for enterprise deals)
  • Technologies used (for tech stack targeting)

Configure the n8n HTTP Request node to pull fresh leads daily from SixDegree's API. The workflow checks for new contacts every morning at 8 AM local time, ensuring your AI agent always has fresh leads to call.

Step 2: Phone Number Standardization in n8n

International phone formats cause most dialing failures. Our n8n workflow includes a standardization step that:

  1. Validates each number using the libphonenumber-js library
  2. Adds country codes where missing
  3. Formats numbers consistently (+1 XXX-XXX-XXXX for US numbers)
  4. Routes invalid numbers to a separate list for manual review

This one step improves call connection rates by 40% by eliminating dialing errors before they happen. Invalid numbers get flagged immediately rather than wasting AI call credits.

Step 3: Configuring the Vapi AI Voice Agent

Vapi's dashboard lets you create AI agents with custom voices and conversation flows. For sales calls, we recommend:

  • Voice: Choose a professional but approachable tone (we use "British Male - Oliver" for most clients)
  • First Message: "Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. I saw your interest in [Solution] and wanted to quickly see if you're still exploring options?"
  • Model: Groq for fastest response times (under 500ms latency)

The key is sounding human without being overly casual. At 2:15 in the video, you'll see how we test different greetings to optimize pickup rates.

Step 4: Building the Conversation Flow

An effective sales call flow follows this structure:

  1. Opening: Confirm you're speaking to the right person
  2. Qualifying: "Are you still looking for [solution]?"
  3. Pain Points: "What challenges are you facing with [problem]?"
  4. Timeline: "When were you hoping to implement this?"
  5. Close: "Would a quick 15-minute demo help?"

Vapi's conversation designer lets you build branching paths for common responses. For example, if the prospect says "We already have a solution," the AI can ask "What made you choose them?" to uncover potential dissatisfaction.

Step 5: Automating CRM Updates

The final piece logs all call outcomes automatically. Our n8n workflow:

  • Records call transcripts in Google Sheets with timestamps
  • Creates CRM tasks for follow-ups based on call results
  • Sends Slack alerts for hot leads that need immediate attention
  • Updates lead scores based on conversation quality signals

This creates a complete audit trail and ensures no lead falls through the cracks. At 12:30 in the video, you'll see how the system handles a booked meeting by automatically sending calendar invites to both parties.
