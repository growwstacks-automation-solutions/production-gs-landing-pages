Step 1: Connect Google Sheets

The workflow begins where your content planning already happens - in your editorial calendar spreadsheet. Make.com watches for new or updated rows containing your blog topics and keywords.

At 0:45 in the demo, you'll see how to select your specific Google Sheet and worksheet. The system gives you three monitoring options:

  • Watch since a specific row ID (for historical data)
  • Monitor all rows (for new sheets)
  • Select rows manually (for targeted automation)

Pro Tip: Create a dedicated "AI Ideas" column in your sheet where team members can drop topics anytime. Make.com will process them automatically.

Step 2: Integrate ChatGPT

The magic happens when you connect OpenAI's API (shown at 2:10 in the video). Make.com passes your sheet's topics to ChatGPT using your custom prompt template, ensuring output matches your brand voice.

Key configuration points:

  1. Your OpenAI API key (create one at platform.openai.com)
  2. Temperature setting for creativity vs consistency
  3. Maximum token limit for response length
  4. Custom instructions for tone and format

This integration works securely without exposing your API key in the sheet itself. The connection happens entirely within Make.com's encrypted environment.

Step 3: Automate Content Generation

With both ends connected, the workflow becomes self-sustaining. Every new topic triggers ChatGPT to generate content based on your specifications. The demo shows three powerful variations:

  • Blog post ideas (5-10 title options per topic)
  • Detailed outlines with sections and talking points
  • Full introductory paragraphs ready for editing

The results return to your Google Sheet in seconds, creating a seamless human-AI collaboration system. Your team gets instant starting points instead of blank pages.
