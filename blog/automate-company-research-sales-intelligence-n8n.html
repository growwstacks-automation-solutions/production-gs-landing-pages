Research First, Messaging Second

Cold outreach fails when it's generic. The average sales rep spends 15-30 minutes researching each company before crafting a message — but still sends something that sounds like it was written for 100 other businesses. This creates a painful cycle where research eats up valuable time without improving response rates.

The breakthrough comes from flipping the sequence: research automation first, messaging second. By using AI to systematically extract and analyze company intelligence before outreach begins, sales teams can personalize at scale without sacrificing hours to manual research.

92% of sales teams report that personalized outreach performs better, but only 34% have systems to efficiently research prospects before contacting them. This workflow closes that gap.

Step-by-Step Breakdown

Step 1: Receive Company Research Request

The workflow triggers via webhook when a sales rep or system submits a company name. This could come from your CRM, a Slack command, or direct API call.

Step 2: Normalize Search Query

Cleans and standardizes the input to ensure consistent search execution — removing special characters, correcting common misspellings, and extracting the most likely official company name.

Step 3: Execute Google Search

Uses the SERP API to fetch organic results, featured snippets, and authoritative sources in real-time, avoiding stale database information.

In summary: The workflow receives a company name, cleans it for searching, then pulls fresh data from Google — all before any analysis begins.

AI-Powered Company Analysis

Steps 4-7 transform raw search data into structured intelligence. The AI doesn't just extract facts — it identifies relationships between information points to build a coherent company profile.

For example, when analyzing a SaaS company, the system will distinguish between their product features (what they sell) and their value proposition (why customers buy). This distinction is crucial for crafting relevant outreach.

Key differentiator: Commercial tools provide data points. This workflow delivers contextual understanding — the "why" behind the facts that makes outreach resonate.

Sales Insight Generation

Steps 8-9 transform structured analysis into human-like sales insights. The AI models how an experienced SDR would interpret the information, highlighting:

This step ensures the output isn't just accurate — it's actionable for your sales team's specific approach.
