Crypto markets move 24/7, but most traders still check prices manually - refreshing CoinMarketCap or CoinGecko every few hours. This creates three major problems: missed opportunities when prices move rapidly, emotional decision-making from inconsistent monitoring, and wasted time that could be spent analyzing trends rather than gathering data.

The solution? Automated price monitoring that works while you sleep.
