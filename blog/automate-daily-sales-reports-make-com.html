How Automation Solves the Daily Reporting Headache

This Make.com workflow acts as your virtual sales operations assistant. While you sleep, it:

  1. Queries your database for yesterday's sales activity
  2. Filters to only include relevant records
  3. Formats the data into an easy-to-read visual dashboard
  4. Delivers the report directly to your team's Slack channel

The system runs on a schedule you control - daily at 7 AM, weekly on Monday mornings, or whatever cadence fits your business. No more manual exports, no more chasing team members, just reliable data delivered when you need it.

Setting Up Your Sales Data Database

The foundation of any good reporting system is clean, organized data. This workflow assumes you're using Airtable (as shown in the video at 1:30), but the same principles apply to any database:

  • Create a table with columns for all key metrics (sales, leads, conversions, etc.)
  • Include a date field to filter by time period
  • Ensure your team updates this daily or connect it to your CRM for automatic updates

At 2:15 in the tutorial, you'll see how we structure our "Summary" table with fields for unique visitors, clicks, and yesterday's sales - but you can customize this to track whatever metrics matter most to your business.
