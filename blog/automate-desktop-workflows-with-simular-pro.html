What Is Simular Pro?

Simular Pro is an AI-powered desktop automation platform that transforms how businesses handle repetitive computer tasks. Unlike traditional automation tools that require extensive scripting, Simular Pro learns workflows through demonstration and AI-powered natural language instructions.

The platform combines autonomous AI agents with a visual workflow builder and proprietary Simlang scripting language. This hybrid approach makes automation accessible to non-technical users while providing advanced customization for developers.

Key capability: Simular Pro can automate virtually any desktop task - from web research and data extraction to form filling and appointment booking - with human-like understanding but computer-level speed and precision.

How Simular Pro's AI Agents Work

Simular Pro's AI agents operate by understanding your intent, planning the necessary steps, and executing them autonomously. The process begins when you describe your task in natural language or demonstrate it through the teaching mode.

The AI then breaks down your request into logical steps, similar to how a human would approach the task but optimized for efficiency. At 4:32 in the video, you can see how the agent plans a research paper summarization workflow:

  1. Open Google Chrome browser
  2. Navigate to Google Scholar
  3. Search for recent AI research papers
  4. Extract information from top results
  5. Generate summaries using AI
  6. Display or save the compiled information

What makes Simular Pro unique is its ability to maintain context throughout the workflow. The AI understands where it is in the process and can adapt to minor interface changes or unexpected situations.

Teaching Mode vs. Simlang Scripting

Simular Pro offers two approaches to workflow creation: teaching mode for beginners and Simlang scripting for advanced users. Teaching mode lets you demonstrate tasks manually while the AI records your actions and converts them into repeatable workflows.

For more precise control, Simular Pro generates Simlang code - a specialized automation scripting language. As shown at 7:15 in the video, you can modify this code directly to customize workflows beyond what's possible through demonstration alone.

Key differences:

Teaching Mode Simlang Scripting
No coding required Code-level customization
Faster initial setup More precise control
Good for simple workflows Essential for complex automation

The hybrid approach means businesses can start with teaching mode and gradually incorporate scripting as their automation needs grow in complexity.
