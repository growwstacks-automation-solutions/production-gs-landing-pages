How Chax IQ Automates Remediation

The platform's secret weapon is its AI engine powered by PDFix technology. It analyzes documents at scale and performs four critical remediation functions automatically:

  1. Structural tagging: Identifies headings, lists, tables and applies proper PDF tags
  2. OCR processing: Converts scanned images into searchable, screen-readable text
  3. Content analysis: Detects and describes images, charts and complex elements
  4. Validation scoring: Rates documents on both compliance and actual usability

This automated processing handles 60-80% of remediation work before human review begins - a game-changer for organizations facing tight deadlines.
