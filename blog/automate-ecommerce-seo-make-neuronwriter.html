The E-Commerce SEO Content Challenge

E-commerce businesses face an impossible content dilemma. Google demands fresh, detailed product pages and category descriptions to rank - often hundreds per store. Yet human writers can't produce this volume while maintaining brand voice and legal compliance, especially in regulated industries like supplements, healthcare, or financial products.

One Australian supplement retailer solved this by automating their entire content workflow. Their Make.com system now generates:

  • SEO-optimized product pages with compliant health claims
  • Category descriptions (like "vegan chocolate") that convert
  • Brand pages maintaining consistent messaging
  • Blog posts driving organic traffic

3 minutes per batch: What took their team 40+ hours weekly now runs automatically, with every piece scoring higher on SEO metrics while passing strict Australian Therapeutic Goods Administration regulations.

4 Content Types This System Handles

The workflow distinguishes between four content categories, each with unique SEO and branding requirements:

1. Category Pages

Generic product groupings like "vegan protein powder" need broad SEO optimization while avoiding brand-specific claims. The AI uses different writing guidelines than for products.

2. Brand Pages

These require consistent brand voice and unique selling propositions. The system pulls from brand guidelines while ensuring all claims meet compliance standards.

3. Product Pages

Technical specifications, benefits, and usage instructions must balance SEO keywords with regulatory compliance. The workflow automatically flags risky language.

4. Blog Content

More conversational than product pages, blog posts still require compliance checks. The system maintains this distinction while optimizing for search intent.

How Compliance Automation Works

For regulated industries, content automation isn't just about volume - it's about risk reduction. This workflow includes a specialized compliance module that:

  1. Analyzes all generated content against regional regulations
  2. Flags and rewrites non-compliant claims
  3. Underlines all changes for human review
  4. Logs compliance decisions in the spreadsheet

The Australian supplement client needed particularly strict controls. Their workflow references Therapeutic Goods Administration guidelines, automatically removing:

  • Unapproved health claims
  • Misleading benefit statements
  • Incorrect dosage suggestions

All while maintaining the content's SEO value and readability.

Built-in Error Handling for Reliability

When processing hundreds of pages automatically, errors are inevitable. This workflow includes robust error management:

  • API failure detection and retries
  • Clear spreadsheet error logging
  • Automatic restart from last success
  • Email alerts for critical failures

The branching logic shown at 4:20 in the video demonstrates how each module reports status back to the spreadsheet. If content generation fails for one product, the system:

  1. Logs the error with details
  2. Skips to the next item
  3. Flags the failed item for retry

This ensures batches complete successfully even if individual items encounter temporary issues.
