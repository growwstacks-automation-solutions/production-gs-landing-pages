The Email Problem Service Businesses Face

Field service managers know the drill: a new client signs up in Jobber → you manually copy their details into an email template → send a welcome message. A job gets scheduled → you write another email with the appointment details. Completed work → another follow-up email. Multiply this by dozens of clients weekly, and you're spending 5–10 hours monthly on repetitive email tasks that could be automated.

The worst part? These manual processes create gaps where emails get forgotten during busy periods. Clients show up confused about appointment times, or new customers feel ignored after signing up — both damaging your hard-earned reputation.

Service businesses using Jobber report sending 42% fewer manual emails after implementing Zapier automations, while improving client response times from hours to minutes.

Step 1: Connect Zapier to Your Jobber Account

Start by logging into your Zapier account and navigating to App Connections in the left menu (shown at 0:32 in the video). This is where Zapier manages all your integrated apps. Click "Add Connection" and search for Jobber in the app directory.

When you select Jobber, Zapier will prompt you to authorize access to your account. This secure connection allows Zapier to read job details, client information, and trigger events — while also letting it send data back to Jobber when needed. Double-check you're connecting the correct Jobber workspace if you manage multiple accounts.

Step 2: Set Up Zapier Inside Jobber

Now switch to your Jobber dashboard and open the Apps section (1:15 in the video). Search for Zapier in Jobber's marketplace to access the integration tools. This two-way connection ensures Jobber knows which Zapier account to communicate with.

The configuration page explains how Zapier works with Jobber and provides the interface for managing automations. Locate the connection section and verify it's linked to the same Zapier account you authorized earlier. This alignment prevents errors when triggers fire or actions run later.

Step 3: Generate Your First Email Automation

With both platforms connected (1:50 in the video), Jobber's automation generator becomes your idea starter. Type "email" in the prompt field to see workflow suggestions like:

  • Send welcome email when new client created
  • Email job details when appointment scheduled
  • Follow-up email after service completion

Select any suggestion to open it in Zapier for customization. You can edit the trigger conditions, rewrite email templates with dynamic fields (like client names or job dates), add filters to exclude certain events, and test with real data before activation.

Pro Tip: Start with one simple automation (like new client welcome emails) to test the integration before building more complex workflows.
