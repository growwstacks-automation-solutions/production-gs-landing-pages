Make.com Setup in 3 Simple Steps

Setting up automated email responses in Make.com only requires three main components. Even complete beginners can implement this in under 9 minutes:

Step 1: Gmail Watch Emails Trigger

This module monitors your inbox for new messages based on your criteria. You can filter by:

  • Specific senders or domains (like @yourcompany.com)
  • Keywords in subject lines
  • Specific folders/labels
  • Unread status

Step 2: AI Response Generator

Using ChatGPT or another AI module, the system:

  • Extracts the sender's name and email content
  • Generates a personalized reply
  • Formats it in proper email HTML

Step 3: Send Response Action

The final module sends the AI-generated reply back to the original sender with:

  • Proper subject line (like "Re: [Original Subject]")
  • Formatted HTML body
  • Your signature and branding

Pro Tip: Start by automating responses to your most common, repetitive emails first. As you gain confidence, expand to more complex scenarios.

In Make.com, create a new scenario and add the "Gmail - Watch Emails" module. The key settings to pay attention to:

Building the AI Response System

The magic happens when Make.com passes the email content to an AI like ChatGPT to generate thoughtful replies. Here's the step-by-step:

1. Extract Key Email Data

First, use Make's "Set Multiple Variables" module to capture:

  • Sender's email address
  • Sender's name
  • Original subject line
  • Full email body text

2. Craft the AI Prompt

The prompt tells ChatGPT how to respond. A good template:

"Create an email that will respond to this message: [Email Body]. Greet the user with their name ([Sender Name]). Keep the response professional yet friendly. Sign off with [Your Name]."

3. Configure the AI Module

Use the OpenAI (ChatGPT) module in Make.com with:

  • Model: GPT-4 for best results
  • Temperature: 0.7 for balanced creativity
  • Max tokens: 500 for sufficient length

Important: Always test with simple emails first. The AI performs best when the incoming email has clear questions or requests to respond to.

Optimization Tips

  • Add filters to exclude emails containing certain keywords
