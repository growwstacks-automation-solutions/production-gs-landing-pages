The Event Management Challenge

Event planners often juggle multiple disconnected systems - leads in HubSpot, email campaigns in Brevo, and task management in Monday.com. This creates a nightmare of manual updates, inconsistent data, and missed opportunities when information falls through the cracks.

The client in this case study was spending 15+ hours weekly just keeping systems in sync. Their event managers would update a lead status in Monday.com, then manually make the same change in HubSpot and Brevo - a process prone to errors and delays.

85% of event teams report wasting time on manual data entry between systems, according to recent EventMB research. This workflow eliminates that inefficiency by automating cross-platform updates.

Workflow Overview

The solution combines two powerful Make.com automations with customized Monday.com boards to create a seamless event management system:

  1. Lead Capture Automation: Captures new leads from forms or manual entry and syncs them to Monday.com, Brevo and HubSpot simultaneously
  2. Status Sync Automation: Monitors lead lifecycle stage changes across all platforms and updates the others in real-time

These automations connect through a centralized Monday.com workspace with mirrored boards for events, marketing campaigns, leads, and customer journey tracking - all visible through an operations dashboard.

Lead Capture Automation

The lead capture workflow triggers whenever a new contact is added through any channel - whether from a form submission, manual entry in HubSpot, or imported list in Brevo. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Lead Source Detection

The Make.com scenario first identifies where the lead originated (form, manual entry, import) and captures all relevant fields including name, email, lead source, and interest type.

Step 2: Multi-Platform Creation

The lead is simultaneously created in:

  • Monday.com's lead board with all captured fields
  • Brevo as a new contact with proper tags
  • HubSpot with the correct lifecycle stage

Step 3: Confirmation & Notification

The workflow sends a welcome email to the new lead and notifies the assigned team member in Monday.com - all within seconds of the initial capture.

Result: What used to take 15-20 minutes of manual entry across 3 platforms now happens automatically in under 30 seconds with perfect accuracy.

Status Sync Automation

The status sync workflow maintains consistency across platforms whenever a lead's lifecycle stage changes. For example, when a lead moves from "new" to "qualified" in Monday.com:

Step 1: Change Detection

Make.com monitors the status field in Monday.com's lead board and detects when any value changes.

Step 2: Cross-Platform Update

The workflow updates:

  • HubSpot's lifecycle stage field
  • Brevo's contact status
  • Monday.com's customer journey tracking board

Step 3: Automated Actions

Depending on the new status, the workflow may:

  • Move the lead to a different group in Monday.com
  • Trigger a follow-up email sequence in Brevo
  • Create new tasks for the sales team

Real-world impact: At 4:32 in the video, you'll see how changing a status in Monday.com instantly updates all connected platforms while maintaining complete data relationships.

Monday.com Boards Structure

The Monday.com workspace serves as the central hub for the entire event management operation. Key boards include:

Events Management Board

The main board contains event names, types, owners, statuses, due dates, budgets, and variance calculations. Multiple views (timeline, calendar, table) provide different perspectives on the same data.

Marketing Campaign Board

Connected to the main events board, this tracks campaign names, channels, audiences, statuses, and launch dates - automatically mirroring relevant information to the main board.

Leads Board

All contacts from forms or manual entry appear here first, with lead source, interest type, status, and assignment fields that trigger the automations.

Pro Tip: The mirrored board structure means updates only need to happen in one place, yet appear everywhere they're needed - eliminating duplicate data entry.

Cross-Platform Dashboards

The operations dashboard in Monday.com provides real-time visibility across all connected systems:

Lead Status Tracking

Pie charts show lead distribution by status across all platforms, with drill-down capability to see individual records.

Campaign Performance

Visualizations compare campaign metrics from Brevo email analytics with HubSpot conversion data.

Event Timeline

A consolidated calendar view shows all upcoming events, deadlines, and tasks from multiple boards in one place.

This dashboard eliminates the need to log into multiple systems to get a complete picture of event operations - saving managers 2-3 hours daily in status meetings and manual reporting.

Task Automation Examples

The Monday.com automations handle routine task management based on event changes:

Event Type Changes

When an event type changes (e.g., from webinar to conference), the system automatically:

  • Creates tasks in the events delivery board
  • Generates subitems with standard checklist items for that event type
  • Notifies the assigned team members

Lead Qualification

When a lead's status changes to "qualified", the workflow:

  • Moves the lead to the qualified group
  • Updates the customer journey tracker
  • Sends an email to the sales team
  • Creates follow-up tasks in the appropriate boards
