Why n8n Beats Cloud-Based Automation Tools

But as workflows grow more complex, three problems emerge: escalating costs, data privacy concerns, and limited customization options. n8n solves all three by giving you an open-source platform that runs on your own infrastructure.

The fundamental difference comes down to control. With cloud-based tools, your data flows through third-party servers and you're charged per action. n8n flips this model - your automations execute on hardware you control, with no per-task fees. This becomes particularly valuable when scaling beyond basic integrations.

Key advantage: A typical business spending $800/month on Zapier can replace those workflows with n8n running on a $10/month cloud server, achieving 98% cost savings while gaining more powerful automation capabilities.

Understanding n8n's Workflow Structure

n8n organizes automations as visual workflows composed of connected nodes. Each node performs a specific function - fetching data, processing information, or taking action. What makes n8n unique is how these nodes handle data internally, passing structured information between steps without complex coding.

The platform follows a simple but powerful pattern: Trigger → Get Data → Process → Act. This structure appears in everything from simple notifications to complex multi-system integrations.
