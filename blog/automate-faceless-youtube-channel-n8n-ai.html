Complete System Overview

The automation workflow connects six key components that transform raw ideas into published videos. Each piece handles a specific part of the process before passing the baton to the next:

1. Content Generation

AI models generate video topics, titles, and scripts based on your channel's niche. These are stored in Google Sheets for easy access.

2. Storyboarding

Another AI breaks scripts into scenes with specific visual and audio requirements, creating a production-ready storyboard.

3. Audio Processing

Background music and voiceovers are generated automatically and stored for use across multiple videos.

4. Video Rendering

Specialized services assemble all elements into finished videos based on the storyboard.

5. Quality Control

The system checks render status and only proceeds with successfully completed videos.

6. YouTube Upload

Finished videos are uploaded automatically with all metadata already in place.

Automation advantage: This entire process runs on a schedule you set - hourly, daily, or weekly - with no manual intervention required after initial setup.
