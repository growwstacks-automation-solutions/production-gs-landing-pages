The Three-Part Financial Automation System

Tracking financial markets manually is time-consuming and inconsistent. Between checking news, analyzing your portfolio, and reviewing charts, it's easy to miss important signals or waste hours on repetitive tasks. This n8n workflow solves all three challenges in one automated system.

The automation runs on a schedule you define, combining free tools with AI analysis to deliver professional-grade market insights. At 11:10 AM each day (or whatever time you set), it executes three connected processes that would take hours to do manually.

Key benefit: This system gives you consistent, data-driven market analysis without requiring you to manually check multiple sources or interpret charts yourself. The AI handles the pattern recognition while you focus on decision-making.

Automated Financial News Summarization

The first component pulls the latest financial headlines from investing.com using RSS. Instead of overwhelming you with dozens of full articles, it creates concise summaries highlighting the most important information.

This solves the common problem of information overload in financial news. The system filters noise and delivers only the relevant updates, saving you from scrolling through irrelevant stories or duplicate coverage.

Portfolio Analysis from Google Sheets

The second component connects directly to your Google Sheets portfolio. It reads your current holdings and generates a situational report showing performance, allocation, and other key metrics.

This automation eliminates the need for manual spreadsheet updates and calculations. Whether you track stocks, crypto, or other instruments, the system gives you an instant snapshot of your portfolio's status without opening a single spreadsheet.

Implementation tip: The Google Sheets integration uses OAuth for security, and n8n automatically handles token refreshes when needed. You simply authenticate once during setup.

AI-Powered Chart Generation & Analysis

The most advanced component generates TradingView-style charts via the ChartIMG API, then sends each chart to an AI model for technical analysis. This gives you professional chart interpretations without needing to study technical analysis yourself.

The system creates candlestick charts with volume and MACD indicators - the same tools professional traders use. The AI analyzes trends, key price levels, and market sentiment based solely on the visual chart data.

Smart Scheduling & Rate Limit Handling

Free API plans often have rate limits that can break automations if not handled properly. This workflow includes intelligent delays between chart requests to stay within the ChartIMG free tier limits (1 request per second, 50 charts per day).

A 20-second delay between processing each instrument in your portfolio ensures reliable operation without hitting API limits. The time-based trigger lets you set exactly when the automation runs to match your market schedule.

Notification Delivery to Telegram & Discord

The final step delivers everything to your preferred channels. The tutorial shows Telegram and Discord integration, but you could adapt it to email or other platforms.

Each message combines the generated chart image with the AI's technical analysis in a format optimized for the platform's character limits. You get clean, actionable insights delivered where you already communicate.

Pro tip: The Discord integration includes special formatting for code blocks and line breaks to make the technical analysis more readable in chat.
