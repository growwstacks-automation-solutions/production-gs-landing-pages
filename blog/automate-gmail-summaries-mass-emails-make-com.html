Step-by-Step: Building Your AI Summarizer

The magic happens when you connect these simple steps (demonstrated between 5:30-7:45 in the video):

Step 1: Set Up Your Trigger

Create a Google Sheet to feed information into the system. Each new row will trigger the automation. This serves as your control panel for managing email workflows.

Step 2: Connect Gmail

Add the Gmail module and configure it to watch specific labels or folders. The system will automatically process new emails matching your criteria.

Step 3: Insert the AI Magic

Between the trigger and Gmail, add Make.com's AI module. Give it simple English instructions like "Summarize this email thread in 3 bullet points focusing on action items."

Pro Tip: Start with "Summarize this in [number] sentences focusing on [specific elements]" for best results. Refine prompts based on output quality.
