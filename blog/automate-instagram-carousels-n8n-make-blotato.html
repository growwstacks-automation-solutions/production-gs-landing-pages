The Instagram Carousel Problem

Instagram carousels consistently outperform single-image posts with 1.4x higher reach and 2.3x more engagement according to Meta's latest data. Yet most businesses post them infrequently because each carousel requires:

  • Brainstorming compelling content angles
  • Writing cohesive slide-by-slide copy
  • Creating or sourcing matching visuals
  • Crafting an engaging caption with hashtags
  • Scheduling at optimal posting times

The result? Businesses either post inconsistently or waste hours each week on content creation. This automation solves both problems by handling the entire process from ideation to posting.

Time savings: What typically takes 2-3 hours per carousel now completes automatically in under 10 minutes - a 90% reduction in content creation time.

AI-Powered Content Generation

At the heart of this automation is a sophisticated AI system prompt that structures professional carousels. The prompt instructs OpenAI to:

  1. Analyze your input topic (e.g. "Listing agent selling luxury waterfront home")
  2. Structure a 5-slide carousel with hook, 3 key points, and CTA
  3. Generate DALL-E image prompts matching each slide's content
  4. Write a complete Instagram caption with 3 strategic hashtags

The beauty lies in the prompt's flexibility. While we demonstrated real estate examples, the same system works for:

  • eCommerce product showcases
  • Professional service explanations
  • Educational content series
  • Before/after case studies

Pro tip: Limit hashtags to 3 per post - both Instagram and TikTok now cap visibility at 5 hashtags, and fewer often performs better.

Make.com Workflow Breakdown

The Make.com version (shown at 2:15 in the video) requires just one customization point:

  1. Input your topic in the first module (e.g. "CPA explaining Q2 tax changes")
  2. Connect your OpenAI API account
  3. Link your Blotato Instagram connection

The workflow then:

  • Generates carousel content via OpenAI
  • Creates DALL-E images for each slide
  • Waits 5 minutes for image generation to complete
  • Posts directly to your Instagram via Blotato

For businesses wanting to review before posting, simply disconnect the Blotato module and connect an Airtable instead. The automation will save drafts for your approval.

n8n Workflow Breakdown

The n8n version (demonstrated at 6:40) improves on Make.com's fixed waiting period with a smart loop that:

  1. Checks image generation status continuously
  2. Proceeds as soon as all images are ready
  3. Typically completes 2-3 minutes faster than the Make.com version

Both workflows produce identical carousel quality - choose based on which platform you prefer. The n8n edge comes from its more efficient waiting mechanism.

Implementation note: New users typically see best results after spending 1-2 hours refining their AI prompt for industry-specific terminology and brand voice.

Blotato Integration & Scheduling

Blotato handles the final mile - posting your carousels to Instagram. Key features:

  • Auto-scheduling: Posts grab the next available slot in your content calendar
  • Multi-platform support: Same carousel can post to Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.
  • Visual previews: See exactly how posts will appear before they go live

To set up (shown at 4:55):

  1. Connect your Instagram account in Blotato settings
  2. Define your posting schedule (e.g. daily at 9am and 4pm)
  3. Let the automation fill your queue with AI-generated carousels

This "set and forget" approach ensures consistent posting without daily manual effort.

Human-in-the-Loop Option

For businesses wanting quality control before posting:

  1. Configure the workflow to save drafts to Airtable
  2. Receive email notifications when new carousels are ready
  3. Review and approve selections in 5 minutes/day
  4. Manually post or approve for auto-posting

This hybrid approach maintains oversight while still saving 90% of creation time. Many clients start this way before transitioning to full automation.

Implementation tip: Create a separate "Approved" view in Airtable to quickly see which carousels are ready for posting.

Prompt Optimization Tips

While the default prompt works well, customization unlocks maximum value:

  • Brand voice: Add descriptors like "professional but approachable" or "casual with humor"
  • Structure: Specify slide count and content flow (hook → education → CTA)
  • Visual style: Guide DALL-E with terms like "minimalist" or "bright and energetic"
  • Hashtag strategy: Provide 2-3 sample hashtags as examples

The most successful implementations spend 1-2 hours refining prompts before automating at scale. Consider:

  1. Running 5-10 test generations
  2. Saving all variations to Airtable
  3. Identifying which elements work best
  4. Updating the system prompt accordingly
