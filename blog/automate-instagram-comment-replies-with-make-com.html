Setting AI Response Rules

The "prompt store" mentioned at 1:45 in the video contains rules governing when and how AI should respond. This prevents inappropriate automation while allowing helpful engagement:

  • Do respond to: Product questions, sizing inquiries, warranty info requests
  • Don't respond to: Competitor mentions, negative sentiment, "chatbot" comments
  • Human review needed: Complex support issues, refund requests, complaints

These guardrails ensure automation enhances customer experience rather than creating frustration. The system can even detect when someone is testing whether they're talking to a bot (like commenting "chatbot") and intentionally not respond to maintain the illusion of human interaction.
