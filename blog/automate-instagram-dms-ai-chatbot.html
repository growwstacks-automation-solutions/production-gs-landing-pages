Step 1: Account Setup & Instagram Connection

Before automation, ensure you have an Instagram Professional account (Business or Creator). Personal accounts can't use automation tools due to API restrictions. At 4:20 in the tutorial, you'll see the exact permissions needed when connecting your account.

The setup process involves:

Step 1: Create your automation platform account

Sign up using Google, Facebook, or email. Most tools offer free trials with limited contacts.

Step 2: Connect your Instagram account

Navigate to account settings and select Instagram. You'll be prompted to log in and grant necessary permissions.

Step 3: Verify account type

The system will confirm you're using a Professional account. If not, it will guide you through switching.

Pro tip: Connect during business hours when you can immediately test responses. Some permissions require re-authentication every 90 days.

Step 2: Using Ready-Made Template Automations

Most platforms offer pre-built templates for common use cases. At 6:10 in the video, the "AutoDM Links from Comments" template is demonstrated. These templates provide complete conversation flows you can customize:

Popular Instagram automation templates:

  • Instant Reply to DMs: Responds to all direct messages with your chosen text
  • Comment-to-DM Converter: Sends a DM when users comment with specific keywords
  • Lead Qualifier: Asks budget, timeline, or other qualifying questions
  • FAQ Responder: Answers common product/service questions automatically

To use a template, simply select it, choose which Instagram posts it applies to, and customize the messaging. The video at 7:35 shows how to edit the default responses to match your brand voice.

Step 3: Building Custom Automations From Scratch

For unique workflows, create custom automations. At 9:15 in the tutorial, you'll see how to set up a booking inquiry responder from scratch:

Step 1: Define the trigger

Choose what starts the automation - a DM containing certain words, a comment on a specific post, or a story reply.

Step 2: Set keyword filters

Specify which words or phrases activate the response. Separate multiple keywords with commas.

Step 3: Craft your message sequence

Write the initial response and any follow-ups. Use variables like {first_name} for personalization.

Step 4: Add buttons or quick replies

Include interactive elements so users can easily respond or get more information.

Step 5: Set live and test

Activate the automation and send test messages to verify it works as intended.

Conversion tip: At 10:50 in the video, notice how adding a simple "Yes, send me details" button increased conversion rates by 22%.

Step 4: Testing & Optimizing Your Automated Responses

After setup, rigorously test your automations. The video at 11:30 demonstrates sending test messages from another account to check:

  • Response time: Should be under 15 seconds
  • Message accuracy: Replies should match the trigger words
  • Conversation flow: Follow-ups should make logical sense
  • Mobile display: Check how messages appear on phones

Monitor analytics to identify which automations generate the most leads and which might need refinement. Most platforms show open rates, response rates, and conversion metrics.
