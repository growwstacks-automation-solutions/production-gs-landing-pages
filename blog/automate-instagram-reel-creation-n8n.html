How the Automation System Works

The workflow follows a smart four-stage process that mimics how successful creators manually produce content, but with AI doing the heavy lifting:

1. Content Discovery

Scrapes reels from specified competitor accounts using Apify's Instagram Reel Scraper (68k users). Filters out duplicates and irrelevant content automatically.

2. Content Analysis

Transcribes audio using AssemblyAI, extracts key insights, and identifies pain points addressed in each reel.

3. Content Creation

Generates fresh scripts in your brand voice, plus three lead magnets and three social posts per reel.

4. Content Organization

Stores everything in a Google Sheet with timestamps, captions, and generated assets ready for scheduling.

The system produces 5-7 publish-ready reels per run with accompanying lead magnets and social posts - enough content for a full week of posting from one 15-minute workflow execution.

Step 1: Scraping Competitor Reels with Apify

The system begins with an HTTP request node configured to use Apify's Instagram Reel Scraper. At 4:30 in the video, Anna shows how to:

  1. Create an Apify API key (free tier available)
  2. Configure the scraper with target account usernames
  3. Set limits on reels per account (default: 2 for testing)
  4. Map the output to filter irrelevant content automatically

The scraped data includes video URLs, captions, timestamps, and engagement metrics. A Google Sheet node then checks for duplicates using a self-managed filtering system that compares new content against previously scraped reels.

Pro Tip: Start with 3-5 competitor accounts in your niche. The system works best when scraping accounts that consistently post high-quality content you want to model.

Step 2: Automatic Transcription with AssemblyAI

At 11:20, the tutorial demonstrates how the system processes scraped reels through AssemblyAI for transcription. Key advantages:

  • No need to download videos - processes directly from Instagram URLs
  • Accurate transcription even with background music or accents
  • 15-second delay node ensures complete processing before next steps

The transcription forms the raw material for content generation. The system checks each transcript for relevance (business/sales topics) before proceeding, avoiding wasted processing on off-topic content.

Cost Note: AssemblyAI offers 3 hours of free transcription monthly - enough for testing. Paid plans start at $15/month for 10 hours.

Step 3: AI-Powered Content Generation

The heart of the system (shown at 18:45) uses four specialized ChatGPT nodes:

1. Content Analyzer

Identifies business insights, pain points, and step-by-step guidance from transcripts. Filters out non-relevant content.

2. Script Generator

Creates fresh 100-word scripts in your brand voice based on analyzed content.

3. Lead Magnet Creator

Generates three lead magnets per reel (frameworks, checklists, swipe files).

4. Social Post Writer

Drafts three companion posts (Instagram captions + LinkedIn posts) per reel.

Each node uses tailored prompts (included in the free template) to ensure outputs match your content style and business focus.
