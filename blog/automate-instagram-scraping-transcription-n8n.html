The Problem With Manual Instagram Research

Marketing teams waste countless hours manually checking competitors' Instagram profiles, trying to analyze their video content strategy. The process is tedious: watching each video, taking notes on hooks and messaging, manually counting likes and comments, then attempting to spot patterns across dozens of posts.

Worse, video content requires either real-time viewing or manual transcription - both incredibly time-consuming. Most teams eventually give up, settling for superficial analysis that misses the strategic insights hidden in competitors' video content.

Manual Instagram research takes 5-7 minutes per video analyzed: Watching, note-taking, counting metrics, and transcribing key points. For 20 videos from 5 competitors, that's 8-12 hours of work per week - time better spent creating your own content.

Workflow Overview

This n8n automation solves the Instagram research problem by combining three powerful tools:

  1. Apify Instagram Scraper: Extracts video URLs, captions, engagement metrics
  2. AssemblyAI: Automatically transcribes audio from scraped videos
  3. Google Sheets: Organizes all data for easy analysis and reporting

The workflow begins with a Google Sheet containing competitor profile names. n8n processes each profile through Apify's scraper, collects the video data, sends audio to AssemblyAI for transcription, then compiles everything back into Sheets.

End-to-end processing time: About 2-5 minutes per profile (depending on video count), compared to hours of manual work. The system handles everything while your team focuses on strategic insights.

Setting Up Apify Instagram Scraper

Apify's Instagram scraper handles the complex task of extracting structured data from Instagram's constantly changing interface. After creating an Apify account (which includes $5 in free monthly credits), you'll:

Step 1: Configure the Scraper

In the Apify console, select the Instagram scraper from their store. Configure it to extract:

  • Video URLs and audio files
  • Captions and hashtags
  • Engagement metrics (likes, views)
  • Publication timestamps

Step 2: Set Parameters

Key parameters to adjust:

  • Result limit (how many videos per profile)
  • Date range (only scrape recent content)
  • Content type (Reels, IGTV, etc.)

Pro Tip: Start with 2-3 videos per profile for testing. Once validated, increase to 10-20 for full competitive analysis.

Configuring the n8n Loop

The magic happens in n8n's loop node, which processes each competitor profile sequentially. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Initialize the Loop

Set batch size to 1 so each profile gets processed individually. The loop pulls profile names from your Google Sheet and feeds them to Apify's API.

Step 2: Synchronous Execution

Configure the HTTP request to Apify as synchronous - n8n waits for scraping to complete before moving to the next step. This ensures data is available when needed.

Step 3: Retrieve Results

After scraping completes, another HTTP request fetches the structured data from Apify's storage. The loop ensures each profile's data stays properly associated.

Why loops matter: Without proper sequencing, you might retrieve stale data or mix metrics between competitors. The loop guarantees clean, organized results.

Transcribing Videos With AssemblyAI

With video URLs and audio files scraped, the workflow sends each to AssemblyAI for automatic transcription:

Step 1: API Setup

Create a free AssemblyAI account (includes $50 in monthly credits). Grab your API key and configure it in n8n's credential manager.

Step 2: Create Transcriptions

The workflow posts each audio URL to AssemblyAI's API. Transcription typically takes 1-2 minutes per video (faster for shorter content).

Step 3: Retrieve Transcripts

Using the returned transcription ID, the workflow polls AssemblyAI until completion, then fetches the full text with timestamps.

Accuracy Note: AssemblyAI achieves 92-95% accuracy on clear English speech. Background music or heavy accents may reduce this slightly.

Organizing Data in Google Sheets

The final step compiles all scraped and transcribed data into a structured Google Sheet:

Step 1: Initial Data Export

The workflow first exports basic video data (URLs, metrics, captions) to Sheets as soon as it's scraped.

Step 2: Transcript Integration

Once transcripts are ready, the workflow matches them to videos using audio URLs (a reliable unique identifier) and updates the Sheet.

Step 3: Analysis-Ready Structure

The final Sheet contains 12+ fields per video, organized for easy filtering, sorting, and analysis:

  • Profile name and video URL
  • Engagement metrics (likes, views)
  • Full transcript with timestamps
  • Hashtags and captions
  • Publication date

Extension Idea: Add a ChatGPT node to analyze transcripts for common hooks, emotional triggers, or messaging patterns automatically.
