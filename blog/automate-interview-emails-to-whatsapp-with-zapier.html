How Zapier Automation Works

Zapier acts as a bridge between your email and WhatsApp through a simple concept called "Zaps" — automated workflows with two parts:

  1. Trigger: An event that starts the automation (like receiving an email with "interview" in it)
  2. Action: What happens next (like sending a WhatsApp notification)

The magic happens in between these steps where Zapier automatically transfers the right information from your email to your WhatsApp message. You set up the rules once, and Zapier handles the rest — no ongoing maintenance required.

Setting Up Your First Zap

Creating this automation takes just five straightforward steps:

Step 1: Create Your Zapier Account

Start with a free Zapier account at zapier.com. The free plan includes all the features needed for this basic automation.

Step 2: Set Up Your Trigger

Click "Create Zap" and select Gmail as your trigger app. Choose "New Email Matching Search" as the trigger event, then enter your search term ("interview"). Connect your Gmail account when prompted.

Step 3: Test Your Trigger

Zapier will scan your recent emails for ones containing your keyword. Select a test email to verify the trigger works correctly.

Step 4: Set Up Your WhatsApp Action

Add "WhatsApp Notifications by Zapier" as your action app. Choose "Send Message" as the action event. Customize your notification template with details from the email.

Step 5: Publish Your Zap

Test the complete workflow, then turn it on. Your automation will now run continuously in the background.

Customizing Your WhatsApp Notifications

The basic notification tells you an interview-related email arrived, but you can make it much more useful by including specific details:

  • Sender information: Add the recruiter's name or company
  • Email subject: Include the full subject line
  • Action buttons: Create quick reply options like "Schedule" or "Decline"
  • Priority tagging: Flag urgent messages with ⚠️ or 🔥 emojis

Testing and Troubleshooting

Like any new system, your Zap needs proper testing before relying on it completely. Here's our recommended testing protocol:

Pro tip: Create a test email with "TEST INTERVIEW" in the subject line and send it to yourself before activating your Zap. This verifies the entire workflow without risking missed real opportunities.

If your notifications aren't working:

  1. Check that your Gmail account is properly connected in Zapier
  2. Verify your WhatsApp number is correctly entered
  3. Ensure your search term isn't too narrow (try just "interview" first)
  4. Check Zapier's status page for any service outages
