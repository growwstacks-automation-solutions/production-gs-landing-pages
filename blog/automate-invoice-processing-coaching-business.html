The Invoicing Problem Every Coach Faces

Monthly invoicing is one of the most tedious yet critical tasks for coaching businesses. At the start of each billing cycle, you're faced with:

  • Manually creating invoices from templates
  • Tracking which clients need to be billed
  • Sending individual emails with attachments
  • Following up when payments are late
  • Updating records when payments arrive

This process often takes hours each month - time you could spend coaching clients or growing your business. Even worse, manual processes lead to errors like missed invoices or incorrect amounts.

84% of small businesses report wasting at least 5 hours monthly on manual invoicing tasks, according to FreshBooks research. For coaching businesses with recurring clients, this number is often higher.

System 1: Automatic Invoice Generation

The first piece of the automation puzzle is invoice generation. Here's how it works:

  1. Make.com connects to your client database (Google Sheets in this example)
  2. It searches for clients whose payment due date is approaching (set to 21 days in the tutorial)
  3. The system pulls all necessary client details from your database
  4. It generates a new invoice using your pre-designed template

At 2:15 in the video, you'll see how to set up the Google Sheets search module to find clients with approaching due dates. The key is using numeric operators to filter for the exact timeframe you want to trigger invoice generation.

Pro Tip: Use a standardized naming convention for your invoice templates. This makes it easier for the automation to locate and use the correct template for each client type.

From Spreadsheet to Professional PDF

While spreadsheet templates are easy to work with, you don't want to send clients an editable document. The automation handles the conversion to PDF automatically:

  1. After generating the invoice in Google Sheets, the system downloads the file
  2. It converts the spreadsheet to PDF using PDF.co (shown at 6:30 in the video)
  3. The PDF is uploaded back to Google Drive for record-keeping
  4. The final PDF is then ready to be sent to the client

This conversion process ensures your invoices maintain consistent formatting and appear professional. PDFs also prevent clients from accidentally modifying invoice details.

The Complete 4-System Automation

While this tutorial focuses on invoice generation, the complete solution includes four connected systems:

1. Invoice Generation - Creates invoices from templates when due dates approach

2. Invoice Delivery - Sends invoices and daily payment reminders

3. Payment Confirmation - Verifies when payments are received

4. Database Update - Marks invoices as paid and thanks clients

Together, these systems handle the entire invoice lifecycle without manual intervention. You simply maintain your client database - the automation handles everything else.
