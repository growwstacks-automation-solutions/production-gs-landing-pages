How Real-Time Job Board Scraping Works

The system begins with API connections to LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, and Glassdoor that check for new postings every 15 minutes. You configure your ideal parameters:

  • Job titles and keywords (e.g., "Full Stack Developer" + "React")
  • Location preferences (remote, specific cities, or hybrid)
  • Salary ranges and company size filters
  • Maximum number of results (typically 50-100 per search)

When new matches are found, the system immediately extracts all available details: job description, required skills, company overview, and application URL. This data is normalized into a standard format regardless of source, then passed to the AI analysis engine.

Pro tip: The system can also scrape LinkedIn posts (not just job listings) to find unadvertised opportunities - a goldmine most recruiters miss completely.

Hiring Manager Discovery Engine

Here's where the magic happens. For each job, the system:

  1. Identifies the company domain from the job posting
  2. Scrapes LinkedIn for relevant department heads (engineering, HR, etc.)
  3. Verifies email patterns using company domain analysis
  4. Cross-references with internal databases for additional contacts

The result? Validated email addresses for 3-5 decision-makers per company, with an 87% accuracy rate.
