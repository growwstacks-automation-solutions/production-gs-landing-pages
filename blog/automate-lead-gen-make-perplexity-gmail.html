How the Automated Lead Gen System Works

The system follows a precise sequence: identify fresh leads → research with AI → craft personalized emails → send at optimal times → track responses → follow up automatically. Each component works together to create a lead generation machine that runs 24/7.

At its core, the automation uses Make.com to connect your lead sources (like LinkedIn or Apollo.io) with Perplexity AI for research and Gmail for sending. The Google Sheets integration serves as a lightweight CRM to track all interactions.

Step 1: Lead Identification

The system first scans your lead spreadsheet for contacts marked "not contacted." It checks for essential details like email address and company name before proceeding.

Step 2: AI Research

Perplexity AI researches each company to understand their recent projects, industry trends, and potential needs. This takes 15-30 seconds per lead but happens automatically in the background.

Step 3: Email Composition

Using a carefully crafted prompt (shown at 4:52 in the video), the AI generates a 3-4 sentence email focused on the prospect's specific business situation.

In summary: The system identifies fresh leads → researches them with AI → crafts hyper-personalized emails → sends through Gmail → logs all activity → follows up automatically.

The Science Behind the Follow-Up Sequence

80% of sales require 5 follow-ups, yet most businesses give up after 2. This system automates a complete 5-touch sequence with strategic timing between each message.

Follow-ups alternate between value reminders ("Did you see this case study?") and gentle check-ins ("Just circling back"). The system tracks days since last contact and only sends the next message when appropriate.

Properly spaced follow-ups can increase reply rates by 300% - but manually tracking timing is impossible at scale. Our automation handles it perfectly every time.

How the System Tracks Leads Automatically

The Google Sheet serves as your command center, tracking:

  • Initial contact date and time
  • Which follow-up emails were sent and when
  • Whether the lead replied (automatically detected via Gmail filters)
  • Days since last contact (calculated automatically)

At 12:45 in the video, you'll see how the system updates the sheet in real-time. This prevents duplicate outreach and ensures perfect follow-up timing for every lead.

Avoiding Spam Filters and Deliverability Issues

The system includes multiple safeguards to protect your sender reputation:

  • Rate limiting (max 15 emails/hour from Gmail)
  • Randomized send times between 9AM-5PM in recipient's timezone
  • Automatic detection of bounced emails
  • Built-in 5-minute cooling period between sends

These measures help your emails land in the primary inbox instead of spam. The tutorial at 18:20 shows exactly how to configure these settings in Make.com.
