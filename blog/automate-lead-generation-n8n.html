AI-Powered Lead Data Cleaning

The real magic happens in n8n's validation nodes. Before any lead reaches your CRM, the workflow:

  1. Checks email/phone formats with built-in validation
  2. Eliminates duplicates across all sources
  3. Verifies professional details using Hunter or Apollo nodes
  4. Routes incomplete records to a "Do Nothing" node (automatic discard)
