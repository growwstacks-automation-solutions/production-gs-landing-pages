This system solves three critical pain points:

  • Instant budget qualification using AI (no more wasting time on unqualified leads)
  • Automated meeting scheduling (eliminating back-and-forth emails)
  • Real-time team notifications (keeping everyone aligned without manual updates)

System Overview

The workflow connects four powerful tools to create a seamless lead-to-meeting pipeline:

  1. Tally forms - Embedded lead capture forms on your website
  2. Airtable AI agents - Automatic lead scoring and summarization
  3. Make.com - The automation engine connecting everything
  4. Google Calendar/Slack - Meeting scheduling and team alerts

Tally Form Setup

Tally provides customizable forms that integrate directly with Airtable. The tutorial shows how to:

  • Create fields for critical qualification data (budget, company size, needs)

  • Test submissions to ensure data flows correctly

Airtable AI Agents

The magic happens with two specialized AI agents in Airtable:

  1. Qualification Agent - Flags leads as qualified if budget exceeds $10K
  2. Summary Agent - Creates concise Slack-ready summaries of lead needs

At 7:45 in the video, you'll see the AI analyzing a sample submission about engineering procurement automation - instantly identifying it as qualified and generating this summary:

"James from Gamesoft needs help automating product procurement with $10K budget. Interested in AI automation for delivery channels."

Make.com Workflow

The Make.com scenario handles all automation logic with these key modules:

  • Airtable trigger - Watches for new qualified leads
  • Email module - Sends personalized meeting invites
  • Slack module - Alerts sales team with lead details
  • Update module - Marks leads as contacted in Airtable

The workflow runs automatically every 15 minutes (configurable) and processes up to 10 leads per execution.

Email & Calendar Automation

The system sends two critical communications:

  1. Personalized email - Thanks lead for inquiry and provides calendar link
  2. Calendar invite - Pre-scheduled based on your availability rules

At 18:30 in the video, you'll see the actual email template with merged fields pulling the lead's name, company, and specific needs from the form submission.

Slack Notifications

The AI-generated summary gets posted to your sales team Slack channel with:

  • Lead contact information
  • Budget amount
  • Concise needs summary
  • Qualification status

This eliminates manual data entry and ensures immediate follow-up while details are fresh.
