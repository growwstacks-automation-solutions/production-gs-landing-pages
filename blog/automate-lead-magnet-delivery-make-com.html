Every marketer knows lead magnets work - but the manual delivery process kills scalability. When someone signs up for your free guide, template, or checklist, you're stuck:

  • Checking for new submissions throughout the day
  • Manually attaching files or copying download links
  • Forgetting to follow up when busy
  • Wasting 15-20 minutes per signup on repetitive tasks

This friction causes many businesses to abandon lead magnets altogether, missing out on valuable email subscribers. At 2:45 in the tutorial video, we show how these manual steps add up to hours of lost productivity each week.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Step 1: Create Your Make.com Scenario

Start a new scenario in Make.com and set the trigger to watch for new form submissions. The platform supports Google Forms, Typeform, Bubble, and most other form builders through webhooks or API connections.

Step 2: Connect Your Form Provider

Authenticate with your form service and select the specific form you want to monitor. Make.com will automatically detect new submissions in real-time.

Step 3: Set Up File Delivery

Connect to Dropbox, Google Drive, or your preferred cloud storage to retrieve the lead magnet file. The workflow will dynamically select the correct resource based on which form field was completed.

Step 4: Configure Email Delivery

Set up the email template with your brand messaging and attach the downloaded file. Use HTML formatting for professional-looking automated emails.

Step 5: Add CRM Integration

Optionally connect to Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, or your CRM to automatically add the new contact to your mailing list with proper tags.
