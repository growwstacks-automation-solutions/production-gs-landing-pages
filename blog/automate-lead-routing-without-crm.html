The Hidden Costs of Manual Lead Routing

Every minute your sales team spends manually assigning leads is a minute they're not closing deals. The average small business loses 3-5 hours per week on lead distribution tasks - forwarding emails, updating spreadsheets, and resolving disputes about who gets which leads.

Even worse, manual routing creates bottlenecks. When your top salesperson is out sick or on vacation, leads pile up unanswered. Important opportunities slip through the cracks because there's no system to automatically reassign them.

The opportunity cost: For a team closing $50,000/month in sales, those wasted hours represent $1,250-$2,000 in lost revenue every week from delayed follow-ups and missed opportunities.

How Round-Robin Routing Works

Round-robin is the simplest fair distribution system. Imagine three sales agents: John, Sara, and Mary. The first lead goes to John, the second to Sara, the third to Mary, then the cycle repeats with lead #4 going back to John.

This automated approach eliminates favoritism claims and ensures every agent gets equal opportunity. The system remembers where it left off, so even if you add new agents or someone leaves, the distribution stays perfectly balanced.

5-Minute Setup Guide

Setting up automated lead routing requires no technical skills. Here's exactly how to do it:

Step 1: Create Your Group

Log into your Make.com account and navigate to the Advanced RoundRobin section. Create a new group (we'll name ours "Sales") and you'll immediately see your group email address.

Step 2: Add Your Agents

Simply enter each agent's name and email address. The system supports unlimited agents - add as many as you need.

Step 3: Connect Your Lead Sources

Use your new group email address in:

  • Contact forms (WPForms, Contact Form 7, etc.)
  • Email forwarding rules
  • Any system that can send emails

That's it! Your leads will now automatically distribute evenly among your team with zero ongoing maintenance.

Beyond Basic: Advanced Routing Options

While simple round-robin works perfectly for many teams, you can customize the system for more sophisticated needs:

Weighted Distribution

Give your top performers more leads by adjusting weights. For example, John might get 2 leads for every 1 that Sara and Mary receive.

Time-Based Routing

Only send leads during business hours, or route to different teams based on time of day (e.g., morning vs. evening shifts).

AI Classification

Use AI to analyze lead content and route high-value opportunities to your closers while distributing smaller leads to junior team members.
