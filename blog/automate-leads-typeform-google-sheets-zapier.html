Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Step 1: Create Your Zap

Log into Zapier and click "Make a Zap." Name it something clear like "Typeform to Google Sheets Leads."

Step 2: Set the Trigger

Select Typeform as your trigger app. Choose "New Submission" as the trigger event. Connect your Typeform account if you haven't already.

Step 3: Select Your Form

Pick the specific Typeform you want to automate from the dropdown. Zapier will pull a test submission to verify the connection.

Step 4: Set Up the Action

Choose Google Sheets as your action app. Select "Create Spreadsheet Row" as the action. Connect your Google account and select your target spreadsheet.

Pro Tip: Create a dedicated "Leads" sheet if you don't already have one. Keep headers simple like "Name," "Email," "Phone," etc.

Field Mapping Made Simple

The magic happens in the field mapping stage where you connect Typeform questions to spreadsheet columns:

  1. For each Google Sheet column header (like "Email"), click the dropdown
  2. Select the matching Typeform question (like "What's your email address?")
  3. Repeat for all fields you want to capture

Zapier remembers these mappings permanently. Even if you add new questions later, you can easily update your Zap without starting over.

Testing and Publishing Your Zap

Before going live, Zapier lets you test with real data:

  1. Click "Test & Continue" to send a sample submission
  2. Verify the data appears correctly in your Google Sheet
  3. Fix any mismatched fields if needed
  4. Click "Publish" to activate the automation

That's it! Your Zap will now run automatically for every new submission. You can monitor activity in the Zap history tab.
