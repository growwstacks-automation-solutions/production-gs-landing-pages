The Friday Reporting Nightmare

Every sales team knows the drill - Friday morning arrives, and you're staring at multiple Lemlist campaigns with scattered data across different tabs. Manually exporting CSV files, copying stats into spreadsheets, and formatting client-ready reports eats up half your day before you can even analyze what's working.

The worst part? By the time you finish compiling last week's numbers, new campaign data is already coming in, making your reports outdated before clients even see them. This endless cycle leaves no time for actual strategy or optimization.

83% of sales teams spend more time compiling reports than analyzing what the data means for their outreach strategy. Automation flips this ratio.

Make.com Workflow Overview

This automation solves the reporting headache with a three-part system that runs automatically every Friday morning. At 11:00 AM sharp, Make.com:

  1. Pulls all campaign data from Lemlist's API
  2. Organizes it into a structured Google Sheets template
  3. Generates client-ready reports with performance trends

The workflow begins with a schedule trigger set for Fridays at 11:00 AM (as mentioned at 3:15 in the video). This timing allows the system to capture all weekly data while giving you afternoon hours to review before client meetings.

Lemlist Data Extraction

The first critical module connects to Lemlist's API using your account credentials. It retrieves campaign statistics including open rates, reply rates, and interest markers across all active campaigns.

A nested iterator (shown at 1:45 in the video) processes each campaign separately, extracting the campaign ID first before fetching detailed statistics. This ensures clean, organized data flows to the next steps rather than a jumbled array of metrics.

Pro Tip: The workflow handles Lemlist's pagination automatically, solving the common issue of missing data beyond the first 100 records.

Google Sheets Report Template

The magic happens in the customizable Google Sheets template. We've designed it with several smart features:

  • Summary Dashboard: High-level metrics across all campaigns
  • Campaign Drilldown: Individual sheets for each campaign
  • Trend Analysis: Week-over-week performance comparisons
  • Automated Formatting: Conditional formatting highlights key metrics

The template uses pivot tables (referenced at 0:45) to segment data by target audience, geography, and other dimensions. ChatGPT can help generate additional formulas for advanced analysis if needed.

Automated Report Delivery

For each campaign, the workflow checks whether data already exists in the Google Sheet (at 2:20 in the video). If found, it updates the existing row. If new, it adds the campaign to the appropriate sheet.

This intelligent routing ensures your reports maintain complete historical data while staying organized. The system can handle hundreds of campaigns without manual intervention, as demonstrated by the 296 records already processed in the example.

Tracking Interested Leads

A secondary workflow (mentioned at 4:30) focuses specifically on leads who have expressed interest. It:

  1. Identifies all "interested" markers in Lemlist
  2. Retrieves complete activity history for those leads
  3. Creates a dedicated "Hot Leads" tab in the report

This gives your sales team immediate visibility into the most promising contacts each week, along with context about what messaging resonated with them.
