How the Automation Works

The system uses a simple but powerful chain: Google Forms collects your post ideas and preferences → Make.com connects everything → AI generates the post → You review and publish. Here's the complete flow:

  1. Fill out a 2-minute Google Form with your post topic and preferences
  2. Responses automatically go to Google Sheets
  3. Make.com detects new form submissions
  4. AI (ChatGPT/Perplexity) generates a post using your inputs
  5. The draft gets emailed to you for quick review
  6. Copy-paste into LinkedIn (or connect directly via API)

At 3:15 in the video tutorial, you'll see how the form responses trigger the entire workflow automatically. The beauty is in the simplicity - no coding required, just smart connections between tools you already use.

Step 1: Setup Your Google Form

The Google Form serves as your content request system. Keep it simple but include these essential fields:

  • Post Topic: What you want to write about (e.g. "Latest industry trends")
  • Keywords: Specific terms to include (e.g. "AI automation, lead generation")
  • Target Audience: Who should find this valuable (e.g. "SaaS founders")
  • Tone Preference: Professional, conversational, or technical
  • Call-to-Action: What you want readers to do next

Pro Tip: Add a multiple-choice question for common post types (thought leadership, case study, news reaction) to help the AI structure content appropriately.

Step 2: Connect Make.com

Make.com (formerly Integromat) acts as the automation brain. Here's how to set it up:

Step 2.1: Create a New Scenario

Select Google Sheets as your trigger module. Connect it to the spreadsheet receiving your form responses. Set it to watch for new rows.

Step 2.2: Add Filtering

Add a router to only process complete submissions (skip blank or test entries). This prevents wasted AI credits.

Step 2.3: Prepare AI Inputs

Use the "Set multiple variables" module to organize the form data into clear instructions for the AI. Combine fields logically like: "Write a [tone] post about [topic] for [audience] that includes [keywords]."

Time-saver: At 8:45 in the video, you'll see how to structure the perfect AI prompt using all collected form data.

Step 3: Configure AI Integration

This is where the magic happens. Connect your preferred AI tool (we recommend Perplexity or ChatGPT):

Option A: Perplexity AI

1. Get your API key from account settings
2. Add the HTTP module in Make.com
3. Configure to send your crafted prompt
4. Parse the JSON response for the generated text

Option B: ChatGPT

1. Use Make.com's native OpenAI module
2. Set temperature to 0.7 for balanced creativity
3. Include examples of your writing style in the system prompt

The video at 12:30 shows both methods side-by-side. Perplexity often provides more up-to-date information while ChatGPT excels at stylistic matching.

Step 4: Test Your Workflow

Before going live, run several test cases:

  1. Submit test form entries with different topics and tones
  2. Check that Make.com processes them correctly
  3. Verify AI output quality matches your expectations
  4. Adjust prompts for any consistent issues

Common refinements include:

  • Adding more style examples to prompts
  • Setting character limits for cleaner outputs
  • Including instructions to avoid certain overused phrases

At 18:00 in the tutorial, you'll see real-time debugging of a workflow that initially produced posts that were too salesy - and how we fixed it with prompt adjustments.
