The Marketing Report Problem

Marketing teams waste countless hours every morning jumping between multiple platforms - checking Google Ads performance, reviewing Facebook campaign metrics, analyzing Shopify sales data, and interpreting Google Analytics traffic. By the time they've compiled all this information manually, it's often midday, and opportunities to optimize campaigns are already lost.

The worst part? This manual data gathering is repetitive, error-prone, and provides no actual analysis - just raw numbers that require additional interpretation. Marketing leaders need insights, not just data dumps.

68% of marketers report spending 2-3 hours daily just compiling reports from different platforms, time that could be better spent on strategy and optimization.

How AI Solves This

AI models like Claude can analyze complex datasets and provide natural language summaries - but they lack direct access to your marketing platforms. This is where n8n bridges the gap. By connecting AI to your actual business data through the MCP server, you get the best of both worlds:

  • Real-time accurate data from all your platforms
  • AI-powered analysis highlighting key trends and opportunities
  • Automated delivery to your inbox or Slack before your workday begins

The workflow runs on a schedule (typically midnight), gathers all the fresh data, asks AI to summarize it, and sends you only the insights you need - no more dashboard hopping.

Building the Workflow

This n8n automation consists of three main components working together:

  1. The AI model (Claude) that analyzes and summarizes the data
  2. The MCP server that connects to all your marketing platforms
  3. The email/Slack output that delivers the final report

When configured correctly, these elements create a seamless pipeline from raw data to actionable insights without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Setup AI Model

The first step is configuring your AI model in n8n. For this workflow, we're using Claude Sonnet for its balance of cost and capability:

  1. Create an API key at platform.claude.com/settings/keys
  2. In n8n, add a new "AI Agent" node to your workflow
  3. Select "Enthropic Chat Model" and paste your API key
  4. Choose Claude Sonnet 4.5 (cost-effective for daily reports)

Pro Tip: The prompt you give the AI determines the quality of your report. Be specific about what metrics to analyze and how to format the output.

Step 2: Connect Data Sources

With the AI ready, we connect the marketing platforms through the MCP client tool in n8n:

  1. Add the "MCP Client" tool to your workflow
  2. Configure OAuth2 authentication with mcp.advisor.com
  3. Select your workspace (contains all connected platforms)
  4. Enable dynamic client registration

The workspace acts as a central hub for all your marketing data - Google Ads, Facebook, Shopify, Google Analytics, and more. Once connected, the AI can query any available metric from these platforms.

Step 3: Configure Email Output

The final step is setting up how you'll receive the AI-generated report:

  1. Add an "Email" node to your n8n workflow
  2. Configure your SMTP settings (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  3. Set the subject line (e.g., "Yesterday's Marketing Report")
  4. Map the AI's JSON output to the email body
  5. Test with your recipient email address

At 2:15 in the video tutorial, you'll see how we initially forgot this step and had to go back to configure the email properly - a common mistake to watch for.
