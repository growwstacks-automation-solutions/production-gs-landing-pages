Teams Transcription Workflow (No License Needed)

The breakthrough comes from combining two standard Microsoft 365 features that most businesses already have:

  1. Teams Meeting Transcription: Available in all business plans, this creates a text record of any meeting
  2. Copilot Agents: The ability to create custom AI assistants is included in base Copilot access

Here's how to capture transcripts without special permissions (demonstrated at 1:15 in the video):

  1. Start or join any Teams meeting
  2. Click "More options" → "Record and transcribe"
  3. Select "Start transcription" (not recording)
  4. Confirm the spoken language

After the meeting, download the transcript as a Word document from the meeting chat. This raw material becomes the input for your automated notes system.

Building Your Meeting Notes Copilot Agent

The magic happens when you create a specialized Copilot Agent dedicated to meeting notes. Unlike generic Copilot, these Agents remember specific instructions forever, applying them consistently to every meeting.

To create your Agent (shown at 3:42 in the video):

  1. Open Copilot in Teams (left navigation pane)
  2. Click "Create Agent" → "Configure"
  3. Name it (e.g., "Meeting Notes Generator")
  4. Add a description like "Transforms Teams transcripts into professional meeting minutes"

Pro Tip: Create suggested prompts like "Summarize meeting notes" so your team can reuse the Agent with one click.

Custom Instructions That Make the Magic Happen

The real power comes from the Agent's instructions (shared at 4:30 in the video). These tell Copilot exactly how to format your notes. Here's the key structure we recommend:

Always produce meeting notes in a three-column table with:

  • Topic (what was discussed)
  • Meeting Summary (key points)
  • Next Steps (action items with owners)

Additional instructions can specify word limits, tone (professional but conversational), and rules like "never invent action items not mentioned in the transcript." The Agent will follow these guidelines every time.
