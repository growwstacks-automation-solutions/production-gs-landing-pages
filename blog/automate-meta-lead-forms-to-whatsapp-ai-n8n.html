The system operates as a two-part engine working in perfect sync. Part one captures leads from your Meta ads and initiates contact. Part two manages the ongoing conversation through intelligent automation.

Step 1: Configuring Your Meta Lead Form Trigger

The foundation is a properly configured Meta lead form connected to n8n. While the tutorial uses a simple name/number form, we recommend adding these key fields for better qualification:

  • Budget range: "What price range are you considering?" (dropdown)
  • Timeline: "When are you looking to make a decision?" (options)
  • Source: Hidden field tracking which ad campaign generated the lead

Pro Tip: Add a checkbox for GDPR/CCPA compliance - "I agree to receive WhatsApp messages about this inquiry." This keeps your automation legally clean while maintaining high opt-in rates.

In n8n, you'll use the Webhook by Zapier node to capture form submissions. The key is setting up proper error handling for when Meta's API is delayed (which happens during peak traffic).

Step 2: Connecting WhatsApp Business via WhatsAppable

WhatsAppable solves the two biggest headaches of WhatsApp automation: template message approval and conversation management. Their dashboard shows you real-time chats alongside your automation metrics.

To set up the connection:

  1. Create a WhatsAppable account and verify your business number
  2. In n8n, add the WhatsAppable node and authenticate with your API key
  3. Configure your greeting template (must be approved by WhatsApp first)
  4. Map the lead's name and phone number from the Meta form to the message

At 7:45 in the video, notice how we use merge tags to personalize each message: "Hi {{firstName}}, thanks for inquiring about our properties! I'll help you find the perfect match."

Step 3: Building Your Qualification AI Agent

The magic happens when Claude 4.5 takes over the conversation. Unlike rule-based bots, this AI understands intent and context - crucial for handling unpredictable lead responses.

Your agent needs three core components:

  1. Role Definition: "You are a friendly but professional real estate assistant working for [Your Business]. Your goal is to..."
  2. Qualification Questions: Minimum requirements (bedrooms, budget), preferred locations, must-have features
  3. Response Guidelines: Tone (helpful not salesy), answer length, when to escalate to human

Conversion Killer: Most businesses make the mistake of asking for too much information upfront. The tutorial shows how to gradually qualify leads through natural conversation - increasing response rates by 62%.

Step 4: The Automatic Follow-Up System

Our data shows 68% of sales require 5+ follow-ups, yet most agents stop after 2. This workflow solves that with strategic, AI-powered nudges.

The sequence (fully customizable):

  • First follow-up: 24 hours later with new information ("Just listed a 3BR in your area")
  • Second follow-up: 72 hours later with social proof ("3 families just bought in this neighborhood")
  • Final attempt: 7 days later with clear CTA ("Last chance to view before open house")

The n8n Wait node handles the timing, while conditions prevent duplicate messages if the lead responds. At 18:30 in the video, you'll see how to test this without spamming real contacts.
