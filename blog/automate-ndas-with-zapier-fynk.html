The Hidden Cost of Manual NDAs

NDAs should be simple — they're standard documents used constantly in business. Yet most companies waste hours on manual NDA processes: chasing emails back and forth, editing Word documents, converting to PDFs, and tracking signatures. This administrative burden delays critical conversations with investors, clients, and partners.

The average NDA process takes 47 minutes of manual work — time that could be spent on revenue-generating activities instead.

How the Zapier + fynk Automation Works

This integration creates a completely touchless NDA process. Instead of manual document handling, you simply share a request link — on your website, in emails, or even via WhatsApp. When the counterparty submits their details, the automation handles everything else.

Here's what happens automatically:

  1. fynk generates a customized NDA using your template
  2. The document is sent to your counterparty for signing
  3. Once signed, the executed NDA is stored securely in your account
  4. Your team receives automatic notifications at each step

For one-way NDAs (where only your counterparty signs), the entire process completes without any manual intervention. Two-way NDAs still require your signature but eliminate all the document creation and tracking work.

Implementation Steps

Setting up this automation requires just a few simple steps in Zapier:

Step 1: Create Your NDA Template

Upload your standard NDA to fynk and designate any fields that need customization (names, dates, etc.). These will auto-populate from the request form.

Step 2: Set Up Your Request Form

Create a simple form (using your preferred tool) to collect counterparty details. This could be embedded on your website or shared directly.

Step 3: Connect with Zapier

The Zapier automation triggers when the form is submitted, sending the data to fynk to generate the NDA and initiate the signing process.

Implementation tip: Add a second Zap to notify your team in Slack or email when the NDA is signed.
