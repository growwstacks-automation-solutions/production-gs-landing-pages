Four-Stage Workflow

The automation follows a precise, four-step sequence for each new lead:

Stage 1: Capture

n8n instantly detects the new form submission through a webhook or API connection. This triggers the entire workflow within milliseconds.

Stage 2: Analyze

The lead's raw message is sent to OpenAI, which extracts key details like company size, budget indicators, and specific needs.

Stage 3: Structure

AI-processed data is formatted into clean CRM fields with a lead score (hot/warm/cold) and assigned priority.

Stage 4: Route

The qualified lead enters Odoo CRM while simultaneously triggering personalized email/Slack notifications.

In summary: Raw lead → AI analysis → Structured data → CRM entry + notifications = 30 seconds total.
