The Manual Review Problem

Service businesses lose hundreds of potential 5-star reviews every month because asking manually feels awkward and time-consuming. Staff members dread making review request calls, often putting them off until it's too late. Even when they do call, the conversations feel transactional and reduce response rates.

The breakthrough came when we discovered AI voice agents could handle these conversations naturally while maintaining 24/7 availability. Unlike robocalls or IVR systems, modern AI agents like Assistable use conversational tones that customers perceive as human - making them far more effective for sensitive requests like reviews.

72% of customers are willing to leave a review when asked politely, but only 7% do so without prompting. Automated calling bridges this gap at scale.

How AI Calling Works in GoHighLevel

The integration between GoHighLevel and Assistable creates a seamless workflow for outbound review requests. When a contact receives the "get review" tag in GHL, it triggers an automation that:

  1. Initiates a call through the AI voice agent
  2. Delivers a personalized greeting using the contact's name
  3. Asks if they'd be willing to leave a review
  4. Texts them the review link if they agree
  5. Logs the call outcome back in GoHighLevel

All of this happens automatically without any staff involvement. The system can process hundreds of calls per day, only escalating to human agents when absolutely necessary.

Setting Up the Automation

The core automation (shown at 5:12 in the video) uses just a few key nodes in GoHighLevel:

  1. Trigger: Listens for the "get review" tag being added to a contact
  2. Make AI Call: Connects to the Assistable agent using your configured assistant ID
  3. Variables: Passes contact details and custom prompts to personalize the call
  4. Outcome Handling: Tags contacts based on call results (agreed, declined, etc.)

Once built, this automation can be reused across all your sub-accounts with no additional setup. Simply tag contacts to initiate calls - no manual intervention required.

Batch Calling Feature

For businesses with hundreds of contacts, the system includes intelligent batch calling (shown at 5:45):

  • Processes contacts in groups of 10-20 to avoid overwhelming your phone lines
  • Automatically spaces calls 2-3 minutes apart for natural pacing
  • Pauses during peak business hours if needed
  • Resumes automatically until all tagged contacts are processed

This means you can tag 500 contacts on Monday morning, and the system will methodically work through them over the next 48 hours - all while your team focuses on revenue-generating activities.
