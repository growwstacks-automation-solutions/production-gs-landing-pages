The Email Outreach Problem

Manual email outreach is broken. Most businesses either send generic templates that get ignored or spend hours researching each lead - only to send emails that still miss the mark. At 10-15 minutes per personalized email, scaling quality outreach becomes impossible.

The breakthrough comes from separating the research and writing processes. This Make.com workflow uses AI to handle the time-consuming research, then structures the findings into proven email templates automatically. The result? Emails that reference specific blog posts, LinkedIn updates, or case studies from each recipient's background - without manual digging.

94% reduction in manual work: What normally takes 8 hours to research and write 100 emails now happens automatically in 30 minutes, while actually improving personalization quality through AI-powered research.

How the AI Research Process Works

The magic happens in the research phase. Rather than just inserting a name into a template, the workflow uses Perplexity AI to analyze each lead's:

  • Company website (looking beyond the homepage)
  • LinkedIn profile and activity
  • Industry trends relevant to their role
  • Recent content they've published or engaged with

Key settings ensure quality research without breaking the bank. Using Perplexity's Stoner model keeps costs at $0.02-$0.05 per email while still extracting meaningful insights. The AI is instructed to avoid obvious homepage facts and instead find "small non-obvious things" that demonstrate genuine familiarity.

Email Template Engineering

The email template follows a proven structure that converts while allowing for personalization:

Template structure enforced by AI: 1) Personalized greeting 2) Specific reference to their work 3) Your value proposition 4) Relevant case study 5) Clear CTA - all while maintaining natural language.

Critical technical details make the emails work:

  • HTML formatting with break tags for proper email display
  • Company name shortening ("XYZ" instead of "XYZ Marketing Agency")
  • No sign-off (lets your Gmail signature handle it)
  • Strict rules preventing AI hallucinations or format deviations

Make.com Workflow Breakdown

The complete automation flows through these key steps:

Step 1: Lead Input

Google Sheets serves as the lead source with columns for name, company, LinkedIn URL, and other identifiers. The workflow can process 100+ rows at once.

Step 2: AI Research

Perplexity AI receives each lead's details with instructions to research deeply and output only relevant findings for email personalization.

Step 3: Email Generation

The researched insights populate a structured template, creating a completely unique email for each recipient while maintaining your core message.

Step 4: Subject Line Creation

A separate GPT-3.5 module generates attention-grabbing subject lines following the "[Interesting fact] + [Your company]" formula that outperforms generic lines.

Step 5: Send & Log

Gmail sends each email with proper formatting, while the lead and email details log to your CRM automatically.

Deliverability Best Practices

Automated outreach lives or dies by inbox placement. This workflow includes several key safeguards:

  • Real Gmail/Outlook accounts: Sends come from your actual email via official APIs, not third-party services that trigger spam filters
  • Natural send pacing: Emails go out staggered throughout the day rather than in bursts
  • High personalization: Unique content for each recipient reduces spam flags
  • List hygiene: Automatically removes bounced emails to protect sender reputation

Additional pro tips include warming up new sending domains and maintaining a healthy reply-to-send ratio by tracking responses in your CRM.
