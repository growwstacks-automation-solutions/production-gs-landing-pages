The Email Personalization Dilemma

Every marketer knows personalized emails get up to 50% higher open rates and 75% more clicks than generic blasts. But manually researching each contact and crafting custom messages is incredibly time-consuming. At 15-20 minutes per email, even a modest outreach campaign of 50 contacts becomes a 12+ hour project.

The breakthrough came when we realized AI could automate the research while Make.com could handle the workflow orchestration. By combining these tools, we created a system that delivers personalization at scale without the manual effort.

Key insight: Personalized doesn't mean handwritten. The human touch comes from relevant content, not manual creation.

The Make.com Solution

Make.com (formerly Integromat) provides the perfect platform for this automation because it connects all the necessary components: your contact database, AI research tools, and email generation systems. The workflow follows three key phases:

  1. Pull contact data from your source (Google Sheets, Airtable, CRM)
  2. Research each contact using AI (Perplexity in this example)
  3. Generate personalized email content (using GPT-4)

What makes this powerful is that each step happens automatically once configured. At 2:15 in the tutorial video, you'll see how the entire process flows from one module to the next without manual intervention.

Step 1: Connect Your Contact Data

The workflow starts with your existing contact list. In the tutorial, we use Google Sheets with columns for first name, last name, and any known background information. Make.com's Google Sheets connector pulls this data automatically.

Key configuration points:

  • Select the correct spreadsheet and worksheet
  • Map columns to variables (first name, last name, etc.)
  • Set appropriate row limits for batch processing

Pro tip: Always test the connection by running just the first module to verify you're pulling the correct data before building the rest of the workflow.

Step 2: Automate AI Research

With contact data flowing into the workflow, we next connect to Perplexity AI to research each individual. The AI receives two key pieces of information:

  1. A system prompt explaining what we want (research this person)
  2. The user prompt with the actual name and any background info

At 3:48 in the video, you'll see how the Perplexity module returns a comprehensive research summary in seconds - work that would take a human 10-15 minutes per contact.

This step demonstrates why specialized AI tools often outperform general-purpose models for specific tasks like professional research.

Step 3: Generate Personalized Emails

The final phase uses GPT-4 to transform the research into compelling email copy. We provide:

  • Brand voice guidelines in the system prompt
  • The contact's first name for personalization
  • The AI-generated research as context

The result (visible at 5:20 in the tutorial) is a professionally written email that references specific, relevant details about the recipient. While the initial output may need minor tweaking, it captures the essence of what would take most people 5-7 minutes to draft manually.

Important: Always include opt-out instructions and comply with email regulations when automating outreach.
