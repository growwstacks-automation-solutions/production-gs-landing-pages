How Automation Solves the Proposal Deck Challenge

This n8n workflow eliminates the manual grunt work by automatically generating professional proposal decks from meeting transcripts. The system captures all the essential elements of your client conversation and transforms them into a polished deliverable in minutes.

The automation works through two connected workflows: First, it logs meeting details and transcripts to Google Sheets. Then, it processes this information through AI to create a structured, professional slide deck in Gamma.app - complete with key discussion points, proposed solutions, and next steps.

Workflow Breakdown: From Meeting to Deck in Minutes

The complete automation consists of two n8n workflows that work together seamlessly. The first captures and organizes meeting data, while the second transforms that data into a client-ready proposal deck.

Here's how the magic happens:

Step 1: Automatically Logging Meeting Details

The workflow begins when your meeting ends in Fireflies.ai. A webhook triggers n8n to capture all meeting details - attendees, transcript, AI summary, and duration. This data gets cleaned and logged in a Google Sheet that serves as your meeting repository.

The logging process includes intelligent data handling to ensure only complete meetings get processed. If critical information is missing, the workflow can trigger alerts or skip processing until manual review.

Step 2: Intelligent Transcript Processing

Raw meeting transcripts can be messy, with repetitive speaker labels and fragmented thoughts. This workflow includes a custom JavaScript function that cleans up the transcript into a more readable format while preserving all key information.

The code node identifies speaker changes and organizes the content logically, removing redundant name tags while maintaining clear attribution. This creates a cleaner foundation for the AI to generate proposal content.

Step 3: AI-Powered Deck Generation

With clean meeting data available, the workflow sends the information to Gamma.app through its API. The AI analyzes the transcript to identify key discussion points, decisions, and action items - then structures this into a professional slide deck format.

A critical feature is the human approval step before final generation. This lets you review the AI's interpretation and make adjustments if needed, ensuring quality control while still saving 80% of the manual work.
