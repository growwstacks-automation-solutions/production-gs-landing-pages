Instant Lead Response That Converts 391% Better

The workflow's first automation solves the critical response gap. When a lead submits any form (website, Facebook, etc.), n8n triggers an instant phone connection sequence:

Step 1: Webhook Capture

n8n detects the form submission within milliseconds, extracting all lead details (name, phone, email, service needed).

Step 2: Automated Call Initiation

The system calls the sales rep's phone first, then bridges in the lead once answered - no dialing, no voicemails.

Step 3: CRM Record Creation

Simultaneously, a complete lead record populates in your CRM (HighLevel, HubSpot, or Google Sheets).

391% conversion lift: Immediate response converts 4x more leads than delayed follow-up. The system also logs every interaction automatically.
