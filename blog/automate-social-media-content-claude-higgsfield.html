The blank page wins every morning, and the pressure to post daily leads to rushed, low-quality content that doesn't convert.

Traditional solutions - hiring content creators, videographers, and editors - cost thousands per month. That's why forward-thinking businesses are turning to AI automation. By combining Claude's strategic content planning with Higsfield's visual generation capabilities, you can create a complete content machine for less than the cost of a single dinner out.

Turning Claude Into Your Social Media Manager

The foundation of this system is Claude Projects - essentially giving Claude long-term memory about your brand. Unlike a regular chat that forgets everything when closed, a Project remembers your voice, niche, and content style across all sessions.

To set this up, create a new Project in Claude and paste in your brand context: who you are, who you create content for, and your tone. For example: "AI tools for creators who aren't engineers. Calm, honest, slightly skeptical voice. We talk like a friend who actually tested the tool, not a guru selling a course."

The secret weapon? Add 3-5 competitor channel URLs. Claude studies their viral posts to identify what's working in your niche right now - the exact insights a human strategist would charge thousands to provide.

Building 3 Higsfield Canvas Templates

While Claude handles the strategy and writing, Higsfield Canvas generates all the visuals. Think of Canvas as a visual workflow builder where you connect "nodes" (each handling one task) into automated pipelines. We'll create three templates that cover every content type you need:

  1. Carousel Template: For Instagram/LinkedIn slides with clean text rendering
  2. Talking Head Template: Videos with your AI avatar speaking scripts
  3. Cinematic B-Roll Template: Professional footage without filming

Once built, these templates become reusable content factories - just feed in Claude's prompts and get polished content out.

Carousel Template (7 Slides in 15 Minutes)

The simplest template generates carousel slides. It uses just two nodes: a prompt box feeding into GPT Image 2 (the only model that renders text cleanly). Set the aspect ratio to 1:1 and resolution to 4K.

Claude provides the complete visual prompt for each slide. To generate a 7-slide carousel, duplicate the node pair six times, paste each slide's prompt, and run the pipeline. Total cost: about 84 credits ($0.84). Total time: under 15 minutes.

Pro Tip: Save your first carousel as a template. For future posts, just duplicate the canvas and replace the prompts - no rebuilding required.

Talking Head Video Template

This is where the magic happens - creating videos of your AI avatar speaking, gesturing, and lip-syncing without filming anything. You have two options:

  1. Digital Twin: Upload 20 photos to create a permanent AI version of yourself
  2. Custom Character: Use a generated persona if you prefer anonymity

The template chains your character image with Seedance 2.0 (for video generation). Each 15-second script segment gets its own prompt box and video generator. Connect them all to your character node, set aspect ratio to 9:16 (vertical), and enable audio generation.

Claude chunks your script into natural 8-13 second segments. Each generation produces 4 variations - pick the best takes just like a real shoot. The lip-sync accuracy will shock you.

Cinematic B-Roll Template

For professional B-roll, we chain two models: Flux 2 Max generates photographic stills, then Cling 3.0 animates them into 8-second clips. This two-step process gives far better results than generating video directly from text.

The key is feeding each shot two reference images: your character (for consistency) and a workspace photo. Claude's visual description gets chunked into individual shots - typically 8 per post. Each has its own motion prompt (e.g., "hands close the laptop lid slowly").

These B-roll clips become reusable assets. Save them in a library to drop into future videos, saving generation costs over time.

Final Assembly and Posting

The only manual step is light editing in CapCut or similar software:

  • Talking Heads: Stitch segments together, add captions/music
  • B-Roll: Sequence shots rhythmically, add text overlays
  • Carousels: Already complete - just download and post

Best part? Claude wrote all your captions and hashtags upfront. Just copy-paste them from the original output - no additional writing needed.
