How the Automated Content Machine Works

The workflow follows four key steps that transform a simple product screenshot into polished, platform-optimized content. What used to require Photoshop skills and hours of manual labor now happens automatically with just a Telegram message.

At 3:15 in the video tutorial, you can see the complete n8n workflow in action. The system begins when you send a product image via Telegram with simple instructions like "extract the blue product." From there, AI handles the complex image processing while n8n orchestrates the entire content creation pipeline.

Step 1: AI-Powered Product Extraction

The system uses advanced AI to identify and isolate specific products from images, even in cluttered environments. You can request products by color ("extract the blue one"), position ("front left product"), or other distinguishing features. The AI reconstructs missing parts and returns a high-resolution 4K image.

This replaces what would normally require a designer to manually cut out products in Photoshop — a process that can take hours for complex images. The AI accomplishes it in seconds with equal or better quality.

Step 2: Automatic Background Removal

Once extracted, the product goes through an automatic background removal process. This creates a clean PNG with transparency, ready to be placed in any marketing design. The system handles tricky edge cases like hair, fur, or transparent materials that typically challenge manual editing.

At 7:45 in the video, you can see how the workflow processes the image through multiple AI services to achieve perfect results every time, regardless of the original image quality.

Step 3: Dynamic Template Creation in Abyssale

Abyssale serves as the design engine for the workflow. You create templates once with placeholders for products, text, and branding elements. The n8n workflow then dynamically populates these templates with the processed product images and any additional content.

The magic happens in Abyssale's layer manager (shown at 12:30 in the video). Each element you want to automate — product image, price, description, CTA button — gets named and positioned. The workflow references these names when injecting content, ensuring perfect placement every time.

Step 4: Multi-Platform Content Generation

The final step automatically generates platform-specific versions from your master template. A single product becomes an Instagram post, Facebook ad, LinkedIn carousel, and TikTok video — all with the proper dimensions, cropping, and formatting for each platform.

At 18:20 in the tutorial, you'll see how the workflow can even schedule and publish the content directly to your social media accounts through integrations with platforms like Buffer or directly via API.

In summary: Send product image via Telegram → AI extracts and cleans the product → Workflow injects it into your Abyssale templates → System generates and publishes platform-optimized content automatically.
