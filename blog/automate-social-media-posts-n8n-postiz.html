How the Workflow Operates

The automation begins with your topic settings - defining your industry focus and preferred content types (video or image posts). At 1:15 in the video, you'll see how simple dropdowns control the entire workflow's direction.

From there, the system handles everything automatically:

  1. AI research via Perplexity generates 10 relevant news items
  2. Another AI assistant crafts engaging post text with platform-specific variations
  3. The workflow determines if it should create an image or video
  4. Content gets uploaded to Postiz for distribution
  5. All your connected social channels receive the post automatically

The merge node is the workflow's secret weapon - it elegantly combines the image and video paths so you don't need separate posting logic for each content type. This smart architecture makes the workflow easier to maintain and modify.

AI Research and Content Creation

Traditional social media tools force you to come up with post ideas manually. This workflow flips that model by using AI to research your industry and generate fresh content daily.

The Perplexity AI node (shown at 2:30 in the video) scrapes the web for trending topics in your niche. The system then processes these findings through another AI layer that:

  • Creates attention-grabbing headlines
  • Writes full post text in your brand voice
  • Generates shortened versions for platforms with character limits

Unlike generic AI tools, this workflow tailors content specifically for social media - with the right mix of engagement hooks and informational value that drives interactions.

Visual Content Generation

Social media algorithms prioritize posts with visuals, but creating images and videos takes even more time than writing text. This workflow automates that too.

The system intelligently generates professional image prompts based on your post text, then feeds them to Gemini AI (Google's image generation model). At 3:45 in the tutorial, you can see how the workflow:

  1. Analyzes the post text to determine visual needs
  2. Creates detailed, brand-appropriate image prompts
  3. Generates multiple visual options
  4. Selects the strongest image for your posts

For video posts, the process is similar but generates short, engaging video clips instead. The workflow automatically adjusts content formatting for each platform's specifications.

Cross-Platform Posting

Managing multiple social accounts is where most automation tools fall short. This workflow shines by handling posting across all your channels simultaneously.

Postiz supports over 40 platforms (shown at 4:20 in the video), including:

  • All major networks: LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook
  • Emerging platforms like Blue Sky and Mastodon
  • Video platforms including YouTube and TikTok
  • Community sites like Reddit and niche forums

The workflow first retrieves all your connected channels, then uses an aggregate node to prepare the posts. You can choose to publish immediately, schedule for later, or create drafts for manual review - giving you complete control over your posting strategy.

Setting Up Postiz

Deploying Postiz is simpler than you might think. The entire system runs in a Docker container that you can host on Railway with just a few clicks (demonstrated at 5:50 in the video).

The key steps are:

  1. Create a Railway account (free tier available)
  2. Click the Postiz deploy button
  3. Configure your environment variables
  4. Set up platform connections using each service's API

Postiz provides detailed documentation for connecting each social platform. While the initial setup requires some technical steps, the payoff is a completely free, self-hosted automation system that saves hours every week.
