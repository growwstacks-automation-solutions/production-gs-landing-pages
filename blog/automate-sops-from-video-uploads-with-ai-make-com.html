The Manual SOP Problem Every Operations Team Faces

Creating standard operating procedures from training videos is one of the most time-consuming tasks in business operations. A 30-minute training session can take 2-3 hours to properly document - transcribing audio, organizing steps, applying formatting, and creating reference materials.

This manual process creates three major pain points: inconsistent documentation quality across teams, delayed availability of critical training materials, and valuable employee time wasted on administrative work rather than core operations.

90% reduction in documentation time: What traditionally takes hours of manual work now happens automatically in minutes. The AI-powered workflow handles transcription, structuring, and formatting while your team focuses on higher-value work.

How the Video-to-SOP Automation Works

The automation triggers whenever a new video file is uploaded to a designated Google Drive folder. Make.com monitors this folder continuously, processing the most recent MP4 file through a series of AI-powered steps that transform raw video into professional documentation.

At 1:15 in the tutorial video, you can see the complete workflow visualized across two tabs - one showing the automation builder and another demonstrating the live execution. This dual-view approach makes it easy to understand how each module connects to create the final result.

Step-by-Step Breakdown of the Workflow

Step 1: Google Drive Folder Monitoring

The automation begins with a Google Drive module configured to watch a specific folder for new MP4 uploads. When triggered, it captures the most recently added video file while ignoring other file types.

Step 2: Audio Transcription via Assembly AI

The downloaded video file is sent to Assembly AI, a specialized transcription service that converts the audio to text with timestamps. This creates the raw transcript that will form the basis of both documentation outputs.

Step 3: AI-Powered Title Generation

An OpenAI module analyzes the transcript to generate a clear, descriptive title that accurately represents the video content. This title will be used for both output documents.

Step 4: Structured SOP Creation

A second OpenAI module transforms the raw transcript into a properly formatted SOP document. The AI identifies key steps, applies logical headings, and organizes information into bullet points and numbered lists.

Step 5: Markdown to HTML Conversion

The formatted SOP text (in markdown) is converted to HTML to preserve all structural elements when added to Google Docs. Without this step, headings and lists would appear as plain text.

Step 6: Google Docs Creation

Two separate Google Docs are created - one containing the raw transcript and another with the structured SOP. Both use the AI-generated title with clear labels in brackets.

Step 7: Email Notification

A Gmail module sends a notification with direct links to both documents. The email arrives in your inbox within minutes of video upload, with clickable links that open the docs instantly.

In summary: The workflow monitors for new videos → transcribes audio → generates a title → creates structured SOP → formats documents → sends notification with links. All steps happen automatically without manual intervention.

The AI Transformation: From Raw Transcript to Structured SOP

The magic happens when OpenAI processes the raw transcript into a professional SOP document. The AI doesn't just transcribe words - it understands context, identifies key steps, and applies proper documentation structure.

At 2:30 in the video, you can see side-by-side comparisons of the input (raw transcript) and output (formatted SOP). The transformation includes:

  • Logical section headings (H2/H3) for major process steps
  • Numbered lists for sequential actions
  • Bullet points for parallel options or variations
  • Bold text for important warnings or key terms
  • Consistent paragraph spacing and readability

This structured output means your team gets documentation that's immediately usable for training and reference - no reformatting required.

Professional Document Formatting Automatically Applied

The markdown-to-HTML conversion ensures all structural elements from the AI-generated SOP are preserved in the final Google Doc. This includes:

  • Heading hierarchy maintained (H1, H2, H3)
  • Numbered lists and bullet points preserved
  • Bold and italic formatting retained
  • Code blocks or special notations properly formatted
  • Line breaks and spacing consistent

The result is documentation that looks professionally formatted from the moment it's created. You can further customize the Google Docs template to match your company branding, but the core structure requires no additional work.

Instant Email Notification with Clickable Links

The final step delivers both documents directly to your inbox with clickable links that open the Google Docs instantly. The email includes:

  • Subject line with the AI-generated title
  • Direct link to the structured SOP document
  • Direct link to the raw transcript (with timestamps)
  • Clear labeling distinguishing both documents
  • Option to forward to team members or stakeholders

This notification system means you never need to search through Google Drive to find the outputs. The documents come to you, ready to review, edit, or share with one click.
