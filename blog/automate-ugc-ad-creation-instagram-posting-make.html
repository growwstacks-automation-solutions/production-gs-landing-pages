The 3-Tool Automation Solution

This workflow combines three specialized tools through Make.com:

  1. Lovable: Creates a simple web interface where marketers upload product images and describe the ad concept
  2. OpenAI Sora 2: Generates 15-30 second UGC-style videos from the product image and description
  3. Make.com: Orchestrates the entire workflow from trigger to final Instagram posting

The magic happens in the handoff between systems. Lovable initiates the process but doesn't wait for Sora 2's slower video generation. Instead, Make.com implements a status polling system that updates Lovable when the video is ready.

Building the Lovable Input Interface

We needed dead-simple way for non-technical team members to trigger UGC generation. Lovable let us create a clean web app with just three fields:

  • Product image upload
  • Product name field
  • Optional video concept description

The "Generate Video" button initiates webhook to Make.com. At 1:32 in the tutorial video, you'll see how we configured Lovable to handle the asynchronous nature of AI video generation — it polls Make.com every 30 seconds until the video is ready.

Pro tip: Add sample prompt examples your Lovable interface to guide marketing team creates consistent UGC styles across products.

Sora 2 AI Video Generation

The real breakthrough came with OpenAI's Sora 2 model. Unlike generic AI video tools, we engineered specific prompts that produce UGC-style content:

"You are a UGC ad video creator skilled at crafting short, authentic product showcase videos that look like real user reviews. Create a 20-second of a [25-35 year old woman] sitting in a cozy room holding [Product Name]. She should speak directly to camera like a genuine user, highlighting these features: [Key Features]."

At 4:17 in the video, you'll see how we configure the Sora 2 module in Make.com to use this prompt template while dynamically inserting the product-specific details from Lovable.

Automated Instagram Posting

While optional, the Instagram posting component completes the automation circle. Key considerations:

  1. Make.com uploads the generated video to Dropbox (not Google Drive — see 7:08 in the video for why)
  2. The Instagram module posts using your business account credentials
  3. You can add auto-generated captions using product details from Lovable

The entire process — from image upload to live Instagram post — completes in under 8-10 minutes with zero manual intervention.
