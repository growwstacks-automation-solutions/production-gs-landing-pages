The UGC Ad Creation Problem

User-generated content (UGC) ads perform 4x better than traditional product ads - but creating them manually is painfully slow. Most eCommerce businesses struggle with:

  • Finding authentic-looking creators
  • Coordinating scripts and filming
  • Editing raw footage into polished ads
  • Scaling production across multiple products

The result? Most stores either settle for low-performing static images or spend thousands on professional video production. Neither solution scales profitably.

84% of consumers say UGC influences purchases more than brand content - but only 12% of small businesses can produce UGC ads consistently due to time and cost constraints.

How n8n Solves This

n8n automation bridges the gap between authentic-looking content and scalable production. This workflow:

Key AI Integrations

This workflow leverages three specialized AI services working together:

1. OpenAI for Script Generation

Takes product details and generates natural-sounding UGC scripts in different styles (enthusiastic, professional, etc.).

2. Wave Speed for Voiceovers

Converts the generated scripts into human-like voice recordings with adjustable tone and pacing.

3. Video Generation AI

Combines product images with the voiceover and adds automatic captions, transitions, and background elements.

Cost note: These APIs charge per usage - approximately $0.10-$0.50 per completed video depending on length and quality settings.

Execution Flow Explained

Here's what happens when you run the workflow:

  1. Upload product images to your connected Dropbox folder
  2. n8n detects new files and extracts product details
  3. OpenAI generates 3-5 script variations per product
  4. Wave Speed creates voice recordings for each script
  5. Video AI combines visuals with audio and adds captions
  6. Finished videos save to your specified output folder

The entire process typically completes in 3-5 minutes per video, with multiple videos processing in parallel.
