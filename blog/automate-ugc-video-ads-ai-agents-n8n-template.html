The UGC Video Challenge

User-generated content (UGC) videos convert 3-5x better than traditional product videos, but creating them at scale is notoriously difficult. Most businesses struggle with finding authentic-looking creators, managing production timelines, and maintaining consistent quality across videos. The manual process often takes weeks per video and costs $200-$500 per piece.

This AI-powered solution solves all three pain points simultaneously. By automating both the script generation and video production, you can create unlimited UGC-style videos for a fraction of the cost and time. The system maintains consistent quality while allowing for product-specific customization.

Real-world results: Early adopters report producing 50-100 UGC videos per month at under $3 per video, with the entire process taking less than 10 minutes of hands-on time per video.

How the System Works

The automated UGC video system consists of two interconnected n8n workflows that handle the entire process from product input to final video delivery. The first workflow manages the video generation initiation, while the second handles completion notifications and data updates.

At its core, the system uses AI agents to transform simple product details into compelling video scripts tailored for different video generation models. The workflow automatically selects the appropriate video generation platform based on your preferences and budget.

Setting Up the Google Sheet

The Google Sheet serves as the control panel for your entire video generation system. It contains fields for product name, description, image URL, target audience, and your preferred video generation model (VO3.1 or SOAR2).

When you mark a row as "ready to generate," the n8n workflow automatically detects the change and begins processing. This simple interface means even non-technical team members can request new videos without needing to understand the underlying automation.

Configuring the AI Agent

The AI agent is the secret sauce that transforms basic product details into compelling video scripts. It follows carefully crafted instructions to ensure the generated content feels authentic and complies with platform guidelines.

The system message given to the AI includes three critical sections: an overview of its role, compliance/quality guidelines, and specific output formatting requirements. This structure ensures consistent, platform-compliant outputs every time.

Cost efficiency: Using OpenAI's 4.1 mini model for prompt generation keeps costs under $0.01 per video script while maintaining high quality.

Video Generation Process

Once the AI agent generates the optimized video prompt, the system sends it to your chosen video generation platform via API. The workflow automatically includes all necessary parameters like aspect ratio, image references, and callback URL.

The callback system is particularly clever - it allows the video platform to notify your n8n workflow when generation is complete, triggering the next steps automatically. This asynchronous approach means your workflow isn't blocked waiting for videos to render.

Handling Multiple Video Models

The workflow uses a switch node to route requests to different video generation models based on your selection in the Google Sheet. This modular design makes it easy to add support for new video platforms as they emerge.

Currently, the template supports both VO3.1 and SOAR2, each requiring slightly different API parameters and response handling. The branching logic ensures the right instructions reach the right platform without manual intervention.

Webhook Integration

The webhook-based completion system is what makes this workflow truly hands-off. When the video platform finishes rendering, it calls your n8n webhook with the completed video URL and metadata.

The second workflow then updates your Google Sheet automatically, marking the status as "completed" and adding the video link. You'll know exactly when each video is ready without constantly checking the platform or your email.
