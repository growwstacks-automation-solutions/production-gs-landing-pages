The Client Onboarding Problem

Voice AI agencies lose an average of 14 hours per client just collecting information and configuring agents. Between payment processing, API key collection, phone number assignment, and knowledge base setup—what should be exciting new client relationships turn into administrative nightmares.

The breaking point comes when you're managing 10+ clients simultaneously. Manual processes that worked for your first few clients collapse under scale. Missed emails, configuration errors, and billing discrepancies eat into your margins and damage your reputation.

82% of Voice AI agencies report losing clients during onboarding due to delays and miscommunication. Automation isn't just about efficiency—it's about delivering the seamless experience clients now expect.

How Automated Onboarding Works

The white-label solution creates a closed-loop system where client actions automatically trigger the next step in your onboarding workflow. When a client selects a plan and completes payment (1), their account is instantly created (2), triggering customized data collection forms (3) that feed directly into agent creation automations (4).

This end-to-end flow eliminates manual data entry and reduces errors. At 2:15 in the video, you'll see how the system automatically progresses clients through each stage—from payment to fully configured agent—without any intervention from your team.

Payment Integration

Stripe integration allows you to create multiple product tiers with different billing cycles. Clients see your branded pricing table (not the platform's), and when they click "Buy Now," they're taken to your custom payment portal.

The system handles all subscription management—including failed payment recovery and plan upgrades. When payment succeeds, it automatically creates a client account with secure credentials and triggers the first onboarding form.

Multi-Step Data Collection

Custom forms collect exactly what you need to configure each client's AI agent. The first form captures business details, while subsequent forms gather API keys, phone number preferences, and knowledge base parameters.

Conditional logic ensures clients only see relevant fields based on their plan. All submissions are stored in your dashboard and fed directly into the agent creation workflow—no copying and pasting between systems.

Agent Creation Automation

Completed forms trigger n8n workflows that create agents in your Vapi or 11Labs account. The automation configures voice parameters, knowledge bases, and phone numbers—then assigns everything to the client's new portal.

At 4:30 in the video, watch how the system creates a fully functional agent in under 90 seconds using the collected data. This same workflow can also set up inbound/outbound campaigns and web widgets automatically.

Client Portal Features

Clients access a branded dashboard where they can view agent performance, update billing, and request support. The portal shows exactly what they need—hiding backend complexity while giving them control over their subscription.

Built-in analytics help clients understand call volume and success rates. Optional add-ons like dedicated support widgets or custom reporting can become additional revenue streams for your agency.
