The Cost of Manual Webinar Registration

Every marketer knows the frustration: you spend thousands driving traffic to your webinar registration page, only to lose 60% of interested attendees at the form stage. Manual processes create three critical bottlenecks:

  • Conversion killer: 60% abandonment rate on traditional registration forms (versus 15% with conversational interfaces)
  • Data chaos: Registrant details scattered across emails, spreadsheets, and CRM notes

The turning point: After seeing competitors automate 80% of their webinar operations, forward-thinking marketers are replacing forms with WhatsApp chatbots that handle the entire attendee journey from first touch to post-event follow-up.
