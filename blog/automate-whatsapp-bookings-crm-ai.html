Every hour spent manually replying to WhatsApp messages, Instagram DMs, and website inquiries is an hour not spent serving paying clients or growing your business. Yet most service providers—coaches, consultants, salons, and clinics—still handle every booking request personally.

The result? Missed messages outside business hours, inconsistent follow-up, and leads slipping through the cracks. Our data shows businesses lose 22% of potential clients simply by responding slower than 30 minutes.
