Workflow 1: Instant WhatsApp Confirmation for Form Submissions

Every business knows the frustration - a customer fills out your contact form, then waits hours (or days) for a response. With this n8n workflow, they receive a personalized WhatsApp confirmation message the moment they submit your form, before your team even sees the lead.

The workflow triggers when a new row appears in your Google Sheet (connected to your form). It uses Peach's WhatsApp API to send a template message with the customer's name and submission details automatically.

Real-world results: A dental clinic using this workflow saw 38% more appointment bookings from form submissions, simply by confirming requests within seconds rather than hours.

Step-by-Step Setup:

  1. Google Sheets Trigger: Configure the "On Row Added" trigger in n8n to monitor your form submission sheet
  2. Peach WhatsApp Node: Connect your Peach account using an API key from the developer settings
  3. Template Mapping: Use drag-and-drop to connect form fields (name, phone) to your WhatsApp template variables

The entire workflow requires just two nodes and takes under 15 minutes to configure. Once live, it eliminates the most common point of lead leakage - the waiting period after form submission.

Workflow 2: Automatic WhatsApp Follow-Up After Customer Calls

Missed call-backs cost businesses up to 20% of potential revenue. This workflow ensures every call to your support line triggers an immediate WhatsApp follow-up, keeping customers engaged even when your team is busy.

When integrated with call systems like Exotel, the workflow captures the caller's number automatically and sends a pre-approved WhatsApp template with next steps, documentation links, or a way to continue the conversation asynchronously.

Key benefit: A logistics company reduced call-back failures by 72% using this automation, while actually decreasing support staff workload by 15%.

Implementation Notes:

  • Uses a webhook to capture call events from your telephony system
  • Automatically maps caller ID to WhatsApp number fields
  • Supports multiple templates based on call reason (sales vs support)

The psychological impact is powerful - customers perceive your business as more responsive when they receive an instant WhatsApp after calling, even if the full resolution takes longer.

Workflow 3: Mass WhatsApp Broadcasts from Google Sheets

Sending bulk WhatsApp messages manually is time-consuming and error-prone. This workflow lets you launch personalized broadcasts to your entire contact list with one click, directly from your Google Sheets data.

The n8n automation aggregates contacts from your sheet, formats them for Peach's broadcast API, and sends templated messages with individual personalization (names, locations, etc.). You can schedule broadcasts or trigger them manually.

Efficiency gain: A fashion retailer reduced their promotional message sending time from 8 staff-hours weekly to 15 minutes, while improving open rates by 40% through better timing and personalization.

Key Components:

  1. Trigger: Manual or schedule-based (e.g. every Monday 10 AM)
  2. Google Sheets Node: Pulls your contact list with phone numbers and personalization fields
  3. Aggregate Node: Bundles contacts into batches compliant with WhatsApp rate limits
  4. Peach Broadcast Node: Sends messages using your approved template

Unlike email broadcasts, WhatsApp messages have 98%+ open rates, making this workflow exceptionally valuable for time-sensitive announcements.

Workflow 4: AI-Powered Lead Qualification on WhatsApp

The most advanced workflow combines WhatsApp automation with AI to qualify leads automatically. When someone fills your form, an AI agent initiates a WhatsApp conversation to assess budget, timeline, and requirements - then updates your CRM with the qualification status.

This two-part workflow first connects the lead to your AI agent, then listens for the qualification result via webhook to update your systems. The entire process happens without human involvement, allowing your sales team to focus only on hot leads.

Conversion impact: A real estate client saw 3X more qualified appointments by using AI to filter out non-serious inquiries before agent contact.

How It Works:

  • Workflow 1: Google Sheets trigger → Peach AI agent connection
  • Workflow 2: Peach webhook → CRM/sheet update with qualification data
  • AI Agent: Pre-configured in Peach to ask industry-specific qualification questions

The system handles the tedious early-stage conversations that often frustrate sales teams, while capturing structured data that improves lead scoring and follow-up.

Implementation Tips for Each Workflow

While these workflows are simple to set up, these professional tips will help you avoid common pitfalls and maximize results:

For Form Confirmations:

  • Use WhatsApp template messages rather than free-form texts for better deliverability
  • Include a clear CTA (e.g. "Reply BOOK to schedule") to drive next actions
  • Set up a fallback SMS for non-WhatsApp numbers (add SMS node in n8n)

For Call Follow-Ups:

  • Create different templates for sales vs support calls
  • Include a satisfaction survey link for support issues
  • Set business hour boundaries to avoid late-night messages

For Broadcasts:

  • Always include an opt-out instruction in your template
  • Test with small batches before full broadcasts
  • Use merge tags for personalization (e.g. {{first_name}})

For AI Qualification:

  • Start with 3-5 key qualification questions
  • Train your AI agent on actual lead conversations
  • Set up alerts for highly-qualified leads

Remember to monitor your first few automated messages to ensure templates render correctly and workflows trigger as expected. Most issues can be resolved with small template or mapping adjustments.
