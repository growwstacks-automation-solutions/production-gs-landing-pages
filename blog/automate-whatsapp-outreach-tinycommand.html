The Manual Outreach Problem

Manual WhatsApp outreach hits a wall once you have more than 50-100 contacts. Researching each company, formatting phone numbers correctly, writing personalized messages, and tracking responses becomes a full-time job. Most businesses either give up on scaling or resort to generic spam that gets ignored.

The breakthrough comes when you realize WhatsApp's power lies in personalization — but personalization is exactly what makes scaling impossible. TinyCommand bridges this gap by automating the research and customization while maintaining the human touch that makes WhatsApp effective.

84% of consumers say personalized messaging makes them more likely to engage with a business, yet only 12% of companies have scaled personalized outreach beyond their first 100 contacts.

Workflow Step-by-Step

Let's break down exactly how this automation works from start to finish. Each component serves a specific purpose in creating a reliable, scalable outreach system.

Step 1: Time-Based Trigger

The workflow activates every 10 minutes to process one record at a time. This pacing prevents overwhelming your WhatsApp Business account while maintaining steady outreach.

Step 2: Fetch Prospect Record

A "Find One" node retrieves the next contact from your database that hasn't been processed yet, ensuring no duplicates or skipped records.

Step 3: Company Enrichment

The system automatically researches the company domain to gather details like description, location, and website. This data becomes the foundation for personalization.

Step 4: Quality Check

An "If Else" condition verifies the enrichment succeeded. Records without sufficient data are skipped to maintain message quality.

Step 5: Phone Number Formatting

TinyCommand's AI formats the raw phone number into proper international WhatsApp format based on the company's location.

Step 6: Message Personalization

Another AI node transforms your base template into a unique message incorporating specific details about the recipient's company.

Step 7: WhatsApp Delivery

The formatted message sends via WhatsApp Business API with proper sender identification and tracking.

Step 8: Database Update

Finally, the system marks the record as processed and stores the exact message sent for future reference.

In summary: Every 10 minutes, the system picks one contact → enriches company data → formats their number → generates a personalized message → sends via WhatsApp → updates your records. All automatically.

Unlike basic mail merge that just inserts names, this system understands context. It might reference the company's industry in the opening, mention their location naturally mid-message, and tailor the call-to-action based on their website content.

International Number Handling

Global outreach introduces complexity with varying phone number formats. The workflow automatically detects and corrects common issues:

  • Missing country codes
  • Incorrect local prefixes
  • Extra digits or formatting characters
  • Landline vs mobile number identification

By combining the raw number with the company's location data, TinyCommand ensures messages reach their destination regardless of where the contact is based.

Tracking and Reporting

Every action in the workflow updates your database automatically. You'll always know:

  • Which contacts have been messaged
  • The exact content sent to each one
  • Timestamps for all communications
  • Any records that failed processing

This creates a complete audit trail and prevents duplicate outreach while giving your sales team context for follow-ups.

Compliance Considerations

WhatsApp Business API has strict anti-spam policies. This workflow maintains compliance by:

  • Sending only to opted-in contacts
  • Limiting message frequency
  • Including clear sender identification
  • Providing relevant value in each message

The personalization itself acts as a compliance safeguard — messages tailored to specific businesses don't trigger spam filters the way bulk blasts do.
