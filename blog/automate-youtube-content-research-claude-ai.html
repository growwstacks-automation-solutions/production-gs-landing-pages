Implementation Tips

From the creator's experience (timestamp 7:30-8:00), here's how to get the most from this automation:

1. Start Small Then Expand

Begin with 1-2 niche keywords and basic metrics. As you see value, add more dimensions to analyze.

2. Compare Multiple Weeks

Look for patterns across weekly reports rather than over-optimizing based on single data points.

3. Combine With Creative Judgment

Use the data to inform content decisions, not replace your unique perspective and voice.

4. Schedule Creation Time

Set aside 2-3 hours each Monday to review the report and plan your week's content accordingly.
