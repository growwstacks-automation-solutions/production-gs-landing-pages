The Onboarding Problem Every High-Ticket Business Faces

High-ticket course creators and coaching businesses face a hidden productivity killer: manual onboarding. For each new student, you're typically spending hours collecting information, generating contracts, setting up payment plans, and provisioning access - all while trying to deliver exceptional service.

The Swiss trading academy featured in this case study was losing 3 hours per student to administrative tasks. At their enrollment volume, this meant hundreds of hours monthly spent on repetitive work that could be automated.

Key insight: Manual onboarding doesn't scale with your business. The more successful you become, the more time you waste on administrative tasks instead of delivering value to clients.

Complete Workflow Breakdown

The automation begins when a prospective student clicks the application button, triggering a Typeform that collects all necessary information. From this single submission point, the system handles everything else automatically.

Step 1: CRM Creation

The system first creates a contact in Google Contacts and updates the master Google Sheets CRM with all applicant details. This ensures every team member has immediate access to the latest information.

Step 2: Course-Specific Routing

Based on the course type selected in the application (visible at 1:45 in the video), the system branches to handle different contract templates, pricing structures, and onboarding sequences.

Step 3: Automated Document Generation

The system generates all necessary contracts and forms, pre-populated with the applicant's information. No more copying and pasting between documents!

In summary: One form submission triggers contact creation, CRM updates, and document generation - eliminating hours of manual data entry.

Automated Contract Generation & Signing

The contract automation (demonstrated at 3:20 in the video) showcases the system's intelligence. Rather than sending generic documents, it generates fully customized contracts that include:

  • Student's personal details (name, address, contact information)
  • Specific course selection with correct pricing
  • Chosen payment plan terms
  • Course start date and schedule details

These contracts are then automatically sent via BoldSign for electronic signing. The moment the contract is signed, the system updates the CRM and triggers the next phase of onboarding.

Smart Payment Processing & Invoicing

The financial automation (shown at 6:30) handles all payment processing complexity. Based on the payment plan selected:

  • Full payment: Generates deposit and final payment invoices
  • Installment plans: Creates complete series of invoices with correct dates and amounts
  • Custom plans: Can be configured to handle any payment structure

All invoices are automatically created in Beexio (or your accounting software of choice) with correct line items, due dates, and customer details. This eliminates billing errors and ensures perfect financial records from day one.

Post-Signing Automation Magic

After contract signing (visible at 5:10), the system executes a series of crucial onboarding steps automatically:

  1. Sends welcome email with course access credentials
  2. Adds student to group call calendar invites
  3. Updates CRM status to "Active Student"
  4. Triggers any course-specific onboarding sequences

This ensures students immediately feel welcomed and supported, while the business maintains perfect records without manual follow-up.
