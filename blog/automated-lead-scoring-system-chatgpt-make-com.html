The Lead Qualification Problem

Sales teams typically spend 30-40% of their time manually qualifying leads - reviewing forms, checking LinkedIn profiles, and guessing which prospects are worth pursuing. This inefficient process means hot leads often wait days for follow-up while sales reps waste time on unqualified prospects.

The breakthrough comes when we realize lead scoring doesn't require human judgment. ChatGPT can analyze lead attributes against your ideal customer profile with remarkable accuracy. By automating this scoring, we free sales teams to focus exclusively on ready-to-buy prospects.

Key insight: Manual lead qualification costs the average sales team 15 hours per week per rep - time better spent closing deals. Automated scoring eliminates this waste while improving response times to hot leads by 80%.

How AI Scoring Works

The system triggers when a prospect submits a Google Form with key qualification questions. ChatGPT then analyzes each response against a structured scoring rubric:

  1. Job Title: Decision-makers score higher than junior staff
  2. Budget: Prospects with appropriate budgets earn more points
  3. Company Size: Ideal customer size receives maximum points
  4. Urgency: Immediate needs score higher than future projects
  5. Industry: Target industries receive bonus points

Each factor contributes to a total score from 0-100. The AI then classifies leads into three categories automatically:

Hot (80-100): Immediate sales follow-up
Warm (60-79): Nurturing sequence
Cold (below 60): Automated education

System Architecture

The complete automation runs on Make.com with these key components:

Step 1: Form Submission Trigger

Google Forms captures lead details and triggers the workflow instantly upon submission. The form includes strategic questions designed to feed the scoring algorithm.

Step 2: Immediate Welcome Email

Gmail sends a personalized welcome message within minutes, starting the engagement process while the AI works on scoring.

Step 3: AI Scoring Module

ChatGPT analyzes the form responses against the scoring rubric, calculating a 0-100 score and assigning a category (hot/warm/cold).

Step 4: Team Notifications

Slack and email alerts notify the sales team about new hot leads instantly, with all scoring details included.

Step 5: Category-Specific Follow-up

The system routes each lead to appropriate next steps based on classification - immediate sales contact for hot leads, nurturing sequences for warm leads, and educational content for cold leads.

In summary: Form submission → Welcome email → AI scoring → Team alerts → Category-specific follow-up - all automated with zero manual steps.

Scoring Logic Breakdown

The secret to accurate scoring lies in the detailed rubric ChatGPT follows. Here's how points are allocated:

Job Title (Max 25 points)

  • C-level executives: 25 points
  • Directors/VPs: 20 points
  • Managers: 15 points
  • Individual contributors: 5 points

Budget (Max 30 points)

  • Above $50k: 30 points
  • $25-50k: 20 points
  • $10-25k: 10 points
  • Below $10k: 0 points

Company Size (Max 20 points)

  • Enterprise (1000+): 20 points
  • Mid-market (100-999): 15 points
  • Small business (10-99): 10 points
  • Startup (1-9): 5 points

The system also includes bonus points for urgency signals and industry fit. This structured approach ensures consistent, objective scoring across all leads.

Classification and Routing

Once scored, leads flow through intelligent routing logic:

Hot Leads (80-100 points)

Receive immediate personal email from a sales rep and calendar invitation for a discovery call. The sales team gets real-time Slack and email alerts with all lead details.

Warm Leads (60-79 points)

Enter a 14-day email nurture sequence with case studies and value propositions. They also receive periodic check-in emails from the sales team.

Cold Leads (Below 60 points)

Receive educational content and invitations to webinars or events. They remain in the marketing database for future outreach when their situation may change.

Key benefit: This automatic routing ensures every lead receives appropriate attention without sales reps wasting time on poor-fit prospects.
