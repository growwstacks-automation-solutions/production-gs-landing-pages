The Manual News Reading Problem

Information overload has become the silent productivity killer for modern professionals. Industry surveys show knowledge workers spend 5-7 hours weekly just scanning news sources — yet retain less than 20% of what they read. The cognitive cost of context-switching between articles often outweighs the benefits.

Traditional solutions like email newsletters or aggregated feeds merely repackage the firehose of information without solving the core problem: too much content, too little time to extract actionable insights. This creates a dangerous gap between being "informed" and being strategically aware.

Key insight: Professionals don't need more information — they need better distillation of the information they already receive.

How the Automated Summarizer Works

The system operates on a simple but powerful three-step workflow built entirely in Make.com with Gemini AI handling the cognitive heavy lifting:

  1. RSS Monitoring: Make.com watches your configured news feeds for new articles (updated every 10 minutes)
  2. AI Processing: Full articles are sent to Gemini AI with instructions to extract the 3 most important bullet points
  3. Delivery: Summaries are formatted and emailed on your preferred schedule (immediately, daily digest, etc.)

Configuring Gemini AI for Optimal Summaries

The magic happens in the AI prompt engineering. After testing dozens of variations, we found this template delivers the most consistent executive-ready summaries:

"Analyze this article for a [INDUSTRY] professional. Extract ONLY the 3 most actionable insights as bullet points. Prioritize: 1) New data/statistics 2) Competitive moves 3) Emerging trends. Exclude: Opinions, fluff, promotional content. Format: • [Concise 8-12 word phrase] → [1-sentence explanation]"

This structure forces Gemini to focus on strategically relevant information rather than regurgitating article intros. The "→" arrow convention visually separates the headline from the explanatory sentence, creating scan-friendly digests.

Make.com Workflow Setup

The technical implementation requires just 5 modules in Make.com:

  1. RSS Trigger: Configured with your target feeds (supports multiple sources)
  2. HTTP Request: Fetches full article text when paywalls allow
  3. Gemini AI Module: Processes text with your optimized prompt
  4. Text Parser: Extracts clean bullet points from AI response
  5. Email/Slack: Delivers formatted summary to your preferred channel

The entire workflow consumes under 20 Make.com operations per article, meaning you can process ~50 daily articles on the free plan. Scaling to enterprise volumes simply requires upgrading your Make.com subscription tier.
