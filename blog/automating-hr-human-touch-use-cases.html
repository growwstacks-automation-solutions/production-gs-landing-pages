Implementation Steps For Your Business

Step 1: Identify Key Touchpoints

Map your customer journey to identify 3-5 critical moments where automated personalization would have maximum impact (new signups, milestones, renewals, etc.)

Step 2: Select Your Gift Stack

Choose a mix of physical and digital gifts that can be automatically triggered at each touchpoint. Services like Handwritten (for notes) and local vendors (for gifts) integrate directly with automation platforms.

Step 3: Configure Call Analysis

Define your evaluation criteria based on company values, compliance requirements, and customer experience goals. AI systems can be trained on your specific FAQs and ideal interaction patterns.
