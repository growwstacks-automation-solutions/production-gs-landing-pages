Why Gemini and ChatGPT Fall Short for Business Automation

Business owners trying to automate with Google Gemini or ChatGPT quickly hit frustrating limitations. These tools reset with each new conversation, can't connect to most business apps, and require manual intervention at every step. They're great for one-off questions but terrible for ongoing automation.

The fundamental problem is memory. Without infinite memory, AI tools can't learn from past interactions or improve over time. They also lack access to the tools businesses actually use - you can't connect Gemini to your CRM, e-commerce platform, or social media accounts.

Key limitation: Gemini connects to just 18 Google apps, while ChatGPT's integrations are limited to about 30 services. Autonomous AI agents can connect to thousands of business tools through APIs and webhooks.

At 2:15 in the video, we see Gemini's connected apps panel showing only Google products. Workspace Studio similarly fails to connect to external business tools. This ecosystem lock-in makes these tools impractical for real business automation.
