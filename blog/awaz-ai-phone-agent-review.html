What Is Awaz AI?

Businesses waste thousands on human receptionists who can only work 40 hours a week, make mistakes, and require training. Awaz changes this equation completely - offering an AI voice agent that handles calls 24/7 at a fraction of the cost.

Using 11 Labs' industry-leading voice technology, Awaz sounds indistinguishable from human operators. It can answer customer questions, schedule appointments, make outbound sales calls, and even update your CRM automatically after each conversation.

Key capability: During testing, Awaz successfully walked a customer through WordPress troubleshooting, scheduled a follow-up call, and sent an SMS summary - all without human intervention.

Pricing Breakdown

The base AppSumo deal starts at just $49 for one agent, but there are important ongoing costs to consider. Unlike most lifetime deals, Awaz charges per minute for calls since it uses real phone infrastructure.

Higher tiers offer more agents and lower per-minute rates. The $500 top tier includes unlimited agents at 20¢/minute - effectively getting you a full-time sales agent for about $12/hour with no benefits or downtime.

Agent Setup Walkthrough

Creating your first AI agent takes about 15 minutes. The dashboard guides you through four key tabs: Settings, Prompt, Actions, and Variables.

In Settings, you'll choose from dozens of 11 Labs voices (though you can't yet clone your own). The Prompt section is where most customization happens - you can paste in your website copy, FAQs, or even full call scripts.

Pro tip: Use ChatGPT to generate your agent's objective and freestyle prompt based on your business description. This ensures natural, on-brand conversations.

Real Call Demo

During testing, Awaz successfully handled a WordPress support call at 3:42 in the video. The AI walked the "customer" through changing font sizes and clearing cache - two common support requests.

While there was one minor confusion about login URLs, the agent recovered gracefully. The conversation flowed naturally, with appropriate pauses and conversational markers ("Got it", "Let me know").

Outbound Campaigns

Beyond answering calls, Awaz excels at outbound outreach. You can upload contact lists (CSV format) and create campaigns with:

  • Daily spending limits
  • Specific call windows
  • Automatic follow-up scheduling
  • CRM updates after each call

This transforms Awaz into a 24/7 sales team that never gets tired or frustrated. At 20¢/minute (top tier), you could have 300 minutes of sales calls per day for just $60.

CRM & Calendar Integrations

Awaz offers native connections to Google Calendar for appointment booking and Zapier for CRM updates. Every call generates a complete transcript that can trigger workflows in other apps.

You can also set up webhooks for custom integrations or use the built-in variables system to capture specific call outcomes ("interested", "needs follow-up", etc.).

Implementation tip: Start by connecting just your calendar. Once comfortable, add CRM updates for closed deals or support tickets.
