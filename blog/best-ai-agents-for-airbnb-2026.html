1. Voice Booking AI - Handle Reservations 24/7

Imagine losing bookings because you missed a call at 2 AM from an international guest. The voice booking AI solves this by handling complete reservation calls - collecting dates, locations, guest counts, and even applying discount codes automatically.

During the demo (shown at 2:15 in the video), the AI successfully guided a guest through selecting dates, checking availability in Milan, and completing a booking - including sending a text message with property options and providing a 5% discount code at checkout.

90% of booking calls can be fully automated - only requiring human intervention for complex group bookings or special requests. The AI integrates directly with your property management system via n8n to check real-time availability.

2. Property Q&A + Maintenance AI

No more 3 AM calls about WiFi passwords or appliance issues. This AI handles both informational questions and maintenance requests with human-like understanding.

In the demo (starting at 6:30), the AI walked a guest through troubleshooting a washing machine issue, then - when the problem persisted - automatically scheduled a maintenance visit during the guest's preferred time window while notifying the host team via Discord.

Key features: Cross-references property manuals, categorizes urgency (critical/high/medium), schedules appointments, and maintains complete support logs - reducing host workload by 85% on common issues.

3. WhatsApp Automation - Text-Based Support

Many guests prefer texting over calling. This WhatsApp AI provides identical functionality to the voice AI but through messaging - perfect for international guests or those who want written instructions.

The system can send visual guides (like appliance manuals), process maintenance requests, and even handle booking inquiries - all while maintaining conversation history for reference. At 12:40 in the video, you'll see how it seamlessly integrates with the same n8n backend as the voice system.

4. Listing Quality Checker AI

Missing details in listings lead to guest complaints and poor reviews. This AI reviews new listings against property requirements, catching omissions like language requirements or missing amenities before publication.

The demo at 14:20 shows how it compares listing drafts against property briefs, flagging discrepancies with 98% accuracy. This prevents costly mistakes that could lead to bad guest experiences or even cancellation penalties.

5. AI Insights Dashboard - Real-Time Analytics

This centralized dashboard (shown at 15:50) provides hosts with instant visibility into operations: bookings made, inquiries handled, maintenance tickets resolved, and guest satisfaction metrics.

The AI can even analyze trends and suggest optimizations - like noting that properties with certain amenities have 22% higher satisfaction scores, or identifying seasonal pricing opportunities.

Example insight: "Today 34 guests checked in, there were 8 questions (7 handled by AI), and the voice agent made 22 new bookings this month" - all available at a glance instead of piecing together multiple reports.
