Implementation Tips

After deploying these solutions for dozens of real estate AI agents, we've identified key implementation best practices:

Step 1: Define Your Call Flows

Map out your ideal call flow - from initial greeting to qualification questions to appointment booking. Identify where human handoffs should occur.

Step 2: Train the AI

Provide sample conversations and your listing database. The more context. The more data you feed, the better the agent performs.

Step 3: Test Extensively

Run test calls from different numbers, trying various scenarios. Refine scripts based on where conversations break down.

Step 4: Monitor and Optimize

Review call recordings and analytics weekly. Look for drop-off points and continuously improve your flows.

Pro tip: Start with a limited pilot (perhaps just after-hours calls before going full-scale. This builds confidence in the system.
