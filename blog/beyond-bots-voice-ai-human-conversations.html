Beyond IVR: How Modern Voice AI Works

Traditional IVR systems force callers through rigid menu trees ("Press 1 for billing"). When customers deviate from the script - which they always do - the system fails. Modern voice AI solves this with three key innovations:

  1. Speech-to-speech processing that analyzes both words and intent in real-time
  2. Contextual memory that remembers details across conversation turns
  3. Backend integration to verify identities, process transactions, and pull real-time data

In the banking demo (2:15 in the video), the AI assistant "Diana" doesn't just recognize the words "fixed deposit" - she understands the customer wants to open an FD account, knows which information to collect, and can execute the transaction end-to-end.
