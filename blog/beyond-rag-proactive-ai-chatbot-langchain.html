The Limitations of Traditional RAG

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems revolutionized AI chatbots by grounding responses in documentation - but they still operate reactively. When Alice contacts support about a defective bulb, a traditional chatbot waits for her to:

  1. Find the right manual
  2. Formulate the perfect question
  3. Navigate through irrelevant answers

Sencho Bot eliminates this friction by proactively accessing Alice's purchase history the moment she logs in. It knows her exact model number, warranty status, and common issues - before she asks a single question.

92% of customers abandon support chats when forced to repeat information the company already has. Proactive personalization cuts first-response time by 63%.

The Troubleshoot-First Approach

At 1:55 in the video, notice how Sencho Bot always attempts to solve the problem before offering ticket creation. This "troubleshoot-first" flow:

  • Presents step-by-step guides with visual aids
  • Verifies completion of each step
  • Only escalates after confirmation the solution failed

Early adopters report this approach deflects 40% of potential tickets by empowering users to solve simple issues themselves. The bot maintains a polite tone throughout, asking "Would you like me to create a support ticket now?" only after exhausting self-help options.
