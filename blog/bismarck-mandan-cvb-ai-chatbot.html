How Bismarck-Mandan CVB's AI Chatbot is Revolutionizing Visitor Experience

Visitors to Bismarck-Mandan no longer need to piece together trip details from multiple websites. Solving Visitor Challenges with AI

Planning a trip to an unfamiliar city can be overwhelming. Visitors often spend hours searching multiple websites to piece together hotel options, restaurant recommendations, and activities that fit their family's needs and schedule. The Bismarck-Mandan Convention & Visitors Bureau recognized this pain point and saw an opportunity to leverage AI technology to create a better experience.

"We're always looking for new ways to help visitors navigate our community," explained Sherry from the CVB. "Many aren't familiar with the area, and we wanted to make trip planning as easy as having a conversation." The result is an AI chatbot that serves as a 24/7 digital concierge, available on their website at noboundariesnd.com.

Key advantage: Unlike static travel guides, the chatbot provides personalized recommendations based on each visitor's specific needs, travel dates, and preferences - then allows them to refine the results in real-time.

How the Chatbot Works

The AI chatbot uses natural language processing to understand visitor requests and generate tailored recommendations. In the demonstration video (timestamp 1:45), Sherry shows how a family planning a back-to-school shopping trip and final summer vacation can get complete itinerary suggestions just by asking the chatbot.

Users simply describe their trip parameters - like "family of four visiting July 20-23" - and the chatbot recommends suitable hotels, nearby attractions, restaurants, and even local coffee shops. The system considers factors like family-friendliness, proximity between locations, and current events happening during the visit dates.

Interactive refinement: If the initial recommendations don't quite match what the visitor wants, they can ask for adjustments like "more upscale restaurants" or "indoor activities only" and the chatbot will regenerate suggestions accordingly.

Benefits for CVB Members

One key consideration in developing the chatbot was ensuring fair representation for all local businesses while providing value to CVB members. "Our members get a little higher billing," Sherry noted. "They get recognized first, but we also want people to realize all we have in our community."

If the chatbot can't find suitable options among member businesses, it will then suggest non-member establishments. This balanced approach helps visitors while incentivizing businesses to join the CVB. Members benefit from increased visibility and the chatbot's ability to match them with ideal customers based on specific requests.

Interactive Features and Personalization

Beyond text recommendations, the chatbot includes an interactive map showing locations of suggested businesses and attractions. This helps visitors visualize distances and plan their daily routes efficiently. As Sherry demonstrated (timestamp 3:10), users can download their customized itineraries for offline access during their trip.

The system becomes more helpful with specific requests. Early tests showed that asking simply for "Mexican restaurants" might return chain establishments, but specifying "authentic Mexican" yielded different, more specialized results. The CVB encourages visitors to be as detailed as possible in their requests to get the best recommendations.

Valuable Data Insights

The chatbot provides benefits beyond visitor assistance. CVB staff can review the questions being asked to identify gaps in their website content and understand what information visitors are seeking. "It's going to help us build a better website," Sherry explained, noting how the data reveals visitor perceptions and unmet needs.

Interestingly, many locals have been testing the chatbot out of curiosity. Their interactions provide additional training data to improve the system's accuracy. The CVB welcomes this local engagement and encourages feedback to continuously enhance the chatbot's performance.

Innovation leadership: Bismarck-Mandan is among the first destination marketing organizations in North Dakota to implement an AI chatbot, demonstrating their commitment to using technology to enhance visitor experiences and support local businesses.
