What is Bot Mockups V2?

For agencies selling chatbot solutions, the biggest challenge has always been showing clients what their bot will look like before investing in development. Traditional methods involve time-consuming manual mockups or building partial prototypes. Bot Mockups V2 solves this by creating instant, AI-powered demos that look and feel like the real thing.

The new version goes beyond simple visual mockups to become a complete sales acceleration platform. As shown in the video walkthrough at 1:15, it now includes lead generation, voice AI capabilities, and automatic demo creation that analyzes a company's website to generate relevant conversation flows.

Key difference from V1: While the original Bot Mockups was purely a demo tool, V2 helps you find leads, create demos tailored to their business, and track engagement - all in one platform.

The Game-Changing Lead Generation Tool

Finding qualified leads is half the battle in selling chatbot services. At 3:25 in the video, we see how Bot Mockups V2's lead generation tool lets you search for businesses by niche and location, showing key metrics that indicate sales potential.

The system identifies whether prospects have a website, run Google ads, or already have a chatbot - all valuable signals for targeting your outreach. For example, the demo finds auto body shops in Indianapolis, revealing that Glendale Collision doesn't even have a website - indicating potential for both web development and chatbot services.

150 free lead enrichment credits are included monthly, letting you find contact details for decision makers at these businesses without additional cost.

Instant Demo Creation in Under 3 Minutes

At 5:10 in the video, we see how the platform can create a complete web chat demo in under 3 minutes with zero manual input. The AI analyzes the target website, takes a screenshot, and generates both the visual mockup and conversation prompts automatically.

Key features shown include:

  • Automatic color matching of the chat widget to the website's design
  • AI-generated welcome messages and conversation flows
  • Mobile preview capability to show how it would look on phones
  • Photo analysis feature where the chatbot can interpret images sent by users

What makes this powerful for sales is that you can create a tailored demo for a specific lead immediately after finding them in the lead generation tool.

Voice AI Demos That Sound Human

At 8:45, the video demonstrates V2's new voice AI capabilities. You can duplicate an existing chat demo into a voice call simulation with different voice options (Emma vs. Sarah in the example).

The system handles complete call flows including:

  • Natural-sounding greetings and responses
  • Appointment scheduling with date/time handling
  • Information collection (name, phone number)
  • Call conclusion with confirmation details

All conversations are recorded and available in the call history section (shown at 14:20), letting you review performance and make prompt adjustments.

New Instagram Profile Demo Feature

At 11:30, we see the Instagram profile demo feature - completely new in V2. This allows you to:

  • Upload up to 9 photos to create a realistic Instagram profile
  • Customize the business handle, category, and bio
  • Set follower/following counts to match the business size
  • Demonstrate Instagram messaging with AI responses

This is particularly valuable for businesses that rely heavily on Instagram for customer engagement, like the bakery example shown later in the video.

Powerful Lead Enrichment Capabilities

At 13:10, the video shows how you can enrich leads with contact information. For Long's Bakery, the system finds:

  • Company social media profiles
  • Review data
  • Specific contacts like Mike Reinhardt and Mike Long

These enriched leads are automatically added to your contacts list, ready for outreach. The platform maintains a clear separation between finding leads (free) and enriching them (uses credits), as explained at 16:40.

Detailed Analytics & Conversation Tracking

At 14:50, we see the analytics dashboard that tracks:

  • Voice minutes used
  • Lead enrichment credit consumption
  • Complete conversation histories for every demo

The conversation tracking is particularly valuable for training purposes. You can review exactly how the AI responded in each demo situation and adjust prompts accordingly for better future performance.
