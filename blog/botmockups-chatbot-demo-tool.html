BotMockUps bridges this gap by letting you create functional prototypes in minutes. Instead of describing hypothetical interactions, you can share a working demo that collects real information. One digital agency reported closing 72% more deals after implementing BotMockUps in their sales process.

Creating Your First Demo

Starting a new mockup takes seconds. Simply name your project (like "Brown County Sheet Metal Roofing") and describe the use case. The platform will generate a starter conversation flow you can customize.

Pro Tip: Use the description field to outline your ideal customer journey. The AI will suggest appropriate conversation paths based on your input.
