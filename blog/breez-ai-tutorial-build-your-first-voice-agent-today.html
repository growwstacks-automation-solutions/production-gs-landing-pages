Choosing Ears, Brain and Voice

At 1:20 in the tutorial, you'll see how to select the core components that define your agent's capabilities:

  • Ears: The speech-to-text engine that transcribes caller speech (OpenAI Whisper shown in the demo)
  • Brain: The large language model that processes requests and generates responses (OpenAI GPT shown)
  • Voice: The text-to-speech model that generates natural responses (11 Labs' Jessica voice used)

Breez AI offers multiple options for each component, allowing you to mix and match based on your needs and budget.
