Workflow Breakdown: PDF to Spreadsheet

The automation shown at 6:50 has three simple but powerful components that work together seamlessly:

Step 1: Google Drive Trigger

Watches a specific folder for new PDF invoices. When one appears, it automatically kicks off the workflow.

Step 2: AI Data Extraction

Uses OpenAI (or alternatives like Gemini) to parse the PDF and extract key fields: vendor name, date, invoice number, line items, amounts, taxes, and totals.

Step 3: Google Sheets Update

Takes the structured data and adds it as a new, organized row in a spreadsheet - creating a searchable, filterable database of all invoices.

In summary: Throw a PDF into a folder → AI reads it → Data appears in your spreadsheet. The entire process happens automatically with zero manual intervention after setup.
