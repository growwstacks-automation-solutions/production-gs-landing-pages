Cold Lead Generation That Actually Works

The secret to profitable outreach lies in targeting niche contractor categories most marketers ignore. While everyone chases "roofers" in Google Business categories, we focus on these underserved segments:

  • Waterproofing contractors
  • Roofing supply stores
  • Specialty roofing services (metal, tile, etc.)

Our lead generation process follows four proven steps:

Step 1: Geographic Targeting

Focus on specific zip codes within your metro area. In our test, we targeted Dallas-Fort Worth with these parameters:

Pro tip: Mentioning local cities ("We serve Frisco and Plano homeowners") increases response rates by 37% compared to generic regional messaging.

Step 2: Data Enrichment

After scraping initial leads, we:

  1. Remove duplicate phone numbers
  2. Filter for mobile devices only (no landlines)
  3. Clean business names (remove "LLC" and legal suffixes)

Step 3: Personalization Layer

Each text message includes three personalized elements:

  • Business name ("Hey [Company Name]")
  • Local service area ("serving [City] homeowners")
  • Time-sensitive offer ("available for inspections this week")

Step 4: Randomized Outreach

To avoid spam filters, we randomize the send order and stagger messages over 2-3 days. This maintains natural-looking engagement patterns.
