What Makes Relay.app Different?

Most automation tools require you to manually trigger workflows or set up complex rules. Relay.app takes a fundamentally different approach by creating autonomous AI agents that work like digital team members. These agents don't just follow instructions - they proactively look for work to do based on their assigned roles.

As Jacob, Relay.app's founder, demonstrates in the video, these agents integrate directly with your existing tools (like Gmail, Slack, and Google Calendar) and perform real work on your behalf. Unlike traditional bots that simply move data between apps, Relay agents can make judgment calls, learn from feedback, and improve over time.

Key difference: While most automation requires you to push work through systems, Relay agents pull work toward completion by actively monitoring for tasks that need attention - just like a human assistant would.

How to Create Your First AI Agent

Creating your first AI agent in Relay.app takes just minutes. As shown at the 1:15 mark in the video, you start by clicking "Create New Agent" and selecting the type of assistant you need. The platform offers templates for common roles like personal assistants, sales reps, and marketers.

Next, you connect the tools your agent will need to do its job. For a personal assistant, this typically includes email, calendar, and communication platforms. Relay.app integrates with all major business tools through secure OAuth connections.

Pro tip: Start with one simple agent focused on a single pain point (like meeting reminders) rather than trying to build your entire digital team at once. Small wins build confidence in the platform.

Building a Meeting Reminder Workflow

The video demonstrates creating a practical workflow where an agent sends reminder emails to meeting guests who haven't RSVP'd. At 1:45, Jacob shows how to describe this task in plain English: "When I have a meeting upcoming in 3 hours, see if any of the guests have not RSVPd, and send them a polite reminder email."

Relay.app's AI then converts this description into an actual workflow. It asks clarifying questions (like which calendar to use) and builds the automation logic automatically. Once activated, this agent will run independently, checking every meeting and handling reminders without any manual intervention.

  1. Agent monitors your calendar for upcoming meetings
  2. 3 hours before each meeting, checks guest RSVPs
  3. Identifies guests who haven't responded
  4. Composes and sends personalized reminder emails
  5. Logs all actions for your review
