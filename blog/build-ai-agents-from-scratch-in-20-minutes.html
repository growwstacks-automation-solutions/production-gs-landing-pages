AI Agents Explained

Unlike traditional automation that follows rigid rules, AI agents make contextual decisions. They analyze inputs (like email content), evaluate conditions (urgency, priority), and choose appropriate actions - all without human oversight.

In Make.com, these agents combine visual workflow building with AI decision modules. You define the logic in plain English, not code. The system handles complex branching, remembers context across steps, and adapts when variables change.
