Creating Your Company Knowledge Base

The foundation of an effective AI chatbot is a well-structured knowledge document. Start with a blank Google Doc and include these essential sections:

  1. Company Introduction: Who you are, your mission, and what you offer
  2. Product Information: Detailed descriptions of all features and capabilities
  3. Pricing: All subscription tiers, what's included, and any limitations
  4. Onboarding: How new customers get started with your product
  5. Support: Common troubleshooting steps and FAQs

Pro Tip: Format your knowledge base like an employee training manual. The more detailed and organized your document, the more accurate your chatbot's responses will be.

Once completed, download the document as a .txt file (recommended) or PDF. At 7:30 in the video, you'll see the exact knowledge base structure used in our demo, including how to handle formatting for optimal AI comprehension.

Pabbl Chartflow Setup

Pabbl Chartflow provides a no-code platform for building AI chatbots with your knowledge base. Here's how to get started:

  1. Visit pabbl.com/chartflow
  2. Sign up for a free account (includes 100 monthly messages)
  3. Navigate to "AI Assistant" in the dashboard
  4. Click "Add Assistant" and name your chatbot

The platform offers pre-built templates for common use cases like customer support agents, but we recommend starting with the "AI Agent" template as shown at 9:45 in the video. This provides a balanced configuration that you can customize later.

AI Assistant Configuration

The configuration panel is where you connect your knowledge base to the AI. Key settings to adjust:

  • Temperature: Controls response creativity (0 for factual, 1 for creative)
  • Model: OpenAI provides the most reliable results
  • API Key: Connect your OpenAI account (detailed at 11:20 in the video)
  • Stop Keywords: Words like "human" that pause AI responses

The most critical step is uploading your knowledge base document under "Knowledge Source." The system will process your file and create a vector ID confirming successful upload (visible at 13:50 in the video).

Implementation Insight: Clients who spend time refining their knowledge base see 92-97% accuracy rates in chatbot responses compared to 70-80% with hastily prepared documents.

Testing Your Chatbot

Before deploying, thoroughly test your chatbot with various question types:

  1. Basic product questions
  2. Pricing inquiries
  3. Scenario-based questions ("What if I need X feature?")
  4. Troubleshooting issues

At 16:30 in the video, you'll see the testing process where we ask about CRM automation features and pricing - with the chatbot pulling exact details from the knowledge base. Any inaccuracies indicate sections that need refinement in your document.

Deployment Options
