Choosing the Right Voice Personality

GoHighLevel offers multiple natural-sounding voice options for your AI receptionist. Key considerations when selecting:

  • Clarity: Choose voices that enunciate clearly
  • Pacing: Moderate speed works best for customer service
  • Accent: Match your local customer expectations
  • Tone: Professional yet friendly works for most businesses

Post-Call Actions
