Retell AI Setup in 5 Minutes

The foundation of any AI voice agent is the platform that handles the telephony and voice synthesis. Retell AI stands out for its seamless ChatGPT integration and affordable pay-as-you-go pricing.

After creating your Retell account (2 minutes), you'll land on the agent creation page. The critical initial settings are:

  1. Agent Type: Select "Single Prompt Agent" for simplicity
  2. Model: GPT-5 Fast (most current and responsive)
  3. Voice: Choose from 20+ options (we used "Ethan")
  4. Language: English default, but supports 12 languages

The beauty of Retell's interface is that you can test your agent immediately after these basic settings. We'll enhance it with proper prompting next, but even this barebones setup can handle simple calls.

Prompt Engineering for Natural Conversations

An AI voice agent lives or dies by its prompt. The difference between a robotic interrogation and fluid conversation comes down to how you structure the instructions.

Our nightclub promoter example demonstrates key prompt engineering principles:

Universal AI Voice Agent Prompt Structure:

  1. Agent identity and purpose
  2. Business information (name, location, services)
  3. Current date/time awareness
  4. Conversation flow guidelines
  5. Information collection protocol

We fed this structure to ChatGPT with instructions to tailor it for a nightclub. The AI generated a complete prompt including dynamic variables for date, location, and services. The result was an agent that could:

  • Answer questions about music and amenities
  • Handle booking inquiries naturally
  • Politely redirect off-topic questions
  • Collect contact information professionally

The same template works for any business - just change the details. That's what makes this scalable as a service.

Voice and Personality Configuration

The voice you choose becomes your client's brand ambassador. Retell offers multiple options across genders, accents, and tones.

For our nightclub example, we selected "Ethan" - a clear, energetic male voice fitting the venue's vibe. But different businesses demand different personalities:

  • Medical practices: Softer, reassuring tones
  • Law firms: Authoritative and precise
  • Childcare: Warm and friendly

Speech settings allow further customization:

Key Settings: Pronunciation adjustments for industry terms, speech rate (slower for elderly callers), and silence handling (auto-end after 2 minutes of no response).

Remember - the voice should match both the business type and its target demographic. A/B testing different options with actual callers often reveals surprising preferences.

Advanced Call Handling Features

Basic agents answer calls. Professional-grade agents handle complex scenarios seamlessly. Retell provides several enterprise features:

1. Call Transfers

Configure transfers to specific numbers based on:

  • Time of day (after-hours to daytime staff)
  • Caller request ("Press 1 for sales, 2 for support")
  • Conversation triggers ("I need to speak to a manager")

2. Knowledge Base Integration

Upload documents or connect websites so your agent can answer questions about:

  • Business hours
  • Pricing and packages
  • Service details

3. Real-Time Transcription

Send call transcripts to:

  • CRM systems
  • Email follow-up sequences
  • Task management platforms

Implementation Tip: Start simple with just call answering, then layer in these advanced features as you and your clients become comfortable with the technology.
