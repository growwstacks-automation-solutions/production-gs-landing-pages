Step 1: Call Routing

Incoming calls hit your existing business number first. The system detects if it's after hours or all lines are busy, then routes to the AI agent.

Step 2: Natural Language Processing

The AI greets callers in your brand voice (customizable), understands HVAC-specific requests like "no AC" or "furnace repair", and asks clarifying questions.

Step 3: Calendar Integration

Using real-time API connections, the agent checks technician availability while the caller is still on the line - no awkward callbacks needed.

n8n Setup: Connecting Your Phone System

The heart of this solution is n8n's visual workflow builder. You'll create a webhook that receives call data from your VoIP provider (we recommend Twilio or Plivo for HVAC businesses). The key nodes to configure:

  • Webhook Receiver: Captures caller ID and initial voice transcript
  • GPT-4 Node: Processes the conversation with your custom HVAC prompts
  • Calendar Check: Queries Google Calendar or other scheduling systems
  • SMS Gateway: Sends confirmation texts with appointment details
