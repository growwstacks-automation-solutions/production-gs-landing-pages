Why Voice AI Beats Chatbots for Real Estate

While chatbots have become common on real estate websites, they suffer from a critical flaw - they feel transactional, not personal. Visitors typing into a box don't experience the rapport-building that happens in natural conversation. Voice AI changes this dynamic completely.

The difference becomes obvious when comparing conversion rates. Where chatbots might capture 10-15% of lead information, our voice agent implementation consistently achieves 65-70% completion rates. Why? Because voice allows for adaptive questioning that feels like talking to a helpful assistant, not filling out a form.

Key insight: Buyers discussing a $1M+ purchase decision want human connection, not text boxes. Our tests show voice AI appointments convert to closed deals 3x more often than form submissions.
