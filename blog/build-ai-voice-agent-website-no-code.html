Professional Agent Architecture in 4 Layers

Enterprise-grade voice agents succeed because they're built on four structured layers most DIY solutions miss:

  1. Brain: The prompt system containing business rules, knowledge boundaries, and conversation flow
  2. Ears: Speech-to-text that understands accents and background noise
  3. Mouth: Text-to-speech with natural pacing and tone adjustments
  4. Hands: Action systems for bookings, forms, and CRM integrations
