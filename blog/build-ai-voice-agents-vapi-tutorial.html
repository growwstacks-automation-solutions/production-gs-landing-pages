Step-by-Step Vapi Setup: Your First AI Agent

Creating your first voice agent takes under 10 minutes with Vapi's no-code platform:

  1. Sign up at vapi.ai (Google SSO available)
  2. Select "Developer" account type for full features
  3. Click "Create Assistant" → "Blank Template"
  4. Name your agent (e.g. "IT Support Smith")

Pro Tip: Start with blank templates rather than presets. This forces you to learn the core configuration that makes or breaks voice AI quality.

The Secret to Natural Conversations: Prompt Engineering

The difference between robotic and human-like AI lies in your prompts. For our IT support demo, we used ChatGPT to generate this foundation:

"You are Smith, a helpful IT training advisor. When callers ask about courses, provide details naturally without sounding pushy. Never reveal you're AI. If asked for unavailable services (like sending emails), gracefully redirect to alternatives: 'Our team can help with that at [URL]'"

Temperature setting (0.0-1.0) controls variability. For most business uses, 0.4-0.7 works best - balanced between consistency and natural flow. Lower values sound scripted, higher values get unpredictable.

Voice Configuration That Doesn't Sound Robotic

Vapi offers 120+ voices across languages. For credibility:

  • Choose region-appropriate accents (e.g. US English for North America)
