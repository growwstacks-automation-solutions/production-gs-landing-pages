Gemini 3's audio capabilities represent a quantum leap in conversational AI. Why Gemini 3 Changes Everything

Traditional voice bots follow a three-stage pipeline: speech-to-text processing, intent recognition, then text-to-speech response generation. This assembly-line approach creates latency and breaks conversation flow. Gemini 3 eliminates these bottlenecks with native audio understanding that doesn't require intermediate text conversion.

The architectural differences are profound. Legacy systems force customers to wait through complete sentences before processing begins. Gemini agents detect when you start speaking and instantly adjust their response - just like human conversations. This enables natural interruptions, follow-up questions, and fluid dialogue that traditional systems can't match.
