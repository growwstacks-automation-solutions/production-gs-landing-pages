Crafting the Perfect System Prompt

The system prompt is your agent's personality blueprint. ElevenLabs recommends six key components (demonstrated at 8:20 in the video): personality traits, environment context, tone guidelines, goal setting, guardrails, and tools description. Together these create consistent, brand-aligned interactions.

For our retail assistant, we defined a friendly, efficient persona focused on order tracking and returns. The environment specifies this is primarily phone-based support. Guardrails prevent the agent from making promises about delivery times or refund policies - crucial for compliance. The video shows how these elements combine to create natural yet controlled conversations.

Selecting the Right Voice Personality

ElevenLabs' voice library offers thousands of options across genders, ages, and accents. At 12:30 in the tutorial, we switch from the default male voice "Eric" to "Rachel" - a conversational American female voice better suited for our retail brand. The change takes one click with immediate effect.

Voice selection impacts customer perception and comfort. Technical support often benefits from authoritative, steady voices while hospitality works better with warm, friendly tones.
