The Offline AI Revolution for Technical Teams

Field technicians, repair teams, and manufacturing staff often work in environments without reliable internet access. Yet these same teams need instant access to technical manuals, troubleshooting guides, and equipment specifications. Traditional solutions involve bulky PDFs or printed manuals that are difficult to search in the field.

Model HQ solves this by letting you create specialized AI bots that package all your documentation into an easily searchable interface that works 100% offline. The demo shows creating an AC repair technician bot that can answer questions while referencing attached service manuals - perfect for HVAC technicians working in homes with spotty connectivity.

72% of field technicians report wasting at least 1-2 hours per week searching through documentation. Custom AI bots can reduce this search time to seconds while ensuring answers come from approved sources.

Building an AC Repair Technician Bot

Creating a custom bot begins with selecting "Build New" from the Model HQ interface. The demo at 1:30 walks through configuring an "AC Repair Bot" step-by-step:

Step 1: Name Your Bot

Give your bot a descriptive name that reflects its purpose. The demo uses "AC Repair Bot" to clearly indicate this will assist HVAC technicians.

Step 2: Select Model Size

Choose between small (1.5B parameter), medium, or large models based on your hardware capabilities. The demo intentionally uses a small model to show it can run on modest hardware.

Step 3: Configure the Prompt

The system prompt defines the bot's personality and expertise. For the AC bot: "You are an experienced AC technician who explains things simply in easy-to-understand language."

Pro Tip: When creating technical bots, emphasize simple explanations in your prompt. Field technicians often need to explain concepts to customers who lack technical background.
