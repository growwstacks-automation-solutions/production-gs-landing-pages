How the n8n Email Agent Works

Unlike basic email filters or canned responses, this workflow combines three powerful components: real-time triggering, AI analysis, and context-aware replies. When a new email arrives, the system:

  1. Captures the complete message including sender details and previous thread history
  2. Analyzes the content using your configured AI model (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
  3. Generates a professional response tailored to your brand voice
  4. Sends the response automatically or holds it for approval (your choice)

The magic happens in the system prompt—a short instruction that shapes the AI's behavior. A well-crafted prompt like "You are an email assistant. Summarize emails and draft professional replies" produces remarkably human-like responses without sounding robotic.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Step 1: Create Your Workflow

Open n8n dashboard, start a new blank workflow. This creates a clean canvas where you'll connect your email provider to AI and back to email.

Step 2: Set Up the Email Trigger

Add either a Gmail or Outlook trigger node configured to watch for new emails. Authenticate your account if you haven't already—this allows n8n to monitor your inbox.

Step 3: Add the AI Agent Node

Choose your preferred AI provider (OpenAI works well for most cases) and paste your system prompt. This is where you define the agent's personality and response style.

Step 4: Configure the Reply Mechanism

Add an email send node connected to the same provider as your trigger. Map the AI's output to the reply body field, and ensure thread IDs are linked so replies stay in the correct conversation.

Pro tip: Test with personal emails first by sending messages from an alternate account. Watch how the agent handles different message types before going live with business communications.

Advanced Customization Options

The system prompt is your control panel—experiment with different instructions to:
