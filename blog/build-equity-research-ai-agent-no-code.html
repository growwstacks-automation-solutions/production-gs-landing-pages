How the AI Research Agent Works

This five-step automation replaces hours of manual research with a scheduled workflow that:

  1. Triggers monthly (or on-demand) to analyze your watchlist
  2. Pulls real-time data from Yahoo Finance and market news
  3. Processes information through Perplexity's Sonar Reasoning Pro model
  4. Generates a comprehensive report using ChatGPT
  5. Emails you a polished PDF with key findings

The entire process runs unattended, giving you institutional-quality research without the institutional overhead.

Step 1: Setting Up Your Relay App Workflow

Relay App provides the no-code foundation for your research agent. After creating your free account:

  1. Click "New Workflow" in the top right corner
  2. Set a monthly trigger (first of each month recommended)
  3. Name your workflow (e.g., "Equity Research Agent")

This scheduled trigger ensures your research stays current without manual intervention. For active traders, consider setting it to run weekly instead.

Step 2: Creating Your Stock Tracking Table

The heart of your research agent is a simple table tracking your watchlist:

  1. Add a "Create Table" action from Utilities
  2. Create two columns: "Stock Name" and "Ticker"
  3. Populate with companies you want to analyze (e.g., Amazon - AMZN)

This table scales effortlessly - add 5 stocks or 50. The agent will process each one automatically when triggered.

Pro Tip: Add a "Priority" column to flag stocks needing deeper analysis or more frequent updates.

Step 3: Configuring Perplexity AI for Financial Analysis

Perplexity's Sonar Reasoning Pro outperforms ChatGPT for equity research due to its live web search capabilities. Configure three parallel research steps:

  1. Fundamentals Analysis: Yahoo Finance data with prompt: "Analyze [Ticker]'s financial health using Yahoo Finance data including P/E ratio, EPS growth, and debt levels"
  2. Qualitative Research: "Search Twitter and news for recent developments about [Stock Name] including leadership changes or growth initiatives"
  3. Market Sentiment: "Find analyst comments, technical analysis, and trading patterns for [Ticker]"

This triple-verification approach ensures comprehensive coverage from multiple angles.

Step 4: Automated Report Generation with ChatGPT

With research data collected, ChatGPT compiles it into an institutional-style report:

  1. Add a ChatGPT action using GPT-4 Turbo
  2. Feed it all three Perplexity research outputs
  3. Use this prompt structure: "Compile an equity research report for [Stock Name] ([Ticker]) including: 1) Financial fundamentals 2) Market sentiment 3) Technical analysis 4) Analyst consensus 5) Summary recommendation"

The AI structures the data into a professional format with clear sections and actionable insights.

Step 5: PDF Creation and Email Delivery

The final steps transform your report into a shareable format:

  1. Convert the ChatGPT output to a Google Doc for formatting
  2. Generate a PDF version (preserves formatting for email)
  3. Set up email delivery with subject: "[Your Name] Equity Research: [Stock Name]"
  4. Attach the PDF and schedule delivery

You'll receive polished, professional reports in your inbox without lifting a finger.
