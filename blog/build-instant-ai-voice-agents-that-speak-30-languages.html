Custom Intake Questions

These aren't generic questions - they're tailored to the specific business type based on the AI's analysis of similar businesses. This level of customization would normally require hours of manual work by a subject matter expert.

Smart Call Forwarding

Triller's call forwarding features ensure the AI complements rather than replaces human staff. You can configure rules so the agent:

This balanced approach means customers always get immediate assistance while maintaining human touchpoints when needed.
