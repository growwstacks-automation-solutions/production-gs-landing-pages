How the AI Lead Gen System Works

This isn't just another scraping tool - it's a complete AI agent that handles the entire lead generation process from start to finish. The system works through four automated stages:

  1. Target Identification: Scrapes Google Maps for businesses matching your ideal customer profile
  2. Data Enrichment: Extracts emails, phone numbers, and social profiles from each listing
  3. Quality Filtering: Automatically removes businesses with poor reviews or missing contact info
  4. Personalized Outreach: Writes and sends customized emails referencing specific details about each business

At 4:30 in the tutorial, you'll see how the AI analyzes each business's Google Maps listing - including their services, review count, and average rating - to craft emails that feel hand-written.

Step 1: Setting Up the Google Maps Scraper

The foundation is a Google Maps scraper that finds businesses matching your criteria. We use EPI's scraper (shown at 7:15) which offers 1,200 free credits - enough to test the system with real data.

Key configuration points:

  • Search Term: Your ideal client type (e.g. "real estate agents" or "dentists")
  • Location: City/region to target (works globally)
  • Result Limit: Start with 50-100 to test before scaling

Pro Tip: The scraper returns detailed business info including website, phone, Facebook profile, services offered, and average review score - all of which the AI will use later for personalization.

Step 2: Filtering Quality Leads Automatically

Not all leads are created equal. At 12:40 in the video, you'll see the three-stage filtering process that ensures only high-potential leads make it through:

  1. Duplicate Removal: Checks against your existing lead database
  2. Review Score Filter: Automatically excludes businesses with less than 3.5-star average
  3. Contact Verification: Confirms each lead has at least two contact methods (email + phone)

This filtering happens in real-time as leads are scraped, saving you from manually reviewing thousands of listings. The system logs all filtered leads with the reason for exclusion.

Step 3: Writing Personalized Emails With AI

The magic happens when the AI crafts emails referencing specific details about each business. At 18:20, you'll see how it transforms raw data into messages like:

"Hi [Business Name], I noticed your 4.9-star rating from 18 reviews - your customers clearly love your service! I help similar [Business Type] reduce [Pain Point] by 30%. Would you have 15 minutes this week to discuss?"

The AI pulls from multiple data points to make each email feel hand-written:

  • Business name and services from Google Maps
  • Review count and average rating
  • Services offered (customizes value proposition)
  • Location (for local references)

Step 4: Sending Through Your Email System

The final step (shown at 24:50) connects to your email provider to send the AI-generated messages. We demonstrate Gmail integration, but it works with any SMTP provider or email service like Mailchimp.

Critical safeguards built in:

  • Rate Limiting: Avoids triggering spam filters
  • Tracking: Logs all sent emails with timestamps
  • Reply Handling: Can route responses to your sales team

The system marks each lead as "contacted" to prevent duplicate outreach if you run it multiple times.
