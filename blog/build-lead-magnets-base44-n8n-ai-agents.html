Complete Workflow Breakdown

Let's examine each component of this automation:

Step 1: Base44 Form Configuration

The front-end form includes fields for:

  • Name and contact information
  • Business challenge description (free text)
  • Current solution pain points
  • Desired outcomes

Step 2: n8n Trigger Setup

The workflow begins when Base44 receives a submission. n8n's webhook trigger captures:

  • All form field data
  • Timestamp and referral source
  • Device and browser information

Step 3: AI Analysis & Response

An n8n AI node processes the submission using a prompt like:

"Analyze this business challenge from [industry] consultant prospect. Generate a 3-step implementation roadmap addressing their specific pain points. Include one relevant case study example. Tone: professional but approachable."

3 Common Implementation Mistakes

When setting up this workflow, avoid these pitfalls:

1. Over-Engineering the Form

Limit fields to only what's needed for the AI to generate a valuable response. More than 5-7 fields drops completion rates.

2. Generic AI Prompts

Train your n8n AI agent on your specific methodology, case studies, and frameworks - not just general business advice.

3. Missing the Human Touchpoint

While the AI handles initial interaction, ensure a real person takes over within 24-48 hours for the sales conversation.
