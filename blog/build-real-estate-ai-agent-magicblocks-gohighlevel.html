Configuring Key Facts for Lead Qualification

The real magic happens in the Key Facts section where you define what information your AI agent collects. The real estate template includes:

  • Buyer/Renter status
  • Property type preferences
  • Budget range
  • Timeline urgency
  • Contact information

Each key fact can be collected through direct questions or inferred from conversation context. For example, when a prospect mentions "looking for a 3-bedroom under $1M," the AI automatically updates both the property type and budget fields without repetitive questioning.

Setting Up Compliance Guardrails

Real estate has strict compliance requirements that generic AI tools often violate. MagicBlocks includes pre-configured guardrails that:

  • Prevent property valuations
  • Avoid financial advice
  • Maintain fair housing compliance

You can add custom rules like avoiding specific neighborhoods or disclosures required in your state. These guardrails ensure every conversation stays professional while still moving leads through the qualification process.

Pro tip: Add your brokerage's specific compliance language verbatim to the guardrails section to ensure brand-appropriate responses.

The $200M Sales Journey Framework

The heart of the system is the HA Sales Framework visualized as interconnected blocks in MagicBlocks' journey editor. Each block has a specific purpose:

  2. Align: Understand the prospect's specific needs
  3. Personalize: Tailor recommendations based on collected facts
  4. Pitch: Present relevant properties or services

The AI dynamically navigates this journey based on real-time conversation analysis. If a prospect expresses urgency (like "need to move next month"), the system can fast-track to the close block while still collecting all key facts.
