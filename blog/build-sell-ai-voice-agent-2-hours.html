The 3-Question Sales Script That Landed Our First Client

Our entire sales process revolves around three simple questions designed to make the prospect realize their missed call problem:

  1. "Do you miss phone calls?" (Creates awareness)
  2. "How many calls do you think you miss per day?" (Quantifies the problem)
  3. "What's the average value of a booking?" (Calculates the cost)

With our HVAC client, this revealed he was missing 10 calls/day at $200 each - $2,000 daily. The moment he did that math himself was when he asked "How do I sign up?"
