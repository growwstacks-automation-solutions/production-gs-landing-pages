The Voice AI Implementation Challenge

Implementing a custom voice AI agent has traditionally required integrating at least three different systems: a voice engine (like Vapi or Retell), an automation hub (like n8n or Make.com), and a telephony provider (like Twilio). While powerful, this approach creates significant complexity:

Each integration point becomes a potential failure source, debugging requires checking multiple dashboards, and changes often require updates across all connected systems. The result? What should be a simple voice automation project can take days or weeks to implement.

80% of businesses abandon voice AI projects during implementation due to technical complexity and integration challenges.
