Bland's unique prompt structure breaks down into four clear sections:

Techniques for Maximum Realism

The difference between an obvious robot and a believable agent comes down to three techniques:

  1. Natural Pauses: Program brief silences before responses to mimic human thinking time
  2. Background Audio: Cafe or office noise subconsciously signals a "real" location
  3. Imperfections: Occasional "um" or "let me think" phrases increase authenticity

Bland's voice library already includes these human elements, but you can enhance them further in the advanced settings. The platform also offers unique features like:

  • Noise Cancellation: Filters out caller background noise for clearer audio
  • Dynamic Transfer: Seamlessly passes complex queries to human staff
  • Call Analysis: Extracts key details (names, needs) for your CRM
