Step-by-Step Building Process

Step 1: Access Google AI Studio

Navigate to Google AI Studio and select the voice app builder option. The platform is free to use with your Google account and requires no technical setup or API keys.

Step 2: Describe Your Agent

Write a clear description of what you want your voice agent to do. Include details about:

  • Your industry and specific use case
  • Key functions (appointments, inquiries, etc.)
  • Preferred voice characteristics
  • Any visual design preferences

Step 3: Build and Test

Click the build button and wait approximately 90 seconds. Test the agent immediately through the built-in interface to verify it meets your requirements.

Deployment Options for Production

While the web version works great for demos and prototypes, production deployment requires connecting to real phone systems. Here are your options:

Web Deployment

Google hosts your agent with a shareable URL - perfect for demonstrations or limited use cases. Visitors interact through their browser with WebRTC for near-instant voice responses.

Phone Integration

For handling real phone calls, extract your agent's system prompt and conversation logic from Google AI Studio. Then deploy it through platforms like Vapi or Retail that provide phone number integration and call handling infrastructure.

Important note: Phone networks introduce slight delays (200-500ms) that aren't present in web-based testing. Factor this into your conversation design with natural pauses.
