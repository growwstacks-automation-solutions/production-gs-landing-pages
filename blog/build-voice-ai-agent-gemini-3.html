Step-by-Step: Building Your Agent

Creating your first voice AI agent takes just three actions:

Step 1: Access Google AI Studio

Visit ai.google and select "Create conversational voice apps." This specialized interface handles all the complex AI orchestration behind the scenes.

Step 2: Enter Your Prompt

Describe your desired agent in plain English. For example: "Build me a modern website for a gym with a friendly female voice agent that provides workout ideas and nutrition tips."

Step 3: Click Build

Gemini generates your complete voice agent and website simultaneously. The system automatically:

  • Designs the user interface
  • Creates conversational flows
  • Develops backend logic
  • Integrates voice capabilities

Under 60 seconds: The gym demo in our video was built with just one sentence and deployed in 58 seconds flat. More detailed prompts produce even more sophisticated results.

Customizing Your AI Assistant

Basic prompts create functional demos, but professional implementations require specific customization. Gemini 3.0 responds to detailed instructions about:

The 3 Essential Prompt Elements:

  1. Business Details: Clearly describe your services, operating hours, location
  2. Voice Personality: Specify gender, tone (friendly/professional), speaking style
  3. Website Design: Request modern/minimalist/vibrant layouts with specific sections

For example, compare these prompt versions:

Basic: "Build a voice agent for my plumbing business."

Professional: "Create a 24/7 plumbing assistant with a calm male voice that explains common issues, provides emergency contact info, and shows service areas on an interactive map. Use a blue/white color scheme with our logo."

Professional Deployment Options

While Google AI Studio creates instant demos, professional client presentations require proper deployment:

Option 1: Share Link (Fastest)

Copy the auto-generated URL to demonstrate the prototype. Simple but shows Google branding.

Option 2: Export to GitHub (For Developers)

Technical teams can download the codebase for customization before production deployment.

Option 3: Deploy to Google Cloud (Most Professional)

Publish through Google Cloud for enterprise-grade hosting. Takes 5-60 minutes and allows custom domains.

Client-ready in hours: The gym demo was deployed to a custom domain (ironpitnessai.slackai.com) within 90 minutes - complete with SSL security and professional branding.

Adding Your Custom Domain

Mapping your business domain creates a polished client experience:

  1. Deploy your agent through Google Cloud
  2. Navigate to Domain Mappings in Cloud Console
  3. Add your verified domain (requires DNS record updates)
  4. Wait 5-60 minutes for SSL certificate generation

The system automatically handles all technical configuration. You simply:

  • Copy the provided TXT record to your domain provider
  • Add the CNAME record when prompted
  • Select your preferred subdomain

Once complete, your voice agent appears under your professional domain (e.g. assistant.yourbusiness.com).
