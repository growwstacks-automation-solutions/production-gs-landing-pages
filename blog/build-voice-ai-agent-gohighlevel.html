Voice Personalization Options

What makes GoHighLevel's Voice AI stand out is the depth of customization available (shown at 3:20 in the video). Beyond selecting an accent, you can fine-tune:

  • Speech Speed: Adjust from deliberate (good for technical info) to conversational pace
  • Interruption Sensitivity: Control whether the AI allows natural overlaps in conversation
  • Background Noise: Add ambient sounds like a call center or coffee shop for authenticity
  • Backchanneling: Enable verbal nods ("uh-huh", "I see") for more natural flow

The advanced settings tab (3:45) even lets you adjust the AI's "temperature" - how creative or strictly factual its responses should be. For sales calls, slightly higher creativity often performs better.
