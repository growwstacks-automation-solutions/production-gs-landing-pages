Why Build Your Own AI Assistant?

Most business owners waste countless hours on repetitive tasks - checking weather, identifying products, answering basic customer questions. Commercial AI assistants assistants often come with monthly fees, data privacy concerns, or require technical expertise to implement.

The Xiao platform changes everything by offering completely free speech-to-text, LLM, and vision capabilities without requiring credit card details. Combined with the affordable Unihiker K10 hardware, you get Jarvis-like capabilities at a fraction of the cost.

Key benefit: This solution delivers under 2-second latency response times for most queries while remembering context from previous interactions - something even paid services struggle with.

Hardware Requirements

You'll need just three physical components to build this project:

  1. Unihiker K10 board - An affordable ($50) single-board computer with built-in mic and camera support
  2. USB-C cable - For power and data transfer
  3. Internet connection - Either WiFi or Ethernet

The Unihiker stands out for its balance of price and performance. Unlike Raspberry Pi alternatives often require additional purchases for audio/visual capabilities, while the Unihiker includes everything in one package.

Setting Up the Xiao Platform

Xiao's cloud platform provides the AI brains for our assistant. The setup process takes just minutes:

  1. Create free account at xiaozhi.me (Google login works)
  2. Navigate to the Device Management section
  3. Connect your Unihiker board to the same network
  4. Enter the activation code from the board to link devices

Pro tip:: The open-source version available globally has some limitations during peak hours, but provides all core functionality completely free.

Flashing the Unihiker Board

The board needs Xiao firmware flashed before use:

    1. Download latest firmware (v1.8.5) from DF Robot
    2. Use ESP32 S3 chipset in flash tool
    3. Connect board via USB-C
    4. Erase existing firmware
    5. Flash new firmware

    This process takes about 5 minutes. The video tutorial shows each step in detail at the 4:30 mark.

Configuring Your AI Assistant

Personalization options include:

  • Name your assistant (e.g., Jarvis)

  • Select voice personality (friendly, professional, humorous)
  • Customize wake words and sleep commands

The platform even lets you write background stories for your assistant to shape its responses. Our test assistant remembered being "designed as an engaging companion" and referenced this in later conversations.
