Step 1: Connect WhatsApp to n8n

The foundation is linking your WhatsApp Business account to n8n. This requires creating API credentials in the Meta Developer portal (shown at 2:30 in the video).

Step 1.1: Get Your WhatsApp API Keys

Navigate to business.facebook.com and create a new app. Under Settings → Basic, you'll find the App ID and App Secret needed for n8n authentication.

Step 1.2: Configure n8n WhatsApp Connection

In n8n, create a new credential using the Business App ID and Secret. The tutorial shows exactly where to paste these values (3:15 timestamp) to establish the connection.

Pro Tip: Always test your WhatsApp connection immediately after setup by sending a message to your business number. The workflow should log the incoming message in n8n's execution history.
