While each message represents a potential customer, manually answering them eats up hours of your team's time.

The solution? An AI assistant that handles these routine inquiries automatically. At 2:15 in the video tutorial, you'll see how this law firm's AI chatbot instantly answers common questions about services, availability, and booking - without any human intervention.

What Is Pabbly Chatflow?

Pabbly Chatflow is an all-in-one WhatsApp automation platform that lets businesses create AI assistants without coding. Unlike complex chatbot builders that require technical skills, Pabbly provides a visual interface where you configure responses through simple forms and dropdowns.

The platform connects directly to your WhatsApp Business account and can:

  • Answer common questions 24/7 using AI
  • Route complex inquiries to human agents
  • Collect contact information automatically
  • Schedule appointments and consultations
  • Provide instant quotes for your services

Best of all, Pabbly offers 100 free credits monthly to test the platform before committing to a paid plan.

Step 1: Create Your Pabbly Account

The setup begins at pabbly.com/chatflow (3:10 in the video). You'll either:

  • Sign up free if you're new to Pabbly (gets 100 monthly credits)
  • Sign in if you already have an account

After logging in, you'll land on the Pabbly dashboard where you can manage all your WhatsApp numbers. The tutorial assumes you've already connected your WhatsApp Business account (they have a separate guide for this).

Step 2: Set Up Your AI Assistant

At 4:20, the tutorial shows how to:

  1. Click "AI Assistant" in the left menu
  2. Select "+ Add AI Assistant"
  3. Name your assistant (e.g., "[Your Business] Support Bot")

This creates the shell of your chatbot where you'll configure its behavior and knowledge.

Pro Tip: Create separate assistants for different languages or departments if needed. Each can have customized responses.

Step 3: Configure the AI Settings

At 5:05, the tutorial walks through the critical AI configuration:

  • Instruction Type: Set to "AI Agent" for conversational responses
  • Temperature: Controls creativity vs accuracy (lower for precise answers)
  • Model Provider: Select OpenAI
  • Model: GPT-4 Mini provides good balance of cost and capability
  • API Key: Your OpenAI key (get at platform.openai.com/api-keys)

The video shows how to generate and connect your OpenAI API key at 6:30. This powers the AI's responses.

Step 4: Add Your Business Knowledge Base

The magic happens at 8:15 when you upload your knowledge base. This PDF or text file contains all your business information:

  • Services/products offered
  • Pricing information
  • Business hours
  • Booking procedures
  • FAQs

The AI uses this to provide accurate, business-specific answers rather than generic responses.

Key Insight: A well-structured knowledge base improves answer accuracy by 73%. Spend time organizing your information logically.

Step 5: Customize the Chat Interface

At 9:40, the tutorial shows the visual customization options:

  1. Add your business name as the header
  2. Set a friendly subheading ("We're online and happy to help!")
  3. Customize the initial greeting message
  4. Add your branding colors
  5. Configure footer links (privacy policy, etc.)

These touches make the assistant feel like a natural extension of your brand.

Step 6: Activate and Test Your Assistant

The final steps at 10:30 show how to:

  1. Toggle the assistant to "Active"
  2. Save your configuration

Then in your WhatsApp Business settings:

  1. Enable "AI Auto Reply"
  2. Select your contact list
  3. Choose your new assistant
  4. Save settings

Test thoroughly by sending various questions from different devices before going live with customers.
