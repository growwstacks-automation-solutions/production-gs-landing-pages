The Appointment Management Challenge

Dental practices typically spend 2-3 hours daily managing appointment requests - answering phone calls, responding to messages, checking calendar availability, and manually entering bookings. This administrative burden pulls staff away from patient care and creates frustrating delays for customers trying to schedule visits.

The traditional approach requires constant human availability during business hours, leads to double-bookings from manual calendar errors, and often results in missed messages after hours. Patients frequently complain about long wait times just to schedule a simple cleaning.

83% of dental practices report that appointment scheduling is their most time-consuming administrative task, yet only 12% have automated any part of the process.
