What Are AI Agents and Why Your Business Needs Them

Every growing business hits the same wall - repetitive internal processes consuming valuable time. Onboarding new employees, answering common customer questions, managing internal knowledge - these tasks are essential but don't scale well with human labor alone.

AI agents solve this by acting as specialized digital employees. Unlike basic chatbots that follow scripted flows, agents can dynamically research information, make context-aware decisions, and handle open-ended conversations while maintaining your company's voice and standards.

85% of customer service interactions could be handled by AI agents, according to Gartner research. The challenge has always been creating these assistants without technical expertise - until now.

Creating an Onboarding Agent in 5 Minutes

Employee onboarding is one of the most impactful uses for AI agents. New hires typically spend their first week asking the same basic questions about company culture, tools, and processes - questions that existing employees answer repeatedly.

In the demo at 3:30, we create an onboarding agent by simply describing its purpose: "An agent that helps new employees navigate company culture, mission, and initial setup." The system then:
