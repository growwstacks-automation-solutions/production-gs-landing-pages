The Universal Phone Problem Every Business Faces

Business phone systems haven't evolved in decades. Customers still face the same frustrating choices: navigate endless robotic menus, wait on hold indefinitely, or give up entirely. On the business side, missed calls represent lost revenue, while handling calls manually eats into productivity.

The statistics tell a grim story: 85% of customers hang up after being placed on hold for just 2 minutes, and small businesses miss an average of 27% of incoming calls entirely. After-hours calls? Forget about them - they either go to voicemail (which 80% of callers won't leave) or trigger the dreaded "we're closed" message.

The hidden cost: Every missed call costs businesses an average of $200 in lost opportunity. For a company receiving just 10 calls per day, that's $60,000 per year slipping through the cracks.
