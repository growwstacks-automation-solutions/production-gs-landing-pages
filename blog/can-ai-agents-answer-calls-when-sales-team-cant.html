The Missed Call Crisis in Dealerships

Dealerships lose an average of 22% of potential sales simply because calls go unanswered when staff are busy or after hours. Customers who reach voicemail or get frustrated and hang up rarely call back - they move on to competitors who answer immediately.

During peak hours, sales teams juggle multiple customers on the lot while phones ring unanswered. Traditional solutions like hiring additional receptionists prove cost-prohibitive, especially for smaller dealerships. This creates a persistent gap in lead conversion that directly impacts revenue.

Key stat: 67% of car buyers who don't get through on their first call purchase from a different dealership within 72 hours (Cox Automotive).

How the AI Handled a Complex Inquiry

The test call demonstrated several advanced capabilities that go beyond simple call routing:

Implementation note: The AI was configured with fallback protocols to transfer to a human immediately if the conversation entered negotiation phases or complex financing questions.
