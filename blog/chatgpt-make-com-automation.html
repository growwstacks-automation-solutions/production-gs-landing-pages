How to Create Your First ChatGPT Research Agent

Setting up your research agent is simpler than most beginners expect. At 4:32 in the video, you'll see the exact prompt structure that makes this work. The magic lies in giving ChatGPT clear instructions about your product format, SEO requirements, and brand voice—not just generic "find good products" commands.

Here's the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Define Your Product Criteria

List your requirements—price range, product type, shipping constraints. This focuses the AI's research.

Step 2: Structure Your Output

Specify exactly how you want the data formatted (title, SEO keyword, description, URL) so it flows seamlessly into your store.

Step 3: Connect to Make.com

3 Common Mistakes to Avoid

After implementing this for dozens of clients, we've identified these pitfalls:

1. Overcomplicating the initial workflow: Start with one product category and basic content types. Expand only after the core system works flawlessly.

2. Neglecting prompt engineering: Generic ChatGPT prompts produce mediocre results. Invest time in crafting detailed instructions (like those shown at 4:32 in the video).

3. Setting and forgetting: Automation isn't magic. Review outputs weekly for the first month, then monthly thereafter to maintain quality.

Remember: The goal isn't fully autonomous operation—it's 10X efficiency. You still need to oversee the system, just at a strategic rather than tactical level.
