How Claude's Visual Understanding Works

Traditional automation fails because it relies on fixed element selectors that break when websites update. Claude solves this by combining several advanced AI capabilities:

1. Visual Perception

Claude actually sees your screen like a human, understanding buttons, forms, and navigation elements based on their appearance and context rather than just code structure.

2. Intent Recognition

When you say "find contact emails for marketing agencies in London," Claude understands the goal and figures out how to achieve it across websites, rather than needing step-by-step instructions.

3. Adaptive Execution

If a workflow hits a snag (like a popup or changed layout), Claude problem-solves in real-time just like a human would - a capability shown at 12:30 in the video when it handles an unexpected login prompt.

The key difference: Traditional automation follows recipes, Claude understands meals. This makes it resilient to the constant changes that break conventional automation scripts.

Step 2: Describe Your Workflow

Simply explain what you want to automate in plain English, like "Research top digital marketing agencies in Chicago and compile their services, pricing, and contact info into a Google Sheet."

Step 3: Review and Refine

Claude will ask clarifying questions if needed, then execute the task while you watch. You can provide feedback to improve future runs.
