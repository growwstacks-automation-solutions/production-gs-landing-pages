The Hidden Costs of n8n Workflows

Most business automation projects fail before they start because of the steep learning curve required by tools like n8n. What begins as excitement about potential time savings quickly turns to frustration when faced with hundreds of nodes, complex logic gates, and debugging challenges.

The reality is stark: building even simple workflows in n8n typically requires weeks of learning and experimentation. More complex automations can take months to perfect as you wrestle with API integrations, error handling, and edge cases.

80% of automation projects stall in the planning phase because business owners underestimate the technical skills required. What seems simple in theory (like automating invoice processing) becomes overwhelming when faced with the reality of building it node-by-node.

Claude Code represents a fundamental shift in workflow automation by removing the technical barrier entirely. Instead of learning a complex interface, you describe what you need in plain English - just like messaging ChatGPT.
