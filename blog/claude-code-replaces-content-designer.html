Thumbnail Creator: Never Design Manually Again

The most immediate time-saver is automated thumbnail generation. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Provide Brand Assets

Upload a PNG of your face and any logos to the project's assets folder. These become reference points for all future thumbnails.

Step 2: Describe Your Vision

Tell Claude Code in plain English: "Create a thumbnail showing me smiling in the bottom right corner with the Claude logo upside down in my hand." The AI understands spatial relationships.

Step 3: Generate & Refine

Claude uses Key.AI's image-to-image generation to produce variations. At 12:45 in the video, you'll see how minor tweaks ("make my face 20% larger") can be applied instantly.

Pro Tip: Save your favorite thumbnail compositions as templates. Claude can generate 5-10 variations from one master design in under 90 seconds.

Carousel Generator: Multi-Platform Content in Minutes

Carousels perform exceptionally well algorithmically but are notoriously time-consuming to create. The Claude Code system automates this through a 5-phase workflow:

  1. Gather - Provide 3-5 reference carousels you like (screenshots work)
  2. Prompt - Claude analyzes the styles and creates a "design DNA" file
  3. Generate - AI produces slides matching your brand guidelines
  4. Review - You approve or request tweaks (change colors, fonts, etc.)
  5. Evolve - Successful designs become templates for future carousels
