The Writer + Editor AI System

Most failed AI content attempts make one critical mistake - they use a single prompt expecting perfect results. Our workflow separates creation into distinct phases:

1. Writer Agent

Generates raw drafts based on your topic and style guidelines. Focuses on content depth and idea generation.

2. Editor Agent

Applies rigorous checks for brand voice consistency, removes passive voice/corporate jargon, and ensures hook effectiveness. The editor can request revisions from the writer - exactly like human teams.

Pro tip: The editor's 'anafa check' (anti-corporate-speak filter) alone saves 2-3 revision rounds compared to human writers.

Complete Content Creation Workflow

Here's the step-by-step process from idea to published post:

Step 1: Brainstorming

Claude suggests topics based on your recent meetings, industry trends, or content gaps.

Step 2: Hook Selection

Choose from AI-generated hooks combining proven templates with your unique insights.

Step 3: Draft Creation

Writer agent produces initial content following your style guidelines.

Step 4: Editorial Review

Editor agent refines for voice consistency, removes jargon, and optimizes structure.

Step 5: Human Review (Optional)

You provide final tweaks - which feed back into the learning system.

Step 6: Publishing

Direct to LinkedIn or via your scheduling tool (Buffer, Hootsuite).

In summary: Idea → Hook → Draft → Edit → Publish → Learn. The entire cycle completes in under 10 minutes for most posts.
