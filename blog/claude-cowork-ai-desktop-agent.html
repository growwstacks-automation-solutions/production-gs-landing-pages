What Makes Claude Cowork Different

Most AI tools live in your browser as chatbots, but Claude Cowork breaks this mold by becoming an active participant in your desktop workflow. Unlike traditional AI assistants that simply answer questions, Claude Cowork takes direct action on your files and folders - organizing, processing, and transforming them into valuable business assets.

What sets Claude apart is its agentic capabilities - it doesn't just respond to prompts but plans, makes decisions, and executes multi-step workflows. As shown in the demo at 2:15, Claude asked clarifying questions before organizing files, created a plan of action, and then delivered a summary report - all hallmarks of true agentic behavior.

Key differentiator: Claude Cowork combines AI reasoning with direct system access, allowing it to manipulate files, process documents, and build applications - transforming passive AI into an active productivity partner.
