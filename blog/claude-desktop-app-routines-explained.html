The Problem With Traditional Automation

For years, business professionals have struggled with a fundamental limitation of automation tools - they require your computer to be constantly running. Whether it's scheduled tasks in ChatGPT or workflows in other AI platforms, the automation stops the moment you close your laptop or your device goes to sleep.

This creates an impossible choice: either leave your computer running 24/7 (wasting energy and risking overheating) or accept that your automations only work when you're actively using your device. Neither option is practical for busy professionals who need reliable automation without device babysitting.

84% of professionals abandon automation tools when they realize the requirement for constant device availability, according to recent surveys. The cognitive load of managing these limitations often outweighs the benefits.

Game Changer: Cloud-Based Routines

The true innovation in Claude's desktop app isn't just the interface - it's the introduction of cloud-based routines. Unlike traditional scheduled tasks that run locally on your device, these routines execute in Claude's cloud infrastructure.

This means your automations continue working even when:

Routines eliminate the #1 barrier to automation adoption - the requirement for constant device availability. Now you can set up workflows that run on schedule, 24/7, without any local hardware requirements.

Real-World Example: Automated Meeting Summaries

One of the most practical applications of Claude routines is automated meeting summarization. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Create a New Routine

Select "New Routine" in the desktop app and choose "Remote" execution. This ensures the task runs in the cloud rather than locally.

Step 2: Configure the Meeting Summarizer

Write a prompt that specifies what you want Claude to do with your meeting transcripts. For example: "Summarize my Zoom meetings from the past 24 hours, highlighting key decisions, action items, and follow-ups needed."

Step 3: Set Your Schedule

Choose when you want the summary to run (e.g., every weekday at 9:00 AM). The routine will execute at this time regardless of whether your computer is on.

Step 4: Add Connectors

Connect your Zoom account (or other video conferencing tool) to allow Claude to access meeting transcripts automatically.

Result: You wake up to perfectly formatted meeting summaries in your Claude app every morning, without ever lifting a finger or leaving your computer running overnight.
