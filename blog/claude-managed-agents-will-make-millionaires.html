Cognitive Architecture Explained

The breakthrough with Claude agents comes from implementing cognitive architecture - systems that mimic how human brains learn and consolidate information. Without this, even powerful AI models suffer from "memory loss" between sessions.

The solution involves three key components: databases for long-term memory, documents for reference materials, and overnight processing routines where the agent analyzes its daily interactions to update its knowledge base.

  • Daily operation: The agent handles queries and tasks during business hours
  • Nightly processing: It reviews conversations, updates databases, and refines its understanding
  • Continuous improvement: Each day it starts smarter than the day before
