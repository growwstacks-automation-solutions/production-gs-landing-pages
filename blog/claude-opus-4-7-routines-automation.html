How Routines Work (Without Your Computer)

Routines represent a fundamental shift from traditional automation tools. Unlike rigid workflows that break when encountering unexpected inputs, Claude can reason through problems and continue working autonomously.

Three key triggers can launch a Routine:

  1. Schedule: Set daily, weekly, or hourly execution
  2. Webhook: Trigger from any connected tool
  3. GitHub Events: For developer-specific workflows

Critically, Routines run on Anthropic's servers - not your local machine. Your laptop can be closed, your browser shut down, and your automation will still execute on schedule.

Live Demo: Morning Email Triage Routine

The video demonstrates creating a Routine that:
