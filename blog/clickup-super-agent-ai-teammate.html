Most AI assistants just respond to prompts—but what if your AI could think, plan, execute, and remember like a real teammate? ClickUp Super Agents are autonomous AI coworkers that live inside your workspace, understand context, and deliver real outcomes. Here's how they're changing work.

What Makes Super Agents Different

Traditional AI chatbots frustrate teams because they lack context—you have to explain everything from scratch each time. Super Agents solve this by living inside your ClickUp workspace with complete visibility into your tasks, docs, chats, and connected apps.

Unlike passive tools that wait for commands, Super Agents proactively manage outcomes. Give them a goal like "Prepare the quarterly marketing report" and they'll break it into steps, gather data from multiple apps, draft the document, and request your review—all without micromanagement.

Key difference: Regular AI responds to prompts. Super Agents own outcomes from start to finish.

How Super Agents Work

Super Agents operate like human teammates with profiles, activity logs, and the ability to be @mentioned anywhere in ClickUp. They maintain context across conversations, remembering your preferences and past interactions.

Behind the scenes, they execute multi-step workflows that would normally require manual work across multiple apps. Need to sync Salesforce contacts with your email campaign? A Super Agent can pull the data, format it correctly, and push it to your marketing platform—then notify you when done.

24/7 operation: Super Agents work around the clock processing tasks, checking for updates, and triggering follow-up actions based on your business rules.

Super Agent Capabilities

ClickUp Super Agents shine in three key areas that set them apart from basic automation:

1. Cross-App Workflows

They seamlessly connect your entire tech stack—pulling data from one app, processing it, and pushing results to another. No more manual copy-paste between tools.

2. Intelligent Collaboration

Assign them tasks, schedule recurring work, or trigger them through automations. They'll even ask clarifying questions when needed, just like a human would.

3. Continuous Learning

Super Agents improve over time by analyzing your team's patterns, approvals, and feedback to optimize their approach to your specific needs.

Getting Started With Super Agents

ClickUp offers two paths to deploy Super Agents in your workspace:

1. Pre-built Agents: Browse ClickUp's catalog of ready-to-use agents for common functions like research, reporting, or customer follow-ups. Customize them with natural language instructions.

2. Custom Agents: Use Super Agent Studio to describe what you need in plain English. The system configures the agent's tools, triggers, and permissions—no coding required.

Implementation tip: Start with one high-impact workflow (like meeting follow-ups) to experience quick wins before expanding.
