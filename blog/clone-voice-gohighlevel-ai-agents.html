Publishing Your Voice Correctly

Here's the critical step most users miss (revealed at 18:05 in the video):

GoHighLevel doesn't accept private voice clones. You must publish your voice to 11 Labs' public library, making it available to other users (who pay when using it).

To publish:

  1. Navigate to your cloned voice in 11 Labs
  2. Click the three-dot menu
  3. Select "Share Voice"
  4. Toggle "Publish to Voice Library" ON

The creator mentions earning small royalties when others use his published voice - an unexpected benefit of this requirement.

GoHighLevel Integration Steps

With your voice published, integrate it with GoHighLevel:

Step 1: Copy Voice ID

In 11 Labs, click the three-dot menu on your voice and select "Copy Voice ID." This unique identifier tells GoHighLevel which voice to use.

Step 2: Add Custom Voice in GoHighLevel

Navigate to Settings > AI Voice Agents > Custom Voices. Each GoHighLevel agency account can host up to 10 custom voices.

Step 3: Paste & Import

Paste the Voice ID and click "Import Voice." The system verifies the voice and adds it to your available options within 1-2 minutes.
