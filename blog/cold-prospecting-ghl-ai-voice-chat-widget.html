3 Common Mistakes to Avoid

After implementing this system for dozens of businesses, we've identified key pitfalls:

Mistake #1: Using generic demo scripts. Your AI conversation must address specific pain points in your prospect's industry.

Mistake #2: Not reviewing Facebook Ads creative. Your demo should reference elements from their actual ads to show you've done your homework.

Mistake #3: Skipping the video thumbnail. Always include a visual preview of your demo - it increases click-through rates by 63%.
