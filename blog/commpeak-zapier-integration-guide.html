The Problem With Manual Calendar Alerts

SMS reminders have a 98% open rate compared to 20% for email calendar invites. But manually sending text reminders for every event is impractical - consuming up to 3 hours per week for administrative staff. This is where automation creates game-changing efficiency.

Two Approaches to Automation

Zapier offers two distinct paths to connect CommPeak SMS with Google Calendar events. The right choice depends on your technical comfort level and customization needs.

Option 1: Pre-built CommPeak templates offer the fastest setup - ideal when you need basic notifications quickly. These templates handle common use cases like appointment reminders with minimal configuration.

Option 2: Building from scratch provides complete control over trigger conditions and message content. This approach supports advanced workflows like conditional messaging based on event details or attendee lists.

Method 1: Using CommPeak Templates

CommPeak's pre-built Zapier templates offer the fastest path to automation. At the 1:15 mark in the tutorial video, you'll see how to access these time-saving templates.

Step 1: Access the Template Library

Navigate to Zapier's CommPeak integrations page. Browse the available templates and select "Send SMS for New Google Calendar Event." Click "Try It" to begin setup.

Step 2: Configure Google Calendar Trigger

Name your Zap and connect your Google account. Select the specific calendar to monitor and choose "New Event" as the trigger. Test with a recent event to confirm the connection works.

Step 3: Set Up CommPeak Action

Connect your CommPeak account and select an approved sender ID. Enter the recipient number (which can be dynamic from calendar fields) and compose your message template using available event data.

Pro tip: Use the "Customize Message" option to insert dynamic fields like {event_title} and {start_time} for personalized alerts.

Method 2: Building From Scratch

For complete control over your SMS alerts, building the Zap from scratch delivers maximum flexibility. This method supports complex conditions and custom messaging logic.

Step 1: Create New Zap

From the Zapier dashboard, click "Create Zap." Search for and select Google Calendar as your trigger app. Choose "New Event" as the trigger event type.

Step 2: Set Up Trigger Conditions

Connect your Google account and select the specific calendar to monitor. You can filter events by title keywords or other attributes if needed. Test the trigger with sample data.

Step 3: Add CommPeak Action

Search for and select CommPeak as your action app. Choose "Send SMS" as the action event. Connect your CommPeak account and configure the message details.

Advanced option: Add a filter step before the SMS action to only notify for events marked as "Important" or containing specific keywords.
