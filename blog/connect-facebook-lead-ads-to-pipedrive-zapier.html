Zapier Setup Overview

Zapier acts as the bridge between Facebook Lead Ads and Pipedrive, automatically transforming form submissions into CRM contacts. The entire setup requires just five steps:

  1. Create a new Zap with Facebook Lead Ads as the trigger
  2. Connect your Facebook account and select the specific lead form
  3. Add Pipedrive as the action app with "Create Person"
  4. Map form fields to corresponding Pipedrive fields
  5. Test and activate your automation

Unlike complex API integrations, this approach requires no coding and can be completed in under five minutes. The video at 0:45 shows the complete process start-to-finish.

Step 1: Trigger Configuration

Begin by creating a new Zap in your Zapier account. Search for "Facebook Lead Ads" as your trigger app and select "New Lead (Instant)" as the trigger event. This instant option ensures leads flow to Pipedrive within seconds of submission.

When connecting your Facebook account, ensure you're logged in as a user with admin or advertiser permissions for the page containing your lead form. Zapier will display all available pages and forms - select the exact form you want to monitor. If you manage multiple campaigns, create separate Zaps for each form to maintain clear tracking.

Pro Tip: Always test your trigger by submitting a real form entry before proceeding. This ensures Zapier can access your lead data and provides sample data for field mapping.

Step 2: Action Setup

With your trigger configured, add Pipedrive as your action app. Search for "Pipedrive" and select "Create Person" as your action. This matches each Facebook lead to a new contact record in your CRM.

Connect your Pipedrive account when prompted. Ensure you're logged into the correct Pipedrive workspace, especially if your company uses multiple instances. Zapier will ask which Pipedrive fields you want to populate - we'll map these precisely in the next step.

Step 3: Field Mapping

Field mapping determines how information flows from your Facebook form to Pipedrive. For basic contacts, always map:

  • Facebook "Full Name" → Pipedrive "Name" field
  • Facebook "Email" → Pipedrive "Email" field
  • Facebook "Phone Number" → Pipedrive "Phone" field

For advanced tracking, map additional fields like company name, job title, and any custom questions from your form. The video at 2:15 demonstrates how to handle complex field mappings, including dropdowns and multi-select questions.

Consistent field mapping prevents data fragmentation across your CRM. Take time to align all fields properly during initial setup to avoid cleanup later.

Testing and Activation

Before going live, always run a test. Zapier will attempt to create a sample contact in Pipedrive using your mapped fields. Check Pipedrive to confirm:

  1. The contact appears in the correct pipeline
  2. All mapped fields contain the expected data
  3. No critical information is missing or misplaced

If any issues arise, revisit your field mappings before activating. Once confirmed, name your Zap clearly (e.g., "Facebook Leads → Pipedrive - Main Form") and toggle it on. New leads will now flow automatically.
