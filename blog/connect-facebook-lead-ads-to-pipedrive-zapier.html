Step 1: Setting Up Your Facebook Lead Ads Trigger

Log into your Zapier account and click "Create Zap." In the trigger search bar, type "Facebook Lead Ads" and select it from the dropdown. Zapier will prompt you to connect your Facebook account - make sure you're logged into the same account that manages your ads.

Once connected, select "New Lead" as your trigger event. This tells Zapier to watch for any new form submissions on your Facebook lead ads. At 1:15 in the video tutorial, you'll see exactly where to find this option in the Zapier interface.

Important Settings:

  • Choose the correct Facebook Page that runs your lead ads
  • Select the specific lead form you want to monitor
  • Grant all requested permissions to ensure proper data access

Step 2: Connecting Pipedrive as Your Action App

After setting up the trigger, click "Add Action" and search for Pipedrive. Select "Create Deal" as your action - this will automatically generate a new deal in Pipedrive for each Facebook lead.

Zapier will ask you to connect your Pipedrive account. Use the same login credentials your sales team uses to access the CRM. Once connected, you'll map each Facebook lead field to corresponding Pipedrive deal fields:

Pro tip: Map the lead's name to the deal title, email to the contact field, and any custom questions to Pipedrive custom fields. This ensures all data appears exactly where your team expects to find it.

At 2:30 in the video, you'll see how to handle field mapping for complex forms with multiple questions. The key is matching each piece of lead data to its proper destination in Pipedrive.

Step 3: Testing and Activating Your Automation

Before going live, use Zapier's test mode to verify everything works. Submit a test lead through your Facebook form, then check Pipedrive to confirm the deal created correctly with all fields populated.

If any fields didn't transfer properly, you can edit the field mapping without starting over. Once testing succeeds, name your Zap (something like "Facebook Leads → Pipedrive") and toggle it on. Your automation is now live!

Ongoing Maintenance:

  • Check Zapier's history tab weekly for any failed transfers
  • Update field mappings if you change your Facebook lead form
  • Consider adding a Slack notification as an extra action to alert your team

Advanced Tips for Customizing Your Workflow

While the basic integration works great, you can enhance it with these pro techniques:

Lead Scoring: Add a Filter step to only create Pipedrive deals for high-quality leads. For example, you might only transfer leads who answered "Yes" to being ready to buy in the next 30 days.

Automatic Tagging: Use Pipedrive's custom fields to tag deals based on which Facebook ad campaign generated them. This helps track ROI by campaign.

Multi-Step Zaps: After creating the deal, add steps to send a welcome email or schedule a follow-up task in your team's project management tool.
