What Is Yaraa AI Sales Agent?

Yaraa AI Sales Agent is an artificial intelligence solution that acts as a 24/7 sales representative on your WordPress website. Unlike basic chatbots that only answer FAQs, Yaraa engages visitors in natural conversations about your products and services, qualifying leads even when your team is unavailable.

The powerful differentiator is its native integration with Google Sheets. Every conversation - including contact details when provided - automatically syncs to a dedicated spreadsheet, creating a ready-made leads database for your sales team.

Key benefit: Yaraa captures 72% more leads than traditional contact forms by engaging visitors in dialogue rather than requiring them to initiate contact.

How to Install from Google Marketplace

The installation process begins in the Google Workspace Marketplace, where Yaraa AI Sales Agent is available as a trusted application. This ensures secure integration with your Google account and Sheets.

To install:

  1. Search for "Yaraa AI Sales Agent" in Google Marketplace
  2. Click on the listing and select "Install"
  3. Choose the Google account where you want the Sheets integration
  4. Grant permissions for Sheet creation/editing (required for automation)

Note: The permission request specifically states what access Yaraa needs - only to create and edit Sheets related to your leads, not your entire Google Drive.

Configuring Your Agent Settings

After installation, you'll land in Yaraa's configuration dashboard where you can customize how the AI agent represents your business. These settings determine the agent's behavior and capabilities.

Essential configurations include:

  • Website URL: Where the agent will be deployed
  • Business classification: Helps tailor industry-specific responses
  • Response style: Choose between concise or elaborate answers
  • Microphone mode: Enable voice interactions if desired

Deploying on WordPress

Yaraa provides a custom WordPress plugin that handles all the technical integration with your site. The deployment process is straightforward:

  1. Download the plugin file from Yaraa's dashboard
  2. Upload to WordPress via Plugins → Add New
  3. Activate the plugin
  4. The AI agent will automatically appear on your site

No coding or design adjustments are needed - the agent integrates seamlessly with most WordPress themes. The floating chat button position and colors can be customized through Yaraa's dashboard if desired.

Setting Up Google Sheets Integration

The magic happens in the Integrations tab of Yaraa's dashboard. Here you'll find the Google Sheets connection settings that automate your lead capture.

Key features:

  • Automatically creates a "Yaraa AI Leads" spreadsheet in your Google Drive
  • Structures columns for contact info, conversation date, and full transcript
  • Provides manual sync button for immediate updates
  • Shows sync status and last update time

Pro tip: You can connect this Sheet to other automation tools like Make.com or Zapier to trigger follow-up emails or CRM updates when new leads come in.
