Programming Smart Call Handling Rules

Guardrails prevent AI from overpromising or handling sensitive topics. Configure these rules under "Advanced Settings":

1. Scope Boundaries

Example: "Only discuss IT support topics. For HR questions, say 'I'll connect you with our People team.'"

2. Sensitive Topics

Block discussions about: account details, payments, personal data. The AI will immediately transfer these calls.

3. Fallback Responses

When unsure, the agent says: "Let me check with a colleague and call you back within 30 minutes."

4. Transfer Triggers

After 2 unresolved questions or if the caller says "representative," route to a human.

Critical setting: Enable "Energy Matching" so the AI mirrors caller tone. At 11:45, it shifts from professional to empathetic when the caller gets frustrated.
