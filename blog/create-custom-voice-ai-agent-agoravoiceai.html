The 2-Minute Agent Creation Process

Creating your first voice AI agent requires just four simple steps that anyone in your team can complete:

Step 1: Login and Create New Agent

After logging in with Google, click "Create Agent" on your dashboard. Name your agent (e.g., "Acme Customer Support") and optionally upload a representative image.

Step 2: Set Basic Configuration

Choose from 12 natural-sounding voices and set a welcome message like "Hi, I'm your Acme virtual assistant. How can I help today?"

Step 3: Define Personality and Behavior

The platform provides default instructions ensuring your agent stays professional and factual. You can customize these to match your brand tone - friendly, formal, or technical.

Step 4: Upload Knowledge Documents

This is where your agent becomes uniquely valuable. Upload PDFs or documents containing your FAQs, product specs, service details - anything you want your agent to reference.

In summary: Name your agent → Choose voice → Set personality → Upload knowledge → Publish. That's the complete setup process demonstrated at 1:15 in the video tutorial.
