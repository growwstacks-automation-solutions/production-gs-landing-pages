Why Voice Renewals Outperform Email

At 2:15 in the video, the AI agent handles a customer objection seamlessly—something impossible with automated emails. While email renewal reminders see 5-15% response rates, voice AI calls achieve 85-92% engagement according to 2025 PYMNTS data. The difference comes down to three factors:

First, voice calls create immediate urgency. A human-sounding agent mentioning "your policy expires tomorrow" triggers action better than a subject line. Second, the conversation format allows for real-time Q&A—customers can ask about payment options or coverage details. Third, verification happens naturally during the call, reducing fraud risk.

Key insight: Customers who verify their identity during an AI call complete renewals 63% faster than those going through email links, based on a study by Deloitte Digital.

Creating Your First AI Agent

At 1:10, the tutorial demonstrates the agent creation flow. You'll select "Standard Prompt Agent" as your base template—this handles most outbound call scenarios. The three critical configuration areas are:

  • Agent Name: Internal reference only (e.g., "Policy Renewal - Auto")
  • Description: Plain-English explanation of the agent's purpose
  • Greeting Message: First thing customers hear (keep under 10 words)

The example uses a policy renewal scenario, but these same steps work for appointment reminders, membership renewals, or subscription saves. The key is writing a description that clearly states:

1) What triggers the call 2) What verification is needed 3) The desired outcome

Using Dynamic Variables for Personalization

At 2:00, the tutorial shows how dynamic variables make each call unique. For the policy renewal agent, these four variables create a personalized experience:

  1. {policy_name} - The specific policy being renewed
  2. {customer_name} - Pulled from your CRM
  3. {date_of_birth} - Used for verification
  4. {car_model} - Adds conversational specificity

When testing, you can input sample values directly. In production, these variables automatically populate from your data source. The AI weaves them naturally into conversation—at 3:20, you'll hear how it uses the car model to build rapport before discussing renewal.
