Getting Started — Completely Free

The platform (called Verb) offers a generous free tier that includes unlimited image generation, basic AI model access, and 24/7 hosting for your chatbot. You can sign up instantly using Discord or Google — no credit card required.

After logging in, you'll see a clean dashboard where all your AI "verbs" (their term for chatbots) will appear. The free plan shows zero credits initially, but don't worry — credits are only needed for premium features. The core chatbot functionality works without any payment.

Before creating your first verb, explore the public gallery to see examples others have made. You can chat with these pre-made verbs to understand the level of personality and customization possible. Notice how each has distinct mannerisms, knowledge areas, and response styles.

Step-by-Step: Creating Your AI "Verb"

Click "Create Verb" to begin building your custom AI assistant. The process is divided into four intuitive steps that guide you through crafting a fully personalized chatbot:

Step 1: Basic Profile

Upload a banner and profile picture for your verb. These visuals help give your AI personality before anyone even chats with it. Then set the name, age, birth date (yes, your AI can have a birthday!), and relevant tags.

Step 2: Personality Foundation

Write the core personality description that defines how your verb thinks and behaves. This includes traits, values, worldview, backstory, and important life experiences. The more detail you provide here, the more consistent and believable your AI will be.

Step 3: Preferences & Behaviors

Define what your verb likes/dislikes, hobbies, activities, and specific beliefs. These details make conversations feel natural when users ask about preferences or opinions.

Step 4: System Configuration

Set technical parameters like response style (short messages vs. detailed paragraphs), whether it can use slang, and special capabilities like web search or image generation.

Discord Integration in Minutes

Connecting your custom AI to Discord takes just a few simple steps:

  1. Create a new application in the Discord Developer Portal
  2. Copy your bot token from the "Bot" tab
  3. Enable all three privileged gateway intents
  4. Paste the token into Verb's Discord configuration
  5. Use the generated invite link to add your bot to servers

Once connected, your AI will be online 24/7 with no additional hosting required. Users can interact with it through direct messages or in any server channels you specify. The platform handles all the backend infrastructure seamlessly.
