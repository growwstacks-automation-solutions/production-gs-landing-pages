Why Mobile Deployment Changes Everything

Most AI agents never fulfill their potential because they're trapped on computers. The moment you deploy them to your iPhone, they transform from passive tools into active assistants that work for you throughout your day.

Consider these three game-changing advantages of mobile deployment:

1. Contextual availability: Your phone goes everywhere with you - the grocery store, the gym, client meetings. Mobile-deployed agents can act on real-world triggers as they happen.

At 4:32 in the video, you'll see how a simple "quick capture" button on the home screen lets me record business ideas the moment they occur - with automatic categorization and filing to my second brain system.

2. Push notification power: Critical alerts reach you immediately, not when you happen to check your computer. The lead qualification example later in this article demonstrates how this can transform response times.

3. Voice integration: Speaking to your agents while driving, walking, or cooking creates new workflow possibilities that keyboard-bound interfaces can't match.

Connecting Your First Agent

Follow these steps to deploy an N8N agent to your iPhone (full walkthrough at 10:45):

Step 1: Create Agent in LERDI

From the web dashboard:

  1. Click "New Agent"
  2. Name your agent (e.g., "Content Generator")
  3. Select webhook as the connection type
  4. Add starter prompts (optional but recommended)

Step 2: Configure N8N Workflow

The video shows a complete blueprint at 12:30. Key components:

  • Webhook trigger to receive mobile inputs
  • AI processing node (GPT, Claude, etc.)
  • HTTP request to send responses back to LERDI

Step 3: Test the Connection

Use the test event feature (14:20) to verify:

  1. Mobile inputs reach your workflow
  2. Responses appear in the LERDI chat
  3. Typing indicators work properly
