Three-Step Deployment Process

The demo reveals how any business can deploy a fully-trained AI agent in less time than a coffee break. The secret lies in Belrroni's streamlined three-step workflow:

  1. Onboarding: Teach your agent about your business
  2. Scheduler: Connect your calendar system
  3. Deployer: Configure call handling and go live

This approach eliminates the months-long development cycles of traditional AI implementations. At the 1:15 mark in the video, you'll see the entire setup completed before the agent demonstrates its property knowledge.

Step 1: The Onboarding Tab

The magic begins with uploading your listings file—whether properties, services, or inventory. This trains the AI to speak knowledgeably about your offerings using your exact terminology and specifications.

In the demo, the agent references specific details about the Hollywood Hills property ("four bedrooms," "expansive outdoor living spaces") because it learned from the uploaded listing. The training happens instantly—no waiting for machine learning models to retrain overnight.

Pro Tip: Include photos and videos in your training data. The AI references these visual assets during calls to enhance descriptions, creating a multimedia experience for callers.

Step 2: Scheduler Integration

Calendar connectivity transforms the AI from an information source to a productivity powerhouse. The demo shows seamless Google Calendar integration, but Belrroni supports all major platforms.

Once connected, your agent can view real-time availability, book appointments, send calendar invites, and even handle rescheduling—all while maintaining natural conversation flow. Notice at 2:30 how the agent smoothly transitions from property details to scheduling options.

Step 3: Deployment Options

The final step tailors the agent to your industry and call handling needs. Healthcare practices might prioritize HIPAA compliance and emergency transfers, while real estate offices focus on showing coordination.

Belrroni's industry presets (visible at 3:45 in the demo) pre-configure best practices for your vertical. The system then provides a dedicated phone number or integrates with your existing lines—your choice.

24/7 Availability: Unlike human staff, your Belrroni agent works holidays, weekends, and overnight without overtime pay, capturing leads whenever they call.
