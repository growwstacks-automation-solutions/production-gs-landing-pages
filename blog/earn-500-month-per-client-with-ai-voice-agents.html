Step 2: Select Voice and Language

Choose from dozens of natural-sounding voices across different genders, accents, and languages. The platform even lets you preview each voice before selecting.

Step 3: Configure Call Flow

Set up the basic conversation path - greeting, information collection, and conclusion. The system provides templates you can customize for different industries.

Configure email alerts that send call transcripts to the business owner immediately after each call completes.
