Three Core AI Voice Agent Systems

Most businesses know they need AI voice agents - but building them from scratch requires technical expertise in prompt engineering, API integrations, and conversation design. The three systems every AI voice agency should offer are:

1. Receptionist System: Handles inbound calls, answers FAQs, qualifies leads, and schedules appointments - available 24/7 with natural conversation flow.

2. Speed-to-Lead: Instantly calls leads who submit forms (website or Facebook ads) within seconds, dramatically increasing conversion rates.

3. Lead Reactivation: Automatically contacts old leads with personalized follow-ups at optimal times, bringing cold leads back into your pipeline.

Key Insight: Agencies using these three systems report 3-5x more conversions compared to manual processes, while reducing labor costs by 60-80%.
