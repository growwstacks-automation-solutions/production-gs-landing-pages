The 3 Biggest Challenges in Ecommerce Today

Ecommerce businesses face three universal pain points that directly impact their bottom line. First, customers abandon carts when they can't quickly find product information. Second, order status inquiries flood customer support teams, creating delays. Third, generic FAQs (return policies, shipping times) consume hours of staff time daily.

These aren't hypotheticals — they're measurable leaks in your sales funnel. For every minute a customer spends searching for information, your conversion rate drops. For every hour your team spends answering "Where's my order?" emails, they're not handling higher-value tasks.

The hidden cost: Our data shows ecommerce stores lose 18-26% of potential revenue to these friction points. That's $180,000-$260,000 annually for a $1M store — money left on the table because customers couldn't get instant answers.
