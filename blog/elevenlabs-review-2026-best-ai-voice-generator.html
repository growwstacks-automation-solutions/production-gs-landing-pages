Current Limitations

While impressive, ElevenLabs isn't perfect:

Emotional Control: While better than competitors, fine-tuning emotional delivery still requires trial and error. The "happy" setting might sound forced compared to a genuine human performance.

Ethical Concerns: The same technology enabling creative projects could potentially be misused for deepfake audio. ElevenLabs has implemented safeguards but the risk remains.

Pro Tip: For best results with long scripts, break them into 10-15 minute segments and generate each separately using consistent voice settings.
