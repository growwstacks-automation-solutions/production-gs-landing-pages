Why Google Maps is a Goldmine for Lead Generation

Every local business you see on Google Maps has already verified their information with Google. This makes it one of the most reliable sources for business leads - yet most companies still search manually, wasting hours copying and pasting contact details.

The breakthrough comes from combining Google Maps' verified business data with AI-powered automation. At 2:15 in the video, you'll see how the workflow extracts complete records in seconds - information that would take 15-20 minutes to find manually for each business.

Key advantage: Google Maps leads have 3-5x higher response rates than purchased lists because you're contacting businesses that actually exist and have publicly shared their information.

The Complete Lead Extraction Workflow

This n8n automation connects four powerful services to create an end-to-end lead generation system:

  1. Appify - Extracts raw business data from Google Maps based on your search criteria
  2. OpenAI - Cleans and standardizes the scraped data for consistency
  3. Google Sheets - Organizes the final lead database in a shareable format
  4. AnyMail Finder - Verifies email addresses to maximize deliverability

The workflow handles everything from the initial search to having a ready-to-use contact list - all without writing code or manually transferring data between systems.

Step 2: Connecting Appify for Google Maps Scraping

Appify acts as the bridge between n8n and Google Maps. At 6:18 in the tutorial, you'll learn how to:

  • Business type (e.g., "wedding planners")

  • Additional filters like rating or years in business
