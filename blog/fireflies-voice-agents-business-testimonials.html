Unlike simple chatbots, voice agents create human-like dialogue experiences. How to Set Up a Voice Agent

The demo walks through creating a testimonial agent in real-time (starting at 15:30). Here's the streamlined process:

Step 1: Define Your Questions

Input 3-5 questions that will guide the conversation. The platform suggests defaults like "What made you decide to try our service?" but you can customize these to your business needs.

Step 2: Set Conversation Style

Add instructions like "Encourage storytelling" or "Ask leading questions" to shape the agent's interaction style. These nuances help capture more authentic responses.

Step 3: Configure Settings

Set session duration (max 10 minutes), add your logo, and include any background information the agent should reference during conversations.

Step 4: Share the Link

Distribute your unique agent link via email, text, or embed it in your website. Customers can access it anytime to record their testimonial.

In summary: Setup takes under 10 minutes. Focus on crafting questions that elicit emotional, story-driven responses rather than simple yes/no answers.

Pricing & Credit System Explained

Fireflies operates on a credit system for voice agents (discussed at 21:45):

  • 100 free credits included with paid plans ($96/year)
  • 1 credit = 1 minute of conversation
  • Additional credits: $5/50 or $20/200

This translates to approximately $1 per 5-minute testimonial after the free trial. Compared to the 30-60 minutes typically spent manually collecting each testimonial, the ROI becomes clear for businesses needing regular customer feedback.

Budget tip: Set session duration limits (default 10 minutes) to conserve credits. Most testimonials only need 3-5 minutes.
