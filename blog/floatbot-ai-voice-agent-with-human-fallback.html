Seamless Context Transfer to Humans

The biggest frustration in hybrid AI/human calls occurs when customers must repeat their information after being transferred. Floatbot.AI eliminates this by maintaining complete call context that transfers instantly to human agents.

The human agent receives this as a summarized dashboard, allowing them to pick up exactly where the AI left off.
