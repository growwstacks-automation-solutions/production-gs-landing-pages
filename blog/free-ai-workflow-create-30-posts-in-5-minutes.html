Workflow Overview

This automation transforms YouTube videos into multiple content formats through three simple steps:

Step 1: Video to Text

A Google Sheet collects YouTube URLs, then connects to RapidAPI's video transcription service to extract the full text.

Step 2: AI Content Transformation

Google Gemini (using the 1.5 Flash model) processes the transcript with custom formatting instructions to create platform-specific content.

Step 3: Multi-Format Output

The final content gets saved to Google Docs as:

  • LinkedIn long-form posts
  • Twitter/X threads
  • Email newsletter content
  • Social media captions

Pro Tip: Add "rewrite at a 9th grade reading level" to your Gemini prompt if your audience prefers simpler language.

Step 1: YouTube to Text

The workflow begins with a simple Google Sheet where you paste YouTube video URLs. Make.com's Google Sheets module watches for new entries and triggers the automation.

Key configuration points:

  1. Spreadsheet Setup: Name your sheet "Content Repurposer" and ensure it has a header row
  2. API Connection: Uses RapidAPI's free tier video transcription service
  3. Authentication: Requires your RapidAPI key in the HTTP request headers

The text aggregator module then combines all transcript segments into a single, clean text block ready for AI processing.

Step 2: AI Content Transformation

Google Gemini 1.5 Flash transforms the raw transcript into polished content using these prompt engineering techniques:

Critical formatting instruction: "Output must include: 1) LinkedIn post with H1 headline, 3 key takeaways, and CTA 2) Twitter thread with 5 tweets 3) Newsletter with intro, 3 sections, and conclusion."

The system message to Gemini includes:

  • Brand voice guidelines
  • Platform-specific length requirements
  • Structural formatting rules
  • CTA placement instructions

This ensures consistent quality across all generated content without manual editing.

Step 3: Output Formats

The final content gets organized in Google Docs with clear section headers:

LinkedIn Post

Formatted with engaging headline, body paragraphs broken into digestible sections, and strategic call-to-action. Average length: 800-1200 words.

Twitter Thread

Series of 5-7 connected tweets with logical flow, proper threading indicators, and hashtag optimization.

Newsletter

Complete email structure with personalized greeting, value-packed body content, and professional sign-off.

Time saved: What normally takes 6-8 hours of manual work now happens automatically in 5 minutes.
