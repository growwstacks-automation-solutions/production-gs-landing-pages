The Lead Capture Problem

Manual lead capture creates three critical business problems: lost responses, data entry errors, and delayed follow-ups. Studies show that 67% of leads are mishandled when transferred manually between systems.

The solution is automation that instantly moves form submissions to your CRM. This Typeform-Zapier-Notion workflow eliminates:

  • Spreadsheet exports/imports
  • Copy-paste errors
  • Forgotten follow-ups

Key benefit: Leads enter your sales pipeline 8x faster compared to manual processing, with zero transcription mistakes.

Zapier Automation Steps

Step 1: Create a New Zap

Search "Typeform to Notion" in Zapier's template library. Even if the exact template isn't perfect, starting with any similar workflow saves setup time.

Step 2: Configure the Trigger

Select "Typeform" as the trigger app and "New Submission" as the trigger event. Connect your Typeform account and select your specific form.

Step 3: Set Up the Action

Choose "Notion" as the action app and "Create Database Item" as the action event. Connect your Notion account and select the target database.

Critical step: Use your test submission to verify field mapping before activating the automation.

Field Mapping Strategies
