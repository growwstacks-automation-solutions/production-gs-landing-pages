The Problem With Manual Follow-Ups

Sales teams waste countless hours manually sending meeting reminders and follow-up emails. Research shows that 30-40% of scheduled meetings get missed because prospects forget or deprioritize them. Meanwhile, sales reps spend 2-3 hours per week just tracking meeting confirmations.

The solution? Automated nurturing sequences that build trust while saving time. Studies show automated sequences can increase meeting attendance by 30-50% compared to manual follow-ups.

Key insight: Prospects who receive 3+ touchpoints before a meeting are 2.5x more likely to attend. Automated nurturing provides these touchpoints without manual work.

How The Nurturing System Works

This Make.com automation creates a complete nurturing sequence using three timed emails:

  1. 3 Days Before: Sends a testimonial video to build credibility
  2. 1 Day Before: Sends a personalized reminder with meeting time
  3. Day Of Meeting: Sends the meeting link with clear instructions

The entire system runs on a free Make.com account and uses Google Sheets as a lightweight CRM. No expensive software required.

Setting Up Your Free CRM

The foundation is a simple Google Sheet with these columns:

  • Name: Prospect's full name
  • Email: Contact email address
  • Status: Pipeline stage (demo scheduled, proposal sent, etc.)
  • DateTime: Scheduled meeting date and time

Make.com connects to this sheet to pull leads who need nurturing. The integration takes just 2 minutes to set up:

  1. Create a new Google Sheet with the columns above
  2. Connect Make.com to your Google account
  3. Add the "Google Sheets - Search Rows" module

That's it - your free CRM is ready. No complex setup or expensive subscriptions required.

3 Days Before Automation

This workflow sends a valuable piece of content (like a testimonial video) to build credibility before the meeting.

Here's how it works:

  1. Searches your CRM for leads with "demo" status
  2. Filters for meetings happening exactly 3 days from today
  3. Sends a personalized email with embedded video

Pro tip: Use AI to generate personalized email copy that references the prospect's name and meeting date. The video should showcase client success stories relevant to their needs.

1 Day Before Automation

This reminder email keeps your meeting top-of-mind without being pushy.

The workflow:

  1. Identifies meetings happening tomorrow
  2. Extracts just the first name from your CRM (no awkward "Hi [Full Name]")
  3. Sends a concise reminder with meeting time

Example template:

"Hi [First Name], quick reminder we have a meeting tomorrow at [Time]. Looking forward to discussing how we can help with [Their Pain Point]."

This simple touchpoint significantly reduces no-shows while maintaining professionalism.

Day of Meeting Automation

The final email removes all friction by providing the meeting link right when they need it.

Key features:

  • Sends morning of the meeting
  • Includes one-click join button
  • Reiterates meeting time
  • Optional: Adds calendar event attachment

This eliminates last-minute "Where's the link?" emails and ensures prospects join on time.

Measuring Results

After implementing this system, track these key metrics:

  • Meeting Attendance Rate: Should increase by 30-50%
  • Email Open Rates: Aim for 60%+ across all three emails
  • Time Saved: Typically 2-3 hours per week per salesperson

To track performance:

  1. Add a "Showed Up?" column to your Google Sheet
  2. Update manually after each meeting
  3. Calculate weekly/monthly attendance rates

Important: The system pays for itself when it saves just 2-3 no-shows per month. For teams with 10+ weekly meetings, that's $5,000+ in recovered revenue monthly.
