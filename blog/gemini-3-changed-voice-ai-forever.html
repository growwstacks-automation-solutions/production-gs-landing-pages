The DRRO Method: Demo → ROI → Reach-out → Offer

The most effective Voice AI implementations follow the DRRO framework. This method bridges the gap between technical possibility and client belief by making the value tangible:

Step 1: Demo

Build a branded voice-enabled demo tailored to the prospect's industry. For a real estate wholesaler, this means an AI that asks property-specific questions ("Is the house in good shape or needs work?") rather than generic prompts.

Step 2: ROI

Calculate exactly how much money they're losing without your solution. Gemini 3 auto-generates calculators showing that missing 42% of calls from 5,000 mailers could mean $945,000 in lost annual revenue.

Step 3: Reach-out

Send the interactive demo via email or text. Include a Loom video walking through the presentation (pro tip at 9:15 in the video). This lets the prospect experience the AI before your sales call.

Step 4: Offer

Present your full solution with the demo as proof. Gemini-created demos increase conversion rates by 3-5x because clients have already "felt" the technology working for their specific use case.

Building Your First Gemini 3 Demo (Step-by-Step)

Creating a Voice AI demo with Gemini 3 requires no technical skills. Here's the exact process shown at 6:48 in the video:

Step 1: Access Google AI Studio

Navigate to ai.dev and select "Build and Start". This gives you access to Gemini 3's full demo creation capabilities.

Step 2: Describe Your Ideal Client Scenario

Use natural language like: "Build an interactive presentation where I'm pitching Voice AI services to a real estate wholesaler. Include an ROI calculator showing lost revenue from missed calls and a booking link for consultations."

Step 3: Customize the Demo

Gemini generates slides with voice interaction points. Refine these by adding specific qualifying questions relevant to the industry (property condition, ideal closing timeline).

Step 4: Deploy and Share

Click "Deploy App" to get a shareable link. Send this to prospects alongside a short Loom video explaining how it works.

Pro Tip: Record yourself going through the demo slides on Loom (as shown at 9:15). This personal touch increases engagement by 72%.
