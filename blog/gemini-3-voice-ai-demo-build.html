Step 2: Automated Brand Research

Adding Branded Custom Domains

For maximum impact, deploy demos on subdomains that match your prospect's branding (e.g., demo.bostonplumbing.com). Here's how:

Step 1: Create CNAME Record

In your DNS provider (Cloudflare, GoDaddy, etc.), add a CNAME record pointing your subdomain to ghss.googlehosted.com

Step 2: Map Domain in Google Cloud

Navigate to Domain Mappings in your Google Cloud project and add the subdomain. SSL certificates provision automatically within 48 hours.
