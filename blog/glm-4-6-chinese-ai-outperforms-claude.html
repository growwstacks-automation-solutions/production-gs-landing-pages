GLM 4.6 Technical Specifications

Chinese AI company Z.AI has released GLM 4.6 - a 355 billion parameter model that challenges the dominance of Western AI tools at a fraction of the price. With a 200k token context window (handling ~25,000 lines of code simultaneously) and pricing at just $0.60 per million input tokens (compared to Claude's $3), it represents potentially the best value in AI today.

The multimodal 4.6V version adds native image processing, allowing it to analyze screenshots and recreate them as functional HTML/CSS. Unlike chatbots, GLM 4.6 functions as an active agent during inference - capable of searching the web, executing code, calling APIs, and reading files automatically to complete complex tasks.

Key differentiator: Where Claude charges $200/month for professional access, GLM 4.6's equivalent plan costs just $3/month - allowing 23-64x more usage for the same annual budget while delivering comparable quality on most tasks.

Without breaking the existing game, GLM 4.6 understood the context and enhanced the codebase flawlessly - producing little stars that appeared randomly and temporarily increased snake speed when collected.

Production-ready quality: The generated code included proper comments, organized structure, and responsive design - equivalent to what a professional developer might produce in 1-2 hours of work.
