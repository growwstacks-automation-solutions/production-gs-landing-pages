Step 1: Enabling the Outbound Calling Feature

GoHighLevel's outbound calling isn't enabled by default - you'll need to activate it in Labs first. Here's how:

Step 1: Access Agency View

From your subaccount dashboard, click your profile icon and select "Agency View". This gives you access to account-wide settings.

Step 2: Navigate to Labs

Go to Settings → Labs. This is where GoHighLevel rolls out beta features before full release.

Step 3: Search for Voice AI

Type "voice" in the search bar and locate "Voice AI Outbound Calling". Click "Activate Feature" and select which subaccounts should have access.

Important: You must refresh your browser after activation for the changes to take effect. The outbound calling option will then appear in your AI Agents → Voice AI section.

The Critical Verification Process

Before making any outbound calls, GoHighLevel requires business verification to comply with telecom regulations. This isn't just bureaucracy - it protects your account from being flagged as spam.

The verification process requires:

  • Business country/region
  • Government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  • Business documentation if calling on behalf of clients

Client responsibility: Never submit your personal ID for client accounts. Each business must verify themselves - doing it for them violates terms and puts your agency at risk. At 2:30 in the video, we show exactly how to guide clients through verification safely.

Creating Compliant Opt-In Language

TCPA compliance requires clear opt-in language for any automated calls. GoHighLevel won't activate outbound calling until your forms include this.

Generating Compliant Text

Use ChatGPT with this prompt: "I need opt-in TOS for my AI voice agent that will call contacts after form submission. Include compliance for [your country] and clear consent language."

Adding to Forms

Paste the generated text into your form's terms section. GoHighLevel will automatically check for compatibility and flag any missing requirements.

Pro tip: Update all lead capture forms, not just the ones triggering calls. This future-proofs your compliance as you expand automation. At 4:15 in the video, we demonstrate the exact update process with real examples.

Building Your First Outbound Workflow

With verification and compliance handled, you're ready to create your first outbound calling automation.

Step 1: Create New Workflow

Navigate to Automations → Workflows and create a new workflow from scratch. Name it clearly like "AI Outbound - Form Follow-Up".

Step 2: Set Trigger

Add a trigger like "Form Submitted" and select the form you updated with opt-in language. This ensures only consented contacts receive calls.

Step 3: Add Voice AI Action

Search for "Voice AI Outbound Call" in actions. Select your verified phone number and configure call parameters like retry rules.

Timing matters: Set the call to trigger immediately or within 1 minute of form submission. Delaying longer than 5 minutes significantly reduces conversion rates.
