Creating your AI voice agent begins in the Voice AI section of GoHighLevel. The tutorial uses a fencing business example named "Fencing 123" with the agent named "Scarlet" to demonstrate the complete configuration process.

Voice & Behavior Parameters

Advanced settings make the difference between a robotic answering service and a natural-sounding virtual receptionist. The tutorial demonstrates several crucial adjustments:

Conversation Settings

  • Call duration limit: Set to 7-8 minutes to prevent endless loops
  • Idle frequency: 4 seconds of silence triggers a follow-up
  • Interruption sensitivity: Increased for natural conversation flow

Voice Enhancements

  • Response speed: Faster setting for natural pacing
  • Background noise: Coffee shop ambiance added to avoid "eerie silence"
  • Speaking rate: Slightly increased from default

Pro Tip: The background noise feature (demonstrated at 5:45 in the video) significantly improves call quality by eliminating the unnatural silence between responses that makes many AI agents sound robotic.

Knowledge Base Creation

Your AI agent's effectiveness depends entirely on the quality of its knowledge base. The tutorial shows two methods to build this:

Web Crawler Method

For businesses with existing websites, the web crawler can extract relevant information automatically. The demonstration uses a Google Sheet containing structured business information that's processed in about 5 minutes.

Alternative Method

Businesses without websites can create a knowledge base using GoHighLevel's built-in page builder, publishing it to a temporary subdomain for the AI to reference.

The knowledge base should include:

  • Service areas
  • Common customer questions

System Prompt Configuration

The system prompt acts as the "brain" of your AI agent, determining how it interprets and responds to inquiries. The tutorial provides a complete prompt template that includes:

  • Business type (fencing in the example)
  • Company mission statement
  • Service offerings
  • Desired agent personality (friendly, upbeat, caring)
  • Conversational tone (warm, professional)
  • Target emotional impact (making customers feel confident)

At 9:30 in the video, you can see the exact prompt being customized for the fencing business example before being pasted into the system prompt field in advanced mode.

Critical Note: Advanced mode must be selected before creating the agent, as this setting cannot be changed later. The tutorial emphasizes this at 7:15 to prevent having to recreate agents.
