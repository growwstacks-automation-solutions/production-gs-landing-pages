Crafting the Killer Demo Prompt

The secret sauce lies in the advanced prompt (13:45 in video). Unlike basic chatbots, your GoHighLevel agent uses a structured role-play script that:

  • Introduces itself naturally
  • Requests prospect information politely
  • Adapts to the business type dynamically
  • Handles objections gracefully

The demo prompt follows this proven structure:

1. Hook: "Hi, I'm [Name], the friendly AI assistant at [Business]. Can I show you a quick demo?"
2. Qualification: Collect name, business details naturally
3. Role-play: "Now I'll act as your AI assistant..."
4. Value demonstration: Handle sample customer interaction
5. Transition: "What stood out most during our demo?"
