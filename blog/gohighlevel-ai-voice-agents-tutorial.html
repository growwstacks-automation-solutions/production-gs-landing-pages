Call Duration Optimization

Long calls drain your budget without improving conversions. Through analyzing thousands of calls, we've found the sweet spot is 60-90 seconds - enough time to gather essential information without frustrating callers.

Key settings to adjust:

Critical Setting: Enable automatic call termination at 2 minutes to prevent runaway conversations that increase costs without adding value.

Monitor your call analytics weekly to identify ideal duration for your specific use case. Appointment booking calls typically run longer than simple information requests, so customize based on your workflow.

Effective Prompt Engineering

Overly complex prompts waste money and confuse callers. The most effective prompts read like you're explaining the task to a fifth grader - simple, direct language that focuses on the essential information needed.

Structure your prompts with:

  1. Clear role definition ("You are a receptionist for...")
  2. Specific objectives ("Your goal is to...")
  3. Simple instructions ("Ask for... then...")
  4. Natural transition phrases

Avoid industry jargon and keep responses concise. Well-structured prompts can reduce token usage by 30-40%, directly lowering your costs while improving caller experience.

Calendar Integration

Nothing frustrates callers more than being told available times that don't match reality. Proper calendar integration ensures your AI agent always offers accurate availability.

Implementation steps:

  1. Connect your calendar in the settings
  2. Set operating hours
  3. Define appointment types/durations
  4. Test with multiple date scenarios

The video shows a live demo at 7:15 where the AI agent checks real calendar availability and books an appointment while the caller is still on the line - no follow-up emails or callbacks needed.

Background Sound Strategies

Complete silence feels unnatural on calls, while loud distractions frustrate callers. The right background noise creates a professional atmosphere that puts callers at ease.

Top background choices:

  • Office ambiance for professional services
  • Coffee shop sounds for relaxed environments
  • Very light music for retail/customer service

Adjust volume to 20-30% - audible but not distracting. The subtle cues trick the brain into perceiving a human environment, increasing caller comfort and conversion rates.

Managing Caller Interruptions

Natural conversation includes interruptions, but poorly handled breaks destroy call flow. The interruption sensitivity setting (default 75%) determines how quickly your AI agent responds when callers speak over it.

Adjust based on your industry:

  • 50-60% for fast-paced businesses
  • 70-75% for most service industries
  • 80%+ for slower, deliberate conversations

Monitor call recordings for awkward pauses or cut-off responses, then fine-tune accordingly. The right setting makes conversations feel natural without frustrating either party.
