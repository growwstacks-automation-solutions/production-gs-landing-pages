Step-by-Step Inbound Agent Setup

Creating a high-converting inbound agent starts in GoHighLevel's AI Agents section. Navigate to Voice AI > Create Agent (not Browse Marketplace). The initial setup requires:

Step 1: Basic Configuration

  • Agent Name: Use something recognizable like "Rebecca - [Business Name]"
  • Business Name: Must match your caller ID for consistency
  • Phone Line: Select the number calls will route through
  • Voice Selection: GoHighLevel partners with ElevenLabs for the most natural voices available

Step 2: Advanced Settings Most Miss

Click "Advanced Settings" to access critical configurations most businesses overlook:

  • Interruption Sensitivity: Set to High (people naturally interrupt)
  • Background Sounds: Enable office/coffee shop ambiance to prevent "digital silence"
  • Voice Temperature: Adjust expressiveness based on your industry (higher for creative fields)

6 Actions That Convert (Including Appointment Booking)

GoHighLevel voice agents support six powerful actions that automate your sales process. Most businesses only use 1-2, missing key conversion opportunities.

Seamlessly transfer to a human when needed (e.g., complex questions). Set triggers like "I want to speak to someone."

2. Workflow Trigger

3. SMS Follow-Up

4. Contact Field Update

Outbound Calling Setup & Compliance

Outbound calling (you initiating calls) requires additional verification but unlocks powerful lead follow-up. The process:

Step 1: Enable Outbound in Labs

Navigate to Agency View > Settings > Labs > Sub Account > Voice. Enable "Outbound Calling."

Step 2: Business Owner Verification

Critical: Upload the business owner's government ID (not yours). This prevents compliance issues.

Step 3: Configure Opt-In Language

Use our template (available below) or generate compliant language specifying:

  • Purpose of calls
  • Opt-out instructions
  • Business information

Step 4: Create Outbound Workflow

Build automations that trigger calls when:

  1. Forms are submitted
  2. Leads enter specific pipelines
  3. Follow-up reminders trigger
