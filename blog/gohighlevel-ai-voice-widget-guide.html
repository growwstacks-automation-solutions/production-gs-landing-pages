Step-by-Step Setup Process

The setup process is surprisingly simple, but there are a few critical steps many users miss that affect performance. Here's the exact sequence:

Step 1: Access Chat Widgets

Navigate to Sites → Chat Widgets in your GoHighLevel dashboard. This is where all your communication tools are managed.

Step 2: Create New Widget

Click "New" and select "Voice AI" from the options. If this option is grayed out, you may need to upgrade your AI voice agents (a quick process our team can assist with).

Step 3: Basic Configuration

Name your widget (what visitors will see), select your AI voice agent (which powers the conversations), and choose basic styling options like color and icon.

Avoiding the #1 Naming Mistake

Most confusion with voice widgets stems from the three separate naming components:

  1. Widget Name: What appears to visitors on your website (e.g., "Rebecca" in the demo)
  2. AI Agent Name: The underlying conversation intelligence (e.g., "Olivia" in the demo)
  3. Voice Name: The synthetic voice used (e.g., "Emily" from the voice library)

These can all be different, which causes confusion when users expect changing one to affect the others. The key is to:

  • Make the widget name customer-facing and friendly
  • Use descriptive names for your AI agents based on function
  • Test multiple voices to find the most natural fit

Pro Customization Options
