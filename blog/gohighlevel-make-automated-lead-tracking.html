The Lead Leakage Problem

Manual lead tracking creates three critical problems for businesses: 1) Leads fall through the cracks when sales teams forget to log them, 2) Follow-up emails get delayed or forgotten entirely, and 3) Pipeline data becomes inconsistent across teams. Research shows that 23% of leads are never followed up with when using manual processes.

The solution requires eliminating human intervention at three key points: initial capture, data logging, and first contact. That's where the GoHighLevel + Make.com automation shines. At 1:15 in the video, you'll see how the system handles all three automatically.

Key insight: Manual processes introduce a 6-48 hour delay between lead capture and first contact. This automation reduces that to under 60 seconds while eliminating 100% of tracking errors.

The Dual-Trigger System

Most automation systems fail when leads come from multiple sources or get moved between pipeline stages. This solution uses two triggers in GoHighLevel to ensure complete coverage:

1. Opportunity Created Trigger

Captures new leads regardless of how they enter your system - whether through web forms, manual entry, or imports. This acts as your safety net for all new contacts.

2. Opportunity Changed Trigger

Activates when existing leads move into your designated "New Leads" pipeline stage. This catches leads that might have been created in another stage (like "Cold" or "Uninterested") but later qualify as new opportunities.

Implementation tip: Always set filters on both triggers to only process leads in your designated "New Leads" stage. This prevents duplicate processing.

Real-Time Data Flow

The magic happens in the seamless connection between GoHighLevel and Make.com. When either trigger fires in GoHighLevel, it instantly sends lead data (name, email, phone, and pipeline stage) via webhook to Make.com.

Make.com then performs two critical actions simultaneously:

  1. Data logging: Adds a new row to your Google Sheet with all lead details
  2. Communication: Triggers a personalized welcome email using the lead's information

At 2:30 in the video, you can see the webhook configuration that makes this real-time transfer possible. The entire process from lead capture to logged data and sent email happens in under 30 seconds.
