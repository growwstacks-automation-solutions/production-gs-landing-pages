TCPA-Compliant AI Caller Setup

The biggest hurdle with automated calling isn't technology - it's compliance. GoHighLevel's outbound AI caller includes built-in TCPA compliance features, but you must configure them properly. At 2:15 in the video, you'll see the three disclaimer options that identify your call as automated while maintaining a natural flow.

The "conversational" disclaimer sounds most natural but runs slightly longer, while the "concise" option gets straight to the point. Your choice depends on your industry and typical customer expectations. Real estate investors might prefer brevity, while healthcare providers may want the more detailed version.

Compliance checkpoint: Always include 1) Automated call identification, 2) Your business name, 3) Purpose of the call, and 4) Contact information. GoHighLevel's templates include all required elements, but you should still consult legal counsel for your specific use case.

Agent Configuration Essentials

Creating an effective AI caller starts with proper agent setup. Navigate to Agents → Outbound → Create Agent in your GoHighLevel dashboard. The name should clearly identify the agent's purpose since you'll likely create multiple variations for different campaigns or lead sources.

At 3:40 in the tutorial, you'll see the critical "Intent Message" field. This is where you specify why you're calling - for example, "I'm calling about your recent request for a cash home offer" or "Following up on your roofing quote request." Keep this under 10 seconds for maximum impact.

  • Business Name: Use your DBA or recognizable brand
  • Language/Voice: Match your target demographic
  • Caller ID: Must be your A2P-approved toll-free number
  • Greeting Length: Ideal 8-12 second range

Form Integration & Trigger Automation

The real power comes from triggering calls instantly after form submission. At 7:20 in the video, you'll see how to connect your AI caller to any GoHighLevel form. The key is enabling "Voice" in your form settings and selecting your configured AI agent.

For maximum compliance, include a clear disclosure on your form that by submitting, the lead consents to receive calls (including automated calls) about their inquiry. This creates an established business relationship under TCPA rules for the next 90 days.

Pro Tip: Create a separate AI caller agent for each major lead source (Facebook ads, organic search, etc.) so you can track performance by channel and tailor conversations accordingly.

Voice & Conversation Optimization

While the AI voices are remarkably natural, you'll get better results by optimizing your script. At 4:55 in the tutorial, notice how the example gets straight to the point: "Hi [Name], this is [Your Company] calling about your request..." This approach respects the recipient's time while clearly stating the call's purpose.

Avoid lengthy introductions or complex questions. The AI performs best with simple, direct conversations focused on scheduling an appointment or answering basic questions. For more complex interactions, have the AI transfer to a human or schedule a callback.

  1. State your name and company immediately
  2. Mention the specific reason for calling
  3. Ask one clear question (e.g., "Are you available Thursday at 2pm?")
  4. Provide an easy next step

Multi-Channel Follow-Up Strategy

AI calling works best as part of a sequenced follow-up system. At 12:30 in the video, you'll see how to combine calls with texts, emails, and voicemail drops. The most effective sequences contact leads 4+ times per week through different channels without becoming spammy.

Here's a high-converting sequence we've implemented for clients:

  • Instant: AI call within 30 seconds of form submission
  • +5 min: Text message with key information
  • +1 hour: Personal voicemail if call wasn't answered
  • +1 day: Follow-up email with additional value
  • +3 days: Second AI call attempt

First, they deal with urgent customer needs - homeowners facing repairs or needing quick sales. Second, the sales cycle is relatively short. Third, customers expect and appreciate rapid response times. If your business fits this profile, AI calling can become your most powerful lead conversion tool.

Performance Tracking & Optimization

GoHighLevel provides basic call analytics, but you'll want to track key metrics manually at first. Create a simple spreadsheet to record call outcomes: answered vs. not answered, call duration, appointments scheduled, and any objections heard during calls.

Review this data weekly to identify patterns. You might discover that calls between 10am-2pm get higher answer rates, or that a particular script variation converts better. Use these insights to continuously refine your approach.

  1. Track answer rates by day/time
  2. Note common objections to address in your script
  3. Monitor call duration (ideal 2-5 minutes)
  4. Calculate appointment conversion percentage
