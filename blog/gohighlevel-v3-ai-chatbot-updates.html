Traditional chatbots fail miserably when clients send voice messages or photos - which happens constantly in service industries. At 2:15 in the video demo, you'll see the V3 chatbot perfectly handle a voice request about a hairstyle photo, then accurately describe the image's contents.

The magic happens in two simple toggles under bot settings: "Allow this bot to respond to images" and "Allow this bot to respond to voice notes." Once enabled, your chatbot can:

2. Multi-Calendar Booking System

Service businesses with multiple offerings previously needed separate chatbots for each service type. The V3 update solves this elegantly with multi-calendar booking.

At 5:48 in the tutorial, you'll see how to configure a single chatbot to handle bookings across multiple service calendars. For a hair salon, this means:

  • Coloring services route to specialist stylists' calendars
  • Basic cuts go to junior stylists
  • Special treatments book with senior staff

The system automatically detects service keywords in conversations and routes accordingly. You can even set different pricing tiers and availability rules per calendar while maintaining one seamless client experience.
