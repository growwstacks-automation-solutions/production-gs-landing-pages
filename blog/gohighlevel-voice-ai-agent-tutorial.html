Crafting the Perfect Prompt: Our 95% Success Formula

At 7:15 in the video, we reveal the exact prompt structure that achieves 95% completion rates. The secret lies in establishing immediate context and guiding the caller through a logical flow:

  1. Context setting: "Hi, this is [Name] with [Company]. I see you requested a [service] quote from our [Facebook/Google] ad."
  2. Process explanation: "To get your accurate quote, I'll ask 3 quick questions about your property - this takes about 90 seconds."
  3. Objection handling: Built-in responses for common questions like "Why do you need my address?"
  4. Qualifying questions: Essential questions only - no unnecessary data collection
  5. CTA: Clear next steps whether booking or follow-up

Pro tip: The prompt works because it focuses entirely on helping the caller get what they want faster than human receptionists could.
