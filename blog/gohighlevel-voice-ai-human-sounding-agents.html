Secret #1: The Business Profile Setup

Most agencies dive straight into building Voice AI workflows without properly configuring the business profile. This creates an immediate credibility gap with carriers that flag calls as potential spam. At 3:22 in the video, you'll see how incomplete business details trigger robotic voice filters.

The solution is meticulous profile completion before any AI deployment. Every field must match official business registration documents exactly - no abbreviations, no missing LLC designations. The authorized representative section is particularly critical for verification.

Key insight: Carriers cross-reference your GoHighLevel business profile with official records. A 100% match means 78% faster verification and eliminates robotic voice artifacts.

Step-by-Step Business Profile Setup

  1. Navigate to Settings → Business Profile immediately after account creation
  2. Upload high-resolution logo (minimum 500×500 pixels)
  3. Enter legal business name exactly as registered (include LLC/Inc if applicable)
  4. Input complete physical address (no PO boxes)
  5. Designate authorized representative (owner or primary contact)
  6. Verify tax ID/EIN matches business registration
  7. Use domain-matching email addresses (no Gmail/Yahoo)
