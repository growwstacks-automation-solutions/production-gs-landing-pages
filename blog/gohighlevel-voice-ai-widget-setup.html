Account Setup & Configuration

Before creating your first AI agent, you'll need to enable the Voice AI features in your GoHighLevel account. Navigate to Agency Settings → Labs to find the new Voice AI options. The September 2025 update groups related features together, making it easier to activate everything you need with just a few clicks.

This dramatically improves the user experience by preventing the frustrating "start over" feeling many chatbots create. Combined with the new MCP support, it enables truly continuous conversations regardless of channel.

Voice & Conversation Settings

The Voice Settings section is where you'll make your AI truly sound like part of your team. With over 200 voice options now available, you can match gender, accent, and tone to your brand personality. For most businesses, the "Jessica" voice (available in multiple accents) provides an excellent balance of clarity and warmth.

Advanced conversation controls let you fine-tune how your AI interacts:

  • Speech Speed: Moderate works for most audiences, but consider Slow for older demographics or complex services
  • Interruption Sensitivity: 75 is the sweet spot - high enough to allow natural conversation flow but not so sensitive that background noise triggers false interruptions
  • Temperature: Controls creativity vs precision (0.85 works well for most business applications)
  • Emotional Tone Variability: Adds natural inflection changes to prevent robotic monotone delivery

Knowledge Base Configuration

Your AI's knowledge base is where it learns about your business, services, and common customer questions. The September 2025 update expands the maximum character limit to 4,000 - enough for detailed information without overwhelming the AI. Structure this content as clear, concise answers to anticipated questions rather than marketing copy.

Effective knowledge bases follow three rules:

  1. Answer the question directly in the first sentence
  2. Provide 1-2 supporting details or examples
  3. End with a natural transition to the next likely question

For example, instead of "We offer comprehensive coaching programs with various pricing tiers," try "Our VIP coaching program costs $197/month and includes weekly group sessions, private Slack access, and our complete course library. Most members see results within 30 days. Would you like me to explain the curriculum or share success stories?"
