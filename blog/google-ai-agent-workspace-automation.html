What Changed in Google Workspace?

Business owners have struggled for years with the "morning email avalanche" - spending 2+ hours daily just sorting, labeling, and responding to messages before real work begins. Google's new Workspace Studio (launched December 2025) solves this by letting you build AI agents that operate within Gmail, Drive, Sheets and Chat.

Unlike traditional automation tools that require technical skills, Workspace Studio uses natural language. You simply describe what you want ("notify me about emails from key clients") or choose from pre-built templates. The platform has already automated over 20 million tasks in its first month.

Key differentiator: These agents run on Gemini 3, Google's most advanced AI, and work natively within tools you already use. No separate apps, no complex integrations - just tell it what to do in plain English.
