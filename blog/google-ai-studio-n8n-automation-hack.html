Building a Lead Scraper Live

The tutorial demonstrates creating a Google Maps lead scraper from scratch in under 30 minutes. This real-world example shows the practical power of combining these platforms:

Step 1: Define Your App in AI Studio

Using natural language prompts, specify that you want an app that scrapes leads from Google Maps based on search queries, city, and country inputs. Define the exact data fields you want to collect.

Step 2: Set Up the n8n Webhook

Create a new workflow in n8n with a webhook trigger. Configure it to receive POST requests from your AI Studio app. This becomes the bridge between your app's interface and your data storage.

Step 3: Connect to Google Sheets

Add a Google Sheets node in n8n to append the scraped lead data to a spreadsheet. Map the incoming fields from the webhook to the appropriate columns in your sheet.

Pro Tip: Start with a small set of fields (5-7) for your first version. You can always add more later, but keeping it simple reduces troubleshooting complexity.
