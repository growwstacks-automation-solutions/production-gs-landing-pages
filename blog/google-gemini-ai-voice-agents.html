Step-by-Step Build Process

Step 1: Access Google AI Studio

Navigate to aisstudio.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Select "Create conversational voice apps" from the dashboard. This is where the magic happens - no coding interface required.

Step 2: Describe Your Business Needs

Type a simple prompt explaining your business and what the agent should do. For example: "This is for a plumbing company that handles emergency repairs. The agent needs to qualify urgent calls, explain our 24/7 service fees, and schedule same-day appointments."

Step 3: Select Your Gemini Model

Choose between Flash (faster response) or Pro (more accurate) models. For most voice applications, Flash provides the best balance of speed and quality.

Step 4: Test and Refine

Click "Start session" to have live conversations with your agent. Notice at 3:42 in the video how natural refinements like "Make the response sound more concerned about emergency situations" instantly improve the agent's tone.

Pro tip: Record common customer questions from your actual voicemails and have the agent practice answering them. This creates hyper-realistic training scenarios.
