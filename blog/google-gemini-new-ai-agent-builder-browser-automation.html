The New AI Agent Builder

Businesses spend countless hours on repetitive tasks - preparing for meetings, analyzing customer communications, compiling reports. Google's new AI Agent Builder changes this by letting anyone create custom AI assistants without coding.

The platform includes pre-built agents for common tasks and allows you to build custom agents that integrate with Google Workspace. At 2:15 in the video, you can see how simply describing what you need ("build me an agent that helps prepare for customer meetings") creates a fully functional assistant.

Key capability: These agents can access and analyze your Gmail, Calendar, and Drive data (with permissions) to automate tasks like meeting preparation, document summarization, and customer research.

Pricing starts at $21/user/month for Gemini Business, with Enterprise at $30/user/month. While targeted at businesses, even solopreneurs can benefit from the productivity gains these agents enable.

Gemini 2.5 Browser Automation

Manual data entry and web research are time-consuming yet critical for many businesses. Gemini 2.5 introduces browser automation that outperforms competitors in accuracy and speed.

At 5:40 in the video, you can see Gemini 2.5 compared side-by-side with OpenAI's model for tasks like retrieving crypto prices. Gemini completes the task faster and more accurately - 92% success rate versus 85% for OpenAI.

Benchmark results show Gemini is not only more accurate (92% vs 85% OpenAI) but also faster and more cost-effective per task completed.

This browser automation enables workflows like:

  • Automated data collection from multiple websites
  • Filling out web forms automatically
  • Monitoring competitor pricing changes
  • Automating repetitive SaaS platform tasks

Notebook LM Mobile & Drive Updates

Notebook LM, Google's AI-powered research assistant, received two significant updates that make it more useful for mobile professionals and teams managing large document collections.

First, at 9:20 in the video, you'll see the new mobile sharing feature. Now you can share content directly from mobile apps to Notebook LM - no more emailing yourself links or saving to Drive first.

Second, the new Google Drive source discovery (shown at 11:15) lets you search your entire Drive for documents related to a topic. For example, searching for "TikTok algorithm" surfaces all relevant docs, slides, and notes across your Drive automatically.

Time-saving potential: These updates could save knowledge workers 2-3 hours per week previously spent manually organizing research materials.

Google Stitch Design Iterations

Design iteration is a bottleneck for many businesses, requiring back-and-forth with designers for simple changes. Google Stitch's new variant generation (shown at 8:10) accelerates this process.

After describing a design ("daily content ideas website"), you can now generate multiple variants with different colors, layouts, or styles in seconds. This is particularly valuable for:

  • Marketing teams testing different landing page designs
  • E-commerce stores experimenting with product page layouts
  • Agencies presenting multiple concepts to clients

The estimated time display helps manage expectations, and you can iterate on preferred designs or discard unwanted options instantly.

AI Studio Model Search

With over 20 models now available in Google AI Studio, finding the right one was becoming challenging. The new search functionality (shown at 7:30) lets you quickly locate models by name or capability.

While not as flashy as other updates, this small quality-of-life improvement makes it easier to:

  • Compare different model capabilities
  • Switch between models for specific tasks
  • Discover new models as they're added

For developers building on Gemini, this reduces friction in the model selection process.

Business Impact & Use Cases

These updates collectively represent a significant leap in practical AI applications for businesses. At 13:50, the video mentions Goldman Sachs' prediction that AI could replace 300 million jobs - but more accurately, it will transform how work gets done.

Key business applications include:
