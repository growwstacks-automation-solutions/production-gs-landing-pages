How the AI-Powered Review System Works

Traditional review requests beg every customer - resulting in mixed ratings. Our system uses AI to intelligently filter who gets asked:

  1. Service Completion: Triggered when a painter/plumber marks a job complete
  2. 2-Day Delay: SMS goes out 48 hours later (feels more authentic)
  3. Sentiment Analysis: AI evaluates customer response to "How was your service?"
  4. Smart Routing: Happy customers get the Google review link; unhappy ones get personalized follow-up

The magic happens in step 3 - by only prompting satisfied customers, businesses see their average rating climb while review volume increases. One roofing client went from 3.8 to 4.7 stars in 90 days using this method.
