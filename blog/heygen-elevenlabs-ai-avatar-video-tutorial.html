Step-by-Step Workflow

Follow this proven process to create your first AI avatar video with a custom voice. The entire workflow can be completed in under 30 minutes once you're familiar with the tools.

Step 1: Write Your Script

Start with a concise, conversational script. AI voices sound most natural with short sentences (12-15 words max) and simple vocabulary. Avoid complex clauses and technical jargon where possible. Write as if you're explaining the concept to a colleague over coffee.

Step 2: Generate the Voice in ElevenLabs

Navigate to ElevenLabs' VoiceLab and select a voice that matches your brand personality. Paste your script and generate the audio. Listen carefully to the output - at 4:30 in the video, you'll see how small text adjustments can dramatically improve voice naturalness.

Step 3: Create the Avatar Video in HeyGen

Upload your audio file to HeyGen and select an avatar. Choose one that matches your voice style - a youthful avatar pairs well with an energetic voice, while a professional-looking avatar suits a measured, authoritative tone. The platform will automatically sync the avatar's mouth movements to your audio.

Step 4: Export and Share

Once satisfied with the preview, export your video in 1080p or 4K resolution depending on your HeyGen plan. The rendered video can be downloaded or shared directly to platforms like YouTube or LinkedIn.

Pro Tip: Create a library of 5-10 script templates for different video types (product demos, FAQs, tutorials) to streamline future production.

Pro Tips for Realism

These seven techniques will make your AI avatar videos nearly indistinguishable from human-presented content:

  1. Script for speech patterns: Write how people actually talk, with contractions and occasional filler words
  2. Control pacing with punctuation: Commas create natural pauses; periods create full stops
  3. Match voice to avatar: A mature avatar needs a deeper, slower voice
  4. Keep videos under 3 minutes: Shorter content maintains viewer attention
  5. Always preview fully: Watch the entire video before exporting
  6. Regenerate bad takes: Don't settle for awkward-sounding phrases
  7. Prioritize clarity: Simple, understandable content outperforms perfection

At 10:20 in the video, you'll see how adjusting just a few words in the script can transform an unnatural-sounding sentence into fluid speech.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

After helping dozens of clients implement this workflow, we've identified these frequent pitfalls:

  • Overly formal scripts: AI voices struggle with legalese and technical jargon
  • Ignoring audio quality: Even slight robotic tones undermine credibility
  • Skipping previews: Always watch the full video before publishing
  • Mismatched pacing: Fast voices with slow-moving avatars feel unnatural
  • Overusing effects: Excessive animations distract from your message

Remember: Your first few videos won't be perfect. Focus on incremental improvements with each new creation.
