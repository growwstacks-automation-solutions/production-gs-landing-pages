Webhook: The Starting Gun

The webhook is what makes instant response possible. Unlike systems that poll for new data every few minutes, a webhook receives information the moment it's submitted - like a starting gun that sets your entire digital employee in motion.

When someone submits your form:

  1. The form platform (Jotform, Typeform, etc.) sends an HTTP POST request
  2. Your Make.com webhook instantly catches this request
  3. All form data gets packaged into a neat JSON payload
  4. The system immediately triggers the next steps

This happens in milliseconds - far faster than any human could notice a new form submission, let alone begin processing it.

Text Parser: Your Data Cleaner

Form data is often messy - extra spaces, inconsistent formatting, or combined fields. The text parser acts as your digital employee's brain, using regular expressions (regex) to find and extract exactly what you need.

For example, this simple regex pattern captures names:

 ([A-Za-z]+(?:\s[A-Za-z]+)*)

It looks for:

  • One or more letters ([A-Za-z]+)
  • Optionally followed by a space and more letters ((?:\s[A-Za-z]+)*)

This ensures "John Smith" gets captured perfectly whether entered as "john smith", "JOHN SMITH", or even "john smith" with extra spaces.

Google Sheets: Permanent Record

With clean data extracted, your digital employee creates a perfect record in Google Sheets. Each lead gets its own row with columns for:

The system maps each data point to the correct column automatically - no copy-paste errors, no missed fields, no typos. You now have a searchable, filterable database of every lead that's ever contacted you.
