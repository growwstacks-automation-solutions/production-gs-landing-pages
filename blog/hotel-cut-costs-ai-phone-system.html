10-Minute Setup: ElevenLabs + Twilio

Creating your hotel's AI receptionist requires just two services: ElevenLabs for the voice AI brain, and Twilio for the phone number. The entire setup process breaks down into three simple steps you can complete between shifts:

Step 1: Create Your Free ElevenLabs Agent

Navigate to ElevenLabs' Agents platform (not their standard voice cloning tool) and click "New Agent." Name it after your property - for example, "Riverfront Hotel Concierge."

Step 2: Connect Your Website as a Knowledge Base

Paste your hotel website URL into both the system prompt (which we'll detail next) and the knowledge base section. This gives your AI access to all publicly available information about your property.

Step 3: Purchase a Twilio Phone Number

For $1.15/month, buy a local number through Twilio and connect it to your ElevenLabs agent. This number can be listed on your website as "Quick Questions Line" or replace your main line entirely.

Implementation summary: 1) Create free ElevenLabs agent → 2) Connect hotel website → 3) Buy $1.15 Twilio number → Done. The system is active immediately after publishing.

Creating the Perfect Hotel AI System Prompt

The "brain" of your AI agent is its system prompt - instructions telling it how to behave. For hotels, we recommend this structure (shown at 4:12 in the video):

"You are the friendly voice AI receptionist for [Hotel Name]. Your primary role is to answer common guest questions accurately using information from our website. Always respond in a warm, professional tone. If asked about: check-in/out times, amenities, parking, WiFi, location, or nearby attractions - provide detailed answers. For complex requests like complaints or special needs, politely transfer to human staff."

At 4:35, you'll see how ChatGPT can help refine this prompt to match your property's specific brand voice and common inquiries. The AI will automatically extract all necessary details from your website - no manual data entry required.

Connecting Your Hotel Website as a Knowledge Base

While the system prompt gives your AI general instructions, the knowledge base provides specific facts. At 5:18 in the tutorial, we add the hotel's website URL as both a system prompt reference and a separate knowledge base document.

This dual approach ensures the AI can: 1) Answer general questions using its training, and 2) Provide precise details like "Our bar opens at 7 AM daily" directly from your site. The AI automatically stays updated as you change website content - no need to manually update answers.

Pro tip: Create a "Guest Information" page on your website with FAQs. When the AI detects this page, it will use it as a prioritized knowledge source for common questions.

Buying and Assigning Your AI Phone Number

Twilio provides the phone infrastructure for your AI agent. At 6:40 in the video, we:

  1. Purchase a local number for $1.15/month (search by your hotel's area code)
  2. Copy the Twilio Account SID from the console dashboard
  3. Import the number into ElevenLabs using the SID
  4. Assign our "Riverfront Hotel" agent to the number

The entire process takes under two minutes. Once published, calls to this number will be answered by your AI immediately. You can list this as a separate "Quick Questions" line or replace your main number entirely.
