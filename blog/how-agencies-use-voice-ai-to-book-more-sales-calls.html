How To Implement Voice AI In Your Agency

Transitioning from manual follow-up to AI-powered conversion requires three key steps:

Step 1: Map Your Current Lead Flow

Document every touchpoint from lead capture to booked appointment. Identify where delays or drop-offs occur (often the first 5 minutes post-submission).

Step 2: Build Conversation Scripts

Create natural dialogue flows for common scenarios. Include branching logic based on responses (e.g., "If they say X, ask Y").

Step 3: Integrate With Your Stack

Connect the AI to your CRM, calendar, and lead sources. Set up triggers so every new lead instantly routes to the AI system.

Pro tip: Start with your highest-value, time-sensitive leads first. The ROI from legal, financial, and healthcare clients typically justifies the investment immediately.
