Exactly How the AI Books Appointments

The appointment booking AI follows a proven four-step process that mirrors how your best receptionist works - just faster and available 24/7:

Step 1: Instant Answer & Greeting

The AI answers within the first ring using your customized greeting ("Thanks for calling Acme Plumbing, this is Sam. How can I help you today?"). First impressions matter - callers report not realizing they're talking to AI in tests.

Step 2: Need Identification

Using natural language processing, the AI understands the customer's request ("My bathroom pipe burst" triggers emergency plumbing protocols). It asks clarifying questions just like a human would.

Step 3: Availability Check

The system checks your connected calendar in real-time, offering available slots that match your business rules (emergency jobs get same/next-day options first).

Step 4: Confirmation & Details

Once booked, the AI confirms details, collects necessary information (address, contact info), and sends automated confirmations. It even catches errors like misheard email addresses.

In summary: Answer → Understand → Schedule → Confirm. The entire process takes 60-90 seconds - faster than most human interactions - while eliminating hold times and callbacks.
