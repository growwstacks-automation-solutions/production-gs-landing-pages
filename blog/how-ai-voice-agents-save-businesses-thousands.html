By morning, they've moved on to a competitor who answered immediately. This scenario plays out daily across industries - real estate, healthcare, auto sales, and service businesses.

The financial impact is staggering. Our analysis shows the average small business loses $48,700 annually in missed opportunities from unanswered after-hours calls. The problem compounds because today's consumers expect instant responses - 78% of callers won't leave a voicemail if no one answers.

Key insight: After-hours callers represent your hottest leads. They're actively researching now, not casually browsing. Every unanswered call is essentially turning away ready-to-buy customers.
