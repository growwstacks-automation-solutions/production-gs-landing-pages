How AI Handles Natural Conversations

What makes modern voice AI remarkable isn't just what it says, but how it listens. Notice three key capabilities in our demo:

  1. Context retention: The agent remembers earlier details (party size, celebration type) throughout the 3-minute call
  2. Speech disambiguation: It clarifies uncertain words like "Jasp" versus "Jasper" naturally
  3. Proactive questioning: Rather than just taking orders, it asks helpful follow-ups about dietary needs

Technical note: These systems use large language models trained on thousands of restaurant calls, allowing them to handle the "ums," interruptions, and backtracking common in real conversations.
