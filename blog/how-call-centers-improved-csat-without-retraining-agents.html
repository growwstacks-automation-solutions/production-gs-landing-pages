3 Pillars of CSAT Transformation

Crisp's technology rests on three technical breakthroughs that directly impact customer satisfaction:

1. Noise Cancellation

Real-time processing removes background noise, echo, and crosstalk - the top distractions that derail customer calls. Unlike basic noise gates, the AI distinguishes between unwanted sounds and human speech patterns.

2. Accent Conversion

Neural networks subtly adjust pronunciation and pacing without changing the speaker's voice. This helps non-native agents and customers understand each other better, reducing "Can you repeat that?" requests by 40%.

3. Automated Follow-ups

The system detects action items and commitments during calls, then creates tickets and reminders automatically. Agents spend 73% less time on post-call paperwork according to 2025 Contact Center Metrics Report.
