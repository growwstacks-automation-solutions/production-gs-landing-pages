The Coach Lead Qualification Problem

Every coach knows the frustration: endless discovery calls with people who aren't ready to invest, can't afford your services, or don't fit your ideal client profile. You waste hours answering the same basic questions ("What's your pricing?" "Do you work with beginners?") instead of focusing on your paying clients.

The WhatsApp qualification chatbot solves this by automating the initial screening process. As shown in the demo, it asks three key questions that determine whether a lead is worth your time:

Key qualification points: 1) Their main goal (to assess fit), 2) Current level (to gauge readiness), and 3) Monthly budget (to confirm affordability. This simple 3-question filter can eliminate 80% of unqualified leads before they ever reach your calendar.

How WhatsApp Chatbot Qualification Works

The WhatsApp Business API allows you to create automated conversation flows that feel personal but run 24/7. When a potential client messages your business number (or sends a specific keyword like "fit" in the demo), the chatbot immediately begins the qualification process.

Unlike web forms that people abandon, WhatsApp has a 98% open rate and 45-60% response rate for business messages. People are already using WhatsApp daily, so they're more likely to complete your qualification questions than fill out yet another web form.

Setting Up Your First Qualification Flow

The demo shows a fitness coaching example, but the same structure works for any service business. The setup involves three key components you'll configure in your WhatsApp Business account:

Step 1: Welcome Message

Your opening message sets the tone and explains the purpose of the questions. The demo uses: "Hey, welcome. I help people to get stronger, leaner and more confident. Can I ask you a few quick questions to see if coaching is a good fit for you?"

Step 2: Multiple Choice Questions

Each question uses button responses (like "Lose fat" or "Build muscle") to make it easy for prospects to answer. The demo collects:

  • Main fitness goal (saved to "main_goal" field)
  • Current fitness level (saved to "level" field)
  • Monthly budget range (saved to "budget" field)

Step 3: Response Storage

All answers are automatically saved to the contact's profile in your WhatsApp Business account, visible in your unified inbox. This creates a complete qualification record before you ever speak to the lead.

Conditional Routing by Budget

The most powerful feature shown in the demo is conditional messaging based on budget responses. When a prospect selects their comfortable monthly spend, they receive different next steps:

Budget-based routing: $500+ = strategy call link | $100-250 = group program info | Under $100 = free guide. This ensures you only spend time with truly qualified leads while still providing value to others.

In the demo, when someone selects "$500+", they immediately receive: "Love that commitment. You are a great fit for one-on-one coaching. Want to book a free strategy call?" with a clickable booking link. Lower budgets receive appropriate alternatives without any manual work from you.

Triggering the Flow with Keywords

You don't want every WhatsApp message to trigger the full qualification flow. The demo shows how to set up specific keywords (like "fit") that start the automated sequence:

  1. Create your qualification flow in WhatsApp Business Manager
  2. Navigate to Flows → Keywords
  3. Add a new keyword (e.g. "fit", "coaching", "consult")
  4. Assign it to your qualification flow

Now when prospects message that word to your business number, they automatically begin the qualification process. You can have multiple keywords triggering different flows for various services or programs.

Tracking and Managing Qualified Leads

All qualified leads appear in your WhatsApp Business unified inbox with their complete profile data. As shown at 5:30 in the demo video, you can:

  • See their phone number and all collected responses
  • View conversation history
  • Send follow-up messages manually
  • Export data to your CRM

This creates a complete record of every interaction, so you're never starting from scratch with a new lead. Even if someone doesn't book immediately, you have their qualification data for future follow-up.
