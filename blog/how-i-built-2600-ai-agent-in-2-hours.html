4-Part System Overview

The entire automation consists of four interconnected workflows that handle client onboarding from payment to successful account creation. What's remarkable is that only one component uses AI - proving you don't need complex technology to deliver massive value.

1. Payment to CRM Entry

Triggered when a payment is received. Creates a CRM entry, sends welcome email with account setup link, and notifies the team via Slack.

2. Automated Follow-Up

Runs daily to check for clients who haven't created accounts 3 days after payment. Sends reminder email and updates CRM status.

3. Human Escalation

Triggers 5 days post-payment if account still isn't created. Alerts team via Slack for manual follow-up and updates CRM.

4. AI-Powered Welcome

The only AI component - sends a personalized welcome email when account is created, using client details to craft a tailored message.

Why this works: 75% of the system is simple automation anyone can build. The strategic combination creates an outcome worth $2600 to the client.

Building Flow 1: Payment to CRM Entry

The first workflow begins when a client makes a payment. At 2:45 in the video, you'll see the exact n8n setup using a webhook trigger from the payment processor.

Key components:

  • Webhook trigger captures payment data (name, email, etc.)
  • Gmail node sends welcome email with account creation link
  • Slack node notifies team of new payment
  • Google Sheets creates CRM entry with status "account creation"

The magic is in the date calculations - the workflow automatically sets follow-up dates at 3 days and 5 days post-payment, eliminating manual calendar tracking.

Building Flow 2: Automated Follow-Up

At 8:20 in the tutorial, you'll see the daily check that identifies clients needing follow-up. The workflow:

  1. Queries the CRM daily for entries where status = "account creation" AND follow-up date = today
  2. Sends reminder email with original account creation link
  3. Updates CRM status to "first follow-up sent" and marks follow-up date as complete

This simple automation handles what would otherwise require manual CRM checks and email drafting - saving approximately 15 minutes per client.

Building Flow 3: Human Escalation

The third workflow (demonstrated at 11:05) ensures no client falls through the cracks. If 5 days pass without account creation after the automated reminder, the system:

  • Identifies clients with status "first follow-up sent" AND human escalation date = today
  • Sends Slack alert to team with client details for manual follow-up
  • Updates CRM status to "human notified" to prevent duplicate efforts

This safety net provides peace of mind that every paying client receives proper attention, whether through automation or human intervention.

Building Flow 4: AI-Powered Welcome

The only AI component (shown at 14:30) triggers when a client finally creates their account. The workflow:

  1. Captures account creation details through a form submission
  2. Feeds client data to Claude AI with instructions to craft a personalized welcome
  3. Sends the AI-generated email with tailored subject and body
  4. Updates CRM status to "account created" and notifies team

AI Tip: The system message to Claude specifies tone (friendly), structure (JSON with subject/body fields), and content requirements (include joke, express excitement, reinforce goals). This ensures consistent, on-brand outputs.

At 16:45, you'll see the hilarious yet professional email Claude generates - proving AI doesn't need to be complex to deliver value.
