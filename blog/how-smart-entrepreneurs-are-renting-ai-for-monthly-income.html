The $200K Missed Call Problem

Small businesses face a silent revenue killer - missed calls and slow lead response times. When the phone rings at 9 PM or during lunch hours, most businesses either miss the call entirely or take days to respond. This isn't just inconvenient—it's costing the average small business $200,000 to $240,000 annually in lost revenue.

The breakthrough came when early adopters realized AI voice agents could solve this 24/7 without hiring staff. Monica, the AI agent featured in the training, demonstrates how transparent AI answering ("Hi, I'm Monica, an AI agent...") actually achieves 92% conversation completion rates versus traditional voicemail abandonment.

Key Insight: Businesses spending on ads are literally burning money if they're not answering calls immediately. The Domino's "30 minutes or free" principle now applies to all industries - speed of response directly correlates with conversion rates and revenue.
