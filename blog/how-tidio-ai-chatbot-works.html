What is Tidio AI Chatbot?

Imagine losing potential customers because they couldn't quickly get answers to simple questions at 2 AM. Tidio solves this by combining AI chatbots with human live chat in one dashboard. It's like having a 24/7 sales and support team that never sleeps, takes breaks, or gets overwhelmed by multiple conversations.

Tidio's AI analyzes your website content and learns from real customer interactions to provide accurate, instant responses. When the AI encounters a complex question, it seamlessly hands off to your human team or collects contact details for follow-up. This hybrid approach means customers always get help, while your team only handles conversations that truly require human expertise.

Key benefit: Businesses using Tidio typically see 40-60% more leads captured and 30% faster response times compared to traditional contact forms or email support.

4-Step Setup Process (No Coding)

The biggest misconception about AI chatbots is that they require technical expertise. Tidio breaks this barrier with a setup process so simple, most businesses complete it during their lunch break.

Step 1: Create Your Tidio Account

Sign up with your email or connect your Google account. Tidio immediately asks which platform your website uses (Shopify, WordPress, Wix, etc.) to provide the optimal installation method.

Step 2: Add Tidio to Your Website

Depending on your platform, you'll either install a plugin or copy-paste a small code snippet into your website header. This takes 2 minutes on average and requires no coding knowledge.

Step 3: Customize the Chat Widget

Personalize the chat button colors, position, welcome message, and automated triggers to match your brand. Set rules for when the chat should automatically open (after time on page, exit intent, etc.).

Step 4: Activate the AI Chatbot

Choose from pre-built templates for eCommerce, services, or SaaS businesses. Alternatively, let Tidio's AI scan your website content to automatically learn how to answer common questions.

Pro tip: At 3:12 in the video tutorial, you'll see how to train Tidio's AI using your existing FAQ pages and customer service emails for even more accurate responses.

How Tidio Captures Leads Automatically

Traditional lead forms sit passively on your website, hoping visitors will fill them out. Tidio's AI proactively engages visitors at the perfect moment to maximize conversions.

The chatbot can be configured to ask for contact information:

  • Before starting a chat: "Can I get your email so we can continue this conversation later if needed?"
  • During conversations: "I'd love to send you that discount code - what's the best email to send it to?"
  • At strategic moments: When a visitor spends time on pricing pages or shows exit intent

All collected leads automatically sync to your Tidio dashboard where you can tag them, add notes, and initiate follow-up sequences. For eCommerce stores, Tidio can even connect abandoned carts to specific leads.

How Tidio Responds to Customers 24/7

Customers expect instant answers - 90% rate an "immediate" response as important when they have a customer service question. Tidio delivers this through two response modes:

1. AI-Powered Instant Answers

The chatbot handles common questions about business hours, shipping policies, product details, and pricing. It improves over time by learning from your website content and past customer interactions.

2. Human Takeover for Complex Issues

When the AI detects a question it can't confidently answer (like specific return requests), it either:

  • Transfers to an available team member
  • Collects the visitor's contact info and promises a response within a set timeframe
  • Provides relevant help articles while noting the need for follow-up

Critical feature: Tidio's mobile app lets your team respond to conversations from anywhere, ensuring no lead falls through the cracks even when you're away from the office.
