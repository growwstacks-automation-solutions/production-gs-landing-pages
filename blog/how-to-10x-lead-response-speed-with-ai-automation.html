Make.com Setup Walkthrough

Make.com (formerly Integromat) provides the automation backbone connecting your email to AI. Here's how to configure it:

Step 1: Create Your Scenario

Log into Make.com and create a new scenario. Search for the "Gmail" and "OpenAI" modules - these will form the core of your workflow.

Step 2: Configure Triggers

Set the Gmail module to watch for new emails matching your lead criteria (e.g. emails containing "quote request" or "pricing").

Pro tip: Add filters to exclude replies from your own domain to prevent infinite loops.

Gmail Integration

Connecting Gmail takes just 2 minutes:

  1. Click "Add connection" in the Gmail module
  2. Authenticate with your business Gmail account
  3. Set filters to watch only relevant incoming emails

The system will now monitor your inbox continuously. At 7:15 in the video, you can see how to test this by sending a sample inquiry to yourself.

OpenAI Configuration

The magic happens when we connect OpenAI:

  1. Get your API key from platform.openai.com
  2. Paste it into Make.com's OpenAI module
  3. Select GPT-4 (or your preferred model)
  4. Set temperature to 0.7 for balanced creativity

Cost is negligible - about $0.01 per response. The 11:40 video timestamp shows how to test different temperature settings for your brand voice.
