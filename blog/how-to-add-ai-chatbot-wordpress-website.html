Why Every WordPress Site Needs an AI Chatbot

Most small business owners know they should be available to answer customer questions 24/7 - but hiring round-the-clock support staff isn't realistic. Missed inquiries mean lost sales opportunities and frustrated customers. An AI chatbot solves this by providing instant responses to common questions at any hour.

The chatbot shown in this tutorial learns from your website content and can answer questions about your services, pricing, and policies. Unlike basic chat widgets, it understands natural language queries and improves its responses over time.

Key benefit: Businesses using AI chatbots report a 35% reduction in customer service costs while improving response times from hours to seconds.

Getting Started with Elsite Chatbot Platform

The first step is creating an account on Elsite (linked in the video description). After signing up with your email and creating a password, you'll access the dashboard where you can create your first chatbot.

At the 2:15 mark in the video, you'll see how to select an AI chatbot template. While Elsite offers templates for different industries like e-commerce and healthcare, they all function the same way - the differences are primarily visual styles.

Pro tip: Choose the "Business Assistant" template if you're not sure - it's the most flexible starting point for most WordPress sites.

Training Your AI Chatbot

The real power comes from training your chatbot with your business knowledge. Start by entering your website URL - the AI will scan your pages to learn about your services. As shown at 4:30 in the video, you can then refine its knowledge through the training panel.

Add specific questions customers might ask, like "How much does your service cost?" along with professional responses. The AI learns to recognize different phrasings of the same question. You can also upload documents or connect additional knowledge sources.

Training efficiency: Spend 1-2 hours initially training your chatbot, and it will handle hundreds of customer interactions per month with minimal maintenance.

Customizing the Chatbot Appearance

Make the chatbot feel like part of your brand by customizing its appearance. You can change the profile picture, greeting message, colors, and position on screen. The video at 6:45 shows the various customization options.

Consider your audience when choosing colors and tone. A law firm might want a professional blue theme with formal greetings, while an e-commerce store could use brighter colors and a friendly tone.

Embedding the Chatbot on WordPress

Once configured, Elsite provides an embed code to add the chatbot to your WordPress site. As demonstrated at 8:10 in the video, you'll paste this code into your WordPress HTML editor or use a plugin like "Header and Footer Scripts."

The chatbot will appear on every page of your site. Visitors can minimize it when not needed, but it's always available for questions. No technical WordPress knowledge is required - just copy and paste the provided code snippet.

Advanced Features and Integrations

Beyond basic Q&A, your chatbot can collect leads, book appointments, and connect with other tools. Set up follow-up messages to capture emails from interested visitors. Integrate with your CRM to automatically create new leads from chatbot conversations.

Paid plans unlock more advanced features like multilingual support, analytics dashboards, and API access. Start with the free plan to test the basics, then upgrade as your needs grow.
