Step 1: Create Your Voice AI Agent

Navigate to the AI Agents section in your GoHighLevel dashboard and click "Create New Agent." You'll need to make several key decisions:

Agent Configuration Options:

  • Name: Use a clear identifier like "[Business] Sales AI V1"
  • Language: Default is English, but 12 languages are supported
  • Voice: Choose from 8 male/female voices with different tones
  • Time Zone: Set to your primary service area
  • Inbound/Outbound: Start with inbound for website calls

Pro Tip: At 5:42 in the video, you'll see how to enable personalization so the AI greets callers by name ("Hi [First Name]") using data from your CRM or form submissions.

Step 2: Train Your Knowledge Base

The secret to effective Voice AI is comprehensive training. GoHighLevel provides four powerful methods to teach your AI about your business:

Training Methods:

  1. Web Crawler: Indexes your entire website (shown at 7:15 in the video)
  2. FAQ Upload: Spreadsheet of common questions and answers
  3. Text Input: Direct entry of service descriptions and policies
  4. File Upload: PDFs, DOCs, or CSVs with additional information

For most businesses, we recommend starting with the web crawler to capture 60-70% of your knowledge base, then supplementing with specific FAQs. The system continues learning from every real conversation.

Step 3: Configure Advanced Settings

At 10:30 in the tutorial, you'll see the powerful "Advanced Mode" options that transform your AI from basic Q&A to a full sales assistant:

Key Advanced Features:

  • Appointment Booking: Sync with your calendar and set availability rules
  • Lead Qualification: Ask specific questions to score leads
  • Call Routing: Transfer to humans based on keywords or complexity
  • Post-Call Actions: Automate follow-ups and CRM updates

Implementation Tip: Use the prompt template shown at 11:45 to create a professional yet friendly AI personality tailored to your brand voice. This ensures consistency with your other marketing.

Step 4: Add to Your Website

With your AI trained and configured, it's time to connect it to your website. GoHighLevel provides multiple implementation options:

Integration Methods:

  1. Chat Widget: Adds a voice call button to your existing chat
  2. Standalone Button: Dedicated "Call Our AI" button
  3. Form Replacement: Voice AI instead of contact forms
  4. Page-Specific: Different AI behaviors per page

At 14:20 in the video, you'll see how to test the integration before going live. This ensures the AI responds appropriately to your actual website visitors.

Step 5: Test and Refine

Your Voice AI will improve dramatically in the first 30 days as it handles real conversations. Implement this refinement process:

Optimization Checklist:

  • Daily: Review call recordings and transcripts
  • Weekly: Add new questions to your knowledge base
  • Monthly: Analyze which conversations required human transfer
  • Quarterly: Update voice, scripts, and flows based on performance

Performance Benchmark: Well-trained Voice AI should handle 60-80% of inquiries without human intervention. If your transfer rate is higher, revisit your knowledge base training.
