The 3 Cold Email Mistakes Burning Your Domains

Most businesses approach cold email with the same flawed tactics: blasting thousands of emails from a single domain, skipping the warm-up process, and using shared sending platforms that tank deliverability. The result? 92% of their emails land in spam before they even start.

After helping 47 clients automate their outreach, we discovered three critical errors that destroy campaign performance:

Mistake #1: Using shared sender platforms like SmartLead or Instantly that pool IP reputations. One spammy user can sink deliverability for everyone.

Mistake #2: Sending more than 15 emails/day from any single account. Google's 2026 algorithms flag anything above this threshold as suspicious.

Mistake #3: Neglecting the 14-day warm-up period. Cold domains need gradual volume increases to establish trust with email providers.

The 14-Day Inbox Warmup Process

New domains require gradual sending volume increases to establish trust. Our warm-up sequence:

Days 1-7

5 emails/day from each account with 30% reply simulation. Platforms like Zap Mail automatically handle this while maintaining proper SPF/DKIM records.

Days 8-14

Increase to 10 emails/day with 50% reply simulation. This trains providers that your accounts engage in natural conversations.

Day 15+

Full sending capacity (15/day for Google, 5/day for Microsoft). Never exceed these thresholds - we rotate through accounts to maintain limits.

Critical: Warm-up emails must mimic human patterns - varied send times, personalized content, and natural reply delays. Automated systems that blast identical emails at fixed intervals get flagged immediately.

Campaign Setup That Converts at Scale

1. List Cleaning

Run all leads through Million Verifier before importing. Bounced emails destroy sender reputation faster than any other factor.

2. Campaign Naming

Use the format: [Company]_[Industry]_[Date]. Example: "Acme_Lenders_Nov2025". This prevents overlap when managing multiple clients.

3. Email Copy Rules

  • Plain text only (no HTML)
  • First line personalized using LinkedIn activity
  • Maximum 3 sentences per paragraph
  • Single CTA - book call or reply

4. Sending Windows

8 AM - 5 PM EST only. Early/late sends get lower open rates and higher spam complaints.
