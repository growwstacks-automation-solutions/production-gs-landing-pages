Setting Up ZenRows

ZenRows handles the hardest part of scraping - bypassing antibot protections and rendering JavaScript content. The setup process (shown at 5:10 in the video) involves:

Handling Scraping Challenges

Not all websites scrape equally. At 12:30 in the video, you'll see our initial failures with Wix sites that load content dynamically. Three common challenges and solutions:

Dynamic Content: Enable JavaScript rendering in ZenRows and increase timeout settings.

Inconsistent Structures: Focus on individual product pages rather than category listings where structures vary more.

Antibot Measures: Use ZenRows' automatic proxy rotation and request throttling to appear more human-like.

The key is testing different URL structures and ZenRows configurations until you get clean, consistent data extraction.
