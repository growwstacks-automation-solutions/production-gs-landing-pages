The Manual Texting Problem

Service businesses adding 5-20 new clients per week waste hours manually texting each one. Between copying phone numbers from Jobber, crafting individual messages, and inevitable human forgetfulness, this critical touchpoint often falls through the cracks. Clients wonder if their appointment is confirmed, while you scramble to follow up.

The solution? Automating the entire process so texts send instantly when clients are added to Jobber - whether through your CRM integration or manual entry. This eliminates the blank page syndrome while ensuring every client receives immediate, professional confirmation.

Time saved: This automation reclaims 5-10 minutes per new client. For a business adding 50 clients/month, that's 4-8 hours saved - enough time to service 2-4 additional clients.

How the Automation Works

This Zapier workflow acts as a bridge between Jobber and your texting platform (OpenPhone or Twilio). When a new client appears in Jobber, Zapier automatically triggers a personalized text using the client's information.

The magic happens through Jobber's native Zapier integration, which requires no coding. You'll create a simple "Zap" with just two steps: 1) detecting new Jobber clients, and 2) sending your predefined message through OpenPhone/Twilio.

Step 1: Set Up Jobber Trigger

In Zapier, create a new Zap and select Jobber as your trigger app. Choose "New Client Created" as your trigger event - this tells Zapier to watch for whenever clients are added to your Jobber account.

Connect your Jobber account by logging in through Zapier's secure portal. Test the connection by pulling in your most recent client - you'll see all available fields like name, phone number, and service address that can be used in your automated text.

Pro Tip: Jobber offers multiple triggers beyond new clients. "New Job Created" and "New Request Created" work equally well for different messaging scenarios.

Step 2: Connect OpenPhone Action

Add an action step in Zapier and select OpenPhone (or Twilio if preferred). Choose "Send Message" as your action. Connect your OpenPhone account using the same login credentials as your mobile app.

Map the phone number field from Jobber to OpenPhone's recipient field. This ensures texts go to the correct client every time. Zapier will automatically format the number correctly during transmission.

Step 3: Customize Your Message

Compose your message template in OpenPhone's action settings. Insert dynamic fields from Jobber by clicking the dropdown - for example, add the client's first name for personalization.

A sample effective message: "Hi {First Name}, thank you for choosing [Your Business]! Your {Service Type} is confirmed for {Appointment Date}. We've sent payment details to your email - let us know if you have any questions!"

Best practice: Keep messages under 160 characters to avoid splitting into multiple texts. Include only essential information with a clear next step.

Optional: Add a Human Delay

For a more natural feel, insert Zapier's "Delay" action between the trigger and sending the text. Set a 5-60 minute pause so messages don't arrive instantly at odd hours.

This small tweak makes automated texts feel more personal while still ensuring timely communication. The delay is fully customizable based on your business hours and preferences.

Testing Your Automation

Zapier's test mode lets you verify everything works before going live. Create a test client in Jobber, then run the Zap to confirm the text delivers correctly.

For thorough testing, check both the content (dynamic fields populate correctly) and delivery (text arrives at the right number). Once confirmed, turn on your Zap to automate all future client additions.
