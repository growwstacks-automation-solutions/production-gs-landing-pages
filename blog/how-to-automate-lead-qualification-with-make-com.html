The Lead Qualification Problem

Every sales rep knows the frustration of spending hours researching companies, only to realize halfway through a call that the prospect isn't a fit. Worse, emailing unqualified leads damages your domain reputation - ISPs track when recipients mark your emails as spam.

Manual qualification creates three specific problems:

1. Time drain: Researching each company takes ~1 minute. For a 300-lead campaign, that's 5 hours of non-revenue work.

2. Inconsistency: Human researchers average 70-80% accuracy when fatigued, while AI maintains 92%+.

3. Risk: Every unqualified email increases your spam risk score by 3-5% with major providers.

How Automation Solves It

This Make.com workflow combines three powerful tools:

  1. Google Sheets as your lead database
  2. Perplexity AI to research company websites
  3. GPT-4 to apply your qualification criteria

The automation processes rows sequentially, marking each as complete to prevent duplicates. Qualified leads flow into a separate sheet ready for your outreach sequence.

Step 1: Setting Up Your Spreadsheet

Start with two tabs in Google Sheets:

  • Raw Leads: Contains your imported prospect list with columns for company name, email, and website
  • Qualified Leads: empty sheet where matches your Raw Leads columns plus AI research fields

Pro tip: Use AI to extract domains from emails if missing websites are missing. The transcript shows a ChatGPT-generated Excel formula that automates this.

Step 2: Configuring Make.com

The automation follows this sequence:

  1. Search next unprocessed row in Raw Leads
  2. Send company name and website to Perplexity AI
  3. Pass AI research to GPT-4 with your criteria
  4. Move qualified leads to new sheet
  5. Mark row as processed

Key configuration: Set the Google Sheets trigger to search for rows where the "processed" column is empty. After each run, update that cell to prevent reprocessing.

Step 3: AI Qualification Logic

Your GPT-4 evaluates companies against criteria like:

  • Does their services page mention needs you solve?
  • Do client case studies match your vertical?
  • Is company size in your target range?

The AI returns one of three verdicts:

Qualified Not Qualified Needs Review

Only "Qualified" and "Needs Review" transfer to your clean sheet.

Step 4: Automating The Process

Schedule the automation to:

  • Process 3 leads per minute (avoids API rate limits)
  • Run continuously until all rows processed
  • Email summary email upon completion

At this pace, a 300-lead sheet completes in 100 minutes unattended. The video shows how to test with 5-10 leads first to validate your criteria.
