The Leak in Your Funnel

Every B2B business knows the frustration: hundreds of website visitors, but only a handful fill out contact forms. The rest remain anonymous - interested enough to visit your site, but unwilling to identify themselves. Traditional marketing funnels lose these leads forever.

This workflow solves that problem by combining three powerful tools: RB2B for visitor identification, n8n for automation logic, and Prosper for AI-powered LinkedIn outreach. Together they create a system that works while you sleep, turning passive visitors into active conversations.

Key stat: RB2B identifies 70-80% of anonymous website visitors, providing email addresses and LinkedIn profiles for follow-up. This means recovering 3-4x more leads from your existing traffic.

RB2B Visitor Tracking

RB2B uses cookie tracking and data enrichment to identify companies and individuals visiting your website. The system requires just a JavaScript snippet added to your site - no complex configuration. Within hours, you'll start seeing real names, email addresses, and LinkedIn profiles instead of anonymous "Visitor #1234" entries.

The dashboard shows visitor trends over time, company breakdowns, and individual contact details. Importantly, RB2B respects privacy regulations - you'll need to update your privacy policy to disclose this tracking method.

Implementation tip: Let RB2B run for 3-5 days before connecting to n8n. This ensures you have sufficient visitor data to test the automation workflow.

n8n Automation Workflow

The n8n workflow acts as the brain of this system, connecting RB2B data to your CRM and LinkedIn outreach. It starts with a webhook trigger that receives new visitor data from RB2B in real-time.

Each lead first checks against your CRM (like HubSpot) to avoid duplicate outreach to existing contacts. New leads proceed through an AI-powered qualification step before being sent to Prosper for LinkedIn messaging.

Step 1: Webhook Configuration

Create a webhook trigger in n8n and copy the URL into RB2B's integration settings. This establishes the connection between the two systems.

Step 2: CRM Lookup

The workflow searches your CRM using the visitor's name and company. This prevents messaging existing clients or duplicates.

Step 3: LinkedIn Profile Extraction

An AI module extracts the LinkedIn username from RB2B's profile URL, formatting it for Prosper's API requirements.

In summary: The n8n workflow (1) receives visitor data, (2) checks your CRM, (3) formats LinkedIn info, and (4) routes qualified leads to Prosper - all automatically.

LinkedIn Messaging with Prosper

Prosper solves LinkedIn's LinkedIn's API limitations by providing a reliable way to automate connection requests and messages. The platform uses AI to personalize outreach based on each prospect's profile, increasing response history, and your qualification criteria.

After connecting your LinkedIn account, you'll create message templates that Prosper's AI personalizes for each prospect. The system handles follow-up timing automatically, with typical sequences including 3-5 touches over 2 weeks.

Response boost: Prosper users see 25-40% response rates by combining AI personalization with multi-touch nurturing. Messages reference specific profile details rather than generic templates.

AI Qualification Process

The system doesn't just message every visitor - AI qualification ensures you only outreach to relevant prospects. You define criteria like industry, job title, or company size that match your ideal customer profile.

Prosper's AI analyzes LinkedIn profiles against your criteria, filtering out irrelevant contacts. This prevents wasting time on unqualified leads while ensuring your sales team only sees hot prospects.

Customizable: The AI learns from your successful connections, continuously improving qualification accuracy over time.

Response Rate Optimization

The secret to high response rates lies in three factors: (1) message personalization, (2) proper timing, and (3) value-focused content. content. Prosper handles all three automatically.

AI generates opening lines referencing specific profile details, creating instant relevance. Messages send during peak LinkedIn's peak activity hours. The system also A/B tests different message structures to optimize performance.

Pro tip: Include a clear, low-commitment CTA like "Would you be open to a quick call about [specific pain point]?" This outperforms generic "Let's connect" requests.

CRM Integration

The workflow integrates seamlessly with your existing CRM. New qualified leads automatically create contact records with all collected data - name, company, LinkedIn URL, and any profile insights.

Respondents can be routed to specific sales sequences based on their engagement level of engagement. The system also updates contact records when prospects move through different nurturing stages.

Two-way sync: The n8n workflow checks your CRM before outreach, preventing duplicate messaging to existing contacts while enriching their records with new data.
