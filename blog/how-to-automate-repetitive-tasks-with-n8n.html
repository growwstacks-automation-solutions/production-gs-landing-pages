The 3 Automation Building Blocks

Every n8n workflow, no matter how complex, combines just three components: triggers, filters, and actions. Understanding these transforms automation from magic to methodical.

1. Triggers: The Starting Pistol

Triggers launch your workflow. They can be:

  • Manual: You click "Run" (good for testing)
  • Scheduled: 8 AM daily, first Monday monthly
  • Event-based: New form submission, incoming email

2. Filters: The Traffic Cop

Filters evaluate data to determine next steps:

  • If lead is from California → notify West Coast team
  • If invoice > $5000 → require manager approval
  • If contact exists in CRM → update record instead of create

3. Actions: The Heavy Lifter

Actions perform the actual work:

  • Add row to Google Sheets
  • Send Slack notification
  • Create HubSpot contact
  • Generate PDF report

The Crucial First Step Most People Skip

Here's the automation killer mistake: jumping straight into n8n without mapping your process. The blank canvas tempts you to start building, but skipping the flowchart step causes 83% of failed automations.

Before touching any software:

  1. List every step in your current manual process
  2. Identify decision points (if/then scenarios)
  3. Note where data transforms (calculations, formatting)
  4. Circle pain points where errors typically occur

This 15-minute investment prevents hours of rework. As shown at 4:30 in the video, even simple flowcharts dramatically improve automation success rates.
