5-Minute Setup Process

Here's how to get your social media automation running in less time than it takes to manually schedule one week's worth of posts:

Step 1: Generate Your Late API Key

In your Late account settings, create a new API key specifically for Make.com integration. This secure token allows the two services to communicate.

Step 2: Create a New Make.com Scenario

Start a blank scenario in Make.com and search for the Late module. You'll see options for triggering workflows based on new content or pulling specific post types.

Step 3: Connect Your Social Platforms

Add the social media services you use (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.) and authenticate each one. Make.com supports OAuth for secure connections.

Step 4: Map Your Content Fields

Define how Late content fields translate to each social platform's posting requirements - this ensures proper formatting automatically.

Step 5: Activate and Test

Turn on your scenario and test with a sample post. The entire setup typically takes under 5 minutes for basic workflows.

Pro Tip: Start with a simple daily post workflow, then expand to more complex automations as you get comfortable with the platform.
