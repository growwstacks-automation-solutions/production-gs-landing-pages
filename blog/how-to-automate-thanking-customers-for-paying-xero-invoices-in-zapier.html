Zapier + Xero Integration Overview

This automation connects Xero's accounting data with Gmail's email capabilities through Zapier's workflow automation. The three-step process works like this:

  1. Trigger: Xero detects a new payment
  2. Action 1: Zapier retrieves the full invoice details including PDF
  3. Action 2: Gmail sends a personalized thank you email with attached receipt

The entire workflow executes within minutes of payment receipt, with zero manual intervention required. At 2:15 in the video tutorial, you'll see how the trigger configuration captures all relevant payment details.

Step 1: Trigger on New Payments

The automation begins when Xero records a payment. In Zapier:

  1. Create a new Zap
  2. Select Xero as the trigger app
  3. Choose "New Payment" as the trigger event
  4. Connect your Xero account

This trigger provides the payment amount, customer email, invoice number, and other key details needed for the thank you email. The tutorial shows how to map these fields at 3:42.

Step 2: Retrieve Invoice Details

After the trigger, we need the full invoice to attach as a PDF:

  1. Add an action step in Zapier
  2. Select Xero again
  3. Choose "Find Invoice" as the action
  4. Search by invoice number from the trigger

Pro Tip: Always test this step (shown at 5:18) to verify Zapier can successfully retrieve the invoice PDF before configuring the email.

Step 3: Send Personalized Thank You

The final step creates and sends the email:

  1. Add another action step
  2. Select Gmail
  3. Choose "Send Email"
  4. Configure these fields:
    • To: Customer email from trigger
    • Subject: "Thank you for your payment on invoice #[invoice number]"
    • Body: Personalized message with customer name and payment amount
    • Attachments: Invoice PDF from the find step

At 7:55 in the video, you'll see how to switch to HTML mode for branded email templates.

Testing and Troubleshooting

Before going live:

  1. Run test payments through Xero
  2. Verify each step works correctly
  3. Check your inbox for the test email
  4. Confirm the PDF attachment is correct

Common issues and fixes:

  • Missing PDF: Recheck the invoice find step mapping
  • Wrong customer: Verify email field mapping
  • Delayed emails: Upgrade Zapier plan for faster processing

Advanced Customization Options

Once the basic workflow works, consider these enhancements:

  • Branded templates: Create HTML emails with your logo and colors
  • Payment method variants: Different messages for credit cards vs bank transfers
  • Amount-based messaging: Special thanks for large payments
  • CRM updates: Add the interaction to your CRM automatically

Best Practice: Keep a library of 3-5 thank you message variants that rotate randomly to avoid sounding robotic.
