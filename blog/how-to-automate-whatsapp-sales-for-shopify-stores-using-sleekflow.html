How SleekFlow Integrates with Shopify

Connecting SleekFlow to your Shopify store takes just minutes through their commerce hub. Once linked, SleekFlow automatically syncs your:

  • Product catalog with images, variants, and pricing
  • Inventory levels in real-time
  • Customer order history
  • Abandoned cart data

This integration means your sales team (or automated flows) can access all product information directly within WhatsApp conversations. At 3:12 in the video, you'll see how the product catalog appears right in the chat interface.

Automated Product Suggestions in Chat

When a customer messages your WhatsApp number, SleekFlow can automatically suggest relevant products based on:

  • Their browsing history on your Shopify store
  • Previous purchases
  • Keywords in their messages ("looking for blue sneakers")
  • AI analysis of their needs

The system displays products in a carousel format within WhatsApp, with images, prices, and an "Add to Cart" button. Customers can browse your catalog without ever leaving the chat.

Pro tip: Configure your most profitable or seasonal items as default suggestions to maximize revenue from each conversation.

Cart Management Without Leaving WhatsApp

SleekFlow creates a persistent cart for each customer within WhatsApp. As they add items, the system:

  1. Tracks all selected products and variants
  2. Calculates totals including shipping and taxes
  3. Updates in real-time if items go out of stock
  4. Can apply discount codes automatically

Customers see their cart summary directly in the chat, eliminating the need to visit your store to complete purchases. At 5:45 in the video, watch how easily items can be added and removed from the cart within WhatsApp.

Instant Payment Link Generation

When a customer is ready to purchase, SleekFlow generates a unique, secure payment link tied to their exact cart contents. This link:

  • Directs to your Shopify checkout with all items pre-loaded
  • Includes shipping options the customer selected in chat
  • Expires after a set time to prevent fraud
  • Tracks conversions back to the WhatsApp conversation

The payment process happens entirely within WhatsApp's secure environment, reducing friction and increasing completion rates. Businesses report 30-50% higher conversion rates compared to traditional checkout flows.

AI-Powered Customer Service Flows

SleekFlow's AI capabilities handle routine inquiries automatically, freeing your team for complex questions. The AI can:

  • Answer common product questions using your Shopify product descriptions
  • Suggest complementary items based on purchase history
  • Provide order status updates by accessing Shopify data
  • Escalate to human agents when needed

At 7:20 in the video, you'll see how the AI seamlessly transitions between automated responses and human support based on conversation complexity.

Managing Multiple Shopify Stores

For agencies or brands with multiple Shopify stores, SleekFlow provides:

  • Unified inbox for all store conversations
  • Separate product catalogs for each store
  • Store-specific automated flows
  • Performance analytics by store

This centralized management saves hours compared to handling each store's customer service separately. You can even configure cross-store recommendations when appropriate.

Implementation note: When connecting multiple stores, clearly name each connection and train your team on which products belong to which brand to avoid confusion.
