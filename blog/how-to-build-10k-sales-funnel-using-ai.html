Key Components of a $10K Funnel

Every high-converting sales funnel needs three core pages working together seamlessly. Each serves a specific purpose in moving prospects toward your offer:

1. The Opt-In Page

This is your lead capture machine - designed to convert visitors into subscribers by offering valuable content (lead magnet) in exchange for their email. Effective opt-in pages focus on one clear benefit and remove all distractions.

2. The Webinar Registration/Thank You Page

After opt-in, visitors land here to register for your webinar or access your lead magnet. This page reinforces the value they'll receive and builds anticipation for your presentation.

3. The Sales Page

Your money page - where you make the offer and close the sale. A well-designed sales page addresses objections, demonstrates value, and makes the purchase decision easy.

Tracking is critical: GoHighLevel provides detailed analytics on each page's performance - views, opt-ins, registrations, and sales. This data shows exactly where your funnel needs optimization.

Setting Up Claude AI for Funnel Creation

The foundation of successful AI funnel building is proper setup. Before writing any code, you need to equip Claude with everything it needs to understand your project:

Step 1: Create the claude.md File

This is your project's "battle plan" that Claude references throughout development. It should include:

  • Funnel structure and page requirements
  • GoHighLevel-specific coding constraints
  • Brand guidelines and design references
  • Key objectives and success metrics

Step 2: Establish Brand Guidelines

Create a brand.md file with:

  • Color palette and fonts
  • Tone of voice guidelines
  • Competitor references to avoid
  • Examples of designs you like

Step 3: Install Essential Skills

Claude needs specific capabilities for funnel creation:

  • Front-end designer skill
  • Landing page mastery skill
  • CRO (conversion rate optimization) skill

Pro Tip: Start each page in a new chat session to avoid context overload. Claude can reference the claude.md file for continuity between sessions.

Never start coding until the client approves the design mockups. This prevents wasted effort on revisions later.

Step 3: AI Implementation

Feed the approved designs to Claude with:

  • Specific section-by-section instructions

AI-Assisted Copywriting That Converts

Great design needs great copy to convert. Here's how to leverage AI for persuasive sales copy:

Step 1: Use a Copy Framework

Structure your copy using proven formulas like:

  • Problem-Agitation-Solution
  • Before-After-Bridge
  • Features-Advantages-Benefits

Step 2: AI First Draft

Feed your framework and product details to an AI copy tool to generate the first draft. This handles 80% of the heavy lifting.

Step 3: Human Refinement

Add your unique voice and:

  • Strengthen emotional triggers
  • Sharpen value propositions
  • Inject client-specific social proof

Conversion Tip: Always get copy approved before designing the page. This prevents costly redesigns when copy changes.
