The featured implementation handled over 1500 conversations in just 6 months - answering FAQs, qualifying leads, and providing a unique interactive experience. Unlike chatbots, voice agents create memorable engagements that visitors actually enjoy using.

Step 1: Retail AI Agent Setup

Creating your voice agent begins in Retail AI's interface. The platform offers different agent types, but for website deployment, we recommend starting with a "Single Prompt Agent" - simpler to implement while still powerful.

After naming your agent (like "Website Receptionist"), you'll configure its core personality and responses. This includes selecting a voice (Haley is currently popular), response speed, and the GPT model powering its intelligence (GPT-4.1 Mini offers good balance of cost and capability).

Crafting Effective Voice Prompts

Voice agents require different prompting than text chatbots. At 2:45 in the video, we demonstrate using a specialized GPT to generate initial prompts tailored for voice interactions.

Effective voice prompts should:

  • Establish clear personality and tone upfront
  • Include example dialogues for common scenarios
  • Set expectations about what the agent can/can't do
  • Guide the conversation toward business goals

Remember: Voice interactions feel more personal than text. Your prompt should create an engaging, helpful persona that reflects your brand.

Step 2: Website Integration

With your Retail AI agent configured, the next step is connecting it to your website using the Web Call API. This is where Google Anti-gravity shines - it can generate the necessary implementation code based on simple instructions.

The integration requires:

  1. Your Retail AI agent ID
  2. A Retail API key (create a dedicated one for this project)
  3. Basic design preferences for the call button

At 7:20 in the video, we show how to provide these details to Anti-gravity and receive ready-to-deploy code. The result? A click-to-talk button that initiates voice conversations directly on your site.

Enhancing with Knowledge Base

Basic agents answer from their prompt alone. For deeper capability, Retail AI lets you upload entire websites to the agent's knowledge base (demonstrated at 11:30 in the video).

This allows your agent to:

  • Reference specific product pages
  • Quote accurate pricing information
  • Explain services in detail
  • Provide consistent answers with your website content

The knowledge base automatically updates when your site changes, keeping the agent's information current without manual prompt adjustments.

Conversation Analytics and Tracking

Retail AI provides comprehensive analytics for every conversation your agent handles. You can:

  • Review full transcripts
  • Identify frequently asked questions
  • Track average call duration
  • Monitor conversion rates

These insights help refine both your agent's responses and your overall customer experience. Many businesses discover unmet customer needs through analysis of these conversations.

Overcoming Implementation Challenges

Like any new technology, voice agents present some implementation challenges:

Design Integration: The initial button placement might not match your site's aesthetic. This is easily adjusted through your website builder or by modifying the Anti-gravity instructions.

Unexpected User Behavior: Some visitors will test the agent's limits with off-topic questions. Your prompt should include graceful handling of such interactions while gently guiding back to relevant topics.

Technical Limitations: Browser permissions may require users to explicitly enable microphone access. Clear instructions improve first-time user success rates.
