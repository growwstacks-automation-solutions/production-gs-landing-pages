Three Types of AI Agents You Can Build

Business owners know they need to be available 24/7 to answer customer questions and capture leads, but staffing around the clock is expensive and impractical. KnockKnock solves this with three specialized AI agents that work together to handle all customer interactions.

The chat agent manages text-based conversations through your website, the voice agent handles phone calls with natural-sounding speech, and the autoconnect agent monitors visitor behavior to automatically engage high-intent prospects. Together, they create a complete automated customer service and sales system.

85% of customers expect immediate responses to their inquiries, yet most businesses take hours or days to reply. AI agents can respond in seconds, dramatically improving customer satisfaction and conversion rates.

Setting Up Your Knowledge Base

Before creating your agents, you need to train them with your business information. The easiest way is to simply provide your website URL - the system will crawl your site and learn everything about your products, services, and company in about 2-3 minutes.

For e-commerce businesses, you can integrate directly with Shopify or WooCommerce to give your AI real-time access to product details, inventory levels, and order information. This ensures your agents always provide accurate, up-to-date answers about what you sell.

If you have information not on your website (like internal policies or specialized FAQs), you can manually add it through the text field. The system also connects to your Google Calendar, allowing agents to schedule appointments directly.

Configuring the Chat Agent

The chat agent handles all text-based customer interactions through your website. You can choose from different AI models (GPT-4.1 Mini up to GPT-5) depending on your needs and budget.

In the behavior settings, you'll define the agent's personality and response style. Give it a name that fits your brand, then specify its role, purpose, and tone. Detailed instructions tell it how to greet customers, what topics to avoid, and when to escalate to a human.

Routing logic determines how conversations flow: AI-only mode lets the agent handle everything while notifying you, while AI-to-human gives visitors the option to switch to a live person. The chat flow builder lets you create custom conversation paths for common scenarios.

You can fully customize the chat interface's appearance - colors, header, footer, and even upload an avatar image. This maintains brand consistency while the AI handles the actual conversations.

Creating Your Voice Agent

The voice agent brings your AI to phone conversations using ElevenLabs' lifelike speech synthesis. After selecting your preferred model, you'll choose from multiple voice options and languages to find the perfect fit for your brand.

Similar to the chat agent, you'll define the voice agent's name and behavior settings. These instructions determine how it handles calls - what information to provide, how to qualify leads, and when to transfer to your team.

The routing options (AI-only or AI-to-human) work the same as with chat. When configured properly, callers won't realize they're speaking with AI until you want them to - the voices sound completely natural and can handle complex conversations.

The Game-Changing Autoconnect Agent

While chat and voice agents respond to initiated conversations, the autoconnect agent proactively engages visitors based on their behavior. It monitors every site visitor in real-time, calculating an "intent score" that predicts how likely they are to convert.

When a visitor's intent score crosses your threshold (like someone spending time on your pricing page), the agent automatically sends them a targeted message. For example: "Did you know our basic plan starts at $200? Would you like me to book a demo?"

Businesses using autoconnect see 3-5x more leads because it engages visitors at the exact moment they're most interested, rather than waiting for them to reach out. The agent can even initiate audio/video calls with high-intent prospects automatically.

You'll receive instant notifications when the agent identifies a hot lead, and it can automatically schedule meetings via your connected calendar. This transforms passive website browsing into active sales conversations without any staff involvement.

Training and Improving Your AI

The AI Conversations section shows every response your agents have given. If you're not satisfied with an answer, you can edit it directly - the system learns from your corrections and will use the improved version for similar future questions.

This continuous feedback loop means your agents get smarter over time. As you refine responses to common inquiries, the AI adapts to better match your preferred communication style and business policies.

Regularly reviewing conversations also helps identify gaps in your knowledge base. If the AI struggles with certain questions, you can add that information to your training materials to ensure complete, accurate responses going forward.
