6-Step Process to Build Your First Agent

Building a revenue-generating AI voice agent requires just six straightforward steps. Focus on high-value niches like dental clinics or real estate where each call carries significant monetary value.

Step 1: Choose Your Niche

Specialization is key. Dental clinics alone represent a $200 million opportunity in the US. Research the 20 most common questions for your chosen niche - for dentists, this includes pricing, insurance acceptance, and emergency care availability.

Step 2: Create the Personality

Define your agent's tone and demeanor. A dental agent should sound warm and reassuring to calm anxious patients. Voice cloning tools can even match your client's preferred accent or language.

Step 3: Upload Business Knowledge

Feed the agent all relevant information - services, pricing, locations, cancellation policies, and insurance details. This creates an instant expert that can accurately answer 90% of common inquiries.

Step 4: Design the Conversation Flow

Use visual blocks to map out call scenarios: appointment booking, FAQ responses, lead capture, and emergency protocols. Integrate with Calendly for real-time scheduling and Twilio for SMS confirmations.

Step 5: Connect a Phone Number

Purchase a local number through services like Twilio ($1-$2/month) and route all calls to your AI agent. Businesses keep their existing numbers while gaining 24/7 coverage.

Step 6: Test and Refine

Make 10 test calls to identify gaps. Aim for 92% resolution rate with under 3-second response latency. Continuous improvement ensures maximum client satisfaction.

Implementation timeline: From zero to fully functional agent in under 3 hours. Charge $1,500-$5,000 per implementation with 80-95% profit margins.
