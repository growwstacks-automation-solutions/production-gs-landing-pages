Structured Data Outputs That CRMs Love

The real power comes from Vapi's ability to return structured JSON data from each call. We configure three critical outputs:

1. Qualification Verdict

Every lead receives one of three scores:

  • Qualified: Verified contact with buying signals
  • Maybe: Right profile but needs nurturing
  • Unqualified: Wrong contact or no interest

2. Reason Codes

Specific tags explaining the decision:

  • "Contact verified - expressed interest"
  • "Title mismatch - not decision maker"
  • "Invalid contact information"

3. Conversation Insights

Key phrases and buying signals extracted from the transcript help sales teams personalize follow-ups.

Make.com Integration: The Connection Hub

Make.com serves as the central nervous system connecting Vapi to your CRM and databases. The workflow:

Step 1: Lead List Processing

New leads trigger the automation which:

  • Checks for duplicate contacts
  • Validates email/phone formatting
  • Appends firmographic data

Step 2: Call Initiation

Make passes lead details to Vapi including:

  • Name and verified contact info
  • Company and title from your CRM
  • Custom call script variables

Step 3: Result Handling

When Vapi returns the qualification data, Make:

  • Updates CRM records
  • Routes hot leads to sales
  • Tags unqualified contacts
  • Triggers nurture sequences
