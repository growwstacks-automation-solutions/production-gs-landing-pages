Outbound Agent: Cold Calling Automation

While inbound agents answer calls, outbound agents initiate them - perfect for appointment reminders, follow-ups, or lead generation. The tutorial at 19:30 shows how to build a cold calling agent that:

  • Pulls contacts from a Google Sheet
  • Calls each number sequentially

The key difference? Instead of assigning a phone number for incoming calls, you configure the agent to use n8n (formerly n8n) for triggering outbound calls based on your contact list.

Connecting to Google Sheets via n8n

The n8n workflow (22:40 in video) bridges your contact list to Retell AI through three simple nodes:

1. Manual Trigger

Starts the workflow - can later be scheduled or triggered by other events.

2. Google Sheets Node

Reads your contact list with names and phone numbers.

3. HTTP Request Node

Sends each contact to Retell AI's API with your agent ID and calling parameters.

The demo at 25:15 shows the system successfully calling the first contact from the sheet with personalized messaging. This same framework works for appointment reminders, satisfaction surveys, or other outbound campaigns.

Legal Considerations for AI Calling

Before deploying outbound agents, note these compliance essentials from 21:10 in the tutorial:

  • TCPA Compliance: In the US, you must have prior express written consent for sales calls to mobile numbers
  • DNC Lists: Scrubbing against Do Not Call registries is mandatory for cold outreach
  • Disclosure: Many jurisdictions require AI agents to identify themselves as automated systems

For service calls (appointment confirmations, etc.), rules are more lenient but still require opt-out mechanisms. When in doubt, consult legal counsel before launching campaigns.
