What Are Enterprise AI Agents?

Enterprise AI agents are intelligent assistants that can automate complex business tasks by connecting to your data sources and systems. Unlike simple chatbots that just answer questions, these agents can perform actual work - retrieving information from multiple systems, processing data, making decisions, and completing workflows.

The breakthrough is that you can now build these powerful agents without writing code. Modern platforms provide visual interfaces where you simply connect your data sources, define the agent's capabilities through natural language prompts, and configure workflows through drag-and-drop builders.

Key difference: Traditional automation requires scripting each step, while AI agents understand intent and can figure out how to accomplish tasks by connecting available tools and data sources.

The agent connects to your calendar and conferencing system. After each meeting, it:
