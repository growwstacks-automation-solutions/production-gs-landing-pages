Crafting Your Agent's Personality

The system prompt defines your agent's core behavior. Unlike generic chatbots, you can specify nuanced personality traits that persist across conversations. For a professional business assistant, use this framework:

Example system prompt: "You are an advanced AI assistant modeled after a refined, calm digital butler. Speak with confident clarity and soft precision. Prioritize efficiency and helpfulness while maintaining polite, articulate responses with subtle humor when appropriate."

Next, craft the greeting message - the agent's first words when a conversation begins. For customer service applications: "Thank you for contacting [Business Name]. My name is [Agent Name], your virtual assistant. How may I help you today?"

Choosing the Perfect Voice

ElevenLabs offers dozens of pre-made voices across genders, ages and accents. For business use:

  • Jason - Neutral North American accent ideal for general business
  • Emily - Clear, professional female voice for customer service
  • Ethan - Warm, approachable tone for healthcare or education

Preview each voice by having it read your greeting message. Listen for natural pacing and clear pronunciation of industry terms. The voice should match your brand personality - a law firm might prefer formal tones while a startup could use more casual delivery.

Testing and Refining Your Agent

After saving your agent, test it with real-world questions it might encounter. Common refinement areas:

  1. Overly verbose responses: Add "Keep responses under 2 sentences unless explaining complex concepts" to your system prompt
  2. Off-topic replies: Specify "If asked about unrelated topics, politely redirect to [your business focus]"
  3. Emotional tone: Adjust descriptors like "friendly but professional" or "authoritative yet approachable"

Small, targeted prompt edits often solve issues better than lengthy overhauls. Record test calls to identify areas needing adjustment.
