5-Minute Build: Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1: Access Google's AI Studio

Navigate to Google's AI Studio (formerly Bard) and select the Gemini Pro Preview model. This gives you access to the same powerful AI that handles complex Google Search queries.

Step 2: Craft Your Instruction Prompt

The key is writing clear instructions about your business. For our salon example, we included: business name/location, services offered, pricing ranges, and booking policies. The AI uses this to generate appropriate responses.

Step 3: Test in the Playground

Before deploying, simulate customer calls to refine responses. At 2:15 in the video, you'll see how we tested phrases like "Can I get braids this afternoon?" to ensure the AI understands time constraints.

Pro Tip: Add 2-3 example conversations to your prompt. This "trains" the AI to handle your most common booking scenarios naturally.
