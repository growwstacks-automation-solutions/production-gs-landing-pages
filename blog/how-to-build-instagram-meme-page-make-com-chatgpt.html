Setting Up the Workflow

In Make.com, create a new scenario (their term for workflow) with these key components:

Step 1: Schedule Trigger

Set your posting frequency - daily is ideal for meme pages. This tells the system when to generate and post new content.

Step 2: ChatGPT Integration

Connect your OpenAI account and configure the prompt that will generate your meme captions.

Step 3: Image Search

Add a stock photo service like Pexels or Unsplash to find matching background images.

Step 4: Instagram Post

Connect your Business account and map the outputs from previous steps to the post fields.

In summary: 1) Set schedule → 2) Generate text → 3) Find image → 4) Post to Instagram. The entire process runs automatically once configured.

Crafting the Perfect Prompt

Your ChatGPT prompt determines meme quality. Vague prompts produce generic results, while specific ones generate gold.

Instead of "Make a funny meme," try: "Create a short, relatable caption about boring Monday meetings in corporate America. Include 5 relevant hashtags like #MondayMotivation #OfficeLife." The video at 5:20 shows prompt engineering in action.

Pro tip: Maintain a spreadsheet of your top-performing prompts and rotate through them. This creates variety while maintaining quality.

Automating Image Selection

The right image makes or breaks a meme. Automate this by:

  • Using specific search terms like "bored office worker" or "stressful meeting"
  • Filtering for horizontal images with blank space for text
  • Setting color preferences that match your brand
