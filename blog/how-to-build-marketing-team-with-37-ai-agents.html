The 37-Agent Marketing Team Breakdown

Traditional marketing teams require specialists for each channel - social media managers, content writers, designers, SEO experts, and sales qualifiers. The 37-agent system replaces all these roles with specialized AI workers that collaborate seamlessly.

The system includes:

  • Content Team: LinkedIn post writer, newsletter agent, X (Twitter) writer, presentation creator
  • Visual Team: Image generator for personal photos and infographics
  • SEO Team: Keyword researcher, article orchestrator, content outliner and writer
  • Sales Team: Lead qualifier, outreach agent, appointment setter
  • Support Team: Google Drive manager, Notion assistant, research agent

Key advantage: Unlike human teams where specialists work in silos, these AI agents share context and collaborate on projects - like when the SEO team creates multiple articles in a topic cluster while the content team promotes them across channels.

Revolutionary Chat Feature for Agent Control

The biggest challenge with multi-agent systems has been the "broken telephone" effect - where messages passed between multiple agents lose fidelity. Relevance AI's new chat feature solves this by letting you communicate directly with specific agents.

At 4:20 in the video, you can see how tagging multiple content agents (LinkedIn, newsletter, X) with a single YouTube link generates coordinated cross-channel content in your brand voice. The agents work sequentially but maintain consistent messaging.

Memory feature: Agents can be instructed to remember specific outputs as reference points. At 7:15, the demo shows telling an agent to remember a post as an example of brand voice - ensuring future content maintains consistency.
