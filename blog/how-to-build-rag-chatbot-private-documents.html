RAG Explained: How It Works Without Fine-Tuning

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines document search with AI in a three-step pipeline:

  1. Chunking: Breaks documents into manageable sections (e.g., paragraphs or pages)
  2. Embedding: Converts text to numerical vectors that capture meaning (like GPS coordinates for ideas)
  3. Retrieval & Generation: Finds relevant chunks when questions are asked, then synthesizes answers

Unlike fine-tuning which retrains the entire AI model (costing thousands), RAG simply references your documents in real-time. At 3:12 in the video, you'll see how the embedding model processes Discord messages into searchable vectors while preserving all original meaning.
