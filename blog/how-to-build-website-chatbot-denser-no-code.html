What Makes Denser's Chatbot Different

Traditional chatbots either require complex coding or provide generic answers that frustrate visitors. Denser solves both problems by combining automatic website indexing with retrieval-based AI responses.

The system scans your entire site structure (like the demo showing 22 URLs indexed in seconds), then builds a knowledge base that answers questions using your actual content. Visitors see answers with clickable references back to your pages - increasing trust and engagement.

Key advantage: Unlike rule-based chatbots, Denser adapts automatically when you update your website content. No manual retraining needed - just reindex your changed pages.

The 4-Step Setup Process

Creating your chatbot takes less time than most lunch breaks. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Create New Chatbot

Select "Website Chatbot" from Denser's dashboard. Name your bot (like "Retrieval Demo" in the video) and click create.

Step 2: Add Knowledge Base

Enter your website URL (e.g. retriever.denser.ai). Specify URL patterns to include/exclude certain pages if needed.

Step 3: Start Indexing

Denser scans all linked pages (22 in the demo) and extracts their content. This happens in seconds for most sites.

Step 4: Deploy

Copy the embed code and paste it into your website's HTML. The chatbot appears instantly with your branding.

Pro tip: Enable "Suggested Questions" in advanced settings to guide visitors toward common queries about your products/services.
