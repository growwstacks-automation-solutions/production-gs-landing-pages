Most businesses using GHL's Voice AI widget struggle to collect visitor contact information - especially phone numbers. Unlike inbound calls where numbers are automatic, website chat interactions leave you guessing who you're talking to. Here's how to automatically capture phone numbers, emails, and names through simple conversational prompts.

The GHL Phone Capture Problem

When using GHL's Voice AI widget for website chat, you face a critical limitation: the system doesn't automatically collect visitor contact information like it does with phone calls. This leaves you talking to anonymous visitors who might be ready to buy - but you have no way to follow up if they leave the chat.

The core issue stems from how the widget was designed. For inbound calls, the system captures the caller ID automatically. But for website interactions, there's no built-in mechanism to request and store contact details. This creates a frustrating gap where valuable leads slip through your fingers daily.

68% of website visitors will provide contact information if asked politely during a helpful conversation - but only 12% will fill out a traditional contact form.

Creating the Contact Capture Agent

The solution involves creating a specialized GHL Voice AI agent designed specifically to collect contact information through natural conversation. At 1:15 in the video, you'll see how to set up this agent with just three simple actions.

Start by creating a new agent in your GHL dashboard. Give it a clear name like "Contact Capture Agent" and write a friendly introduction message. The key is to make the conversation feel helpful, not interrogative. Your agent should explain why you're asking for information and how it will benefit the visitor.

For the prompt, keep it simple at first. You'll want to collect three key pieces of information:

  1. Full name (ask them to spell it if needed)
  2. Phone number (with confirmation)
  3. Email address

Setting Up Capture Actions

The magic happens in the actions section where you'll configure how the captured information gets stored. Create three separate actions - one for each piece of information you want to collect (name, phone, email).

For phone numbers, use the "Update Contact" action and select the standard phone field in GHL. The system will automatically store the number in the correct format. At 2:30 in the video, you can see how to set up the capture confirmation that ensures accurate data collection.

Pro Tip: Create a custom single-line text field called "Voice AI Capture" to store the raw conversation data for reference. This helps with troubleshooting if any information gets misrecorded.

The workflow trigger is equally important. Set it to activate whenever the contact capture field changes. This ensures your CRM gets updated in real-time as visitors provide their information through the chat.

Testing the Workflow

Before going live, thoroughly test your contact capture agent. At 4:15 in the video, you'll see a live demonstration of how the conversation flows from the visitor's perspective.

Initiate a test chat through your website widget. The agent should:

  1. Greet the visitor and offer assistance
  2. Ask for their name and confirm the spelling
  3. Request their phone number and repeat it back
  4. Ask for their email address
  5. Thank them and explain next steps

Check your GHL contact records to verify all information was captured correctly. Pay special attention to phone numbers - they're the most likely to have entry errors. The confirmation step in your workflow should catch most mistakes before they reach your CRM.

Where Data Gets Stored

All captured information flows directly into your GHL CRM, just like traditional form submissions. Phone numbers populate the standard phone field, emails go to the email field, and names update the contact record.

At 5:45 in the video, you can see how the test contact's information appears in the GHL dashboard. The system creates a complete contact profile with all the details collected through the Voice AI conversation.

This integration means you can:

  • Trigger your standard follow-up sequences
  • Add contacts to appropriate tags and lists
  • Notify your sales team in real-time
  • Track the source as "Voice AI Website Chat"

The method effectively bridges the gap between anonymous website chats and actionable lead records in your CRM.
