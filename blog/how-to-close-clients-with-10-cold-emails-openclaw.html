Why Most Cold Emails Fail

80-90% of cold outreach fails because it focuses on the sender ("I, I, I") rather than the recipient. Decision-makers receive hundreds of generic pitches weekly, instantly recognizing when an email isn't truly about their needs.

Crafting Winning Emails

The most effective cold emails follow this structure:

1. Immediate Value Focus: First sentence highlights a specific challenge or opportunity for their business

2. Credible Insight: Reference a recent achievement, product launch, or industry trend affecting them

3. Clear Offer: Invite them to receive something valuable (not buy something)
