3-Step Setup: From Lost Orders to Automated Revenue

The AI Voice Now setup process is designed for restaurant owners who need solutions, not tech projects. Unlike traditional POS integrations that take weeks, you can complete the initial configuration in under 5 minutes:

Step 1: Business Information

Start by clicking the "Let's Go" button and entering your restaurant's basic details - name, phone number, and primary location. If you have multiple locations (2:15 in the video), simply indicate this and our team will handle the additional configurations for you.

Step 2: Service Configuration

Select whether you offer pickup only or pickup and delivery. Delivery restaurants will need to provide delivery zones and fees. Then choose how you want orders delivered - either to a dedicated tablet or directly into your POS system.

Step 3: Call Handling Preferences

Set your call forwarding number for human escalation (when the AI encounters complex requests), payment processing preferences, and select between pay-per-use or subscription pricing. All fields have helpful explanations and none are mandatory - you can always update them later.

Pro tip: Even if you're unsure about some configuration details, submit the form with what you know. Our team will follow up to complete any missing information and ensure the system matches your exact workflow.

Configuration Options That Fit Your Workflow

AI Voice Now adapts to your existing operations rather than forcing you to change how you work. During setup, you'll make three key decisions that customize the system for your restaurant:

Menu Integration: Upload a PDF of your current menu, provide a link to your online menu, or connect directly to your POS system for automatic synchronization. The AI learns your offerings and pricing, including daily specials if connected to your POS.

Order Delivery Method: Choose between a dedicated tablet that prints tickets like your current system, or direct POS integration that enters orders exactly as your staff would. Both options maintain your existing kitchen workflow.

Payment Processing: Enable secure phone payments through your existing merchant account, or keep payments offline for pickup/delivery. The system supports both approaches without requiring changes to your current payment setup.

Choosing the Right Pricing Model for Your Volume

AI Voice Now offers two simple pricing options designed for different call volumes and business models:

Pay-Per-Use: Ideal for restaurants starting with missed calls only. You pay only for calls the AI handles (typically $0.50-$1 per call), with no monthly commitment. At 30 recovered orders per week, this averages $60-$120/month.

Subscription: Best for high-volume restaurants wanting to handle all inbound calls. Unlimited AI order taking for $99-$199/month depending on features. Typically pays for itself after 100-200 calls.

No wrong choice: You can switch between models at any time. Many restaurants start with pay-per-use to validate results, then upgrade to subscription after seeing the impact on their revenue.
