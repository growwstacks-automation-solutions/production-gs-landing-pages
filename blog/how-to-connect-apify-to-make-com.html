Why Connect Apify to Make.com?

Businesses waste countless hours manually transferring data from web scrapers to their business applications. Apify excels at extracting website data, while Make.com specializes in automating workflows between apps. Together, they create a powerful automation pipeline that eliminates manual data handling.

By connecting these platforms, you can automatically send scraped product prices to your CRM, push collected leads to your email marketing tool, or feed market research data directly into your analytics dashboards - all without touching a spreadsheet.

Time savings: What typically takes 2-3 hours of manual work daily can be fully automated, freeing up your team for higher-value tasks while ensuring data flows consistently into your systems.

Step 1: Access Your Make.com Dashboard

Begin by logging into your Make.com account. The dashboard is your control center where all automation scenarios are managed. If you don't have an account yet, sign up for a free plan - it's sufficient for testing this integration.

Once logged in, familiarize yourself with the interface. The left sidebar contains the main navigation, while the center area displays your existing scenarios. For this integration, we'll be working in the "Scenarios" section where you create and manage automated workflows.

Step 2: Create a New Scenario

Click the "Create a new scenario" button prominently displayed on your dashboard. This opens a blank canvas where you'll build your automation. Make.com calls these automated workflows "scenarios" - think of them as recipes that tell apps how to work together.

Name your scenario something descriptive like "Apify to CRM Integration" so you can easily identify it later. The naming convention is important as your number of automations grows - clear names prevent confusion down the line.

Step 3: Connect the Apify Module

In the scenario editor, click "Add module" and search for "Apify". Select the Apify connector from the list of available apps. This establishes the technical bridge between Make.com and Apify's API.

Choose the "Run actor task" action - this is the most common way to integrate Apify, allowing you to execute your predefined scrapers (called "actors" in Apify). You'll need your Apify API token, which you can find in your Apify account settings under "Integrations".

Pro tip: Store your API keys securely in Make.com's "Connections" section rather than hardcoding them into scenarios. This makes them reusable across multiple automations.

Step 4: Authorize the Connection

Make.com will prompt you to authorize access to your Apify account. Click the authorization button and log into Apify when redirected. This OAuth process creates a secure link between the platforms without exposing your credentials.

After authorization returns you to Make.com, select which Apify actor (scraper) you want to run. You can choose from your personal actors or public actors in Apify's store. Configure any required inputs like URLs to scrape or data extraction parameters.

Step 5: Test and Save Your Scenario

Run a test execution to verify the connection works. Make.com will trigger your Apify actor and display the scraped results. Check that the data structure matches what you expect before proceeding.

Once confirmed, add subsequent modules to process the Apify data - like sending it to Google Sheets, your CRM, or another destination. Finally, save your scenario and set it to run on a schedule or trigger (like receiving new data).

Success: You've now built a complete automation pipeline that runs web scrapers and processes their results without manual intervention. This same pattern can be adapted for countless data collection and processing workflows.
