Zapier's webhook integration with Rebrandly eliminates these delays completely. When someone clicks your branded link, Zapier can instantly trigger a chain reaction across your entire tech stack - updating CRMs, sending Slack alerts, segmenting email lists, and more.

5,000+ app connections: Unlike native integrations that only connect two platforms, Zapier webhooks let Rebrandly trigger actions across virtually any tool in your marketing stack simultaneously. One click can update multiple systems at once.

How Webhooks Work (Baseball Analogy)

Webhooks can seem technical, but the concept is simple. Think of Rebrandly as a pitcher throwing baseballs (click data) to Zapier, which acts as the catcher.

In the configuration (around 2:15 in the video), you'll select "Catch Hook" as your trigger type. This tells Zapier to wait for Rebrandly to send data, just like a catcher waits for the pitcher's throw. The webhook URL you generate is like the catcher's mitt - it's specifically designed to receive data from Rebrandly.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Step 1: Create Your Webhook in Zapier

Start by creating a new Zap in Zapier and select "Webhooks by Zapier" as your trigger app. Choose "Catch Hook" as the trigger event - this prepares Zapier to receive data from Rebrandly.

Step 2: Configure Rebrandly Webhook

In your Rebrandly account settings, navigate to the webhooks section and paste the unique URL Zapier provides. This creates the connection between the two platforms.

Step 3: Test the Connection

Click one of your Rebrandly links to generate test data. Zapier will show you the incoming data structure, allowing you to select which parameters to use in your automation.

Step 4: Add Your Actions

Now add whatever actions you want to trigger - whether that's creating a Slack message, updating a Google Sheet, or modifying a HubSpot contact record.

In summary: 1) Create webhook trigger in Zapier, 2) Connect Rebrandly, 3) Test data flow, 4) Add your desired actions. The entire setup takes most teams under 10 minutes.

Common Issues & Troubleshooting

While the integration is straightforward, here are solutions to the most common challenges teams face:

  • Data not appearing: Double-check that you've copied the entire webhook URL from Zapier into Rebrandly exactly
  • Missing fields: Make sure you've configured which data points to send in Rebrandly's webhook settings
  • Delayed triggers: Webhooks should be near-instant - if delays occur, check your Zapier task history for errors
  • CRM not updating: Verify you've mapped the Rebrandly fields to the correct CRM properties in your Zap

Remember that Zapier's interface will show you exactly what data is being received from Rebrandly, making troubleshooting much easier.
