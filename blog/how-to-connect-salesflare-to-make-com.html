Why Automate Salesflare with Make.com?

Sales teams waste countless hours manually entering data between systems - updating CRM records, creating follow-up tasks, and syncing information across platforms. This not only drains productivity but leads to errors and missed opportunities when data falls through the cracks.

By connecting Salesflare to Make.com, you create a seamless flow of information between your CRM and other business applications. Companies using this integration report saving 5-10 hours per week per salesperson while improving data accuracy by eliminating manual entry.

Key benefit: Automated workflows ensure your Salesflare CRM always contains up-to-date information without any manual work, allowing your team to focus on selling rather than data entry.

Getting Started with Make.com

Make.com (formerly Integromat) provides a visual platform for connecting apps and automating workflows without coding. To begin:

  1. Sign up for a free Make.com account at make.com
  2. Navigate to your dashboard and click "Create a new scenario"
  3. Name your scenario (e.g. "Salesflare Contact Sync")

The scenario canvas is where you'll build your automation by connecting modules - the building blocks that represent actions in different apps. At the 1:15 mark in the video tutorial, you can see how to access the module library and search for Salesflare.

Creating the Salesflare Connection

Before building workflows, you need to authenticate the connection between Make.com and your Salesflare account:

  1. Click the plus icon on your scenario canvas
  2. Search for and select "Salesflare" from the app list
  3. Choose a module (we'll start with "Create a contact")
  4. Click "Add" next to the connection field
  5. Enter your Salesflare API key (found in Settings > API Keys)

Security note: Your API key acts like a password - never share it publicly or include it in documentation. Make.com stores it securely once authenticated.

Building Your First Workflow

Workflows in Make.com typically combine a trigger (what starts the automation) with one or more actions (what happens next). A common first workflow might be:

  1. Trigger: New form submission on your website
  2. Action: Create contact in Salesflare
  3. Action: Add follow-up task in Salesflare

After adding these modules, you'll map fields between them - telling Make.com which pieces of data should go where. For example, map the "Email" field from your form to the "Email" field in Salesflare.

Testing and Activating Your Workflow

Before going live, always test your workflow:

  1. Click "Run once" to execute a test
  2. Check the execution log for errors
  3. Verify the data appears correctly in Salesflare
  4. Make adjustments if needed

Once satisfied, toggle the switch to activate your scenario. Make.com will now automatically process this workflow according to your schedule (instant, every 15 minutes, hourly, etc.).

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Most connection problems stem from a few root causes:

  • Authentication errors: Usually mean your API key has expired or been revoked
  • Field mapping issues: Occur when required fields are missing or data types don't match
  • Rate limits: Salesflare may throttle excessive API calls - space out your workflows

Make.com's execution logs provide detailed error messages to help diagnose problems. For persistent issues, their support documentation covers most common scenarios.
