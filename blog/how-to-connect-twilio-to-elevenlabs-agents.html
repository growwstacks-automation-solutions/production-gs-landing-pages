Twilio Setup: Getting Your First Number

The first step is acquiring a Twilio phone number with voice capabilities. Many beginners make the mistake of buying any available number without checking its features.

Critical detail: You must select a number with the phone handset icon (voice capability), not just SMS. At 1:32 in the video, the tutorial shows exactly how to identify and purchase the correct number type using Twilio's free trial credits.

Step-by-Step Number Purchase:

  1. Log into your Twilio console
  2. Navigate to Phone Numbers > Buy a Number
  3. Filter for numbers with voice capability (handset icon)
  4. Purchase using free trial credits
  5. Copy the number to your clipboard

Twilio's free trial: New accounts receive ~$15 in credits - enough for a phone number and hundreds of test calls. No credit card required for initial setup.

ElevenLabs Configuration Made Simple

Connecting your Twilio number to ElevenLabs is surprisingly simple - no webhook coding required. The platform handles all the technical integration automatically.

At 2:15 in the video, you'll see the exact steps:

  1. Navigate to ElevenLabs' Phone Numbers tab
  2. Click "Import Number" and select Twilio
  3. Label your number (e.g., "Customer Support")
  4. Paste your Twilio phone number
  5. Add your Twilio Account SID and Auth Token
  6. Click Import - ElevenLabs configures everything automatically

Security note: Your Twilio Auth Token acts like a password - keep it confidential and never share it publicly.
