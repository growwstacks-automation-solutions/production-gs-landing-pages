Typeform Trigger Setup

Before connecting to Zapier, your Typeform must be properly configured:

Step 1: Publish Your Form

Unpublished forms can't trigger automations. Click "Publish" in Typeform's upper right and confirm it's live.

Step 2: Enable Response Collection

Under Settings → Responses, ensure "Collect responses" is toggled on. Test your form won't send data to Zapier.

Step 3: Test Submission

Submit test entry to verify your form works. This creates sample data you'll use later when setting up Zapier.

Pro Tip: Use distinct test submissions (like "TEST1", "TEST2") so you can easily identify remove them in your destination apps during setup.

Zapier Automation Steps

With your Typeform ready, create your first "Zap" (Zapier's term for automation):

Step 1: Choose Trigger

Select "Typeform" as trigger app and "New Submission" as trigger event. Zapier will watch for new entries.

StepAccount Connection

Authenticate with your Typeform using OAuth. Grant necessary permissions for Zapier to access your form responses.

Step 3: SelectForm

Choose your published form from dropdown. Zapier will now monitor this specific form for new submissions.

Step 4: Test Trigger

Run test to confirm Zapier can retrieve sample submissions from your Typeform. This verifies if any setup errors exist.

Critical Checkpoint: If your test fails, recheck your Typeform's publish status and response collection settings before proceeding.

Testing & Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

No Data Flowing

Check: 1) Form publish status 2 Zapier account permissions 3 Internet connectivity 4 Zap status (paused/off).

Incorrect Field Mapping

Re-test trigger to refresh available fields. Edit action step to remap fields correctly.

Duplicate Entries

Enable "Only trigger for new submissions" in zap settings. Add filter to check for existing records.

Monitoring Tip: Zapier's Task History shows every automation run, including errors. Check here first when debugging.
