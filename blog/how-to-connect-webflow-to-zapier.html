Connecting Webflow to Zapier in 3 Steps

Unlike complex API integrations that require technical skills, Zapier's Webflow connection works through simple authentication:

  1. Install the Zapier app from Webflow's marketplace (takes 10 seconds)
  2. Authorize your workspace by selecting it from the dropdown
  3. Choose your trigger (like "New Comment" or "Form Submission")

At the 1:15 mark in the tutorial video, you'll see how the authentication flow automatically detects your Webflow sites and asks for one-time permission to access them. This secure OAuth process means you never share passwords directly with Zapier.
