Step 3: Find the Make.com App

With hundreds of apps available, locating Make.com is easiest using the search function. Simply type "Make" or "Make.com" into the search bar at the top of the Xero app store.

The video at 4:20 shows the exact listing we're using - look for the official Make.com app with the blue and white logo. Be cautious of similarly named third-party integrations - the official connector provides the most reliable connection and full feature support.

Step 4: Install the Make.com App

Clicking "Get this app" begins the installation process. Xero will guide you through a series of prompts to authenticate and connect your accounts. This is a secure OAuth process that doesn't require sharing passwords between systems.

At 5:45 in our walkthrough, we demonstrate the permission screen where you'll select which Xero organizations to connect. If you manage multiple companies in Xero, you can choose to connect all of them or select specific organizations for automation.

Step 5: Grant Necessary Permissions

The final step is authorizing data access between systems. Make.com will request permissions to read and write specific types of Xero data depending on your planned workflows.

At 6:30 in the video, we review the permission screen in detail. For most automation scenarios, you'll want to grant access to contacts, invoices, transactions, and reporting data. These permissions can be adjusted later if needed.

Security note: Make.com only accesses the Xero data you explicitly authorize for each workflow. The connection uses bank-level encryption and follows strict data protection standards.

Troubleshooting Tips

While the Xero-Make.com connection is generally reliable, here are solutions to common issues:

Authentication Errors

If you receive an authentication error, simply reauthorize the connection through Xero's connected apps settings. This typically resolves within minutes.

Data Sync Delays

Make.com workflows typically run hourly by default. For time-sensitive processes, adjust the schedule to run every 5-15 minutes in your scenario settings.

Permission Issues

If a workflow fails due to insufficient permissions, revisit the app connection in Xero to grant additional data access as needed.

Remember: Make.com maintains detailed execution logs for every workflow run, making it easy to identify and resolve any issues that arise.
