Getting Your API Key

The first step in connecting Zapier to Craftify AI is obtaining your unique API key. This key acts as a secure bridge between the two platforms, allowing them to communicate while keeping your data protected.

To find your API key:

  1. Log in to your Craftify AI account
  2. Navigate to Settings > Business Settings
  3. Select the Integrations tab
  4. Locate the Zapier integration option
  5. Click "Connect" to generate your API key

This key will be a long string of letters and numbers. Treat it like a password - don't share it publicly or include it in unsecured documents. You'll only need to enter it once in Zapier to establish the permanent connection.

Connecting to Zapier

With your API key in hand, you're ready to connect Craftify AI to your Zapier account. This process establishes the foundation for all your future automations.

In your Zapier account:

  1. Navigate to App Connections (under your account menu)
  2. Search for "Craftify AI" in the app directory
  3. Select "Add Connection"
  4. Paste your Craftify AI API key when prompted
  5. Click "Continue" to complete the connection

Pro Tip: Name your connection something memorable like "Main Craftify Account" if you manage multiple brands or accounts. This makes it easier to select the right connection when building zaps later.

Creating Your First Zap

Now comes the exciting part - building your first automation between Craftify AI and another platform. At 2:45 in the video tutorial, you can see how to set up a basic zap that triggers when new content is created in Craftify AI.

To create a simple content distribution zap:

  1. In Zapier, click "Make a Zap"
  2. Search for and select "Craftify AI" as your trigger app
  3. Choose "New Content Created" as your trigger
  4. Select your Craftify AI connection
  5. Choose an action app (like Twitter, Mailchimp, or WordPress)
  6. Map the content fields from Craftify to your action app
  7. Test and turn on your zap

This basic workflow can be expanded with filters, delays, and multiple actions to create sophisticated content distribution systems that run automatically.

Troubleshooting Tips

While the Zapier-Craftify AI integration is generally reliable, you may occasionally encounter issues. Most problems can be resolved with these troubleshooting steps.

If your zaps stop working:

  1. Verify your API key is still active in both Craftify AI and Zapier
  2. Check that your Craftify AI subscription is current
  3. Review Zapier's status page for any service outages
  4. Test the connection in Zapier's "Connected Accounts" section
  5. Reconnect the apps if necessary

For content-specific issues, ensure your Craftify AI templates are producing the expected output format and that your zap's field mappings are still correct after any platform updates.
