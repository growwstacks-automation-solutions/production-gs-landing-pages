The Script Secret Behind 3.6x ROAS

Traditional UGC scripts fail because they're either too salesy (triggering ad blindness) or too vague (missing conversion triggers). The winning formula uses GPT prompts that replicate high-performing organic content structures:

  1. Specific before/after contrast: "My skin was so dry it would flake by 2pm → Now it stays hydrated through my 10-hour shifts"
  2. Authentic hesitation: "I almost didn't buy because [objection]... but then [social proof]"
  3. Metric-driven results: "Within 3 days I noticed [specific change]"
