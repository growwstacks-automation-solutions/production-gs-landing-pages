Setting Up in GoHighLevel

Let's walk through the exact steps to create your voice clone, as demonstrated at the 2:15 timestamp in the video tutorial.

Step 1: Access Voice Settings

Navigate to Settings → Labs in your GoHighLevel sub-account. Enable both "Voice AI Clone Voice" and "New Voice AI Prompt Builder" toggles. These experimental features power the voice cloning capability.

Step 2: Create Your Voice Clone

Go to AI Agents → Voice AI Agent List. Select any agent and click "My Voices" → "Add Custom Voice" → "Clone". You can either:

  • Upload existing audio files (minimum 30 seconds)
  • Record directly in the platform (recommended for best results)

Step 3: Assign to Agent

Once processed (usually within seconds), select your new voice from the dropdown in any voice AI agent's settings. The agent will now speak with your cloned voice.

Pro Tip: For maximum realism, record 2-3 minutes of varied speech - include highs and lows in your tone, different speaking speeds, and emotional variations.

Pro Tips for Best Results

While the basic process is simple, these professional techniques will make your AI voice clone virtually indistinguishable from the real you.

Script Natural Conversations: Don't just read monotonously. Record actual conversations you might have with clients - greetings, common questions, even small talk. This gives the AI more natural speech patterns to work with.

Use a Quality Microphone: The better your input audio, the better the clone. A USB condenser microphone can dramatically improve results over built-in laptop mics.

Vary Your Speech: Include different emotional tones - excited, concerned, professional, friendly. This gives the AI more vocal range to work with in different call scenarios.

Update Regularly: As the technology improves, re-record your voice samples every 3-6 months to take advantage of better cloning algorithms.
