Creating Your First Voice Agent

Creating a basic voice agent requires just three simple steps:

Step 1: Write Your Prompt

Describe what you want your agent to do in plain English. For a car service reminder bot, you might write: "You are a friendly representative from Mercedes calling to remind customers about upcoming service appointments." OMI Dimension's AI will enhance your prompt with additional context and personality.

Step 2: Choose Guided Flow

The guided flow option walks you through essential configuration steps like language selection and voice customization. This ensures your agent has all the necessary components to function properly.

Step 3: Create Your Agent

Click "Create Agent" and OMI Dimension will generate your voice bot in seconds. The platform handles all the complex AI programming behind the scenes while you focus on the business logic.

Pro tip: Start with a simple agent focused on one specific task before adding more complex functionality. This makes testing and refinement much easier.

Testing Your AI Agent

Before deploying your voice agent, conduct thorough testing using OMI Dimension's web call feature. This lets you simulate real conversations and identify areas needing improvement. In our example:

  • The agent successfully handled the service reminder scenario
  • It offered specific appointment times when asked
  • The conversation flowed naturally with appropriate pauses

However, testing also revealed limitations - the agent struggled with unexpected questions and couldn't access the customer's actual service history. These are common issues you'll want to address before going live.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

When creating your first voice AI agent, watch out for these frequent pitfalls:

  • Overly complex initial prompts: Start simple and gradually add complexity
  • Ignoring the testing phase: Thorough testing prevents embarrassing live failures
  • Choosing the wrong voice: A mismatch with your brand can undermine trust
  • Lack of fallback responses: Prepare your agent for unexpected questions

The car service bot in our example would benefit from integration with a customer database to personalize calls - an enhancement we'll cover in future tutorials.
