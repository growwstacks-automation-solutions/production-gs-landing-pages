How to Enable Outbound AI Voice Agent in GoHighLevel (Step-by-Step Guide)

Many businesses struggle with scaling personalized outbound calls. GoHighLevel's new AI voice agent feature automates this process while maintaining compliance. This guide shows exactly how to activate the beta feature and configure it properly.

Enabling the Beta Feature

Before October 30, 2025, GoHighLevel's AI voice agent for outbound calls was in beta testing. To access it, you'll need to manually enable the feature through your agency's Labs settings. This temporary step won't be required once the feature goes live to all users.

Navigate to your agency view in GoHighLevel, then go to Settings > Labs. In the search, type "voice" to find the "Voice AI Outbound Calling" option. Activate this feature and select which sub-accounts should have access.

Important: After October 30, 2025, this step won't be necessary as the feature will be available to all users automatically. However, you'll still need to complete the setup steps below.

Setting Up Your AI Agent

Once the beta features are enabled, you'll need to configure your specific AI voice agent. Go to AI Agents > Voice AI in your sub-account where you enabled the feature. Here you'll find the "Enable Outbound Calls" option.

The system will prompt you to verify your ID before proceeding. This is a compliance requirement for all outbound calling systems. You'll need to provide identification documents that match your account information.

Creating Your Disclaimer

GoHighLevel requires a custom disclaimer explaining that calls are made by an AI. This disclaimer updates across 14 different forms in your account automatically. You'll need to generate this text yourself using ChatGPT or similar tool.

The disclaimer must be in your own language (English if that's your market) and clearly state the artificial nature of the calls. Once pasted into GoHighLevel, click the blue button to run a compatibility check and apply the disclaimer across your system.

Compliance note: Failure to include proper disclosures may violate telemarketing regulations in many jurisdictions. Consult legal counsel ensure your disclaimer meets requirements.

Understanding Call Limits

During beta testing, GoHighLevel imposes strict limits on AI outbound calls to prevent abuse:

  • 1 call per minute per location
  • Maximum 100 calls per day per location
  • Each phone number can be called once daily
  • No more than 4 calls to same number in two weeks

These limits help maintain system stability during testing and prevent recipients from receiving excessive calls. The company may adjust these thresholds after beta based on performance data.

Building Your First Workflow

With setup complete, you can create automation workflows that trigger AI outbound calls. In your sub-account's Automation section, create a new workflow starting with trigger like "Contact Tag Added."

Add the "Voice AI Outbound Call" action (marked as beta). Associate it with your configured AI agent and verified phone number. The system will automatically respect all call limits you configured earlier.

Pro tip: Combine AI calls with follow-up SMS or emails in your workflow for a multi-channel outreach strategy that increases engagement rates.
