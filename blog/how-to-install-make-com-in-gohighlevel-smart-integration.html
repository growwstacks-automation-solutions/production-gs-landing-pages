How to Install Make.com in GoHighLevel (Smart Integration)

Why This Integration Matters

Most GoHighLevel users know they should be automating their CRM workflows - but the technical hurdle of connecting automation tools stops them before they even begin. Without Make.com integration, you're left manually transferring data between systems, wasting hours each week on repetitive tasks.

When properly connected, Make.com becomes the bridge between GoHighLevel and hundreds of other apps - automatically syncing contacts, triggering follow-ups, and eliminating manual data entry. The installation process is simpler than most users realize, taking just minutes to complete.

Time-saving impact: Businesses using Make.com with GoHighLevel report saving 8-12 hours per week on average by automating lead management, follow-ups, and data synchronization.

Step 1: Locate the App Marketplace

After logging into your GoHighLevel account (as shown at 0:32 in the video), you'll land on the main dashboard. Many users miss the app marketplace because it's tucked away in the left sidebar navigation, below more prominent sections like Calendar and Pipelines.

Scroll down the sidebar until you see the "Marketplace" option - this is your gateway to hundreds of integrations that can expand GoHighLevel's capabilities. Clicking this will open the app marketplace where you can browse or search for Make.com.

Step 2: Find Make.com

The GoHighLevel marketplace contains dozens of apps, which can feel overwhelming. Instead of browsing manually, use the search bar at the top (visible at 0:47 in the tutorial) - simply type "Make" and press enter to filter the results instantly.

Make.com should appear as the first result. Click on its listing to view the integration details page, which explains what the connection enables. Take a moment to review these capabilities - you'll see how Make.com can automate lead capture, contact updates, and workflow triggers between GoHighLevel and other platforms.

Step 3: Install and Configure

When you're ready to proceed, click the "Install" button (shown at 1:15 in the video). GoHighLevel will display the permissions Make.com is requesting - these are standard for the integration to function properly. Don't let the list intimidate you; these permissions allow the automation magic to happen.

After approving the permissions, the installation completes automatically. You'll receive confirmation that Make.com is now connected to your GoHighLevel account. The entire process typically takes less than 2 minutes from start to finish.

Pro tip: Bookmark the Make.com connection in GoHighLevel for easy access later. You'll want to return frequently to build and manage your automations.
