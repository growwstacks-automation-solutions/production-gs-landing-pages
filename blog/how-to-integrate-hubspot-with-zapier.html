Step-by-Step Integration Guide

Connecting HubSpot to Zapier takes less than 5 minutes but unlocks endless automation possibilities. Follow these steps carefully to establish a secure, reliable connection between the platforms.

Step 1: Access the HubSpot Marketplace

Log into your HubSpot account and click the marketplace icon (usually in the top navigation bar). This portal contains all available integrations that extend HubSpot's functionality.

Step 2: Search for Zapier

Type "Zapier" into the marketplace search bar. Select the official Zapier integration from the results - look for the verified checkmark next to the listing.

Step 3: Install the Integration

Click the install button and follow the prompts to connect your Zapier account. You'll need admin permissions in both HubSpot and Zapier to complete this step successfully.
