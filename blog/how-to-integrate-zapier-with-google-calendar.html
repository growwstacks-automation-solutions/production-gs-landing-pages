Connecting Your Accounts

The authentication flow between Zapier and Google Calendar is straightforward but requires careful attention at two points:

Step 1: Selecting the Correct Google Account

When prompted to sign in (around 2:30 in the video), ensure you choose the Google account that contains the calendar you want to automate. Many professionals have both personal and work Google accounts, and selecting the wrong one is the most common setup mistake.

Step 2: Reviewing Permissions

Google will request permission for Zapier to "View and edit your Google Calendar events." This level of access is necessary for the automation to function but should only be granted to trusted apps like Zapier.

Security note: You can revoke these permissions anytime via your Google Account's "Third-party apps" settings.

Troubleshooting Tips

If your integration isn't working as expected:

  • Reauthenticate: Sometimes connections expire. Reconnect via App Connections.
  • Check time zones: Ensure both Zapier and Google Calendar use the same time zone setting.
  • Review triggers: Test if the triggering event (email, form, etc.) is working correctly.
  • Upgrade plan: Free Zapier accounts have delays; paid plans process zaps faster.

Most issues can be resolved by retracing the connection steps shown at 1:45 in the video tutorial.
