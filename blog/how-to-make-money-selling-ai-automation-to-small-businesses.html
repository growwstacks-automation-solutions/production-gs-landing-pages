5 Most Profitable Automation Workflows

These five automation patterns deliver the highest ROI for small businesses. Each can be built in Make.com without coding:

1. Lead Capture & Follow-Up

Automatically add leads from forms, Facebook ads, or Instagram DMs to your CRM and send immediate SMS/email responses. Works for realtors, tutors, and service businesses.

2. Appointment Reminders

Sync calendar events to send customized SMS/email reminders 24-48 hours before appointments. Reduces no-shows by up to 30% for dentists, salons, and consultants.

3. Content Marketing

Auto-generate social posts from blog content, send review requests after purchases, or trigger discount offers to inactive customers. Ideal for restaurants and retailers.

4. Invoice Automation

Create and send invoices when projects are marked complete, then follow up on overdue payments. Saves freelancers and contractors 6+ hours monthly.

5. Team Notifications

Alert staff via Slack/email when high-value leads come in, sales close, or appointments are missed. Critical for repair services and sales teams.

Where to Find Your First Paying Clients
