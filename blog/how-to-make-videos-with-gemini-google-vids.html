Getting Started with Google Vids AI

Google Vids transforms existing content - documents, slides, or simple prompts - into complete video presentations. The AI handles the heavy lifting of storyboarding, scripting, and media selection while you maintain creative control.

To access these powerful features, you'll need either a Google Workspace Business or Enterprise plan, or a Google AI Pro subscription. The basic Workspace plans don't include the full AI video creation capabilities. Once you're set up with the right subscription, simply navigate to Google Vids from your Workspace apps.

Creating Your First AI Video in 5 Steps

Let's walk through creating a video for a landscaping business - but these steps apply to any industry. At the 1:15 mark in the tutorial video, you'll see this process in action.

Step 1: Start with a Storyboard

Open Google Vids and select "Storyboard." Enter a prompt like: "Create an overview video of our services for my landscaping business using this document. Focus on the environmental benefits, cost savings over time, and our personalized design process."

Step 2: Let Gemini Generate Your Outline

Gemini analyzes your document or slides to create an editable video outline. The AI understands context and prioritizes key messages from your source material.

Step 3: Choose Your Style

Preview different video styles that match your brand personality. Google Vids offers professional templates for various industries and content types.

Step 4: Generate Your First Draft

Click "Create" and watch as Gemini builds a complete video draft with suggested scenes, scripts, AI voiceovers, stock media, and background music.

Step 5: Review and Refine

The initial draft will be about 80% complete. Play through it to identify areas needing your personal touch before finalizing.

In summary: 1) Start with a storyboard prompt, 2) Let Gemini analyze your content, 3) Select a style, 4) Generate the draft, 5) Review and customize. Five simple steps to transform documents into videos.

Customizing Your AI-Generated Video

The real magic happens when you personalize the AI's work. "This is a great start," the tutorial narrator says at 2:30, showing the initial draft. "Now I can easily edit video to make it more like my brand."

You can tweak every element: fonts, colors, voiceovers, scripts, and scene order. For even more customization, use Google's Veo models to create AI video clips from text or your existing imagery. Simply open the Veo tab in Vids, add your image, and type a prompt describing the animation you want.

Once your custom clip is ready, insert it directly into your video scene. This level of personalization ensures your AI-assisted videos still feel authentically yours while saving you hours of manual editing.

YouTube Optimization with Gemini

Creating the video is only half the battle - you also need compelling metadata to help it get discovered. At 3:15 in the tutorial, you'll see how Gemini solves this too.

Upload your completed video back into Gemini with the prompt: "Create a YouTube title and description for this video that will help it get discovered by homeowners interested in landscaping. Include relevant keywords and a compelling hook."

Gemini will generate SEO-optimized titles and descriptions tailored to your target audience. "Yes, perfect," the narrator confirms. "Now we've got a complete package, our polished video, and ready-to-go YouTube titles and description."
