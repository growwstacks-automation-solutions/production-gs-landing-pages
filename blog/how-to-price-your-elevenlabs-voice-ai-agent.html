Choosing the Right Plan Tier

ElevenLabs offers four plan tiers (Starter, Creator, Pro, Scale), each with different token allocations and overage rates. The optimal choice depends entirely on your projected usage:

  • Starter ($5): Only viable for testing - supports ~100 short calls/month
  • Creator ($22): Good for small deployments (~1,000 minutes/month)
  • Pro ($99): Best value for most businesses (~5,000 minutes)
  • Scale (Custom): Economical above ~11,000 minutes
