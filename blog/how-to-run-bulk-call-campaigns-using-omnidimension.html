The Lead Outreach Problem Businesses Face

Most businesses accumulate thousands of leads through websites, trade shows, and marketing campaigns - but only contact a fraction of them. Manual calling teams can't possibly keep up with the volume, leaving potential revenue sitting untouched in CRMs.

The math is brutal: if your team makes 50 calls per day (already an aggressive target), reaching 5,000 leads would take 100 business days. By then, 87% of leads go cold according to Harvard Business Review data.

Key insight: Businesses that contact leads within 1 hour are 7x more likely to qualify them compared to those who wait just 24 hours. Yet most teams take 48+ hours to follow up.

Step 1: Creating Your Calling Bot

Your calling campaign starts with building an AI agent tailored to your outreach goals:

Step 1: Enhance Your Prompt

Use OmniDimension's prompt enhancement feature to refine your bot's script. The system suggests improvements to make conversations more natural and effective.

Step 2: Customize Voice and Tone

Select from multiple voice options and adjust speaking style to match your brand personality. A financial services bot should sound different than a fitness center outreach.

Step 3: Deploy to Phone Number

Attach your bot to a dedicated caller ID. This becomes the number that appears on recipients' phones during your campaign.

Pro tip: Test your bot with internal numbers before launching campaigns. Refine based on how real people respond to the flow.

Step 2: Configuring Your Bulk Call Campaign

Navigate to the Bulk Call section and create a new campaign:

  1. Name your campaign (e.g., "October Chair Promotion")
  2. Select your deployed bot from the dropdown
  3. Set concurrent call volume (start with 5-10 calls at once)
  4. Choose your caller ID (must match your bot's number)

The interface at 3:42 in the video shows how these settings appear during setup. Notice how the system displays your bot's details for verification before launch.

Step 3: Uploading and Preparing Your Contacts

Your CSV file needs these key elements:

  • Phone number column formatted as +[country code][number]
  • Dynamic variables (name, location, etc.) for personalization
  • No header rows - just clean data

After uploading at 4:18 in the tutorial, you'll see a preview of how your data maps to the calling flow. This is critical for ensuring personalization works correctly.

Important: OmniDimension automatically validates phone numbers before calling. Invalid formats are flagged in the campaign report.

Advanced: Scheduling and Retry Logic

The system offers powerful options for optimizing contact rates:

Call Scheduling

Launch immediately or set future start times. Ideal for time-sensitive promotions or follow-up sequences.

Automatic Retries

Configure 2-3 retry attempts when calls go unanswered. Choose between:

  • Immediate retry (within 15 minutes)
  • Same time next day
  • Custom retry schedule

Rescheduling Requests

When leads say "Call me later," the bot automatically schedules follow-ups at the requested time (demoed at 6:07 in the video).

Tracking and Analyzing Your Results

The campaign dashboard provides real-time metrics:

  • Call completion rates (answered vs. unanswered)
  • Conversation analytics (duration, outcomes)
  • Call recordings for quality review
  • Disposition trends showing common objections

At 7:32 in the tutorial, you'll see how the system logs both successful connections and failed attempts with detailed reasons (busy, no answer, etc.).

Optimization tip: Review recordings of your most successful calls. Identify what's working and refine your bot script accordingly.
