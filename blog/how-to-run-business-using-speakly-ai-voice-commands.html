The Voice-to-Text Revolution: 4X Faster Than Typing

Typing has been the bottleneck of business productivity for decades. The average professional types at 40 words per minute but speaks at 150 wpm - nearly 4 times faster. Speakly AI bridges this gap by converting your spoken words into clean, editable text instantly across all your apps.

Unlike basic transcription tools, Speakly integrates directly with your workflow. Whether you're in Gmail, Slack, Word or project management apps, your voice becomes the fastest input method. A 10-minute typing task becomes a 2-3 minute voice command session.

Key stat: Speakly users complete email responses, meeting notes and task delegation 4x faster than typing - saving the average business owner 3+ hours per week just on basic communications.

AI Auto-Editing: From Casual Speech to Professional Text

We all use filler words like "um", "uh" and "like" when speaking casually. Traditional transcription forces you to manually clean these up - wasting the time you saved by speaking instead of typing. Speakly's AI auto-editing solves this by transforming raw speech into polished text automatically.

The system removes verbal fillers, corrects mistakes, and formats sentences for clarity. What you say as "um so I think uh we should probably like schedule a meeting" becomes "We should schedule a meeting." This happens in real-time across all your apps.

Before & After: "Hey team, uh let's maybe discuss the Q2 goals and uh prepare some slides?" becomes "Team, let's discuss Q2 goals and prepare slides." - professional and actionable instantly.

Seamless Multilingual Translation for Global Teams

International business means constant language barriers. Traditional translation workflows involve multiple steps: speak, transcribe, translate, edit. Speakly compresses this into one seamless action where your spoken words become translated text instantly.

Speak in English and produce Spanish, French or other language text ready for clients and teams. The system maintains tone and context while removing the friction of multilingual communication. No more copying text between translation tools - your voice becomes the universal input.

GenSpark Agent: Your Voice-Controlled AI Assistant

Speakly's GenSpark mode takes voice control to the next level. By double-tapping your mic, you activate an AI assistant that can:

  • Summarize meeting notes automatically
  • Draft complete emails from voice prompts
  • Generate presentation slides with spoken instructions
  • Create spreadsheets through verbal commands
  • Research topics and compile findings hands-free

This transforms voice from just an input method into a complete control layer for your AI tools. Instead of typing prompts, you speak naturally and watch the work complete itself across your apps.
