Speed vs. Scaling: Jared's Tested Framework

Many agencies get trapped in "analysis paralysis" or move too fast without validation. Jared's framework balances both:

Speed Phase

  • Test core concept with minimal viable product
  • Use basic landing pages and Loom videos
  • Allocate small ad budget ($500-$2000)
  • 1-2 week validation window

Scaling Phase

  • Only after proven concept
  • Bring in specialists (designers, strategists)
  • Refine messaging based on real data
  • Increase ad spend with confidence

Jared shares a cautionary tale at 16:45 about rushing a Google Business Profile offer to market that burned $2k in ads with zero sales. The V2 version, developed after proper strategy sessions, became a profitable service line.

Execution Lessons from 16+ Client Implementations

Through implementing solutions for 16+ voice AI clients, Jared's team identified three critical execution principles:

  1. Trust Before Tech: Clients need to trust you → your company → the product, in that specific order
  2. Consultant Mindset: Shift from selling features to diagnosing business pains
  3. Fail Forward: Early automation mistakes (like misfired sequences) became trust-building opportunities when handled transparently

One powerful example: when a client came for voice AI but actually had a lead generation problem, Jared's team pivoted to Google Business Profile optimization - demonstrating true consultative selling. This flexibility contributed to their 14 AI award nominations by solving real business problems rather than pushing predefined solutions.
