The Missed Call Crisis (62% of Calls = $31,500 Lost)

Every service business owner knows the frustration - you're with a client, in a meeting, or simply away from the phone when an important call comes in. The statistics are staggering: businesses miss 62% of their incoming calls, representing massive lost revenue opportunities.

For a typical dental clinic, each missed call equals about $150 in potential business. With an average of 7 missed calls per day, that's $31,500 in lost monthly revenue. The traditional solutions - hiring more staff or using call centers - are expensive and don't provide 24/7 coverage.

AI receptionists solve this at 10% of the cost: By answering every call instantly, handling routine inquiries, booking appointments, and only transferring truly complex calls, AI phone agents recover this lost revenue while providing better customer service than voicemail or missed calls.

The 10% Pricing Model - How to Charge $3,150/mo

The most effective pricing strategy for AI receptionists is value-based - charging a percentage of the revenue you're saving the business. Here's the exact math used for the Smile Dental case:

Pricing Calculation:
7 missed calls/day × $150/call = $1,050 daily loss
$1,050 × 30 days = $31,500 monthly lost revenue
10% of $31,500 = $3,150/month service fee

This creates a win-win - the business recovers $28,350 in additional revenue while paying you $3,150, and you get high-margin recurring revenue. The same model works for law firms ($500/call), HVAC companies ($300/call), and other service businesses.

4-Step Build Process on Retail AI

Retail AI makes building professional AI receptionists accessible without coding. The four core components:

Step 1: Prompt Engineering

Define the AI's role, conversation flow, and when to transfer calls. Use a structured prompt with sections for role, critical rules, appointment booking, and knowledge base usage.

Step 2: Function Setup

Add call transfer, end call, check availability, and book appointment functions. These enable the AI to interact with calendars and phone systems.

Step 3: Business Knowledge

Create a knowledge base with business hours, services, staff info, and FAQs so the AI can answer common questions accurately.

Step 4: Phone Number

Purchase a local number ($2/month) and connect it to your AI agent for real inbound calls.
