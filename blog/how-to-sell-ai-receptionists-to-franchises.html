The 5-Step Franchise Deployment Process

Fur's team standardized their rollout into a repeatable process that scales:

They offer 4 base templates (after-hours, appointment scheduling, etc.) rather than building custom. Clients choose one, preventing scope creep.

Step 2: Intake Form

A structured questionnaire captures essential details - hours, services, key personnel - feeding directly into the agent configuration.

Step 3: QA Checklist

Every agent undergoes 23-point verification (call flows, transcripts, workflows) before client review.

Step 4: Client Feedback Loop

Initial deployments include 30 days of transcript monitoring and tweaks based on real call data.

Step 5: Bulk Rollout

After perfecting the prototype location, they deploy to all franchise locations simultaneously via HighLevel's multi-location tools.
