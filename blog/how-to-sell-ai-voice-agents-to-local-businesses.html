The Missed Call Crisis Costing Businesses Thousands

Local service businesses lose an average of 28% of potential revenue from missed calls. When a painter, electrician, or plumber is on a job, they physically can't answer their phone - leading to frustrated customers going to competitors. Traditional solutions like hiring receptionists cost $3,000+/month and still can't provide 24/7 coverage.

AI voice agents solve this perfectly by answering every call, qualifying leads, and even scheduling appointments. The technology has advanced to where callers can't tell they're talking to AI - it understands context, asks follow-up questions, and handles complex conversations.

Key stat: Businesses using AI voice agents report a 37% increase in booked appointments and 24% higher customer satisfaction scores compared to voicemail systems.
