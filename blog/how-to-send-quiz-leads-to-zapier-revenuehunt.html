The Quiz Lead Capture Problem

Interactive quizzes generate 3-5x more leads than traditional forms—but most businesses lose those leads to manual processes. Without automation, quiz responses pile up in spreadsheets, get forwarded between teams, or worse, slip through the cracks entirely. By the time someone follows up, the lead's initial excitement has faded.

The solution? Automatic routing through Zapier. When a quiz-taker completes your RevenueHunt quiz, their entire response—including answers, recommended products, and contact info—can instantly flow into your CRM, email tool, or Slack. No manual exports. No missed opportunities.

Real-world impact: Businesses using this integration see 40% faster follow-up times and 28% higher conversion rates from quiz leads to customers. The key is engaging while the quiz experience is still fresh in the lead's mind.

Preparing Your Quiz for Zapier

Before connecting to Zapier, optimize your quiz to capture the right information. While RevenueHunt tracks all responses by default, you'll want to explicitly collect:

  1. Contact information: Add name, email, or phone number fields using the Customize → Questions tab
  2. Qualifying data: Include questions that reveal buyer intent (e.g., "How soon are you looking to purchase?")
  3. Product preferences: Structure questions to surface which products/services interest the lead most

At 1:15 in the video tutorial, you'll see how to insert these fields into existing quiz slides without redesigning your entire flow. The goal is balancing lead capture with quiz engagement—too many fields upfront can drop completion rates.

Connecting RevenueHunt to Zapier

The connection process involves three key steps:

Step 1: In RevenueHunt, navigate to Settings → Integrations and click "Connect" next to Zapier

Step 2: Copy your unique API key from the popup (this authenticates the connection)

Step 3: Paste the key into Zapier's authentication window when prompted

Once connected, you'll see a confirmation in both platforms. This one-time setup establishes the pipeline for all future quiz responses from this RevenueHunt account.

Testing the Integration

Before building complex automations, verify the connection works:

  1. Take a test quiz through RevenueHunt's preview mode
  2. Complete all fields (use real test data you can recognize)
  3. In Zapier, create a new Zap with RevenueHunt 2.0 as the trigger
  4. Select "New Response" as the trigger event
  5. Click "Test Trigger" - your test response should appear within seconds

If successful, you'll see the full response data including:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone if collected)
  • Answers to each quiz question
  • Recommended products/services
  • Timestamp and response ID

Once comfortable with basic integrations, explore these powerful automations:

  • Lead scoring: Assign points based on quiz answers to prioritize follow-up
  • Product-specific sequences: Trigger different email flows based on recommended products
  • SMS follow-up: Send text messages to high-intent leads within minutes

The most effective workflows use quiz data to personalize every touchpoint. For example, if someone's quiz results suggest they're interested in Product A, your follow-up emails should focus on that product—not your general catalog.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

If responses aren't appearing in Zapier:

  1. Reauthenticate: Disconnect and reconnect the RevenueHunt integration in Zapier
  2. Check API key: Verify the key in RevenueHunt matches what's in Zapier
  3. Test with new data: Older quiz responses may not trigger—take a fresh test quiz
  4. Review Zap history: Zapier logs all attempts with error details

For ongoing reliability, monitor your active zaps in RevenueHunt's integration tab. This shows which automations are connected to each quiz and their last activity time.
