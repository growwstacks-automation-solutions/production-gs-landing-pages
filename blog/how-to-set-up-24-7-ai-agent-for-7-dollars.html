Security First: Why Isolation Matters

Before diving into setup, we must address the elephant in the room: security. An improperly configured AI agent can become a backdoor to your entire digital life. Consider what happens if someone accesses your OpenClaw token:

  • Connected Etsy accounts could be manipulated
  • Google Drive files containing product designs could be stolen
  • Payment accounts linked for automation could be compromised

This is why we strongly recommend never running OpenClaw on your personal computer. The Hostinger VPS provides an isolated environment where breaches don't expose your primary devices. Etsy Automation Workarounds

Here's where things get tricky: Etsy aggressively blocks bot activity. When our test agent tried scanning for viral clip art, it immediately hit a wall. The solution? Brave Search API.

Brave's privacy-focused browser provides an "undetectable" way to research Etsy trends. By feeding this API key to Claude, the agent can bypass restrictions while still delivering valuable insights:

  • Watercolor florals remain consistently popular
  • Boho elements are trending upward
  • Seasonal bundles (like St. Patrick's Day) show predictable spikes

While not perfect, this method provides enough market intelligence to guide your product creation without risking account bans.
