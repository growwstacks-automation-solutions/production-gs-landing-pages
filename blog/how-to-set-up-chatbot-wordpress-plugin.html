5-Minute Installation Process

Installing the Jotform AI Chatbot takes just three simple steps:

Step 1: Install the Plugin

From your WordPress dashboard, navigate to Plugins → Add New and search for "Jotform AI Chatbot". Click Install Now, then Activate.

Step 2: Authorize Your Account

After activation, hover over the new Jotform menu item and select "Create AI Chatbot". You'll need to authorize with your Jotform account (free tier available).

Step 3: Basic Setup

Choose your chatbot's role (support agent, sales, etc.), select an avatar, and name your assistant. The video at 4:30 shows how to customize these initial settings.

Pro Tip: Even with default settings, your chatbot will be functional immediately after installation while you refine its appearance and behavior.

Control exactly where and when your chatbot appears:
