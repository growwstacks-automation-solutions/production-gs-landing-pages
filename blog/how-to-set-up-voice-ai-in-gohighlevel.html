Why Voice AI Matters for Your Business

Every missed call represents lost revenue and frustrated customers. Traditional answering services are expensive and limited to business hours. Voice AI changes this equation by providing instant, natural-sounding responses anytime a customer calls.

GoHighLevel's built-in voice AI agent lets you automate common inquiries while capturing lead information automatically. At 2:15 in the video, you'll see how seamlessly the AI handles a real customer question about services.

Businesses using voice AI see 37% more qualified leads from after-hours calls and 28% reduction in missed appointment opportunities according to recent automation benchmarks.

Step 1: Creating Your Knowledge Base

The foundation of your voice AI is its knowledge base - the information source it uses to answer questions. GoHighLevel offers multiple ways to build this:

Option 1: Web Crawler

Simply enter your website URL and GoHighLevel will automatically extract relevant content. This works best for FAQ pages, service descriptions, and blog content.

Option 2: Manual Entry

For more control, you can manually add questions and answers. This ensures precise responses for your most common inquiries.

Option 3: Document Upload

Upload PDFs, Word docs, or text files containing your business information. The AI will parse this content for relevant answers.

Pro Tip: The web crawler takes 5-7 minutes to process content. Use this time to plan your agent configuration.

Step 2: Configuring Your Voice Agent

With your knowledge base ready, navigate to Agent List and select "Create Agent". Choose "Custom Agent" for full control over settings.

Naming Your Agent

Give your agent a friendly, memorable name that reflects its purpose. "Appointment Scheduler" or "Customer Support" work well.

Welcome Message

This is your virtual receptionist's greeting. Keep it warm and professional while indicating the types of assistance available.

Knowledge Base Selection

Connect the agent to the knowledge base you created earlier. This enables it to answer questions about your business.

Choosing the Right Voice Personality

GoHighLevel offers multiple voice options to match your brand personality. At 3:42 in the video, you'll hear the Nova voice in action.

Built-in Voices

Options like Nova provide natural-sounding speech with appropriate inflection and pacing. Listen to samples before selecting.

Custom Voice

For a truly branded experience, you can upload recordings of a specific voice (like your own or a team member's).

Conversion Tip: A slightly slower speech rate (like Nova's default setting) improves comprehension and reduces caller frustration.

Setting Up Information Collection

Your voice AI can capture valuable lead information during conversations. Configure these fields carefully.

Standard Fields

Name, email, and phone number are collected by default. These sync directly with your GoHighLevel CRM.

Custom Fields

Add specific questions relevant to your business, like "What service are you interested in?" or "When would you like to schedule?"

User Assignment

Designate which team member receives leads from the voice AI. This ensures proper follow-up.

Testing and Tuning Your AI Agent

Before going live, thoroughly test your agent using the web call feature (shown at 4:18 in the video).

Test Scenarios

Try common questions, interruptions, and edge cases. Note where responses could be improved.

Knowledge Base Refinement

Add answers for any questions the AI struggled with. The more comprehensive your knowledge base, the better the AI performs.

Tone Adjustment

Use the advanced settings to modify formality, enthusiasm, and verbosity to match your brand voice.

Advanced Configuration Options

For power users, GoHighLevel offers several advanced voice AI settings.

Call Handling Rules

Set conditions for when to transfer to a human, like when a caller says "representative" or after three unanswered questions.

Integration Hooks

Connect your voice AI to other tools via webhooks when specific events occur (like a new lead capture).

Analytics Dashboard

Track call volume, common questions, and conversion rates to continuously improve performance.
