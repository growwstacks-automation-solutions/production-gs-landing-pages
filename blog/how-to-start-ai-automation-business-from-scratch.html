The 62% Missed Call Opportunity

When someone searches for "plumber near me" and calls the top results, 62% of those calls go unanswered according to industry data. The plumber might be under a sink or the massage therapist with a client - they're too busy doing their job to answer every call.

This creates a massive opportunity. Your AI automation service texts the caller immediately after a missed call: "Sorry we missed you! How can we help?" This simple message keeps potential customers engaged instead of calling your competitor.

Key stat: For a business with $500 average customer value missing 20 calls/week, implementing this system recovers $3,200/month in lost revenue - justifying your $300/month fee 10 times over.

How the Missed Call System Works

The system has two components working together: 1) Automatic text responses to missed calls, and 2) AI-powered conversation handling when customers reply.

When a call comes in and isn't answered, your system immediately texts the caller with a personalized message from the business. If they reply, AI scans the business's website to answer questions and can even book appointments - all without the business owner lifting a finger.

Step-by-Step Setup Process

Setting up the missed call text back takes less than 2 minutes per business:

Step 1: Connect Google My Business

The business owner signs into their Google My Business account through your white-labeled software. This automatically pulls in their business information.

Step 2: Enable Missed Call Text Back

In settings, you toggle on the missed call feature. The system uses a default message that the owner can customize: "Hi, this is [Business Name]. I saw we just missed your call. How can I help you?"

Step 3: White Label the Software

Add your logo and connect your payment processor so 100% of the $300/month payments go straight to your bank account - no revenue sharing.

In summary: Connect GMB → Enable feature → White label. That's all it takes to start capturing missed calls for clients.

Adding AI Conversation Automation

When customers reply to your missed call text, AI takes over the conversation. Here's how it works (timestamp 8:15 in the video):

  1. Paste the business's website URL into the conversation AI tool
  2. The AI scans and learns all content from their site
  3. When customers text questions, AI answers using the website info
  4. You can add specific FAQs like "What services do you offer?"

The AI improves over time as you give feedback on its responses. Business owners love it because it handles customer inquiries 24/7 without their involvement.
