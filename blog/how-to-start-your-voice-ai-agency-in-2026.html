Client Acquisition: The ACA Framework That Works

Cold outreach fails for 90% of new agencies. The ACA framework (Acknowledge, Compliment, Ask) delivered 2 clients in 3 weeks for student Tyrone:

  1. Start with warm contacts (people who know you)
  2. Acknowledge their current situation ("How's the paper factory?")
  3. Compliment their work ("You're doing amazing work there")
  4. Ask for referrals ("Know anyone who needs voice AI?")

This avoids direct selling while leveraging existing trust. At 1:45:20, I demonstrate the exact LinkedIn message template that converts at 15%.
