How to Test a Voice AI Agent in Jotform (Beginner's Guide)

Just built an AI agent but unsure how it will sound to customers? Testing your voice AI before deployment is crucial for creating natural, professional interactions. This guide walks you through Jotform's voice testing features so you can preview and perfect your agent's voice in minutes.

Why Voice Testing Matters

Nothing breaks customer trust faster than a robotic, unnatural-sounding AI agent. Studies show that 83% of customers will abandon an interaction if the voice sounds artificial or unprofessional. Testing ensures your agent delivers information in a natural, engaging way that aligns with your brand voice.

Jotform's built-in testing tools let you preview exactly how your agent will sound to customers before going live. This prevents embarrassing moments and ensures your AI makes the right first impression every time.

Key benefit: Testing multiple voice options helps you find the perfect match for your brand personality and target audience demographics.

Selecting Your Agent

Begin by navigating to your Jotform dashboard and selecting the AI agent you want to test. In our example, we're using a travel planning agent named "Gavin" from Vista Horizons Travel Agency.

Click the "Edit" button to access your agent's configuration settings. This is where you'll find all the tools needed to test and refine your voice agent before deployment to customers.

Accessing Voice Settings

In the left sidebar, locate and select the "Voice Agent" option. This opens the voice configuration panel where you can preview and customize your agent's vocal characteristics.

The interface provides immediate access to all voice parameters including gender, age, accent, and language options. You can test different combinations to find the perfect fit for your use case.

Previewing the Voice

With the voice agent selected, click the "Preview" button to start a test conversation. Your agent will greet you with its current voice settings, allowing you to evaluate tone, pacing, and pronunciation.

In our travel agent example, Gavin introduces himself: "Welcome to Vista Horizons Travel Agency! I'm your travel planning AI chatbot, here to help you plan the perfect trip." This immediate feedback lets you assess whether the voice matches your brand personality.

Testing Conversation Flow

Engage your agent in a realistic dialogue to test its conversational abilities. Ask questions you expect real customers to pose, and evaluate both the content and delivery of responses.

For example, when asked about the best time to visit Hong Kong, our travel agent responds: "The ideal time to visit Hong Kong is usually from October to December during autumn, when the weather is mild and less humid." The voice should deliver this information naturally, with appropriate pacing and emphasis.

Pro tip: Test edge cases and unexpected questions to ensure your agent maintains composure and provides helpful responses even when challenged.

Customizing Voice Options

If the default voice isn't quite right, Jotform offers extensive customization. Click the voice settings gear icon to access options for language, accent, gender, and age.

You can preview each variation by clicking the play button next to the option. This lets you compare different voices side-by-side until you find the perfect match for your brand and audience.
