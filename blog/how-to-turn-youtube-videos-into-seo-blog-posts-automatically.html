The Content Repurposing Problem

Content creators know they should repurpose videos into blog posts for SEO and audience reach, but the manual conversion process is painfully time-consuming. At 1-2 hours per post (transcribing, formatting, creating images, and publishing), most creators either give up or post inconsistently - wasting their video content's potential.

The breakthrough came when we realized every video already contains all the raw material needed for a great blog post: the spoken content, natural section breaks, and key insights. The challenge wasn't creating content - it was efficiently transforming one format into another without manual busywork.

Key stat: Creators who manually convert videos to blogs typically only repurpose 20-30% of their content. Automation increases this to 90%+ while cutting the time investment by 75%.

How the Automation Works

The system begins when a new video URL is added to a designated Google Sheet - the single point of input for the entire workflow. Make.com monitors this sheet and triggers the automation whenever a new row appears, creating a seamless content pipeline that requires zero technical knowledge to operate.

From there, the workflow follows a precise sequence: it extracts the video transcript, sends it to AI for restructuring into blog format, generates matching images, formats all elements for WordPress, and creates a publish-ready draft - complete with proper headings, embedded video player, and SEO elements. The entire process happens in the background while the creator focuses on more strategic work.

AI Content Generation Process

The magic happens in the AI transformation stage where GPT-5 (via OpenRouter) converts raw video transcripts into polished blog content. Unlike generic AI writing tools, this system uses carefully engineered prompts that preserve the original video's tone while adding proper blog structure.

The AI is specifically instructed to create content with:

  • An engaging introduction that hooks readers
  • Clear H2 and H3 section headings that mirror the video's natural flow
  • Bullet points summarizing key takeaways
  • A conclusion that prompts engagement
  • Natural internal linking opportunities

At 3:15 in the tutorial video, you can see how the prompt engineering ensures the AI output matches both the creator's voice and WordPress formatting requirements - a critical detail most DIY solutions miss.

WordPress Integration Details

The WordPress integration goes far beyond just dumping text into the post editor. The Make.com scenario populates all critical fields that most creators manually set:

  1. Title generation: Creates SEO-optimized titles that balance keywords with readability
  2. Content formatting: Applies proper HTML tags for headings, paragraphs, and lists
  3. Video embedding: Converts YouTube URLs to proper iframe embed code
  4. Category assignment: Matches posts to appropriate categories based on sheet input
  5. Publishing control: Sets dates and maintains draft status for editorial review

Pro tip: The system can be configured to either create drafts for review or publish immediately, depending on your workflow preferences and quality control needs.

Image Generation and SEO

One of the most time-consuming manual tasks - creating featured images - is completely automated in this workflow. The AI image generator produces visuals that match the article's content and tone, while the system handles all the technical SEO elements most creators overlook.

The automation ensures every image includes:

  • Properly formatted alt text for accessibility and SEO
  • Optimized file sizes for fast loading
  • Consistent styling that matches your brand
  • Correct attachment to the WordPress media library

This attention to detail means your blog posts aren't just automatically created - they're automatically optimized for search engines and user experience from the first draft.
