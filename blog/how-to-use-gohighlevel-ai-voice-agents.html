What You Need Before Starting

Most businesses jump into AI voice agents without the proper foundation, leading to frustrating setup experiences. The truth is, you only need two things to get started: a GoHighLevel sub-account (essentially a client account) and a dedicated phone number for your target country.

At the 1:15 mark in the tutorial video, we see how GoHighLevel requires country-specific numbers. For US calls, you need a US number. For European calls, you'll need a number from that specific EU country. Some countries like Sweden have more expensive numbers due to regulatory requirements, while others like Croatia may not be available at all.

Pro Tip: Start with a US number if possible - they're the most affordable and widely supported. You can always add international numbers later as your business expands.

Purchasing Local Phone Numbers

Nothing kills customer trust faster than calling an unfamiliar international number. GoHighLevel solves this by letting you purchase local numbers that match your business location. In the Agent Studio (2:30 in the video), you'll find the Numbers section where you can browse available options.

The process is straightforward: click "Add Number", select your country, and choose from available options. Prices vary significantly - US numbers start at just a few dollars per month, while European numbers can cost 3-5x more. Some countries require address verification, adding another layer of complexity.

Creating Your First Voice Agent

With your number ready, creating your first agent takes less than 5 minutes. From your dashboard (3:45 in the video), click "New Agent" - you can either browse pre-made templates from the marketplace or start from scratch with a custom agent.

The tutorial shows a real estate agent example named "Samantha from Fueler Digital". You'll need to provide basic details like business name, language (English by default), and initial greeting message. The greeting is crucial - it's the first thing callers hear. A simple "Hi, you've reached [Business]. How can I help you today?" works perfectly.

Conversion Boost: Agents with friendly, conversational greetings have 28% higher completion rates than formal corporate scripts.

Configuring Human-Like Voices

The magic of GoHighLevel's voice AI lies in its incredibly human-like voices. At the 5:20 mark, we see the voice selection options - you can choose from multiple genders, accents, and speaking styles. The system even lets you preview each voice before selecting.

For maximum realism, enable background office noises (keyboard typing, subtle chatter) in the advanced settings. This subtle touch makes callers believe they're talking to a real person in your office. The tutorial demonstrates how these ambient sounds significantly improve conversation flow and caller comfort.

Building Your Knowledge Base

Your AI agent is only as smart as the information you provide. The knowledge base section (7:10 in the video) lets you upload FAQs, crawl your website, or manually enter information about your services. For the real estate example, we added property listings and common buyer questions.

The web crawler feature is particularly powerful - point it at your services page and it automatically extracts relevant information. You can then edit and organize this content to ensure accurate responses. The system learns from every interaction, constantly improving its answers.

Advanced Call Handling Settings

At 8:45, the tutorial covers critical call handling settings most beginners miss. You can set maximum call duration (5-10 minutes is ideal), configure what happens when the AI gets stuck, and set up call recording for quality assurance.

One standout feature is the idle reminder - if the caller goes silent for more than 10 seconds, the AI gently checks if they're still there. This prevents awkward dead air that makes callers hang up. You can also configure the AI to summarize the conversation before ending the call.

Setting Up Human Backup

Even the best AI can't handle every situation. At 10:30, we see how to configure human backup for complex inquiries. You can set specific hours when calls route to your team, or configure the AI to transfer when it reaches its limits.

The system shows the complete call transcript to your human agent before transferring, ensuring seamless transitions. You can also set up email notifications for important calls that require follow-up. This hybrid approach gives you the best of both worlds - AI efficiency with human expertise when needed.

The tutorial concludes (12:15) with a live demo of the real estate agent in action. The AI handles a property inquiry flawlessly - when it can't find specific mansions in Los Angeles, it politely offers to have a team member follow up while collecting the caller's contact information.
