Why Self-Hosting Beats Cloud Plans

Most businesses assume automation platforms require monthly subscriptions - that's exactly what companies like Zapier and Make want you to believe. The truth? n8n's open-source architecture lets you run the same powerful automations without recurring fees when self-hosted.

Cloud services impose artificial limits to justify their pricing. n8n's $24/month starter plan caps you at 10,000 monthly executions - about 330 per day. Hit that limit during a marketing campaign? You're forced to upgrade to the $50/month pro plan. Self-hosting removes these restrictions entirely while costing 70% less.

Key advantage: Self-hosted n8n gives you unlimited executions, complete data privacy, and full customization - everything enterprises pay thousands for - at the fixed cost of your VPS server (as low as $7/month).

One-Click n8n Installation

Gone are the days of manual server configuration. After purchasing your VPS (3:40 in the video), the hosting dashboard provides instant access to your preconfigured n8n instance. The entire setup process takes three steps:

Step 1: Server Initialization

Click "Access Instance" in your hosting dashboard. This launches a terminal where the system automatically installs all n8n dependencies.

Step 2: Admin Account Creation

When you first visit your n8n URL (usually your-server-ip:5678), you'll create an admin account with email and password. This is your private instance - no shared logins or company access.

Step 3: Security Configuration

The setup wizard guides you through basic security questions. Enable HTTPS and set up fail2ban protection (shown at 5:20) to prevent unauthorized access.

Pro tip: Bookmark your n8n URL and server credentials in a password manager. Unlike cloud services, there's no "forgot password" option for self-hosted instances.
