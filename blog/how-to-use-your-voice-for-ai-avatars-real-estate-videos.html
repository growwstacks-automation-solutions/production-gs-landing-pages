How Voice Cloning Works

Traditional text-to-speech sounds robotic and impersonal. Voice cloning captures your unique vocal characteristics - pacing, inflection, and emotional tone - to create a digital replica that sounds authentically like you.

The process couldn't be simpler: record a 3-5 minute voice memo on your phone (as demonstrated at 4:30 in the video), upload it to the platform, and the AI analyzes thousands of speech patterns to build your voice model. No studio time or special equipment needed.

Pro tip: Record your sample while actually walking through a property to capture natural conversational flow - the AI will preserve your authentic speaking style.

Creating Your AI Avatar

Your digital twin starts with a single photo - preferably a clear headshot with neutral expression. The AI transforms this into a 3D model that can turn, gesture, and emote naturally.
