Zapier + TikTok Setup in 5 Steps

Creating your TikTok lead automation requires zero coding skills and takes less time than brewing your morning coffee. At 2:15 in the video tutorial, you'll see the exact clicks needed to connect these powerful platforms.

Step 1: Create Your Zap

Log into Zapier and click "Make a Zap." This starts your automation workflow. Name it clearly like "TikTok Leads → HubSpot" so your team can identify it later.

Step 2: Set TikTok as Trigger

Search for "TikTok" in the trigger app selector. Choose "New Lead" as your trigger event - this detects every form submission from your ads.

Step 3: Connect Your Accounts

Authorize Zapier to access your TikTok ads account. This secure connection only shares lead data - no posting permissions are granted.

Step 4: Choose Your CRM Action

Select your CRM (like Salesforce or HubSpot) as the action app. Pick "Create Contact" or equivalent to build new records from TikTok leads.

Step 5: Map Your Fields

Match TikTok form fields (name, email, etc.) to corresponding CRM fields. Zapier remembers these mappings for all future leads.

In summary: Create Zap → TikTok trigger → CRM action → field mapping → test. The entire process takes under 5 minutes for basic setups.
