The 5 Problems With Using ElevenLabs Dashboard Directly

When selling AI voice agents to businesses, presentation matters just as much as the technology itself. Clients aren't just paying for the AI - they're paying for a professional experience with measurable outcomes. Relying on the standard ElevenLabs dashboard creates five critical roadblocks for scaling agencies:

1. No white-labeling: Your clients see ElevenLabs branding everywhere, positioning you as a reseller rather than a premium solution provider. This undermines your perceived value and makes price negotiations harder.

2. Creator-focused interface: While great for developers, the ElevenLabs dashboard isn't designed for clients to track their usage in a simple, segmented way. Clients get overwhelmed by technical options rather than seeing clear business metrics.

3. Manual billing headaches: You have to manually track every client's usage, calculate invoices, and chase payments each month. At 2:43 in the video, we show how this becomes unsustainable once you have more than a handful of clients.

4. Limited analytics: Basic metrics like call minutes don't show the business outcomes clients care about - appointments booked, leads generated, or support tickets resolved.

5. Fragmented experience: Clients already work in GoHighLevel. Making them log into a separate system creates friction and feels unprofessional compared to an integrated solution.

GoHighLevel Integration Process

The magic happens when you embed your white-label dashboard directly into GoHighLevel sub-accounts:

Step 1: Generate Embed URL

In ChatTouch, navigate to the client detail page and create an embed URL for GoHighLevel. At 7:38 in the video, we show exactly where to find this option.

Step 2: Add to GHL Menu

In your GoHighLevel agency settings, create a new custom menu item. Paste the embed URL and configure it to open as an iframe within the current window.

Step 3: Set Permissions

Choose which sub-accounts should see the dashboard and what user roles have access. Most agencies give all team members visibility.

The Result: Clients now access their voice agent analytics without leaving GoHighLevel. At 9:15 in the video, you can see how seamless the embedded dashboard looks in action.
