The Broken Appointment Booking Process

Most service businesses follow the same frustrating cycle: leads come in, get a single follow-up attempt, then fall through the cracks when life gets busy. According to Chat GPT data shared in the webinar, over 95% of businesses still don't use any form of AI on phone calls - meaning they're relying entirely on human teams for lead follow-up.

The problem? Human teams can't scale. Even the most disciplined salesperson will struggle to:

  • Consistently follow up with every lead
  • Handle objections perfectly every time
  • Work outside business hours
  • Maintain energy on repetitive calls

The result: Most businesses convert less than 5% of their leads into appointments. The webinar revealed that adding voice AI to existing SMS workflows can boost this conversion rate by 60% or more.

The Prince Charming SMS Technique

At 32:15 in the video, Dan explains the "Prince Charming SMS" - the critical first touchpoint that makes the entire system work. This isn't your generic "Hi [First Name]" text. The exact copy used is:

"Hey, it's [Your Name] from [Company]. Is this the same [Lead Name] that was looking for [Service] in [Month] last year?"

This approach works because it:

  1. References a specific past interaction (even if imagined)
  2. Creates curiosity by implying there's a reason for reaching out
  3. Gets significantly higher response rates than generic outreach

When the lead responds (which happens 30-70% of the time), the conversation continues with:

"Fantastic! I didn't want to spam you first. Are you still looking for that [Service]?"

This gentle approach maintains engagement while setting up the next phase of the conversation.

Introducing the Double Banger System

The "double banger" system (named at 42:30 in the video) combines two powerful technologies:

1. SMS Appointment Booking: The Prince Charming SMS starts the conversation, with AI handling responses to book appointments.

2. Voice AI Follow-Up: For non-responders or stalled conversations, voice AI calls to continue the dialogue.

Here's why this combination works:

  • Some leads prefer texting, others prefer calls
  • Voice AI can handle more complex objections than text
  • The system works 24/7 without human intervention
  • You capture appointments from leads who would otherwise go cold

In the demo, they showed how leads that don't respond to SMS might still convert via voice - adding an extra 10-20% to appointment rates.

How Voice Cloning Works

One of the most impressive parts of the demo (starting at 1:22:45) shows how the system can clone a salesperson's voice with just 30 seconds of audio. The process:

  1. Upload a recording of your best salesperson
  2. The AI isolates their voice and speech patterns
  3. It analyzes their common phrases and objection handling
  4. The cloned voice then handles calls with the same style

This means your AI agent doesn't just sound like your team - it sells like your team. The webinar showed a cloned voice handling a call with the same regional accent and speech patterns as the original salesperson.

The Outcome-Based Pricing Model

At 51:10, Dan explains how to price this service profitably. Instead of charging by the minute (like most voice AI providers), he recommends:

1. Charge per appointment: $60-100 per booked appointment depending on your industry

2. Add a monthly retainer: $3,000-5,000/month for handling inbound calls too

This approach:

  • Aligns your incentives with the client's success

  • Makes the service more valuable than cheap per-minute alternatives

Implementation Steps

To implement this system for your business:

  1. Start with SMS: Set up the Prince Charming SMS sequence in your CRM
  2. Add AI responses: Configure automated replies for common responses
  3. Integrate voice AI: Set up calls for non-responders after 2-4 hours
  4. Clone your best: Record your top salespeople to train the AI
  5. Test and optimize: Track what timing and messaging works best
