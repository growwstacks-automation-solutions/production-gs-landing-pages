How the Conversational Setup Works

The setup process follows an intuitive question-and-answer format that feels like training a new team member. Here's what to expect:

Step 1: Business Introduction

The AI begins by asking about your company name, industry, and what you do. For a coaching program called "Get Done Program," it might suggest an opening like: "Hi! Thanks for calling the next level today."

Step 2: Caller Information Needs

You specify what details are most helpful to collect from callers - contact information, company size, specific challenges, or revenue levels. The AI recommends the most natural way to ask for sensitive information.

Step 3: Primary Goals

Define whether the agent should primarily qualify leads, book appointments, provide information, or handle support issues. The AI builds appropriate conversation paths for each goal.

3-5 minutes is all it takes to complete the setup conversation and have a fully functional voice AI agent ready to handle calls.
