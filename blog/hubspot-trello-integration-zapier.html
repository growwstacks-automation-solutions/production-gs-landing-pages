The Manual Update Problem

Sales teams using HubSpot as their CRM often rely on Trello for deal execution - tracking tasks, due diligence, and internal updates. Without integration, this creates a data synchronization nightmare. Every stage change in HubSpot requires manual Trello updates, and vice versa.

At 5-10 minutes per deal per update, teams waste 5-10 hours weekly on duplicate data entry. Worse, inconsistencies creep in when updates are missed, causing misaligned pipelines and reporting errors.

The hidden cost: Beyond wasted time, manual updates create version control issues where different team members work from conflicting information. Deals slip through cracks when Trello cards aren't moved to match HubSpot stages, or vice versa.

How the Integration Works

This Zapier integration creates a complete two-way sync between HubSpot deals and Trello cards using three specialized Zaps:

1. HubSpot → Trello (New Deals)

Automatically creates Trello cards with deal details when new HubSpot deals are created, placing them in the correct list matching the deal stage.

2. HubSpot → Trello (Stage Changes)

Moves Trello cards to matching lists when deal stages change in HubSpot, using stored card ID references to maintain the connection.

3. Trello → HubSpot (Card Moves)

Updates HubSpot deal stages when cards are moved between lists in Trello, completing the two-way synchronization.

Key benefit: The system maintains perfect alignment regardless of where changes originate - your team can work in whichever platform they prefer without worrying about manual updates.

Zap 1: New Deal Creation

When a sales rep creates a new deal in HubSpot, this Zap automatically generates a corresponding Trello card with all essential details:

Step 1: Trigger on New HubSpot Deal

The Zap monitors your HubSpot deal pipeline for new creations. It captures all deal properties including custom fields.

Step 2: Create Trello Card

Using the deal data, the Zap creates a card in your designated Trello board. The card includes:

  • Deal name as card title
  • Property details (type, units, address)
  • Deal stage as the target list
  • Custom fields mapped to card properties

Step 3: Store Card Reference

The Zap saves the Trello card ID back to HubSpot as a custom property, creating the critical connection for future updates.

Implementation tip: Use a standardized naming convention for Trello lists that exactly matches your HubSpot deal stages to ensure seamless mapping.

Zap 2: Deal Stage Changes

When deals progress through your pipeline in HubSpot, this Zap ensures Trello cards move to the corresponding lists automatically:

Step 1: Trigger on Stage Change

The Zap watches for updates to the deal stage property in HubSpot, ignoring other property changes.

Step 2: Find Matching Trello Card

Using the stored card ID from Zap 1, the system locates the exact Trello card associated with the deal.

Step 3: Move Card to Correct List

The card is moved to the Trello list matching the new HubSpot deal stage (e.g., "Under Contract" deal → "Under Contract" list).

At 2:15 in the video, you can see this in action - when the deal stage changes to "Listing Agreement Signed" in HubSpot, the Trello card instantly moves to the matching list.

Why this matters: Sales managers get real-time visibility into pipeline progress without needing to check both systems or remind team members to update Trello.

Zap 3: Trello Card Moves

When your operations team moves cards in Trello (indicating deal progress), this Zap updates HubSpot to maintain synchronization:

Step 1: Trigger on Card Move

The Zap detects when cards are moved between lists on your designated Trello board.

Step 2: Identify Related HubSpot Deal

Using custom fields or card naming conventions, the system finds the matching HubSpot deal.

Step 3: Update Deal Stage

The HubSpot deal stage is updated to match the new Trello list (e.g., card moved to "Initial Contact" list → deal stage set to "Initial Contact Made").

As shown at 3:40 in the video, moving a card to "Initial Contact Made" in Trello automatically updates the corresponding HubSpot deal within seconds.

Operational benefit: Your ops team can work entirely in Trello while ensuring CRM data stays current - no more missed updates because someone forgot to check HubSpot.

Implementation Requirements

To set up this integration, you'll need:

Platform Access

  • HubSpot Professional or Enterprise account
  • Trello Business Class or Enterprise
  • Zapier Premium plan (for multi-step Zaps)

Configuration

  • Standardized deal stages in HubSpot
  • Matching list names in Trello
  • Custom property in HubSpot to store Trello card IDs

Testing Plan

  • Verify new deal creation → card generation
  • Test stage changes in both directions
  • Confirm edge cases (archived deals, renamed lists)

Pro tip: Document your stage/list mappings for future reference and onboarding new team members. This becomes your single source of truth for how the systems connect.
