What Sunbeam Does Differently

Traditional SEO tools require constant human input - you need to manually run audits, identify opportunities, and implement changes. Sunbeam flips this model entirely by operating as a fully autonomous agent that manages every aspect of SEO proactively.

The agent continuously monitors your website, identifies optimization opportunities, creates content, fixes technical issues, and implements strategies - all without requiring any prompts or approvals. As Fenuka from Daydream explains: "Think of it as hiring a human being to run your SEO function, except it works 24/7 and never sleeps."

Key differentiator: Sunbeam doesn't wait for instructions. It uses its understanding of SEO best practices to take action independently, just like a human expert would - but with machine speed and consistency.

From Agency to Autonomous Agent

Daydream's journey began as a traditional SEO agency working with enterprise clients. This hands-on experience proved invaluable when they pivoted to building Sunbeam. As Fenuka notes: "We had all this documentation around how to run good SEO strategies from our agency work - that became the training data for our agent."

The team spent months coding agents and watching the autonomous AI space evolve before deciding the timing was right to launch Sunbeam. Their agency experience gave them three critical advantages:

  • Real-world SEO workflows to model
  • Proven strategies that worked for clients
  • Clear differentiation from competitors

This "agency-first" approach allowed them to build Sunbeam with confidence that it would deliver real business value from day one.

The Sunbeam dashboard provides founders with visibility into what the agent is working on while giving them control over its autonomy level. Key features shown in the demo include:

1. Performance Metrics

The home view shows critical SEO metrics like organic traffic growth and LLM visibility scores - giving founders instant insight into performance.

2. Three Core Functions

Sunbeam operates across three main areas:

  • Content Creation: Writing new articles or improving existing ones
  • Technical SEO: Identifying and fixing website technical issues
  • Research: Continuous competitive analysis and opportunity identification

3. Autonomous Mode

The most powerful feature is the ability to let Sunbeam operate completely independently. When toggled on, the agent will:

  • Identify optimization opportunities without being asked
  • Implement changes automatically
  • Only notify you after the fact for major decisions

Perfect for: Seed to Series A startups who need SEO expertise but don't have the resources to hire a full-time specialist.

Why Quality Trumps Cost Optimization

Running advanced AI models continuously isn't cheap. Sunbeam currently uses premium models to ensure the highest quality outputs, prioritizing effectiveness over cost efficiency.

As Fenuka explains: "In a world where something like this doesn't even exist, the challenge is proving this is possible and can be as amazing as you imagine. Once you've proven that, you can optimize costs."

The $21M in funding gives Daydream the runway needed to:

  • Use the best available AI models without compromise
  • Focus entirely on SEO outcomes rather than cost savings
  • Iterate on the product while it's live in market

This quality-first approach positions Sunbeam as the premium solution in autonomous SEO - a strategic decision that should pay dividends as the market matures.

Key Lessons From Building The Team

When asked what he wished he knew at the start, Fenuka emphasized one critical lesson: "The people you work with are everything - full stop. Doesn't matter what idea you're working on or how funded you are."

This people-first philosophy shaped Daydream's culture and hiring approach:

  • Prioritized team cohesion over individual skills
  • Built a culture where pivots could happen smoothly
  • Created an environment where people love coming to work

As Fenuka puts it: "If you're enjoying the process with people you love, you can figure anything out. Without that, it won't be great no matter what." This mindset proved crucial when pivoting from agency services to building Sunbeam.
