Setup Preparation

Before creating your automation, you'll need to complete two essential setup steps in GoHighLevel:

1. Connect Your Instagram Business Account

Navigate to Integrations → Facebook in your GoHighLevel dashboard. Make sure your Instagram business account is properly connected through Facebook's system. This connection powers all Instagram automation features.

2. Choose Your Target Post

Select the Instagram post where you want to automate comments. Promotional posts with clear offers (discounts, free guides, consultations) work best. In the video example, we used a "$50 off your first mow" post from a landscaping company.

Pro Tip: Create a dedicated "comment-to-DM" post rather than using your regular content. This makes tracking conversions easier and sets clear expectations for your audience.

Step-by-Step Configuration

With your Instagram account connected, follow these steps to build your automation workflow:

Step 1: Create New Workflow

Go to Automation → Workflows and click "New Workflow." Use a clear naming convention like "Instagram - [Post Name] Comment to DM" for easy management.

Step 2: Select Template

Choose the pre-built "Instagram Comment Automation" template from the Convert Leads category. This gives you a complete starting structure.

Step 3: Configure Trigger

Set your trigger to detect comments on your selected Instagram post. You can specify keywords that will activate the automation (like "discount" or "info").

Step 4: Customize Responses

Edit the automated comment reply and DM message. Personalize with the commenter's name and tailor the message to your specific offer. Add multiple message variations for natural engagement.

Step 5: Add Action Buttons

Include clickable buttons in your DM that link to your website, booking page, or lead magnet. These turn passive readers into active leads.

In summary: Create workflow → Select template → Set Instagram post trigger → Customize messages → Add action buttons → Publish.

Testing & Optimization

Before going live, thoroughly test your automation:

1. Test With Multiple Accounts

Use both personal and business accounts to comment on your post. Verify the automated reply and DM deliver correctly from different perspectives.

2. Check Timing

Ensure the DM arrives within 30 seconds of the comment. Delayed responses significantly reduce conversion rates.

3. Review Analytics

Monitor your GoHighLevel reports to see:

  • How many comments triggered the automation
  • DM open rates
  • Button click-through rates

Optimize by testing different:

  • Comment trigger keywords
  • DM message copy
  • Button placements and wording

Key metric: Aim for at least 60% DM open rate and 20% button click rate. If below these benchmarks, revise your message or offer.
