How the Instant Call System Works

The complete workflow takes a lead from website form to your phone in under a minute:

Step 1: Lead Submits Website Form

Customer fills out your contact form with name, phone, and service request details. TCPA compliance language ensures proper consent.

Step 2: Webhook Triggers AI Call

Your CRM (like GoHighLevel) sends the lead data via webhook to the Retail Voice AI platform within seconds.

Step 3: AI Places Immediate Call

The AI calls the lead's phone number while they're still on your website, using a natural female voice with 1-second response latency.

Step 4: Natural Conversation

The agent introduces your company, confirms the service need, and asks qualifying questions - all while sounding completely human.

Step 5: Warm Transfer

Once the lead is engaged, the AI introduces you and transfers the call seamlessly to your phone.

Key advantage: Unlike basic IVR systems, this AI understands context, remembers details, and handles complex conversations before transferring.

Setting Up the Retail AI Agent

The Retail platform provides the voice AI capabilities. Here's how to configure it:

Agent Personality: Uses GPT 5.2 for natural conversations at 20¢/minute (GPT40 available at 50¢/minute for faster responses). Female voice preferred for higher acceptance rates.

Call Flow: The agent follows a carefully scripted but flexible conversation path:

  • Waits for "hello" before speaking (more natural than interrupting)
  • Summarizes the service request from form data
  • Asks discovery questions to qualify the lead
  • Verifies understanding by repeating key details back
  • Handles common objections and FAQs about your services
  • Initiates warm transfer to your number

Dynamic Variables: The agent pulls customer name, service details, and other info from your CRM to personalize each call.

GHL Webhook Configuration

Connecting GoHighLevel to Retail requires a custom webhook:

Trigger: Form submission in GHL starts the workflow.

Phone Formatting: Convert the lead's phone number to E164 format for proper dialing.

Webhook Details:

  • Endpoint: api.redretail.com/v2/create-phone-call
  • Authorization: Bearer token from Retail API settings
  • JSON Body: Includes from/to numbers, agent ID, and dynamic variables

The webhook passes all necessary lead data to Retail while maintaining TCPA compliance through proper consent handling.
