The MCP Revolution: Why Traditional Web Apps Are Obsolete

Businesses are losing conversions because their web experiences don't accommodate how users actually interact with information today. Over 60% of product research now begins in AI chat interfaces, yet most companies still build websites assuming direct human navigation.

MCP (Multi-Client Protocol) apps solve this by creating interfaces that work simultaneously for human users and AI assistants. When you build with Skybridge, every button click, form entry, or UI interaction is immediately visible to both the human and the AI - enabling truly collaborative experiences that guide users to better outcomes.
