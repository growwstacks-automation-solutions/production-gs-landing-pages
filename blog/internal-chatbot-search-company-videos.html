The $1M Video Waste Problem No One Talks About

Fortune Business Insights reveals a shocking statistic: 68-80% of corporate video assets go completely unused. Companies invest thousands creating training videos, recording meetings, and producing tutorials - only to have employees struggle to find relevant information when they need it.

The current process is painfully inefficient: First, search for the right video by filename. Then watch the entire recording hoping to find the relevant section. For a 15-minute tutorial, this might mean wasting 14 minutes watching irrelevant content before finding the 60-second answer you need.

Time savings example: When searching for "how to insert a formula in Excel," employees would traditionally need to watch 9 minutes of a 16-minute tutorial. The AI solution returns the answer immediately with 4 precise timestamps - reducing search time from 9 minutes to 9 seconds.

How the AI Video Search Solution Works

This Azure-based chatbot solution transforms your video library in three key ways:

1. Automatic Transcript Indexing

The system continuously monitors your SharePoint or OneDrive for new videos. When detected, it automatically extracts and indexes:

  • Voiceover transcripts from audio
  • Subtitles/text from video frames
  • File descriptions (for videos without audio)

2. Natural Language Understanding

Unlike simple keyword search, the AI understands questions like:

  • "How do I insert a page number in Word?"
  • "What were the action items from last quarter's strategy meeting?"
  • "Show me the Japanese experimental module installation"

3. Precise Timestamped Answers

Results include:

  • The exact answer to the question
  • Links to jump directly to relevant moments in videos
  • Multiple timestamps when information appears in different videos

Key differentiator: Microsoft's native tools like Video Stream only show full transcripts. This solution understands intent and pinpoints exact moments - a game-changer for long recordings.
