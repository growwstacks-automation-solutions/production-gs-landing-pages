Claude Dispatch: Your iPhone Remote Control

The biggest productivity breakthrough came from treating my iPhone as a remote control for Claude - an AI assistant running on my Mac. Instead of manually checking every app for updates, I now dispatch tasks from my phone that Claude handles end-to-end.

At 2:35 in the video, you'll see a live demo where I ask Claude to: "Please go through my projects and tasks database in Notion, pull anything due this week or that seems important, draft a summary and drop it in my email inbox." Within minutes, I have a professionally formatted weekly briefing waiting in my drafts.

Key insight: Claude Dispatch works best when you've built a "Co-work OS" - a personalized knowledge base that gives the AI context about your work style, preferences and frequently accessed information. This transforms generic AI responses into outputs that feel like they came from a trusted colleague.

How to Set Up Claude Dispatch:

  1. Install Claude Co-work on your Mac (desktop app required)
  2. Connect your productivity tools (Notion, Gmail, Calendar etc.)
  3. Create personalized instruction pages for recurring tasks
  4. Set up scheduled dispatches for routine workflows
  5. Use your iPhone to send ad-hoc requests throughout the day

Whisper Flow: 300,000 Words Processed

Typing on an iPhone remains one of the biggest productivity drains - until you try Whisper Flow. This AI voice interface sits on top of all your apps, turning speech into perfectly formatted text wherever you need it.

At 7:12 in the video, I demonstrate dictating an email that automatically formats lists and maintains my writing style. The secret sauce? Whisper Flow learns your voice patterns, frequently used phrases and preferences over time - unlike Apple's built-in dictation which treats each session as a blank slate.

Pro tip: Create voice snippets for common responses like "Thanks for this, I'll review and get back to you by [date]." Whisper Flow will insert these with proper context - saving you from retyping the same replies dozens of times per week.
