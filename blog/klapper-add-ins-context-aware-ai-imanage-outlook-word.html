The Private AI Difference for Legal Teams

Legal professionals face a growing dilemma: the promise of AI productivity gains versus the risk of exposing confidential client data to third-party vendors. Most AI tools require sending documents outside the firm's control, creating compliance headaches and potential breaches of client confidentiality agreements.

KLapper solves this by operating entirely within a law firm's Microsoft Azure tenant. Unlike vendor-hosted solutions, KLapper's large language models, RAG databases, and processing all occur within the firm's private cloud environment. This means sensitive legal documents never leave the firm's control while still delivering powerful AI assistance.

100% private architecture: KLapper's end-to-end private approach means no prompts, documents or metadata are ever sent outside the firm's Azure tenant, addressing even the strictest client confidentiality requirements.

This technical approach enables granular control that:

How Context Awareness Works

KLapper's true power comes from its multi-layered context understanding. Unlike tools that only consider the immediate document, KLapper evaluates:

  1. User identity: Who is making the request and their permissions
  2. Location context: Where in iManage's folder structure the user is working
  3. Document context: The specific file being viewed or edited
  4. Supplemental context: Any additional reference documents provided

This comprehensive context ensures responses are highly relevant while maintaining strict security controls. As shown at 15:20 in the demo, narrowing the folder context produces more focused results.
