What Is Make.com?

Make.com (formerly Integromat) is a visual automation platform that connects your apps and automates workflows without requiring any coding knowledge. Think of it as a more powerful version of Zapier with greater customization options.

The platform gets its name from its mission to help businesses "make" better decisions by automating repetitive tasks. With over 2,000 app integrations, Make.com can connect nearly any tools your business already uses.

Key difference: While Zapier focuses on simple "if this then that" automations, Make.com allows for complex workflows with conditional logic, data transformations, and multi-step processes - all through a drag-and-drop interface.

How Make.com Works

Make.com organizes automations into "scenarios" - their term for complete workflows. Every scenario consists of two core components:

1. Trigger: The event that starts the automation (like receiving an email with a specific subject line).

2. Action: What happens after the trigger fires (like sending a Slack message or updating a spreadsheet).

Between these components, you can add filters, delays, data transformations, and conditional logic to create sophisticated automations. The entire workflow is built visually using a drag-and-drop editor.

Make.com Dashboard Overview

The Make.com interface is organized into several key sections:

Organization: Your account overview showing usage, credits, and settings.

Teams: Manage collaborators and access permissions for your automations.

Scenarios: Where all your automations live - the core of the platform.

AI Agents: A newer feature for creating AI assistants that can take actions.

Templates: Pre-built automations you can customize and deploy quickly.

Connections: Where you link your apps and services to use in workflows.

Gmail-to-Slack Automation Demo

Let's walk through building a real automation that sends a Slack message when you receive an email with a specific subject line.

Step 1: Create the Trigger

1. Click "Create Scenario" and select Gmail as your first app
2. Choose "Watch emails" as the trigger
3. Connect your Gmail account
4. Set filter criteria to watch for emails with "make video" in the subject line

Step 2: Add the Action

1. Click the "+" button to add an action step
2. Search for and select Slack
3. Choose "Send message" as the action
4. Connect your Slack account
5. Select the channel and compose your message

Testing the workflow: At 2:45 in the video, you'll see the creator send a test email with "make video" as the subject. Within seconds, the corresponding Slack message appears in the designated channel.

Popular Make.com Use Cases

Beyond our simple demo, Make.com powers thousands of business automations. Here are three common patterns:

1. Lead Follow-Up Systems
Automatically add new leads from Facebook/Instagram to your CRM, then trigger personalized email sequences.

2. AI-Powered Email Processing
Use OpenAI to summarize incoming emails, then send digest versions to Slack or Teams.

3. Client Onboarding Flows
Automatically create project folders, assign tasks, and send welcome messages when a new client signs up.

These workflows typically save businesses 5-10 hours per week on manual administrative work.

Make.com Alternatives

While Make.com is powerful, it's not the only automation platform available:

Zapier: Easier for beginners but more limited in advanced functionality.

Pabbly Connect: Budget-friendly with unlimited workflows on higher plans.

HighLevel: All-in-one platform combining CRM, automations, and business tools.

Key consideration: Make.com charges based on operations (steps taken), while Zapier charges per task. For complex workflows, Make.com can be more cost-effective.
