7 Common Mistakes to Avoid

After helping 200+ students launch voice AI businesses, these are the pitfalls that derail most beginners:

1. Using Robotic Free Tools

Low-quality AI voices destroy credibility. ElevenLabs' free tier provides commercial-grade output that actually converts.

2. Pricing Too Low

$5 gigs attract nightmare clients. Start at $50 minimum to filter serious buyers.

3. No Niche Focus

"Voiceovers for anyone" gets lost in the noise. Specialize in explainer videos, audiobooks, or YouTube automation.

4. Ignoring Commercial Rights

Some free tools retain rights to your outputs. Always verify licensing terms.

5. Manual Processes

Not automating file delivery and invoicing caps your earning potential at 20 hours/week.

6. No Portfolio Samples

Clients need to hear before they buy. Create 3-5 industry-specific demos.

7. Chasing Perfection

AI voices will never be 100% human. Focus on the 95% quality that satisfies most business needs.
