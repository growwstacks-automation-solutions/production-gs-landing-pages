60-Second Setup Process

Creating your Google Ads AI agent takes less time than making a single manual campaign adjustment. The process involves three simple steps shown at 0:45 in the video:

Step 1: Create Your Agent

Navigate to the Agents tab in Make.com and click "Create Agent." Select Make's provider and choose the large language model for optimal performance. Give your agent a descriptive name like "Google Ads Manager."

Step 2: Configure Basic Settings

Add a system prompt that defines your agent's personality and general approach to Google Ads management. At this stage, you don't need to specify tool details - those come next.

Step 3: Connect Google Ads

Search for and select the Google Ads tool from Make's integrations. Initiate the authentication flow to grant necessary permissions. Once connected, your agent will have access to all available Google Ads management functions.

In summary: Name your agent → Set basic configuration → Connect Google Ads → Done. The entire setup takes under a minute for basic functionality.

Google Ads Authentication Flow

The authentication process follows standard OAuth protocols. At 1:30 in the video, you'll see the agent generate a Google sign-in link. When you follow this link, you'll be asked to grant specific permissions to your Make.com agent.
