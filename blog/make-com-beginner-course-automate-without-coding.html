What Is Make.com?

Make.com (formerly Integromat) is a visual automation platform that connects your apps and services into powerful workflows without requiring any coding knowledge. Imagine being able to automatically transfer data between Google Sheets and Airtable, send personalized emails based on form submissions, or post to social media on a schedule - all without manual intervention.

The platform uses a drag-and-drop interface where you connect different "modules" (triggers, actions, routers) like building blocks. As you build, you can watch the data flow through your workflow in real-time, making it easy to troubleshoot and optimize your automations.

Key benefit: Make.com gives you the power to automate up to 80% of repetitive tasks while providing detailed execution logs so you can track every run and make adjustments as needed.

Getting Started With Your First Automation

Follow these steps to create your first Make.com automation:

Step 1: Set Up Your Account

Create a free Make.com account and explore the dashboard. The free plan allows you to build and test basic automations.

Step 2: Choose Your First Scenario

Start with something simple like:

  • Save Gmail attachments to Google Drive
  • Add new Google Form responses to Airtable
  • Post Instagram content to Twitter automatically

Step 3: Build the Workflow

Drag your trigger (what starts the automation) onto the canvas, then add actions (what happens next). Connect them and configure each module's settings.

Step 4: Test and Deploy

Run test executions to ensure everything works, then activate your scenario. Monitor the execution logs to catch any errors.

Pro tip: Start with template scenarios from Make.com's library to learn faster. Modify them to fit your specific needs as you gain confidence.
