What Is Make.com?

Businesses today use an average of 130 SaaS applications, yet most of these systems don't communicate with each other. Employees waste countless hours manually transferring data between systems, introducing errors and delays. Make.com solves this problem by serving as the "plumbing" between your tech stack.

Originally launched as Integromat, Make.com is a visual automation platform that connects different systems in your technology ecosystem. Unlike simple point-to-point integration tools, Make.com handles complex workflows with conditional logic, data transformation, and multi-step processes across multiple applications.

3,000+ native integrations: Make.com connects with virtually every major business application including HubSpot, NetSuite, Slack, ZoomInfo, and financial systems. This eliminates the need for custom API development in most cases.

The HubSpot Integration Challenge

While HubSpot offers many native integrations, businesses often hit limitations when they need to connect HubSpot with specialized systems or create complex, conditional workflows. The gaps become particularly apparent in areas like:

  • ERP connections: Syncing deal data with financial/fulfillment systems
  • Lead enrichment: Automatically enhancing contact records with external data
  • Conditional routing: Sending leads to different teams based on multiple criteria
  • Multi-system workflows: Processes that touch CRM, marketing, and operations systems

Make.com fills these gaps by providing a visual interface to build sophisticated automations that would otherwise require custom development. As Oisín Scurry from Make.com explains, "We sit in a sweet spot between being accessible to non-technical users while still handling complex automations."

Make.com vs. Zapier vs. Custom Development

When evaluating automation solutions, businesses typically consider three options: simple tools like Zapier, full custom development, or platforms like Make.com that sit in the middle. Here's how they compare:

Zapier: Great for simple, point-to-point integrations with limited logic. Typically handles 1-2 steps with basic conditions.

Make.com: Handles complex, multi-step workflows with advanced logic, data transformation, and error handling. Supports 10x more apps than Zapier.

Custom Development: Provides complete flexibility but requires significant technical resources and ongoing maintenance.

As Dan Martell describes it, Make.com is "the intermediary between a Zapier and an AWS" - more advanced than basic automation tools but far more accessible than full custom development.

Real-World Example: Lead Routing Workflow

In the video, Oisín demonstrates a powerful lead routing automation that showcases Make.com's capabilities. Here's how it works:

  1. A new lead comes in from a Facebook ad campaign
  2. Make.com checks if the contact exists in HubSpot
  3. For new contacts: Enriches data with ZoomInfo, creates in HubSpot, routes to sales
  4. For existing contacts: Updates record, routes to customer success
  5. Routes leads geographically to appropriate teams (US vs. EMEA)

This entire workflow - which would typically require manual work or custom development - can be built in Make.com in about an hour. The visual interface makes it easy to understand and modify as business needs change.

Successful Make.com implementations follow this proven process:

  2. System inventory: Identify all systems involved in the process
  3. Automation design: Plan the automated flow with conditional logic
  4. Build & test: Create the automation in Make.com and test thoroughly
  5. Deploy & monitor: Launch the automation and monitor for errors
