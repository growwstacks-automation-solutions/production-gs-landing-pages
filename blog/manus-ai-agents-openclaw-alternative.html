What Are Manus AI Agents?

Business owners struggling to implement AI often face a frustrating choice: either invest days learning technical setups like OpenClaw, or settle for limited chatbot functionality. Manus AI Agents, originally developed by Chinese startup Monica and now backed by Meta's infrastructure, bridges this gap with autonomous capabilities accessible through messaging apps.

Unlike basic chatbots, Manus Agents perform multi-step reasoning tasks including web research, data analysis, website creation, and content generation. The key innovation is delivering these advanced capabilities through a conversational interface in Telegram (with WhatsApp and Slack coming soon), requiring zero technical configuration.

90-second setup vs. 10-hour configuration: Where OpenClaw requires server provisioning and API key management, Manus Agents activate with a simple QR code scan in Telegram. This makes sophisticated AI accessible to non-technical users while still offering powerful automation capabilities.

The 90-Second Setup Process

The video demonstrates how business owners can bypass complex AI deployments with Manus' streamlined onboarding. At the 4:30 timestamp, you'll see the exact steps:

  1. Navigate to the Agents tab in the Manus dashboard
  2. Click "Get Started on Telegram"
  3. Scan the QR code with your mobile device
  4. Name your bot through Telegram's BotFather
  5. Enter your token and chat ID in Manus

This process typically completes in under two minutes, compared to OpenClaw's multi-hour setup involving server configuration, environment variables, and API key management. The demo account shown had limited 500 credits but was fully operational immediately after setup.

No-code AI adoption: Manus eliminates the technical barriers that prevent many businesses from leveraging AI. Where OpenClaw serves developers willing to invest setup time, Manus targets business users who need results without infrastructure management.

Pricing and Cost Considerations

The video reveals important cost differences (15:20 mark):

  • Manus: Operates on a credit system where actions consume credits from your balance (demo account started with 500 credits)
  • OpenClaw: You pay directly for API calls to models like GPT-4, with costs varying based on usage

For light-to-moderate users, Manus' credit system may offer more predictable budgeting. However, power users running extensive automations might find OpenClaw's pay-per-API-call model more economical, especially when using smaller open-source models.

Total cost of ownership: While OpenClaw's API costs may be lower for some use cases, factor in the value of your time saved with Manus' instant setup and managed infrastructure.
