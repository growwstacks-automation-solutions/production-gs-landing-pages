How Manus Skills Solve the Document Problem

Unlike basic templates, Manus Skills are agentic AI that actively process client intake data to generate customized outputs. They make contextual decisions about content structure, pricing calculations, and recommendations based on your business rules and past examples.

The system works by analyzing your uploaded document examples to understand your brand voice, standard sections, and formatting preferences. Building Your First Proposal Skill (Step-by-Step)

Creating a Manus Skill requires no coding - just answering prompts and providing examples. Here's the exact process to automate your consulting proposals:

Step 1: Access the Skills Builder

Click the puzzle piece icon in Manus to open the skills manager. Select "Add" then choose "Build with Manis" to create a custom skill tailored to your consulting practice.

Step 2: Define the Skill Purpose

Describe what the skill should do when triggered. For proposals: "When someone fills out our Typeform intake, create a project blueprint and proposal document using our standard structure."

Step 3: Set Output Preferences

Choose whether to generate documents for review or enable full automation (sending directly to clients). Most consultants start with draft generation for quality control.

Step 4: Upload Examples

Provide 2-3 sample proposals showing your preferred structure, branding, and content style. The AI analyzes these to replicate your approach.

Step 5: Configure Data Sources

Connect your Typeform or specify how client data will be provided (plain text Q&A, PDF attachments, etc.).

5 minute setup: That's all it takes to create a basic Manus Skill that can generate your first automated proposal draft. The system continues learning as you use it, improving outputs over time.
