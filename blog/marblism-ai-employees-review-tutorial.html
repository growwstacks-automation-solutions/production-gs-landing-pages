What Is Marblism?

Most business owners know they should be more productive - answering emails faster, posting consistently on social media, following up with leads, and providing better customer service. But between meetings, operations, and putting out daily fires, these important tasks keep getting pushed to tomorrow. Marblism solves this by providing a complete team of AI employees that handle these routine tasks through simple chat interfaces.

Unlike traditional AI tools that require technical setup, Marblism presents each AI employee as a chat interface where you can give instructions in plain English. The platform currently offers five specialized AI roles: personal assistant, content creator, customer support rep, sales associate, and receptionist - with more roles coming soon.

85% of small business owners report spending over 15 hours per week on administrative tasks that could be automated. Marblism's AI employees can reclaim most of this time while maintaining (or improving) your professional communication standards.

Your AI Employees Overview

Marblism's AI employees aren't just chatbots - they're designed to function like human team members with specific roles and responsibilities. Each AI employee has a name, personality, and specialized skillset tailored to their job function.

The onboarding process is remarkably simple compared to traditional automation tools. At the 2:15 mark in the video tutorial, you can see how connecting your email and calendar takes just a few clicks. The AI employees then analyze your existing communications to learn your style and preferences.

Step 1: Company Setup

Provide basic information about your business including website (which the AI will scrape for style and content), brand colors, logo, and preferred communication tone. This ensures all AI-generated content matches your brand identity.

Step 2: Employee Configuration

For each AI employee, you can enable/disable specific capabilities. For example, your personal assistant can be allowed to draft email replies but not send them without approval.

Step 3: Tool Integrations

Connect your email (Gmail, Outlook, etc.), calendar, website CMS, and other tools. The AI employees need these connections to actually perform their jobs.

Pro Tip: Start by enabling just 1-2 AI employees and gradually add more as you become comfortable with the platform. The personal assistant (Eva) and content creator are great starting points.
