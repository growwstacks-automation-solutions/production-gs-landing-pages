4 Types of Voice AI Agents

Choosing the right agent type is crucial for your use case. Many beginners make the mistake of jumping straight to complex conversational flows when a simpler solution would suffice.

Retell AI offers four agent architectures:

  1. Single Prompt: One comprehensive prompt controls the entire conversation. Best for simple demos or very specific functions (like verifying business hours).
  2. MultiPrompt: Multiple prompts handle different conversation branches. Ideal for production use cases like appointment rescheduling or lead qualification.
  3. Conversational Flow: Rigid, step-by-step dialog trees. Perfect for highly structured interactions like medical intake forms.
  4. Custom: Fully customized agents using your own code. Required for unique business logic not covered by templates.

Voice AI Best Practices

After deploying dozens of Retell AI solutions, we've identified key patterns that separate successful implementations from disappointing ones.

1. Design for interruption: Unlike chatbots, voice conversations get interrupted constantly. Build your prompts to handle mid-sentence breaks gracefully.

2. Set clear expectations: Early in the call, explain what the AI can and can't do. This prevents frustration when complex requests arise.

3. Optimize transfer points: Identify exactly when human intervention is needed and make that transition seamless.
