Why Foundry Changes Everything

Businesses today face an AI chaos problem. Most companies have multiple disconnected chatbots - some on ChatGPT, some on Claude, some custom-built - with no central visibility or control. Answers vary wildly depending on which system a customer happens to reach. Microsoft Foundry solves this by providing one platform for all your AI agents, with enterprise-grade security and management tools.

The breakthrough comes from Microsoft's approach to AI orchestration. Instead of treating AI as standalone chatbots, Foundry enables teams of specialized agents that work together like human departments. This multi-agent architecture delivers far more sophisticated automation than single chatbots can achieve.

36% better answers: Microsoft's testing shows Foundry IQ delivers significantly more accurate responses than traditional single-source chatbots by cross-referencing multiple knowledge bases simultaneously.

The Foundry IQ Difference

Traditional chatbots search one knowledge source and return whatever they find, often with incomplete or outdated information. Foundry IQ revolutionizes this by searching multiple sources at once - SharePoint, company files, Bing, and more - then verifying answer quality before responding.

When a question comes in, Foundry IQ doesn't just keyword match. It understands context, breaks down complex questions into components, and searches each part against the most relevant knowledge sources. If the initial answers don't meet confidence thresholds, the system automatically researches until it finds satisfactory information.

This intelligent retrieval system enables Foundry agents to handle compound questions like "I need to cancel my flight tomorrow and bring an extra bag - what will that cost?" where most chatbots fail. The agent breaks this into cancellation policy and baggage policy components, researches each separately, then combines the answers.

Building Your First Agent in 5 Minutes

Creating a basic customer support agent in Foundry requires no coding and follows a simple 4-step process:

Step 1: Access the Foundry Portal

Navigate to ai.asure.com and sign in with a Microsoft account (free to create). Click "Create New Agent" in the top right corner.

Step 2: Choose Your AI Model

Select from 1900+ available models. For customer support, GPT-4o provides the best balance of speed, accuracy, and cost.

Step 3: Define System Instructions

Tell the agent its role and boundaries. Example: "You are a helpful airline customer service agent. Be friendly and professional. Answer questions about baggage, cancellations and rebooking policies. If you don't know something, say so clearly."

Step 4: Add Knowledge Sources

Upload PDFs, connect to SharePoint, or link websites containing your policies and FAQs. Foundry IQ will make this content searchable.

Pro Tip: Enable Bing Search as a tool to let your agent answer real-time questions like "What's the weather in New York tomorrow?" when your knowledge base doesn't contain the information.

Multi-Agent Workflows

Foundry's real power emerges when you create teams of specialized agents working together. Instead of one AI trying to do everything, you can build:

  • Customer Question Agent: Handles initial inquiries and triage
  • Inventory Check Agent: Verifies product availability
  • Refund Processor Agent: Handles returns and payments
  • Escalation Agent: Routes complex issues to humans

These agents collaborate through visual workflows you design in Foundry's drag-and-drop interface. For example, a procurement workflow might route a supplier request through three specialized agents working in parallel before combining their findings into a final recommendation.

The Enterprise Control Plane

Foundry's control plane provides fleet-wide visibility and management for all your AI agents in one dashboard. Key features include:

  • Cost Monitoring: Track spending per agent, department, or workflow
  • Performance Analytics: View success rates, error rates, and response times
  • Security Management: Enforce guardrails and compliance rules
  • Bulk Policies: Apply spending limits or security rules across all agents

The control plane integrates with Microsoft Defender and Purview for enterprise-grade security. Every agent receives an Entra ID for authentication and access control, even third-party agents you didn't build in Foundry.
