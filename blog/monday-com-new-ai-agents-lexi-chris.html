The Sales Automation Problem Monday.com Solved

Sales development reps (SDRs) spend just 35% of their time actually selling. The rest disappears into manual data entry, lead research, and coordination tasks across multiple disconnected tools. Monday.com's product team identified this productivity drain as their core challenge when developing their new AI agents.

"BDRs are supposed to build relationships and create opportunities," explains Yaniv, monday.com's product manager. "In reality, they spend huge chunks of time on operational busy work, switching tools, cleaning data, and running repetitive steps." The solution needed to automate these tasks while maintaining human oversight and control.

65% of sales reps' time is wasted on non-selling activities according to Salesforce research. Monday.com's AI agents target exactly this productivity gap, automating the manual work so teams can focus on high-value conversations.

Meet Lexi: Your AI Lead Generation Agent

Lexi specializes in solving the "blank page problem" that stalls outbound sales efforts. Instead of starting each morning with manual LinkedIn searches and spreadsheet compilation, sales teams can activate Lexi to:

  1. Analyze your ideal customer profile (ICP)
  2. Search multiple data sources for matching leads
  3. Allow preview and refinement of results
  4. Automatically populate your CRM board with qualified leads

At the 2:45 mark in the demo video, you can see Lexi in action - filtering leads by geographic region and job title before adding them to the sales pipeline. The agent handles all data mapping automatically, ensuring LinkedIn profiles, job titles, and other key details land in the correct CRM columns.

Lexi reduces lead research time by 80% while improving targeting accuracy. Teams can specify exact criteria (like "VP-level contacts in European SaaS companies") and refresh the lead list with one click as their ideal profile evolves.

Meet Chris: Your AI Sales Call Agent

While Lexi handles lead generation, Chris specializes in initial outreach and qualification. This AI agent can:

  • Conduct phone calls using natural conversation flow
  • Send follow-up texts and emails
  • Schedule meetings via Calendly integration
  • Update deal status based on call outcomes

The demo at 6:20 shows Chris handling a live call - introducing itself, asking qualifying questions, and offering to schedule a meeting when the prospect isn't available. All calls are recorded and transcribed automatically, with summaries added to the CRM timeline for human review.

What makes Chris particularly powerful is its integration with the rest of monday.com's workflow system. When a call completes, the agent can trigger subsequent actions - like assigning the lead to an account executive or scheduling a product demo - based on the conversation outcome.

How the Agents Work Together

Lexi and Chris create a complete outbound sales automation system when used in combination:

The AI Sales Workflow:

  1. Lexi identifies and qualifies leads based on your ICP
  2. Leads are added to your CRM with all relevant data
  3. Chris initiates contact through calls, texts, or emails
  4. Based on response, Chris schedules meetings or marks leads for follow-up
  5. All activity is logged in monday.com for visibility and coaching

This end-to-end automation addresses what Yaniv calls "the biggest pain point - switching." Instead of reps constantly jumping between LinkedIn, their phone system, email, and CRM, the entire flow happens in one place with AI handling the heavy lifting.

Implementation and Customization

Monday.com designed these agents for flexibility. Teams can choose to:

  • Use both agents together for complete automation
  • Deploy just Lexi for lead generation while keeping outreach manual
  • Use Chris for call handling with human-curated lead lists

Key customization options include:

  • Adjusting Lexi's search parameters and ICP definition
  • Editing Chris's call scripts and conversation flows
  • Setting approval workflows before certain actions
  • Configuring which CRM boards and columns receive the data

At 9:15 in the video, you can see how users maintain visibility and control. All AI actions are logged alongside human interactions in the same workflow timeline, with complete call recordings and transcripts available for review.
