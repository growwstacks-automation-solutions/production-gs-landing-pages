The $250,000 Problem: Missed Calls Cost

Mortgage brokers leave massive revenue on the table every night and weekend. When potential borrowers call after business hours, they typically get voicemail - if they call at all. Industry data shows 40-50% of mortgage inquiries happen outside 9-5, with most callers unwilling to leave messages.

Here's the math that keeps brokers awake: If your office averages 20 inbound calls per day, you're missing 8-10 qualified leads daily. At a conservative 5% conversion rate and $5,000 average commission per loan, that's $250,000 in lost annual revenue from unanswered calls alone.

Key insight: These aren't cold leads - they're people actively seeking mortgage solutions who can't reach your team when they're ready to talk.

Why Compliance Matters in Mortgage AI

Mortgage voice AI must adhere to strict regulations including:

  • TRID requirements for loan disclosures
  • State licensing laws for loan originators
  • TCPA compliance for call recording consent

Quality systems include built-in compliance features like automatic disclosure triggers and call recording notifications. Always verify your vendor understands mortgage-specific regulations.
