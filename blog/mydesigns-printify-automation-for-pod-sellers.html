The POD Publishing Pain Point

Print-on-demand sellers face a brutal time sink: manually publishing each design across multiple products. What should be the exciting part of the business - getting your creations to market - becomes a tedious chore of repetitive tasks:

  • Uploading designs one-by-one to your POD platform
  • Adjusting mockup positioning for each product type
  • Writing unique SEO-optimized listings for every variation
  • Setting prices and managing variants across sizes/colors

Curtis Blackmore, CEO of MyDesigns.io, experienced this firsthand with thousands of designs accumulating across various storage platforms. "I needed a way to publish them without it taking months," he explains in the video. This frustration led to creating a solution that gives POD sellers the leverage of a full production team.

The bottleneck: Most sellers spend 5-15 minutes per product manually - meaning publishing 14 designs across t-shirts, sweatshirts and hoodies could take 7-21 hours. MyDesigns reduces this to minutes through automation.

How MyDesigns Solves This

MyDesigns.io acts as a central hub between your designs and your sales channels (Etsy, Shopify, etc.), with deep integration to Printify for fulfillment. The platform provides four key automation advantages:

1. Bulk Design Processing

Upload dozens of designs simultaneously rather than one at a time. The system organizes them in a visual folder structure for easy management.

2. Multi-Product Publishing

Publish each design across multiple products (t-shirts, hoodies, etc.) in one operation rather than separate listings.

3. AI-Powered Listings

Vision AI scans your designs and generates optimized titles, descriptions and tags tailored to your marketplace.

4. Saved Workflow Profiles

Create reusable publishing templates with all your preferred settings (mockups, pricing, variants) for different product lines.

As Curtis demonstrates, these features combine to create what he calls "your automation team" - allowing one person to accomplish what would normally require multiple employees or countless hours.

Bulk Processing Workflow

The video demonstration shows the complete workflow from design upload to published product:

Step 1: Connect Accounts

Link your Printify account and sales channels (Etsy/Shopify) with one-click authentication. This establishes the pipeline from design to fulfillment to storefront.

Step 2: Upload Designs

Select multiple designs (14 Christmas nurse designs in the demo) and upload them simultaneously to your MyDesigns workspace.

Step 3: Configure Products

Choose product types (t-shirt, sweatshirt, hoodie) and configure print areas, colors and mockups in bulk. Adjust one design's positioning and sync across all others.

Step 4: Generate Mockups

Select multiple mockup angles and color combinations that will automatically render for each product variant.

Pro Tip: Etsy now allows up to 20 mockups per listing. MyDesigns makes it easy to generate and arrange these at scale.

AI-Powered Listing Generation

How Vision AI Works

  1. Select all designs for batch processing
  2. Choose product types (t-shirt, sweatshirt, hoodie)
  3. Select AI model (MD2.5 shown in demo)
  4. Generate titles, descriptions and tags in bulk

The AI analyzes each design visually, understands the product types, and writes marketplace-optimized copy that includes:

In the demonstration, Vision AI created complete listings for 14 designs (each with 3 products) in about 15 seconds - work that would take hours manually.
