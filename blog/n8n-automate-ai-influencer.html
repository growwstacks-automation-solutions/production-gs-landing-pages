Step 1: Character Creation

The foundation of any successful AI influencer is a consistent, recognizable character. Follow these steps to create your virtual persona:

1. Reference Images

Gather 5-10 high-quality reference images showing your character from different angles. These will train the AI to generate consistent variations.

2. Style Guide

Define your influencer's personality traits, speaking style, and content preferences. This ensures all generated content aligns with your brand.

3. Outfit Variations

Create multiple outfit options that can be mixed and matched. The system demonstrated at 12:30 can generate infinite outfit combinations while maintaining character consistency.

Pro Tip: Use Midjourney or Stable Diffusion to create your base character images. The tutorial shows how to integrate these tools with n8n for automated generation.
