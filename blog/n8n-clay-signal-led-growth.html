3 High-Value Signal Types We Detect

Not all business events indicate buying intent. Through hundreds of implementations, we've identified three signal categories that deliver 80% of results:

1. Physical Expansion Signals

New facilities, fleet purchases, and location openings (like the refrigeration company example at 3:20) indicate capital investment and immediate needs. These signals have a 87% accuracy rate for triggering relevant purchases within 90 days.

2. Leadership & Funding Signals

Executive changes (especially in operations/facilities) and funding rounds create 4-6 month windows where decision-makers actively evaluate vendors. Our AI cross-references these with your ideal customer profile (ICP).

3. Technographic Shifts

Adoption of complementary technologies (like a freight company implementing new routing software) often precedes related purchases. These signals identify companies entering your solution space organically.

Implementation tip: Start with physical expansion signals (highest conversion) before layering in other types. The workflow at 5:45 shows how we prioritize signal categories.
