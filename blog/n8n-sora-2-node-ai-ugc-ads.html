The UGC Ad Production Challenge

Creating authentic user-generated content (UGC) ads has been one of the most persistent pain points for eCommerce brands and social media marketers. Traditional methods require either require:

  • Hiring influencers or actors (costing $500-$5,000 per video)
  • Using complex AI video tools with steep learning curves
  • Settling for generic stock footage that lacks authenticity

Worse, scaling production means repeating these costs and complexities for every product variation or seasonal campaign. Most small businesses simply can't have the budget or bandwidth.

84% of consumers say UGC content as more authentic than brand-produced ads, yet only 12% of SMBs can afford to produce it at scale (Social Media Today, ).

3-Node Workflow Breakdown

Here's exactly how to build this automation in n8n (demonstrated at 5:48 in the video):

Step 1: Form Trigger

Create a simple form with two fields:

  • Text area for the UGC ad description
  • File upload for the product image

Step 2: Image Resizer

Configure to resize images to 720x1280 (vertical) or 1280x720 (horizontal) depending on your platform requirements.

Step 3: SORA 2 Node

Connect to OpenAI API, select SORA 2 model, and map:

  • The form description → Prompt field
  • The resized image → Reference image
  • Set duration (4-8 seconds)

Pro tip: At 12:30 in the video, you'll see how to add an AI agent node to automatically enhance your prompts for better results.

Advanced Prompting Tips

To get the best results from SORA 2 (shown at 15:20 in the video):

  1. Specify the speaker's demographics (age, gender, profession)
  2. Include 2-3 key product benefits
  3. Add emotional hooks ("game changer", "can't live without")
  4. Keep it conversational ("I use this every morning" vs generic features)

The AI agent node can automatically enhance basic descriptions into optimized prompts, as demonstrated with the water bottle example.
