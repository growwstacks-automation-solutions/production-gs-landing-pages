How The System Works

The automation is split into two intelligent workflows that work together seamlessly. The first part handles creative ideation - generating compelling ad concepts, titles, and opening frames. The second part produces the actual video content using the most cost-effective AI models available.

This separation serves an important purpose. While premium AI models produce stunning results, they can be expensive to run continuously. By reserving them only for the final video generation phase, we keep costs manageable while still delivering professional quality.

Cost-Effective AI Routing

The system intelligently routes tasks to different AI models based on their complexity and cost. For example, generating an ad concept might use a less expensive model costing just $0.20 per execution, while the final video rendering might use a premium $1.20 model only when absolutely necessary.

This tiered approach means you're not overpaying for simple tasks while still getting premium quality where it matters most. The workflow automatically selects the most cost-effective model for each step while maintaining quality standards through rigorous automated checks.

Airtable Configuration

The entire system is driven by a simple Airtable base that acts as your control panel. Here you configure:

  • Product URLs and images
  • Preferred AI models (male/female actors, styles)
  • Aspect ratios (portrait for TikTok/Reels, landscape for YouTube)
  • Call-to-action text
  • Activation status for each product

Once configured, the workflow automatically pulls from this base to generate new ads. The included template comes pre-loaded with sample data so you can see exactly how everything connects before adding your own products.

Quality Control Process

Before any ad goes live, it passes through multiple automated quality checks. Validator agents look for common issues like:

  • Cut-off body parts (a frequent AI generation flaw)
  • Unnatural movements or physics violations
  • Visual inconsistencies between frames
  • Brand voice mismatches

Ads that fail these checks are either automatically regenerated or flagged for human review. This ensures only polished, professional content makes it to your campaigns.

Video Generation Workflow

The video production process follows a precise sequence:

  1. Concept Generation: AI creates multiple ad concepts based on product details
  2. Storyboarding: The system plans shot sequences and transitions
  3. Asset Creation: Generates all visual elements needed for the ad
  4. Editing: Combines assets into a polished 8-second sequence
  5. Quality Review: Automated validators check the final output
  6. Delivery: Approved ads are saved to your designated folder

8 seconds - The optimal length for highest-converting product ads across social platforms. This system is specifically tuned to create compelling narratives within this tight timeframe.
