Cost Comparison: Why n8n Wins Long-Term

Most businesses evaluating automation platforms start with cost - and for good reason. What appears affordable at small scales can become prohibitively expensive as your automation needs grow. Our analysis reveals n8n's pricing model creates substantial savings, especially for Odoo businesses with complex workflows.

The fundamental difference lies in how each platform counts executions. n8n treats an entire workflow - regardless of steps - as a single execution. Zapier counts each individual step as a separate task. This distinction becomes critical at scale:

Example: A 10-step workflow running 1,000 times would count as 1,000 executions in n8n (10×1,000) but 10,000 tasks in Zapier (10×1,000). At Zapier's $20/month plan (750 tasks), this scenario would cost $266/month versus n8n's $20/month plan (3,500 executions).

n8n's open-source foundation provides additional cost advantages. While Zapier locks you into their hosted platform, n8n can be self-hosted for free (minus infrastructure costs). Even using n8n's cloud offering, the execution-based pricing proves more predictable and scalable for growing Odoo businesses.
