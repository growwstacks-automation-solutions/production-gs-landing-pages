Traditional AI image generators fail at this fundamental requirement. SeedDream 4.0, while excellent for single images, couldn't maintain character consistency across a campaign. Enter NanoBanana PRO - Google's new image model built on Gemini 3 that specializes in multi-image reasoning and hyper-consistent outputs.

85% of marketers say inconsistent character appearance is their #1 AI-generated content complaint - forcing them to either accept jarring differences between ads or manually Photoshop every image. NanoBanana PRO's ability to maintain perfect character consistency across unlimited variations changes everything.

Implementation Steps in Make.com

Adding NanoBanana PRO to your existing automation requires just 8 key steps in Make.com:

Step 4: Filter Configuration

Configure the HTTP node with NanoBanana PRO's API endpoint and parameters.
