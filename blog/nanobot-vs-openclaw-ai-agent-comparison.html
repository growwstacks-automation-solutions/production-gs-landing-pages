Nanobot's Radical Simplicity

Nanobot's architecture represents a fundamental rethink of how AI agents should be built. Where OpenClaw uses dozens of interconnected systems, Nanobot focuses on four core components:

  1. Input: Receives and processes incoming data
  2. Reasoning: Determines appropriate actions
  3. Action: Executes tasks and outputs
  4. Memory: Stores context for future interactions

This streamlined approach eliminates the layers of abstraction that make larger frameworks so difficult to work with. As shown at the 2:15 mark in the video, you can literally trace an entire decision cycle through Nanobot's code in minutes - something impossible with most modern AI agents.
