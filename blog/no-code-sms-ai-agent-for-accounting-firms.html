How the AI SMS Agent Works

The system combines Twilio for SMS infrastructure with OpenAI's language model to understand and respond to client questions. When a message arrives at your dedicated business number:

  1. AI analyzes the question using your firm's knowledge base (service details, FAQs, pricing)
  2. Instant response generated in your brand voice (1-2 sentences as configured)
  3. Human handoff triggered automatically for complex queries or when client requests

The entire conversation remains in SMS format - clients never know they're talking to AI unless the response makes it obvious (which we configure not to do).

Twilio Phone Number Setup

First, you'll need a Twilio phone number dedicated to client communications. The video shows the exact setup process at 3:42:

  1. Create a Twilio account (free trial available)
  2. Purchase a local or toll-free number ($1-2/month)
  3. Note your Account SID and Auth Token (credentials)
  4. Connect the number to your AI platform

Pro tip: Choose a number with your area code to appear local to clients. Toll-free numbers work nationwide but may seem less personal for small accounting firms.

Cost Analysis

Running this solution involves three cost components:

  1. Twilio SMS: $0.0075-$0.05 per message (incoming/outgoing)
  2. OpenAI API: ~$0.002 per AI response (varies by length)
  3. Platform fees: Optional (some charge per message or monthly)

Example: A firm receiving 500 client messages/month would pay approximately $25 for Twilio and $15 for OpenAI - totaling $40/month to automate what would cost $800+ in staff time.
