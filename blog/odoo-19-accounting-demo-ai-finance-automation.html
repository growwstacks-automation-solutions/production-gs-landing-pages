Instant Audit Reports in Minutes

Creating audit reports traditionally requires days of manual work compiling financial data, writing narratives, and formatting documents. Odoo 19's new audit report creator eliminates this bottleneck by automating the entire process.

Users simply name the report, specify the date range for journals to include, and select from pre-made legal text templates that automatically adapt to your financial data. The system includes all standard accounting reports and allows for custom filtering to highlight specific results.

90% faster audit reporting: What previously took days now completes in minutes, with the ability to add signatures from multiple team members, include template annexes, and attach supporting documents directly from the Documents app before generating a professional PDF instantly.

Smart Tax Management

Keeping track of tax filing deadlines and requirements across different jurisdictions creates constant stress for finance teams. Odoo 19's centralized tax return menu provides complete visibility into all filing obligations.

The system automatically adapts to local tax requirements in different countries, listing each return type with its deadline and current status. Tax mapping has been simplified by moving it from fiscal positions to the tax record itself, making it easier to specify which fiscal positions and taxes are affected by changes.

AI-Powered Bank Reconciliation

Bank reconciliation remains one of the most time-consuming accounting tasks, often requiring hours of manual matching each month. Odoo 19 uses AI to automatically reconcile most transactions by matching them with existing invoices and bills based on partner, label, and amount.

For the remaining transactions, intuitive action buttons like Set Account help non-accountants correctly categorize items with helpful descriptions guiding account selection. The system supports collaborative review workflows, allowing accountants to quickly filter and mark transactions as reviewed.

85% automatic reconciliation: Odoo 19 handles the majority of bank transactions automatically, with tools to manually correct any OCR-processed statement balances and easy options to request accountant review when needed.

Real-Time Financial Reporting

Delayed financial reporting creates blind spots that impact business decisions. Odoo 19 provides comprehensive, real-time reporting including all standard accounting reports and partner reports accessible even to enterprise users with just the invoicing app installed.

The system enables payment follow-up through automated reminders sent via WhatsApp and other channels, helping businesses maintain healthy cash flow. Users can filter and customize reports to showcase specific results without needing accounting expertise.

Flexible Document Signing

Getting financial documents signed often creates bottlenecks with team members in different locations. Odoo 19's document signing workflow allows multiple signers to complete documents from anywhere.

Users can add signature fields at any point in documents and include additional sign fields as needed. The system integrates with Odoo's document management features, enabling teams to maintain complete audit trails of all signed financial documents.

Seamless System Integration

Disconnected financial systems create inefficiencies and data inconsistencies. Odoo 19's accounting features natively integrate with other Odoo apps like Documents for attaching supporting materials to financial reports.

The system connects with sales, inventory, and other operational modules to provide real-time financial visibility across the entire business. This eliminates manual data transfers between systems and ensures financial reports always reflect current operations.

What's New in Odoo 19

Odoo 19 introduces significant accounting improvements that transform financial workflows. The new audit report creator automates what was previously a manual, time-intensive process.

Bank reconciliation now leverages AI for automatic matching, while tax management has been streamlined with the centralized return dashboard. Reporting access has been expanded for non-accounting users, and document signing workflows have been enhanced for better collaboration.

Key upgrades: Instant audit reports, smarter bank reconciliation, simplified tax mapping, comprehensive reporting access, and improved document signing - all designed to save finance teams hundreds of hours annually.
