The Ad Production Problem This Solves

Most small businesses know they need video ads - 85% of marketers report video delivers higher ROI than static content. But between scripting, filming, and editing, producing just one professional 10-second ad can take 8+ hours and cost $500-$2000 if outsourced. This creates a massive gap between knowing you need video ads and actually having them.

The breakthrough comes from combining three technologies: Make.com for workflow automation, ChatGPT for script generation, and Sora-2 for AI video rendering. Together they eliminate the three biggest bottlenecks - creative direction, production resources, and editing time.

Key stat: The average small business website contains enough marketing content to generate 12-15 unique video ad variations automatically - that's $6,000-$15,000 worth of video production value sitting unused on your site right now.

Workflow Overview: From URL to Ad in 30 Minutes

The complete automation requires just four connected steps in Make.com, running hands-free after initial setup. At 2:15 in the video tutorial, you can see the entire workflow visualized as a single scenario with clear connection points between modules.

  1. Website Extraction: HTTP request pulls all HTML content from your target webpage
  2. AI Script Generation: ChatGPT analyzes content and outputs a professional video script with emotional hooks
  3. Sora-2 Rendering: AI generates the actual video based on the script (5-12 minute render time)
  4. Delivery & Storage: Completed MP4 automatically saves to your Google Drive

The true power lies in the prompt engineering that transforms raw website HTML into effective video scripts. The system message to ChatGPT specifies mandatory ad elements like: "Include a hook, visual direction, voice over script, call-to-action, and emotional tone (inspiring, urgent, or trustworthy)."

Step 1: Website Content Extraction

The workflow begins with a simple HTTP GET request to your target URL. This foundational step (shown at 3:42 in the video) requires no special authentication or APIs - just the webpage address you want to turn into an ad.

For best results, use pages with clear value propositions like:

  • Service/product pages with benefit-focused copy
  • Lead generation landing pages
  • "About Us" pages with origin stories
  • Testimonial/case study pages

The raw HTML output from this step feeds directly into the AI script generator. No manual content preparation is needed - the system works with your existing marketing messages.

Step 2: AI Video Script Generation

At 5:18 in the tutorial, you'll see the sophisticated prompt that transforms website content into video scripts. The key components are:

System Message: "You are an expert in creating and directing creative advertising videos for short form content of 10 seconds length." This establishes the AI's role and output format constraints.

The prompt then specifies mandatory ad elements that must appear in every generated script:

  • Hook: Attention-grabbing opening (problem/solution format)
  • Visual Direction: Scene descriptions for Sora-2 to render
  • Voiceover Script: Natural language audio track
  • Call-to-Action: Clear next step for viewers
  • Emotional Tone: Selected from 3 high-conversion options

This structured approach ensures every generated video has proper advertising architecture, not just random scenes from your content.

Step 3: Sora-2 Video Rendering

The AI-generated script feeds directly into Sora-2's API through Make.com's ChatGPT connector (demonstrated at 7:30). Important configuration notes:

  • Duration: Set to 10 seconds (leaves buffer under Sora-2's 12-second max)
  • Model: Sora-2 Pro preferred but standard works well too
  • Wait Time: 30 minutes allows for API queue delays

At 8:15, the video addresses Sora-2's current reliability limitations. While the technology is groundbreaking, about 15% of video generations may fail or hang at partial completion. The workflow accounts for this with:

  1. Status checking loops
  2. Automatic retries
  3. Error notifications

These safeguards make the system production-ready despite API inconsistencies.

Step 4: Automatic Delivery & Storage

Completed videos download through Sora-2's API and save directly to Google Drive (shown at 9:45). The workflow includes smart file naming with timestamps, making it easy to:

  • Track generation history
  • Compare multiple variations
  • Organize by campaign

Example filename: Sora2-Ad-20251201-1425.mp4 (December 1, 2025 at 2:45 PM)

From Google Drive, you can easily:

  1. Share directly to social platforms
  2. Import to video editors for finishing touches
  3. Add to ad management platforms

The entire process runs unattended after initiation - perfect for generating batches of ads overnight.
