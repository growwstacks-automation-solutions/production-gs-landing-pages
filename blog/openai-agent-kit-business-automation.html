How to Build Your First Agent in 5 Minutes

Here's the step-by-step process to create a basic customer support agent:

Step 1: Start with a template

Choose "Customer Support" from the pre-built templates. This gives you a foundation with common nodes already connected.

Step 2: Connect your knowledge base

Add a "Search Documents" node and link it to your help center or internal wiki. The agent will use this to find answers.

Step 3: Add response formatting

Insert a "Format Response" node that structures answers clearly, with source links for verification.

Step 4: Set safety guardrails

Add a "Content Filter" node to prevent inappropriate responses based on your guidelines.

Step 5: Test and publish

Use the built-in testing panel to ask sample questions, then publish to your website with one click.
