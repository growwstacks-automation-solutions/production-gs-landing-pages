How to Structure Calls for 50% Faster Bookings

The biggest mistake businesses make is treating the AI greeting like a voicemail. Unlike human receptionists, AI agents can't improvise responses to random questions about your opening message.

At 2:45 in the video, the expert reveals his proven formula: 1) Keep greetings to one unpunctuated sentence, 2) Always end with a direct question, and 3) Offer 2-3 clear paths like "Are you calling about billing, new services, or booking an appointment?"

Result: This structure reduced average call times from 3-4 minutes to just 90 seconds while increasing conversions. The directed paths prevent meandering conversations that often end without bookings.

Voice Selection: Clone vs Premade Voices

While voice cloning creates impressive continuity, premade voices often perform better for general use. The key is matching voice characteristics to your specific audience and use case.

For booking agents, use the voice filter to select: 1) High-quality voices only, 2) Appropriate regional accents, and 3) Gender matching your staff. HVAC companies in Texas saw 28% better conversions using a southern-accented female voice versus the default male voice.
