The Cold Email Dilemma

Every sales team faces the same challenge: generic emails get ignored, but personalized outreach doesn't scale. Manual research for each prospect can take 10-15 minutes - making 100 personalized emails a full day's work. Even then, deliverability issues mean many never reach the inbox.

This system solves both problems simultaneously. At 3:15 in the video, you'll see how it automatically researches each company and prospect, then generates emails that reference specific details about their business. The result? Emails that feel handcrafted, delivered at scale.

82% of buyers accept meetings when the outreach references their specific business challenges - but only 29% respond to generic templates. This gap is why personalized automation delivers 3-5x better response rates than manual blasts.

System Overview

The workflow begins with your lead list in Google Sheets or your CRM. For each prospect, it performs three key operations automatically: researches the company using Perplexity AI, generates a personalized email with DeepSeek, and sends it through Resend for maximum deliverability.

Here's the complete sequence:

  1. Make.com pulls prospect data from your spreadsheet/CRM
  2. Perplexity AI researches the company website and prospect's LinkedIn
  3. DeepSeek generates an email referencing specific company details
  4. Resend delivers the email with high inbox placement
  5. The system logs the sent email to prevent duplicates

This end-to-end automation handles what would normally require hours of manual work per day, while maintaining genuine personalization that drives responses.

Setting Up Make.com

Make.com (formerly Integromat) serves as the automation backbone. At 5:30 in the tutorial, you'll see how to create a new scenario and connect your Google Sheets. The key configuration is setting filters to only process rows where the "email sent" column is blank - this prevents duplicate outreach.

Critical Make.com settings:

  • Connect your Google Sheets account (or CRM API)
  • Set the filter: "Where Email Sent Date does not exist"
  • Limit to 100-250 rows per execution (avoids rate limits)
  • Add error handling for failed API calls

Pro Tip: Use Make.com's scheduling feature to run this automation daily during business hours. This spaces out your emails naturally rather than sending hundreds at once.

AI Research with Perplexity

Perplexity AI acts as your research assistant, visiting each company's website and the prospect's LinkedIn profile to gather relevant details. At 8:45 in the video, notice how the prompt instructs Perplexity to create a "detailed paragraph overview" - this becomes the foundation for personalization.

The research phase accomplishes three things:

  1. Understands the company's products/services
  2. Identifies the prospect's role and likely pain points
  3. Finds recent news or updates to reference

This research typically takes Perplexity 15-30 seconds per prospect - something that would take a human 10+ minutes to do manually. The output flows directly into the email generation step.

Email Generation with DeepSeek

DeepSeek (or your preferred LLM) synthesizes the research into a personalized email. At 12:20 in the tutorial, you'll see the critical prompt that ensures quality output: instructions to avoid hashtags, emojis, and keep the email under 10 lines for optimal readability.

The email generation follows this structure:

  • Personalized opener referencing the company
  • Value proposition tied to their specific business
  • Clear call-to-action (typically a meeting link)
  • Concise signature with your details

Key Insight: The prompt specifically instructs the AI to "use cold email best practices" and "pass the iPhone scroll test" - meaning the entire email is visible without scrolling on mobile devices. This small detail can double response rates.

High Deliverability with Resend

Resend.com solves the deliverability challenge that plagues most cold email efforts. Unlike sending through Gmail or Outlook (which may flag bulk emails as spam), Resend provides enterprise-grade infrastructure that maximizes inbox placement.

At 16:50 in the video, you'll see the Resend module configuration:

  1. From: Your professional email (e.g., you@yourdomain.com)
  2. To: The prospect's email from your spreadsheet
  3. Subject: Consistent but not spammy (e.g., "Partnership Inquiry")
  4. Body: The AI-generated personalized content

Resend's free tier allows 100 emails per day - perfect for small to medium outreach campaigns. For larger volumes, their paid plans maintain the same high deliverability.

Tracking and Avoiding Duplicates

The system automatically logs each sent email with a timestamp in your spreadsheet. At 18:30 in the tutorial, notice how the "Email Sent Date" column updates after each successful send - this prevents the system from contacting the same prospect twice.

For complete campaign management:

  • Add columns for Email 1 Date, Email 2 Date, etc.
  • Create separate Make.com scenarios for follow-up sequences
  • Include filters for response status (replied, booked meeting)
  • Set up notifications for positive responses

This tracking transforms your spreadsheet into a lightweight CRM, giving you full visibility into campaign performance without additional software.

Cost Breakdown

One of the most surprising aspects of this system is its affordability. While commercial tools like Apollo.io or Clay.com charge $100+/month, this DIY solution costs less than $5 per day to run at scale.

Cost components:

  1. Make.com: Free trial (1,000 operations/day) or $9/month
  2. Resend: Free for first 100 emails/day, then $20/month
  3. Perplexity: Free 1-year pro trial via PayPal
  4. DeepSeek: $2 provides enough credits for 500+ emails

At scale: Sending 100 personalized emails daily costs approximately $3.50 in AI credits and infrastructure - compared to $15-25 for equivalent manual outreach when factoring in labor costs.
