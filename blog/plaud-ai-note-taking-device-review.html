What Is Plaud AI Really?

Plaud AI isn't just another gadget — it's a dedicated AI note-taking device trusted by 1.5 million professionals globally. Unlike phone-based recording apps, Plaud AI works independently, meaning your battery isn't drained and recordings continue even when calls come in. The device records audio, transcribes in 112 languages, identifies multiple speakers, creates summaries, and has AI assistance built directly into the hardware.

There are three models available: the Plaud Note Pro at $189, the standard Plaud Note at $159, and the wearable Plaud Note Pin also at $159. The Pro version, which I used at the AI Bot Summit, offers enterprise-grade security with ISO 2701 compliance, making it safe for sensitive business conversations where confidential information is shared.

Plaud AI integrates with over 8,000 applications including CRM systems like HighLevel, ensuring that captured conversations flow directly into your business workflow without manual data entry.

From Capture to CRM Automation

The integration between Plaud AI and business systems like HighLevel creates a seamless workflow from conversation capture to execution. Here's the five-step process that happens automatically:

Step 1: Capture

Record conversations with leads or clients using the Plaud device. The recording continues uninterrupted regardless of phone activity or environmental noise.

Step 2: Process

Plaud AI transcribes the conversation and applies custom templates to extract specific information like contact details, pain points, and action items.

Step 3: Integrate

The structured data flows directly into your CRM system, creating new contacts and tagging them based on the conversation context.

Step 4: Automate

Your CRM generates follow-up tasks, sets reminders, and prioritizes actions based on the captured information.

Step 5: Execute

Return to your office with a complete, organized to-do list from the conference — ready for immediate action rather than days of organization.

This automation eliminates the 3-day business card organization process and replaces it with immediate, prioritized execution that closes deals faster.
