Speech Recognition at Scale

Generic speech-to-text services fail when handling diverse accents, specialized vocabularies (like utility account numbers), or noisy call environments. PolyAI builds ensembles of regional models - separate systems for US vs UK English, zip code recognition, and locale-specific naming conventions.

Through their NVIDIA partnership, PolyAI developed voice activity detection that automatically adapts to caller speech patterns, eliminating the frustrating "interruption game" common with rigid IVRs. The platform also allows boosting specific words (like account numbers) anywhere in a conversation, not just at predetermined points.

70% fewer transcription errors: PolyAI's specialized speech recognition models reduce errors by 70% compared to generic services, addressing the root cause of most poor AI agent experiences.

Multimodal Customer Engagement

Voice alone can't handle all customer service scenarios efficiently. PolyAI agents maintain voice as the primary channel while seamlessly integrating forms, location sharing, and media exchange - all within the same live call.

During the PG&E deployment, this allowed customers to verbally request a payment extension while simultaneously completing the digital form, with the AI agent processing both inputs in context. The solution reduced average handling time by 75% for complex transactions that previously required multiple touchpoints.

LLM Orchestration for Contact Centers

PolyAI's Raven models are LLMs specifically fine-tuned on customer service dialogues, producing more natural responses than general-purpose chatbots. The platform triggers backend integrations synchronously or asynchronously based on conversation flow, with dedicated inference instances for reliability.

Enterprises maintain control over data processing locations, with architectural flexibility to meet regional compliance requirements. This combination of specialized training and infrastructure enables features like PG&E's 50x scaling during outages while maintaining accurate, regulated communications.
