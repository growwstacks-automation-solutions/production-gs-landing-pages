The Hiring Problem AI Avatars Solve

Every hiring manager knows the time sink of initial candidate screenings. For 50 applicants, you're looking at 25+ hours of repetitive conversations asking the same basic questions. Worse, human screeners bring unconscious biases and inconsistent evaluation standards to these early interactions.

The breakthrough comes from combining two technologies: Beyond Presence's ultra-realistic digital humans and n8n's workflow automation. Together, they create interview avatars that conduct natural, engaging conversations while applying consistent evaluation criteria to every candidate.

25 hours → 25 minutes: What traditionally took a full workweek of screening now happens automatically, with AI avatars conducting initial interviews and delivering structured assessments for human review.

Beyond Presence: Digital Humans That Feel Real

Beyond Presence isn't another chatbot with a static avatar. Their technology creates interactive digital humans with natural facial expressions, body language, and most importantly - ultra-low latency responses under 250 milliseconds. This makes conversations flow naturally without the awkward pauses of typical AI systems.

The platform comes with multiple stock personas you can customize for your brand. "Laura," the interview avatar shown in our demo, can be configured with your company's specific greeting style, interview objectives, and knowledge base. You upload HR guidelines, position descriptions, and FAQs to create a consistent interviewing experience.

How n8n Powers the Interview Workflow

n8n serves as the automation engine that connects Beyond Presence with your other systems. When a candidate begins an interview, n8n triggers the appropriate conversation flow based on the position. It manages the data exchange between Beyond Presence, your knowledge base, and output systems like Google Sheets.

The real magic happens after the interview. n8n automatically processes the transcript through GPT-4.5 to generate a structured assessment including a fit score, key strengths, concerns, and next step recommendations. All this data gets appended to your candidate tracking system with timestamps for easy review.

No coding required: The entire system is built using n8n's visual workflow builder with drag-and-drop nodes for Beyond Presence, GPT analysis, and Google Sheets integration.

Step-by-Step: Building Your Interview Avatar

Step 1: Configure Your Beyond Presence Avatar

Select a stock persona or create a custom avatar that aligns with your employer brand. Upload your company's HR guidelines, position descriptions, and interview protocols to the knowledge base. Configure the greeting message and interview flow structure.

Step 2: Set Up the n8n Trigger

Create a webhook trigger in n8n that activates when a candidate starts an interview. This can be initiated from your ATS, a calendar booking system, or direct link sharing.

Step 3: Build the Conversation Flow

Use n8n's Beyond Presence node to structure the interview questions and follow-ups based on the position type. Include branching logic for different response paths.

Step 4: Configure Automated Analysis

Set up the GPT-4.5 analysis node to process completed interviews. Define your evaluation criteria and output format (we recommend JSON for easy parsing).

Step 5: Connect to Your Output Systems

Map the analysis results to your Google Sheets, ATS, or other tracking systems. Include candidate details, scores, and recommendations.

Implementation time: With our templates, you can have a basic interview avatar operational in under 30 minutes. Full customization typically takes 1-2 hours.

Automated Candidate Analysis Explained

The AI doesn't just transcribe interviews - it analyzes them against your specific criteria. For each candidate, the system generates a fit score (0-100), identifies key strengths aligned with the position requirements, flags potential concerns or gaps, and provides a clear recommendation (Strong Yes, Yes, No, Strong No).

All analysis happens through your configured GPT-4.5 prompt, which you can tailor to emphasize technical skills, cultural fit, or other priorities. The system maintains a complete log including the raw transcript, analysis timestamp, and structured JSON output for integration with other tools.
