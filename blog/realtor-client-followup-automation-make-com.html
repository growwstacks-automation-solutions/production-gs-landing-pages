How the Automation Works

The system solves three critical problems simultaneously: remembering when to follow up, knowing what to say, and actually sending the message. Here's the elegant workflow:

  1. Track interactions: Your CRM or spreadsheet records each client contact with date and outcome
  2. Calculate timing: Make.com checks daily which clients hit the 7-day no-response mark
  3. Generate message: AI crafts a personalized follow-up based on your last conversation
  4. Send & update: The message sends automatically and updates your records

Unlike batch email blasts, this creates genuine one-to-one communication at scale. At 4:30 in the tutorial, you'll see how the AI references specific details from previous interactions to craft messages that feel hand-written.

The 7-Day Timing Logic

The variable module at 10:20 handles the crucial timing calculation. It:

  • Takes the "Last Contacted" date from your spreadsheet
  • Calculates days elapsed since that date
  • Compares it to your desired follow-up window (default 7 days)

The filter then ensures messages only send when the timing is perfect. You can customize this - some clients might need 3-day follow-ups, others 14-day. The system adapts based on columns in your spreadsheet.

Key insight: The 7-day window isn't arbitrary. Our data shows this is when client interest begins to wane but before they've fully disengaged.

Automatically Updating Records

The final piece at 12:30 shows how the system updates your records after each follow-up. This prevents duplicate messages and creates an accurate contact history. The workflow:

  1. Marks the new contact date in your spreadsheet/CRM
  2. Records the message that was sent
  3. Resets the follow-up timer

This creates a self-maintaining system. As one client put it: "It's like having an assistant who never forgets to follow up, but better because it references past conversations I'd probably forget."
