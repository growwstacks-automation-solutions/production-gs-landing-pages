Current Limitations to Consider

While impressive, Agent 3 has some constraints businesses should note:

  • Platform lock-in: Uses Replit-specific services like their mail system
  • Learning period: May require some trial and error to phrase requests optimally

At 5:30, the creator mentions needing to upgrade to publish the daily stock alert - a common limitation for advanced features.
