Creating Your First Voice Agent (Step-by-Step)

Follow these steps to build an AI receptionist that handles appointment scheduling - the #1 use case for medical practices and service businesses:

Step 1: Select Agent Type

After logging in, click "Create Agent" and choose "Voice" then "Conversation Flow". This creates a branched dialogue agent rather than a simple Q&A bot.

Step 2: Pick a Template

Select the "Medical Center Receptionist" template (shown at 3:42 in the video). It includes pre-built logic for appointment booking, rescheduling, and cancellation flows.

Step 3: Configure Settings

In the right sidebar, set your preferred voice (28 options) and language. The default prompt already defines the agent's professional medical receptionist persona.

Step 4: Test Immediately

Click the audio test button to have a live conversation with your agent. Try rescheduling an appointment as shown at 5:18 in the tutorial.

Pro tip: The knowledge base section lets you upload your FAQ documents, service menus, and policy details so the agent can answer specific questions about your business.
