  • Multi-prompt agents - Handles branching logic for complex calls
  • Conversational flow - Our recommended choice) - Fully dynamic dialogs that adapt to caller needs

When creating your agent (4:15 timestamp):

  1. Select "Conversational Flow" for most business uses
