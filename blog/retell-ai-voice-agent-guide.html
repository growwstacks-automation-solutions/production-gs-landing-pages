Inbound vs. Outbound Calling Strategies

Retell AI handles both incoming and outgoing calls with equal sophistication. At 12:30 in the tutorial, we demonstrate two powerful use cases:

Inbound: Customers call your business number and interact with the AI agent. Perfect for 24/7 availability without staff overhead.

Outbound: Upload a CSV of phone numbers for the agent to call automatically. Ideal for appointment reminders, follow-ups, or lead nurturing campaigns.

The same agent handles both modes, ensuring consistent branding and customer experience across all touchpoints.
