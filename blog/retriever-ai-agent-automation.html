What Retriever AI Can Actually Do

Most business owners and operators waste countless hours on repetitive digital tasks - web research, data entry, social media posting, and more. These tasks are essential but time-consuming when done manually. Retriever AI changes this equation completely.

This isn't just another AI assistant that provides information - it's an autonomous agent that actually performs actions in your browser. Give it a task in plain English, and it will navigate websites, interact with elements, extract data, and complete workflows just like a human would - but much faster and without errors.

Key capability: Retriever AI can automate virtually any browser-based task including form filling, web scraping, social media posting, stock research, data extraction, and executing complex multi-step workflows across multiple websites.

Chrome Extension Capabilities

The Retriever AI Chrome extension brings this powerful automation directly into your browser. After a simple installation, you can access the AI agent from any tab.

Key features of the extension include:

  • Direct control over your current browser session
  • Ability to automate actions on any website you're viewing
  • Option to create shortcuts for frequently repeated tasks
  • Integration with MCP clients for expanded functionality

At the 5:15 timestamp, the video shows how the extension can be used to automate LinkedIn posts without ever leaving the platform - a game-changer for social media managers and business owners.

The cloud version shines for:
