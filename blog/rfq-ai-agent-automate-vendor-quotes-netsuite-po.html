The Hidden Costs of Manual Procurement

Most finance teams underestimate how much time manual RFQ processes consume. For every purchase request, procurement specialists spend hours:

  • Researching and identifying potential vendors
  • Drafting and sending customized RFQ emails
  • Chasing late responses from suppliers
  • Manually extracting data from PDF quotes
  • Building comparison spreadsheets
  • Negotiating pricing via back-and-forth emails
  • Creating POs in NetSuite

Time study: The average $50M company processes 120 RFQs monthly, consuming 45 minutes per RFQ - totaling 90 hours of staff time. That's 2.25 FTEs dedicated entirely to manual procurement tasks.

How RFQ Automation Works

The AI agent handles the complete procurement cycle from RFQ creation to PO generation. Here's the automated workflow:

Step 1: RFQ Form Completion

Users complete a digital RFQ form with item details, quantities, and specifications. The form includes smart fields that auto-populate based on past purchases.

Step 2: Vendor Identification

The AI analyzes the items and selects vendors from your approved supplier list based on historical performance, item specialization, and geographic proximity.

Step 3: Automated Email Dispatch

The system sends professionally formatted RFQ emails using customizable templates. It tracks opens and follows up with non-responsive vendors.

Efficiency gain: What typically takes 15 minutes per vendor (finding contacts, customizing emails) now happens automatically in under 60 seconds.

Intelligent Vendor Selection

The platform goes beyond simple vendor databases. Its AI evaluates multiple factors when recommending suppliers:

  • Historical performance: On-time delivery rates, quality issues
  • Item specialization: Vendor expertise with specific product categories
  • Geographic optimization: Shipping costs and lead times
  • Capacity analysis: Current vendor workload
  • Diversity status: Minority/women-owned business preferences

At 2:15 in the demo video, you'll see how the system surfaces vendor recommendations with confidence scores based on these criteria.

AI-Powered Price Negotiation

The platform's most innovative feature is its built-in negotiation assistant. When vendors respond with initial quotes:

  1. The AI analyzes the quote against historical pricing benchmarks
  2. It suggests optimal counteroffer ranges based on market data
  3. Users can negotiate via chat interface (shown at 3:40 in the video)
  4. The AI drafts professional counteroffer emails
  5. It tracks all negotiation threads in the RFQ record

Results: Early adopters report achieving 8-12% better pricing through AI-assisted negotiations while maintaining stronger vendor relationships.

Automated Bid Comparison

When all quotes are received (or deadlines pass), the AI analyzes every response:

  • Extracts line-item pricing from emails and PDFs
  • Normalizes data for apples-to-apples comparison
  • Flags vendors who didn't quote all requested items
  • Calculates total cost including shipping and taxes
  • Generates a scored recommendation for each line item

The system produces a vendor comparison matrix (shown at 4:55 in the demo) that lets procurement teams make data-driven decisions in minutes instead of hours.

NetSuite PO Generation

After selecting winning bids:

  1. The system pre-populates a NetSuite PO with all line items
  2. It applies proper GL coding based on item categories
  3. Attaches the approved quote as supporting documentation
  4. Routes for approval based on dollar thresholds
  5. Posts the completed PO to NetSuite

The demo shows the end-to-end flow from RFQ to PO in NetSuite at 5:30. What typically takes 20-30 minutes of manual data entry now happens with one click.
