The Perfect Real Estate Conversation Flow

Every successful buyer interaction follows a proven script structure that AI agents execute flawlessly:

  1. Brand introduction — "This is Sarah calling from True Brokers"
  2. Property confirmation — "I saw you just inquired about that three-bedroom on Oak Street"
  3. Timeline qualification — "Are you currently renting or do you have a place to sell first?"
  4. Budget verification — "We're preapproved around $425k"
  5. Neighborhood preferences — "Are you set on Oak Street or open to nearby neighborhoods?"
  6. Specialty matching — "I'd love to connect you with Marcus who specializes in family relocations"
  7. Calendar scheduling — "Does tomorrow afternoon work or is morning better?"

This flow ensures no critical question gets missed while maintaining natural conversation rhythm. The AI adapts to buyer responses in real-time, just as a top ISA would.

The 14-Day Follow-Up System

When buyers don't answer the initial call (about 60% of cases), the AI launches an automated 14-day nurture sequence:

  • Day 1: Voicemail + text with property details
  • Day 3: New listing alert in their price range
  • Day 7: Neighborhood market update
  • Day 10: Price drop notification
  • Day 14: "Last chance" callback attempt

The system tracks all interactions in your CRM, ensuring human agents have full context when they eventually connect. This persistent follow-up converts 28% of initially unreachable leads into booked showings.
