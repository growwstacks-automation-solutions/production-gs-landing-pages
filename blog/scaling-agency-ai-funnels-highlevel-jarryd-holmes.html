The $24,000 Mistake That Changed Everything

Every successful entrepreneur has a "before" story - the struggle that shaped their approach. For Jarryd Holmes, it was a $24,000 loss that nearly ended his agency before it began. He trusted the wrong ad agency that promised the world but delivered just one sale from a heavily discounted offer.

This failure became the catalyst for his HighLevel journey. As he explains: "I worked out that doing sales coaching wasn't my path - helping businesses with marketing and sales automation was where I could make the biggest impact." The pivot to HighLevel allowed him to systemize his approach and scale beyond one-on-one services.

Key insight: Jarryd's automated webinar system onboarded 26 clients in December-January (traditionally the slowest months) by focusing on HighLevel's custom values and VSL funnels. This proved the power of automation over manual sales efforts.

Why Focusing on One Thing 10X'd Results

New HighLevel users often fall into what Jarryd calls "shiny object syndrome" - trying to implement every feature at once. He shares how initially overwhelming clients with all of HighLevel's capabilities actually hurt conversions.

The breakthrough came when he simplified his approach: "We focused on moving the needle with one systemized offer - starting with white label Suite as an intro, then automated webinars." This focus allowed them to perfect their process before expanding.

Simple starter offers that work: Jarryd recommends beginning with either the social content planner ("Do you struggle to post consistently?") or form fill automation ("Do your website inquiries go unanswered?"). Both are easy to implement but deliver immediate client value.

The Voice AI Breakthrough That Won Awards

Jarryd's agency gained recognition through their innovative use of HighLevel's voice AI capabilities. By combining this with chat automation, they supported 16 clients with voice AI integrations and earned 14 nominations at the Australian National AI Awards.

What makes their approach unique? As Jarryd explains: "We don't just implement voice AI - we design complete call handling systems that qualify leads, book appointments, and provide 24/7 responsiveness for service businesses." This full-funnel thinking sets them apart.

The Niching Myth: Solution vs Industry Focus

Conventional wisdom says to niche by industry, but Jarryd challenges this: "We've got clients from Yamaha dealers to skip bin businesses - what unites them is the problem we solve, not their industry." This product-focused niching allows for wider reach while maintaining specialization.

He shares a powerful example: After success with one skip bin business, they emailed 10 others in the area with a Loom video showing results. This simple campaign converted multiple clients in the same industry - but only after proving the solution worked generally first.

The Community Secret Behind Rapid Growth

Jarryd emphasizes HighLevel's collaborative community as his "secret weapon." When stuck on a complex automation for a training organization, he reached out to the Funnel Doc who provided a solution within days.

"The support is incredible," Jarryd notes. "When we lost a critical phone number, the community and HighLevel team had it restored within hours." This ecosystem accelerates learning and problem-solving in ways isolated agencies can't match.

Jarryd's #1 Advice for HighLevel Startups

For new HighLevel users, Jarryd's message is clear: "Done is better than perfect." He advocates for simple, testable offers over elaborate builds. His V2 strategy: launch a basic version, validate demand, then refine.

He shares how his Google Business Profile offer initially bombed with $2,000 in wasted ad spend. After refining based on feedback, the V2 became profitable. The key? Testing quickly and cheaply before heavy investment.

Key Takeaways

Jarryd Holmes' journey from near-failure to award-winning agency owner reveals universal lessons for HighLevel users. His focus on solving specific problems (not just selling features), collaborative approach, and willingness to test quickly provide a roadmap for sustainable growth.

In summary: 1) Start with one core offer, 2) Leverage the HighLevel community, 3) Test solutions before perfecting them, and 4) Niche by problem solved rather than industry. This approach helped Jarryd 8X his ROI from community events alone.
