The 300ms Latency Problem

Voice AI applications face an invisible barrier - the speed of light. When calls from Australia must route to US-based AI services, the round-trip latency creates 250-300ms delays before processing even begins. This physics limitation causes:

  • 12% drop in call completion rates due to unnatural pauses
  • 31% higher misinterpretation of fast speech patterns
  • Limited ability to use advanced models like GPT-4 due to compounding delays

The LiveKit dashboard solution emerged from this frustration. By hosting AI models regionally and optimizing the entire voice pipeline, response times plummet to 25-30ms - faster than human reaction time.

Key insight: Every 100ms of latency reduces conversation naturalness by 18%. The dashboard's regional hosting cuts 270ms of transit time, making AI agents feel instantly responsive.
