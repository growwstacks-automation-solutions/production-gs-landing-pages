The $15/Hour Problem Every Local Business Has

Walk into any busy salon, restaurant, or service business and you'll find the same expensive problem - a human receptionist answering repetitive questions about hours, appointments, and locations. At $15/hour plus benefits, these employees cost businesses $2,400+/month just to handle basic inquiries that follow predictable patterns.

Worse yet, many businesses pay overtime rates for after-hours coverage or lose potential customers when calls go unanswered. The owner of a mid-sized roofing company shared how they missed 37 calls in one month because their receptionist was overloaded - representing $18,500 in lost projects.

Key insight: 82% of calls to local service businesses fall into just 5 categories: operating hours, appointment scheduling, location/directions, service pricing, and after-hours emergencies. These are perfect for AI automation.

Modern voice AI can handle these routine calls at 10% the cost of human staff. An AI receptionist doesn't take breaks, never calls in sick, and provides consistent 24/7 coverage. The technology has reached a point where callers can't tell they're speaking to an AI unless told directly.
