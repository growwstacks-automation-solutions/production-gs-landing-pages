How the AI Receptionist Works

The system uses advanced natural language processing to understand callers and respond appropriately. It can handle common scenarios like appointment scheduling, service inquiries, and basic customer support. For complex issues, it can transfer to a human or take detailed messages.

Setting up a new client takes just three simple steps:

  1. Customize the script: Input the business name, services, and common questions
  2. Connect the phone number: Either port their existing number or get a new one
  3. Set up calendar integration: Connect to their scheduling system if they have one

No coding required: The entire setup uses simple forms and dropdown menus. You don't need any technical skills to get a business up and running.
