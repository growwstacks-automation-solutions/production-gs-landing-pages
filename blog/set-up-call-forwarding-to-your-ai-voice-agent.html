Method 1: Instant Call Forwarding Setup

Instant forwarding routes all calls directly to your AI agent, bypassing your phone completely. This is ideal for businesses that want 24/7 automated response or specific departments like after-hours support.

Step 1: Connect Your Phone Number

First, ensure your voice agent platform (like Vapi or Retell AI) has a connected phone number through Twilio or another VoIP provider. This will be the destination for forwarded calls.

Step 2: Enable Forwarding

On your smartphone or VoIP system:

  1. Open Settings → Phone → Call Forwarding
  2. Toggle forwarding to "On"
  3. Enter your AI agent's full number including country code (e.g., +1XXX-XXX-XXXX)
  4. Save settings

Pro tip: Always test with a second phone before going live. Call from another number to verify the AI agent answers immediately (shown at 2:15 in the video).

Method 2: Conditional Forwarding (Busy/No Answer)

For businesses that want human-first response with AI backup, conditional forwarding only routes calls to your agent when you're unavailable. This maintains personal touch while preventing missed opportunities.

Step 1: Disable Live Voicemail

On iOS devices, you must first disable Live Voicemail in Settings → Phone → Live Voicemail to allow conditional forwarding to work properly.

Step 2: Enter Forwarding Codes

Using your phone's dial pad:

  1. No Answer Forwarding: Dial * * 61 * +[agent number] # then call
  2. Busy Forwarding: Dial * * 67 * +[agent number] # then call
  3. Unreachable Forwarding: Dial * * 62 * +[agent number] # then call

Each successful configuration will show a "Settings registration succeeded" message (demonstrated at 4:10 in the video).

Testing & Troubleshooting Your Setup

Proper testing ensures your call forwarding works as intended before relying on it for business calls. Follow this verification process:

Test 1: Instant Forwarding

Call your number from another phone - your AI agent should answer immediately without your phone ringing. If not:

  • Verify you entered the full number including country code
  • Check that forwarding is toggled on in settings
  • Wait 2 minutes and test again (carrier updates can take time)

Test 2: Conditional Forwarding

For each scenario (busy, no answer, unreachable):

  1. Initiate the condition (e.g., make your line busy by starting another call)
  2. Call from a second phone - should route to AI agent after 20-30 seconds
  3. Verify agent handles the call properly

Remember: To disable all forwarding, dial # # 21 # and call. Individual conditions can be disabled with # # 61 #, # # 67 #, or # # 62 # (shown at 6:45 in the video).
