Step-by-Step Installation

Getting started takes just minutes. From your WordPress dashboard, navigate to Plugins → Add New and search for "AI Chatbot for WordPress by JotForm". Install and activate the plugin.

After activation, you'll need to connect your WordPress site to JotForm's servers. The plugin will guide you through this simple authorization process (timestamp 1:15 in the video). Once connected, you can either:

  • Create a new chatbot from scratch with your own description
  • Use one of JotForm's pre-configured agent templates

For first-time users, we recommend starting with the feedback chatbot template to understand how the system works before creating custom agents.

Agent Configuration Options

The real power comes from customizing your chatbot's personality and capabilities. At 2:30 in the tutorial, you'll see the key settings that shape your agent's behavior:

1. Agent Role

This defines how your chatbot presents itself (e.g., "Customer Support Specialist" or "Sales Consultant"). The role influences the type of language and level of expertise the AI demonstrates.

2. Tone of Voice

Choose between friendly, professional, or casual tones. While custom tone options aren't available yet, the three presets cover most business needs effectively.

3. Chat Guidelines

This is where you fine-tune personality traits. For example: "You are a 40-year-old professional who avoids slang and provides concise, accurate answers."

Appearance Customization

First impressions matter. At 3:45 in the video, you'll see how to make the chatbot visually match your brand:

  • Color scheme: Change all interface colors including the chat bubble, buttons, and background
  • Layout: Choose between minimal (just an icon) or extended (shows greeting text)
  • Position: Place the chat button on left or right side of screen
  • Visibility: Set to appear automatically or require user click

These options ensure the chatbot integrates seamlessly with your site's design rather than looking like a generic add-on.

Setting Up the Knowledge Base

The most powerful feature appears at 5:20 in the tutorial. The knowledge base transforms your chatbot from generic to expert by feeding it:

  1. Website content: The bot automatically crawls your pages to learn about your offerings
  2. Uploaded documents: Add PDFs, Word files, or text with product details
  3. URL sources: Point to external knowledge bases or documentation
  4. Q&A pairs: Pre-program answers to common customer questions

Pro tip: Start with 10-15 key Q&A pairs covering your most frequent inquiries. This gives the AI a strong foundation before it begins learning from conversations.

Advanced Visibility Rules

Not every page needs a chatbot. At 6:10, the tutorial shows how to control where your agent appears:

  • Device targeting: Show only on mobile or desktop as needed
  • Page rules: Hide on contact pages (where forms live) or show only on product pages
  • Timing: Set the chatbot to appear after X seconds or when scrolling

These rules prevent chatbot fatigue while ensuring it appears where most valuable. For eCommerce sites, we recommend showing the chatbot on product pages but hiding on checkout pages to reduce distractions.
