The Hidden Cost of Manual Slides

Every week, knowledge workers waste hours performing mechanical slide work that creates zero strategic value. Meetings happen, notes are taken in Zoom or Fireflies, data lives in CRMs and forms — then someone manually reconstructs all that raw information into PowerPoint or Google Slides.

The disruption isn't about making slide creation faster — it's about eliminating slide creation as a discrete task entirely. When Gamma's AI design engine connects to Claude's reasoning and Zapier's automation, presentations become a byproduct of work already being done.

70% of presentation time is wasted on formatting: Teams spend just 30% of their slide time on actual thinking and strategy. The rest goes to layout decisions, visual consistency, and rebuilding the same structures repeatedly.

Zapier as the Automation Glue

Zapier connects Gamma and Claude to thousands of tools without code, creating seamless pipelines where:

  • Zoom meeting ends → transcript goes to Claude
  • Claude finishes analysis → triggers Gamma
  • Gamma completes deck → shares to Slack/Email

This end-to-end automation means no human ever needs to "make slides" — they simply appear as a natural output of work happening elsewhere in your systems.
