The Thermostat Adjustment Problem

Property managers face a constant battle with thermostat settings. Guests arrive expecting perfect temperatures, while owners want energy-efficient defaults when properties are vacant. Manual adjustments create:

  • 15+ minutes wasted per booking adjusting temperatures
  • Guest complaints when settings aren't perfect at check-in
  • Energy waste when thermostats aren't reset after checkout

The solution shown in the video automates this entire process by connecting smart thermostats to booking calendars through Airtable. When a booking starts, the system automatically adjusts to the guest's preferred temperature. At checkout, it returns to the owner's default setting.
