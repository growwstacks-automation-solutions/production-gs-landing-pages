The Onboarding Time Drain

Picture this: A new client just paid for your premium service. Exciting! But now comes the tedious part - digging through folders to find the right onboarding documents, composing a personalized email, and manually updating your CRM. What should be a celebratory moment becomes a 30-40 minute administrative chore.

For coaches, consultants, and service providers offering multiple packages (like 1-hour consultations vs. 3-month programs), the problem compounds. Each service requires different materials, but manually identifying and sending them creates friction that can sour the client experience before you even begin working together.

68% of clients judge a business by how organized their onboarding process feels. Manual document hunting creates unnecessary friction during what should be your first impression.

How Automation Solves It

The solution lies in connecting three simple systems: Your payment records (Google Sheets), document storage (Dropbox), and email system. When a new payment appears in your sheet, Make.com automatically:

  1. Identifies which service was purchased
  2. Retrieves the correct documents from storage
  3. Sends a personalized email with attachments
  4. Updates your CRM with the new client status

What used to take 40 minutes now happens in seconds - while you sleep, take meetings, or enjoy family time. Clients receive immediate confirmation with all necessary materials, creating professional momentum from day one.

For each service path (demonstrated at 9:45), you'll configure two key actions:

The tutorial shows how to clone these actions for each service type (13:20), saving setup time while ensuring every client gets a tailored experience. Your email template can include meeting scheduling links, next steps, or warm welcomes - all automated.

CRM Integration

The final piece (shown at 16:10) updates your CRM automatically. Whether you use HubSpot, Salesforce, or another system, Make.com can:

  • Mark the contact as a paying customer
  • Note which service they purchased
  • Add timestamps for accurate sales reporting

This eliminates manual data entry errors while giving your team real-time visibility into new clients. The entire system runs silently in the background, handling what used to be your least favorite administrative tasks.
