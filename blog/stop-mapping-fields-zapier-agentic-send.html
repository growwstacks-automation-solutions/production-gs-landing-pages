How Agentic Send Works

Agentic Send introduces a revolutionary approach: instead of mapping fields, you describe your intent in plain English. At 1:30 in the video, we see how the AI reads the entire payload, understands context, and handles formatting automatically.

The system works by analyzing all available data from your trigger app. It identifies phone numbers even if they're in different formats, understands time references in various formats, and can even personalize messages based on other data in the payload. You simply tell it what you want to accomplish, like "Send an SMS reminder 15 minutes before each meeting with the client's name and appointment time."

Here's what happens behind the scenes when you use Agentic Send:

  1. The AI analyzes all incoming data from your trigger app
  2. It identifies relevant information (phone numbers, names, times, etc.)
  3. The system formats everything correctly based on your instructions
  4. Messages are sent via your preferred channel (SMS, WhatsApp, or call)

Setting Up Your First Agentic Send

Creating your first Agentic Send workflow takes just minutes compared to the hours required for traditional field mapping. Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Create a new Zap

Start as you normally would by selecting your trigger app. This could be your calendar, CRM, e-commerce platform, or any of the 8,000+ apps Zapier supports.

Step 2: Choose Agentic Send as your action

When selecting your action app, look for "Agentic Send" under SMS, WhatsApp, or Voice Call options. This replaces the traditional field-mapping interface.

Step 3: Describe what you want to happen

In plain English, tell the system what you want to accomplish. For example: "Send an SMS to the client's mobile number 15 minutes before their appointment with the time and location."

Step 4: Test and activate

Run a test to see how the AI interprets your instructions. You can refine your description if needed, then activate your Zap.

Pro Tip: Start with simple instructions, then gradually add complexity as you see how the AI handles your data. The system learns from your adjustments.
