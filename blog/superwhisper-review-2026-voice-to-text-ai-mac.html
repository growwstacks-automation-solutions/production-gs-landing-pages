Why Superwhisper is Different

Traditional dictation software simply transcribes words without understanding their meaning or context. This leads to hours wasted editing transcripts filled with filler words, incorrect punctuation, and poorly structured sentences. Superwhisper solves this by combining advanced AI with intelligent post-processing.

Unlike cloud-based alternatives, Superwhisper processes everything locally on your Mac by default. This means your private conversations and sensitive information never leave your device. The AI understands whether you're writing code documentation, marketing emails, or technical specifications - adapting its formatting accordingly.

70% less editing time: Users report Superwhisper's automatic cleanup of filler words and grammar corrections reduces post-dictation editing by nearly three-quarters compared to basic transcription tools.

2-Minute Setup Process

Getting started with Superwhisper couldn't be simpler. There's no complex configuration or technical knowledge required - just drag the application to your Applications folder and grant microphone permissions when prompted.

At first launch, you'll select your preferred language model (local for privacy or cloud for enhanced accuracy). The intuitive interface presents all options clearly, with helpful tooltips explaining each setting. Within moments, you'll be dictating text anywhere on your Mac using the default Option+Space hotkey.

Key Features That Stand Out

Superwhisper's real power lies in its post-processing capabilities. The AI automatically removes verbal hesitations ("um", "ah") and filler words while maintaining natural flow. It intelligently formats text based on context - adding proper punctuation to emails, correct indentation to code, and appropriate headings to documentation.

The sidebar houses preconfigured modes for different use cases, along with options to create custom configurations. Each mode can have its own vocabulary terms, formatting rules, and processing intensity. This level of customization is unprecedented in voice-to-text software.

9 customizable modes: Organize different workflows (coding, emails, documentation) and quickly switch between them using keyboard shortcuts (Option+Shift+1 through 9).

Real-World Use Cases

Superwhisper shines in three specific scenarios that traditionally consume hours of typing time. First, AI assistance - when working with tools like Cursor or Claude, explaining features verbally is faster and clearer than typing. Superwhisper captures these explanations perfectly formatted for prompts.

Second, email composition. Responding to messages or drafting newsletters becomes effortless as Superwhisper automatically adds proper greetings, punctuation, and sign-offs. The result sounds professional without robotic stiffness. Third, technical documentation - dictating GitHub readmes or API specs produces clean, commit-ready text immediately.

Custom Modes for Specific Workflows

The ability to create specialized modes separates Superwhisper from competitors. Each custom mode can have unique instructions about how to process speech input. For developers, this might mean strict code formatting rules. For content creators, it could involve specific tone adjustments.

Creating a new mode is straightforward. Define the processing rules, select the appropriate model (local or cloud), and specify formatting preferences. The system remembers these settings per mode, allowing seamless switching between different workflows without reconfiguration.

Privacy Benefits of Local Processing

In an era of increasing data privacy concerns, Superwhisper's local processing capability provides peace of mind. All voice processing happens on your Mac unless you explicitly choose cloud models for specific tasks. No recordings are sent to external servers by default.

The local models are surprisingly capable, handling technical jargon and domain-specific vocabulary well. For situations requiring maximum accuracy, cloud models can be enabled per-mode while maintaining local processing for other uses. This flexibility lets you balance privacy and precision as needed.
