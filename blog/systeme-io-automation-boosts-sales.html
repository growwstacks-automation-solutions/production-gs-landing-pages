The Phone Number Problem

Every marketer knows phone numbers convert better than emails - texts get 98% open rates versus 20% for email. Yet most businesses let these golden opportunities collect dust in their CRM. Why? Because manually checking for new leads and extracting phone numbers creates frustrating delays.

The solution lies in automating instant notifications. When Systeme.io detects a form submission, it can immediately email you the lead's details - including that precious phone number. This cuts follow-up time from hours to seconds.

Key stat: Leads contacted within 5 minutes are 9x more likely to convert than those reached after 30 minutes.

How The Automation Works

This workflow uses Systeme.io's built-in automation rules to trigger email notifications whenever someone completes your opt-in form. Unlike complex CRM setups, it requires just three simple components:

  1. Opt-in form: Any Systeme.io page collecting phone numbers
  2. Trigger: "Funnel step form subscribe" event
  3. Action: "Send email" with personalized lead details

The magic happens through Systeme.io's personalization tags. These automatically insert form field values (name, email, phone) into your notification email template. No manual data entry required.

Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1: Access Automation Rules

Navigate to your opt-in page in Systeme.io and click "Automation Rules" in the left sidebar. This is where you'll build the notification workflow.

Step 2: Create New Rule

Click "Add New Rule" and select "Funnel step form subscribe" as your trigger. This ensures the automation runs whenever someone submits your form.

Step 3: Configure Email Action

Instead of typical actions like adding tags, choose "Send email to specific address." Compose a message using Systeme.io's personalization tags to include:

  • First name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any other collected form fields

Pro tip: Use a subject line like "New Lead: {{first_name}}" to make notifications instantly recognizable in your inbox.

Testing The Workflow

Always test new automations with real submissions. At 1:23 in the video, you'll see the creator submit a test form and receive the notification email within seconds.

Key testing checklist:

  1. Submit test form with dummy data
  2. Verify email arrives within 60 seconds
  3. Check all personalization tags populated correctly
  4. Confirm phone number format is SMS-ready

If notifications delay, check your Systeme.io email sending settings and spam folder. The automation itself typically processes within seconds.

Advanced Use Cases

While the basic workflow delivers tremendous value, you can enhance it further:

  • Team notifications: Add multiple email recipients or a distribution list
  • Lead scoring: Include custom form responses to prioritize follow-ups
  • CRM integration: Forward emails to tools like HubSpot or Salesforce
  • SMS forwarding: Use email-to-SMS gateways for instant texting

The core principle remains: capture phone numbers immediately and act fast. This automation ensures you never miss that critical first contact window.
