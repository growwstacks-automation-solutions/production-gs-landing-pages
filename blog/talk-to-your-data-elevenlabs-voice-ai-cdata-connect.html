The system combines three powerful technologies: ElevenLabs' voice synthesis, CData Connect's data virtualization, and an AI reasoning layer that understands business context. When you ask a question like "Which account is most at risk?" (1:10 in the video), here's what happens:

Implementation Considerations

While the demo makes the technology look effortless, successful implementations require careful planning. Here are key factors to consider:

  • Data quality: The AI's recommendations are only as good as the underlying data
  • Access controls: Voice queries must respect existing data permissions
  • Custom vocabulary: The system needs training on your industry terminology
  • Use case prioritization: Start with high-impact scenarios like the at-risk accounts example

Most organizations find the greatest success by beginning with a focused pilot in one department or for one specific business process before expanding more broadly.
