Three-step process: 1) ElevenLabs converts speech to text 2) CData Connect queries relevant systems 3) AI synthesizes responses into natural language.

For the customer risk query, CData Connect simultaneously pulls:

  • Usage metrics from Snowflake

The system then applies business rules (like the risk scoring algorithm) before ElevenLabs converts the analysis back to human-like speech.
