The Knowledge Base Foundation

The biggest challenge businesses face with AI automation is ensuring consistent, accurate responses that reflect their brand. Without proper training, AI agents can provide generic or incorrect information that damages credibility.

Tectonic AI solves this through its knowledge base system - where you feed the AI your website content, FAQs, documents, and brand guidelines. As shown at 1:15 in the video, you can create multiple knowledge bases for different products or services, ensuring each AI agent draws from the right information.

Key insight: Businesses using knowledge bases see 42% higher customer satisfaction scores from AI interactions compared to untrained chatbots. The system allows continuous refinement - if an answer isn't perfect, you can manually correct it and the AI learns for next time.
