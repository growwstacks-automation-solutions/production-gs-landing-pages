What is Tidio's Lyro AI Chatbot?

Customer support teams waste countless hours answering repetitive questions that are already answered on company websites. Tidio's Lyro AI solves this by automatically scanning your website content to build a knowledge base for instant customer responses.

Unlike rule-based chatbots that require manual programming, Lyro learns from your existing content. It analyzes product pages, FAQ sections, policy documents, and terms of service to answer questions accurately in your brand voice.

Key benefit: Lyro reduces customer support volume by 30-50% for most businesses by handling routine inquiries automatically, allowing your team to focus on complex issues.

Step-by-Step Setup Process

Setting up Tidio's chatbot takes just 15 minutes if you have an existing website. Here's the streamlined process:

Step 1: Account Creation

Visit Tidio's website and create an account using your business email. You'll receive a verification code to confirm your identity.

Step 2: Business Information

Provide basic details about your company, industry, and customer volume. This helps Tidio optimize the AI for your specific use case.

Step 3: Website Connection

Enter your website URL. Lyro will immediately begin scanning your content to build its knowledge base. The initial sync typically takes 2-5 minutes.

Pro tip: Focus Lyro's learning on your most important pages first - product details, refund policies, and shipping information. You can add more sources later.

How Knowledge Base Sync Works

Lyro's AI scans your website content every 24 hours to stay updated. It intelligently extracts:

  • Product specifications and details
  • Refund and return policies
  • Shipping timelines and costs
  • FAQ content and common questions

You can manually add specific answers to override the AI's interpretations. For example, if your policy states "refunds within 30 days," but you only honor them for undamaged items, you can add this qualification.

The dashboard provides suggestions to strengthen your knowledge base, like identifying policy loopholes or unclear terms that could confuse customers.

Shopify Product Integration

For eCommerce businesses, Tidio offers direct Shopify integration:

  1. Navigate to the Products section in Tidio
  2. Click "Connect Shopify Store"
  3. Install the Tidio app from Shopify's marketplace
  4. Authenticate and select which products to sync

Once connected, Lyro can answer detailed product questions by accessing your Shopify catalog. The integration automatically updates product information twice daily.

Efficiency boost: This integration eliminates the need to manually update product details in your chatbot whenever inventory changes.

Customizing Responses & Escalation Rules

Tidio provides several customization options to align the chatbot with your brand:

Communication Style

Choose between neutral, friendly, or professional tones. You can disable emojis or casual language if they don't fit your brand.

Escalation Rules

Configure when conversations should transfer to human agents:

  • When customers use specific frustrated language
  • After 3 unsuccessful answer attempts
  • For questions containing certain keywords

The system provides templates for common scenarios like returns, subscriptions, and age-restricted products.
