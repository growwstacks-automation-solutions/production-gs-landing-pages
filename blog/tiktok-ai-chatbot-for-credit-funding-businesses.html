Creating Lead Capture Functions

The real power comes from automated lead capture. At 3:15, the demo shows a "Get Discount" function that collects the user's name and phone number before providing a promo code.

This approach works because:

  • Users willingly provide contact details for value (discounts, quotes, etc.)
  • The system logs all conversations with timestamps in your unified inbox
  • Captured leads sync automatically to your CRM

Building Your First Function:

  1. Navigate to AI Functions in your dashboard
  2. Click "Create New Function"
  3. Define what triggers the function (e.g. "Can I get a discount?")
  4. Set which information to collect (name, phone, email, etc.)
  5. Create the response message with your offer

Testing and Activating Your Chatbot

Before going live, thoroughly test your chatbot from a personal TikTok account (shown at 5:40 in the video). Ask all the common questions your customers have about funding options, eligibility, and application processes.

Key activation steps:

  1. Set your AI agent as the default responder
  2. Enable "Auto Reply" in settings
  3. Verify model priority order (e.g. OpenAI first, Claude backup)
  4. Test discount/lead capture functions
  5. Monitor initial conversations for refinements

Conversion tip: Funding businesses that offer something of value (like a rate discount or free credit review) in exchange for contact details see 42% higher lead capture rates than generic "contact us" prompts.
