The Simple 3-Step Training Process

Tiny Talk revolutionizes chatbot training by eliminating technical complexity. Instead of writing rules or coding responses, you simply feed your chatbot knowledge sources that it processes automatically.

Step 1: Upload Your Knowledge Sources

Add PDFs, Word docs, text files, or website URLs containing information about your business. Tiny Talk can process up to 40 million characters across these sources.

Step 2: Configure Response Settings

Adjust parameters like temperature (creativity vs accuracy), match count (how many sources to reference), and system prompts to shape how your chatbot responds.

Step 3: Test in Playground

Immediately test your trained chatbot with sample questions to verify it's pulling correct information from your knowledge base.

Pro tip: For websites that block crawlers (like WPX-hosted sites), manually copy content into text files to ensure complete training coverage.

Customizing Your Chatbot's Appearance and Behavior

Tiny Talk provides extensive customization options to match your brand identity and user experience goals. From visual styling to conversation flow, you can tailor nearly every aspect of your chatbot's presentation.

The messenger settings allow you to upload custom avatars, change color schemes, and organize content into tabs. You can even create announcement banners and external links within the chat interface - perfect for promotions or important notices.

Automating Lead Capture and Qualification

One of Tiny Talk's most powerful business features is its built-in lead qualification system. After initial chatbot interactions, visitors can be prompted to provide contact details through customizable forms.

The platform captures essential information like names, emails, and phone numbers while allowing you to create custom attributes for specific qualification questions. All captured leads populate the contacts section for easy follow-up and integration with your CRM.

Powerful Integrations: WhatsApp and Beyond

Tiny Talk stands out with its WhatsApp integration, allowing customers to message your AI chatbot directly through the popular messaging app. This eliminates friction for users who prefer familiar communication channels.

The platform also supports Zapier connections and webhooks for pushing lead data to other systems. Coming soon is Slack integration, expanding options for team collaboration around chatbot interactions.

Implementation Options for Your Website

Tiny Talk offers three flexible implementation methods to suit different website setups:

  • Public URL: Simple link you can share anywhere
  • iFrame Embed: Full chatbot window embedded on a page
  • Widget Script: Floating chat button that appears sitewide

Each option maintains full functionality while providing different user experience approaches. The widget implementation (shown at 14:30 in the video) offers the most seamless integration for most business websites.
