The Hidden Costs of Traditional Voice Agent Setup

Building a compliant voice agent typically requires stitching together 5-7 different services: a telephony provider like Twilio, voice synthesis from ElevenLabs, conversation logic in n8n or Make.com, CRM integrations, and compliance frameworks. Each piece requires technical configuration, ongoing maintenance, and separate billing.

Trillet.AI consolidates this stack into one platform where all components are pre-integrated and compliant out of the box. As shown in the demo, what normally takes weeks of developer time can now be accomplished in minutes by non-technical users through guided templates.

Time to deployment: Traditional voice agent setups average 3-6 weeks for initial configuration and compliance verification. Trillet.AI demonstrated a working production agent in under 10 minutes during the trial.
