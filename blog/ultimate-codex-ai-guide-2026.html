Getting Started with Codex: Installation & Setup

The days of complex AI setup processes are over. Getting started with Codex takes just minutes:

  1. Visit chat.openai.com/codex and download the Mac OS application
  2. Drag the Codex icon into your Applications folder
  3. Complete the simple onboarding (select your work type - engineering, product, marketing, etc.)
  4. Connect your existing ChatGPT account to bring in projects and tools

During first-time setup, Codex will request access to documents and folders. These permissions allow it to work across your computer like a human assistant would. The onboarding typically takes less than 5 minutes before you're ready to start your first project.
