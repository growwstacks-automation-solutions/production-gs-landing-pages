How to Enable 11 Labs in Your HighLevel Account

Activating this game-changing feature takes just minutes. You'll need admin access to your HighLevel agency account to enable the integration.

Here's the step-by-step process (demonstrated at 2:15 in the video):

Step 1: Navigate to Agency Settings

From your HighLevel dashboard, click on your profile icon in the top right corner, then select "Agency Settings" from the dropdown menu.

Step 2: Find the Labs Section

Scroll down until you see the "Labs" section (it's towards the bottom of the settings page). This is where experimental features live.

Step 3: Enable 11 Labs Support

Toggle the switch labeled "11 Labs Support" to the on position. You can then select which sub-accounts should have access to this feature.

Pro tip: The integration may take up to 24 hours to fully activate across all selected accounts. If you don't see the voice options immediately, check back later.

Two Ways to Add Voices to Your Library

Once 11 Labs is enabled, you have two methods for adding voices to your HighLevel account:

Method 1: Voice ID Import

Navigate to your 11 Labs account library and copy the unique ID for any voice you want to use. Then in HighLevel's AI Assistants section, click "Custom Voice" and paste the ID.

Method 2: Built-in Selector

HighLevel now includes a voice browser that lets you preview and select voices directly within the platform. This is simpler for most users.

Important: You can add up to 10 voices per account. Once added, voices can't be deleted (though you can reassign them to different assistants). Choose carefully!

Assigning Voices to Specific AI Assistants

The real power comes when you match specific voices to specific purposes within your business. Here's how:

Step 1: Navigate to AI Assistants

From your HighLevel dashboard, go to "AI Assistants" in the left-hand menu.

Step 2: Select an Assistant

Choose which assistant you want to customize (e.g., sales calls, support, appointment reminders).

Step 3: Assign a Voice

Click on the voice settings for that assistant and select from your available voice library.

Best practice: Create distinct personas. Your sales assistant should sound different than your support assistant, creating a more natural experience for callers.

The Secret to Voice Cloning (Advanced Feature)

For the ultimate in brand consistency, you can clone your own voice using 11 Labs' advanced cloning feature. This requires:

  • 2 hours of high-quality voice recordings
  • Recording in small segments (15-20 minutes at a time)
  • Varied content to capture your natural vocal range

Once cloned, you can import your personal voice directly into HighLevel. Imagine customers calling your business and hearing "you" answer - even when you're not there!

Note: Cloned voices are private by default. You must change the privacy setting to "public" before importing into HighLevel.

Quick Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter issues with the 11 Labs integration:

  • Ensure you're on a HighLevel Pro plan or higher (required)
  • Double-check that 11 Labs is enabled in agency settings
  • Wait up to 24 hours for full activation across sub-accounts
  • Remember -assign voices without rebuilding assistants if needed

Most problems can be resolved by verifying these basic setup requirements.
