Zapier Integration Now in Beta

HR teams have long struggled with disconnected systems that require manual data entry between platforms. Vacation Tracker's new Zapier integration solves this pain point by enabling seamless connections with thousands of other business applications.

The open beta program allows users to create custom workflows that trigger automatically based on selected actions in Vacation Tracker. As shown in the video at 0:45, this could include automatically updating payroll systems when leave is approved or notifying team channels when someone schedules time off.

Beta testing opportunity: Vacation Tracker is welcoming all users to participate in the Zapier beta program. Interested teams can email hello@vatracker.io to get started with connecting their favorite apps.

App Experience Improvements

Beyond the headline Zapier integration, February's update includes several quality-of-life improvements that address common user frustrations. The reporting interface now automatically hides empty and non-relevant columns, reducing clutter and making key metrics easier to spot.

Dashboard usability has been enhanced with new tooltips in the team availability section that clarify the different leave types at a glance. For administrators, the audit logs now provide complete visibility by including records of deleted automations - a feature many larger organizations had requested.

VT Academy Tutorials

With each new feature release, Vacation Tracker recognizes that users need guidance to fully leverage the platform's capabilities. VT Academy addresses this need with practical, step-by-step video tutorials that are added weekly.

The academy content goes beyond basic how-tos, offering expert tips for optimizing leave management workflows. As mentioned at 1:30 in the video, these resources help users transform from basic Vacation Tracker users to leave management pros who can fine-tune their setup for maximum efficiency.

Environmental Impact

Vacation Tracker continues to innovate in corporate social responsibility by connecting leave management with environmental action. Through their partnership with One Tree Planted, user leave requests in February resulted in 1,096 new trees planted.

This initiative demonstrates how routine business processes can create meaningful environmental change. The program provides HR teams with a compelling story to share about their company's sustainability efforts while maintaining operational efficiency.

1,096 trees planted in February 2026 thanks to Vacation Tracker users taking time off. This environmental program adds purpose to the practical benefits of streamlined leave management.
