The 3-Layer Framework for Effective Voice Agents

Effective voice agents work across three coordinated layers:

1. Voice Identity

Choose from realistic AI voices that match your brand. The agent introduces itself naturally ("Thanks for calling City Plumbing, this is Sam") so callers feel they're talking to your team.

2. Knowledge Core

A simple system prompt teaches the agent about your business - services, hours, booking process. This takes 3-5 sentences, not complex programming.

3. Action Triggers

After the call, the agent can book appointments, send confirmations, or alert you via text for follow-up. Everything happens automatically.

Pro Tip: Start with just collecting caller names and phone numbers if you're unsure. Even this basic setup captures leads you would have missed.
