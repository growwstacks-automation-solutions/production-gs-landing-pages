The Compliance Gap in AI Voice

Most service providers exploring AI voice solutions face a rude awakening - the impressive demo capabilities come with hidden operational burdens. While AI agents can handle calls beautifully, they introduce complex compliance requirements that most platforms completely ignore.

Daniel Rosenrouch of Viirtue explains: "Groups like Synthflow skip the important parts we in the voice community have known are critical from day one - USF billing, compliance pieces, tax automation. Voice is a federally regulated industry, and that tooling is what we bring to the table right out of the box."

Key insight: The FCC has already ruled that AI voice calls require the same opt-in requirements as SMS, with violations carrying penalties of thousands of dollars per call. Most AI voice platforms don't help you comply with these regulations.

Automated Tax Compliance

Voice services come with a complex web of taxes beyond standard sales tax - USF contributions, state telecom taxes, and local fees that vary by jurisdiction. Most quoting tools completely miss these voice-specific taxes.

Viirtue's platform automatically calculates and applies all applicable taxes at the quoting stage, generates compliant invoices, and handles all tax filings. This eliminates the manual reconciliation work that plagues most service providers.

Critical feature: Even if you're just starting with 3-4 customers, Viirtue automatically generates required 477 reports. "You don't have to sit and figure that out," notes Rosenrouch. "Even if you're diminimis, you're compliant."
