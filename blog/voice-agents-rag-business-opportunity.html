How RAG Makes Voice AI Smarter

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) solves the #1 problem with voice AI: hallucinations and generic responses. By connecting your agent directly to business data - CRM records, knowledge bases, product catalogs - RAG ensures every answer is grounded in your specific information.

Here's how it works in practice: When a customer asks "What's the status of my order?", the RAG system 1) Pulls their record from your database 2) Retrieves the latest shipping info 3) Generates a natural-sounding update. No more "I don't have access to that information" - just accurate, personalized service.
