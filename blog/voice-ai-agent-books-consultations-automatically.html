How the Voice AI Agent Works

The system combines ElevenLabs' conversational AI with n8n's automation capabilities. When a client calls (or uses the web widget), the AI:

  1. Greets them naturally and asks about their needs
  2. Checks your real-time calendar availability through n8n
  3. Suggests available time slots
  4. Collects name, email, and phone number
  5. Books the appointment directly in your calendar
  6. Sends confirmation details to both you and the client
  7. Answers common questions (pricing, cancellation policy, etc.)
