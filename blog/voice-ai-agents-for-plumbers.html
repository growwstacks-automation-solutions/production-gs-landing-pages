How to Implement Voice AI

Getting started requires just three steps:

Step 1: Call Flow Design

Map out your ideal customer call experience - what questions should the AI ask? How should it handle emergencies? What information does it need to collect?

Step 2: System Integration

Connect the voice AI to your scheduling software, CRM, and phone system. Most modern solutions integrate directly with tools like HouseCall Pro, Jobber, and ServiceTitan.

Step 3: Voice Training

Provide sample recordings of how you want the AI to sound - professional yet approachable for plumbing customers. The system learns your preferred phrases and tone.

Implementation timeline: Most plumbing businesses go live with basic call answering in 7-10 days, with full implementation (reminders + follow-ups) completed within 3 weeks.
