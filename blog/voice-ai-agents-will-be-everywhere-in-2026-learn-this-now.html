Getting Started with Retail AI

Retail AI stands out as one of the most affordable and realistic Voice AI platforms available today. Their interface makes it simple to create your first agent without any coding knowledge. Just sign up for a free account at retailai.com (no credit card required).

Once logged in, you'll see a clean dashboard where you can manage all your voice agents. The "Create Agent" button is prominently displayed - click it and select "Voice Agent" to begin. You'll be presented with four agent types, but for your first agent, choose "Simple Prompt Agent" and start from blank.

Creating Your First Voice Agent

The agent creation screen has three main areas: the prompt editor (left), settings (middle), and testing panel (right). Your prompt tells the agent how to behave - what to say, how to respond, and what information to provide. Rather than writing this from scratch, use ChatGPT to generate a professional-quality prompt.

For example, asking ChatGPT to "write a prompt for a voice agent representing Apple.com" produces comprehensive instructions covering product information, troubleshooting, and sales guidance. Paste this into Retail AI's prompt editor, review it for accuracy, and save. You can now test your agent in the right panel - it will respond exactly as instructed.

Adding a Knowledge Base

While your agent can answer general questions, it needs access to specific product information to be truly useful. Retail AI's knowledge base feature solves this by connecting your agent to websites or documents containing the latest details.

Instead of adding an entire website (which could include thousands of irrelevant pages), target specific sections. For our Apple example, we connected just the iPhone product page (apple.com/iphone). This ensures the agent has current specifications and pricing without unnecessary data that could slow it down.

Advanced Customization Options

Retail AI offers extensive customization to make your agent sound exactly how you want. You can choose from multiple voices (male, female, various accents), adjust speech patterns (speed, pauses), and even simulate background noise for authenticity.

The most powerful feature is post-call data extraction. This lets you automatically capture key information from calls (customer needs, product interests) and feed it into your CRM or follow-up systems. Combined with Retail AI's phone number integration, you can deploy a complete call handling solution without any coding.
