Step 3: Connecting to Zoho Desk

The magic happens when your AI agent seamlessly integrates with Zoho Desk. At 4:20 in the tutorial, we walk through the exact API connection process.

Integration Requirements

Before starting, ensure you have:

  • Zoho Desk administrator access
  • API credentials with ticket creation permissions
  • Your knowledge base organized in Zoho Desk

Configuration Steps

  1. Navigate to Integrations → Managed Integrations
  2. Select Zoho CRM from the marketplace
  3. Authenticate with your Zoho account
  4. Enable these critical permissions:
    • Create tickets
    • Read knowledge base
    • Access customer profiles
  5. Map your ticket fields (priority, category, etc.)

Test thoroughly: Use the built-in testing tool to verify tickets create correctly with all required fields before going live.

Step 4: Setting Up Human Call Transfers

Even the best AI can't solve every issue. At 5:40 in the video, we configure the crucial call transfer functionality that makes the system truly seamless.

Transfer Requirements

You'll need:

  • A purchased phone number assigned to your voice AI port
  • Your support team's direct numbers or queue
  • Business hours configuration (for after-hours handling)

Configuration Steps

  1. Click the "Transfer to Agent" node in your workflow
  2. Select your transfer number (support queue or individual agent)
  3. Configure transfer messages:
    • What the AI tells the customer before transferring
    • What context the human agent receives
  4. Set after-hours rules (voicemail or emergency support)

Pro Tip: Enable "whisper mode" so the human agent hears a quick summary of the AI's interaction before taking over the call.

Pro Tip: Training Your AI Knowledge Base

The AI's ability to resolve issues without human help depends entirely on your knowledge base quality. At 6:30 in the video, we show how to optimize yours.

Knowledge Base Best Practices

  • Structure for voice: Rewrite articles to sound natural when spoken aloud
  • Anticipate follow-ups: Link related articles the AI can suggest
  • Add synonyms: Help the AI recognize different ways customers phrase the same issue
  • Prioritize solutions: Place the most common resolutions at the top of each article

Continuous improvement: Review the "unresolved issues" report weekly to identify knowledge gaps. Each new article you add increases your auto-resolution rate.
