Voice AI links solve this by transforming any QR code into a personal product expert. How Voice AI Links Actually Work

Behind every Voice AI link is a sophisticated but easy-to-configure system. The process begins when a customer scans your QR code with their smartphone camera (no special app required). This launches a web-based voice interface that connects to your AI agent.

The agent first greets the user with a branded welcome message, then listens for questions. Using natural language processing, it understands the intent behind queries like "How do I set this up?" or "What's the warranty on this part?" The system then retrieves the most accurate response from your knowledge base and speaks it back in a natural, conversational tone.

The foundation of any effective Voice AI solution is a well-structured knowledge base. This isn't just a document dump - it's the curated information your AI will use to answer customer questions accurately.

In Bolka Voice AI, you can create multiple knowledge bases for different products or use cases. The platform accepts website URLs (which it will crawl for relevant content) or direct PDF uploads. For best results, focus on uploading product manuals, FAQs, troubleshooting guides, and specifications - the documents your support team currently uses to answer common questions.

Configuring Your Voice Agent

With your knowledge base ready, the next step is configuring your AI agent's personality and capabilities. This is where you define how the voice sounds, what language it uses, and how it handles ambiguous questions.

The agent settings include:

  1. Prompt engineering: Define the agent's role ("You are a helpful product expert for iPhone 17") and tone (professional, friendly, technical, etc.)
  2. Fallback responses: What the AI says when it doesn't understand a question
  3. Audio preferences: Voice gender, accent, and speaking pace
  4. Conversation flow: Whether the agent should ask follow-up questions or end after each answer

Generating and Deploying Your Links

The magic happens when you generate your Voice AI links. These are special QR codes that, when scanned, immediately launch your configured voice agent. One link can serve thousands of users simultaneously - no per-user licensing or complex provisioning required.

When generating links in Bolka Voice AI, you can:

Deployment is simple - place the QR codes on product packaging, instruction manuals, retail displays, or even embed them in digital ads. There's no app to download; the experience works instantly through the smartphone's native camera and browser.
