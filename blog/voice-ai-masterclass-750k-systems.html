$754k Recruitment Reactivation Case Study

Recruitment agencies sit on goldmines of untapped potential - thousands of qualified candidates who applied but weren't hired. Traditional email reactivation campaigns achieve dismal 1-3% response rates. Our voice AI solution delivered 32%.

The system works by analyzing Airtable records of past applicants, filtering out those contacted within the last 7 days, then initiating personalized Vapi calls. Each call greets the candidate by name, references their original application date, and presents new opportunities matching their profile.

32% reactivation rate at $20k implementation cost: This solution generated $754,000 in recovered placement fees by reactivating 58 previously "dead" candidates who were hired at an average $13,000 placement fee. The entire system runs on no-code tools accessible to beginners.

Key System Components:

  • Smart call scheduling: Limits calls to 9am-7pm local time with concurrency optimized to 100/day on peak days
  • Dynamic variables: Personalizes each call with applicant name, original application date, and relevant job openings
  • Fallback routing: Automatically switches to SMS after missed calls with 4% higher completion rate
  • Real-time dashboards: Shows clients exactly which candidates are progressing through the pipeline

The Voice AI Tech Stack That Scales

Building reliable voice AI solutions requires more than just connecting an API to Twilio. Our tech stack combines the best of no-code automation with enterprise-grade reliability.

At the core is Vapi for voice calls, Make.com for workflow orchestration, and OpenAI's LLMs for natural language processing. But the real magic happens in the integration layer:

4% higher completion rates: Our system achieves this by dynamically adjusting call timing based on recipient behavior patterns, falling back to SMS when appropriate, and personalizing each interaction with CRM data.

Core Components:

  • Call controller: Manages concurrency (100 calls/day peak), time zones, and do-not-call lists
  • Dynamic variables: Pulls applicant names, dates, and job details from Airtable in real-time
  • Transcript analysis: LLMs process call transcripts to update CRM records and trigger follow-ups
  • Fallback routing: Automatically switches channels if calls go unanswered

For enterprise clients needing HIPAA compliance, we deploy on-premise LLMs via AWS at an additional $2,500-$10,000 cost depending on call volume.

Voice AI Compliance Considerations

Nothing kills a voice AI project faster than compliance risks. We build legal safeguards into every implementation from day one.

All solutions include mandatory call recording disclosures, do-not-call list filtering, and automatic transcript redaction for sensitive data. For healthcare clients, we deploy on-premise LLMs with AWS HIPAA buckets, though this adds $2,500-$10,000 to project costs.

Essential compliance layers: 1) TCPA-compliant call recording notices, 2) Automatic DNC list scrubbing, 3) State-by-state calling time restrictions, 4) Data encryption in transit and at rest, 5) Right-to-be-forgotten workflows.

Industry-Specific Requirements:

  • Healthcare: HIPAA-compliant storage, BAA agreements with LLM providers
  • Financial: FINRA call logging, voiceprint authentication
  • E-commerce: PCI-compliant payment handling, order verification

We document all compliance measures in plain English for clients and provide audit-ready reports - turning a potential objection into a selling point.
