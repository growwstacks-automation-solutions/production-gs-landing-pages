The Phone Call Problem Every Groomer Faces

Pet grooming businesses lose countless hours to repetitive phone calls. A typical day involves answering the same questions dozens of times: "Do you have availability today?" "How much for a small dog haircut?" "Can I bring my dog without an appointment?"

This call volume creates three major problems: staff frustration from answering the same questions repeatedly, missed opportunities when calls go unanswered during busy grooming times, and inconsistent information when different team members quote different prices or policies.

68% of grooming businesses report that phone management is their #1 operational headache, according to a Pet Industry Survey. The average groomer spends 2-3 hours daily just handling basic inquiries that could be automated.
