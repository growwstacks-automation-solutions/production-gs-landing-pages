Workflow Automation Costs

Most businesses assume connecting AI agents to their CRM, calendar, and email systems requires expensive enterprise software. The reality? Self-hosted n8n workflow automation costs less than £10 per month (about $13).

Unlike cloud solutions that charge per workflow run or user seat, self-hosted n8n gives you unlimited workflows at a fixed, predictable cost. Whether you're connecting one agent or a hundred, the monthly expense remains the same.

Key insight: Self-hosting isn't just cheaper - it's more secure. Your business data stays within your infrastructure rather than in someone else's cloud. At 1:20 in the video, we show exactly how this setup works.

Voice Agent Pricing

Voice agents built with Vapi cost approximately 8p per minute of conversation time. When you add a Twilio number for calling capabilities, the total comes to about 1-2p per minute for mobile calls (half that for landlines).

These aren't theoretical numbers. One client made 14,000 calls with a 48% connection rate for just £220 total. That's less than 2p per attempted call - cheaper than any human employee could possibly manage.

Volume discount: At higher volumes, the per-minute cost decreases further. Another client handling 383,000 calls spent only $6,000 total - about 2 cents per call.

Text Agent Economics

For email, WhatsApp, SMS, or live chat agents, the primary cost is the GPT API. Each full text response costs between a tenth of a cent to one cent. Even high-volume text automation rarely exceeds a few dollars per month.

The database component (Supabase) is completely free, allowing you to build custom dashboards and track interactions without additional expense. This combination creates an automation stack where the marginal cost of each additional customer interaction approaches zero.

How Costs Scale

Unlike human labor costs that increase linearly with volume, AI agent costs scale sub-linearly. The n8n workflow platform maintains its sub-£10 monthly cost regardless of usage. Voice and text costs decrease per unit as volume increases.

This creates a powerful economic advantage: your cost per customer interaction decreases as your business grows, rather than increasing as it would with traditional staffing models.
