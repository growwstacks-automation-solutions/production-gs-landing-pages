Twilio WhatsApp Setup in 3 Steps

Connecting your AI agent to WhatsApp takes just minutes using Twilio's sandbox environment:

Step 1: Create Free Twilio Account

Twilio offers a free tier that's perfect for testing. Sign up at twilio.com and verify your email and phone number.

Step 2: Enable WhatsApp Sandbox

Navigate to Messaging > Try it Out > Send a WhatsApp Message. Follow the instructions to link your personal WhatsApp number by sending the provided code.

Step 3: Connect Lovable Webhook

Once your Lovable agent is running (usually within 2-3 minutes), copy the edge function URL from the Cloud section and paste it into Twilio's sandbox settings under "When a message comes in."

Pro tip: The sandbox number can only interact with numbers you've explicitly approved. For production use, you'll need to apply for a dedicated WhatsApp Business number through Twilio.
