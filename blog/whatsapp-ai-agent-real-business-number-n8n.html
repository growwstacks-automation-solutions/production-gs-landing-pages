Architecture Overview: How the AI Agent Works

When a message arrives on your WhatsApp Business number, n8n captures it instantly through the WhatsApp Business API. The text gets sent to an AI model (GPT-4 in this case) which generates a human-like response based on conversation history and your business knowledge base.

The entire exchange - including timestamps, customer message, and AI response - gets recorded in a Google Sheet. This creates a transparent audit trail while keeping all conversations in one searchable location.
