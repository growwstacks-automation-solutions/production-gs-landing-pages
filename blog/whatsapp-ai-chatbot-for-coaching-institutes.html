Building Your Coaching Institute's Knowledge Base

The chatbot's intelligence comes from a comprehensive knowledge base document you create. This should include:

  • Detailed descriptions of all courses offered
  • Class schedules and timings
  • Admission process steps
  • Fee structures and payment options
  • Study material provided
  • Testing methodology
  • Faculty qualifications

Pro tip: Include sample study plans for different timeframes (1 month, 3 months, 6 months) to give the AI concrete examples to work from. The more specific your knowledge base, the more accurate the chatbot's responses will be.

Step-by-Step Setup Process

Step 1: Create Your PABLY Chatflow Account

Visit pably.com/chatflow and sign up for their free tier (100 monthly messages). This lets you test the system before committing.

Step 2: Connect Your WhatsApp Number

Follow the verification process to link your institute's official WhatsApp number to the platform.

Step 3: Build Your Knowledge Base

Upload a comprehensive document (PDF or text) containing all your course information, policies, and sample responses.

Step 4: Configure the AI Assistant

Set response tone (more formal vs friendly), enable/disable features, and establish fallback protocols for unanswered questions.

Step 5: Test Thoroughly

Have staff members pose as students and send various query types to refine responses before going live.

Implementation timeline: Most institutes can go from zero to fully operational chatbot in 3-5 business days. The longest step is compiling the knowledge base - budget 2 days for this critical foundation.
