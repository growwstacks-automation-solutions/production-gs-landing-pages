The WhatsApp Lead Problem

WhatsApp has become the default communication channel for customers worldwide, with over 2 billion monthly active users. Yet most businesses handle it poorly - either ignoring messages entirely or requiring staff to manually respond around the clock.

The result? Missed opportunities when you're offline, inconsistent responses, and valuable lead information slipping through the cracks. At 0:45 in the video, you'll see how this AI chatbot solves all three problems simultaneously.

How the Chatbot Works

The chatbot follows a structured yet intelligent flow to capture and qualify leads:

  1. Instant greeting: Sends a welcome message with your logo when a customer messages
  2. Option selection: Presents buttons for different products/services
  3. AI qualification: Asks intelligent follow-up questions based on the user's selection
  4. Lead capture: Collects contact details and stores them automatically
  5. Knowledge access: Answers additional questions by scanning your website content

Building the Chatbot Flow

The entire chatbot is built visually using Chatling.ai's drag-and-drop interface. No coding is required - you simply connect pre-built nodes that handle different parts of the conversation.

Key components you'll assemble:

  • Start node: Where conversations begin
  • Send message: For text, images, or buttons
  • Capture response: Gathers user input
  • AI response: Handles intelligent replies
  • Google Sheets: Stores captured leads

At 4:10 in the tutorial, you can see how these nodes connect to form the complete workflow.

AI Response Configuration

The magic happens in the AI response node. Here you:

  1. Set instructions for how the AI should behave ("You are a lead generation agent...")
  2. Connect it to variables from previous user inputs
  3. Choose the AI model (GPT-4-0 Mini works well)
  4. Define how responses should be stored

Pro tip: The AI generates different follow-up questions based on which option the user selects, making each conversation feel personalized.

At 6:30 in the video, you'll see exactly how to configure these AI settings.

WhatsApp Connection

Connecting to WhatsApp requires:

  • A Meta Business Account
  • A WhatsApp Business Account
  • A dedicated phone number (not on personal WhatsApp)

The connection process takes 5-10 minutes:

  1. Log in with your Meta account
  2. Select your business
  3. Verify your phone number
  4. Set up two-factor authentication

At 9:40 in the video, you'll see the complete WhatsApp setup walkthrough.

Testing the Chatbot

Before going live, use the built-in testing tool:

  1. Enter a test phone number
  2. Send sample messages
  3. Verify the conversation flow
  4. Check that leads appear in your Google Sheet

The testing interface (shown at 10:50 in the video) lets you simulate real conversations and identify any issues before customers use it.

Important: Test during off-hours when real customers are less likely to message, as the bot will respond to all incoming messages once activated.
