Automatic Lead Qualification & Scoring

The AI doesn't just collect leads - it qualifies them intelligently. Based on conversation patterns, response timing, and question types, each lead receives a temperature score:

  • Hot: Asking about pricing, availability, or next steps (70% conversion likelihood)
  • Warm: Gathering information but not ready to commit (30-50% conversion)
  • Cold: General inquiries with no immediate intent (10-20% conversion)

This scoring happens in real-time, allowing your team to prioritize follow-ups strategically. The system even suggests optimal contact times based on when the lead was most engaged.
