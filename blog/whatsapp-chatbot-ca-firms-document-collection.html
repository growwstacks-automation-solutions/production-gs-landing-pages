The Document Collection Challenge for CA Firms

Every CA knows the frustration - you're ready to file a client's ITR or GST return, but missing documents delay the process for days or weeks. Our research shows CA firms spend 15-20 hours per week just following up with clients for basic documents like PAN cards and Aadhaar cards.

The traditional document collection process creates multiple pain points:

  • Clients forget which documents they've already submitted
  • Emails get lost in crowded inboxes
  • Physical documents require scanning and organization
  • Team members waste time on repetitive follow-ups

Key insight: 78% of CA firms report document collection as their #1 operational bottleneck during tax season. The average ITR filing gets delayed by 9 days waiting for client documents.
