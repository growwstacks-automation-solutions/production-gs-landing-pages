Connecting n8n to WhatsApp via Webhook

Here's where the magic begins (timestamp 3:10). In n8n, we create an HTTP webhook that listens for incoming WhatsApp messages. Every "Hello" or product inquiry triggers our workflow instantly.

The connection uses a simple POST method - no complex coding. You'll copy the webhook URL from n8n and paste it into Wasender's dashboard. Test immediately by sending a WhatsApp message to your number - you should see it appear in n8n's execution log within seconds.

Real-Time Google Sheets Updates

No more manual data entry errors (timestamp 10:05). Each order auto-populates in your Google Sheets: confirmed orders in one tab, pending payments in another. The system even calculates totals and formats addresses correctly.

We use n8n's Google Sheets node with simple "append row" operations. The columns map directly to the information collected from customers - product, quantity, customer name, and delivery details. Your team wakes up to perfectly organized orders ready for processing.
