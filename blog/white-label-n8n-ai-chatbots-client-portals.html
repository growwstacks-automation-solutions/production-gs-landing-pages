While the backend workflow might be perfectly functional, the frontend experience frequently feels technical and unbranded. This creates a disconnect between the agency's premium positioning and the raw automation tools they're deploying.

White labeling transforms this dynamic by wrapping your n8n chatbots in complete brand alignment. Step 1: Customizing the Chatbot Widget

The foundation of white-labeling begins with the embeddable chat widget. This is the client-facing interface that visitors interact with on websites. Rather than presenting a generic chat bubble, you can customize:

  • Primary and accent colors matching brand palettes
  • Custom greeting messages in the client's brand voice
  • Button styles and placement options
  • Font selections and text sizing
  • Avatar images or company logos

As shown at 0:45 in the video, the preview feature lets you test different configurations in real-time. This ensures the final widget aligns perfectly with the client's digital presence before deployment.

Step 2: Connecting to Your n8n Workflow

With the widget design finalized, the technical integration with n8n takes less than 2 minutes. At 1:30 in the tutorial, you'll see the simple process of:

  1. Copying your n8n webhook URL from the production workflow
  2. Pasting it into the widget configuration panel
  3. Testing the connection to verify message flow

This maintains all your existing n8n logic while adding the branded frontend layer. The connection remains fully bi-directional — client messages flow to n8n for processing, and responses return through the white-labeled interface.

Pro Tip: Use n8n's webhook response testing feature to ensure your branded widget receives properly formatted replies from your automation workflows.

Step 3: Setting Up Client Portals

The true value of white-labeling shines when you provide clients their own analytics dashboard. At 2:50 in the video, we demonstrate:

  • Creating individual client accounts with unique logins
  • Assigning specific chatbots to each client profile
  • Generating secure portal access links
  • Customizing the portal with client branding elements

Each portal becomes a branded command center where clients can monitor chatbot performance, review conversation transcripts, and access support resources — all without seeing your agency's branding or technical infrastructure.
