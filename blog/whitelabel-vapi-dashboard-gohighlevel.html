The 5 Problems Every VAPI Agency Faces

When selling voice AI solutions to businesses, presentation matters just as much as technology. Clients aren't just buying an AI agent - they're investing in a professional experience with measurable outcomes. Yet most agencies rely on VAPI's default interface, creating five critical roadblocks:

1. No Whitelabeling: Your clients see VAPI branding instead of your agency's identity, positioning you as a reseller rather than a premium solution provider.

2. Developer-Focused Interface: While VAPI's dashboard works well for technical users, it overwhelms non-technical clients with unnecessary complexity and jargon.

3. Manual Billing Headaches: Agencies waste countless hours tracking usage, calculating invoices, and chasing payments each month instead of focusing on growth.

4. Limited Analytics: The native dashboard lacks custom KPIs that prove your value - like booked appointments, conversion rates, or industry-specific metrics.

5. Mobile Unfriendly: Clients trying to check performance on their phones face broken experiences, forcing agencies to create separate mobile reports.

Step-by-Step Dashboard Setup

Creating your whitelabel dashboard takes less than 30 minutes with no coding required. Here's the exact process:

Step 1: Account Creation

Visit ChatDash.com and create your trial account. The onboarding wizard will guide you through each step:

  • Select your agency name and primary color scheme
  • Upload your logo (or use a placeholder temporarily)
  • Set your custom subdomain (e.g., dashboard.youragency.com)

Step 2: Connect Your VAPI Agent

Navigate to the API keys section in your VAPI dashboard to retrieve:

  • Public API key
  • Private API key

Paste these into ChatDash to authenticate your workspace. The system will automatically detect all your existing agents.

Step 3: Add Your First Client

Create a client organization (e.g., "ABC Company") and add team members who need access. Each client gets:

  • Custom login credentials
  • Role-based permissions
  • Branded email templates

GoHighLevel Integration

Embedding your dashboard directly into GoHighLevel creates a seamless experience where clients never leave your ecosystem. Here's how:

Step 1: Generate Embed URL

In ChatDash, navigate to Client Details → Embed URL and register your GoHighLevel domain (without http/https). This creates a secure iframe link.

Step 2: Add Custom Menu in GHL

In your GoHighLevel agency view:

  1. Go to Settings → Custom Menu Links
  2. Create new link with title "AI Agent Dashboard"
  3. Paste your embed URL
  4. Set to open within current window
  5. Assign to specific sub-accounts
