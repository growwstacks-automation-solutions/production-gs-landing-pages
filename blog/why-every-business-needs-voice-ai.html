Implementation Strategies That Work

Successful voice AI adoption follows three key principles:

  1. Start with high-volume, repetitive tasks: Prescription refills, appointment scheduling, and FAQ handling deliver fastest ROI
  2. Phase implementation: Begin with inbound calls before expanding to outbound
  3. Measure what matters: Track call resolution rates, not just cost savings

Pro tip: Record 20-30 sample calls before implementation. Use these to train your AI agent on real-world scenarios, not hypothetical ones.
