The Disconnected Tools Problem

Modern businesses run on an average of 137 different software applications according to recent studies. Sales teams juggle CRMs and email platforms. Marketing manages social media and analytics tools. Finance teams toggle between accounting software and spreadsheets. The result? Critical business data trapped in disconnected silos.

Employees waste hours each week manually transferring information between systems - copying customer details from support tickets into CRMs, exporting reports to spreadsheets for analysis, or sending status updates across multiple platforms. This manual work isn't just inefficient - it introduces errors, creates version control nightmares, and pulls your best people away from strategic work.

83% of employees report spending at least 1-3 hours daily on repetitive manual tasks that could be automated according to a McKinsey survey.

How Make.com Solves This

Make.com (formerly Integromat) is a visual automation platform that acts as the connective tissue between your business applications. Instead of writing code or relying on IT teams, business users can design workflows that move data seamlessly between systems based on triggers and conditions.

The platform supports over 1,000 pre-built integrations with popular business tools like Salesforce, HubSpot, QuickBooks, Shopify, and Slack. Even when a direct integration doesn't exist, Make's API capabilities allow connecting to virtually any web service. This means you can finally make your software stack work together the way your business actually operates.

The Visual Workflow Advantage

What sets Make.com apart is its intuitive visual interface. Workflows are built by dragging and dropping modules onto a canvas and connecting them logically. You can see exactly how information flows from one system to another, what transformations happen along the way, and how errors are handled.

This visual approach makes automation accessible to non-technical team members while still providing the power technical users need. Business analysts can design processes without writing code, while developers can dive deep into API configurations when needed. The transparency also makes troubleshooting and optimization far easier than with traditional automation tools.

Beyond Simple Automation

Make.com goes far beyond simple "if this then that" automations. You're designing complete business processes with branching logic, parallel paths, error handling, and data transformation. A single workflow can:

  • Pull new leads from a web form into your CRM
  • Trigger personalized email sequences based on lead behavior
  • Update accounting systems when deals close
  • Notify relevant team members via Slack
  • Generate reports in Google Sheets

All while maintaining data consistency across every system and providing full visibility into the process. This is operational excellence made tangible.

Scaling Operations Without Headcount

In today's economic climate, businesses are being asked to do more with less. Make.com enables teams to scale operations without proportionally increasing staff by automating the repetitive work that currently consumes valuable time.

One client automated their customer onboarding process, reducing the time spent per new customer from 45 minutes to under 5 minutes. Another eliminated 20 hours per week of manual data entry between their eCommerce platform and accounting system. These efficiencies compound across departments, creating organizational capacity without adding headcount.

The result: Businesses using Make.com report an average 27% improvement in operational efficiency within the first 90 days of implementation.
