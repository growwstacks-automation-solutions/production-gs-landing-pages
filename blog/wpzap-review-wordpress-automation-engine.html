The $100/Month WordPress Automation Problem

Every WordPress site owner faces the same frustrating reality: essential business workflows require paying monthly fees to third-party automation tools. Form submissions, order processing, user onboarding - these basic functions shouldn't cost hundreds annually, yet they do.

The numbers reveal the absurdity: 470 million WordPress sites paying $30-$100/month just to connect plugins that already live on the same server. That's $14 billion annually flowing to middleware platforms when the data never needed to leave WordPress in the first place.

43% of all websites run WordPress, yet nearly all rely on external automation tools. This creates unnecessary costs, slower performance due to API calls, and data privacy concerns as sensitive information travels outside your server.

How WPZap Solves This Differently

WPZap rethinks WordPress automation from the ground up by operating natively within your WordPress dashboard. Instead of routing data through external servers like Zapier does, WPZap creates direct connections between your plugins.

But the business benefits matter more - one-time pricing replaces monthly fees, and you regain control over your automation logic.
