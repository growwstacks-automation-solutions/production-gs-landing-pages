Timed Bonus Delivery That Increases Conversions 27%

The most powerful automation shown is the timed bonus delivery system:

  1. Zapier monitors WebinarJam for when the live broadcast ends
  2. 20 minutes later, it checks who watched through the pitch
  3. Attendees receive bonuses immediately via email/SMS
  4. No-shows get replay reminders with bonus teasers

This "reward completion" mechanic increases replay viewing by 43% compared to sending bonuses immediately to everyone.
