How the Content Publishing Pipeline Works

  1. Topic Collection (Google Sheets): Content topics are stored in a designated column. Make.com monitors the sheet, retrieving new topics and triggering the comprehensive research and publishing workflow.
  2. AI-Powered Research (Perplexity AI): Perplexity AI conducts web searches for each topic, gathering the latest news, trending information, insights, and current data ‚Äî providing an accurate foundation for content creation.
  3. Multi-Platform Routing (Make.com Router): The router module splits the workflow into three parallel branches, enabling simultaneous processing for Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn without sequential delays.
  4. Facebook Pipeline: ChatGPT generates a Facebook-optimized post based on research findings. DALL-E creates a relevant image matching the content theme. The post publishes automatically with text and image.
  5. Instagram Pipeline: ChatGPT creates Instagram-optimized content with visual-first approach and hashtag strategy. DALL-E generates a platform-appropriate image. The post publishes maintaining feed aesthetic and engagement optimization.
  6. LinkedIn Pipeline: ChatGPT generates a professional LinkedIn post from research with business-appropriate tone. This branch skips image generation, maintaining a text-focused professional approach. The post publishes automatically.
ChatGPT generates platform-specific content while DALL-E creates matching visuals for Facebook and Instagram

The three-branch router architecture was the key engineering decision. By processing Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn in parallel rather than sequentially, the entire multi-platform publishing cycle completes in minutes ‚Äî with the effort previously required for a single manual platform post.

Inside the Technical Architecture

The Make.com scenario monitors Google Sheets for new topic entries, triggers Perplexity AI to conduct comprehensive research, then routes the research data through a three-branch router. Each branch has its own ChatGPT prompt engineered specifically for its target platform ‚Äî Facebook's engagement-driven format, Instagram's visual-first approach with hashtag optimization, and LinkedIn's professional business tone.

Perplexity AI conducting web research to gather current trends, news, and insights as the content foundation

The Facebook and Instagram branches include DALL-E image generation modules with prompts crafted to produce visuals that match the content theme and meet each platform's image dimension requirements. LinkedIn skips image generation entirely, maintaining its text-focused professional approach. Error handling is built into each branch so that a failure on one platform doesn't block publishing on the others.

All content starts from the same Perplexity AI research foundation, ensuring consistent messaging across platforms while the formatting and tone adapt to each audience's expectations and platform best practices.
