How It Works — Intelligent Email Automation

This system intelligently automates email responses, saving time and improving efficiency.

  1. New Email Trigger: The workflow starts when a new email arrives in your Gmail inbox.
  2. Fetch Email Content: n8n retrieves the content of the new email, including the sender, subject, and body.
  3. AI Analysis: The email content is sent to OpenAI for analysis to determine if a reply is needed.
  4. Decision Logic: Based on OpenAI's analysis, the workflow determines whether to proceed with drafting a reply.
  5. Draft Reply: If a reply is needed, OpenAI generates a draft reply based on the email content.
  6. Format Reply: n8n formats the draft reply into a professional and coherent message.
  7. Send Reply (Optional): The workflow can automatically send the reply or save it as a draft for review.
  8. Log Activity: The entire process is logged for tracking and analysis.

💡 AI-Powered Efficiency: By leveraging OpenAI, this system can quickly and accurately determine which emails require a response and generate relevant draft replies, significantly reducing manual effort.
