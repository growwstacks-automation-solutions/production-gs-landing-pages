The Problem

Content teams producing monthly thought leadership essays face a significant challenge: consistent generation of high-quality, engaging topics. Brainstorming sessions can be time-consuming and often yield repetitive or uninspired ideas. This leads to delays in the content calendar and missed opportunities to establish thought leadership.

The manual process of researching trends, identifying relevant themes, and crafting compelling topics is resource-intensive and prone to bottlenecks. Furthermore, ensuring that each topic aligns with the overall content strategy and resonates with the target audience requires extensive manual filtering and scoring. This creates a need for a more efficient and scalable solution.

The Solution

We developed an automated workflow that leverages Claude AI to generate essay topic candidates, triggered by a scheduled job. The system filters and scores these topics based on predefined criteria, then stores the high-quality candidates in Airtable for editorial review. This ensures a consistent stream of fresh, relevant topic ideas.

This solution was built using n8n, a powerful workflow automation platform, due to its flexibility and ability to seamlessly integrate with both Claude AI and Airtable. n8n allows for complex logic and data manipulation, making it ideal for this multi-step process. The use of Airtable provides a structured and collaborative environment for managing and reviewing the generated topics.

How It Works — Streamlining Content Ideation

The automated workflow operates through a series of interconnected steps, ensuring a seamless and efficient topic generation process.

  1. Scheduled Trigger: The workflow is initiated by a scheduled trigger within n8n, ensuring consistent topic generation at predefined intervals (e.g., weekly or monthly).
  2. Claude AI Integration: The workflow sends a prompt to Claude AI, requesting the generation of 6-8 essay topic candidates based on specific keywords and themes.
  3. Topic Filtering: The generated topics are then filtered based on predefined criteria, such as relevance to the target audience and alignment with the overall content strategy.
  4. Quality Scoring: Each topic is assigned a score based on factors like originality, potential for engagement, and feasibility of execution.
  5. Airtable Integration: High-scoring topics are automatically added to an Airtable base, creating a structured and collaborative environment for editorial review.
  6. Editorial Review: The content team reviews the generated topics in Airtable, providing feedback and selecting the most promising candidates for development.
  7. Content Calendar Integration: Approved topics are seamlessly integrated into the content calendar, ensuring a smooth and efficient content planning process.
  8. Performance Tracking: The workflow tracks the performance of each essay, providing valuable insights for future topic generation and content optimization.

💡 AI-Powered Creativity: By leveraging Claude AI, the workflow introduces a new level of creativity and innovation to the topic generation process, helping content teams break free from traditional brainstorming limitations.
