The Problem

Home service businesses, especially those dealing with boiler installations, face significant challenges in managing jobs and maintaining customer relationships. Manual job logging and scheduling are time-consuming and prone to errors. This often leads to missed appointments and delayed customer communication, resulting in dissatisfaction and lost revenue.

Furthermore, keeping client records updated across multiple platforms like Google Sheets, calendars, and CRM systems like Go High Level is a logistical nightmare. Lack of automated follow-ups for reviews and surveys means missed opportunities to gather valuable feedback and improve service quality, ultimately hindering business growth.

The Solution

The solution is an n8n workflow designed to streamline boiler installation job management and automate customer follow-ups. This system captures new job details via webhooks, automatically logs them into Google Sheets, creates corresponding appointments in Google Calendar, and updates client records in Go High Level.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility in integrating with various platforms and its ability to create complex workflows with ease. This allows for seamless data transfer between different applications, ensuring that all systems are synchronized and up-to-date. The automated SMS follow-ups for reviews and surveys further enhance customer engagement and provide valuable insights for business improvement.

How It Works — Seamless Job Management and Customer Engagement

This n8n workflow automates the entire boiler installation job management process, from initial job logging to post-installation customer follow-ups.

  1. Webhook Trigger: A webhook is set up to capture new job details from the source system (e.g., a website form or CRM).
  2. Google Sheets Logging: The workflow automatically logs the new job details into a designated Google Sheet for record-keeping and tracking.
  3. Google Calendar Appointment: A new appointment is created in Google Calendar, ensuring the job is scheduled and visible to the installation team.
  4. Go High Level Update: The client's record in Go High Level is updated with the new job details, providing a comprehensive view of the customer's history.
  5. Initial SMS Confirmation: An SMS message is sent to the customer confirming the job details and appointment time.
  6. Post-Installation SMS Follow-up: After the installation is complete, an automated SMS is sent to the customer requesting a review of the service.
  7. Survey Invitation: A second SMS is sent a few days later, inviting the customer to complete a satisfaction survey.
  8. Data Consolidation: All data collected from reviews and surveys is consolidated and analyzed to identify areas for improvement.

💡 Centralized Data Management: By integrating multiple platforms into a single workflow, all job-related data is centralized, reducing the risk of errors and improving overall efficiency.

This automation is ideal for home service businesses that struggle with manual job management and customer follow-ups. It's particularly beneficial for companies using Google Sheets, Google Calendar, and Go High Level, as it seamlessly integrates these platforms into a unified workflow.
