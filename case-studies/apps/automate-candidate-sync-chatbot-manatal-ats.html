The Problem

Recruitment teams at Ming Hwee were facing a significant bottleneck in their hiring process. Candidate data collected through their chatbot platform required manual transfer to their Manatal Applicant Tracking System (ATS). This process was not only time-consuming but also prone to errors, leading to inaccurate candidate profiles and delays in the hiring pipeline.

The HR department struggled with spending excessive time on administrative tasks instead of focusing on strategic recruitment activities. The lack of seamless integration between the chatbot and ATS resulted in duplicated efforts, increased operational costs, and a slower time-to-hire. This manual data entry also increased the risk of data loss and compliance issues.

The Solution

GrowwStacks developed a custom n8n workflow to automate the synchronization of candidate data from the Ming Hwee Chatbot to the Manatal ATS. This solution involved creating a webhook trigger in n8n to capture candidate information submitted through the chatbot, mapping the data fields to the corresponding fields in Manatal, and using the Manatal API to create new candidate records.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility, scalability, and ability to handle complex data transformations. The webhook integration allowed for real-time data transfer, ensuring that candidate information was immediately available in the ATS. This eliminated the need for manual data entry, reduced errors, and significantly improved the efficiency of the recruitment process.

How It Works — Real-time Candidate Data Synchronization

The n8n workflow automates the process of transferring candidate data from the chatbot to Manatal ATS in real-time, ensuring that recruitment teams have immediate access to the latest candidate information.

  1. Chatbot Submission: A candidate submits their information through the Ming Hwee Chatbot.
  2. Webhook Trigger: n8n receives the candidate data via a webhook triggered by the chatbot submission.
  3. Data Mapping: n8n maps the data fields from the chatbot to the corresponding fields in Manatal ATS.
  4. API Authentication: n8n authenticates with the Manatal API using secure credentials.
  5. Candidate Creation: n8n uses the Manatal API to create a new candidate record with the mapped data.
  6. Confirmation Response: n8n sends a confirmation response back to the chatbot, indicating successful data transfer.
  7. Error Handling: n8n includes error handling to manage any issues during data transfer and alerts the team.
  8. Logging: n8n logs all data transfer activities for auditing and troubleshooting purposes.

💡 Real-time Data: The automated workflow ensures that candidate data is immediately available in Manatal ATS, eliminating delays and improving the speed of the recruitment process. This real-time synchronization allows HR to quickly assess and engage with promising candidates.

The Right Fit — and When It Isn't

This solution is ideal for organizations using chatbots for initial candidate screening and Manatal ATS for managing their recruitment pipeline. It's particularly beneficial for companies looking to improve the efficiency of their hiring process and reduce the administrative burden on their HR teams.

However, this solution may not be suitable for organizations that do not use a chatbot for candidate screening or those that have highly customized data requirements that cannot be easily mapped to Manatal ATS fields. In such cases, a more tailored automation solution may be required.
