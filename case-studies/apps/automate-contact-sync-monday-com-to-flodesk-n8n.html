The Problem

Marketing teams often face challenges in maintaining consistent contact information across different platforms. Manually transferring contact details from project management tools like Monday.com to email marketing platforms like Flodesk is time-consuming and prone to errors. This can lead to outdated subscriber lists, missed opportunities, and inefficient campaign management.

Without an automated solution, marketing professionals spend valuable time on repetitive data entry, which detracts from strategic tasks such as campaign optimization and content creation. The lack of real-time synchronization between platforms results in discrepancies, impacting the effectiveness of marketing efforts and potentially damaging customer relationships.

The Solution

The solution is an automated workflow built with N8N that seamlessly synchronizes contacts from Monday.com to Flodesk. When a contact's status in Monday.com changes to 'Sync Now', the workflow automatically retrieves the contact's details, matches them to the appropriate segments in Flodesk, updates the subscriber information, and marks the task as complete in Monday.com.

N8N was chosen for its flexibility and ability to integrate with multiple platforms. Its user-friendly interface and robust features make it an ideal choice for building complex workflows that require real-time data synchronization. By automating this process, marketing teams can ensure accurate and up-to-date contact information, saving time and improving campaign performance.

How It Works — Real-time contact updates

This workflow monitors Monday.com for changes in contact status and automatically updates Flodesk with the latest information, ensuring your marketing campaigns are always reaching the right audience.

  1. Monitor Monday.com: The workflow starts by monitoring the Monday.com board for any changes in the 'Next Campaign' status column.
  2. Trigger on Status Change: When the status of a contact changes to 'Sync Now', it triggers the workflow to initiate the contact synchronization process.
  3. Retrieve Contact Details: The workflow retrieves all relevant contact details from Monday.com, including name, email, and any other custom fields.
  4. Match Segments in Flodesk: The workflow identifies the appropriate segments in Flodesk based on the contact's attributes and preferences.
  5. Update Subscriber Information: The workflow updates the subscriber's information in Flodesk, ensuring all details are accurate and up-to-date.
  6. Mark Task as Complete: Once the contact is successfully synchronized, the workflow marks the task as complete in Monday.com, providing a clear audit trail.
  7. Error Handling: The workflow includes error handling to manage any issues that may arise during the synchronization process, such as invalid email addresses or connection problems.

💡 Data Integrity: By automating contact synchronization, you minimize the risk of human error and ensure that your contact data is always accurate and consistent across platforms.
