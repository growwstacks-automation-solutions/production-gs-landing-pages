The Problem

Many businesses struggle with time-consuming manual lead generation. Sales teams spend hours searching Google Maps for potential clients, manually copying contact details, and verifying information. This process is not only inefficient but also prone to errors, leading to wasted time and missed opportunities.

Furthermore, data enrichment is a significant challenge. Simply extracting basic contact information is not enough. Sales teams need additional data points, such as website URLs, social media profiles, and industry classifications, to effectively qualify and engage with leads. Manually gathering this information is a tedious and often inaccurate process.

The Solution

We developed an automated workflow that scrapes business leads from Google Maps using AI agents, extracts contact details, and stores them in Google Sheets. This system leverages n8n for workflow orchestration, OpenAI for data extraction and validation, and Google Sheets for data storage and management. By automating these processes, we significantly reduced the time and effort required for lead generation.

The workflow is designed to automatically validate and enrich lead data by visiting each business's website and extracting relevant information. This ensures that sales teams have access to accurate and comprehensive lead data, enabling them to focus on building relationships and closing deals. The use of n8n allows for easy customization and scalability, making it a perfect fit for businesses of all sizes.

How It Works — Automated Lead Data Pipeline

This workflow automates the entire lead generation process, from scraping Google Maps to enriching data and saving it to Google Sheets.

  1. Scrape Google Maps: The workflow starts by scraping Google Maps for businesses based on specific keywords and locations.
  2. Extract Business Details: It extracts key details such as business name, address, phone number, and website URL.
  3. Validate Website: The workflow then validates the website URL to ensure it is active and accessible.
  4. Extract Data with AI: Using OpenAI, the system extracts additional data points from the website, such as industry classification and social media profiles.
  5. Enrich Contact Information: The AI agent enriches contact information by finding email addresses and other relevant details.
  6. Format Data: The extracted and enriched data is formatted for easy import into Google Sheets.
  7. Save to Google Sheets: The formatted data is saved to a Google Sheets spreadsheet, ready for sales teams to use.
  8. Monitor and Update: The workflow continuously monitors for new leads and updates existing data as needed.

💡 AI-Powered Data Extraction: By using OpenAI, the workflow can extract and validate data from websites with high accuracy, reducing the need for manual review and correction.
