The Problem

Many agency placement firms struggle with proactively gathering client feedback and boosting their online reputation. Manually requesting Google reviews is time-consuming, often inconsistent, and yields low response rates. This results in a lack of social proof, hindering their ability to attract new clients and build trust in the market.

Furthermore, addressing negative feedback promptly is crucial for maintaining a positive brand image. Without an automated system, negative reviews can linger, potentially damaging the agency's reputation and impacting future business. The challenge lies in efficiently collecting feedback, identifying promoters, and swiftly resolving detractors to ensure client satisfaction and a strong online presence.

The Solution

We built an automated workflow using n8n that sends WhatsApp review requests to clients 14 days after a placement. This system collects feedback ratings, automatically sends Google review links to clients who provide positive responses, and escalates negative feedback to the agency's internal team for immediate follow-up. The workflow ensures a consistent and efficient feedback collection process.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility in integrating with WhatsApp and Google Reviews, allowing for a seamless and automated feedback loop. Its ability to handle complex logic and data transformations made it the ideal platform for creating a system that not only collects reviews but also intelligently manages and responds to feedback based on sentiment.

How It Works — Streamlining Feedback Collection

The automated workflow simplifies the process of gathering client feedback and managing online reviews, ensuring a consistent and efficient approach.

  1. Placement Trigger: The workflow is initiated 14 days after a client placement is recorded in the agency's system.
  2. WhatsApp Request: An automated WhatsApp message is sent to the client, requesting feedback on their placement experience.
  3. Feedback Rating: The client provides a rating (e.g., 1-5 stars) via WhatsApp, indicating their level of satisfaction.
  4. Positive Feedback: If the rating is positive (e.g., 4 or 5 stars), the workflow automatically sends a Google review link to the client.
  5. Google Review Submission: The client is directed to the agency's Google review page, where they can easily submit their positive feedback.
  6. Negative Feedback: If the rating is negative (e.g., 1-3 stars), the workflow escalates the feedback to the agency's internal team.
  7. Internal Follow-Up: The agency's team receives a notification and takes immediate action to address the client's concerns and resolve any issues.
  8. Data Logging: All feedback and actions are logged in a central database for tracking and analysis, providing valuable insights into client satisfaction and areas for improvement.

💡 Proactive Engagement: Automating the review request process ensures that feedback is consistently collected, leading to a more accurate representation of client satisfaction and a stronger online reputation.
