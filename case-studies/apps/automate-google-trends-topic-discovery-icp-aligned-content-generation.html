The Problem

Content creators and marketers often struggle with identifying trending topics that resonate with their ideal customer profile (ICP). Manually sifting through Google Trends data is time-consuming and inefficient, often leading to missed opportunities. Furthermore, ensuring that content ideas align with the specific interests and values of privacy-conscious, mid-career decision-makers presents a significant challenge.

The traditional content creation process involves hours of manual research, brainstorming sessions, and content scoring. This not only drains resources but also increases the risk of creating content that fails to capture the attention of the target audience. The lack of a streamlined, automated system results in inconsistent content quality and missed opportunities to engage with potential customers effectively.

The Solution

We developed an automated workflow that extracts trending topics from Google Trends, filters them based on relevance to privacy-conscious mid-career decision-makers, and generates content ideas using AI. The system then scores these ideas against six predefined criteria, ensuring that only high-quality, ICP-aligned topics are selected for content creation.

This solution leverages n8n as the primary automation platform, integrating with Google Trends for data extraction, OpenAI for content idea generation, and Airtable for data storage and management. The choice of this tech stack ensures scalability, flexibility, and seamless integration with existing marketing tools, providing a comprehensive content automation solution.

How It Works — Streamlining Content Creation with Automation

This automated workflow simplifies the content creation process, ensuring that only relevant and high-scoring topics are selected for content development.

  1. Extract Trending Topics: The workflow begins by extracting the latest trending topics from Google Trends using the n8n platform.
  2. Filter for ICP Relevance: The extracted topics are then filtered based on predefined criteria to ensure relevance to privacy-conscious, mid-career decision-makers.
  3. Generate Content Ideas: Using OpenAI, the system generates content ideas for each filtered topic, providing a range of potential angles and formats.
  4. Score Content Ideas: Each content idea is scored against six criteria, including relevance, originality, and potential impact, to identify the most promising topics.
  5. Store High-Scoring Topics: The high-scoring topics are then stored in Airtable, creating a centralized repository of content ideas for future use.
  6. Automate Content Brief Creation: The system automatically generates content briefs for the selected topics, providing writers with a clear outline and key points to cover.
  7. Schedule Content for Publication: The workflow integrates with social media scheduling tools to automate the publication of content across various platforms.

💡 Data-Driven Content Strategy: By automating the topic discovery and content idea generation process, content creators can ensure that their content strategy is data-driven and aligned with the interests of their target audience.
