The Problem

Agencies managing foreign domestic worker (FDW) contracts in Singapore face significant challenges in processing job offer forms. The manual process is time-consuming and prone to errors, leading to delays and potential compliance issues. Each form requires manual data entry, PDF creation, and storage, which is inefficient and costly.

The lack of automation results in increased administrative overhead, diverting resources from more critical tasks. Furthermore, ensuring data accuracy and consistency across all forms is difficult, leading to potential discrepancies and legal complications. The need for a streamlined, automated solution is evident.

The Solution

The solution is an automated workflow built with n8n that streamlines the entire job offer form processing. This system receives form data via webhook, automatically generates a PDF file in Google Drive, shares it publicly, and stores all the details in an Airtable database. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures data accuracy.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility and ability to integrate seamlessly with Google Drive and Airtable. This tech stack provides a robust, scalable, and cost-effective solution for automating document management and data storage. The workflow ensures that all job offer forms are processed quickly and accurately, reducing administrative burden and improving overall efficiency.

How It Works — Automated Data Flow

The automated workflow efficiently processes job offer forms, ensuring accuracy and saving time. Here's a detailed breakdown of each step:

  1. Form Submission: The process begins when a job offer form is submitted. The data is captured and sent via webhook to n8n.
  2. Webhook Trigger: n8n receives the form data via the webhook, triggering the automated workflow.
  3. Data Extraction: The workflow extracts relevant information from the form data, such as candidate details, job specifications, and salary information.
  4. PDF Generation: Using the extracted data, n8n generates a PDF document in Google Drive, creating a professional and standardized job offer form.
  5. File Sharing: The generated PDF is then shared publicly with a shareable link, allowing easy access for all relevant parties.
  6. Airtable Integration: All the extracted data is stored in an Airtable database, providing a centralized location for tracking and managing job offer forms.
  7. Data Validation: The workflow validates the data to ensure accuracy and completeness, reducing the risk of errors.
  8. Notification: A notification is sent to the relevant personnel, informing them that the job offer form has been processed and is ready for review.

💡 Data Accuracy: Automating the data entry process significantly reduces the risk of human error, ensuring that all information is accurate and consistent.

The Right Fit — and When It Isn't

This automated job offer form processing system is ideal for agencies managing foreign domestic worker (FDW) contracts in Singapore. It is particularly beneficial for agencies that process a large volume of job offer forms and need to ensure data accuracy and compliance.

However, this system may not be the right fit for agencies that process a very small number of job offer forms or have very specific and complex requirements that cannot be easily automated. In such cases, a manual process may be more efficient.
