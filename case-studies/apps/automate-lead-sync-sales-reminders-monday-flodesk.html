The Problem

The client, a fast-growing sales organization, struggled with inefficient lead management. Their sales team was spending excessive time on manual data entry, transferring new leads from Monday.com to Flodesk for email marketing campaigns. This lack of synchronization led to delays in follow-ups, missed opportunities, and inconsistent messaging, ultimately impacting their sales conversion rates.

Furthermore, the manual territory assignment process was prone to errors and delays. Sales reps often received leads outside their designated territories, leading to confusion and decreased efficiency. The absence of automated reminders for timely follow-ups resulted in lost sales opportunities and a decline in overall sales performance. The client needed a solution to streamline lead management, automate sales reminders, and ensure seamless data synchronization between their CRM and email marketing platform.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive n8n workflow that automates lead synchronization between Monday.com and Flodesk. The workflow monitors Monday.com for new contacts, automatically syncing them to Flodesk. It also handles territory assignments based on predefined rules, ensuring leads are routed to the correct sales representatives. Automated follow-up reminders are sent to sales reps, prompting timely engagement with new leads.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility and ability to seamlessly integrate with both Monday.com and Flodesk. Its visual interface allowed for easy configuration and customization of the workflow, while its robust error handling capabilities ensured data integrity and reliability. The workflow also includes features for processing unsubscribes and logging errors, providing a complete solution for automated lead management.

How It Works — Automated Lead Distribution & Follow-Ups

This n8n workflow automates the process of syncing new leads from Monday.com to Flodesk, assigning territories, and sending follow-up reminders to sales representatives.

  1. New Lead in Monday.com: The workflow is triggered when a new lead is added to a specified board in Monday.com.
  2. Fetch Lead Details: The workflow retrieves all relevant details of the new lead, including contact information, company details, and lead source.
  3. Territory Assignment: Based on predefined rules (e.g., location, industry), the workflow assigns the lead to the appropriate sales representative.
  4. Sync to Flodesk: The lead's information is automatically synced to Flodesk, adding the lead to a designated segment for email marketing campaigns.
  5. Send Follow-up Reminder: A follow-up reminder is sent to the assigned sales representative via email or Slack, prompting them to contact the new lead.
  6. Log Activity: The workflow logs the activity in a data table, recording the date, lead details, assigned representative, and reminder status.
  7. Handle Unsubscribes: If a lead unsubscribes from Flodesk, the workflow automatically updates the lead's status in Monday.com to prevent further communication.
  8. Error Handling: The workflow includes error handling to catch and log any issues that may arise during the process, ensuring data integrity and reliability.

💡 Data Integrity: Robust error handling and logging mechanisms ensure data accuracy and prevent data loss during synchronization.
