The Problem

Sales and marketing teams face the challenge of manually searching LinkedIn for potential leads, a time-consuming and often inefficient process. Sifting through countless company profiles to identify those that align with their ideal customer profile (ICP) requires significant effort and resources. This manual approach often leads to missed opportunities and wasted time on unqualified leads.

Furthermore, even when potential leads are identified, the process of manually enriching their data and assessing their fit for a particular product or service is cumbersome. Without a streamlined system for scoring and prioritizing leads, sales teams struggle to focus their efforts on the most promising prospects, resulting in lower conversion rates and reduced ROI on their outreach efforts.

The Solution

The solution is an automated LinkedIn company search and lead qualification workflow built with n8n. This workflow leverages the Ghost Genius API to search LinkedIn companies based on specific criteria. It then enriches the data of these companies, filters them based on website presence and follower count, and uses OpenAI to score each lead based on their fit for a particular product or service.

By using n8n, the entire process is automated, from initial search to lead qualification and CRM integration. This eliminates the need for manual data entry and reduces the risk of human error. The integration with Google Sheets ensures that all qualified leads are automatically added to a centralized CRM, preventing duplicates and providing sales teams with a clear and organized pipeline of potential customers.

How It Works — Automated Lead Qualification Process

This workflow automates the process of finding and qualifying leads on LinkedIn, ensuring that only the most promising prospects are added to your CRM.

  1. LinkedIn Search: The workflow starts by using the Ghost Genius API to search LinkedIn companies based on predefined keywords and criteria.
  2. Data Enrichment: Once the companies are found, their data is enriched with additional information, such as industry, location, and employee count.
  3. Website Check: The workflow verifies if the company has a website, as this is a key indicator of their online presence and potential for engagement.
  4. Follower Count Filter: Companies are filtered based on their follower count, ensuring that only those with a significant following are considered.
  5. AI Scoring: Each lead is then scored using OpenAI, which assesses their fit for a particular product or service based on their company description and other relevant data.
  6. Qualification Threshold: Leads that meet a predefined scoring threshold are automatically qualified as potential customers.
  7. CRM Integration: Qualified leads are automatically added to a Google Sheets CRM, along with their contact information and scoring data.
  8. Duplicate Prevention: The workflow checks for duplicates in the CRM before adding new leads, ensuring that no lead is added more than once.

💡 AI-Powered Lead Scoring: Using OpenAI to score leads allows for a more nuanced and accurate assessment of their fit for a particular product or service, compared to traditional methods.
