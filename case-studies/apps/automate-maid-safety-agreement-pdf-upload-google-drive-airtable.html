How It Works — Automated Document Management

This workflow automates the processing of maid safety agreement forms, ensuring compliance and efficient record-keeping.

  1. Webhook Trigger: The workflow is initiated when a new maid safety agreement form is submitted via webhook.
  2. Generate PDF: The submitted form data is used to automatically generate a PDF document.
  3. Upload to Google Drive: The generated PDF is uploaded to a designated folder in Google Drive.
  4. Set Permissions: Public sharing permissions are automatically set for the uploaded PDF, ensuring easy access.
  5. Update Airtable: The corresponding record in Airtable is updated with the Google Drive link.
  6. Error Handling: The workflow includes robust error handling to manage any issues during the process.
  7. Notification: A notification is sent to the relevant personnel upon successful completion of the workflow.

💡 Compliance Assurance: Automating the document management process ensures that all maid safety agreements are processed consistently and accurately, reducing the risk of non-compliance.
