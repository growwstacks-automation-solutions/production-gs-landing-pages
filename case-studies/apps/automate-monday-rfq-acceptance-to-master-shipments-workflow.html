The Problem

Logistics and sales operations teams often face challenges in managing the quote-to-shipment process. The manual transfer of data from accepted Request for Quotes (RFQs) in Monday.com to the master shipment tracking system is time-consuming and prone to errors. This can lead to delays in shipment processing, inaccurate data, and increased operational costs.

Furthermore, the lack of real-time updates and notifications between teams results in miscommunication and duplicated efforts. The inability to prevent duplicate bookings and ensure data consistency across platforms creates inefficiencies and potential revenue loss. The need for a streamlined, automated solution is critical to optimize the entire workflow.

The Solution

The solution is an automated workflow built with n8n that seamlessly integrates Monday.com with an email notification system. This workflow triggers when an RFQ status changes to 'Accepted' in Monday.com, automatically moving the item to a Master Shipments board. It then updates mapped fields, notifies sales teams via Missive email, and prevents duplicate bookings, ensuring data accuracy and consistency.

This tech stack was chosen for its ability to handle complex data transformations and integrations, providing a reliable and scalable solution. n8n's flexibility and ease of use make it ideal for automating the entire RFQ acceptance process, reducing manual effort and improving overall operational efficiency. The integration with Missive ensures timely and accurate communication between teams.

How It Works — Streamlining RFQ to Shipment Process

This workflow automates the entire RFQ acceptance process, ensuring a smooth transition from quote to shipment.

  1. RFQ Acceptance: The workflow is triggered when an RFQ's status changes to 'Accepted' in Monday.com.
  2. Move to Master Shipments: The accepted RFQ is automatically moved to a Master Shipments board in Monday.com.
  3. Update Mapped Fields: Key data fields are updated and synchronized between the RFQ and Master Shipments boards.
  4. Prevent Duplicate Bookings: The system checks for existing bookings to prevent duplicates, ensuring accuracy.
  5. Notify Sales Team: A notification email is sent to the sales team via Missive, informing them of the accepted RFQ.
  6. Data Validation: The workflow validates the data to ensure all required fields are complete and accurate.
  7. Error Handling: If any errors occur during the process, notifications are sent to the appropriate personnel for review.

💡 Real-Time Visibility: Automated notifications and data synchronization provide real-time visibility into the status of each RFQ, improving communication and coordination between teams.
