The Problem

Retail businesses using both Retail Express and ActiveCampaign often face challenges in manually transferring quote data between the two systems. This process is time-consuming, prone to errors, and can lead to inconsistent data across platforms. Sales teams spend valuable time on administrative tasks instead of focusing on customer engagement and closing deals.

The lack of automated synchronization results in delayed quote follow-ups, missed sales opportunities, and inefficient use of resources. Without a streamlined system, it's difficult to maintain accurate customer information and track quote progress effectively, hindering overall sales performance.

The Solution

We developed an n8n workflow to automate the synchronization of new quotes from Retail Express to ActiveCampaign. This system automatically creates or updates contacts in ActiveCampaign, formats phone numbers, searches for existing deals, and creates new deal records with aggregated product details. The workflow ensures seamless data transfer between the two platforms.

The choice of n8n was strategic due to its flexibility, scalability, and ability to handle complex data transformations. n8n's open-source nature and robust integration capabilities made it the ideal platform for building a custom solution that perfectly fits the client's needs, ensuring efficient and reliable data synchronization.

How It Works — Automated Quote-to-Deal Pipeline

The n8n workflow monitors Retail Express for new quotes and automatically transfers the data to ActiveCampaign, streamlining the sales process.

  1. New Quote Trigger: The workflow is triggered when a new quote is created in Retail Express.
  2. Fetch Quote Details: n8n retrieves detailed information about the new quote, including customer details and product information.
  3. Format Phone Number: The customer's phone number is formatted to ensure consistency in ActiveCampaign.
  4. Search for Contact: The workflow searches ActiveCampaign for an existing contact with the same email address.
  5. Create/Update Contact: If the contact exists, it's updated with the latest information. If not, a new contact is created.
  6. Search for Existing Deal: The workflow searches for an existing deal associated with the contact.
  7. Create New Deal: If no deal exists, a new deal is created with the quote details and aggregated product information.
  8. Update Deal Records: The deal record is updated with the latest quote information, ensuring accurate tracking of the sales pipeline.

💡 Data Accuracy: Automating the data transfer ensures that customer and quote information is always accurate and up-to-date in ActiveCampaign, reducing errors and improving sales efficiency.
