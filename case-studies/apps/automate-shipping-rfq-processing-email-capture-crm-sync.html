The Problem

Shipping companies often struggle with the manual processing of RFQs submitted via email. This involves sifting through numerous emails, extracting relevant data, and manually entering it into their CRM system. The process is time-consuming and prone to errors, leading to delays in responding to potential clients and missed opportunities.

Furthermore, inaccurate or incomplete data can result in incorrect quotes and dissatisfied customers. The lack of a streamlined system also makes it difficult to track and manage RFQs effectively, hindering sales teams' ability to prioritize and follow up on leads. This inefficiency impacts overall productivity and revenue.

The Solution

The solution is an automated workflow that processes shipping RFQs submitted via email. This system extracts and cleans the data from the email, validates contact information against Pipedrive, and creates leads and activities in the CRM. It also enriches the data with email verification and geolocation.

This tech stack was chosen for its ability to seamlessly integrate email parsing, CRM management, and data enrichment. N8N serves as the backbone, orchestrating the data flow between Pipedrive and Google Sheets. This ensures that all RFQ data is accurately captured, validated, and readily available for the sales team.

How It Works — Streamlining RFQ Intake

The automated workflow efficiently processes shipping RFQs, ensuring no potential lead is overlooked. Here's a detailed breakdown:

  1. Email Capture: The system monitors a designated email inbox for incoming RFQs.
  2. Data Extraction: Upon receiving an email, the workflow extracts key information such as contact details, shipment details, and requested services.
  3. Data Cleaning: The extracted data is cleaned and formatted to ensure accuracy and consistency.
  4. Pipedrive Validation: The system checks if the contact already exists in Pipedrive to avoid duplicates.
  5. Lead Creation: If the contact is new, a new lead is created in Pipedrive with the extracted RFQ data.
  6. Activity Logging: An activity is logged in Pipedrive to remind the sales team to follow up on the RFQ.
  7. Email Verification: The contact's email address is verified to ensure deliverability and reduce bounce rates.
  8. Geolocation: The contact's location is determined to provide additional context for the sales team.

💡 Data Enrichment: By adding email verification and geolocation data, the sales team gains a more complete picture of the potential client, enabling more personalized and effective communication.
