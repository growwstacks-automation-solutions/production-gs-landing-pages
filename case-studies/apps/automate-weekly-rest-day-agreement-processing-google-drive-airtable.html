The Problem

Many employers and agencies struggle with the time-consuming process of creating, storing, and tracking weekly rest day agreements for domestic workers. This manual process is prone to errors, making it difficult to ensure compliance with labor regulations. The lack of automation leads to inefficiencies and increased administrative burden.

Without a streamlined system, employers and agencies face challenges in maintaining accurate records of rest day agreements. This can result in potential legal issues and penalties for non-compliance. The need for a reliable and automated solution is critical to reduce risks and improve operational efficiency.

The Solution

We developed an n8n workflow that automates the entire process of generating, storing, and logging weekly rest day agreements. The workflow receives form data via webhook, generates a PDF agreement, uploads it to Google Drive, shares it publicly, and logs all details in Airtable.

This solution was chosen for its ability to seamlessly integrate with Google Drive and Airtable, providing a comprehensive and automated system. n8n's flexibility and open-source nature allowed for customization to meet the specific needs of employers and agencies, ensuring full compliance with labor regulations.

How It Works — Streamlining Agreement Management

The n8n workflow automates the creation, storage, and logging of weekly rest day agreements, ensuring compliance and efficiency.

  1. Webhook Trigger: The workflow is initiated when a form is submitted with the necessary data.
  2. Data Extraction: The submitted data is extracted and formatted for use in the agreement.
  3. PDF Generation: A PDF agreement is generated using the extracted data.
  4. Google Drive Upload: The generated PDF is uploaded to a specified Google Drive folder.
  5. Public Sharing: The PDF is shared publicly with a shareable link.
  6. Airtable Logging: All relevant details, including the PDF link, are logged in Airtable.
  7. Notification (Optional): A notification is sent to the employer or agency confirming the agreement's creation and storage.

💡 Compliance Assurance: Automated logging in Airtable ensures a clear audit trail, simplifying compliance reporting and reducing the risk of penalties.
