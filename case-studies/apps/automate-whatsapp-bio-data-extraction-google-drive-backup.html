The Problem

Matrimony services often grapple with the challenge of efficiently managing and processing large volumes of unstructured data received through WhatsApp. The manual extraction of bio data from documents and images is time-consuming and prone to errors. This can lead to delays in matching potential partners and a decrease in overall service quality.

Furthermore, the lack of a centralized system for storing and organizing this data makes it difficult to track customer information and ensure data security. Inconsistent data handling can result in compliance issues and a loss of customer trust. The need for a streamlined, automated solution is critical for improving operational efficiency and delivering a superior customer experience.

The Solution

The solution involves an automated workflow built using n8n that extracts bio data from documents and images received via WhatsApp. The workflow leverages AI to analyze the data, creates customer-specific folders in Google Drive, uploads the files, and sends the extracted data to Airtable for storage and organization. This system eliminates manual data entry and ensures data is readily accessible.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility, open-source nature, and ability to seamlessly integrate with WhatsApp, Google Drive, Airtable, and AI services. This allows for a customizable and scalable solution that can adapt to the evolving needs of the matrimony service. The workflow automates the entire process, from data extraction to storage, improving efficiency and accuracy.

How It Works — Streamlining Data Management

The automated workflow streamlines the entire data management process, from receiving data to storing it in a structured format.

  1. Receive WhatsApp Data: The workflow starts by receiving documents and images via WhatsApp.
  2. AI Data Extraction: AI algorithms analyze the documents and images to extract relevant bio data.
  3. Create Customer Folder: A new folder is created in Google Drive for each customer.
  4. Upload Files: The received documents and images are uploaded to the customer's folder in Google Drive.
  5. Send Data to Airtable: The extracted bio data is sent to Airtable for structured storage.
  6. Data Validation: The data in Airtable is validated to ensure accuracy and completeness.
  7. Automated Notifications: Notifications are sent to relevant personnel regarding the data processing status.

💡 Data Centralization: Centralizing data in Google Drive and Airtable ensures easy access and efficient management, improving overall operational efficiency.
