The Problem

Shipping companies often face challenges in efficiently managing booking confirmations and generating shipping RFQs. The manual process is often riddled with time-consuming paperwork, leading to delays and increased operational costs. Sales teams spend valuable time on administrative tasks instead of focusing on securing new business.

The lack of automation can also result in inconsistent communication with customers, potentially leading to dissatisfaction and lost opportunities. Ensuring that all necessary terms and conditions are included in each booking confirmation is a critical but error-prone task. The need for a streamlined, automated solution is evident to improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.

The Solution

An n8n workflow was designed to automate the shipping booking confirmation process. This workflow is triggered when a Request for Quote (RFQ) is accepted. It automatically generates professional PDF confirmations containing all necessary terms and conditions, ensuring consistency and accuracy. The system then sends automated emails to customers via Missive, providing timely updates and documentation.

The choice of n8n was driven by its flexibility and ability to integrate seamlessly with Monday.com and other essential tools. The workflow updates Monday.com with the attached PDF documents, providing a centralized location for all relevant information. This integration ensures that the sales team has access to the latest booking confirmations, improving collaboration and decision-making.

How It Works — Streamlining the Shipping Confirmation Process

The n8n workflow automates the entire process, from quote acceptance to document archival, ensuring efficiency and accuracy.

  1. RFQ Acceptance: The workflow is triggered when a Request for Quote (RFQ) is marked as accepted in the system.
  2. Data Extraction: Relevant data from the accepted RFQ, such as customer details, shipping information, and terms, is extracted.
  3. PDF Generation: A professional PDF confirmation document is automatically generated using the extracted data and a pre-defined template.
  4. Email Composition: An email is composed with the generated PDF attached, ready to be sent to the customer.
  5. Email Sending: The email is automatically sent to the customer via Missive, ensuring timely delivery of the booking confirmation.
  6. Monday.com Update: The Monday.com board is updated with the attached PDF document, providing a centralized location for all relevant information.
  7. Notification: A notification is sent to the sales team, informing them that the booking confirmation has been sent and the Monday.com board has been updated.

💡 Consistent Branding: The automated PDF generation ensures that all booking confirmations adhere to the company's branding guidelines, maintaining a professional image.

This automation is ideal for shipping companies that handle a high volume of RFQs and booking confirmations.
