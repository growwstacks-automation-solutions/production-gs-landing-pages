The Problem

Many businesses struggle with inefficient contact management, especially when using multiple platforms like Monday.com for project management and Flodesk for email marketing. Manually updating contact information and segmenting subscribers based on project statuses is time-consuming and prone to errors. This leads to delayed communication, missed opportunities, and inconsistent messaging.

The lack of synchronization between these systems results in data silos, making it difficult to maintain an accurate and up-to-date contact list. Sales and marketing teams spend valuable time on administrative tasks instead of focusing on strategic activities. This disjointed approach hinders effective lead nurturing and reduces overall marketing ROI. The need for a streamlined, automated solution is evident.

The Solution

We developed an n8n workflow that automates contact management and email marketing synchronization between Monday.com and Flodesk. This workflow monitors Monday.com boards for changes in item statuses, automatically updating corresponding subscriber segments in Flodesk. It also handles territory assignments and syncs representative emails, ensuring that contact information is always current and accurate.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility and ability to integrate seamlessly with both Monday.com and Flodesk. Its visual interface allows for easy customization and maintenance, while its robust error handling ensures reliable performance. By automating these critical processes, businesses can eliminate manual data entry, improve data accuracy, and enhance the effectiveness of their email marketing campaigns.

How It Works — Streamlining Contact Updates

This n8n workflow automates the process of keeping contact information consistent between Monday.com and Flodesk, ensuring that marketing efforts are always based on the most current data.

  1. Monitor Monday.com: The workflow continuously monitors specified Monday.com boards for any changes in item statuses or contact details.
  2. Retrieve Updated Data: When a change is detected, the workflow retrieves the updated contact information from Monday.com.
  3. Map Data Fields: The retrieved data is then mapped to the corresponding fields in Flodesk to ensure accurate data transfer.
  4. Update Flodesk Segments: Based on the updated status in Monday.com, the workflow adds or removes the contact from relevant Flodesk segments.
  5. Sync Rep Emails: The workflow also syncs representative emails, ensuring that the correct contact person is associated with each subscriber.
  6. Assign Territories: Territory assignments are updated in Flodesk based on the corresponding data in Monday.com.
  7. Log Activity: All updates are logged for auditing and reporting purposes, providing a clear record of changes.

💡 Data Consistency: By automating the synchronization process, the workflow ensures that contact information is consistent across both Monday.com and Flodesk, reducing the risk of errors and improving data quality.
