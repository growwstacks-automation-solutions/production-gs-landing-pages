The Problem

TrueU.ai faced a significant challenge in maintaining consistent and engaging social media content. Their video scripts, while rich in educational content, required a time-consuming manual process to transform into interactive poll questions. This lack of automation led to delays in content deployment and a strain on resources, hindering their ability to maximize social media engagement.

The manual creation of poll questions also resulted in inconsistent quality and a limited variety of question types. This not only reduced audience participation but also failed to fully leverage the potential of their video content. The need for a streamlined, automated solution became critical to enhance their social media strategy and improve overall content effectiveness.

The Solution

To address these challenges, we developed an automated workflow using n8n that generates poll questions directly from TrueU.ai's approved video scripts. This system leverages the power of OpenAI to analyze the scripts and create reflective, non-judgmental Yes/No poll questions. The generated questions are then automatically saved to Airtable, providing a centralized repository for easy access and deployment.

This solution was chosen for its ability to seamlessly integrate with TrueU.ai's existing tech stack, including Airtable, and for its scalability to handle future content needs. n8n's flexibility and OpenAI's advanced AI capabilities made it the perfect combination to automate the poll question generation process, ensuring consistent quality and significant time savings.

How It Works — Streamlining Content Creation with AI

The workflow automates the entire process of generating poll questions, from script analysis to question storage, ensuring efficiency and consistency.

  1. Input Video Script: The approved video script is fed into the n8n workflow as the initial input.
  2. Analyze Script with OpenAI: The script is sent to OpenAI's API for analysis, identifying key themes and topics.
  3. Generate Poll Questions: OpenAI generates five reflective, non-judgmental Yes/No poll questions based on the script's content.
  4. Format Questions: The generated questions are formatted to ensure they are concise and engaging for social media.
  5. Save to Airtable: The formatted poll questions are automatically saved to an Airtable base, organized for easy access.
  6. Review and Approve: The social media team reviews the generated questions in Airtable and approves them for scheduling.
  7. Schedule Polls: The approved poll questions are scheduled for posting on various social media platforms.

💡 AI-Powered Content: By using OpenAI, the workflow ensures that the generated poll questions are relevant, engaging, and aligned with the video script's content, maximizing audience interaction.

The Right Fit — and When It Isn't

This automated poll question generation system is ideal for businesses that rely heavily on social media for engagement and content distribution. It's particularly beneficial for companies with a high volume of video content and a need for consistent, high-quality poll questions. The system streamlines content creation, saves time, and enhances audience interaction.

However, this solution may not be the right fit for businesses with limited video content or those that prefer a highly customized, manual approach to social media engagement. Additionally, companies that lack the technical resources to manage and maintain the n8n workflow may find it challenging to implement and sustain the system effectively.
