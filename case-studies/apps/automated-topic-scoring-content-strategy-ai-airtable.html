The Problem

Content teams face the challenge of efficiently identifying and prioritizing topics that resonate with their audience and align with business goals. Manually evaluating each topic against multiple criteria, such as search intent, relevance, and potential impact, is time-consuming and prone to subjective bias.

This manual process often leads to missed opportunities for high-performing content and inefficient allocation of resources. Without a streamlined system for topic scoring, content creators struggle to focus on the most promising ideas, resulting in lower engagement and reduced ROI from content marketing efforts.

The Solution

The solution is an automated workflow that leverages AI to score content topics against six key criteria: search intent, personal consequence, brand alignment, data availability, competitive landscape, and content format. This workflow integrates Airtable for topic management and n8n for automation, ensuring a seamless process from topic ideation to content creation.

By automating the topic scoring process, content teams can quickly identify and prioritize the most promising topics, saving time and improving content quality. The use of AI ensures objective and consistent evaluation, while the integration with Airtable provides a centralized platform for topic management and collaboration.

How It Works — AI-Driven Topic Evaluation

This workflow automatically scores content topics based on predefined criteria, ensuring that only the most promising ideas are pursued. Here's a breakdown of the process:

  1. Topic Submission: A new content topic is entered into an Airtable base, triggering the workflow.
  2. Data Extraction: The workflow extracts the topic details from Airtable, including title, description, and any relevant keywords.
  3. AI Scoring Request: The extracted data is sent to OpenAI's API, requesting a score for the topic against six criteria: search intent, personal consequence, brand alignment, data availability, competitive landscape, and content format.
  4. AI Score Calculation: OpenAI's model analyzes the topic data and assigns a score for each criterion, providing a comprehensive evaluation of the topic's potential.
  5. Airtable Update: The workflow updates the Airtable record with the AI-generated scores, including individual scores for each criterion and an overall topic score.
  6. Status Assignment: Based on the overall score, the workflow assigns a status to the topic, such as "High Priority," "Review," or "Rejected."
  7. Notification Routing: If the topic receives a high score, the workflow sends a notification to the content team, indicating that the topic is ready for content creation.
  8. Review Process: If the topic receives a low score, the workflow routes it for review and refinement, allowing the team to improve the topic or reject it if necessary.

💡 AI-Powered Insights: By leveraging AI, this workflow provides objective and data-driven insights into topic potential, helping content teams make informed decisions and prioritize high-impact content.

The Right Fit — and When It Isn't

This automated topic scoring system is ideal for content teams looking to streamline their topic selection process and improve content quality. It's particularly well-suited for businesses that rely heavily on content marketing and want to maximize their ROI.

However, this system may not be the right fit for businesses with very small content teams or those that don't have a clear understanding of their target audience and content goals. In such cases, a more manual approach may be more appropriate.
