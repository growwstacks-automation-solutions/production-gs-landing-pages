The Problem

Many businesses using VAPI for AI-powered calls struggle with manual data entry to create contacts in their GoHighLevel CRM. This process is time-consuming and prone to errors, leading to delays in lead follow-up and potential loss of valuable opportunities.

Without automation, sales and marketing teams spend countless hours transferring data from VAPI call logs to GoHighLevel. This not only reduces productivity but also increases the risk of inaccurate contact information, hindering effective communication and personalized engagement.

The Solution

The solution is an n8n workflow that automatically creates GoHighLevel contacts from VAPI calls, using Supabase as a data source. This workflow listens for webhook triggers from VAPI, retrieves relevant client data from Supabase, and creates new contacts in GoHighLevel with all necessary information.

This tech stack was chosen for its robust integration capabilities and scalability. n8n's flexibility allows for seamless connection between VAPI, Supabase, and GoHighLevel. Supabase provides a reliable database for storing and managing client data, while GoHighLevel offers a powerful CRM platform for nurturing leads and managing customer relationships.

How It Works — Streamlining Contact Creation

This workflow automates the process of creating contacts in GoHighLevel from VAPI calls, ensuring no lead is missed and all contact information is accurate.

  1. VAPI Call Trigger: A new call is made via VAPI, triggering a webhook event.
  2. n8n Webhook: n8n receives the webhook trigger from VAPI, initiating the workflow.
  3. Extract Call Data: n8n extracts relevant data from the webhook payload, such as phone number and call duration.
  4. Supabase Lookup: n8n queries Supabase to fetch client details based on the phone number.
  5. Data Validation: n8n validates the data retrieved from Supabase to ensure accuracy.
  6. GoHighLevel Contact Creation: n8n creates a new contact in GoHighLevel using the validated data.
  7. Contact Enrichment: Additional information from the VAPI call is added to the GoHighLevel contact.
  8. CRM Update: The GoHighLevel CRM is updated with the new contact information, ready for follow-up.

💡 Real-Time Contact Creation: New contacts are created in GoHighLevel the moment a VAPI call ends, ensuring immediate lead follow-up and maximizing conversion opportunities.
