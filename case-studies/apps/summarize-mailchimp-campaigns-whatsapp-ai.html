How It Works — Automated Campaign Analysis and Reporting

The workflow automates the process of analyzing Mailchimp campaigns and delivering insights via WhatsApp, saving marketers time and improving decision-making.

  1. Extract Campaign Data: The workflow retrieves data from Mailchimp campaigns, including metrics like open rates, click-through rates, and conversions.
  2. Clean and Transform Data: The extracted data is cleaned and transformed into a format suitable for AI analysis.
  3. Summarize with AI: OpenAI is used to summarize the campaign performance, highlighting key trends and insights.
  4. Suggest Improvements: The AI also suggests improvements for future campaigns based on the analysis.
  5. Craft Optimized Email Elements: The workflow generates optimized email subject lines and body copy based on the AI's suggestions.
  6. Send WhatsApp Update: A WhatsApp message is sent to stakeholders with a summary of the campaign performance and suggested improvements.
  7. Store Data in Google Sheets: The campaign data and AI-generated insights are stored in a Google Sheet for future reference and analysis.
  8. Schedule Regular Reports: The workflow is scheduled to run regularly, providing stakeholders with automated campaign reports.

💡 Actionable Insights: By automating the analysis and reporting process, marketers can quickly identify areas for improvement and make data-driven decisions to optimize their campaigns.
