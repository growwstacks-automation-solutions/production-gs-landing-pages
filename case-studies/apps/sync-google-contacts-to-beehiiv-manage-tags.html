The Problem

Many businesses rely on Google Contacts to manage their customer and prospect information. However, keeping this data synchronized with email marketing platforms like beehiiv can be a manual and time-consuming process. This often leads to inaccurate subscriber lists and missed opportunities for targeted email campaigns.

Without an automated solution, marketers and business owners spend countless hours manually updating subscriber information in beehiiv, ensuring that new contacts are added and existing contacts are properly tagged. This lack of real-time synchronization results in outdated data, inefficient workflows, and reduced engagement rates.

The Solution

To address these challenges, we developed an automated solution that seamlessly synchronizes new or updated Google Contacts with beehiiv. This system automatically adds new contacts as subscribers and manages subscriber tags in beehiiv based on Google Contacts group membership, ensuring that the beehiiv audience is always up-to-date and properly segmented.

This solution leverages Make.com to create a robust and reliable integration between Google Contacts and beehiiv. Make.com was chosen for its flexibility, scalability, and ability to handle complex data transformations, making it the ideal platform for automating this critical contact management process.

How It Works — Real-time Contact Synchronization

This system monitors Google Contacts for new or updated entries and automatically reflects those changes in beehiiv, ensuring your subscriber list is always current.

  1. Monitor Google Contacts: The system continuously monitors Google Contacts for any new or updated contact information.
  2. Retrieve Contact Details: When a new or updated contact is detected, the system retrieves all relevant details, including name, email, phone number, and group memberships.
  3. Check for Existing Subscriber: The system checks if the contact already exists as a subscriber in beehiiv using the email address as the unique identifier.
  4. Add New Subscriber: If the contact does not exist in beehiiv, the system adds them as a new subscriber with the retrieved contact details.
  5. Update Existing Subscriber: If the contact already exists in beehiiv, the system updates their information with the latest details from Google Contacts.
  6. Manage Subscriber Tags: The system analyzes the Google Contacts group memberships and updates the corresponding subscriber tags in beehiiv.
  7. Remove Obsolete Tags: If a contact is removed from a group in Google Contacts, the system removes the corresponding tag from the subscriber in beehiiv.
  8. Log Activity: The system logs all synchronization activities, including new subscriber additions, updates, and tag management actions, for auditing and troubleshooting purposes.

💡 Data Consistency: By automating the synchronization process, you can ensure that your contact information is consistent across all platforms, reducing the risk of errors and improving data quality.
