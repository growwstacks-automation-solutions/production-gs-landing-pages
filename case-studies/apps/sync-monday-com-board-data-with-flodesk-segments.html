The Problem

Many marketing teams rely on Monday.com for project and customer data management and Flodesk for email marketing. However, manually transferring data between these platforms is time-consuming and prone to errors. This leads to outdated email lists and ineffective segmentation, resulting in lower engagement and conversion rates.

Without an automated solution, marketers spend countless hours copying and pasting data, which diverts their attention from strategic activities. The lack of real-time data synchronization also means that email campaigns are often sent to the wrong audience segments, diminishing the impact of marketing efforts and potentially damaging customer relationships.

The Solution

We built an n8n workflow that automatically synchronizes user data from Monday.com boards to Flodesk segments. This ensures that email lists are always current and accurate, enabling marketers to send highly targeted campaigns. The workflow eliminates manual data entry, saving significant time and reducing the risk of errors.

n8n was chosen for its flexibility, scalability, and ability to integrate seamlessly with both Monday.com and Flodesk. Its no-code interface allows for easy customization and maintenance, making it an ideal solution for marketing teams without extensive technical expertise. The result is a streamlined process that enhances email marketing effectiveness and improves customer engagement.

How It Works — Real-Time Data Synchronization

This n8n workflow monitors your Monday.com boards for new or updated user information and automatically updates your Flodesk segments, ensuring your email marketing is always on point.

  1. Trigger Event: The workflow is triggered when a new item (user) is added or an existing item is updated in a specified Monday.com board.
  2. Fetch User Data: n8n retrieves the relevant user data from the Monday.com board, including name, email, and any custom fields.
  3. Data Transformation: The retrieved data is transformed into a format compatible with Flodesk's API.
  4. Check for Existing User: The workflow checks if the user already exists in Flodesk using the email address as a unique identifier.
  5. Update or Create User: If the user exists, their information is updated in Flodesk. If the user is new, a new subscriber is created.
  6. Add to Segment: The user is added to the appropriate Flodesk segment based on the data from the Monday.com board.
  7. Error Handling: The workflow includes error handling to manage any issues that may arise during the data transfer process.
  8. Logging: Detailed logs are maintained to track the data synchronization process and identify any potential problems.

💡 Data Integrity: By automating the data transfer process, the risk of human error is significantly reduced, ensuring that your Flodesk segments are always accurate and up-to-date.
