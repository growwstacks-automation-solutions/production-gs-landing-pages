The Problem

Retail businesses using REX Retail Express often struggle with keeping their customer data synchronized with their CRM, ActiveCampaign. Manually transferring customer information from REX to ActiveCampaign is time-consuming and prone to errors. This leads to inaccurate contact details, missed marketing opportunities, and inefficient customer relationship management.

Without an automated system, retailers face the challenge of maintaining up-to-date customer lists, segmenting customers for targeted campaigns, and ensuring that their marketing efforts are based on the most current data. This can result in poorly targeted marketing, reduced customer engagement, and ultimately, lost revenue.

How It Works — Automated Customer Data Synchronization

This workflow automates the synchronization of customer data from REX Retail Express to ActiveCampaign, ensuring accurate and up-to-date contact information for marketing and sales activities.

  1. New Order Trigger: The workflow is triggered when a new order is placed in REX Retail Express.
  2. Fetch Order Details: The workflow retrieves the details of the new order, including customer information such as name, email, and phone number.
  3. Format Phone Number: The customer's phone number is formatted to ensure consistency and accuracy in ActiveCampaign.
  4. Check for Existing Contact: The workflow searches ActiveCampaign for an existing contact with the same email address.
  5. Update or Create Contact: If a contact exists, it is updated with the new order information. If not, a new contact is created.
  6. Add to Marketing List: The contact is added to a specific marketing list in ActiveCampaign based on the order details.
  7. Log Activity: The workflow logs the activity, including the order details and contact information, for auditing and reporting purposes.

💡 Data Accuracy: By automating the data synchronization process, the workflow ensures that customer information in ActiveCampaign is always accurate and up-to-date, reducing the risk of errors and improving the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
