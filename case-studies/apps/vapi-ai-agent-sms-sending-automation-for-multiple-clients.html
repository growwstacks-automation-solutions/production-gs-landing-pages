The Problem

Sales teams using AI agents for lead generation often face the challenge of efficiently following up with leads. Manually sending SMS messages after each AI agent call is time-consuming and prone to errors. This manual process limits the number of leads that can be contacted daily, resulting in missed opportunities and lower conversion rates.

Without an automated system, sales representatives struggle to personalize messages at scale, leading to generic interactions that fail to resonate with potential clients. The lack of a streamlined process also makes it difficult to track and measure the effectiveness of follow-up efforts, hindering optimization and improvement.

The Solution

The solution is an automated SMS sending workflow that integrates VAPI AI agent calls with GoHighLevel (GHL) CRM. This system automatically routes SMS messages based on the assistant ID to identify the client, finds the contact in their CRM, and sends a personalized SMS with either a booking or trial link. This automation ensures timely and relevant follow-ups, improving lead engagement and conversion rates.

By using n8n as the primary automation platform, the workflow leverages its flexibility and integration capabilities to connect VAPI and GHL seamlessly. This tech stack enables sales teams to automate their follow-up process, personalize messages at scale, and track the effectiveness of their efforts, leading to increased efficiency and revenue.

How It Works — Automated SMS Follow-Up Process

This automation streamlines the process of sending personalized SMS messages after VAPI AI agent calls, ensuring timely and relevant follow-ups.

  1. VAPI AI Agent Call: The process begins when a VAPI AI agent completes a call with a lead.
  2. n8n Workflow Trigger: The VAPI call triggers the n8n workflow, sending the call data to n8n.
  3. Identify Client: The workflow uses the assistant ID from the call data to identify the client associated with the call.
  4. Find Contact in GHL: The workflow searches for the contact in the client's GoHighLevel CRM using the lead's phone number or email.
  5. Personalize SMS: The workflow personalizes the SMS message with the lead's name and relevant information from the call.
  6. Send SMS: The workflow sends the personalized SMS message to the lead via GoHighLevel.
  7. Include Booking/Trial Link: The SMS includes a booking or trial link, encouraging the lead to take the next step.

💡 Personalized Follow-Up: By automating the SMS sending process and personalizing messages, sales teams can improve lead engagement and conversion rates.
