What is Cognito Forms?

Founded in 2013 by a group of consultants, Cognito Forms is a form-building tool designed to help SMBs make the most out of online forms. Boasting a 4.6/5 average rating on G2, it stands out from other form builders by its ease of use and advanced features.

Top Cognito Forms features

When it comes to digital forms, users have come to expect certain capabilities that Cognito Forms delivers exceptionally well:

  • Versatility and ease of design with a drag-and-drop interface
  • Seamless embeddability into websites and applications
  • User-friendly form export and data management options
  • Extensive native and third-party integration capabilities

7 Cognito Forms integrations to up your productivity game

In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore seven powerful integrations that can transform how you use Cognito Forms in your business operations. Each integration solves specific workflow challenges and can be implemented using Make's visual automation platform.

1) Improve efficiency by gathering answers in a single place

Digital forms generate substantial amounts of data that can quickly become overwhelming without proper organization. The Cognito Forms to Google Sheets integration solves this by automatically funneling every form submission into a structured spreadsheet.

This integration provides several key benefits:

  • Centralized data storage eliminates scattered responses
  • Real-time updates ensure you're always working with current information
  • Built-in data validation reduces errors from manual entry
Sample automation flow for Cognito Forms to Google Sheets integration

2) Sync your forms with your CRM to automate data entry

Sales teams waste countless hours manually transferring lead information from forms into their CRM systems. By connecting Cognito Forms directly to your CRM (like HubSpot), you can eliminate this tedious process entirely.

Key advantages of CRM integration include:

  • Instant lead creation in your sales pipeline
  • Automatic assignment based on form responses
  • Seamless contact record updates without duplicate entries

Pro tip: Set up lead scoring rules in your CRM that trigger automatically based on form responses to prioritize your hottest leads immediately.

Automated lead creation from Cognito Forms to HubSpot CRM

3) Make tasks out of form entries to stay on top of your to-do list

For teams using Trello to manage projects, converting form submissions into actionable tasks can dramatically improve workflow efficiency. This integration automatically creates Trello cards for each form entry, complete with all relevant details.

Implementation benefits include:

  • Reduced manual task creation for project managers
  • Automatic categorization based on form responses
  • Visual tracking of form-related tasks across your workflow
Form submissions automatically converted to Trello cards

4) Get notified every time someone fills your form to never miss a beat

In fast-moving business environments, timely response to inquiries can make the difference between winning and losing opportunities. Integrating Cognito Forms with SMS notification services ensures your team knows immediately when important forms are submitted.

This solution provides:

  • Instant alerts for time-sensitive submissions
  • Flexible notification routing based on form content
  • Mobile accessibility for teams on the go
SMS alert system triggered by form submissions

5) Automatically add webinar users from form entries to ensure no one is left behind

Webinar platforms like WebinarJam require careful attendee management to maximize participation rates. Automating the registration process through Cognito Forms integration eliminates manual work while improving attendee experience.

Key features of this integration:

  • Instant enrollment upon form submission
  • Automatic reminder email scheduling
  • Seamless calendar invite delivery
Automated webinar registration workflow

6) Use form entry data to improve the quality of customer support

Customer support teams can leverage Cognito Forms integrations to streamline ticket creation and routing. Connecting forms to helpdesk systems like Help Scout ensures inquiries are properly categorized and assigned without manual intervention.

Benefits for support operations include:

  • Automatic ticket creation from form submissions
  • Smart routing based on issue type
  • Reduced response times through pre-qualification
Automated support ticket creation from form entries

7) Automatically verify email addresses of respondents and add them to MailerLite

Building clean email lists is crucial for effective marketing campaigns. The Cognito Forms to MailerLite integration with email verification ensures your lists remain high-quality by validating addresses before adding them to your subscriber base.

This solution offers:

  • Automatic email validation to reduce bounce rates
  • Segmentation based on form responses
  • Compliance with email marketing regulations
Email validation and list management automation
