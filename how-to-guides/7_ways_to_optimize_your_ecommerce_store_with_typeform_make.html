Why Typeform? Features, pricing, integrations

Online forms have been instrumental in helping businesses organize data efficiently, much like email has been throughout internet history. They play a crucial role in ecommerce by assisting with lead generation, client support, and customer onboarding schemes.

Typeform stands out among form-building tools for three key reasons: it's a market leader in online forms, offers a novel approach to form building, and integrates seamlessly with other apps through Make. Its user-friendly interface and advanced features make it particularly valuable for ecommerce businesses.

Typeform's clean interface makes form creation intuitive

Among Typeform's most notable features are its conditional logic capabilities (called "Logic Jumps"), extensive customization options, and ability to integrate payment processing platforms like Stripe directly into forms. These features enable businesses to create sophisticated, interactive experiences for their customers.

Pro tip: Typeform's pricing plans range from $35/mo to $70/mo, making it competitive with other leading form builders while offering superior user experience.

7 ways to use Typeform for ecommerce optimization

The power of online forms is often underestimated in ecommerce. Below are seven actionable strategies you can implement immediately to optimize your online store using Typeform.

1. Get creative with email marketing campaigns

Typeform transforms traditional email marketing by providing interactive, transactional experiences. Unlike static emails, forms create purposeful interactions driven by clear objectives and facilitated by smooth user experiences.

For example, a menswear brand could embed a Typeform in an email offering a discount in exchange for feedback about clothing styles. This creates a win-win scenario: customers get value (discounts) while businesses gain valuable insights.

Example of transactional email using Typeform

2. Make it easy for customers to provide feedback

Feedback is essential for ecommerce improvement, but collecting it efficiently can be challenging. Typeform helps create both passive feedback loops (like contact pages) and active ones (triggered after purchases).

An effective strategy is automating "Thank you" emails with embedded Typeform surveys after customer registrations. This ensures every customer has an opportunity to provide feedback while the experience is fresh in their minds.

Customer feedback drives business improvement

3. Build buyer personas in record time

Buyer personas are fictional representations of your ideal customers based on real data. Typeform's survey templates help gather this data efficiently through site deployments or email campaigns.

When creating buyer persona surveys, remember the transactional nature of these interactions. Offer incentives for participation while avoiding overly personal questions that might deter responses.

Developing accurate buyer personas with Typeform

4. Bridge the gap between digital and physical sales

Typeform's order forms provide a simple solution for physical stores transitioning to online sales. Clear landing pages with prominent order form buttons create a seamless shopping experience.

The process can be further streamlined by funneling form entries to Google Sheets or email inboxes, accelerating order fulfillment. This approach proved invaluable for many businesses during the pandemic.

Connecting physical stores to digital sales channels

5. Let users customize their orders

Modern consumers expect personalized shopping experiences. Typeform order forms enable extensive customization beyond basic options like size and color.

Businesses can offer customization for quantity, packaging, shipping preferences, and warranties. This level of personalization helps meet individual consumer needs while differentiating your brand.

Customizable order forms enhance customer experience

6. Funnel data to where it belongs

Keeping form responses organized is a common challenge. Typeform integrates with tools like Google Sheets, Airtable, and CRMs to automate data organization.

Setting up automatic notifications ensures timely action on new entries. For example, order form submissions can trigger inventory updates, shipping scheduling, and other fulfillment tasks.

Automating data organization prevents confusion

7. Integrate payments into your Typeform forms

Typeform's verified Stripe partnership allows embedding payment blocks directly into order forms. This creates a seamless checkout experience without redirecting customers to external payment pages.

The Stripe integration follows standard processing fees (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction) with no monthly fees, making it accessible for businesses of all sizes.

Seamless payment processing within Typeform
