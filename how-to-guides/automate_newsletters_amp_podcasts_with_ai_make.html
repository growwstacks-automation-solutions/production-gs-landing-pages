The Problem

Many businesses struggle with creating consistent content like newsletters and podcasts due to the time-consuming manual processes involved. A real estate company faced this exact challenge - spending over 8 hours monthly to compile their newsletter, often skipping months when resources were tight.

The manual workflow involved finding relevant news articles, writing summaries, formatting content, and distributing through email platforms. They also wanted to launch a podcast but lacked the production bandwidth. This created gaps in customer communication and missed engagement opportunities.

The Tools

Make.com's automation platform combined with AI tools creates a powerful solution for content automation. Here's the complete toolkit used:

Content Curation

Feedly pulls in news articles via RSS feeds based on specified topics. The AI then evaluates each article for relevance and quality before including it in the content pipeline.

AI Processing

OpenAI's models handle multiple tasks: classifying articles as local/national, checking geographic relevance, scoring impact (1-5), writing summaries, and generating cohesive introductions in specific writing styles.

Content Storage

Google Sheets serves as the database for approved articles, storing titles, sources, dates, locations, and impact ratings. Make's Data Store is a flexible alternative for more complex implementations.

The newsletter automation workflow showing AI classification steps

Distribution

Mailchimp delivers the final newsletter content to subscribers using predefined templates. The system supports alternatives like Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign, or even direct email sending through Gmail.

Audio Production

ElevenLabs converts written summaries into natural-sounding voiceovers for podcasts. The AI handles text-to-speech conversion with adjustable tone, pacing, and emphasis.

The Setup

The automated system handles everything from content collection to distribution with minimal human intervention. Here's how the complete workflow operates:

Newsletter Automation

The process begins with Feedly monitoring specified RSS feeds for new articles. Each article undergoes AI evaluation through multiple steps:

First, OpenAI classifies content as local or national using custom prompts. Local articles get additional geographic relevance checks. A scoring system (1-5) then evaluates impact, with only articles scoring 3+ proceeding to the next stage.

Pro tip: When setting up your scoring system, calibrate it with sample articles to ensure your threshold captures the right quality level for your audience.

Approved articles move to a Google Sheet database. On a monthly schedule, Make aggregates the top-rated articles, formats them, then passes them to OpenAI for summarization. The AI generates both individual article summaries and an overall newsletter introduction matching specified writing styles.

The podcast automation workflow showing AI summarization steps

Podcast Automation

The podcast workflow shares the initial content collection and evaluation steps with the newsletter. After article selection, the system:

Identifies the top 3 most impactful articles using AI ranking. Generates two-sentence audio-friendly summaries styled for business listeners. Converts these summaries to voice recordings using ElevenLabs' text-to-speech. Publishes the compiled audio through Spreaker's podcast hosting platform.

Pro tip: For podcast narration, experiment with different AI voice profiles to find one that best matches your brand personality and content tone.
