Why Automate Xero with Make

Xero is powerful cloud accounting software, but manual data entry still consumes valuable time for small business owners and accounting teams. Make bridges this gap by connecting Xero to your other business apps and automating repetitive accounting workflows.

With Make's visual automation builder, you can create workflows that handle tasks like invoice generation, expense tracking, purchase order creation, and contact management automatically. This eliminates tedious manual work while reducing the risk of human error in your financial records.

Pro tip: Start by automating your most repetitive Xero tasks - these typically offer the highest time savings with the lowest implementation complexity.

Available Xero Modules

Make's Xero integration includes multiple trigger and action modules that cover core accounting functions. Triggers watch for specific events in Xero, while actions perform operations in your Xero account.

Trigger Modules

Triggers initiate workflows when specific events occur in Xero. The available triggers include monitoring for new or updated receipts, credit notes, bank transfers, purchase orders, and invoices. Each trigger can start a customized workflow tailored to your accounting processes.

Action Modules

Action modules perform operations in Xero. You can create receipts, invoices, contacts, and purchase orders. Additional actions let you update existing records, retrieve specific documents, delete contact groups, and upload files to attach to Xero records.

The Xero module interface in Make showing available triggers and actions

Connecting Xero to Make

Setting up the Xero-Make connection is straightforward. When you add your first Xero module to a scenario, Make guides you through authorizing access to your Xero account. This secure connection uses Xero's official API and follows their authentication protocols.

The authorization process redirects you to Xero's website where you can review and approve the permissions Make requests. After granting access, you're returned to your scenario where you can begin building automation workflows between Xero and your other connected apps.
