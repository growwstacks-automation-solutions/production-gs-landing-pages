Capsule CRM Triggers and Actions

Capsule CRM's integration with Make provides a comprehensive set of modules that enable thousands of potential automation combinations. The platform offers a variety of triggers that watch for specific events in your CRM system, allowing you to initiate workflows precisely when needed.

The trigger options include monitoring for new opportunities, tasks, cases, and parties. These serve as starting points for your automation scenarios, ensuring your workflows begin exactly when relevant changes occur in your CRM data.

Pro tip: Use the "Watch for new opportunities" trigger to automatically notify your sales team whenever high-value leads enter your pipeline, ensuring prompt follow-up.

For manipulating data within Capsule CRM, Make provides numerous action modules. These allow you to create, delete, update, and show records across various CRM entities including:

  • Entries and opportunities
  • Tracks and cases
  • Tags and tasks
  • Parties and custom fields
  • Milestones and more

Search modules extend the functionality further, letting you retrieve specific sets of data based on parameters you define. This enables sophisticated filtering and data handling within your automated workflows.

Simple Automation Examples

Make simplifies numerous routine CRM tasks through straightforward integrations. These basic automations can save hours of manual work while reducing human error in data entry and task management.

Common time-saving examples include automatically adding new Google Contacts to Capsule CRM as people, creating Todoist tasks from CRM activities, or syncing Asana tasks with your CRM system. These integrations bridge productivity tools with your customer data.

Pro tip: Start with simple one-to-one integrations to understand the automation flow before building more complex multi-app scenarios.

The basic use cases demonstrate Make's ability to connect Capsule CRM with other everyday business tools:

  • Sync CRM tasks with Todoist for personal task management
  • Add form submissions from Wufoo as new contacts
  • Create CRM records from Asana task completions
  • Update contact records from Google Contacts changes

Advanced Workflow Scenarios

Where Make truly shines is in handling complex, multi-step workflows that go beyond simple point-to-point integrations. These advanced scenarios often involve data transformation, conditional logic, and parallel processing.

One powerful example is automatically adding new Eventbrite attendees to Capsule CRM while preventing duplicate records. This involves checking existing contacts before creating new ones, ensuring your CRM stays clean and organized.

This approach ensures your team gets both immediate awareness and comprehensive information without manual effort. The router enables parallel processing of different notification types from a single trigger event.
