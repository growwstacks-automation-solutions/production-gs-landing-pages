What This Workflow Does

This powerful automation streamlines the creation of SEO-optimized product titles, descriptions, and tags for your Shopify store. Instead of manually crafting compelling copy for each new product, this workflow leverages the power of AI to do it for you. It watches for new product additions, sends the product image and details to an AI content extractor, and then automatically updates your Shopify listings with high-quality, search-engine-friendly text.

The core problem this workflow solves is the time-consuming and often inconsistent process of product copywriting. For e-commerce businesses, especially those with large or frequently updated catalogs, generating unique and effective product descriptions can be a major bottleneck. How It Works

This automation operates in a seamless, four-step process to transform your product listing workflow:

1. Watch for New Products in Shopify

The workflow begins by constantly monitoring your Shopify store for any new products that are added. As soon as a new item is detected, Make captures all its available details, including the product image URL, to prepare for the next steps.

2. Describe the Image with AI

Next, the product image URL and any relevant product data are sent to an AI Content Extractor. Using a custom prompt, the AI is instructed to generate a structured JSON output containing an engaging product title, a detailed description (formatted with HTML tags), and a list of SEO-friendly tags. This ensures the generated content is not only creative but also ready for direct use.

The Shopify "Watch products" module, initiating the automation when a new product is added.

3. Parse the AI Response

The AI's output, which is in JSON format, is then processed by a JSON parsing module. This step extracts the generated title, description, and tags into separate, usable data fields. This makes it easy to map the content to the correct fields in your Shopify product editor.

Configuring the AI Content Extractor with a custom prompt to generate product details.

4. Update the Product in Shopify

Finally, the extracted and parsed data is used to update the corresponding product in Shopify. The new, AI-generated title, HTML-formatted description, and SEO tags are automatically applied, ensuring your product listing is optimized and ready for customers without any manual intervention.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your SEO product copy automation up and running:

  1. Create a New Scenario in Make: Log in to your Make.com account and create a new scenario.
  2. Add Shopify "Watch Products" Module: Search for the Shopify app and select the "Watch products" module. Connect your Shopify account. This module will trigger the scenario whenever a new product is added to your store.
  3. Add Make AI Content Extractor "Describe an Image" Module: Add the Make AI app and choose the "Describe an image" module. Map the product image URL from the Shopify module. In the prompt field, use a detailed instruction to guide the AI in generating a JSON output for the product title, description (with HTML tags), and tags.
  4. Add JSON "Parse JSON" Module: Connect a JSON app and select the "Parse JSON" module. Map the AI's JSON output from the previous step to this module to extract the individual data fields.
  5. Add Shopify "Update a Product" Module: Finally, add another Shopify module and select "Update a product." Map the parsed title, description, and tags from the JSON module to the corresponding fields in Shopify.
  6. Test and Activate: Run the scenario once with a test product to ensure everything is working correctly. Once confirmed, activate your scenario to automate product copy generation for all new products.
The final step: updating the Shopify product with the AI-generated title, description, and tags.
