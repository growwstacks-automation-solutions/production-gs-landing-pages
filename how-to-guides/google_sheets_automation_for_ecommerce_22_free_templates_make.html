1. Save New Paid Shopify Orders to a Google Sheets Spreadsheet

A highly effective solution to combat this problem is to embed a Typeform on your e-commerce website. Typeforms are not only highly customizable, allowing you to capture any specific information you require from your customers, but they also offer enhanced spam protection. Crucially, Make.com can automatically funnel all submissions from these Typeforms directly into a Google Sheets spreadsheet for easy bulk analysis.
