So, what's available?

Plivo is a cloud API platform and global carrier service provider for voice calls & SMS. It gives you everything needed to create voice & SMS applications to reach customers across over 200 countries. With Make, you can link these communication capabilities with other apps to automate processes and streamline workflows.

The Plivo integration in Make offers several modules that enable countless automation possibilities:

Triggers

These modules activate your scenarios based on incoming communications:

  • SMS received
  • SMS received (instant)
  • New recording added
  • Call received
  • Call received (instant)

Actions

These modules perform outbound communication tasks:

  • Send SMS
  • Make a call and speak text
  • Make a call and play audio
  • Respond to an incoming call
Plivo's platform interface showing SMS and voice call management features

What can I do with these?

With these modules, you can automate numerous communication tasks that would otherwise require manual intervention. The integration allows you to create sophisticated workflows that respond to customer interactions in real-time while connecting with your existing business systems.

Some practical applications include:

  • Send SMS reminders & alerts to customers
  • Forward incoming calls to personal phone numbers
  • Receive SMS notifications on your phone
  • Run automated SMS polls and surveys
  • Trigger voice call notifications for urgent alerts

Pro tip: When setting up SMS automations, always include opt-out instructions to comply with telecom regulations. Make it easy for recipients to stop messages if they choose.

Automating an SMS Poll

To demonstrate the power of this integration, let's walk through setting up an automated SMS poll that captures responses and sends acknowledgments.

Step 1 — Run a live SMS poll

Running a live SMS poll allows you to engage customers in a conversational way. You can conduct quick surveys, run contests, or gather feedback through simple text responses. Plivo handles the SMS delivery while Make connects the responses to your data systems.

The first part involves setting up a scenario between Plivo and Google Sheets to receive and record poll answers automatically. When someone texts a response to your poll question, Make captures it and logs it in a spreadsheet row along with the sender's number and timestamp.

Scenario structure for capturing SMS poll responses in Google Sheets

Step 2 — Send SMS to customers

After setting up the basic response collection, we can enhance the experience by automatically acknowledging each submission. This builds goodwill and confirms their response was received.

In your Google Sheet, add a "Response" column with a standard reply message like "Thank you for your response!" Then modify your Make scenario to retrieve this message and send it back to each respondent.

Spreadsheet structure for managing SMS poll responses and acknowledgments

The enhanced scenario uses Google Sheets' "Get a cell" module to fetch your standard response, then the Plivo "Send SMS" module to deliver it. By aggregating these modules, you ensure each thank-you message goes to the correct respondent.

Configuration for retrieving acknowledgment messages from spreadsheet

Pro tip: For better engagement, personalize responses using merge tags that insert the respondent's name or specific answer into your reply message when possible.

This complete workflow demonstrates how Make and Plivo together can transform simple SMS capabilities into intelligent, automated communication systems that save time while improving customer experiences.
