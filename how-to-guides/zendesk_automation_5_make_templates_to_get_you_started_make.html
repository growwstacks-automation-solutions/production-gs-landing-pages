Flattening the learning curve

Zendesk's native automation system presents several challenges that can frustrate users. The documentation, while thorough, is often lengthy and difficult to navigate when troubleshooting issues. The user interface relies heavily on fields and dropdowns, making it hard to visualize how data flows between different automation steps.

Perhaps most frustrating is the lack of robust error handling. When an automation fails, you're left digging through documentation trying to diagnose what went wrong. This creates unnecessary friction in what should be a time-saving feature.

Pro tip: Before diving into complex Zendesk automations, start with simple templates to understand how triggers and actions work together. This builds confidence before tackling more advanced workflows.

Make templates solve these pain points by providing pre-built, tested automation solutions that work immediately. They serve as both practical tools and educational resources, demonstrating how different app modules interact. Each template is fully customizable, allowing you to modify it as you become more comfortable with automation concepts.

1. Add new Zendesk tickets to Google Sheets

This integration automatically sends incoming Zendesk tickets and their complete information to a Google Sheets spreadsheet. It's particularly valuable for onboarding new customer service representatives, allowing managers to evaluate their performance before granting full Zendesk access.

At $5/month per user, Zendesk costs can add up quickly for growing support teams. This template provides a cost-effective way to train new hires while maintaining oversight of their ticket handling capabilities.

2. Create Zendesk tickets from Google Forms responses

Google Forms serve countless business purposes from customer registration to satisfaction surveys. This template transforms form responses into fully-formed Zendesk tickets, allowing your support team to begin working on them immediately.

The automation preserves all form data in the ticket, ensuring no information gets lost in translation between systems. It's particularly useful for businesses that collect customer feedback or service requests through forms but want to manage the actual support process in Zendesk.

3. Add new Zendesk users to a MailChimp list

Building high-quality lead lists is essential but often neglected due to time constraints. This template automatically adds new Zendesk contacts to your MailChimp lists, ensuring your marketing communications reach all relevant customers.

Given the high cost of customer acquisition, this automation provides excellent ROI by maximizing the value of existing customer relationships. The template can be configured to segment contacts based on ticket type, product interest, or other Zendesk fields.

Pro tip: Combine this with other marketing automations to create personalized nurture sequences based on support ticket history.

4. Create Zendesk tickets from new RingCentral messages

This integration, affectionately called "The Dragnet," creates Zendesk tickets for every customer communication received through RingCentral - including voice calls, SMS, MMS, and even fax messages. It ensures no customer inquiry falls through the cracks, regardless of which channel they use to contact you.

While RingCentral excels at message collection, Zendesk provides superior tools for tracking and resolving customer issues. This template bridges the gap, creating a unified customer service experience across all communication channels.

5. Create a new ticket in Zendesk from new Shopify orders

Exceptional customer service begins before problems arise. This template automatically creates Zendesk tickets from Shopify orders, allowing proactive customer outreach when issues are detected.

For example, if you discover a product defect after shipping numerous orders, this automation helps you identify affected customers and reach out with solutions before they contact you. This proactive approach can significantly reduce support volume while improving customer satisfaction.
