FAQ

Common Questions from Healthcare Practices

Are your automations HIPAA compliant? ▾
We design all healthcare automations using your existing HIPAA-compliant systems as the data backbone — we do not route PHI through non-compliant third-party services. We also include BAA (Business Associate Agreement) considerations in our project scoping process. That said, your compliance officer should review all workflows before go-live, which is standard practice.
Will this integrate with our EHR (Epic, athenahealth, Kareo)? ▾
Yes. We build integrations for the most common EHR platforms including athenahealth, Kareo, AdvancedMD, eClinicalWorks, and others. For systems with limited APIs, we build smart workarounds using available export/import pathways and webhook-based triggers.
How quickly can automations be live? ▾
Most healthcare automation projects go live within 3–6 weeks. We start with the highest-impact workflow first (typically scheduling or no-show reduction), then layer in additional automations after you see the initial results.
What's the typical investment for a medical practice? ▾
Healthcare automation projects typically range from $6,000–$30,000 depending on EHR complexity and scope, with monthly support retainers of $500–$2,500. Most practices see full payback within 60–90 days from reduced no-shows and billing efficiency alone.
Can the Voice AI handle multilingual patients? ▾
Yes. Our Voice AI solutions support Spanish and other languages natively. For practices serving multilingual communities, we can configure language detection and automatic routing to the appropriate language experience.
