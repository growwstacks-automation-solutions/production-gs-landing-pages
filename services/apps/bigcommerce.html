Our Approach

3 Ways We Automate BigCommerce

We choose the right integration method based on your data destinations, business rules, and the complexity of your e-commerce operations.

01

BigCommerce API

BigCommerce has well-documented Storefront and Management APIs covering carts, checkouts, orders, products, customers, inventory, and catalogues. We use these APIs directly — via Python, JavaScript scripts, or Make.com's HTTP module — to build custom workflows that go beyond what native integrations can handle, enabling precise control over every aspect of your store data.

Storefront API Management API Cart & Checkout Orders Products & Inventory
02

Make.com Integration

Make.com has a native BigCommerce module with triggers for new carts, abandoned carts, order events, and customer creation — plus actions for getting carts, creating and updating cart items, managing inventory, and making direct API calls. We build multi-step Make.com scenarios that connect BigCommerce to Airtable, WooCommerce, Slack, Google Drive, and any other platform your business uses.

Make.com Cart Triggers Order Events Abandoned Cart Inventory Sync
03

Zapier & n8n Integration

Zapier's BigCommerce module supports triggers for abandoned carts, new carts, customer created, and order events — plus actions for creating products and making API calls — ideal for teams already using Zapier across their stack. For teams needing self-hosted, privacy-first automation with no per-operation costs, n8n connects to BigCommerce via HTTP request nodes and delivers the same e-commerce event handling on your own infrastructure with unlimited executions.

Use Cases

What We Build With BigCommerce

Real automations we have delivered for e-commerce brands, multi-platform retailers, and operations teams running on BigCommerce.

📦

BigCommerce Products → Airtable Sync

We list all products from BigCommerce, search Airtable for each product to check if it already exists, and then create or update the Airtable record using the Airtable API based on matching rules. This gives operations and merchandising teams a live, filterable product database in Airtable without manual exports from BigCommerce.

🔄

BigCommerce Orders → WooCommerce + Slack + Google Drive

We build cross-platform order pipelines where a new BigCommerce order triggers a Make.com scenario that creates a matching customer in WooCommerce, logs the order in Google Sheets, sends a Slack notification to the fulfilment team, and creates an order folder in Google Drive — all from a single BigCommerce order event.

🛒

Abandoned Cart Recovery Automation

We use BigCommerce's abandoned cart trigger in Make.com or Zapier to fire a recovery workflow the moment a cart is abandoned. The customer receives a personalised recovery email or SMS through a connected platform — with cart contents, total value, and a direct checkout link — significantly recovering lost revenue without any manual follow-up.

📊

Order & Sales Reporting to Google Sheets

We build scheduled Make.com scenarios that extract BigCommerce order data on a daily or weekly basis and push it into structured Google Sheets reports — including order totals, product lines, customer details, and fulfilment status. Finance and operations teams get live, filterable sales reports without logging into BigCommerce or manually exporting data.

🏷️

Inventory Management & Low Stock Alerts

We connect BigCommerce inventory data to Make.com workflows that monitor stock levels and trigger alerts when inventory falls below defined thresholds. Slack notifications are sent to the purchasing team, reorder records are created in Airtable, and purchase orders can be triggered automatically — preventing stock-outs without anyone manually watching inventory levels.

👤

New Customer → CRM & Email Marketing Sync

When a new customer is created in BigCommerce, we automatically push their details to your CRM — HubSpot, Salesforce, or Attio — and add them to the correct email marketing list in Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or ActiveCampaign. This ensures every new customer is immediately enrolled in your onboarding or nurture sequence without any manual import.
