Our Approach

3 Ways We Automate Calendly

We choose the right integration method based on your connected tools, booking volume, and the business processes that should trigger when a meeting is booked or cancelled.

01

Make.com Integration

Make.com has a comprehensive Calendly module with triggers for invite created, invite cancelled, and event cancellation — plus actions for watching events, listing attendees, scheduling meetings, and managing organisations. We use Make.com to build multi-step booking workflows that connect Calendly to Airtable, Google Docs, Close CRM, Slack, ClickUp, and any other platform your business uses — all triggered the moment a booking is made.

Invite Created Invite Cancelled List Attendees Schedule Meeting Multi-step Flows
02

Zapier & n8n Integration

Zapier's Calendly module supports invite created and invite cancelled triggers — ideal for teams connecting booking events to CRMs, notification tools, or task platforms already managed through Zapier. For teams needing self-hosted, privacy-first automation with no per-operation costs, n8n handles Calendly webhooks natively and connects booking events to any downstream platform on your own infrastructure with unlimited executions.

Zapier Triggers Invite Events n8n Self-Hosted Unlimited Executions Data Privacy
03

Calendly API Integration

The Calendly API gives full programmatic access to events, invitees, event types, scheduling links, organisation memberships, and webhook subscriptions. We use the API directly inside Make.com for operations beyond the native module — such as fetching detailed invitee data, managing scheduling links programmatically, or building enterprise-grade booking systems with custom business logic applied at every step.

REST API Events & Invitees Scheduling Links Webhooks Custom Logic

Use Cases

What We Build With Calendly

Real booking automations we have delivered for recruitment teams, sales organisations, tutoring platforms, and service businesses using Calendly.

🎤

Interview Booking → Airtable + Google Docs + Google Calendar

When a candidate books an interview via Calendly, we automatically create a record in Airtable with all invitee details, generate a Google Doc pre-filled with interview notes template for the recruiter, and add the interview to the interviewer's Google Calendar. The entire onboarding prep for a new interview happens automatically the moment the candidate books.

💼

Calendly Booking → Close CRM Lead Creation

When a Calendly invite is created, we search Close CRM for the invitee by email. If the contact does not exist we create a new lead with all booking details mapped correctly. If they do exist we update their record. This ensures every person who books a call is immediately captured as a lead in your sales CRM without any manual data entry from your team.

🔔

New Booking → Slack Notification

We build Make.com scenarios that send a formatted Slack notification to the relevant channel or team member the moment a Calendly invite is created or cancelled. The notification includes the invitee's name, email, meeting type, scheduled time, and any custom questions they answered on the booking form — giving your team instant awareness of every new booking.

📚

Tutoring Booking → Airtable + Webflow + SendGrid

When a tutor session is booked via Calendly, we find the relevant tutor record in Airtable, look up their upcoming bookings, create the booking event in Webflow CMS, and send a confirmation email via SendGrid to both the student and tutor — all in a single Make.com scenario triggered by the Calendly invite event. Complex multi-step, fully automated.

🏗️

Site Visit Booking → ClickUp Task + Google Calendar

For field service businesses booking site visits, we connect Calendly to ClickUp — automatically creating a task with the client details, visit address, and scheduled time when a booking is made. The task is assigned to the relevant team member and the visit is added to the team's Google Calendar, ensuring field teams are always briefed and prepared.

📋

Recruitment Form → Calendly Invite → Full Pipeline

We build end-to-end recruitment pipelines where a candidate submits a hiring form, the data is processed and a Calendly scheduling link is sent automatically, and when they book their interview the booking triggers Airtable record creation, Google Doc generation, interviewer calendar updates, and a Slack notification — all in one continuous automated flow.
