Our Approach

3 Ways We Automate Clio Manage

We choose the right integration method based on your firm's workflow complexity, existing tools, and the legal processes that need to be automated across your practice.

01

Clio Native Workflows

Clio Manage has a built-in workflow and automation engine suitable for smaller-scale, contained legal process automations — trigger-based task assignments, status change notifications, deadline reminders, and internal process routing. We configure these native automations to handle routine in-system tasks so your team can focus on client work rather than administrative follow-up.

Trigger-based Tasks Deadline Reminders Status Notifications Internal Routing Native Integrations
02

Make.com, Zapier & n8n

Make.com has a native Clio module with triggers for new matters, search and get operations, task management, contact handling, calendar events, and communications — connected to hundreds of external platforms. Zapier extends this to teams already managing tools through Zapier. For law firms needing self-hosted, privacy-first automation with no per-operation costs, n8n connects to Clio via the REST API on your own infrastructure with unlimited executions and full data control.

Matter Triggers Contact Management n8n Self-Hosted Data Privacy 1000+ Apps
03

Clio REST API

Clio's well-structured REST API covers every module — matters, contacts, tasks, calendars, communications, billing, documents, time entries, and relationships. We use the API directly inside Make.com or through custom Python and JavaScript scripts to build enterprise-grade legal automation workflows — including AI-assisted matter creation, lead intake automation through Clio Grow, and complex cross-platform integrations.

Matters API Contacts API Clio Grow Leads Billing & Time Python / JS

Use Cases

What We Build With Clio Manage

Real legal practice automations we have delivered for law firms, solo practitioners, and legal operations teams using Clio Manage and Clio Grow worldwide.

📁

New Matter → Evernote Notebook + Toggl Time Entry

When a new matter is created in Clio, our Make.com scenario automatically creates a corresponding Evernote notebook pre-structured for matter notes, and logs a Toggl time entry so billable hours are tracked from the moment the matter opens. The entire setup for a new matter takes seconds rather than minutes, and nothing is ever missed or forgotten by the responsible attorney.

📩

Website Lead → Clio Grow + ActiveCampaign

When a prospective client submits an enquiry form on your website, we use the Clio Grow API to create a contact in the inbox leads with their full name, email, message, and source information. Simultaneously, the lead is added to ActiveCampaign and enrolled in the correct nurture sequence — ensuring every prospect is followed up immediately without any manual data entry from the intake team.

🤖

Clio Matter → ChatGPT Summary → Email or Doc

We build AI-powered matter processing workflows where Clio matter data — case description, notes, client details, and task history — is passed to ChatGPT to generate a structured matter summary or status update. The output is automatically emailed to the client, saved to a Google Doc, or stored as a Clio note — saving attorneys significant time on routine matter communication and reporting.

📅

Clio Calendar Events → Team Notifications

We connect Clio Manage calendar events to Slack and email notifications — alerting the relevant team members when court dates, client meetings, or deadlines are scheduled or updated. We also sync Clio calendar events to Google Calendar or Outlook so attorneys always have their legal schedule reflected in their personal calendar without any manual duplication.

⚖️

Matter Relationship & Contact Management

We automate Clio's contact and matter relationship management — when a new client is created, we automatically link them to the relevant matter, set up their communication preferences, assign the responsible attorney, and create an initial task checklist. Using the Clio API, we can manage complex relationship structures between clients, matters, and opposing counsel automatically.

💼

Note-taking, Billing & Task Automation

We build comprehensive Clio automation suites covering note creation, time entry logging, task assignment, billing triggers, and document management — all connected to external tools your firm uses. When specific Clio events occur — matter status changes, document uploads, task completions — cascading automations ensure related systems are updated, teams are notified, and billing records are maintained accurately.
