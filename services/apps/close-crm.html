Our Approach

3 Ways We Automate Close CRM

We choose the right integration method based on your sales process complexity, connected tools, and the business logic that needs to be applied when leads, opportunities, and custom activities change state.

01

Close CRM Native Automations

Close CRM has a built-in workflow and pipeline builder for smaller-scale, contained automations — lead status change sequences, automatic task assignment, email follow-up triggers, and internal notifications. We configure these native automations to handle routine in-CRM actions so your sales reps focus on conversations rather than administrative follow-up within the platform.

Pipeline Rules Task Assignment Status Triggers Email Sequences Internal Alerts
02

Make.com & Zapier Integration

Make.com's native Close CRM module covers all key triggers — new lead, lead status change, smart view updates — and all key actions across leads, contacts, opportunities, tasks, and custom activities. We use Make.com and Zapier to connect Close CRM to Calendly, Slack, Airtable, WhatsApp, Facebook Lead Ads, and any other platform in your stack — with full conditional routing and business rule logic at every step.

New Lead Trigger Status Changed Smart Views Opportunities Custom Activities
03

Close CRM REST API

Close CRM's well-documented REST API covers leads, contacts, opportunities, tasks, activities, smart views, and custom fields. We use it directly inside Make.com for advanced filtering and API call operations — including fetching leads with complex query parameters, updating multiple custom fields simultaneously, or tracking custom activity events and pushing KPI data into Airtable for structured reporting and analysis.

Use Cases

What We Build With Close CRM

Real sales automation workflows we have delivered for B2B sales teams, SaaS companies, and agencies using Close CRM as their primary sales platform.

📅

Calendly Booking → Close Lead + Task Creation

When a prospect books a call via Calendly, our Make.com scenario watches the new event and automatically creates a lead in Close CRM with all booking details mapped to the correct fields, plus a task assigned to the rep to prepare for the call. The rep receives a Slack notification so they are briefed before the meeting without checking Calendly or Close separately.

🔔

Close Lead Status Change → Slack Notification

We build Make.com scenarios that watch for lead status changes in Close CRM and instantly send a formatted Slack notification to the relevant channel — including the lead name, company, new status, assigned rep, and deal value. Sales managers get real-time visibility into pipeline movement without logging into Close CRM to check manually throughout the day.

⚡

Custom Activity → Opportunity + Task + WhatsApp

Close CRM's custom activity system is extremely powerful. We build automation flows where a specific custom activity event — such as a demo completed, a proposal sent, or a trial started — triggers a Make.com scenario that creates a new opportunity at the correct pipeline stage, updates an existing task, creates a follow-up task, and sends a personalised WhatsApp message to the prospect automatically.

📊

Close CRM KPI Data → Airtable Reporting

We build Make.com scenarios using the Close CRM API to query leads with custom filter parameters — filtering by status, rep, date range, or custom fields — and push the resulting KPI data into Airtable. Airtable's flexible views and dashboard capabilities then serve as a live sales reporting layer on top of Close CRM data, updated automatically on a daily or real-time schedule.

📋

Form / Perspective → Close CRM Lead

We connect web forms, lead generation platforms, and prospecting tools to Close CRM — automatically creating or updating leads the moment a new prospect is identified. We apply deduplication logic to search for existing leads before creating new ones, set the correct pipeline stage, assign the right rep based on routing rules, and send the rep an immediate notification to follow up while the lead is warm.

🔗

Multi-Condition Path Routing Workflows

We build large-scale Close CRM automation workflows with multiple conditional branches — routing leads based on company size, industry, deal value, rep assignment, or custom field values. Different paths trigger different actions: high-value leads get immediate Slack alerts and calendar invites, mid-tier leads get email sequences, and low-value leads get tagged and routed to a nurture pipeline automatically.
