3 Ways We Automate Gmail

We choose the right integration method based on your email volume, business processes, and the complexity of logic that needs to be applied when emails arrive or are sent.

01

Make.com & Zapier Integration

Make.com's native Gmail module supports watching new emails, copying emails, creating drafts, deleting emails, managing labels, and extracting email attachments — all as triggers or actions in multi-step scenarios. We use Make.com and Zapier to build Gmail workflows that parse incoming emails, route them based on content, extract attachment files, and push data to Shopify, Google Sheets, ClickUp, Slack, or any other platform in your stack.

02

Gmail Workspace API

The Gmail Workspace API provides complete programmatic control over your Gmail account — sending emails, reading messages, managing labels, handling attachments, configuring mailbox settings, and accessing thread history. We use it directly through Python scripts, Google Apps Script, or Make.com's HTTP module to build advanced Gmail automation that no-code platforms alone cannot handle — including complex email routing, bulk operations, and mailbox management at scale.

03

ChatGPT-Powered Email Parsing

For emails that contain unstructured text — customer orders, supplier invoices, support requests, or sales enquiries — we combine Gmail's email watch trigger with ChatGPT to intelligently extract structured data from the email body. ChatGPT identifies product names, quantities, dates, customer details, and intent — and this parsed data is then used to create records in Shopify, Google Sheets, CRMs, or any downstream system automatically.

Use Cases

What We Build With Gmail

Real email automation workflows we have delivered for e-commerce businesses, operations teams, agencies, and growing companies using Gmail as their primary communication platform.

🤖

Email → ChatGPT Parser → Shopify Order

When an email arrives in Gmail, our Make.com mailhook scenario extracts the full email content and passes it to ChatGPT with a parsing prompt. ChatGPT identifies the product name, quantity, customer details, and order date from the unstructured email text and returns structured JSON. That data is then used to automatically create a customer and order in Shopify — turning inbound emails into e-commerce orders without any manual processing.

📎

Gmail Attachment → Nextcloud + ClickUp

When an email arrives with attachments matching specific criteria — such as invoices, design files, or contracts — our scenario extracts the attachment, uploads it to the correct Nextcloud folder for organised cloud storage, and creates a ClickUp task with the attachment linked and all relevant email details included. Your team always has incoming files organised and tracked without any manual saving or task creation.

📊

Gmail Text Parser → Google Sheets

We build Make.com scenarios that watch for specific incoming emails, run the email body through a text parser module to extract structured data — order numbers, amounts, names, dates — and append that data as a new row in Google Sheets. This turns recurring inbound emails such as supplier confirmations, payment notifications, or system alerts into structured, queryable database entries automatically.

🗂️

Xero Invoice → Gmail Label Management

We connect Xero to Gmail so that when an order or invoice event occurs in Xero, the corresponding Gmail email is automatically found and labelled with the correct status — Paid, Overdue, Sent, or any custom label. This keeps your Gmail inbox organised according to invoice status without manually searching for and labelling individual emails, giving finance teams a clean, categorised email view at all times.

📋

Monday.com Item Update → Conditional Gmail Send

We build workflows where a Monday.com board item update — such as a status change, a file attachment, or a column value change — triggers a Make.com scenario that retrieves the item details, evaluates conditional business rules, and sends a beautifully formatted Gmail email to the relevant recipient if conditions are met. Complex email routing logic based on CRM or project data, fully automated.

📨

Mass Mail Merge & Automated Email Campaigns

We build Gmail-powered mass mailing workflows where a list of contacts from Google Sheets, Airtable, or a CRM triggers personalised emails sent through Gmail via Make.com. Each email is personalised with the recipient's name, company, and any custom fields from the source data — creating a high-personalisation mail merge at scale without requiring a separate email marketing platform for transactional or targeted campaigns.
