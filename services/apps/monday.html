Our Approach

3 Ways We Automate Monday.com

We choose the right integration method based on your operational complexity, the external platforms you need to connect, and whether real-time data push or internal board automation is the priority.

01

Monday.com Native Automations

Monday.com's built-in automation engine handles internal board workflows — when an item status changes, update another item; when a column value changes, notify a team member; when a deadline is reached, send a reminder. We also configure Monday's native webhook module to send item data to external APIs the moment a trigger fires, enabling real-time data push to any external system directly from within Monday's own automation builder.

Status Change Triggers Cross-Board Updates Webhook Sending Deadline Reminders Custom Apps
02

Make.com & Zapier Integration

Make.com and Zapier both have native Monday.com modules covering all key triggers — item created, status changed, column updated — and all key actions. We use Make.com to connect Monday.com to Power BI, Zoho Analytics, Shopify, ChatGPT, Gmail, Outlook, Instantly, and Google Drive — building multi-step automation scenarios that react to Monday board events and push data across your entire tech stack in real time.

Item Created Status Changed Column Updated Power BI Push 1000+ Apps
03

Monday.com Board Implementation

Beyond automation, we implement complete Monday.com workspaces — building structured boards for CRM, purchase order management, warranty claim tracking, project management, and accounting — with the correct column types, group structures, formulas, dashboards, and views. We also integrate Monday Marketplace apps that connect board data across multiple boards natively, creating a fully operational business management system inside Monday.

Use Cases

What We Build With Monday.com

Real Monday.com implementations and automations we have delivered for e-commerce brands, sales teams, operations departments, and growing businesses across the US and globally.

🛒

Full E-Commerce Operations on Monday.com

We implemented a complete e-commerce management system on Monday.com for a US-based brand — covering product management, purchase order tracking, accounting, customer service, warranty management, and supplier communication. Every department operates from a unified Monday workspace with custom boards, cross-board status mirroring using Marketplace apps, and dashboards giving management real-time visibility across the entire operation.

📊

Monday.com → Power BI & Zoho Analytics (15-min Sync)

We build Make.com scenarios that push Monday.com board data to Power BI and Zoho Analytics on a 15-minute schedule — using iterator modules to process each board item and write it to the analytics platform in the correct data structure. Management gets live BI dashboards refreshed every 15 minutes without anyone manually exporting Monday data or maintaining complex ETL pipelines between the tools.

🤖

Marketing Campaign Events → Monday + ChatGPT + Outlook

When marketing campaign events fire from platforms like Instantly, our Make.com scenario updates the relevant Monday.com board items with campaign performance data, passes the information to ChatGPT to draft intelligent reply suggestions, and sends those AI-assisted drafts via Outlook email — giving the sales team AI-prepared responses to campaign leads directly from their Monday board without switching between tools.

⚠️

Warranty Claim Management System

We built a full warranty claim management system on Monday.com — handling incoming customer warranty claims, tracking quarantine status, managing supplier warranty agreements, and recording supplier responses. Customer claims are captured from a web frontend and automatically created as Monday board items. Supplier communications are sent directly from Monday, and status updates flow between the customer-facing and vendor-facing boards automatically.

💼

Monday.com CRM Implementation

We implemented a complete CRM on Monday.com for a Brazilian client — covering lead management from multiple sources, prospect qualification tracking, active deal management, account management, and shipment tracking. Custom dashboards show deal status by stage, lead source performance, hot prospect calendars, and pipeline value — giving the sales team a full CRM experience built entirely within Monday.com's familiar interface.

📧

Monday Board Updates → Conditional Gmail Sending

We build Make.com scenarios where Monday.com board status changes or column updates trigger conditional Gmail email sending to relevant stakeholders. Different status values route emails to different recipients with different content — a customer receives an order update, a supplier receives a purchase order, and an internal team receives a progress notification — all triggered automatically by a single Monday board update with no manual email drafting required.
