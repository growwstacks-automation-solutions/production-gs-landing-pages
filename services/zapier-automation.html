Zapier Tables is a built-in database that lets your Zaps store, retrieve, and update data without needing external tools like Airtable or Google Sheets. Zapier Interfaces lets you build custom forms, portals, and dashboards that trigger Zaps on submission.

Together, they're powerful for:

  • Lead tracking: Store leads in a Table, build a submission form with Interfaces, and trigger follow-up Zaps automatically
  • Approval workflows: Create approval portals where managers can approve/reject requests that trigger downstream actions
  • Internal tools: Build lightweight CRM dashboards, inventory trackers, or project management views — all connected to your Zaps natively

You don't always need them — simple app-to-app Zaps work perfectly without Tables or Interfaces. But for businesses that need a central data store or custom intake forms, they eliminate the need for separate tools and reduce complexity significantly.
