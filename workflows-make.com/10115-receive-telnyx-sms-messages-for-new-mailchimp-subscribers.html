What This Workflow Does

This automation instantly notifies your team via SMS whenever someone subscribes to your Mailchimp email list. By connecting Mailchimp with Telnyx's SMS platform, you create a real-time alert system that helps capitalize on new subscriber interest while it's fresh.

Traditional email marketing workflows often create delays between signup and follow-up. This solution bridges that gap by delivering subscriber information directly to phones within seconds of registration. Sales and support teams can respond immediately rather than waiting for daily email reports.

How It Works

1. New Mailchimp Subscription Trigger

The workflow monitors your Mailchimp audience for new subscriptions. When someone joins your list, Mailchimp sends the subscriber details to Make.com in real-time.

2. Data Processing

Make.com extracts key information like the subscriber's name, email address, and signup source. You can customize which data points to include in your SMS alerts.

3. SMS Message Composition

The template formats the subscriber data into a concise SMS message. You control the message template, allowing branding and specific call-to-actions.

4. Telnyx SMS Delivery

Finally, the composed message sends through your Telnyx account to designated phone numbers. Delivery receipts are logged for tracking purposes.
