What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missed meeting cancellations in Calendly. When clients cancel appointments, the event often disappears from your calendar without real-time notification, leaving you unaware of newly available time slots.

The workflow monitors Calendly for cancellation events and immediately sends an SMS alert via Telnyx to your designated phone number. Unlike email notifications that may go unread for hours, SMS messages provide instant visibility with 98% open rates within 3 minutes. You'll know exactly when slots open up so you can quickly offer the time to waitlisted clients or adjust your schedule.

How It Works

1. Calendly detects a cancellation

The workflow starts when Calendly's webhook sends an event notification about an invitee canceling their scheduled meeting. Make.com receives this data in real-time.

2. Data is processed and formatted

The workflow extracts key details like the invitee's name, original meeting time, and event type. This information is structured into a readable SMS message format.

3. Telnyx sends the SMS alert

Make.com triggers Telnyx's SMS API to deliver the cancellation notice to your pre-configured phone number. The message includes all relevant details for quick action.

Pro tip: Customize the SMS template to include a rescheduling link or special offer to convert cancellations into new bookings immediately.
