What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common business challenge of manually tracking incoming phone calls. When customers call your Telnyx phone number, the workflow automatically records each call's details in a Google Sheets spreadsheet, creating a searchable call log.

The system captures essential call metadata including caller number, call duration, timestamps, and call direction. This eliminates the need for receptionists or sales teams to manually jot down call information, reducing errors and saving significant administrative time.

How It Works

1. Telnyx triggers on new call

The workflow activates whenever Telnyx receives an incoming call to your configured phone number. Telnyx's webhook sends the call details to Make.com in real-time.

2. Process call data

Make.com structures the incoming call information, extracting key fields like caller ID, call start/end times, and duration. You can add custom processing to enrich the data with business context.

3. Update Google Sheets

The formatted call record gets appended to your designated Google Sheet, creating a chronological log. Each call becomes a new row with timestamped details for future reference.

Pro tip: Add a second Google Sheets action to update a summary tab with call statistics like daily call volume and average duration.
