What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of missing critical emails when you're away from your computer. It connects your Gmail account to Telnyx's SMS gateway, instantly forwarding selected emails as text messages to your mobile phone.

Business professionals, customer support teams, and e-commerce managers often struggle with delayed responses because they don't see important emails immediately. This workflow ensures you're notified in real-time, whether you're in meetings, traveling, or simply not checking your inbox.

How It Works

1. New Email Detection

The Make.com scenario monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages. You can configure it to watch specific labels, folders, or apply filters based on sender, subject keywords, or other criteria.

2. Message Processing

When a matching email arrives, the workflow extracts key information like the sender, subject line, and message body. It formats this content for SMS delivery, typically condensing longer emails while preserving critical details.

3. SMS Delivery via Telnyx

The formatted message is sent through Telnyx's reliable SMS API to your designated mobile number. Telnyx handles carrier routing globally, ensuring high deliverability rates even for international numbers.

Pro tip: Set up a dedicated phone number for business alerts to separate these notifications from personal messages.
