Connect Salla to Facebook Conversion API

Automatically send purchase events from your Salla store to Facebook for accurate ad attribution and performance tracking

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for ecommerce businesses running Facebook ads: accurately tracking purchases when customers block browser cookies or use privacy-focused browsers. The workflow automatically sends complete purchase data from your Salla store directly to Facebook's Conversion API (CAPI), ensuring you get full credit for conversions driven by your ads.

Without this integration, you could be losing visibility into 20-40% of your conversions due to browser restrictions. The server-to-server connection bypasses these limitations, providing Facebook's algorithm with the complete purchase data it needs to optimize your ad delivery and reporting.

How It Works

1. New purchase detected in Salla

The workflow monitors your Salla store for completed orders. When a customer completes a purchase, Salla triggers the automation with all relevant order details.

2. Data formatting for Facebook CAPI

The workflow transforms the Salla order data into the exact format required by Facebook's Conversion API, including product details, order value, customer parameters, and event metadata.

3. Secure transmission to Facebook

Using your Facebook Pixel ID and access token, the workflow securely sends the conversion event directly to Facebook's servers via API, bypassing any browser restrictions.

4. Error handling and retries

If the connection fails temporarily, the workflow automatically queues the event and retries according to Facebook's best practices, ensuring no purchase data is lost.
