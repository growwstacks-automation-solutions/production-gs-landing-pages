What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring leads from Facebook advertising campaigns to your CRM and email marketing system. Every time someone submits a Facebook Lead Ad form, the workflow automatically:

Creates a new subscriber in your MailerLite email list with all the lead's information, ensuring they receive your nurturing sequences immediately. Simultaneously, it creates a complete contact record in HubSpot CRM so your sales team can follow up without delay.

How It Works

1. Facebook Lead Ads Trigger

The workflow starts whenever a new lead submits your Facebook Lead Ad form. Make.com receives the submission data in real-time including all form fields.

2. Data Processing

The automation maps the Facebook form fields to corresponding fields in MailerLite and HubSpot. You can customize this mapping during setup to match your specific form structure.

3. MailerLite Subscription

The lead's information gets added as a new subscriber to your specified MailerLite group or list. They'll immediately enter any automation sequences you've set up.

4. HubSpot Contact Creation

Simultaneously, the workflow creates a new contact in HubSpot CRM with all available lead data. The contact record includes lead source tracking to show it came from Facebook Ads.

Pro tip: Add a tag in both systems indicating the lead source and specific campaign for better tracking and segmentation.
