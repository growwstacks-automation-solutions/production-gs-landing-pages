What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of form responses getting stuck in spreadsheets instead of becoming active leads in your email marketing system. Every time someone completes your Typeform, their information automatically flows into MailerLite as a new subscriber.

The workflow eliminates manual copying and pasting of email addresses while ensuring no potential customer falls through the cracks. It works instantly, adding subscribers within seconds of form submission so you can begin nurturing them immediately with automated email sequences.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Typeform Submission

The workflow starts when someone submits your Typeform. The automation watches for new responses in real-time.

2. Extract Form Data

The system pulls all relevant fields from the submission, including email address (required) plus any additional information like name, company, or custom answers.

3. Create MailerLite Subscriber

The workflow maps the Typeform fields to corresponding MailerLite subscriber fields, then creates a new contact in your list with all the collected data.

4. Optional: Group Assignment

Based on form responses, the automation can assign subscribers to specific groups in MailerLite for targeted email campaigns.

5. Success Confirmation

The system verifies the subscriber was added successfully and can optionally log the result or notify your team.
