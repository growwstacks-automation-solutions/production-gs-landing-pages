What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges Airtable and Leap AI to create a continuous training pipeline for your machine learning models. Whenever new records are added to your Airtable base, the workflow automatically processes this data and uses it to train or update your AI models in Leap.

For businesses leveraging AI, manually transferring data between systems creates bottlenecks and delays. This solution eliminates those friction points by establishing a real-time connection between your operational data in Airtable and your analytical capabilities in Leap AI.

How It Works

1. New Record Detection

The workflow monitors your specified Airtable base for new records or updates to existing ones, triggering the automation whenever changes occur.

2. Data Preparation

Records are formatted and validated to meet Leap AI's input requirements, with options to exclude sensitive fields or anonymize personal data.

3. Model Training Initiation

The prepared data automatically triggers a new training job in your Leap AI account, using your predefined model configuration.

4. Result Notification

Upon completion, the workflow can notify your team via email or Slack with training metrics and model performance updates.

Pro tip: Schedule regular "full retrain" triggers alongside the incremental updates to maintain model accuracy as your data evolves.
