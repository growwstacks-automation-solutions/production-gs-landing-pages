What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of making Zoom meeting recordings accessible to global teams by automatically translating content into multiple languages. Traditional manual transcription and translation services are expensive, slow, and inconsistent. Our AI-powered workflow processes recordings the moment they're available in Zoom, delivering translated transcripts, concise summaries, and even natural-sounding audio versions to stakeholders via Slack.

The system leverages OpenAI's Whisper for highly accurate speech-to-text conversion, then translates the content while preserving context and technical terminology. ElevenLabs generates human-like audio summaries that team members can listen to at 2x speed, while Slack integration ensures the right people receive relevant meeting artifacts without manual distribution.

How It Works

1. Zoom Recording Trigger

The workflow automatically detects when new recordings are available in your Zoom cloud, eliminating the need to manually upload files. It captures both the audio file and meeting metadata like participants, date, and duration.

2. AI Transcription with Whisper

OpenAI's Whisper processes the audio, creating an accurate transcript in the original language with speaker differentiation. The model handles accents, background noise, and technical jargon better than most commercial transcription services.

3. Smart Translation

The system translates the transcript to your target language(s) while maintaining meeting context. Industry-specific terms and names remain intact through custom dictionaries you can configure.

4. Summary Generation

Key points, decisions, and action items are extracted into a concise written summary. The AI identifies important segments based on speaker emphasis, duration, and recurring themes.

5. Audio File Creation

ElevenLabs converts the translated summary into natural-sounding speech using voice cloning technology. You can select from multiple voice profiles to match your brand tone.

6. Slack Delivery

The complete package—original transcript, translations, written summary, and audio file—is delivered to designated Slack channels or users. Custom formatting ensures clarity and easy reference.
