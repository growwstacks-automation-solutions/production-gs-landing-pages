Automatically Buffer Your New Spotify Saved Tracks

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of forgetting to share your newly discovered music on social media. When you save a track on Spotify, it often represents music you're excited about and want to share with your audience. However, manually sharing each track to Buffer can be time-consuming and easy to overlook.

The workflow automatically detects when you save a new track on Spotify and instantly adds it to your Buffer queue. This ensures your social media followers consistently see your music recommendations without any manual effort. The automation maintains your personal branding while saving you valuable time each week.

How It Works

1. Spotify Triggers the Workflow

Make.com continuously monitors your Spotify account for new saved tracks. The moment you save a song, it triggers the workflow to begin processing.

2. Track Details Are Extracted

The workflow retrieves key information about the saved track including song title, artist, album, and cover art. This data is formatted for optimal social media presentation.

3. Content Is Prepared for Buffer

The system creates a social media post combining the track details with your preferred formatting. You can customize the message template to match your brand voice.

4. Post Is Added to Buffer Queue

The prepared content is automatically sent to your Buffer account and added to your posting queue. It will be shared according to your existing Buffer schedule.

Pro tip: Customize the message template to include relevant hashtags or commentary that reflects your music taste and engages your audience.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for music bloggers, influencers, DJs, and anyone who regularly shares their musical discoveries. If you use Spotify to find new music and Buffer to manage your social media presence, this workflow bridges the gap between these platforms perfectly.
