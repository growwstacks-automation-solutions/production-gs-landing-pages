What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually analyzing retail sales data to uncover business improvement opportunities. Many small retailers collect sales data in their POS systems but lack the time or analytical skills to extract meaningful insights from it.

The workflow automatically exports your daily Loyverse sales data to Google Sheets, then uses OpenAI's ChatGPT at month-end to analyze trends, identify patterns, and generate actionable recommendations. This gives you regular, data-driven insights without manual spreadsheet work or hiring analysts.

How It Works

1. Daily Sales Export

The workflow connects to your Loyverse account each day, extracts the latest sales transactions, and organizes them in a structured Google Sheets spreadsheet with columns for products, quantities, prices, times, and other key metrics.

2. Data Aggregation

As new sales come in, the system automatically categorizes and summarizes the data by product, day of week, time of day, and other dimensions that help identify sales patterns.

3. Monthly AI Analysis

At the end of each month (or any interval you choose), ChatGPT receives the aggregated sales data and generates a plain-English report highlighting trends, anomalies, and specific recommendations to improve your retail operations.

Pro tip: For best results, provide ChatGPT with some context about your business goals and challenges in the prompt configuration. This helps tailor the recommendations to your specific needs.
