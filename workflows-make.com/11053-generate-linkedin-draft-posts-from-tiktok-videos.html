What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of repurposing short-form video content into professional LinkedIn posts. Many marketers and business owners struggle to translate their engaging TikTok videos into written content suitable for LinkedIn's professional audience. The manual process typically involves watching the video multiple times, transcribing key points, and rewriting them - consuming 30-60 minutes per post.

The workflow automatically processes TikTok video links you email to a dedicated address. It extracts the video content, uses ChatGPT to analyze and rewrite it in a professional tone, then delivers a polished LinkedIn draft to your inbox. You maintain full control to review and edit before posting, while cutting the creation time down to about 1 minute per post.

How It Works

1. Submit TikTok Video

Email any TikTok video link to your dedicated workflow email address. The system automatically detects new messages with TikTok URLs in the body or attachments.

2. Video Processing

The workflow extracts the video transcript, captions, and description. For videos without clear audio, it analyzes visual elements and on-screen text to understand the core message.

3. ChatGPT Transformation

ChatGPT processes the raw video content, identifying key takeaways and rewriting them in professional language suitable for LinkedIn. It structures the post with an engaging hook, clear value proposition, and call-to-action.

4. Draft Delivery

The generated LinkedIn post arrives in your inbox as a draft. You can review, edit if needed, then copy-paste directly into LinkedIn's post composer.

Pro tip: Save multiple versions of this workflow with different ChatGPT prompts to create variations like technical explainers, thought leadership pieces, or case study formats from the same video.
