Organize Your YouTube Video Database with Google Sheets

Automatically track and analyze your YouTube content in a searchable spreadsheet

What This Workflow Does

This automation creates a centralized database of your YouTube videos in Google Sheets, automatically updating with the latest metrics. Instead of manually checking YouTube Studio or exporting CSV files, you'll have all your video data organized in a searchable, filterable spreadsheet that updates itself.

The template captures key metrics like views, watch time, engagement rates, and subscriber changes over time. Content creators and marketers use this to identify trends, compare video performance, and make data-driven decisions about future content.

How It Works

1. Connects to YouTube API

The workflow authenticates with your YouTube account to access your channel's video data through the official API.

2. Extracts Video Metrics

For each video, it pulls the title, description, publish date, thumbnail URL, and performance metrics like views, likes, and comments.

3. Organizes in Google Sheets

The data gets structured into logical columns with timestamps, allowing you to sort, filter, and create pivot tables.

4. Scheduled Updates

The automation runs on a schedule (daily by default) to keep your database current without manual effort.

Who This Is For

This solution benefits YouTube creators, video marketers, and agencies who need better visibility into their content performance. It's especially valuable for:

  • Marketing teams managing multiple YouTube channels
  • Agencies reporting on client video performance
  • Content strategists planning future videos based on data
