What This Workflow Does

Managing a growing YouTube channel becomes increasingly complex as your video library expands. This automation connects your YouTube channel to Airtable, automatically creating a searchable, organized database of all your published videos complete with performance metrics and categorization options.

The system eliminates manual data entry by automatically capturing video titles, descriptions, publish dates, view counts, engagement metrics, and more. You can then enrich this data with custom tags, content categories, and production notes to create a powerful content management system that helps you analyze what works and plan future videos strategically.

How It Works

1. YouTube API connection

The workflow connects to YouTube's API to fetch your channel's video data, including both current metrics and historical performance trends.

2. Data transformation

Raw API responses are processed into a structured format optimized for Airtable, calculating key ratios like engagement rate and converting timestamps to readable dates.

3. Airtable synchronization

Processed video data gets added or updated in your designated Airtable base, maintaining a complete historical record while avoiding duplicates.

4. Scheduled updates

The automation runs on your preferred schedule (daily/weekly) to keep all metrics current without manual intervention.

Pro tip: Add a "Content Pillar" field in Airtable to categorize videos by topic or campaign. This lets you filter performance reports by strategic initiative.
