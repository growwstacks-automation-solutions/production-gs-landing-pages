What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually sharing every new YouTube video across your Discord community channels. Content creators and community managers often struggle to maintain consistent engagement when juggling multiple platforms. The workflow automatically detects new YouTube uploads and posts them to designated Discord channels within minutes.

By eliminating manual sharing, you ensure your most engaged community members never miss new content while freeing up hours each month. The instant notifications help capitalize on YouTube's algorithm boost for new videos, increasing early view counts and discussion participation.

How It Works

1. YouTube Channel Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your YouTube channel for new uploads using YouTube's API. It detects videos as soon as they're published or reach a specified visibility status.

2. Video Information Extraction

When a new video is found, the system extracts key details like title, description, thumbnail URL, and video link. This data gets formatted for optimal Discord presentation.

3. Discord Channel Posting

The workflow then sends a rich embed message to your specified Discord channel, including the video thumbnail, title, and direct link. You can customize the message format with timestamps or discussion prompts.

Who This Is For

This automation benefits content creators, community managers, and brands who maintain active YouTube and Discord presences. It's particularly valuable for:

What You'll Need

  1. A YouTube channel with upload privileges
  2. A Discord server with webhook permissions
  3. A Make.com account (free tier available)
  4. 5-10 minutes for initial setup

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy the template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your YouTube channel in the "YouTube" module
  3. Create a Discord webhook in your server settings
  4. Paste the webhook URL into the "Discord" module
  5. Customize the message format if desired
  6. Turn on the scenario and test with a new upload

Pro tip: Create a dedicated #new-videos channel in Discord and pin the webhook instructions there for easy access during setup.
