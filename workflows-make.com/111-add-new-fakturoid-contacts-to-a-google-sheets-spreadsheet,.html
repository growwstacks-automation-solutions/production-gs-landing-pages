What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying customer information from Fakturoid (a popular invoicing and accounting platform) into Google Sheets for reporting, analysis, or sharing with team members. Every time a new contact is created in your Fakturoid account, the workflow automatically adds that contact's complete details as a new row in your designated Google Sheet.

The template eliminates human error in data transfer and ensures your spreadsheet always reflects the latest customer information. It's particularly valuable for businesses that need to:

  • Maintain a backup of customer data outside Fakturoid
  • Share customer information with team members who don't have Fakturoid access
  • Perform custom reporting or analysis that's not possible within Fakturoid
  • Integrate customer data with other systems that connect to Google Sheets

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Fakturoid Contact

The workflow starts whenever a new contact is created in your Fakturoid account. Make.com monitors your Fakturoid instance in real-time through API connections.

2. Data Extraction

The automation retrieves all available contact information including name, email, phone number, address, and any custom fields you've defined in Fakturoid.

3. Google Sheets Preparation

The workflow checks your designated Google Sheet to ensure proper column headers exist and prepares the new row structure.

4. Data Mapping

Contact fields from Fakturoid are mapped to corresponding columns in your Google Sheet according to your preferred format.

5. Row Addition

The complete contact record is appended as a new row at the bottom of your spreadsheet, with all data properly formatted.
