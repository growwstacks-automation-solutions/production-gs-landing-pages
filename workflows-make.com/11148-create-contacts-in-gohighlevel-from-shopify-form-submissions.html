What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of disconnected customer data between Shopify stores and CRM systems. When customers submit forms on your Shopify store (contact forms, lead capture forms, etc.), their information often gets stuck in Shopify without flowing into your marketing systems. This creates missed opportunities for follow-up and relationship building.

The workflow automatically creates GoHighLevel contacts whenever a form is submitted on your Shopify store. It maps all form field data to the corresponding GoHighLevel contact fields, ensuring your sales and marketing teams have immediate access to new leads. This eliminates manual data entry, reduces errors, and accelerates your ability to engage new contacts with targeted campaigns.

How It Works

1. Shopify Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Shopify store for new form submissions. Whenever a customer submits any form (contact, lead capture, product inquiry, etc.), the automation is triggered.

2. Data Extraction and Mapping

The system extracts all submitted form fields (name, email, phone, custom fields) and prepares them for transfer to GoHighLevel. You can configure which Shopify fields map to which GoHighLevel contact properties.

3. Contact Creation in GoHighLevel

The workflow creates a new contact in GoHighLevel with all the submitted data. It can also apply tags, add to specific lists, or trigger automation sequences based on the form type.

4. Error Handling and Notifications

If any issues occur during the transfer (like duplicate contacts or validation errors), the system can notify your team or attempt alternative processing methods.
