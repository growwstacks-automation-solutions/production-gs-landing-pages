Sync Eventbrite Events with Access Charity CRM

Automatically transfer event data from Eventbrite to your Access Charity CRM to streamline nonprofit event management

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Nonprofit · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for nonprofits and charities that use Eventbrite for event management and Access Charity CRM for donor relationships. Manually transferring event data between these systems is time-consuming and prone to errors, leading to incomplete donor records and missed engagement opportunities.

The workflow automatically synchronizes new Eventbrite events and attendee data with your Access Charity CRM on a scheduled basis (default every 15 minutes). It ensures your donor database always reflects current event participation, enabling better relationship management and more accurate reporting on donor engagement.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Event Check

The workflow runs at your chosen interval (default 15 minutes) to check Eventbrite for new or updated events.

2. Data Extraction

For each event found, the system retrieves key details like event name, date/time, location, description, and ticket information.

3. Attendee Data Collection

The workflow gathers attendee information including names, contact details, ticket types, and any custom questions answered during registration.

4. CRM Record Creation

Event and attendee data is formatted to match your Access Charity CRM structure and creates or updates records accordingly.

5. Change Detection

If an event is modified in Eventbrite after initial sync, the workflow detects changes and updates the corresponding CRM records.

Pro tip: Configure custom field mappings during setup to ensure event data populates the most useful fields in your CRM for reporting and segmentation.

  1. Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template to your Make.com account
