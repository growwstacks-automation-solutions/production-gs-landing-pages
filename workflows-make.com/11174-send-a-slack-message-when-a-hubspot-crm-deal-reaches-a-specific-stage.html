What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges HubSpot CRM and Slack to create real-time notifications when deals progress through your sales pipeline. Whenever a deal changes to a specific stage you define (like "Contract Sent" or "Negotiation"), the workflow automatically posts a customized message to your chosen Slack channel with all relevant deal details.

Sales teams waste hours manually tracking deal movements and notifying stakeholders. This workflow eliminates that friction by instantly alerting the right people when action is needed, helping accelerate deal velocity and improving forecast accuracy through better visibility.

How It Works

1. HubSpot triggers on deal stage changes

The workflow constantly monitors your HubSpot deals for stage changes. When a deal moves to or from a stage you specify, it captures all relevant deal data including amount, owner, company, and previous/new stages.

2. Data processing and message formatting

The workflow extracts key information and formats it into a clean Slack message template you customize. You can include dynamic fields like deal value, expected close date, and even emoji indicators for different stages.

3. Conditional Slack notifications

Based on rules you set, the workflow determines which Slack channel should receive the notification. You can route different deal types or amounts to specific channels, or even send direct messages to deal owners.

Pro tip: Add @mentions for deal owners in your Slack messages to ensure immediate visibility and accountability for stage transitions.
