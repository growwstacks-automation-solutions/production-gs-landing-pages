What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying invoice data between Fakturoid and accounting spreadsheets. Every time a new invoice is created in Fakturoid, the workflow automatically captures all relevant details and adds them as a new row in your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet.

The template handles all field mappings between systems, including invoice numbers, dates, client information, line items, taxes, and payment status. This creates a perfect audit trail while eliminating human errors from manual data entry. Finance teams gain real-time visibility into receivables without spreadsheet maintenance work.

How It Works

1. Invoice Trigger

The workflow monitors your Fakturoid account for new invoice events through instant webhook notifications or periodic checks.

2. Data Extraction

All invoice fields are captured including custom fields you've configured in Fakturoid. The system handles PDF attachments and line item details.

3. Spreadsheet Update

Make.com adds a new row to your Google Sheet with columns mapped to corresponding Fakturoid fields. Date formatting and currency conversions are applied automatically.

4. Error Handling

If the update fails, the system retries automatically and notifies you if persistent issues occur. Success confirmations verify each record creation.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column in Sheets to track when records were added. This helps verify automation performance during audits.
