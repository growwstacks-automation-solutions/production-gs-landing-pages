What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of sharing webinar recordings with attendees. After your Zoom webinar concludes, the system automatically detects when the recording becomes available, compiles all attendee emails, and sends personalized messages with the recording link - all without any manual intervention.

For businesses running regular webinars, this eliminates hours of administrative work while ensuring 100% of attendees receive the valuable content. The template includes customizable email templates that maintain your brand voice and can include additional resources like slides, surveys, or related offers to maximize engagement.

How It Works

1. Zoom Webinar Completion Trigger

The workflow activates when your Zoom webinar ends and the recording processing begins. Make.com monitors your Zoom account for this status change.

2. Recording Availability Check

The system polls Zoom's API until the recording is fully processed and the shareable link becomes available, typically within 1-2 hours post-webinar.

3. Attendee List Compilation

The workflow retrieves the complete list of webinar attendees from Zoom, including their names and email addresses for personalization.

4. Personalized Email Generation

For each attendee, the system generates an email using your branded template, inserting their name, webinar title, and the recording link.

5. Automated Email Dispatch

Emails are sent through your connected email service (Gmail, Outlook, Mailchimp, etc.) with tracking enabled for engagement monitoring.

Pro tip: Add a 30-minute delay before sending to include a "Thank you for attending" message that references specific Q&A or moments from the live session.
