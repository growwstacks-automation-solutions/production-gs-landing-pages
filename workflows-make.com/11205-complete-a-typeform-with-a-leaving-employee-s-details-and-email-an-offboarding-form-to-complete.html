Collect departure details and automatically send completion forms to streamline HR processes

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the traditionally manual and error-prone employee offboarding process into a streamlined digital experience. When an employee leaves your organization, the system automatically sends them a Typeform to collect all necessary departure information, then triggers follow-up emails with required completion forms based on their responses.

HR teams waste countless hours chasing departing employees for equipment returns, exit interviews, and system access revocations. This workflow eliminates that friction by creating a standardized, automated process that ensures compliance while reducing administrative overhead by 70-80% per offboarding case.

How It Works

1. Trigger the offboarding process

The workflow initiates when an HR system marks an employee as "departing" or when manually triggered for ad-hoc cases. This can integrate with your existing HRIS or work as a standalone solution.

2. Send customized Typeform

The system automatically emails the departing employee a personalized Typeform link. The form adapts based on employee role, location, and reason for departure to collect only relevant information.

3. Process responses automatically

As the employee completes the form, the system categorizes responses and triggers appropriate follow-up actions - equipment return labels, benefits termination notices, or knowledge transfer requests.

4. Send completion forms

Based on form responses, the workflow emails all required completion documents (NDAs, final paycheck acknowledgments, etc.) with clear deadlines and instructions.

5. Update HR systems

The automation syncs collected data with your HRIS, IT systems, and payroll platforms to ensure all departments have current offboarding status.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to your Typeform that shows/hides questions based on employee type (voluntary vs involuntary departure) to improve completion rates.
