Automate Employee Offboarding: Typeform to BambooHR Integration

Streamline exit processes by automatically updating HR records when employees complete their departure forms

Get This Workflow → Make.com · HR · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common HR challenge of maintaining accurate employee records during offboarding. When employees leave, HR teams typically spend hours manually transferring exit information from forms to HR systems. This workflow eliminates that inefficiency by automatically syncing Typeform responses to BambooHR.

The solution captures all critical offboarding details - last working day, reason for leaving, equipment returns, and exit interview feedback - then updates the corresponding BambooHR employee record instantly. This ensures compliance, reduces administrative errors, and gives HR teams real-time visibility into departures.

How It Works

1. Employee completes offboarding Typeform

Departing employees receive a customized Typeform that collects all necessary exit information. The form can include conditional logic to show/hide questions based on employment type or departure reason.

2. Make.com triggers on form submission

The workflow automatically starts when the employee submits their completed Typeform. Make.com captures all response data and prepares it for processing.

3. Data validation and transformation

The system verifies data completeness, formats fields correctly for BambooHR, and maps Typeform responses to the appropriate BambooHR employee record fields.

4. BambooHR record update

The workflow updates the employee's BambooHR profile with the offboarding details, including status change, departure date, and any custom fields your organization tracks.

5. Confirmation and notifications

HR receives an instant notification with the submitted data and a confirmation that BambooHR has been updated. Optional: Send the employee a confirmation receipt.

Pro tip: Add a webhook step to notify IT automatically when equipment return fields indicate outstanding devices, triggering your asset recovery process.
