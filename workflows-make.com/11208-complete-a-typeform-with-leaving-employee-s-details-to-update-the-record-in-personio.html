Automate Employee Offboarding: Typeform to Personio

Streamline exit processes by automatically updating HR records when employees leave your company

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the manual, error-prone process of employee offboarding into a seamless digital workflow. When an employee leaves your organization, their exit details collected through a Typeform automatically update their record in Personio - your HR information system.

The workflow eliminates duplicate data entry, reduces compliance risks, and ensures all departments access accurate offboarding information in real-time. HR teams save hours per case while maintaining perfect records for payroll, benefits termination, and compliance reporting.

How It Works

1. Employee completes offboarding Typeform

The departing employee receives a customized Typeform with fields for last working date, equipment returns, exit interview feedback, and other critical offboarding information.

2. Make.com captures the submission

The workflow triggers immediately when the Typeform is submitted, extracting all response data with timestamps for audit purposes.

3. Personio record updates automatically

The system maps Typeform responses to corresponding Personio fields, updating the employee's status, departure details, and exit documentation without manual HR intervention.
