What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized outreach to new Salesforce leads while maintaining quality control. When a new lead enters your Salesforce pipeline, the system instantly generates a tailored introduction message using ChatGPT, then stores it in a Google Sheet where your team can review and approve messages before they're sent.

Traditional manual personalization is time-consuming and inconsistent, often leading to generic templates or missed opportunities. This workflow ensures every lead receives a message that references their specific company, role, and potential needs - at scale. The Google Sheets integration creates an audit trail and allows for team collaboration on messaging strategies.

How It Works

1. New lead detection in Salesforce

The workflow monitors your Salesforce instance for new lead records. When a qualified lead is identified (based on your criteria), it triggers the automation.

2. Data preparation for ChatGPT

Key details like the lead's name, company, title, industry, and any custom fields are extracted and formatted into a prompt for ChatGPT.

3. AI message generation

ChatGPT analyzes the lead data and generates a personalized introduction message following your specified tone, length, and content guidelines.

4. Google Sheets storage

The generated message is automatically logged in a designated Google Sheet along with the original lead data, timestamp, and status fields.

5. Team review workflow

Your sales team receives notifications to review pending messages in the sheet, make any necessary adjustments, and mark them as approved for sending.
