What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating personalized outreach messages at scale for new Salesforce leads. Sales teams often struggle to balance personalization with efficiency - generic templates get ignored while hand-crafted messages don't scale.

The workflow automatically generates contextually relevant introduction messages using ChatGPT whenever a new lead enters Salesforce. These AI-crafted messages incorporate lead-specific details like company name, industry, and role before being stored in Airtable for human review and approval prior to sending.

How It Works

1. New lead detection in Salesforce

The workflow monitors your Salesforce instance for new lead records based on your criteria (like lead status or source). When a qualifying lead appears, it triggers the automation.

2. Data enrichment for personalization

Key fields from the Salesforce lead record are extracted - company name, industry, contact role, and any custom fields you've marked as important for messaging.

3. ChatGPT message generation

The lead data is sent to ChatGPT with your predefined messaging guidelines and tone preferences. The AI generates 2-3 personalized introduction options.

4. Airtable storage for review

The generated messages are saved to a designated Airtable base along with the original lead data. Your team can review, edit, or approve messages before they're sent.

Pro tip: Include 2-3 key pain points or goals from your ideal customer profile in the ChatGPT prompt to generate more targeted messages.
