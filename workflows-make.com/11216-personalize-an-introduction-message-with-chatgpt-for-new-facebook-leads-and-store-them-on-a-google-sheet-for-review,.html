What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for businesses using Facebook lead generation: how to respond quickly with personalized messages that convert leads into customers. Generic "thanks for contacting us" responses often get ignored, while manually crafting unique messages for each lead doesn't scale.

The workflow automatically generates human-like, contextually relevant introduction messages using ChatGPT whenever a new lead comes through Facebook. It analyzes available lead information to craft messages that reference specific details about how the lead found you or what they're interested in. These personalized messages are then stored in a Google Sheet where your team can review them before sending or use them for follow-up sequences.

How It Works

1. New Facebook lead trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits a lead form on your Facebook page or clicks your call-to-action button. The system captures all available information about the lead including their name, contact details, and any custom form responses.

2. ChatGPT message generation

The lead information is sent to ChatGPT with your customized prompt template. ChatGPT analyzes the data and generates a warm, professional introduction message tailored to this specific lead. You control the tone, length, and key points to include.

3. Google Sheets storage

The personalized message along with all lead details are automatically recorded in your designated Google Sheet. The sheet organizes leads by date with columns for the message, lead source, and status (pending review, sent, etc.).

4. Optional approval step

Before messages are sent, your team can review them in the Google Sheet and make any adjustments. This ensures quality control while still saving the bulk of the creative work.

Pro tip: Add a second ChatGPT step to generate follow-up message ideas based on how the lead responds to your initial contact. This creates a complete nurturing sequence.
