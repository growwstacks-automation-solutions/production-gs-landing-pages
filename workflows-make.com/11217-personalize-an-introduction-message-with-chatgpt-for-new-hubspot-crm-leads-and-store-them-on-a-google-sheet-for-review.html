What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating meaningful first contact with new leads at scale. When generic "Hi [First Name]" messages see declining response rates, this workflow uses AI to craft genuinely personalized introductions based on each lead's specific profile data from HubSpot CRM.

The system automatically generates contextually relevant messages that reference the prospect's industry, role challenges, and engagement history. Each message is then logged in Google Sheets with timestamps, allowing sales teams to review effectiveness and continuously improve their outreach strategy.

How It Works

1. New lead detection in HubSpot

The workflow triggers when a new lead reaches your predefined qualification threshold in HubSpot. It captures all available profile data including company information, job title, and previous interactions with your content.

2. Data preparation for ChatGPT

Lead details are structured into a clear prompt that instructs ChatGPT to generate a professional yet personalized introduction. The template ensures brand voice consistency while allowing for natural variation based on lead specifics.

3. AI message generation

ChatGPT creates 2-3 message variations that authentically reference the lead's context. The AI considers industry jargon, likely pain points, and appropriate tone based on the prospect's seniority level and company size.

4. Google Sheets logging

The final selected message, along with all metadata, is recorded in a designated Google Sheet. This creates a searchable database of all outreach for performance analysis and compliance purposes.

Pro tip: Add a second automation that updates HubSpot with which personalized messages actually received responses, creating a feedback loop to train your AI over time.
