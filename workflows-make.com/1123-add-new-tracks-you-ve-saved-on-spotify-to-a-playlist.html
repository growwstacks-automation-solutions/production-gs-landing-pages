What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common frustration of losing track of songs you've saved on Spotify. Instead of manually sorting through your "Liked Songs" to add tracks to thematic playlists, this workflow automatically detects newly saved tracks and adds them to designated playlists based on your preferences.

The system continuously monitors your Spotify account for new saved tracks, then processes them through customizable rules before adding to your specified playlists. This maintains organized music collections without the time-consuming manual work typically required.

How It Works

1. Track Detection

The workflow periodically checks your Spotify account for newly saved tracks using Spotify's API. It identifies any songs added to your library since the last check.

2. Playlist Matching

Each new track is analyzed against your predefined playlist criteria. You can configure rules based on genre, artist, tempo, or other audio characteristics Spotify provides.

3. Automated Addition

Matched tracks are automatically added to their designated playlists. The system logs each addition and can optionally notify you via email or Slack about updates.
