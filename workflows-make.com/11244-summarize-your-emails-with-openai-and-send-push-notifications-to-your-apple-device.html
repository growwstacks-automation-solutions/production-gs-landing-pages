What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of email overload by leveraging AI to extract key information from your inbox. Instead of spending valuable time reading lengthy messages, you receive concise summaries as push notifications on your Apple devices. The workflow connects your email provider with OpenAI's powerful language models to analyze message content, identify important points, and deliver actionable insights directly to your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

Professionals typically spend 2.5 hours per day managing email, with much of that time wasted on low-value messages. The system is particularly valuable for executives, sales teams, and anyone who receives high volumes of business correspondence.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified email account for new messages. You can set filters to process only certain senders, subjects, or message types.

2. AI Analysis

Each qualifying email is sent to OpenAI's API where advanced natural language processing extracts key points, action items, and important details while preserving context.

3. Summary Generation

The AI generates a concise summary in your preferred format (bullet points, paragraph, or custom template) with adjustable length and detail level.

4. Notification Delivery

The formatted summary is pushed to your Apple devices through secure channels, appearing alongside other notifications with customizable alerts.

Pro tip: Create different summary styles for different types of emails - brief bullet points for internal updates, more detailed summaries for client communications.
