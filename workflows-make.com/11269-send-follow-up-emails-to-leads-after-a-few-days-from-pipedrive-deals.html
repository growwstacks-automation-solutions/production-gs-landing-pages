What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of timely follow-ups in sales pipelines. When deals sit in Pipedrive without proper nurturing, conversion rates drop significantly. The workflow automatically sends personalized follow-up emails at strategic intervals after a deal reaches specific stages in Pipedrive.

By automating this process, you ensure no lead falls through the cracks while freeing your sales team from manual email tracking. The system pulls relevant deal information from Pipedrive to create context-rich emails that feel personal rather than generic.

How It Works

1. Pipedrive Deal Trigger

The workflow monitors your Pipedrive account for deals that reach designated stages (like "Contact Made" or "Proposal Sent"). You can configure which stages should trigger follow-up sequences.

2. Delay Timer

After a deal reaches the trigger stage, the system waits for your specified number of days (typically 2-5 days) before sending the follow-up. This delay ensures the email arrives at the optimal moment in the buyer's journey.

3. Email Composition

The template pulls key details from Pipedrive like contact name, company, deal value, and previous interactions to craft personalized emails. You can customize the email content to match your brand voice and sales process.

4. Delivery & Tracking

Emails are sent through your connected email service (like Gmail or Outlook). The system can optionally log these communications back to Pipedrive for complete visibility into all touchpoints.

Pro tip: Configure different follow-up sequences for different deal stages. Early-stage deals might need educational content while late-stage deals benefit from urgency-building messages.
