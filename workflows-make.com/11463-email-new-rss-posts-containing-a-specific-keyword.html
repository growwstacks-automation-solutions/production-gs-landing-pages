What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming problem of manually monitoring multiple RSS feeds for relevant content. Instead of checking feeds throughout the day, you'll receive instant email notifications whenever new posts contain your specified keywords. The workflow connects your RSS sources directly to your email, filtering out irrelevant content so you only see what matters.

Businesses use this to track industry trends, competitor mentions, or specific product references. A marketing team might monitor "AI marketing tools" across industry blogs, while a PR agency could track client names in news outlets. The automation handles the monitoring work, freeing up hours each week for more strategic tasks.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Connection

The workflow begins by connecting to your specified RSS feed URLs. You can add multiple feeds from blogs, news sites, or any content source with an RSS output.

2. Keyword Filtering

Each new post gets scanned for your predefined keywords. The system checks both titles and content, with options for exact matches or partial matches depending on your needs.

3. Email Notification

When a match is found, the workflow automatically generates and sends an email with the post details. You control the email format, recipients, and frequency of alerts.

Pro tip: Combine this with Slack or Teams notifications for instant team awareness of important content.
