What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between Teamwork project management and Slack communication by instantly notifying your team whenever new tasks are created. Instead of manually checking Teamwork or sending update messages, your team receives automatic alerts in Slack with key task details.

The workflow solves the common problem of task visibility in distributed teams. When team members aren't constantly monitoring their project management tool, important tasks can get overlooked. This integration ensures immediate awareness of new assignments, helping your team stay on top of deliverables without switching between apps.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Task in Teamwork

The workflow monitors your Teamwork account for new task creation events. Whenever a team member or client adds a new task to any of your projects, Make.com detects this change instantly.

2. Extract Task Details

The workflow pulls relevant information from the new task including task name, project, due date, priority level, and assigned team members. This data is structured for clear communication in Slack.

3. Format Slack Message

Make.com formats a clean, actionable Slack message with emoji indicators for priority levels and clickable links back to the Teamwork task. The message highlights who needs to take action and by when.

4. Deliver to Slack Channel

The formatted message is posted to your designated Slack channel, ensuring the right people see the update immediately. Team members can react, comment, or click through to Teamwork directly from Slack.

Pro tip: Customize the message format to include only the most relevant fields for your team. Too much information can reduce the effectiveness of notifications.
