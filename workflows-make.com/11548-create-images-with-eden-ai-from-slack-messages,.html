What This Workflow Does

In today's fast-paced digital environment, visual content is king, but creating it can be a bottleneck. Marketing teams, content creators, and even sales departments often have brilliant ideas in Slack conversations that never become visuals because the process of briefing a designer, waiting for drafts, and making revisions is too slow. This automation solves that by connecting your team's communication hub directly to powerful AI image generation.

This Make.com workflow listens for specific Slack messages—like a channel post or a direct message containing an image description—and automatically sends that prompt to Eden AI. Eden AI processes the request using one of its many integrated AI models and returns a generated image. The result is a seamless pipeline where a simple text command in Slack can produce a custom graphic, illustration, or photo-realistic image in minutes, not days.

The business value is immense: it democratizes design, drastically reduces time-to-market for visual content, and empowers non-technical team members to contribute directly to creative projects. Imagine your social media manager typing "Create a vibrant graphic about summer productivity tips" in Slack and having a ready-to-post image delivered to a designated folder before the meeting ends.

How It Works

The workflow acts as a intelligent bridge between your team's conversations and AI-powered creativity. Here's a step-by-step breakdown of the automation process.

Step 1: Trigger from Slack

The scenario starts by monitoring a specific Slack channel, direct message, or a keyword. When a user sends a message formatted as an image request (e.g., "/image a futuristic cityscape at dusk"), the Make.com trigger module captures the message text, the user who sent it, and any other relevant metadata.

Step 2: Process the Prompt

The captured text is then parsed and formatted. This step can include cleaning the input, adding predefined style parameters (like "digital art" or "photorealistic"), or combining it with brand-specific guidelines stored in a database. This ensures the prompt sent to the AI is optimized for the best possible output.

Step 3: Generate Image with Eden AI

The refined prompt is sent to the Eden AI module. Eden AI acts as a gateway, allowing you to choose from various image generation engines (like DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, or Midjourney via API). The module specifies parameters like image size, number of variations, and style. Eden AI handles the API call and returns the generated image URL.

Step 4: Deliver & Notify

Finally, the workflow delivers the result. It can post the generated image back to the originating Slack channel or thread, send it via direct message to the requester, and simultaneously save the file to a cloud storage platform like Google Drive, Dropbox, or Notion for asset management. A completion notification ensures the team knows their visual is ready.

Pro tip: Use a dedicated Slack channel like #ai-image-requests to keep all prompts and generated images organized. This creates a searchable visual library for your team.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your Slack-to-AI-image generator running in under 30 minutes.

  1. Duplicate the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made template into your Make.com account.
