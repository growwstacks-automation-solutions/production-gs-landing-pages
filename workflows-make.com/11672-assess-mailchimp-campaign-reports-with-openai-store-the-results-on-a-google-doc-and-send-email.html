What This Workflow Does

For marketers and business owners, understanding email campaign performance is crucial but time-consuming. Manually sifting through Mailchimp reports to find trends, diagnose problems, and plan improvements can eat up hours every week. This automation solves that by turning raw data into intelligent, actionable insights automatically.

The workflow connects Mailchimp, OpenAI, Google Docs, and your email in a seamless pipeline. After a campaign ends, it pulls the report, sends the key metrics to OpenAI for analysis, saves the AI's findings and improvement suggestions into a structured Google Doc, and then emails that document to you or your team. It transforms a reactive reporting task into a proactive strategic tool.

How It Works

The automation runs on a schedule or is triggered by a new Mailchimp report, executing a precise series of steps to deliver insights.

Step 1: Fetch Campaign Data from Mailchimp

The workflow connects to your Mailchimp account and retrieves the performance data for a specified campaign. This includes metrics like open rate, click-through rate, unsubscribe count, bounce rate, and engagement over time.

Step 2: Analyze Performance with OpenAI

The raw metrics are sent to OpenAI (like ChatGPT) with a tailored prompt. The AI acts as a marketing analyst, interpreting the numbers. It doesn't just summarize; it identifies strengths, weaknesses, potential causes for drops in engagement, and generates specific, testable suggestions for improvement.

Step 3: Create a Structured Report in Google Docs

The AI's analysis is then formatted and sent to Google Docs. The workflow creates a new document or appends to an existing one, organizing the insights with clear headings, bullet points, and data highlights. This builds a permanent, searchable library of campaign learnings.

Step 4: Share Insights via Email

Finally, the workflow sends an email (via your preferred service like Gmail or Outlook) to designated recipients. The email contains a summary and a direct link to the newly created Google Doc, ensuring the right people see the insights immediately without having to go looking for them.

Pro tip: Use this workflow on a weekly schedule to analyze your last 5 campaigns. This gives the AI more data to work with, allowing it to identify longer-term trends and provide even more valuable strategic advice.

Quick Setup Guide

You can be up and running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, establish connections for Mailchimp, OpenAI, Google Docs, and your email service.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Set the workflow to run on a schedule (e.g., every Monday at 9 AM) or trigger it manually for specific campaigns.
  4. Customize the AI Prompt: Adjust the prompt sent to OpenAI to focus on the metrics and suggestions most relevant to your business goals.
  5. Set Document & Email Details: Specify the Google Drive folder for reports and enter the email addresses of your report recipients.
  6. Test & Activate: Run a test with a past campaign to ensure the document is created and the email sends correctly, then turn the scenario on.
