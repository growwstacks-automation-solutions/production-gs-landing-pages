What This Workflow Does

For marketing teams and social media managers, understanding Instagram performance is crucial, but manually compiling reports is a significant drain on time and resources. This automation solves that by seamlessly connecting your Instagram Business account to Microsoft Power BI. It automatically extracts key media insights—like impressions, reach, saves, and video views—on a scheduled basis and pushes this data into a structured Power BI dataset.

The result is a centralized, always-updated data source ready for sophisticated analysis. Instead of weekly manual exports and spreadsheet gymnastics, you gain immediate access to trends, performance benchmarks, and visual dashboards that clearly show what content drives real business value. This workflow transforms raw engagement numbers into strategic intelligence, enabling you to optimize your content calendar and prove marketing ROI with hard data.

How It Works

The workflow acts as a reliable data pipeline, operating silently in the background to keep your reports current.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger & Data Fetch

The automation is initiated on a schedule you set (e.g., weekly). It connects to the Instagram Graph API using secure OAuth and retrieves insights for all your media posts from the defined period.

Step 2: Data Structuring & Transformation

Raw API data is parsed and mapped into a clean, consistent format. The workflow ensures each post's metrics (Media ID, Impressions, Reach, Saved, Video Views, Replies, Media URL) are correctly aligned for database insertion.

Step 3: Secure Data Transfer to Power BI

The formatted data is sent via the Power BI API directly to your specified dataset and table. The process validates the connection and handles errors gracefully, ensuring data integrity.

Step 4: Dataset Refresh & Reporting

Once the new data is in Power BI, you can refresh your reports and dashboards. The new metrics instantly populate your charts, time-series analyses, and KPI scorecards, ready for review.

Pro tip: Pair this Instagram data with your advertising spend (from Meta Ads Manager) and website conversion data in Power BI. This lets you build a complete funnel view, calculating cost-per-lead and ROI for your organic social efforts.

What You'll Need

  1. An active Make.com account (free tier sufficient).
  2. An Instagram Business Account or Instagram Creator Account with access to Insights.
  3. A Facebook Developer App configured with the Instagram Basic Display API permissions to generate access tokens.
  4. A Microsoft Power BI account with a workspace and a dataset/table created. The table must have the specific columns: Media ID, Impressions, Reach, Saved, Video Views, Replies, and Media URL.
  5. Basic familiarity with connecting APIs in Make.com (the template provides the blueprint).

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to deploy the template in under 30 minutes:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com workspace.
  2. Configure Instagram Connection: In the first module, update the OAuth connection to use your Facebook App credentials and grant access to your Instagram account.
  3. Set the Schedule: Adjust the Schedule module to run at your preferred frequency (e.g., every Monday at 9 AM).
  4. Connect Power BI: In the final module, establish the connection to your Power BI workspace. Specify the exact Dataset and Table name where the data should land.
  5. Map the Data: Ensure the data mapping aligns with your Power BI table's column order: Media ID, Impressions, Reach, Saved, Video Views, Replies, Media URL.
  6. Test & Activate: Run a single test execution to verify data flows correctly into your Power BI table. Once confirmed, activate the scenario.
