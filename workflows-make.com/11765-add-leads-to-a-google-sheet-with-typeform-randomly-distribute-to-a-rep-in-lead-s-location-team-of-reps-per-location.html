What This Workflow Does

Manual lead distribution is a silent profit-killer for sales teams. When a new inquiry comes in from a website form or Typeform, someone has to notice it, copy the data, decide which rep gets it, and then notify them. This process is slow, prone to errors, and often unfair—leading to reps competing for "good" leads while others get overlooked. The result? Missed opportunities, frustrated team members, and a leaky sales funnel.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless, impartial pipeline. The moment a prospect submits a Typeform, their details—including critical location data—are instantly captured and logged in a master Google Sheet. The system then intelligently identifies all sales representatives assigned to that lead's region and randomly selects one to receive the assignment. This ensures every qualified lead is acted upon within minutes and distributed with perfect fairness, maximizing both conversion potential and team morale.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple but powerful trigger-action logic, connecting Typeform and Google Sheets with smart decision-making in between.

Step 1: Capture the Lead

The automation is triggered instantly when a new submission is received in your Typeform. It pulls all the form response data, such as the contact's name, email, phone number, company, and most importantly, their location (which could be captured via a field for city, state, zip code, or country).

Step 2: Log to Central Master Sheet

The lead's complete information is appended as a new row in your designated master Google Sheet. This serves as your permanent, searchable record of all inbound leads, complete with a timestamp. This sheet becomes the single source of truth for your sales pipeline.

Pro tip: Structure your master Sheet with clear columns: Timestamp, Lead Name, Email, Location, Assigned Rep, and Status. This makes future reporting and pipeline analysis incredibly easy.

Step 3: Identify Eligible Reps by Location

The workflow references a second, pre-configured Google Sheet that acts as your "Sales Team Directory." This sheet lists all your sales representatives alongside their assigned territories (e.g., Rep A: New York, Chicago; Rep B: Chicago, Detroit). The system filters this list to find all reps whose territory matches the lead's submitted location.

Step 4: Randomly Assign & Update

From the filtered list of eligible reps, the automation uses a randomizer function to pick one. This guarantees a statistically even distribution over time. Finally, it updates the lead's row in the master Sheet with the name of the assigned sales representative, completing the record.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to clone the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Typeform: In the first Make module, authorize the connection to your Typeform account and select the specific form that will trigger the workflow.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: Authorize Make to access your Google account. Then, in the "Add Row" module, point it to your "Master Lead Log" Sheet and map the Typeform fields (name, email, location) to the correct Sheet columns.
  4. Configure Your Team Directory: In the "Search Rows" module, point it to your "Sales Team Directory" Sheet. Set the filter condition so it searches the "Territory" column for a match with the lead's location from Typeform.
  5. Set the Randomizer: The template includes a router and randomizer. Ensure it's set to pick one item from the array of reps found in the previous step.
  6. Test and Activate: Do a test run by submitting your Typeform. Check that a new row appears in your Master Sheet with the lead's details and a randomly assigned rep from the correct location. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on.
