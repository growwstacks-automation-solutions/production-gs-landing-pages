What This Workflow Does

For growing businesses, financial visibility is crucial but often delayed. When an invoice is created in Xero, key team members—like sales reps, account managers, or the finance lead—might not know about it for hours or even days. This delay can slow down follow-up, confuse customer communications, and create blind spots in cash flow management.

This automation solves that by creating an instant bridge between your accounting software and your team's communication hub. Every time a new invoice is saved in Xero, this Make.com workflow triggers and posts a formatted message directly into a designated Slack channel. It eliminates manual checking, reduces email clutter, and ensures everyone who needs to know is informed in real-time.

The result is a more proactive, aligned, and efficient financial operation. Your team can celebrate new sales immediately, support can provide better service knowing an invoice has been sent, and finance has a transparent record of billing activity shared with the wider team.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, reliable automation with just a few key steps.

Step 1: Monitor Xero for New Invoices

The scenario starts with a Make.com webhook or the Xero module watching for new "Invoice Created" events. It acts as a silent sentinel, constantly checking your Xero organization for any new billing activity without requiring any manual login or refresh.

Step 2: Extract Key Invoice Details

Once a new invoice is detected, the workflow pulls essential information from Xero. This typically includes the invoice number, contact name, total amount, date, due date, and a public link to view the invoice directly. This data is structured and prepared for the notification.

Step 3: Format and Send the Slack Message

Make.com then connects to your Slack workspace and posts a message to your chosen channel. The message is formatted to be clear and actionable at a glance, often using Slack's block kit for better readability. It turns raw accounting data into a concise team update.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated #new-invoices channel in Slack for these alerts. This keeps the notifications organized, allows for easy searching, and lets team members mute or join the channel based on their role.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this workflow live in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Xero: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Xero account. Select the correct Xero organization you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Slack: In the Slack module, authorize Make.com to post to your workspace. You'll need to create a Slack app (a simple process guided by Make).
  4. Choose Your Channel: Specify the exact Slack channel name (like #invoices) where you want the notifications to appear.
  5. Customize the Message (Optional): Adjust the text and format of the Slack message. You can add emojis, change wording, or include extra fields from Xero.
  6. Turn it On: Activate the scenario. Create a test invoice in Xero to trigger the workflow and verify the Slack message appears correctly.
