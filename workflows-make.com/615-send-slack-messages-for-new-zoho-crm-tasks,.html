What This Workflow Does

For teams using Zoho CRM, task management is critical but often inefficient. New tasks are created for deals, support cases, or projects, but team members might not check the CRM immediately, leading to delays, missed deadlines, and communication gaps. Manually sending emails or Slack messages about each new task is a repetitive, time-consuming chore that distracts from actual work.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge between Zoho CRM and Slack. Every time a task is logged in Zoho CRM—whether it’s a follow-up call, a proposal deadline, or a support ticket action item—this workflow instantly posts a formatted notification to a designated Slack channel. It turns your team chat into a real-time activity feed for your CRM, ensuring everyone is aware of their responsibilities the moment they are assigned.

The business value is clear: reduced administrative overhead, faster response times, improved accountability, and a significant decrease in missed tasks. Teams can focus on execution instead of coordination, leading to better customer experiences and smoother internal operations.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, trigger-based automation built on Make.com. It listens for events in Zoho CRM and performs actions in Slack without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Monitoring Zoho CRM for New Tasks

The scenario starts with a "Watch Tasks" module in Make.com, connected to your Zoho CRM account. It continuously monitors a specific Zoho CRM module (like Tasks, Leads, or Deals) for the creation of new records. You can set filters to watch for specific criteria, such as tasks assigned to a particular team or with a certain priority level.

Step 2: Extracting and Formatting Task Details

Once a new task is detected, Make.com captures all relevant data: the task subject, due date, assigned user, related record (like a contact or deal name), and any custom fields. This data is then structured into a clear, readable format suitable for a Slack message.

Step 3: Posting the Notification to Slack

The formatted data is sent to the "Send Channel Message" module in Make.com, connected to your Slack workspace. The notification is posted to a channel you specify (e.g., #sales-tasks, #team-updates). The message can include @mentions for the assigned person, buttons linking directly back to the task in Zoho CRM, and priority indicators.

Pro tip: Use Slack's message formatting and Make's text aggregator to create rich, actionable notifications. Include a direct "View Task" link to Zoho CRM to drive immediate action.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template in your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Zoho CRM: In the Make scenario, click the Zoho CRM module and authorize the connection by logging into your Zoho account. Select the specific module you want to watch (e.g., "Tasks").
  3. Connect Slack: Authorize the Slack connection in the corresponding module. Choose the Slack channel you want the messages to be sent to.
  4. Customize the Message: Edit the "Create a Message" text aggregator to include the task details you want to see (Subject, Due Date, Assigned To, Link). You can format it using Slack's markdown.
  5. Test and Activate: Create a test task in Zoho CRM and run the scenario once to ensure the notification appears correctly in your Slack channel. Then, turn on the scheduler to run the scenario automatically (e.g., every 5 minutes).
