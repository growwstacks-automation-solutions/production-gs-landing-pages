What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Textline for customer communication, manually transferring new customer details into a spreadsheet is a repetitive, error-prone task. This process often leads to data delays, inconsistencies, and missed opportunities for timely follow-up.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between Textline and Google Sheets. Every time a new customer is created in your Textline account, this Make.com workflow instantly captures their information—such as name, contact details, and notes—and appends it as a new row in your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet. It turns a manual administrative chore into a flawless, hands-free operation.

The result is a live, centralized customer database that is always accurate and up-to-date. Your sales, support, and marketing teams can access the latest information without ever leaving their spreadsheets, enabling better coordination and more informed customer interactions.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action principle, reliably moving data between your apps without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Monitor for New Textline Customers

The scenario starts by watching your Textline account. It uses a "Watch Customers" module that instantly detects whenever a new customer profile is created within your Textline workspace.

Step 2: Extract and Structure Customer Data

Once triggered, Make.com collects all relevant data points from the new customer record. This can include the customer's ID, name, phone number, email, any assigned tags, custom attributes, and the date they were added.

Step 3: Add a Row to Google Sheets

The workflow then takes this structured data and uses the "Add a Row" module for Google Sheets. It connects to your specified spreadsheet and worksheet, mapping each piece of customer information to the correct column, creating a clean, organized new entry.

Pro tip: Use this automated sheet as a source for other processes. For example, set up a separate automation to send a welcome email from your marketing platform whenever a new row appears in this sheet.

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available).
  2. A Textline account with active customers.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Get the template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the scenario to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Textline: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Textline account by logging in and granting permissions.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: In the "Add a Row" module, select and authorize the Google account that owns your target spreadsheet.
  4. Configure the sheet: Select your specific Google Sheets file and worksheet. Map the data from Textline (like "Customer Name", "Phone") to the corresponding columns in your sheet.
  5. Activate and test: Turn the scenario ON. Create a test customer in Textline to trigger the workflow and verify that a new row appears correctly in your spreadsheet.
