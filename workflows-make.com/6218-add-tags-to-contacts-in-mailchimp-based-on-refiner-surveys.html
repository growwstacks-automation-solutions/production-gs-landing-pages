What This Workflow Does

Collecting Net Promoter Score (NPS) feedback is crucial, but its real value is lost if you can't act on it quickly. Manually sorting through survey responses and updating email lists is a tedious, error-prone process that delays critical customer engagement. This creates a gap between hearing from your customers and being able to respond to them meaningfully.

This automation bridges that gap instantly. Whenever a customer submits an NPS survey via Refiner, this Make.com workflow automatically adds a corresponding tag (like "NPS: 9" or "NPS: 3") to their contact profile in your Mailchimp audience. It transforms raw feedback into actionable marketing segments in real-time, eliminating manual work and ensuring no customer insight slips through the cracks.

How It Works

The workflow acts as a smart connector between your survey tool and your email marketing platform, executing a series of steps each time a survey is completed.

Step 1: Capture the Survey Submission

The workflow is triggered the moment a new response is recorded in your Refiner survey. It immediately captures the submitted NPS score (a number from 0-10) and the respondent's email address.

Step 2: Route the Score

Make.com's router module checks the numerical score. Based on your pre-defined logic, it determines the exact tag that needs to be applied in Mailchimp, corresponding directly to the score (e.g., a score of 7 routes to apply the "NPS: 7" tag).

Step 3: Update the Mailchimp Contact

The workflow then searches your designated Mailchimp audience for a contact matching the submitted email address. If found, it adds the specific NPS score tag to that contact's profile. This update happens within seconds of the survey submission.

Pro tip: Use this tagged data to create dynamic segments in Mailchimp. You can instantly build audiences like "All Promoters (9-10)" or "Detractors Needing Follow-up (0-6)" for hyper-targeted email campaigns.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, authorize the connections to your Refiner and Mailchimp accounts.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select your specific Refiner survey from the dropdown in the first module.
  4. Map the Data: Ensure the module capturing the survey response is correctly mapping the 'email' and 'nps_score' variables from Refiner.
  5. Set the Mailchimp Action: In the final module, select your target Mailchimp audience and confirm the tag naming convention (e.g., NPS: [Score]) matches the tags you created in Mailchimp.
  6. Test & Activate: Run a single test by submitting a dummy survey response. Verify the correct tag appears on a test contact in Mailchimp. Once confirmed, activate the scenario.
