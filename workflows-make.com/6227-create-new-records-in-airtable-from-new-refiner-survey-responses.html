What This Workflow Does

Manually copying survey responses from Refiner into a spreadsheet or Airtable is a tedious, error-prone task that delays insights. Customer feedback—especially Net Promoter Score (NPS), star ratings, and qualitative comments—loses its impact if it sits isolated in a survey tool. Teams miss critical signals from detractors and fail to capitalize on praise from promoters.

This automated workflow solves that by creating a seamless bridge between Refiner and Airtable. Every time a customer submits a survey, their response is instantly transformed into a structured record in your Airtable base. Key metrics like NPS score and star rating are captured alongside their open-ended feedback and email address (if provided), creating a centralized, searchable hub of customer sentiment.

The result is real-time visibility into customer experience. Your support, success, and product teams can immediately see feedback trends, identify at-risk accounts, and celebrate wins without ever logging into Refiner or manually exporting a CSV file.

How It Works

The automation is built on Make.com and acts as a intelligent data pipeline between your survey tool and your database.

Step 1: Trigger on New Survey Response

The workflow is initiated the moment a new response is recorded in your Refiner survey. Make.com watches for this event continuously, ensuring no feedback is missed, even outside business hours.

Step 2: Parse and Structure the Data

The raw survey data is processed. The workflow extracts the specific answers to your configured questions: the NPS score (0-10), the star rating, the open-ended "value" feedback, and the respondent's email address.

Step 3: Create a Record in Airtable

The parsed data is then mapped to the corresponding fields in your pre-defined Airtable table. A new row is created containing all the survey response details, timestamped for accurate trend analysis.

Pro tip: Use Airtable's "Group by" and "Filter" views to instantly create segments like "All Detractors (NPS 0-6)" or "5-Star Reviews." You can then link these views to dashboards or set up notifications for your team.

What You'll Need

To use this template, you'll need the following accounts and assets set up:

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available).
  2. An active Refiner account with a survey collecting NPS, star rating, and open feedback.
  3. An Airtable account with a base and a table ready to receive the data.
  4. Your Airtable table must have these columns (field names can be adjusted): Email, NPS, Stars, and What do you value most about our service?
  5. The API connections (keys) for both Refiner and Airtable to authorize the Make.com scenario.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, authorize the connections to your specific Refiner account and Airtable base.
