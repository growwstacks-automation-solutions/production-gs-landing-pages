What This Workflow Does

Managing email list unsubscribes manually is a tedious, error-prone task that can lead to compliance issues and damage your sender reputation. Every time a user fills out a contact form, survey, or dedicated unsubscribe form on your Gravity Forms-powered website, you need to find their email in Mailchimp and manually update their status. This process wastes valuable team time and risks missing requests, which violates regulations like GDPR and CAN-SPAM.

This automated workflow solves that problem entirely. It connects your Gravity Forms form directly to your Mailchimp audience. When a form is submitted—whether it's a contact form with an "unsubscribe" checkbox or a dedicated opt-out page—the automation instantly finds the matching contact in Mailchimp and updates their status to "unsubscribed." This happens in seconds, with zero manual intervention, ensuring legal compliance, improving list hygiene, and freeing your team to focus on more strategic work.

How It Works

The automation acts as a real-time bridge between your website and your email marketing platform. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Form Submission Trigger

The workflow is activated the moment a user submits your designated Gravity Forms form. Make.com watches for new form entries in real-time.

Step 2: Data Extraction & Mapping

The automation extracts the submitted data, most critically the user's email address. It can also capture additional fields like name or unsubscribe reason if your form includes them.

Step 3: Mailchimp Contact Lookup

Using the captured email address, the workflow searches your specified Mailchimp audience to find the existing contact record.

Step 4: Unsubscribe Action

Once the contact is found, the automation sends an immediate update to Mailchimp, changing the contact's subscription status to "unsubscribed." This ensures they will no longer receive marketing emails.

Pro tip: Use a hidden field in your Gravity Forms form to tag the reason for unsubscribing (e.g., "too many emails," "content not relevant"). You can map this to a custom field in Mailchimp for valuable churn analysis.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation live in under 30 minutes. Follow these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, authorize the connections to your specific Gravity Forms site and your Mailchimp account.
  3. Select Your Form: Point the trigger module to the exact Gravity Forms form you want to use for unsubscribes.
  4. Choose Your Mailchimp Audience: In the Mailchimp module, select the audience/list from which contacts should be unsubscribed.
  5. Map the Email Field: Ensure the email address field from your Gravity Forms entry is correctly mapped to the email identifier in the Mailchimp "Update Contact" action.
  6. Test & Activate: Submit a test entry using an email that exists in your Mailchimp audience. Verify the contact's status changes to "unsubscribed," then turn the scenario on.
