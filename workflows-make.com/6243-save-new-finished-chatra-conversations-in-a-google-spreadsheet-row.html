What This Workflow Does

Live chat is a powerful tool for instant customer support, but the valuable data from these conversations often gets lost in the platform's history. Manually copying chat details into a spreadsheet is tedious, error-prone, and rarely gets done consistently. This creates a gap in your customer intelligence—you can't easily analyze support trends, measure agent performance, or follow up with visitors based on their chat topics.

This Make.com automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between Chatra and Google Sheets. Every time a conversation is marked as finished in Chatra, the workflow automatically triggers. It captures key data points—like visitor information, conversation timestamps, the chat summary, and any tags—and appends them as a new, organized row in your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet. This turns transient chat data into a permanent, searchable business asset.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action principle, ensuring zero manual intervention once set up.

Step 1: Monitor Chatra for Finished Conversations

The automation continuously watches your Chatra account for a specific event: a conversation changing its status to "finished." This is the trigger that kicks off the entire process. Make.com polls Chatra at regular intervals, ensuring no completed chat is missed.

Step 2: Extract and Format Conversation Data

Once a finished conversation is detected, the workflow retrieves the relevant data from Chatra. This typically includes the visitor's name and email, the start and end time of the chat, the conversation summary or full transcript, the assigned agent, and any custom tags applied to the chat.

Pro tip: You can customize which data points are captured. Focus on fields that are most valuable for your reporting, like customer issue category, satisfaction rating, or product mentioned.

Step 3: Add a New Row to Google Sheets

The formatted data is then sent to Google Sheets. The workflow locates your specified spreadsheet and appends a new row at the bottom. Each piece of information is placed into its own column, creating a clean, structured log. The template includes a sample spreadsheet structure to get you started immediately.

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.
