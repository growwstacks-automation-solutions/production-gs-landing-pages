What This Workflow Does

Running LinkedIn lead generation campaigns is effective, but manually tracking results is a time-consuming hassle. Marketing and sales teams often waste hours each week logging into LinkedIn, exporting form data, compiling spreadsheets, and emailing summaries. This manual process is prone to delays and errors, causing missed follow-up opportunities and unclear campaign performance.

This automation solves that problem. It acts as your personal reporting assistant, connecting directly to your LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms. Every Monday morning, it automatically fetches all new submissions from the past seven days, counts them, compiles the lead details, and sends a clean, formatted report directly to your Gmail inbox. You get consistent, actionable data without ever having to lift a finger, freeing up your time for strategic work.

How It Works

The workflow runs on a scheduled timer, creating a reliable "set-and-forget" system for lead reporting.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

Every Monday at 9 AM, the workflow automatically activates. This scheduled trigger ensures you start your week with a fresh lead report, providing perfect timing for sales team meetings and weekly planning.

Step 2: Fetch LinkedIn Lead Data

The automation connects to the LinkedIn API using your credentials. It requests all submissions from your specified Lead Gen Form(s) that were collected in the last 7 days. This lookback window is configurable to match your reporting cycle.

Step 3: Process and Count Leads

The system processes the returned data, extracting key details for each lead like name, email, company, and job title. Simultaneously, it counts the total number of new submissions, giving you the crucial "lead count" metric at a glance.

Step 4: Format and Send Email via Gmail

All the compiled information is formatted into a clear, readable email. The subject line states the lead count and date range. The email body lists each new lead with their details. This report is then sent automatically from your connected Gmail account to your designated recipient(s).

Pro tip: Use this email report as a weekly agenda item for your sales huddle. The lead list provides immediate talking points for lead assignment and qualification discussions.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect LinkedIn: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your LinkedIn Ad Account. Select the correct Ad Account and enter your Lead Gen Form ID.
  3. Configure the Schedule: The timer is set for Mondays at 9 AM. Adjust this in the Schedule module if you prefer a different day or time.
  4. Connect Gmail: Authorize the Gmail module with the account you want to send reports from.
  5. Set Email Details: Enter the recipient email address and customize the email subject/body if desired. The template includes placeholders for the lead count and details.
  6. Test & Activate: Run a single test cycle to ensure data flows from LinkedIn to your inbox correctly. Once confirmed, turn the scenario ON.
