What This Workflow Does

For B2B marketers running LinkedIn ad campaigns, understanding lead generation performance is critical. However, manually logging into LinkedIn Campaign Manager every day to count form submissions, then copying that data into a spreadsheet is a tedious, error-prone, and time-consuming task. This process often leads to outdated reports, inconsistent data, and missed optimization opportunities.

This automation solves that problem. It acts as your personal data assistant, connecting directly to your LinkedIn Ads account on a daily schedule. It counts all new leads generated from your Lead Gen Forms within a specified period (like "yesterday"), and automatically exports that clean, accurate count into a designated Google Sheets spreadsheet. The result is a single, always-updated source of truth for your lead generation metrics, ready for analysis, reporting, and decision-making.

How It Works

The workflow runs on a reliable, scheduled trigger within Make.com, performing a series of precise actions to gather and record your data.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

Every day at 8 AM (configurable), the scenario automatically activates. This time-based schedule ensures you start your day with fresh data without lifting a finger.

Step 2: Fetch Lead Data from LinkedIn

The workflow authenticates with your LinkedIn Campaign Manager account via an API connection. It queries the platform for all Lead Gen Form submissions that occurred during the target time frame, such as the previous full day.

Step 3: Process and Count Submissions

Make.com processes the raw data returned from LinkedIn. It filters and aggregates the submissions, calculating a total count for the period. This step transforms API data into a simple, actionable number.

Step 4: Write to Google Sheets

The final count, along with the date and potentially the campaign name, is sent to your pre-configured Google Sheets spreadsheet. The automation finds the next empty row and inserts the new record, building a historical log over time.

Pro tip: Use this automated data to create a live dashboard in Google Sheets or Google Data Studio. Combine lead counts with your ad spend data to automatically calculate your cost-per-lead (CPL) for each campaign, giving you instant ROI visibility.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect LinkedIn Ads: In the first module, click to add a connection. Log into your LinkedIn advertising account when prompted to grant Make.com the necessary API permissions.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: In the module that writes data, authenticate with your Google account and specify the exact spreadsheet and worksheet where you want the data to land.
  4. Configure the Schedule: By default, it runs daily at 8 AM. You can adjust this in the "Schedule" module to any time or frequency that suits your reporting cycle.
  5. Test Run: Execute the scenario once manually to ensure it pulls data correctly and writes it to your sheet. Check that the count and date appear as expected.
  6. Activate: Turn the scenario "ON." It will now run automatically on its schedule, populating your spreadsheet with daily lead counts.
