What This Workflow Does

For online businesses using ClickFunnels to capture leads, manually transferring those contacts to an email marketing service like Smaily is a tedious, error-prone task. It creates delays in follow-up, risks data entry mistakes, and wastes valuable time that could be spent on strategy and sales. This disconnect can cause leads to go cold, reducing the overall effectiveness of your marketing funnel.

This automated workflow solves that problem completely. It acts as a real-time bridge between ClickFunnels and Smaily. Every time a new contact is created within any of your ClickFunnels funnels—whether from an opt-in form, order form, or webinar registration—this Make.com scenario instantly captures that data and adds the contact as a new subscriber to your designated Smaily list. The process is seamless, reliable, and happens in the background 24/7.

How It Works

The automation is built on a simple but powerful trigger-and-action principle within Make.com.

Step 1: The Trigger – New Contact in ClickFunnels

The workflow is initiated by the "Watch Contacts" module from ClickFunnels. This module constantly monitors your ClickFunnels account for any new contact creation events. As soon as a visitor submits their information, the module instantly captures the contact's details, such as name, email address, phone number, and any custom fields from your funnel.

Step 2: The Action – Add Subscriber to Smaily

Immediately after the trigger fires, the workflow routes the captured contact data to the "Add a Subscriber" module in Smaily. This module takes the email address and other mapped fields (like first name) and creates a new subscriber record in your specified Smaily list. The contact is now ready to receive your automated welcome sequence or be added to a segment for targeted campaigns.

Pro tip: Use this automation's data mapping feature to send custom field data from ClickFunnels (like "Product Interest" or "Lead Score") to Smaily. This allows for incredibly personalized and segmented email campaigns right from the start.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template on Make.com and create a copy in your own account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, click on the ClickFunnels module and authorize the connection to your ClickFunnels account. Repeat this process for the Smaily module.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Ensure the ClickFunnels "Watch Contacts" module is set to monitor the correct funnel(s) or all funnels, depending on your needs.
  4. Map the Data: In the Smaily "Add a Subscriber" module, map the fields from ClickFunnels (like `Email` and `First Name`) to the corresponding subscriber fields in Smaily.
  5. Test & Activate: Turn on the scenario and perform a test by filling out one of your ClickFunnels opt-in forms. Check your Smaily list to confirm the new subscriber appears within seconds.
