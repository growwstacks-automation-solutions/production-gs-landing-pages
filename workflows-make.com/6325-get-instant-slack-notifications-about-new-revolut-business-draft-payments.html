What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Revolut Business, keeping track of draft payments is a manual, time-consuming task. Finance teams or founders must constantly log into the Revolut dashboard to check for new payment requests, leading to delays in approvals, potential missed payments, and poor cash flow visibility. This creates friction, especially in remote or fast-paced environments where instant communication is key.

This automation solves that by creating a real-time bridge between your Revolut Business account and your team's communication hub, Slack. Every time a new draft payment is initiated—whether for a supplier invoice, a contractor fee, or any other outgoing transaction—the workflow instantly captures that event and posts a detailed notification to a designated Slack channel or direct message. This eliminates manual checking, puts critical financial information directly where your team works, and accelerates the entire payment approval process.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple but powerful trigger-action principle, ensuring zero latency between a payment being drafted and your team being informed.

Step 1: Monitoring Revolut Business

The automation is triggered at regular intervals, checking the Revolut Business API for any newly created "draft" payments. It acts as a vigilant watchtower, scanning for transactions that require attention without you needing to lift a finger.

Step 2: Capturing Payment Details

When a new draft is detected, the workflow pulls all relevant details: the payment amount, currency, recipient name, reference, and the unique payment ID. This data is structured and prepared for the notification.

Pro tip: You can filter notifications based on amount thresholds (e.g., only alert for payments over $1,000) or specific counterparties to reduce noise for your team.

Step 3: Crafting & Sending the Slack Alert

The workflow then formats the payment information into a clear, actionable Slack message. It can tag specific users or user groups, include a direct link to the payment in Revolut for quick access, and use Slack's formatting to highlight urgent items. The message is posted instantly to your chosen channel.
