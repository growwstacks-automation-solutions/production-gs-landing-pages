What This Workflow Does

If you use Todoist for planning your day and Habitica for building habits, you likely waste time manually copying tasks between the two apps. This disconnect means tasks you carefully organize in Todoist don't benefit from Habitica's powerful gamification—missing out on rewards, experience points, and the social accountability that makes habits stick.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge. Every time you create a new task in Todoist, it automatically appears as a to-do in your Habitica list. This ensures your planned work instantly becomes a trackable habit, complete with due dates, priorities, and the potential for in-game consequences if you don't complete it. It turns your productivity system into a cohesive, motivating loop.

The workflow is designed for individuals and teams who want to leverage the best of both worlds: Todoist's superior task capture and organization, combined with Habitica's behavioral psychology and game mechanics to boost follow-through. It eliminates the friction of managing two separate lists, saving you mental energy and time every single day.

How It Works

The automation runs on Make.com, acting as a real-time translator between Todoist and Habitica.

Step 1: Monitor Todoist for New Tasks

The scenario starts by watching your specified Todoist project or label. Whenever a new task is created, Make.com instantly captures all its details: the task name, description, due date, and priority level.

Step 2: Format Data for Habitica

Make.com then processes this information. It maps the task title to the Habitica to-do name, converts the due date into Habitica's format, and can optionally assign a difficulty level based on Todoist's priority (e.g., a Priority 1 task becomes a "Hard" habit).

Pro tip: You can add a filter in this step to only sync tasks with specific labels (like "#habit" or "#daily"), preventing your Habitica list from getting cluttered with one-off project tasks.

Step 3: Create the To-Do in Habitica

Finally, Make.com uses Habitica's API to create a new to-do item in your account. The task appears in your Habitica list, ready to be checked off. When you complete it in Habitica, you earn experience, gold, and potentially help your party—all for a task you originally planned in Todoist.

Who This Is For

This automation is perfect for productivity enthusiasts, students, remote workers, and self-improvement practitioners who use both apps but hate the manual sync. It's ideal if you find yourself motivated by gamification but need a robust system for capturing and organizing tasks. Teams using Habitica for group accountability can also benefit by having shared Todoist project tasks automatically become team challenges.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier works for moderate task volume).
  2. An active Todoist account (Pro or free).
  3. An active Habitica account (free).
  4. Your Todoist API token (found in Settings > Integrations).
  5. Your Habitica User ID and API Token (found in Settings > API).

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy the template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the template on Make.com and create a copy in your account.
  2. Connect Todoist: In the first module, authenticate with your Todoist API token and select the project or label you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Habitica: In the Habitica module, enter your User ID and API Token to grant Make.com access to create to-dos.
  4. Test the flow: Create a test task in your chosen Todoist project. Within seconds, check your Habitica to-do list to confirm it appears.
  5. Activate the scenario: Turn on the scenario in Make.com. It will now run automatically, syncing new tasks 24/7.
