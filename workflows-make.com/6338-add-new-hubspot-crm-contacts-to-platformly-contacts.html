What This Workflow Does

For businesses using HubSpot as their primary CRM and Platformly for sales engagement or marketing automation, a major operational headache is keeping contact lists synchronized. Manually exporting from HubSpot and importing into Platformly is time-consuming, error-prone, and creates lag where hot leads go cold. This workflow solves that by creating a seamless, real-time bridge between the two systems.

Whenever a new contact is added to a specified list or database in HubSpot, this automation instantly triggers. It captures all the relevant contact data—like name, email, company, and phone number—and uses it to create a perfectly matched new contact record inside your Platformly account. This ensures your sales team can begin their outreach sequences immediately, with no administrative delay, maximizing the chance of conversion.

How It Works

The automation runs on Make.com, acting as the intelligent middleware between HubSpot and Platformly. It follows a simple but powerful sequence.

Step 1: Monitor HubSpot for New Contacts

The workflow starts with a HubSpot module set to watch for new contact creations. This can be configured to trigger on any new contact, or only contacts added to specific lists or with certain properties, giving you precise control over which leads are synced.

Step 2: Map and Prepare Contact Data

Once triggered, the workflow extracts the new contact's information. A router or filter can be added here to qualify the lead (e.g., only sync contacts with an email address). The data is then structured to match the field requirements in Platformly.

Pro tip: Use this step to enrich the data. You can add a step to pull in company details from HubSpot or append tags in Platformly based on the contact's HubSpot lifecycle stage.

Step 3: Create the Contact in Platformly

The prepared data packet is sent to Platformly via its API. The "Create a Contact" module executes, adding the new person to your designated Platformly list or campaign. A success confirmation is sent back through the workflow.

Step 4: Handle Errors and Log Results

The final part of the workflow manages exceptions. If the Platformly creation fails (e.g., duplicate email), the error is caught and can be sent to a Slack channel or logged in a Google Sheet for review, ensuring no lead falls through the cracks.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation live takes about 15 minutes if you have your accounts ready.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Authorize Connections: In the Make.com scenario, connect your HubSpot and Platformly accounts by logging in and granting API permissions.
  3. Configure the Trigger: In the HubSpot module, select the specific list or define the conditions that should trigger the sync (e.g., "Contact Created").
  4. Map the Data Fields: In the Platformly module, map the incoming HubSpot data (like `contact.properties.email`) to the corresponding Platformly contact fields.
  5. Test and Activate: Turn on the scenario and test it by creating a test contact in HubSpot. Verify it appears correctly in Platformly.
