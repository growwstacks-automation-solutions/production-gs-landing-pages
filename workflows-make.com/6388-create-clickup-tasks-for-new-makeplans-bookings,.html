What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical bottleneck for service businesses: the manual gap between a client booking an appointment and your team starting the corresponding work. When a new appointment is scheduled in MakePlans, this workflow instantly creates a tailored task in your ClickUp project list. It bridges your scheduling calendar with your project execution platform, turning a booked time slot into an actionable work item without any human intervention.

The business value is immense. It eliminates the risk of missed follow-ups, ensures every booked client immediately enters your workflow, and drastically reduces the administrative time your team spends copying data from one system to another. For consultancies, agencies, clinics, or any appointment-based service, this means faster project kickoffs, improved client satisfaction, and more billable hours focused on actual service delivery rather than data entry.

Pro tip: This template is especially powerful for businesses offering multi-step services. A booked initial consultation can automatically generate a full project plan in ClickUp, complete with assigned team members and deadline reminders.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action principle, orchestrated by Make.com.

Step 1: New Booking Detection

The Make.com scenario monitors your MakePlans calendar. Whenever a new appointment is booked by an invitee, it captures the event as a trigger. It pulls all relevant details: client name, email, service type, appointment date and time, and any custom notes added during booking.

Step 2: Data Mapping & Transformation

The captured data is formatted and prepared for ClickUp. You can map fields directly—for example, the client's name becomes the ClickUp task name, the appointment date becomes the due date, and the service type can be added as a tag or custom field. This step ensures the information is usable and structured within your project management system.

Step 3: Automated Task Creation

Make.com then uses the ClickUp module to create a new task in your specified list. The task is populated with the mapped data. You can configure it to assign the task to a specific team member, set a priority level, add it to a particular folder, or even attach a pre-defined checklist template based on the service booked.

Pro tip: You can extend this workflow to also send a confirmation email to the client from ClickUp's data, creating a fully automated onboarding sequence from booking to first communication.

What You'll Need

To implement this template successfully, you'll need the following accounts and basic configurations:

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available).
  2. A MakePlans account with an active calendar where appointments are booked.
  3. A ClickUp account with at least one Space, Folder, and List where you want the tasks to be created.
  4. Understanding of the specific data fields you want to transfer (e.g., client name, email, service type, date).
  5. Optional: A predefined ClickUp task template for different service types to ensure consistency.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your automation running in minutes:

  1. Click the "Get This Workflow" button to open the template on Make.com.
  2. Create a new scenario in Make.com and select this template.
  3. Connect your MakePlans account to the scenario and select the specific calendar to monitor.
  4. Connect your ClickUp account and choose the destination List (and optional Folder/Space).
  5. Map the data fields: Configure which MakePlans booking details (like "Invitee Name") correspond to which ClickUp task fields (like "Task Name").
  6. Test the workflow by creating a dummy booking in MakePlans to see if a task appears correctly in ClickUp.
  7. Activate the scenario. Your automation is now live, creating tasks for every new real booking.
