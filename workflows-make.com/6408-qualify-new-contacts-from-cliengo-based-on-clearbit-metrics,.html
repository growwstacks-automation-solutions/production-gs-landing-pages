What This Workflow Does

Every website visitor who engages with your chatbot represents a potential opportunity, but manually researching each one is a massive time sink for sales and marketing teams. This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless, real-time lead qualification pipeline.

The workflow triggers the moment a new contact is captured through your Cliengo website chatbot. It automatically verifies the email address for deliverability, then enriches the lead with valuable firmographic and demographic data from Clearbit—such as company size, industry, funding, and the contact's seniority level. Finally, it creates a fully-formed, scored contact record in your preferred database, ready for your sales team to pursue.

The business value is clear: you eliminate hours of tedious manual research per week, ensure your sales team only spends time on verified, high-potential leads, and dramatically speed up your response time to hot prospects, increasing conversion rates.

How It Works

Step 1: Capture from Cliengo

The workflow is initiated when a visitor on your website provides their contact information through the Cliengo chatbot. Make.com watches for new "user created" events in your Cliengo account, capturing the prospect's name, email, and any other details from the chat conversation.

Pro tip: Configure your Cliengo bot to ask for the company name during the conversation. This simple addition gives the enrichment tools a head start, resulting in even more accurate data.

Step 2: Verify & Enrich the Email

The captured email address is first sent to Hunter (or a similar service) for real-time verification. This step confirms the email is valid and deliverable, filtering out typos or fake addresses before you invest further. Once verified, the email is sent to Clearbit's powerful APIs.

Step 3: Enrich with Clearbit Company & Person Data

Clearbit uses the email domain and address to pull a wealth of data. It identifies the company, providing details like industry, employee count, estimated revenue, and technology stack. It also enriches the person's profile with their likely job title, seniority, and social profiles.

Step 4: Score the Lead & Create a Contact

Based on the enriched data, the workflow applies a simple scoring logic. For example, a lead from a tech company with 50-500 employees might get a high score. It then formats all this information—original chat data, verification status, and enrichment insights—into a complete contact record and creates it in your connected app, such as a CRM, Airtable, or Google Sheets.
