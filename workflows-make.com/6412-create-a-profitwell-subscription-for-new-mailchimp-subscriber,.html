What This Workflow Does

For subscription businesses, a critical gap often exists between marketing efforts and revenue analytics. You might capture hundreds of leads through Mailchimp email campaigns, but if those leads aren't tracked in your subscription analytics platform like ProfitWell, you're flying blind. You can't accurately calculate marketing ROI, track cohort performance, or forecast revenue from specific campaigns.

This automation workflow solves that disconnect. Every time a new subscriber joins your Mailchimp list, this Make.com scenario instantly creates a corresponding subscription record in ProfitWell. It bridges your email marketing funnel with your financial metrics, ensuring your Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR), churn, and customer lifetime value calculations are complete and accurate.

The result is a unified system where a lead's journey from initial sign-up to becoming a paying customer is seamlessly tracked. This eliminates manual spreadsheet updates, reduces data errors, and gives leadership a real-time, holistic view of how marketing activities directly influence the bottom line.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, two-step automation that acts as a real-time bridge between your marketing and finance stacks.

Step 1: Monitor New Mailchimp Subscribers

The workflow starts with a Mailchimp module set to watch for the "Subscriber Added" event. This trigger activates instantly whenever a new contact is added to your specified Mailchimp audience, whether via a signup form, manual import, or API. It captures key data like the subscriber's email address, first name, last name, and the timestamp of their signup.

Pro tip: Use Mailchimp's audience segmentation or tags to filter which subscribers trigger the ProfitWell creation. For example, you might only sync subscribers from a "Product Waitlist" segment to track them as potential customers.

Step 2: Create a Subscription in ProfitWell

Immediately after the trigger, the workflow takes the subscriber data and uses the ProfitWell "Create a Subscription" API action. It maps the Mailchimp contact's email as the unique customer identifier in ProfitWell. You can configure the subscription with a $0 plan (for leads) or assign your actual starter plan, and set the subscription status accordingly. This creates a record that feeds into all of ProfitWell's retention and revenue analytics dashboards.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Mailchimp: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Mailchimp account and select the specific audience you want to monitor.
  3. Connect ProfitWell: In the second module, add your ProfitWell Public and Secret API keys. Then, specify the Plan ID you want to assign to new subscribers (this could be a free/lead plan).
  4. Map the Data: Ensure the subscriber's email from Mailchimp is mapped to the 'customer_id' field in ProfitWell. You can also map first/last name to the subscription notes or custom properties.
  5. Test & Activate: Turn on the scenario and test it by adding a new test contact to your Mailchimp list. Verify that a subscription appears in your ProfitWell dashboard shortly after.
