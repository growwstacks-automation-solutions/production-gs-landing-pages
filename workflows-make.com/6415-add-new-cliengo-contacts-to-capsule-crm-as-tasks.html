What This Workflow Does

Every minute counts when a potential customer reaches out on your website. If that lead sits in a chatbot log while your sales team is busy, you risk losing them to a competitor who responds faster. This automation solves that critical bottleneck by creating a seamless bridge between your Cliengo chatbot and Capsule CRM.

The workflow automatically detects when a new contact is captured through Cliengo—whether they asked a question, requested a demo, or left their email. It then instantly creates a corresponding, prioritized task in Capsule CRM assigned to the right salesperson. This eliminates manual copying, pasting, and task creation, ensuring immediate and consistent follow-up.

Beyond simple data transfer, this automation injects intelligence into your sales process. It can categorize leads based on keywords from the chat, set task due dates, and include the full conversation transcript for context. This turns a passive lead entry into an active, contextualized sales opportunity ready for action.

How It Works

The automation runs on Make.com, acting as the intelligent middleware between Cliengo and Capsule. It follows a reliable, event-driven sequence.

Step 1: Trigger on New Cliengo Contact

The workflow is activated the moment Cliengo registers a new contact. Make.com watches for this event in real-time, capturing all available data points like the visitor's name, email, phone number, the page they were on, and the complete chat transcript.

Step 2: Data Parsing & Enrichment

Make.com then processes this raw data. It can parse intent from the chat messages, assign a lead score, or categorize the inquiry (e.g., "Sales," "Support," "Pricing"). This step ensures the task created in CRM is meaningful and prioritized correctly.

Pro tip: Use this step to filter out spam or bot submissions. You can set a rule to only create tasks for contacts who provided an email address or mentioned specific keywords, saving your sales team time.

Step 3: Create Task in Capsule CRM

Finally, the workflow uses the enriched data to create a new task in Capsule CRM. It maps the Cliengo fields to the appropriate Capsule fields: the contact's details are added, a task description is written using the chat context, a due date is set (e.g., "Follow up within 1 hour"), and the task is assigned to a team member or queue.
