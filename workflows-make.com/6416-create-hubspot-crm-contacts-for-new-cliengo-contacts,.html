What This Workflow Does

Every minute a sales lead sits in a chat widget without being actioned is a minute you risk losing them. Manually copying visitor details from Cliengo into HubSpot is a tedious, error-prone task that slows down your sales team and can lead to missed opportunities.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between your website's front door and your CRM's heart. Whenever a new contact is captured through a Cliengo chat conversation—whether they asked for a demo, downloaded a guide, or just shared their email—this workflow instantly triggers and creates a corresponding, fully-fledged contact record in your HubSpot CRM. The lead's information, including their initial query, is transferred accurately and immediately, ensuring your sales pipeline is always fed with fresh, actionable leads.

The business value is clear: you eliminate a repetitive administrative task, guarantee data consistency, and dramatically reduce the time between lead capture and sales follow-up. This isn't just about saving time; it's about increasing revenue by ensuring no potential customer falls through the cracks.

How It Works

The automation is elegantly simple, running silently in the background to connect two critical business tools.

Step 1: Trigger on New Cliengo Contact

The workflow is activated the moment Cliengo registers a new contact from a chat session. Make.com watches for this event, capturing the payload of data which includes the visitor's name, email address, phone number, the page they were on, and the chat transcript.

Pro tip: Use Cliengo's custom fields to capture specific qualifying information (e.g., "Company Size," "Use Case"). This data can be mapped directly to custom properties in HubSpot for highly targeted follow-up.

Step 2: Create Contact in HubSpot CRM

Make.com then takes the collected data and uses it to create or update a contact in your specified HubSpot portal. It maps the Cliengo fields to the correct HubSpot contact properties (like first name, email, etc.). The new contact is instantly available in HubSpot, ready for your sales team to engage, add to lists, or trigger nurturing sequences.

What You'll Need

To implement this automation, you will need the following accounts and access:

  1. A Make.com account (free or paid plan).
  2. An active Cliengo account with contacts being generated.
  3. A HubSpot account (Sales Hub or Marketing Hub) with administrator or integration access to generate API keys.
  4. The API credentials or OAuth access for both Cliengo and HubSpot to authorize the connection in Make.com.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Authorize Connections: In the Make.com scenario editor, connect your Cliengo and HubSpot accounts by following the OAuth or API key prompts.
  3. Map the Data: Review the data mapping between the Cliengo module and the HubSpot "Create a Contact" module. Ensure fields like email, first name, and last name are correctly aligned.
  4. Test the Flow: Initiate a test by creating a dummy contact in Cliengo or using the "Run once" feature in Make. Verify that a contact appears correctly in your HubSpot CRM.
  5. Activate: Turn on the scenario scheduler. It will now run automatically, watching for new Cliengo contacts in real-time.
