What This Workflow Does

For businesses leveraging email marketing and social proof, a major operational gap exists between acquiring a new lead and systematically asking them for feedback. Manually copying subscriber details from MailChimp into Endorsal is tedious, error-prone, and often forgotten, causing you to miss golden opportunities to collect testimonials from engaged subscribers.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge between your email list and your testimonial platform. It periodically checks your designated MailChimp audience for new subscribers and automatically creates corresponding contact records in Endorsal. This ensures your testimonial pipeline is always populated with fresh, relevant contacts, turning audience growth directly into social proof growth without any manual intervention.

Pro tip: Ensure your MailChimp signup form includes a field for phone number. Endorsal requires a phone number for each contact, and this workflow will map that field directly. If the field is missing, the automation will pause, requiring you to add it to your audience settings.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a scheduled, "watch-and-sync" model, keeping your systems aligned in the background.

Step 1: Scheduled Check for New Subscribers

The automation is triggered at regular intervals you define (e.g., every hour). It connects to your MailChimp account via API and queries your specified audience list, looking for subscribers added since the last check.

Step 2: Data Extraction and Formatting

For each new subscriber found, the workflow extracts key information: email address, first name, last name, and the crucial phone number field. It formats this data into a structure that matches Endorsal's contact creation API requirements.

Step 3: Create Contact in Endorsal

The formatted data is sent to Endorsal's API, where a new contact is created. The contact is instantly available within your Endorsal dashboard, tagged and ready to be included in automated testimonial request campaigns or manual outreach sequences.

Step 4: Logging and Error Handling

The workflow includes modules to log each successful sync and handle exceptions—like a missing phone number—gracefully. You can receive notifications for failures, ensuring you can quickly rectify any issues and maintain a smooth, uninterrupted sync.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free or paid) to host and run the automation.
  2. A MailChimp account with an existing audience list containing subscribers.
  3. An Endorsal account where you want to collect and manage testimonials.
  4. API Keys: A MailChimp API key and an Endorsal API key to authorize the connection.
  5. A Phone Number Field in your MailChimp audience. This is mandatory for Endorsal contacts and must be included in your signup forms.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect MailChimp: In the first module, add your MailChimp connection using your API key and select the specific audience you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Endorsal: In the Endorsal module, authenticate with your Endorsal API key.
  4. Verify Field Mapping: Ensure the data mapping between MailChimp fields (email, name, phone) and Endorsal contact fields is correct. The phone number mapping is critical.
  5. Set the Schedule: Configure the schedule module to run as often as you need (e.g., every 1, 6, or 24 hours).
  6. Test: Run the scenario once manually. Add a test subscriber to your MailChimp list and verify they appear as a contact in Endorsal.
  7. Activate: Turn the scenario on. It will now run automatically based on your schedule.
