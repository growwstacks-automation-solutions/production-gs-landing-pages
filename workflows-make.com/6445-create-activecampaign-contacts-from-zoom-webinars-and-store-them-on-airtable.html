What This Workflow Does

Hosting webinars is a powerful lead generation strategy, but the manual follow-up process is a major bottleneck. Marketing teams often waste hours after each event downloading Zoom registrant lists, cleaning data, uploading contacts to their CRM, and then manually entering details into a separate spreadsheet for analysis. This delay means hot leads go cold, and human errors can corrupt your valuable contact data.

This automated workflow solves that by creating a seamless bridge between your webinar platform and your marketing stack. Whenever a new Zoom webinar begins, this Make.com scenario is instantly triggered. It retrieves all approved registrants, creates or updates their contact records in ActiveCampaign to kickstart email nurturing sequences, and simultaneously adds a structured record for each contact into a designated Airtable base. This turns a multi-hour, error-prone task into a fully hands-off process that runs in the background.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical, reliable sequence to ensure data integrity and timely action.

Step 1: Zoom Webinar Trigger

The workflow is activated the moment a scheduled Zoom webinar starts. Make.com watches for this specific event, ensuring the automation runs at the perfect time to capture the final, approved registration list.

Step 2: Retrieve Registrant Data

The scenario connects to your Zoom account and fetches the list of registrants for that specific webinar. It pulls key details like name, email address, registration time, and any custom questions answered during sign-up.

Step 3: Create/Update ActiveCampaign Contacts

Each registrant is processed individually. The workflow checks if the email already exists in your ActiveCampaign account. If it does, the contact is updated (e.g., tagged as a webinar attendee). If not, a new contact is created. This prevents duplicates and ensures your list stays clean.

Step 4: Add Records to Airtable

In parallel, a detailed record for each contact is sent to your Airtable base. This includes the contact's information, the webinar topic, date, and a timestamp. This creates a searchable, relational database of all webinar leads for future reporting and segmentation.

Pro tip: Use the provided Airtable template as your starting base. It's pre-structured with fields for webinar data, lead status, and follow-up notes, making analysis and team collaboration effortless from day one.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, establish connections (called "connections") for Zoom, ActiveCampaign, and Airtable using your API keys.
  3. Configure the Zoom Module: Select the specific Zoom webinar you want to trigger the automation, or set it to watch all webinars.
  4. Map Your Airtable Base: Point the Airtable module to your base and table, ensuring the field names (like "Email", "Webinar Title") match the data coming from Zoom.
  5. Test and Activate: Run a single test execution with a past webinar to verify contacts flow into ActiveCampaign and Airtable correctly. Then, turn the scenario "ON" to automate future webinars.
