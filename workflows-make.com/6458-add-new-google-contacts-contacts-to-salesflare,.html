What This Workflow Does

For sales and business development teams, contact data often lives in silos. You meet a potential client, add them to your personal Google Contacts, but then that lead gets stuck there, invisible to your sales CRM. This disconnect creates missed opportunities, delays in follow-up, and forces your team to waste hours on manual copy-paste tasks.

This automated workflow solves that problem permanently. It acts as a real-time bridge between Google Contacts and Salesflare. Whenever you or anyone in your organization adds a new contact to a designated Google Contacts list, the automation instantly captures that information and creates a corresponding contact record in Salesflare. This ensures your sales team always has the freshest leads to work with, directly within their primary sales tool.

The business value is clear: faster lead response times, elimination of human error in data entry, and the ability to immediately trigger Salesflare's native automation sequences (like welcome emails or task assignments) based on a new contact's arrival. It turns a fragmented process into a seamless, efficient pipeline.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple but powerful sequence, orchestrated by Make.com.

Step 1: Monitoring Google Contacts

The workflow starts by watching a specific Google Contacts list for any new additions. Make.com polls this list at regular intervals you define (e.g., every 15 minutes or instantly via webhook if configured). When a new contact is detected, the automation proceeds.

Step 2: Extracting Contact Details

Make.com retrieves all relevant data from the new Google Contact entry. This typically includes the contact's full name, email addresses, phone numbers, company, job title, and any notes. This data is structured and prepared for the next step.

Pro tip: Use Google Contact Groups to control which contacts sync. For instance, create a group called "Sales Leads" and configure the automation to watch only that group, preventing personal contacts from cluttering your CRM.

Step 3: Creating the Contact in Salesflare

The workflow then authenticates with your Salesflare account and uses its API to create a new contact. The data from Google Contacts is mapped to the corresponding fields in Salesflare. The contact is instantly added to your Salesflare database, ready for your sales team to engage.

Step 4: Optional Enrichment & Notification

You can extend the workflow to add more intelligence. For example, you could add a step to enrich the contact data with a service like Clearbit before saving to Salesflare, or send a Slack notification to your sales channel announcing a new high-potential lead has been added.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to deploy this template in under 10 minutes.

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template in your Make.com account.
  2. Create a Copy: In Make.com, click "Copy" to create your own version of the scenario.
  3. Connect Google Contacts: In the first module, click to add a connection. Authenticate with your Google account and select the specific Contact list you want to monitor.
  4. Connect Salesflare: In the second module, click to add a connection. Authenticate with your Salesflare API credentials.
  5. Map the Data Fields: Review the data mapping between Google Contacts fields and Salesflare fields. Adjust if necessary (e.g., map "Company" in Google to "Account" in Salesflare).
  6. Test & Activate: Run a single test by adding a dummy contact to your Google list. Verify it appears correctly in Salesflare. Once confirmed, turn the scenario ON.
