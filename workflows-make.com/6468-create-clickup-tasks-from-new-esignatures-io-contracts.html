What This Workflow Does

For businesses that rely on contracts—whether for services, partnerships, or client onboarding—the gap between sending a contract and starting the work is a critical vulnerability. Manually checking for new contracts, copying details, and creating tasks in project management tools like ClickUp is slow, error-prone, and leads to delays. This automation bridges that gap instantly.

This workflow connects eSignatures.io, a popular electronic signature platform, with ClickUp, a powerful project management tool. Every time a new contract is sent out for signature via eSignatures.io, this Make.com scenario automatically triggers and creates a corresponding task in your designated ClickUp workspace. It transforms a contractual event into an immediate, trackable action item for your team.

The business value is clear: it eliminates manual handoffs, reduces administrative overhead, and ensures that project kickoff or service delivery begins the moment a contract is in play. This leads to faster revenue realization, improved client satisfaction, and gives managers complete visibility into the pipeline from signature to execution.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple but powerful sequence, ensuring data flows securely and accurately between your apps.

Step 1: Monitor for New Contracts

The workflow starts with a "Watch Contract" module in Make.com, connected to your eSignatures.io account. It continuously monitors for a new event: a contract being sent out for signatures. As soon as this happens, the scenario is triggered, and data like the contract title, recipient details, and unique ID is captured.

Step 2: Structure the Task Data

Make.com then processes this information. You can map the contract details to the specific fields required in ClickUp. For example, the contract title can become the ClickUp task name, the client email can be added to the description, and a due date can be calculated based on the contract's sent date.

Pro tip: Use this step to add context. You can pull in data from other apps (like your CRM) using the client's email to enrich the task with project value or previous history.

Step 3: Create the Task in ClickUp

The final action is the "Create a Task" module in ClickUp. The workflow sends the structured data to your specified ClickUp workspace, list, and folder. It creates the task with all the predefined details, and can even assign it to a specific team member or set a priority level based on the contract type.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the scenario to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, authorize the connections to your eSignatures.io and ClickUp accounts when prompted.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific eSignatures.io account you want to monitor for new "contract sent" events.
  4. Map the Task Details: In the ClickUp module, choose your target Workspace, Space, Folder, and List. Then, map the data from eSignatures.io to the ClickUp task fields (Name, Description, Assignee, Due Date, etc.).
  5. Test & Activate: Send a test contract in eSignatures.io and verify that a task appears correctly in your ClickUp list. Once confirmed, turn on the scenario to run automatically.
