What This Workflow Does

In sales, speed is everything. The longer a new lead sits unnoticed in your CRM, the colder it gets. Manually checking HighLevel for new opportunities creates delays, missed connections, and lost revenue. This automation solves that by creating a direct, real-time bridge between your HighLevel CRM and your sales team's preferred communication channel: Telegram.

Every time a new opportunity is created in HighLevel—whether from a web form, a call, or an imported list—this workflow instantly triggers a formatted message to a specified Telegram chat or group. This means your sales reps get a mobile notification with key details the moment a potential customer expresses interest, enabling them to respond in minutes, not hours or days.

The business value is clear: reduced lead response time, increased conversion rates, and a more agile, responsive sales process. It transforms your CRM from a passive database into an active alert system, ensuring your team is always the first to engage.

How It Works

This Make.com scenario acts as a vigilant watcher and a swift messenger, operating silently in the background to keep your team informed.

Step 1: Monitoring HighLevel for New Opportunities

The workflow starts with a "Watch Opportunities" module in Make.com, which polls your HighLevel account at regular intervals. It looks for any new records created in the Opportunities module. You can configure it to monitor specific pipelines or locations if needed.

Step 2: Formatting the Alert Message

Once a new opportunity is detected, the workflow extracts relevant data such as the contact name, opportunity title, estimated value, pipeline stage, and a direct link to the HighLevel record. It then structures this information into a clean, readable message tailored for Telegram.

Pro tip: Customize the message template to include emojis (e.g., 🔥 for hot leads) and action-oriented text like "New Lead: Contact within 5 min!" to drive urgency.

Step 3: Sending the Telegram Notification

The formatted message is sent via the Telegram Bot API to a pre-defined chat ID. This could be a private chat with a sales manager, a broadcast channel for the entire team, or a dedicated sales alert group. The notification appears instantly on your team's mobile devices and desktops.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free or paid plan).
  2. A HighLevel account with API access enabled.
  3. A Telegram account and a Telegram Bot created via BotFather.
  4. The Chat ID of the Telegram user or group where you want to receive alerts.
  5. Basic information about your HighLevel opportunities structure (pipeline names, custom fields).

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the scenario to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect HighLevel: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your HighLevel account using OAuth.
  3. Configure the Telegram Bot: In the Telegram module, paste the API token from your BotFather bot and enter the target Chat ID.
  4. Test the Flow: Create a test opportunity in HighLevel and verify the Telegram message arrives correctly.
  5. Activate: Turn on the scenario. It will now run automatically, checking for new opportunities at your set interval.
