What This Workflow Does

For e-commerce businesses, managing shipment tracking is a constant task. After a package is delivered, its tracking number often lingers in your AfterShip dashboard, cluttering your active view and making it harder to spot genuinely delayed shipments. Manually logging into AfterShip to delete each completed tracking is a repetitive, time-consuming chore that distracts from more important work.

This automation solves that by connecting your Telegram messenger directly to your AfterShip account. Now, you or your team can simply send a tracking number to a dedicated Telegram bot, and it will be instantly deleted from your AfterShip dashboard. This turns a multi-step, web-based task into a one-second action you can perform from your phone, whether you're in a warehouse, on the go, or handling customer support.

The workflow empowers your team to maintain a clean, actionable shipping overview without administrative overhead. It’s a perfect example of how automation can eliminate digital busywork, allowing you to focus on growth and customer satisfaction instead of dashboard maintenance.

How It Works

The automation acts as a secure bridge between Telegram and AfterShip, listening for commands and executing them precisely.

Step 1: Trigger from Telegram

Whenever a message is sent to your specific Telegram bot, the Make.com scenario is activated. It captures the text of the message, which should be a valid tracking number you wish to delete.

Step 2: Validate & Process the Command

The workflow checks if the received message is a plausible tracking number (format validation) and may verify the sender is an authorized user. This prevents accidental deletions from unauthorized chats or malformed inputs.

Step 3: Delete in AfterShip

Using the AfterShip API, the automation sends a "delete tracking" request for the provided tracking number. It uses the secure API key you configured, ensuring the action is performed under your account without exposing your login details.

Step 4: Confirm the Action

Once AfterShip confirms the deletion, the workflow can send a confirmation message back to the Telegram chat, notifying the user that the task was completed successfully. If an error occurs (e.g., tracking not found), an error message is returned for troubleshooting.

Pro tip: Use this in a private Telegram group with your customer service team. They can delete trackings right after confirming delivery with a customer, keeping the workflow collaborative and auditable.

  2. An AfterShip Account: A professional or higher plan that provides API access (most paid plans include this).
  3. An AfterShip API Key: Generated from your AfterShip dashboard with permissions to delete trackings.

  5. A Telegram Bot Token: Created via BotFather on Telegram, which will allow Make.com to receive messages.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to deploy this automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Create Your Telegram Bot: Message @BotFather on Telegram, create a new bot, and securely copy the provided HTTP API token.
  3. Get Your AfterShip API Key: Log into your AfterShip account, navigate to Settings > API, and generate a new key with delete tracking permissions.
  4. Connect the Modules: In your Make.com scenario, paste your Telegram Bot Token into the "Telegram" module and your AfterShip API Key into the "AfterShip" module.
  5. Test & Activate: Turn the scenario ON. Send a test tracking number to your bot. Verify it disappears from your AfterShip dashboard and you receive a confirmation message.
