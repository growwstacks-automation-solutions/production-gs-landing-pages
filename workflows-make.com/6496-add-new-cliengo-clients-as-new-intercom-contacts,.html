Automatically Add Cliengo Chat Leads to Intercom

A free Make.com template that syncs new website chat clients to your Intercom contacts every 15 minutes, eliminating manual data entry and speeding up follow-up.

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Cliengo & Intercom · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

Every conversation on your website chat is a potential customer. But when sales and support teams have to manually check Cliengo and then create contacts in Intercom, leads get cold, data gets mistyped, and opportunities are lost. This disconnect between your lead capture tool and your customer communication platform creates a frustrating bottleneck.

This automated workflow solves that by creating a seamless bridge between Cliengo and Intercom. It acts as a diligent assistant that checks for new chat clients at a regular interval (every 15 minutes by default) and instantly creates a corresponding, enriched contact in your Intercom workspace. This means the moment a visitor becomes a lead, your team can see them in Intercom and start a personalized follow-up sequence without lifting a finger.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple but powerful sequence to ensure no lead is missed and all data is accurately transferred.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

The workflow is initiated by a Schedule module set to run automatically every 15 minutes. This acts as the heartbeat of the automation, consistently polling for new data without any manual intervention.

Step 2: Fetch New Cliengo Clients

Once triggered, the scenario connects to your Cliengo account via the Make.com module. It searches for and retrieves a list of new clients (leads) that have been created since the last time the automation ran. This ensures it only processes the most recent conversations.

Step 3: Process and Map Lead Data

Each new client's data is processed. Key information like the visitor's name, email address, phone number, initial message, and the page URL where the chat started is extracted and structured for the next step.

Pro tip: You can easily customize this step within Make.com to include custom fields from Cliengo, such as "Company Name" or "Product Interest," to create even more detailed Intercom contact profiles.

Step 4: Create Contact in Intercom

The workflow then connects to your Intercom account. For each new Cliengo client, it creates a new contact. The mapped data from Cliengo populates the Intercom contact fields, instantly building a prospect profile that your sales and support teams can use.

Step 5: (Optional) Trigger Internal Notifications

As an advanced step, you can extend this template to send an instant notification to a Slack channel or via email when a high-value lead is captured, ensuring your team reacts with lightning speed.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button to open the template on Make.com and create a copy in your own account.
  2. Connect Cliengo: In the first module, click to add a connection. You'll be prompted to log into your Cliengo account and authorize Make.com to access your data.
  3. Connect Intercom: Do the same for the Intercom module, authorizing the connection with your Intercom workspace.
  4. Adjust the Schedule (Optional): By default, it runs every 15 minutes. You can click the Schedule module to change it to hourly, daily, or even more frequently for real-time needs.
  5. Turn it On: Flip the scenario switch to "ON." The automation will now run on its scheduled time, syncing new leads automatically.
