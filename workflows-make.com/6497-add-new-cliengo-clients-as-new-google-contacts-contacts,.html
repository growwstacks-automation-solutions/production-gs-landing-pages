What This Workflow Does

Every conversation on your website chatbot is a potential lead, but manually transferring that information into your contact management system is a slow, error-prone task. This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between Cliengo, your AI-powered chatbot, and Google Contacts, your central address book.

The workflow acts as a silent assistant, checking for new client records in your Cliengo account at regular intervals (default is every 15 minutes). When it finds a new lead—a visitor who provided their contact details during a chat—it automatically formats their information and creates a new, fully populated contact in your specified Google Contacts list. This ensures no lead falls through the cracks due to administrative delay.

The business value is clear: accelerated lead response times. When a contact is instantly available in Google Contacts, your sales team can initiate a follow-up email or call within minutes of the website conversation ending, dramatically increasing conversion rates and improving the customer experience.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple, reliable sequence to transfer data securely and accurately.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

A scheduler module initiates the workflow on a recurring basis. You can set this to run as often as every minute or as infrequently as once a day, depending on your lead volume and need for immediacy.

Step 2: Fetch New Clients from Cliengo

The workflow connects to your Cliengo account via API and retrieves a list of recent clients. It is designed to only fetch records that have been created since the last time the automation ran, ensuring it processes only new, un-synced leads.

Step 3: Map and Format Contact Data

This is the intelligence layer. The automation takes the raw data from Cliengo (like name, email, phone, company) and maps it to the correct fields in Google Contacts. It can also combine fields or add context, such as noting "Source: Website Chatbot" in the contact notes.

Step 4: Create Contact in Google Contacts

The formatted data is sent to the Google Contacts API, which creates a new contact in your chosen Google account. The contact is immediately available in Gmail, Google Calendar, and any other app in the Google Workspace ecosystem.

Pro tip: Use the "Notes" field in Google Contacts to store the initial query or conversation snippet from Cliengo. This gives your sales team invaluable context for a personalized follow-up.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your own Make.com account.
  2. Connect Cliengo: In the first module, click to add your Cliengo account. Make.com will guide you through a secure OAuth login to authorize the connection.
  3. Connect Google Contacts: Repeat the process for the Google Contacts module, signing into the Google account where you want contacts saved.
  4. Configure the Schedule: Open the scheduler and adjust the interval (e.g., every 5, 15, or 60 minutes) based on how quickly you need leads to appear.
  5. Test & Activate: Run the scenario once manually to ensure a test lead flows from Cliengo to Google Contacts correctly. Then, turn the scenario "ON" to let it run automatically.
