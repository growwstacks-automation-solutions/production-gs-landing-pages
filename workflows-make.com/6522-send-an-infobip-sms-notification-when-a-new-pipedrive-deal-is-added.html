What This Workflow Does

In fast-paced sales environments, every minute counts. The gap between a new deal entering your Pipedrive CRM and your sales team becoming aware of it can mean the difference between closing a deal and losing it to a competitor. Manually checking the CRM or relying on slow email notifications creates bottlenecks and delays.

This automation solves that problem by creating an instant, reliable communication bridge. Every time a sales representative or a web form adds a new deal to Pipedrive, this Make.com workflow triggers automatically. It retrieves key deal information—such as the organization name and the primary contact person—and sends it directly as an SMS message via Infobip to designated team members or a sales manager. This ensures your team is alerted in real-time, right on their phones, enabling immediate follow-up.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, three-step automation that operates silently in the background, turning a CRM event into an actionable alert.

Step 1: Monitor Pipedrive for New Deals

The scenario starts with a Pipedrive module set to watch for the "Deal Added" event. It acts as a digital sentry, continuously checking your Pipedrive account. The moment a new deal record is created, the workflow captures all its associated data and passes it to the next step.

Step 2: Format the SMS Message

Here, the workflow takes the raw data from Pipedrive—like the organization name and person—and structures it into a clear, concise SMS message. You can customize this message template to include any other vital details, such as deal value, pipeline stage, or a custom internal note, ensuring the alert provides maximum context.

Step 3: Send the Alert via Infobip

The final step uses the Infobip module to send the formatted message. You configure it with your Infobip API credentials and specify the recipient phone number(s). The SMS is dispatched instantly, landing on your team's mobile devices within seconds of the deal being created in Pipedrive.

Pro tip: Use this workflow to alert different teams based on deal criteria. For example, send high-value deal alerts to senior managers and standard leads to account executives by adding a router after the Pipedrive trigger.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available).
  2. An active Pipedrive account with deals being created.
  3. An Infobip account with SMS capabilities and API credentials.
  4. The phone number(s) of the team members who should receive the alerts.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button to copy the scenario to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Pipedrive: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Pipedrive account and select the specific pipeline you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Infobip: In the SMS module, enter your Infobip API credentials (API key and base URL).
  4. Set the Recipient: Enter the mobile number that should receive the alerts. You can use a single number or map it from a Pipedrive field.
  5. Customize the Message: Edit the text template to include the deal data most relevant to your team.
  6. Turn it On: Activate the scenario. It will now run automatically for every new deal.
