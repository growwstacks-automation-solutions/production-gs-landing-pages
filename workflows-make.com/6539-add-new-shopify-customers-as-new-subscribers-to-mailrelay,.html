What This Workflow Does

For e-commerce businesses, a new customer is the most valuable lead. Yet, many store owners miss the critical opportunity to immediately engage them through email marketing. Manually exporting customer lists from Shopify and importing them into an email service like Mailrelay is tedious, error-prone, and often forgotten.

This automated workflow solves that problem completely. It acts as a silent bridge between your store and your marketing platform. The moment a customer creates an account or completes a purchase on your Shopify store, their contact details are instantly and accurately added as a new subscriber to your designated Mailrelay list. This ensures your email marketing funnel is always populated with warm, qualified leads ready to be nurtured.

The business value is clear: you transform a transactional event into the start of a marketing relationship. This automation directly contributes to higher customer lifetime value by enabling timely follow-up sequences, personalized offers, and brand loyalty campaigns from day one.

How It Works

The automation runs on Make.com, monitoring your Shopify store and acting on new data in real-time.

Step 1: Trigger on New Customer

The workflow is activated by the "Customer Created" event in Shopify. Whenever a new customer profile is saved in your Shopify admin—whether from a checkout, account sign-up, or manual addition—it sends a webhook with all the customer data to Make.com.

Step 2: Map the Customer Data

Make.com receives the customer information, including email, first name, last name, and any other available fields. This step involves mapping the Shopify data fields to the corresponding subscriber fields required by Mailrelay (e.g., email, name).

Pro tip: Use this step to clean or format data. For example, you can combine first and last name into a single "Full Name" field for Mailrelay, or add a default tag like "Shopify Customer" to all imported subscribers for easy segmentation later.

Step 3: Create Subscriber in Mailrelay

The final module uses Mailrelay's API to create a new subscriber in your chosen group or list. The mapped data is sent securely, and the subscriber is added instantly. The workflow can be configured to handle duplicates or errors gracefully, ensuring list hygiene.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Shopify: In the first module, authenticate your Shopify store by logging in or providing API credentials from your Shopify admin.
  3. Connect Mailrelay: In the Mailrelay module, authenticate with your account's API key (found in Mailrelay's Control Panel under "API & SMTP").
  4. Map the Fields: Ensure the customer email and name fields from Shopify are correctly mapped to the subscriber fields in Mailrelay. The template provides a standard mapping you can adjust.
  5. Choose Your List: Select the specific Mailrelay group ID where you want new subscribers to be added.
  6. Test & Activate: Turn on the scenario and perform a test by creating a dummy customer in Shopify. Check your Mailrelay list to confirm the subscriber appears.
