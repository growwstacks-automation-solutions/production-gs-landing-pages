What This Workflow Does

Product teams often struggle with feedback lag. Ideas submitted by customers, support, or sales reps into ProdPad can sit unnoticed until someone manually checks the dashboard. This delay means missed opportunities for quick validation and slows down your entire product development cycle.

This automation solves that by creating a real-time bridge between your product feedback repository (ProdPad) and your team's communication hub (Telegram). Every time a new idea is logged, it instantly triggers a formatted message in your designated Telegram channel or group. This ensures your product managers, designers, and engineers are aware of new input the moment it arrives, fostering immediate discussion and prioritization.

The workflow eliminates the need for manual copy-pasting or scheduled digest emails. It turns product feedback from a static list into a dynamic, conversational stream, integrating seamlessly into your team's daily workflow where they are already collaborating.

How It Works

The automation acts as a vigilant connector, watching for new entries in ProdPad and delivering them precisely to your team's chat.

Step 1: Monitor ProdPad for New Ideas

The workflow continuously watches your ProdPad account using a "Watch Events" or scheduled check module. The instant a new idea record is created, the automation captures all its details: title, description, submitter, tags, and any custom fields you use.

Step 2: Format the Notification Message

Make.com then structures this data into a clear, actionable Telegram message. You can customize this to include a direct link back to the idea in ProdPad, highlight the priority, mention the customer segment, or format it with emojis for quick scanning.

Pro tip: Structure your message with a clear call-to-action like "Review?" or "Needs sizing." This prompts immediate next steps within the chat thread.

Step 3: Send to Your Telegram Channel

Finally, the workflow uses the Telegram Bot API to post the formatted message into your pre-configured Telegram group or channel. Your team sees it appear alongside other conversations, enabling them to react, comment, or assign follow-up tasks instantly, all within the same environment.

What You'll Need

  1. A ProdPad account with API access enabled (typically available on paid plans).
  2. A Telegram account and administrative access to a group or channel where you want notifications posted.
  3. A Telegram Bot Token, which you can create by talking to @BotFather on Telegram. This bot will need to be added to your target group/channel.
  4. A Make.com account (free tier works) to host and run the automation scenario.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect ProdPad: In the first module, authenticate your ProdPad account using your API credentials.
  3. Connect Telegram: In the Telegram module, add the Bot Token you created and specify the exact Chat ID of your target group or channel.
  4. Customize the Message: Edit the "Send Message" module to format the notification how your team prefers. Include key fields like {idea_title} and {idea_link}.
  5. Test & Activate: Turn on the scenario, then create a test idea in ProdPad. Within seconds, you should see a notification appear in your Telegram chat.
