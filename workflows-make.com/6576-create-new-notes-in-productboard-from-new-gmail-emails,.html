What This Workflow Does

For product managers and founders, valuable customer insights are often buried in email inboxes. Feature requests, bug reports, and user feedback sent to support@ or your personal email are critical for shaping your roadmap, but manually copying this information into your product management tool is a tedious, error-prone, and time-consuming process. This disconnect leads to lost ideas, slow response times, and a product roadmap that isn't fully informed by user needs.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between Gmail and Productboard. It acts as a dedicated assistant that monitors your specified Gmail labels or filters, captures every relevant email, and transforms it into a structured note inside your Productboard project. The sender, subject, and body of the email are automatically mapped, ensuring that every piece of feedback is captured, organized, and ready for your team to review, prioritize, and act upon.

How It Works

The workflow runs on a schedule you control, checking for new emails and processing them into your product backlog without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Monitor Your Gmail Inbox

The scenario starts by watching a specific Gmail label or using a search query (like "from:support@customer.com" or "subject:feature request"). Every 15 minutes (or your chosen interval), it retrieves any new, unprocessed emails that match your criteria.

Step 2: Extract and Structure Email Data

For each new email found, the automation extracts key details: the sender's name and email address, the subject line, and the full body content. It can also parse the email to find specific patterns, like a customer's account ID or the severity of a bug report.

Step 3: Create a Note in Productboard

Using the extracted data, the workflow connects to your Productboard account via the API. It creates a new note within your designated project or board. The email subject becomes the note title, the body becomes the description, and the sender's details are added for context, creating a complete, actionable feedback item for your product team.

Pro tip: Use Gmail's powerful filtering to send only high-priority feedback to this automation. For example, create a filter for emails from your "Power Users" segment or containing keywords like "[Feedback]" or "[URGENT]" in the subject.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and clone the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Gmail: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Gmail account. Set up the trigger to watch a specific label or use a custom search query.
  3. Connect Productboard: In the Productboard module, add your API key. Then, specify the Project ID and any other fields you want to populate (like tags or custom fields).
  4. Map the Data: Ensure the email subject, body, and sender information are correctly mapped to the corresponding fields in the Productboard "Create a note" action.
  5. Test and Activate: Run the scenario once to test with a single email. Verify the note appears correctly in Productboard, then schedule the scenario to run automatically (e.g., every 15 minutes).
