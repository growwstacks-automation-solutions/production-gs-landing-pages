Automate LinkedIn Posts from Airtable

Publish content directly to LinkedIn the moment you add it to your Airtable database. Eliminate manual posting and maintain a consistent professional presence.

What This Workflow Does

For professionals, founders, and marketers, maintaining an active LinkedIn presence is non-negotiable, but it’s incredibly time-consuming. The process of drafting a post, copying it, logging into LinkedIn, pasting, adding a link, and hitting publish can take 5-10 minutes per post. Multiply that by several posts a week, and you're losing hours better spent on high-value work.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge between your content planning hub (Airtable) and your publishing platform (LinkedIn). When you or your team adds a new record to a designated Airtable table—containing the post text, a link, and an image URL—the workflow automatically publishes it as a native LinkedIn post on your profile. It turns your Airtable base into a powerful, collaborative content command center.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action principle, orchestrating data between two powerful platforms without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Watch for New Airtable Records

The automation continuously monitors a specific Airtable table for new records. You can design this table with columns like "Post Copy," "Link URL," "Link Title," "Image URL," and "Status." The moment a record is added (or its status is changed to "Ready"), the workflow captures all the data.

Step 2: Format the LinkedIn Post

Make.com takes the raw text and link data from Airtable and structures it into the precise format required by LinkedIn's API. It combines the post copy with the link, ensuring the preview appears correctly. If an image URL is provided, it's attached to create a richer, more engaging post.

Step 3: Publish to Your LinkedIn Profile

Using your pre-authorized LinkedIn connection, the workflow sends the fully formatted post directly to your LinkedIn profile via LinkedIn's official API. The post goes live immediately, or you can build in logic to schedule it for a specific time based on a date field in Airtable.

Pro tip: Use a "Status" field in Airtable (e.g., Draft, Approved, Ready). Configure the automation to only trigger on records marked "Ready." This gives your team an approval workflow before anything goes live.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Airtable: In the first module, add your Airtable connection using your API key and select the base and table to watch.
  3. Connect LinkedIn: Authorize the LinkedIn module with your account. This is a one-time secure login.
  4. Map Your Fields: Match the Airtable field names (e.g., "Post Text," "URL") to the corresponding inputs in the LinkedIn module.
  5. Test & Activate: Add a test record to your Airtable table and run the scenario once to ensure it posts correctly. Then, turn on the schedule to run automatically.
