What This Workflow Does

For businesses operating in or with the Czech Republic, staying updated on the Czech Koruna (CZK) exchange rate is a daily operational necessity. Manual visits to the Czech National Bank (ČNB) website, copying rates, and emailing them to colleagues is a repetitive, time-consuming task that is prone to error and delay.

This automation workflow solves that problem entirely. It acts as your digital finance assistant, connecting directly to the official ČNB data feed on a schedule you set (e.g., every weekday morning). It retrieves the latest, authoritative exchange rates, formats them into a clear, professional email, and sends it automatically to a designated list of recipients—your finance team, procurement officers, or sales managers.

The value is immediate: elimination of manual work, guaranteed accuracy from the primary source, and timely information delivery that enables faster, more informed financial decisions regarding pricing, procurement, and reporting.

How It Works

The workflow follows a simple, reliable sequence triggered by time, ensuring your team gets data when they need it.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

The automation is initiated by a Schedule module in Make.com. You configure it to run at a specific time, such as 9:00 AM on business days. This replaces the need for an employee to remember to check the rates manually.

Step 2: Fetch Data from ČNB

Once triggered, the scenario uses an HTTP or API module to call the Czech National Bank's public exchange rate data feed. It retrieves the official mid-rate for CZK against key currencies like EUR, USD, GBP, and others in a structured JSON or XML format.

Step 3: Parse and Format the Data

The raw data from the bank is processed within Make.com. The workflow extracts the specific currency pairs and rates you care about, often formatting dates and numerical values for better readability. It prepares this data to be inserted into an email template.

Step 4: Send the Email via Gmail

The final step uses the Gmail module to send an email. The workflow populates a pre-designed email template with the parsed rates, adds a subject line (e.g., "Daily ČNB Exchange Rates - [Date]"), and sends it to your predefined list of recipients. The email lands directly in their inbox, ready for use.

Pro tip: Extend this workflow by adding a Router after fetching the data. Create a branch that only sends an alert email if a specific rate (like CZK/EUR) moves beyond a predefined threshold that impacts your costs. This turns a simple report into an intelligent alert system.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation live is a quick, four-step process:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above. This will open the template in your Make.com account where you can copy it to your own scenarios.
