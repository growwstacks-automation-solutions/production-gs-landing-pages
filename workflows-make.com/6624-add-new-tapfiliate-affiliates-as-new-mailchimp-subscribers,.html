What This Workflow Does

Managing an affiliate program involves constant recruitment and communication. Every new affiliate who signs up through your platform, like Tapfiliate, represents a potential revenue stream. However, if they aren’t immediately added to your email marketing list (e.g., MailChimp), you miss the chance to onboard them effectively, share training materials, and keep them engaged with promotions.

This automation solves that gap. It watches for new affiliate registrations in Tapfiliate and instantly adds them as subscribers in your MailChimp list. The process is fully automatic, ensuring no manual copy-pasting of names and emails, no delays in communication, and perfect data consistency between your affiliate management and email marketing systems.

Beyond just adding a contact, this workflow can be configured to map affiliate data like first name, last name, and custom fields into MailChimp merge fields. This allows for personalized email campaigns that address affiliates directly, improving relationship building and program performance from the very start.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-and-action principle, orchestrated by Make.com.

Step 1: Trigger on New Affiliate

The Make.com scenario monitors your Tapfiliate account for new affiliate creations. Whenever a person completes the signup form and is registered as an affiliate in Tapfiliate, the workflow is triggered instantly.

Step 2: Extract Affiliate Details

The workflow retrieves key data from the Tapfiliate response: typically the affiliate's email address, first name, last name, and any other relevant fields you wish to capture (like signup date or referral source).

Step 3: Format Data for MailChimp

Make.com maps the extracted fields to the corresponding MailChimp subscriber fields. It ensures the first name and last name are placed in the correct merge fields, as required by the template. Additional data can be mapped to custom MailChimp fields for segmentation.

Step 4: Add Subscriber to MailChimp List

The formatted data is sent to MailChimp via its API, adding the new affiliate as a subscriber to your specified list. The workflow can also set the subscriber status (e.g., subscribed) and apply tags for easy identification, such as "Affiliate" or "New Signup".

Pro tip: Use MailChimp tags or groups to segment your affiliate list. You can then create automated email sequences specifically for affiliates, sending them commission reports, promotional codes, and training content.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation live takes about 15 minutes.

  1. Get the template: Click "Get This Workflow" above to open the template in your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Tapfiliate: In the first module, add your Tapfiliate API key. Set the trigger to watch for "New Affiliate" events.
  3. Connect MailChimp: In the MailChimp module, add your API key and select the target audience/list.
  4. Map fields: Ensure the data from Tapfiliate (like 'first_name') is correctly mapped to the MailChimp merge fields ('First Name').
  5. Test: Create a test affiliate in Tapfiliate or use historical data to run a test scenario. Verify the subscriber appears correctly in your MailChimp list.
  6. Activate: Turn the scenario on. It will now run automatically for every new affiliate signup.

Pro tip: Before going live, run a few test cycles with dummy data to confirm field mapping. This prevents incorrect data syncing to your main email list.
