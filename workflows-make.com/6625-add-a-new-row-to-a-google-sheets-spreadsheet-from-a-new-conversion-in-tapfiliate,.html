What This Workflow Does

If you run an affiliate or referral program with Tapfiliate, you know that tracking conversions is critical. However, relying solely on Tapfiliate's interface for reporting can be limiting. You might need to share data with your finance team, create custom commission reports, or combine affiliate data with other sales metrics. Manually exporting and copying this data is a tedious, error-prone task that wastes valuable time.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between Tapfiliate and Google Sheets. Every time a conversion is recorded in your Tapfiliate dashboard—whether it's a new sale, a lead, or a sign-up—this workflow triggers automatically. It captures all the essential details like the affiliate's name, commission amount, conversion date, customer information, and campaign source, then appends it as a new, organized row in your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet.

The result is a live, centralized log of all affiliate activity. This gives you a single source of truth that's easy to access, analyze, and share, transforming raw conversion data into actionable business intelligence without any manual effort.

How It Works

The automation is built on Make.com and operates in a simple, reliable sequence. Once set up, it runs silently in the background, ensuring your spreadsheet is always up-to-date.

Step 1: The Trigger – A New Conversion

The workflow is initiated by the "Watch Conversions" module in Make.com. It continuously monitors your Tapfiliate account for any new conversion event. This is an instant trigger, meaning the moment a conversion is logged in Tapfiliate, the automation process begins.

Step 2: Data Extraction & Mapping

Make.com receives the conversion payload from Tapfiliate. This contains a rich set of data points. The workflow is configured to map the most important fields, such as conversion ID, amount, affiliate name and ID, commission value, status, and custom referral data you might be tracking.

Pro tip: You can easily customize which data points are sent to Sheets. Add custom fields like "Promo Code Used" or "Product Category" to make your reports even more powerful.

Step 3: Writing to Google Sheets

The final step uses the "Add a Row" module for Google Sheets. The mapped data is formatted and inserted into the next available row of your specified spreadsheet. You define the column headers once during setup, and every subsequent conversion populates the correct columns automatically, maintaining perfect data structure.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation live in under five minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to copy the ready-made template to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, authorize the connections to your Tapfiliate and Google Sheets accounts when prompted.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select your Tapfiliate account in the "Watch Conversions" module. No further configuration is typically needed here.
  4. Set Up Your Sheet: In the Google Sheets "Add a Row" module, paste the URL of your target spreadsheet and select the specific worksheet (tab). Map the incoming Tapfiliate data fields to your desired column headers.
  5. Test & Activate: Run a single test conversion in Tapfiliate (or use a past one) to ensure data flows correctly into your sheet. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on. It will now run automatically.
