What This Workflow Does

Managing a reseller or partner network involves tracking dozens, if not hundreds, of active subscriptions, renewal dates, and partner-specific tasks. When this data is locked inside Pipedrive's Resellers Portal, it becomes invisible to other teams like support, finance, and executive leadership, leading to missed renewals, poor communication, and operational blind spots.

This free Make.com workflow solves that by creating a live, two-way bridge between Pipedrive and Trello. Every time a subscription is created or updated in your Pipedrive Resellers Portal, this automation instantly creates or updates a corresponding card on a designated Trello board. This transforms raw CRM data into a visual, collaborative project management system that your entire team can use.

The result is a single source of truth for partner health. Sales managers can see upcoming renewals at a glance, support teams can access partner details instantly, and leadership gains real-time visibility into the entire partner ecosystem's performance—all without ever logging into Pipedrive.

How It Works

The automation operates on a trigger-action principle, ensuring your Trello board is always in sync with your Pipedrive data.

Step 1: Monitoring the Pipedrive Resellers Portal

The workflow starts by watching a specific object in your Pipedrive Resellers Portal—typically the "Subscriptions" module. It can be triggered on a schedule (e.g., every hour) or in real-time via webhooks whenever a new subscription is added or an existing one is modified.

Step 2: Extracting and Structuring Partner Data

Once a change is detected, the automation pulls all relevant data: Partner Company Name, Contact Person, Subscription Plan, Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR), Start Date, Renewal Date, and Status. It structures this information into a clean format, ready to be mapped to Trello.

Step 3: Creating or Updating Cards in Trello

The workflow then connects to your specified Trello board. It checks if a card for this subscription already exists (using a unique ID from Pipedrive). If not, it creates a new card in the correct list (e.g., "Active Partners"). If it does exist, it updates the card's details. Critical data like the renewal date is set as the Trello card's due date, automatically triggering Trello's reminder system.

Step 4: Enriching Cards for Actionability

Finally, the automation enriches the Trello card to make it immediately useful. It adds labels based on subscription tier (e.g., "Gold Partner"), creates a checklist for standard onboarding or renewal tasks, and writes a detailed description in the card body with all the Pipedrive data and a direct link back to the original record for quick access.

Pro tip: Use Trello's "Butler" automation rules in conjunction with this workflow. For example, automatically move cards to a "Renewal Due" list when the due date is 7 days away, or assign the card to a specific account manager based on the partner's region.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to copy the template to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Pipedrive: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Pipedrive account. Select the specific Resellers Portal and the "Subscription" object you want to monitor.
