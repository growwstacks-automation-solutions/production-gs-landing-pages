What This Workflow Does

Every time a potential customer fills out your survey, you have a golden opportunity—but only if you can reach them quickly. Manual data entry between your survey tool and your CRM is a slow, error-prone bottleneck that lets hot leads go cold. This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge between Surveybot and Pipedrive.

The workflow listens for new responses in your Surveybot account. The moment a submission comes in, it automatically extracts the respondent's information—like name, email, company, and their specific answers—and creates a fully-fledged "Person" record in your Pipedrive CRM. This means your sales team has a warm, informed lead ready for follow-up in seconds, not hours or days.

Pro tip: Use this automation not just for lead capture, but for lead qualification. Structure your survey to ask key qualifying questions (e.g., budget, timeline, authority). The answers can be added to the Pipedrive person's notes or custom fields, allowing your team to prioritize outreach effectively.

How It Works

The automation is built on Make.com and runs on a simple trigger-action principle. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: The Trigger – A New Survey Response

The workflow is activated whenever a new response is submitted to a designated survey in your Surveybot account. Make.com watches for this event via a webhook or instant poll, capturing the entire response packet as soon as it's available.

Step 2: Data Parsing & Routing

The raw response data is parsed. The automation identifies key fields such as the respondent's email address, first and last name, company, and any other custom questions you've configured. This data is cleaned and formatted to match Pipedrive's expected input structure.

Step 3: Creating the Pipedrive Person

Using the prepared data, the automation makes an API call to your Pipedrive account. It creates a new "Person" record, populating the standard fields (name, email, phone) and can also map survey answers to custom fields or notes within the person's profile. The new lead is now visible to your entire sales team.

Step 4: Optional Enrichment & Notification

After the person is created, you can extend the workflow. For example, you could automatically add the new person to a specific deal or pipeline stage, assign them to a sales owner, or send a notification to your team's Slack channel announcing a new, qualified lead is ready for contact.

What You'll Need

To set up this automation, ensure you have the following ready:

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available).
  2. An active Surveybot account with a survey already created.
  3. A Pipedrive account with admin or API access to create persons.
  4. The API keys or OAuth credentials for both Surveybot and Pipedrive to authorize the connection in Make.com.
  5. A clear mapping plan of which survey questions correspond to which Pipedrive person fields (e.g., "Email" → "Email", "Company Name" → "Org name").

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the pre-built template in your Make.com account.
  2. Authorize Connections: In the Make.com scenario editor, connect your Surveybot and Pipedrive accounts by logging in and granting necessary permissions.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific survey in your Surveybot account that you want to monitor for new responses.
  4. Map the Data: In the "Create a Person" Pipedrive module, map the data from the Surveybot response to the correct Pipedrive fields (e.g., map `respondent.email` to the Pipedrive Email field).
  5. Test & Activate: Use the "Run once" feature to test the workflow with a real or test survey submission. Verify a person appears in Pipedrive. Once confirmed, turn the scenario "ON".
