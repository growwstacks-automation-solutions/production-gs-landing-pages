What This Workflow Does

For course creators and educators using Thinkific, managing students after they enroll often involves a separate, manual process to bring them into your internal project management system. You might be copying email addresses from Thinkific, sending them individually to your team, and then manually adding them as members to a Trello board used for student support, cohort management, or content delivery. This is time-consuming, prone to errors, and delays your ability to engage with students promptly.

This automation solves that by creating a direct, real-time bridge between Thinkific and Trello. Every time a new user signs up or enrolls in a course on your Thinkific site, this workflow instantly triggers. It captures the student's details and automatically adds them as a member to a specified Trello board. This means your team immediately sees who the new student is, can assign them to relevant cards, and begin providing support or resources without any manual intervention.

How It Works

The workflow is built on Make.com and operates on a simple trigger-action principle, ensuring reliability and speed.

Step 1: Monitor for New Thinkific Users

The scenario starts by watching your Thinkific account for the "User Created" event. This could be a new sign-up, a free enrollment, or a paid purchase—any new user in your Thinkific school triggers the workflow instantly.

Step 2: Extract User Information

Make.com retrieves key data about the new user, primarily their email address and name. This information is parsed and prepared to be sent to Trello. You can also map additional fields like the course they enrolled in or their sign-up date if needed for logging.

Step 3: Add User as Trello Board Member

The core action: Make.com connects to your designated Trello board and uses the student's email address to add them as a new member. Trello then sends an invitation to that email, granting them access to the board according to the permissions you've set (e.g., as a normal member or observer).

Pro tip: Use a dedicated "Student Hub" Trello board with pre-made lists like "New Enrollments," "In Progress," and "Course Complete." This automation populates your member list, and you can set up rules to automatically create a welcome card for each new member.

What You'll Need

  1. A Thinkific account with at least one published course or site where users can sign up.
  2. A Trello account and a specific board where you want to add new members. You must be an admin of that board.
  3. A free or paid Make.com account to host and run the automation scenario.
  4. The API keys or OAuth access to connect Make.com to both Thinkific and Trello (Make.com guides you through this simple process).

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, authorize the connections to your Thinkific and Trello accounts when prompted.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select your Thinkific site from the dropdown. The "User Created" trigger is pre-set.
  4. Set the Target Board: In the Trello module, select the exact board where new students should be added as members.
  5. Test and Activate: Use the "Run once" feature to test with a recent student. Check your Trello board to confirm they were added. Then, turn the scenario on to run automatically.
