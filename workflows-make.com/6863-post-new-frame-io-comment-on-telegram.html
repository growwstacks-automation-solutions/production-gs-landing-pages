What This Workflow Does

For creative teams, video producers, and marketing agencies, managing feedback on video content can be a major bottleneck. Comments are scattered within Frame.io, requiring constant manual checking. Team members and clients miss updates, leading to delayed revisions and frustrated stakeholders.

This automation solves that by creating a real-time bridge between Frame.io and Telegram. Every time a client or team member leaves a comment on a video, storyboard, or image in Frame.io, this workflow instantly captures that comment and posts it directly into a designated Telegram group or channel. It transforms passive, siloed feedback into active, conversational alerts that the whole team can see and act upon immediately.

The result is a dramatic acceleration of the review and approval process. Projects move faster, communication is transparent, and nothing falls through the cracks. It's like having a dedicated assistant who ensures every piece of feedback gets the attention it deserves.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, reliable pipeline that connects two powerful apps.

Step 1: Monitor Frame.io for New Comments

The scenario starts by watching a specific Frame.io project or asset for any new comment activity. Using Frame.io's webhook or instant watch feature, Make.com detects the moment a comment is posted, capturing all relevant data like the comment text, commenter's name, timestamp, and a direct link to the asset.

Step 2: Structure the Notification Message

Make.com then formats this raw data into a clear, actionable message. It typically includes a header like "New Frame.io Feedback", the asset name, the comment, who said it, and a clickable link. This step ensures the Telegram message is useful and doesn't require additional context.

Pro tip: Customize the message format to include project codes or priority flags (e.g., "[URGENT]") based on keywords in the comment or the commenter's role, making triage even faster for your team.

Step 3: Post to the Telegram Chat

Finally, the formatted message is sent to a pre-defined Telegram chat via the Telegram Bot API. The notification appears instantly in the group, triggering discussions, assigning tasks, or simply informing the team that client feedback has been received. The entire process from comment to notification takes seconds.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier is sufficient to start).
  2. A Frame.io account with admin or contributor access to the projects you want to monitor.
  3. A Telegram account and a Telegram Bot token (created via @BotFather).
  4. The ID of the Telegram group or channel where you want notifications to appear.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Frame.io: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Frame.io account and select the specific project or asset to watch.
  3. Connect Telegram: In the Telegram module, input your Bot token and the chat ID of your destination group or channel.
  4. Customize the Message: Adjust the text template in the "Compose Message" step to match your team's terminology. Add emojis or formatting for clarity.
  5. Test and Activate: Turn on the scenario and leave a test comment on a Frame.io asset. Within moments, you should see the notification pop up in your Telegram chat.
