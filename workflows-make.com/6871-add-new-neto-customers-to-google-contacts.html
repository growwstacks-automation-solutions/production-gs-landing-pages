What This Workflow Does

For e-commerce businesses using Neto, every new customer represents a valuable opportunity for future sales and relationship building. However, manually copying customer details from your store into your address book or CRM is a tedious, error-prone task that often gets delayed or forgotten. This disconnect means your marketing and support teams are working with outdated or incomplete customer lists.

This automation solves that problem by creating a live bridge between Neto and Google Contacts. The moment a customer completes a purchase or creates an account in your Neto store, their contact information—name, email, phone, and address—is automatically formatted and added as a new contact in your designated Google Contacts list. It turns a manual administrative chore into a zero-touch, background process.

The result is a unified, up-to-date customer directory that's instantly accessible for email campaigns, customer support, and personalized follow-ups. Your team spends less time on data entry and more time on activities that actually grow the business.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, reliable automation built on Make.com. It listens for a specific event in Neto and performs a corresponding action in Google Contacts.

Step 1: Trigger on New Customer

The workflow is activated whenever a new customer record is created in your Neto platform. Make.com watches for this event in real-time using Neto's API.

Step 2: Gather Customer Data

Once triggered, the automation retrieves all relevant details about the new customer. This typically includes their first and last name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address, and any custom fields you've configured in Neto.

Pro tip: You can add a filter module to only sync customers who have made a purchase, or to exclude test orders, ensuring your contact list remains focused on real leads.

Step 3: Format and Create Contact

The workflow then structures this data into the format required by Google Contacts API. It maps Neto fields to the corresponding Google Contact fields (e.g., "Customer Email" becomes "Email address").

Step 4: Add to Google Contacts

Finally, the formatted data is sent to Google Contacts, creating a new contact in your specified Google account. The contact can be placed into a particular contact group (like "Neto Customers") for easy organization.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available). This is the automation platform that will host and run the workflow.
  2. Admin access to your Neto store to generate an API key or set up OAuth connection for Make.com to access customer data.

  4. 5-10 minutes for initial setup to connect your accounts and activate the workflow.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the pre-built template in your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Neto: In the Make.com scenario, click the Neto module. You'll be prompted to sign in to your Neto account and authorize Make.com to access customer data. This is a secure, standard OAuth process.
  3. Connect Google Contacts: Similarly, click the Google Contacts module and sign in to the Google account where you want the new contacts to appear. Grant the necessary permissions.
  4. Review Field Mapping: The template will have default mappings (Neto customer name → Google Contact name). Glance through these to ensure they match your Neto customer field names.
  5. Activate & Test: Turn the scenario ON. To test, create a test customer in your Neto store (or use a recent real one). Within moments, you should see that contact appear in your Google Contacts.
