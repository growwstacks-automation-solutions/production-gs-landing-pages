What This Workflow Does

For marketing teams using SALESmanago, manually transferring leads from sign-up sheets, event registrations, or form submissions is a repetitive, error-prone bottleneck. Valuable leads sit idle in a spreadsheet while your automated nurture sequences wait, costing you engagement and potential sales.

This Make.com workflow solves that by creating a seamless, real-time bridge between Google Sheets and SALESmanago. Every time a new row is added to your designated spreadsheet—whether from a Google Form, a CSV import, or manual entry—the workflow automatically triggers. It reads the contact details, formats the data, and creates a new, fully profiled contact inside your SALESmanago account within seconds.

The result is a fully automated lead onboarding process. Your marketing engine starts working immediately—tagging leads, adding them to segments, and triggering welcome emails—while your team is freed from tedious copy-paste tasks. This ensures data consistency, accelerates time-to-lead, and maximizes the ROI of your marketing automation investment.

How It Works

The automation follows a clean, reliable sequence to move data from a simple spreadsheet to a powerful marketing platform.

Step 1: Monitor the Google Sheet

The workflow starts by watching a specific Google Sheets spreadsheet for any new rows. You define the sheet and worksheet. Make.com checks for updates at regular intervals you set (e.g., every 15 minutes, hourly, or instantly with webhooks if configured). When it detects a new row, it captures all the data in that row.

Step 2: Parse and Structure the Data

Each column in your sheet (like Email, First Name, Last Name, Phone, etc.) is mapped to a corresponding data point. The workflow extracts these values, handling empty cells gracefully. You can also add data transformation steps here, like standardizing phone number formats or capitalizing names, to ensure clean data enters your system.

Pro tip: Use a dedicated "Consent" column (e.g., "Opt-in") in your sheet. Configure the workflow to only process rows where this column is marked "YES," ensuring GDPR/compliance automation from the very first step.

Step 3: Create the Contact in SALESmanago

Using the SALESmanago module in Make.com, the workflow authenticates with your account via API and sends a "create contact" request. It passes all the mapped fields—email as the primary identifier, plus any additional properties and tags you've defined. The contact is created in SALESmanago with the status you specify.

Step 4: Confirm and Log (Optional)

For reliability, you can add a final step to log the success or failure of each operation. This could send a notification to a Slack channel for high-priority leads, update a separate log sheet, or simply mark the original row with a timestamp. This creates an audit trail and allows for easy troubleshooting.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps.

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Google Sheets: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Google account and select the specific spreadsheet and worksheet you want to monitor.
  3. Connect SALESmanago: In the SALESmanago module, enter your API credentials (Key, Secret, Client ID) to establish the secure connection.
  4. Map Your Fields: This is the crucial step. Match each column from your Google Sheet (like "emailAddress") to the correct SALESmanago contact property (like "email").
  5. Test with One Row: Run the scenario once manually with a test row in your sheet. Verify the contact appears correctly in your SALESmanago account.
  6. Activate & Schedule: Turn on the scenario and set its scheduling (e.g., "Run every 15 minutes"). Your automation is now live.
