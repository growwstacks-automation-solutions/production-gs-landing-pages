What This Workflow Does

For e-commerce businesses, a sale is just the beginning of the customer relationship. The real opportunity lies in nurturing that new buyer into a loyal, repeat customer. However, critical customer data from Shopify often remains trapped within your store's admin, invisible to your sales and support teams in Customerly.

This automation bridges that gap. Every time a new order is placed in your Shopify store, this Make.com workflow instantly springs into action. It retrieves the customer's details—name, email, order value, and more—and creates a corresponding lead profile in your Customerly CRM. This eliminates manual data entry, ensures your sales team has immediate visibility into hot leads, and allows marketing to trigger personalized follow-up sequences based on actual purchase behavior.

Instead of treating customers as anonymous transactions, this workflow helps you build a connected customer journey from the first click to post-purchase support and future sales.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, reliable pipeline that moves data seamlessly between your key business platforms.

Step 1: Trigger on New Shopify Order

The scenario is activated the moment a new order is created in your Shopify store. Make.com watches for this event in real-time, ensuring no new customer slips through the cracks.

Step 2: Retrieve the Customer's Details

Using the order ID, the workflow makes an API call back to Shopify to gather the complete customer record associated with that purchase. This includes contact information and relevant order data.

Step 3: Format Data for Customerly

The raw data from Shopify is then mapped and structured into the specific format required by Customerly's API to create a new lead or contact. You can customize which fields are transferred.

Step 4: Create the Lead in Customerly

Finally, the workflow sends the formatted data to Customerly, creating a new lead in your specified project or list. The lead is instantly available for your team to engage with, complete with tags noting their first purchase details.

Pro tip: Enhance this workflow by adding a step to tag leads in Customerly based on the product category they purchased from (e.g., "Tag: Bought_Skincare"). This allows for hyper-targeted email nurturing campaigns later.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Shopify: In the first module, authorize Make.com to connect to your Shopify store using your store's URL and a private app API key.
  3. Connect Customerly: In the final module, authorize the connection to Customerly using your API key, which you can find in your Customerly project settings.
  4. Test the Flow: Create a test order in your Shopify store (or use a real new order) and run the scenario once to ensure a lead appears correctly in your Customerly project.
  5. Activate & Monitor: Turn the scenario on. It will now run automatically for all future orders. Check the scenario's history periodically to ensure it's running smoothly.
