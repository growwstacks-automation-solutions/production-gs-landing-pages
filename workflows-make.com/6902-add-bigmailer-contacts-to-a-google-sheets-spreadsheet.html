What This Workflow Does

For businesses using BigMailer for email marketing, a growing contact list is a sign of success—but managing that data can become a manual, time-consuming chore. Every new sign-up from a website form, lead magnet, or event requires someone to open BigMailer, find the new contact, and copy their details into a master spreadsheet. This process is not only boring but also prone to errors, delays, and creates data silos where your sales or analytics team can't access the latest leads.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a silent bridge between your email marketing platform and your data hub. Every time a new contact is created in your BigMailer account, this Make.com scenario instantly captures that event and appends the contact's information as a new row in your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet. The result is a live, automatically updated backup of your entire audience that's always in sync, without you lifting a finger.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, reliable automation built on Make.com's visual platform. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Trigger on New Contact

The scenario starts by watching your BigMailer account. The moment a new contact is added to any of your lists, the "Watch Contacts" module triggers the entire workflow. This happens in real-time, ensuring no lead is ever missed, even if they sign up at midnight.

Step 2: Gather Contact Details

Make.com retrieves the full set of data for the new contact. This includes their email address, first and last name, subscription date, any tags applied, and custom fields you've set up in BigMailer (like company, phone number, or lead source).

Pro tip: Use BigMailer's custom fields to capture valuable lead qualification data (e.g., "Budget," "Timeframe"). This automation will bring that data into your sheet, turning it into an instant lead scoring tool.

Step 3: Add Row to Google Sheets

The workflow then connects to your Google account and adds a new row to your pre-defined spreadsheet. Each piece of contact information is mapped to a specific column. You control the mapping, so your sheet stays organized exactly how you need it for reporting or further action.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above. This will copy the pre-built scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect BigMailer: In the first module, click to connect your BigMailer account. Make.com will open a secure window for you to log in and grant access.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: Do the same for the Google Sheets module, selecting the Google account where your spreadsheet lives.
  4. Configure Your Sheet: In the Google Sheets module, select your target spreadsheet and worksheet. Then, map the data from BigMailer (like `contact.email`) to the correct column letters in your sheet (like column A).
  5. Test & Activate: Use Make.com's "Run once" feature to test with a sample contact. Verify the data appears correctly in your sheet, then toggle the scenario to "On." It will now run automatically for every new contact.
