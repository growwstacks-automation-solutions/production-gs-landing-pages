What This Workflow Does

For businesses managing field technicians, the gap between on-site work logs and central project planning is a major source of inefficiency. A technician completes a job in Odyssee Field Service, but that information sits isolated until someone manually copies it into ClickUp for scheduling, invoicing, or follow-up. This delay causes scheduling conflicts, billing errors, and frustrated customers waiting for updates.

This automation solves that by creating a live bridge between Odyssee and ClickUp. Every time a new task is logged by a field technician—whether it's a repair, installation, or inspection—this workflow instantly creates a corresponding, detailed task in your designated ClickUp space or list. The field data becomes immediately actionable for your project managers, dispatchers, and support team, turning two separate systems into one cohesive operation.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, reliable automation built on Make.com. It acts as a smart messenger between your field service software and your project command center.

Step 1: Trigger on New Odyssee Task

The scenario starts by watching Odyssee Field Service for new tasks. You can set it to check at frequent intervals (e.g., every 5 minutes) or use a webhook for instant triggering. When a new task is detected, the workflow captures all relevant data: customer information, job description, location, priority, assigned technician, and any custom notes.

Step 2: Format and Prepare the Data

Make.com then processes this data. It can filter tasks based on type or status (e.g., only create ClickUp tasks for "Completed" jobs or "High Priority" requests). It also maps the Odyssee field names to the correct field structure in ClickUp, ensuring the information lands in the right place.

Step 3: Create the Task in ClickUp

The processed data is sent to ClickUp via its API. The workflow creates a new task in your specified List, Folder, or Space. It populates the task name, description, due date (if provided), assigns it to the correct team member, adds relevant tags, and even attaches the customer details to custom fields. The ClickUp task now holds all the context needed for the next steps in your process.

Pro tip: Use this automation as a foundation. You can easily extend it to also notify a Slack channel when a high-priority field task comes in, or to start a time-tracking clock in ClickUp once the task is created.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation live takes about 15-20 minutes. Follow these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make scenario, click on the Odyssee module and authorize the connection by logging into your Odyssee account. Repeat the process for the ClickUp module.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Set the Odyssee module to watch for "New Tasks." Choose your desired filter, such as specific job types or statuses.
  4. Map the Data: In the ClickUp "Create Task" module, select your target Workspace, Space, and List. Then, map the data from Odyssee to the ClickUp task fields (Name, Description, Assignee, Due Date, Custom Fields).
  5. Test and Activate: Run the scenario once in test mode. Create a dummy task in Odyssee and verify it appears correctly in ClickUp. Once confirmed, schedule the scenario to run automatically (e.g., every 5 minutes) and turn it on.
