What This Workflow Does

Every time a potential customer fills out your Typeform—whether it's a contact form, demo request, or survey—you face a critical choice: manually copy their details into your CRM or risk losing the lead to follow-up delays. This manual process is slow, error-prone, and drains valuable sales time.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a digital bridge between Typeform and SharpSpring. The moment a new response is submitted, the workflow instantly triggers, extracts the respondent's information (like email, name, and company), and creates a fully-fledged lead record in your SharpSpring CRM. This means your sales team is notified of a hot lead within seconds, not hours.

The business value is clear: faster lead response times lead to higher conversion rates. By automating this foundational sales process, you ensure no lead falls through the cracks and your team can focus on selling, not administrative data entry.

How It Works

The workflow follows a simple, reliable sequence to capture and qualify leads automatically.

Step 1: Monitor for New Typeform Responses

The automation continuously watches your specified Typeform for any new submissions. It uses the Make.com "Watch Responses" trigger, which activates the instant someone clicks "Submit."

Step 2: Extract and Structure Lead Data

The workflow pulls all the data from the form response. It maps key fields from Typeform (e.g., "What's your email address?") to the corresponding field names SharpSpring expects (e.g., "Email"). This step ensures clean, organized data enters your CRM.

Pro tip: Ensure your Typeform includes a required email field. This is the minimum data needed to create a valid lead in SharpSpring and is the cornerstone of this automation.

Step 3: Create the Lead in SharpSpring

Using the structured data, the automation makes an API call to SharpSpring to create a new lead. It populates the lead's contact information and can also assign custom tags, a lead source (e.g., "Typeform Webinar Sign-up"), or even assign the lead to a specific sales rep based on form answers.

Step 4: Confirm and Log the Action

The workflow can be configured to send a confirmation (like logging the new lead ID to a spreadsheet or sending a notification to a Slack channel) to verify successful creation. This provides an audit trail and peace of mind.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Typeform: In the first module, click the Typeform icon and authenticate your account. Then, select the specific form you want to monitor.
  3. Connect SharpSpring: In the "Create a Lead" module, click the SharpSpring icon. Enter your SharpSpring API credentials (Account ID, Secret Key, Partner ID) when prompted.
  4. Map Your Fields: This is the most important step. In the SharpSpring module, map the data from your Typeform response to the correct SharpSpring lead fields. At a minimum, map the email address.
  5. Test and Activate: Turn on the scenario and submit a test response to your Typeform. Check your SharpSpring lead list within a minute to confirm the new lead appears correctly.
