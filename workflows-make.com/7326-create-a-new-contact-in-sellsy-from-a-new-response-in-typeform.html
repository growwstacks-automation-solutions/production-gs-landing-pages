What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Typeform for lead generation, surveys, or applications, a critical bottleneck occurs after submission: someone must manually copy the respondent's information from Typeform into the company's CRM, like Sellsy. This process is slow, prone to errors, and leads to delayed follow-up, which can cost you sales.

This automated workflow solves that problem completely. It acts as a digital bridge between your Typeform and Sellsy account. Every time a new response is submitted, the workflow instantly triggers, extracts the respondent's data (like name, email, company, and answers), and creates a new, rich contact record in your Sellsy CRM—all within seconds and without any human intervention.

The result is a perfectly synchronized system where your sales team has immediate, accurate access to new leads, allowing them to act while the prospect is most engaged. It transforms a manual, administrative task into a seamless, reliable part of your sales infrastructure.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple, powerful sequence. Once set up, it runs silently in the background, ensuring your CRM is always current.

Step 1: Trigger on New Typeform Response

The workflow is initiated the moment someone submits your Typeform. The Make.com scenario watches your specific Typeform for new entries and immediately captures the entire response packet as a data bundle.

Step 2: Parse and Structure the Response Data

The automation then parses the JSON response from Typeform. It identifies key pieces of information you want to transfer to Sellsy, such as the respondent's email address from "What is your email?", their name from "Full name", and any other custom answers you've configured in your form.

Step 3: Create Contact in Sellsy

Using the structured data, the workflow makes an authenticated API call to your Sellsy account. It instructs Sellsy to create a new contact in your specified database, populating fields like first name, last name, email, phone, company, and even custom notes or tags based on the form answers.

Pro tip: Use this workflow to add lead scoring tags in Sellsy automatically. For example, if a Typeform answer indicates a "high budget," you can configure the automation to add a "High-Value Lead" tag to the new contact, helping your sales team prioritize instantly.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation live in under 10 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the template on Make.com and create a copy in your own account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, click on the Typeform module and authenticate your Typeform account. Then, do the same for the Sellsy module, entering your Sellsy API credentials when prompted.
  3. Select Your Form: In the Typeform trigger module, select the specific Typeform you want to monitor for new responses.
  4. Map Your Data Fields: In the Sellsy "Create a Contact" module, map the data from your Typeform response to the correct Sellsy contact fields. For example, map the answer from "email_question" to Sellsy's "Email" field.
  5. Test and Activate: Turn on the scenario and submit a test response to your Typeform. Check your Sellsy account to confirm the contact was created correctly. Once verified, your automation is live!
