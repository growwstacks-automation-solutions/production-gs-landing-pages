What This Workflow Does

For businesses using ActiveCampaign for email marketing and Placetel for VoIP phone services, a major operational gap exists: contacts live in separate systems. When a new lead signs up via a form or campaign, your sales team has no automatic way to see that lead's information when they call in. This forces manual copying of data, delays follow-ups, and creates a poor customer experience where agents lack context.

This Make.com automation solves that by creating a real-time bridge between ActiveCampaign and Placetel. Every time a new contact is created or updated in ActiveCampaign, the workflow instantly checks if they exist in Placetel. If they don't, it creates a new contact record with their name, email, phone, and custom fields. If they do exist, it updates their details, ensuring your phone system always has the latest information. This turns two isolated tools into a unified customer intelligence platform.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action logic, with smart error handling and data mapping to ensure reliability.

Step 1: ActiveCampaign Triggers the Workflow

The automation watches your ActiveCampaign account for the "Contact Created" or "Contact Updated" event. This trigger fires immediately when a new lead is added from any source—a web form, imported list, or integration.

Step 2: Data is Prepared and Mapped

Make.com captures the contact's full data bundle: name, email address, phone number, custom fields (like Lead Score, Company), and tags. This data is then formatted to match Placetel's contact field structure.

Pro tip: Use a router module to filter out contacts without phone numbers or from specific lists (e.g., unsubscribed) before syncing, to keep your Placetel directory clean and relevant.

Step 3: Check for Existing Placetel Contact

The workflow searches Placetel's contact list using a unique identifier, typically the email address. This prevents duplicate entries and determines whether to "Create" or "Update."

Step 4: Add or Update the Contact

Based on the search result, the automation either creates a brand-new contact in Placetel or updates the existing record with the new information from ActiveCampaign. The contact is then immediately available for your sales or support team to see on incoming calls.

Quick Setup Guide

Once you've cloned the template, follow these steps to configure it for your business.

  1. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, add your ActiveCampaign and Placetel API credentials to their respective modules. Test the connections to ensure they're successful.
  2. Set the Trigger: Configure the ActiveCampaign "Watch Contacts" module to monitor for new or updated contacts. You can optionally filter by specific lists or tags.
  3. Map the Data Fields: In the Placetel module, map the incoming ActiveCampaign data to the correct Placetel contact fields. Ensure phone numbers are in a format Placetel accepts.
  4. Configure the Search Logic: Set the unique identifier (email is recommended) that the workflow will use to search for existing contacts in Placetel to avoid duplicates.
  5. Test with a Sample Contact: Create a test contact in ActiveCampaign and run the scenario once to verify it appears correctly in your Placetel contact directory.
  6. Schedule and Activate: Set the scenario to run in real-time (instant) and turn it on. Your contact sync is now live.
