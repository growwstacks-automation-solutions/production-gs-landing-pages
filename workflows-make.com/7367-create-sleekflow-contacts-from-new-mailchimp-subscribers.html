What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Mailchimp for email marketing and SleekFlow for conversational sales and WhatsApp commerce, a critical gap often exists. A new lead subscribes to your newsletter, but that valuable contact information sits isolated in your marketing platform. Your sales team, operating in SleekFlow, remains unaware, missing the opportunity for immediate, high-conversion follow-up.

This free Make.com template solves that disconnect. It automatically detects every new subscriber added to a specified Mailchimp mailing list and instantly creates a corresponding contact in your SleekFlow account. This seamless sync turns a passive email subscriber into an active lead in your sales pipeline within seconds, eliminating manual data entry and ensuring no lead falls through the cracks.

The business value is clear: accelerated sales cycles and improved marketing ROI. By automating this handoff, you ensure timely engagement, which is proven to dramatically increase conversion rates. How It Works

The automation is built on a simple but powerful trigger-action sequence in Make.com, designed for reliability and ease of use.

Step 1: Monitoring New Mailchimp Subscribers

The scenario starts by watching your chosen Mailchimp list. The moment a new email address is added—whether from a website form, lead magnet, or event sign-up—Make.com captures the event. It pulls all available subscriber data, such as first name, last name, email, and any custom merge fields.

Pro tip: Use Mailchimp's audience segments or tags to qualify leads before they sync. For example, you could modify the trigger to only fire for subscribers who joined via a "High-Intent" form, ensuring your sales team focuses on the hottest leads first.

Step 2: Formatting and Preparing Contact Data

Make.com then processes the raw data from Mailchimp. This step often involves data mapping—ensuring the "FNAME" field from Mailchimp correctly maps to the "First Name" field in SleekFlow. You can also add static data or apply simple logic here, like assigning a default "Source: Mailchimp" tag to the new SleekFlow contact for tracking.

Step 3: Creating the Contact in SleekFlow

Finally, the workflow uses the SleekFlow module in Make.com to create a new contact. The prepared data is sent via SleekFlow's API, instantly populating your contact list. The new lead is now visible to your sales team within SleekFlow, ready for personalized outreach via WhatsApp, Instagram, or other connected channels.

What You'll Need

To implement this template, you'll need the following accounts and access:

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available). This is the automation platform that will host and run the scenario.
  2. A Mailchimp account with at least one audience list. You'll need an API key from Mailchimp to connect it to Make.com.
  3. A SleekFlow account with admin or integration permissions. You'll need an API key from SleekFlow as well.
  4. Basic understanding of which Mailchimp list you want to monitor and how you want contacts to appear in SleekFlow (e.g., which tags or segments to apply).

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to clone the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, establish connections to your Mailchimp and SleekFlow accounts by entering your API keys when prompted.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific Mailchimp audience list you want to monitor for new subscribers.
  4. Map the Data: In the SleekFlow "Create a Contact" module, map the incoming Mailchimp fields (email, first name, etc.) to the correct SleekFlow contact fields. You can add custom fields or tags here.
  5. Test & Activate: Run the scenario once with a test contact to ensure the data flows correctly into SleekFlow. Once verified, turn the scenario to "ON" to let it run automatically.
