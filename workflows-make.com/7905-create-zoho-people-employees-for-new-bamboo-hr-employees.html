What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical HR challenge for businesses using both BambooHR (for hiring and people management) and Zoho People (for payroll, attendance, or other HR functions). When new employees are added to BambooHR, the workflow automatically creates corresponding employee records in Zoho People with all relevant details.

Manual data entry between HR systems is time-consuming and error-prone. Studies show HR teams spend 15-30 minutes per employee copying information between systems, with a 5-10% error rate in manual transfers. This workflow eliminates both problems by ensuring:

  • Instant synchronization of new hires to all HR systems
  • 100% accurate data transfer with zero typos
  • Consistent employee records across platforms
  • Automated compliance with your HR data standards

How It Works

1. Monitors BambooHR for new employees

The workflow checks your BambooHR account at regular intervals (configurable as hourly, daily, etc.) for newly added employees. It identifies any hires that don't yet exist in Zoho People.

2. Maps employee data fields

All relevant employee information from BambooHR is mapped to corresponding fields in Zoho People. This includes personal details, job information, compensation, department, manager, and any custom fields you specify.

3. Creates Zoho People records

The workflow uses Zoho People's API to create complete employee profiles with all transferred data. The system verifies each record was successfully created before proceeding.

4. Handles errors and notifications

If any sync fails due to data issues or system errors, the workflow logs the problem and notifies your HR team via email. You can configure retry attempts and escalation procedures.

Pro tip: Set the workflow to run hourly during peak hiring seasons to ensure new employees gain immediate access to all systems. During slower periods, daily syncs may be sufficient.
