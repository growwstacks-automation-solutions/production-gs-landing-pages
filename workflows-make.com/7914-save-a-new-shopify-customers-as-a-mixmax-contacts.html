What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for ecommerce businesses using Shopify with Mixmax for sales outreach. When new customers complete purchases, their information often gets stuck in Shopify while sales teams waste time manually transferring data to Mixmax for follow-up sequences.

The workflow automatically creates Mixmax contacts whenever new customer records appear in Shopify. It captures all essential details including name, email, phone number, and order information - ensuring your sales team has complete customer profiles immediately available for personalized outreach.

How It Works

1. Shopify Triggers the Workflow

Make.com monitors your Shopify store for new customer creation events. The moment someone completes a purchase and their customer record is saved, the workflow activates.

2. Customer Data Gets Processed

The system extracts all relevant customer information from Shopify including contact details, order history, and any custom fields you've configured. This data gets formatted for Mixmax's contact structure.

3. Mixmax Contact Creation

Using Mixmax's API, the workflow creates a new contact with all the Shopify customer data. You can customize which fields get mapped and how information gets organized in Mixmax.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only sync customers who meet specific criteria (like purchase amount or product type) to focus your sales team's outreach efforts.
