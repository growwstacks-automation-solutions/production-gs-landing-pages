Automatically Sync Google Contacts to Mixmax

Eliminate manual data entry by automatically creating Mixmax contacts whenever new contacts are added to Google Contacts

Get This Workflow → Make.com · CRM Sync · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for sales and marketing teams who use Google Contacts alongside Mixmax for outreach. When contacts are added to Google Contacts (whether manually or through other integrations), they don't automatically appear in Mixmax. This creates manual work for teams who need to maintain contacts in both systems.

The workflow eliminates this friction by instantly creating a corresponding Mixmax contact whenever a new entry appears in Google Contacts. It captures all standard contact fields including name, email, phone numbers, and company information, ensuring your Mixmax campaigns always target the most complete and up-to-date contact list.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors your Google Contacts for new additions using Make.com's Google Contacts trigger. Whenever a new contact is created, the workflow immediately captures all available contact details.

2. Data Formatting

The contact information is formatted to match Mixmax's expected data structure. This includes mapping Google Contact fields to their Mixmax equivalents and ensuring proper formatting of names, email addresses, and other details.

3. Mixmax Contact Creation

Using Mixmax's API through Make.com, the workflow creates a new contact entry with all the captured information. The process happens in seconds, ensuring your sales team has immediate access to new contacts in Mixmax.

Pro tip: Add a filter step to only sync contacts with email addresses if you want to avoid creating Mixmax entries for contacts without email information.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for:

  • Sales teams using Mixmax for email outreach who maintain their primary contact list in Google Contacts
  • Marketing teams running campaigns through Mixmax who need to ensure new leads are automatically available
  • Businesses that receive contacts through various channels (forms, business cards, events) into Google Contacts
  • Teams tired of manually copying contacts between systems or dealing with outdated Mixmax contact lists
