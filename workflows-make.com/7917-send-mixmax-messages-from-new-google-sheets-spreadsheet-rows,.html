Send Mixmax Messages from Google Sheets

Automate personalized email outreach using spreadsheet data

What This Workflow Does

This automation connects Google Sheets with Mixmax to transform spreadsheet rows into personalized email messages. Whenever new data is added to your specified Google Sheet, the workflow automatically sends customized Mixmax emails to your recipients. This eliminates manual copying and pasting while ensuring consistent, timely outreach.

Perfect for sales teams, recruiters, and marketers, this solution maintains the personal touch of hand-written emails while operating at scale. The template includes sample data structure so you can see exactly how to format your spreadsheet for optimal results.

How It Works

1. New Row Detection

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new rows added, checking at intervals you specify (typically every 15-60 minutes).

2. Data Validation

Each new row is checked for required fields like recipient email, subject line, and message content to ensure complete data.

3. Message Assembly

The system merges your spreadsheet data with Mixmax templates, personalizing each message with recipient-specific details.

4. Email Delivery

Mixmax sends the completed message while tracking opens, clicks, and replies - all logged back to your spreadsheet.

Pro tip: Use separate columns in your sheet for first name, company, and other personalization tokens to create highly tailored messages that don't feel automated.
