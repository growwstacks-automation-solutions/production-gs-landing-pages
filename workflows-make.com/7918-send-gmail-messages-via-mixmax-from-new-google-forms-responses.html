What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of sending follow-up emails after someone submits your Google Form. Instead of copying information from each response and pasting it into emails, the system automatically sends a personalized Mixmax message with all the tracking and scheduling capabilities you expect from the platform.

The workflow triggers instantly when a new form submission arrives. It extracts all response data you specify (name, email, answers to key questions) and inserts them into your pre-designed Mixmax email template. The recipient gets a professional, customized message within seconds of submitting your form - without any manual work from your team.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

Make.com monitors your Google Form continuously. The moment someone submits the form, the system captures all their responses as structured data.

2. Response data gets processed

The workflow extracts specific fields you want to include in your follow-up email, like the respondent's name, email address, and answers to key questions.

3. Personalized Mixmax email sends automatically

Using Mixmax's API, the system sends your pre-designed email template with all the variables populated from the form responses. The email includes full Mixmax functionality like open tracking, scheduling options, and attachment support.

4. Delivery confirmation gets logged

The workflow records when each email is successfully sent, creating an audit trail of all automated follow-ups. You can connect this to Google Sheets or your CRM if needed.
