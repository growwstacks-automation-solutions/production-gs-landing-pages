What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your note-taking system (Evernote) and your sales communication platform (Mixmax). Every time you create a new note in a designated Evernote notebook, the workflow automatically adds it as a contact note in Mixmax.

Sales teams often waste valuable time manually copying notes between systems. This workflow eliminates that friction, ensuring customer insights captured in Evernote are immediately available during sales conversations in Mixmax. The automation maintains context by matching notes to the correct contact records based on email addresses or other identifiers.

How It Works

1. New Note Detection

The workflow continuously monitors specified Evernote notebooks for new notes. When a note is added or updated, Make.com detects the change instantly.

2. Note Processing

The system extracts key details from the Evernote note including title, content, creation date, and any relevant tags. It can also parse structured data if your notes follow a consistent format.

3. Contact Matching

Using email addresses or other identifiers present in the note, the workflow locates the corresponding contact in Mixmax. Advanced matching logic ensures notes are attached to the correct records.

4. Note Creation in Mixmax

The system creates a new contact note in Mixmax containing the Evernote content. The note is timestamped and formatted for optimal readability within Mixmax's interface.

Pro tip: Create dedicated Evernote notebooks for different sales stages (e.g., "Discovery Calls", "Product Demos") to maintain organized contact notes in Mixmax.
