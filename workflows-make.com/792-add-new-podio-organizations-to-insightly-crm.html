What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for businesses using both Podio for project management and Insightly as their primary CRM. When new client organizations are added to Podio, they often need to be manually entered into Insightly - a time-consuming process prone to errors and delays.

The workflow automatically detects new organizations in Podio and creates corresponding records in Insightly CRM with all relevant details. It maintains data consistency across platforms, eliminates duplicate entry work, and ensures your sales team has immediate access to new client information.

How It Works

1. New organization detection

The workflow continuously monitors your specified Podio app for newly added organizations. It triggers immediately when a new record appears in your organizations table.

2. Data extraction

Key organization details are pulled from Podio including company name, contact information, custom fields, and any tags or categories assigned. The system can be configured to extract only the specific fields you need transferred.

3. Insightly record creation

The workflow maps the extracted Podio data to corresponding fields in Insightly and creates a new organization record. Field mapping is customizable to match your specific data structure in both systems.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in both systems to store the original Podio item ID. This creates a permanent reference link between the records for future updates.

4. Error handling

The workflow includes validation steps to check for duplicate organizations in Insightly before creation. If errors occur during the transfer, notifications can be sent to your team for manual resolution.
