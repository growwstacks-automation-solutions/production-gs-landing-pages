What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for sales and support teams who communicate with clients via Telegram but need those conversations logged in their CRM (Mixmax). Without automation, agents must manually copy-paste messages, leading to missed notes, inconsistent records, and wasted time.

The workflow automatically detects new Telegram messages and creates corresponding notes in Mixmax, attached to the correct contact. This maintains a complete communication history where your team needs it most, improving response quality and customer experience while saving 5-10 minutes per conversation.

How It Works

1. Telegram Message Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Telegram channels or private chats for new incoming messages through Telegram's API.

2. Contact Matching

Make.com identifies the Mixmax contact associated with the Telegram sender using email, phone number, or custom identifier matching.

3. Note Creation

The system formats the Telegram message with metadata (timestamp, sender) and creates a new note in the matched contact's Mixmax record.

Pro tip: Add a step to automatically tag conversations based on keywords (like "urgent" or "billing") to prioritize follow-ups in Mixmax.
