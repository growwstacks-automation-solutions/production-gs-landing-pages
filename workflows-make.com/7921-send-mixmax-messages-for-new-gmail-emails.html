What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges Gmail and Mixmax to create responsive email sequences without manual intervention. When specific emails arrive in your Gmail inbox—whether customer inquiries, support tickets, or sales leads—the system automatically triggers pre-configured Mixmax messages tailored to each scenario.

Unlike basic email forwarding, this workflow intelligently processes incoming messages. It can filter emails by sender domain, subject keywords, or other criteria before initiating Mixmax sequences. This ensures only relevant communications trigger automated responses while maintaining personalization through dynamic message templates.

How It Works

1. Gmail email monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Gmail account for new messages matching your specified criteria (like containing specific keywords or coming from certain domains).

2. Email processing and filtering

Each qualifying email gets parsed to extract key details—sender address, subject line, body content—which then determine which Mixmax sequence to trigger.

3. Mixmax message triggering

Based on the email content, the system selects and sends the appropriate Mixmax message template, automatically populating it with details from the original email.

Pro tip: Combine this with Mixmax's scheduling features to automatically send follow-up messages at optimal times after the initial response.
