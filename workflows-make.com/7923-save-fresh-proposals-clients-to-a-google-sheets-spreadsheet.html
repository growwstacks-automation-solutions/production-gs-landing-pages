What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying client information from Fresh Proposals to spreadsheets. Every time you onboard a new client in Fresh Proposals, the workflow instantly captures their details and adds them as a new row in your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet.

The template maintains data accuracy by transferring all client fields exactly as they appear in Fresh Proposals, eliminating transcription errors. It works silently in the background, ensuring your client spreadsheet is always current without any manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Fresh Proposals triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Fresh Proposals account for new client creation events. When detected, it immediately retrieves the complete client record.

2. Data is formatted for Google Sheets

The workflow maps Fresh Proposals client fields to your spreadsheet columns, maintaining proper data types and handling special characters.

3. New row added to Google Sheets

The formatted client data is appended as a new row in your specified Google Sheets spreadsheet, with timestamps marking when the record was added.

Pro tip: Use the spreadsheet's filter views to instantly sort clients by proposal status, industry, or other custom fields transferred from Fresh Proposals.
