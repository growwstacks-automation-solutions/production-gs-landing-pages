What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking accepted proposals in spreadsheets. Every time a client accepts a proposal in Fresh Proposals, the workflow instantly creates a new row in your Google Sheets with all relevant details - client name, proposal amount, terms, and acceptance date.

Sales teams and service businesses waste hours each week copying proposal data between systems. This not only consumes valuable time but introduces errors and delays in revenue tracking. The automation ensures your proposal tracker is always up-to-date without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Fresh Proposals Trigger

The workflow monitors your Fresh Proposals account for new "Accepted" status changes. When a proposal changes to Accepted, it captures all proposal details including custom fields.

2. Data Mapping

Make.com extracts key fields from the proposal and maps them to corresponding columns in your Google Sheet. You can customize which fields to include and how they're formatted.

3. Google Sheets Update

The workflow appends a new row to your specified Google Sheet with the proposal data. You can choose to add to an existing sheet or create a new one for each month/quarter.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column to track exactly when each proposal was accepted. This helps with reporting on sales cycle length.
