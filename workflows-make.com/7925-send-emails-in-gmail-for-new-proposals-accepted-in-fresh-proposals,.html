What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for service businesses using Fresh Proposals - the manual process of sending confirmation emails when clients accept proposals. Every time a proposal status changes to "accepted" in Fresh Proposals, the workflow automatically sends a customized email through Gmail.

The system eliminates the risk of missed notifications and ensures clients receive immediate confirmation after accepting your proposal. It pulls relevant proposal details into the email template, maintaining professional communication while saving your team valuable time on administrative tasks.

How It Works

1. Fresh Proposals Trigger

The workflow monitors your Fresh Proposals account for status changes. When a proposal moves to "accepted" status, it triggers the automation.

2. Data Extraction

The system extracts key proposal details including client name, proposal title, acceptance date, and any custom fields you've set up in Fresh Proposals.

3. Email Composition

Using your pre-configured Gmail template, the workflow inserts the extracted proposal details into appropriate email fields (subject line, body content, etc.).

4. Automated Delivery

The completed email sends automatically through your Gmail account, with optional CCs to team members or other stakeholders.
