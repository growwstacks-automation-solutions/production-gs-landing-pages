What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in the sales process - the delay between when a client accepts your proposal and when your team becomes aware of it. Manual checking of proposal status wastes valuable time and can lead to missed opportunities.

The workflow automatically detects when a proposal changes to "Accepted" status in Fresh Proposals and immediately sends a detailed notification to your Slack channel. This ensures your team can begin onboarding or fulfillment processes without delay, capitalizing on the client's enthusiasm.

How It Works

1. Fresh Proposals Triggers the Workflow

The workflow monitors your Fresh Proposals account for any status changes to "Accepted." Make.com checks for these updates at regular intervals (typically every 5-15 minutes).

2. Proposal Data Gets Processed

When an accepted proposal is detected, Make.com extracts key details like client name, proposal value, services requested, and any custom fields you've set up in Fresh Proposals.

3. Notification Sent to Slack

The workflow formats this information into a clean Slack message and posts it to your specified channel. You can customize the message format to include emojis, attachments, or direct links back to the proposal.
