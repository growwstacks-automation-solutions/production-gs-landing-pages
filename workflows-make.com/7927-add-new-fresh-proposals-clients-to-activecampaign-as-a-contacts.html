What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in many sales processes - the delay between a client accepting your proposal and their information being available in your CRM. When a new client is created in Fresh Proposals, this Make.com workflow instantly adds them as a contact in ActiveCampaign with all their relevant details.

The integration eliminates the need for manual data entry, which often leads to errors, delays in follow-up, and lost opportunities. By automating this transfer, your sales team gains immediate visibility into new clients and can begin nurturing them through your established ActiveCampaign sequences without any lag time.

How It Works

1. New client detection

The workflow monitors Fresh Proposals for new client creations, triggering whenever a new client record appears in your account.

2. Data extraction

Make.com extracts all relevant client information including name, email, company details, and any custom fields you've set up in Fresh Proposals.

3. ActiveCampaign sync

The workflow maps the extracted data to corresponding fields in ActiveCampaign and creates a new contact record with all available information.

4. Optional list/tag assignment

You can configure the workflow to automatically add new clients to specific ActiveCampaign lists or apply tags based on criteria like proposal type or deal size.
