What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical bottleneck for service businesses using Google Contacts alongside Fresh Proposals. Every time sales teams add a new prospect or client to Google Contacts, they must manually recreate that contact in Fresh Proposals before sending proposals - a redundant process that wastes valuable time.

The workflow automatically detects new Google Contacts and syncs them to Fresh Proposals as ready-to-use client records. It transfers all essential details including contact name, email, phone number, company information, and any notes - ensuring your proposal tool always has updated client data without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New contact detection

The workflow monitors your Google Contacts in real-time using Make.com's polling trigger. When any new contact gets added to your specified contact groups or labels, it immediately initiates the sync process.

2. Data mapping

Contact fields from Google are intelligently mapped to corresponding fields in Fresh Proposals. You can customize which information transfers and how it's formatted in the destination system.

3. Client creation

The workflow creates a new client record in Fresh Proposals with all mapped contact details. Optional steps can assign default tags, set proposal permissions, or trigger welcome emails.

Pro tip: Add a conditional filter to only sync contacts marked as "Sales Leads" or "Active Clients" to prevent cluttering Fresh Proposals with irrelevant contacts.
