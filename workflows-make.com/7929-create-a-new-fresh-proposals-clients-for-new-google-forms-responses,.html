What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for service businesses that use Google Forms for lead capture and Fresh Proposals for client management. Every time a potential client submits your contact form, questionnaire, or service request form, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding client record in Fresh Proposals with all their information.

Manual data transfer between these systems is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates those problems by instantly moving complete, accurate client data the moment a form is submitted. It ensures no leads fall through the cracks while freeing your team from repetitive administrative work.

How It Works

1. New form submission triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Google Form for new responses. As soon as someone submits the form, the workflow activates and retrieves all the response data.

2. Data is formatted for Fresh Proposals

The system maps each form field to the corresponding client field in Fresh Proposals. This includes basic contact information, company details, and any custom fields you've set up in both systems.

3. Client record is created automatically

Using Fresh Proposals' API, the workflow creates a new client with all the submitted information. The record appears in your Fresh Proposals account exactly as if you had entered it manually.

Pro tip: Add a hidden field in your Google Form to track the lead source. Map this to a custom field in Fresh Proposals for better analytics on which marketing channels drive the best clients.
