What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Pipedrive CRM and Fresh Proposals, automatically creating client records whenever new contacts are added to your sales pipeline. It solves the common problem of having to manually transfer client information between systems, which is time-consuming and prone to errors.

When a sales rep adds a new qualified lead to Pipedrive, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding client profile in Fresh Proposals with all relevant contact details. This ensures your proposal team always has access to current client information when preparing quotes and contracts.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors your Pipedrive account for new contact additions or updates to existing contacts.

2. Data Mapping

Key contact fields from Pipedrive (name, email, company, etc.) are mapped to corresponding fields in Fresh Proposals.

3. Client Creation

The workflow creates a new client record in Fresh Proposals with the mapped data, ready for proposal generation.

4. Error Handling

The system includes checks to prevent duplicates and handles any API errors that might occur during the sync process.
