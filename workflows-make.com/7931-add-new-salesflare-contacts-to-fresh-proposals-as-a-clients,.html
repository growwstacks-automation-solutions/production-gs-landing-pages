What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for sales teams using both Salesflare (CRM) and Fresh Proposals (proposal software). When new contacts are added to Salesflare, they often need to be manually entered into Fresh Proposals before you can create client proposals. This workflow eliminates that manual step.

The automation triggers whenever a new contact is created in Salesflare. It then automatically adds that contact as a client in Fresh Proposals with all relevant contact information. This ensures your sales team can immediately start creating proposals for new leads without switching between systems or copying data manually.

How It Works

1. New contact detection

The workflow monitors your Salesflare account for new contact creations. When Make.com detects a new contact, it captures all available contact details including name, email, company, and any custom fields you've configured.

2. Data formatting

The contact information is formatted to match Fresh Proposals' expected client data structure. This includes mapping Salesflare fields to corresponding Fresh Proposals fields and ensuring proper data types.

3. Client creation

The formatted contact data is sent to Fresh Proposals via its API, creating a new client record. The workflow verifies successful creation and can optionally notify your team via email or Slack.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Fresh Proposals to track which contacts came through this automation. This helps with reporting and troubleshooting.
