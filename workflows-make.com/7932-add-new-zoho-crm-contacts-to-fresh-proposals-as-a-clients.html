What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your CRM and proposal system by automatically creating Fresh Proposals client records whenever new contacts are added to Zoho CRM. It solves the common problem of sales teams wasting time manually copying client details between systems, which often leads to errors, outdated information, and delayed proposal creation.

The workflow triggers instantly when a new contact record appears in Zoho CRM, extracting all relevant client information and pushing it to Fresh Proposals in the correct format. This ensures your proposal team always works with accurate, up-to-date client data without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors your Zoho CRM for newly created contact records using Make.com's instant trigger. It captures the complete contact profile including name, email, phone, company details, and any custom fields.

2. Data Transformation

Contact information is formatted to match Fresh Proposals' expected data structure. The system handles field mapping conversions automatically, ensuring compatibility between the two platforms.

3. Client Creation in Fresh Proposals

Make.com authenticates with your Fresh Proposals account via API and creates a new client record using the transformed Zoho CRM data. The workflow can be configured to include specific contact details relevant to your proposal process.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only sync contacts that reach a specific deal stage, ensuring your proposal team focuses on qualified leads.
