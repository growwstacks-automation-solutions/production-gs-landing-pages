What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common bottleneck where accepted proposals sit in your sales pipeline while your project team waits to begin work. By automatically creating Trello cards the moment a Fresh Proposals deal is accepted, you eliminate manual data entry and accelerate project kickoffs.

The workflow monitors your Fresh Proposals account for newly accepted proposals. When detected, it extracts key details like client name, project scope, and contract value, then creates a corresponding Trello card in your specified board and list. This gives your project team immediate visibility into new work without email alerts or manual card creation.

How It Works

1. Fresh Proposals triggers the workflow

Make.com continuously monitors your Fresh Proposals account for newly accepted proposals. The moment a client accepts a proposal, Fresh Proposals sends this status change to Make.com.

2. Proposal details are extracted

The workflow pulls all relevant information from the accepted proposal including client details, project description, contract value, and any custom fields you've set up in Fresh Proposals.

3. Trello card is created automatically

Using the extracted data, Make.com creates a new card in your specified Trello board and list. You can customize exactly which fields map to Trello card properties like title, description, labels, due dates, and custom fields.

4. Optional team notifications

The workflow can optionally notify your team via Slack, email, or other channels that a new project has been added to Trello, keeping everyone in sync.
