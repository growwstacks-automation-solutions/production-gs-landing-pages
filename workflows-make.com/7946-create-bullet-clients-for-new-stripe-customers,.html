What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for businesses using Stripe for payments and Bullet as their CRM. Every time you gain a new customer in Stripe, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding client record in Bullet with all their contact and billing information.

Manual data entry between payment processors and CRM systems is not only time-consuming but prone to errors. This workflow eliminates that friction by automatically maintaining parity between your payment and customer management systems. Sales teams get immediate visibility into new customers, support teams have accurate contact details, and marketers can segment based on purchase behavior.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

The workflow starts when Stripe detects a new customer through any channel - checkout page, subscription signup, or manual creation in the Stripe dashboard.

2. Data extraction

Make.com receives the customer details via webhook, including name, email, billing address, and payment method information.

3. Bullet client creation

The system maps all relevant Stripe fields to corresponding Bullet client fields and creates a new record in your Bullet account.

4. Verification

Optional error handling ensures the sync completes successfully and can alert you if any issues occur during the process.

Pro tip: Before running the scenario live, trigger the webhook once manually to load all mappable parameters between Stripe and Bullet.
