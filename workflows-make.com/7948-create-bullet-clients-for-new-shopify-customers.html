What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for ecommerce businesses using Shopify with Bullet CRM—the tedious manual process of transferring customer data between platforms. Every time a new customer makes a purchase in your Shopify store, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding client record in Bullet with all their details.

The system runs at scheduled intervals, checking Shopify for new customers and seamlessly adding them to Bullet. This ensures your sales team has immediate access to customer information without waiting for manual imports or risking data entry errors.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Shopify Check

The workflow connects to your Shopify store at regular intervals (configurable from minutes to days) to check for new customers since the last run.

2. Customer Data Retrieval

For each new customer found, the workflow collects all available information including name, contact details, order history, and any custom fields you've set up in Shopify.

3. Bullet Client Creation

The system then creates a new client record in Bullet CRM, mapping Shopify customer fields to their corresponding Bullet fields automatically.

Pro tip: Configure field mappings during setup to ensure Shopify data populates the correct Bullet fields—especially important for custom fields.
