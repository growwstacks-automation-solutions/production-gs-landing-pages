What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common retail challenge of providing customers with accessible purchase receipts while reducing operational overhead. Traditional paper receipts are easily lost, environmentally unfriendly, and require manual handling at checkout. Email receipts often go unread or get marked as spam.

The workflow automatically detects new sales in your Loyverse POS system, extracts the transaction details, and sends them as concise SMS messages via Twilio. Customers receive their receipt instantly on their mobile device - a format they're 5x more likely to check than email. The system runs every 15 minutes by default (configurable) to ensure timely delivery without overwhelming your staff.

How It Works

1. Monitor Loyverse for new receipts

The workflow checks your Loyverse account at regular intervals for new transactions using the Loyverse API. It identifies completed sales that haven't yet been processed for SMS delivery.

2. Extract receipt details

For each new transaction, the system pulls essential information including customer phone number (if collected), items purchased, prices, taxes, payment method, and store location details.

3. Format SMS message

The raw transaction data gets transformed into a customer-friendly SMS format, typically including store name, transaction ID, date/time, item list, subtotal, taxes, and total amount.

4. Send via Twilio

Twilio's API delivers the formatted message to the customer's mobile number. The system logs each sent receipt for tracking and includes error handling for invalid numbers.

Pro tip: Add your loyalty program signup link to each receipt SMS to convert one-time buyers into repeat customers.
