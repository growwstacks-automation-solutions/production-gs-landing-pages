What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical business problem of maintaining clean email lists in Google Sheets. Every time a new row is added to your spreadsheet, the workflow automatically validates the email address using Loqate's professional verification service and updates the sheet with the validation status.

Manual email validation is time-consuming and error-prone. This workflow eliminates hours of tedious work while ensuring your marketing lists, customer databases, and lead sheets remain accurate. The system provides real-time feedback on email validity, catching typos, disposable addresses, and inactive mailboxes before they impact your campaigns.

How It Works

1. New row detection

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new entries. When a row is added with an email address, it triggers the validation process.

2. Loqate validation

Each email address is sent to Loqate's verification API which checks multiple factors including syntax, domain existence, mailbox availability, and risk indicators.

3. Status update

The workflow writes the verification results back to your Google Sheet, typically including validation status (valid/risky/invalid), confidence score, and any specific flags.

Pro tip: Add conditional formatting rules to your Google Sheet to color-code rows based on validation status for instant visual feedback.
