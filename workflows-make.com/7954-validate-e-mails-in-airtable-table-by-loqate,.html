What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical business problem of maintaining clean email lists in Airtable. When new records are added—whether from web forms, imports, or manual entry—the workflow automatically verifies each email address using Loqate's professional validation service.

Invalid addresses are flagged or removed based on your configuration, preventing them from polluting your marketing lists. The solution reduces bounce rates by 60-80% for most businesses while ensuring compliance with email service provider requirements for list hygiene.

How It Works

1. New Record Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Airtable base for new records containing email addresses. This can be configured to watch specific tables or views based on your data structure.

2. Email Extraction

The system identifies the email field(s) to validate, supporting multiple address fields per record if needed. It handles common formatting variations and can process bulk imports.

3. Loqate Verification

Each address undergoes three validation checks: syntax validation, domain verification, and mailbox existence confirmation. Loqate returns a confidence score and detailed validation flags.

4. Conditional Processing

Based on the verification results, the workflow can: update record status, move invalid addresses to a quarantine table, trigger follow-up actions, or delete completely invalid entries.
