What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying payment data from Stripe to accounting spreadsheets. Every time a customer makes a payment through Stripe, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding record in your Zoho Sheets with all relevant transaction details.

Businesses using both Stripe and Zoho Sheets typically waste 5-10 hours per month manually transferring payment data. This workflow eliminates that repetitive work while ensuring perfect data accuracy. The automated sync happens in real-time, so your financial records are always up-to-date.

How It Works

1. New Stripe Charge Trigger

The workflow monitors your Stripe account for new charges using Stripe's API. Whenever a customer payment is processed, the automation immediately captures the event.

2. Data Extraction

Key payment details are extracted including amount, currency, customer email, payment date, invoice ID, and payment status. The workflow can be customized to include additional metadata as needed.

3. Zoho Sheets Record Creation

The payment data gets formatted and added as a new row in your specified Zoho Sheet. You can configure which columns receive which data points for seamless integration with your existing accounting system.
