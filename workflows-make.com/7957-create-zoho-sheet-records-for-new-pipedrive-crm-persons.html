What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for sales teams using Pipedrive CRM who need to maintain updated contact records in Zoho Sheets for reporting, analysis, or operational workflows. Instead of manually exporting and importing data, the Make.com template automatically:

  • Watches for new contacts added to Pipedrive
  • Extracts relevant contact details (name, email, company, etc.)
  • Creates corresponding records in your specified Zoho Sheet
  • Runs on a schedule you define (typically every 15-60 minutes)

For sales managers, this means always having current CRM data available in spreadsheets for territory planning, lead distribution, or performance tracking without any manual work.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Pipedrive Contact

The workflow starts when Make.com detects a new person record in your Pipedrive account. This happens automatically at your configured interval.

2. Data Extraction

Key contact fields are pulled from Pipedrive including standard fields (name, email, phone) plus any custom fields you specify during setup.

3. Zoho Sheets Preparation

The template formats the extracted data to match your Zoho Sheet structure, handling data type conversions as needed.

4. Record Creation

Make.com adds a new row to your designated Zoho Sheet with all mapped contact information, including timestamps for tracking.

Pro tip: Add a "Last Synced" column in your sheet to quickly identify when each contact was updated from Pipedrive.
