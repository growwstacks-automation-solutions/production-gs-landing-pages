What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring new email contacts from Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) to your Google Sheets contact lists. Every time someone engages with your email campaigns through Amazon SES, their contact information automatically appears in your designated spreadsheet.

The workflow eliminates the risk of missed contacts during manual transfers and ensures your marketing team always works with up-to-date contact lists. Whether you're running newsletters, promotional campaigns, or transactional emails, having immediate access to new contacts in your spreadsheet enables faster follow-ups and better campaign tracking.

How It Works

1. Amazon SES detects new contacts

The workflow monitors your Amazon SES account for new contacts added through email interactions, subscriptions, or API submissions.

2. Contact data is processed

Each new contact's information is extracted and formatted according to your Google Sheets column structure, including email addresses, names, and any custom attributes.

3. Data is added to Google Sheets

The formatted contact information is appended as a new row in your specified Google Sheet, with optional timestamping for tracking when contacts were added.

Pro tip: Add a conditional step to verify contact information before spreadsheet entry to maintain data quality.
