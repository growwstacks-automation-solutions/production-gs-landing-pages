Automatically add new contacts from spreadsheets to your email marketing lists

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your lead collection in Google Sheets and your email marketing in Zoho Campaigns. It automatically detects new rows added to your specified Google Sheet and subscribes those contacts to your chosen Zoho Campaigns mailing list.

The workflow solves the common problem of manually transferring contact information between systems, which is time-consuming and prone to errors. By automating this process, you ensure that new leads are added to your email campaigns immediately, improving your marketing response times.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Google Sheets

The workflow regularly checks your specified Google Sheet for new rows, using a timestamp or row ID to identify additions since the last check.

2. Processing New Contacts

Each new row is processed to extract contact information like name, email address, and any other relevant fields you've configured.

3. Adding to Zoho Campaigns

The contact details are then automatically added to your specified Zoho Campaigns mailing list, with all field mappings preserved.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Connect your Google Sheets account in Make.com
  2. Connect your Zoho Campaigns account
  3. Specify which sheet to monitor and which columns contain contact data
  4. Choose the target mailing list in Zoho Campaigns
  5. Set your preferred checking frequency
  6. Activate the workflow

Pro tip: Use a dedicated "Status" column in your Google Sheet to track which contacts have been processed, allowing for easy troubleshooting.
