What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for marketers and businesses collecting leads through Google Forms: ensuring only valid, deliverable email addresses enter your Zoho Campaigns mailing lists. Without validation, forms often collect typos, fake emails, or disposable addresses that hurt your sender reputation and campaign performance.

The workflow automatically monitors new Google Forms responses, validates each email address through ZeroBounce's industry-leading verification service, and only subscribes confirmed valid addresses to your specified Zoho Campaigns list. This maintains list hygiene while eliminating hours of manual verification work.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New Form Responses

The workflow checks your Google Form at regular intervals (typically every 15-60 minutes) for new submissions containing email addresses.

Step 2: Email Validation with ZeroBounce

Each collected email address is sent to ZeroBounce for real-time verification. The service checks for syntax errors, disposable domains, spam traps, and other red flags.

Step 3: Filter Valid Addresses

Only emails that pass ZeroBounce's validation (with statuses like "valid" or "catch-all") proceed to the next step. Invalid addresses are logged separately for review.

Step 4: Add to Zoho Campaigns

Verified emails are automatically added to your designated Zoho Campaigns mailing list along with any additional form data (name, phone, etc.) you want to include.

Pro tip: Configure Zoho Campaigns to send a welcome email immediately after subscription to confirm opt-in and improve engagement rates.
