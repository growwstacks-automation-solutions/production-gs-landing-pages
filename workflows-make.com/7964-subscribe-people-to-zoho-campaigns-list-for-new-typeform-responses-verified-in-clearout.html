What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for marketers and business owners who collect leads through Typeform - ensuring only valid, deliverable email addresses make it into your Zoho Campaigns mailing lists. Without automation, you'd need to manually export Typeform responses, validate each email, then import the clean list - a time-consuming process prone to human error.

The workflow automatically monitors your Typeform for new responses, sends each email address through Clearout's validation service, and only subscribes verified contacts to your specified Zoho Campaigns list. This maintains list hygiene, improves email deliverability, and saves hours of manual data processing each week.

How It Works

1. New Typeform Response Trigger

The workflow checks your Typeform at regular intervals (configurable from 15 minutes to 24 hours) for new submissions containing email addresses.

2. Email Validation with Clearout

Each collected email address is sent to Clearout's API for real-time verification. Clearout checks domain validity, mailbox existence, and filters out disposable/temporary email addresses.

3. Conditional Routing

Based on Clearout's validation results, the workflow routes emails to different paths - valid addresses proceed to Zoho Campaigns, while invalid or risky addresses can be flagged for review.

4. Zoho Campaigns Subscription

Verified email addresses are automatically added to your specified Zoho Campaigns mailing list, with optional custom fields mapped from the Typeform response data.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add tags in Zoho Campaigns based on Typeform responses (like "Webinar Attendee" or "Product Interest") for powerful segmentation in your email campaigns.
