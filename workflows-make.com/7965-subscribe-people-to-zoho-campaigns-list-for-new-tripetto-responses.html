What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in digital marketing operations - the manual transfer of leads from forms to email marketing systems. When potential customers complete your Tripetto forms, their information often sits idle until someone manually exports and imports the data to Zoho Campaigns. This delay costs you precious engagement opportunities.

The workflow automatically detects new Tripetto form submissions and immediately subscribes those respondents to your specified Zoho Campaigns mailing list. It handles the entire process in the background, ensuring every lead enters your email nurture sequence without human intervention. The system checks for new responses at regular intervals (configurable from 5-60 minutes) for real-time list building.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Tripetto for New Responses

The workflow begins by querying Tripetto's API at your specified interval. It checks for any new form submissions since the last run, filtering for completed forms where respondents provided valid email addresses.

2. Validating and Formatting Contact Data

Each new response is processed to ensure data quality. The system verifies email formats, standardizes name fields, and maps your Tripetto form fields to Zoho Campaigns' expected contact fields (first name, last name, custom fields, etc.).

3. Adding Contacts to Zoho Campaigns

Validated contacts are pushed to your designated Zoho Campaigns mailing list via API. The workflow handles all authentication and error checking to ensure successful additions. You can configure whether to update existing contacts or only add new ones.

Pro tip: Use Tripetto's conditional logic to ask additional qualifying questions, then route respondents to different Zoho Campaigns lists based on their answers for better segmentation.

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Tripetto account in the first module
  3. Specify which form(s) to monitor for responses
  4. Connect your Zoho Campaigns account in the final module
