What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of disconnected payment and marketing systems. When customers purchase through Stripe, their information often gets stuck in your payment processor rather than flowing to your email marketing platform. Manually exporting and importing customer lists wastes time and leads to missed opportunities to engage new buyers.

The workflow automatically detects new Stripe customers at regular intervals and subscribes them to your chosen Zoho Campaigns mailing list. This ensures every paying customer immediately enters your email nurturing sequence, receiving welcome messages, product guides, and special offers tailored to their purchase.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Stripe for new customers

The workflow checks your Stripe account at scheduled intervals (e.g., hourly or daily) for newly created customer records. It captures essential information like name, email address, and purchase details.

2. Formatting customer data

Make.com structures the Stripe data to match Zoho Campaigns' expected format. This may include combining first/last names, standardizing date formats, or extracting specific product information for segmentation.

3. Adding to Zoho Campaigns

The workflow uses Zoho Campaigns' API to create new contacts in your specified mailing list. It can apply tags or custom fields based on the customer's purchase behavior for advanced segmentation.

Pro tip: Add a slight delay (15-30 minutes) between purchase and email subscription to avoid overwhelming customers with multiple immediate emails from different systems.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy the template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Stripe account in the first module
  3. Configure which customer fields to monitor (default is email, name)
  4. Connect your Zoho Campaigns account
  5. Select the target mailing list and any default tags
  6. Set your preferred polling interval (start with daily if unsure)
  7. Test with a new Stripe customer to verify the flow
