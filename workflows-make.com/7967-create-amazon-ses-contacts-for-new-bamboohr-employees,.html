What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common HR challenge of manually setting up email communications for new employees. When someone joins your company and their details are entered into BambooHR, this workflow automatically creates them as a verified contact in Amazon SES.

The solution eliminates the lag time between employee onboarding and email system access. IT teams no longer need to manually transfer data between systems, reducing errors and ensuring new hires can receive company communications immediately.

How It Works

1. New Employee Detection

The workflow monitors your BambooHR account for newly added employee records at regular intervals you specify (from every 15 minutes to daily).

2. Data Extraction

Essential contact information (name, email, department) is pulled from the BambooHR record and formatted for Amazon SES requirements.

3. Contact Creation

The system automatically creates the new employee as a verified contact in your Amazon SES account, ready to receive emails.

4. Confirmation & Logging

Each successful contact creation is logged, and optional notifications can be sent to HR or IT teams for verification.
