What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of maintaining accurate email contact lists when new users sign up for your Zendesk support system. Instead of manually exporting and importing contacts between systems, the workflow automatically adds new Zendesk users to your Amazon SES contact list.

Customer support teams waste hours each week on repetitive data entry tasks. This workflow eliminates that manual work while ensuring your email communications have higher deliverability rates by maintaining clean, up-to-date contact lists in Amazon SES. The integration runs in the background at scheduled intervals, requiring zero ongoing maintenance once configured.

How It Works

1. Monitor new Zendesk users

The workflow checks your Zendesk account at regular intervals (configurable from 5 minutes to daily) for newly created user accounts. It captures essential contact information including email addresses, names, and any custom fields you specify.

2. Format data for Amazon SES

Each new user's information gets transformed into the proper format required by Amazon SES's contact API. The workflow handles all necessary data mapping and field conversions automatically.

3. Create SES contacts

The processed contact data gets sent to your Amazon SES account through its API, creating new contacts in your designated contact lists. The workflow includes error handling to manage rate limits or temporary API issues.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add contacts to specific SES contact lists based on Zendesk user tags or organizations for better segmentation.
