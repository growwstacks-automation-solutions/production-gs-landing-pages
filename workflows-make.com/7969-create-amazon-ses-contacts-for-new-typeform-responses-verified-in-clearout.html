What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of maintaining a clean email list when collecting leads through Typeform. Many businesses struggle with form submissions containing typos, disposable emails, or invalid addresses that hurt deliverability rates when imported directly to Amazon SES.

The workflow automatically validates each new Typeform response through Clearout's email verification service before adding contacts to Amazon SES. This ensures your email marketing campaigns only target verified, deliverable addresses while filtering out risky or invalid entries that could damage your sender reputation.

How It Works

1. Typeform Response Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Typeform for new submissions at regular intervals you configure (e.g. every 15 minutes or instantly via webhook).

2. Email Field Extraction

Identifies and extracts the email address from each form response while preserving all other collected data like name, company, and custom fields.

3. Clearout Verification

Submits each email to Clearout for real-time validation, checking for syntax errors, disposable domains, spam traps, and mailbox existence.

4. Result Routing

Based on Clearout's verification score, the workflow either proceeds to Amazon SES contact creation or routes invalid emails to a separate log for follow-up.

5. Amazon SES Integration

Verified emails are automatically added to your specified Amazon SES contact list with all associated form data properly mapped to your contact fields.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to notify your team when high-value form submissions (like enterprise leads) fail verification, allowing manual follow-up attempts.
