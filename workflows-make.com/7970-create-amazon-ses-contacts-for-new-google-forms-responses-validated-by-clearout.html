What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for businesses using Google Forms to collect email addresses - ensuring only valid, deliverable emails enter your Amazon SES contact list. Manual validation is time-consuming and prone to errors, while unverified lists lead to poor deliverability and potential domain reputation damage.

The workflow automatically monitors your Google Form for new responses, validates each email address through Clearout's verification service, and only adds confirmed valid addresses to your Amazon SES contacts. This creates a clean, high-quality email list that improves your marketing results and protects your sender reputation.

How It Works

1. New Response Detection

The Make.com scenario polls your Google Form at regular intervals (default every 15 minutes) to check for new submissions. Each new response triggers the validation process.

2. Email Validation

Email addresses from the form are sent to Clearout for real-time verification. Clearout checks for valid syntax, domain existence, mailbox availability, and risk factors like disposable email providers.

3. Verified Contact Creation

Only emails that pass Clearout's validation are added to your Amazon SES contacts. The workflow can include additional form fields (like name or company) as contact attributes for segmentation.

Pro tip: Configure Amazon SES to automatically add new contacts to specific contact groups based on form questions. This enables targeted email campaigns straight from your form responses.
