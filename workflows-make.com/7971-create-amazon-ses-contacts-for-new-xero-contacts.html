What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for businesses using Xero accounting alongside Amazon SES for email marketing. Every time you add a new customer or supplier in Xero, their contact details automatically sync to your Amazon SES email lists—no manual copying required.

The workflow runs in the background, checking for new Xero contacts at scheduled intervals (typically hourly or daily). When it detects a new contact, it creates a corresponding entry in Amazon SES with all relevant details. This ensures your email campaigns always target your complete, up-to-date customer base.

How It Works

1. Monitor Xero for new contacts

The workflow regularly polls your Xero account's contact list using the Xero API. It tracks which contacts have already been processed to avoid duplicates.

2. Extract contact details

For each new contact, the system pulls key information like name, email address, phone number, and any custom fields you've configured in Xero.

3. Format for Amazon SES

The contact data gets transformed to match Amazon SES's expected format, with proper field mappings for email marketing segmentation.

4. Create SES contact

The workflow uses Amazon SES's API to create a new contact entry with all the transferred details, ready for your email campaigns.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add contacts to specific SES contact lists based on Xero contact categories or tags for better email targeting.
