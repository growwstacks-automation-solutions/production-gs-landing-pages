What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining clean, verified email contact lists in Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) when collecting leads through Tally forms. Many businesses struggle with invalid email submissions that waste resources and hurt deliverability metrics.

The workflow automatically validates each new Tally form response through Clearout's email verification service before creating the contact in Amazon SES. This ensures your email marketing campaigns only target valid, deliverable addresses while eliminating manual verification steps.

How It Works

1. New Tally Form Submission

The workflow triggers whenever someone submits your Tally form, capturing all provided contact information including email address.

2. Clearout Email Validation

Each submitted email address is automatically checked against Clearout's verification service to confirm its validity and deliverability.

3. Conditional Routing

Only emails that pass verification proceed to the next step. Invalid addresses can be routed to a separate list for follow-up.

4. Amazon SES Contact Creation

Verified contacts are automatically added to your specified Amazon SES contact lists with all relevant form data mapped to appropriate fields.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add tags in Amazon SES based on which Tally form was submitted, allowing for targeted email campaigns.
