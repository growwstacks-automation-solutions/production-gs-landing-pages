What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually copying data from Tally form submissions to Google Sheets. Every time someone submits your Tally form, the workflow automatically adds their responses as a new row in your specified Google Sheet.

Businesses using Tally for surveys, lead generation, or internal requests often waste hours each week transferring data to spreadsheets for analysis. This workflow eliminates that repetitive work while ensuring perfect accuracy - no more missed submissions or copy-paste errors.

How It Works

1. Monitors your Tally form for new responses

The workflow checks your Tally account at regular intervals (configurable from 5-60 minutes) for new submissions. When detected, it immediately processes each response.

2. Structures the form data

Each form field (text answers, multiple choice selections, file upload links) is extracted and prepared for spreadsheet insertion. The workflow handles all data types Tally supports.

3. Adds rows to your Google Sheet

The system connects to your specified Google Sheet and appends each response as a new row, with fields mapped to your column structure. Timestamps are automatically added for tracking.

Pro tip: Use separate Sheets tabs for different form types (e.g., customer feedback vs. job applications) by duplicating and customizing this workflow.
