What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manual lead entry between web forms and CRM systems. When potential customers complete your Tripetto forms, their information often sits in spreadsheets or email inboxes until someone manually transfers it to your Odoo CRM. This delay causes missed opportunities and inconsistent data.

The workflow automatically creates complete lead records in Odoo CRM whenever new Tripetto form responses arrive. It maps all form fields to corresponding CRM fields, ensuring no data is lost in translation. Your sales team gets real-time access to qualified leads without any administrative overhead.

How It Works

1. New Tripetto Response Trigger

The workflow monitors your Tripetto account for new form submissions. Each time someone completes your lead generation form, the automation instantly captures all response data.

2. Data Transformation

The system maps form fields to Odoo CRM properties, converting formats as needed. For example, it might combine first/last name fields or parse phone numbers into standardized formats.

3. Lead Creation in Odoo

Make.com creates a new lead record in your Odoo CRM with all the captured information. The automation can set default values for sales teams, lead sources, or campaign tracking.

4. Optional Notifications

You can configure the workflow to alert sales reps via email or Slack when high-priority leads come through, ensuring prompt follow-up.
