What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for businesses using Typeform for lead generation and Odoo as their CRM system. Manually transferring form responses to your CRM is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates that friction by automatically creating new Odoo CRM leads whenever someone submits your Typeform.

The system watches for new Typeform responses at regular intervals, extracts all the submitted information, and maps it to the appropriate fields in Odoo CRM. This ensures your sales team gets complete, accurate lead information immediately after submission - without anyone having to copy and paste data between systems.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Typeform response

The workflow starts when Make.com detects a new submission on your configured Typeform. It can check for responses as frequently as every minute, ensuring minimal delay between form submission and lead creation.

2. Data extraction and transformation

The system retrieves all the response data from Typeform, including answers to all form fields. You can configure which fields to include and how to format the data before sending it to Odoo.

3. Lead creation in Odoo CRM

Using Odoo's API, the workflow creates a new lead record with all the collected information. You can map Typeform fields to specific Odoo CRM fields, including custom fields you've created in either system.

Pro tip: Add a hidden field in your Typeform to track the source of each lead (e.g., "Website Contact Form" or "Event Registration"). Map this to a custom field in Odoo for better lead source tracking.
