What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of lost leads and delayed follow-ups that occur when Facebook Lead Ads submissions sit unprocessed in spreadsheets or email inboxes. By instantly creating Odoo CRM leads from every new Facebook lead, your sales team gains immediate visibility into fresh opportunities with complete contact information and lead source details.

The workflow eliminates manual data transfer between platforms, reducing administrative work while improving data accuracy. Marketing can see which campaigns generate the most qualified leads, while sales receives complete lead profiles automatically enriched with Facebook campaign data for better context during outreach.

How It Works

1. Monitoring for new Facebook leads

The Make.com scenario checks your Facebook Lead Ads account at regular intervals (typically every 15-30 minutes) for new form submissions. Each new lead triggers the workflow.

2. Extracting lead details

The automation retrieves all available lead information including name, contact details, and any custom form responses. It also captures metadata like the campaign name and ad set for attribution tracking.

3. Formatting for Odoo CRM

Lead data is transformed to match Odoo's expected format, with field mappings you configure during setup. This ensures clean data imports without manual cleanup.

4. Creating the Odoo lead record

The workflow creates a new lead in your Odoo CRM with all captured information, automatically assigning it to the appropriate sales team or individual based on your rules.
