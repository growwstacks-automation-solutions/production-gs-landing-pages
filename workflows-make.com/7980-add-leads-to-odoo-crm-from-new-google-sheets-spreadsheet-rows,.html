What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of leads getting stuck in spreadsheets instead of flowing into your CRM system. Many businesses collect lead information through Google Forms, web scrapers, or manual entry into Sheets, but then struggle with transferring this data to their CRM like Odoo.

The workflow automatically monitors your specified Google Sheet for new rows and creates corresponding lead records in Odoo CRM. It eliminates the need for manual copy-pasting or CSV imports, ensuring your sales team always has access to the latest leads without delay.

How It Works

1. Monitoring the Google Sheet

The workflow regularly checks your designated Google Sheet for new rows added since the last check. It can be configured to run every few minutes, hourly, or on any schedule that matches your lead generation volume.

2. Extracting Lead Data

For each new row found, the automation extracts all relevant lead information including contact details, company information, lead source, and any custom fields you've set up in your spreadsheet columns.

3. Creating Odoo CRM Leads

The extracted data is then mapped to corresponding fields in Odoo CRM and used to create new lead records. The workflow handles all the API connections between Google Sheets and Odoo behind the scenes.

Pro tip: Use the example spreadsheet as a template to ensure your column headers match the expected field names in Odoo. This makes field mapping automatic.
