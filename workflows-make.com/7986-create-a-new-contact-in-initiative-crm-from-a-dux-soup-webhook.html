What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your LinkedIn prospecting efforts and your CRM system. When Dux-Soup captures a new lead on LinkedIn, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact record in Initiative CRM with all relevant details.

The solution eliminates manual data entry, reduces human error, and ensures no leads fall through the cracks between prospecting and CRM management. Your sales team gains immediate access to new leads in their familiar CRM environment.

How It Works

1. Dux-Soup captures LinkedIn lead

When Dux-Soup identifies and saves a new LinkedIn profile during your automated or manual prospecting, it triggers the webhook.

2. Make.com receives the webhook

The workflow activates immediately upon receiving the Dux-Soup payload containing the lead's information.

3. Data transformation

The workflow maps Dux-Soup fields to your Initiative CRM contact fields, ensuring proper formatting for names, companies, and custom fields.

4. CRM contact creation

Make.com creates a new contact in Initiative CRM using the transformed data, complete with LinkedIn profile links and any tags from Dux-Soup.

Pro tip: Add a secondary step to notify your sales team via Slack or email when high-priority leads (based on title or company) enter your CRM.
