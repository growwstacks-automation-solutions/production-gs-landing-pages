What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for sales teams and service businesses - manually transferring contact information from scheduling tools like Calendly into their CRM system. Every time a prospect books a meeting through your Calendly page, their details instantly appear as a new contact in Initiative CRM.

The workflow eliminates duplicate data entry, reduces human error, and ensures no leads fall through the cracks between scheduling and CRM follow-up. It's particularly valuable for businesses with high meeting volumes where manual transfers become time-consuming and unreliable.

How It Works

1. New Calendly event trigger

The workflow monitors your Calendly account for new scheduled events. When someone books time on your calendar, it immediately captures all their submitted information including name, email, phone number, and any custom questions answered during booking.

2. Data formatting

The system processes the raw Calendly data to match Initiative CRM's expected field formats. This includes properly formatting names, standardizing phone numbers, and mapping custom questions to appropriate CRM fields.

3. CRM contact creation

The formatted contact information is sent to Initiative CRM via API to create a new contact record. The workflow includes error handling to retry failed attempts and logging to track all synchronization attempts.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Initiative CRM to track the original Calendly event type. This helps your sales team understand the context of each meeting when following up.
