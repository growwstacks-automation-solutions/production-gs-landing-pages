What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for businesses using HubSpot CRM - maintaining accurate contact information. When new contacts enter your CRM, invalid email addresses can lead to wasted marketing efforts, bounced messages, and inaccurate reporting.

The workflow automatically validates each new HubSpot contact using Loqate's email verification service. If an email fails validation, the contact is immediately flagged or deactivated in HubSpot, preventing it from entering your marketing sequences or sales pipelines with incorrect data.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors HubSpot for newly created contacts, triggering whenever a contact record is added to your CRM.

2. Email Validation

Each contact's email address is sent to Loqate's verification API, which checks for proper syntax, valid domain, and active mailbox.

3. Validation Results Processing

Loqate returns a validation score indicating whether the email is deliverable. The workflow interprets this score to determine if the email is valid.

4. HubSpot Contact Update

For invalid emails, the workflow automatically updates the HubSpot contact record - either deactivating it or adding a custom property flagging it as unverified.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to notify your sales team when invalid contacts are detected, allowing them to follow up for corrected information.
