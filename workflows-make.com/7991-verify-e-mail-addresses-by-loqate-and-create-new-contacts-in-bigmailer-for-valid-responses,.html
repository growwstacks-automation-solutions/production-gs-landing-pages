What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of invalid email addresses entering your marketing database from form submissions. When potential customers complete your Google Form, their email address automatically undergoes rigorous verification through Loqate's validation service before being added to BigMailer.

The workflow prevents common issues like typos, disposable emails, and fake addresses from polluting your contact lists. By ensuring only valid emails reach BigMailer, you maintain higher deliverability rates, improve campaign performance, and reduce wasted marketing effort on unreachable contacts.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new response is submitted to your connected Google Form. It extracts the email address field along with any other relevant contact information.

2. Loqate Verification

The email address is sent to Loqate's verification API which checks: proper formatting, valid domain existence, and mailbox availability. This happens in real-time without the recipient receiving any verification email.

3. Validation Filter

The workflow evaluates Loqate's response to determine if the address meets your validation criteria. You can customize what constitutes a "valid" email based on your business requirements.

4. BigMailer Integration

Only successfully verified emails proceed to create new contacts in your BigMailer account. The workflow maps all form fields to the corresponding BigMailer contact fields automatically.

5. Error Handling

Invalid emails trigger alternative paths for follow-up or logging. The workflow maintains complete records while preventing bad data from entering your CRM.
