What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for businesses capturing leads through online forms: ensuring only valid email addresses enter your CRM. When prospects submit information through Google Forms, the workflow automatically verifies each email's deliverability using Loqate's professional validation service before creating contacts in ActiveCampaign.

Invalid email addresses waste marketing resources, skew analytics, and can damage sender reputation. By implementing this automated gatekeeper, you maintain a clean contact database while eliminating manual verification steps that slow down lead processing.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new response is submitted to your connected Google Form. It captures all form fields including the critical email address field.

2. Loqate Email Verification

The submitted email address is sent to Loqate's verification API which checks: syntax validity, domain existence, and mailbox availability. Loqate returns a confidence score indicating whether the address is likely deliverable.

Pro tip: Configure the confidence threshold to match your risk tolerance. Higher thresholds (85%+) reduce false positives but may reject some valid addresses.

3. Conditional Routing

Based on Loqate's verification result, the workflow either: creates a new ActiveCampaign contact (for valid emails) or routes the submission for manual review (for questionable addresses).

4. ActiveCampaign Contact Creation

Verified emails trigger the creation of a fully populated contact record in ActiveCampaign, including all mapped form fields. The contact is automatically added to specified lists and tagged for follow-up.
