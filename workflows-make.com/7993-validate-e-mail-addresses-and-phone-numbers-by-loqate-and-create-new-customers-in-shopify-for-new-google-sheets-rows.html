What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical ecommerce challenge: ensuring customer contact information is valid before creating Shopify customer profiles. Every time a new row is added to your Google Sheet (from order forms, lead captures, or wholesale applications), the workflow:

1. Validates email addresses through Loqate's verification service to confirm proper formatting and domain existence
2. Checks phone numbers for correct country codes and formatting
3. Only creates Shopify customers for records passing validation
4. Optionally flags invalid entries for review

How It Works

1. New Row Detection

Make.com monitors your Google Sheet for new rows added, typically from order forms, event registrations, or wholesale applications.

2. Loqate Validation

Each new row's email and phone fields are sent to Loqate's verification API. The service checks email syntax, domain validity, and phone number formatting.

3. Conditional Processing

Based on Loqate's validation results, the workflow either proceeds to create a Shopify customer or routes invalid records to a separate processing path.

4. Shopify Customer Creation

Validated customer data is mapped to Shopify's customer fields, creating complete customer profiles with verified contact information.

Pro tip: Add a "Validation Notes" column to your Sheet where the workflow can record why certain records failed verification for easy troubleshooting.
