What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for WooCommerce store owners: ensuring only valid, deliverable email addresses enter your customer database. Every time a new row is added to your Google Sheet (containing potential customer information), the workflow automatically:

1. Validates each email address using Loqate's professional verification service
2. Creates a new WooCommerce customer account only for verified addresses
3. Maintains a clean customer database by rejecting invalid or risky emails

Manual email verification and customer creation is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates hours of administrative work while improving data quality - reducing bounced emails, failed deliveries, and fraudulent accounts.

How It Works

Step 1: New Row Detection

Make.com monitors your specified Google Sheet for new rows containing customer information. The trigger activates whenever a new entry is added to your lead capture form, order spreadsheet, or customer import file.

Step 2: Email Validation

Each email address is sent to Loqate for comprehensive verification. The service checks domain validity, syntax correctness, and mailbox existence while flagging disposable emails and common misspellings.

Step 3: Conditional Processing

Based on Loqate's validation results, the workflow either proceeds to customer creation or follows your configured rejection path (notification, spreadsheet flagging, or follow-up request).

Step 4: WooCommerce Account Creation

For verified emails, the workflow maps all spreadsheet fields to corresponding WooCommerce customer attributes, creating complete customer profiles with validated contact information.

Pro tip: Add a "Validation Status" column to your Google Sheet that updates automatically, giving your team visibility into which leads have been processed successfully.
