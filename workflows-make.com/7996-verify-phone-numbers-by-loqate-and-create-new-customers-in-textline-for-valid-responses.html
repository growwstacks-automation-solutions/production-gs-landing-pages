What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical data quality problem for businesses collecting customer phone numbers through online forms. Without validation, 20-30% of submitted numbers contain errors, leading to failed communications and wasted marketing spend.

The workflow automatically verifies each submission using Loqate's phone validation service, then creates clean customer records in Textline only for properly formatted, active numbers. This ensures your SMS campaigns, appointment reminders, and customer service messages reach real people.

How It Works

Step 1: Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Google Form containing a phone number field. It captures all form data while focusing on the phone number for validation.

Step 2: Loqate Phone Verification

The submitted number is checked against Loqate's global phone database. The service verifies the number's format, carrier, and active status while identifying disposable/VOIP numbers that often indicate low-quality leads.

Pro tip: Configure Loqate to check for number portability too - this helps maintain deliverability even when customers change carriers.

Step 3: Conditional Customer Creation

Only numbers passing verification get created as Textline contacts. The workflow maps all original form fields to the customer profile, ensuring your team has full context when following up.
