What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring contact information between business applications. When you add a new contact in Google Contacts, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact in Signable, ready to receive documents for electronic signing.

Businesses waste countless hours copying contact details between systems or dealing with errors from manual data entry. This workflow eliminates those inefficiencies while ensuring your signing workflows begin immediately with new contacts. The automation runs in the background whenever a new contact is added, requiring zero manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors your Google Contacts for new additions, checking at regular intervals you configure (typically every 15-60 minutes).

2. Data Extraction

When a new contact is detected, the workflow extracts relevant fields like name, email, phone number, and any custom fields you've mapped.

3. Contact Creation in Signable

The extracted information is formatted according to Signable's API requirements and a new contact record is created automatically.

4. Error Handling

If any step fails (like invalid email format), the workflow can notify your team or retry according to your configured rules.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Duplicate the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Google account and authorize access to Contacts
  3. Connect your Signable account using OAuth
  4. Map the contact fields you want transferred between systems
  5. Set your preferred polling interval (how often to check for new contacts)
  6. Test with a new contact to verify the workflow functions correctly
