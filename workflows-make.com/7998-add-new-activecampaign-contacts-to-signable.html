What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your ActiveCampaign CRM and Signable e-signature platform, automatically creating Signable contacts whenever new leads or customers are added to ActiveCampaign. It solves the common problem of sales teams manually copying contact details between systems - a process that often causes delays in sending contracts and increases data entry errors.

By implementing this workflow, you ensure that every new prospect in your sales pipeline immediately becomes available for document signing in Signable. This is particularly valuable for businesses that need to send service agreements, NDAs, or proposals shortly after lead capture. The automation typically processes new contacts within minutes, keeping your sales and legal workflows perfectly synchronized.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors your ActiveCampaign account for new contact creations through Make.com's instant trigger. This could be from form submissions, imported lists, or manual additions by your team.

2. Data Preparation

Contact details (name, email, company info, and any custom fields) are extracted and formatted for Signable's API requirements. The workflow can be configured to only process contacts meeting specific criteria like lead score thresholds or tagged segments.

3. Signable Contact Creation

The processed contact data is sent to Signable via API, creating a new contact record ready to receive documents. The system handles field mapping between ActiveCampaign's structure and Signable's expected format.

4. Success Verification

The workflow confirms successful contact creation in Signable and can optionally log this in ActiveCampaign as a note or update custom fields to track sync status.
