What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges Google Forms and Signable, eliminating manual data transfer between systems. Whenever someone submits your Google Form, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact in Signable with all the collected information.

Businesses using forms for client onboarding, contract requests, or service agreements benefit most. The automation ensures new leads or clients immediately enter your document signing workflow without administrative delays.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Form for new responses. Each submission initiates the automation sequence.

2. Data Parsing

Make.com extracts relevant fields from the form response, such as name, email, company, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. Contact Creation

The system sends the parsed data to Signable via API, creating a new contact record with all provided details.

4. Error Handling

If any step fails, the workflow can notify administrators or retry the process according to your configuration.
