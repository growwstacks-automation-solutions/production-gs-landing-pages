Automate Document Storage from Signable to Google Drive

Never manually transfer signed documents again. This workflow automatically saves completed Signable envelopes to your Google Drive with proper naming and organization.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually downloading signed documents from Signable and uploading them to Google Drive. Every time a document is signed in Signable, the workflow automatically:

  • Captures the completed document with all signatures
  • Names the file according to your preferred convention
  • Stores it in the correct Google Drive folder
  • Maintains a complete audit trail of the transaction

Legal teams, HR departments, and sales organizations using this automation report saving 5-8 hours per week on document management while eliminating the risk of lost contracts.

How It Works

1. Trigger on Signed Documents

The workflow monitors your Signable account for newly completed envelopes. As soon as all parties sign, it immediately captures the final document.

2. Apply Naming Conventions

Using data from the signing event (client name, date, document type), the workflow automatically generates a consistent filename that makes documents easy to find later.

3. Organize in Google Drive

The system places each document in the appropriate folder based on your predefined rules. Common structures include by client, project, or date-based hierarchies.

4. Confirm Successful Transfer

After saving to Drive, the workflow logs the action and can optionally notify your team via email or Slack that a new signed document is available.
