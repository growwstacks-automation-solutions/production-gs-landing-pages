What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually downloading signed documents from Signable and uploading them to Dropbox. Every time a contract or agreement is signed in Signable, the workflow automatically saves the completed document to your specified Dropbox folder with no human intervention required.

The system creates a reliable backup of all signed documents while maintaining proper organization in your cloud storage. This is particularly valuable for businesses that handle numerous contracts, as it eliminates the risk of losing track of signed documents or having team members work from outdated versions.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New signed envelope in Signable

The workflow monitors your Signable account for new signed envelopes. As soon as all parties complete signing, the workflow is triggered automatically.

2. Document retrieval

The system securely accesses the signed document through Signable's API, ensuring it retrieves the final version with all signatures and annotations intact.

3. File preparation

The document is processed with your preferred naming convention (e.g., "Contract_[ClientName]_[DateSigned].pdf") and any relevant metadata is extracted for organization purposes.

4. Secure transfer to Dropbox

The prepared document is uploaded to your designated Dropbox folder, maintaining all security permissions and creating a timestamped record of when it was stored.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to create subfolders by client name or month to keep your Dropbox organized automatically.
