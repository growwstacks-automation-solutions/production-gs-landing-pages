What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of delayed response times to customer form submissions. When potential customers fill out your Tally forms, every minute counts - studies show response times under 5 minutes increase conversion rates by 400% compared to slower responses.

The workflow monitors your Tally account for new submissions and instantly pushes formatted notifications to your Telegram account or team chat. Unlike email alerts that get buried in inboxes, Telegram messages provide immediate mobile notifications with 90%+ open rates within minutes.

How It Works

1. Polling for New Responses

The Make.com scenario checks your Tally forms at regular intervals (configurable from 1-60 minutes) using Tally's API. It identifies only new submissions since the last check.

2. Data Extraction

For each new response, the workflow extracts all form fields including custom questions, timestamps, and respondent information. This data gets structured for clear Telegram messaging.

3. Notification Formatting

The system formats an actionable message with emoji markers, field labels, and priority indicators. You can customize templates to highlight critical information like urgent requests.

4. Telegram Delivery

Formatted messages get sent to your pre-configured Telegram chat, channel, or group. The automation includes read receipts and can @mention specific team members for follow-up.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to route different form types to separate Telegram channels (e.g., sales inquiries vs. support tickets).
