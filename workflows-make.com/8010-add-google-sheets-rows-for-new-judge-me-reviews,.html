What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual work of tracking and analyzing customer reviews from your Judge.me-powered store. Every time a customer leaves a product review, Make.com automatically adds it as a new row in your Google Sheets document with all relevant details.

For ecommerce businesses, customer reviews are gold mines of feedback but manually collecting them wastes valuable time. This workflow eliminates copy-paste work while creating a structured database of reviews you can analyze for insights. It captures star ratings, review text, customer names, dates, and product information - everything needed to monitor satisfaction trends.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Judge.me review

The workflow starts when Judge.me receives a new product review through its API. Make.com detects this event instantly via webhook or periodic checks.

2. Extract review details

The automation parses all review data including rating (1-5 stars), text content, customer name, review date, product ID, and verification status.

3. Format for Google Sheets

Make.com structures the data into columns matching your spreadsheet format, handling special characters and date formatting automatically.

4. Add row to Google Sheets

The workflow appends the formatted review data as a new row in your specified Google Sheet, with each detail in its designated column.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column to track when reviews were logged. This helps monitor automation reliability.
