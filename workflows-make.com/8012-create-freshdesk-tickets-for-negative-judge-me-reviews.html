What This Workflow Does

Negative product reviews can damage your brand reputation and sales if left unaddressed. This automation instantly converts 1-2 star Judge.me reviews into prioritized Freshdesk tickets, ensuring your customer support team can respond promptly and turn dissatisfied customers into brand advocates.

The workflow monitors your Judge.me review feed in real-time, filtering for negative ratings. When detected, it automatically creates a detailed support ticket in Freshdesk with all relevant context - including the review content, product details, and customer information. This eliminates manual monitoring and data entry while guaranteeing no complaint falls through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Review Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Judge.me account for new reviews using webhooks or API polling. It evaluates each new review against your configured criteria for negativity (typically 1-2 star ratings).

2. Negative Review Detection

When a negative review is identified, the system extracts all relevant data including the review text, star rating, customer name, product details, and review URL. This information is structured for ticket creation.

3. Ticket Generation

The workflow connects to your Freshdesk account via API, creating a new ticket with "Negative Review" priority. It populates all ticket fields with the extracted review data and assigns it to your designated support team or queue.

4. Notification & Tracking

Your team receives immediate notification of the new ticket through Freshdesk's alert system. The workflow can optionally log all actions in a spreadsheet or notify managers via email for high-priority cases.
