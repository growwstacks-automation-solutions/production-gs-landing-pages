What This Workflow Does

Customer reviews are gold for e-commerce businesses, but manually checking for new feedback wastes valuable time. This automation instantly notifies your team in Slack whenever a customer leaves a new review on your Judge.me platform.

The workflow eliminates the need for constant manual monitoring while ensuring your team never misses important customer feedback. Whether it's glowing praise that deserves celebration or constructive criticism requiring immediate attention, your team will know the moment it happens.

How It Works

1. New review detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Judge.me account for new customer reviews through API connections.

2. Data extraction

When a new review appears, the system extracts key details like star rating, customer name, review text, and product information.

3. Slack message creation

The workflow formats this information into a clear Slack message and posts it to your designated channel, complete with appropriate emoji reactions based on rating.

4. Optional team alerts

You can configure the system to tag specific team members for negative reviews or highlight exceptional feedback for the whole company.
