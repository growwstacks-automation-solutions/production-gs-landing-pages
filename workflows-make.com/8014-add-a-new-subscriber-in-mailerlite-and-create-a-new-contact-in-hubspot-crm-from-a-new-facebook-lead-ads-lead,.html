What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for businesses running Facebook lead generation campaigns - the need to manually transfer leads between marketing and sales systems. When a potential customer submits information through your Facebook Lead Ad, this workflow automatically creates a subscriber in your MailerLite email marketing platform while simultaneously adding them as a contact in HubSpot CRM.

The solution eliminates duplicate data entry, ensures immediate follow-up from both marketing and sales teams, and maintains data consistency across systems. By syncing leads in real-time, you can trigger targeted email nurturing sequences while giving your sales team fresh, qualified leads to pursue.

How It Works

1. Facebook Lead Ads Trigger

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Facebook Lead Ad form. It captures all form field data including email, name, phone number, and any custom fields you've configured.

2. Data Processing

The system maps Facebook form fields to corresponding fields in MailerLite and HubSpot. You can customize this mapping to ensure data lands in the right places in each system.

3. Dual System Sync

The workflow simultaneously creates a new subscriber in your MailerLite account (adding them to specified groups) and creates a new contact record in HubSpot CRM (with proper lifecycle stage and owner assignment).

4. Confirmation & Error Handling

The system verifies successful creation in both platforms and can notify your team if any issues occur during the sync process.

Pro tip: Add a delay module before the HubSpot step to give MailerLite time to process the subscriber before your sales team reaches out.
