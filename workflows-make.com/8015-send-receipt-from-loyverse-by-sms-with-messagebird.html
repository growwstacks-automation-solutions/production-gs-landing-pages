What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the hassle of manually sending receipts to customers after Loyverse POS transactions. Whenever a new sale is recorded in Loyverse, the system automatically retrieves the receipt details and sends them via SMS using MessageBird's reliable messaging platform.

Retail businesses using Loyverse point-of-sale systems can provide instant digital receipts to customers without printing paper copies or requiring staff to manually send messages. The workflow captures all transaction details including items purchased, prices, taxes, and payment method - formatting them into a clear SMS message that customers receive within seconds of their purchase.

How It Works

1. New Loyverse receipt trigger

The workflow starts when Loyverse records a new transaction. Make.com monitors your Loyverse account for these events in real-time.

2. Receipt data extraction

The system retrieves all relevant receipt details including customer phone number, transaction ID, items purchased, subtotals, taxes, and final amount.

3. SMS message formatting

Transaction data is formatted into a customer-friendly SMS message that fits within standard message length limits (160 characters).

4. MessageBird delivery

The formatted receipt is sent via MessageBird's global SMS gateway directly to the customer's mobile phone.

Pro tip: Add your store's branding and a thank you message to personalize receipts. You can also include a short promotional offer to encourage repeat visits.
