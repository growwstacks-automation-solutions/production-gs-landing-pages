What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between sales and customer success teams. When sales reps add new contacts to Sellsy (your CRM), those contacts often need to be manually added to Corymbus (your customer success platform). This manual process is time-consuming, prone to errors, and creates delays in customer onboarding.

The workflow automatically detects new contacts in Sellsy and creates corresponding records in Corymbus within seconds. It transfers all essential contact information including name, email, phone number, company details, and any custom fields you've configured. This ensures your customer success team has immediate access to new contacts without waiting for manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New contact in Sellsy

The workflow monitors your Sellsy account for new contact additions. As soon as a sales rep saves a new contact, the automation is triggered.

2. Data retrieval

Make.com fetches the complete contact record from Sellsy, including all available fields and custom data. This ensures no important information is left behind.

3. Data transformation

The workflow maps Sellsy fields to their corresponding Corymbus fields. You can customize this mapping to match your specific data structure and requirements.

4. Duplicate checking

Before creating a new record, the system checks if the contact already exists in Corymbus based on email address or other unique identifiers you specify.

5. Contact creation in Corymbus

If no duplicate is found, the workflow creates a new contact in Corymbus with all the mapped data. You'll have a complete, identical record in both systems.

6. Error handling and notifications

If any step fails, the workflow retries automatically and can notify your team via email or Slack if manual intervention is needed.
