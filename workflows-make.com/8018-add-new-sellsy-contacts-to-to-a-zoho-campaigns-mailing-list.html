What This Workflow Does

This automation template helps businesses eliminate manual data entry by connecting different applications and automating workflows between them. It solves the common problem of information silos where employees waste time copying data between systems or dealing with inconsistent records across platforms.

The workflow demonstrates how to automatically sync contact information between business apps like Sellsy and Zoho Campaigns. This ensures your marketing lists always contain up-to-date customer data without manual updates, reducing errors and saving valuable time.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger Setup

The workflow begins when a new contact is added to your Sellsy CRM. The automation detects this new entry and captures all relevant contact details.

Step 2: Data Processing

The system processes the contact information, formatting it appropriately for the destination system and applying any business rules you've configured.

Step 3: Destination Sync

The formatted contact data is automatically added to your Zoho Campaigns mailing list, ensuring your marketing communications reach the right audience.

Pro tip: You can modify this template to connect nearly any two business apps - just replace the trigger and action modules with your preferred applications.

This workflow benefits any business using multiple software applications that don't natively integrate well. It's particularly valuable for:

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available)
  2. Access to both source and destination applications (Sellsy and Zoho in this example)
  3. API keys or login credentials for the apps you want to connect
  4. Basic understanding of the data fields you want to sync between systems

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Duplicate the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Sellsy account in the trigger module
  3. Configure which contact fields to capture
  4. Connect your Zoho Campaigns account in the action module
  5. Map the Sellsy fields to corresponding Zoho fields
  6. Test with a sample contact to verify the sync works
  7. Activate the workflow for live use
