What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual work of copying new Sellsy contacts into spreadsheets. Every time a new contact is created in your Sellsy CRM, the workflow automatically retrieves their details and adds them as a new row in your specified Google Sheet.

The template includes common contact fields like name, email, phone number, and company information. It maintains an organized, up-to-date spreadsheet that serves as a backup of your CRM data and enables easy sharing with team members who may not have direct access to Sellsy.

How It Works

1. New contact trigger

The workflow monitors your Sellsy account for new contact creations using Make.com's Sellsy connector. When triggered, it captures the contact ID and timestamp.

2. Contact data retrieval

Make.com fetches the complete contact details from Sellsy using the captured ID, including both standard fields and any custom fields you've configured.

3. Data formatting

The workflow structures the contact information into columns matching your Google Sheet format, handling data types and special characters appropriately.

4. Google Sheets update

Finally, the formatted contact data is appended as a new row in your specified Google Sheet, with each field placed in the correct column.
