What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for ecommerce businesses using both Shopify and Sellsy - the manual transfer of customer data between systems. Every time a new customer places an order, their information needs to be available in Sellsy for your sales and support teams. Doing this manually wastes hours each week and leads to errors.

The workflow automatically detects new Shopify customers at regular intervals and creates corresponding contact records in Sellsy. It maps all relevant customer fields including contact details, order history, and shipping information. This ensures your CRM always has complete, up-to-date customer data without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New customer detection

The workflow polls Shopify's API at scheduled intervals (typically every 15-60 minutes) to check for newly created customer accounts since the last check.

2. Data extraction

For each new customer, the workflow retrieves all available information including name, email, phone, address, and order details from Shopify.

3. Field mapping

Customer data is transformed to match Sellsy's contact field structure. You can customize which Shopify fields map to which Sellsy properties.

4. Contact creation

The workflow uses Sellsy's API to create a new contact record with all mapped customer information, ensuring your sales team has immediate access.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only sync customers meeting specific criteria (like order value) to keep your CRM focused on high-value contacts.
