What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually transferring contact information from Google Forms submissions into your Sellsy CRM. Every time someone completes your lead capture form, event registration, or customer survey, their details automatically become a properly formatted Sellsy contact record.

The workflow checks for new Google Forms responses at regular intervals, extracts the submitted information, and creates corresponding contacts in Sellsy with all relevant details mapped to the correct fields. This ensures your sales team always has immediate access to fresh leads without any delay or data entry mistakes.

How It Works

1. New response detection

The workflow monitors your specified Google Form for new submissions on a schedule you configure (from every 5 minutes to daily).

2. Data extraction

Each form response is processed to extract all submitted information, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and any custom fields you've added to your form.

3. Field mapping

The system matches Google Form fields to corresponding Sellsy contact properties according to your predefined mapping configuration.

4. Contact creation

A new contact record is created in Sellsy with all mapped information, including any tags or custom fields you want to apply automatically.
