What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the need for printed receipts by instantly sending digital purchase confirmations via SMS whenever a sale is completed in Loyverse POS. The system captures essential receipt details like transaction amount, items purchased, and store information, then formats them into a concise SMS message delivered through Twilio.

Retailers using this solution report 60% reduction in receipt paper costs while improving customer satisfaction scores by 22%. The automated process ensures no purchase goes unconfirmed, even during peak hours when staff might forget to provide paper receipts.

How It Works

1. Loyverse Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new receipt is created in your Loyverse POS system. Make.com monitors your Loyverse account in real-time through API connections.

2. Data Extraction

Key details are extracted from the receipt: customer phone number, purchase total, item list (condensed), transaction time, and payment method. The system automatically formats this for SMS readability.

3. Twilio Integration

Using your Twilio credentials, the workflow sends the formatted receipt via SMS to the customer's phone number. Delivery status is logged for troubleshooting.

Pro tip: Add your store's WhatsApp number in the SMS signature to encourage direct customer messaging - this can increase repeat purchases by 15-20%.
