What This Workflow Does

This automation seamlessly connects Typeform survey responses to your Qualtrics directory, eliminating the need for manual data entry. Whenever someone completes your Typeform survey, their response is automatically processed and added as a contact in your Qualtrics directory.

The workflow solves the common problem of survey data being trapped in silos. Instead of exporting CSV files and manually uploading them, this automation keeps your Qualtrics directory continuously updated with fresh respondent data. It's particularly valuable for research teams, customer feedback programs, and any organization running regular surveys.

How It Works

1. New Response Detection

The workflow monitors your Typeform account for new survey responses at regular intervals you specify.

2. Data Extraction

Key information from the response is extracted, including contact details and survey answers you want to store in Qualtrics.

3. Contact Creation

The system maps the Typeform fields to corresponding Qualtrics directory fields and creates a new contact record.

4. Error Handling

The workflow includes checks to handle duplicates, missing data, and API errors to ensure data integrity.
