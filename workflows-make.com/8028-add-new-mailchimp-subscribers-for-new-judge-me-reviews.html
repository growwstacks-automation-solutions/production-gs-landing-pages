What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually collecting customer emails from product reviews and adding them to your email marketing list. Every time a customer leaves a review on your Judge.me-powered store, their contact information is automatically added to your Mailchimp subscriber list.

The workflow eliminates tedious copy-paste work while ensuring you never miss an opportunity to connect with satisfied customers. It transforms passive reviews into active marketing opportunities by building your email list with engaged customers who have already demonstrated interest in your products.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Judge.me review

The workflow starts whenever a new review is submitted on your Judge.me-powered product pages. Make.com monitors your Judge.me account for new review activity.

2. Extract reviewer information

The system pulls key details from the review including customer name, email address, and optionally their review content and rating. This data is formatted for Mailchimp integration.

3. Add subscriber to Mailchimp

The customer information is automatically added to your specified Mailchimp audience list. You can configure custom fields, tags, and segmentation rules.

4. Optional follow-up actions

The workflow can trigger additional actions like sending a thank-you email or adding the customer to a specific email sequence based on their review rating.
