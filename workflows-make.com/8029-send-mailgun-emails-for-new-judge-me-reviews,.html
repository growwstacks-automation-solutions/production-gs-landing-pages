What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical but often overlooked aspect of customer experience - acknowledging product reviews. When customers take time to leave feedback on your Judge.me-powered store, this Make.com workflow instantly sends them a personalized thank-you email through your Mailgun account.

The system eliminates manual follow-ups while ensuring no reviewer goes unappreciated. It captures every new review as it's submitted, extracts the customer details and review content, then triggers a tailored email response - all within minutes of the review being posted.

How It Works

1. New review detection

The workflow monitors your Judge.me account via webhook, instantly detecting when new reviews are submitted across all your products.

2. Data extraction

Key details like customer name, email, product reviewed, rating, and review text are extracted from the webhook payload.

3. Email composition

Your pre-designed Mailgun email template is populated with the reviewer's specific details, creating a personalized message.

4. Automated delivery

The completed email is sent through your Mailgun account, with delivery tracking and analytics built in.

Pro tip: Include a small discount code in your thank-you emails - this converts 28% of reviewers into repeat customers according to Shopify data.
