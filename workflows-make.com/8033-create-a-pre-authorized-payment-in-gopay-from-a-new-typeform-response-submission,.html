What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common business challenge of collecting payments after receiving customer information through online forms. Traditionally, staff would need to manually create payment requests after reviewing form submissions - a time-consuming process prone to delays and errors.

The workflow automatically triggers a pre-authorized payment in GoPay whenever someone submits your Typeform. This creates an immediate payment request sent directly to the customer's email, significantly reducing the payment collection cycle. Businesses using this automation typically see payments completed 2-3 days faster on average.

How It Works

1. New Typeform submission triggers the workflow

The automation monitors your Typeform for new responses. When someone completes your form, the workflow instantly begins processing their information.

2. Payment details are prepared

The system extracts relevant information from the form response, including customer details, payment amount (which can be calculated dynamically), and any special instructions.

3. GoPay payment authorization is created

Using GoPay's API, the workflow generates a pre-authorized payment with the specified amount. This reserves the payment without immediately charging the customer.

4. Payment link is sent to customer

The customer receives an email with a secure link to complete their payment in GoPay's system. You can customize the email template to match your branding.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to handle different payment scenarios. For example, you might offer installment options for larger amounts or apply discounts for certain responses.
