What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of collecting payment information from Google Forms submissions and creating pre-authorized payments in GoPay. Businesses using forms for registrations, orders, or applications often waste hours copying data between systems and risk errors in payment processing.

The workflow automatically checks for new Google Forms responses every 15 minutes (configurable), extracts the payment details, creates a pre-authorized payment in GoPay to reserve funds, and logs the transaction details to a Google Sheets spreadsheet for record-keeping. This creates a seamless payment collection system without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The scenario runs on a schedule (default: every 15 minutes) to check your Google Form for new responses since the last run.

2. Response Processing

For each new form submission, the workflow extracts the payment details like amount, customer information, and payment reference from the form fields you specify.

3. GoPay Payment Creation

The system connects to your GoPay account via API and creates a pre-authorized payment with the details from the form. This reserves the funds without immediately capturing them.

4. Google Sheets Logging

Each payment transaction is recorded in your designated Google Sheet with timestamps, payment status, and all relevant details for reconciliation and reporting.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Google account and authorize access to Forms and Sheets
  3. Connect your GoPay account via API credentials
  4. Map your form fields to the payment creation module
  5. Specify your Google Sheet ID and tab name for logging
  6. Adjust the schedule frequency if needed (default: 15 minutes)
  7. Test with sample form submissions

Pro tip: Add a timestamp field to your form to help track when submissions were originally received, separate from when they were processed.
