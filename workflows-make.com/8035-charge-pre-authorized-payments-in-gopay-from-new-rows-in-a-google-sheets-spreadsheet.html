What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of manually processing recurring or batch payments in GoPay. Many businesses waste hours each week copying payment details from spreadsheets to payment gateways, risking human error and delayed transactions.

The workflow automatically detects new rows in your Google Sheets spreadsheet and processes the corresponding payments in GoPay. It runs on a schedule (default every 15 minutes) to ensure timely processing while maintaining a complete audit trail in your spreadsheet.

How It Works

1. Monitor Google Sheets for new rows

The workflow checks your designated spreadsheet at regular intervals, identifying new rows that haven't been processed yet.

2. Extract payment details

For each new row, the system reads the customer's pre-authorized payment token, amount, and any reference information you've included.

3. Process payments in GoPay

Using GoPay's API, the workflow securely charges each payment with the specified amounts and records the transaction details.

4. Update spreadsheet with results

The system marks processed rows and records payment statuses, timestamps, and any transaction IDs for complete reconciliation.

Pro tip: Add conditional formatting to your spreadsheet to highlight failed payments for quick visibility.
