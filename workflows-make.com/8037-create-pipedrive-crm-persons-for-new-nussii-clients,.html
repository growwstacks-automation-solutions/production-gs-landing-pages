What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of client data silos between your proposal software (Nussii) and CRM (Pipedrive). When sales teams manually transfer client information between systems, they waste valuable time and risk data entry errors that can impact customer relationships.

The workflow automatically creates a complete Pipedrive person record whenever a new client is added in Nussii. It maps all relevant contact details, company information, and custom fields between the platforms, ensuring your sales team has accurate, up-to-date client data immediately available in your CRM.

How It Works

1. New Client Trigger

The workflow monitors your Nussii account for new client creations through real-time webhooks or periodic checks.

2. Data Extraction

All available client information is retrieved from Nussii, including contact details, company data, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. Duplicate Check

The system verifies whether the client already exists in Pipedrive by comparing email addresses and other unique identifiers.

4. CRM Record Creation

For new clients, a complete Pipedrive person record is created with all mapped fields. Existing clients can be either skipped or updated based on your configuration.
