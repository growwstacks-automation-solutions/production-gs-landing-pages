What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in the sales-to-CRM handoff process for businesses using Nusii for proposals and HubSpot as their CRM. Every time a client accepts your proposal in Nusii, their contact information is automatically created as a new record in HubSpot without any manual data entry.

The workflow eliminates the risk of missed follow-ups or incomplete CRM records that often happen when sales teams must manually transfer client information between systems. It ensures your entire team has immediate access to new client details in HubSpot, where you can trigger nurturing sequences, assign tasks, and track the full customer journey.

How It Works

1. New Client Trigger in Nusii

The workflow monitors your Nusii account for new client creations. When a proposal is accepted and a client record is established, Make.com detects this event instantly.

2. Data Extraction and Mapping

The automation extracts all relevant client information from Nusii including name, email, company details, and any custom fields you've configured. This data is formatted to match HubSpot's contact field structure.

3. HubSpot Contact Creation

Make.com uses HubSpot's API to create a new contact record with all the mapped information. The workflow can be configured to add contacts to specific lists or apply tags for segmentation.

4. Error Handling and Logging

If any issues occur during the sync, the workflow includes built-in error handling with retry logic and detailed logging for troubleshooting.
