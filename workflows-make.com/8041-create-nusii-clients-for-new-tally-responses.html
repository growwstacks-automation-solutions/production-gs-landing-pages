What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for service businesses using Tally forms for lead capture and Nusii for proposals. Manually transferring client information between these systems wastes valuable time and introduces errors. The workflow automatically creates new Nusii client records whenever someone submits your Tally form, ensuring seamless handoff from lead to proposal.

By connecting these platforms, you eliminate duplicate data entry while ensuring no potential client falls through the cracks. The automation runs continuously in the background, checking for new form submissions at regular intervals and populating all relevant client details directly into Nusii.

How It Works

1. Monitors Tally for new responses

The workflow periodically checks your Tally form for new submissions based on your preferred schedule (default: every 30 minutes). It identifies any responses that haven't been processed yet.

2. Extracts relevant client information

For each new response, the automation pulls key details like name, email, company, and any custom fields from your Tally form that are relevant for proposals.

3. Creates Nusii client records

The system maps the Tally form data to corresponding fields in Nusii, automatically generating properly formatted client records ready for proposal creation.

Pro tip: Customize the field mappings to ensure all information you need for proposals transfers accurately between systems.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Tally and Nusii accounts via OAuth
  3. Select which Tally form to monitor
  4. Map your Tally form fields to corresponding Nusii client fields
  5. Set your preferred polling interval (how often to check for new responses)
  6. Test with a form submission to verify the client creation works
  7. Turn on the scenario to run automatically
