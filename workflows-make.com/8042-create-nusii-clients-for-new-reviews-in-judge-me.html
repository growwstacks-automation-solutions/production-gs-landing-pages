What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between customer feedback and business growth by automatically creating Nusii client records from Judge.me reviews. Every time a customer leaves a positive review of your product or service, the workflow instantly captures their information and creates a corresponding client profile in Nusii.

The system eliminates manual data entry while ensuring no valuable lead from customer feedback falls through the cracks. It's particularly valuable for service businesses that rely on social proof and want to convert satisfied customers into repeat clients or referrals.

How It Works

1. New Review Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Judge.me account for new customer reviews. It can be configured to watch for specific products, services, or minimum star ratings.

2. Data Extraction

Key information from the review is extracted, including the customer's name, contact details, review content, and the specific product or service they reviewed.

3. Nusii Client Creation

The workflow then uses this information to automatically create a new client record in Nusii, complete with the review details and any custom fields you specify.

4. Follow-up Trigger

Optionally, the workflow can trigger personalized follow-up emails or add tasks to your CRM to ensure timely engagement with these warm leads.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Install the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Judge.me account in the first module
  3. Configure your review filters (star rating, products, etc.)
  4. Connect your Nusii account in the final module
  5. Map the review fields to your Nusii client fields
  6. Test with a sample review and activate the workflow

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Nusii to track which clients came from Judge.me reviews. This helps measure the ROI of your review collection efforts.
