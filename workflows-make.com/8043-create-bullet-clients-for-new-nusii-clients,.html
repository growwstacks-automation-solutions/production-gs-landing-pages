What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in many service businesses' operations - the disconnect between proposal tools and CRM systems. When you win a new client in Nusii (your proposal software), their information needs to flow into Bullet (your CRM) to kickstart your client onboarding and project management processes.

Without automation, this requires manual copy-pasting of client details between systems - a time-consuming process prone to errors. This workflow eliminates that friction by automatically creating identical client records in Bullet the moment they're added to Nusii, ensuring your sales and delivery teams always work with up-to-date information.

How It Works

1. New client detection

The workflow monitors your Nusii account for new client creations using Make.com's real-time triggers. As soon as a client record appears, the automation captures all available details including contact information, company data, and custom fields.

2. Data transformation

Make.com processes the Nusii client data to match Bullet's expected format. This step handles field mapping, data cleaning, and any necessary transformations to ensure seamless integration between the different systems.

3. Bullet client creation

The processed data gets sent to Bullet via its API, creating a new client record with all the transferred information. The workflow can be configured to include additional default values or tags specific to your Bullet setup.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Bullet to track which clients came from Nusii proposals. This helps measure your proposal conversion rates directly in your CRM.
