What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of adding new clients to your Zoho People directory when they sign proposals in Nusii. The moment a client accepts your proposal, their contact details automatically flow into Zoho People as a new record.

Businesses using both Nusii for proposals and Zoho People for HR management often waste hours each week copying client information between systems. This workflow solves that problem with a seamless integration that keeps both platforms in sync without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New Client Trigger

The workflow monitors your Nusii account for new client creations. When a new client record appears, the automation immediately captures all available contact details.

2. Data Transformation

Make.com reformats the Nusii client data to match Zoho People's contact field structure. This ensures all information transfers correctly without requiring manual adjustments.

3. Zoho People Sync

The processed client data gets pushed to your Zoho People account as a new contact record. The workflow verifies successful creation and can notify you if any issues occur.
