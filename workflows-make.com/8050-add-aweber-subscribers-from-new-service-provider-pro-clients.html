What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in many service businesses' marketing funnels - capturing new clients in your email marketing system the moment they sign up for your services. Without automation, this process often gets overlooked during busy periods, causing you to miss valuable opportunities to nurture client relationships through email.

The workflow automatically detects new clients in Service Provider Pro and adds them as subscribers to your AWeber email list. It transfers all relevant contact information, ensuring your welcome sequences and marketing emails reach the right people at the perfect time - when they've just engaged with your services.

How It Works

1. New client detection

The workflow monitors your Service Provider Pro account for new client additions through real-time webhooks or periodic checks.

2. Data extraction

When a new client is detected, the system pulls their contact information including name, email address, and any other configured fields from Service Provider Pro.

3. AWeber integration

The client data gets formatted according to AWeber's API requirements and added to your specified email list with proper field mapping.

4. Confirmation and error handling

The system verifies successful subscriber addition and can notify you of any failures through your preferred notification channel.

Pro tip: Add a 1-2 day delay before sending the first marketing email to avoid overwhelming new clients immediately after signup.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy this template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Service Provider Pro account in the first module
  3. Connect your AWeber account in the subscriber addition module

  6. Activate the scenario to begin processing new clients automatically
