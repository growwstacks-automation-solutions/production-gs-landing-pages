What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of missing critical messages in Service Provider Pro when you or your team aren't actively monitoring the platform. Many service businesses lose valuable response time waiting to check messages, which can impact customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

The workflow automatically detects new messages in Service Provider Pro and forwards them instantly to a Telegram bot. This ensures your team receives real-time notifications on mobile devices, enabling faster response times and better customer service. The integration maintains message context while adding the convenience of Telegram's robust notification system.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Service Provider Pro

The Make.com scenario continuously checks your Service Provider Pro account for new messages using API triggers. It can detect messages in specific folders or categories based on your configuration.

2. Processing Message Content

When a new message arrives, the workflow extracts relevant details like sender information, message content, timestamps, and any attachments. You can customize which fields to include in the forwarded notification.

3. Sending to Telegram

The formatted message gets sent to your pre-configured Telegram bot, which delivers it to specified chat groups or individual team members. The notification appears instantly on mobile devices with Telegram installed.

Pro tip: Configure different Telegram channels for different message types (urgent requests vs general inquiries) by adding filters in your Make.com scenario.
