What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between customer feedback collection and team action. When customers leave reviews on your Judge.me-powered store, the workflow automatically converts each review into a comment within your ProofHub project management system. This eliminates manual copying/pasting and ensures no valuable customer insight gets lost in translation between platforms.

The system captures all review details including star ratings, written feedback, customer names, and timestamps. These appear in ProofHub as fully contextual comments that your team can immediately assign, discuss, or convert into tasks—all without leaving your project management environment.

How It Works

1. New Review Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Judge.me account for new reviews through Make.com's polling system. When detected, it captures the complete review payload including metadata.

2. Data Formatting

The system structures the review into a standardized format, adding emoji indicators for star ratings and proper formatting for readability in ProofHub.

3. ProofHub Comment Creation

Using ProofHub's API, the workflow creates a new comment in your designated project/task. It intelligently maps review data to appropriate comment fields while maintaining all original context.

4. Notification Triggers

Optionally, the workflow can trigger ProofHub notifications or tag specific team members based on review content or rating value (e.g., flagging negative reviews for immediate attention).
