What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between personal task management in Todoist and team project tracking in ProofHub. Many professionals use Todoist for individual productivity while their teams collaborate in ProofHub, creating duplicate work and visibility gaps when manually transferring tasks between systems.

The workflow solves this by automatically creating ProofHub tasks whenever new items are added to specified Todoist projects. It captures task names, descriptions, due dates, and priority levels, ensuring your personal tasks seamlessly integrate with team workflows without manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Todoist Task

The automation monitors your Todoist account for new tasks in specified projects. When detected, it immediately captures all relevant task details including content, due date, priority, and any labels.

2. Action: Create ProofHub Task

Make then connects to your ProofHub account and creates a matching task in the designated project. The system maps Todoist fields to corresponding ProofHub fields, maintaining data consistency across platforms.

3. Optional: Notification

You can configure the workflow to send confirmation notifications via email or Slack when tasks successfully transfer, providing visibility into the synchronization process.

Pro tip: Set up separate workflows for different Todoist projects to route tasks to appropriate ProofHub projects automatically.
