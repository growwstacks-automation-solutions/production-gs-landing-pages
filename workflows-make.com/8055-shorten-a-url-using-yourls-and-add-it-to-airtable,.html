What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually shortening URLs and maintaining tracking records. Marketing teams often waste hours each week creating short links across various campaigns and documenting them in spreadsheets. The workflow automatically:

1. Takes any long URL from your source (email campaigns, social media, etc.)
2. Creates a branded short version using YOURLS
3. Logs both versions in Airtable with timestamps and metadata
4. Makes the shortened link immediately available for team use

Pro tip: Configure your YOURLS instance with custom domains to reinforce brand recognition in all shared links.

How It Works

Step 1: URL Submission

The workflow triggers when a new URL is added to your designated source, whether through a webhook, form submission, or direct database entry.

Step 2: YOURLS Processing

Make.com connects to your YOURLS instance through its API, sending the original URL and receiving back the shortened version with your custom domain.

Step 3: Airtable Logging

The system creates a new record in your specified Airtable base, storing both URL versions, creation timestamp, and any relevant campaign metadata.

Step 4: Notification & Distribution

Optionally, the workflow can notify team members via Slack or email, or make the shortened link available through other integrated apps.
