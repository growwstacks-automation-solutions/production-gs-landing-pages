What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of monitoring RSS feeds, shortening links, and sharing them in Discord communities. Content marketers, community managers, and social media teams waste hours each week copying links, visiting shortening services, and posting updates across multiple channels.

The workflow automatically detects new items in your RSS feed, processes them through your YOURLS link shortener, and posts the clean, branded links directly to your Discord channels. It maintains consistency in your shared content while eliminating repetitive manual work.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your specified RSS feed for new items. When new content is detected, it immediately captures the title and original URL for processing.

2. YOURLS Link Shortening

Each new URL is sent to your YOURLS installation, which generates a branded short link. The workflow preserves the original content title while creating a clean, trackable link.

3. Discord Message Formatting

The system formats a Discord message combining the original content title with your shortened URL. You can customize the message format to include emojis, additional text, or specific formatting.

4. Automated Posting

Using Discord webhooks, the formatted message is automatically posted to your specified channel. The workflow includes error handling to retry failed posts and confirmation of successful delivery.

Pro tip: Configure multiple RSS feeds to different Discord channels based on content type. For example, send blog posts to #announcements and product updates to #releases.
