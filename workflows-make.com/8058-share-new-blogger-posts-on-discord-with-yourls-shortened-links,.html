What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually sharing new blog posts across platforms. Every time you publish a new post on Blogger, the workflow automatically creates a shortened link using your YOURLS service and shares it in your designated Discord channel.

The solution eliminates several manual steps: copying the post URL, visiting your URL shortener, creating the shortened link, then switching to Discord to compose and send the message. For active bloggers, this can save hours per month while ensuring consistent, timely sharing of content with your community.

How It Works

1. New post detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Blogger account for new posts. When it detects a new publication, it immediately captures the post URL and title.

2. URL shortening

The system sends the full blog post URL to your YOURLS service, which generates a clean, shortened link. This makes the shared link more visually appealing and provides click tracking capabilities.

3. Discord message creation

The workflow formats a Discord message containing the post title, shortened URL, and any additional text you've configured. You can customize the message format to match your community's style.

4. Automated posting

Finally, the workflow posts the prepared message to your specified Discord channel, notifying your community about the new content instantly after publication.

Pro tip: Add relevant hashtags or emoji reactions to your automated Discord messages to increase engagement with your shared posts.
