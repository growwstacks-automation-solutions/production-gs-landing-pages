What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually transferring contact information from Google Forms to your SMSC (Short Message Service Center) database. Every time someone submits your Google Form - whether it's for event registrations, lead capture, customer feedback, or membership applications - their information is instantly and accurately added as a new contact in SMSC.

The workflow eliminates the need for spreadsheet exports, copy-pasting, or re-typing contact details. It ensures your SMS marketing lists, customer databases, and communication systems are always up-to-date with the latest submissions, enabling faster response times and more effective outreach campaigns.

How It Works

1. New form response trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Form for new submissions. As soon as someone completes the form, the automation is triggered.

2. Data extraction and formatting

Make.com extracts all the response data and formats it according to SMSC's contact field requirements. This includes proper handling of phone numbers, names, and any custom fields you've included in your form.

3. Contact creation in SMSC

The formatted data is sent to SMSC via API, creating a new contact record with all the submitted information. The workflow handles authentication and data mapping automatically.

4. Success confirmation

The system verifies the contact was successfully created in SMSC and can optionally log the transaction or notify administrators.
