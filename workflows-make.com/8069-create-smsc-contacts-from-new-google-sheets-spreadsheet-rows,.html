What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually transferring contact information from spreadsheets to your SMSC (Short Message Service Center) system. Every time your team adds a new row to your designated Google Sheet - whether it's a new lead, customer, or event attendee - the workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact record in SMSC.

The template handles all the field mapping between your spreadsheet columns and SMSC contact fields. You can customize which columns correspond to which contact attributes, ensuring your data transfers exactly as needed. This eliminates duplicate data entry, reduces human error, and keeps your contact lists perfectly synchronized.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Your Google Sheet

The workflow continuously checks your specified Google Sheet for new rows. You can configure how often it checks (from every few minutes to every 30 seconds) based on your needs and Make.com plan level.

2. Processing New Rows

When a new row is detected, the workflow extracts all the relevant contact information. It validates that required fields are present and formatted correctly before proceeding to the next step.

3. Creating SMSC Contacts

The workflow connects to your SMSC account via API and creates a new contact using the data from the spreadsheet row. All field mappings are preserved according to your configuration.

4. Error Handling and Notifications

If any issues occur during contact creation (like missing required fields or API errors), the workflow can notify your team via email or Slack and optionally skip or retry the record.
