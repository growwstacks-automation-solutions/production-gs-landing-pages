What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your ActiveCampaign email marketing platform and SMSC SMS service. Whenever a new contact is added to ActiveCampaign—whether through a form submission, manual entry, or import—the workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact record in your SMSC account.

The solution eliminates the need for manual data transfers between systems, ensuring your SMS marketing lists stay perfectly synchronized with your email contacts. This is particularly valuable for businesses running coordinated email and SMS campaigns where consistent contact data is crucial.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors your ActiveCampaign account for new contact additions through webhooks or periodic checks.

2. Data Extraction

Key contact details (name, phone number, email, custom fields) are extracted from ActiveCampaign and formatted for SMSC.

3. SMSC Integration

The workflow authenticates with your SMSC API and prepares the contact data for upload.

4. Contact Creation

The new contact is added to your specified SMSC list with all mapped fields populated.

5. Success Handling

The workflow confirms successful sync and can optionally tag the ActiveCampaign contact or send notifications.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy this template to your Make.com account
  2. Connect your ActiveCampaign and SMSC accounts
  3. Map the contact fields between systems
  4. Set any filters for which contacts should sync
  5. Test with a new ActiveCampaign contact
  6. Activate the workflow

Pro tip: Create a custom field in ActiveCampaign to track SMS opt-in status, then configure the workflow to only sync contacts who have opted in for SMS marketing.
