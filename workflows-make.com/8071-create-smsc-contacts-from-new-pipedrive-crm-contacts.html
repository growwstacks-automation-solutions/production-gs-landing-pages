What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between sales and marketing teams by ensuring every new contact added to Pipedrive CRM automatically becomes available in your SMSC (SMS marketing platform). Instead of manually exporting and importing contacts—a process prone to errors and delays—this workflow handles the transfer seamlessly in the background.

The moment a sales rep adds a new lead in Pipedrive, the system captures all relevant contact information and pushes it to SMSC with proper field mappings. This means your marketing team can launch SMS campaigns to fresh leads immediately, while sales benefits from having all communication history in one place.

Pro tip: Combine this with a welcome SMS automation to engage new leads within minutes of them entering your CRM, significantly improving conversion rates.

How It Works

1. New Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors Pipedrive for new contact creations through Make.com's instant webhook or polling mechanism. No need for manual exports or scheduled syncs.

2. Data Formatting

Contact details are transformed to match SMSC's expected format. Phone numbers are standardized, custom fields are mapped, and any CRM-specific data is filtered or reformatted as needed.

3. SMSC Integration

Using SMSC's API, the workflow creates a new contact record with all transferred data. The system handles authentication and any required API rate limiting automatically.

4. Error Handling

If any step fails (like an invalid phone number), the workflow logs the error and can be configured to retry or notify administrators while allowing other contacts to process successfully.
