What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges Telegram's convenience with SMS's universal reach. When your team receives a Telegram message (like a delivery request or emergency alert), the workflow automatically converts it to an SMSC SMS message. This ensures critical information reaches recipients who may not have smartphones or stable internet access.

Businesses use this to maintain communication continuity across different technologies. Field staff can send quick Telegram updates that automatically convert to SMS for customers, combining the speed of instant messaging with the reliability of cellular networks. The system handles message formatting, character limits, and delivery tracking automatically.

How It Works

1. Telegram Message Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Telegram channel or chat for new messages. It captures both the message content and metadata like sender information.

2. Content Processing

Make.com formats the message for SMS constraints (160-character segments), removes unsupported emojis, and can append standardized headers like "[Alert]" or "[Dispatch]".

3. SMSC Delivery

The processed message routes through your SMSC gateway to the recipient's mobile number. Delivery receipts can optionally trigger confirmation messages back to Telegram.

Who This Is For

This solution benefits any business that needs to:

  • Communicate with customers using basic mobile phones
  • Coordinate field teams using Telegram but notify clients via SMS
  • Ensure delivery of time-sensitive alerts regardless of internet connectivity
  • Maintain message logs across both platforms automatically

Pro tip: Combine this with number validation to automatically format international numbers correctly before SMS dispatch.
