What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between task creation in Checkvist and team communication in Slack. Every time a team member adds a new item to your shared Checkvist list—whether it's a task, subtask, or note—the workflow instantly sends a formatted notification to your designated Slack channel.

Manual task notifications waste valuable time and often lead to missed updates. This solution eliminates those inefficiencies by creating a real-time connection between your task management system and team communication platform. The automation captures all relevant task details from Checkvist and delivers them directly to where your team collaborates.

How It Works

1. Checkvist Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Checkvist list for new items using Make.com's instant trigger. As soon as an item appears, the automation captures all its details including title, description, assignee, due date, and any custom fields.

2. Data Processing

Make.com processes the Checkvist item data, allowing you to add conditional logic. You can filter notifications based on priority levels, specific lists, or custom criteria to prevent notification overload.

3. Slack Notification

The workflow formats the collected information into a clean Slack message using blocks and attachments. You can customize the message layout to highlight key details like due dates or assignees, and even include direct @mentions for specific team members.

Pro tip: Add emoji reactions or action buttons to your Slack notifications so team members can quickly acknowledge tasks or mark them complete without leaving Slack.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Checkvist account in the first module
  3. Specify which list to monitor for new items
  4. Connect your Slack account in the notification module

  7. Turn on the scenario to begin automatic notifications
