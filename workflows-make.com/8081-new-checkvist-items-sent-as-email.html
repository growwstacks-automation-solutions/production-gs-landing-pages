What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of task management visibility by instantly notifying you via email whenever new items are added to your Checkvist task lists. Instead of constantly checking your Checkvist dashboard or relying on team members to notify you about new tasks, this workflow automatically sends all relevant details to your inbox the moment a task is created.

The solution is particularly valuable for project managers, team leads, and collaborators who need to stay informed about new tasks being assigned or added to shared lists. Whether you're overseeing multiple projects or simply want to stay on top of your personal task management, this automation ensures you never miss important updates.

How It Works

1. Detects new Checkvist items

The workflow continuously monitors your specified Checkvist lists for any newly created items using Make.com's instant trigger functionality.

2. Gathers task details

When a new item is detected, the automation collects all relevant information including the task name, description, due date, priority level, and any custom fields.

3. Formats the email notification

The system structures the collected data into a clean, readable email format with clear labeling of all task attributes.

4. Sends the email

The completed notification is delivered to your specified email address(es) immediately, with the option to customize the sender name and subject line.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to include Checkvist list names in the email subject so you can quickly identify which project the new task belongs to.
