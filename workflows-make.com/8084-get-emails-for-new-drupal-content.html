What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of content teams needing to manually check Drupal for new published content or relying on inconsistent notification systems. When multiple editors, reviewers, or stakeholders need to be informed about content updates, manual processes create delays and potential oversights.

The workflow automatically detects new content in your Drupal CMS and sends immediate email notifications to designated recipients. It eliminates the need for team members to constantly monitor the CMS dashboard while ensuring timely awareness of all content updates across your organization.

How It Works

1. Drupal Triggers the Workflow

Whenever new content is published in Drupal, the system sends a webhook notification to Make.com. This can be configured to trigger on specific content types or publication statuses.

2. Content Details Are Extracted

The workflow pulls relevant information from the Drupal content node including title, author, publication date, URL, and any custom fields you specify. This data is formatted for the email notification.

3. Email Notification Is Sent

A customized email template is populated with the content details and sent to your specified recipients. The email can include direct links to the content for quick review.

Pro tip: Configure different recipient lists for different content types - for example, send marketing content to your communications team while sending product updates to your sales team.
