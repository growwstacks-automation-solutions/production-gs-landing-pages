What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of maintaining consistent contact information across Teamleader (a popular CRM for service businesses) and ActiveCampaign (a powerful marketing automation platform). When sales teams update client details in Teamleader, those changes often need to be manually copied to ActiveCampaign - until now.

The workflow automatically detects contact updates in Teamleader and instantly syncs them to ActiveCampaign. New contacts are created, while existing ones are updated, preventing duplicate entries. This ensures your marketing team always works with current customer data without manual intervention.

Pro tip: Combine this with our ActiveCampaign to Teamleader sync template for complete bidirectional contact management.

How It Works

1. Contact Update Detection

The workflow continuously monitors Teamleader for any contact changes, whether new additions or updates to existing records.

2. Data Extraction

When a change is detected, the system pulls all relevant contact information including name, email, phone, company, and custom fields.

3. ActiveCampaign Matching

The workflow checks ActiveCampaign for existing contacts using email as the unique identifier to prevent duplicates.

4. Smart Sync Logic

New contacts are created in ActiveCampaign, while existing ones are updated with the latest information from Teamleader.
