What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of delayed awareness when clients accept your Qwilr proposals, contracts, or other documents. Without automation, sales teams must manually check Qwilr for acceptance status or wait for email notifications that often get buried in inboxes.

The workflow instantly sends Telegram messages to your team whenever a page is accepted in Qwilr, including key details like the client name, document title, and direct link. This enables faster follow-up actions like scheduling kickoff calls, processing payments, or updating CRMs.

How It Works

1. Qwilr Triggers the Workflow

Make.com monitors your Qwilr account for any page acceptance events through real-time webhooks. The moment a client accepts a document, the workflow activates.

2. Data is Processed

The system extracts relevant information from the Qwilr event including the document title, client details, acceptance timestamp, and direct link to the signed version.

3. Telegram Notification is Sent

A formatted message containing all critical details is automatically delivered to your specified Telegram group or channel. Team members receive mobile notifications instantly.

Pro tip: Customize the Telegram message template to include your branding and specific next steps for your team to take upon acceptance.
