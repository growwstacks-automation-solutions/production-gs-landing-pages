What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of document approval delays in business workflows. When using Qwilr for proposals, contracts, or other important documents, teams often waste time manually checking for approvals or waiting for email notifications that get buried in inboxes.

The workflow automatically detects when a page is accepted in Qwilr and immediately posts a customizable notification to your Slack channel. This gives your entire team real-time visibility into document approvals without any manual tracking, helping accelerate follow-up actions and improving overall workflow efficiency.

How It Works

1. Qwilr Triggers the Workflow

The automation monitors your Qwilr account for any page status changes to "accepted." This could be a signed contract, approved proposal, or any other document requiring team notification.

2. Make.com Processes the Approval

The workflow extracts key details like the document title, who approved it, and direct links. You can customize which metadata to include based on your team's needs.

3. Slack Receives the Notification

A formatted message posts to your designated Slack channel with all relevant approval details. Team members can immediately click through to view the approved document and take next steps.

Pro tip: Configure different Slack channels for different document types (e.g., #sales-contracts vs #hr-docs) to keep notifications organized.
