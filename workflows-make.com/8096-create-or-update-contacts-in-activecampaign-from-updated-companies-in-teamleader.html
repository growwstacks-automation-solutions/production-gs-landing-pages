What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap between sales and marketing operations. When your sales team updates company information in Teamleader (like new addresses, phone numbers, or key contacts), those changes often don't automatically reflect in your marketing automation platform. This creates data silos where marketing campaigns operate on outdated information.

The workflow bridges this gap by instantly creating or updating ActiveCampaign contacts whenever a company record changes in Teamleader. It intelligently matches existing contacts to prevent duplicates while ensuring all your marketing automations, lead scoring, and segmentation work with the most current company data available.

How It Works

1. Trigger: Company update in Teamleader

The workflow monitors Teamleader for any changes to company records. This could be edits to basic information, new contacts added to companies, or status changes.

2. Data mapping and transformation

The workflow extracts relevant company data from Teamleader and maps it to corresponding ActiveCampaign contact fields. You can customize which fields transfer and how they're formatted.

3. Contact lookup in ActiveCampaign

Before creating new records, the system checks ActiveCampaign for existing contacts using configurable matching criteria (typically email or company name).

4. Create or update contact

Depending on the lookup results, the workflow either creates a new ActiveCampaign contact with all the company data or updates an existing record with the latest information.

5. Optional tagging and segmentation

You can configure the workflow to add specific tags or apply custom fields in ActiveCampaign based on the type of update from Teamleader (e.g., "New Location" or "Key Contact Added").

Pro tip: Add a "Last Synced" date field in ActiveCampaign to track when information was updated from Teamleader, helping your team verify data freshness.
