What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for sales teams and service providers who use Qwilr for proposals and Airtable for CRM or project management. Every time a client accepts your Qwilr proposal, this workflow automatically creates a detailed record in your Airtable base, eliminating manual data entry and ensuring your systems stay perfectly synchronized.

The template captures all essential information from accepted Qwilr pages - client details, proposal content, pricing, terms, and acceptance metadata - then structures it appropriately in your Airtable. This creates a seamless handoff from sales to fulfillment while maintaining complete visibility across your organization.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New page accepted in Qwilr

The workflow activates whenever a client accepts your Qwilr proposal. Make.com monitors your Qwilr account for these acceptance events in real-time.

2. Data extraction from Qwilr

The template pulls all relevant information from the accepted page, including custom fields you've set up in Qwilr. This ensures no critical deal details are missed during the transfer.

3. Data transformation

Make.com reformats the Qwilr data to match your Airtable structure, handling date formats, currency values, and field mappings automatically.

4. Record creation in Airtable

The workflow creates a new record in your specified Airtable base, populating all mapped fields with the proposal data. You maintain complete control over which fields get updated.

Pro tip: Add a conditional step to notify your fulfillment team immediately when high-value proposals are accepted, creating instant visibility for priority clients.
