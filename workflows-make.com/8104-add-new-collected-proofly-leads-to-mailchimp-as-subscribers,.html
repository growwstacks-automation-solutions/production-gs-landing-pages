What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in many marketing funnels - the manual transfer of leads from collection tools like Proofly to email marketing platforms like Mailchimp. Every minute a lead sits unprocessed decreases your conversion chances.

The workflow monitors your Proofly account for new leads and instantly adds them to a specified Mailchimp list as subscribers. It handles all field mapping automatically, ensuring clean data transfer without spreadsheet exports or copy-paste errors that plague manual processes.

How It Works

1. New lead trigger

The workflow constantly monitors your Proofly account for new lead events. When a visitor submits a form or interacts with a campaign, Proofly generates a lead record.

2. Data extraction

Make.com retrieves all available lead information including contact details, custom fields, and metadata about the lead source and campaign.

3. Field mapping

The workflow matches Proofly fields to corresponding Mailchimp merge fields according to your configuration. This ensures data lands in the right places in Mailchimp.

4. Subscriber addition

Make.com adds the lead to your specified Mailchimp list with proper consent flags and tags based on the Proofly campaign they came from.

Pro tip: Configure tags in Mailchimp based on the Proofly campaign source to enable targeted follow-up sequences.

What You'll Need

  1. Active Proofly account with lead collection forms/campaigns
  2. Mailchimp account with at least one audience/list created
  3. Make.com account (free plan sufficient for basic usage)
  4. API keys for both Proofly and Mailchimp

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the template in your Make.com account
  2. Connect your Proofly account via API key
  3. Connect your Mailchimp account via OAuth
  4. Map Proofly fields to Mailchimp merge fields
  5. Select your target Mailchimp audience/list
  6. Test with a sample lead and activate the workflow
