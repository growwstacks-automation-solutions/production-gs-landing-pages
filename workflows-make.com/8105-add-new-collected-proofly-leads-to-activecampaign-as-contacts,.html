What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of leads getting stuck in silos between your lead capture system (Proofly) and your CRM/marketing automation platform (ActiveCampaign). Without automation, valuable leads collected through Proofly forms might sit unnoticed until someone manually imports them - creating delays that hurt conversion rates.

The workflow automatically detects new leads in Proofly and instantly creates corresponding contacts in ActiveCampaign with all their collected information. This ensures your sales and marketing teams can begin nurturing leads immediately while they're most engaged.

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow constantly monitors your Proofly account for new lead submissions through forms, popups, or other collection methods.

2. Data extraction

All collected lead information (name, email, phone, custom fields) is extracted from Proofly and prepared for transfer.

3. ActiveCampaign contact creation

The lead data is mapped to ActiveCampaign's contact fields and a new contact record is created automatically.

4. Optional list/tag assignment

You can configure the workflow to add contacts to specific lists or apply tags based on lead source or other criteria.

Pro tip: Add a delay step before ActiveCampaign creation to allow for lead validation or scoring if needed.
