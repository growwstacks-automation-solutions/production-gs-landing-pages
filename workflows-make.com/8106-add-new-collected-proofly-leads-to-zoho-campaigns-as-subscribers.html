What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in digital marketing operations - the manual transfer of leads from collection tools like Proofly to email marketing platforms like Zoho Campaigns. Every minute a lead sits unprocessed is a missed opportunity for engagement.

The workflow automatically detects new leads collected through Proofly forms and instantly adds them as subscribers in your Zoho Campaigns account. It handles all the data mapping between platforms, ensuring your email sequences can begin nurturing leads immediately after collection.

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Proofly account for new lead submissions through configured forms. Each new entry triggers the automation.

2. Data extraction

Key lead information is extracted from the Proofly submission, including contact details, form responses, and any custom fields you've set up.

3. Field mapping

The workflow matches Proofly fields to corresponding Zoho Campaigns subscriber fields, ensuring data lands in the right places.

4. Subscriber creation

The lead is added to your specified Zoho Campaigns mailing list with all mapped information intact.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add tags based on the Proofly form source for better segmentation in Zoho Campaigns.
