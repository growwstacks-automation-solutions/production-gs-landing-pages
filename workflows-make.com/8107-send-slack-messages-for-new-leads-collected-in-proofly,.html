What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of delayed lead response times by instantly notifying your sales team via Slack whenever a new lead submits information through Proofly. Instead of manually checking Proofly or waiting for email notifications, your team gets real-time alerts with all the lead details right in their Slack workspace.

The workflow captures every form submission from Proofly, extracts the lead information (including custom fields), formats it into a clear Slack message, and delivers it to your designated Slack channel. This eliminates the risk of leads slipping through the cracks while dramatically improving your team's ability to respond quickly - a key factor in conversion rates.

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Proofly account for new form submissions using Make.com's polling mechanism. As soon as a submission is detected, the automation triggers.

2. Data extraction

The system retrieves all available lead information from the Proofly submission, including standard fields like name and email, plus any custom fields you've configured in your forms.

3. Slack message formatting

The raw lead data gets transformed into a well-structured Slack message with clear labeling of each field. The template includes basic formatting but can be customized to match your team's preferences.

4. Channel delivery

The formatted message gets sent to your pre-configured Slack channel, appearing as a notification to all channel members. You can set up different channels for different types of leads or campaigns.

Pro tip: Add an emoji reaction button to your Slack notifications so team members can quickly claim leads without leaving Slack.
