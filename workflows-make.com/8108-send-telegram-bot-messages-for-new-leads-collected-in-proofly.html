Telegram Alerts for New Proofly Leads

Get instant notifications in Telegram whenever a new lead is captured in your Proofly forms

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of delayed lead response times by instantly notifying your team via Telegram whenever a new lead submits information through your Proofly forms. Instead of manually checking for new submissions or relying on slow email notifications, your sales team gets real-time alerts with all the lead details they need to follow up immediately.

Studies show that responding to leads within 5 minutes makes you 9x more likely to convert them. This workflow ensures no lead slips through the cracks by delivering complete contact information directly to your team's preferred communication channel, enabling faster engagement and higher conversion rates.

How It Works

1. New Lead Capture

The workflow triggers automatically whenever a new submission is received in your connected Proofly form. It captures all form fields including custom data points you've configured.

2. Data Processing

Make.com structures the lead information into a standardized format, applying any filters or transformations you've set up (like formatting phone numbers or calculating lead scores).

3. Telegram Notification

The system sends a customizable message to your specified Telegram channel or chat, including all relevant lead details. The message can include buttons for quick actions like calling the lead directly.

This automation is ideal for:

What You'll Need

  1. An active Proofly account with at least one form

  1. Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template to your Make.com account
