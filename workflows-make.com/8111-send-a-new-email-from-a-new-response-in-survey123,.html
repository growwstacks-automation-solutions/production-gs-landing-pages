What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually processing survey responses and sending follow-up communications. When customers complete your Survey123 form, they often expect immediate acknowledgment or next steps. Manual processing creates delays that can frustrate respondents and cause missed opportunities.

The workflow automatically captures new Survey123 submissions, extracts the response data, and sends customized emails within seconds. You can configure it to send confirmation emails to respondents, notifications to your team, or both. The template includes placeholders for common survey fields like name, email, and response details.

How It Works

1. Survey Response Trigger

The workflow monitors your Survey123 account for new submissions. When someone completes your form, Make.com detects the new entry instantly.

2. Data Extraction

The system pulls all response data including custom fields you've defined in your survey. This becomes available for use in your email templates.

3. Email Composition

Using your pre-designed template, the workflow inserts the respondent's answers into appropriate fields. You can customize the email layout, branding, and content.

4. Automated Delivery

The completed email sends through your connected email service (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) to the designated recipients - either the respondent, your team, or both.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to route different response types to specific team members or departments based on survey answers.
