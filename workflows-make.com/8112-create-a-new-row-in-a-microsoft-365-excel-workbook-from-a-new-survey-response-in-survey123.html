What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical data management challenge faced by organizations using Survey123 for ArcGIS to collect field data, customer feedback, or service requests. Instead of manually copying responses into spreadsheets—a time-consuming and error-prone process—this workflow automatically creates a new row in your Microsoft 365 Excel workbook for every survey submission.

The system captures all response data including timestamps, ensuring your Excel records always reflect the most current survey data without any lag. Field teams can focus on data collection while office staff instantly access responses in Excel for analysis, reporting, or further processing.

How It Works

1. Survey Submission Trigger

The workflow activates immediately when a new response is submitted in Survey123. Make.com detects the new entry through Survey123's webhook integration.

2. Data Extraction

The system retrieves all survey answers along with metadata like submission time and device location (if enabled in your survey). This ensures comprehensive data capture beyond just the questionnaire responses.

3. Excel Row Creation

Using Microsoft 365's API, the workflow creates a new row in your specified Excel workbook. Each survey question maps to its designated column, maintaining your preferred data structure.

4. Data Validation

Optional modules can validate responses before Excel insertion, checking for required fields, data formats, or business rules to maintain data quality.
