What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of lost or delayed leads from Facebook advertising campaigns. When potential customers submit your Facebook Lead Ads form, their information often sits unprocessed until someone manually transfers it to your CRM. This template eliminates that friction point by automatically creating a new lead in noCRM the moment someone submits your Facebook form.

The workflow ensures your sales team receives complete lead information immediately, while the prospect is still engaged. Studies show that responding to leads within 5 minutes increases conversion rates by 9x compared to waiting just 30 minutes. By automating this handoff, you'll capture more qualified leads and give your team the best chance to convert them.

How It Works

1. Facebook Lead Ads Trigger

The workflow monitors your Facebook Lead Ads account for new form submissions. When someone completes your lead form, Facebook sends the data to Make.com within seconds.

2. Data Processing

The automation extracts all form fields including contact details, custom questions, and metadata about the ad campaign that generated the lead. It validates required fields (especially email) and formats the data for noCRM.

3. noCRM Lead Creation

Make.com creates a new lead record in your noCRM account with all available information. The workflow maps Facebook form fields to corresponding noCRM fields automatically.

4. Success Confirmation

The system logs each successful sync and can optionally notify your team via email or Slack when high-value leads come through.

Pro tip: Add a lead scoring step to prioritize hot prospects. Score based on form answers, ad campaign, or response time to help your sales team focus on the most promising leads first.
