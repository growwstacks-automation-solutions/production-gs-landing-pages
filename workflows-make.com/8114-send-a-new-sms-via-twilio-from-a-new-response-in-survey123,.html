What This Workflow Does

This automation instantly sends SMS messages via Twilio whenever someone submits a response through your Survey123 form. It eliminates manual follow-ups and ensures respondents receive immediate acknowledgment of their submission - critical for time-sensitive surveys, service requests, or field data collection.

The template maps common survey fields like name, phone number, and problem description into personalized SMS messages. For example, a utility company could automatically text: "John, we received your outage report at 123 Main St. Crew dispatched - ETA 45 minutes. Reply STOP to unsubscribe."

How It Works

1. Survey123 Triggers the Workflow

Whenever a respondent submits your Survey123 form, Make.com detects the new response through ArcGIS webhooks. The workflow captures all survey answers as structured data.

2. Data Mapping and Validation

The workflow extracts key fields like phone number and formats them for SMS delivery. It verifies phone numbers are valid before sending to avoid Twilio errors.

3. Dynamic SMS Composition

Your predefined SMS template merges with survey responses. For the sample survey, it might create: "Thanks [Name]! We'll contact you at [Phone] about: [Problem]".

4. Twilio SMS Delivery

Make.com sends the composed message through your Twilio account, which handles carrier delivery globally. Delivery status is logged for troubleshooting.

Pro tip: Add a survey question asking preferred contact method (SMS/email) to respect communication preferences and comply with regulations like TCPA.
