What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between Zistemo's time tracking and invoicing capabilities with Trello's visual project management. Whenever a new item is created in Zistemo—whether it's a client project, task, or service item—the workflow automatically generates a corresponding card in your Trello board with all relevant details.

The integration eliminates manual data entry between systems, ensuring your project management stays perfectly synchronized with your time tracking and billing data. Team members get immediate visibility into new work items without having to switch between applications or manually update multiple systems.

How It Works

1. New Item Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Zistemo account for new items using Make.com's polling trigger. When a new item appears, the automation captures all available data including description, due date, assigned team members, and any attachments.

2. Data Mapping

Key information from the Zistemo item is mapped to corresponding Trello card fields. The automation can extract client names, project codes, time estimates, and priority levels to create comprehensive Trello cards.

3. Card Creation

The workflow creates a new card in your specified Trello board and list. You can configure the template to include custom labels, checklists, due dates, and attachments based on the Zistemo item properties.

4. Notification

Optionally, the workflow can notify team members via Slack or email when new cards are created, ensuring immediate awareness of new tasks or projects.
