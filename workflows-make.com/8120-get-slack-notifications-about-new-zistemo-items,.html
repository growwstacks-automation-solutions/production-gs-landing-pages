What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of delayed awareness about new time entries, expenses, and projects in Zistemo. Without automation, team members must manually check Zistemo or wait for periodic reports, creating bottlenecks in project management and financial tracking.

The workflow instantly notifies relevant Slack channels whenever new items appear in Zistemo. Project managers get real-time visibility into team time tracking, accounting receives immediate expense alerts, and leadership stays informed about new project creations - all without manual monitoring.

How It Works

1. Zistemo Triggers the Workflow

Make.com monitors your Zistemo account for new items using webhooks or API polling. When a time entry, expense, project, or other configured item type is created, the workflow immediately activates.

2. Data Processing and Filtering

The workflow extracts relevant details like project name, user, hours logged, or expense amount. Optional filters can route only specific item types or values to particular Slack channels.

3. Slack Notification Delivery

Make.com formats the notification with your preferred level of detail and sends it to designated Slack channels or direct messages. Notifications can include interactive buttons or links back to Zistemo for quick access.

Pro tip: Add emoji reactions or color-coding to your Slack notifications to visually distinguish between time entries, expenses, and projects at a glance.
