What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your scheduling (Google Calendar) and task management (Zistemo) systems. Every time you or your team schedules a meeting or event in Google Calendar, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding task in Zistemo with all relevant details.

The solution eliminates manual data entry between systems, ensuring your time tracking always reflects your actual schedule. Consultants, agencies, and professional service firms particularly benefit from this integration by maintaining perfect alignment between scheduled appointments and billable tasks.

How It Works

1. New Calendar Event Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Google Calendar for new events. You can configure it to watch specific calendars or apply filters based on event titles, attendees, or other criteria.

2. Data Extraction and Mapping

When a new event is detected, the workflow extracts key information like event title, description, start/end times, and attendees. This data is then formatted to match Zistemo's task structure.

3. Task Creation in Zistemo

The formatted event details are used to automatically create a new task in your Zistemo account. The task includes all relevant information from the calendar event, ready for time tracking and project management.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only create tasks for events with specific keywords (like "Client" or "Meeting") to avoid cluttering your task list with personal events.
