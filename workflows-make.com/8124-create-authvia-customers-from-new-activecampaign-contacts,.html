What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring new leads from your marketing automation system (ActiveCampaign) to your payment processing platform (Authvia). Every time a sales or marketing team adds a new contact in ActiveCampaign, this workflow automatically creates a corresponding customer record in Authvia.

The integration ensures your billing system always stays in sync with your CRM, eliminating duplicate data entry and reducing errors that occur when manually copying contact details between systems. This is particularly valuable for subscription businesses, SaaS companies, and agencies that need to quickly onboard new paying customers.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

Make.com monitors your ActiveCampaign account for newly created contacts, checking at regular intervals you configure (typically every 15-60 minutes).

2. Data Mapping

The workflow extracts relevant contact information like name, email, phone number, and any custom fields you specify, then formats this data to match Authvia's customer record structure.

3. Customer Creation

Make.com uses Authvia's API to create a new customer record with the mapped contact details, ensuring all required fields are populated according to your Authvia account settings.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only sync contacts tagged as "Customer" or from specific ActiveCampaign lists to prevent unnecessary records in Authvia.
