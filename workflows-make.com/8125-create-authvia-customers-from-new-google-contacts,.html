What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common pain point for businesses using both Google Contacts and Authvia - the tedious manual process of transferring new contact information between systems. Every time you add a new contact in Google, whether from an email exchange, business card, or event registration, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding customer record in Authvia.

The integration ensures your sales and customer service teams always have access to complete, up-to-date customer information without requiring duplicate data entry. This is particularly valuable for businesses that collect initial contact information through Gmail conversations or Google Forms, then need those contacts available in Authvia for customer management and marketing automation.

How It Works

1. New contact detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Google Contacts for new additions using Make.com's instant trigger capability. As soon as a new contact is created or imported, the automation is notified.

2. Data extraction

Make.com extracts all available contact information including name, email addresses, phone numbers, company details, and any custom fields you've configured in Google Contacts.

3. Authvia customer creation

The workflow maps the Google Contact fields to the corresponding Authvia customer profile fields and creates a new customer record via Authvia's API. You can customize which fields are included and how they're mapped.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Authvia to track which contacts were automatically imported from Google, making it easy to filter and segment these customers later.
