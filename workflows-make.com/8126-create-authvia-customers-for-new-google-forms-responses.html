What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually creating customer records in Authvia from Google Form submissions. Every time someone completes your signup form, survey, or application, their information automatically flows into Authvia as a properly formatted customer record.

Businesses using this template typically see a 90% reduction in onboarding time while eliminating nearly all data entry errors. The workflow ensures all required fields are populated correctly, payment details are properly formatted, and new customers are immediately ready for billing in your Authvia system.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Form for new responses. As soon as someone submits the form, the automation begins processing their information.

2. Data Validation

Key fields are checked for completeness and formatting. The system can be configured to either skip incomplete submissions or send them for manual review.

3. Customer Record Creation

Validated form data is mapped to the corresponding Authvia customer fields, creating a new customer record with all necessary billing and contact information.

4. Confirmation & Error Handling

The workflow confirms successful creation or logs any errors for follow-up. You can optionally configure email notifications for each new customer.

Pro tip: Add a webhook step to notify your sales team immediately when high-value customers sign up through your form.
