What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for ecommerce businesses using Shopify with Authvia payment processing. When new customers sign up on your Shopify store, their information isn't automatically available in Authvia - until now.

The workflow eliminates manual customer data entry between systems, reducing administrative work by 80-90% for most merchants. It ensures payment profiles are created in Authvia immediately after customer registration, preventing failed transactions during first purchases.

How It Works

1. New Customer Trigger

The workflow monitors your Shopify store for new customer creation events in real-time.

2. Data Mapping

Essential customer details (name, email, billing info) are extracted and formatted for Authvia's API requirements.

3. Authvia Profile Creation

The mapped data automatically creates a corresponding customer profile in your Authvia payment system.

4. Error Handling

If any sync fails, the system retries or notifies your team for manual intervention.
