What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of creating merchant accounts in Authvia from spreadsheet data. Every time a new row is added to your designated Google Sheet, the workflow instantly creates a corresponding merchant record in Authvia with all the required details.

The template handles field mapping between your spreadsheet columns and Authvia's merchant creation API, ensuring data is properly formatted before submission. It includes validation checks to prevent errors and can be customized to match your specific spreadsheet structure.

How It Works

1. New row detection

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new rows added, either manually or through other integrations. It captures the entire row data for processing.

2. Data validation

Essential fields are checked for completeness and format. The workflow pauses if critical information is missing and can notify your team for correction.

3. Merchant creation

Validated data is transformed into the proper structure for Authvia's API and submitted to create the new merchant account automatically.

4. Result logging

The workflow updates your spreadsheet with creation status (success/failure) and any relevant reference IDs from Authvia for tracking purposes.

Pro tip: Use a dedicated "Status" column in your sheet to track which rows have been processed. This creates an audit trail and prevents duplicate processing.
