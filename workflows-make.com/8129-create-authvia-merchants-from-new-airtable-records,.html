What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical bottleneck in merchant onboarding by instantly creating Authvia merchant accounts whenever new records appear in your Airtable base. Instead of manually copying data between systems or waiting for your team to process applications, merchants are activated immediately with accurate details.

The workflow is particularly valuable for payment processors, SaaS platforms, and marketplaces that need to onboard multiple merchants daily. It handles the entire creation process including field mapping, data validation, and error handling - reducing setup time from hours to minutes while eliminating human errors in data transfer.

How It Works

1. New Airtable Record Detection

The workflow monitors your specified Airtable base for new records using Make.com's instant trigger. You can configure it to watch specific views or tables where merchant applications are submitted.

2. Data Validation & Transformation

Before creating the Authvia merchant, the workflow validates required fields and can transform data formats (like phone numbers or addresses) to match Authvia's requirements. Invalid records trigger notifications to your team.

3. Authvia Merchant Creation

Validated data is automatically sent to Authvia's API to create the merchant account with all necessary details mapped from your Airtable fields. The template includes example mappings you can customize.

4. Status Updates & Error Handling

Successful creations update the Airtable record with the new Authvia merchant ID and status. Failed attempts trigger retries and ultimately notify your team if manual intervention is needed.

Pro tip: Add a Slack or email notification step to alert your support team when high-priority merchants are created, allowing for immediate follow-up.
