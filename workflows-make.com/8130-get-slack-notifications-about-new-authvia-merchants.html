What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually monitoring new merchant signups in Authvia, your payment processing platform. Without automation, your team might miss important new accounts or delay critical onboarding steps like fraud checks, welcome communications, or account activation.

The workflow instantly detects when a new merchant registers in Authvia and sends a customizable Slack notification to your designated channel or direct message. This ensures your sales, support, and compliance teams stay informed in real-time without constantly checking the Authvia dashboard.

How It Works

1. Authvia Triggers the Workflow

Make.com monitors Authvia's API for new merchant creation events. When detected, the workflow pulls all relevant merchant data including account details, business information, and any custom fields you've configured.

2. Data Processing

The workflow formats the merchant information into a clean, readable structure. You can customize which fields to include, add conditional logic to highlight specific details, or enrich the data with additional API calls.

3. Slack Notification Delivery

Make.com sends the formatted merchant data to your specified Slack channel as a rich notification. The message can include clickable links back to the Authvia merchant profile, formatted text for easy scanning, and even interactive Slack buttons for quick actions.

Pro tip: Add emoji reactions or action buttons to your Slack notifications so team members can quickly claim responsibility for onboarding tasks.
