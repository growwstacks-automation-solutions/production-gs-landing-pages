What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually transferring customer information from Authvia to spreadsheets. Every time a new customer signs up through Authvia, their complete details are automatically added as a new row in your designated Google Sheet.

The workflow ensures your customer database stays perfectly synchronized without any staff intervention. You'll always have real-time visibility into your customer base while eliminating duplicate data entry across systems.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Authvia customer

The workflow activates instantly whenever Authvia registers a new customer, capturing all available customer data fields.

2. Process customer data

Make.com formats the customer information according to your Google Sheet structure, handling any necessary data transformations.

3. Add row to Google Sheet

The system appends the complete customer record to your specified Google Sheet, maintaining all your existing column formatting and formulas.

Pro tip: Use this Google Sheet as the source for other automations like welcome emails, CRM updates, or accounting entries to create a complete onboarding system.
