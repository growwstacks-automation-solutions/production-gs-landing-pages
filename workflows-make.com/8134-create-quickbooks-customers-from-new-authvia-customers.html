What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of transferring customer information between Authvia (your payment processing system) and QuickBooks (your accounting software). Every time a new customer is created in Authvia, Make.com automatically creates a corresponding customer record in QuickBooks with all essential details.

The workflow ensures your accounting system always has up-to-date customer information without requiring your team to manually enter data. It handles the transfer of key customer details including contact information, company name, and billing address - maintaining consistency between your payment and accounting systems.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger in Authvia

The workflow monitors Authvia for new customer creation events. When a customer signs up through your payment forms or is manually added to Authvia, the automation is triggered.

2. Customer data extraction

Make.com retrieves the complete customer record from Authvia, including all configured meta fields like email, phone number, address details, and company information.

3. Data formatting for QuickBooks

The system maps Authvia's customer data structure to QuickBooks' expected format, ensuring proper field matching between the two systems.

4. Duplicate checking

Before creating a new record, the workflow checks QuickBooks to verify the customer doesn't already exist, preventing duplicates.

5. QuickBooks customer creation

The formatted customer data is pushed to QuickBooks, creating a complete customer record ready for invoicing and accounting.

Pro tip: Configure custom field mappings in Make.com to handle any differences between Authvia's data structure and your QuickBooks customer fields.
