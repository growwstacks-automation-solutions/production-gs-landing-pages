What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for businesses using Stripe for online payments and Clover POS for in-person sales. Without integration, staff must manually re-enter customer information between systems—a time-consuming process prone to errors that creates disjointed customer experiences.

The workflow automatically creates a matching customer record in Clover POS whenever a new customer completes a Stripe transaction. This synchronization happens in real-time, ensuring your sales team has immediate access to complete customer information whether the purchase happened online or in-store.

How It Works

1. New Customer Trigger

The workflow monitors Stripe for new customer creation events, whether from checkout pages, subscriptions, or manual additions.

2. Data Transformation

Customer details are formatted to match Clover POS's expected structure, with address standardization and phone number formatting.

3. Duplicate Check

The system queries Clover POS to verify the customer doesn't already exist, using email and phone as matching criteria.

4. Record Creation

If no duplicate is found, the workflow creates a new customer in Clover POS with all available Stripe data.
