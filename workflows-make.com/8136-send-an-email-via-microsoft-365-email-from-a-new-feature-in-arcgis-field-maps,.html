What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges field data collection with office communication by automatically sending Microsoft 365 emails whenever your field staff adds new features in ArcGIS Field Maps. It eliminates the delays and errors of manual data reporting, ensuring the right teams get notified immediately about field observations, safety issues, or equipment status updates.

Perfect for utility companies, environmental agencies, and construction teams, this workflow transforms how field data reaches decision-makers. When inspectors log issues like damaged infrastructure or safety hazards, relevant departments receive structured emails with all contextual details - location coordinates, timestamps, photos, and custom field entries.

How It Works

1. Field staff submits data

Using ArcGIS Field Maps on mobile devices, field personnel capture geotagged observations, photos, and form data during inspections or surveys.

2. Make.com detects new features

The workflow constantly monitors your ArcGIS feature layer for new submissions, triggering whenever field data meets your configured criteria.

3. Email content assembles automatically

The system extracts relevant field data - including maps, timestamps, and custom form responses - to build informative email content.

4. Microsoft 365 delivers notifications

Personalized emails send to predefined recipients or dynamic distribution lists based on the field data type and priority level.
