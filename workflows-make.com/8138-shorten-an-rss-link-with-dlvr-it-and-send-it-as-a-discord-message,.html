What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the messy problem of sharing long RSS feed links in Discord channels. It automatically processes new RSS feed items, creates clean shortened links through dlvr.it, and posts them to your designated Discord channel with optional custom formatting.

For community managers and content marketers, this eliminates the manual copy-paste routine while providing valuable link tracking. The shortened links appear more professional and can increase click-through rates by 20-30% compared to raw URLs.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow constantly checks your specified RSS feed for new items. When new content appears, it triggers the automation process.

2. Link Shortening

Each new RSS item's URL is sent to dlvr.it's link shortening service. This creates a clean, trackable short link while preserving the original destination.

3. Discord Message Formatting

The system formats a Discord message with your preferred structure - typically including the article title and the new shortened link.

4. Automated Posting

The formatted message is automatically sent to your specified Discord channel through a webhook, keeping your community updated in real-time.

Pro tip: Add emoji reactions or @mentions to your automated messages to boost engagement from your Discord community.
