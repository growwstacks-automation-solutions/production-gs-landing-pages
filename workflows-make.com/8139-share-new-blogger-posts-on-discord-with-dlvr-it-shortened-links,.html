What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually sharing new blog posts across platforms. Whenever you publish new content on Blogger, the workflow automatically detects the post, creates a shortened link through dlvr.it, and shares it in your designated Discord channel with a customizable message.

For content creators and community managers, this eliminates the risk of forgetting to promote new posts while ensuring your Discord community receives updates immediately after publication. The professional shortened links make your shares cleaner and more clickable, while the automated process saves 5-10 minutes per post that would otherwise be spent copying links, formatting messages, and posting manually.

How It Works

1. New post detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Blogger account for new publications using Make.com's polling system. When a new post is detected, it extracts the post title, URL, and other relevant metadata.

2. Link shortening

The full Blogger URL is sent to dlvr.it's link shortening service, which generates a clean, trackable short link. This makes your shares more visually appealing in Discord and provides basic click analytics.

3. Discord message creation

The workflow formats a Discord message using your preferred template, combining the post title, shortened link, and any additional text or emojis you want to include. You can customize this message format to match your community's style.

4. Automated posting

Finally, the formatted message is sent to your specified Discord channel via webhook, appearing as if posted by your designated bot. The entire process happens within seconds of publication without any manual intervention.

Pro tip: Add UTM parameters to your links before shortening to track Discord-sourced traffic in Google Analytics. This helps measure how effectively your Discord community engages with your content.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Copy the template from Make.com to your account
  2. Connect your Blogger account in the workflow settings
  3. Set up your dlvr.it API credentials for link shortening
  4. Create a Discord webhook for your target channel and add it to the workflow
  5. Customize the message format to match your preferred posting style
  6. Test with a new Blogger post to verify the automation works
  7. Schedule the workflow to run at your desired frequency (recommended: every 15-30 minutes)
