What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of shortening URLs for marketing campaigns and social media posts. Instead of copying links between multiple tools, the workflow automatically takes any URL from your Google Sheets, processes it through dlvr.it's shortening service, then logs the shortened link back in your spreadsheet with timestamps.

Marketing teams running multiple campaigns can benefit from having all shortened links automatically organized in one searchable database. The template maintains consistency in your link tracking while eliminating repetitive manual work that often leads to errors or duplicate entries.

How It Works

1. Trigger from new spreadsheet entries

The workflow monitors your designated Google Sheet for new URL additions in a specific column. When a new link appears, it immediately initiates the shortening process.

2. URL processing with dlvr.it

Each URL gets sent to dlvr.it's API which generates a branded shortened link while preserving all original tracking parameters. The service provides analytics on clicks and geographic data.

3. Automated spreadsheet updates

The shortened URL gets written back to your Google Sheet along with metadata like creation timestamp and optional custom tags. This creates a complete audit trail of all your marketing links.

Pro tip: Add a "Campaign Name" column to your spreadsheet to automatically categorize links. You can then filter reports by campaign to compare performance.
