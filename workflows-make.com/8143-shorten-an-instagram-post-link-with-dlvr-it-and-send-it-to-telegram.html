What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying Instagram post links, shortening them through a service like dlvr.it, and then sharing them on Telegram channels or groups. For social media managers, influencers, and businesses maintaining Telegram audiences, this workflow eliminates repetitive tasks while ensuring consistent link presentation.

The system automatically detects new Instagram posts (either from your own account or competitors you track), processes the long URLs through dlvr.it's shortening service, and posts the clean links to your designated Telegram channel with optional custom messaging. This maintains professional-looking communications while providing the tracking benefits of shortened links.

How It Works

1. Instagram Post Detection

The workflow monitors your Instagram account for new posts using Make.com's Instagram trigger. You can configure it to watch your own profile or specific competitor/industry accounts you want to track.

2. Link Shortening via dlvr.it

When a new post is detected, the system extracts the full Instagram URL and sends it to dlvr.it's API for shortening. The service returns a clean, trackable short link that you can customize with your preferred domain.

Pro tip: Configure dlvr.it to use your branded short domain if available (e.g., yourbrand.co instead of dlvr.it/XXXXX) for professional consistency across all shared links.

3. Telegram Message Composition

The workflow assembles your Telegram message using the shortened link. You can customize the message template to include post captions, relevant hashtags, or calls-to-action alongside the link.

4. Automated Posting to Telegram

Finally, the system posts your composed message to your designated Telegram channel or group using Telegram's bot API. The message appears instantly with your clean, shortened link ready for audience engagement.
