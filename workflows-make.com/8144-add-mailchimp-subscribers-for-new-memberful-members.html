Add Mailchimp Subscribers for New Memberful Members

Automatically sync new members from your Memberful membership site to your Mailchimp email list

Get This Workflow → Make.com · Membership · Free Template

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap for membership site owners using Memberful for member management and Mailchimp for email marketing. Without integration, you risk missing email marketing opportunities with paying members who may not manually subscribe to your list.

The workflow automatically adds every new Memberful member as a subscriber in your Mailchimp audience. It captures their signup information immediately, ensuring they receive all your email communications without requiring additional opt-ins. This maintains compliance while maximizing your email marketing reach to your most valuable audience - paying members.

How It Works

1. New member triggers the workflow

When someone signs up for your Memberful membership, the workflow instantly detects the new member through Memberful's webhook integration.

2. Member data is processed

The system extracts relevant member information including name, email address, and membership level. You can customize which fields to capture based on your Memberful setup.

3. Subscriber is added to Mailchimp

The workflow connects to your Mailchimp account via API and adds the new member as a subscriber. You can specify which audience/list they should be added to.

4. Optional segmentation

The template can be extended to add tags or place members in specific segments based on their membership level (e.g., "Premium Member" tag).
