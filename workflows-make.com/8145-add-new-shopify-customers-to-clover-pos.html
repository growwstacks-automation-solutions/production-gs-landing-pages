What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for omnichannel retailers: maintaining a single customer database across online (Shopify) and in-store (Clover POS) sales channels. Without integration, staff must manually re-enter customer details from online orders into your POS system - a time-consuming process prone to errors that creates fragmented customer profiles.

The workflow automatically detects new Shopify customers at configured intervals (typically hourly or daily), then creates corresponding customer records in Clover POS with all relevant details: contact information, order history, shipping addresses, and any custom fields you specify. This gives your team complete visibility into customer purchasing behavior across all channels.

How It Works

1. Shopify Customer Monitoring

The workflow regularly polls your Shopify store's API for new customer registrations since the last check. It captures the complete customer profile including name, email, phone, addresses, and order details.

2. Data Transformation

Customer data is reformatted to match Clover POS's expected structure. The system can handle field mapping differences between platforms (e.g., Shopify's "first_name/last_name" vs. Clover's "full_name" field).

3. Duplicate Checking

Before creating a new Clover customer, the workflow checks for existing records with matching email addresses to prevent duplicates. You can configure whether to skip duplicates or update existing profiles.

4. Clover POS Integration

The system authenticates with your Clover POS via OAuth 2.0 and creates the new customer record with all mapped fields. Success/failure notifications can be sent to your team.

Pro tip: Add custom field mapping for loyalty program status to ensure online and in-store purchases earn/redeem points consistently.
