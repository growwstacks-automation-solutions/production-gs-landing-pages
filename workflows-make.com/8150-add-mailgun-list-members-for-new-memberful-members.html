What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for membership site owners using Memberful with Mailgun for email marketing. Without automation, you'd need to manually export new members from Memberful and import them to Mailgun - a time-consuming process that often leads to delays in welcoming new members.

The workflow automatically detects when a new member signs up through Memberful and immediately adds them to your specified Mailgun mailing list. This ensures your email campaigns reach all members from day one, improving engagement and reducing administrative overhead.

How It Works

1. New Member Detection

The workflow monitors your Memberful account for new member signups using webhooks or API polling, depending on your configuration.

2. Data Extraction

When a new member is detected, the system extracts their contact information including name, email address, and any other relevant fields you choose to include.

3. Mailgun Integration

The member data is then formatted according to Mailgun's API requirements and added to your specified mailing list automatically.

4. Success Verification

The workflow confirms the successful addition to Mailgun and can optionally trigger additional actions like sending a confirmation email to you.

Pro tip: Add a delay step before Mailgun submission if you want to implement a double opt-in process for your mailing list.
