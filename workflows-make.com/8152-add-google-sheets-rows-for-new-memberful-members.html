What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying new member information from Memberful into spreadsheets. Every time someone signs up for your membership site through Memberful, their details are instantly added as a new row in your designated Google Sheet.

The workflow captures all relevant member data including name, email address, subscription plan, signup date, and any custom fields you've configured in Memberful. This creates a centralized, searchable database of your members that updates in real-time without any human intervention.

How It Works

1. New member trigger

The workflow monitors Memberful for new member signups through its API. When a new registration occurs, Memberful sends the member data to Make.com.

2. Data formatting

Make.com processes the raw member data, organizing it into the correct format for your Google Sheets columns. You can customize which fields are included and how they're structured.

3. Sheet update

The formatted data is appended as a new row in your specified Google Sheet. The automation handles all authentication and permissions automatically through your connected accounts.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column to track exactly when each member was added to your sheet. This helps with reporting and analytics.
