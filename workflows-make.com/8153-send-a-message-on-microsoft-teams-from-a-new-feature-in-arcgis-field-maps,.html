What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between field operations and office teams by instantly notifying relevant stakeholders when new GIS data is collected. Whenever your field staff creates a new feature in ArcGIS Field Maps (like marking a maintenance issue or completing an inspection), a tailored message automatically posts to your designated Microsoft Teams channel.

The workflow eliminates manual reporting steps while ensuring time-sensitive field data reaches decision-makers immediately. Field teams can focus on data collection rather than communication overhead, while office teams receive structured updates without chasing field staff for reports.

How It Works

1. Field data collection triggers the workflow

When field staff create or update features in your ArcGIS Field Maps layer, the system detects these changes in real-time. The workflow monitors your specified GIS layer for new entries.

2. Data processing and formatting

The automation extracts relevant field attributes (location, timestamps, custom fields) and prepares them for Teams notification. You can customize which data points to include and how they're formatted.

3. Automated Teams notification

A rich message containing the field data posts to your pre-configured Microsoft Teams channel. The notification can include maps, photos, or other attachments collected with the feature.

Pro tip: Configure different Teams channels for different field layers to route notifications to the right teams automatically.
