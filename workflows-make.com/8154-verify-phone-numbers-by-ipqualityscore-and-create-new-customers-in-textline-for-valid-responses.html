What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical business problem of invalid or fraudulent phone numbers entering your customer database. When prospects submit information through Google Forms, their phone numbers are automatically validated using IPQualityScore's advanced fraud detection before being created as customers in Textline.

The workflow filters out disposable numbers, VOIP services, and known scam patterns that basic validation misses. This ensures your sales and support teams only contact legitimate customers, improving communication success rates while reducing wasted effort on invalid contacts.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates when a new submission is received in your Google Form. It extracts the submitted phone number along with other customer information for processing.

2. IPQualityScore Validation

The phone number is sent to IPQualityScore's API for comprehensive validation. This checks number validity, carrier information, risk score, and over 20 fraud indicators.

3. Validation Decision

The workflow evaluates IPQualityScore's response to determine if the number meets your validation criteria. You can customize thresholds for acceptable risk levels.

4. Customer Creation in Textline

For valid numbers, the workflow automatically creates a new customer record in Textline with all submitted information. Invalid submissions are logged separately without creating contacts.
