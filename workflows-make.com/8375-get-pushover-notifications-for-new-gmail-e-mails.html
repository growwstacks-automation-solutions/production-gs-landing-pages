What This Workflow Does

Every business that generates leads faces the same bottleneck: manual lead qualification. Your sales team spends hours each week sifting through spreadsheets, checking contact details, and deciding which leads are worth pursuing. This process is slow, inconsistent, and prone to human error—hot leads get cold while your team is busy with data entry.

This automation solves that by turning your Google Sheets lead list into a real-time qualification engine. Whenever a new lead is added (from a form, import, or any source), the workflow instantly evaluates it against your custom scoring criteria—like budget, timeline, company size, or industry. Based on the score, it automatically routes the lead: sending a personalized Slack message to a sales rep for immediate follow-up, updating the lead status in your CRM, and even adding low-scoring leads to a nurture sequence. The result is a fully automated pipeline that ensures no lead falls through the cracks and your team focuses only on the most promising opportunities.

How It Works

The workflow runs on Make.com, connecting Google Sheets, Slack, and your CRM without any coding. It acts as a 24/7 qualification assistant that never sleeps, ensuring every lead is instantly processed and assigned.

Step 1: New Lead Detection

The automation monitors your Google Sheets lead list for new rows. It can be triggered by a new form submission, a manual entry, or a bulk import. The workflow reads the lead's data fields—name, email, company, budget, timeline, and any custom attributes you track.

Step 2: Scoring & Routing

Using a simple formula, the workflow assigns a score based on your predefined rules. For example, a lead with a budget over $10k, a 30-day timeline, and a matching industry might get a high score. The automation then decides the next step: high-scoring leads go to a dedicated Slack channel for immediate action, medium-scoring leads get added to a follow-up queue, and low-scoring leads enter an automated nurture campaign.

Step 3: Notification & Assignment

For qualified leads, the workflow sends a rich Slack message with key details and a direct link to the lead's record. It can also update your CRM with the qualification status, assign the lead to a specific salesperson, and log the activity for reporting. This creates a closed-loop system where every lead is automatically scored, assigned, and tracked—eliminating manual handoffs and ensuring consistent follow-up.
