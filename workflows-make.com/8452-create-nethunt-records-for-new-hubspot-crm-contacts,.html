What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in sales operations by eliminating manual data transfer between HubSpot CRM and NetHunt. When marketing teams add new contacts in HubSpot, sales reps often waste time recreating these records in NetHunt before outreach can begin.

The workflow automatically creates NetHunt records within seconds of contact creation in HubSpot, complete with with all mapped contact fields. This ensures your sales team always works with up-to-date prospect information without administrative overhead.

How It Works

1. HubSpot Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors HubSpot's contact database for new entries using Make.com's instant polling technology. Any new contact creation triggers the automation sequence.

2. Data Mapping & Transformation

Contact fields from HubSpot are mapped to corresponding NetHunt fields. You can customize which properties transfer and apply formatting rules (like combining first/last names).

3. NetHunt Record Creation

The workflow authenticates with your NetHunt account via API and creates a new record with the transformed contact data. Required fields are validated to prevent errors.

4. Success Confirmation & Logging

Each successful sync is logged with timestamps. Optionally, you can configure email notifications to sales teams when high-value contacts sync.

Pro tip: Add conditional filters to only sync marketing-qualified leads (MQLs) meeting your sales-ready criteria, preventing NetHunt clutter.
