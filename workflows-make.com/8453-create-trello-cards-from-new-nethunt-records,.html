Create Trello Cards from New NetHunt Records

What This Workflow Does

For service-based businesses, agencies, and sales teams, the gap between capturing a lead and starting the work is where opportunities get lost. Manually copying information from your CRM (NetHunt) to your project board (Trello) is tedious, error-prone, and creates delays. This workflow solves that by creating a seamless, automatic bridge.

Every time a new record—like a qualified lead, a new deal, or a customer inquiry—is created in your NetHunt CRM, this Make.com automation instantly creates a corresponding card in your designated Trello board. The card is populated with key details: contact name, deal value, notes, priority, and any custom fields. This ensures your project or delivery team has immediate visibility and can start work without waiting for a manual handoff.

Pro tip: Use this automation not just for new leads, but also for updating Trello cards when a NetHunt record changes stage (e.g., from "Proposal Sent" to "Closed Won"). This keeps project status perfectly in sync with sales progress.

How It Works

The automation is a straightforward, reliable sequence powered by Make.com. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Trigger on New NetHunt Record

The workflow is triggered the moment a new record is added to a specific NetHunt pipeline or view you define. Make.com watches for this event in real-time.

Step 2: Extract and Format Data

The automation pulls all relevant data from the new NetHunt record. This includes standard fields like email, company, and deal amount, plus any custom fields you’ve set up for project specifics.

Step 3: Create a Trello Card

Using the extracted data, the workflow creates a new card on your chosen Trello board and list (e.g., "New Projects" or "To-Do"). It maps the NetHunt data to the Trello card's title, description, checklist, labels, due date, and assigned members.

Step 4: Confirm and Log

The automation can send a confirmation (via email or Slack) that a new card was created and log the action for audit purposes, ensuring full transparency.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in less than 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect NetHunt: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your NetHunt account. Select the specific pipeline or view you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Trello: Authorize Make.com to access your Trello. Choose the target Board and List where new cards should appear.
  4. Map Your Data: Customize the data mapping. Decide which NetHunt fields (e.g., "Company Name," "Deal Value") go into the Trello card title, description, checklist, or labels.
  5. Test & Activate: Run a test by creating a dummy record in NetHunt. Verify a card appears correctly in Trello. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on.
