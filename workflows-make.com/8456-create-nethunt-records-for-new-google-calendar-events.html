What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of sales teams forgetting to log client meetings in their CRM after scheduling calendar events. Every time you or your team schedules a new meeting in Google Calendar, this Make.com workflow automatically creates a corresponding record in NetHunt CRM.

The integration ensures no potential leads slip through the cracks by eliminating manual data entry between scheduling and CRM logging. It captures all relevant meeting details including attendees, meeting purpose, date/time, and notes - saving your sales team 15-30 minutes per meeting in administrative work.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new calendar events

The workflow monitors your Google Calendar for new events. You can configure it to watch specific calendars or filter events by keywords in the title/description.

2. Extract meeting details

Make.com pulls all relevant event data including title, description, attendees, start/end times, location, and organizer information.

3. Create NetHunt record

The workflow maps the calendar event fields to corresponding NetHunt CRM fields, creating a complete client record with all meeting context.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only create records for external meetings (like sales calls) rather than internal team meetings by filtering for specific keywords or calendar types.
