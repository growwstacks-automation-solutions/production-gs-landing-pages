What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of timely follow-up after sales calls or customer support interactions logged in NetHunt CRM. Instead of requiring team members to manually compose and send emails after each call, the workflow automatically triggers personalized Gmail messages containing call details, next steps, or attachments.

For businesses handling dozens of calls daily, this eliminates hours of repetitive email work while ensuring consistent, professional follow-up communication. The system captures all relevant NetHunt call log data - including duration, participants, notes, and attachments - then formats this information into ready-to-send emails through your Gmail account.

How It Works

1. NetHunt detects new call log

The workflow constantly monitors your NetHunt account for newly created call records. Each time your team logs a call in NetHunt (whether manually entered or synced from your phone system), the automation immediately captures all available details.

2. Data is formatted for email

Make.com processes the raw call log information, extracting key details like call duration, participants, notes, and any attached files. This data is then inserted into your predefined email template, creating a complete follow-up message ready for sending.

3. Gmail sends personalized email

The formatted email is delivered through your Gmail account, appearing as if sent manually by your team. Recipients receive professional follow-ups with all relevant call context, while your team saves the time previously spent crafting these messages.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to customize emails based on call type - sales calls might include different follow-up content than support calls, for example.
