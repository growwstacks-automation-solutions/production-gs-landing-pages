What This Workflow Does

In fast-paced sales environments, critical customer information often gets trapped inside your CRM. When a sales rep logs a call in NetHunt, managers and team members might not see it for hours or even days, leading to missed opportunities for collaboration and slow follow-ups. This delay can cost deals and frustrate customers who expect timely responses.

This automation solves that problem by creating a real-time bridge between NetHunt CRM and your team's communication hub, Slack. Every time a new call log is created—whether it's a discovery call, a negotiation, or a support check-in—the workflow instantly sends a formatted notification to a designated Slack channel or direct message. This ensures the entire team is immediately aware of key customer interactions, enabling faster decision-making and a more cohesive sales strategy.

How It Works

The workflow is a simple yet powerful trigger-action sequence built on Make.com. It monitors your NetHunt CRM and pushes updates directly to Slack without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Monitor NetHunt for New Call Logs

The scenario starts with a NetHunt module set to "Watch Call Logs." It continuously polls your NetHunt account for newly created call records. You can filter this to watch specific pipelines, users, or deal stages if needed.

Step 2: Format the Notification Message

Once a new call log is detected, Make extracts the relevant data: who the call was with, its duration, the outcome noted by the rep, and a direct link back to the contact or deal in NetHunt. This data is structured into a clear, readable message.

Step 3: Send the Alert to Slack

The formatted message is sent via the Slack "Send a Message" module. You configure it to post to a specific public channel, private channel, or even send a direct message to a team member or manager. The notification appears in Slack with all the context needed for quick action.

Pro tip: Customize your Slack message with emojis (e.g., 📞 for a new call, 🎯 for a qualified lead) and use Slack's formatting to make key details like the contact name or deal value stand out. This increases visibility and engagement from your team.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect NetHunt: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your NetHunt account using your API credentials.
  3. Connect Slack: Authorize the Slack module to post messages to your chosen workspace. You'll need to create a Slack app or use a webhook.
  4. Configure the Channel: Set the Slack module to post to your desired channel (e.g., #sales-updates) or user.
  5. Customize the Message: Adjust the message template to include the call log fields most important to your team (contact, notes, duration, link).
  6. Test & Activate: Create a test call log in NetHunt and run the scenario once to ensure the notification appears correctly in Slack. Then, turn on the schedule to run automatically.
