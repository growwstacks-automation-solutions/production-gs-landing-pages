What This Workflow Does

For sales and recruiting teams, the gap between finding a prospect's contact information and actually engaging them is where opportunities are lost. Manually copying data from a lead enrichment tool like RocketReach into your CRM is a tedious, error-prone task that delays follow-up by hours or even days.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between discovery and action. Every time a new lookup is completed in RocketReach—providing you with a verified email, phone number, and professional details—this workflow automatically creates a corresponding contact in your ActiveCampaign account. The enriched data is instantly available, allowing you to trigger targeted email sequences, assign lead scores, and update sales pipelines without lifting a finger.

The result is a fully automated lead intake system. Your team's productivity skyrockets because they can focus on selling and building relationships, while the administrative busywork of data entry is handled silently in the background. This ensures your marketing and sales engines are always fed with fresh, actionable leads.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, reliable sequence that turns a RocketReach lookup event into a structured ActiveCampaign contact record.

Step 1: Monitor RocketReach for New Lookups

The automation starts by watching your RocketReach account for the "lookup completed" event. This is the trigger. Whenever you or a team member successfully finds contact details for a prospect, RocketReach signals that the lookup is ready, and the workflow instantly springs into action.

Step 2: Parse the Enriched Lead Data

Make.com receives the full dataset from the completed lookup. This includes the prospect's name, email address, phone number, job title, company, location, and often links to social profiles. The workflow extracts and formats each piece of this valuable information, preparing it for import into your CRM.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to filter lookups based on specific criteria, like only processing results with a high-confidence email score, to ensure only the highest-quality leads enter your CRM.

Step 3: Create or Update the Contact in ActiveCampaign

Using the prepared data, the automation connects to your ActiveCampaign account via its API. It first checks if a contact with that email already exists to prevent duplicates. If it's a new lead, it creates a full contact record, mapping the RocketReach fields to the appropriate custom fields in ActiveCampaign. You can also configure it to add specific tags, assign the contact to a list, or even trigger an automation journey immediately upon creation.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the template in your Make.com account and create a new scenario from it.
  2. Connect RocketReach: In the first module, authenticate Make.com with your RocketReach API key. Set the watch trigger to monitor for "Lookup Completed" events.
  3. Connect ActiveCampaign: In the "Create Contact" module, authenticate with your ActiveCampaign API credentials (URL and Key).
  4. Map the Data Fields: This is the crucial step. Drag and drop the data from the RocketReach module (like `Email`, `FirstName`, `Company`) into the corresponding fields in the ActiveCampaign contact creation module. Create custom fields in ActiveCampaign first if needed.
  5. Add Logic (Optional): Insert a router or filter before the ActiveCampaign step to only process lookups that meet certain conditions, like having a valid email.
  6. Test and Activate: Run a test lookup in RocketReach or use the Make.com scheduling tool to run the scenario once manually. Verify a contact appears correctly in ActiveCampaign. Then, turn the scenario to "ON".
