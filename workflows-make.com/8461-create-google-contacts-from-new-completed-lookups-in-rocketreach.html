What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for sales teams and recruiters who use RocketReach for prospect research. Every time you complete a contact lookup in RocketReach, the system automatically creates a corresponding contact in Google Contacts with all available details.

Manual data entry between these platforms wastes valuable selling time and introduces errors. This workflow ensures your Google Contacts database stays perfectly synchronized with your prospect research activities, giving you instant access to contact information across all your devices and applications.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new RocketReach lookups

The workflow monitors your RocketReach account for completed contact lookups. Each time you find a new prospect, the automation instantly captures all available contact details.

2. Format contact data for Google

The system structures the information according to Google Contacts' requirements, including name, email, phone, company, job title, and social profiles. You can customize which fields to include.

3. Check for duplicates

Before creating a new contact, the workflow checks your Google Contacts to prevent duplicates. You can configure it to skip, update, or create new entries with timestamps.

4. Create Google Contact

The verified contact information gets added to your Google Contacts, optionally assigned to specific contact groups for better organization.
