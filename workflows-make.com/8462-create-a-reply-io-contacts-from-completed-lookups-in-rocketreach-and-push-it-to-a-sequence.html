What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical bottleneck in sales prospecting by seamlessly connecting RocketReach contact lookups with Reply.io outreach sequences. Every time your team completes a new contact lookup in RocketReach, the workflow automatically creates that contact in Reply.io and adds them to your designated sequence - all without any manual intervention.

Sales teams waste countless hours copying contact information between tools, often introducing errors or delays that hurt conversion rates. This workflow ensures that as soon as you identify a qualified lead in RocketReach, they're immediately entered into your Reply.io outreach funnel with all their contact details intact.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New RocketReach Lookup Completion

The workflow monitors your RocketReach account for completed contact lookups. When a new lookup is finished, it captures all available contact details including name, email, phone number, company information, and any custom fields.

2. Data Processing and Formatting

The contact information is formatted to match Reply.io's expected structure. You can customize field mappings to ensure all relevant data transfers correctly between the platforms.

3. Create Reply.io Contact

The workflow uses Reply.io's API to create a new contact record with all the information from RocketReach. It checks for duplicates based on your specified matching criteria.

4. Add to Sequence

Finally, the new contact is automatically added to your predefined Reply.io sequence, starting their journey through your outreach campaign immediately.
