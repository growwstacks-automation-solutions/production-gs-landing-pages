Automatically Create Pipedrive Contacts from RocketReach Lookups

Eliminate manual data entry. Every time a new contact lookup is completed in RocketReach, a fully detailed contact is instantly created in your Pipedrive CRM.

What This Workflow Does

Sales and business development teams spend valuable hours manually copying and pasting contact information from prospecting tools like RocketReach into their Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system, such as Pipedrive. This manual process is not only time-consuming but also prone to errors, leading to incomplete records, missed outreach opportunities, and a cluttered sales pipeline.

This automated workflow solves that problem by creating a seamless, real-time connection between RocketReach and Pipedrive. Whenever a sales representative or researcher completes a successful contact lookup in RocketReach—finding a verified email, phone number, or professional details—the workflow automatically triggers. It takes all the enriched data and creates a new, fully-formed contact record in your Pipedrive CRM without any human intervention.

The result is a synchronized sales engine where prospect data flows instantly from discovery to pipeline. Your team can focus on engaging leads rather than administrative data entry, ensuring no prospect falls through the cracks and every piece of intelligence is captured where it matters most.

How It Works

The automation acts as a intelligent bridge, listening for new data in one app and acting upon it in another. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Trigger on New RocketReach Lookup

The workflow is initiated by the "Watch Lookups" module in Make.com, which monitors your RocketReach account. The moment a lookup job is completed (e.g., an email address is found for a LinkedIn profile), it captures the entire result set, including name, job title, company, email, phone, and social links.

Pro tip: Configure the trigger to filter for only "Successful" lookups to avoid creating empty contacts from failed searches, keeping your Pipedrive database clean.

Step 2: Map and Structure the Contact Data

Make.com's router and data transformation tools then take the raw output from RocketReach. It maps each relevant field to the corresponding field in Pipedrive's contact schema. For example, the "email" value from RocketReach is mapped to Pipedrive's "Email" field, "current_title" becomes "Job Title," and "company" populates the "Organization" field.

Step 3: Create the Contact in Pipedrive

The final action module uses the structured data to execute the "Create a Person" API call to your Pipedrive account. A new contact is instantly created in your specified Pipedrive pipeline or under a relevant owner. The contact is now ready for outreach, task assignment, or inclusion in a deal.

To implement this workflow, you will need the following accounts and access:

  1. A Make.com account (free or paid plan) to build and run the automation.
  2. An active RocketReach account with API access enabled (check your plan for API credits).
  3. A Pipedrive CRM account with admin or user permissions to create contacts via API.

  5. (Recommended) A designated Pipedrive "User" or "Pipeline" where new automated contacts should be assigned.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your automation live in under 15 minutes:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the pre-built template in your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, click the RocketReach module and authenticate your account. Repeat the process for the Pipedrive module.
  3. Configure the Trigger: In the "Watch Lookups" module, ensure it's set to monitor for "Completed" lookups. You can optionally add a filter to only proceed if the lookup result contains an email.
  4. Map the Data Fields: In the Pipedrive "Create a Person" module, map the incoming RocketReach data to the correct Pipedrive fields. Standard mappings are pre-set, but you can customize them (e.g., add the LinkedIn URL to a custom field).
  5. Set the Owner & Pipeline: Choose which Pipedrive user should own these new contacts and which pipeline they should belong to. This keeps your sales process organized.
  6. Test & Activate: Run a single test operation by performing a lookup in RocketReach. Check Pipedrive to confirm a contact was created correctly. Once verified, turn the scenario "ON."
