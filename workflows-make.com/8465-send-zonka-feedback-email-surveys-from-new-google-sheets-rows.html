What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of sending customer feedback surveys by connecting your Google Sheets contact list directly to Zonka Feedback. Whenever you add a new row with customer details (email, name, purchase details etc.), the system automatically triggers a personalized survey email through Zonka.

Businesses using this template save 5-10 hours per week previously spent copying/pasting customer data between systems. More importantly, it ensures every customer receives timely feedback requests at the optimal moment in their journey - whether after a purchase, support ticket resolution, or subscription renewal.

How It Works

1. New Row Detection

The workflow monitors your specified Google Sheet for new rows being added, checking at intervals you configure (from minutes to days).

2. Data Validation

Each new row is validated to ensure required fields (email address at minimum) are present before proceeding.

3. Survey Trigger

Valid customer records trigger Zonka Feedback's API to send your pre-configured email survey template with merge fields populated from the spreadsheet.

4. Response Tracking

Survey responses flow back into your Zonka dashboard while the original Google Sheet row is marked as "Survey Sent" with a timestamp.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only survey customers meeting specific criteria (order value above $50, subscription tier, etc.) by adding filter modules to the workflow.
