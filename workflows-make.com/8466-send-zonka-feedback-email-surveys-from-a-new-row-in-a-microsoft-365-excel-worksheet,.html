What This Workflow Does

Manually sending feedback surveys is a time-consuming and error-prone task. You have to export customer lists, copy-paste email addresses, and remember to follow up—a process that often gets delayed or forgotten. This results in low response rates, stale feedback, and missed opportunities to improve customer experience.

This automation solves that by creating a direct pipeline between your Microsoft 365 Excel worksheet and Zonka Feedback. Every time you or your team adds a new row with customer details (like name and email), the workflow instantly triggers and sends a personalized email survey. It turns your static spreadsheet into a dynamic feedback collection engine, ensuring every customer is heard promptly and consistently.

How It Works

The workflow is built on Make.com and acts as a smart bridge between your data and your survey platform.

Step 1: Monitor Your Excel Worksheet

The automation constantly watches a specific Microsoft 365 Excel worksheet for new rows. It's designed to work with a structured sheet that has clear headers, such as "Customer Name," "Email," and "Product Purchased."

Step 2: Capture the New Row Data

When a new row is added, Make immediately captures all the data from that row. It parses each column, preparing the information to be used in the next step. This ensures no manual data transfer is needed.

Step 3: Send the Survey via Zonka Feedback

Using the captured data, the workflow connects to your Zonka Feedback account. It creates and dispatches a personalized email survey to the customer's email address, often including their name for a tailored touch. The survey is sent within moments of the row being added.

Pro tip: Use this workflow not just for post-purchase feedback, but also for onboarding sequences, service follow-ups, or event satisfaction surveys. Simply adjust the trigger Excel sheet and the Zonka Feedback survey template.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Microsoft 365: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Excel Online file. Select the specific workbook and worksheet you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Zonka Feedback: In the survey-sending module, authorize your Zonka Feedback account and select the specific email survey you want to send.
  4. Map Your Data: Link the Excel column headers (like `Email` and `Customer Name`) to the corresponding fields in Zonka Feedback (like recipient email and personalization tokens).
  5. Test and Activate: Add a test row to your Excel sheet and run the scenario once to ensure the survey sends correctly. Then, schedule the scenario to run automatically (e.g., every hour) or turn on instant webhook triggers if available.
