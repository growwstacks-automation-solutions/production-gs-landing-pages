What This Workflow Does

In today's competitive market, understanding customer sentiment is non-negotiable, but manually sending feedback requests is time-consuming and inefficient. Businesses often miss the critical moment to ask for feedback, resulting in low response rates and stale data. This creates a blind spot where you're making decisions without real-time insights into customer satisfaction.

This automated workflow solves that by connecting your customer database in Airtable directly to Zonka Feedback, a powerful survey platform. Every time a new record is added—whether it's a new client, a completed sale, or a closed support ticket—an SMS survey is dispatched automatically. This ensures you capture feedback at the perfect moment, turning every customer interaction into a data point for improvement.

How It Works

The automation acts as a silent bridge between your data and your customers, operating in a reliable three-step cycle.

Step 1: Monitor Your Airtable Base

The Make.com scenario continuously watches a specific Airtable table for new records. You define the trigger, such as when a status field changes to "Order Delivered" or when a new row is created in your "Customers" table. The workflow instantly captures the new data, including the customer's name and phone number.

Step 2: Format and Send the SMS Survey

Make takes the customer data and prepares a personalized request. It connects to your Zonka Feedback account via API and triggers a pre-configured SMS survey campaign. The customer receives a direct text message with a short, mobile-friendly survey link, often asking for a rating or brief feedback.

Step 3: Close the Loop (Optional)

While the core workflow ends with the survey send, you can extend it. For example, you can set up a separate scenario in Make to watch for Zonka Feedback responses. If a customer reports a low score, you can automatically create a task in your project management tool or send an alert to your team for immediate service recovery.

Pro tip: Personalize the survey invite by including the customer's name from Airtable in the SMS body. A simple "Hi [Name], how was your experience today?" can increase response rates by up to 30%.

What You'll Need

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available) to build and run the automation.
  2. An Airtable base with a table containing customer contact details, specifically phone numbers.
  3. A Zonka Feedback account with an active SMS survey campaign set up and configured.
  4. API connections (keys) for both Airtable and Zonka Feedback, which you can obtain from their respective account settings.

Quick Setup Guide

You can be up and running in under 30 minutes. Follow these steps to configure your automated feedback system.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the provided Make.com scenario into your own account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make scenario, update the Airtable and Zonka Feedback modules with your own API keys and account details.
  3. Map Your Data: Point the Airtable module to your specific base and table. Ensure the phone number field is correctly mapped to the Zonka Feedback "recipient" field.
  4. Select Your Survey: In the Zonka Feedback module, choose the exact SMS survey campaign you want to trigger for new records.
  5. Test and Activate: Add a test record to your Airtable table and run the scenario once to ensure an SMS is sent correctly. Then, schedule the scenario to run automatically (e.g., every 15 minutes).
