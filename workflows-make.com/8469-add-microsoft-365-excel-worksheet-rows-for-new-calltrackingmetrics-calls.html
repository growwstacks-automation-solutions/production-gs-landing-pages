What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of copying call tracking data from CallTrackingMetrics into Excel spreadsheets. Marketing teams and call centers typically waste 5-10 hours per week on this repetitive task, often with data entry errors that skew reporting.

The workflow automatically captures every new call from CallTrackingMetrics and adds it as a structured row in your Microsoft 365 Excel worksheet. It preserves all critical call details including caller information, duration, source tracking, and custom fields you've configured.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new calls

The workflow activates instantly when CallTrackingMetrics registers an incoming call, whether answered or missed. No polling delays - data flows in real time.

2. Extract call details

Make.com retrieves the complete call record including timestamp, caller number, duration, tracking number, UTM parameters, and any custom fields like call disposition or notes.

3. Format for Excel

The data gets transformed to match your Excel column structure, with proper formatting for dates, phone numbers, and other specialized fields.

4. Add to worksheet

The system appends a new row to your designated Excel file, either creating a new sheet or adding to an existing call log tab based on your configuration.

Pro tip: Add a "Data Received" timestamp column in Excel to track when calls were logged, separate from the actual call time.
