What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of customer data silos between your ecommerce platform and contact management system. Every time a new customer registers or makes a purchase in your Prestashop store, their contact information is automatically added to your Google Contacts.

Manual data entry between systems is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates those issues by creating a seamless, real-time connection between Prestashop and Google Contacts. Your sales and support teams will always have access to up-to-date customer information across all Google services.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

The workflow monitors your Prestashop store for new customer registrations or orders. When a new customer record is created, the automation is triggered.

2. Data extraction

The system retrieves relevant customer information from Prestashop, including name, email, phone number, and shipping/billing addresses.

3. Contact creation

The workflow formats the Prestashop data according to Google Contacts' structure and creates a new contact in your specified Google account.

4. Error handling

If any issues occur during the sync process, the workflow can notify your team or retry the operation according to your configuration.

Pro tip: Add custom fields in Google Contacts to store Prestashop-specific data like customer IDs or purchase history for better segmentation.
