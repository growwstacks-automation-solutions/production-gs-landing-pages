What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for ecommerce businesses using Prestashop - the need to manually notify customers about their orders. Every time a customer completes a purchase, the workflow automatically triggers a personalized email through Gmail, confirming their order details.

The solution eliminates the risk of forgetting to send order confirmations, ensures consistent communication, and saves store owners hours of manual work each week. It's particularly valuable for stores processing multiple orders daily, where manual email sending becomes impractical.

How It Works

1. New Order Detection

The workflow constantly monitors your Prestashop store for new orders through Make.com's Prestashop integration. As soon as an order is placed, the system detects it instantly.

2. Data Extraction

Key order details like customer information, products ordered, quantities, prices, and shipping information are extracted from Prestashop's order records.

3. Email Composition

The workflow composes a professional email using your Gmail account, populating a template with the specific order details. You can customize the email design and content to match your brand.

4. Automated Sending

Gmail sends the notification immediately to the customer's email address. The system logs each sent email for tracking purposes.

Pro tip: Add a second email trigger for order status changes (like shipping updates) to create a complete customer communication system.
