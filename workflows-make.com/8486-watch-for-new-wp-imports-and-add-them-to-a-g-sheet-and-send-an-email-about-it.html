What This Workflow Does

Every new lead that enters your HubSpot CRM triggers this automated qualification process. Instead of manually reviewing each contact, the workflow instantly evaluates lead data against your predefined criteria—like company size, industry, job title, or website activity—and calculates a qualification score. Only leads that meet your threshold get posted to a dedicated Slack channel with key details, so your sales team can focus on conversations that matter, not data entry.

This eliminates the classic sales bottleneck: leads sitting unassigned for hours or days while your team is busy. By automatically filtering out unqualified contacts and highlighting the most promising ones, you ensure that only the most promising leads are passed to your sales team. This reduces response time from days to minutes.

How It Works

Step 1: New Lead Detection

When a new contact is created in HubSpot, the workflow captures the lead's full profile—including custom properties you've set for qualification. This includes form submissions, chat conversations, imported lists, or manually added contacts.

Step 2: Automated Scoring

Using a router, the workflow checks each lead against your rules. For example: Does the lead have a company email? Is their job title decision‑maker? Did they visit your pricing page? Each rule adds points, and the total determines if the lead is “hot,” “warm,” or “cold.”

Step 3: Smart Filtering

Leads that score below your minimum are logged in a separate module (e.g., a Google Sheet) for later nurturing. Qualified leads proceed to the next step.

Step 4: Slack Notification

A formatted message is posted to a designated Slack channel, showing the lead’s name, company, score, and a direct link to their HubSpot record. The message can also tag the appropriate sales rep based on territory or round‑robin assignment.
