What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of timely, personalized follow-up with new CRM contacts. When sales teams manually send welcome messages or outreach, delays happen and personalization suffers. The workflow automatically detects new persons in Pipedrive and immediately sends them a professionally recorded audio message via Slybroadcast.

Unlike bulk email campaigns that get ignored, audio messages create authentic human connections. Recipients hear your actual voice delivering a warm welcome or valuable offer, increasing engagement rates by 300-800% compared to text-based outreach. The system works 24/7, ensuring no new lead goes uncontacted.

How It Works

1. Pipedrive Contact Monitoring

The workflow continuously watches your Pipedrive account for new person records. You can configure it to trigger on specific contact properties like lead source or deal stage.

2. Audio Message Selection

Based on rules you define, the system selects the appropriate pre-recorded audio file from your Slybroadcast library. Different messages can be sent for different customer segments.

3. Personalized Delivery

The workflow initiates the Slybroadcast campaign, inserting the contact's name and other CRM data into your audio message for personalization. Delivery occurs within minutes of the contact being added.

4. Activity Logging

Every sent message is recorded back in Pipedrive as an activity, creating a complete audit trail of your audio outreach efforts.

Pro tip: Record multiple versions of your audio message with different tones (enthusiastic, professional, conversational) and A/B test which performs best with your audience.
