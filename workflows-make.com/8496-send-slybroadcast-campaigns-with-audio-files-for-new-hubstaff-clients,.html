What This Workflow Does

For agencies and service businesses using Hubstaff for client and project management, the first impression is critical. Manually reaching out to each new client with a welcome message is time-consuming and often gets delayed. This automation solves that by instantly triggering a personalized voice message campaign via Slybroadcast the moment a new client is registered in your Hubstaff account.

The workflow acts as a seamless bridge between your operational backend and your client communication frontend. It eliminates the manual task of copying client details, recording messages, and managing send times. Instead, it delivers a consistent, professional, and surprisingly personal audio welcome that can outline next steps, share important links, or simply make the client feel valued from day one.

This isn't just about sending a message; it's about systematizing client onboarding to improve retention, reduce administrative overhead, and allow your team to focus on delivering great work rather than administrative follow-ups.

How It Works

The automation runs on a scheduled cycle or in real-time, connecting two powerful platforms to create a hands-off client engagement engine.

Step 1: Monitor Hubstaff for New Clients

The Make.com scenario is set to watch your Hubstaff organization's client list at regular intervals (e.g., every hour). It uses the Hubstaff module to search for recently created clients, filtering for those added since the last check.

Step 2: Prepare the Client Data

When a new client is detected, the workflow captures essential details like the client's name, company, and most importantly, their phone number. This data is then formatted and passed to the next step, ensuring the message can be personalized.

Pro tip: Ensure phone numbers are stored in a consistent format (e.g., E.164 with country code) in Hubstaff for Slybroadcast to process them correctly.

Step 3: Send the Audio Campaign via Slybroadcast

The workflow then activates the Slybroadcast module. It uses your pre-recorded audio file (a welcome message) and sends it to the new client's phone number. The message is delivered directly to their voicemail without ringing their phone, ensuring it's received non-intrusively.

Step 4: Log and Confirm

The scenario can be configured to log the action in a Google Sheet, send a confirmation to your team's Slack, or update the client's record in Hubstaff, creating a full audit trail of the automated onboarding step.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Hubstaff: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Hubstaff account and select your organization.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Set the schedule interval (e.g., every 6 hours) for checking new clients.
  4. Connect Slybroadcast: Authorize the Slybroadcast module with your API credentials.
  5. Upload Your Audio: Point the Slybroadcast module to the URL of your hosted welcome audio file or select it from your Slybroadcast library.
  6. Map the Data: Ensure the phone number field from Hubstaff is correctly mapped to the recipient field in Slybroadcast.
  7. Test & Activate: Run a test by adding a dummy client in Hubstaff or using the manual run feature. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on.
