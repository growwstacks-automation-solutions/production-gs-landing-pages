What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually scheduling remote support sessions, consultations, or interviews after receiving form submissions. Every time a customer or client completes your Google Form, the workflow automatically:

  • Creates a new TeamViewer meeting with customized settings
  • Sends the meeting details directly to the respondent
  • Logs all scheduled sessions in your preferred system

For businesses handling multiple remote sessions daily, this eliminates 5-15 minutes of administrative work per meeting while ensuring no submissions fall through the cracks. The instant response also improves customer experience compared to manual scheduling.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Google Form. Make.com monitors for new responses in real-time.

2. Data Extraction

The system pulls all relevant information from the form, including the respondent's name, email, preferred meeting time, and any custom fields you've added.

3. TeamViewer Meeting Creation

Using TeamViewer's API, the workflow automatically generates a new meeting with your predefined settings. You can configure duration, access controls, and other parameters.

4. Notification Delivery

The meeting details (including join link) are immediately sent to the respondent via email or other channels. You can customize the message template.

5. Data Logging

All meeting information is recorded in your preferred system (spreadsheet, CRM, database) for tracking and follow-up.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to set different meeting durations based on form responses. For example, "Quick Question" could trigger a 15-minute session while "Full Consultation" schedules 60 minutes.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Forms questionnaire for collecting meeting requests
  2. A TeamViewer account with API access (Business or Premium plan)
  3. A Make.com account (free plan works for basic usage)
  4. Email service (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) for sending meeting invitations

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Connect your Google Form in Make.com by authenticating with your Google account
  2. Configure TeamViewer API connection using your account credentials
  3. Map form fields to meeting parameters (name, email, duration, etc.)
  4. Customize the notification template with your branding and instructions
  5. Test with sample submissions to verify meeting creation and notifications
  6. Activate the workflow and start receiving automated meeting requests
