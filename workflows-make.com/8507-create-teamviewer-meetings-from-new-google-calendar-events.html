What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of creating virtual meeting rooms and notifying participants. When team members schedule events in Google Calendar, the workflow instantly generates corresponding TeamViewer meeting sessions and distributes joining instructions to all attendees.

The solution solves three key pain points: wasted time switching between scheduling and conferencing tools, inconsistent meeting setup processes across teams, and delayed participant notifications that lead to meeting start delays.

How It Works

1. New Google Calendar Event Detection

The workflow monitors your Google Calendar for new events. When triggered, it extracts all relevant meeting details including title, time, description, and participant emails.

2. TeamViewer Meeting Creation

Make connects to your TeamViewer account to generate a new meeting session with the calendar event's parameters. The system automatically sets the meeting name, scheduled time, and any custom configurations you've predefined.

3. Participant Notification

Each attendee receives an email with the TeamViewer meeting link, joining instructions, and calendar event details. The message can be customized with your branding and specific participation guidelines.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to exclude certain calendar events (like "Busy" blocks) from triggering meeting creation, saving unnecessary TeamViewer sessions.
