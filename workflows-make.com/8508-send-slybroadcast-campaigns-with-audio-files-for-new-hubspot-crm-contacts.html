What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually sending personalized audio messages to new leads and customers. When businesses rely solely on email or text messages, they miss the opportunity to create memorable first impressions that build trust faster.

The workflow automatically detects new contacts in your HubSpot CRM and sends them a customized audio campaign through Slybroadcast. This could be a welcome message from your CEO, a product demo audio clip, or an exclusive offer - all delivered in a human voice that stands out in today's digital noise.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The system continuously monitors your HubSpot CRM for new contacts based on your criteria (like specific lead sources or contact properties).

2. Audio Campaign Trigger

When a matching contact appears, the workflow retrieves their details and prepares your pre-recorded audio message (or generates one dynamically using text-to-speech).

3. Personalized Delivery

Slybroadcast delivers the audio message to the contact's phone number, with options for voicemail drop or live call. The system can insert personalized elements like the recipient's name.

4. CRM Sync

Delivery status and engagement data sync back to HubSpot, creating a complete record of the interaction alongside other marketing activities.

Pro tip: Record multiple versions of your audio message for different audience segments. The workflow can select the most relevant version based on HubSpot contact properties.
