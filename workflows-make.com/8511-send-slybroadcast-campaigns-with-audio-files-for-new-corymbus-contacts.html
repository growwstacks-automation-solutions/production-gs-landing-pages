Automate Voice Campaigns for New CRM Contacts

What This Workflow Does

In today's competitive landscape, the first touchpoint with a new lead can make or break a deal. Traditional email follow-ups often get lost in crowded inboxes, leading to missed opportunities and cold leads. This automation solves that problem by leveraging the power of the human voice.

This workflow connects your Corymbus CRM to Slybroadcast, a voice messaging platform. Every time a new contact is added to Corymbus, the system automatically triggers a personalized audio message to be sent via Slybroadcast. This transforms your lead follow-up from a manual, delayed task into an instant, personal, and scalable communication channel.

The core value lies in its immediacy and personal touch. Instead of a salesperson needing to remember to call or send an email, the lead receives a warm, recorded voice message within minutes of expressing interest. This dramatically increases the likelihood of engagement, helping you stand out and build rapport faster than competitors relying solely on digital text.

How It Works

The automation runs on a reliable, clockwork schedule, ensuring no new contact slips through the cracks.

Step 1: Monitor New Corymbus Contacts

The workflow is set to check your Corymbus account at regular intervals (e.g., every 15 minutes). It searches for contacts that have been created since the last check. This "watchdog" module is the trigger for the entire sequence.

Step 2: Prepare Contact & Campaign Data

For each new contact found, the automation gathers relevant data. This can include the contact's name, phone number, company, and any custom fields you use for segmentation (like lead source or product interest). This data is formatted and prepared for the next step.

Step 3: Send Audio Campaign via Slybroadcast

The workflow then connects to your Slybroadcast account. It uses the prepared contact data to select or reference the appropriate audio file (which you've pre-recorded or dynamically generated). It initiates a campaign, sending the personalized voice message directly to the lead's phone number.

Step 4: Log & Confirm Delivery

Finally, the system can log the action—recording which contact received which message and when—back into Corymbus or a separate spreadsheet. This creates an audit trail for your sales team and confirms the automated follow-up was executed successfully.

Pro tip: Record multiple versions of your welcome audio message tailored to different lead sources (e.g., webinar attendees vs. website downloads). The automation can be extended to choose the right message based on data from Corymbus, making your outreach hyper-relevant.

What You'll Need

To implement this workflow, you'll need the following accounts and assets set up:

  1. A Make.com account (free or paid plan) to host and run the automation.
  2. An active Corymbus CRM account with contacts being added (manually or via forms).
  3. A Slybroadcast account with credits available for sending voice messages.
  4. At least one pre-recorded audio file (MP3 or WAV) uploaded to Slybroadcast to use as your campaign message.
  5. The phone numbers for your new contacts must be stored in a compatible format within Corymbus.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Corymbus: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Corymbus account. Set the "Watch" interval (e.g., every 15 minutes).
  3. Connect Slybroadcast: In the Slybroadcast module, authorize the connection and select the specific audio file you want to send for new contacts.
  4. Map the Data: Ensure the phone number field from Corymbus is correctly mapped to the recipient field in Slybroadcast. Add any personalization (like using the contact's first name in a dynamic audio script if supported).
  5. Test & Activate: Turn on the scenario and create a test contact in Corymbus. Verify that the audio message is sent correctly. Once confirmed, your automation is live.
