What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of sending physical postcards to customers, clients, or leads whenever their information is added to your Airtable database. Traditional postcard campaigns require repetitive data entry, design work, and mailing coordination - tasks that consume valuable time while introducing potential errors.

The workflow automatically detects new records in your specified Airtable base, formats all necessary mailing information, and submits print-ready postcards to Merci Facteur's printing service. Each postcard can include personalized messages and design elements pulled directly from your Airtable fields, creating authentic customer touchpoints without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. New Record Detection

The Make.com scenario monitors your Airtable base for new records, triggering whenever contact information meets your specified criteria.

2. Data Formatting

Contact details are automatically structured to match Merci Facteur's API requirements, including address standardization and message formatting.

3. Postcard Submission

The workflow submits the print request to Merci Facteur with all design specifications, mailing details, and your chosen postcard template.

4. Delivery Confirmation

Optional tracking updates can be written back to your Airtable record, providing visibility into mailing status.

Pro tip: Create a "Postcard Sent" checkbox field in Airtable to prevent duplicate mailings if records are modified.

  4. Postcard design template uploaded to Merci Facteur

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Duplicate the template in your Make.com account

  4. Map Airtable fields to Merci Facteur's required format
  5. Select your default postcard design template
  6. Test with sample records before activating
