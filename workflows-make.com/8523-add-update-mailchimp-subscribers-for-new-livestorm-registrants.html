What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring webinar registrant data between Livestorm and Mailchimp. Every time someone registers for your Livestorm webinar, their contact details automatically flow into your designated Mailchimp audience, complete with any custom registration form fields you've collected.

The workflow intelligently checks whether each registrant already exists in your Mailchimp list. For new contacts, it creates a subscriber record with all relevant details. For existing contacts, it can update their information while preserving their email engagement history and subscription status.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Livestorm Registration

The workflow activates whenever a new registration is recorded in your Livestorm account. Make.com monitors your Livestorm events in real-time through API connections.

2. Data Extraction

The system pulls all available registrant information including name, email address, registration timestamp, and any custom fields from your Livestorm registration form (like company name, job title, or survey responses).

3. Mailchimp List Check

Before adding the contact, the workflow checks your specified Mailchimp audience to see if this email address already exists. This prevents duplicate entries and allows for smart updates.

4. Add or Update Subscriber

Depending on whether the contact exists in Mailchimp, the workflow either creates a new subscriber or updates the existing one. You can configure which fields should be updated for existing contacts.

Pro tip: Use Mailchimp tags to categorize registrants by webinar topic or date. This enables targeted follow-up sequences based on which events they attended.
