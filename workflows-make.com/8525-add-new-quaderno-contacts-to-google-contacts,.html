What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Quaderno for billing, invoicing, and tax compliance, customer data is gold. But when that data is trapped in one system, it creates silos. Your sales team needs contact details in Google Contacts for outreach, your support team needs them for service, and you need them accessible everywhere. Manually copying and pasting this information is a tedious, error-prone task that wastes valuable time.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a digital bridge between Quaderno and Google Contacts. The moment a new contact is created in Quaderno—whether it's a new customer, a lead, or a vendor—this workflow triggers. It securely retrieves the contact's details and automatically creates a corresponding entry in your specified Google Contacts list. The process is instantaneous, accurate, and requires zero ongoing effort from you or your team.

How It Works

The workflow is built on Make.com and operates on a simple trigger-action principle. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: The Trigger in Quaderno

The scenario constantly monitors your Quaderno account for a specific event: the creation of a new contact. This could be through a manual entry, an API integration, or an imported list. Make.com detects this new entry instantly.

Step 2: Data Retrieval & Mapping

Once triggered, the workflow fetches all relevant data fields for the new contact from Quaderno. This typically includes the contact's full name, email address, phone number, company name, and physical address. The workflow is pre-configured to map these Quaderno fields to the correct corresponding fields in Google Contacts.

Pro tip: You can easily customize which fields are synced. For instance, you might choose to include the Quaderno contact ID as a note in Google Contacts for easy cross-referencing later.

Step 3: Creation in Google Contacts

With the data prepared, the workflow authenticates with your Google account and executes the "Create a Contact" action. It places the new contact into a designated contact group or label (like "Clients" or "Quaderno Sync"), keeping your address book organized. The entire process from trigger to completion takes just seconds.
