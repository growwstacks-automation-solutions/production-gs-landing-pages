What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Quaderno billing system and Drip email marketing platform. Every time a new customer is created in Quaderno—whether through an invoice, subscription, or checkout process—the workflow instantly adds them as a subscriber in Drip with all their relevant contact information.

Manual data entry between systems creates delays, errors, and missed marketing opportunities. This solution ensures your sales and marketing systems stay perfectly synchronized, allowing you to trigger timely onboarding sequences, deliver personalized content, and maintain accurate customer profiles across platforms.

How It Works

1. New contact detection

The workflow monitors your Quaderno account for newly created contacts using Make.com's instant trigger. This could be from invoice generation, subscription signups, or manual contact creation.

2. Data mapping

Key customer information like name, email, company details, and custom fields are extracted from Quaderno and formatted for Drip's API requirements. The workflow handles data transformation to ensure compatibility between systems.

3. Subscriber creation

The processed contact data is sent to Drip via API, creating a new subscriber or updating an existing one. The workflow includes duplicate checking to maintain list hygiene.

4. Tagging and segmentation

Optionally, the workflow can apply specific tags in Drip based on Quaderno data (like customer type or purchase history) to enable targeted email campaigns.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to route different customer types to specific Drip campaigns. For example, map Quaderno's customer categories to Drip tags for segmented onboarding sequences.
