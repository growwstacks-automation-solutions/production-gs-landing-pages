What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Quaderno for invoicing and tax management, customer contact data is gold. But if that data sits isolated from your email marketing platform in Mailchimp, you're missing a huge opportunity to nurture those relationships. Manually copying and pasting contact details is tedious, error-prone, and often gets forgotten.

This automation solves that disconnect. It acts as a real-time bridge between your financial operations and your marketing engine. Whenever a new contact is created in Quaderno—be it a new customer, lead, or partner—this workflow instantly and accurately adds them as a subscriber to your designated Mailchimp audience. It turns a manual, administrative task into a seamless, background process that fuels your growth.

How It Works

The workflow is built on Make.com and follows a simple, reliable trigger-action pattern.

Step 1: Monitor for New Quaderno Contacts

The scenario starts by watching your Quaderno account for the "Contact Created" event. This is the trigger. It could be a new client you just invoiced, a lead who downloaded a tax guide, or a supplier you've added to the system.

Step 2: Extract and Prepare Contact Data

Once a new contact is detected, Make captures the relevant data: email address, first name, last name, company, and any custom fields you've defined. This step ensures we have clean, structured information ready for Mailchimp.

Pro tip: Use this step to filter contacts. You might only want to add contacts from a specific country or those tagged as "Customer" to keep your marketing list highly targeted.

Step 3: Add Subscriber to Mailchimp

The prepared data is then sent to your Mailchimp account via its API. The contact is added to your chosen audience list with a "subscribed" status. You can map Quaderno fields directly to Mailchimp merge tags for perfect personalization.

Step 4: (Optional) Log and Notify

The final module can log the successful addition in a Google Sheet or send a brief notification to a Slack channel for your team's visibility, completing a fully transparent, hands-off process.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: Authorize the scenario to access your Quaderno and Mailchimp accounts. This is a secure, one-time process.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific Quaderno event (Contact Created) that will start the automation.
  4. Map the Data: In the Mailchimp module, choose your target audience and map the fields from Quaderno (like email to EMAIL, first name to FNAME).
  5. Test & Activate: Run a test with an existing contact to ensure data flows correctly, then turn the scenario on. It will now run automatically.
