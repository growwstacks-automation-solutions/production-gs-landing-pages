What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of missed call notifications and delayed response times in customer-facing teams. When businesses receive calls through Callingly, team members often don't get alerted in real-time, leading to missed opportunities and frustrated customers.

The workflow automatically detects new incoming calls in Callingly and posts detailed notifications to your Slack channels. This ensures your sales, support, or operations team can immediately see and respond to important calls without manual monitoring or call logging.

How It Works

1. Call detection in Callingly

The workflow continuously monitors your Callingly account for new incoming calls using Make.com's API integration. Each time a call is received, the system captures all relevant details including caller information, call duration, and timestamps.

2. Data processing

Make.com formats the call data into a structured message, extracting key details like the caller's phone number, call purpose (if available), and any custom fields from your Callingly setup. The system can filter calls based on your criteria (e.g., only business hours calls).

3. Slack notification

The formatted message gets posted to your designated Slack channel with @mentions for relevant team members. The notification includes clickable elements allowing quick response or call assignment directly from Slack.

Pro tip: Add caller location data to your Slack notifications by enabling Callingly's geolocation features. This helps teams prioritize local customers.
