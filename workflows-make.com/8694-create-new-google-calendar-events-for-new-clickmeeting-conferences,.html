What This Workflow Does

For coaches, consultants, and businesses that run frequent webinars or virtual meetings, managing two separate calendars—one for scheduling (ClickMeeting) and one for your daily agenda (Google Calendar)—creates a major administrative headache. It's frustratingly easy to forget to manually copy a meeting over, leading to missed appointments, double-booked time slots, and a scattered view of your day.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a silent, reliable bridge between ClickMeeting and Google Calendar. Every time you create a new conference, workshop, or client call in ClickMeeting, this workflow instantly captures the details and creates a corresponding event in your designated Google Calendar. The result is a perfectly synchronized schedule where every commitment is visible in one place, eliminating manual work and the risk of human error.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, trigger-based automation built on Make.com. It listens for new events in one platform and intelligently creates them in another.

Step 1: The Trigger – A New ClickMeeting Conference

The automation is activated the moment you (or your system) create a new conference room in ClickMeeting. Make.com watches for this specific event using the ClickMeeting module's "Watch Conferences" trigger.

Step 2: Data Mapping & Formatting

Once triggered, the workflow extracts all relevant data from the new ClickMeeting conference: the event title, start date and time, duration, description, and the unique access link for participants. It then formats this data to match Google Calendar's required structure.

Pro tip: You can customize this step to add your company branding to the calendar event description or include specific instructions for attendees automatically.

Step 3: Creating the Google Calendar Event

The formatted data is then sent to Google Calendar via the "Create an Event" module. The workflow specifies which calendar to use (e.g., your primary calendar or a specific business calendar), sets the correct time zone, and inserts all the meeting details. Within seconds, the event appears in your Google Calendar, complete with the ClickMeeting join link.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to copy the template to your Make.com account.
  2. Authorize Connections: In your new Make scenario, click on the ClickMeeting and Google Calendar modules. Follow the prompts to log in and grant Make.com permission to access your accounts.
  3. Select Your Calendar: In the Google Calendar module, choose which specific calendar (e.g., "Work," "Primary") you want the new events to be created in.
  4. Test the Flow: Turn on the scenario and create a test conference in ClickMeeting. Check your Google Calendar within a minute to confirm the event was created successfully.
  5. Activate & Relax: Once confirmed, the automation is live. Every future ClickMeeting conference will now auto-populate in your calendar.
