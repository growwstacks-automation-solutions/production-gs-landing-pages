What This Workflow Does

Webinars are a powerful lead generation tool, but the manual process of transferring registrant data from platforms like ClickMeeting into your CRM is tedious and error-prone. Sales teams often waste hours copying emails, names, and details, leading to delayed follow-up and missed opportunities.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between ClickMeeting and Pipedrive. Every time a person registers for your webinar, their information is instantly and accurately created as a new contact in your Pipedrive sales pipeline. This ensures your team has immediate access to warm leads, enabling timely and personalized engagement that significantly boosts conversion rates.

How It Works

The workflow is a straightforward, trigger-based automation built on Make.com. It listens for new events in ClickMeeting and performs corresponding actions in Pipedrive without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Trigger on New Registration

The workflow is activated the moment a new attendee completes registration for a webinar in your ClickMeeting account. Make.com captures this event in real-time, pulling all available registration data such as name, email address, company, and the specific webinar they signed up for.

Step 2: Format and Prepare Contact Data

The raw data from ClickMeeting is then structured to match Pipedrive's contact field requirements. This step ensures that first names, last names, email addresses, and other custom properties are correctly mapped. You can also add logic here to tag contacts based on the webinar topic or assign a lead score.

Pro tip: Use this step to enrich the data. You can add a step to find the registrant's company LinkedIn URL or append notes like "Lead Source: Webinar - [Webinar Name]" for better tracking.

Step 3: Create or Update Contact in Pipedrive

Finally, the workflow sends the formatted data to Pipedrive to create a new contact. Advanced setups can include a search for an existing contact by email address first to prevent duplicates. If a match is found, the workflow can update the existing record with the new webinar attendance information instead.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes. Follow these steps after clicking "Get This Workflow" to import the template into your Make.com account.

  1. Import the Template: Use the provided link to open the template on Make.com and click "Copy to my scenarios".
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the new scenario, click on the ClickMeeting and Pipedrive modules. Authenticate and connect your accounts by following Make.com's OAuth steps.
  3. Map Your Data Fields: Review the data mapping between ClickMeeting and Pipedrive. Ensure fields like 'Email', 'First Name', and 'Last Name' are correctly aligned. Add any custom Pipedrive fields you want to populate.
  4. Test the Flow: Use Make.com's "Run once" feature to test with a recent registrant. Verify that a contact appears correctly in your Pipedrive account.
  5. Activate & Schedule: Turn on the scenario scheduler. Set it to run in real-time (instant) so every new registration triggers the workflow immediately.

Pro tip: After your initial test, create a "test" webinar in ClickMeeting and register with a personal email to do a full live test without affecting real leads.
