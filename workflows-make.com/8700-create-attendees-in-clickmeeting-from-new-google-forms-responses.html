What This Workflow Does

Managing webinar registrations manually is a tedious, error-prone task that drains valuable time from marketing and sales teams. You have to collect responses from a form, manually copy each attendee's details into your webinar platform, and then send out confirmation emails—a process that often leads to delays, missed registrations, and frustrated potential attendees.

This automated workflow solves that problem completely. It connects Google Forms, a free and versatile tool for creating sign-up forms, directly to ClickMeeting, a popular platform for hosting webinars and online meetings. Every time a person fills out your registration form, this system instantly and automatically creates them as an attendee in your specified ClickMeeting event. The moment they hit "submit," their seat is secured.

The business value is clear: you save hours of administrative work per event, eliminate copy-paste errors, and provide a professional, immediate registration experience that increases show-up rates. It turns a multi-step manual process into a seamless, hands-off operation.

How It Works

The automation acts as a intelligent bridge between your registration form and your webinar dashboard.

Step 1: Trigger on New Form Response

The workflow is activated the instant a new submission arrives in your Google Forms. Make.com watches your form for new responses, capturing all the data entered by the registrant, such as name, email address, company, and any custom questions you've added.

Step 2: Format Data for ClickMeeting

The captured data is then mapped and formatted to match the fields required by ClickMeeting's "Create an attendee" API. This step ensures the information flows correctly from your form fields into the correct slots in the webinar platform.

Pro tip: Use this step to add custom parameters, like tagging attendees based on which marketing campaign they came from, for better post-event segmentation.

Step 3: Create Attendee in ClickMeeting

The formatted data is sent to ClickMeeting, creating a new attendee record in your designated webinar session. The attendee is officially registered, and their status is updated within ClickMeeting's interface.

Step 4: (Optional) Send Confirmation & Follow-up

Once the attendee is successfully created, the workflow can trigger additional actions, such as sending a personalized confirmation email via Gmail or Outlook, adding the contact to a CRM like HubSpot, or notifying your team via Slack.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In Make.com, authorize the connections to your specific Google Forms and ClickMeeting accounts.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the exact Google Form you are using for registrations.
  4. Map the Data: In the "Create Attendee" module, map the Google Form fields (e.g., "Email Address") to the corresponding ClickMeeting attendee fields.
  5. Enter Your Event Details: Input your ClickMeeting Webinar ID and ensure any custom fields align.
