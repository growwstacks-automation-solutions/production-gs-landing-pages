What This Workflow Does

Every time a visitor interacts with your Tars chatbot, you have a golden opportunity to capture their interest. But that opportunity vanishes quickly if you don't follow up immediately. Manual follow-up processes are slow, inconsistent, and prone to human error, causing valuable leads to go cold.

This automation solves that problem by creating an instant bridge between Tars conversations and your Gmail inbox. Whenever someone completes a conversation with your Tars chatbot, this Make.com workflow automatically triggers and sends a personalized email directly to that person. The process happens in seconds, ensuring your business responds while the visitor is still engaged and thinking about your solution.

The workflow captures the email address and conversation data from Tars, formats it into a professional email, and delivers it through your Gmail account. This turns casual website visitors into warm leads without any manual intervention from your team.

How It Works

Step 1: Tars Triggers the Workflow

The automation begins when a new conversation is recorded in your Tars account. Make.com monitors your Tars account continuously and detects when a visitor provides their email address and completes a chat session.

Step 2: Data Extraction and Processing

Make.com extracts the conversation details, including the visitor's email address, name (if provided), the content of their messages, and any custom fields from your chatbot form. This data is structured and prepared for email composition.

Step 3: Personalized Email Creation

Using the extracted data, the workflow creates a personalized email. It can insert the visitor's name, reference their specific query, include relevant links or resources, and maintain your brand voice—all automatically.

Step 4: Automatic Gmail Delivery

The composed email is sent through your connected Gmail account. The visitor receives what appears to be a personally written follow-up email, but it was generated and sent automatically moments after their chatbot conversation ended.

Pro tip: Use this automation to segment leads automatically. Configure different email templates based on the conversation topic or intent captured in Tars, sending more targeted follow-ups that increase conversion rates.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Clone the template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template to your Make.com account.

2. Connect your apps: Authorize Make.com to access your Tars account and Gmail account through secure OAuth connections.

3. Configure the Tars trigger: Select which Tars bot should trigger the workflow and define what constitutes a "new conversation."

4. Customize the email template: Edit the Gmail module to match your branding, tone, and include personalized fields from the Tars conversation data.

5. Test and activate: Initiate a test conversation in Tars, verify the automated email sends correctly, then activate the workflow to run 24/7.

Pro tip: Start with a simple "Thank you for chatting" email, then gradually add more sophisticated logic like lead scoring, attachment of relevant PDFs, or routing to different team members based on conversation topics.
