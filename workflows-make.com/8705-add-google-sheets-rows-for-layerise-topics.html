What This Workflow Does

Customer feedback is a goldmine of insights, but it's useless if trapped in siloed platforms. Layerise excels at collecting and organizing user feedback topics, but teams often struggle to analyze this data alongside other business metrics in tools like Google Sheets. Manually copying and pasting topics is a tedious, error-prone task that wastes valuable hours each week and delays actionable insights.

This automation workflow solves that problem by creating a seamless, hands-off bridge between Layerise and Google Sheets. Every time new feedback topics are categorized in Layerise, they are automatically imported as new rows in your designated spreadsheet. This transforms raw feedback into structured, analyzable data instantly, ensuring your product, support, and marketing teams always have the latest customer sentiment at their fingertips without lifting a finger.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action principle, orchestrated by Make.com. Here’s the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Monitoring Layerise for New Topics

The scenario starts by watching your Layerise account for new or updated feedback topics. You can configure it to check at regular intervals (e.g., every hour) or trigger in real-time if supported, ensuring no insight is missed.

Step 2: Extracting Topic Data

When a new topic is detected, Make.com retrieves all relevant data: the topic title, description, sentiment score, category, volume (number of mentions), associated product area, and the date it was created or updated.

Step 3: Structuring & Mapping Data for Sheets

The workflow then maps each piece of Layerise data to a corresponding column in your Google Sheets. It formats dates, ensures consistent text, and can even add calculated fields, like priority flags based on sentiment or volume.

Step 4: Adding a New Row to Google Sheets

Finally, the structured data is appended as a new row at the bottom of your specified Google Sheet. The automation can be set to add timestamps, unique IDs, or status labels, creating a clean, historical log of all customer feedback topics.

Pro tip: Use this automated data feed to power a live dashboard in Google Sheets or Google Data Studio. You can create charts that show trending topics over time, helping you visualize which issues are growing or shrinking.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the pre-built scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, click the Layerise module and connect your account. Repeat for the Google Sheets module, selecting your target spreadsheet.
