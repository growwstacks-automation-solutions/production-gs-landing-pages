What This Workflow Does

If you use a Tars chatbot on your website, you're generating valuable conversations with potential customers every day. But what happens after that chat ends? Manually copying email addresses from chat logs into your email marketing platform is tedious, error-prone, and leads get cold while you're busy with other tasks.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a silent bridge between Tars and ActiveCampaign. Every time a new conversation starts in your Tars chatbot, this Make.com workflow instantly captures the visitor's contact information—primarily their email address—and either creates a new contact in your ActiveCampaign list or updates an existing one. This means your sales and marketing teams can begin nurturing that lead within minutes, not hours or days, dramatically increasing your chances of conversion.

The business value is clear: you stop losing leads that fall through the cracks. By automating this data transfer, you ensure your marketing funnel is always fed with fresh, qualified leads, allowing you to focus on engaging with people instead of managing spreadsheets.

How It Works

The workflow follows a simple but powerful sequence, triggered by real-time activity on your website.

Step 1: A New Tars Conversation Begins

The automation is triggered the moment a website visitor initiates a chat with your Tars bot. Make.com watches for this "new conversation" event continuously.

Step 2: Data Extraction

The workflow parses the conversation data sent by Tars. It looks for the key identifier: the user's email address. It can also be configured to capture additional fields like first name, last name, the page URL where the chat started, or custom answers from the chatbot script.

Step 3: ActiveCampaign Lookup & Action

Before creating a duplicate, the automation checks your ActiveCampaign account to see if a contact with that email already exists. If it's a new lead, it creates a brand new contact record. If the contact already exists, it updates their record with any new information from the latest conversation, keeping your CRM data current.

Step 4: Enrichment & Tagging (Optional)

You can extend the workflow to add tags in ActiveCampaign based on the conversation topic (e.g., "Asked about Pricing," "Interested in Demo"). This allows for immediate segmentation and personalized follow-up email sequences.

Pro tip: Use the "page URL" data from Tars to tag contacts based on which product or service page they were visiting when they chatted. This provides incredible context for your sales team.

What You'll Need

To use this template, you'll need a few accounts and permissions set up:

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available). This is where the automation runs.
  2. A Tars account with your chatbot live on your website. You'll need API access or webhook permissions.
  3. An ActiveCampaign account with an active list ready to receive new contacts. You'll need your API URL and Key.
  4. The email address field must be captured in your Tars chatbot flow for this to work seamlessly.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and it will be copied to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Tars: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Tars account using your Tars API credentials.
  3. Connect ActiveCampaign: In the ActiveCampaign module, input your account's API URL and Key (found in Settings > Developer).
  4. Map the Data: Ensure the email field from Tars is correctly mapped to the email field in ActiveCampaign. Add mappings for name or other custom fields if desired.
  5. Test & Activate: Turn on the scenario and start a test conversation on your Tars bot. Check your ActiveCampaign account to confirm the contact appears instantly.
