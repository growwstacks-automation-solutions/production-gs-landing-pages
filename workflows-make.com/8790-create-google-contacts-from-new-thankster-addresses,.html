What This Workflow Does

If you use Thankster to send personalized cards or gifts, you know the value of building relationships. But manually copying each recipient's address from Thankster into your Google Contacts is a tedious, error-prone task that steals time from more important work. This disconnect means customer data often sits siloed, missing from your central CRM where it could fuel future marketing campaigns or client follow-ups.

This free Make.com workflow solves that problem permanently. It acts as a digital bridge between Thankster and Google Contacts. Every single time a new address is created in your Thankster account—whether for a holiday card, a thank-you note, or a client gift—the automation instantly captures that data and creates a corresponding, fully-formed contact in your Google Contacts. The process is seamless, accurate, and runs 24/7.

The result is a self-maintaining customer database. Your Google Contacts list grows organically with every relationship-building touchpoint, giving you a unified view of clients, prospects, and partners without any extra effort from you or your team.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple, reliable sequence. Once set up, it operates silently in the background.

Step 1: Trigger on New Thankster Address

The workflow is activated the moment a new address entry is saved in your connected Thankster account. Make.com watches for this event continuously, ensuring no recipient is ever missed.

Step 2: Extract and Format Contact Data

The automation pulls the relevant details from the Thankster address, such as the recipient's full name, street address, city, state, and ZIP code. It intelligently parses and structures this data to match the field requirements of Google Contacts.

Pro tip: You can easily modify the workflow to capture additional data points from Thankster, like phone numbers or custom notes, and map them to corresponding fields in Google Contacts.

Step 3: Create Contact in Google Contacts

Using the formatted data, the workflow instructs Google Contacts via a secure connection to create a new contact. The contact is saved instantly to your designated Google account, complete with all address information.

Step 4: Optional Logging or Notification

For peace of mind, the workflow can be configured to send you a brief notification (e.g., via email or Slack) confirming a new contact was added, or to log the activity in a Google Sheet for audit purposes.
