What This Workflow Does

If you use Thankster for direct mail or gifting campaigns, you're collecting valuable address data from potential leads, customers, or partners. However, that data often sits siloed in Thankster, disconnected from your sales team's primary tool: Pipedrive CRM. Manually transferring each new address is a tedious, error-prone task that slows down your sales cycle and leads to missed opportunities.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a seamless bridge between Thankster and Pipedrive. Whenever a new address is created in your Thankster account, this workflow triggers automatically. It captures all the relevant details—name, address, email, notes—and uses them to create a fully-formed contact record in your Pipedrive CRM. Your sales team gets instant, automatic visibility into new leads without lifting a finger.

How It Works

The workflow is built on Make.com and follows a simple, reliable sequence. Once set up, it runs continuously in the background, monitoring for new data and acting upon it.

Step 1: Monitor Thankster for New Addresses

The workflow starts by watching your Thankster account. Using the "Watch Addresses" module, it polls Thankster at regular intervals. The moment a new address entry is detected, the automation captures the entire data packet and passes it to the next step.

Step 2: Map and Format the Contact Data

Make.com then processes the raw data from Thankster. It maps fields like "First Name," "Company," "Street Address," and "Email" to the corresponding field names in Pipedrive. This step ensures clean, consistent data transfer and can include logic to handle missing information or apply custom tags.

Pro tip: Use this step to add a custom lead source tag like "Thankster Campaign" to every new contact. This helps your sales team prioritize follow-ups and track campaign ROI directly in Pipedrive.

Step 3: Create the Contact in Pipedrive

The formatted data is sent to Pipedrive via the "Create a Person" module. A new contact is instantly created in your specified Pipedrive pipeline. The workflow can also assign the contact to a specific owner, add it to an organization, or trigger a notification for immediate follow-up.
