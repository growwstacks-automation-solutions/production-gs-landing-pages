What This Workflow Does

In the competitive world of e-commerce, a simple thank you can be your secret weapon. This automation solves the problem of impersonal, transactional customer relationships by automatically sending a personalized Thankster greeting card every time a customer completes a paid order on your Shopify store.

Instead of manually tracking orders, designing cards, and managing mailings—tasks that often get deprioritized—this workflow handles everything instantly. The moment an order status changes to "paid" in Shopify, the system triggers, collects the customer's details, and sends a beautifully designed physical or digital card through Thankster. This creates a "wow" moment that customers remember, directly impacting loyalty and repeat business.

Pro tip: Use this automation not just for thank yous, but also for post-purchase follow-ups, review requests, or birthday greetings. The same trigger can power multiple customer touchpoints.

How It Works

The workflow acts as a silent ambassador for your brand, working in the background to strengthen customer relationships with every order.

Step 1: Monitor New Shopify Orders

The automation continuously watches your Shopify store for new orders. It uses a webhook or scheduled check to detect when an order's financial status updates to "paid," ensuring you only send cards for confirmed purchases.

Step 2: Extract Customer & Order Details

Once a paid order is detected, the system pulls relevant data: customer name, email, order number, purchase date, and any special notes. This information becomes the foundation for your personalized card message.

Step 3: Format Data for Thankster

The workflow structures the extracted data to match Thankster's API requirements. It can insert dynamic variables like the customer's first name into your card template, making each card feel uniquely created for that individual.

Step 4: Send the Card Automatically

Finally, the system sends a request to Thankster with all the formatted data. Thankster processes the card—whether physical or digital—and dispatches it to your customer, completing the automated cycle of appreciation.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes. Follow these steps to configure the template for your store.

  1. Import the template: Click "Get This Workflow" and import it into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Shopify: In the first module, authenticate your Shopify store using your admin credentials.
  3. Connect Thankster: In the card-sending module, add your Thankster API key to establish the connection.
  4. Map your data: Ensure customer name and order details are correctly mapped from Shopify to the Thankster card fields.
  5. Test with a recent order: Run the scenario once manually with a past order to verify the card sends correctly.
  6. Activate the scenario: Turn on the scheduler or webhook to run automatically, then let it handle all future orders.

Pro tip: Start by sending digital cards to test the workflow quickly and cost-effectively. Once you're confident, switch to physical cards for maximum impact.
