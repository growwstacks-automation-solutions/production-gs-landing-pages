What This Workflow Does

Running webinars is a powerful marketing strategy, but the manual process of moving contacts from your CRM to your webinar platform is a major bottleneck. Marketing teams often waste hours copying and pasting email addresses, risking errors, and causing registration delays that hurt attendance rates.

This automated workflow solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between your HubSpot CRM and Zoom Webinars. Whenever a contact is added to a specific marketing list in HubSpot—such as a "Webinar Interest" list—the automation instantly registers them for your designated Zoom webinar. The contact receives their confirmation and join link immediately, creating a professional and frictionless experience that maximizes sign-up and show-up rates.

Pro tip: Use this automation not just for general sign-ups, but for targeted, list-based campaigns. For example, create a list for contacts who downloaded a specific whitepaper and automatically invite them to a related deep-dive webinar.

How It Works

The automation acts as a real-time connector between two critical business platforms.

Step 1: Trigger from HubSpot

The workflow is triggered the instant a new contact is added to a pre-defined static or active list in your HubSpot portal. Make.com watches for this event continuously.

Step 2: Map Contact Data

The system captures the new contact's details—like first name, last name, and email address—from the HubSpot trigger. You can configure it to pull additional custom properties (job title, company) to pass to Zoom.

Step 3: Create Zoom Registrant

Using the captured data, the automation makes an API call to Zoom Webinars to register the contact for your specified webinar. It sends the contact's information to Zoom's registration system.

Step 4: Confirm & Log

Upon successful registration, the workflow can be extended to log the action in HubSpot, send a custom confirmation email, or update internal dashboards, ensuring everything is tracked.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: Authorize the HubSpot and Zoom modules in Make.com using your API credentials.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific HubSpot list that will trigger the registration.
  4. Set the Webinar: In the Zoom module, select the webinar ID for which you want to register contacts.
  5. Map Data Fields: Ensure the contact's email, first name, and last name from HubSpot are correctly mapped to the Zoom registration fields.
  6. Test & Activate: Add a test contact to your HubSpot list and verify they appear as a registrant in Zoom. Then turn the scenario on.

Pro tip: Before going live, run a test with a small, controlled segment of your list. Verify the registration confirmation email from Zoom is received and looks correct.
