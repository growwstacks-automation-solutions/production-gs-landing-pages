What This Workflow Does

For businesses using ActiveCampaign for email marketing and Daylite for CRM and sales management, a critical gap often exists: new leads and subscribers in ActiveCampaign don't automatically appear in the sales team's Daylite workspace. This forces salespeople to manually check for new contacts, copy-paste information, and risk missing timely follow-ups. This disconnect slows down sales cycles and creates data silos between marketing and sales departments.

This Make.com automation solves that problem by acting as a real-time bridge. It automatically detects when a new contact is added to your ActiveCampaign account, retrieves their details (like name, email, company, and custom fields), and instantly creates a corresponding contact record in Daylite. The workflow is scheduled to run every 15 minutes, ensuring your sales team has access to fresh leads almost as soon as they come in, without anyone lifting a finger.

Pro tip: Use this sync to pass ActiveCampaign tags (like "Webinar Attendee" or "Trial User") to Daylite. This allows your sales team to immediately understand a lead's context and tailor their outreach, dramatically increasing conversion rates.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple but powerful sequence to keep your contact databases perfectly synchronized.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

The workflow is initiated by a Schedule module set to run every 15 minutes. This acts as a regular "check-in" with your ActiveCampaign account to see if anything new has arrived since the last run.

Step 2: Fetch New Contacts from ActiveCampaign

Make.com connects to your ActiveCampaign account via API and searches for contacts created after the last successful run. It retrieves all relevant data for each new contact.

Step 3: Map and Transform Data

The contact information from ActiveCampaign is mapped to the corresponding field structure in Daylite. This step ensures that first names, last names, email addresses, phone numbers, and custom field data are correctly formatted for the next step.

Step 4: Create Contact in Daylite

Finally, Make.com uses the Daylite API to create a new contact record for each fetched lead. The contact is instantly available in your Daylite database, complete with all the synced information, ready for your sales team to engage.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes by following these steps.

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to copy the template to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect ActiveCampaign: In the first module, create a connection to your ActiveCampaign account using your API URL and Key (found in Settings > Developer).
  3. Connect Daylite: In the module that creates the contact, establish a connection to Daylite using your Account ID and Private Key (generated in Daylite's Preferences > Advanced).
  4. Adjust the Schedule (Optional): The default schedule is every 15 minutes. You can change this interval in the Schedule module to hourly, daily, or any other frequency that suits your lead volume.
  5. Turn it On: Activate the scenario. It will immediately begin checking for new contacts and syncing them to Daylite.
