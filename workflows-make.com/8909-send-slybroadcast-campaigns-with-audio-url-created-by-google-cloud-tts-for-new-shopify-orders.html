What This Workflow Does

In today's competitive e-commerce landscape, generic text notifications often get ignored. This automation solves that by transforming every new Shopify order into a personalized voice message delivered directly to your customer's phone via SMS. It captures key order details—like the customer's name, order number, and items purchased—converts them into natural-sounding speech using Google's AI, and dispatches them as an engaging audio campaign through Slybroadcast.

The business value is profound: you replace silent, impersonal updates with a branded audio experience that builds stronger customer relationships. This system works 24/7, ensuring timely communication that can increase customer satisfaction, reduce support tickets asking "where's my order?", and open new avenues for promotional outreach. It turns a routine transactional alert into a memorable touchpoint.

How It Works

The workflow is a seamless, multi-step process that connects your storefront to your customer's pocket.

Step 1: Monitor New Shopify Orders

The automation starts by watching your Shopify store at regular intervals. The moment a new order is placed, it triggers the workflow and captures the specific data points you want to announce, such as the customer's first name, order total, and product names.

Step 2: Synthesize Speech with Google Cloud TTS

The extracted text data is sent to Google Cloud Text-to-Speech. This AI service generates a high-quality, natural-sounding audio file (.mp3 or .wav) from the text. You can choose the voice, language, and speaking rate to match your brand's personality.

Step 3: Upload & Share via Google Drive

The newly created audio file is automatically uploaded to a designated folder in your Google Drive. The workflow then generates a shareable link to the file, ensuring the audio is accessible for the SMS campaign.

Step 4: Launch the Slybroadcast Campaign

Finally, the workflow sends the audio file link, along with any accompanying text, to Slybroadcast. Slybroadcast then delivers it as a voice message SMS campaign to your predefined list of phone numbers—typically the customer who just placed the order.

Pro tip: Use this for post-purchase sequences. Follow up the order confirmation voice message with a shipping update message a day later to keep customers informed and delighted.

What You'll Need

To use this template, ensure you have the following accounts and access set up:

  1. A Make.com account (free or paid plan).
  2. A Shopify store with admin API access to fetch order data.
  3. A Google Cloud Platform account with the Text-to-Speech API enabled and credentials (service account key).
  4. A Google Drive account where audio files will be stored.
  5. A Slybroadcast account with an active plan for sending voice SMS campaigns.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your voice message campaign live in under 30 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Shopify: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Shopify store and select the order trigger events.
  3. Configure Google TTS: Add your Google Cloud service account JSON key to the Text-to-Speech module. Set your preferred voice and language settings.
  4. Set Up Google Drive: Authorize Google Drive and specify the folder ID where audio files should be saved.
  5. Link Slybroadcast: Enter your Slybroadcast API credentials in the final module. Define your target phone number list or map it from the Shopify customer data.
  6. Test & Activate: Run a test with a recent order. Once you confirm the audio is generated and sent correctly, turn the scenario on.
