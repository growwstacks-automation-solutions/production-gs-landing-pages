What This Workflow Does

If you use VideoAsk for lead generation—through consultations, webinar sign-ups, or feedback forms—you know the frustration of manually transferring contact details to your email marketing platform. This disjointed process wastes valuable time, introduces errors, and delays your follow-up, causing potential leads to go cold.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge between VideoAsk and Mailchimp. Every time someone submits a response to your VideoAsk form, their email address and any other collected details are instantly and accurately added as a subscriber to your designated Mailchimp list. It turns a manual, error-prone task into a reliable, hands-off system that ensures no lead slips through the cracks.

The business value is clear: faster lead nurturing, a consistently growing email list, and hours of administrative work saved each month. Your team can focus on creating engaging video content and crafting effective email campaigns, confident that the data flow between these two powerful tools is fully automated.

How It Works

The workflow acts as an intelligent middleman, listening for new data in VideoAsk and pushing it to Mailchimp in the correct format.

Step 1: Trigger on New VideoAsk Response

The automation is activated the moment a new response is submitted to your VideoAsk form. It captures the entire response packet, including the respondent's email address, name, and any custom answers from your form fields.

Step 2: Format and Prepare the Data

The workflow then processes this data. It checks for required fields (like a valid email), formats names correctly, and maps VideoAsk question answers to corresponding Mailchimp merge fields (e.g., "Job Title" from VideoAsk to the "TITLE" field in Mailchimp).

Pro tip: Use this step to clean data. You can add a filter to ignore test submissions or responses from your own team email addresses, keeping your list clean.

Step 3: Add Subscriber to Mailchimp

Finally, the processed data is sent to Mailchimp's API. The contact is added to your specified audience list. You can configure the subscriber status (e.g., "subscribed" or "pending" for double opt-in) and apply tags based on which VideoAsk form was used, enabling powerful segmentation from day one.
