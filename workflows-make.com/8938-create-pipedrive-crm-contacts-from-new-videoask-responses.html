What This Workflow Does

Businesses using interactive video forms through VideoAsk face a critical bottleneck: manually transferring lead information into their CRM. This process is slow, prone to errors, and causes delays in sales follow-up, potentially losing hot leads who engaged with your video content.

This automated workflow solves that by creating a seamless bridge between VideoAsk and Pipedrive. Every time a prospect completes your VideoAsk form—whether it's a sales qualification, feedback request, or application—their response data is instantly and accurately transformed into a new contact record in Pipedrive. This ensures your sales team has immediate access to enriched lead data, including video responses, ready for personalized outreach.

The automation handles the entire data flow behind the scenes. It captures the respondent's name, email, answers to multiple-choice questions, and even the URL to their video recording, structuring it all within the appropriate Pipedrive contact fields. This turns a manual, time-consuming administrative task into a zero-touch, reliable process that operates 24/7.

How It Works

The workflow is triggered by new activity in your VideoAsk account and executes a series of actions in Make.com to format and deliver the data to Pipedrive.

Step 1: Monitor for New VideoAsk Responses

The scenario starts with a Watch Responses module in VideoAsk. It polls your account at regular intervals, instantly detecting when a new form submission or video response is completed. This trigger captures the entire response payload.

Step 2: Parse and Structure the Response Data

Make.com then processes the incoming data. It extracts key values like the respondent's first name, last name, email address, and answers to specific questions. The router or filter modules can be used to handle different form types or apply basic qualifying logic based on the answers provided.

Pro tip: Use this parsing step to assign a lead score or tag based on VideoAsk answers. For example, if a respondent indicates a high budget, you can automatically mark them as a "High-Priority" lead in Pipedrive.

Step 3: Create or Update the Contact in Pipedrive

The final action is the Create a Person module in Pipedrive. The formatted data from Step 2 is mapped directly to the corresponding fields in Pipedrive. This creates a new, fully populated contact record. You can also include the link to the video response in the contact's notes, giving sales reps invaluable context for their first call.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps.

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to open the pre-built template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, authorize the connections to your VideoAsk and Pipedrive accounts. This usually involves logging in and granting permission.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific VideoAsk form or "ask" you want to monitor for new responses in the Watch Responses module.
  4. Map the Data Fields: In the Pipedrive "Create a Person" module, map the data from VideoAsk to the correct Pipedrive fields. Standard mappings are: Email → email, First Name → first_name, Last Name → last_name.
  5. Test and Activate: Turn on the scenario and submit a test response to your VideoAsk form. Verify that a new contact appears in your Pipedrive CRM with all the correct data. Once confirmed, your automation is live.
