What This Workflow Does

Businesses using VideoAsk for lead generation, candidate screening, or customer feedback face a critical bottleneck: manual follow-up. Every insightful video response ends up trapped in an admin dashboard, requiring someone to watch, transcribe notes, and manually create a task in a project management tool like Trello. This process is slow, error-prone, and causes valuable opportunities to slip through the cracks.

This automated workflow solves that by creating a seamless bridge between VideoAsk and Trello. Every time a new response is submitted—whether it's a sales inquiry, a job application video, or product feedback—it automatically triggers the creation of a detailed Trello card. The card is populated with key respondent data, a direct link to the video, and can be assigned to the right team member, instantly moving the response from a passive inbox to an active workflow.

How It Works

The automation runs on Make.com, acting as the intelligent middle layer that listens for new data and takes action.

Step 1: Trigger on New VideoAsk Response

The scenario starts by monitoring your specific VideoAsk form or funnel. The moment a respondent completes and submits their video, Make.com instantly captures the entire response package, including the video file URL, transcript, answers to form questions, and contact details.

Step 2: Parse and Structure the Data

Make.com processes the incoming data. You can set up modules to filter responses (e.g., only forward responses with a certain keyword), extract specific answers to use as the card title, or format a comprehensive description that includes the video link and a summary.

Pro tip: Use the respondent's answer to a key question (like "What's your biggest challenge?") as the Trello card title. This makes each card immediately actionable for your team.

Step 3: Create the Trello Card

The workflow then connects to your Trello board and creates a new card in the designated list (e.g., "New Leads," "Applications to Review"). It maps the structured data from VideoAsk to the card's fields: title, description, checklist items, labels for categorization, due dates, and even attaches the respondent's email as a comment for easy contact.

Step 4: Notify and Assign (Optional)

As a final step, you can extend the automation to add specific team members to the card or send a notification to a Slack channel alerting the team that a new video response has been logged and is ready for action.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario, authorize the VideoAsk and Trello modules by logging into your accounts and granting access.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific VideoAsk form or funnel you want to monitor for new responses.
  4. Map Your Trello Board: Select your target Trello board and the list (e.g., "Inbox" or "To Process") where new cards should be created.
  5. Customize Data Mapping: Drag and drop data from the VideoAsk response into the Trello card fields. Set the card title, description, and any labels.
  6. Test and Activate: Use the "Run once" feature to test with a real past response. Confirm the card appears correctly in Trello, then turn the scenario to "On" to automate future responses.
