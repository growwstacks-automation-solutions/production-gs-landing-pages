What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in modern marketing: capturing leads through engaging video interactions and immediately moving them into your SMS marketing funnel. VideoAsk allows you to create interactive video forms that feel personal and engaging, but manually transferring respondent data to your SMS platform (TextMagic) is slow and error-prone. This workflow bridges that gap automatically.

Every time a potential customer completes your VideoAsk form—whether they're signing up for a webinar, requesting a demo, or answering a survey—their contact information is instantly added as a new contact in your TextMagic account. This means they can receive timely, personalized SMS follow-ups within minutes, not days. The business value is clear: faster lead response times, higher conversion rates, and a fully automated lead-to-nurture pipeline that requires zero manual effort from your team.

For businesses using video for lead generation, this integration turns a passive data collection point into an active marketing channel. It ensures no lead falls through the cracks because of delayed processing. You can start building relationships via SMS immediately, increasing the likelihood that the prospect will take the next step with your business.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New VideoAsk Response

The workflow starts when a new response is submitted to your VideoAsk form. Make.com monitors your VideoAsk account for this event, capturing the entire response payload including the respondent's name, email, phone number, and any custom answers they provided.

Step 2: Extract and Format Contact Data

The automation then parses the response data, focusing on the fields needed for TextMagic. It typically extracts the first name, last name, and mobile phone number. It can also pull additional data like company name or specific answers to use for segmentation later.

Step 3: Create Contact in TextMagic

Using the extracted data, Make.com calls the TextMagic API to create a new contact in your specified list or address book. The contact is added with all available details, ready to be used in your SMS campaigns.

Step 4: Optional Data Enhancement & Notification

You can add additional steps, such as sending a confirmation email to the respondent, updating a spreadsheet log, or sending a notification to your team via Slack or email that a new lead has been captured and added to the SMS list.

Pro tip: Use the VideoAsk response data to add custom fields or tags to the TextMagic contact. For example, if someone answered "Yes" to a question about interest in a product, tag them as "Product Interested" in TextMagic. This allows for highly targeted SMS campaigns later.
