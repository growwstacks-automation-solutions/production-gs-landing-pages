What This Workflow Does

Maintaining a clean email list is one of the most overlooked yet critical tasks for any business that relies on email communication. Invalid, risky, or fake email addresses silently drain your marketing budget, hurt your sender reputation, and sabotage campaign performance. Manually verifying hundreds or thousands of emails is tedious, error-prone, and simply doesn't scale.

This automated workflow solves that problem head-on. It connects your Airtable database—where you likely store leads, customers, or newsletter subscribers—directly to MailerCheck, a professional email verification service. The system periodically checks for new or unverified records in Airtable, sends each email to MailerCheck for validation, and instantly updates the record with a clear status like "Valid," "Risky," or "Invalid." This creates a self-cleaning database that ensures your marketing, sales, and notification emails only go to real, engaged recipients.

How It Works

The automation runs on a schedule you control, creating a continuous feedback loop between your data and the verification service.

Step 1: Monitor Airtable for New Records

The workflow is triggered on a schedule (default: every 15 minutes). It queries your specified Airtable base and table, looking for records where the "Status" field is empty or marked as "Unverified." It filters for new entries based on the "Created Time" column, ensuring it only processes the latest additions.

Step 2: Send Emails to MailerCheck for Verification

For each new record retrieved, the workflow extracts the email address from the designated column and sends it to the MailerCheck API. MailerCheck performs a series of checks including syntax validation, domain lookup, and mailbox existence, returning a detailed result.

Step 3: Update Airtable with Verification Results

Immediately after receiving the result from MailerCheck, the workflow maps the verification status (e.g., "deliverable," "undeliverable," "risky") back to your Airtable record. It updates the "Status" field with this clear label, giving you an instantly actionable view of your list's health. The record is now tagged and ready for segmentation or further action.

Pro tip: Use Airtable views or filters to segment your list by status. Create a view for "Valid" emails to target your next campaign, and a view for "Risky" emails to consider a re-engagement strategy before sending bulk emails.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 10 minutes.

  1. Clone the template: Click "Get This Workflow" and it will open in your Make.com account. Press the "Copy" button to create your own scenario.
  2. Connect your apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, click on the Airtable module and authenticate your account. Select your base and table. Then, click the MailerCheck module and add your API key.
  3. Map your fields: Ensure the module field mappings point to the correct column names in your Airtable table ("Email", "Status", "Created Time").
  4. Set the schedule: The trigger is set to run every 15 minutes by default. You can change this to hourly, daily, or even trigger it via a webhook for real-time verification.
  5. Run a test: Add a test record to your Airtable table and run the scenario once to see it flow through. Confirm the status updates correctly.
  6. Turn it on: Activate the scenario. It will now run automatically on your chosen schedule, keeping your list perpetually clean.
