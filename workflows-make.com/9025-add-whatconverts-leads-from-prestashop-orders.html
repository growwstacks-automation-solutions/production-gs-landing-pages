What This Workflow Does

For e-commerce businesses, understanding which marketing channels actually generate sales is a constant challenge. You might see traffic from Google Ads, Facebook, or SEO, but connecting that traffic to final purchases in PrestaShop often involves manual work or guesswork. This disconnect means you could be wasting budget on campaigns that don't convert while undervaluing your top performers.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless bridge between your PrestaShop store and WhatConverts. Every time a customer completes an order, the workflow instantly captures that sale as a new lead in WhatConverts. It attaches all the crucial details—order value, items purchased, customer contact information—to the original marketing source tracked by WhatConverts. This turns anonymous sales into attributed revenue, giving you a crystal-clear picture of your marketing ROI.

How It Works

The workflow runs on Make.com, acting as a smart middleman between your two platforms. It listens for new events and moves data between them automatically.

Step 1: Trigger on New PrestaShop Order

The workflow is activated the moment a new order is placed in your PrestaShop store. Make.com monitors your PrestaShop in real-time, waiting for this specific event.

Step 2: Gather Complete Order & Customer Data

Once triggered, the workflow collects all relevant information from the new order. This includes the customer's name, email, phone, shipping address, the products they bought, the total order value, and any custom notes.

Pro tip: You can map custom PrestaShop fields (like "Lead Source" from a form) into WhatConverts lead properties. This allows for even more granular tracking beyond standard web attribution.

Step 3: Format and Send Data to WhatConverts

The workflow then structures this data into the exact format WhatConverts requires to create a new lead. It matches the customer details to the correct lead fields and prepares the API call.

Step 4: Create the Lead in WhatConverts

Finally, the workflow sends the formatted data to your WhatConverts account via its API. A new lead is instantly created, linked to the original marketing source (like a specific ad click or phone call), and enriched with the full sales data from PrestaShop.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation live in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template to your Make.com account.
  2. Connect PrestaShop: In the first module, add your PrestaShop store URL and API key. Test the connection to ensure Make.com can access your orders.
  3. Connect WhatConverts: In the "Create a Lead" module, add your WhatConverts API Token and select your main profile.
  4. Map the Data (Optional): Review how the PrestaShop order fields are mapped to WhatConverts lead fields. Adjust if you want to send additional custom data.
  5. Activate & Test: Turn the scenario on. Place a test order in your PrestaShop store (or use a recent real order) and verify that a corresponding lead appears in your WhatConverts dashboard with all the correct details.
