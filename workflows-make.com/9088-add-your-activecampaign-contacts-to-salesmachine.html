What This Workflow Does

For businesses using ActiveCampaign for marketing automation and Salesmachine for sales execution, a critical gap often exists: new leads captured in marketing campaigns don't automatically appear in the sales team's CRM. This forces sales reps to manually check for new contacts, copy-paste data, or worse, miss time-sensitive opportunities entirely. The delay between lead capture and sales outreach can cost you conversions.

This automated workflow solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between ActiveCampaign and Salesmachine. It continuously monitors your ActiveCampaign account for new contacts and instantly adds them as leads or contacts in your Salesmachine instance. The sales team gets a real-time, automated feed of qualified leads, complete with essential data like email, name, company, and any tags or lead scores, enabling immediate and informed follow-up.

Pro tip: Use ActiveCampaign tags or lead scores as triggers. You can configure the workflow to only sync contacts tagged as "Sales Qualified" or with a lead score above a certain threshold, ensuring your sales team focuses on the hottest prospects.

How It Works

The automation runs on a reliable, scheduled cycle to keep your systems in perfect sync without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Monitor for New ActiveCampaign Contacts

The workflow is triggered at regular intervals (e.g., every 15 minutes) to check your ActiveCampaign account. It searches for contacts created since the last check, ensuring no new lead is ever missed.

Step 2: Extract and Format Contact Data

For each new contact found, the automation extracts key information. This typically includes the contact's email address, first and last name, phone number, company, custom fields, and any associated tags or lead score from ActiveCampaign.

Step 3: Create or Update the Contact in Salesmachine

The workflow then uses the Salesmachine API to create a new lead record. It maps the fields from ActiveCampaign to the corresponding fields in Salesmachine. If a contact with the same email already exists, the workflow can be set to update the existing record instead, preventing duplicates.

Step 4: Log and Handle Errors

The process includes error handling. If a sync fails (e.g., due to an API issue or invalid data), the error is logged, and you can be notified via email or Slack. This ensures data integrity and allows for quick troubleshooting.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your automation running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect ActiveCampaign: In the first module, create a connection to your ActiveCampaign account using your API URL and key.
  3. Connect Salesmachine: In the Salesmachine module, establish a connection using your API key from the Salesmachine settings.
  4. Set the Schedule: Configure the "Schedule" module to run as often as you need (e.g., every 5, 15, or 60 minutes).
  5. Map Your Fields: Review the "Create a Lead" module in Salesmachine. Ensure the data from ActiveCampaign (like `email`, `firstName`) is correctly mapped to the right fields in Salesmachine.
  6. Test and Activate: Run a single test cycle to create a contact in ActiveCampaign and verify it appears correctly in Salesmachine. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on.
