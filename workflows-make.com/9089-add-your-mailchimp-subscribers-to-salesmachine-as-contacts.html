What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Mailchimp for email marketing and Salesmachine for sales engagement, a critical gap often exists: new leads captured in Mailchimp don't automatically appear in the sales team's pipeline. This forces sales reps to manually check for new subscribers, copy-paste their details, and create contacts—a process that is slow, error-prone, and causes missed opportunities when leads go cold.

This free Make.com workflow solves that problem by creating a seamless, automated bridge between your marketing and sales systems. It watches your Mailchimp audience at regular intervals, detects new subscribers instantly, and creates corresponding contact records in Salesmachine. The result is a unified system where every new prospect is immediately ready for sales outreach, ensuring your team can act fast while interest is highest.

How It Works

The automation runs on a simple, reliable trigger-and-action logic that requires zero ongoing maintenance once set up.

Step 1: Monitor for New Mailchimp Subscribers

The workflow is triggered on a schedule you set (e.g., every 15 minutes). It queries your specified Mailchimp list and identifies any subscribers who have joined since the last check.

Step 2: Extract and Map Lead Data

For each new subscriber, the workflow pulls key information like their email address, first name, last name, and any custom fields you collect (like company or job title). This data is structured and prepared for the next step.

Pro tip: Use Mailchimp's merge tags to collect valuable sales intelligence during sign-up, such as "Budget" or "Timeline." This data will automatically flow into Salesmachine, giving your reps powerful context for their first call.

Step 3: Create or Update Contact in Salesmachine

The workflow sends the formatted subscriber data to Salesmachine via its API. It creates a new contact record, populating fields like name, email, and source (set as "Mailchimp"). You can configure it to add tags or assign the lead to a specific sales rep based on your rules.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to open the template in your Make.com account and create a copy.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, authorize the connections to your Mailchimp and Salesmachine accounts when prompted.
  3. Configure the Trigger: Select the specific Mailchimp list you want to monitor and set your preferred polling schedule (e.g., every 5 minutes).
  4. Map the Data Fields: In the Salesmachine module, map the incoming Mailchimp fields (email, FNAME, LNAME) to the corresponding contact fields in Salesmachine.
  5. Test and Activate: Run a single test cycle with a recent subscriber to ensure the contact appears correctly in Salesmachine. Once confirmed, turn the scenario on.
