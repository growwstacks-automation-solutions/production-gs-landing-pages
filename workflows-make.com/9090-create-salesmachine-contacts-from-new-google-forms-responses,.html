What This Workflow Does

Every minute a lead sits in a spreadsheet is a minute your sales team can't act on it. Manual data entry from Google Forms into your CRM is a slow, error-prone process that costs you deals and productivity. This automation solves that by creating a real-time bridge between your lead capture forms and your sales engagement platform.

The workflow watches for new submissions on your Google Form. The moment someone fills it out—whether it's a demo request, contact inquiry, or event registration—the automation triggers. It takes the respondent's information (name, email, company, etc.) and uses it to instantly create a new, fully populated contact inside your Salesmachine account. This means your sales reps can start their outreach sequences immediately, while the lead is hottest.

How It Works

The automation runs on a simple, powerful sequence that requires zero ongoing effort from your team.

Step 1: Monitor for New Form Responses

The workflow is set to check your specified Google Form at regular intervals (e.g., every 5 minutes). It uses the Google Forms module in Make.com to detect any new submissions that have come in since the last check.

Step 2: Extract and Structure the Data

Once a new response is found, the automation parses the data. It maps each answer from the form to a specific data field. For example, the "Email Address" question maps to the contact's email field, "First Name" maps accordingly, and any custom questions map to notes or custom properties in Salesmachine.

Pro tip: Use consistent naming in your Google Form questions (like "Work Email" instead of just "Email") to make field mapping foolproof and easier to maintain.

Step 3: Create the Contact in Salesmachine

The structured data is then sent to Salesmachine via its API. The workflow uses the "Create a Contact" action, populating all the relevant fields. The contact is created in your Salesmachine account with the lead source automatically tagged as "Google Form," providing valuable tracking data.

Step 4: Confirm and Log (Optional)

For reliability, the workflow can include a final step to log the successful creation in a Google Sheet or send a confirmation to a Slack channel. This gives you a passive audit trail and peace of mind that every lead is being processed.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the template into your own Make.com scenario.
  2. Connect Your Apps: Authorize the Google Forms and Salesmachine modules by logging into your accounts when prompted by Make.com.
  3. Select Your Form: In the Google Forms module, choose the specific form you want to monitor from the dropdown list.
  4. Map the Fields: In the Salesmachine "Create Contact" module, map the data from the form response to the correct Salesmachine contact fields (e.g., map "Email" to "email").
  5. Test and Activate: Use the "Run once" feature to test with a recent form submission. Verify the contact appears correctly in Salesmachine, then schedule the scenario to run every 5 minutes and turn it on.
