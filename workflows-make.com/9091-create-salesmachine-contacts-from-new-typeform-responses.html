What This Workflow Does

Every minute counts in sales. When a potential customer fills out your Typeform—whether it's a contact form, demo request, or survey—that information is gold. But if it sits in a spreadsheet or an email inbox, its value decays rapidly as your competition reaches out first. This workflow solves that critical delay by creating an instant, automated bridge between your lead capture forms and your sales engagement platform.

It watches for new Typeform responses and, as soon as one is submitted, it packages the data—like name, email, company, and any custom answers—and creates a corresponding contact in Salesmachine. This means your sales team sees a new, fully-formed lead in their system within moments of submission, equipped with all the context they need to start a personalized conversation immediately.

The business value is clear: faster lead response times directly correlate with higher conversion rates. By automating this data transfer, you're not just saving an admin 15 minutes per lead; you're giving your sales team a decisive time advantage and ensuring no lead falls through the cracks due to manual process errors.

How It Works

The automation acts as a smart, reliable courier between Typeform and Salesmachine, operating on a simple trigger-action principle.

Step 1: Monitoring for New Responses

The workflow is set to check your Typeform account at regular, configurable intervals (e.g., every 5 minutes). When it detects a new form submission, it immediately captures the entire response payload, including hidden fields and calculated scores.

Step 2: Parsing and Structuring the Data

It then extracts the specific information needed to create a Salesmachine contact. This typically includes the respondent's email address (as the unique identifier), first and last name, company, phone number, and any other custom fields you've mapped from your Typeform questions.

Step 3: Creating the Salesmachine Contact

Using the structured data, the automation calls the Salesmachine API to create a new contact. The contact is instantly added to your specified Salesmachine list or workspace, often with tags or custom attributes populated based on the Typeform answers, making the lead immediately actionable for sales outreach.

Pro tip: Enhance this workflow by adding a step to send a personalized "Thank You" email via your email service provider (like Gmail or SendGrid) immediately after the contact is created, improving the prospect's experience from the very first touchpoint.

What You'll Need

To set up this automated connection, you will need the following accounts and access:

  1. A Typeform account with an active form that collects contact information.
  2. A Salesmachine account with administrative or API access to create contacts.
  3. A Make.com account (free tier is sufficient to start).
  4. The API keys or OAuth credentials for both Typeform and Salesmachine (Make.com will guide you through connecting these securely).
  5. A clear mapping of which Typeform question fields correspond to which Salesmachine contact properties (e.g., "Email" field to "email").
