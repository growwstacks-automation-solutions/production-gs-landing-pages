What This Workflow Does

Every dollar spent on Google Ads is an investment in generating leads. But when those leads arrive via Lead Forms, the real challenge begins: manually copying data from the ads platform into a spreadsheet for your sales team. This process is not only tedious but fraught with risk—leads get missed, data is entered incorrectly, and response times slow down, costing you conversions.

This automation solves that problem completely. It acts as a digital bridge between your Google Ads account and your Microsoft Excel worksheet. Every time someone submits a lead form on your search, display, or YouTube ads, this workflow instantly captures their information—name, email, phone number, and any other custom fields—and adds it as a new, perfectly formatted row in your designated Excel file. How It Works

The workflow follows a simple, reliable sequence to transfer data from point A (Google Ads) to point B (Excel) without any human intervention.

Step 1: Trigger on New Lead

The automation is initiated the moment a new submission comes through your Google Ads Lead Form. Make.com watches your connected Google Ads account for this specific event, detecting it in near real-time.

Step 2: Extract Lead Data

The workflow parses the form submission, pulling out all the submitted fields. While it expects core information like Full Name, User Email, and User Phone, it can capture any additional questions you've added to your form, such as company name, job title, or specific inquiries.

Step 3: Format and Map to Excel

This is where the magic happens. The automation structures the raw lead data to match the column layout of your target Excel worksheet. It ensures each piece of information goes into the correct cell, maintaining consistency for easy reading and analysis.

Step 4: Add Row to Worksheet

Finally, the workflow connects to your Microsoft 365 Excel file (hosted on OneDrive or SharePoint) and inserts the newly formatted data as the next available row. It can also add timestamps, source identifiers, or campaign tags automatically, enriching your data set from the start.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column in your Excel sheet and configure the workflow to populate it with the exact submission time. This allows you to measure and improve your lead response time—a critical sales metric.

What You'll Need

To implement this workflow successfully, you'll need the following set up beforehand:

  1. A Google Ads account with Lead Forms enabled on your campaigns.
  2. A Make.com account (free or paid) to host and run the automation.
  3. A Microsoft 365 (Office 365) account with access to Excel Online and either OneDrive or SharePoint to store the destination worksheet.
  4. An Excel worksheet created with column headers for the data you wish to capture (e.g., "Timestamp", "Full Name", "Email", "Phone", "Campaign Name").
  5. The lead form should collect, at a minimum, the user's full name, email address, and phone number.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Get the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button above to clone the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Google Ads: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Google Ads account by logging in with OAuth.
