What This Workflow Does

Every dollar spent on Google Ads is an investment in generating leads. But if those leads get stuck in a spreadsheet, emailed to a salesperson, or forgotten in a notification inbox, that investment is wasted. The critical window for contacting a new lead is minutes, not hours. Manual processes create delays, errors, and lost opportunities.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between your advertising and your sales pipeline. The moment someone submits their information on your Google Ads Lead Form, this Make.com workflow springs into action. It takes the lead's name, email, and phone number, and uses it to create two essential records in Pipedrive: a Person (your new contact) and a Deal (the new sales opportunity). This happens instantly, automatically, and without any human intervention, ensuring your sales team has a hot, fully-formed lead to work on immediately.

How It Works

The workflow follows a simple, powerful sequence to transform a form submission into a sales-ready opportunity.

Step 1: Trigger from Google Ads

The workflow is triggered the instant a new submission comes in through your Google Ads Lead Form. Make.com watches for this event continuously, capturing all the form data fields you've configured.

Step 2: Create a Person in Pipedrive

Using the captured data, the workflow creates a new "Person" record in your Pipedrive CRM. It maps the form fields—like "Full Name" to the person's name, "User Email" to their email, and "User Phone" to their phone number—directly into the corresponding Pipedrive fields. This builds a complete contact profile from the outset.

Pro tip: Ensure your Google Ads Lead Form collects at least the three core fields: Full Name, Email, and Phone. This provides the minimum data needed to create a valuable CRM record. You can add custom questions to pre-qualify leads and populate even more Pipedrive fields automatically.

Step 3: Create a Linked Deal in Pipedrive

Immediately after creating the Person, the workflow creates a new "Deal" in Pipedrive. This deal is automatically linked to the newly created person. You can pre-configure the deal title (e.g., "Google Ads Lead: [Lead Name]"), set an initial pipeline stage (like "New Lead"), assign an owner, and even add a note containing the original form submission details for context.

Step 4: (Optional) Notify Your Team

While not in the base template, you can easily extend this workflow to send a notification via Slack, email, or SMS to your sales rep the moment the deal is created. This ensures immediate awareness and can dramatically shrink your lead response time.

To set up this workflow, you'll need the following accounts and information ready:

  1. A Make.com account (free tier available).
  2. An active Google Ads account with a Lead Form extension set up on your campaigns.
  3. A Pipedrive CRM account with admin or owner permissions to create API connections.
  4. The specific Field Names from your Google Ads Lead Form (e.g., "Full Name", "User Email").
  5. (Optional) Your Pipedrive Pipeline ID, Stage ID, and User ID for assigning the new deal.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" and clone the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Google Ads: In the first module, authenticate your Google Ads account and select the specific Lead Form you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Pipedrive: In the subsequent modules, authenticate your Pipedrive account. Make.com will need permission to create Persons and Deals.
  4. Map Your Data: Map the data from the Google Ads module (like formData.Full Name) to the corresponding fields in the Pipedrive "Create a Person" and "Create a Deal" modules.
  5. Test & Activate: Run a test by submitting your own Google Ads Lead Form. Check Pipedrive to confirm a new Person and Deal were created correctly. Then, turn the scenario on.
