What This Workflow Does

Running Google Ads is a powerful way to attract potential customers, but the real challenge begins after they click. When a user fills out your lead form, that valuable contact information often gets stuck in your Google Ads dashboard, requiring manual export and upload to your email marketing platform. This delay can cost you conversions, as leads cool off without immediate follow-up.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between your paid advertising and your email marketing. Every time a new submission comes through your Google Ads lead form, the workflow is triggered. It instantly takes the lead's data—starting with their essential email address—and either adds them as a new subscriber or updates their existing profile in your specified Mailchimp audience. This turns your ad spend into a live, growing email list without you lifting a finger.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a simple trigger-action principle, ensuring reliability and speed.

Step 1: The Trigger – A New Lead Form Submission

The automation starts when Make.com detects a new submission from your connected Google Ads lead form. This happens in real-time, so there's no polling delay.

Step 2: Data Parsing & Routing

The workflow receives the full submission data. It checks for the presence of a valid email address (the only mandatory field) and prepares the data package to be sent to Mailchimp.

Step 3: Subscriber Management in Mailchimp

Make.com sends an API call to your Mailchimp account. Using the lead's email as a unique identifier, it either creates a new subscriber in your chosen audience or updates an existing subscriber's information if they are already on the list. This prevents duplicates and keeps contact records fresh.

Pro tip: Extend this template by adding a step to send an instant "Thank You" or "Lead Magnet" email via Mailchimp's automation features. This immediate touchpoint can double your engagement rates.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the template into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Google Ads: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Google Ads account and select the specific lead form you want to monitor.
  3. Connect Mailchimp: In the Mailchimp module, authorize your account and select the target Audience ID where subscribers should be added.
  4. Map Your Fields: Match the data fields from your Google Ads form (e.g., `First Name`, `Phone`) to the corresponding merge tags in Mailchimp (e.g., `FNAME`, `PHONE`).
  5. Test & Activate: Turn on the scenario and submit a test lead through your form. Verify the subscriber appears correctly in your Mailchimp audience.
