Automatically Sync ActiveCampaign Contacts to Redtail CRM

Eliminate manual data entry and ensure your sales team instantly receives qualified leads from your marketing campaigns.

What This Workflow Does

For financial advisors, insurance agents, and sales teams using Redtail CRM, manually transferring new leads from marketing platforms like ActiveCampaign is a tedious, error-prone bottleneck. Valuable leads can sit in a marketing list for days before a salesperson sees them, causing missed opportunities and lengthening sales cycles.

This automated workflow solves that problem by creating a seamless bridge between your marketing automation and your CRM. It continuously monitors your ActiveCampaign account for new contacts or updated lead information and instantly creates or updates corresponding records in Redtail CRM. This ensures that every qualified lead is immediately actionable by your sales team, with all their marketing engagement history intact.

The result is a unified sales and marketing engine where data flows automatically. Marketing can nurture leads with confidence, knowing successful conversions are handed off instantly. Sales can trust that their CRM always contains the most current, complete prospect information, allowing for faster, more informed follow-up.

How It Works

The automation acts as a smart, real-time data courier between ActiveCampaign and Redtail. Here's the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Monitor for New Contacts

The workflow is triggered on a schedule (e.g., every 15 minutes) to check your ActiveCampaign account for any new contacts added since the last check. It can also be configured to watch specific lists or tags for highly targeted lead routing.

Step 2: Retrieve Contact Details

For each new contact found, the automation gathers all available data: name, email, phone number, company, custom fields, lead score, and even tags indicating their interests or campaign source. This creates a comprehensive profile for the sales team.

Step 3: Check for Duplicates in Redtail

Before creating a new record, the automation searches Redtail CRM using the contact's email address to see if they already exist. This prevents duplicate entries and maintains a clean, reliable database.

Step 4: Create or Update the CRM Record

If the contact is new, a full record is created in Redtail CRM, mapping all the relevant fields from ActiveCampaign. If the contact exists, the workflow can update their record with the latest information from ActiveCampaign, such as a higher lead score or new tags.

Step 5: Log the Activity & Handle Errors

Every sync attempt is logged. If an error occurs—like a missing required field—the workflow can notify an admin via email or Slack instead of failing silently, ensuring data integrity is maintained.

Pro tip: Enhance this workflow by adding a step to automatically assign the new Redtail contact to a specific sales rep or team based on the lead's geographic location, product interest (from ActiveCampaign tags), or lead score, creating a fully automated lead distribution system.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click "Get This Workflow" to copy the ready-made template into your Make.com account.
