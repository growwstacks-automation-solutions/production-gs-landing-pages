What This Workflow Does

For businesses using Teamleader for CRM and sales management alongside Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) for email marketing, a major operational headache is keeping contact lists synchronized. Sales teams add new leads and clients into Teamleader, but marketing campaigns run on a separate list in Brevo. Manually exporting and importing contacts is time-consuming, error-prone, and causes delays in engaging new leads.

This automated workflow solves that problem completely. It acts as a real-time bridge between your two critical systems. Whenever a sales representative creates a new contact record in Teamleader, this automation instantly detects the event and creates a corresponding, enriched contact in your Brevo account. This ensures your marketing team has immediate access to new leads for onboarding sequences, newsletters, or targeted campaigns, without a single manual step.

Pro tip: Ensure the email field is mandatory for new contacts in your Teamleader setup. This automation uses the email as the primary identifier, so having it populated is essential for a successful sync.

How It Works

The automation follows a simple but powerful "trigger-action" sequence on the Make.com platform, ensuring reliability and speed.

Step 1: The Trigger in Teamleader

The workflow is initiated by the "Watch Contacts" module in Teamleader. It constantly monitors your Teamleader account for the specific event of a new contact being created. The moment this happens, it captures the entire contact record—including name, email, company, phone number, and any custom fields or tags.

Step 2: Data Processing & Mapping

Make.com receives the contact data and prepares it for Brevo. This step often involves routing and filtering. For instance, you could add logic to only sync contacts tagged as "Marketing Qualified" or from a specific company. The data is then mapped from Teamleader's field structure to the expected field structure in Brevo.

Step 3: The Action in Brevo

The final module is the "Create a Contact" action in Brevo. Using the mapped data, it sends an API call to your Brevo account to create a new contact. You can configure it to add the contact to specific lists, update attributes, and assign tags based on the data from Teamleader, enabling immediate segmentation for marketing campaigns.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation live in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the Template: Click the "Get This Workflow" button to open the template on Make.com and create a copy in your own account.
  2. Connect Your Apps: In the Make.com scenario editor, click on the Teamleader module and authenticate your account using your API key. Repeat the process for the Brevo module.
  3. Test the Connection: Use the "Run once" function to test the trigger. Create a test contact in Teamleader and see if the workflow executes and creates the contact in Brevo.
  4. Activate the Scenario: Once testing is successful, schedule the scenario to run automatically (e.g., "immediately" or every few minutes) and turn it on. Your sync is now live.
