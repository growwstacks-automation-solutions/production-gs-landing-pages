Automatically Sync Cloze Contacts to ActiveCampaign

What This Workflow Does

For sales and marketing teams using Cloze for relationship management and ActiveCampaign for email marketing, a major pain point is data silos. When a sales rep updates a contact's information after a call in Cloze, that critical data often doesn't make it to the marketing team's lists in ActiveCampaign. This leads to missed email nurturing opportunities, outdated segmentation, and a disjointed customer experience.

This free Make.com template solves that problem by creating a real-time, one-way sync from Cloze to ActiveCampaign. It monitors the "person audit" log in Cloze—a record of any change to a contact—and automatically triggers the creation of a corresponding contact in your designated ActiveCampaign account. This ensures your marketing automation is always working with the freshest, most accurate data from your sales conversations.

How It Works

The automation acts as a reliable bridge between your two systems, operating silently in the background.

Step 1: Monitor Cloze for Changes

The workflow starts with a Cloze module set to watch for "Person Audit" events. This could be a new contact being added, an email address being updated, a job title change, or notes being logged after an interaction.

Step 2: Parse the Updated Contact Data

Make.com receives the webhook from Cloze containing all the details of the changed contact. The workflow parses this data, extracting key fields like name, email, company, phone number, and any custom fields or tags you want to transfer.

Pro tip: Use Cloze tags to control automation. For instance, tag a contact as "Marketing-Qualified" in Cloze, and set the workflow to only sync contacts with that tag, ensuring your ActiveCampaign lists are highly targeted.

Step 3: Create or Update the Contact in ActiveCampaign

The workflow then uses the ActiveCampaign module to search for an existing contact by email. If found, it can update the record with the new information from Cloze. If not found, it creates a brand new contact. You can also assign the contact to a specific list or add tags in ActiveCampaign based on the data from Cloze.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your integration running in under 15 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Clone the template: Click "Get This Workflow" and duplicate the scenario into your Make.com account.
  2. Connect Cloze: In the first module, authorize Make.com to access your Cloze account using your API credentials.
  3. Connect ActiveCampaign: In the ActiveCampaign module, input your account API URL and key (found in your ActiveCampaign settings).
  4. Map your fields: Review the router and ensure the data from Cloze (like `firstName`, `email`) is correctly mapped to the corresponding fields in ActiveCampaign.
  5. Test and activate: Turn on the scenario, then make a test change to a contact in Cloze. Check your ActiveCampaign account within a minute to confirm the new contact appears.
