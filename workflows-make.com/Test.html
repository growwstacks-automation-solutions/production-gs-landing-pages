What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and time-consuming process of manually tracking unpaid invoices and following up with customers. It automatically identifies overdue Stripe payments, sends reminder emails, and escalates chronic late payers to management - all while maintaining a complete audit trail in Google Sheets.

For businesses processing recurring payments through Stripe, late payments can disrupt cash flow and require significant staff time to chase. This workflow reduces collection workload by 60-80% while ensuring no overdue invoice slips through the cracks. The escalation feature helps identify problematic customers before their late payments become a serious financial burden.

Automated tracking of unpaid invoices with customer contact history

How It Works

1. Monitor Stripe for unpaid invoices

The workflow checks your Stripe account daily for invoices that have passed their due date without payment. It captures key details like customer name, invoice amount, days overdue, and payment history.

2. Send tiered reminder emails

First-time offenders receive a polite payment reminder. If payment isn't received within a set period (typically 3-7 days), a firmer follow-up is sent. All communications are logged in Google Sheets for reference.

3. Escalate repeat offenders

When a customer accumulates multiple unpaid invoices, the system automatically notifies your management team with all relevant details. This allows for timely decisions about payment plans, service restrictions, or other interventions.

Pro tip: Customize escalation thresholds based on customer value. For high-value clients, you might allow more late payments before escalating compared to smaller accounts.
