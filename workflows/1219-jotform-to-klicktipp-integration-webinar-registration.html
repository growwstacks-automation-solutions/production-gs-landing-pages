What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring webinar registrations from Jotform to your email marketing system. Instead of exporting CSVs and manually importing to KlickTipp, the workflow automatically:

  • Captures new Jotform webinar registrations in real-time
  • Validates and cleans contact data
  • Adds registrants to specific KlickTipp lists based on form responses
  • Triggers confirmation emails with calendar invites
  • Updates attendance records post-event

Marketing teams using this automation report saving 5-8 hours per webinar while improving registration-to-attendance conversion rates by 20-30% through timely, personalized follow-ups.

How It Works

1. Jotform Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your webinar registration form in Jotform. The system captures all form fields including custom questions, payment status (if applicable), and timestamp.

2. Data Validation

Before processing, the workflow verifies email validity, checks for duplicate registrations, and standardizes formatting (like phone numbers and job titles). Invalid entries are flagged for review.

3. KlickTipp Integration

Valid registrants are added to your specified KlickTipp list, with tags applied based on their form responses. The system can also:

  • Add registrants to specific autoresponder sequences
  • Assign lead scores based on engagement potential
  • Trigger immediate confirmation emails
KlickTipp dashboard showing automated webinar follow-up sequences

4. Post-Webinar Sync

After your webinar concludes, the workflow can update KlickTipp records with attendance status and send post-event surveys or recording links to attendees.

Pro tip: Add a "How did you hear about us?" field in Jotform to automatically tag registrants by lead source in KlickTipp for better campaign tracking.
