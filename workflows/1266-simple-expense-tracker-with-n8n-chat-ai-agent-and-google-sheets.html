What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common frustration of manually tracking business expenses in spreadsheets. Instead of opening your accounting software or spreadsheet every time you make a purchase, you can simply send a chat message like "Lunch $12 at Chipotle" or "Uber $25 to client meeting". The AI agent will automatically extract the amount, categorize the expense, and log it in your Google Sheets with proper formatting.

The system handles natural language inputs, understands context, and maintains a clean, organized expense record without manual data entry. It's particularly valuable for business owners, freelancers, and teams who need to track expenses but want to avoid the administrative overhead of traditional methods.

The AI interface for submitting expenses via chat message

How It Works

1. Message Submission

Users send expense details through a chat interface (like Slack, WhatsApp, or Telegram). The message can be in natural language format, such as "Coffee $4.50 with client at Starbucks" or "Parking $12 downtown".

2. AI Processing

The n8n AI agent analyzes the message to identify key components: amount spent, expense category, vendor name, and any relevant notes. It uses natural language processing to understand context even with informal phrasing.

3. Data Validation

The system checks for missing information and may ask follow-up questions if needed ("Was this a business meal?"). It also verifies numerical amounts and dates before proceeding.

4. Spreadsheet Logging

Validated expense data gets formatted and added to your Google Sheets expense tracker with proper columns for amount, date, category, vendor, and notes. The sheet can include monthly tabs and summary calculations.
