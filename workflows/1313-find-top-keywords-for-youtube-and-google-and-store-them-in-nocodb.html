What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the time-consuming process of keyword research for YouTube and Google search. It systematically discovers high-potential keywords based on your seed terms, analyzes their performance metrics, and stores the filtered results in a NocoDB database for easy access and analysis.

The automation eliminates hours of manual research by continuously monitoring keyword trends and updating your database with fresh, relevant suggestions. Content teams can focus on creation rather than research, with confidence they're targeting the most valuable keywords for their audience.

Example of keyword data organized in NocoDB for analysis

How It Works

1. Keyword Discovery

The workflow begins by taking your seed keywords and expanding them into hundreds of related terms using keyword suggestion APIs. It analyzes search volume, competition, and relevance metrics to identify the most promising candidates.

2. Performance Filtering

Each keyword is evaluated against customizable criteria like search volume thresholds, competition levels, and cost-per-click data. The system automatically filters out low-potential keywords, saving you from manual evaluation.

3. Database Storage

Qualified keywords are structured and stored in NocoDB with all relevant metrics. The database becomes a living resource that your team can query, sort, and analyze to inform content strategy decisions.
