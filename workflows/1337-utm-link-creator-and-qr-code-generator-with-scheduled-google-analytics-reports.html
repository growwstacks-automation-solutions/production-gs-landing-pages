What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves three critical marketing challenges in one automated system. First, it eliminates the tedious manual process of creating UTM-tagged URLs for campaign tracking. Second, it converts these tracking links into scannable QR codes for offline marketing materials. Finally, it automatically schedules and delivers Google Analytics reports showing how these tagged links perform.

Marketing teams waste countless hours building tracking URLs and compiling campaign reports. This workflow reduces that manual work by 80% while ensuring data consistency across all channels. The automated reporting means you'll never miss important performance trends because someone forgot to check analytics.

How It Works

1. UTM Link Generation

The workflow starts by accepting your base URL and campaign parameters (source, medium, campaign name, etc.). It constructs properly formatted UTM links following Google Analytics best practices, ensuring consistent tracking across all marketing efforts.

2. QR Code Creation

Each generated UTM link is automatically converted into a QR code image. The workflow can output these in multiple formats suitable for print materials, digital displays, or packaging. Dynamic QR codes can be created for time-sensitive campaigns.

3. Analytics Reporting

At scheduled intervals, the workflow queries Google Analytics for performance data on your UTM-tagged URLs. It compiles key metrics like clicks, conversions, bounce rates, and more into formatted reports delivered via email or saved to cloud storage.
