How It Works

1. Stock Data Collection

The workflow begins by fetching real-time or historical stock data from your preferred market data API. This includes price movements, volume data, and technical indicators that form the basis for analysis.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

The GPT agent processes the market data using predefined technical analysis parameters combined with natural language understanding. It identifies patterns, evaluates market conditions, and generates actionable insights in plain English.

3. Telegram Notification

Analysis results are formatted into clear, concise messages and sent to specified Telegram channels or private chats. Traders receive immediate alerts about potential opportunities or risk warnings.

4. Airtable Logging

Every analysis and signal is automatically recorded in Airtable with timestamps, creating a searchable database of trading opportunities and outcomes for future reference and performance analysis.
