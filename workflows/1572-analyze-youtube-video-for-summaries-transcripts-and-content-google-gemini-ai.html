Analyze YouTube Videos with AI for Summaries & Transcripts

Automatically extract key insights, generate summaries, and create searchable transcripts from any YouTube video using Google Gemini AI

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually analyzing YouTube videos by using AI to automatically generate summaries, transcripts, and content insights. Content creators, marketers, and educators often spend hours reviewing videos to extract key information - this workflow eliminates that manual work while providing more consistent and actionable results.

The system connects to the YouTube API to access video content, processes it through Google Gemini AI for natural language understanding, and outputs structured data including: video summaries, full transcripts, key topics identified, sentiment analysis, and suggested action items based on the content. This creates an instantly searchable knowledge base from your video content.

Sample output showing AI-generated video summary and transcript sections

How It Works

1. Video URL Input

The workflow starts by accepting a YouTube video URL or ID. This can be triggered manually for individual videos or automatically when new videos are published to a channel.

2. Metadata Extraction

The YouTube API retrieves the video's title, description, tags, and other metadata to provide context for the AI analysis.

3. Audio Processing

The system extracts and processes the audio track, converting speech to text with timestamped transcription.

Detailed view of the automated analysis process steps

4. AI Content Analysis

Google Gemini AI processes the transcript to generate: concise summary, key points list, content categorization, and sentiment analysis.

5. Output Delivery

The structured results are delivered to your preferred destination: Google Docs, Notion, Airtable, or your CMS for immediate use.

Pro tip: Use the timestamped transcript to create clickable chapter markers in your YouTube descriptions, improving viewer engagement and watch time.
