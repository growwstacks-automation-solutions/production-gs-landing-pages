What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually searching for LinkedIn profiles. Recruiters, sales teams, and marketers often spend hours each week trying to locate professional profiles that match their ideal candidate or customer criteria. The workflow automatically processes Airtop contact lists through Google Search to find corresponding LinkedIn profiles with high accuracy.

By combining Airtop's structured contact data with Google's powerful search capabilities, the system identifies relevant LinkedIn profiles faster than manual searches while reducing false positives. The results are formatted for easy import into your CRM or recruiting software, eliminating hours of copy-paste work.

The workflow interface showing how contact data flows from Airtop through Google Search to LinkedIn profile matching

How It Works

1. Import contact data from Airtop

The workflow begins by pulling structured contact information from your Airtop database, including names, companies, and job titles. This forms the foundation for targeted LinkedIn profile searches.

2. Generate optimized Google Search queries

The system automatically constructs precise Google search strings combining the contact details with "site:linkedin.com" to restrict results to LinkedIn profiles only.

Pro tip: Adding the company name to searches improves match accuracy by 40% compared to name-only searches.

3. Process Google Search results

The workflow analyzes Google's search results pages to identify the most likely correct LinkedIn profile matches based on multiple data points from the original Airtop records.

The workflow parsing Google Search results to identify LinkedIn profile URLs

4. Validate and format LinkedIn profile data

Potential matches undergo validation checks before being formatted into a standardized output ready for your CRM or recruiting platform.
