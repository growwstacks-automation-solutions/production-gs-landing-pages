What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the time-consuming process of researching companies and creating compelling narratives about them. It combines Bright Data's LinkedIn scraping capabilities with Google Gemini's AI to transform raw company data into engaging stories.

The system automatically extracts key information from LinkedIn company profiles - including mission statements, recent posts, employee testimonials, and growth metrics - then uses AI to craft professional narratives that highlight the company's unique value proposition, culture, and achievements.

Authentication setup for Bright Data and Google Gemini integration

How It Works

Step 1: LinkedIn Data Extraction

The workflow uses Bright Data's web scraping tools to securely pull structured data from LinkedIn company profiles. This includes information about company size, industry, recent posts, job listings, and employee insights.

Step 2: Data Structuring

Raw scraped data is organized into logical categories and cleaned to remove irrelevant information. The system identifies key patterns and metrics that will form the basis of the company story.

Example structured data output from LinkedIn scraping

Step 3: AI Story Generation

Google Gemini processes the structured data to generate multiple narrative versions. The AI considers different angles - investor-focused, customer-focused, or talent acquisition-focused - based on your requirements.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompt templates to emphasize specific aspects like company culture, innovation, or social impact depending on your use case.

Step 4: Output Delivery

The final company stories are delivered in your preferred format - whether as Google Docs, Notion pages, or directly into your CRM system. The workflow can run on schedule or be triggered manually.
