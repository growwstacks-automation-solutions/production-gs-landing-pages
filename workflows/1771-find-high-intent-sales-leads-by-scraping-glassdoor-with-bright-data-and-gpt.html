What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of identifying companies actively hiring for roles that indicate buying intent. Traditional lead generation often misses these signals, leaving sales teams chasing cold prospects. The workflow systematically scrapes Glassdoor job postings, analyzes them with GPT, and outputs qualified leads ready for outreach.

By focusing on companies posting jobs related to your solution area (like "Salesforce Developer" for CRM tools), you target accounts with validated needs. The AI layer extracts key buying signals from job descriptions, scoring leads based on technology mentions, team expansion plans, and other intent indicators.

Sample output showing enriched lead data extracted from job postings

How It Works

1. Configure target parameters

The workflow starts by defining your ideal customer profile parameters - job titles, locations, industries, and technology keywords to monitor. These filters ensure you only scrape relevant postings matching your solution.

2. Bright Data scraping

Using Bright Data's residential proxies, the workflow scrapes Glassdoor without triggering bot detection. It extracts job titles, descriptions, company details, and posting dates while maintaining compliance with Glassdoor's terms.

3. GPT analysis

Each scraped job posting gets processed by GPT to identify buying signals. The AI extracts technology stack mentions, team growth indicators, pain points, and even suggests outreach angles based on the job context.

GPT processing job descriptions to extract buying signals

4. Lead scoring & output

The system scores each company based on hiring volume, role seniority, and technology relevance. High-scoring leads get formatted with contact details and enrichment data, ready for CRM import or outreach campaigns.

Pro tip: Combine this with LinkedIn scraping to identify hiring managers for the roles you target, creating complete outreach packages.
