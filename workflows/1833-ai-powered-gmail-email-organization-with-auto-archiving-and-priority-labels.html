How It Works

1. Email Analysis

The workflow triggers when new emails arrive, analyzing content, sender, subject line, and your past interactions. It evaluates urgency, importance, and category using machine learning models trained on common email patterns.

2. Priority Scoring

Each email receives a priority score from 1-10 based on multiple factors. Urgent client requests might score 9-10, while newsletters score 1-2. You can adjust these thresholds in the workflow settings.

3. Automated Labeling

The system applies color-coded labels like "Urgent", "Follow Up", "Read Later", or custom categories you define. These make important messages visually distinguishable at a glance.

Priority labeling system in action with color-coded categories

4. Smart Archiving

Non-urgent emails (below your set priority threshold) are automatically archived, removing inbox clutter while remaining fully searchable. The system excludes important senders from auto-archiving based on your contacts list.

Pro tip: Start with conservative archiving thresholds and gradually increase them as you gain confidence in the system's accuracy.
