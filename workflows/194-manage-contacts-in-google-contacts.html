What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow template automates contact management in Google Contacts, eliminating manual data entry and reducing errors. It connects Google Contacts with other business applications to keep your address book synchronized and up-to-date automatically.

Whether you need to add new contacts from form submissions, update existing records from CRM systems, or categorize contacts based on specific criteria, this workflow handles it all. It's particularly valuable for businesses that manage large contact lists across multiple platforms.

Automated contact management saves hours of manual work each week

How It Works

1. Trigger Setup

The workflow begins by monitoring your specified trigger source, such as new form submissions, CRM updates, or scheduled syncs. This could be from platforms like Typeform, HubSpot, or Google Forms.

2. Data Processing

Incoming contact data is automatically formatted and validated according to your predefined rules. The workflow can standardize phone numbers, split names into first/last, and check for duplicates.

3. Google Contacts Integration

Processed contact information is then synchronized with Google Contacts. The workflow can create new contacts, update existing ones, or apply labels based on your business rules.

4. Confirmation & Logging

After each operation, the workflow logs the changes and can notify your team via email or Slack. This creates an audit trail and ensures transparency in your contact management process.
