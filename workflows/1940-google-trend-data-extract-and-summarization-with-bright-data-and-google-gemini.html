What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of extracting Google Trends data, processing it through Bright Data's powerful scraping capabilities, and generating AI-powered summaries using Google Gemini. It solves the time-consuming challenge of manual trend research by providing scheduled, automated reports with actionable insights.

Traditional market research methods require hours of manual data collection and analysis. This workflow delivers comprehensive trend reports in minutes, identifying emerging patterns, geographic variations, and related search queries that human analysts might miss. The AI summarization transforms raw data into executive-ready insights.

How It Works

1. Trend Data Extraction

The workflow begins by using Bright Data's advanced web scraping capabilities to extract Google Trends information for your specified keywords, locations, and time periods. This happens without violating Google's terms of service through proper API usage and ethical scraping practices.

2. Data Normalization

Raw trend data is processed and standardized into a consistent format. The workflow handles different data types (search volume, related queries, geographic distribution) and normalizes values for accurate comparison and analysis.

Transforming raw trend data into structured formats for analysis

3. AI Analysis with Google Gemini

Google Gemini receives the processed data and applies natural language processing to identify key patterns, significant changes, and meaningful insights. The AI generates concise summaries highlighting the most important findings in business-friendly language.

4. Report Generation

The final step compiles the raw data and AI insights into a formatted report, which can be delivered via email, saved to cloud storage, or sent to business intelligence platforms. The workflow includes options for different report formats based on your needs.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run weekly for continuous market intelligence without manual effort. Combine with competitor keyword tracking for comprehensive competitive analysis.
