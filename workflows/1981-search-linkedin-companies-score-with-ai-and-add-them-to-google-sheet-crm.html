What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually searching LinkedIn for potential clients or partners, evaluating their suitability, and adding them to your CRM. Sales teams and recruiters often waste hours each week on these repetitive tasks, risking inconsistent data entry and missed opportunities.

The workflow automatically searches LinkedIn based on your criteria (industry, company size, location etc.), uses AI to score each company's potential based on customizable factors, then adds qualified prospects directly to your Google Sheets CRM with all relevant details. This creates a systematic, scalable approach to lead generation.

AI-scored LinkedIn company data automatically populated in Google Sheets

How It Works

1. LinkedIn company search

The workflow starts by executing targeted searches on LinkedIn using your predefined parameters. You can specify industries, company sizes, locations, and other filters to focus on your ideal customer profile.

2. AI scoring and enrichment

Each company profile gets analyzed by AI that evaluates factors like growth signals, hiring activity, and engagement patterns. The system assigns a score (e.g. 1-100) indicating how strong a prospect they are based on your historical conversion data.

3. CRM integration

Qualified companies automatically flow into your Google Sheets CRM with all relevant fields populated - company name, size, industry, website, LinkedIn URL, AI score, and key decision-makers when available.
