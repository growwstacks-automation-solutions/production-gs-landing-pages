What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually sharing Medium articles to LinkedIn, which content creators and marketers often do multiple times weekly. The workflow automatically detects new Medium publications, formats them for optimal LinkedIn engagement, posts them at scheduled times, and sends Telegram confirmations.

By eliminating manual copying, pasting, and scheduling, this system saves professionals 3-5 hours per month while ensuring consistent content distribution. The Telegram alerts provide peace of mind that posts are going live as intended, without requiring constant platform checking.

Automatically formatted LinkedIn post with Medium article link and hashtags

How It Works

1. Medium article detection

The workflow checks your Medium RSS feed or profile for new publications at scheduled intervals (daily/weekly). It captures the article title, URL, publication date, and other metadata.

2. LinkedIn post creation

The system formats the Medium content into an engaging LinkedIn post template. This includes adding your custom introduction, relevant hashtags, and proper link formatting that complies with LinkedIn's algorithm preferences.

3. Automated posting

Using LinkedIn's API, the workflow publishes the post to your profile at optimal times for engagement. You can configure posting schedules and delays between publications to avoid overwhelming your network.

Instant Telegram notification confirming successful LinkedIn post

4. Telegram notifications

Immediately after posting, the system sends a Telegram message to your specified chat with the post link and status. This serves as both confirmation and an audit trail of your content distribution.

Pro tip: Add a 2-hour delay between publishing on Medium and sharing to LinkedIn to give the article time to be indexed by search engines.
