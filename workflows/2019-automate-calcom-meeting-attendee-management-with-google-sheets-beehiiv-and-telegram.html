What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual work involved in managing meeting attendees from Cal.com bookings. Instead of copying information between systems or missing important follow-ups, the workflow automatically captures every attendee detail, organizes it in Google Sheets, and triggers appropriate actions across your tools.

The system eliminates the risk of human error in data entry while saving 30-60 minutes per day for professionals who host frequent meetings. It creates a seamless flow from initial booking to post-meeting engagement without requiring any manual intervention from your team.

Automated attendee tracking in Google Sheets with custom columns for each data point

How It Works

1. Cal.com booking trigger

The workflow starts when someone books a meeting through your Cal.com link. It captures all submitted information including name, email, meeting time, and any custom questions answered during booking.

2. Google Sheets logging

Attendee details are automatically added to your designated Google Sheet with timestamps. The system creates a complete record including metadata like booking confirmation status and cancellation flags if applicable.

3. Telegram notifications

Your team receives instant Telegram alerts about new bookings with key details. Notifications can be customized to include only the most relevant information for quick reference before meetings.

Sample Telegram notification showing meeting details and attendee information

4. Beehiiv list management

Attendees are automatically added to your Beehiiv newsletter list with proper tagging based on meeting type. This enables targeted follow-up content that references the specific conversation or topic discussed.

Pro tip: Configure different Beehiiv segments for each meeting type to send hyper-relevant follow-up content that references the specific discussion topics.
