What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating consistent, engaging carousel content for TikTok and Instagram. The workflow uses GPT-Image-1 to generate themed image series complete with captions and hashtags, then formats them for each platform's specifications before publishing or scheduling.

Social media managers often spend hours creating carousels that maintain brand consistency while delivering value to followers. This automation handles the entire process from content ideation to publication, reducing a multi-hour task to minutes while improving content quality through AI-assisted creation.

Sample output showing AI-generated carousel images with consistent branding

How It Works

1. Content Brief Input

The workflow starts with your content brief - this could be product details, campaign themes, or educational topics. The system extracts key information to guide the AI content generation.

2. AI Image and Caption Generation

GPT-Image-1 creates a series of visually cohesive images along with engaging captions. The AI maintains consistent styling across all carousel slides while varying content appropriately.

3. Platform-Specific Formatting

The automation adjusts dimensions, aspect ratios, and file formats for each social platform's requirements, ensuring optimal display quality on both Instagram and TikTok.

Technical overview of the carousel generation and publishing process

4. Approval & Scheduling

Before publishing, the workflow can route content for human approval. Once approved, it schedules posts based on your optimal engagement times or immediate publication.

Pro tip: Use the approval step to maintain quality control while still saving 90% of the manual work. A quick visual check takes seconds compared to hours of creation.
