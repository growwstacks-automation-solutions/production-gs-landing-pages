What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the most time-consuming parts of newsletter creation by combining two powerful AI tools. It first uses Dumpling AI to crawl and extract content from specified websites, then processes that content through GPT-4o to generate concise, engaging summaries perfect for newsletter inclusion.

The automation solves the manual effort of visiting multiple sites, copying content, and rewriting summaries - a process that typically takes hours each week. By automating these steps, content creators can focus on strategy and audience engagement rather than repetitive content processing tasks.

Dumpling AI extracts clean article content from websites while preserving structure

How It Works

1. Website Crawling with Dumpling AI

The workflow starts by sending a list of URLs to Dumpling AI, which intelligently crawls each site to extract the main article content while ignoring ads, navigation, and other page elements. This ensures you get clean, structured content ready for summarization.

2. Content Processing

Extracted articles are then formatted and prepared for AI analysis. The workflow can filter content by length, remove duplicate articles, and apply basic preprocessing to improve summarization quality.

3. AI Summarization with GPT-4o

GPT-4o analyzes each article and generates a concise summary tailored for newsletter readers. You can customize the summary length, tone, and style to match your brand voice and audience preferences.

GPT-4o transforms raw articles into polished newsletter-ready summaries

4. Output Formatting

The final step formats the summarized content into your preferred newsletter template, complete with proper headings, formatting, and any additional branding elements you specify.
