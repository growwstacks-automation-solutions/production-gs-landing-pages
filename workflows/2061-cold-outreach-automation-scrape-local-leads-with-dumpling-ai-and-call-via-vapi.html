What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of finding and contacting local business leads. Traditional cold outreach requires hours of manual research and calling, often with low conversion rates. The workflow combines Dumpling AI's powerful web scraping with Vapi's conversational AI to automate the entire process from lead discovery to initial contact.

Sales teams using this template typically see 5-10x more conversations per day while maintaining personalization. How It Works

1. Targeted Lead Scraping

Dumpling AI scans local directories and business listings based on your ideal customer profile. It extracts business names, phone numbers, websites, and other key details while filtering out irrelevant matches.

2. Contact Data Enrichment

The workflow automatically verifies phone numbers, identifies decision-makers, and adds social media profiles to create complete contact records. This ensures your outreach targets the right people with accurate information.

3. AI-Powered Calling

Vapi's voice AI initiates personalized calls using natural conversation flows. The AI handles initial introductions, basic qualification questions, and schedules callbacks for your human team when appropriate.
