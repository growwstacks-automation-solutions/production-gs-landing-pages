What This Workflow Does

This automation system solves the time-consuming process of manually curating and distributing job opportunity newsletters. Recruitment agencies, job boards, and career services typically spend 10-15 hours weekly compiling listings from multiple sources, formatting content, and sending emails - this workflow automates 90% of that process.

The system aggregates job postings from Bolt.new via RapidAPI, processes them through ChatGPT for enhanced descriptions and categorization, then formats and delivers professional newsletters through Mails.so. It handles the entire pipeline from data collection to distribution with customizable filters, scheduling, and content templates.

Visual overview of the automated job newsletter workflow architecture

How It Works

Step 1: Job Data Collection

The workflow begins by pulling fresh job listings from Bolt.new's API via RapidAPI connection. You can configure search parameters like location, job categories, experience level, and keywords to target relevant opportunities.

Step 2: Content Enhancement

Raw job descriptions pass through ChatGPT for summarization, keyword extraction, and readability improvements. The AI adds consistent formatting, highlights key requirements, and can generate thematic groupings like "Top Remote Developer Jobs".

AI-enhanced job descriptions improve engagement and readability

Step 3: Newsletter Assembly

The system compiles processed jobs into HTML email templates with configurable layouts. You can include your branding, introductory content, and custom sections. The workflow automatically handles image optimization and responsive design.

Step 4: Targeted Distribution

Final newsletters deploy through Mails.so's API to your subscriber lists. Advanced setups can segment audiences by job preferences or create multiple newsletter variants from the same job data pool.

Pro tip: Add a manual approval step before sending to review curated content when first implementing this workflow.
