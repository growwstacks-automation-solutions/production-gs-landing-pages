What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually repurposing YouTube content across multiple social platforms. Every time you upload a new YouTube video, the workflow automatically creates optimized Facebook and Instagram posts complete with AI-generated captions tailored to each platform's audience.

Content creators waste hours each week reformatting videos, writing platform-specific descriptions, and managing separate posting schedules. How It Works

1. YouTube Video Detection

The workflow monitors your YouTube channel for new uploads using webhooks. As soon as a video goes live, it captures all metadata including title, description, and tags.

2. AI Caption Generation

An AI service analyzes the video's audio track to generate accurate captions. The system automatically adjusts caption style - creating concise versions for Instagram and more detailed versions for Facebook.

3. Platform-Specific Formatting

The video gets reformatted to each platform's optimal specifications. Landscape YouTube videos become square/vertical for Instagram while maintaining 1080p quality. Thumbnails are auto-cropped to platform dimensions.

Technical diagram of the automated video repurposing process

4. Scheduled Publishing

Posts are queued for optimal times on each platform based on your audience's activity patterns. The system handles all scheduling conflicts and platform-specific posting rules automatically.
