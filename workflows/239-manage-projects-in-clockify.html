What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates project management in Clockify, eliminating manual data entry and synchronization across your business tools. It solves the common problem of disjointed time tracking where employees waste hours each week manually logging activities across multiple systems.

The template creates a seamless connection between Clockify and your project management tools, automatically creating projects, assigning team members, and syncing task statuses. This ensures accurate time tracking while reducing administrative overhead by up to 80% for teams managing multiple client projects.

Automated project sync eliminates manual data entry between systems

How It Works

1. Project Creation Trigger

The workflow monitors your project management system for new projects. When detected, it automatically creates a corresponding project in Clockify with matching client, team, and budget parameters.

2. Team Member Assignment

Team members are automatically added to Clockify projects based on their assignments in your project management tool. The system handles permissions and access levels according to your predefined rules.

3. Time Entry Synchronization

Time logged in Clockify is automatically categorized by project and task, then synced back to your project management system. This creates a closed-loop system where all time data remains consistent across platforms.
