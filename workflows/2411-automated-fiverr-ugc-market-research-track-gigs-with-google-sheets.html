What This Workflow Does

This cutting-edge automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching Fiverr's User-Generated Content (UGC) marketplace. It continuously monitors thousands of gigs across categories, capturing critical data points like creator pricing, delivery times, ratings trends, and portfolio samples. The system automatically updates a centralized Google Sheets database, giving you real-time visibility into the UGC creator landscape.

Marketing teams waste countless hours checking Fiverr manually, often missing emerging talent or price fluctuations. This workflow eliminates that inefficiency while providing structured data you can analyze for strategic insights. It's particularly valuable for agencies managing large creator networks or brands scaling UGC production.

How It Works

Step 1: Configure Your Search Parameters

The workflow starts by authenticating with Fiverr's API and your Google Sheets account. You define search criteria like category filters, price ranges, and minimum ratings. These parameters can be adjusted anytime as your research needs evolve.

Step 2: Automated Data Collection

The system scrapes Fiverr gig pages on your schedule (daily/weekly), extracting 20+ data points per creator including response rate, revision policies, and portfolio samples. Advanced error handling ensures data quality even if Fiverr's interface changes.

Step 3: Structured Google Sheets Output

All data lands in a pre-formatted Google Sheet with separate tabs for raw data, trend analysis, and creator shortlists. Conditional formatting highlights standout performers and emerging opportunities.

Pro tip: Create a separate sheet tab to track creators you've contacted, with columns for response status and follow-up dates.
