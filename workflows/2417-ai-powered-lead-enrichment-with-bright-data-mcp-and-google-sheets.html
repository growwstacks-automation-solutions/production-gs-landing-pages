What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually researching and enriching lead data. Many sales teams waste hours looking up company details, technographics, and intent signals for each lead. Our workflow automatically pulls AI-powered consumerrofiles from Bright Data's MCP (Machine Learning Consumer Profiles) platform and organizes the enriched data in Google Sheets.

The system can process hundreds of leads per hour,appending valuable firmographic, behavioral, and predictive scoring data to your CRM or marketing automation platform. This enables your team to prioritize leads based on actual fit rather than just basic contact information.

Example of enriched lead data in HubSpot

How It Works

1. Lead Data Collection

The workflow starts by ingesting lead information from your CRM, marketing automation platform, or spreadsheet. This typically includes basic contact details like name, email, company name, and job title.

2. AI-Powered Enrichment

Each lead is processed through Bright Data's MCP API which returns enriched data including company size, technographics, funding status, intent signals, and predicted buyer readiness scores.

3. Data Organization

The workflow structures the enriched data into standardized fields Google Sheets, making it easy to analyze and segment leads. You can configure which data points are most valuable for your sales process.

Pro tip: Combine this with your existing lead scoring system to create a powerful prioritization matrix that surfaces the hottest leads.

  1. Download and import the JSON workflow into your n8n instance
