What This Workflow Does

This automation creates a fully functional AI chatbot for your website that connects to a Langflow backend for intelligent responses. Unlike generic chatbot solutions, this workflow allows complete customization of the chatbot's appearance, tone, and knowledge base to match your brand identity.

The system handles incoming customer queries in real-time, routes them through your Langflow AI pipeline, and delivers branded responses. It can integrate with your knowledge base, CRM, and support systems to provide accurate, context-aware answers while collecting valuable customer interaction data.

Example Langflow interface for building custom chatbot logic

How It Works

1. Chat Interface Setup

The workflow begins with a customizable chat widget embedded on your website. Visitors interact with this interface, which captures their queries and sends them to the n8n workflow.

2. Query Processing

Incoming messages are processed to extract intent and context. The workflow checks for previous interactions with the user and retrieves relevant information from connected systems.

3. Langflow Integration

The processed query is sent to your Langflow backend, where your custom AI logic generates an appropriate response. Langflow can access your knowledge base, product data, and other resources to craft accurate answers.

Sample conversation showing branded chatbot responses

4. Response Delivery

The AI-generated response is formatted with your brand voice guidelines and delivered back to the user through the chat interface. The system logs the complete interaction for analytics and training.

Pro tip: Train your chatbot with real customer service transcripts to ensure it learns your brand's communication style and common query patterns.
