What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of finding and vetting TikTok influencers for marketing campaigns. Traditional influencer research involves manually browsing profiles, analyzing engagement metrics, and crafting personalized outreach emails - a process that can take hours per campaign.

The workflow combines Bright Data's web scraping capabilities with Claude AI's natural language processing to automatically discover relevant TikTok creators, evaluate their audience and content quality, then initiate personalized email outreach. It transforms what would normally be a days-long manual process into an automated system that delivers qualified leads in minutes.

Example of AI-generated influencer analysis showing key metrics and scoring

How It Works

1. TikTok profile discovery with Bright Data

The workflow begins by scraping TikTok search results or hashtag pages using Bright Data's web scraping API. It collects profiles matching your target criteria (niche, follower range, location, etc.) while handling proxies and CAPTCHAs automatically.

2. AI-powered influencer evaluation

Each discovered profile gets analyzed by Claude AI, which evaluates content quality, audience demographics, engagement patterns, and brand fit. The AI generates a suitability score and detailed notes about why the influencer might be a good (or poor) match for your campaign.

Pro tip: Configure Claude's evaluation criteria to match your brand values - for example, prioritizing educational content over entertainment if you're in the B2B space.

3. Automated personalized outreach

The system crafts customized outreach emails referencing specific content pieces and insights from the AI analysis. It tracks email opens and clicks to identify engaged prospects, automatically following up with non-responders after a set period.
