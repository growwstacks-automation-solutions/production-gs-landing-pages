What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the fundamental challenge freelancers face on Upwork: connecting directly with clients outside the platform's restrictive messaging system. The workflow monitors new Upwork job postings in your niche, extracts key decision-maker information, and uses LinkedIn scraping combined with AI to find verified email addresses.

By automating the most time-consuming parts of lead research, you can focus on crafting personalized outreach instead of manual data gathering. The system organizes all extracted contacts into a structured database with job details, company information, and communication history - transforming scattered opportunities into a manageable sales pipeline.

The workflow connects Upwork job data with LinkedIn profiles to identify decision-makers

How It Works

1. Upwork job monitoring

The workflow continuously scans Upwork for new job postings matching your filters (skills, budget, location). It extracts the job description, client name, and company details while ignoring spammy or irrelevant posts.

2. LinkedIn profile matching

Using the client name and company from Upwork, the system searches LinkedIn to find the most likely profile of the hiring manager or project owner. AI helps verify the match by comparing job history and company connections.

3. Email discovery & validation

The workflow analyzes the LinkedIn profile along with company domain patterns to generate probable email addresses. It then verifies these through multiple validation checks before marking them as confirmed contacts.

Multi-step validation ensures high email accuracy before outreach

4. CRM organization

All validated leads are automatically organized in your preferred system (Google Sheets, Airtable, or CRM) with job details, contact info, and outreach status. The workflow can trigger follow-up sequences based on response patterns.
