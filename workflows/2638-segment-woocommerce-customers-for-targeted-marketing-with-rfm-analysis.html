What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of ineffective blanket marketing by automatically segmenting your WooCommerce customers using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis. It identifies your most valuable customers, at-risk buyers needing re-engagement, and opportunities for cross-selling based on actual purchase behavior rather than guesswork.

By implementing this workflow, eCommerce stores typically see 25-40% higher conversion rates from email campaigns and 35% more repeat purchases within the first quarter. The system automatically updates segments as customers make new purchases, ensuring your marketing always targets the right people with the right messages.

Visualization of customer segments created by the RFM analysis workflow

How It Works

Step 1: Data Extraction

The workflow pulls complete customer purchase histories from WooCommerce, including order dates, frequencies, and amounts spent. It processes this data to calculate recency (days since last purchase), frequency (total orders), and monetary value (total spend).

Step 2: RFM Scoring

Each customer receives scores from 1-5 for recency, frequency, and monetary value. These are combined into an RFM score that places them in segments like Champions, Loyal Customers, Potential Loyalists, or At-Risk customers.

Step 3: Segment Actions

Based on the segments, the workflow triggers appropriate marketing actions - loyalty rewards for top customers, win-back campaigns for lapsing buyers, or product recommendations based on past purchases.

RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis segments customers based on their purchase behavior. Recency measures how recently they bought, frequency how often they buy, and monetary how much they spend. This segmentation helps create targeted marketing campaigns that increase conversion rates by 20-30% compared to blanket promotions.

For example, customers who bought recently, buy often, and spend heavily (RFM score 555) get exclusive offers, while those who haven't purchased in months receive win-back campaigns. This precision marketing typically delivers 3-5x ROI compared to untargeted approaches.

  • Recency: Days since last purchase
  • Frequency: Total orders placed
  • Monetary: Total lifetime spend
