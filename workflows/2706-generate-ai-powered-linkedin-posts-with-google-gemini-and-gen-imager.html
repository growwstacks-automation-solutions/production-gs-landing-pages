What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered workflow solves the challenge of consistently creating engaging LinkedIn content. It automatically generates professional posts based on your input topics, complete with AI-crafted text and complementary images. The system leverages Google Gemini for intelligent post creation and Gen-Imager for relevant visuals.

Content creators and marketers spend hours weekly crafting LinkedIn posts. This automation reduces that time to minutes while maintaining quality and brand voice. The workflow produces draft content ready for review or can be configured for direct publishing with human oversight.

How It Works

1. Topic Input

The workflow begins when you submit a topic or keyword via form, spreadsheet, or API. This triggers the content generation process.

2. AI Content Generation

Google Gemini analyzes the topic and generates multiple post variations with professional tone, relevant hashtags, and engagement hooks.

3. Image Creation

Gen-Imager produces complementary visuals based on the post content, ensuring brand-appropriate imagery.

4. Approval & Publishing

The generated content can be routed for human review or scheduled directly to LinkedIn based on your preferences.
