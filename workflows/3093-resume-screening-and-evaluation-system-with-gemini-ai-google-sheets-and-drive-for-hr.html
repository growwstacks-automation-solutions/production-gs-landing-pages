What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered resume screening system transforms your hiring process by automating the initial candidate evaluation stage. When applicants submit resumes through your web form, the workflow automatically processes each document using Gemini AI to extract key qualifications, skills, and experience.

The system evaluates candidates against your job requirements, scoring each applicant based on predefined criteria. Results are organized in Google Sheets with clear rankings, while original resumes are securely stored in Google Drive. HR teams get instant access to pre-screened candidate lists, reducing manual review time by 70-80%.

AI-powered dashboard showing candidate evaluation results with skills matching and scoring

How It Works

1. Candidate Submission

Applicants submit their resumes through your existing web form or job portal. The system accepts various file formats including PDF, DOCX, and text files.

2. AI Processing

Gemini AI analyzes each resume, extracting key information like work history, education, skills, and certifications. The AI compares these against your job requirements to generate suitability scores.

3. Data Organization

Candidate information and evaluation results are automatically organized in Google Sheets, with resumes stored in designated Google Drive folders. The system creates a searchable database of all applicants.

Automatically generated candidate tracking sheet with AI evaluation metrics

4. HR Notification

HR teams receive notifications about new applicants with direct links to top-ranked candidates. The system can be configured to send different alert levels based on candidate scores.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets and Drive accounts
  3. Configure your Gemini API credentials
  4. Set up your evaluation criteria in the workflow settings
  5. Connect your web form or application portal
  6. Test with sample resumes and adjust scoring as needed

Pro tip: Start with broad evaluation criteria, then refine based on the candidates your team actually interviews and hires. The AI will learn from these patterns over time.
