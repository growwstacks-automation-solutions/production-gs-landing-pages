What This Workflow Does

This automation streamlines the process of responding to spare parts inquiries by automatically generating accurate price quotes automatically. When customers email asking about parts availability and pricing, the system extracts key details, checks your inventory database (Google Sheets), generates professional responses using AI, and sends professional quotes without manual intervention.

The workflow solves three major pain points: eliminating repetitive manual quote preparation, reducing response delays, and human errors in pricing calculations. Businesses using this system typically reduce quote turnaround time from hours to minutes while maintaining consistent, professional communication.

Automated email processing and response generation

How It Works

1. Email trigger

The workflow monitors your designated Gmail inbox for incoming spare parts inquiries. It identifies relevant emails based on subject lines or keywords like "quote request" or "price quote".

2. Data extraction

Gemini AI analyzes the email content to extract key details: part numbers, quantities, customer contact information, and any special requirements. This replaces manual data entry with AI-powered accuracy.

3. Price lookup

The system cross-references extracted part numbers against your Google Sheets inventory database to retrieve current pricing, availability, and lead times. It can handle complex pricing rules like quantity discounts.

Automated price lookup from inventory database

4. Quote generation

Gemini AI composes professional response emails using your brand voice and templates. It includes all relevant pricing, availability details, and optional upsell suggestions based on compatible accessories.

5. Email delivery

The completed quote is automatically sent from your business email address with proper formatting and attachments if needed. The system logs all interactions in your CRM or tracking sheet.
