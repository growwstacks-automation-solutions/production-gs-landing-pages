What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of email overload by automatically analyzing and categorizing incoming Gmail messages using GPT-4o mini's advanced AI capabilities. Instead of manually sorting through dozens or hundreds of emails daily, this workflow intelligently labels each message based on its content, sender, and context.

The system can distinguish between different types of emails like customer inquiries, internal communications, newsletters, invoices, and more. It then applies appropriate Gmail labels and can optionally route messages to specific folders or trigger follow-up actions based on the classification.

AI-powered email categorization in action showing different labels applied

How It Works

1. New email detection

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages using n8n's Gmail trigger. When a new email arrives, it captures the full message content including subject, body, sender, and attachments.

2. AI analysis with GPT-4o mini

The email content is sent to GPT-4o mini which analyzes the text and determines the most appropriate category based on your predefined classification rules. The AI considers context, intent, and relationships between words to make accurate classifications.

3. Label application

Based on the AI's classification, the workflow applies the corresponding Gmail label to the message. You can customize these labels to match your specific workflow needs (e.g., "Urgent", "Follow-up", "Newsletter", "Receipt").
