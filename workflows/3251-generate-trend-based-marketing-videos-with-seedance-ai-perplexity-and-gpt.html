What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of creating trend-based marketing videos by combining three powerful AI tools. It solves the challenge of producing timely, engaging video content at scale by automatically identifying viral topics, generating optimized scripts, and creating professional videos without manual intervention.

Marketing teams struggle to keep up with trending topics while maintaining production quality. This workflow bridges that gap by analyzing real-time trends through Perplexity AI, crafting compelling narratives with GPT, and transforming them into polished videos using Seedance AI's specialized video generation capabilities.

How It Works

1. Trend Identification

The workflow begins by querying Perplexity AI to identify currently trending topics in your industry. It analyzes search patterns, social mentions, and news trends to surface the most relevant subjects for your audience.

2. Content Research

For each identified trend, the system gathers supporting data points, statistics, and contextual information. This ensures your video content is both timely and substantiated with credible information.

3. Script Generation

GPT processes the research data to create optimized video scripts. The AI considers your brand voice guidelines, optimal video length for the platform, and proven engagement techniques like hooks and CTAs.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to include 2-3 trending hashtags in each script to boost discoverability on social platforms.

4. Video Production

Seedance AI transforms the script into a complete marketing video. It selects appropriate visuals, adds motion graphics, incorporates your branding, and optimizes pacing for maximum engagement.

5. Distribution

The final videos are automatically saved to your designated storage (Google Drive, Dropbox) and can be scheduled for publication through connected social platforms.
