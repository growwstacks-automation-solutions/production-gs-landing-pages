What This Workflow Does

Vendor due diligence is a critical but time-consuming process that typically involves manually researching financial records, legal histories, reputation analysis, and compliance documentation. This n8n workflow automates 80% of the vendor screening process by leveraging Gemini AI for document analysis and Jina AI for semantic search across multiple data sources.

The system automatically gathers and analyzes vendor information from public records, news sources, financial databases, and compliance portals. It generates a comprehensive risk assessment report with red flags highlighted, saving procurement teams 15+ hours per vendor evaluation while improving consistency and reducing human error.

The workflow interface showing vendor data collection and analysis steps

How It Works

1. Vendor Information Collection

The workflow starts by collecting vendor details from your CRM, procurement system, or manual input. It then queries multiple data sources including business registries, financial databases, and news aggregators to gather comprehensive background information.

2. AI-Powered Document Analysis

Gemini AI processes collected documents (financial statements, contracts, compliance certificates) to extract key risk indicators, financial health metrics, and contractual obligations. It flags anomalies and potential concerns for human review.

3. Semantic Search & Risk Scoring

Jina AI performs semantic search across collected data to identify connections, negative news mentions, and regulatory violations. The system assigns a risk score based on predefined criteria and compliance requirements.

4. Report Generation & Alerts

All findings are compiled into a standardized due diligence report with executive summary, risk heatmap, and recommendation. Critical issues trigger immediate alerts to designated stakeholders.
