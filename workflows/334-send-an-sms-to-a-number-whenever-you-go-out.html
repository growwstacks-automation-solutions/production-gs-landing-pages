What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates SMS notifications whenever you leave a predefined location, eliminating manual check-ins and improving communication efficiency. It's particularly valuable for professionals who need to notify clients, family members, or team members when they're en route.

The system integrates location data from your mobile device with SMS messaging services, creating a seamless automated notification system. Whether you're a field service technician, delivery driver, or caregiver, this automation ensures timely updates without requiring any manual action.

Example of location-based trigger settings in the workflow

How It Works

1. Location Detection

The workflow monitors your device's location using geofencing technology. When you exit a predefined area (like your office or home), it triggers the automation sequence.

2. SMS Trigger

Upon detecting your departure, the system prepares a customized SMS message with relevant details. You can include dynamic information like timestamp, your current location, or estimated arrival time.

3. Message Delivery

The workflow connects to your preferred SMS gateway (like Twilio or Plivo) to send the notification instantly. Recipients receive the message within seconds of your departure.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Pushcut iOS app or alternative location trigger
  3. SMS API account (Twilio, Plivo, etc.)
  4. Mobile device with location services enabled
  5. Recipient phone number(s) to receive alerts

Quick Setup Guide

  5. Test the workflow by leaving your defined location area
