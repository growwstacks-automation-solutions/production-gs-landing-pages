What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation template solves the challenge of systematically collecting and organizing customer feedback for restaurants. Instead of relying on manual methods like comment cards or verbal feedback that often gets lost, this workflow automatically captures customer responses from email forms and stores them in Google Sheets for easy analysis.

The system helps restaurant owners and managers track customer satisfaction over time, identify recurring issues, and make data-driven decisions to improve service quality. By automating the collection and storage process, you ensure no valuable feedback slips through the cracks while saving hours of manual data entry each week.

Sample customer feedback form that triggers the automated workflow

How It Works

1. Customer submits feedback form

The workflow triggers when a customer submits feedback through your email form or website contact form. The system captures all response data including ratings, comments, and contact information.

2. Data validation and processing

The automation checks for complete responses, validates email addresses, and formats the data for consistent storage. This ensures your feedback database remains clean and analysis-ready.

3. Automatic storage in Google Sheets

Validated feedback gets logged in your designated Google Sheet with timestamps. The system creates a structured record with columns for each data point (food rating, service rating, comments, etc.).

Automatically populated Google Sheet with customer feedback data

4. Optional notification alerts

The workflow can be configured to send instant alerts to managers when negative feedback is received, enabling quick response to customer concerns before they escalate.
