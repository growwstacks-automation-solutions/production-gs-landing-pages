What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of sales teams wasting time manually researching companies mentioned in Slack conversations. When team members share potential leads in designated Slack channels, the workflow automatically extracts company names or domains and enriches them with detailed business intelligence.

The enriched data appears directly in the Slack thread as a reply, giving everyone immediate context without leaving the conversation. This eliminates the need for sales reps to switch between multiple tools to research companies, saving valuable time while improving the quality of prospect information.

Example of how enriched lead data appears directly in Slack

How It Works

1. Slack message monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors specified Slack channels for new messages containing company names or domains. It uses natural language processing to identify potential leads even when they're mentioned casually in conversation.

2. Company data extraction

When a potential lead is detected, the system extracts the clearest identifier (company name, website domain, or LinkedIn URL) and prepares it for enrichment. Fuzzy matching ensures accurate identification even with typos or partial names.

3. Extruct AI enrichment

The extracted identifier is sent to Extruct AI's enrichment API, which returns comprehensive company data including funding history, employee count, technologies used, and recent news mentions. The AI also provides buying signals when available.

Extruct AI provides detailed company intelligence from multiple data sources

4. Slack thread reply

The workflow formats the enriched data into a clean Slack message and posts it as a reply in the original thread. The reply includes actionable insights and direct links to the full company profiles for deeper research when needed.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to only enrich messages with specific emoji reactions or hashtags to avoid processing irrelevant conversations.
