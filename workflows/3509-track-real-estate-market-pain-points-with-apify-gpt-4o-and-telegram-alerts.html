What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for real estate professionals: staying ahead of market trends and understanding buyer/seller pain points in real-time. Traditional market research methods are time-consuming and quickly become outdated.

The workflow automatically scrapes Google for real estate-related search queries using Apify, analyzes these queries with GPT-4o to identify common pain points and emerging trends, then delivers actionable insights directly to your Telegram channel. It runs daily to ensure you always have fresh market intelligence.

AI analysis of real estate market search queries showing common pain points

How It Works

1. Automated Google query scraping

Apify scrapes Google for real estate-related search queries based on your configured keywords. It captures both the search terms and the organic results that appear.

2. AI-powered pain point analysis

GPT-4o processes the scraped data to identify patterns, categorize pain points, and highlight emerging trends. The AI provides context around why certain queries are trending.

3. Telegram alert delivery

The analyzed insights are formatted into digestible reports and sent to your Telegram channel. You receive both summarized findings and raw data for deeper analysis.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for real estate agents, brokers, developers, and investors who need to stay ahead of market shifts. Marketing teams at real estate firms will find it invaluable for content strategy and lead generation. Property tech startups can use it to identify product opportunities.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Apify account with API access
  3. OpenAI API key for GPT-4o access
  4. Telegram bot token and channel ID
  5. List of real estate keywords to monitor

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Apify, OpenAI, and Telegram credentials
  3. Configure your target keywords in the Apify scraper
  4. Set your preferred Telegram channel for alerts
  5. Adjust the schedule (default is daily at 9 AM)
  6. Test and activate the workflow

Pro tip: Start with broad real estate terms (like "home buying problems") then refine based on initial findings. The AI can help identify more specific pain points to monitor.
