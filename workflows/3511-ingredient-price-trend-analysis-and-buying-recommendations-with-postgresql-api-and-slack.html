What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves a critical challenge for food manufacturers, restaurants, and ingredient purchasers: optimizing procurement costs by tracking price fluctuations. Manual price monitoring is time-consuming and often misses optimal buying opportunities.

The system connects to your ingredient price data sources (whether from APIs, spreadsheets, or manual inputs), analyzes historical trends in PostgreSQL, and delivers actionable buying recommendations directly to your Slack channel. It helps you purchase at the most favorable prices while avoiding shortages.

Visualization of historical price trends for key ingredients

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls current ingredient prices from your configured APIs or imports manually entered pricing data. It handles multiple data formats and normalizes them for analysis.

2. Historical Analysis

Each new price point gets stored in PostgreSQL alongside historical data. The system calculates moving averages, identifies seasonal patterns, and detects significant price deviations.

3. Recommendation Engine

Based on the analysis, the workflow generates purchase recommendations: "Buy now", "Wait for better price", or "Stock up". It considers your inventory levels and consumption rates.

Sample Slack alert with clear purchase recommendations

4. Slack Notifications

Actionable alerts are sent to designated Slack channels, complete with visual trend graphs and clear recommendations. Team members can discuss and act immediately.

Pro tip: Configure different Slack channels for different ingredient categories (proteins, grains, spices) to streamline decision-making.
