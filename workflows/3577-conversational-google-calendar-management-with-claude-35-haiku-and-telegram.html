What This Workflow Does

This innovative workflow transforms how you manage your Google Calendar by creating an AI-powered Telegram bot that understands natural language commands. Instead of manually clicking through calendar interfaces, you can simply message the bot with requests like "Schedule a 30-minute coffee chat with Alex next Tuesday afternoon" or "What does my week look like?"

The bot leverages Claude 3.5 Haiku's advanced language understanding to interpret your requests, check your calendar availability, and make appropriate scheduling decisions. It handles complex scenarios like rescheduling conflicts, setting up recurring meetings, and even suggesting optimal meeting times based on your preferences and existing commitments.

Example conversation with the AI calendar bot showing natural language processing

How It Works

1. Telegram Message Reception

The workflow begins when you send a message to your Telegram bot. The n8n workflow captures this message and prepares it for processing by Claude AI.

2. AI Interpretation

Claude 3.5 Haiku analyzes your natural language request, extracting key details like event names, participants, dates/times, and any special instructions. The AI understands context like "move my morning meeting" without needing exact times.

3. Calendar Interaction

The workflow then interacts with Google Calendar's API to check availability, create events, modify existing ones, or retrieve schedule information based on the AI's interpretation of your request.

4. Response Generation

Claude formulates a human-like response confirming the action taken or asking clarifying questions if needed. This response is sent back to you through Telegram.

Pro tip: Teach the bot your scheduling preferences (like "I prefer meetings after 10am") to make its automatic time suggestions more accurate.
