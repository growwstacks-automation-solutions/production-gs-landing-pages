What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of sending postcards to CRM contacts by connecting CentralStationCRM with EchtPost's direct mail service. Whenever contacts meet specific criteria (like being tagged or reaching milestones), the workflow automatically generates personalized postcards with their address details and sends them via physical mail.

Traditional direct mail campaigns require exporting lists, designing batches, and coordinating with printers—a process that often takes days. This workflow reduces that to minutes while enabling true one-to-one personalization at scale. It's particularly valuable for businesses that rely on high-touch customer communication but want to scale their outreach efficiently.

The automated workflow pulling contact data and triggering postcard creation

How It Works

1. Contact Trigger

The workflow monitors CentralStationCRM for specific contact triggers—typically when contacts receive a particular tag or reach defined milestones in your customer journey. This could be new leads added to a campaign, customers reaching anniversaries, or segmented lists you want to target.

2. Data Retrieval

For each triggered contact, the workflow retrieves complete profile information including mailing address, name, and any relevant personalization fields. This data is validated to ensure deliverability and formatted correctly for postal requirements.

Pro tip: Add a step to exclude contacts with incomplete addresses or those who've opted out of direct mail to maintain compliance and reduce waste.

3. Postcard Generation

Using EchtPost's API, the workflow merges contact data with your predefined postcard template. This can include variable fields like names, purchase history, or location-specific offers. The system handles all design rendering remotely.

Sample postcard showing personalized fields merged from CRM data

4. Mailing Execution

EchtPost automatically prints, sorts, and mails each postcard with proper postage. The workflow can be configured for immediate sending or scheduled batches to optimize postal rates. Delivery confirmation is available for tracking.
