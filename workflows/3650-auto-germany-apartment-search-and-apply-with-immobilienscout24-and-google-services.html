Automatically find and apply to apartments matching your criteria across Germany

What This Workflow Does

Finding an apartment in Germany's competitive rental market can be incredibly time-consuming and stressful. This n8n workflow automates the entire process by continuously searching Immobilienscout24 (Germany's largest real estate platform) for listings that match your specific criteria, then automatically applying to suitable apartments with your prepared documents.

The system handles all the tedious work: running searches every 15-30 minutes, filtering results by your preferred locations, price range, number of rooms, and other requirements, then submitting complete applications immediately when new matches appear. This gives you a crucial time advantage in markets where good apartments often receive hundreds of applications within hours of being listed.

The workflow filters listings based on your exact apartment requirements

How It Works

1. Configure your search parameters

Set your preferred cities/neighborhoods, maximum rent (including utilities), minimum room count, and other requirements like balcony, elevator, or pet allowance. The workflow stores these as variables for all future searches.

2. Connect to Immobilienscout24

The workflow authenticates with Immobilienscout24's API using your credentials, then begins periodic searches based on your parameters. It can access both public listings and premium offers if you have an upgraded account.

3. Filter and evaluate listings

Each new listing gets checked against your criteria. The system can perform additional checks like calculating rent per square meter or comparing to local averages to flag potential overpricing.

Automated preparation of application documents for German rentals

4. Prepare and submit applications

For listings that pass all filters, the workflow assembles your standard application package (Schufa, pay slips, etc.), formats them appropriately, and submits through Immobilienscout24's system or via email to private landlords.

5. Track responses and follow up

The system logs all applications sent and can automatically follow up after a set period if no response is received. It also compiles statistics about your search success rate.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Immobilienscout24 account (free or premium)
  3. Google account for document storage and email
  4. Scanned copies of your standard application documents (Schufa, pay slips, etc.)
  5. List of your preferred neighborhoods and maximum budget

Pro tip: Consider creating a German-style "Bewerbungsmappe" (application portfolio) with all documents pre-translated if you're not fluent in German. The automation can use this for all applications.
