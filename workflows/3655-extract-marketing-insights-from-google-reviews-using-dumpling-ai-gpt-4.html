What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow transforms raw Google reviews into actionable marketing intelligence by leveraging Dumpling AI and GPT-4. Instead of manually reading through hundreds of reviews, this system automatically extracts sentiment patterns, common complaints, and unexpected compliments that reveal opportunities to improve your marketing strategy.

The workflow saves marketing teams dozens of hours per month by systematically analyzing customer feedback at scale. It identifies emerging trends in customer sentiment before they become obvious through manual review reading, giving your business a competitive advantage in responding to market needs.

AI-generated marketing insights dashboard showing customer sentiment trends

How It Works

1. Fetch Google Reviews

The workflow connects to Google My Business API to retrieve all reviews for your specified business location. It handles authentication and pagination automatically to ensure all reviews are captured.

2. Process with Dumpling AI

Each review is analyzed by Dumpling AI to extract key entities, sentiment scores, and emotional tone. This creates structured data from unstructured review text.

3. Generate Insights with GPT-4

The processed data is sent to GPT-4 which identifies patterns across reviews and generates concise marketing recommendations based on customer feedback.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run weekly to track sentiment trends over time and identify emerging customer needs.
