What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the modern professional's email overload problem by transforming your inbox into a structured, searchable knowledge base. Instead of wasting hours reading and re-reading emails, the system processes your messages overnight, extracting key information using GPT-4 and saving organized summaries to Google Docs.

The workflow handles both individual emails and threaded conversations, preserving important context while eliminating redundant information. You'll start each day with a clean, categorized document containing only the essential insights from your communications - perfect for executives, consultants, and teams managing high-volume email traffic.

Sample output showing categorized email summaries with action items highlighted

How It Works

1. Email Collection & Filtering

The workflow connects to your email account (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) and retrieves messages based on your specified criteria - typically all emails received since the last digest. Advanced filtering options let you exclude certain senders or prioritize important conversations.

2. AI-Powered Summarization

Each email gets processed through GPT-4 with custom instructions you define. The AI extracts key points, decisions, and action items while maintaining the original context. You can adjust summary length and style to match your preferences.

Pro tip: Feed GPT-4 examples of your ideal summaries to train it on your preferred format and terminology.

3. Google Docs Organization

The system creates a new Google Doc (or appends to an existing one) with categorized summaries. By default, it organizes content by sender and importance, but you can customize the structure completely. The document becomes a searchable archive of your email knowledge.

Quick Setup Guide
