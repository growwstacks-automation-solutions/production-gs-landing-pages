What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical gap in sales outreach workflows - the delay between when a lead replies to your email and when your team becomes aware of it. Traditional methods require sales reps to manually check email replies or rely on sporadic notifications, creating response delays that can cost you deals.

The workflow automatically detects when a prospect replies to your Lemlist email sequence and instantly posts a notification in your Mattermost team channel. This ensures your sales team can respond while the lead is still engaged, dramatically improving conversion rates.

Sample Mattermost notification showing lead reply details from Lemlist

How It Works

1. Lemlist detects email replies

The workflow starts when Lemlist identifies that a recipient has replied to your outreach email. Lemlist's webhook sends this reply data to n8n in real-time.

2. n8n processes the reply data

The workflow extracts key information like the lead's name, email address, reply content, and campaign details. It can also enrich this data with additional context from your CRM if needed.

3. Notification sent to Mattermost

n8n formats the notification message and sends it to your specified Mattermost channel. The message includes all relevant details your team needs to respond effectively.

Pro tip: Add lead scoring criteria to prioritize notifications for high-value prospects.
