What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of maintaining a consistent content pipeline for WordPress sites. Content creation is time-consuming, requiring research, writing, SEO optimization, image sourcing, and formatting. This automation handles all these steps by leveraging AI to generate complete articles with relevant images.

The workflow generates SEO-optimized draft posts in WordPress using Gemini AI for content creation and OpenAI's image generation for visuals. It maintains human oversight by saving as drafts rather than publishing immediately, allowing for review before going live.

Sample output showing an AI-generated article with OpenAI-created featured image

How It Works

1. Content generation trigger

The workflow begins when you provide a topic or keyword. This can be manually entered, pulled from a spreadsheet, or triggered by another automation.

2. AI article creation

Gemini AI analyzes your topic and generates a well-structured article with proper headings, paragraphs, and SEO elements. You can customize the tone, length, and style parameters.

3. Image generation

OpenAI's image generation creates relevant visuals based on the article content. The workflow automatically resizes and optimizes these images for web performance.

The automation process from topic input to WordPress draft creation

4. WordPress integration

The completed article and images are uploaded to your WordPress site as a draft post. All metadata (categories, tags, featured image) is properly set.

5. Notification and review

The workflow sends a notification with the draft link for human review. Editors can make final adjustments before publishing.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to generate multiple article variations from the same topic, then have your team vote on the best one to publish.
