What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually finding and verifying local business contacts for sales prospecting, partnerships, or networking. Traditional methods require hours of web searches, directory browsing, and data verification - this workflow automates the entire process.

The system takes a list of business names or categories from Google Sheets, searches for their contact information using SerpAPI and Apify web scraping tools, then uses GPT-4o to verify and structure the extracted data into clean, usable contact records. The verified contacts are then added back to your spreadsheet automatically.

Sample output showing structured business contact information

How It Works

Step 1: Input Business Data

The workflow starts by reading business names or search queries from a Google Sheet. You can specify locations, industries, or other search parameters in your spreadsheet.

Step 2: Search & Scrape Data

SerpAPI performs Google searches for each business, while Apify extracts structured data from business directories and websites. This combination ensures comprehensive coverage.

Step 3: AI Verification

GPT-4o analyzes the scraped data to verify accuracy, remove duplicates, and standardize formats. It can also enrich records with additional insights when available.

Visualization of the automated contact extraction process

Step 4: Output Clean Data

The verified contacts are written back to your Google Sheet with standardized fields including business name, phone, email, address, website, and any additional relevant details.
