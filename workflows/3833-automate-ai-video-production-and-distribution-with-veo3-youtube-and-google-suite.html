What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of creating and distributing video content manually. Marketing teams, content creators, and educators often spend hours scripting, recording, editing, and uploading videos - tasks that can now be largely automated.

The workflow connects Veo3's AI video generation with YouTube publishing and Google Drive storage, creating an end-to-end production pipeline. It handles everything from triggering video creation based on your scripts to optimizing metadata for SEO and organizing finished files in your cloud storage.

AI-generated video example showing professional quality output

How It Works

1. Trigger video creation

The workflow starts when you add a new script or content brief to your designated Google Drive folder. This triggers the Veo3 API to begin video generation based on your parameters.

2. AI video generation

Veo3's machine learning models analyze your script and automatically generate visuals, select stock footage, add transitions, and create voiceovers if needed. The system can maintain consistent branding across videos.

3. Quality review (optional)

Before publishing, the workflow can route the video to a review folder where team members can approve or request edits. This human-in-the-loop step ensures quality control.

4. YouTube publishing

Once approved, the video is automatically uploaded to your YouTube channel with optimized titles, descriptions, and tags. The workflow can also add videos to specified playlists.

5. Cloud storage backup

Final videos are saved to your Google Drive with proper naming conventions and folder organization, creating a searchable video asset library.

What You'll Need

  3. YouTube channel with upload permissions
  4. Google Service Account with Drive access
  5. Content strategy and script templates

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Veo3 API connection with your credentials
  3. Set up Google Service Account authentication
  4. Connect your YouTube channel via OAuth
  5. Define your input folder for scripts in Google Drive
  6. Test with sample content before going live

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of test videos to fine-tune your AI parameters before scaling production.
