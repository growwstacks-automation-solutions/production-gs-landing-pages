How It Works

1. HubSpot CRM Integration

The workflow connects to your HubSpot account via API, pulling all active deals and contact records. It identifies new leads that need enrichment and existing deals approaching key milestones.

2. AI-Powered Lead Research

ScrapeGraphAI automatically researches each lead, extracting company news, funding rounds, executive changes, and other relevant signals. This data enhances your CRM records without manual input.

3. Dashboard Updates

The system pushes enriched data to your Google Sheets dashboard, updating all visualizations and KPI metrics in real-time. Managers get instant visibility into pipeline health and team performance.
