This automation streamlines newsletter subscription management by connecting web forms to Baserow databases. The workflow eliminates manual data transfers between systems, reducing errors and saving hours of administrative work each week.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Capture

The workflow triggers when a visitor submits your newsletter signup form. It captures all form fields including metadata like timestamps and UTM parameters for tracking campaign performance.

2. Baserow Record Creation

New submissions create records in your Baserow database with fields mapped from the form. The workflow checks for existing emails to prevent duplicates before adding contacts.

3. Double Opt-In Processing

If enabled, the system sends a confirmation email and updates the Baserow status field. Only confirmed opt-ins proceed to your main mailing list.

Double opt-in verification flow in the automation

4. List Segmentation

The workflow categorizes subscribers based on form source, interests, or other criteria. These segments sync to your email platform for targeted content delivery.

5. Notification & Follow-Up

Internal teams receive alerts about new high-value signups while subscribers get automated welcome sequences based on their segment.

Pro tip: Add a "How you heard about us" field to your signup form. This data in Baserow helps measure marketing channel effectiveness.
