What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of timely lead engagement by automatically sending personalized WhatsApp welcome messages to new Vtiger CRM leads through Evolution API. Manual follow-ups often get delayed, causing missed opportunities - statistics show response times under 5 minutes increase conversion rates by 21x.

The workflow monitors your Vtiger CRM for new lead entries, extracts their contact information, and instantly sends a branded WhatsApp message. This creates immediate touchpoints while your sales team focuses on qualified conversations rather than initial outreach.

The complete automation flow from Vtiger CRM to WhatsApp messaging

How It Works

1. New lead detection in Vtiger

The workflow continuously checks your Vtiger CRM for newly created lead records using scheduled triggers. It identifies fresh leads that haven't received a welcome message yet.

2. Contact information extraction

For each new lead, the system pulls relevant details including name, phone number, and any custom fields you've configured for personalization (like company name or lead source).

Evolution API setup for WhatsApp message delivery

3. WhatsApp message composition

Using your predefined message templates, the workflow dynamically inserts lead-specific information to create personalized messages. It supports text, emojis, and can be extended to include media attachments.

4. Message delivery via Evolution API

The system sends the composed message through your Evolution API connection to the lead's WhatsApp number, with delivery confirmation and read receipts tracked back to Vtiger.

Pro tip: Add a quick reply button in your welcome message (like "Interested" or "Not now") to immediately gauge lead interest and route them to appropriate follow-up sequences.

Effective WhatsApp welcome messages combine personalization, value, and clear next steps. Best practices include using the lead's name, briefly introducing your company's value proposition, and including a simple call-to-action. Messages should be concise (2-3 sentences max), friendly, and avoid sounding overly promotional or spammy.

The highest-performing messages we've seen follow this structure: 1) Personalized greeting, 2) One sentence about how you help clients, 3) Specific next step (like scheduling a call or answering a question). For example: "Hi [Name], thanks for inquiring about our marketing services! We help businesses like yours get 2x more leads. Would you like to see case studies from your industry?"

  • Include the lead's name in first 5 words
  • Focus on their needs, not your features
  • End with a simple yes/no question
