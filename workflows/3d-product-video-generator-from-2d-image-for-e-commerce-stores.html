How It Works

1. Product Image Collection

The workflow begins by pulling product images from your Shopify store or other e-commerce platform. It identifies new products or updated images that need 3D video generation.

2. Image Optimization

Each image undergoes automatic enhancement - background removal, resolution boosting, and angle correction to prepare it for 3D conversion. This step ensures consistent quality output.

3. 3D Model Generation

The system sends optimized images to a 3D rendering API which creates a rotatable 3D model. Advanced algorithms estimate depth and texture from the 2D source.

4. Video Rendering

The 3D model is animated through a smooth 360-degree rotation and rendered as an MP4 video file. Multiple lighting conditions can be simulated for richer presentation.

5. Shopify Integration

Finished videos are automatically uploaded to your Shopify product pages, replacing or supplementing existing media. The workflow updates product variants and collections as needed.

Pro tip: For best results, provide product images with clean backgrounds and multiple angles when available. The system can work with single images but produces better 3D models with additional views.
