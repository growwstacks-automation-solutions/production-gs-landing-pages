What This Workflow Does

Managing customer reviews on Google Business Profile is crucial for local businesses, but manually monitoring and responding to reviews can be time-consuming. This automation solves three key challenges: missing new reviews, slow response times, and lack of centralized tracking.

The workflow automatically checks your Google Business Profile for new reviews, generates personalized response suggestions using AI, alerts your team via Slack, and logs all review data to Google Sheets for analysis. It ensures you never miss customer feedback while maintaining authentic engagement.

Slack notification showing a new Google review with AI-generated response suggestion

How It Works

1. Review Monitoring

The workflow periodically checks your Google Business Profile API for new reviews using scheduled triggers. It identifies any reviews that haven't been processed yet based on timestamp comparison.

2. AI Response Generation

For each new review, the system analyzes the content using AI (like OpenAI or Claude) to generate a context-aware response suggestion. Positive reviews get thank-you messages, while negative ones receive apology templates with escalation paths.

3. Slack Notifications

The workflow sends detailed Slack alerts to your designated channel, including the review text, star rating, reviewer name, and the AI-generated response suggestion. Team members can approve or modify responses before posting.

4. Google Sheets Logging

All review data gets logged to a Google Sheet with timestamps, ratings, text, response status, and other metadata. This creates a searchable history for reputation management and customer service analysis.
