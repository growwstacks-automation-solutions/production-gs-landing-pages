What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of lost or disorganized WhatsApp conversations with potential customers. When sales teams use WhatsApp for business communications, critical messages often get buried in personal chats or forgotten in busy inboxes. Our template automatically captures every WhatsApp message and logs it to the corresponding lead record in Vtiger CRM.

The system matches incoming messages to existing leads using phone number recognition, or creates new lead records when needed. Each logged message includes the full conversation history, timestamps, and media attachments. This gives your sales team complete context for every customer interaction without manual data entry.

Automated flow from WhatsApp to Vtiger CRM lead records

How It Works

1. WhatsApp Message Capture

The workflow monitors your business WhatsApp account for new incoming messages through the WhatsApp Business API. It captures the message content, sender details, timestamps, and any media attachments.

2. Lead Matching

The system checks Vtiger CRM for existing leads matching the sender's phone number. If no match exists, it can optionally create a new lead record with the WhatsApp contact information.

Sample WhatsApp business message being captured

3. CRM Logging

Each message is logged to the lead's activity history in Vtiger CRM with proper categorization. The system maintains conversation threads and can trigger follow-up tasks or notifications based on message content.

Pro tip: Configure custom fields in Vtiger to track message urgency or product interest mentioned in conversations. This enables powerful filtering and prioritization of follow-ups.
