What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking competitor content updates across multiple websites. Traditional competitive intelligence requires hours of manual checking, copying, and analyzing content - a process prone to human error and inconsistency.

The workflow automatically scrapes competitor websites using Firecrawl, analyzes the content with Gemini and OpenAI AI models to extract key insights, and compiles everything into a structured Google Sheets report. Each week, you get a digest highlighting new content, strategic shifts, and emerging trends from your competitive landscape.

AI-powered competitor content analysis visualized in Google Sheets

How It Works

1. Website Content Crawling

Firecrawl automatically visits your configured competitor websites and extracts all new content published during the specified time period. It captures articles, blog posts, product updates, and other relevant pages.

2. AI Content Analysis

The workflow sends the scraped content to Gemini and OpenAI for semantic analysis. The AI models identify key themes, sentiment trends, strategic messaging changes, and notable content gaps compared to previous periods.

3. Digest Compilation

The AI-generated insights are structured into a comprehensive report format, highlighting the most important competitive developments with executive-friendly summaries.

4. Google Sheets Integration

The final digest is automatically appended to your designated Google Sheet, creating a historical record of competitor activity with timestamps for easy trend analysis.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n account (self-hosted or cloud)
  2. Google Sheets with edit permissions
  3. Firecrawl API access
  4. Gemini and/or OpenAI API keys
  5. List of competitor websites to monitor

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your competitor website URLs in the Firecrawl node
  3. Connect your Google Sheets account and specify the target spreadsheet
  4. Add your Gemini and OpenAI API credentials
  5. Test the workflow with a manual trigger
  6. Schedule it to run weekly
