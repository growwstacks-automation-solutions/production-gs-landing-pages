What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of creating contracts from client information. When a client fills out your Typeform (for services, freelancing, or consultations), this workflow automatically generates a customized contract document in Google Docs using their responses, then emails it to them via Gmail.

The system eliminates hours of repetitive document creation while ensuring consistency across all your contracts. It's particularly valuable for freelancers, agencies, and service businesses that need to onboard clients quickly without administrative bottlenecks.

Visualization of automated contract generation process

How It Works

1. Typeform submission triggers the workflow

When a client completes your Typeform with their contract details (name, services, pricing, etc.), this triggers the automation to begin processing their information.

2. Data is formatted for document creation

The workflow extracts and structures the relevant form responses, preparing them for insertion into your Google Docs contract template.

3. Google Docs generates the contract

Using your pre-designed template, the system creates a new document with all client-specific details automatically populated in the correct fields.

Contract template with merged client information

4. PDF conversion and email delivery

The completed contract is converted to PDF format and sent to the client via Gmail, with optional CC to your team for record-keeping.
