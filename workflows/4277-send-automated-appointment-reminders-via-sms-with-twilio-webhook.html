What This Workflow Does

Missed appointments cost service businesses an average of 15-20% in lost revenue annually. This n8n workflow solves that problem by automatically sending personalized SMS reminders to customers through Twilio's reliable messaging platform.

The system reduces administrative workload while dramatically improving appointment attendance rates. Unlike email reminders that often go unread, SMS messages have a 98% open rate, ensuring your customers actually receive and act on the information.

Example SMS reminder received by customers with personalized details

How It Works

1. Appointment Data Trigger

The workflow activates when new appointment data is received, either from your booking system API or manual entry. It extracts key details like customer name, phone number, appointment time, and service type.

2. Reminder Scheduling

The system calculates optimal reminder times (typically 24 hours and 1 hour before appointments) and queues messages accordingly. You can customize these timeframes based on your business needs.

3. Twilio SMS Delivery

When reminder times arrive, the workflow triggers Twilio's API to send professionally formatted SMS messages. Each text includes personalized details and clear instructions for confirmation or rescheduling.

Workflow nodes handling SMS delivery timing and personalization

4. Response Handling

The system can process customer replies, automatically updating appointment statuses for confirmations or triggering rescheduling workflows when needed.
