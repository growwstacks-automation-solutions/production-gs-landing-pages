What This Workflow Does

Managing vendor contracts manually is time-consuming and error-prone. This smart automation solution eliminates the risk of missed renewals by automatically tracking contract dates, analyzing terms with AI, and notifying your team through Slack and email.

The workflow integrates GPT-4.1 mini to intelligently parse contract documents, extract key dates and clauses, then trigger a series of reminders at strategic intervals before each renewal deadline. This ensures your team always has ample time to evaluate vendor performance, negotiate terms, or explore alternatives.

The workflow architecture showing AI analysis, Slack notifications and Gmail reminders

How It Works

1. Contract ingestion and AI analysis

When a new vendor contract arrives via email, the workflow automatically sends it to GPT-4.1 mini for analysis. The AI identifies key terms including renewal dates, termination windows, auto-renewal clauses, and pricing terms.

2. Centralized date tracking

The extracted dates are logged in a centralized database with custom reminder schedules. Critical dates are added to your calendar with multiple alert thresholds (typically 90, 60, and 30 days before renewal).

GPT-4.1 mini analysis showing extracted contract terms and dates

3. Team notifications

As each reminder threshold is reached, tailored Slack messages are sent to relevant teams (procurement, legal, finance). The notifications include contract summaries, key terms, and recommended actions.

4. Executive alerts

For high-value contracts, the system sends email reminders to executives through Gmail, ensuring visibility at all levels. These include quick-access links to the full contract and negotiation history.

Pro tip: Configure different reminder schedules based on contract value - more frequent alerts for high-value vendors, simpler tracking for low-cost services.

This automation is ideal for procurement teams, office managers, and operations leaders who manage multiple vendor relationships. Companies with 5+ active vendor contracts will see immediate time savings and risk reduction.
