What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms the tedious process of manual video creation into a streamlined, AI-powered workflow. It allows businesses and content creators to convert static images into professional-quality videos simply by sending the images through Telegram. The system leverages GPT-4.1's content analysis capabilities to enhance the video creation process with intelligent suggestions, then uses Seedance/Veo3's video generation API to produce the final output.

The workflow eliminates the need for expensive video editing software or specialized skills, making professional video creation accessible to teams of all sizes. Whether you need product demo videos, social media content, or marketing materials, this automation handles the entire process from image submission to final video delivery.

Sample transformation from static image to animated video with AI enhancements

How It Works

1. Image Submission via Telegram

Users send images through a Telegram chat or channel. The workflow automatically detects new image submissions and begins processing them. Telegram serves as the user-friendly interface that requires no technical knowledge from team members.

2. AI Content Analysis with GPT-4.1

The system sends the image to GPT-4.1 for content analysis. The AI identifies key elements, suggests appropriate video styles, and may generate complementary text or audio scripts based on the image content.

3. Video Generation via Seedance/Veo3

The analyzed image and AI recommendations are sent to Seedance/Veo3's video generation API. The platform creates a professional video with transitions, effects, and (optionally) generated audio based on the input parameters.

4. Delivery & Notification

The finished video is sent back to the original Telegram chat, and relevant team members receive notifications. The workflow can also store the videos in cloud storage or share them directly to social platforms.
