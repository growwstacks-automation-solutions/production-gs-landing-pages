How It Works

1. Email Ingestion and Analysis

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages. When an email arrives, it extracts key details and sends the content to GPT-4 for processing. The AI analyzes the message to determine:

  • Email category (support, sales, internal, etc.)
  • Urgency level based on content and sender
  • Appropriate response type (immediate, delayed, none)

2. Intelligent Routing

Based on the AI analysis, the system routes each email through different pathways:

  • Urgent customer emails get AI-drafted responses for quick review
  • Internal requests generate Slack notifications with action buttons
  • Newsletters and promotions are automatically archived
Team members receive actionable Slack notifications for important emails

3. Continuous Learning

The system improves over time by learning from your corrections and feedback. When you modify an AI-generated response or re-categorize an email, these adjustments train the model to handle similar future emails more accurately.

Pro tip: Spend 10 minutes weekly reviewing the AI's decisions to dramatically improve accuracy. The system learns fastest from consistent feedback on borderline cases.
