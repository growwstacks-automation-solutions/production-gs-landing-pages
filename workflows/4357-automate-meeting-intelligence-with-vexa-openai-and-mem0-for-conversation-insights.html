What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the universal problem of meeting overload and information loss. It captures every important detail from your conversations without manual note-taking, using AI to analyze discussions and extract the most valuable insights.

The workflow connects VEXA's meeting transcription with OpenAI's advanced analysis capabilities, then stores structured outputs in Mem0 for long-term knowledge retention. It transforms hours of meeting recordings into searchable, actionable intelligence that your entire team can benefit from.

AI-generated meeting summary showing key points and action items

How It Works

1. Meeting Capture

VEXA automatically records and transcribes your meetings from Zoom, Teams, or other platforms. The system captures speaker attribution and timestamps for every comment.

2. AI Analysis

OpenAI processes the transcript to identify decisions, action items, unresolved questions, and key discussion themes. It scores importance and urgency while maintaining context.

Raw transcript being processed by AI analysis engine

3. Knowledge Storage

Structured outputs flow into Mem0 where they connect with existing knowledge. The system creates semantic links between related concepts across different meetings and documents.

4. Follow-up Automation

Action items automatically generate reminders and tasks. Important decisions are logged with relevant context. Team members receive personalized digests of relevant insights.

Pro tip: Configure the AI to flag when the same topic gets discussed repeatedly without resolution - a sign your meetings might need restructuring.
