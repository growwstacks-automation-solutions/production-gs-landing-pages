What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how professionals create emails by converting Telegram voice messages into polished, ready-to-send communications using AI. Instead of typing emails on your phone or scrambling to jot down ideas, simply record a voice note and let the system handle the rest.

The workflow automatically transcribes your voice message, structures the content professionally using ChatGPT, and formats it as a complete email—complete with appropriate greeting, body paragraphs, and closing. It's particularly valuable for busy executives, sales teams, and field professionals who need to communicate quickly while maintaining a professional tone.

Recording a voice message in Telegram that will be automatically converted to an email

How It Works

1. Voice Message Trigger

The workflow activates when you send a voice message to a designated Telegram bot or chat. The system captures the audio file and associated metadata (sender, timestamp, etc.).

2. Audio Transcription

Using speech-to-text technology, the voice message is converted into written text. The transcription service handles punctuation, speaker differentiation (if multiple voices), and can identify key phrases.

Automated transcription converts spoken words into editable text

3. AI Email Composition

ChatGPT analyzes the transcribed text, identifying the core message, tone, and intent. It then structures this into a professional email format with appropriate salutation, body content, and closing.

4. Delivery Options

The completed email can be sent directly via your email service, saved as a draft for review, or returned to you in Telegram for final approval before sending.
