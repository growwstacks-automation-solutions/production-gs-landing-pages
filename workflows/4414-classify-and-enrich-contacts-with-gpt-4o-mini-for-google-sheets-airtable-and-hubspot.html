What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the common problem of incomplete or unclassified contact data across multiple business platforms. Many companies struggle with spreadsheets full of raw contact information that lacks proper categorization, enrichment, or integration with their CRM systems.

The automation retrieves new or updated contact data from Google Sheets or Airtable, processes it through GPT-4o-mini for intelligent classification and enrichment, then syncs the enhanced data to HubSpot or back to your original spreadsheet. This creates a continuous loop of data improvement without manual effort.

Comparison showing raw contact data versus AI-enriched output with classifications and additional details

How It Works

1. Data Retrieval

The workflow begins by checking your Google Sheet or Airtable base for new or modified contact records. It identifies which rows need processing based on timestamp or status columns.

2. AI Processing

Each contact record is sent to GPT-4o-mini with custom prompts to analyze and enrich the data. The AI can classify contacts by persona, industry, or buying stage, and add missing details like company information or social profiles.

3. Data Enhancement

The workflow supplements AI output with additional API lookups (like Clearbit or Hunter.io) to verify email addresses, find LinkedIn profiles, or append firmographic data.

4. CRM Sync

Finally, the enriched contact data is formatted and pushed to HubSpot (or another destination) with all new classifications and fields properly mapped to your CRM schema.
