What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of incomplete contact information in your CRM. When new contacts enter HubSpot, they often lack critical details needed for effective sales outreach - like job titles, company information, or social profiles. Manual research is time-consuming and doesn't scale.

The workflow automatically enriches each new HubSpot contact with additional professional and company data from ClearBit's extensive B2B database. It adds verified information directly to the contact record, giving your sales team complete profiles without any manual work.

Enriched contact data showing additional professional and company information

How It Works

1. New contact detection

The workflow triggers whenever a new contact is added to your specified HubSpot list or meets your criteria. The n8n workflow captures the contact's basic information including email address.

2. ClearBit enrichment

The contact's email is sent to ClearBit's API which returns enriched professional data (job title, social profiles) and company information (size, industry, technographics).

3. Data validation

The workflow verifies email deliverability and checks data quality before updating HubSpot, preventing incorrect information from entering your CRM.

4. HubSpot update

All validated enrichment data is mapped to the appropriate HubSpot contact properties, creating a complete profile for your sales team.
