What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for businesses collecting leads through Typeform: incomplete contact information. Many prospects submit forms without providing professional email addresses, making follow-up difficult. The workflow automatically enriches each Typeform submission with verified email addresses and professional details using Dropcontact's database.

When someone completes your Typeform, the system instantly searches for their professional email, LinkedIn profile, job title, and company information. This transforms basic form responses into complete prospect profiles ready for your sales team or CRM. The process happens in minutes without manual research, saving hours per week in lead qualification.

Sample output showing professional details added to a Typeform submission

How It Works

1. Trigger on Typeform submission

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Typeform. It captures all response data including name, company, and any custom fields you've added.

2. Prepare data for Dropcontact

The system formats the submission data to match Dropcontact's requirements, combining first/last name fields and extracting company information where available.

3. Find verified contact details

Dropcontact searches its database and external sources to find the most accurate professional email and social profiles matching the form submitter.

Data mapping between Typeform responses and Dropcontact fields

4. Enrich lead profile

The workflow combines original Typeform responses with Dropcontact's findings to create a complete prospect record with verified contact information.

5. Deliver enriched data

The final enriched profile can be sent to your CRM, email marketing tool, or internal database - ready for immediate follow-up by your team.
