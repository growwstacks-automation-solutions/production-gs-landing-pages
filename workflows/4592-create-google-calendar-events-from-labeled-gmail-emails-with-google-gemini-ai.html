What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring event details from emails to your calendar. Professionals often receive meeting invitations, appointment confirmations, or deadline reminders via email, but manually creating calendar events for each one wastes valuable time and risks missing important commitments.

The workflow automatically scans your Gmail inbox for emails with specific labels, uses Google Gemini AI to intelligently extract event details (dates, times, locations, and descriptions), and creates corresponding Google Calendar events. This ensures you never miss an important appointment while eliminating tedious manual data entry.

AI parsing email content to extract event details

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Gmail inbox for new emails with your specified label (like "To Calendar" or "Events"). This trigger ensures only relevant emails are processed.

2. AI-Powered Parsing

Google Gemini AI analyzes the email content to identify key event details. The AI understands natural language, recognizing dates, times, locations, and event descriptions even when they're written conversationally.

3. Data Validation

The extracted information is checked for completeness and accuracy. If critical details like date/time are missing, the workflow can be configured to either skip the email or flag it for manual review.

Final calendar event created from parsed email data

4. Calendar Event Creation

The workflow creates a Google Calendar event with all extracted details, including proper timezone handling. You can configure default settings like event duration, reminders, and which calendar to use.
