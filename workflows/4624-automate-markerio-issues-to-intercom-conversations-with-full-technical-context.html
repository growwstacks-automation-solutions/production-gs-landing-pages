What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between customer-reported issues and your support team's ability to quickly resolve them. When users submit bug reports through Marker.io's visual feedback tools, this workflow automatically creates detailed Intercom conversations containing all the technical context your team needs.

The template eliminates manual copying of error details between systems, ensuring support agents have immediate access to browser versions, console logs, screenshots, and reproduction steps - all within your existing Intercom workflow. This reduces resolution time while maintaining a seamless customer experience.

Marker.io captures comprehensive technical data that gets forwarded to Intercom

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Marker.io issues

The workflow activates whenever a new issue is submitted through your Marker.io integration. It captures all available technical metadata including environment details, screenshots, and custom fields.

2. Format the technical context

The automation structures the raw technical data into a readable format for support agents, highlighting key details like reproduction steps, affected components, and severity indicators.

3. Create Intercom conversation

A new conversation is initiated in Intercom with the formatted technical details automatically included. The workflow can assign appropriate tags, priority levels, and route to the right team based on your business rules.
