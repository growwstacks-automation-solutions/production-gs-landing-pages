What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually sharing WordPress content across multiple social media platforms. When new content is published, the workflow automatically generates platform-optimized posts using Gemini AI, schedules them through Blotato, and tracks engagement metrics.

Marketing teams waste hours each week reformatting content for different social platforms. This workflow eliminates that bottleneck while ensuring consistent messaging. One digital agency reported saving 20+ hours monthly while increasing their social referral traffic by 65%.

Example of AI-generated social posts scheduled across platforms

How It Works

1. WordPress Trigger

The workflow starts when new content is published in WordPress, detecting either specific categories or all new posts based on your configuration.

2. AI Content Processing

Gemini AI analyzes the article to extract key points and generates multiple social media variations tailored for different platforms (Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, etc.).

3. Platform Optimization

Each social platform receives customized formatting - hashtags for Twitter, longer narratives for LinkedIn, visual emphasis for Instagram.

AI transforming blog content into social-ready formats

4. Blotato Scheduling

Posts are automatically scheduled in Blotato based on your optimal posting calendar, with options for immediate sharing or timed releases.

5. Analytics Integration

The workflow captures engagement metrics to help refine your content strategy, showing which posts drive the most traffic back to your site.

Pro tip: Create different AI templates for various content types (how-tos vs news vs opinion pieces) to maintain brand voice across automated posts.
