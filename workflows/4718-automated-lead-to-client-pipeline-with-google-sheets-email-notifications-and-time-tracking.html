What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms Google Sheets into a powerful yet simple CRM system that automatically manages your lead-to-client pipeline. It tracks lead stages, sends personalized email notifications at each step, and calculates delivery durations - all without expensive CRM software.

The workflow eliminates manual data entry between systems by automatically updating records when leads progress through your pipeline. It provides visibility into conversion timelines while ensuring no lead falls through the cracks due to human oversight.

Example Google Sheets CRM interface showing lead stages and automation triggers

How It Works

1. Lead capture and initial processing

New leads are added to your Google Sheet either manually or through form submissions. The workflow automatically assigns them an initial status and sends a welcome email.

2. Stage progression tracking

When you update a lead's stage in the sheet (like from "Contacted" to "Proposal Sent"), the workflow triggers appropriate follow-up emails and logs the transition timestamp.

3. Time duration calculations

The system calculates how long each lead spends in different stages, helping you identify bottlenecks in your sales process.

4. Client conversion handling

When a lead converts to a client, the workflow sends onboarding materials and starts tracking project delivery timelines automatically.
