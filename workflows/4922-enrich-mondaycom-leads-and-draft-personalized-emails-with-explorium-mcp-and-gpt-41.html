What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually researching leads and crafting personalized outreach emails. When new leads are added to your Monday.com board, the workflow automatically enriches them with detailed company information using Explorium MCP (Market Contact Platform), then generates tailored email drafts using GPT-4.1's advanced natural language capabilities.

The system eliminates hours of manual research by instantly providing sales teams with enriched lead profiles and professionally written outreach templates that reference specific company details, pain points, and potential synergies - all without any manual data entry or copywriting.

Enriched lead data showing company details pulled from Explorium MCP

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow monitors your specified Monday.com board for new lead additions or status changes that trigger the enrichment process.

2. Explorium MCP enrichment

Each lead's company domain or name is sent to Explorium MCP, which returns detailed firmographic data including company size, industry, technologies used, funding rounds, and key decision-makers.

3. AI email drafting

The enriched data is fed into GPT-4.1 along with your email template guidelines to generate a personalized outreach message that references specific company details and suggests relevant value propositions.

4. Monday.com update

The enriched data and draft email are added back to the lead's Monday.com item in dedicated fields, ready for review and sending by your sales team.
