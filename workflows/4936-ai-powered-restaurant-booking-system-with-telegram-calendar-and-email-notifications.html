What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered booking system transforms how restaurants handle reservations by automating the entire process through Telegram. Customers can message natural language requests like "table for 4 tomorrow at 7pm near the window" and the AI assistant will process the request, check availability, and confirm the booking.

The system eliminates phone tag and reduces no-shows by automatically syncing reservations with your calendar system and sending confirmation emails with all details. It handles special requests, dietary restrictions, and even suggests menu items based on the reservation details.

Example of AI processing a customer's booking request via Telegram

How It Works

1. Customer initiates booking via Telegram

The workflow triggers when a customer messages your restaurant's Telegram bot. The AI analyzes the message to extract key details like party size, date/time, and special requests.

2. AI processes the request

Natural language processing converts the message into structured booking data. The system checks your calendar for availability and either suggests alternatives or confirms the booking.

3. Calendar synchronization

Confirmed bookings automatically appear in your Google Calendar or other calendar system, blocking the time slot and including all relevant details from the customer's request.

4. Confirmation messages

The system sends a Telegram confirmation to the customer along with an email containing booking details, cancellation policy, and directions to your location.
