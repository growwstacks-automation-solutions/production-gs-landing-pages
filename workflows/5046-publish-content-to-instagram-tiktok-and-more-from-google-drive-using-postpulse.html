What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually uploading content to multiple social media platforms. Marketing teams often waste hours each week downloading files from Google Drive, reformatting them for different platforms, and manually publishing across Instagram, TikTok, and other channels.

The workflow automatically detects new content in a designated Google Drive folder, processes it through PostPulse, and publishes to all connected social platforms with proper formatting. It maintains consistency while eliminating repetitive manual work, reducing publishing time by 80% or more.

Automated content flow from Google Drive to social platforms

How It Works

1. Content Detection

The workflow monitors a specific Google Drive folder for new content additions. When files are added, it triggers the automation process.

2. File Processing

Content is automatically formatted for each destination platform, with images resized and videos transcoded as needed. PostPulse handles platform-specific requirements.

3. Multi-Platform Publishing

The system publishes to all connected social accounts simultaneously while maintaining each platform's unique presentation requirements.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Drive account with content folders
  2. PostPulse account with connected social platforms
  3. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)
  4. Admin access to social media accounts

Pro tip: Create a dedicated 'Ready to Publish' folder in Google Drive to maintain clear separation between draft and final content.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Drive account and specify the source folder
  3. Authenticate with PostPulse and select destination platforms
  4. Configure content formatting rules for each platform
  5. Test with sample content before enabling full automation
