What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire blog production pipeline from initial research to published WordPress post. It solves the time-consuming manual process of creating regular blog content by combining AI-powered writing with automated publishing.

The system generates SEO-optimized articles using Gemini AI, creates matching visuals with Ideogram AI, and publishes directly to WordPress with proper formatting, categories, and scheduling. Content teams save 8-12 hours per post while maintaining editorial quality and brand consistency.

Gemini AI generating blog content within the automated workflow

How It Works

1. Topic Research & Outline Generation

The workflow begins by analyzing trending topics and keywords using Gemini AI's research capabilities. It identifies high-potential subjects based on your target audience and existing content strategy.

2. AI Content Creation

Gemini AI drafts complete blog posts with proper structure, SEO elements, and brand-aligned tone. The system automatically optimizes for readability and keyword placement while avoiding duplication.

3. Visual Content Generation

Ideogram AI creates custom featured images, illustrations, and infographics that match the blog content's theme. The workflow ensures consistent branding across all visual elements.

Ideogram AI creating blog post visuals automatically

4. WordPress Publishing

The finalized content and images are automatically formatted and published to WordPress with proper categories, tags, and SEO metadata. The workflow supports scheduled publishing and draft status for editorial review.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to send draft posts to your team for review before publishing. This maintains quality control while still saving 80% of production time.
