What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow transforms a single product image into a complete professional advertisement video using cutting-edge AI tools. It combines Gemini 2.5's image enhancement capabilities with FAL WAN's sophisticated animation techniques to create high-converting video ads automatically.

The system handles everything from image optimization to final video rendering, eliminating the need for manual video editing or expensive production teams. Businesses can scale their video ad production exponentially while maintaining consistent quality across all outputs.

The workflow interface showing the automated steps from image input to final video output

How It Works

1. Image Enhancement with Gemini 2.5

The workflow first processes your product image through Gemini 2.5's flash image enhancement. This AI tool automatically improves lighting, sharpness, and colors while removing any distracting backgrounds or imperfections.

2. Dynamic Animation with FAL WAN

The enhanced image then moves through FAL WAN's animation engine, which applies professional-grade motion effects. These include cinematic zooms, smooth pans, and attention-grabbing transitions that showcase your product from multiple angles.

Before and after comparison showing image enhancement results

3. Video Rendering & Output

The final stage combines all elements into a polished video ad, complete with your branding and optional text overlays. The workflow can output videos in multiple formats optimized for different platforms like Instagram, YouTube, or TikTok.
