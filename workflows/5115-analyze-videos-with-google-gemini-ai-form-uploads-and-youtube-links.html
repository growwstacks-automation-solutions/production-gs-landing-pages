What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually reviewing and analyzing video content at scale. Businesses drowning in customer video submissions, training recordings, or competitor YouTube content can now automatically extract valuable insights without human review.

The workflow accepts video files through form submissions or YouTube URLs, processes them through Google Gemini's advanced AI models, and delivers structured analysis including content summaries, sentiment scoring, object recognition, and transcriptions. It transforms unstructured video data into actionable business intelligence.

Sample output from Google Gemini AI video analysis showing extracted metadata and insights

How It Works

1. Video Submission

The workflow accepts video content through two channels: direct file uploads via form submissions or YouTube URL inputs. This dual approach accommodates both original content and existing public videos.

2. Content Processing

Google Gemini AI extracts frames, analyzes visual elements, processes audio tracks, and examines temporal patterns. The AI identifies objects, scenes, speech content, and emotional tones throughout the video timeline.

3. Insight Generation

The system generates structured data including: content summaries, sentiment analysis, object/face detection logs, full transcriptions with timestamps, and thematic classification. This creates a searchable, quantifiable record of video content.
