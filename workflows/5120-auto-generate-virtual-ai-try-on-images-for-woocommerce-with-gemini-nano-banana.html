What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the expensive challenge of creating product visualization images for fashion eCommerce stores. Traditional photoshoots with models cost thousands per garment and can't scale across sizes/colors. The workflow automatically generates realistic virtual try-on images whenever new products are added to WooCommerce.

Using Gemini Nano Banana's AI capabilities, the system creates high-quality model images wearing your products in various sizes and body types. These images integrate directly into your product pages, giving customers confidence in fit and style before purchase - proven to increase conversions by 30-40% while reducing returns by 25%.

AI-generated virtual try-on images adapt to multiple body types while maintaining fabric texture and drape accuracy

How It Works

1. New product detection

The workflow monitors your WooCommerce store for new product additions via API. When a new fashion item is detected (based on your product categories), it extracts key details like product images, colors, and size specifications.

2. AI image processing

The system sends clean product images to Gemini Nano Banana's API along with parameters for different model types. The AI generates realistic try-on images showing how the garment fits various body shapes while preserving fabric textures and design details.

3. Automated publishing

Generated images are automatically optimized, watermarked if needed, and uploaded to WooCommerce as product gallery images. The workflow can create multiple variations per product (different angles, sizes, or model types) based on your configuration.

Technical workflow showing the automated steps from product detection to AI generation and WooCommerce integration

Pro tip: Start with 3-5 model variations per product (petite, average, tall) to test customer response before scaling to more body types.
