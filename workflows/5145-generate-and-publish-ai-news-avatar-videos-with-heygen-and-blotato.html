What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the challenge of creating daily news content at scale by combining AI avatar technology with automated publishing. Traditional video production requires expensive equipment, filming time, and editing resources - this system eliminates those bottlenecks while maintaining professional quality.

The solution pulls fresh news content from RSS feeds, transforms it into video scripts, generates AI avatar presentations through HeyGen, then publishes the finished videos across multiple platforms using Blotato. It's particularly valuable for media companies, content marketers, and educators who need to produce regular video updates without proportional increases in production costs.

HeyGen's interface for configuring AI avatar presentations

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates daily at 10:00 AM (configurable to any schedule) to begin processing fresh news content. This timed trigger ensures consistent output without manual initiation.

2. RSS Feed Aggregation

The system collects the latest AI/LLM news articles from multiple RSS sources, filtering for relevance and removing duplicates. This creates a unified content pipeline from diverse industry sources.

Sample AI news presenter avatar in action

3. Content Processing

An AI agent analyzes and summarizes articles, converting them into concise video scripts optimized for avatar presentation. This includes adding natural pauses, emphasis markers, and visual cue suggestions.

4. HeyGen Video Generation

The system sends scripts to HeyGen's API, which generates videos using your selected avatar, complete with natural lip-syncing and gestures. Multiple videos can be created in parallel for different news segments.

5. Blotato Publishing

Finished videos are automatically uploaded and scheduled across connected platforms (YouTube, social media, CMS) through Blotato's publishing API, with appropriate metadata and formatting for each destination.

Pro tip: Configure different avatars for different content categories (e.g., serious avatar for business news, friendly avatar for lifestyle content) to enhance viewer engagement.
