What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms written news headlines into engaging audio newsletters using cutting-edge AI technology. It solves the problem of information overload by converting text-based news into digestible audio content that busy professionals can consume while commuting, exercising, or multitasking.

The workflow combines Claude's content understanding with GPT-4o's summarization capabilities and OpenAI's text-to-speech to create professional-quality audio briefings. It eliminates hours of manual work required to research, summarize, and record news updates.

Sample output of an AI-generated audio newsletter from news headlines

How It Works

1. News Aggregation

The workflow pulls headlines from your specified news sources through RSS feeds or API connections. It filters content based on your preferences and keywords.

2. AI Summarization

Claude AI analyzes the headlines and full articles when available, identifying key points. GPT-4o then creates concise summaries in a natural speaking style.

3. Audio Generation

OpenAI's text-to-speech converts the summarized content into high-quality audio files. You can customize voice parameters like tone, speed, and accent.

4. Distribution

The final audio files are automatically delivered to your preferred channels - email attachments, podcast feeds, messaging apps, or cloud storage.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. OpenAI API key for GPT and TTS
  3. Anthropic Claude API access
  4. News source RSS feeds or API access
  5. Destination for audio files (email, podcast host, etc.)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your news sources in the RSS/API nodes
  3. Add your OpenAI and Claude API credentials
  4. Set your preferred voice parameters in the TTS node
  5. Connect your distribution channels (email, Dropbox, etc.)
  6. Test with sample headlines and adjust summarization prompts
  7. Schedule the workflow to run daily
