What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered personal assistant workflow transforms Telegram into a powerful productivity hub by connecting it with Google Calendar and Tasks through GPT-4o-mini. It understands natural language requests, manages your schedule, sets reminders, and coordinates tasks—all through simple chat commands.

The solution eliminates constant context switching between apps, reducing the time spent on administrative tasks by up to 70%. Whether you need to schedule meetings, set follow-up reminders, or manage team tasks, this assistant handles it through intuitive conversation.

The Telegram interface showing natural language calendar commands being processed

How It Works

1. Natural Language Processing

GPT-4o-mini interprets free-form messages in Telegram, understanding requests like "Schedule a team sync every Wednesday at 10am" or "Remind me to follow up with Acme Corp in 3 days."

2. Calendar Integration

The workflow connects to Google Calendar to create, modify, or cancel events based on the interpreted commands, handling complex scheduling scenarios automatically.

3. Task Management

Important action items extracted from conversations are added to Google Tasks with appropriate due dates and priorities, keeping your to-do list organized.
