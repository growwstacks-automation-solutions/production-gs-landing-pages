What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling user-generated content (UGC) production for digital marketing campaigns. Instead of manually creating each ad variation, marketers can define campaign parameters in a Google Sheet and let AI generate dozens of professional-quality ads automatically.

The workflow leverages Fal.ai's specialized AI models (including nano-banana, WAN2.2, and Veo3) to transform product data, customer testimonials, and brand guidelines into polished social media ads. It maintains brand consistency while enabling rapid iteration and A/B testing at scale.

Sample outputs showing different ad variations generated from the same product data

How It Works

1. Data Input from Google Sheets

The workflow starts by reading campaign details from a structured Google Sheet. This includes product information, target audience parameters, brand voice guidelines, and any specific creative directions.

2. AI Content Generation

The system sends this data to Fal.ai's API, utilizing different models based on the required output. Nano-banana handles product-focused visuals, WAN2.2 creates lifestyle imagery, and Veo3 generates video variations.

Configuration of Fal.ai nodes showing model selection and parameter mapping

3. Quality Assurance

Generated content goes through automatic checks for brand compliance, technical specifications, and platform requirements before final output.

4. Delivery and Organization

The workflow organizes finished ads by campaign and platform, saving them to designated folders and optionally pushing to your ad platforms via API.
