What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of creating high-quality, authentic-looking ad creatives for e-commerce businesses at scale. Instead of manually photoshopping product images or paying influencers for content, this automation combines your product photos with AI-generated influencer-style backgrounds using Nano Banana Image Generator.

The result is an endless stream of professional ad creatives that look like real user-generated content - the most effective type of social proof for e-commerce. You can generate hundreds of variations in minutes, test different styles, and always have fresh content for your ad campaigns.

Example output showing product seamlessly integrated into influencer-style background

How It Works

1. Product Image Input

The workflow begins by ingesting your product images from a designated source (like Google Drive, Dropbox, or your e-commerce platform). It automatically resizes and prepares the images for optimal composition.

2. Background Generation

Nano Banana Image Generator creates realistic influencer-style backgrounds based on your specifications. You can define parameters like setting (home, beach, cafe), lighting, and aesthetic style.

3. Image Composition

The workflow intelligently composites your product into the generated backgrounds, adjusting perspective, shadows, and lighting to create a natural look. Advanced AI ensures the product appears naturally placed in the scene.

Automated process flow from product image to finished ad creative

4. Output Delivery

Finished creatives are automatically saved to your preferred storage location and formatted for different platforms (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok etc.). You can set up additional automation to directly push these to your ad accounts.
