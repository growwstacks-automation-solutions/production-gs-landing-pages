What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of consistently creating SEO-optimized blog content that ranks well in search engines. Traditional content creation requires hours of keyword research, topic ideation, and manual writing - this workflow automates 80% of that process.

The system monitors Google Trends for emerging topics in your niche, uses AI to research and draft complete articles, then publishes them to WordPress with proper formatting and SEO metadata. It transforms what was traditionally a multi-hour process into an automated pipeline that requires only final human review.

The automated content creation pipeline from trend detection to publication

How It Works

1. Trend Detection

The workflow begins by querying Google Trends API for rising search queries in your specified categories and regions. It filters for topics with increasing traffic potential but low competition.

2. AI Content Research

Selected trends are sent to Perplexity AI for initial research, which gathers authoritative sources and identifies key subtopics. GPT then structures this into a comprehensive outline with target keywords.

3. Article Generation

Using the outline, GPT generates a complete draft with proper heading structure, keyword placement, and readability scoring. The AI incorporates FAQs and semantic terms naturally throughout.

AI-generated content with SEO optimization markers

4. WordPress Integration

The final draft is formatted for WordPress with proper H2/H3 headings, featured images, meta descriptions, and category assignment. The workflow can be configured to publish immediately or save as draft for review.

Pro tip: Set up a Slack notification when new drafts are ready so your team can review before publishing.
