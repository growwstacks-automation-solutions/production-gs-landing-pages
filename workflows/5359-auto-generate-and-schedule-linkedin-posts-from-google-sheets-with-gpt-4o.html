What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the constant struggle of maintaining an active LinkedIn presence. Instead of writing posts manually or forgetting to post altogether, it automatically transforms your content ideas from Google Sheets into polished LinkedIn posts using GPT-4o, then schedules them at optimal times.

The system is perfect for solopreneurs, marketers, and teams who brainstorm content in spreadsheets but struggle with execution. It turns your bullet-point ideas into fully-formed posts with proper formatting, hashtags, and engagement hooks - all while maintaining your unique brand voice.

AI transforms spreadsheet inputs into complete LinkedIn posts with proper formatting

How It Works

1. Input content ideas in Google Sheets

Your team adds post ideas, key messages, and scheduling preferences to a designated Google Sheet. This serves as your collaborative content calendar.

2. AI generates post drafts

GPT-4o analyzes each row's content, transforming bullet points into well-structured LinkedIn posts tailored to your specified tone and style guidelines.

3. Automatic scheduling

The workflow checks your specified publish dates/times and queues posts in LinkedIn according to your optimal posting schedule.
