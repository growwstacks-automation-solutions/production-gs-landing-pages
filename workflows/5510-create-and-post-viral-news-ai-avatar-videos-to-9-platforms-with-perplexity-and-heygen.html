How It Works

1. Trending News Research

Perplexity AI scans multiple news sources and social platforms to identify viral topics in your specified niche. The system evaluates engagement metrics and relevance to surface the most promising content opportunities.

2. Script Generation

The workflow automatically structures the research into compelling video scripts. It includes hooks, key points, and calls-to-action tailored for video format. You can customize tone, length, and style parameters.

3. AI Video Creation

HeyGen transforms the script into a professional video using your chosen AI avatar. The system handles lip-sync, expressions, and background elements. Multiple versions can be generated for A/B testing.

Configuration panel for customizing video output settings

4. Multi-Platform Distribution

The final videos are automatically formatted and published to nine platforms including YouTube, LinkedIn, and TikTok. Captions, hashtags, and descriptions are optimized for each platform's algorithm.
