What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of timely lead engagement by automatically sending personalized WhatsApp welcome messages whenever new sales leads are added to your Google Sheets. Manual follow-ups often result in delayed responses or missed opportunities, especially with high lead volumes.

The workflow connects your Google Sheets lead database with Rapiwa's WhatsApp Business API through n8n. When a new row appears in your designated leads sheet, the system instantly sends a templated yet personalized WhatsApp message containing the lead's name, relevant product/service details, and next steps.

Sample personalized WhatsApp welcome message triggered by the automation

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Google Sheets for new rows added to the leads tab. It identifies fresh entries based on timestamp or status columns you specify during setup.

2. Data extraction and validation

Essential contact details (name, phone number, product interest) are extracted from the sheet. The system validates phone numbers and formats them correctly for WhatsApp messaging, including proper country code handling.

Pro tip: Include a 'Lead Source' column in your sheet to enable different message templates based on where leads originate (website, social media, referrals, etc.)

3. Message personalization

Using your predefined message templates, the system inserts personalized variables from the spreadsheet. Dynamic fields like names, locations, or product references make each message feel individually crafted.

4. WhatsApp delivery via Rapiwa

The formatted message sends through Rapiwa's WhatsApp Business API, ensuring reliable delivery and compliance with WhatsApp's messaging policies. Delivery status logs back to your sheet for tracking.
