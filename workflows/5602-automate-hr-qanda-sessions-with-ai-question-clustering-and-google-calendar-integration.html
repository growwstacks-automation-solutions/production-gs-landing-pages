What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms chaotic HR Q&A sessions into structured, efficient knowledge-sharing opportunities. It solves the common problem of HR teams drowning in duplicate questions while employees wait days for responses. The workflow collects all employee questions through a centralized channel, uses AI to identify and group similar inquiries, then automatically schedules focused discussion sessions.

By automating the tedious parts of Q&A management, HR professionals can focus on crafting thoughtful responses rather than administrative tasks. The system creates a knowledge base of answered questions that grows smarter over time, reducing repeat inquiries by 30-50% in most implementations.

AI clustering groups similar employee questions for efficient response

How It Works

1. Question Collection

Employees submit questions through Slack, email, or web forms. The system timestamps and logs all submissions with optional anonymity settings. A confirmation message assures employees their question was received.

2. AI Clustering

Natural language processing analyzes question content to identify themes. Related questions are grouped into clusters with automatically generated topic labels. HR receives a prioritized dashboard showing question frequency and urgency indicators.

3. Session Scheduling

Based on cluster size and urgency, the system proposes Q&A session times. It checks Google Calendar availability for required attendees and books the optimal slot. Automated invites include prepared talking points from previous similar sessions.

System automatically schedules sessions based on question volume and urgency

4. Knowledge Base Update

After each session, the system archives answered questions in a searchable database. This becomes a self-service resource for employees, reducing future question volume. Unanswered questions trigger follow-up workflows for individual responses.
