What This Workflow Does

This automated content factory solves the constant challenge of creating professional, engaging LinkedIn content. It combines AI-powered research with branded visual creation to produce complete posts ready for scheduling.

The system researches trending topics in your industry using OpenAI, structures compelling posts with optimal formatting, and generates custom branded images through Replicate - all while maintaining your unique voice and style guidelines.

Sample output showing AI-generated LinkedIn post with custom branded imagery

How It Works

1. Topic Research Phase

OpenAI analyzes your industry landscape and identifies high-potential discussion topics based on current trends and your past high-performing content.

2. Content Generation

The AI drafts multiple post variations using proven LinkedIn frameworks (problem-solution, listicles, storytelling) while incorporating your brand voice parameters.

3. Visual Creation

Replicate generates custom images matching your brand colors, style, and content theme, ensuring visual consistency across all posts.
