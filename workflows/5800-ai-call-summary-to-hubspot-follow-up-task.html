How It Works

1. Transcript Processing

The workflow accepts call transcripts from any source (Zoom, Teams, Gong, etc.) and cleans the raw text by removing filler words, repetitions, and irrelevant sections. The AI identifies speakers and normalizes formatting for consistent processing.

2. Key Point Extraction

Natural language processing analyzes the conversation to identify decision points, customer pain points, commitments made, and action items. The system scores and ranks these elements by importance to your business context.

3. HubSpot Integration

The processed data gets structured into HubSpot's call log format with proper field mapping. The summary appears in the associated contact/company record's activity timeline, with relevant tags and custom properties updated automatically.

End-to-end workflow from call recording to HubSpot task creation

4. Follow-up Task Creation

Based on detected action items, the system creates tasks in HubSpot with pre-populated details, due dates (defaulting to 24-48 hours after the call), and links back to the full transcript. Tasks get assigned to the appropriate team member based on topic or role.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to flag urgent follow-ups with priority labels and notifications when high-value opportunities or critical issues are detected in calls.
