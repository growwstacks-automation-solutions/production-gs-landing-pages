What This Workflow Does

This automated client onboarding system transforms how businesses capture and process new client information. It creates customized embedded forms that adapt based on user responses, then intelligently routes the collected data to the appropriate teams and systems.

The workflow solves three major pain points: manual data entry errors, slow response times to new leads, and disjointed client information across systems. By automating these processes, businesses can reduce onboarding time by 60-80% while improving data accuracy and client satisfaction.

Dynamic form fields that adapt based on client responses

How It Works

1. Form Creation & Embedding

The workflow generates a customizable form with conditional logic that shows/hides fields based on previous answers. This form can be embedded on any website or landing page.

2. AI-Powered Triage

When a form is submitted, the workflow uses AI to categorize the lead based on their responses, urgency indicators, and business rules you define.

3. Automated Data Distribution

The system then routes the information to the appropriate systems (CRM, email marketing, project management) and team members based on the lead's category and needs.
