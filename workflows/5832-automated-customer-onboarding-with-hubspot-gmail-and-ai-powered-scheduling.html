What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your customer onboarding from a manual, error-prone process into a seamless experience. When a new contact enters your HubSpot CRM, the workflow instantly triggers a personalized onboarding sequence that combines email communication with intelligent scheduling.

The system automatically sends tailored welcome emails through Gmail, delivers relevant resources based on the customer's profile, and uses AI to schedule the perfect onboarding call - all while logging every interaction back to HubSpot for complete visibility.

How It Works

1. New Contact Detection

The workflow monitors your HubSpot CRM for new contacts or status changes. When a lead becomes a customer or enters your onboarding funnel, the automation immediately begins.

2. Personalized Email Sequence

Using merge tags and conditional logic, the system sends customized welcome emails through Gmail that address the customer by name and reference their specific product or service.

3. AI-Powered Scheduling

The workflow analyzes both your calendar availability and the customer's timezone to propose optimal meeting times. It handles the entire scheduling process automatically.
