What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually entering lead data from CSV files into Pipedrive CRM. Many businesses collect leads from events, webinars, or marketing campaigns in spreadsheet format, but transferring this data to their CRM consumes valuable time that could be spent selling.

The workflow automatically processes CSV files containing lead information, creates corresponding organization and person records in Pipedrive, and maps all relevant fields accurately. It handles the entire import process from file parsing to CRM updates without manual intervention, ensuring your sales team always has the freshest lead data at their fingertips.

Example CSV lead data showing contact information and custom fields that can be imported

How It Works

1. CSV File Processing

The workflow begins by reading the uploaded CSV file, parsing its contents, and validating the data structure. It checks for required fields and prepares the information for CRM import.

2. Data Transformation

Lead data undergoes cleaning and standardization to match Pipedrive's expected formats. This includes normalizing phone numbers, splitting full names into first/last components, and handling special characters.

3. CRM Record Creation

The workflow creates organization records first (when company data exists), then associates person records with their respective organizations. All custom fields from the CSV are mapped to corresponding Pipedrive fields.

What You'll Need

  1. An active Pipedrive account with API access enabled
  2. CSV files containing lead data in a consistent format
  3. Basic familiarity with n8n or willingness to learn
  4. A method to deliver CSV files to the workflow (email, cloud storage, etc.)

Pro tip: Structure your CSV with column headers that match Pipedrive field names for automatic mapping. Include a "Lead Source" field to track where each lead originated.
