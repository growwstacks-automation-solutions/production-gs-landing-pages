What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring customer data from spreadsheets into Salesforce. Many businesses maintain customer lists in Excel but need that data in Salesforce for CRM, sales tracking, and marketing automation.

The workflow automatically creates Salesforce accounts and associated contacts whenever new rows are added to your Excel 365 spreadsheet. It eliminates duplicate data entry, reduces human error, and ensures your Salesforce CRM always reflects your latest customer information.

Example of how Excel columns map to Salesforce account fields

How It Works

1. Excel Data Trigger

The workflow monitors your specified Excel 365 spreadsheet for new or updated rows. You can configure it to run on a schedule or trigger immediately when changes are detected.

2. Data Transformation

The workflow maps Excel columns to corresponding Salesforce fields, ensuring proper formatting for names, addresses, phone numbers, and other account details.

Data being transformed from Excel format to Salesforce structure

3. Account Creation

The workflow creates a new Salesforce account record for each valid row in your spreadsheet, including all mapped company information.

4. Contact Association

For accounts with contact information, the workflow automatically creates associated contact records linked to the new account.

Pro tip: Add a validation step to check for duplicate accounts before creation to maintain data integrity.
