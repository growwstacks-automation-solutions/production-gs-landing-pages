What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered automation transforms your job search from a time-consuming manual process into an efficient, targeted system. It continuously scans multiple job platforms, identifies ideal opportunities matching your criteria, crafts tailored applications using Gemini AI, and maintains organized tracking in Notion.

The workflow eliminates the repetitive tasks that consume hours each week - searching job boards, comparing listings, filling application forms, and tracking submissions. Instead, you get a streamlined pipeline of quality opportunities with automated follow-ups and centralized visibility into your entire job search process.

Automated job application tracking dashboard in Notion showing status, dates, and company details

How It Works

1. Multi-platform job search

The workflow simultaneously searches LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and other platforms based on your configured criteria (job title, location, salary range, etc.). It consolidates all results into a unified list after eliminating duplicates and low-quality matches.

2. AI-powered opportunity evaluation

Gemini AI analyzes each job description against your skills and preferences. It scores opportunities based on fit and highlights key requirements you should address in your application. The system can automatically filter out listings below your threshold score.

3. Automated application submission

For simple applications, the system auto-fills forms with your information. For complex applications requiring cover letters, Gemini AI generates tailored content that incorporates keywords from the job description while maintaining your unique voice.

Example of an AI-generated job application tailored to specific role requirements

4. Notion tracking system

Every application gets logged in your Notion dashboard with key details: company name, position, application date, status, and next steps. The system automatically updates statuses based on employer responses and schedules follow-up reminders.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google Gemini API access
  3. Notion account with database creation permissions
  4. Accounts on your preferred job platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
  5. Your professional information (resume, cover letter templates, etc.)

Pro tip: Before full automation, test the workflow with manual approval steps to ensure AI-generated applications meet your quality standards.
