What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you collect and manage Net Promoter Score (NPS) feedback by eliminating manual processes. It automatically sends NPS surveys to customers through GoHighLevel, captures responses via Gmail, and organizes all data in Notion for analysis.

The workflow solves three major pain points: inconsistent survey timing, manual response tracking, and disorganized feedback storage. By automating these steps, you gain real-time customer sentiment insights while saving 5-10 hours per week on administrative tasks.

Example of structured NPS data in Notion with automated categorization

How It Works

1. Automated Survey Trigger

The workflow initiates when a customer reaches a defined milestone in GoHighLevel (like 30 days post-purchase). It pulls their contact details and prepares a personalized NPS email.

2. Survey Delivery & Response Capture

Gmail sends the survey with tracking enabled. When customers reply, the workflow extracts their score and any comments, then categorizes them as Promoter, Passive, or Detractor.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to send different follow-ups based on the score - referral requests for Promoters, recovery sequences for Detractors.

3. Structured Data Storage

All responses automatically populate a Notion database with timestamped records, customer details, and sentiment tags. This creates a searchable feedback repository for your team.
