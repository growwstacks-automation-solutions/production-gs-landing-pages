What This Workflow Does

Client relationships often suffer from unintentional neglect - especially in service businesses where teams get busy with active projects. This automation solves the "out of sight, out of mind" problem by systematically identifying clients who haven't received communication in 14+ days and triggering personalized re-engagement sequences.

The system integrates GoHighLevel (for CRM and automation), Gmail (for personalized emails), Google Sheets (for tracking interactions), and Slack (for team notifications). It helps maintain consistent touchpoints with past clients, increasing retention rates and uncovering new project opportunities that might otherwise be missed.

Example of client re-engagement dashboard tracking inactive clients

How It Works

1. Weekly Client List Scan

The workflow starts by querying your GoHighLevel CRM every Monday morning to identify clients with no activity in the past 14 days. It checks for missing emails, calls, meetings, or project updates logged against each contact.

2. Personalized Email Creation

For each inactive client, the system generates a personalized email in Gmail using merge tags that pull the client's name, last project details, and other relevant CRM data. The email includes a friendly check-in message and call-to-action.

3. Activity Logging

Every outreach attempt gets logged in a Google Sheet with timestamps, email content previews, and status markers. This creates an audit trail of re-engagement efforts and helps track response rates.

Team notification in Slack about re-engagement actions taken

4. Team Notifications

The system sends a summary Slack message to your team channel listing which clients received follow-ups, along with quick links to their CRM profiles. This keeps everyone informed without manual reporting.
