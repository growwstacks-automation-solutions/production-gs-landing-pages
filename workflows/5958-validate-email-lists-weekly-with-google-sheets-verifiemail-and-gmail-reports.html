Validate email lists weekly with Google Sheets, VerifiEmail and Gmail reports

Automated email validation workflow that checks lists weekly, categorizes results, and updates your Google Sheets with verification reports

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the critical problem of maintaining clean email lists by performing weekly validations through VerifiEmail's verification service. It pulls email addresses from your Google Sheets, checks their validity, then categorizes each address as Valid, Invalid, or Risky before updating your master spreadsheet.

The system generates comprehensive reports showing validation results and bounce rates, which get emailed to your team via Gmail. This prevents wasted marketing spend on invalid addresses while protecting your sender reputation by removing problematic emails before campaigns.

Sample validation report showing categorized email addresses

How It Works

1. Weekly Trigger Activation

The workflow automatically runs every 7 days, pulling the latest email list from your designated Google Sheet. This scheduled execution ensures regular hygiene checks without manual intervention.

2. Email Verification Process

Each address gets sent to VerifiEmail's API for comprehensive validation. The service checks domain existence, mailbox availability, and spam trap risks - far more thorough than basic syntax checks.

3. Results Categorization

Addresses are tagged as Valid (deliverable), Invalid (undeliverable), or Risky (potential spam traps). This classification helps prioritize follow-up actions and segment your marketing lists appropriately.

Pro tip: Use the Risky category to review addresses that might be temporary mailboxes or spam traps before removing them completely.

4. Google Sheets Update

The workflow writes back validation statuses and timestamps to your spreadsheet, maintaining a historical record of list health over time. Color-coding makes results instantly visible.

5. Report Generation

A formatted Gmail report gets sent to designated recipients, showing validation statistics, bounce rate trends, and recommendations for list maintenance.
