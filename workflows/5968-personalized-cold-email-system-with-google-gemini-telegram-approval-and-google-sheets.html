What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized cold outreach while maintaining quality control. Traditional cold email campaigns either use generic templates (low response rates) or require excessive manual work to personalize (not scalable).

The system combines Google Gemini AI for intelligent message generation with Telegram-based human approval to create a hybrid approach. It pulls prospect data from Google Sheets, generates tailored emails using AI, sends them for quick mobile approval via Telegram, then dispatches the approved messages - all while logging responses back to your spreadsheet.

AI-generated email preview with Telegram approval interface

How It Works

1. Prospect Data Collection

The workflow begins by reading prospect information from your Google Sheet, including names, companies, email addresses, and any custom fields you've collected.

2. AI-Powered Email Generation

Google Gemini AI analyzes each prospect's information and generates a personalized email draft. The AI considers company details, job titles, and any available background information to craft relevant messaging.

3. Telegram Approval Workflow

The system sends the proposed email to a Telegram channel or chat where authorized team members can review and approve/reject each message with a single click.

4. Email Dispatch & Tracking

Approved emails are sent through your connected email service, while rejected ones are flagged for revision. All activity is logged back to Google Sheets for performance tracking.
