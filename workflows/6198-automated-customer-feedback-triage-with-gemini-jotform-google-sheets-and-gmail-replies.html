What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually processing customer feedback submissions collected through Jotform. Instead of letting valuable insights sit in an inbox or spreadsheet, the workflow automatically analyzes, categorizes, and routes each submission based on urgency and content.

The system uses Gemini AI to understand the sentiment and intent behind feedback, then takes appropriate action: sending immediate alerts for critical issues, generating knowledge-based responses for common questions, and logging all feedback in Google Sheets for trend analysis.

Example of a Jotform feedback submission that triggers the workflow

How It Works

1. New feedback submission triggers workflow

When a customer submits feedback through your Jotform, the workflow immediately captures all form data including contact information, feedback type, and detailed comments.

2. AI-powered sentiment and priority analysis

Gemini AI analyzes the text content to determine sentiment (positive/neutral/negative) and identifies key topics. Based on predefined rules, it assigns a priority score (low/medium/high).

Gemini AI analyzing feedback content and assigning priority scores

3. Automated routing and response

High-priority issues generate immediate email alerts to your support team with all context. Common questions trigger templated responses with helpful resources. All interactions are logged with timestamps.

4. Centralized tracking in Google Sheets

Every piece of feedback gets organized in a master spreadsheet with columns for customer info, feedback type, priority score, AI-generated summary, and resolution status for easy reporting.

Pro tip: Train Gemini with your product documentation and FAQ content to improve response accuracy for common questions.
