What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow transforms simple text prompts into engaging, viral-ready short-form videos by leveraging multiple AI technologies. It solves the challenge of content creators who need to produce high volumes of video content quickly without extensive video editing skills or resources.

The system combines GPT for script generation, Gemini for visual concept development, and JsonCut for automated video editing. The result is a complete video package ready for publishing on platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts.

Example output showing a completed video generated from text prompt

How It Works

1. Text Prompt Processing

The workflow begins by analyzing your text input using GPT to expand it into a complete video script with scene descriptions, dialogue, and suggested visuals.

2. Visual Concept Generation

Gemini AI then interprets the script to create visual concepts, suggesting appropriate imagery, color schemes, and composition for each scene.

3. Automated Video Assembly

JsonCut takes these elements and automatically assembles them into a cohesive video, adding transitions, background music, and text overlays based on best practices for viral content.
