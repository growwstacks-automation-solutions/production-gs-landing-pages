What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Stripe payments and KlickTipp email marketing, ensuring every new customer or subscription update triggers the right email sequence. When a customer completes a purchase in Stripe, their data is automatically transferred to KlickTipp with dynamic tags based on their purchase behavior.

The workflow solves the common problem of delayed or inconsistent customer onboarding. Manual transfers often lead to missed opportunities, but this automation ensures immediate follow-up while applying intelligent tagging for personalized email sequences. Subscription changes like upgrades, cancellations, or renewals are also handled automatically.

Automated data flow from Stripe purchases to KlickTipp email sequences

How It Works

1. Stripe Trigger

The workflow monitors Stripe for new customer events, including one-time purchases and subscription creations. Each trigger contains complete customer and order details.

2. Data Processing

Customer data is parsed to extract key information like email, name, product purchased, and subscription details. The system applies business rules to determine appropriate actions.

3. Dynamic Tagging

Based on the purchase details, the workflow assigns KlickTipp tags that determine which email sequences the customer enters. Tags can reflect product categories, price points, or customer segments.

Dynamic tagging based on purchase behavior ensures relevant email sequences

4. KlickTipp Integration

The processed data is sent to KlickTipp via API, creating or updating the contact with all relevant information. The system handles field mappings and ensures data consistency.
