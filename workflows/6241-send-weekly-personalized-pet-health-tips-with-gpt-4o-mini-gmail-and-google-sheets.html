What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of providing personalized pet care advice at scale. Veterinary clinics and pet businesses often struggle to maintain regular communication with clients while delivering customized recommendations for each animal. Manual creation of these messages consumes valuable staff time and limits outreach frequency.

The workflow automatically generates weekly pet health tips using AI, pulling pet profiles from Google Sheets and sending personalized emails through Gmail. It considers each animal's breed, age, location, and health history to create relevant advice - from seasonal allergy warnings to age-appropriate exercise suggestions.

Sample AI-generated pet health tip email showing personalized content

How It Works

1. Pet data collection

The system reads pet profiles from your Google Sheet, including essential details like breed, age, medical conditions, and owner contact information. This becomes the foundation for personalized content generation.

2. AI content generation

GPT-4o-mini analyzes each pet's profile to create customized health tips. The AI considers local weather patterns, seasonal hazards, and age-specific needs to generate practical advice owners can immediately implement.

3. Email personalization

The workflow merges AI-generated content with pet details to create visually appealing emails. Each message addresses the owner by name and includes their pet's name throughout for maximum personal connection.

Recommended Google Sheets structure for storing pet profile data

4. Automated scheduling

The system runs weekly (or at your chosen frequency) without manual intervention. You maintain full control and can pause or adjust the schedule as needed through the n8n interface.
