What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious problem of manually entering contact information from business cards received via Telegram. Sales teams, recruiters, and networking professionals often receive digital business cards through messaging apps but waste valuable time copying details into their CRM or spreadsheet.

The workflow automatically extracts structured contact data (name, company, phone, email, etc.) from business card images using OpenRouter's AI vision capabilities, then organizes the information in Google Sheets for easy access and CRM integration. It eliminates manual data entry errors while ensuring your contact database stays current.

AI vision processing extracts structured data from business card images

How It Works

1. Receiving business cards via Telegram

The workflow monitors your Telegram account for new messages containing images. When a business card photo arrives, it automatically triggers the extraction process.

2. AI vision processing with OpenRouter

The image is sent to OpenRouter's AI vision API which analyzes the business card layout, recognizes text fields, and extracts structured contact information with high accuracy.

3. Data validation and formatting

The extracted data goes through validation checks to ensure phone numbers, emails, and other fields meet standard formats before being recorded.

4. Google Sheets integration

The verified contact information is automatically added to your designated Google Sheet, with each field (name, company, phone, etc.) organized in separate columns for easy CRM import.
