What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of Jira backlog bloat - where outdated, low-priority tickets accumulate and make it difficult for engineering teams to focus on what truly matters. The workflow automatically identifies stale tickets, applies intelligent prioritization using GPT-4 analysis, and delivers clear reports to your team via Slack and email.

By implementing this system, product and engineering teams can save 3-5 hours per week typically spent manually reviewing backlogs. The AI-powered analysis provides objective recommendations for ticket prioritization based on your team's historical data and current sprint goals.

Example of automated backlog health dashboard generated by the workflow

How It Works

1. Daily Jira backlog scan

The workflow connects to your Jira instance each morning to scan for tickets that meet your predefined "stale" criteria (e.g., no activity for 30+ days, low priority with no sprint assignment).

2. GPT-4 analysis and recommendations

Identified tickets are analyzed by GPT-4, which considers project context, historical team velocity, and current priorities to recommend whether to archive, reprioritize, or flag for discussion.

3. Automated Slack and email reporting

Concise reports are automatically sent to designated Slack channels and stakeholders' emails, highlighting key actions needed and providing direct links to relevant tickets.
