What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining fresh, relevant content on WordPress sites by automatically generating articles from news sources. It eliminates the manual effort of researching, writing, and publishing content while ensuring your site stays current with industry developments.

The workflow combines news aggregation with AI-powered content creation and social media distribution. It sources relevant articles, processes them through Claude AI to create WordPress-ready content, and automatically shares the published posts on LinkedIn - creating a complete content pipeline with minimal human intervention.

Automated content generation process flow

How It Works

1. News Source Integration

The workflow connects to news APIs or RSS feeds to gather relevant articles based on your configured topics and keywords. This ensures you're always working with fresh, topical content.

2. AI Content Processing

Claude AI analyzes each article, extracting key information and rewriting it in your preferred style and tone. The AI can summarize, expand, or adapt content to match your editorial guidelines.

3. Editorial Approval

Before publishing, the system sends the AI-generated draft for human review. Editors can approve, edit, or reject content, maintaining quality control while benefiting from automation.

4. WordPress Publishing

Approved articles are automatically formatted and published to your WordPress site with proper categories, tags, and metadata for SEO optimization.

5. LinkedIn Distribution

Each published article triggers an automatic LinkedIn post, complete with optimized captions and hashtags, amplifying your content's reach.
