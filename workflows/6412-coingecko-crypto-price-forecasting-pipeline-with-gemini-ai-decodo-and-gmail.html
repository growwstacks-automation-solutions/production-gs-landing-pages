What This Workflow Does

This automated pipeline solves the challenge of manually tracking cryptocurrency prices and generating reliable forecasts. It combines CoinGecko's market data with Gemini AI's predictive capabilities to deliver actionable insights directly to your inbox via Gmail or Decodo notifications.

The workflow eliminates hours of manual research by automatically scraping price data, analyzing trends with AI, and delivering formatted reports on a schedule you define. Perfect for traders, analysts, and crypto enthusiasts who need regular market intelligence without the manual work.

Example of AI-generated crypto price analysis and forecasting

How It Works

1. Data Collection from CoinGecko

The workflow begins by scraping CoinGecko for the latest price data, market caps, and trading volumes of your selected cryptocurrencies. It captures both current metrics and historical trends to feed into the AI analysis.

2. AI Analysis with Gemini

The collected data is processed by Gemini AI, which applies machine learning models to identify patterns, predict future price movements, and generate human-readable analysis. The AI considers technical indicators, market sentiment, and historical performance.

3. Report Generation

The system compiles the AI's findings into a structured report, highlighting key predictions, confidence levels, and recommended actions. Charts and visualizations are automatically generated for easy interpretation.

4. Delivery via Decodo & Gmail

Final reports are delivered through your preferred channels - either as formatted emails via Gmail or as notifications through Decodo's platform. The workflow can be scheduled to run daily, weekly, or on any custom interval.

  1. Download the JSON template file

  7. Set your preferred schedule for updates
