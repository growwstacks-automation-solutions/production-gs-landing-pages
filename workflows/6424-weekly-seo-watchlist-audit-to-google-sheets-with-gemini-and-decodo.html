What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking SEO performance across dozens or hundreds of pages. It combines Gemini AI's content analysis capabilities with Decodo's reliable content extraction to create comprehensive weekly audits of your SEO watchlist pages.

The system automatically fetches page content, analyzes it for SEO factors using Gemini's AI models, structures the findings, and exports everything to Google Sheets for easy team access. This replaces hours of manual work with a scheduled, consistent process that catches issues early and tracks improvements over time.

Sample output showing structured SEO audit data in Google Sheets

How It Works

1. Watchlist Input

The workflow starts with your predefined list of URLs to monitor, which can be pulled from Google Search Console, your CMS, or a manual spreadsheet. This becomes the foundation for all subsequent analysis.

2. Content Extraction with Decodo

Decodo fetches clean, structured content from each page while handling complex page structures, dynamic elements, and login-walled content that traditional scrapers struggle with. This ensures your analysis works with complete content.

3. AI Analysis with Gemini

Gemini processes the extracted content to evaluate SEO factors including semantic relevance, content gaps, optimization opportunities, and technical issues. It generates specific recommendations tailored to each page's content and your target keywords.

Gemini AI evaluating page content against SEO best practices

4. Data Structuring

The workflow organizes findings into clear categories (technical SEO, content quality, on-page elements) and assigns priority scores based on potential impact. This structured data feeds directly into your reporting.

5. Google Sheets Export

All analyzed data exports to a designated Google Sheet with timestamped entries, creating a searchable historical record of your SEO performance. The sheet includes conditional formatting to highlight urgent issues.
