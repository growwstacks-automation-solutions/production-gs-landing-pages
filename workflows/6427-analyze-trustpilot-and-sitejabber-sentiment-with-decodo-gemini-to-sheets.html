What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually monitoring and interpreting customer reviews across multiple platforms. Businesses often struggle to extract actionable insights from hundreds of Trustpilot and Sitejabber reviews, wasting hours on manual analysis while missing important trends.

The workflow automatically scrapes new reviews using Decodo, analyzes sentiment with Gemini AI, and exports structured data to Google Sheets. It transforms unstructured review text into quantifiable metrics like sentiment score, emotion detection, and key themes - enabling data-driven decisions about customer experience improvements.

Sample output showing categorized sentiment data with scores and themes

How It Works

1. Review scraping with Decodo

The workflow starts by scraping the latest reviews from your Trustpilot and Sitejabber business pages using Decodo's reliable web scraping service. It handles pagination, anti-bot measures, and data extraction automatically.

2. Sentiment analysis with Gemini

Each review passes through Gemini AI for advanced sentiment analysis. The AI evaluates emotional tone, identifies positive/negative phrases, and assigns a sentiment score from -1 (very negative) to 1 (very positive).

3. Data enrichment and categorization

The system extracts key themes (e.g., "shipping speed", "product quality") and detects urgent issues. It can flag reviews mentioning specific competitors or containing complaint keywords for immediate attention.

4. Google Sheets export

Structured data exports to your Google Sheet with columns for sentiment score, detected emotions, key phrases, review date, and platform. Conditional formatting highlights negative reviews requiring follow-up.
