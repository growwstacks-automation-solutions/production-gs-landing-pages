What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking competitor keywords across multiple websites. Traditional SEO research requires hours of manual analysis, copying data between tools, and organizing findings in spreadsheets.

The workflow combines Decodo's web scraping capabilities with GPT-4.1-mini's natural language processing to intelligently extract and categorize keywords from competitor sites. Results are automatically compiled in Google Sheets with semantic analysis, saving SEO teams 5-10 hours per week on manual research.

Decodo's web scraping capabilities extract raw content from competitor sites

How It Works

1. Competitor website analysis

The workflow starts by scraping content from specified competitor URLs using Decodo. It extracts page titles, headers, body content, and metadata.

2. AI-powered keyword extraction

GPT-4.1-mini processes the scraped content to identify primary keywords, secondary terms, and semantic relationships between concepts.

3. Competitive gap analysis

The AI compares extracted keywords against your target keyword list to identify opportunities where competitors rank but you don't.

4. Google Sheets integration

All findings are automatically organized in a Google Sheet with separate tabs for primary keywords, long-tail variations, and gap analysis.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run weekly to track how competitor keyword strategies evolve over time.
