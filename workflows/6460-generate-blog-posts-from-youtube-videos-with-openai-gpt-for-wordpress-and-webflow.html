What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of repurposing video content into written format. Content creators often spend hours manually transcribing videos and rewriting them as blog posts. This workflow eliminates that manual work by automatically converting your YouTube videos into polished, SEO-optimized articles ready for WordPress or Webflow.

The system monitors your YouTube channel for new uploads, extracts the transcript, uses AI to transform it into a well-structured article, and publishes it to your CMS. This creates a content flywheel where each video becomes multiple pieces of content, maximizing your content ROI and improving search visibility.

The automated workflow process from YouTube video to published blog post

How It Works

1. YouTube video detection

The workflow starts by monitoring your YouTube channel for new video uploads. You can set it to check at regular intervals (daily, weekly, etc.) or trigger it manually when you publish new content.

2. Transcript extraction

When a new video is detected, the system automatically extracts the full transcript including timestamps. This serves as the raw material for your blog post.

3. AI content transformation

OpenAI GPT processes the transcript, converting it into a properly formatted blog post with headings, paragraphs, and optimized structure. You can customize the AI prompts to match your brand voice and content style.

Example of AI-transformed content from video transcript to blog post

4. CMS publishing

The final step automatically publishes the generated content to your WordPress or Webflow site as a draft or live post, complete with featured images, tags, and proper formatting.

Pro tip: Add a step to cross-link your blog posts back to the original YouTube video to boost engagement on both platforms.
