What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates reactions to posts in your Telegram channels using multiple bot accounts in rotation, combined with AI-powered sentiment analysis through GPT-5-mini. It solves the challenge of maintaining consistent engagement in Telegram communities without manual intervention.

The system intelligently distributes reactions across multiple bot accounts to appear organic, while using AI to determine appropriate reaction types based on post content. This prevents detection as spam while boosting visibility and engagement metrics.

Example of automated reactions distributed across different bot accounts

How It Works

1. New Post Detection

The workflow monitors your Telegram channel for new posts using the Telegram API. When a new message is detected, it triggers the automation sequence.

2. Content Analysis with GPT-5-mini

The post content is analyzed by GPT-5-mini to determine sentiment and appropriate reaction types. This ensures reactions are contextually relevant rather than random.

3. Bot Rotation System

The workflow selects a bot account from your configured pool using a rotation algorithm that distributes activity evenly across accounts to maintain natural patterns.

4. Reaction Execution

The selected bot account adds reactions based on the AI's recommendation, with randomized delays to mimic human behavior patterns.
