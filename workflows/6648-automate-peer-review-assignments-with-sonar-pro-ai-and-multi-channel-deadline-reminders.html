What This Workflow Does

This automation solution tackles the time-consuming process of managing peer reviews in educational settings. It automatically assigns review tasks to students, tracks submission deadlines, and sends multi-channel reminders via email and messaging platforms. The integration with Sonar Pro AI adds intelligent evaluation capabilities that help maintain grading consistency across reviewers.

By automating these administrative tasks, educators can focus more on teaching while ensuring students receive timely feedback. The system reduces manual coordination by up to 80% while improving compliance with review deadlines through automated reminders.

Example of peer review assignment distribution in an educational platform

How It Works

1. Automated Review Assignment

The workflow pulls student data from your LMS or spreadsheet and intelligently pairs reviewers based on predefined criteria like skill level or topic expertise. It avoids conflicts by ensuring students don't review their own work or the same peer repeatedly.

2. Deadline Management

Built-in calendar integration tracks all review deadlines. The system calculates optimal reminder timing based on assignment complexity and sends notifications through multiple channels to maximize response rates.

3. AI-Powered Evaluation

Sonar Pro AI analyzes submitted reviews for consistency, depth, and constructive feedback quality. It flags potential outliers for instructor review and provides standardized grading suggestions.
