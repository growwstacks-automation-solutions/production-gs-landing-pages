How It Works

1. Contract Term Extraction

The workflow ingests signed contracts and extracts key obligations using AI. It creates a structured database of requirements including post frequency, content guidelines, disclosure rules, and performance metrics.

2. Instagram Content Monitoring

n8n polls influencer accounts at scheduled intervals, capturing new posts and stories. The system checks for deleted content and edits, which often indicate attempted compliance fixes.

3. AI-Powered Compliance Analysis

Claude AI compares each post against the contract terms, evaluating: FTC disclosure placement, branded hashtag usage, competitor product visibility, and sentiment alignment. The AI generates confidence scores for potential violations.

Automated Slack alerts provide violation details with direct links to the problematic content

4. Smart Alert Routing

Violations are categorized by severity and routed to appropriate teams via Slack. Critical issues (like missing disclosures) trigger immediate alerts, while minor deviations create summary reports. The system includes escalation paths for unresolved issues.

5. Compliance Reporting

The workflow maintains a real-time dashboard of compliance metrics across all influencers. It generates weekly reports showing violation trends, influencer performance scores, and estimated risk exposure.

Pro tip: Configure the AI to flag potential issues before posts go live by integrating with influencer content approval platforms like CreatorIQ or Upfluence.
