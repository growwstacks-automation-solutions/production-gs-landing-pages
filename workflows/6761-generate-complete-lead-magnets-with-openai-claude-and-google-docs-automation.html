What This Workflow Does

Creating high-quality lead magnets is time-consuming and often becomes a bottleneck in marketing campaigns. This workflow solves that problem by automating the entire process - from content generation to final formatted document.

The system leverages OpenAI and Claude AI to generate different sections of your lead magnet (checklists, guides, templates) based on your topic inputs, then automatically formats them into a professionally designed Google Doc ready for download or email delivery.

Example of an AI-generated lead magnet with proper formatting

How It Works

1. Topic Input and AI Content Generation

The workflow begins by accepting your lead magnet topic and key points. It then distributes content generation tasks between OpenAI and Claude AI based on which model performs best for each content type.

2. Content Assembly and Quality Control

Generated content sections are combined and reviewed for consistency. The system checks for duplicate content and ensures proper transitions between sections.

3. Google Docs Formatting

The workflow applies your brand styling automatically - setting proper headings, fonts, colors, and spacing according to your predefined template in Google Docs.
