What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered workflow revolutionizes outbound sales by combining Claude AI's natural language capabilities with Explorium's data enrichment to generate highly targeted B2B leads with personalized outreach emails. It solves the time-consuming process of manual prospect research while dramatically improving email response rates through hyper-relevant messaging.

The system transforms simple English queries like "Find SaaS companies in healthcare with 50-200 employees using competitor X" into fully enriched prospect lists with personalized email drafts ready for review. This eliminates hours of manual research while ensuring every outreach message demonstrates genuine understanding of each recipient's business context.

AI-generated personalized outreach email referencing specific company technologies and pain points

How It Works

1. Natural Language Query Processing

Claude AI interprets your plain English description of ideal customers (industry, size, technologies used, etc.) and converts it into structured search parameters for lead generation.

2. Explorium Data Enrichment

The workflow sends initial prospect matches to Explorium, which appends missing firmographic data, technographics, intent signals, and organizational hierarchies to each lead record.

3. AI-Powered Email Personalization

Claude analyzes the enriched lead data to craft emails that reference specific technologies, company events, or industry trends relevant to each prospect, creating messages that demonstrate genuine understanding.

End-to-end workflow from natural language query to personalized outreach

4. Gmail Sequence Automation

The system automatically schedules follow-up emails based on prospect engagement, maintaining context across the entire outreach sequence while adapting messages based on opens/clicks.
