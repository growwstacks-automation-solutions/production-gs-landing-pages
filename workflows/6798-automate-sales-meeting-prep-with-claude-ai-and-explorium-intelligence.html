What This Workflow Does

Sales professionals waste countless hours manually researching prospects before meetings. This workflow solves that problem by automatically generating comprehensive briefing documents that combine AI-powered insights with business intelligence data.

The system pulls data from multiple sources, analyzes it using Claude AI for natural language processing, and structures the findings into ready-to-use meeting briefs. Each morning, your sales team receives personalized intelligence packets for their scheduled meetings - saving 2-3 hours of prep time per meeting.

Sample output showing AI-generated meeting preparation content

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow starts by pulling your sales team's calendar events and identifying upcoming meetings. For each meeting, it extracts attendee information and meeting context.

2. Intelligence Gathering

Using Explorium Intelligence, the system collects relevant business data about meeting participants - including company financials, news mentions, and industry trends.

3. AI Analysis

Claude AI processes the raw data into actionable insights, highlighting key talking points, potential pain points, and strategic recommendations for each meeting.

AI processing step in the automation workflow

4. Brief Generation

The system compiles all information into a professionally formatted PDF or email brief, organized by meeting priority and relevance.

5. Delivery

Completed briefs are automatically distributed to sales reps via email or saved to a shared drive 2 hours before each scheduled meeting.
