What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of documenting and acting on meeting outcomes. Most professionals waste hours each week manually reviewing recordings, taking notes, and creating follow-up tasks. This workflow eliminates that manual work by automatically generating concise AI summaries from Fathom recordings and creating structured tasks in Dart.

The system captures key discussion points, decisions made, and action items identified during meetings. It then formats this information into professional summaries stored in your documentation system while simultaneously creating properly assigned tasks with relevant context and deadlines.

AI-generated meeting summary showing key points and action items

How It Works

1. Trigger from Fathom recording

The workflow activates whenever a new meeting recording becomes available in Fathom. It captures the recording URL, participant list, and meeting metadata automatically.

2. AI processing and summarization

The system sends the recording to an AI processing engine that analyzes the conversation, identifies key discussion points, extracts decisions, and highlights action items with assigned owners.

3. Summary document creation

The AI generates a professionally formatted summary that gets saved to your designated Dart document repository. The summary includes timestamps for important moments and links back to the original recording.

Automatically created task in Dart with meeting context

4. Task generation in Dart

The workflow creates follow-up tasks in Dart based on action items identified during the meeting. Each task includes relevant context from the discussion, assigned owners, and suggested deadlines.

Pro tip: Configure your workflow to tag tasks based on meeting type (e.g., "Client Call" or "Team Sync") for better organization in Dart.
