What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation transforms your WhatsApp Business account into an intelligent customer support assistant. When customers message your business WhatsApp number, the workflow automatically processes their inquiry using GPT-4 Mini AI to understand the question's intent, searches your Google Sheets knowledge base for accurate answers, and sends a personalized response through the Rapiwa API.

The system handles common support queries 24/7 without human intervention, freeing your team to focus on complex issues. It maintains conversation context, can escalate to human agents when needed, and logs all interactions in your spreadsheet for quality control and analytics.

AI-powered WhatsApp support conversation example showing natural language understanding

How It Works

1. WhatsApp Message Receipt

The Rapiwa API detects incoming WhatsApp messages and triggers the n8n workflow. The system captures the customer's phone number, message content, and any media attachments.

2. AI Intent Analysis

GPT-4 Mini processes the natural language message to determine the customer's intent, extracting key entities and classifying the query type (order status, product question, etc.).

3. Knowledge Base Search

The workflow searches your Google Sheets database for the most relevant answer based on the AI's analysis, prioritizing accuracy and completeness of information.

Structured Google Sheets knowledge base with categorized support information

4. Response Generation

The AI crafts a natural-sounding response incorporating the knowledge base information, maintaining conversational tone while ensuring accuracy.

5. WhatsApp Delivery

The finalized response is sent back through Rapiwa API to the customer's WhatsApp, completing the automated support cycle in seconds.

Pro tip: Structure your Google Sheets with separate tabs for different product categories or question types to improve search accuracy and response relevance.
