What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually monitoring Facebook comments for brand sentiment. Marketing and PR teams often struggle to keep up with comment volume, missing critical customer feedback that could indicate emerging issues or opportunities.

The workflow automatically analyzes every new Facebook post comment using GPT-4's advanced natural language processing. It categorizes sentiment (positive, negative, neutral), flags concerning trends, sends real-time Slack alerts to your team, and compiles comprehensive reports in Google Sheets and Outlook.

Sample sentiment analysis dashboard showing positive/negative comment trends

How It Works

1. Facebook comment monitoring

The workflow connects to your Facebook Page via the API, scanning new comments as they're posted. It captures the comment text, author, and timestamp for analysis.

2. GPT-4 sentiment analysis

Each comment gets processed by GPT-4, which evaluates emotional tone and intent. The AI assigns a sentiment score and categorizes comments based on your configured thresholds.

3. Real-time Slack alerts

When negative sentiment exceeds your defined threshold, the system immediately notifies your team via Slack. Alerts include the problematic comment and suggested response templates.

Example Slack alert showing a negative comment needing attention

4. Automated reporting

The system logs all analyzed comments in Google Sheets with sentiment scores and trends. Scheduled Outlook emails provide executive summaries with key metrics and visualizations.

Pro tip: Configure different sentiment thresholds for various post types - product announcements may warrant more sensitivity than general content.
