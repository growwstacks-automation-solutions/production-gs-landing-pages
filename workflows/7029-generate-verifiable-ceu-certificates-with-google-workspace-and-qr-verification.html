What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually creating and verifying professional training certificates with Continuing Education Units (CEUs). Traditional certificate processes are time-consuming, prone to errors, and lack verification mechanisms - making it difficult to confirm authenticity.

The workflow automatically generates professional certificates in Google Docs format, embeds unique QR codes for instant verification, and delivers them to recipients via email - all while maintaining a secure digital record of issued credentials. It eliminates manual data entry while adding fraud prevention through the verification system.

Professional certificate template with embedded QR verification code

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls attendee information from your training management system or spreadsheet, including names, course details, and CEU credits earned.

2. Certificate Generation

Using Google Docs templates, the system creates personalized certificates with all attendee details automatically populated in the correct fields.

3. QR Code Embedding

A unique QR code is generated for each certificate linking to a verification page that confirms the credential's authenticity when scanned.

4. PDF Conversion & Delivery

Completed certificates are converted to PDF format and automatically emailed to recipients with professional branding and instructions for verification.

CEU (Continuing Education Unit) and CPE (Continuing Professional Education) both measure professional development, but CEUs are typically used across various industries while CPEs are specific to accounting and finance professions. The automation can handle both credit types by adjusting calculation formulas in the template. The system automatically applies the correct terminology and conversion factors based on the course type selected in your data source.
