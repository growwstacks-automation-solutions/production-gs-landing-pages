What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you discover sales leads by monitoring digital footprints across the web. Instead of manual searches or expensive ad campaigns, it automatically identifies potential customers based on their online activity, qualifies them using AI analysis, and creates enriched lead records in Airtable.

The system listens for intent signals like product comparisons, feature requests, or competitor complaints across social media, forums, and review sites. By focusing on these organic conversations, you gain access to warm leads who are already researching solutions in your space - resulting in 3-5x higher conversion rates than cold outreach.

Example of digital footprint data being analyzed for lead signals

How It Works

1. Digital Footprint Monitoring

The workflow continuously scans predefined sources for mentions of your product category, features, or competitors. It uses natural language processing to identify genuine purchase intent rather than casual mentions.

2. AI-Powered Lead Scoring

Decodo analyzes each potential lead's digital footprint to assess relevance, engagement level, and buying signals. It assigns a score based on factors like discussion frequency, sentiment, and alignment with your ideal customer profile.

3. Automated Enrichment

The system gathers additional context about each lead including contact details, social profiles, and company information. This enrichment happens automatically, saving hours of manual research per lead.

4. Airtable Integration

Qualified leads flow into your Airtable base with all relevant context attached. The workflow creates complete prospect records including conversation history, engagement metrics, and contact information.
