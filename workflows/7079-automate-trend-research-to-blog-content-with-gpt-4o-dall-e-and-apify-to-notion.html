What This Workflow Does

This automation acts as your personal "AI Editor-in-Chief," transforming raw trend research into polished blog drafts complete with AI-generated images. It solves the content creation bottleneck by eliminating manual research, drafting, and image sourcing - delivering ready-to-publish drafts directly to your Notion workspace.

The workflow scrapes trending topics using Apify, analyzes them with GPT-4o to generate three distinct blog post drafts (each with title, outline, and full content), creates matching DALL-E images for each post, and organizes everything in Notion with proper formatting. What typically takes content teams 8-10 hours per post is reduced to minutes with zero manual intervention.

AI-generated blog drafts with images automatically formatted in Notion

How It Works

1. Trend Research Collection

The workflow starts by scraping trending topics from your specified sources using Apify. It collects relevant data points including search volume, social mentions, and related queries to identify high-potential content opportunities.

2. AI Content Generation

GPT-4o analyzes the research data to create three distinct blog post approaches for each trend. Each draft includes a compelling title, SEO-optimized outline, and fully written content in your brand voice.

3. Visual Content Creation

DALL-E generates custom images for each blog post based on the content themes. The AI creates visually cohesive artwork that matches the post's topic and style requirements.

Automation process from trend research to published drafts

4. Notion Integration

The completed drafts (including text content and images) are automatically formatted and published to your specified Notion database. Each entry includes proper rich text formatting, tags, and metadata for easy organization.
