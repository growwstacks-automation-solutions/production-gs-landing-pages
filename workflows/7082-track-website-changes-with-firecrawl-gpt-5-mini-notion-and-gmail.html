What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming problem of manually checking websites for updates. It continuously monitors specified web pages for changes using Firecrawl, processes the differences with GPT-5-Mini to identify meaningful updates, and delivers concise notifications to your Gmail or Notion database.

For businesses tracking competitors, market trends, or regulatory updates, this workflow eliminates hours of manual monitoring while ensuring you never miss important changes. The AI component filters out insignificant updates like formatting changes, focusing only on substantive content modifications that matter to your operations.

Configure Firecrawl to monitor specific website sections for changes

How It Works

1. Website Monitoring Setup

The workflow begins with Firecrawl configured to monitor specific URLs at your chosen frequency (daily, weekly, etc.). You can specify exact page sections to monitor or track entire sites.

2. Change Detection & Analysis

When changes are detected, the raw HTML differences are sent to GPT-5-Mini which analyzes the modifications, determines their significance, and generates a human-readable summary of important updates.

AI analysis of website changes to identify meaningful updates

3. Notification Delivery

The processed updates are then formatted and delivered to your preferred destinations - either as email alerts in Gmail or as organized records in a Notion database for team collaboration.

Pro tip: Configure different notification channels for different types of changes - send critical updates to email while logging all changes to Notion for reference.
