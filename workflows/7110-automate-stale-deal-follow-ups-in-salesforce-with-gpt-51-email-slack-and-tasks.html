What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical sales challenge of stale deals - opportunities that haven't moved forward in your pipeline. By combining Salesforce data with AI-powered messaging through Slack and email, it ensures timely, personalized follow-ups that adapt to each deal's unique context.

The system runs daily to identify deals that meet your "stale" criteria (typically 7-14 days without activity), then generates context-aware follow-up messages using GPT-5.1. These messages reference specific deal details and suggest clear next steps, while automatically creating follow-up tasks for your team.

Automated alerts help sales teams prioritize follow-ups based on deal urgency and context

How It Works

1. Daily Opportunity Scan

The workflow runs every morning at 8:00 AM, querying Salesforce for opportunities that haven't been updated beyond your configured stale_days threshold. It evaluates each deal's stage, last activity date, and owner to determine appropriate follow-up actions.

2. AI-Powered Message Generation

GPT-5.1 analyzes each stale deal's history, notes, and context to craft personalized follow-up messages. The AI considers the prospect's industry, pain points discussed, and where they are in your sales process to generate relevant, natural-sounding outreach.

3. Multi-Channel Delivery

Based on deal priority and stage, the system routes follow-ups through the most effective channels - email for formal communication, Slack for urgent alerts, and Salesforce tasks for internal action items. All activities are automatically logged in Salesforce.

Slack notifications keep teams aligned on critical follow-ups with AI-generated context

Pro tip: Configure different stale_days thresholds for each deal stage - shorter for late-stage opportunities, longer for early nurturing.
