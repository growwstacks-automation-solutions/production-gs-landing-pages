What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the time-consuming and inconsistent manual process of tagging WordPress posts. It automatically analyzes your post content using Claude Opus 4.5's advanced AI capabilities, generates the most relevant tags based on semantic understanding, and applies them to your WordPress posts through the REST API.

The system goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand context, identify key themes, and suggest tags that will maximize your content's discoverability. It can handle both creating new tags and applying existing ones from your WordPress taxonomy, maintaining consistency across your entire content library.

AI content analysis process for generating optimal tags

How It Works

1. Content Analysis Phase

The workflow begins by extracting the full text of your WordPress post, including headings, body content, and metadata. Claude Opus 4.5 analyzes this content to identify key themes, concepts, and relationships that human editors might overlook.

2. Tag Generation

Based on the analysis, the AI generates 5-8 highly relevant tag suggestions. The system weights these by importance and checks them against your existing WordPress tags to maintain consistency in your taxonomy.

3. Tag Application

The workflow then uses the WordPress REST API to either apply existing tags or create new ones as needed. It can be configured to require human approval or run fully automatically depending on your preferences.
