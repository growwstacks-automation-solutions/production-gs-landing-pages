What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating inclusive beauty and fashion content at scale. Many brands struggle to represent diverse skin tones in their Instagram marketing due to the time and cost of manual content creation. The workflow automatically adapts trending content to showcase products and styles across various complexions.

Using GPT-4o and DALL-E, the system analyzes Instagram trends and generates personalized variations showing how they appear on different skin tones. The Slack integration enables team collaboration for reviewing and approving content before posting. This ensures marketing materials remain authentic while benefiting from automation's efficiency.

AI-generated variations showing makeup trends adapted for different complexions

How It Works

1. Trend Analysis

GPT-4o analyzes current Instagram beauty/fashion trends from your specified sources. It identifies key visual elements, color schemes, and styles that are performing well.

2. Content Personalization

The AI creates multiple variations of each trend, adjusting for different skin tones, undertones, and facial features. It generates accompanying captions explaining how the trend adapts to various complexions.

3. Visual Generation

DALL-E produces high-quality images showing the personalized variations. The AI understands nuanced requests like "show this eyeshadow look on deep skin with cool undertones."

n8n workflow interface managing the content generation process

4. Team Collaboration

Generated content gets posted to designated Slack channels for team review. Members can comment, approve, or request adjustments before final publishing.

5. Scheduled Posting

Approved content gets queued for Instagram posting according to your content calendar. The system maintains consistent posting frequency with diverse representation.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON workflow file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your OpenAI API credentials in the workflow settings
  3. Connect your Slack and Instagram accounts to n8n
  4. Set your trend sources and skin tone parameters
  5. Test the workflow with sample prompts
  6. Schedule regular executions based on your content calendar

Pro tip: Start with broad skin tone categories, then refine prompts based on which variations perform best with your audience.
