What This Workflow Does

Cold outreach is time-consuming and often ineffective when using generic templates. This workflow solves both problems by automating personalized email creation using AI. It transforms your Google Sheets lead list into tailored outreach drafts ready for review in Gmail, saving hours per campaign while improving response rates.

The system maintains human oversight (emails go to drafts) while handling the heavy lifting of personalization. Sales teams can focus on building relationships rather than manual email composition, scaling their outreach efforts without sacrificing quality or personal touch.

AI-powered email generation from spreadsheet data

How It Works

1. Trigger the workflow

The process starts with a manual trigger, allowing you to control when emails are generated. This prevents accidental sends and lets you batch process leads at optimal times.

2. Read lead data from Google Sheets

The workflow pulls your prospect list from a designated Google Sheet, including all relevant personalization fields like company name, role, and any custom notes you've added.

3. Filter unsent contacts

Smart filtering ensures you only generate emails for new leads or those who haven't received outreach yet, preventing duplicate messages.

Pro tip: Add a 'Last Contacted' column to your sheet to enable sophisticated filtering based on follow-up timing.

4. Generate personalized email bodies

GPT-4o-mini crafts unique email content for each recipient based on your template and the lead's specific data points, creating messages that feel hand-written.

5. Create Gmail drafts

Completed emails are saved as drafts in your Gmail account for final review and sending, maintaining human control over the outreach process.
