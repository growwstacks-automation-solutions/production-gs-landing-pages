Transform hours of manual competitor research into automated AI-powered insights with this n8n workflow template

What This Workflow Does

SEO professionals and marketers spend hours manually searching keywords to analyze competitor content. Copying and pasting SERP results into spreadsheets is time-consuming and prone to human error. This workflow automates the entire process using Decodo for SERP data extraction and Google Gemini for AI-powered content analysis.

The system automatically collects top-ranking pages for your target keywords, analyzes their content structure, identifies patterns in ranking factors, and generates actionable insights. What traditionally took 4-5 hours per keyword cluster can now be completed in minutes with consistent, data-driven results.

AI-powered SERP analysis dashboard showing competitor content patterns

How It Works

1. Keyword Input

The workflow starts by accepting your target keywords from a spreadsheet, database, or direct input. You can schedule regular updates or trigger analyses based on ranking changes.

2. SERP Data Collection

Decodo extracts complete SERP data including page titles, meta descriptions, URLs, and ranking positions. It captures featured snippets, people also ask boxes, and related searches.

3. Content Analysis

Google Gemini analyzes the top 10 ranking pages for each keyword, evaluating content structure, semantic relationships, intent matching, and E-A-T signals. It identifies common patterns among top performers.

AI analysis showing competitor content structure and semantic relationships

4. Insight Generation

The system generates comparative reports highlighting content gaps, optimization opportunities, and emerging trends in your niche. It scores your content against competitors across multiple ranking factors.
