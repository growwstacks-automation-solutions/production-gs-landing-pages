What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical business challenge of staying competitive in dynamic markets by continuously monitoring competitor pricing. Manual price tracking is time-consuming, error-prone, and can't scale across hundreds of products. This workflow uses ScrapeGraphAI to extract pricing data automatically, analyzes changes, and triggers appropriate team responses through Slack alerts and Jira tickets.

The system helps ecommerce businesses, retail chains, and manufacturers maintain price competitiveness without dedicating staff to constant monitoring. When integrated with your existing tools, it creates a closed-loop system where price intelligence directly informs strategic decisions and tactical responses.

How It Works

1. Scheduled price scraping with ScrapeGraphAI

The workflow begins by automatically visiting competitor product pages at scheduled intervals (daily/hourly). ScrapeGraphAI's AI engine identifies and extracts pricing information even from complex, dynamic pages that traditional scrapers might miss.

2. Price change analysis

Extracted prices are compared against your reference prices and historical data. The system calculates percentage changes and applies your business rules to determine significance (e.g., 5% drop triggers alert).

3. Slack notifications for urgent changes

When prices cross your defined thresholds, formatted Slack messages are sent to designated channels with product details, old/new prices, and direct links. Teams can react immediately without checking dashboards.

Sample Slack alert showing product details and price change information

4. Jira ticket creation for strategic actions

For significant or sustained price changes, the workflow automatically creates Jira tickets with all relevant data. Tickets route to appropriate teams based on your rules - marketing for promotions, procurement for supplier negotiations, etc.

Pro tip: Configure different Jira ticket types and priorities based on the magnitude of price changes and affected product categories.
