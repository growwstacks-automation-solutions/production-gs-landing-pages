What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the critical challenge of staying on top of customer feedback across multiple platforms. E-commerce businesses often struggle to manually monitor and analyze hundreds of product reviews, missing valuable insights and failing to promptly address customer concerns.

The system automatically collects new product reviews from Decodo, analyzes the sentiment using Gemini AI, logs the data in Google Sheets, and sends Telegram alerts for negative feedback. This creates a closed-loop system where customer concerns are addressed within hours rather than days, improving satisfaction and reducing churn.

Automatically populated review data with sentiment analysis columns

How It Works

1. Review Collection

The workflow begins by fetching new product reviews from Decodo's API at regular intervals. It captures the review text, star rating, product details, and customer information.

2. Sentiment Analysis

Each review passes through Gemini's AI model which evaluates the emotional tone and classifies it as positive, neutral, or negative. The system also extracts key phrases and common themes.

3. Data Organization

Processed reviews are logged in Google Sheets with columns for sentiment score, key phrases, and automated timestamps. This creates a searchable historical record of customer feedback.

Instant Telegram notification for urgent customer feedback

4. Alert System

Negative reviews trigger immediate Telegram alerts to customer service teams, including the product link and specific complaints. Positive reviews are batched for weekly performance reports.

Pro tip: Configure different Telegram channels for urgent issues (1-2 star reviews) versus general feedback (3 stars) to prioritize response efforts.
