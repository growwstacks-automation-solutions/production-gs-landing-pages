What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow helps businesses maintain legal compliance and accessibility standards by generating comprehensive reports using AI analysis. It scans website content for potential compliance issues related to privacy policies, terms of service, ADA accessibility, and other regulatory requirements.

The system leverages OpenAI's natural language processing to analyze website content and generate detailed compliance reports. These reports are then automatically saved to Google Drive and shared via email, providing a complete audit trail for your website's legal and accessibility status.

AI-generated compliance report showing accessibility issues

How It Works

1. Website Content Analysis

The workflow begins by analyzing your website content using OpenAI's advanced language models. It examines text, structure, and metadata to identify potential compliance gaps.

2. Compliance Check Generation

AI evaluates content against common legal frameworks (GDPR, CCPA) and accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1). It flags issues like missing privacy disclosures or inaccessible elements.

3. Report Creation

The system generates a detailed PDF report with findings, prioritized recommendations, and compliance scoring. Reports include both technical and content-related issues.

Automation flow between OpenAI, Google Drive and email

4. Automated Delivery

Completed reports are saved to a designated Google Drive folder and emailed to stakeholders. The system can be scheduled to run weekly or monthly audits.
