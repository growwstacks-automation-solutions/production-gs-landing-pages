What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow helps businesses systematically track and analyze competitors by combining web scraping, AI analysis, and real-time reporting. It pulls competitor data from various sources using Decodo, processes the information with Google's Gemini AI to extract strategic insights, sends alerts via Telegram for immediate awareness, and organizes all findings in Google Sheets for long-term tracking.

The solution eliminates manual competitor monitoring, which typically requires hours of research across multiple platforms. Instead, it delivers structured, actionable intelligence about competitor activities, pricing changes, marketing strategies, and market positioning - all updated automatically on your schedule.

Sample output showing competitor analysis results processed by Gemini AI

How It Works

1. Data Collection with Decodo

The workflow begins by extracting competitor data from websites, social media, and review platforms using Decodo's web scraping capabilities. It can monitor product pages, pricing changes, content updates, and customer sentiment across multiple competitors simultaneously.

2. AI Processing with Gemini

Raw data is then sent to Google's Gemini AI for analysis. Gemini identifies patterns, extracts key insights, and summarizes findings in business-friendly reports. It can detect shifts in marketing strategy, product positioning, or customer perception that might require your attention.

3. Real-time Alerts via Telegram

Critical findings are immediately sent to your Telegram channel as alerts. This ensures your team stays informed about important competitor movements without having to check reports manually. The alerts include direct links to the full analysis in your Google Sheets.

4. Structured Storage in Google Sheets

All processed data is organized in a structured Google Sheet that serves as your competitor intelligence hub. The sheet includes timestamped entries, categorized insights, and visual comparisons that make trend analysis simple.
