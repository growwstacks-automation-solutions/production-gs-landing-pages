What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your contract management process by combining AI generation with e-signature integration. It handles the complete contract lifecycle—from initial request to fully executed document—without manual intervention. The system pulls contract requirements from Google Sheets, generates customized agreements using OpenAI, routes them for e-signature, and files completed documents.

Legal teams and business operators waste countless hours drafting standard contracts, chasing signatures, and managing versions. This workflow eliminates those repetitive tasks while ensuring consistency and compliance. By automating the mechanical aspects of contract management, you free up time for strategic legal work and accelerate business deals.

End-to-end contract automation from request to signature

How It Works

1. Contract Request Intake

The workflow monitors a designated Google Sheet for new contract requests. Each row represents a new agreement needed, with columns specifying contract type, parties involved, key terms, and special requirements.

2. AI-Powered Generation

OpenAI analyzes the request details and generates a customized contract using your approved templates and clauses. The AI ensures proper legal language while inserting the specific terms from the request.

3. Document Routing

Generated contracts are automatically sent to designated reviewers if required, then routed to all signing parties via your connected e-signature platform (DocuSign, HelloSign, etc.).

4. Signature Management

The system tracks signing status, sends reminders for pending signatures, and collects completed documents. Fully executed contracts are filed in your document management system.
