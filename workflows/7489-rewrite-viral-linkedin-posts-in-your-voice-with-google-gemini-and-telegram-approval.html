What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining consistent LinkedIn content output while preserving your authentic voice. It identifies viral posts in your niche, uses Google Gemini to rewrite them in your unique style, then routes them through Telegram for human approval before posting.

The system saves content creators 5-10 hours per week by automating the most time-consuming parts of content curation while maintaining quality control. Unlike simple reposting tools, it transforms trending content into original-sounding posts that align with your personal brand.

AI-powered rewriting maintains the core message while adapting the voice and examples

How It Works

1. Identify viral content

The workflow monitors LinkedIn for trending posts in your specified topics or from selected influencers. It evaluates engagement metrics to identify content worth repurposing.

2. AI-powered rewriting

Google Gemini analyzes the selected post and your previous content to create a version that matches your writing style. It preserves key insights while adapting tone, examples, and phrasing.

3. Telegram approval

The rewritten content gets sent to your Telegram for review. You can approve, edit, or reject each piece with simple mobile commands before anything gets posted.

Mobile-friendly Telegram interface for quick content approvals

4. Scheduled posting

Approved posts get added to your LinkedIn content calendar at optimal times. The system maintains natural posting frequency to avoid appearing spammy.
