What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking commercial property listings across multiple platforms. Commercial real estate professionals often spend hours each week scouring websites, compiling spreadsheets, and manually reaching out to potential clients or partners.

The workflow automatically aggregates new commercial property listings using ScrapeGraphAI, organizes them in Notion databases for easy review, and initiates targeted email campaigns through Mailchimp. This eliminates manual data collection while ensuring your outreach stays timely and relevant.

Example commercial property listing that could be captured by this workflow

How It Works

Step 1: Automated Property Scraping

ScrapeGraphAI automatically monitors specified commercial real estate platforms and listing sites, extracting key details like property type, location, price, and contact information. The AI component ensures it adapts to different website structures.

Step 2: Data Organization in Notion

All scraped listings are automatically added to a structured Notion database with custom fields for easy filtering and sorting. The system creates separate views for different property types, locations, or price ranges based on your preferences.

Sample Notion database structure for organizing commercial property listings

Step 3: Targeted Email Campaigns

Based on your predefined criteria, the workflow triggers personalized email sequences through Mailchimp. You can set different campaigns for different property types or locations, ensuring relevant messaging reaches the right recipients.
