What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the challenge of manually comparing competitor content strategies across multiple AI platforms. Marketing teams waste hours copying prompts between ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini while trying to reconcile different outputs with their SEO data. The workflow automatically:

  • Queries all three AI platforms with standardized competitor analysis prompts
  • Extracts key topics and content angles from each response
  • Compares findings with your SE Ranking keyword data
  • Identifies high-opportunity gaps where competitors rank but you don't

The result is a prioritized list of content opportunities with estimated search volume, difficulty scores, and AI-generated content suggestions - all in a format ready for your editorial calendar.

The workflow compares AI responses side-by-side with SEO metrics to highlight strategic gaps

How It Works

1. Competitor URL Input

The workflow starts with your list of competitor domains or specific URLs you want to analyze. You can input these manually or connect to SE Ranking's competitor tracking.

2. Parallel AI Queries

The system simultaneously sends structured analysis prompts to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini through their APIs. Each query asks for:

  • Primary content themes
  • Top performing topics
  • Content structure analysis
  • Suggested related topics

3. Response Normalization

AI responses are cleaned and standardized into comparable data points. The workflow extracts:

  • Common themes across platforms
  • Unique insights from each AI
  • Confidence scores for each finding
Normalized data structure allows comparison across different AI response formats

4. SE Ranking Integration

Identified topics are checked against your SE Ranking project data to:

  • Verify actual search volume
  • Assess keyword difficulty
  • Check your current rankings
  • Score opportunity potential

5. Gap Analysis & Reporting

The final step generates a comprehensive report highlighting:

  • High-value topics you're missing
  • Content clusters to develop
  • Priority scores based on AI consensus and SEO data
  • Suggested content formats for each gap

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run monthly and automatically add high-priority gaps to your content calendar as draft ideas.
