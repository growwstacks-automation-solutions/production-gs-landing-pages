What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming and error-prone process of manual payroll processing by combining AI-powered tax calculations with Google Sheets automation. The system automatically calculates employee paychecks, applies correct tax withholdings based on current regulations, and generates all necessary compliance documents.

By integrating GPT-4's ability to interpret complex tax codes with Google Sheets' calculation power, businesses can eliminate 90% of manual payroll work while ensuring perfect compliance. The workflow automatically stores pay stubs and tax documents in organized Google Drive folders, creating a complete audit trail.

AI-powered tax calculation interface analyzing payroll data

How It Works

1. Employee Data Integration

The system pulls employee hours, rates, and tax status from your existing HR system or timesheets into a centralized Google Sheet. GPT-4 verifies all data meets compliance requirements before processing.

2. AI-Powered Tax Calculations

GPT-4 analyzes each employee's tax situation based on their location, filing status, and current tax tables. The AI applies all federal, state, and local withholdings accurately while accounting for recent regulatory changes.

3. Automated Document Generation

The system generates pay stubs, tax forms, and compliance reports as PDFs stored in Google Drive. Documents are automatically named and organized by pay period for easy retrieval during audits.
