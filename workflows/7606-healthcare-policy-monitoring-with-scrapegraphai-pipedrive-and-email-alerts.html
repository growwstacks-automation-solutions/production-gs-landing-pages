What This Workflow Does

This automation solution continuously monitors healthcare policy changes across government websites, research portals, and regulatory bodies using ScrapeGraphAI's advanced web scraping capabilities. When policies are updated or new regulations are published, the system automatically extracts key details, updates your Pipedrive CRM records, and sends targeted email alerts to relevant research team members.

For medical research organizations, this eliminates the manual burden of tracking dozens of policy sources while ensuring compliance with evolving regulations. The workflow captures policy effective dates, compliance requirements, and related documentation - structuring this information for easy reference and action within your existing CRM system.

AI-powered detection of healthcare policy changes from multiple sources

Pro tip: Configure your scraping targets to include policy FAQ pages and implementation guides - these often contain crucial details not found in the formal regulations themselves.

How It Works

1. AI-Powered Policy Monitoring

ScrapeGraphAI runs scheduled checks on pre-configured healthcare policy sources using natural language processing to identify substantive changes. Unlike simple website monitors, it understands policy context - distinguishing between minor wording tweaks and significant regulatory shifts that require action.

2. Structured Data Extraction

The system extracts policy titles, effective dates, affected practice areas, compliance deadlines, and related documentation links. This structured data is normalized for consistent processing regardless of source format (PDFs, web pages, press releases).

3. CRM Record Updates

Each policy change creates or updates a Pipedrive deal record, tagged with relevant research areas and linked to associated projects. Custom fields track compliance status, implementation tasks, and verification requirements.

Policy tracking record in Pipedrive with compliance task management

4. Targeted Email Alerts

Researchers receive immediate notifications about policies affecting their specific studies, with priority levels based on impact and urgency. Emails include direct links to the full policy text and pre-populated task lists in Pipedrive.

  2. ScrapeGraphAI access (API key)

  4. Email service (SMTP or transactional email API)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON workflow into your n8n instance
  2. Configure ScrapeGraphAI node with your policy source URLs
  3. Connect Pipedrive node with your CRM credentials
  4. Set up email server credentials in the Email node
  5. Map policy fields to your Pipedrive custom fields
  6. Test with a known policy source before enabling automation
