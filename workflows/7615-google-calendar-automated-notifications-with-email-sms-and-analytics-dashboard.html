What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow transforms your Google Calendar into a powerful notification hub that automatically sends email reminders and SMS alerts for upcoming events. It also tracks response rates and generates a live dashboard showing key metrics about your calendar engagement.

The system eliminates manual follow-ups while ensuring participants never miss important meetings. Business owners, consultants, and service providers can use this to reduce no-shows by up to 80% while saving 5-10 hours per week on administrative coordination.

Visualization of the automated notification flow from Google Calendar to email/SMS

How It Works

1. Calendar Event Trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Calendar for new events or changes to existing ones. When an event is created or modified, it automatically captures all relevant details including title, time, participants, and description.

2. Multi-Channel Notification

Based on your configured rules, the system sends email reminders through your preferred email service (Gmail, SendGrid, etc.) and SMS notifications via Twilio or other SMS gateways. Notifications go out at customizable intervals before each event.

3. Response Tracking

The workflow tracks RSVPs, opens, clicks, and replies to measure engagement. This data feeds into the analytics dashboard showing metrics like attendance rates, average response times, and notification effectiveness.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Calendar with events to track
  2. An email service provider (Gmail, SendGrid, Mailgun, etc.)
  3. SMS gateway account (Twilio recommended)
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  5. Google Cloud credentials for API access

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Calendar account in the first node
  3. Configure your email and SMS services in their respective nodes
  4. Set your preferred notification timing rules (e.g., 24h before event)
  5. Deploy the workflow and test with a sample calendar event
