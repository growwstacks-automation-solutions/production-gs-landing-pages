What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the critical challenge of reactive ad campaign management. Most marketers waste hours manually checking dashboards across Meta Ads Manager and Google Ads, often discovering performance issues too late after significant budget has been wasted.

The workflow proactively monitors your campaigns daily, comparing current performance against historical benchmarks. When key metrics like CTR, CPA, or ROAS drop below your defined thresholds, it triggers multi-channel alerts through Slack, email, or SMS - ensuring your team can take immediate corrective action.

Sample performance dashboard showing key metrics being monitored

How It Works

1. Daily Performance Data Fetch

The workflow runs each morning, pulling yesterday's campaign data from both Meta Ads and Google Ads APIs. It collects metrics like impressions, clicks, conversions, and spend for all active campaigns.

2. Performance Benchmark Comparison

Each campaign's current metrics are compared against its 7-day and 30-day rolling averages. You can configure percentage drop thresholds that trigger alerts (e.g., 15% decrease in CTR).

3. Alert Prioritization

The system categorizes performance drops by severity - minor fluctuations might generate an email report, while significant ROAS declines could trigger immediate SMS alerts to key team members.

4. Multi-Channel Notification

Customized alert messages are sent through your preferred channels, including campaign details, performance change percentages, and recommended actions.
