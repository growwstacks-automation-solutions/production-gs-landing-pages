What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of responding quickly to your best leads while maintaining accurate CRM records. When potential customers submit forms on your website, the workflow instantly evaluates their responses against your qualification criteria to identify high-value opportunities.

For leads that meet your standards, it triggers a personalized email response within minutes of submission - far faster than manual follow-up allows. Simultaneously, it checks HubSpot to either create a new contact record or update an existing one, ensuring your sales team always has current information.

Sample personalized email triggered for qualified leads

How It Works

1. Form submission trigger

The workflow activates when a visitor submits your web form, capturing all their responses as structured data. This could be a contact form, demo request, or any other lead capture form on your site.

2. Lead qualification check

Using your predefined criteria (like budget range, company size, or job title), the automation evaluates whether this is a high-potential lead worthy of immediate follow-up. You can set multiple conditions to identify different lead tiers.

3. HubSpot record management

The system checks HubSpot to see if this contact exists, then either updates their record with the new information or creates a fresh profile. This maintains data hygiene while capturing all interactions.

Form data automatically populated in HubSpot contact record

4. Personalized email delivery

For qualified leads, the workflow sends a tailored email using merge fields from their form responses. This could include specific references to their needs, relevant case studies, or direct calendar booking links.
