What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining a consistent, strategic social media presence without spending hours on manual content planning. It transforms a simple Google Sheets input into a complete content engine that generates thematic pillars, organizes them into a publishing calendar, and creates platform-optimized posts using AI.

Marketing teams waste countless hours brainstorming content ideas only to struggle with execution. This workflow ensures your social strategy stays aligned with business goals while adapting to performance data. It's particularly valuable for small teams managing multiple platforms who need to maximize their content ROI.

Example of content planning data structure in Google Sheets

How It Works

1. Input content parameters in Google Sheets

The workflow starts by reading your content strategy parameters from a designated Google Sheet. This includes target audience details, core messaging themes, campaign objectives, and any specific CTAs or hashtags to include.

2. Generate weighted content pillars

OpenAI analyzes your input to create 3-5 primary content pillars weighted by strategic importance. The AI considers your brand voice, past performance data (if available), and industry trends to suggest a balanced content mix.

3. Build publishing calendar

The system automatically distributes pillar content across your chosen timeframe, optimizing for platform-specific best practices. It spaces similar themes appropriately and ensures variety in your posting schedule.

Automatically generated content calendar with pillar distribution

4. Create platform-specific posts

For each calendar entry, the workflow generates multiple post variations tailored to different social platforms. LinkedIn posts emphasize professional insights while Instagram focuses on visual storytelling - all while maintaining consistent messaging.
