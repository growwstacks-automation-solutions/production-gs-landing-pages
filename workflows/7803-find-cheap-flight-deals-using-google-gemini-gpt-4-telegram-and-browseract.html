What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you find and book affordable flights by combining AI intelligence with instant messaging. The system continuously monitors flight prices across multiple platforms, using Google Gemini and GPT-4 to analyze trends and identify genuine deals rather than just advertised discounts.

When the AI detects a significant price drop or error fare on your specified routes, it immediately sends a rich notification to your Telegram account with all booking details. The workflow can be customized to consider your preferred airlines, layover preferences, and budget constraints, functioning like a personal travel agent that works 24/7.

Telegram notification showing analyzed flight deal with price history graph

How It Works

1. AI-Powered Flight Monitoring

Google Gemini continuously scrapes and analyzes flight data from multiple sources, applying machine learning to distinguish between normal fluctuations and genuine deals. The system tracks historical pricing patterns for your specified routes.

2. Deal Verification

GPT-4 cross-references potential deals against your preferences and current market conditions. It eliminates false positives like basic economy fares with excessive restrictions or routes with unrealistic connections.

3. Instant Notification

When a verified deal is found, the workflow triggers a Telegram bot to send an interactive message. This includes the flight details, price comparison graph, and direct booking links.
