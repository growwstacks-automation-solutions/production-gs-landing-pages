What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your sales process by instantly converting completed Calendly meetings into actionable next steps. When a sales call ends, the system automatically generates a personalized AI proposal based on the meeting discussion and immediately sends the client a Stripe payment link to secure the deal.

The workflow eliminates manual follow-up tasks that typically delay deals - no more copying notes into proposal templates, calculating pricing, or chasing clients for payments. It captures the momentum of your sales conversations when buyer intent is highest, significantly improving conversion rates.

Example of AI-generated proposal based on meeting notes

How It Works

1. Calendly Meeting Trigger

The workflow starts when a Calendly event is marked as completed. It pulls all meeting details including attendee information, duration, and any custom questions answered during booking.

2. AI Proposal Generation

Using the meeting metadata and your predefined service templates, an AI analyzes the conversation context to generate a professional proposal with accurate scope, deliverables, and pricing.

3. Stripe Payment Link Creation

The system creates a secure Stripe payment link matching the proposal amount, with configurable options for installment plans or discounts discussed during the call.

4. Automated Follow-up Sequence

The client receives the proposal and payment link within minutes of the call ending, followed by scheduled reminders if the payment isn't completed within your specified timeframe.

Pro tip: Configure your Calendly event questions to capture key deal specifics (budget, timeline, requirements) that the AI can use to personalize proposals.
