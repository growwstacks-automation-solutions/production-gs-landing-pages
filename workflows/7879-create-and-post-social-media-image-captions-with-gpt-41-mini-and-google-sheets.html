What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered workflow revolutionizes your social media content creation by automating caption generation and posting. It eliminates the time-consuming process of manually writing captions for each post while ensuring brand-consistent, engaging content across all your platforms.

The system connects your Google Sheets content calendar with GPT-4.1-mini's advanced natural language capabilities to produce high-quality captions tailored to your images and brand voice. It then automatically publishes the completed posts to your connected social networks on schedule.

AI-generated caption variations ready for selection in Google Sheets

How It Works

1. Content input in Google Sheets

You maintain a simple Google Sheet with your upcoming posts - just add image URLs and basic context. The sheet serves as your content calendar and briefing document for the AI.

2. AI caption generation

GPT-4.1-mini analyzes each image's context and generates multiple caption options following your brand guidelines. It considers platform-specific best practices for character limits and engagement tactics.

3. Human review and selection

The workflow presents 3-5 caption variations per image in your Google Sheet. You quickly select the best option or make minor edits before approval.

4. Automated posting

Approved posts are automatically published to your connected social platforms at scheduled times, with proper image-caption pairing and hashtag inclusion.

Pro tip: Create different Google Sheet tabs for each social platform to maintain distinct voice and formatting requirements.
