What This Workflow Does

Ecommerce businesses often struggle with low-quality leads entering their CRM systems. Fake emails, disposable accounts, and mistyped addresses clog your marketing pipelines and waste resources. This workflow solves that problem by automatically vetting new Shopify customers through ZeroBounce's email validation service before syncing only qualified contacts to HubSpot.

The automation checks each new Shopify order for valid contact information, filters out suspicious or invalid emails, and enriches customer profiles with validation data. Only leads that pass your configured quality thresholds get added to HubSpot, while questionable entries are flagged for review. This maintains CRM hygiene and ensures your sales team focuses on genuine opportunities.

Validated lead records synced from Shopify to HubSpot with ZeroBounce verification data

How It Works

1. New Shopify order trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new order is placed in your Shopify store. It extracts customer contact information including email, name, and order details.

2. ZeroBounce email validation

Each customer email is sent to ZeroBounce's API for comprehensive validation. The service checks for syntax errors, disposable addresses, spam traps, and mailbox existence.

3. Lead qualification filtering

The workflow evaluates validation results against your configured rules. You can set thresholds for acceptable risk scores, require specific domain types, or exclude certain geographic regions.

Pro tip: Combine email validation with purchase amount thresholds to identify high-value leads worth immediate sales follow-up.

4. HubSpot contact creation

Validated leads meeting your criteria are automatically created as contacts in HubSpot. The workflow maps Shopify order data to appropriate HubSpot fields and adds ZeroBounce validation metadata.
