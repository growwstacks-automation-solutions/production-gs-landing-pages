Automatically prioritize event attendees using AI and streamline sales follow-up

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually qualifying hundreds of event RSVPs by using AI to automatically score leads based on their registration details. It eliminates the time-consuming process of sales teams manually reviewing each attendee to determine follow-up priority.

The workflow connects your event registration system with GPT for intelligent lead scoring, HubSpot for CRM integration, and Slack for real-time sales team notifications. High-scoring leads are automatically added to targeted nurture sequences while sales receives immediate alerts about VIP attendees.

AI-scored leads appear in HubSpot with priority indicators for sales teams

How It Works

1. RSVP Data Collection

The workflow triggers when new event registrations come in from your registration platform. It captures all attendee details including name, company, job title, and any custom survey responses.

2. AI-Powered Lead Scoring

GPT analyzes each registration against your ideal customer profile criteria. It assigns scores based on factors like job seniority, company relevance, and response patterns. Custom scoring rules can be configured for different event types.

3. CRM Integration

Scored leads are automatically created or updated in HubSpot with their lead score and event details. The system can add high-scoring leads to specific lists or campaigns for targeted follow-up.

Sales teams receive instant Slack alerts for priority event attendees

4. Sales Team Alerts

High-scoring leads trigger real-time Slack notifications to your sales channel. Notifications include key details like lead score, company information, and registration comments to enable personalized outreach.
