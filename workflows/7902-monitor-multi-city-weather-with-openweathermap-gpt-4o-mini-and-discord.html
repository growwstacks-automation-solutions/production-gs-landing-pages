What This Workflow Does

This automated weather monitoring solution eliminates the need for manual weather checks across multiple locations. It continuously tracks weather conditions in specified cities using OpenWeatherMap's API, processes the data through GPT-4o-mini for intelligent analysis, and delivers concise, actionable alerts to your team via Discord.

Businesses with operations, events, or teams spread across different geographic areas can make weather-informed decisions faster. The system provides more than just temperature readings—it identifies potential disruptions, suggests operational adjustments, and prioritizes alerts based on severity, all without human intervention.

Example of weather data analysis and visualization

How It Works

1. City List Configuration

The workflow begins with your predefined list of cities to monitor. You can easily add or remove locations as your business needs change, with no coding required.

2. OpenWeatherMap API Integration

For each city, the system pulls current conditions and forecasts from OpenWeatherMap. This includes temperature, precipitation, wind speed, humidity, and other critical metrics updated in near real-time.

3. GPT-4o-mini Analysis

Raw weather data gets processed by GPT-4o-mini, which identifies patterns, assesses risks, and generates human-readable summaries. The AI highlights what matters most based on your industry and configured priorities.

4. Discord Alert Delivery

Final alerts arrive in your designated Discord channel with severity indicators. Team members can immediately discuss impacts and take action without switching between weather apps and communication tools.
