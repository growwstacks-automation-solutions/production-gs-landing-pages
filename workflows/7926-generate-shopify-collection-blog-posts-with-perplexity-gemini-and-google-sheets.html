What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating SEO-optimized blog content for Shopify product collections. Many ecommerce stores struggle to maintain fresh, engaging blog content that drives organic traffic while accurately showcasing their products.

The workflow automatically generates high-quality blog posts by combining your Shopify collection data with AI writing tools (Perplexity or Gemini) and organizes everything in Google Sheets. It creates content that's tailored to your products while maintaining natural language and SEO best practices.

Example output of AI-generated Shopify collection blog post

How It Works

1. Pull Shopify collection data

The workflow starts by connecting to your Shopify store via API to retrieve product collection details including titles, descriptions, and product listings.

2. Structure content prompts

Using your collection data, the system creates optimized prompts for AI writing tools that include your brand voice guidelines, SEO keywords, and product specifics.

3. Generate AI content

The workflow sends these structured prompts to either Perplexity AI or Google Gemini (your choice) to generate draft blog posts with proper headings, product mentions, and natural flow.

4. Organize in Google Sheets

All generated content is automatically logged in a Google Sheet with metadata including collection name, generation date, word count, and status flags.

Both Perplexity and Gemini produce excellent ecommerce content, but with different strengths. Perplexity tends to create more research-backed, detailed content while Gemini often produces more conversational, sales-friendly copy.

For Shopify collection blogs, we've found Perplexity works better for informational "guide-style" posts, while Gemini excels at product highlight content. The workflow supports both so you can test which aligns better with your brand voice and audience preferences.

  • Perplexity: Better for detailed, research-based content
  • Gemini: Stronger for conversational, sales-focused copy
  • Test both to see what resonates with your audience
