What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the critical sales challenge of stale leads—potential customers who showed interest but stopped responding. It automatically identifies inactive leads in your Notion CRM, generates personalized follow-up messages using OpenAI's natural language capabilities, and delivers them via Telegram for maximum visibility.

Unlike generic email sequences, this system creates context-aware reminders that reference specific interactions with each lead. The AI analyzes previous communications, notes from your CRM, and engagement history to craft messages that feel human-written and relevant to each prospect's situation.

Sample AI-generated nudge showing personalized reference to previous interaction

How It Works

1. Daily Lead Status Check

The workflow runs each morning to scan your Notion CRM database for leads marked as "active" but with no recent activity (configurable timeframe, typically 7-14 days). It filters out leads who have scheduled future follow-ups or specific "do not contact" flags.

2. Context Gathering

For each stale lead, the system compiles relevant context: previous communications, deal stage, noted pain points, and any attached files or comments from your team. This becomes the input for OpenAI's message generation.

Pro tip: Structure your Notion CRM with consistent fields for "last contact date," "deal stage," and "key interests" to maximize the AI's contextual understanding.

3. AI Message Generation

OpenAI processes the compiled context through a carefully engineered prompt that ensures business-appropriate tone and includes your preferred call-to-action. The AI generates 2-3 message variations, from which the workflow selects the most relevant based on length and keyword inclusion.

Workflow nodes handling the AI message generation process

4. Telegram Delivery

The selected message gets sent to the lead's Telegram account (either via saved contact or channel message). The workflow logs the sent message back to Notion with timestamps and marks the lead for next follow-up in 3-5 days if no response.
