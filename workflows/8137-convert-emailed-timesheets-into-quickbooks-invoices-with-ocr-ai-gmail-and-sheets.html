What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually processing employee timesheets and converting them into client invoices. Every week, businesses waste countless hours collecting emailed timesheets, manually entering data into spreadsheets, verifying calculations, and creating invoices in QuickBooks.

The workflow automatically processes incoming timesheet emails, extracts data using AI-powered OCR technology, validates the information against employee records in Google Sheets, and generates accurate QuickBooks invoices—all without human intervention. It handles both digital and photographed/handwritten timesheets with remarkable accuracy.

Automated timesheet data extraction and validation in Google Sheets

How It Works

1. Timesheet Email Capture

The workflow monitors a dedicated Gmail inbox for timesheet submissions. When a new email arrives with a timesheet attachment (PDF, JPG, or PNG), it triggers the automation process.

2. OCR Data Extraction

AI-powered OCR technology scans the timesheet attachment, whether it's a digital document or photographed handwritten sheet. The system identifies key data points like employee name, dates, hours worked, and project codes.

3. Data Validation

Extracted data is cross-checked against employee records in Google Sheets to verify accuracy. The system flags discrepancies like overtime hours, missing information, or invalid project codes for manager review.

Automatically generated QuickBooks invoice with timesheet details

4. Invoice Generation

Validated timesheet data is formatted into a professional QuickBooks invoice with correct billing rates, taxes, and client details. The system applies all relevant billing rules and approvals before finalizing.

5. Notification & Archiving

Once processed, the system notifies relevant stakeholders and archives all documents with a complete audit trail. Managers receive summary reports of processed timesheets and generated invoices.

Pro tip: For best results, provide employees with a standardized timesheet template that includes clear fields for all required information. This improves OCR accuracy and reduces validation exceptions.
