What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of creating and publishing professional recipe infographics using AI. By simply entering a dish name, the system generates a complete recipe with ingredients, instructions, and nutritional information using Gemini AI, then transforms it into a visually appealing infographic with Nanobanana Pro, and finally publishes it to your chosen platforms via Blotato.

Food bloggers, recipe websites, and culinary content creators spend hours manually creating recipe visuals. This automation eliminates that tedious work while ensuring consistent, high-quality output that engages audiences and drives traffic. The workflow can be triggered manually or set to run automatically for scheduled content production.

Sample output showing an AI-generated recipe infographic with ingredients and preparation steps

How It Works

1. Recipe Generation with Gemini AI

The workflow starts by sending your input dish name to Gemini AI, which generates a complete recipe including ingredients, quantities, preparation steps, cooking time, and nutritional information. The AI ensures the recipe follows standard formatting and includes all necessary details.

2. Infographic Creation with Nanobanana Pro

The generated recipe text is then passed to Nanobanana Pro, which designs a professional infographic layout. The AI selects appropriate food photography, arranges the text elements for optimal readability, and applies your brand colors and styling preferences.

3. Publishing via Blotato

The final infographic is automatically published to your configured channels through Blotato. This could include your website CMS, social media platforms, email newsletter system, or any other destination where you share recipe content.
