What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized LinkedIn outreach while maintaining human-like quality. Traditional mass outreach often feels generic and gets ignored, while manual personalization doesn't scale. This workflow bridges that gap by using AI to craft tailored messages based on real prospect data from your Google Sheets.

The system pulls lead information from your spreadsheet, analyzes each prospect's profile and background using Azure OpenAI, then generates highly relevant connection requests or messages. ConnectSafely ensures compliant sending that won't trigger LinkedIn's anti-spam filters.

AI analyzes prospect data to craft personalized outreach messages

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from Google Sheets

The workflow starts by reading your prospect list from a designated Google Sheet. Each row typically contains the prospect's LinkedIn URL, name, company, role, and any other relevant details you've collected.

2. AI-Powered Personalization

Azure OpenAI processes each lead's information to generate a unique message. The AI considers the prospect's profile, your relationship context, and your predefined messaging guidelines to create natural, engaging outreach.

3. Safe Sending via ConnectSafely

ConnectSafely handles the actual LinkedIn connection requests or messages with built-in rate limiting and human-like interaction patterns to avoid triggering spam detection.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Azure OpenAI API access
  3. ConnectSafely account with LinkedIn permissions

Pro tip: Include 2-3 personalization points in your message template (like mentioning their company news or shared connections) to significantly increase response rates.
