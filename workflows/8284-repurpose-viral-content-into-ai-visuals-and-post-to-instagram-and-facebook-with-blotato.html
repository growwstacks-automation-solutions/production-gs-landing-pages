Repurpose viral content into AI visuals and post to Instagram and Facebook with Blotato

Automatically turn any viral content URL into AI-generated visuals and publish them to multiple social platforms

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of consistently creating engaging social media content by automatically repurposing viral content from various sources (blogs, YouTube, TikTok, etc.) into AI-generated visuals that can be posted to Instagram and Facebook. It eliminates the manual work of content creation while ensuring your social channels stay active with fresh, trending content.

The automation extracts key information from any URL you provide, uses AI to generate visually appealing images or graphics, then schedules and publishes them to your connected social media accounts. This creates a continuous content pipeline that leverages existing viral content while adding your unique AI-generated spin.

Example output showing how viral content is transformed into AI visuals

How It Works

1. Content Input

The workflow starts by accepting a URL to any viral content source. This could be a trending blog post, YouTube video, TikTok clip, or other popular content. The system extracts the key information and prepares it for transformation.

2. AI Visual Generation

Using Blotato's AI capabilities, the workflow analyzes the content and generates visually appealing images, infographics, or quote cards that capture the essence of the original material while making it social-media friendly.

AI visual generation process within the workflow

3. Social Media Publishing

The generated visuals are automatically formatted for each platform's specifications and published to your connected Instagram and Facebook accounts. You can configure posting schedules, captions, and hashtags as needed.
