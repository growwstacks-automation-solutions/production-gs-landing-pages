What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow template solves the challenge of consistently creating and publishing engaging social media content across multiple platforms. By combining Blotato's AI content generation capabilities with n8n's automation power, it eliminates the manual work of:

  • Brainstorming post ideas
  • Writing captions and hashtags
  • Formatting content for different platforms
  • Scheduling and publishing posts

The automation handles everything from content ideation to final publication, ensuring your social channels stay active with minimal daily effort.

How It Works

1. Content Ideation

The workflow starts by analyzing trending topics in your niche using Blotato's AI. It generates multiple post ideas based on your brand voice and audience preferences.

2. AI Content Creation

Blotato's AI generates complete social posts including captions, hashtags, and image suggestions. The system can create variations optimized for different platforms.

AI-generated content examples with platform-specific formatting

3. Content Approval

The workflow can be configured to send drafts for human review or automatically approve content based on predefined quality criteria.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

n8n handles the platform-specific formatting and scheduling, publishing to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and other connected channels.
