What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual entry of expense receipts by transforming photos into structured data. Employees simply snap a picture of a receipt in Telegram, and the system automatically extracts key details (date, amount, vendor, etc.) using AI-powered OCR, then logs the information to your Google Sheets expense tracker.

For businesses, this means real-time expense reporting without spreadsheets or paperwork. Finance teams get accurate, searchable records while reducing processing costs by 80-90%. The workflow includes validation rules and can trigger alerts for unusual expenses or policy violations.

How It Works

1. Receipt Submission

Employees send receipt photos through a dedicated Telegram channel or chat bot. The mobile-friendly interface works anywhere, requiring no special apps beyond Telegram's messaging platform.

2. Data Extraction

The Mindee API analyzes the receipt image using optical character recognition (OCR) to identify and extract key fields: transaction date, total amount, tax, vendor name, and payment method.

3. Data Validation

The system checks for duplicate submissions, validates amounts against company policies, and flags missing or unclear data for review. Twilio can send instant SMS alerts for exceptions.

4. Google Sheets Logging

Structured expense data is appended to your designated Google Sheet with timestamps and submitter details. Formulas can automatically categorize expenses and calculate reimbursements.

Pro tip: Add a second workflow to sync approved expenses to your accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, etc.) for complete automation.
