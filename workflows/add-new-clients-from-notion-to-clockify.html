What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of maintaining consistent client information across Notion (where you manage projects) and Clockify (where you track billable time). Every time a new client is added to your Notion database, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding client in Clockify with all relevant details.

Manual client entry between systems leads to errors, duplicate work, and time wasted switching apps. This workflow eliminates those pain points by ensuring Clockify always has up-to-date client information the moment it's entered in Notion. Agencies and freelancers typically save 2-3 hours per week while improving data accuracy.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Notion database entries

The workflow monitors your specified Notion database for new client entries. You can configure it to watch for specific status changes or creation events.

2. Extract relevant client details

Key information like client name, billing rate, project details, and contact information is extracted from the Notion entry.

3. Create corresponding Clockify client

The workflow uses Clockify's API to create a new client with all the mapped details from Notion, maintaining consistency between systems.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Notion for "Clockify Client ID" that gets updated after successful sync. This creates a two-way reference between systems.
