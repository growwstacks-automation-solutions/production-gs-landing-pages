What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of valuable positive feedback getting lost or forgotten. Many businesses collect glowing testimonials, customer compliments, or employee recognition through forms or surveys, but then struggle to organize and utilize this data effectively.

The workflow automatically captures any positive feedback submitted through Typeform and adds it as a new entry in your Notion database. This creates a centralized, searchable repository of all positive responses that can be referenced for testimonials, performance reviews, or customer satisfaction analysis.

How It Works

1. Feedback Submission

Customers or team members submit feedback through your Typeform. The form should include a long text field for detailed comments.

2. Trigger Activation

The workflow automatically triggers whenever a new form submission is received. It checks for completion and validates the response data.

3. Notion Database Update

The system creates a new entry in your specified Notion database table, mapping the form fields to your database properties. Timestamps and other metadata are added automatically.
