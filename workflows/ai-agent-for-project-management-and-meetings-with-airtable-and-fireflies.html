What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered automation solves the universal problem of meeting follow-through by automatically converting discussion points into trackable tasks. Instead of manually reviewing notes and creating action items, the system analyzes Fireflies.ai meeting transcripts to identify deliverables, deadlines, and responsible parties.

The workflow extracts key information like "Sarah will send the contract by Wednesday" and creates corresponding records in Airtable with proper assignments, due dates, and context. This eliminates the 30-60 minutes teams typically spend post-meeting on administrative work while ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

AI agent analyzing meeting transcript to identify action items

How It Works

1. Meeting Recording & Transcription

Fireflies.ai joins your Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet calls to record and transcribe conversations in real-time. The AI identifies different speakers and timestamps all discussion points.

2. Action Item Extraction

Natural language processing analyzes the transcript to detect commitments like "I'll complete the budget by Friday" or "We need to redesign the login page." The system distinguishes between general discussion and concrete action items.

3. Task Creation in Airtable

Identified action items automatically create records in your Airtable project management base with: assignee names pulled from meeting participants, due dates extracted from conversation context, and relevant discussion snippets for reference.

Pro tip: Train the AI by reviewing its initial task suggestions - the system learns your team's terminology and improves accuracy over time.
