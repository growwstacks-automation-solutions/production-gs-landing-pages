What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered meeting assistant automatically joins your virtual meetings, transcribes conversations in real-time, and extracts key insights while the discussion is still happening. It eliminates the need for manual note-taking and helps teams stay focused on the conversation rather than documentation.

The system identifies action items, decisions, and important discussion points as they occur, then organizes them into shareable summaries. This creates an instant record of meetings that can be referenced later or shared with team members who couldn't attend.

How It Works

1. Meeting Connection

The workflow automatically joins scheduled meetings via calendar integration, supporting platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams.

2. Real-time Transcription

As participants speak, the AI converts speech to text with timestamped speaker identification, creating a searchable transcript.

3. Insight Generation

The system analyzes the conversation to identify key themes, action items, decisions, and even sentiment trends across participants.

4. Post-Meeting Delivery

Automated summaries are distributed to attendees and relevant stakeholders via email or team collaboration tools like Slack.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to push action items directly into your project management tool (like Asana or Trello) to ensure follow-through.

Who This Is For

This solution benefits any team that holds regular meetings - particularly remote teams, project managers, sales teams conducting client calls, and leadership teams making strategic decisions. It's especially valuable for:

  • Teams that need accurate records of decisions and action items
  • Organizations tracking meeting effectiveness over time
  • Companies that want to reduce time spent on manual meeting notes
