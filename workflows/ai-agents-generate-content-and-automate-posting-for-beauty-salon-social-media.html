What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge beauty salons face in maintaining an active, engaging social media presence. Many salon owners struggle to consistently create fresh content while managing their busy schedules. This workflow automates the entire process from content creation to posting.

Using AI agents, the system generates customized social media posts tailored to your salon's services, promotions, and aesthetic. It then automatically schedules and publishes these posts across your social platforms, saving you hours each week while maintaining a professional online presence.

The workflow interface showing AI-generated content creation and scheduling

How It Works

1. Content Generation

The AI analyzes your salon's services, seasonal trends, and customer preferences to generate engaging post ideas. It creates both captions and visual concepts tailored to your brand.

2. Content Approval

Before posting, the system can optionally send generated content for your review. You can approve, edit, or reject posts with a single click.

3. Scheduling & Posting

Approved content is automatically scheduled based on optimal posting times and published to your connected social media accounts.

Pro tip: Connect your booking system to automatically generate before/after posts when clients complete services (with their permission).
