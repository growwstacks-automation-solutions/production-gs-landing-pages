What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered HR automation transforms your recruitment process by automatically processing incoming job applications, extracting key candidate information from CVs, and evaluating applicants against your job requirements. It eliminates hours of manual resume screening while ensuring consistent, objective candidate assessment.

The system analyzes each resume for skills, experience, education, and other qualifications, then scores candidates based on your predefined criteria. Top candidates are automatically routed to your hiring team with detailed evaluation reports, while unqualified applicants receive polite rejection emails.

How It Works

1. CV Processing

The workflow automatically extracts text from submitted resumes (PDF, Word, etc.) using OCR technology. It identifies and categorizes key information including work history, education, skills, and certifications.

2. AI Analysis

Natural language processing evaluates each candidate's qualifications against the job description. The system scores applicants based on skill matches, experience relevance, and other configurable parameters.

3. Candidate Ranking

Applicants are ranked according to their fit score. The workflow can automatically advance top candidates to interview scheduling or flag them for human review based on your thresholds.

Pro tip: Configure different scoring weights for various roles - emphasize technical skills for engineering positions while prioritizing communication abilities for customer-facing roles.
