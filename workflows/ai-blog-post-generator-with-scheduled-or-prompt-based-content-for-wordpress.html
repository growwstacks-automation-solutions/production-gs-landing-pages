What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of maintaining a consistent blog content schedule without the time-consuming manual work of writing and publishing posts. It automates the entire process from content generation to WordPress publication, using AI to create high-quality articles based on your prompts or a predefined schedule.

The system can operate in two modes: scheduled content creation (ideal for maintaining regular posting cadence) or on-demand generation (perfect for responding to trending topics). This flexibility makes it valuable for content marketers, small business owners, and agencies managing multiple client blogs.

How It Works

1. Content Trigger

The workflow starts either on a schedule you define or when manually triggered with a specific topic prompt. Scheduled mode maintains consistency, while prompt-based mode allows for timely content.

2. AI Content Generation

The system sends your topic or prompt to an AI writing assistant (like OpenAI or similar) which generates a complete blog post including title, headings, body content, and meta description.

3. Content Review & Formatting

Before publishing, the workflow can optionally send the draft for human review or automatically format it with your preferred styling, images, and internal linking structure.

4. WordPress Publication

The finalized content is automatically posted to your WordPress site as a draft or published post, complete with categories, tags, and featured images if configured.
