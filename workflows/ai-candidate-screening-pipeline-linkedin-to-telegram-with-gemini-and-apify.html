AI Candidate Screening Pipeline: LinkedIn to Telegram with Gemini & Apify

What This Workflow Does

Manual candidate screening is a major bottleneck in recruitment. Reviewing dozens of LinkedIn profiles, matching them to job descriptions, and coordinating feedback across teams consumes 10-15 hours per week for most recruiters. This creates slow hiring processes that frustrate candidates and cause you to lose top talent to faster-moving competitors.

This AI-powered automation solves that by creating a complete screening pipeline. Candidates share their LinkedIn profiles directly via Telegram messenger. The system automatically scrapes their full profile data, intelligently matches them to your open positions using Google Gemini AI, performs detailed analysis against job requirements, and delivers structured screening reports to your internal talent team—all within minutes instead of days.

The workflow includes built-in spam prevention, audit logging in Google Sheets, and intelligent fallback matching that analyzes both the candidate's message context and their LinkedIn experience. It transforms your recruitment from a manual, inconsistent process into a scalable, data-driven operation that works 24/7.

How It Works

1. Candidate Submission via Telegram

Candidates send their LinkedIn profile URL to your recruitment Telegram bot. The system validates the URL format and checks against spam limits (max 3 submissions per user). It immediately sends a confirmation message to the candidate and notifies your internal team that a new profile has been submitted.

2. LinkedIn Profile Extraction

The workflow extracts the clean LinkedIn URL and initiates a scraping job via Apify's professional LinkedIn scraper. It polls the API up to 10 times (with 15-second intervals) until the complete profile data—including experience, education, skills, and accomplishments—is successfully extracted and ready for analysis.

3. AI-Powered Job Matching

An AI agent first analyzes the candidate's Telegram message for mentioned roles. If no role is specified, it analyzes the LinkedIn profile content against your job description files stored in Google Drive. The system selects up to 3 potential matches, then a second AI agent determines the single best fit based on detailed profile analysis.

4. Recruiter Scoring & Reporting

A recruiter AI agent analyzes the LinkedIn profile against the selected job description, generating a structured report with strengths, weaknesses, risk/reward factors, and an overall fit score (0-10) with justification. All data is logged to Google Sheets with a unique submission ID for complete auditability.

5. Team Notification & Distribution

A formatted summary with candidate details, matched position, and fit score is sent to your internal Telegram talent group. The message includes a direct link to the Google Sheets audit log, enabling quick team collaboration and decision-making without switching between applications.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Create your Telegram bot via @BotFather and note the API token. Create an internal Telegram group and add the bot.
  2. Set up Apify with a free account and get your API token from Settings.
  3. Create a Google Sheet named "LinkedIn Profile AI Candidate Screening" with columns: Submission ID, Date, LinkedIn URL, First Name, Last Name, Telegram Username, Strengths, Weaknesses, Risk Factor, Reward Factor, JD Match, Overall Fit, Justification.
  4. Upload job descriptions to a Google Drive folder named "Job Descriptions" with clear filenames.
  5. Import the template into n8n and configure all credential nodes with your API keys and tokens.
  6. Update company descriptions in the three AI agent nodes to reflect your specific hiring needs and culture.
  7. Test the workflow by sending a LinkedIn URL to your bot and verifying all steps execute correctly.
  8. Activate the workflow and start accepting candidate submissions immediately.
