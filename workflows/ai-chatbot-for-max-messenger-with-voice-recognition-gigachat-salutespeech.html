What This Workflow Does

This automation creates an intelligent voice-enabled chatbot for Max Messenger that combines GigaChat's conversational AI with SaluteSpeech's advanced voice recognition. It transforms how businesses handle customer interactions by enabling natural, voice-driven conversations at scale.

The system automatically converts voice messages to text, analyzes intent using AI, generates contextual responses, and can even convert replies back to speech. This eliminates the friction of traditional text-based chatbots while maintaining all the benefits of automation.

How It Works

1. Voice Input Processing

When a user sends a voice message in Max Messenger, the workflow triggers automatically. SaluteSpeech converts the audio to text with high accuracy, even in noisy environments.

2. Intent Recognition

GigaChat analyzes the transcribed text to understand the user's intent, emotional tone, and context from previous interactions. The AI determines the optimal response strategy.

3. Response Generation

The system generates a natural language response tailored to the user's query. For complex requests, it can retrieve information from connected databases or knowledge bases.
