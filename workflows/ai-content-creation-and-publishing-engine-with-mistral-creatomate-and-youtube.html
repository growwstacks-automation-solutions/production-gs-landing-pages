What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow revolutionizes content creation by automating the entire video production pipeline. It transforms simple text prompts into polished YouTube videos through seamless integration of Mistral AI for script generation, Creatomate for video composition, and YouTube for publishing.

The automation solves the time-intensive challenges of regular video content production. Marketers, educators, and content creators can maintain consistent publishing schedules without the manual effort of scripting, editing, and uploading videos individually.

How It Works

1. Triggering the Workflow

The process begins when you send a message to a Telegram bot containing your video topic or prompt. This trigger initiates the automated sequence, passing your content idea to Mistral AI.

2. AI Script Generation

Mistral AI processes your prompt to generate a coherent video script. The AI considers context, tone, and structure to produce natural-sounding narration text ready for voiceover or captions.

3. Video Composition

Creatomate receives the generated script and assembles a complete video using your predefined templates. It handles scene composition, text overlays, transitions, and can integrate stock footage or your media assets.

4. YouTube Publishing

The finished video automatically uploads to your YouTube channel with optimized metadata. The workflow populates title, description, tags, and thumbnail based on your channel's best practices.

Pro tip: Use this workflow to batch-create content by triggering multiple videos at once. Schedule weekly content drops by automating the Telegram triggers.
