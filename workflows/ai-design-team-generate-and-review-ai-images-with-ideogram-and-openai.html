AI Design Team: Generate & Review AI Images Automatically

Automate your creative workflow. Generate images with Ideogram, review quality with OpenAI, and organize assets in Google Drive—all in one seamless n8n workflow.

What This Workflow Does

Creative teams waste countless hours manually generating AI images, reviewing them for quality, and organizing the resulting assets. This workflow automates that entire process, creating what's essentially an "AI design team" that operates 24/7.

It connects Ideogram for image generation, OpenAI for intelligent quality review, and Google Drive/Sheets for automated asset management. The system generates images based on creative briefs, evaluates them against your quality standards, organizes approved assets with metadata, and notifies your team—all without manual intervention.

Pro tip: This workflow is particularly valuable for marketing agencies, e-commerce brands, and content teams that need consistent visual content at scale. It turns what was a 2–3 hour manual process into a 5-minute automated one.

How It Works

1. Creative Brief Input

The workflow starts with your creative brief—either manually entered or pulled from a project management tool. This includes image specifications, style guidelines, and any brand requirements that need to be followed.

2. AI Image Generation with Ideogram

Using the Ideogram API via HTTP Request nodes, the workflow generates multiple image variations based on your creative brief. It handles API calls, error management, and result processing automatically.

3. Automated Quality Review with OpenAI

Each generated image is sent to OpenAI for evaluation. The AI assesses factors like relevance to the brief, technical quality, brand alignment, and aesthetic appeal, scoring each image against your predefined criteria.

4. Asset Organization & Management

Approved images are automatically uploaded to structured Google Drive folders with proper naming conventions. Metadata—including creation date, prompt used, approval score, and image URL—is logged in a Google Sheet for easy tracking and retrieval.

5. Notification & Delivery

The workflow sends email notifications with links to the generated assets and a summary report. Your creative team receives ready-to-use, pre-approved images without having to search through dozens of options.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your AI design team up and running in under 15 minutes:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Ideogram, OpenAI, Google Drive, Google Sheets, and Gmail in n8n's credential management.
  3. Update folder IDs in the Google Drive nodes to point to your preferred asset organization structure.
  4. Customize quality criteria in the OpenAI node to match your brand's specific standards and requirements.
  5. Test with a simple brief to verify the end-to-end flow works correctly before scaling up.
  6. Schedule or trigger the workflow based on your content production needs—manual trigger, schedule, or webhook from your project management tool.

Pro tip: Start with conservative quality thresholds in the OpenAI review node, then adjust based on your team's feedback. It's easier to relax standards than to discover you're rejecting too many usable images.
