What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually adding products to WooCommerce, especially for stores with large inventories or frequent product updates. It connects your Google Sheets product database directly to WooCommerce, automatically creating products with complete details including images, pricing, variations, and categories.

The workflow goes beyond basic imports by integrating AI to generate optimized Yoast SEO metadata for each product. This includes SEO titles, meta descriptions, focus keywords, and properly structured product descriptions - all automatically formatted to WooCommerce's requirements.

How It Works

Step 1: Google Sheets product data preparation

Your team maintains product information in a structured Google Sheet with columns for all WooCommerce fields. The sheet serves as a single source of truth that multiple team members can edit collaboratively.

Step 2: Automated data validation

The workflow checks each row for required fields, valid formatting, and data consistency before processing. Invalid entries are flagged for correction without interrupting valid imports.

Step 3: AI SEO metadata generation

For each product, the system analyzes the product details and generates SEO-optimized metadata using AI. This includes keyword-rich titles and descriptions that follow Yoast SEO best practices.

Step 4: WooCommerce product creation

The workflow creates the WooCommerce product with all details including images (uploaded from URLs), pricing, inventory, attributes, and variations. It then applies the generated SEO metadata through the Yoast SEO plugin API.
