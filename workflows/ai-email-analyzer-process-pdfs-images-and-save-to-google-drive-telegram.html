What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and time-consuming process of manually reviewing emails and their attachments. Many businesses receive important documents via email - invoices, contracts, applications, or reports - that need to be processed, analyzed, and stored. Traditional manual processing is slow, error-prone, and doesn't scale.

The workflow automatically extracts email attachments (PDFs, images), analyzes them using multiple AI models (DeepSeek, Gemini, OpenRouter), extracts key information, and saves the processed documents to Google Drive while sending notifications via Telegram. It transforms unstructured email content into organized, searchable business data.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow starts when a new email arrives in your designated inbox. It checks for attachments and filters based on your criteria (sender, subject, etc.).

2. Attachment Processing

PDFs and images are extracted from the email. The system converts them to text when needed and prepares them for AI analysis while maintaining original file integrity.

3. AI Analysis

Multiple AI models analyze the content based on your requirements. This could include summarization, data extraction, sentiment analysis, or custom processing rules.

4. Results Storage

Processed documents and extracted data are saved to Google Drive in an organized folder structure. The original email and analysis results are linked for easy reference.

5. Telegram Notification

A summary of the processed email and key findings is sent to your Telegram channel or chat, allowing mobile access and team collaboration.
