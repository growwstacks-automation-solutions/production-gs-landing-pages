What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how your team handles customer emails by combining AI-powered drafting with human oversight. When a new email arrives, the system instantly generates a personalized response using Google Gemini AI enriched with HubSpot contact data, deal information, company details, and ticket history.

The draft email is then sent to Slack for team approval before being sent to the customer. This maintains quality control while dramatically reducing response times. The workflow is particularly valuable for customer support teams, account managers, and sales professionals who need to respond quickly without sacrificing personalization.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New email received

The workflow activates when a new customer email is detected in your connected email system. This could be from Gmail, Outlook, or any email service integrated with n8n.

2. HubSpot data enrichment

The system pulls all relevant customer information from HubSpot including contact details, deal stage, company information, and past interactions. This context is crucial for creating personalized responses.

3. AI draft generation

Google Gemini analyzes the incoming email and HubSpot data to generate a professional, context-aware response. The AI considers tone, previous interactions, and business relationship status.

4. Slack approval workflow

The draft is posted to a designated Slack channel where team members can review, approve with one click, or suggest edits. Approved emails are automatically sent while flagged drafts return for revision.
