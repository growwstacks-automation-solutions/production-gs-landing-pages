What This Workflow Does

Content marketing teams spend countless hours researching, writing, and formatting guest posts for SEO and outreach. This manual process is slow, inconsistent, and difficult to scale. The AI Guest Post Generator automates this entire workflow, transforming your content production from a bottleneck into a scalable system.

This n8n template connects Google Sheets (where you manage your editorial calendar and topics) with OpenAI's powerful language models. It reads your content briefs, applies your brand guidelines and SEO rules, generates complete guest post drafts, and saves them back to Sheets—ready for review and publishing. The automation handles everything from topic selection to content generation, ensuring consistent quality while freeing your team for strategic work.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence that mirrors how content teams operate, but with complete automation.

Step 1: Read Content Planning Data

The automation starts by connecting to your Google Sheet containing guest post planning data. This includes topics, target publications, keywords, tone guidelines, and status columns. It filters for entries marked "Start" to process only approved topics.

Step 2: Merge Writing Instructions

The system combines your writing prompt, brand guidelines, and specific posting rules into a comprehensive instruction set for the AI. This ensures every generated article follows your exact requirements for voice, structure, and SEO optimization.

Step 3: Generate AI Content

Using OpenAI's AI Agent node, the merged instructions are sent to generate a complete guest post. The template is configured for German content but can be adapted to any language. The AI produces articles that match your specified length, style, and technical requirements.

Step 4: Save and Update Status

The generated content is saved into a separate Google Sheet for your content library. Simultaneously, the original row's status is updated from "Start" to "Finished," preventing duplicate processing and giving your team clear visibility into what's been completed.

Pro tip: Use the optional Gmail node to receive notifications when no action was taken (empty rows), helping you keep your content pipeline full without manual checking.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting started takes about 15 minutes if you have your accounts ready.

  1. Download and Import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Connect Credentials for both Google Sheets and OpenAI in n8n's credentials management
  3. Update Sheet IDs in the Google Sheets nodes to point to your actual planning and output sheets
  4. Customize the Prompt in the AI Agent node to match your brand voice, language, and content requirements
  5. Test with One Row by marking a topic as "Start" and executing the workflow manually
  6. Schedule Automation (optional) by changing the manual trigger to a schedule or webhook for hands-off operation
