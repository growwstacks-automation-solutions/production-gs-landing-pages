What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating personalized icebreakers for cold outreach at scale. Traditional manual research is time-consuming and inconsistent, while generic templates get poor response rates. The workflow automatically scrapes professional websites using Dumpling AI, extracts key details about your prospects, and generates personalized conversation starters that get attention.

The system saves these AI-generated icebreakers in Airtable, creating a searchable database of personalized outreach content. This eliminates hours of manual research while improving the quality and consistency of your outreach messages. The automation works for LinkedIn outreach, cold emails, or any scenario where you need to quickly establish rapport with new contacts.

How It Works

1. Website scraping with Dumpling AI

The workflow starts by passing a list of website URLs to Dumpling AI. The AI analyzes each page, extracting key information about the person or company including names, job titles, achievements, and notable content.

2. AI icebreaker generation

Using the scraped data, the system generates 3-5 potential icebreakers per contact. These reference specific details from their online presence to create authentic, personalized conversation starters.

3. Quality filtering

The workflow includes validation steps to ensure icebreakers meet quality standards before saving. This removes any generic or irrelevant suggestions that might slip through.

4. Airtable storage

Final icebreakers are saved to Airtable along with the source URL and timestamp. The structured format makes it easy to search and retrieve icebreakers when needed.
