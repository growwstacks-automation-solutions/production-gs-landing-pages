What This Workflow Does

This AI legal assistant workflow automates two critical legal tasks: answering legal questions and retrieving relevant documents. When a user submits a question, the AI analyzes it using natural language processing, searches your document database, and returns both a summarized answer and the most relevant source materials.

The system is particularly valuable for law firms handling repetitive inquiries, corporate legal departments managing compliance questions, or any practice needing faster access to case law and contracts. It reduces research time from hours to minutes while maintaining audit trails of all sources used.

How It Works

1. Question Submission

Users submit legal questions through a web form, email, or chat interface. The system captures the query along with any context about jurisdiction, practice area, or urgency.

2. AI Analysis

The workflow sends the question to an AI model (like GPT-4 or Claude) trained on legal terminology. The AI breaks down the query into searchable components and identifies relevant legal concepts.

3. Document Retrieval

Using vector search technology, the system scans your document repository (PDFs, Word files, case databases) to find passages most relevant to the question. It prioritizes recent and authoritative sources.

4. Answer Generation

The AI synthesizes information from retrieved documents into a clear, concise answer with proper citations. It flags any areas where human review is recommended.

5. Delivery & Logging

The system delivers the answer through the user's preferred channel while logging the query, sources used, and response for compliance purposes.
