What This Workflow Does

This AI Personal Assistant workflow solves the universal problem of communication overload. For executives, founders, and busy professionals, managing email, Slack messages, and calendar invites can consume 20+ hours per week. This automation acts as your digital chief of staff, proactively monitoring all channels, analyzing priorities, and delivering a concise daily briefing with actionable tasks.

The system doesn't just forward notifications—it understands context. It reads your emails, checks your calendar for follow-ups, scans Slack for unanswered mentions, and uses AI to determine what's truly urgent versus what can wait. It then compiles everything into a prioritized to-do list in Google Sheets and sends you a personalized Slack summary every morning.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a smart, multi-agent architecture that mimics how a human assistant would manage your communications.

1. Scheduled Daily Trigger

Every weekday at 8:00 AM, the workflow automatically begins its analysis. You can also trigger it manually for on-demand reviews before important meetings or at the end of a busy day.

2. Email Assistant Agent

This agent scans your Gmail inbox with intelligent filtering. It doesn't just look at unread emails—it checks labeled folders like "To Respond" and "FYI," examines email history to understand relationship context, identifies company-related opportunities, and categorizes messages by urgency (High, Medium, Low). It even cross-references with your sent emails to track ongoing conversations.

3. Follow-Up Assistant Agent

This component reviews your last 3 days of Google Calendar meetings, fetches transcripts from Fireflies.ai for recorded sessions, and identifies meetings without post-meeting communication. It flags appointments requiring action items or follow-ups and checks whether you've already responded via email or Slack.

4. Slack Assistant Agent

Monitoring both direct messages and channel @mentions, this agent identifies unreplied conversations, prioritizes them based on sender importance and message content, and cross-references them with your email and calendar context to provide complete communication visibility.

5. Master Orchestrator Agent

The brain of the operation. This AI agent synthesizes reports from all three assistants, cross-references with your existing Google Sheets to-do list, prioritizes tasks by business impact and urgency, identifies correlations between different communication channels, and creates your comprehensive daily action plan.

6. Task Management & Delivery

Finally, the workflow appends new tasks to your Google Sheets tracker, sends your personalized daily briefing via Slack DM, maintains conversation memory for context continuity, and tracks outstanding versus completed items—creating a closed-loop system that learns from your behavior over time.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement your AI personal assistant in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your Gmail account using OAuth in the Email Assistant node.
  3. Set up Google Calendar integration and specify which calendars to monitor.
  4. Configure your Slack connection with appropriate channel and DM access.
  5. Add your AI API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) to the Master Orchestrator agent.
  6. Create a Google Sheet for task tracking and link it to the workflow.
  7. Test with manual trigger to verify all connections work correctly.
  8. Schedule the daily execution for your preferred morning review time.

The workflow includes detailed instructions at each connection point. If you get stuck at any step, refer to the n8n documentation for that specific node—most connections require just a few clicks to authorize.
