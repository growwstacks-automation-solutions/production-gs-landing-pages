What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered workflow transforms Telegram into your personal Amazon shopping assistant. Users can simply message a product name or description to a Telegram bot, and receive curated Amazon product recommendations powered by Gemini 2.5 AI through Decodo's integration platform.

The system eliminates manual product research by analyzing Amazon's catalog with AI to surface the most relevant matches based on price, features, reviews, and availability. It's particularly valuable for e-commerce businesses, affiliate marketers, and busy shoppers who want instant access to vetted product options.

How It Works

1. User submits product request via Telegram

The workflow begins when a user sends a product query (like "wireless headphones under $100") to your Telegram bot. The message triggers the automation sequence.

2. Gemini AI processes the request

The system sends the query to Gemini 2.5 which understands the intent, extracts key parameters (product type, price range, features), and prepares an optimized Amazon search strategy.

3. Decodo fetches Amazon product data

Decodo's Amazon integration executes the AI-generated search parameters, retrieving current product listings, prices, ratings, and availability directly from Amazon's API.

4. AI ranks and formats recommendations

Gemini evaluates the Amazon results, ranks products by relevance/value, and formats a response with top 3-5 recommendations including key details and purchase links.

5. Recommendations delivered via Telegram

The final curated recommendations are sent back to the user through Telegram in an easy-to-read format with clickable Amazon links for instant purchasing.
