What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your hiring process by combining AI-powered candidate screening with intelligent interview scheduling. It eliminates manual resume screening and the back-and-forth of scheduling interviews, saving recruiters hours per candidate while ensuring you never miss top talent.

The workflow automatically evaluates incoming applications using OpenAI's GPT models to assess candidate qualifications against your job requirements. Qualified candidates are then seamlessly scheduled for interviews using Google Calendar, with automated notifications sent to both recruiters and candidates.

How It Works

Step 1: Application Receipt

The workflow triggers when new applications are received through your preferred application system (email, ATS, or forms). It extracts candidate information and resumes for processing.

Step 2: AI Screening

OpenAI GPT analyzes each application against predefined criteria you specify. It evaluates qualifications, experience, and responses to identify the strongest matches for your position.

Step 3: Interview Scheduling

For qualified candidates, the system checks interviewer availability in Google Calendar and proposes optimal time slots. Candidates receive scheduling links to book interviews without calendar conflicts.

Step 4: Notification & Follow-up

Both recruiters and candidates receive automated confirmation emails with interview details. The system also logs all interactions in your HR system for tracking.

Pro tip: Configure your OpenAI prompts to evaluate both hard skills and cultural fit by including your company values in the assessment criteria.
