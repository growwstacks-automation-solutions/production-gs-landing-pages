What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the tedious manual process of screening job applicants into an efficient AI-powered system. It connects your ERPNext HR module with AI analysis tools through n8n to automatically evaluate resumes against job requirements, rank candidates, and update your hiring pipeline.

HR teams waste countless hours reviewing unqualified applications. This solution automatically filters out mismatched candidates, highlights top talent based on your historical hiring patterns, and provides structured data to make informed decisions faster. The system learns from each hiring cycle to continually improve its recommendations.

How It Works

1. Application Collection

The workflow monitors your ERPNext Job Opening records for new applications, either through direct submissions or imported from job boards. Each new applicant triggers the automation sequence.

2. AI Resume Analysis

Candidate resumes are processed through AI services that extract skills, experience levels, education, and other qualifications. The system compares these against the job's requirements with weighted scoring.

3. Candidate Ranking

Applicants receive composite scores based on how closely they match the position. The workflow considers both hard requirements (certifications, years of experience) and soft factors (cultural fit indicators from past successful hires).

4. ERPNext Updates

Top-ranked candidates are automatically flagged in ERPNext and moved to the interview stage. Recruiters receive notifications with the AI's assessment summary and recommended interview focus areas.
