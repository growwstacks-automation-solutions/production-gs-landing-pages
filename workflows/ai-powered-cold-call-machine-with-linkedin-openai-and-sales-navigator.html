What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation is a massive time sink for sales teams. Researching companies, finding decision-makers, and crafting personalized outreach for each prospect can consume 10-15 hours per week per sales rep. This AI-powered cold call machine automates the entire front-end of your sales funnel.

The workflow systematically discovers target companies on LinkedIn, enriches their data, uses AI to score their fit for your product or service, identifies key contacts via Sales Navigator, and generates a personalized 20-second cold call script for each lead. All qualified data is automatically organized into a Google Sheets CRM, ready for your sales team to execute.

Instead of spending hours on manual research, your SDRs or founders can focus on having high-conversion conversations with pre-qualified, pre-researched leads. This turns cold calling from a numbers game into a targeted, intelligent outreach process.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical, multi-stage process to ensure only the most relevant leads are passed to your sales team.

1. Initialization & Configuration

The workflow can be triggered manually for a one-time run or scheduled to operate daily/weekly. It first pulls all configuration parameters from a dedicated "Settings" tab in a Google Sheet. This includes your target product keywords, ideal company size, location filters, and API keys for LinkedIn (via Ghost Genius) and OpenAI.

2. Intelligent Company Search

Using the Ghost Genius API, the system searches LinkedIn for companies based on your cleaned keywords. OpenAI helps refine these search terms for better relevance. The search handles pagination, allowing it to process up to 1,000 companies per batch, ensuring broad market coverage.

3. Data Enrichment & Filtering

Each discovered company is enriched with data: website, employee count, LinkedIn followers, and industry. The workflow then applies hard filters: the company must have a LinkedIn page with a website listed and at least 200 followers (indicating activity). It also checks your CRM sheet to avoid duplicate entries.

4. AI-Powered Qualification Scoring

This is the core intelligence layer. A specialized AI model (via OpenAI) analyzes the enriched company data and scores it from 0 to 10 based on industry fit, size/location alignment, and inferred pain points that match your offering. Only companies scoring 7 or higher are saved to the "Companies" sheet, creating a hot list.

5. Decision-Maker Identification & Script Generation

For each qualified company, the workflow uses the Sales Navigator API (via Ghost Genius) to find employees with targeted job titles (e.g., "Head of Marketing", "CTO"). For each profile, it enriches contact data, attempts to retrieve a phone number, and then uses OpenAI to generate a unique, personalized cold call script. All this information—contact details, company score, and the custom script—is saved to the "Leads" tab.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to import and configure the automation in your n8n environment.

  1. Download & Import: Click the "Download Template" button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n dashboard, go to "Workflows" > "Import from file" and select the downloaded JSON.
  2. Configure Credentials: In the n8n workflow, set up the required credentials: your OpenAI API key and Ghost Genius API key in the respective nodes.
  3. Set Up Your Google Sheet: Duplicate the provided Google Sheet template. Note the Sheet ID and share it with the service account email from your n8n Google Sheets node setup. Populate the "Settings" tab with your target keywords, filters, and product details.
  4. Test the Trigger: Run the "Manual Trigger" node once to test the connection flow. Check the "Companies" and "Leads" tabs in your Google Sheet to see the sample output.
  5. Schedule Execution (Optional): Replace the Manual Trigger node with a "Schedule Trigger" node to run the workflow automatically daily or weekly.
  6. Refine & Deploy: Review the first batch of leads and AI-generated scripts. Tweak your settings and keywords in the Google Sheet for better targeting, then let the automation run.
