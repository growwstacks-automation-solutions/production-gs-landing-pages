What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered corporate research system transforms how animal advocacy organizations identify and analyze campaign targets. Traditional corporate research requires painstaking manual analysis of financial reports, supply chain disclosures, and policy statements - a process that can take weeks per company. Our automated workflow uses Claude AI and Gemini to process this data in hours, identifying key vulnerabilities and opportunities for animal welfare campaigns.

The system generates comprehensive dossiers on target companies including their animal-related policies, supply chain exposures, executive statements, and historical positions. It cross-references this data against animal welfare standards to highlight gaps and inconsistencies. Campaign teams receive prioritized recommendations on which companies offer the most strategic opportunities for impact.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow automatically gathers corporate data from SEC filings, sustainability reports, news archives, and specialized databases. It identifies key documents containing animal welfare-related information using targeted search parameters.

2. AI Analysis

Claude AI performs ethical analysis of corporate policies and statements, evaluating them against animal welfare frameworks. Simultaneously, Gemini processes quantitative data on supply chains, financials, and operational metrics to identify material risks.

3. Insight Generation

The system synthesizes findings into executive summaries highlighting campaign opportunities. It identifies persuasive arguments, potential allies within the company, and vulnerabilities in their public positioning on animal welfare issues.

4. Monitoring & Alerts

Ongoing monitoring tracks changes in corporate policies, executive statements, and supply chain practices. The system alerts researchers to significant developments that may require campaign adjustments.
