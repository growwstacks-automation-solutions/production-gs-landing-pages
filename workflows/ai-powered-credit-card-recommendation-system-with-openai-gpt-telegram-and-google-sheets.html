What This Workflow Does

Choosing the right credit card is overwhelming. With hundreds of options, hidden fees, complex reward structures, and eligibility criteria, most people end up with suboptimal cards that don't match their spending patterns. This leads to missed rewards, unnecessary fees, and poor financial fit.

This intelligent automation solves that problem. It acts as a personalized credit card advisor, powered by AI. Users interact via a simple Telegram quiz about their lifestyle, spending habits, and goals. The system processes their answers, scores them against a comprehensive card database in Google Sheets, uses OpenAI GPT to generate nuanced recommendations, and instantly delivers a ranked list of the top 3 most suitable cards—complete with a transparent breakdown of annual value, perks, and why each card fits.

Beyond just matching, it calculates the tangible monetary value of rewards, lounge access, and milestone bonuses, presenting a clear "₹-value" justification for each recommendation. This turns a confusing decision into a data-driven, transparent, and instant process.

How It Works

The workflow orchestrates a seamless conversation between the user and your card database, using AI to bridge the gap.

Step 1: User Onboarding via Telegram Quiz

A Telegram bot asks the user 7–10 key questions: monthly spend categories (travel, dining, groceries, fuel), average transaction size, airport lounge preference, credit score range, and annual fee tolerance. The conversation is natural and guided.

Step 2: Data Capture & Storage in Google Sheets

Each answer is logged in a Google Sheet row dedicated to that user session. This provides a persistent record for analysis, deduplication (if a user restarts), and future auditing. The sheet also acts as the master card database.

Step 3: Intelligent Scoring with n8n Logic

n8n Function nodes map user responses to numerical scores. For example, high travel spend increases the score for travel reward cards. Multi-select answers are weighted appropriately. This creates a quantified "user profile" vector.

Step 4: AI-Powered Analysis & Matching

The user profile and the card database are sent to OpenAI GPT. The AI doesn't just pick the highest reward rate; it evaluates the holistic fit—considering fee waivers, network acceptability, milestone achievability, and the user's stated preferences—generating a reasoned recommendation.

Step 5: Value Breakdown & Delivery

The final step formats the AI's output into a clean, easy-to-understand message sent back via Telegram. It highlights the top 3 cards, estimated annual reward value, key perks, and a one-line rationale for each, empowering the user to make an informed decision.

Pro tip: Use a separate scheduled workflow to keep your Google Sheets card database updated. Scrape bank websites or subscribe to affiliate feeds to automatically refresh rates, fees, and offers, ensuring your recommendations are always based on the latest information.

  4. Google Sheets with edit access (for the card database and user logs).

Quick Setup Guide

Get this system running in your environment in under 30 minutes.

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure Credentials: In n8n, set up credentials for Telegram Bot, OpenAI, and Google Sheets (using the Service Account JSON key).
  3. Prepare Your Google Sheet: Duplicate the provided sheet structure. Populate it with your card data (Card Name, Issuer, Annual Fee, Reward Rate, Categories, Key Perks, etc.).
  4. Update Sheet IDs: In the n8n workflow, replace the placeholder Google Sheet IDs with your actual spreadsheet IDs for both the card database and the user log sheet.
  5. Customize the Telegram Quiz: Adjust the questions in the "Telegram Trigger" node to match your recommendation logic and user language.
  6. Test & Activate: Turn on the workflow, message your Telegram bot, and go through the quiz. Check the Google Sheets log and your Telegram chat for the final recommendation.
