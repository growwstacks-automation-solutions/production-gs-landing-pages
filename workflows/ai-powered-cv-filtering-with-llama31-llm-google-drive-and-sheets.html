What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manual resume screening in the recruitment process. When hiring for multiple positions or receiving hundreds of applications, HR teams often spend 20-30 hours per week just on initial CV screening. The workflow automatically processes new resumes uploaded to a designated Google Drive folder, analyzes them using the Llama3.1 large language model, and updates a centralized Google Sheet with candidate qualifications and screening status.

The AI evaluates each resume against your predefined criteria (skills, experience, education, etc.), assigns a matching score, and flags top candidates. This creates a standardized, bias-reduced first screening layer that works 24/7, dramatically accelerating your hiring pipeline while ensuring no qualified candidates get overlooked in manual reviews.

How It Works

Step 1: New CV Detection

The workflow monitors a specific Google Drive folder for new resume uploads. When a candidate submits their application (typically via a form or email attachment that saves to Drive), the system immediately detects the new file and prepares it for processing.

Step 2: Document Processing

The system extracts text from various resume formats (PDF, DOCX, etc.) and structures the information for analysis. It identifies key sections like work experience, education, and skills while normalizing the data for consistent evaluation.

Step 3: AI Analysis with Llama3.1

The workflow sends the processed resume data to the Llama3.1 LLM with your customized scoring instructions. The AI evaluates how well the candidate matches your job requirements, considering factors like relevant experience, skill proficiency, education level, and other criteria you specify.

Step 4: Results Logging

The system records the AI's assessment in your Google Sheets candidate tracker, including matching score, strengths, potential concerns, and recommended next steps (interview, reject, or additional screening). This creates a searchable, filterable database of all applicants.
