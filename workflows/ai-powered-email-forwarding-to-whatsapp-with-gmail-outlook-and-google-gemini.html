What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves the problem of missing critical emails buried in overflowing inboxes. By combining AI-powered filtering with instant WhatsApp delivery, it ensures important messages reach you immediately through your preferred communication channel.

The workflow continuously monitors your Gmail and Outlook accounts, using Google Gemini's AI to analyze incoming emails. It identifies and forwards only the most important messages to your WhatsApp number or group, saving you from constantly checking your email while ensuring you never miss urgent communications.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow connects to your Gmail or Outlook account and monitors incoming emails in real-time. You can specify which labels/folders to watch or have it scan your entire inbox.

2. AI Content Analysis

Each email passes through Google Gemini's AI model, which evaluates the content based on importance indicators like urgency keywords, sender reputation, and contextual relevance to your business.

3. Smart Filtering

The AI assigns an importance score and decides whether to forward the message. You can adjust sensitivity thresholds to match your preferences.

4. WhatsApp Delivery

Approved messages are formatted for WhatsApp and sent to your specified number or group. The workflow can include key details like sender, subject, and priority content while excluding less important parts.

Pro tip: Train the AI by marking forwarded messages as correct/incorrect decisions. The system learns your preferences over time for increasingly accurate filtering.
