What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of processing high volumes of customer and staff feedback efficiently. Instead of manually reviewing every submission, the workflow uses AI to intelligently categorize and route feedback to the appropriate teams.

Key capabilities include automatically identifying urgent bugs that need immediate attention, separating feature requests from general comments, and logging all submissions in a structured format for future analysis. The system maintains context by including the original feedback text in all destinations.

How It Works

1. Feedback Collection

The workflow triggers when a new submission arrives in your Jotform. It captures all form fields including the feedback details, submitter type (customer/staff), and optional contact email.

2. AI Analysis

Gemini AI processes the feedback text to determine the category (bug, feature request, or general comment), suggest priority level, and extract relevant tags. This structured output drives the routing logic.

3. Intelligent Routing

Based on the AI analysis, the workflow sends actionable items to Trello (organized by type), logs general feedback in Airtable, and alerts developers via Slack for critical bugs. Optional email confirmations are sent if the submitter provided contact info.
