What This Workflow Does

Manual candidate screening is one of the most time-consuming aspects of recruitment, often consuming 60-80% of a recruiter's time while yielding inconsistent results. This AI-powered interview system automates the initial screening phase, allowing HR teams to focus only on the most qualified candidates.

The workflow integrates BeyondPresence's AI interview platform with your existing HR systems via n8n. It automatically schedules, conducts, and evaluates initial interviews 24/7, providing structured candidate assessments with measurable data points. This eliminates scheduling hassles, reduces unconscious bias, and creates a standardized evaluation process.

How It Works

1. Candidate Application Trigger

When a new application arrives in your ATS or recruitment platform, the workflow automatically initiates the AI interview process. The system identifies qualified candidates based on your preset criteria.

2. Automated Interview Scheduling

The workflow sends personalized interview invitations with available time slots. Candidates can complete the interview at their convenience via text or video, depending on your configuration.

3. AI-Powered Evaluation

BeyondPresence's AI analyzes candidate responses for content relevance, communication skills, and job-specific competencies. It scores each response against your ideal candidate profile.

4. Results Delivery

The system compiles comprehensive reports with candidate rankings, response analysis, and red flags. These are delivered directly to recruiters' dashboards or integrated with your HR software.
