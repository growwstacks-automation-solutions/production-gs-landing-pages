What This Workflow Does

This intelligent lead management system automatically captures incoming leads via webhook, uses AI to analyze and score each prospect, then routes them to the appropriate team or campaign based on qualification score. It delivers hot leads to sales instantly while nurturing others automatically.

The workflow eliminates manual processes including daily lead review, scoring and prioritization, assignment decisions, follow-up email composition, CRM data entry, and sales team notifications - delivering qualified, routed leads with AI insights in under 30 seconds.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture

The workflow begins by accepting leads from any source (forms, landing pages, APIs) via webhook trigger, creating a standardized lead record.

2. AI Qualification

OpenAI analyzes each lead's message content, extracting key information about budget, timeline, needs and fit while assigning a 0-100 qualification score.

3. Intelligent Routing

Based on the AI-generated score, leads are automatically directed to appropriate destinations - hot leads (70+) to sales, others to nurture campaigns.

4. Notifications & Storage

Slack alerts are sent for high-priority prospects while all lead data is stored in Airtable for tracking and reporting.
