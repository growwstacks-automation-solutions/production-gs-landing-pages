What This Workflow Does

Manual LinkedIn networking is time-consuming and often hit-or-miss. Sales teams, recruiters, and business developers spend hours searching for relevant profiles, only to connect with people who may not be the best fit. This creates missed opportunities and inefficient use of valuable business hours.

This AI-powered LinkedIn connection recommender automates the entire discovery process. It takes your search criteria, queries LinkedIn through an API, processes hundreds of profiles using artificial intelligence, and delivers a curated list of high-potential connections directly to your email. The system evaluates factors like industry relevance, experience alignment, skill matches, and network proximity to provide intelligent recommendations.

Instead of spending 5-10 hours per week on manual networking research, you receive a ready-to-act-upon list in minutes. This transforms LinkedIn from a manual hunting ground into a strategic, automated lead generation and partnership development engine.

How It Works

1. Profile Analysis Workflow

The process begins with manual input of your target criteria—industry, job titles, locations, or specific skills. This data initiates a LinkedIn search via SerpAPI or similar service, gathering profile information from publicly available sources.

2. AI Processing & Scoring

Profile data flows into an AI model (Ollama or similar) that analyzes each candidate against your criteria. The AI evaluates multiple dimensions: career trajectory relevance, skill overlap, content engagement, and potential mutual value. It assigns a compatibility score to each profile.

3. Recommendation Generation

The system filters and ranks profiles based on AI scores, creating a tiered list of recommendations. Top-tier connections (high compatibility) are prioritized, with supporting rationale provided for each recommendation.

4. Delivery & Notification

A personalized email is generated containing the curated connection list, complete with profile links and key insights. The email is sent via your configured email service (SMTP, Gmail, etc.), delivering actionable intelligence directly to your inbox.

What You'll Need

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this LinkedIn automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure API credentials for SerpAPI in the "Search LinkedIn Profiles" node.
  3. Set up your AI model in the "AI Profile Analysis" node (Ollama or OpenAI).
  4. Connect your email service in the "Send Email" node with SMTP details.
  5. Test with a simple search using basic criteria to verify the flow works.
  6. Customize the AI prompt to match your specific industry and connection goals.
  7. Schedule or trigger manually based on your networking cadence.
