What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating consistent, high-quality LinkedIn content. Instead of spending hours researching, writing, and designing visuals, this engine handles everything from topic discovery to final scoring.

The workflow transforms a simple Google Sheets trigger into a complete content production line. It researches current trends using real-time data, crafts posts in your brand voice, generates abstract visual assets, and evaluates content quality—all automatically. The final output is saved back to your spreadsheet, ready for review or immediate scheduling.

For business owners, marketers, and founders, this means maintaining an active professional presence without the weekly grind. You get data-driven content that actually engages your audience, while reclaiming 5-10 hours previously spent on manual creation.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical pipeline that mimics expert content creation but at machine speed.

1. Trigger & Topic Selection

The workflow activates when you update a row status in Google Sheets. This could be marking a topic as "Ready for Research" or setting a publication date. The system extracts your topic, target audience, and any specific instructions from the sheet.

2. Real-Time Research

Using Perplexity AI, the workflow searches for the latest information, trends, and data points around your topic. It gathers recent articles, statistics, and expert opinions to ensure content is current and relevant.

3. Content Creation & Refinement

OpenAI's GPT-4o generates the initial LinkedIn post, then refines it based on tone preferences and structural guidelines. The system can adjust for different formats—educational posts, thought leadership, or promotional content.

4. Visual Asset Generation

Replicate's FLUX Pro model creates abstract, brand-appropriate images based on the content theme. These aren't stock photos but unique visuals that complement your message without copyright concerns.

5. Quality Scoring & Storage

The workflow evaluates the post using heuristics like clarity, engagement potential, and keyword alignment. Everything—the final text, image URL, and score—gets appended back to your Google Sheets for tracking and approval.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow permissions
  2. Google Sheets with edit access for trigger and storage
  3. OpenAI API key for GPT-4o content generation
  4. Perplexity AI API key for real-time research
  5. Replicate API token for image generation (optional but recommended)
  6. Basic understanding of n8n node configuration (credentials and webhook setup)

Pro tip: Start with a small Google Sheet containing 3-5 topic ideas. Test the workflow end-to-end before scaling to weekly content batches. Adjust the AI instructions in the template to match your brand voice—this makes a significant difference in output quality.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement the automation in under 30 minutes.

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets, OpenAI, Perplexity, and Replicate in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update the Google Sheets node with your specific spreadsheet ID and worksheet name.
  4. Adjust AI parameters in the OpenAI and Perplexity nodes—tweak temperature, tone instructions, and research depth.
  5. Test with a single row in your sheet. Change a status to trigger the workflow and verify all steps execute correctly.
  6. Review the output in your sheet. Make minor adjustments to prompts if needed, then scale to regular use.
