What This Workflow Does

Businesses drown in email attachments, Google Drive links, and scattered documents. Sales reports, customer information, address lists, and financial data arrive daily through email, requiring manual sorting, filing, and processing. This not only wastes 10-15 hours per week but also leads to lost files, inconsistent organization, and delayed decision-making.

The AI-powered MIS Agent solves this by creating an intelligent automation system that monitors your inbox, understands what each email contains, extracts attachments or Drive links, routes them to the correct Google Drive folders, and even processes the data inside spreadsheets. It acts as a digital assistant that never sleeps, ensuring your business information is always organized, accessible, and ready for analysis.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger & Classification

The workflow starts by monitoring your Gmail inbox for incoming emails with attachments or Google Drive links. When a relevant email arrives, an AI text classifier reads the email content and subject line to determine its purpose—whether it's a daily sales report, customer information update, address list, or other business document.

2. Attachment & Link Processing

Based on the classification, the system checks if the email contains direct file attachments or Google Drive links. For Drive links, it extracts the file ID and prepares it for organization. For attachments, it downloads the files directly. This ensures all documents are captured regardless of how they were shared.

3. Smart Folder Routing

Using the AI classification results, the workflow routes each document to pre-defined Google Drive folders. Sales reports go to "Sales Data/Daily," customer information to "CRM/Updates," and address lists to "Contacts/Master." The system maintains your folder structure while automating the filing process.

4. Data Processing & Transformation

For spreadsheet attachments, the workflow can perform advanced data operations like cleaning (removing duplicates, standardizing formats), merging multiple files, joining datasets, calculating totals, and generating summaries. This turns raw email attachments into ready-to-use business intelligence.

5. Notification & Logging

Finally, the system logs all processed emails, records where files were saved, and can send notifications to relevant team members. This creates a complete audit trail and ensures transparency in your automated document management system.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for sales teams receiving daily performance reports, operations managers handling customer information updates, finance departments processing invoice attachments, marketing teams collecting campaign data, and any business that relies on email for document sharing. Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this AI-powered MIS Agent in your business:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials for Gmail, Google Drive, and OpenAI in n8n's credentials management
  3. Update folder IDs in the Google Drive nodes to match your actual Drive folder structure
  4. Customize AI classification prompts to match your specific document categories and terminology
  5. Test with sample emails containing attachments and Drive links to verify correct routing
  6. Activate the workflow and monitor initial processing to ensure everything works as expected
  7. Train your team on the new system and establish procedures for exception handling

Implementation note: For the first week, run the automation alongside manual processes to compare results. This parallel run builds trust and helps identify any edge cases your specific business might encounter.
