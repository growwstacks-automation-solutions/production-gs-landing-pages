What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually curating and rewriting news content for social media. It continuously monitors RSS feeds, uses AI to evaluate article quality and relevance, then generates platform-optimized posts ready for publishing or review.

The system eliminates hours of repetitive work while ensuring consistent posting schedules. Content teams can focus on strategy while the automation handles the heavy lifting of content ingestion, scoring, and formatting.

The workflow architecture showing content flow from RSS ingestion through AI processing to social platforms

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow polls your configured RSS feeds on a schedule you define, checking for new articles and passing them to the AI analysis stage.

2. AI Content Scoring

Each article is evaluated by AI for quality, relevance to your audience, and engagement potential. The system extracts key topics and generates a summary.

3. Content Formatting

High-scoring articles are automatically rewritten into platform-specific formats - concise threads for Twitter/X, professional posts for LinkedIn, etc.

4. Publishing & Tracking

Posts can be automatically published or routed for review. All actions are logged to Google Sheets for performance tracking.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. OpenAI API key (for content analysis)
  3. Upload-Post credentials (for multi-platform publishing)
  4. Google Sheets access (for logging)
  5. RSS feed URLs for your content sources

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your RSS feed URLs in the RSS trigger node
  3. Add your OpenAI API credentials
  4. Connect Upload-Post with your social platform credentials
  5. Set up the Google Sheets integration for logging
  6. Adjust quality thresholds and publishing rules as needed
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor initial runs
