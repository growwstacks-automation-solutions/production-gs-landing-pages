What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered automation transforms your sales call process by instantly generating professional proposals from call transcripts. It eliminates the tedious manual work of drafting proposals while ensuring consistency and completeness.

The workflow analyzes call recordings or transcripts, extracts key discussion points about scope, pricing, and timelines, then structures this information into a polished proposal document. It maintains your brand voice and proposal standards while adapting to each client's specific needs discussed during the call.

The workflow processes call data through AI analysis to generate draft proposals

How It Works

1. Call Transcript Processing

The workflow begins by analyzing your sales call transcript or recording. It identifies key elements like discussed services, pricing ranges, timelines, and client requirements using natural language processing.

2. Data Extraction & Organization

AI extracts structured data from the conversation, categorizing information into proposal sections: scope of work, deliverables, pricing, terms, and next steps. It flags any ambiguous points for human review.

3. Template Application

The system applies your branded proposal template, populating it with the extracted data while maintaining consistent formatting, legal language, and design standards across all proposals.

4. Human Review & Delivery

The draft proposal is routed to your team for final review before being automatically sent to the client via your preferred channel (email, CRM, etc.) with tracking enabled.

Pro tip: Train the AI with your past successful proposals to improve its understanding of your pricing structures and service descriptions.
