What This Workflow Does

Manual video production is time-consuming, expensive, and difficult to scale. Marketing teams struggle to create personalized video content for different products, audiences, and platforms. This creates bottlenecks in campaigns and limits testing of creative approaches.

This AI-powered workflow automates the entire product video creation process. It pulls product data and competitor insights from Foreplay, generates creative video prompts using Google Gemini AI, produces cinematic videos through Sora 2 or similar text-to-video services, and automatically saves finished videos to Google Drive. The result is a scalable system that turns product information into engaging video ads in minutes instead of days.

How It Works

1. Data Collection from Foreplay

The workflow begins by fetching product information and competitor video data from Foreplay. This provides context about what's working in your market, including successful ad angles, messaging styles, and visual approaches that resonate with your target audience.

2. AI-Prompt Generation with Google Gemini

Using the collected data, Google Gemini AI creates detailed, creative text-to-video prompts. These prompts include specific instructions about scene composition, camera angles, lighting, mood, and key messaging points tailored to your product and competitive landscape.

3. Video Generation via Sora 2/Kie.ai

The AI-generated prompts are sent to a text-to-video service (like Sora 2 or Kie.ai) along with product images. The service creates short, cinematic product videos that match the creative direction, complete with visual effects, transitions, and professional-quality rendering.

4. Automated Storage and Organization

Finished videos are automatically uploaded to Google Drive with proper naming conventions, folder organization, and metadata. This creates a centralized library of marketing assets ready for distribution across social media, websites, and advertising platforms.

Pro tip: For best results, ensure your product images are high-resolution and well-lit. The quality of input images directly impacts the visual appeal of AI-generated videos.

What You'll Need

  1. Foreplay account for accessing competitor video data and ad intelligence
  2. Google Gemini API access for AI-powered prompt generation
  3. Text-to-video service account (Sora 2, Kie.ai, or similar) with API credentials
  4. Google Drive account with a dedicated folder for video storage
  5. Product images and descriptions in a structured format (CSV, Google Sheets, or database)
  6. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your business:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials for Foreplay, Google Gemini, your text-to-video service, and Google Drive in n8n's credential management
  3. Set up your product data source – this could be a Google Sheet, Airtable base, or direct database connection
  4. Test with a single product by running the workflow manually to verify all connections work correctly
  5. Schedule automation to run daily, weekly, or trigger based on new product additions
  6. Review and refine the AI prompts based on initial video results to improve quality over time

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of products to fine-tune your prompts before scaling to your entire catalog. This helps establish quality benchmarks and workflow reliability.
