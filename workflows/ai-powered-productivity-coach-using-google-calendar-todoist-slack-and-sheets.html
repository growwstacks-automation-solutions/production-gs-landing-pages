What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered productivity coach analyzes your work patterns across Google Calendar and Todoist to identify inefficiencies and opportunities for improvement. It automatically tracks metrics like meeting-to-work ratio, task completion rates, priority alignment, and focus time consistency.

The system delivers personalized coaching messages via Slack with actionable suggestions based on your data. Unlike manual tracking, it spots subtle patterns you might miss and benchmarks your metrics against productivity best practices. The workflow also logs trends in Google Sheets for long-term analysis.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls events from your Google Calendar and completed tasks from Todoist. It categorizes calendar events (meetings, deep work, etc.) and analyzes task completion patterns.

2. AI Analysis

An AI model processes the raw data to identify productivity patterns. It looks for trends like meeting overload, context switching frequency, priority mismatches, and optimal focus periods.

Pro tip: The AI learns your personal work rhythms over time, providing increasingly accurate suggestions as it gathers more data.

3. Coaching Delivery

Insights are delivered via scheduled Slack messages with clear, actionable recommendations. The system highlights both positive patterns to maintain and areas needing improvement.

4. Trend Tracking

All metrics are logged in Google Sheets with timestamped records, allowing you to track progress over weeks and months through simple visualizations.
