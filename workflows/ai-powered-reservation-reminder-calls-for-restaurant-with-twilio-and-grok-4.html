What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the common problem of restaurant no-shows by automatically calling customers to confirm their reservations. By combining Twilio's telephony capabilities with Grok-4's advanced language generation, the system creates personalized, natural-sounding reminder calls that maintain your brand voice while saving staff time.

The workflow pulls reservation data from your booking system, generates context-aware messages, and makes phone calls at optimal times before appointments. It handles the entire process from data collection to confirmation logging, reducing manual work by 80% while improving customer experience with timely, professional communications.

How It Works

1. Reservation Data Collection

The workflow checks your booking system for upcoming reservations, filtering for those needing confirmation. It extracts key details like customer name, party size, reservation time, and contact number.

2. AI Message Generation

Grok-4 creates natural-sounding scripts using your predefined templates and the specific reservation details. The AI adapts tone and content based on time of day, party size, and customer history when available.

3. Automated Phone Calls

Twilio places the calls at scheduled times (typically 24 hours before reservation), using text-to-speech to deliver the AI-generated message. Customers hear a friendly, professional reminder that sounds human.

4. Confirmation Tracking

The system logs which reservations were successfully reminded and updates your booking system, preventing duplicate calls. Failed attempts trigger follow-up actions based on your preferences.

What You'll Need

  1. Twilio account with phone number
  2. Access to your reservation data (Google Sheets, booking system API, etc.)
  3. OpenRouter API key for Grok-4 access
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  5. 10-15 minutes for initial setup

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Twilio credentials in the settings
  3. Configure the Google Sheets/API connection to your reservation data
  4. Customize the AI message templates to match your brand voice
  5. Set your preferred calling schedule (default is 24 hours before booking)
  6. Test with a few reservations before enabling full automation

Pro tip: Start with a limited test group to refine your message templates before rolling out to all reservations.
