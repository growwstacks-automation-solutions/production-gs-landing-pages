What This Workflow Does

Salon and spa owners waste countless hours managing appointments manually—fielding phone calls, checking availability, dealing with no-shows, and juggling stylist schedules. This creates administrative overhead, reduces time for actual service delivery, and leads to customer frustration when they can't book outside business hours.

This automation solves all these problems by creating an intelligent, AI-powered booking assistant that operates through WhatsApp. Customers can have natural conversations to check availability, book appointments, get personalized stylist recommendations, and modify existing bookings—all without human intervention. The system remembers customer preferences, maintains context across conversations, and keeps all records organized in Google Sheets.

Beyond basic scheduling, the AI provides a premium customer experience. It can recommend services based on past visits, suggest available time slots that match customer preferences, send confirmation messages, and handle complex requests like "I want the same stylist as last time for a haircut next Thursday afternoon."

How It Works

1. Customer Initiates Conversation

A customer sends a message to your salon's WhatsApp number. This could be a simple "I'd like to book an appointment" or a specific request like "Can I get a haircut with Sarah tomorrow afternoon?" The workflow triggers immediately upon receiving the message.

2. AI Understands & Remembers Context

The AI assistant analyzes the message using natural language processing. It checks if this is a returning customer (using phone number) and recalls their previous preferences—favorite stylist, typical services, past appointment history. This memory function prevents repetitive questions and creates a personalized experience.

3. Availability Check & Recommendations

The system queries your Google Sheets database containing stylist schedules, service durations, and salon hours. It identifies available slots that match the customer's request, considering preferred times, staff availability, and service requirements. If the initial request isn't available, it suggests alternatives.

4. Appointment Booking & Confirmation

Once the customer confirms details, the workflow creates a new row in your Google Sheets appointment log with all relevant information: customer name, phone, service, stylist, date, time, and any special notes. It then sends a confirmation message via WhatsApp with appointment details and any pre-appointment instructions.

5. Modification & Cancellation Handling

If a customer needs to reschedule or cancel, they simply message about the change. The AI finds their existing appointment by phone number, updates or removes the Google Sheets record, and sends updated confirmation. This eliminates the need for staff to manually search and update records.

Pro tip: Use the Google Sheets database not just for appointments but also for business analytics. Track popular services, peak booking times, stylist performance, and customer retention—all automatically generated from your booking data.

What You'll Need

  1. WhatsApp Business Account: A registered WhatsApp Business number where customers will message for appointments.
  2. Google Workspace Account: Access to Google Sheets to serve as your appointment database (free Google accounts work perfectly).
  3. AI/LLM Access: An API key from OpenAI, Anthropic, or another LLM provider for the conversational AI capabilities.
  4. n8n Instance: Either n8n.cloud (hosted) or a self-hosted n8n installation to run the workflow.
  5. Service & Staff Database: A Google Sheet with your service menu, prices, durations, and staff availability schedules.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n instance, go to Workflows → Import from file and select the downloaded template.
  2. Configure WhatsApp Connection: Set up the WhatsApp node using either the Twilio WhatsApp API, Meta WhatsApp Business API, or another WhatsApp connector you prefer.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: Add your Google Sheets credentials and point the nodes to your specific spreadsheet for appointments and service data.
  4. Add AI Credentials: Enter your AI provider API key in the AI agent node. Configure the memory buffer to retain customer context appropriately.
  5. Customize Your Data: Populate your Google Sheets with your actual services, staff, working hours, and any custom messages or business rules.
  6. Test Thoroughly: Send test messages from different phone numbers to verify the booking flow, edge cases, and confirmation messages work correctly.
  7. Go Live: Once testing is complete, activate the workflow and share your WhatsApp number with customers as your primary booking channel.

Pro tip: Start with a small group of existing customers for beta testing. Gather their feedback on the booking experience and refine your AI responses before rolling out to all customers.
