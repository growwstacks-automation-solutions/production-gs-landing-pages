What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered Scrum Master assistant automates key agile ceremonies and daily coordination tasks that typically require manual intervention. It acts as a virtual facilitator for your development team, handling standup coordination, sprint tracking, and blocker resolution through intelligent automation.

The system integrates with Slack for team communication, Asana for task management, and OpenAI for intelligent analysis and recommendations. It reduces the administrative burden on human Scrum Masters while ensuring agile processes run smoothly and consistently.

How It Works

1. Daily Standup Automation

The workflow automatically initiates daily standups in Slack, prompting team members for their updates. It collects responses and uses OpenAI to analyze progress, identify blockers, and suggest next steps.

2. Sprint Progress Tracking

By integrating with Asana, the assistant monitors sprint progress in real-time. It calculates completion percentages, identifies at-risk tasks, and provides predictive analytics about sprint success.

3. Blocker Resolution

When the system detects blockers from standup responses or task statuses, it automatically routes them to the appropriate team members or creates escalation tickets based on predefined rules.

4. Retrospective Preparation

At the end of each sprint, the AI compiles key metrics, team sentiment analysis, and improvement suggestions to streamline retrospective meetings.
