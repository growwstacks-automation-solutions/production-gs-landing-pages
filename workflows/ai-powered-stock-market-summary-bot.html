What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manual stock market analysis by combining technical indicators with AI-powered interpretation. It fetches real-time market data, calculates Relative Strength Index (RSI) and Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) values, then uses OpenAI to generate concise, actionable summaries in natural language.

The system delivers these insights directly to your Slack channel every hour, keeping your team informed without requiring anyone to manually analyze charts. For investment firms, trading desks, or active investors, this automation can save 10+ hours per week while improving decision consistency.

How It Works

Step 1: Market Data Collection

The workflow connects to your preferred market data API (like Alpha Vantage or Yahoo Finance) to fetch real-time price information for your watchlist stocks. It retrieves the necessary historical data to calculate technical indicators.

Step 2: Technical Analysis

Using the collected price data, the system automatically calculates RSI (14-period standard) and MACD (12,26,9 standard settings) values. These indicators help identify overbought/oversold conditions and trend strength.

Step 3: AI Interpretation

The raw indicator values are fed into OpenAI's API with carefully crafted prompts that ask for professional-grade market analysis. The AI contextualizes the numbers, explaining what they mean in current market conditions.

Step 4: Report Generation

The workflow formats the AI's analysis into a clean, Slack-optimized message with emoji indicators for quick scanning. It highlights critical levels, potential reversals, and notable changes from previous readings.

Step 5: Scheduled Delivery

Configured to run hourly, the system posts the completed analysis to your designated Slack channel. The template includes error handling to ensure reliability even during market volatility.
