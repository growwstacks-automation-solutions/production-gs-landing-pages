What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms how teams document and share their n8n workflows. By connecting Google Drive, Notion, and LinkedIn with AI-powered processing, it eliminates manual documentation work while amplifying your workflow's visibility.

The system automatically generates comprehensive documentation whenever a new workflow JSON is uploaded to Google Drive. Google Gemini AI analyzes the workflow structure and creates human-readable descriptions, which are then formatted into professional Notion pages. Simultaneously, the AI crafts engaging LinkedIn posts to showcase your automation expertise.

How It Works

1. File Upload Trigger

The workflow activates when a new JSON file is added to your designated Google Drive folder. This provides a simple drag-and-drop interface for initiating the documentation process.

2. AI Analysis

Google Gemini AI examines the workflow structure, identifying key nodes, connections, and functionality. The AI generates both technical documentation and marketing-friendly descriptions.

3. Notion Documentation

The system creates a new Notion page with the workflow title, AI-generated description, and direct download link. Documentation follows your predefined template structure for consistency.

4. LinkedIn Promotion

The AI transforms technical details into engaging social content that's automatically posted to your LinkedIn profile, helping establish your thought leadership in automation.
