What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered proposal generator automates one of the most time-consuming tasks in sales - creating professional proposals after discovery calls. Instead of manually drafting documents, your team simply fills out a short post-call form, and the system generates a fully personalized, on-brand proposal in PandaDoc within minutes.

The workflow combines AI content generation with document automation to ensure every proposal maintains high quality while eliminating hours of repetitive formatting work. It pulls client details, pricing, and service specifics from your CRM or form responses to create tailored proposals that convert at higher rates than generic templates.

How It Works

1. Post-Call Form Submission

After a sales call, your team completes a standardized form with key details about the prospect's needs, budget, and proposed solution. This form triggers the automation workflow.

2. AI Content Generation

The system uses AI to analyze the form responses and generate a customized proposal narrative that addresses the client's specific pain points and requirements.

3. PandaDoc Assembly

The generated content is automatically inserted into your PandaDoc template, including all pricing tables, terms, and legal sections. The system populates all merge fields with client-specific information.

4. Quality Review & Delivery

The completed proposal is routed to the appropriate team member for a quick review before being automatically sent to the client via email or PandaDoc's sharing system.
