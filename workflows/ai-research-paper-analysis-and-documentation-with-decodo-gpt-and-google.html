What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the time-consuming process of analyzing academic research papers by combining Decodo's document processing capabilities with GPT's natural language understanding and Google Docs for structured output. It's designed for researchers, analysts, and knowledge workers who need to process large volumes of academic content efficiently.

The system extracts key information from research papers, generates summaries and insights using AI, and organizes the findings into well-structured Google Docs with proper formatting and citations. This eliminates hours of manual reading, note-taking, and documentation work while ensuring consistent output quality.

How It Works

1. Document Ingestion

The workflow begins by processing research papers in PDF or other document formats through Decodo's specialized document analysis tools that can handle academic formatting.

2. Content Extraction

Key sections (abstract, methodology, findings) are identified and extracted with metadata like authors, publication date, and references for proper attribution.

3. AI Analysis

GPT processes the extracted content to generate executive summaries, highlight key findings, identify research gaps, and suggest related works - all tailored to your specific research focus.

4. Structured Documentation

The analyzed content is automatically formatted into Google Docs with consistent styling, section headers, and proper citations ready for immediate use in your research projects.
