AI Resume Screening with Gmail, GPT-4o & Google Sheets

Automated hiring pipeline that screens resumes, scores candidates, and organizes results - saving recruiters 5-10 hours per job posting

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your hiring process by automatically screening incoming resumes through AI-powered analysis. When candidates email their resumes to your hiring inbox, the workflow extracts the attachments, analyzes them using GPT-4o, and organizes the results in Google Sheets with candidate scores and key qualifications highlighted.

The system eliminates hours of manual resume review while ensuring consistent evaluation criteria. It flags top candidates based on your configured requirements and prioritizes them for human review. Recruiters gain back 70-80% of their screening time while reducing unconscious bias in initial candidate evaluations.

How It Works

1. Resume Collection

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages containing resume attachments. It supports PDF, Word, and text formats, extracting the content while preserving the original files in Google Drive for reference.

2. AI Analysis

Each resume is processed by GPT-4o, which evaluates the candidate against your job requirements. The AI extracts skills, experience duration, education, certifications, and other key qualifications you specify.

3. Scoring & Ranking

Candidates receive scores based on how well they match your criteria. The system weights different factors according to your priorities (e.g., technical skills 40%, experience 30%, education 20%).

4. Results Organization

All candidate data populates a Google Sheet with color-coded ratings, making it easy to identify top applicants. The sheet includes links to original resumes and summary analysis from GPT-4o.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to automatically send rejection emails to candidates who don't meet minimum requirements, improving candidate experience while saving your team time.
