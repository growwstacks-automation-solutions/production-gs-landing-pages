What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow helps you evaluate how well your website content is optimized for large language models (LLMs) used by modern search engines. As Google and other platforms increasingly rely on AI to understand and rank content, traditional SEO tactics alone aren't enough.

The workflow systematically analyzes your web pages for key LLM-friendly characteristics including semantic structure, entity relationships, readability scores, and content depth. It generates actionable reports highlighting areas needing improvement to ensure your content performs well in AI-driven search environments.

How It Works

1. URL Submission

Provide a list of URLs or sitemap for analysis. The workflow can process individual pages or entire website sections.

2. Content Extraction

The system extracts and cleans page content, removing navigation elements and focusing on the core textual information.

3. LLM Readability Analysis

Using AI-powered metrics, the workflow evaluates content structure, semantic relationships between concepts, and readability for machine processing.

4. Scoring and Recommendations

Each page receives a comprehensive scorecard with specific suggestions for improving LLM comprehension and SEO performance.
