What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your Telegram account into a personal speechwriting assistant. Whether you're preparing a business presentation, wedding toast, or keynote address, the system provides real-time feedback on your drafts. It combines the analytical strengths of Open AI with Gemini's emotional intelligence to deliver comprehensive suggestions.

The workflow solves the problem of limited access to professional speech coaching. Instead of waiting for appointments or paying high fees, you get instant analysis through a familiar messaging platform. It evaluates your content for clarity, pacing, emotional impact, and persuasive effectiveness - all through simple Telegram messages.

How It Works

1. Submit Your Speech Draft

Send your speech text or voice message to a dedicated Telegram bot. The system accepts both typed drafts and audio recordings, making it convenient for different working styles.

2. AI Analysis Process

The workflow routes your content to both Open AI and Gemini simultaneously. Open AI evaluates structural elements like logical flow and keyword density, while Gemini assesses emotional tone and audience engagement factors.

3. Consolidated Feedback Delivery

The system combines insights from both AI models into a unified report delivered back through Telegram. You receive specific recommendations categorized by importance, along with rewritten examples of key sections.
