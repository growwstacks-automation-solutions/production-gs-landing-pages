What This Workflow Does

Manual time tracking is one of the biggest productivity drains for consultants, agencies, and professional service businesses. Studies show professionals lose 15-20% of billable hours to inaccurate time tracking or forgotten work sessions.

This AI-powered timesheet generator solves that by automatically collecting and analyzing your digital activity across Gmail (email threads), Google Calendar (meetings), and GitHub (code commits) to create accurate, detailed timesheets in Google Sheets. The workflow uses natural language processing to categorize activities and calculate time spent based on message content, commit complexity, and meeting durations.

How It Works

1. Data Collection Phase

The workflow runs on a schedule (daily or weekly) to pull your activity from connected sources. It scans your Gmail for work-related threads, checks Google Calendar for meetings and events, and reviews GitHub for code commits and pull requests.

2. AI Processing Stage

Using natural language processing, the system analyzes email content, commit messages, and meeting notes to determine the actual work performed. It categorizes activities by project/client and estimates time spent based on message length, commit complexity, and other factors.

3. Timesheet Generation

The processed data is formatted into a structured timesheet in Google Sheets, with columns for date, project/client, activity description, time spent, and category. The sheet includes summary calculations for weekly totals and client billing amounts.

Pro tip: For maximum accuracy, train the AI by reviewing its initial time estimates and manually adjusting where needed. The system learns from your corrections over time.
