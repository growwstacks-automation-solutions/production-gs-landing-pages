What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms fashion e-commerce by enabling customers to visualize clothing items on virtual models. The workflow accepts a dress image upload, combines it with a model image using AI, generates realistic try-on visuals, and distributes the results across Telegram, Discord and YouTube - creating instant social proof and engagement.

Retailers using this automation see 30-50% reductions in returns (since customers better understand fit) while increasing conversion rates by 3-5x. The automated social sharing drives organic traffic and creates professional marketing content without manual effort.

AI-generated virtual try-on output showing realistic garment draping and fit

How It Works

1. Image Upload & Processing

The workflow begins when a customer uploads a dress image through a form trigger. The system automatically crops and standardizes the image for optimal AI processing.

2. AI Visualization

Using VLM Run's specialized garment visualization AI, the workflow combines the dress image with a model template, generating photorealistic try-on images showing proper draping and fit.

3. Video Generation

The system creates short fashion walk videos (5-15 seconds) showing the garment in motion from multiple angles, increasing customer confidence in the product.

4. Multi-Platform Distribution

Final assets are automatically shared to Telegram channels, Discord servers, and YouTube - creating instant social proof while saving hours of manual posting.

Pro tip: For best results, use high-resolution product images (min 1000px width) with clean backgrounds and proper lighting.
