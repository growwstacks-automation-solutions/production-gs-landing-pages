What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the critical challenge of delayed equipment failure detection that plagues many manufacturing and facilities operations. By automatically monitoring your Google Sheets equipment logs, it detects anomalies or threshold breaches that indicate potential equipment issues.

When problems are identified, the system immediately sends detailed alerts to your designated MS Teams channels, ensuring maintenance teams are notified in real-time. This eliminates the hours or days of delay that typically occur with manual monitoring processes, potentially saving thousands in avoided downtime.

How It Works

1. Data Collection from Google Sheets

The workflow periodically checks your designated Google Sheet containing equipment health data. This could include temperature readings, vibration levels, runtime hours, or any other critical metrics you track.

2. Threshold Analysis

Each data point is compared against predefined thresholds you configure. The system can detect simple threshold breaches or more complex patterns indicating gradual deterioration.

3. Alert Triggering

When an issue is detected, the workflow compiles all relevant information including equipment ID, parameter values, and severity level to create a comprehensive alert.

4. MS Teams Notification

The alert is formatted and sent to your specified MS Teams channel, appearing as a prominent message with all critical details. The notification can include actionable buttons or links to your maintenance system.

Pro tip: Configure different alert thresholds for various equipment types and criticality levels to avoid notification fatigue for your team.
