What This Workflow Does

Most website owners know which pages get the most traffic, but they don't understand why those pages perform well or what opportunities they're missing. This automation solves that problem by connecting your Google Analytics data with advanced content analysis tools.

The workflow automatically extracts your top 25-50 performing pages, scrapes their full text content using the high-quality Firecrawl API, and feeds everything into InfraNodus to build a visual knowledge graph. You get clear insights into the main topics driving your success and the content gaps between them—showing exactly where to create new material that will resonate with your audience.

Instead of guessing what content to create next, you get data-driven recommendations based on what's already working. This approach saves dozens of hours of manual analysis each month and typically increases content engagement by 30-50% by focusing on proven topics.

How It Works

1. Extract Top Pages from Google Analytics

The workflow connects to your Google Analytics account via the Google Cloud API and retrieves your highest-traffic pages over a specified period. It cleans the data and prepares URLs for content extraction.

2. Scrape Page Content with Firecrawl

Each URL is processed through Firecrawl's high-quality web scraping API, which extracts clean, structured text content while ignoring navigation, ads, and other page clutter. This ensures accurate topic analysis.

3. Build a Knowledge Graph with InfraNodus

All extracted content is ingested into an InfraNodus graph, which visualizes the relationships between concepts across your top-performing pages. The system identifies topical clusters and calculates their centrality in your content ecosystem.

4. Generate Actionable Insights

Finally, the workflow queries InfraNodus's AI insights endpoint to produce a clear report highlighting your main topic clusters and the most valuable content gaps—structural holes between related topics that represent untapped content opportunities.

Pro tip: Run this workflow monthly to track how your content strategy evolves. You'll see your topic clusters strengthen over time, which improves topical authority and SEO performance.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this content optimization system:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Google Analytics credentials in the Google Cloud node, ensuring you have the necessary permissions to access your property data.
  3. Set up your InfraNodus API connection by creating a bearer token in your InfraNodus account and adding it to the HTTP request nodes.
  4. Adjust the page limit if needed—the default extracts 25 pages, but you can increase this to 50 or more depending on your content volume.
  5. Test with a single URL first to verify all connections work before processing your entire top pages list.
  6. Schedule the workflow to run monthly for ongoing content intelligence.

Pro tip: Customize the output by connecting the insights to your project management tool (like Notion or Trello) to automatically create content briefs from the identified gaps.
