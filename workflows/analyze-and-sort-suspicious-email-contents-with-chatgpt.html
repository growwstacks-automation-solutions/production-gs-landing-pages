What This Workflow Does

This automation solution helps IT security teams efficiently identify and process suspicious emails using ChatGPT's natural language processing capabilities. The workflow automatically analyzes incoming emails for phishing attempts, social engineering tactics, and other security threats.

When potential threats are detected, the system creates detailed Jira tickets with AI-generated threat assessments, allowing security teams to prioritize and respond to incidents systematically. This reduces manual review time by up to 80% while improving threat detection accuracy.

How It Works

1. Email ingestion and preprocessing

The workflow monitors designated email inboxes for incoming messages. When a new email arrives, it extracts key components including sender information, subject line, body content, and attachments.

2. AI-powered threat analysis

ChatGPT analyzes the email content using predefined security criteria. It evaluates language patterns, detects social engineering tactics, and identifies potential phishing indicators. The AI generates a threat score and detailed assessment.

3. Automated categorization

Based on the threat score and analysis, emails are categorized into security priority levels. High-risk threats are immediately flagged for review, while lower-risk items are logged for potential pattern analysis.

4. Jira ticket creation

The system creates structured Jira tickets containing all relevant email details, threat analysis, and recommended actions. Tickets are automatically assigned to the appropriate security team based on threat type.

This workflow is ideal for IT security teams, managed service providers (MSPs), and organizations handling sensitive information. It's particularly valuable for:
