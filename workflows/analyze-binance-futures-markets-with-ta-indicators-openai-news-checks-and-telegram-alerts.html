What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow acts as your 24/7 cryptocurrency market analyst, scanning Binance Futures markets for trading opportunities while you focus on strategy execution. It combines technical indicators with AI-powered news analysis to provide comprehensive market insights.

The system identifies high-volume trading pairs, analyzes price action using customizable technical indicators, scans news sources for relevant market-moving events, and delivers actionable alerts directly to your Telegram channel. Designed for educational purposes, it helps traders make more informed decisions without manual market monitoring.

How It Works

1. Market Pair Selection

The workflow first scans Binance Futures Testnet for trading pairs with sufficient liquidity and volume, filtering out low-activity markets to focus on viable trading opportunities.

2. Technical Analysis

For each qualifying pair, the system applies customizable technical indicators (like RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands) to identify potential entry/exit points based on your configured parameters.

3. News Sentiment Analysis

Simultaneously, OpenAI processes recent news articles related to the cryptocurrency markets, extracting sentiment and key themes that could impact price movements.

4. Alert Generation

When technical signals align with news sentiment (positive or negative), the workflow compiles a comprehensive market report and sends it to your Telegram channel for immediate review.
