Automate Competitor Content Gap Analysis with AI

Discover untapped content opportunities by automatically analyzing competitor websites with Gemini AI and exporting actionable insights to Google Sheets.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual competitor content analysis is time-consuming and often misses critical opportunities. This automation solves that by systematically crawling competitor websites, using AI to map their content structure, and identifying exactly where your content strategy has gaps.

The workflow extracts hierarchical topic maps, entities, and depth scores from competitor content, then organizes everything into a clean Google Sheets report. Instead of spending hours browsing sites and taking notes, you get actionable data showing which topics to target, what content formats are missing, and where you can outrank competitors.

How It Works

1. Competitor Website Crawling

The workflow starts by crawling specified competitor domains using Apify. It systematically navigates through pages, collecting URLs, content, and metadata while respecting crawl limits and depth parameters you configure.

2. AI-Powered Content Analysis

Collected content is sent to Gemini AI for intelligent analysis. The AI identifies main topics, subtopics, entities, and assesses content depth and quality. It creates a structured hierarchy showing how competitors organize their content.

3. Gap Identification & Scoring

The system compares analyzed content against your existing content (or target topics) to identify gaps. Each opportunity is scored based on competition level, search potential, and alignment with your strategy.

4. Google Sheets Export

All insights are formatted and exported to Google Sheets with clear columns for topic, competitor coverage, gap type, priority score, and recommended action. The sheet becomes your content planning dashboard.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run monthly. Regular automated analysis catches new competitor content quickly, keeping your strategy continuously updated without manual effort.
