What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the challenge of manually tracking and analyzing competitor LinkedIn activity. Many marketing teams spend hours each week reviewing competitor posts, trying to identify trends, engagement patterns, and content strategies. This workflow automates the entire process by combining web scraping with AI-powered analysis.

The system scrapes LinkedIn posts from specified competitor profiles using Bright Data's web scraping capabilities. It then sends this content to Google Gemini for AI-powered sentiment analysis, topic extraction, and engagement prediction. Finally, it organizes all insights into a structured Google Sheets report that updates automatically.

How It Works

1. Competitor Profile Identification

The workflow starts by accessing a list of competitor LinkedIn profile URLs you specify. These can be updated dynamically or maintained in a separate configuration file.

2. Content Scraping with Bright Data

Using Bright Data's web scraping tools, the workflow extracts recent posts from each competitor profile, including post text, engagement metrics, and publication dates.

3. AI Analysis with Google Gemini

Each scraped post is sent to Google Gemini for natural language processing. The AI identifies sentiment, extracts key topics, and can even predict potential engagement based on content characteristics.

4. Data Organization in Google Sheets

The analyzed data is structured into a comprehensive Google Sheet with tabs for different analysis dimensions (sentiment trends, topic frequency, engagement correlations).
