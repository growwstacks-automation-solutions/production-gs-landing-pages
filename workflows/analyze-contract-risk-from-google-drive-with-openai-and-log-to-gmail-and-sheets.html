What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow transforms how businesses handle contract review by combining AI analysis with seamless documentation. When a new contract is uploaded to Google Drive, the system automatically processes it through OpenAI to identify potential risks, extract key clauses, and generate a structured analysis.

The workflow solves the time-consuming manual process of contract review, which typically requires legal teams to spend hours reading through documents line by line. By automating the initial analysis, businesses can focus their legal expertise on high-value negotiations rather than basic document review.

How It Works

1. New contract detection

The workflow monitors a specified Google Drive folder for new contract uploads. When a document is added, it triggers the automation sequence.

2. Document processing

The system downloads the contract file and prepares it for analysis, converting formats if necessary and extracting the text content.

3. AI risk assessment

OpenAI analyzes the contract text, identifying potential risks, unusual clauses, and key terms based on predefined criteria and industry standards.

4. Results documentation

The analysis findings are automatically logged to both Google Sheets for record-keeping and sent via Gmail to relevant stakeholders with prioritized action items.

Pro tip: Customize the OpenAI prompt to focus on specific risk factors relevant to your industry, such as data privacy clauses for tech companies or liability terms for manufacturers.
