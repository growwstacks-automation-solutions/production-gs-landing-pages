What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates cryptocurrency market analysis by combining CoinGecko's comprehensive price data with C1's advanced analytics capabilities. It solves the problem of manually tracking multiple cryptocurrencies across different timeframes and metrics, saving traders and analysts hours of repetitive research.

The system pulls real-time market data including prices, trading volumes, market capitalization changes, and liquidity metrics. It processes this data to generate actionable insights about market trends, potential trading opportunities, and portfolio performance.

How It Works

1. Data Collection from CoinGecko

The workflow starts by querying CoinGecko's API for the latest market data on your specified cryptocurrencies. It retrieves current prices, 24-hour trading volume, price changes, and other key metrics.

2. Data Processing and Analysis

The raw data is then processed through C1 analytics to identify patterns, correlations, and anomalies. This includes calculating moving averages, volume-weighted prices, and other technical indicators.

3. Visualization and Alerting

The analyzed data is formatted into interactive charts and reports. The system can trigger alerts when specific conditions are met, such as unusual volume spikes or price crossing key technical levels.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to analyze coins that correlate with your existing portfolio to identify hedging opportunities.
