What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automatically analyzes the emotional tone of customer interactions recorded in Zoho CRM notes using Google Gemini's advanced AI capabilities. It detects positive, neutral, or negative sentiment and sends real-time Gmail alerts to your team when concerning patterns emerge.

The system helps customer-facing teams proactively address dissatisfaction before it escalates while identifying successful interaction patterns worth replicating. By automating sentiment analysis that would normally require manual review, it saves hours per week while providing more consistent evaluation than human judgment alone.

How It Works

1. New note detection in Zoho CRM

The workflow triggers whenever a new note is added to any module in Zoho CRM. It captures the note content along with associated customer and record details for context.

2. AI sentiment analysis with Google Gemini

The note text is sent to Google Gemini's natural language processing API, which evaluates emotional tone on multiple dimensions. The AI returns a sentiment score (positive/neutral/negative) along with confidence levels and key emotional indicators.

3. Alert logic and prioritization

Based on configurable thresholds, the system determines which sentiment results warrant immediate alerts. Negative sentiment with high confidence scores trigger urgent notifications, while positive results may simply be logged for later analysis.

4. Gmail notification delivery

Personalized email alerts are sent to designated team members with the customer details, original note content, sentiment analysis results, and direct links to the CRM record for quick action.

5. CRM record updating

The workflow optionally updates the CRM record with sentiment tags and analysis details, creating searchable data for future reporting and customer health monitoring.

Pro tip: Configure different alert recipients based on sentiment severity - route extreme negative cases to managers while having support agents handle moderate concerns.
