What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually analyzing Facebook Ads performance. Instead of exporting CSV files and creating pivot tables, the workflow automatically pulls your campaign data from Meta's API, processes it through Gemini AI for intelligent analysis, and exports the distilled insights to Google Sheets.

The AI identifies performance patterns humans often miss—like which ad creatives perform best at specific times of day, audience segments showing unexpected engagement, or campaigns where minor bid adjustments could significantly improve results. These insights appear in your spreadsheet with clear recommendations, updated on your preferred schedule.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from Facebook Ads

The workflow connects to Meta's Marketing API to pull campaign performance data including impressions, clicks, conversions, and cost metrics. It gathers both current performance and historical benchmarks for comparison.

2. AI-Powered Analysis with Gemini

Gemini processes the raw data using natural language understanding to identify statistically significant patterns. It evaluates performance trends, creative effectiveness, audience engagement, and conversion bottlenecks—then generates plain-English insights.

3. Insight Formatting & Export

The workflow structures the AI's findings into actionable recommendations with supporting metrics. These get formatted for Google Sheets, including conditional formatting rules that highlight urgent issues and emerging opportunities.

4. Scheduled Updates

You configure how frequently the analysis runs—daily for quick health checks, weekly for deeper insights, or custom intervals matching your reporting cadence. Each update timestamps the data for trend tracking.
