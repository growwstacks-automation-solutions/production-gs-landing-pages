What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of delayed response to customer feedback by instantly analyzing sentiment and alerting relevant teams. Customer experience teams often struggle to manually review every piece of feedback while product teams lack visibility into user sentiment trends.

The workflow automatically processes incoming feedback through sentiment analysis, categorizes it by emotional tone, and delivers prioritized notifications to Mattermost channels. This ensures critical negative feedback gets immediate attention while positive feedback is routed for potential testimonials or case studies.

How It Works

1. Feedback Collection

The workflow begins by monitoring your designated feedback sources, whether from forms, emails, or support tickets. It captures both the raw feedback text and any associated metadata like customer information or product references.

2. Sentiment Analysis

Each feedback item is processed through natural language processing to determine emotional tone. The analysis evaluates word choice, phrasing, and context to score sentiment on a scale from negative to positive.

3. Priority Classification

Based on sentiment scores and configured thresholds, the workflow categorizes feedback into urgency levels. Negative feedback triggers immediate alerts while neutral or positive feedback follows different notification paths.

4. Mattermost Notification

The system formats an appropriate message for Mattermost, including sentiment score, key phrases, and direct feedback quotes. Notifications are tagged and directed to relevant channels based on content analysis.

Pro tip: Configure different Mattermost channels for various sentiment levels (e.g., #urgent-feedback for negative, #positive-reviews for praise) to streamline team workflows.

Who This Is For

This automation delivers the most value for customer support teams, product managers, and customer success specialists. SaaS companies with frequent user feedback will see particularly strong benefits, as will e-commerce businesses monitoring product reviews.
