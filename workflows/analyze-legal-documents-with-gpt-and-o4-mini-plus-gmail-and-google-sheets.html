What This Workflow Does

Legal document review is time-consuming, expensive, and prone to human error. Lawyers and compliance teams spend hours reading through contracts, NDAs, and agreements to extract key terms, identify risks, and summarize content. This manual process slows down business deals and creates consistency challenges.

This automation solves that problem by creating a complete AI-powered document analysis pipeline. Simply upload a PDF contract, and the workflow automatically extracts the text, uses AI to summarize the content, flags critical clauses and potential risks, then delivers the analyzed results via email while logging everything in a Google Sheet for tracking and audit purposes.

It transforms what used to be a 2-3 hour manual review into a 2-minute automated process, freeing legal professionals to focus on strategic negotiation and complex judgment calls rather than repetitive reading.

How It Works

1. Document Upload & Text Extraction

The workflow starts when a PDF document is uploaded via webhook or file upload. It immediately extracts all text content from the document, handling various formats and layouts to ensure complete text capture.

2. AI-Powered Analysis & Summarization

Extracted text is processed through AI models that understand legal terminology. The AI identifies key sections, summarizes lengthy clauses in plain language, and flags unusual or risky terms based on configured parameters.

3. Risk Assessment & Clause Identification

The system analyzes the document against predefined risk criteria—looking for unfavorable terms, missing standard clauses, unusual liability provisions, and compliance issues. Each finding is categorized and scored.

4. Results Delivery & Storage

Analyzed results are formatted into a clear report and sent via Gmail to designated recipients. Simultaneously, all extracted data—including summaries, risk scores, key dates, and parties—is logged in Google Sheets for centralized tracking and reporting.

5. Confirmation & Audit Trail

The workflow sends a confirmation back to the upload source and creates a complete audit trail with timestamps, analysis results, and document metadata for compliance purposes.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. OpenAI API key or compatible AI model access
  3. Google Workspace account with Gmail and Sheets access
  4. Basic understanding of webhook URLs for document upload
  5. PDF documents to analyze (contracts, agreements, NDAs, etc.)

Pro tip: Start with your most standardized documents first—like NDAs or service agreements—as they yield the most consistent AI analysis results. Once configured, you can expand to more complex document types.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for OpenAI, Gmail, and Google Sheets in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Set your recipient email in the Gmail node where analysis results should be sent.
  4. Connect your Google Sheet by specifying the spreadsheet ID and worksheet name.
  5. Test the workflow by uploading a sample PDF via the webhook URL or manual trigger.
  6. Review and customize the AI prompts in the LLM nodes to match your specific legal terminology and review criteria.
  7. Activate the workflow and integrate it into your document intake process.
