What This Workflow Does

This workflow automates the analysis of your Meta Ads creatives using Google's powerful Vision and Video Intelligence APIs. It transforms visual content—both images and videos—into structured data that reveals insights about what drives ad performance. Instead of manually reviewing creatives or relying on subjective assessments, this automation provides objective, data-driven analysis at scale.

For marketing teams and agencies, this means you can automatically extract text, identify objects and logos, detect sentiment, and categorize content across all your ad creatives. The workflow processes your Meta Ads assets, sends them to Google's AI services for analysis, and returns comprehensive insights that help you understand which visual elements correlate with better performance metrics.

How It Works

Step 1: Fetch Meta Ads Creatives

The workflow begins by connecting to your Meta Ads account and retrieving all active creatives from your campaigns. It can be configured to analyze specific campaigns, ad sets, or all active ads across your account.

Step 2: Process Image and Video Assets

Each creative asset is processed based on its type. Images are sent to Google Vision API for analysis, while videos are processed through Google Video Intelligence API. The workflow handles both formats seamlessly.

Step 3: AI-Powered Analysis

Google's AI services analyze each creative, extracting valuable information including detected text (OCR), object labels, logo recognition, content categorization, and for videos—scene changes, object tracking, and content analysis throughout the timeline.

Step 4: Data Enrichment and Structuring

The raw analysis data is then structured into actionable insights. The workflow correlates creative elements with performance data from Meta Ads, creating a comprehensive dataset that shows which visual elements perform best.

Step 5: Output and Integration

The final analyzed data can be output to various destinations—Google Sheets for reporting, a database for further analysis, or back into Meta Ads for automated optimization rules.
