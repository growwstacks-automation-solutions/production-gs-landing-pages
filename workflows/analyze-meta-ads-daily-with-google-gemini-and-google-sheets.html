What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of exporting Meta ads data, analyzing performance metrics, and creating reports. Instead, it automatically pulls your daily ad performance data, processes it through Google Gemini's AI analysis capabilities, and delivers structured insights directly into Google Sheets.

The workflow transforms raw ad metrics into actionable intelligence by identifying trends, spotting underperforming campaigns, and suggesting optimization opportunities. It saves marketing teams hours each week while providing more consistent and data-driven decision-making capabilities.

How It Works

1. Daily Meta Ads Data Extraction

The workflow connects to your Meta Ads account through the API and pulls key performance metrics for your selected campaigns. It gathers data like impressions, clicks, conversions, CPC, ROAS, and more.

2. AI-Powered Analysis with Google Gemini

The raw data gets sent to Google Gemini where AI models analyze performance trends, compare against historical benchmarks, and identify statistically significant changes in campaign metrics.

3. Structured Insights in Google Sheets

The analyzed data gets formatted into clear, actionable insights and written directly into your designated Google Sheet. The output includes performance summaries, optimization recommendations, and flagged issues.

Daily analysis provides the best balance between responsiveness and data stability. While weekly analysis might miss short-term trends, hourly checks could lead to overreacting to normal fluctuations. Daily analysis catches meaningful changes while allowing enough time for statistical significance.

For high-budget campaigns ($5k+/day), running analysis twice daily (morning/evening) can be beneficial. The key is aligning analysis frequency with your ability to implement changes - there's no value in daily insights if you only optimize weekly.

  • Daily recommended for most businesses
  • Twice daily for high-spend accounts
  • Weekly sufficient for small, stable campaigns
