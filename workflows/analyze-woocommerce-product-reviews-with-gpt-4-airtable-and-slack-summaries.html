What This Workflow Does

Customer reviews contain invaluable insights about your products, but manually analyzing hundreds of WooCommerce reviews is time-consuming and inconsistent. This automated workflow solves that problem by using AI to process reviews at scale, delivering actionable insights to your team.

The system fetches new product reviews from your WooCommerce store, analyzes them with GPT-4 for sentiment and key themes, stores the structured data in Airtable for reporting, and sends summarized alerts to Slack channels. This creates a closed-loop feedback system where customer opinions directly inform product improvements and marketing strategies.

How It Works

1. Fetch new WooCommerce reviews

The workflow connects to your WooCommerce REST API to retrieve recent product reviews, including the review text, star rating, product ID, and customer information. It checks for new reviews at your preferred frequency (daily, hourly, or real-time).

2. Analyze sentiment with GPT-4

Each review is processed by GPT-4 which performs advanced natural language processing to determine sentiment (positive/negative/neutral), extract key themes, identify product compliments or complaints, and summarize the customer's main points.

Pro tip: Customize the GPT-4 prompt to focus on aspects most relevant to your business - product quality, shipping experience, customer service mentions, etc.

3. Store analyzed data in Airtable

The enriched review data is saved to an Airtable base with consistent fields for sentiment score, key phrases, product category, and follow-up status. This creates a searchable database of customer feedback that can be filtered, sorted, and visualized.

4. Send Slack notifications

Critical reviews are summarized and routed to appropriate Slack channels - customer service gets complaints needing resolution, marketing receives positive testimonials for potential promotion, and product teams see feature requests.
