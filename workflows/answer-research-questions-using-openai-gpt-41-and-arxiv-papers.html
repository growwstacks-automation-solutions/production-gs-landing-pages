What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how researchers access and process academic knowledge. When you submit a research question, the system intelligently searches relevant arXiv papers, analyzes their content using GPT-4.1, and delivers a synthesized answer with proper citations. It handles everything from broad literature reviews to highly technical explanations.

The workflow solves the "information overload" problem in academia. Researchers typically spend 30-50% of their time just keeping up with publications. This system cuts that time dramatically while improving comprehension through AI-powered summarization and connection of ideas across multiple papers.

How It Works

1. Question Analysis

The workflow first classifies your question by discipline and technical level. This ensures it retrieves papers at the appropriate depth and from the correct arXiv categories.

2. Smart Paper Retrieval

Using semantic search, the system identifies the most relevant arXiv preprints, prioritizing recent papers and highly cited works. It typically analyzes 5-10 key papers to form a comprehensive answer.

3. AI Synthesis

GPT-4.1 reads and cross-references the selected papers, identifying consensus views, conflicting findings, and emerging trends. The AI generates a structured response with clear citations.

4. Quality Control

The final output includes confidence indicators for different claims and flags areas where research is inconclusive or contradictory - crucial for maintaining academic integrity.
