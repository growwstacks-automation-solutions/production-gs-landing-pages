What This Workflow Does

This voice-enabled meeting intelligence system solves the common problem of forgotten decisions and lost knowledge buried in meeting archives. Teams waste approximately 5-7 hours per week searching through old meeting notes and recordings, according to recent productivity studies.

The workflow converts natural voice questions into actionable insights by combining speech-to-text conversion with Pinecone's vector search and OpenAI's contextual understanding. It creates a searchable knowledge base from all your meeting history, making past discussions instantly accessible through simple voice commands.

How It Works

1. Voice Input Processing

The system captures voice queries through any connected device and converts them to text using speech recognition. This allows users to ask questions naturally, as if speaking to a colleague.

2. Contextual Understanding

OpenAI analyzes the question to understand intent and extract key entities. It determines whether the query requires factual recall, synthesis across meetings, or interpretation of discussions.

3. Vector Search

Pinecone's vector database searches meeting embeddings to find the most relevant passages based on semantic similarity, not just keyword matching.

4. Answer Generation

The system synthesizes information from multiple meeting sources to generate concise, accurate answers with source attribution. For complex questions, it can provide summarized timelines or decision pathways.

Pro tip: Train the system on your specific meeting terminology during implementation to improve answer accuracy by 15-20%.
