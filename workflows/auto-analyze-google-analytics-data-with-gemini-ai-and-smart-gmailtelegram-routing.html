What This Workflow Does

Marketing teams spend hours each week manually extracting Google Analytics data, building spreadsheets, and trying to interpret what the numbers actually mean for the business. This process is time-consuming, prone to human error, and often delays critical insights from reaching decision-makers.

This automation solves that by running on a scheduled basis (weekly, daily, or monthly), automatically pulling key performance metrics from Google Analytics, aggregating the data into a clean summary, and sending it to an AI agent powered by Google Gemini. The AI analyzes trends, provides plain-English insights, and classifies the sentiment as Positive, Negative, or Neutral.

The smart routing then ensures the right people get the right information through the right channel: positive performance updates are automatically emailed to stakeholders via Gmail for formal celebration, while negative trends are sent instantly to a designated Telegram group for immediate team discussion and rapid response.

How It Works

Step 1: Scheduled Data Collection

A schedule trigger initiates the workflow at your chosen frequency. The Google Analytics node connects to your GA4 or Universal Analytics property and pulls predefined metrics like sessions, conversion rate, revenue, bounce rate, and acquisition channel performance.

Step 2: Data Aggregation & Preparation

The raw data is cleaned, aggregated, and formatted into a structured summary. Date comparisons (week-over-week, month-over-month) are calculated automatically to highlight trends rather than just presenting static numbers.

Step 3: AI Analysis & Sentiment Classification

The formatted data is sent to the AI Agent node configured with Google Gemini. The AI analyzes the metrics against historical context (using Simple Memory), identifies significant changes, explains potential causes, and classifies the overall sentiment. It generates actionable recommendations in business-friendly language.

Step 4: Smart Routing Based on Sentiment

A conditional router checks the AI's sentiment classification. If positive, the insights are formatted into a professional email and sent via Gmail to your stakeholder list. If negative, an alert is immediately pushed to your designated Telegram group with the key issues highlighted for quick team response.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Analytics account with API access (GA4 property ID or Universal Analytics view ID)
  2. Google Gemini API key or access to another AI model through n8n
  3. Gmail account configured with OAuth 2.0 in n8n for sending emails
  4. Telegram bot token and group chat ID for receiving alerts
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Pro tip: Start with weekly analysis to establish a baseline before moving to daily monitoring. This gives you time to refine your metrics and AI prompts without being overwhelmed by too frequent reports.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the Schedule Trigger: Set your preferred reporting frequency (default is weekly on Monday morning).
  3. Connect Google Analytics: Add your property ID and select the metrics/dimensions most important to your business.
  4. Set up the AI Agent: Add your Google Gemini API credentials and customize the analysis prompt if needed.
  5. Configure Gmail & Telegram: Connect your Gmail account and add your Telegram bot credentials with the target group chat ID.
  6. Test the workflow: Run it once manually to ensure all connections work and the routing logic functions correctly.
  7. Activate: Turn on the workflow and let it run automatically on your schedule.
