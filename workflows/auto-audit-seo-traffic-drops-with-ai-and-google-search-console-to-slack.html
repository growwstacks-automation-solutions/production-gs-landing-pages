What This Workflow Does

This automation continuously monitors your Google Search Console data for significant traffic drops that could indicate SEO problems. Instead of manually checking analytics dashboards, the system automatically analyzes performance trends and alerts your team via Slack when anomalies are detected.

The AI component enhances basic monitoring by identifying patterns that might signal algorithm updates, technical issues, or competitive ranking changes. It filters out normal fluctuations to focus on meaningful changes requiring investigation, saving your team from alert fatigue while ensuring critical issues aren't missed.

How It Works

1. Regular Google Search Console Data Extraction

The workflow pulls your latest search performance data at scheduled intervals (typically daily). It retrieves metrics like clicks, impressions, CTR, and average position for your configured properties.

2. AI-Powered Trend Analysis

An AI model processes the historical data to establish normal performance patterns. It compares current metrics against expected ranges, accounting for seasonality and weekly trends. Significant deviations trigger further investigation.

3. Smart Alert Generation

When anomalies are detected, the system generates detailed alerts including affected pages/keywords, severity assessment, and potential causes. These are formatted for easy reading and include relevant comparison data.

4. Slack Notification Delivery

Formatted alerts are sent to designated Slack channels with priority tagging. The messages include actionable information and direct links to relevant Search Console reports for quick follow-up.
