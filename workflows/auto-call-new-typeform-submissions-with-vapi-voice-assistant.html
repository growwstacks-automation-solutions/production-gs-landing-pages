What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of delayed lead follow-up. When someone submits a Typeform expressing interest in your service, product, or consultation, they're at peak engagement. Waiting hours or days for manual contact drastically reduces conversion chances.

The workflow automatically triggers a personalized AI voice call via Vapi within minutes of form submission. Using data collected in the Typeform—like name, interest area, or location—the voice assistant delivers a tailored message that feels human and responsive. This immediate engagement captures momentum, qualifies leads conversationally, and sets the stage for successful sales or onboarding.

How It Works

Step 1: Typeform Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Typeform for new responses. When a submission arrives—especially one containing a phone number field—it captures all form data including custom fields you've set up for personalization.

Step 2: Short Processing Delay

A brief wait ensures data is fully processed and allows time for any parallel automations (like sending confirmation emails) to complete before the call initiates.

Step 3: Data Mapping & Personalization

The workflow extracts specific fields from the Typeform response and maps them into variables for the voice assistant. This could include first name, product interest, appointment preference, or geographic location.

Step 4: Vapi AI Call Initiation

Using the Vapi API, the workflow initiates an outbound call to the respondent's phone number. It passes the personalized variables to the AI assistant, which uses them in its conversational script to create a context-aware interaction.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to only call respondents who opt-in via a checkbox field in your Typeform. This ensures compliance and respects user preferences.
