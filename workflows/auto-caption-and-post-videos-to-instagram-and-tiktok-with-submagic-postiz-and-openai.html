Auto-caption & Post Videos to Instagram & TikTok

Automate your entire short-form video pipeline—from Google Drive to published social posts with AI captions.

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your video content creation from a manual, time-consuming process into a seamless, hands-off pipeline. It solves the common problem of social media managers and content creators who spend hours each week manually adding captions, formatting videos, and publishing across multiple platforms.

The workflow automatically processes any video uploaded to a designated Google Drive folder, adds professional captions using Submagic's AI, enhances the caption text with OpenAI for maximum engagement, publishes to Instagram and/or TikTok via Postiz, and logs every action in Google Sheets for tracking and analytics. What typically takes 15-20 minutes per video now happens automatically while you focus on creating content.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence that mimics what a human social media manager would do, but without the manual effort.

1. Google Drive Trigger

The workflow monitors a specific Google Drive folder for new video uploads. When a video file is detected, it automatically triggers the entire captioning and posting process.

2. Submagic Caption Processing

The video is sent to Submagic's API, which automatically generates styled captions with proper timing. The system waits for processing completion and downloads the captioned video file.

3. AI Caption Enhancement

OpenAI analyzes the video context and generates optimized social media captions with relevant hashtags, engaging hooks, and platform-specific formatting for Instagram and TikTok.

4. Postiz Integration & Publishing

The finalized video and enhanced caption are sent to Postiz, which handles the actual posting to your connected Instagram and/or TikTok accounts according to your scheduling preferences.

5. Google Sheets Logging

Every published video's details—including platform, timestamp, caption text, and status—are automatically recorded in a Google Sheet for content calendar management and performance tracking.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Drive account with a dedicated folder for video uploads
  2. Submagic API key for automated caption generation
  3. Postiz account connected to your Instagram and/or TikTok business accounts
  4. OpenAI API key for caption enhancement and optimization
  5. Google Sheets for logging and tracking published content
  6. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Pro tip: Before connecting to production social accounts, test the workflow with a private Instagram/TikTok account or use Postiz's scheduling feature to review posts before they go live.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n instance:

  1. Import the template using the download button above
  2. Configure Google Drive node to monitor your specific video folder
  3. Add your API credentials for Submagic, OpenAI, and Postiz in their respective nodes
  4. Connect Google Sheets and specify the spreadsheet for logging
  5. Test with a sample video to ensure all connections work properly
  6. Activate the workflow and start uploading videos to your designated Drive folder
