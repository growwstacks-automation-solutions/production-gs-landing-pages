What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually adding captions to social media videos. Content creators often spend 30+ minutes per video transcribing, timing, and formatting captions - this workflow eliminates that manual work.

The system monitors your Google Drive folder for new video uploads. When detected, it automatically sends the video to Submagic for AI-powered caption generation. The captioned video is then saved back to Drive, ready for posting to Instagram, TikTok, or other platforms.

How It Works

1. Video Upload Trigger

The workflow monitors a specific Google Drive folder for new video file uploads. You can designate your "To Be Captioned" folder where you drop all raw videos.

2. Automatic Caption Processing

When a new video is detected, the workflow sends it to Submagic's API. Submagic uses advanced speech recognition to transcribe the audio and generate perfectly timed captions.

3. Quality Control Check

The workflow includes an optional human approval step where you can review the captions before final processing. This ensures accuracy for important content.

4. Final Output Delivery

The captioned video is saved back to Google Drive in your "Ready to Post" folder with all captions burned into the video file. Some workflows can also directly post to social platforms.
