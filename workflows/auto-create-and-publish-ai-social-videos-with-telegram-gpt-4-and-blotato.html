What This Workflow Does

Creating engaging social media videos is time-consuming. Between scripting, recording, editing, adding music, writing captions, and publishing across platforms, a single video can take hours. This automation solves that by turning a simple Telegram message into a complete, publish-ready video.

The workflow handles everything from AI video generation to multi-platform publishing. It starts when you send a text or image prompt to your Telegram bot. The system then generates a video using AI models like Kling or Blotato, creates matching background music with Piapi, generates compelling captions and titles with GPT-4, styles everything with text overlays using JSON2Video, and finally publishes the finished video to 9 social platforms simultaneously via Blotato.

This end-to-end automation saves content creators, social media managers, and marketing teams 6-12 hours per week while ensuring consistent, high-quality output across all channels. It transforms video creation from a manual, multi-hour process into something that happens automatically in minutes.

The complete automation workflow connecting Telegram, AI video generation, music creation, caption writing, and multi-platform publishing.

How It Works

1. Telegram Trigger

The workflow starts when you send a message to your connected Telegram bot. This can be a text description of the video you want, an image for visual inspiration, or specific instructions about style and tone.

2. AI Video Generation

Based on your prompt, the system calls either Kling or Blotato's AI video generation API to create the core video content. These models understand complex descriptions and can produce coherent, visually appealing short-form videos perfect for social platforms.

3. Music Creation & Merging

Simultaneously, Piapi generates background music that matches the video's mood and length. The workflow then merges the AI-generated video with the custom music track, ensuring perfect synchronization and professional audio quality.

4. Caption & Title Generation

GPT-4 analyzes the video content and generates engaging captions, hashtags, and titles optimized for each social platform's audience and algorithm preferences. This ensures your content performs well across different networks.

5. Video Styling & Text Overlays

JSON2Video applies your brand's visual identity—adding text overlays, logos, color grading, and transitions to create a polished final product that maintains consistent branding across all your content.

6. Multi-platform Publishing

Blotato handles the simultaneous publishing to Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, Threads, Twitter/X, Pinterest, and Bluesky. Each platform receives appropriately formatted content with optimized captions and scheduling.

7. Analytics & Logging

All created videos, their performance data, and publishing details are automatically logged to Google Sheets for easy tracking, analysis, and reporting on your content strategy's effectiveness.

What You'll Need

  1. Telegram Bot Token: Create a bot via BotFather to receive your prompts.
  2. OpenAI API Key: For GPT-4 caption and title generation.
  3. Kling/Blotato API Access: For AI video generation (sign up for their respective services).
  4. Piapi Account: For AI music generation and merging.
  5. Cloudinary Account: To host and serve generated images and videos.
  6. Google Sheets: With columns for Title, Caption, and URL to log results.
  7. Blotato API Key & Platform IDs: Connect your social media accounts for auto-publishing.
  8. Self-hosted n8n instance: This workflow uses Community Nodes and requires self-hosting.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure the Telegram trigger node with your bot token from BotFather.
  3. Add your OpenAI API key to all GPT nodes for caption generation.
  4. Set up Kling/Blotato and Piapi API credentials in their respective nodes.
  5. Connect your Cloudinary account for media hosting in the appropriate nodes.
  6. Link your Google Sheet and ensure it has the required columns (Title, Caption, URL).
  7. Configure Blotato with your API key and add platform-specific account IDs for each social network.
  8. Test with a simple prompt sent to your Telegram bot and verify the complete flow works end-to-end.
