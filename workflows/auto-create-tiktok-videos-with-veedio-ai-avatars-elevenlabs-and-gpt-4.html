What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the massive time drain of creating daily TikTok content. Instead of spending hours scripting, filming, and editing, this workflow automatically produces ready-to-post videos featuring your AI avatar speaking with a cloned voice. It transforms a simple theme or prompt into a complete viral TikTok video in minutes.

The system connects multiple AI services: it analyzes trending topics, generates engaging scripts using GPT-4, creates realistic voiceovers with ElevenLabs, animates your avatar through VEED.io's Fabric API, and publishes directly to TikTok. This eliminates the need for cameras, microphones, or video editing software while maintaining a consistent, professional presence.

For content creators, marketers, and agencies, this represents a fundamental shift from manual content creation to automated content production. You maintain complete creative control over topics and branding while the system handles the repetitive production work, allowing you to scale your video output without scaling your team.

How It Works

1. Trigger & Input

The workflow begins when you send a photo of yourself and a content theme to a Telegram bot. This simple input method makes it accessible even for non-technical users. The photo becomes the basis for your AI avatar, while the theme guides the content generation process.

2. Content Generation

Perplexity AI analyzes current TikTok trends related to your theme. GPT-4 then crafts a concise, engaging 10-second script optimized for viral potential. The system ensures the script matches trending formats while maintaining your brand voice and messaging.

3. Voice & Video Production

ElevenLabs generates a natural-sounding voiceover using your cloned voice profile. VEED.io's Fabric API then creates the final video, animating your AI avatar with perfect lip-sync to the generated audio. The system handles all technical aspects of video rendering and formatting.

4. Publishing & Tracking

The completed video is automatically posted to TikTok via the Blotato API, complete with optimized captions and hashtags. Every video's performance data is logged to Google Sheets, providing insights into what content resonates best with your audience for continuous improvement.

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 different avatar styles and voice settings to test which combination performs best with your audience before scaling production.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Import the downloaded JSON file into your n8n instance using the "Import from File" option in the workflow editor.

2. Create your Telegram bot via @BotFather and obtain your bot token. Configure the Telegram trigger node with this token.

3. Connect all API services: obtain keys from VEED.io (Fabric), ElevenLabs, OpenAI, Perplexity AI, and Blotato. Enter these in their respective nodes.

4. Prepare your Google Sheet with columns for video URL, performance metrics, and timestamps. Connect the Google Sheets node with proper authentication.

5. Test the workflow with a simple theme and your photo. Monitor each step to ensure proper API connections and adjust any parameters as needed.

6. Once testing is successful, activate the workflow and start sending themes via Telegram to generate your first automated TikTok videos.

Pro tip: For best results, provide ElevenLabs with 3-5 minutes of clear voice recording for optimal voice cloning quality before running the workflow.
