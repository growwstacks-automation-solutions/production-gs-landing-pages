What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a major pain point for content creators and social media managers: reaching international audiences with localized video content. Manually dubbing videos is time-consuming, expensive, and slows down your ability to capitalize on trending content. This workflow eliminates those barriers by automatically processing videos through AI dubbing and publishing them to your social channels.

The system takes any video input, sends it to Fal AI's ElevenLabs integration for professional-grade voice dubbing in your target language, then distributes the finished content to both Instagram and TikTok simultaneously. What used to require video editors, translators, and scheduling coordination now happens in minutes with consistent quality.

Whether you're a creator expanding to new markets, a brand launching multilingual campaigns, or an agency managing multiple client accounts, this automation ensures your video content resonates globally without multiplying your production workload.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence that mimics what a human editor would do, but completely automated.

1. Input & Trigger Setup

You provide the source video URL and select the target language (like Spanish "es", French "fr", or German "de"). The workflow can be triggered manually for one-off videos or connected to a schedule for regular content batches.

2. AI Dubbing Processing

The video is sent to Fal AI's ElevenLabs dubbing API, which analyzes the audio, transcribes it, generates a natural-sounding voiceover in the target language, and synchronizes it with the video. The system handles timing, emotion, and pronunciation adjustments automatically.

3. Quality Check & Processing Wait

The workflow includes a waiting period (configurable, typically 30+ seconds) to allow the AI sufficient processing time, then checks if the dubbing is complete. If not ready, it waits and checks again—ensuring you only proceed with a finished, high-quality output.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

Once the dubbed video is ready, it's downloaded and simultaneously prepared for both TikTok (via Upload-Post.com API) and Instagram (via Postiz). You can customize captions, hashtags, and posting schedules for each platform within the workflow.

5. Final Distribution

The workflow triggers the actual publishing to both platforms, with error handling to retry if any platform experiences issues. You receive notifications when content goes live, and all metadata is preserved for tracking performance.

  2. Fal AI account with API access to their ElevenLabs dubbing service
  3. Postiz account for Instagram publishing automation
  4. Upload-Post.com account for TikTok publishing automation
  5. Source videos with clear audio (minimal background music or noise)

Pro tip: Test with short videos (under 60 seconds) first to optimize costs and processing time. Fal AI charges based on video duration, so starting small helps you refine the workflow before scaling.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Import the template using the download button above. The JSON file contains the complete workflow structure.
  2. Configure credentials in n8n for Fal AI, Postiz, and Upload-Post.com. Store API keys securely in n8n's credential system.
  3. Set your parameters in the "Set params" node: video URL and target language code. You can hardcode these or connect to a database/spreadsheet for batch processing.
  4. Customize social media settings in the TikTok and Instagram nodes with your account details, captions, and hashtags.
  5. Test with one video to ensure all connections work. Check the dubbed output quality and platform publishing success.
  6. Schedule or trigger the workflow based on your content calendar—daily, weekly, or when new videos are added to a specific folder/URL.
