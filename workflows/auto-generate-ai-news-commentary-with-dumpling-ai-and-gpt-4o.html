What This Workflow Does

Maintaining an active, insightful presence on platforms like LinkedIn requires constant attention to trending news and the ability to quickly craft thoughtful commentary. Most professionals struggle to find the time for daily research and writing, leading to inconsistent posting and missed opportunities to establish thought leadership.

This automation solves that problem by combining Dumpling AI's news discovery capabilities with GPT-4o's writing prowess. The workflow automatically finds relevant articles based on your topics, extracts the key content, and generates first-person commentary that sounds authentic and engaging. The final drafts are organized in Google Sheets for easy review and scheduling.

Instead of spending 2-3 hours per post on research and writing, you can review and polish AI-generated commentary in just 10-15 minutes. This represents a 90%+ reduction in time investment while maintaining or even improving content quality and consistency.

How It Works

1. Daily Trigger & Topic Collection

The workflow starts on a daily schedule, pulling your predefined content topics from a Google Sheet. It identifies topics that haven't yet received generated commentary, ensuring fresh content for each area of interest.

2. News Discovery with Dumpling AI

For each topic, the workflow queries Dumpling AI's News Search API to find the most relevant and recent articles. It can filter by date, relevance, and source credibility to ensure high-quality source material.

3. Content Extraction & Cleaning

The workflow then uses Dumpling AI's Scraping API to extract the full article text from each source. A Code node cleans this content—removing ads, navigation elements, and irrelevant sections—leaving only the core article for analysis.

4. AI Commentary Generation

GPT-4o receives the cleaned article content along with your brand voice guidelines. It generates first-person commentary that adds value, provides perspective, and encourages engagement—perfect for LinkedIn's professional audience.

5. Output Organization

The generated commentary is appended back to your Google Sheet next to the original topic and article source. This creates an organized content calendar where you can review, edit, and schedule posts directly.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheet with at least two columns: "Topic" and "Generated Commentary"
  2. A Dumpling AI API key with access to both News Search and Scraping endpoints
  3. An OpenAI API key with GPT-4o access
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  5. Basic understanding of your target audience and brand voice for optimal AI prompting

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Create your Google Sheet with topic columns and share it with appropriate edit permissions
  3. Configure the Google Sheets node with your sheet ID and worksheet name
  4. Add your API credentials for Dumpling AI and OpenAI in their respective nodes
  5. Adjust the schedule trigger to match your desired posting frequency (daily is recommended)
  6. Test with one topic to ensure everything works before scaling to multiple topics
  7. Review and refine the AI-generated outputs to match your specific voice and style
