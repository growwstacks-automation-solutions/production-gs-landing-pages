What This Workflow Does

This workflow automatically converts technical video uploads into polished Facebook post drafts, complete with AI-generated images. It eliminates the manual work of watching videos, extracting key points, and crafting social media content.

By analyzing video content with Dumpling AI and leveraging GPT-4o's natural language capabilities, it creates engaging posts that highlight the most valuable information from your videos while maintaining your brand voice.

How It Works

1. Video Upload Trigger

The workflow starts when a new video is added to your designated Google Drive folder, ensuring seamless integration with your existing content creation process.

2. Video Analysis

Dumpling AI processes the video to identify key topics, tools mentioned, and important steps, creating structured data that serves as the foundation for your post.

3. Content Generation

GPT-4o crafts a Facebook-friendly post using the extracted insights, complete with hooks to maximize engagement and appropriate hashtags.

4. Visual Creation

Dumpling AI generates a complementary image based on the video content, ensuring your post has eye-catching visuals.

5. Output Storage

The completed post and image URL are saved to Google Sheets for easy review and scheduling.

What You'll Need

  1. Active accounts with Google Drive and Google Sheets
  2. Dumpling AI API credentials
  3. OpenAI GPT-4o access
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the JSON template file
  2. Import into your n8n instance
  3. Connect your Google Drive, Google Sheets, Dumpling AI, and OpenAI accounts
  4. Specify your source folder and destination spreadsheet
  5. Test with a sample video and adjust prompts as needed

Pro tip: Customize the GPT prompt to match your brand voice by adding specific tone guidelines and hashtag strategies.
