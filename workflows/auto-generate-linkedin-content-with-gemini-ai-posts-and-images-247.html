What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the biggest challenge for businesses on LinkedIn: consistent, high-quality content creation. Instead of spending hours brainstorming, writing, designing, and scheduling posts, this workflow handles everything automatically.

The system monitors a Google Sheet for new post ideas, uses Google Gemini AI to generate engaging LinkedIn content with proper formatting and hashtags, creates custom AI-generated images, and publishes everything directly to your LinkedIn profile. It transforms simple post titles into complete, professional LinkedIn updates with visuals.

Perfect for startups, marketing teams, HR departments, agencies, and solo entrepreneurs who need to maintain an active LinkedIn presence without the time commitment. The automation runs continuously, ensuring your LinkedIn feed stays fresh and engaging 24/7.

How It Works

1. Content Trigger & Management

A Google Sheets trigger monitors your content calendar spreadsheet for new post titles marked as "pending." The workflow processes one post at a time to maintain control and prevent overwhelming your LinkedIn audience.

2. AI Content Generation

Google Gemini AI analyzes each post title and generates complete LinkedIn posts including compelling opening hooks, 3-4 informative paragraphs, engagement questions, relevant hashtags (4-6), and appropriate emojis. The output is professionally formatted for maximum engagement.

3. AI Image Creation

Using the generated post content as context, Gemini creates custom visuals featuring modern workspace elements, professional aesthetics, and 16:9 aspect ratio suitable for LinkedIn. Images contain no text overlays, keeping them clean and platform-appropriate.

4. Image Processing & Storage

Generated images are automatically uploaded to Google Drive with public access permissions. The image URLs are stored back in your spreadsheet for tracking and future reference, creating a complete content archive.

5. LinkedIn Publishing

The LinkedIn API integration handles the complete publishing process: registering image uploads, uploading to LinkedIn's servers, creating posts with text and image, and publishing to your profile. Status updates to "posted" upon successful completion.

Pro tip: Start with 1-2 posts daily and monitor engagement for 2 weeks before increasing frequency. This helps LinkedIn's algorithm recognize your consistent quality without flagging as spam.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template and import into your n8n instance. 2. Connect your Google Sheets, Google Drive, and LinkedIn accounts in n8n credentials. 3. Set up your Google Gemini API key in the AI nodes. 4. Create a simple Google Sheet with columns: Title, Status, Image URL. 5. Add 5-10 post ideas and mark them as "pending." 6. Activate the workflow and test with one post. 7. Review the published content and adjust AI prompts if needed. 8. Scale up by adding more content ideas to your sheet.

Important: Always review the first 3-5 AI-generated posts before full automation. This ensures the tone and quality match your brand voice before scaling.
