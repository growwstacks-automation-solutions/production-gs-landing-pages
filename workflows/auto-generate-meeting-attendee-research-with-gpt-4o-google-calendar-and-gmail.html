What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching meeting attendees by automatically generating comprehensive briefing documents whenever a new event appears in your Google Calendar. The system identifies all participants, researches their professional backgrounds using GPT-4o, and compiles the information into an organized email sent to your Gmail.

For professionals who attend multiple meetings daily, this workflow eliminates hours of manual research while providing more consistent, thorough preparation than human researchers could achieve. The AI identifies key connections, potential talking points, and relevant background information that helps you make stronger first impressions and conduct more productive meetings.

How It Works

1. Calendar Event Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new event is created in your Google Calendar. It extracts all attendee email addresses and the meeting details including title, description, and scheduled time.

2. Attendee Profile Research

For each attendee, the system queries public data sources (like LinkedIn, company websites, and professional directories) to gather career history, education, publications, and other relevant information. GPT-4o analyzes and summarizes this data into concise professional profiles.

3. Contextual Analysis

The AI identifies connections between attendees, potential discussion topics based on their backgrounds, and any notable achievements or recent activity that might be relevant to the meeting purpose.

4. Briefing Document Generation

All researched information is compiled into a professionally formatted meeting briefing document, organized by attendee with key highlights and suggested talking points.

5. Email Delivery

The final briefing is sent to your Gmail inbox with the meeting title as the subject line, arriving well before the event so you have time to review.
