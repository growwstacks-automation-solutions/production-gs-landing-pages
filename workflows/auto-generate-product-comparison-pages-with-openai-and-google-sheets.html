What This Workflow Does

For affiliate marketers, SaaS companies, and content teams, creating high-quality product comparison pages is time-consuming but essential for capturing high-intent search traffic. Manually researching features, pros/cons, and writing balanced comparisons can take hours per article.

This automation solves that by using AI to generate comprehensive "X vs Y" comparison pages instantly. You provide two product names, and the workflow uses OpenAI to research and structure a complete comparison—including titles, feature breakdowns, recommendations, and FAQs—then saves everything to Google Sheets for easy review and publishing.

The result is scalable, SEO-optimized content that helps indecisive buyers make confident purchase decisions, driving conversions without the manual writing overhead.

How It Works

1. Trigger & Input

The workflow can be triggered manually or automatically when a new row is added to a Google Sheet. You input the two products you want to compare (e.g., "Notion vs Evernote" or "Zoom vs Microsoft Teams").

2. AI Analysis & Content Generation

OpenAI's model analyzes the products, considering typical features, pricing, user pain points, and use cases. It then generates multiple content sections: a compelling headline, meta description, detailed feature comparison table, tailored recommendations for different user types, and a relevant FAQ section.

3. Structured Output to Sheets

Each generated section is written to a designated column in a Google Sheet. This creates a clean, row-based content brief that your team can review, edit, and approve before publishing.

4. Ready for Publishing

The finalized content in the sheet can be easily connected to your CMS (like WordPress, Webflow, or Dorik) via additional n8n nodes, allowing for fully automated publishing if desired.

Who This Is For

This template is ideal for affiliate marketers who need to produce volume comparison content for monetization, SaaS companies comparing their solution to competitors, content agencies serving clients in competitive niches, and e-commerce teams creating detailed buying guides. It's perfect for anyone who understands the value of comparison content but lacks the time or resources to write it all manually.

What You'll Need

  1. An OpenAI API key (GPT-3.5 or GPT-4) for content generation.
  2. A Google Cloud Service Account with Sheets API enabled to read/write to your spreadsheet.
  3. A Google Sheet prepared with column headers for the output (title, meta description, features, FAQs, etc.).
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to import and run the workflow.

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of 5–10 product pairs to refine the AI prompts and output format before scaling to hundreds. Adjust the tone and depth in the prompt to match your brand voice.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Create your Google Sheet with columns for: Product A, Product B, Generated Title, Meta Description, Feature Comparison, Recommendations, and FAQs.
  3. Configure the credentials in n8n for OpenAI and Google Sheets using your API keys.
  4. Map the nodes to your specific Google Sheet ID and tab name.
  5. Test the workflow manually with one product pair to ensure the output matches your expectations.
  6. Automate the trigger by connecting the "Manual Trigger" node to a schedule or a webhook that adds new rows to your sheet.
