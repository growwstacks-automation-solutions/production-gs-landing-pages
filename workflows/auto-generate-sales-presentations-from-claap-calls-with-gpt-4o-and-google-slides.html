What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for sales teams - the hours spent creating follow-up presentations after client calls. When using Claap to record sales conversations, this n8n workflow automatically transforms those recordings into professional Google Slides decks using AI analysis.

The system transcribes the call, identifies key discussion points with GPT-4o, structures the content into a logical flow, and generates slides with relevant visuals. This ensures your follow-up materials directly address the prospect's specific needs and pain points discussed during the call, while saving your team 2-3 hours of manual work per presentation.

How It Works

1. Claap recording trigger

The workflow starts when a new call recording is saved in Claap. The system captures the video file and associated metadata like call participants and duration.

2. Audio transcription

The recording's audio is extracted and transcribed using speech-to-text technology. This creates a searchable text version of the entire conversation.

Pro tip: The system can be configured to flag specific keywords during transcription (like "budget" or "timeline") to ensure these critical points are emphasized in the presentation.

3. GPT-4o analysis

AI analyzes the transcript to identify key discussion points, objections raised, value propositions mentioned, and next steps agreed upon. It structures this information into presentation sections.

4. Google Slides generation

Using the structured analysis, the workflow creates a new Google Slides deck with your company's template, populating it with relevant content, charts, and case studies based on the call discussion.
