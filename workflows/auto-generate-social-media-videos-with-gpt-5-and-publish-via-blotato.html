What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining an active, engaging social media presence with consistent video content. Marketing teams often struggle to produce enough quality videos due to time constraints and production complexity. This workflow combines GPT-5's content generation with Blotato's publishing capabilities to create a hands-off video marketing system.

The solution automatically generates video scripts based on your brand guidelines and current trends, creates visually appealing video assets, and publishes them across all connected social platforms at optimal times. It eliminates the need for manual video creation while ensuring your content stays fresh and relevant.

How GPT-5 generates content that Blotato publishes across social platforms

How It Works

Step 1: Content Brief Creation

The workflow starts by analyzing your social media performance data and current trends to generate content briefs. GPT-5 identifies high-performing topics and formats them into video script outlines tailored for each platform's audience.

Step 2: AI Video Script Generation

Using the content briefs, GPT-5 generates complete video scripts including scene descriptions, voiceover text, and suggested visuals. The AI references your brand voice guidelines and past high-performing content to maintain consistency.

Step 3: Video Asset Creation

The workflow automatically sources or generates visual assets (stock footage, animations, text overlays) based on the script. It combines these elements into formatted videos optimized for each social platform's specifications.

Step 4: Automated Publishing via Blotato

Completed videos are sent to Blotato which handles uploading to all connected social accounts. The system adds platform-specific captions, hashtags, and scheduling based on your content calendar and optimal posting times.

Pro tip: Set up a weekly review step where the system sends you generated videos for approval before publishing. This maintains quality control while still saving 90% of production time.
