What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of creating and publishing breaking news content to your Twitter/X account using Perplexity AI. It solves the challenge of maintaining an active, news-focused social media presence without requiring constant manual effort.

The system monitors trending topics or specific keywords, uses Perplexity AI to generate insightful summaries with proper citations, then formats and posts them to your Twitter/X account on a schedule you define. This ensures your followers always receive timely, valuable content while freeing up your team's time.

How It Works

1. Topic Selection and Triggering

The workflow begins by checking configured news sources or trending topics at scheduled intervals. You can set specific keywords, industries, or topics to monitor based on your audience's interests.

2. AI Content Generation

For each identified topic, the workflow queries Perplexity AI to generate a concise, factual summary. Perplexity's unique ability to provide sourced answers ensures your posts maintain credibility and accuracy.

3. Content Formatting

The AI-generated content is automatically formatted for Twitter/X's character limits, with hashtags and mentions added based on your preferences. Optional filters can ensure only high-quality content proceeds.

4. Automated Posting

Finally, the formatted content is posted to your Twitter/X account according to your preferred schedule. The workflow can include safeguards like duplicate checking and engagement-based pacing.

Pro tip: Combine this with engagement analytics to automatically adjust posting frequency based on when your audience is most active.
