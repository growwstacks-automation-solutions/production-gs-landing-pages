What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the tedious problem of manually finding and sharing remote job opportunities. Instead of spending hours each week scouring job boards, you can automate the entire process: sourcing, filtering, formatting, and posting.

It acts as an intelligent job board curator for your Telegram community, Discord server, or any channel where you share opportunities. By combining BrowserAct for web scraping, Google Gemini for AI-powered curation, and Telegram for distribution, it delivers only the highest-quality listings to your audience.

The system eliminates spam, low-paying gigs, and duplicate posts—ensuring your community receives valuable, relevant job leads automatically. This builds trust, increases engagement, and saves you significant manual effort.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Fetch

A schedule trigger runs every 12 hours (or your chosen interval), kicking off the workflow. BrowserAct nodes simultaneously scrape the latest job listings from Remotive and SimplyHired, pulling titles, descriptions, salaries, and links.

2. Merge & Aggregate

The raw job data from both sources is combined into a single list. A Merge node ensures all entries are processed uniformly, regardless of their origin.

3. AI Curation

Each job is passed to a Google Gemini AI Agent node. The AI reviews descriptions, removes duplicates, filters out listings paying less than $20/hour, and discards low-quality or spammy posts. This step ensures only worthwhile opportunities proceed.

4. Format Content

The AI rewrites the remaining jobs into clean, engaging HTML summaries suitable for Telegram. It structures each post with clear headings, bullet points, and direct application links.

5. Publish

The workflow loops through the curated list and sends each formatted job to your Telegram channel via the Telegram Bot node. A Wait node pauses between messages to avoid rate limits and keep posting smooth.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your automated job board running:

  1. Import the template into your n8n workspace using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for Telegram, BrowserAct, and Google Gemini in the respective nodes.
  3. Set up BrowserAct templates for Remotive and SimplyHired scraping (links provided in the workflow notes).
  4. Add your Telegram channel Chat ID to the "Send a text message" node.
  5. Adjust the schedule trigger to your preferred frequency (daily, twice daily, etc.).
  6. Activate the workflow and test with a manual trigger to verify posts appear correctly.

Pro tip: Start with a private test channel to verify formatting and filtering before posting to your main community.
