What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) into engaging LinkedIn content without manual effort. It fetches the daily space image and description from NASA's API, uses AI to translate the text while preserving scientific accuracy, generates strategic hashtags, and publishes everything to your LinkedIn profile.

The workflow solves the challenge of consistently sharing high-quality scientific content while saving hours each week. It maintains your professional presence as a science communicator or space enthusiast with fresh, educational content even when you're busy.

How It Works

1. Daily Trigger

The workflow activates automatically each morning to fetch NASA's latest APOD content through their public API.

2. AI Translation

The English description gets processed through Google Gemini's AI to produce accurate translations in your target language while maintaining all scientific terminology.

3. Hashtag Generation

An AI prompt analyzes the content to generate a mix of evergreen astronomy tags and specific topical hashtags for maximum discoverability.

4. LinkedIn Publishing

The final post combines the stunning space image, translated description, and hashtags before publishing to your LinkedIn profile through their API.

Pro tip: Schedule the workflow to run in the morning when LinkedIn engagement is highest in your timezone.
