What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the problem of delayed responses and after-hours customer inquiries on Telegram. Many businesses struggle to provide 24/7 support, leading to frustrated customers and missed opportunities. Community managers, support teams, and busy professionals often find themselves constantly checking messages or missing important conversations.

The solution is a smart AI assistant that automatically monitors your Telegram chats, analyzes incoming messages using Google Gemini's natural language understanding, and sends personalized, context-aware replies. It acts as a 24/7 personal assistant that never sleeps, ensuring your customers always receive timely responses while significantly reducing manual workload.

By combining scheduled monitoring with intelligent AI analysis, this workflow determines whether a message requires a response, drafts appropriate replies, handles delivery verification, and can even manage CAPTCHA challenges through BrowserAct integration.

How It Works

The automation follows a systematic process to ensure reliable and intelligent message handling:

1. Scheduled Monitoring

Every 15 minutes (configurable), the workflow triggers BrowserAct to fetch the latest chat history from your Telegram account. This regular checking ensures no message goes unnoticed, even during off-hours or weekends.

2. AI Analysis & Decision Making

Google Gemini reviews each conversation, analyzing the context, intent, and content of the last message. The AI determines whether a reply is necessary—distinguishing between questions requiring answers, statements needing acknowledgment, and idle chats that can be left alone.

3. Personalized Response Generation

When a reply is needed, Gemini drafts a personalized message that includes the user's name, addresses their specific query, and maintains your brand voice. The system can be configured for different tones—professional support, casual assistant, or sales representative.

4. Message Formatting & Delivery

A Code node cleans up the text to ensure proper Telegram formatting with correct line breaks and structure. BrowserAct then executes the delivery task, sending the drafted reply while handling any platform-specific requirements or verifications.

5. Delivery Verification & Error Handling

The workflow includes loops to check task status, ensuring messages are delivered successfully. It can handle retries for failed deliveries and log all activities for monitoring and optimization.

Pro tip: Configure the AI persona in the system prompt to match your brand voice. You can create different versions for customer support, sales inquiries, and community management—all running from the same workflow with conditional logic.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your Telegram AI assistant running in minutes:

  1. Download & Import: Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance using the workflow import function.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up your BrowserAct and Google Gemini credentials in n8n's credential management system.
  3. Prepare BrowserAct: Ensure you have the Telegram Personal Assistant template saved in your BrowserAct account and note your workflow ID.
  4. Adjust Schedule: Modify the Schedule Trigger node if you want different monitoring intervals (default is every 15 minutes).
  5. Customize AI Personality: Edit the system prompt in the Chatting & Answering AI agent to match your desired tone and response style.
  6. Test & Activate: Run the workflow once in test mode, send a test message to your Telegram, and verify the response. Then activate the workflow for continuous operation.
