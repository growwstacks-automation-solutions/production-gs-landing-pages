What This Workflow Does

Every e-commerce store faces the same repetitive customer question: "Where is my order?" These inquiries consume hours of support time daily, creating delays for customers and operational bottlenecks for your team. This automation solves that problem completely.

The workflow automatically handles customer support emails about Shopify orders using artificial intelligence. When customers email questions about order status, shipping updates, or delivery estimates, the system intelligently processes their request, retrieves real-time order data from your Shopify store, and generates a friendly, personalized response that gets sent immediately via Gmail.

What makes this solution particularly valuable is its smart filtering—it only responds to genuine order-related inquiries. Other emails (complaints, returns, complex issues) are automatically filtered out and passed to your human support team. This ensures automation enhances rather than replaces your customer service quality.

How It Works

Step 1: Email Trigger & Classification

The workflow monitors your connected Gmail account for incoming customer emails. When a new email arrives, it's immediately analyzed by an AI text classifier that identifies whether it contains order-related questions (WISMO—Where Is My Order). This classification ensures only appropriate emails proceed to automation.

Step 2: Shopify Data Retrieval

For identified order inquiries, the system extracts relevant details (order number, customer email, etc.) and queries your Shopify store via the Admin API. It retrieves the latest order status, shipping information, tracking numbers, and estimated delivery dates—all in real-time.

Step 3: AI Response Generation

Using OpenRouter AI (or your preferred AI model), the workflow generates a natural, empathetic response tailored to the specific order situation. The AI considers the customer's tone, order status, and any delays to craft a reply that maintains your brand voice while providing accurate information.

Step 4: Automated Reply Delivery

The personalized response is automatically sent back to the customer through your Gmail account, creating a seamless experience. The entire process—from receiving the email to sending the reply—typically completes within 60-90 seconds, compared to hours or days with manual handling.

Pro tip: Configure the AI's system message to match your brand's tone—friendly, professional, or casual—to ensure automated replies feel authentic to your customers.

What You'll Need

  1. Gmail account – For receiving customer emails and sending automated replies (business Gmail recommended)
  2. Shopify store with Admin API access – You'll need to create a custom app in Shopify with read_orders permission
  3. OpenRouter API key – Sign up at openrouter.ai for AI model access (free tier available for testing)
  4. n8n instance – Either self-hosted n8n or n8n.cloud account to run the workflow
  5. 20-30 minutes setup time – Most of which involves configuring API connections once

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation running involves straightforward configuration steps:

  1. Import the template – Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect Gmail – Authenticate your Gmail account to the trigger node (watch the included tutorial if needed)
  3. Configure Shopify – Create a custom app in your Shopify admin with read_orders permission, then add credentials to the Shopify node
  4. Add OpenRouter API key – Sign up at openrouter.ai, get your API key, and insert it into the AI agent node
  5. Test with a sample email – Send a test "Where is my order?" email to your connected Gmail to verify the full flow works
  6. Activate the workflow – Turn it on and let it handle incoming customer inquiries automatically

Pro tip: Start by testing with a small subset of emails or a dedicated test email address before enabling full automation on your primary support inbox.
