What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves a critical problem for sales teams and small businesses: inbound WhatsApp leads often arrive at unpredictable times, and manual response, sorting, and logging leads to missed opportunities and inefficient follow-up.

The automation listens for incoming WhatsApp messages, instantly classifies each message using AI (Ollama or any LLM) into categories like "hot lead," "warm lead," "support request," or "needs clarification." It then sends a tailored auto-reply, logs the interaction to Google Sheets, and for qualified leads, automatically books a Google Calendar meeting and sends the Meet link back via WhatsApp.

This creates a seamless, 24/7 lead management system that ensures every message is acknowledged, categorized, and acted upon—without requiring constant human monitoring.

How It Works

Step 1: WhatsApp Trigger & Filter

The workflow starts with a WhatsApp trigger that captures incoming messages. A filter node immediately excludes bot/status messages to prevent automation loops, ensuring only genuine lead communications are processed.

Step 2: AI Intent Classification

Each message is passed to an AI agent (Ollama Chat Model) with a custom prompt designed to identify intent. The AI returns a classification (e.g., "hot_lead") along with a confidence score, providing a clear signal for routing.

Step 3: Smart Routing & Tailored Actions

A Switch node directs the message based on its classified intent. Hot leads receive an enthusiastic reply, are logged to Google Sheets, have a Google Calendar meeting booked (typically 2 hours out), and get the Meet link sent via WhatsApp. Warm leads follow a similar path with a slightly delayed meeting time. Support requests get a ticket confirmation and are logged. Vague messages trigger a smart follow-up question to gather more context.

Step 4: Conversation Memory & Re-classification

If a lead replies to a follow-up question, the workflow uses a Window Buffer Memory node to retain context, allowing the AI to re-classify the new message within the ongoing conversation, ensuring accurate ongoing handling.

What You'll Need

  1. WhatsApp Business API (Meta Cloud API) access – Required to send/receive messages via official webhooks.
  2. Google Sheets & Google Calendar accounts – With OAuth credentials enabled for logging and booking.
  3. An LLM endpoint – Ollama (local), OpenAI, Anthropic, or any n8n-supported model for classification.
  4. An n8n instance – Cloud or self-hosted, to run the workflow.
  5. A Google Spreadsheet ID – Where leads will be logged (create a sheet with columns for timestamp, phone, intent, message).

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n workspace.
  2. Connect credentials for WhatsApp Business API, Google Sheets, and Google Calendar in the respective nodes.
  3. Update the spreadsheet ID in all three Google Sheets logging nodes to your target sheet.
  4. Configure your LLM – Set the Ollama endpoint or switch to your preferred AI model in the chat node.
  5. Adjust the BOT_NUMBERS array in the "Parse WhatsApp Message" node to match your WhatsApp Business phone number ID.
  6. Update phoneNumberId in all WhatsApp Send nodes to your number.
  7. Test the flow by sending a WhatsApp message to your business number and verifying the auto-reply, classification, and logging occur correctly.
