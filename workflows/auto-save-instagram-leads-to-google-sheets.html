What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of losing track of leads that come through Instagram forms. Many businesses use Instagram lead forms for giveaways, consultations, or product inquiries, but manually transferring this data to a CRM or spreadsheet is time-consuming and prone to errors.

The workflow automatically captures all form submissions from your Instagram lead forms and saves them directly to a Google Sheet. Each entry includes the contact details, submission timestamp, and any custom fields from your form. This creates a centralized, searchable database of all your Instagram leads.

How It Works

1. Instagram Lead Form Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your Instagram lead form. n8n detects this new submission through Instagram's API or webhook integration.

2. Data Extraction

The workflow extracts all relevant information from the form submission including name, email, phone number, and any custom questions you've included in your form.

3. Google Sheets Integration

The extracted data is then formatted and appended as a new row in your specified Google Sheet. The workflow can create timestamped entries and organize data into appropriate columns.
