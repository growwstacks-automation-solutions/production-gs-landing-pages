What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the critical problem of slow lead response times that cost businesses valuable sales opportunities. When a prospect fills out a demo request form on your website, they're at their peak interest level. Traditional manual processes mean hours or even days can pass before a sales rep follows up, by which time the lead has often gone cold or chosen a competitor.

The automation creates a complete lead-to-meeting pipeline that operates in real-time. It captures the lead, enriches it with company data, scores its potential, routes it appropriately, and either gets a salesperson to respond immediately or automatically sends the prospect a self-service booking link—all within minutes of form submission.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture & Normalization

The workflow receives leads via webhook from your website forms. It normalizes data from different form providers into a consistent format, ensuring email, name, company, and other fields are properly structured for processing.

2. Real-time Lead Enrichment

Using Clearbit's API, the workflow automatically enriches the lead with company data including employee count, annual revenue, industry, and the contact's seniority level. This transforms a basic email submission into a rich prospect profile.

3. Intelligent Scoring & Routing

A custom scoring algorithm evaluates the enriched data against your ideal customer profile. Leads scoring above a threshold (typically 60+ out of 100) are routed to a "hot leads" channel for immediate sales attention, while others go to standard nurturing sequences.

4. Automated Scheduling & Notification

For high-intent leads, the workflow fetches actual available Calendly slots and posts a formatted alert to your sales team's Slack channel with all lead details and booking links. If no salesperson responds within a set time, the system automatically emails the lead with self-service booking options.

Pro tip: Set your response window to 10 minutes during business hours. This gives your team time to claim hot leads while ensuring no prospect waits longer than 10 minutes for some form of response.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure API credentials for Clearbit, Slack, Calendly, and your email service in n8n's credentials management.

3. Update the webhook URL in your website forms to point to your n8n instance's webhook endpoint.

4. Customize the scoring logic in the "Calculate Fit Score" node to match your ideal customer profile.

5. Test with a sample lead submission and verify the entire flow from capture to notification works correctly.
