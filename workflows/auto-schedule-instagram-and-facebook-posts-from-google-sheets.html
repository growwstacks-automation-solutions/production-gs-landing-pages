Auto-schedule Instagram & Facebook Posts from Google Sheets

Eliminate manual posting. Automatically publish social content from your team's shared Google Sheets calendar.

What This Workflow Does

Manual social media posting is a time-consuming, error-prone task that distracts your team from creating great content. This automation solves that by turning Google Sheets into a powerful, collaborative content calendar that automatically publishes to Instagram and Facebook.

The workflow checks your Google Sheet at regular intervals, finds posts scheduled for publication, processes any images from Google Drive, and publishes them to your chosen platforms. After posting, it updates the sheet with success/failure status and timestamps, creating a complete audit trail. This eliminates the need for manual logins, copy-pasting, or using limited third-party schedulers.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence to ensure reliable, hands-off publishing.

1. Scheduled Trigger & Content Check

The workflow activates automatically (e.g., every 15 minutes) to scan your Google Sheet. It looks for rows where the status is "Pending" and the scheduled time has arrived or passed.

2. Data Retrieval & Filtering

It reads the post details—caption, image URL, platform selection (Instagram, Facebook, or both), and scheduling data—from the sheet. Only ready-to-publish content proceeds.

3. Image Processing

If an image link is provided, the workflow downloads the image from Google Drive and prepares it for social media upload. Text-only posts continue without this step.

4. Platform Routing & Publishing

Based on your selection, the post is routed to the appropriate API. It connects securely to Facebook Graph API (which handles both Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts) and publishes the content with the correct formatting.

5. Status Update & Logging

After publication, the workflow writes back to the Google Sheet, marking the post as "Published" with a timestamp, or "Failed" with an error message. This keeps your calendar always current.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets spreadsheet with columns for: caption, image URL (optional), platform selection, scheduled date/time, and status.
  2. Facebook Business Account with connected Instagram Professional account.
  3. Facebook Developer App with appropriate permissions (pages_read_engagement, pages_manage_posts, instagram_basic, instagram_content_publish).
  4. Google Drive for image storage (if using images).
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.

Pro tip: Create a standardized Google Sheets template for your team with dropdowns for platform selection and status. This reduces errors and makes the automation even more reliable.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your automation running in under 30 minutes with these steps:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Google Sheets connection by adding your spreadsheet ID and sheet name in the configuration node.
  3. Set up Facebook Graph API credentials in n8n using your Facebook Developer App tokens.
  4. Adjust the schedule trigger to match your preferred checking frequency (default is every 15 minutes).
  5. Test with a sample post marked as "Pending" in your sheet to verify the complete flow.
  6. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically—your social posting is now hands-free.
