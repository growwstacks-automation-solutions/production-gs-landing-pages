What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually sharing visual content between social platforms while maintaining an organized archive. It automatically scrapes X (formerly Twitter) for posts containing images, publishes them to a Telegram channel, and records all shared content in Google Sheets for future reference and analysis.

Content creators and marketers often struggle with maintaining consistent cross-platform presence. This workflow eliminates the need for manual copying and pasting while creating a centralized content database. The Google Sheets integration provides searchable records of shared posts with timestamps, making it easy to track performance and repurpose successful content.

How It Works

1. X Post Scraping

The workflow begins by scraping X for new posts matching your criteria (specific accounts, hashtags, or keywords). It filters for posts containing images, ensuring only visual content gets processed.

2. Image Processing

Each qualifying post's images are extracted and prepared for Telegram. The workflow handles multiple images per post, creating galleries when appropriate.

3. Telegram Publishing

Processed images and their accompanying text (if desired) are automatically published to your designated Telegram channel. The workflow maintains original post context while adapting formatting for Telegram's interface.

4. Google Sheets Archiving

Every shared post gets recorded in Google Sheets with metadata including timestamp, original URL, image links, and engagement metrics. This creates a searchable content library.
