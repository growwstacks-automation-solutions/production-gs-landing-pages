What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves a common yet time-consuming LinkedIn marketing challenge: manually sending direct messages to everyone who comments with a specific keyword on your posts. Content creators, coaches, and marketers often use "comment LINK to get the PDF" or "type CODE for the template" strategies to generate engagement and distribute resources.

The workflow automatically monitors your LinkedIn post, detects when someone comments with your predefined keyword, checks if they're a connection, and sends them a personalized DM with your resource link. It handles the entire fulfillment process while you focus on creating content.

Beyond saving hours of manual work, this automation ensures no potential lead is missed—even when your post goes viral and receives hundreds of comments. It delivers instant value, builds trust through prompt response, and turns engagement into measurable conversions.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence to ensure safe, compliant, and effective message delivery.

Step 1: Trigger & Input Collection

A simple web form captures your LinkedIn post URL, the trigger keyword (like "code" or "template"), the resource link you want to send, and your name for personalization. This form acts as the workflow's starting point whenever you have a new post to monitor.

Step 2: Comment Fetching

Using the ConnectSafely.AI API, the workflow retrieves all comments from your specified LinkedIn post. It processes them individually to maintain control and enable per-comment logic, such as connection checks and personalized messaging.

Step 3: Keyword Matching & Filtering

Each comment is checked for your trigger keyword (case-insensitive). Only comments containing the keyword proceed. This ensures you're only messaging genuinely interested users who explicitly requested your resource.

Step 4: Connection Verification

LinkedIn's platform restricts DMs to 1st-degree connections only. The workflow checks each commenter's connection status and automatically skips non-connections, protecting your account from policy violations and failed send attempts.

Step 5: Personalized DM Delivery

For each connected commenter who used the keyword, a personalized direct message is sent. The message includes their name, your resource link, and a friendly signature. This human touch increases response rates and builds relationships.

Step 6: Rate Limiting & Safety

Between each message, the workflow waits 15–30 minutes randomly. This built-in delay mimics human behavior, prevents LinkedIn's spam detection algorithms from flagging your account, and ensures long-term automation sustainability.

What You'll Need

  1. A ConnectSafely.AI account with an active API key.
  2. Your LinkedIn profile connected and authorized within ConnectSafely.AI.
  3. A LinkedIn post with comments (or planned comments) containing your trigger keyword.
  4. The resource URL you want to send (Google Drive, Notion, Calendly, etc.).
  5. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.

Pro tip: Test with a small, controlled post first. Create a test post, comment on it yourself with the keyword, and verify the DM delivers correctly before scaling to larger audiences.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Add your ConnectSafely.AI API credential in the credentials section and select it in each ConnectSafely node.
  3. Activate the webhook trigger and copy the generated form URL.
  4. Open the form in your browser, enter your post URL, keyword, resource link, and name.
  5. Submit the form to trigger a test execution and verify messages send correctly.
  6. Activate the workflow and use the form whenever you publish new "comment for link" content.
