What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manual post-purchase follow-ups by automatically sending personalized thank-you messages and loyalty coupons via WhatsApp when customers complete orders in your Shopify store. Traditional email follow-ups often get lost in crowded inboxes, while WhatsApp messages boast 98% open rates.

The workflow integrates Shopify with WhatsApp Business API through Rapiwa, creating immediate engagement that boosts customer satisfaction and repeat purchases. It eliminates hours of manual messaging while delivering timely incentives that increase customer lifetime value.

How It Works

1. Order completion trigger

The workflow monitors your Shopify store for completed orders through webhook notifications. When a customer completes a purchase, the automation immediately captures all relevant order details.

2. Customer data processing

The system extracts the customer's phone number, name, order details, and purchase history. This data personalizes the WhatsApp message template with specific product references and customer-specific details.

3. WhatsApp message generation

Using Rapiwa's API, the workflow constructs a compliant WhatsApp Business message containing a warm thank-you note, order summary, and unique loyalty coupon code generated specifically for that customer.

4. Message delivery

The personalized message is delivered to the customer's WhatsApp number within minutes of order completion. The system logs delivery status and prepares for potential customer replies.
