What This Workflow Does

If you create YouTube content, you know that most of your potential audience doesn't speak English. This workflow solves the language barrier by automatically translating your video titles and descriptions into multiple languages using Google's advanced Gemini AI. Instead of manually creating separate versions for each market or paying expensive translation services, this automation handles everything in minutes.

The system connects directly to your YouTube channel, extracts your video content, processes it through AI translation, and updates your video metadata with localized versions. This means Spanish, French, German, Japanese, and other language speakers can discover your content through their native language search terms, dramatically expanding your reach without creating separate channels.

Beyond simple word-for-word translation, the AI understands context and cultural nuances, ensuring your titles remain engaging and your descriptions maintain their persuasive power across different languages. This isn't just translation—it's intelligent localization that preserves your brand voice while adapting to international audiences.

How It Works

1. Video Identification and Language Configuration

The workflow starts by identifying which YouTube video to translate using its unique video ID. You specify which languages you want to target—whether it's just a few key markets or a comprehensive multilingual strategy covering dozens of languages.

2. Content Extraction and Analysis

It retrieves your existing video title, description, and metadata directly from YouTube's API. The system checks if you already have a default language set and intelligently handles any existing translations to avoid conflicts or duplication.

3. AI-Powered Translation Processing

Google Gemini AI processes your content, translating it while maintaining the original intent, tone, and marketing effectiveness. The AI considers context, cultural references, and platform-specific formatting requirements for optimal results.

4. Automated YouTube Updates

The translated content is automatically pushed back to YouTube, updating your video's localization settings. The system handles all API requirements, including sending the original content alongside translations as required by YouTube's platform.

5. Verification and Output

Finally, the workflow provides a direct URL to your video's updated localization page and confirms all translations have been successfully applied. You get immediate visibility into what changed and can verify the results instantly.

What You'll Need

  1. YouTube Account with API access (standard YouTube channels qualify)
  2. Google Gemini API Key (available through Google AI Studio with free tier access)
  3. n8n Instance (cloud or self-hosted version)
  4. Video IDs for the content you want to translate
  5. Target Language List (common choices include Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Hindi)

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 high-performing videos and 3-5 languages to test effectiveness before scaling to your entire library. Monitor which languages drive the most new views and engagement.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and Import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure YouTube Credentials by creating OAuth credentials in the YouTube node
  3. Set Up Google Gemini by obtaining an API key from Google AI Studio and adding it to the AI agent node
  4. Customize Language Settings in the "Defines Video ID and Languages" node with your target languages
  5. Test with One Video using a single video ID to verify the translation quality and YouTube updates work correctly
  6. Scale Your Implementation by modifying the workflow to process multiple videos or adding language-specific optimization rules
