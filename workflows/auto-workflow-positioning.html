What This Workflow Does

This automation template helps businesses track and optimize workflow positions across various processes. It automatically monitors where items (like job applications, support tickets, or production orders) are in your workflow pipeline, providing visibility into bottlenecks and inefficiencies.

By implementing this solution, teams can identify stages where work gets stuck, measure time-in-stage metrics, and make data-driven decisions to streamline operations. The template is particularly valuable for HR teams tracking candidates through hiring pipelines, customer support monitoring ticket resolution, or operations managing production workflows.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow connects to your existing systems to pull position data automatically, eliminating manual tracking spreadsheets.

2. Position Analysis

It analyzes how long items spend in each workflow stage and identifies transition patterns between stages.

3. Visualization

The system generates visual reports showing workflow movement and bottlenecks for easy interpretation.

4. Alerting

Configure alerts when items get stuck in certain stages beyond expected time thresholds.
