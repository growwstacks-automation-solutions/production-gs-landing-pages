What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms insurance claims processing by deploying specialized AI agents to handle the heavy lifting of actuarial analysis, claim documentation, and risk assessment. Designed for insurance carriers and third-party administrators, it solves the critical bottleneck of manual claim review that delays settlements and increases operational costs.

The system automatically ingests new claims data through scheduled triggers, then intelligently routes information to an actuarial analysis agent that calculates loss ratios and risk scores. A memo writer agent generates detailed claim summaries with recommendations, while a risk assessment agent evaluates fraud indicators and coverage implications. An orchestrator agent coordinates these specialists to ensure consistent analysis standards across all claims.

How It Works

1. Claims Data Ingestion

The workflow begins by automatically collecting new claims data from your existing systems through API connections or scheduled imports. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures all claims are captured immediately.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

Specialized AI agents process the claims data - one performs actuarial calculations, another drafts comprehensive memos, and a third evaluates risk factors. This division of labor mimics how human teams work but with perfect consistency.

3. Orchestrated Review

The orchestrator agent reviews all outputs, applies business rules, and flags any discrepancies for human review when confidence scores fall below thresholds you define.

4. Automated Distribution

Final reports are automatically distributed via email to product teams and Slack notifications to risk management, creating transparent workflows while reducing claim processing time from days to hours.

Pro tip: For maximum efficiency, configure the workflow to process claims in batches during off-peak hours when system resources are more available.
