What This Workflow Does

Creating daily video content is time‑consuming. Between research, scripting, filming, editing, and publishing across multiple platforms, a single video can take hours. This automation solves that by turning trending AI/tech news into polished video posts—completely automatically.

The workflow fetches the latest stories from sources like Hacker News, uses GPT‑4o to write a compelling 30‑second script, generates a video with a Heygen AI avatar, and publishes natively to Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and more via Blotato. What used to be a full‑day task now runs while you sleep.

How It Works

Step 1: AI Research & Scriptwriting

The workflow starts by scanning trending tech/AI news sources. GPT‑4o analyzes the top stories and writes a viral‑style script with a hook, key insights with statistics, and a clear call‑to‑action. It also creates two caption versions: a detailed one for platforms like YouTube and a concise one for Twitter/Threads.

Step 2: AI Avatar Video Generation

The script is sent to Heygen, where your chosen AI avatar and voice bring it to life. You can configure the avatar's appearance, tone, and background. The video is rendered in about 8 minutes—no cameras, lighting, or editing software required.

Step 3: Multi‑Platform Publishing

Once the video is ready, it's uploaded to Blotato's CDN and published natively to each social platform. The workflow applies platform‑specific optimizations: vertical formatting for TikTok, horizontal for YouTube, appropriate caption lengths, and relevant hashtags.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for content creators, marketing teams, tech influencers, SaaS companies, and media publishers who need to maintain a consistent video presence across multiple platforms without the production overhead. If you're posting AI news, tech updates, educational content, or daily summaries, this system turns a complex workflow into a simple, scheduled task.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self‑hosted)
  2. OpenAI API key for GPT‑4o access
  3. Heygen account with avatar credits
  4. Blotato account for multi‑platform publishing
  5. Social media accounts where you want to post (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above.
  2. Import into n8n via the workflow import feature.
  3. Configure credentials for OpenAI, Heygen, and Blotato in n8n's credentials management.
  4. Set your Heygen avatar ID and voice preferences in the "Setup Heygen" node.
  5. Connect your social platforms in Blotato and map them in the workflow.
  6. Test with a manual trigger to verify the full pipeline works.
  7. Schedule it to run daily at your preferred time (e.g., 10 AM).

Pro tip: Start with one platform (like LinkedIn) to validate the content quality before expanding to all platforms. Monitor engagement for the first week and tweak the AI prompts if needed.
