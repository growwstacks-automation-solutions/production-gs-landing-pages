Automate Applicant Tracking with AI CV Parsing

Free n8n workflow that uses GPT-4.1 to read CVs, extract candidate data, save to Google Sheets, and send automated alerts—cutting screening time by 80%.

What This Workflow Does

Manual CV screening is one of the most time-consuming tasks in recruitment. HR professionals spend hours reading through resumes, copying data into spreadsheets, and trying to compare candidates consistently. This workflow transforms that entire process into an automated, AI-powered system that acts as your 24/7 recruiting assistant.

When a candidate submits their application through a web form, this workflow automatically extracts text from their PDF CV, uses GPT-4.1 to intelligently parse and structure all relevant information (contact details, work experience, education, skills), checks for duplicate applications, saves everything to a Google Sheets database, and sends confirmation emails to both the candidate and recruiter. It turns unstructured CV data into actionable, searchable candidate information instantly.

How It Works

1. Candidate Submission

A public web form collects the candidate's name, email, experience level, and CV upload. This form can be embedded on your career page or shared as a direct link.

2. PDF Text Extraction

The workflow extracts raw text from the uploaded PDF file, preparing it for AI analysis regardless of the CV's formatting or layout.

3. AI-Powered CV Parsing

GPT-4.1 analyzes the CV text with a specialized prompt that extracts structured data: contact information, work history with dates and roles, education, technical skills, certifications, and generates a concise professional summary.

4. Duplicate Prevention Check

The system checks your Google Sheets database for existing entries with the same email address, preventing duplicate candidate records and wasted processing.

5. Database Storage

All structured candidate data is saved as a new row in Google Sheets, creating a searchable, filterable candidate database that grows with every application.

6. Automated Notifications

Two emails are sent simultaneously: a professional confirmation to the candidate acknowledging receipt, and an alert to the recruiter with the AI-generated CV summary for quick review.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompt in the OpenAI node to extract industry-specific skills or prioritize certain qualifications based on your hiring needs. This makes the parsing even more relevant to your business.

What You'll Need

  1. An active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. OpenAI API key with GPT-4.1 access
  3. Google account with Sheets and Gmail access
  4. A Google Sheet prepared with columns for candidate data
  5. Basic understanding of web forms (to share the application link)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the workflow into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for OpenAI and Google (OAuth2) in their respective nodes.
  3. Customize the form fields in the Form Trigger node if you need additional applicant information.
  4. Activate the workflow and copy the production URL from the Form Trigger node.
  5. Share the form URL on your career page or with candidates directly.
  6. Update the recruiter email address in the Gmail node to receive application alerts.
  7. Connect your Google Sheet in the Sheets nodes, ensuring column names match the extracted data.
