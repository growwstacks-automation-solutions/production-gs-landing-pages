What This Workflow Does

Manual contractor payroll is a time-consuming, error-prone process that drains hours from your week. This automation solves that by connecting your Dart project management data directly to Google Sheets, transforming raw time logs into accurate, ready-to-pay calculations.

The workflow automatically scans your target Dartboard on a schedule, aggregates time logs from completed tasks, cross-references each assignee with their individual rates stored in Google Sheets, calculates total pay, and updates your financial records. What used to take hours of spreadsheet work now happens automatically, ensuring contractors are paid accurately and on time while giving you complete financial visibility.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Dartboard Scan

The workflow triggers on your preferred schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) and scans your specified Dartboard for recently completed tasks. It extracts all relevant time tracking data, including assignee names, task durations, and completion dates.

2. Time Log Aggregation

All time entries are grouped by assignee, with durations summed to calculate total billable hours per person. The system handles multiple tasks per person across different projects, creating a comprehensive view of each contractor's work period.

3. Rate Matching & Calculation

The workflow references your Google Sheets rate table, matching each assignee to their correct hourly or project rate. Using simple multiplication (hours × rate), it calculates gross pay for each contractor, applying any predefined rules or adjustments.

4. Google Sheets Update

Final calculations are written back to your Google Sheets payroll tracker, updating columns for total hours, total pay, and calculation date. This creates an automatic audit trail and provides immediate visibility into labor costs for the period.

5. Optional AI Enhancement

For advanced users, the workflow can integrate Gemini AI to analyze task descriptions, categorize work types, or validate time entries against project expectations, adding an intelligent layer to your payroll verification.

What You'll Need

  1. Dart account with a Dartboard containing tasks with time tracking enabled
  2. Google Sheets with a properly formatted rate table (Name, HourlyRate, TotalHours, TotalPay, DateCalculated columns)
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  4. Google account with Sheets API access enabled
  5. Optional: Gemini AI API key if you want to enable intelligent time validation

Pro tip: Before automating your entire team, run a test with one Dartboard and 2-3 contractors. Verify calculations match your manual process, then scale with confidence.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your accounts by adding credentials for Dart and Google Sheets in the respective nodes.
  3. Update the Dartboard ID in the "List tasks" node to target your specific project board.
  4. Prepare your Google Sheet with the exact column headers mentioned above, pre-filling assignee names and rates.
  5. Set the schedule trigger to match your payroll cycle (e.g., every Friday at 5 PM).
  6. Test with historical data by manually triggering the workflow and comparing results to previous manual calculations.
  7. Activate the workflow once you're satisfied with the test results.
