What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your beta testing program by handling the entire onboarding process automatically. It verifies applicant qualifications, manages communications through Gmail, organizes testers in Trello, and provides secure access via personalized QR codes.

The system eliminates manual work while ensuring consistent, professional interactions with your beta testers. From initial application to final feedback collection, every step is automated to save your team time and improve data quality.

How It Works

1. Application Submission

Testers submit their information through your preferred form platform. The workflow automatically captures these submissions and begins processing.

2. Qualification Verification

The system checks each applicant against your criteria (device type, demographics, experience etc.), flagging qualified candidates for the next stage.

3. Trello Card Creation

For approved testers, the workflow creates a detailed Trello card with all relevant information, moving it to your "Active Testers" list for tracking.

4. Welcome Kit Delivery

Personalized welcome emails are sent through Gmail containing testing instructions, timelines, and most importantly - their unique access QR code.

5. Ongoing Engagement Tracking

The system monitors QR code usage, feedback submission, and other engagement metrics, updating the Trello cards accordingly.

What You'll Need

  1. A Trello account with a board setup for your beta program
  2. A Gmail or Google Workspace account for sending emails
  3. A form solution (like Google Forms or Typeform) to collect applications
  4. A QR code generation service (many free options available)

Pro tip: Create different QR codes for various test environments (iOS vs Android, different feature sets) to track usage patterns more precisely.
