What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire blog content creation process from initial research to published WordPress post. It combines the power of Perplexity for AI-powered research, GPT-4 for content generation, and WordPress for seamless publishing.

The workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of creating consistent, high-quality blog content by automating up to 80% of the process. Content creators spend less time on repetitive tasks while maintaining editorial control over the final output.

Visual representation of the automated content creation flow

How It Works

1. Topic Research with Perplexity

The workflow begins by using Perplexity AI to research your specified topic. It gathers relevant information, statistics and sources to create a comprehensive knowledge base for the article.

2. Content Generation with GPT-4

The research data is then passed to GPT-4 which structures it into a well-formatted blog post according to your predefined guidelines for tone, style and SEO optimization.

3. Editorial Review Process

Before publishing, the generated content goes through an optional human review step where editors can make adjustments or approve the content for publication.

4. WordPress Publishing

Finally, the approved content is automatically formatted and published to your WordPress site with proper categories, tags and featured images.

Pro tip: Use this workflow to maintain a consistent publishing schedule even when your team is busy with other projects.
