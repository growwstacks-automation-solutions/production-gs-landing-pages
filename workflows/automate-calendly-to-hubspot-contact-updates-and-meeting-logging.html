What This Workflow Does

Manual data entry between scheduling tools and CRM systems creates errors, delays, and missed opportunities. Sales teams spend hours copying meeting details from Calendly into HubSpot, often forgetting to log engagements or update contact information. This leads to incomplete pipeline visibility and inconsistent follow-up.

This automation solves that by instantly syncing every Calendly booking into HubSpot. When someone books a meeting, the workflow captures their details, checks if they already exist in your CRM, creates or updates their contact record, and logs the meeting as a rich engagement with all contextual information. It ensures your sales team has real-time, accurate data without any manual work.

The result is a perfectly maintained CRM where every interaction is tracked, contacts are always up-to-date, and sales teams can focus on conversations instead of administrative tasks.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New Meeting

The Calendly trigger node fires whenever an invitee books a meeting. It captures the attendee's name, email, meeting start and end times, and any responses to custom questions you've set up in Calendly.

Step 2: Search Existing Contact

The workflow queries HubSpot to see if a contact with the invitee's email already exists. This prevents duplicate records and ensures meetings are linked to the correct person.

Step 3: Decision Logic

If the contact exists, the workflow proceeds to log the meeting against that record. If not, it creates a new contact in HubSpot first with the attendee's name and email.

Step 4: Engagement Logging

For existing contacts, a HubSpot meeting engagement is created tied directly to that contact. For new contacts, the workflow creates the contact then immediately logs the meeting. The engagement includes meeting title, times, attendee details, and custom question notes.
