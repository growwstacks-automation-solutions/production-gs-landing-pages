What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how your business handles incoming calls by deploying an AI-powered virtual receptionist. When a customer calls your business number, Vapi's voice AI answers professionally, asks qualifying questions, checks your real-time Google Calendar availability, and books appointments automatically - all without human intervention.

The system then logs complete call details in Airtable, including the caller's information, scheduled time, and any notes from the conversation. This eliminates manual data entry, prevents scheduling conflicts, and ensures no lead falls through the cracks - even when you're unavailable to answer calls personally.

How It Works

1. Caller connects with AI receptionist

When someone calls your business number, Vapi's conversational AI greets them with a professional welcome message and begins the interaction. The AI is trained to sound natural while gathering necessary information.

2. Qualification and scheduling

The AI asks predetermined questions to understand the caller's needs, then checks Google Calendar for available slots that match your business rules (like minimum notice periods or blocked hours).

3. Real-time calendar booking

Once the caller selects a time, the system instantly creates a Google Calendar event with all relevant details. Both parties receive immediate confirmation with calendar invites.

4. CRM logging in Airtable

Every call's details - including caller information, call purpose, scheduled time, and duration - are automatically recorded in your Airtable base for future reference and analytics.
