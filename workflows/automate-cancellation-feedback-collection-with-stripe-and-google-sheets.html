What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical blind spot for subscription businesses - understanding why customers cancel. When users unsubscribe through Stripe, the workflow automatically sends them a feedback request and logs their responses in Google Sheets for analysis.

Manual cancellation follow-ups are inconsistent and often missed. This automated system ensures you collect structured feedback from every cancellation, creating a valuable dataset to reduce churn and improve your product.

How It Works

1. Stripe cancellation trigger

The workflow activates whenever a customer cancels their subscription in Stripe. It captures key details like the customer email, subscription plan, and cancellation timestamp.

2. Feedback request delivery

The system immediately sends the customer a feedback form via email. This prompt timing increases response rates while the cancellation experience is fresh in their mind.

3. Response collection

Customers can select from common cancellation reasons (price, missing features, etc.) and optionally provide additional comments. Their responses are captured automatically.

4. Data logging

All feedback is recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps, customer details, and cancellation reasons. This creates a searchable database for churn analysis.

Pro tip: Add a conditional step to trigger customer success alerts when high-value accounts cancel with specific feedback reasons.
