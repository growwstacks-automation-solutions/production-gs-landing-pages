Automate Candidate Analysis & Ranking with Jotform & Gemini AI

Instantly screen UGC campaign applicants, score them with AI, filter qualified talent, and automate outreach—all without manual review.

What This Workflow Does

Manual candidate screening for User-Generated Content (UGC) campaigns, influencer recruitment, or high-volume hiring is time-consuming, inconsistent, and delays outreach to top talent. Teams waste hours reviewing hundreds of applications against complex rubrics, leading to missed opportunities and campaign launch delays.

This workflow automates the entire initial screening process. When a candidate submits a Jotform application, the system instantly uses Google Gemini AI to evaluate their submission against your specific criteria, assigns a numerical score (0-10), automatically filters out unqualified applicants, and immediately initiates outreach to qualified talent—all within seconds of submission.

The solution eliminates human bias from initial screening, ensures every applicant is evaluated consistently, and dramatically accelerates your recruitment pipeline. Qualified candidates receive acceptance communications immediately, while your team gets notified to follow up, and all data is logged in Google Sheets for tracking.

How It Works

1. Application Trigger

The workflow activates the moment a candidate submits your Jotform application. The Jotform Trigger node captures all submitted data—including text responses, demographic information, portfolio links, and any custom fields you've configured.

2. AI Scoring & Analysis

The captured application data is sent to Google Gemini AI with your predefined scoring criteria. The AI evaluates factors like content quality, audience relevance, brand alignment, experience level, and specific campaign requirements, then returns a definitive numerical score.

3. Qualification Check

An "If" node compares the AI-generated score against your minimum threshold (typically 6/10 or higher). Candidates meeting or exceeding the threshold proceed to the next steps; those below are filtered out (with optional automated rejection notifications).

4. Data Logging & Notification

For qualified candidates: Their application data and AI score are automatically appended to a Google Sheets tracker for centralized record-keeping. Simultaneously, two emails are triggered—an acceptance email to the candidate and an internal notification to your HR or campaign team.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with basic workflow configuration access
  2. Jotform account with API credentials and an existing candidate application form
  3. Google Gemini API key from Google AI Studio for the scoring analysis
  4. Google Sheets document for candidate tracking (with edit permissions)
  5. Gmail account or SMTP service for sending automated emails
  6. Clear scoring criteria defining what makes a qualified candidate for your specific campaign

Pro tip: Before full deployment, test your AI scoring prompt with 10-15 sample applications to ensure it aligns with your team's manual evaluations. Fine-tune the criteria weights based on these results.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Jotform: Connect your Jotform API credentials and update the form ID in the trigger node.
  3. Set up AI scoring: Add your Google Gemini API key and review/modify the scoring prompt in the AI Agent node to match your criteria.
  4. Connect Google Sheets: Authenticate with Google Sheets and replace the placeholder spreadsheet ID with your tracking document.
  5. Configure emails: Set up Gmail credentials and update recipient addresses in both email nodes.
  6. Test thoroughly: Submit test applications, verify scoring accuracy, and ensure emails deliver correctly before activating.
