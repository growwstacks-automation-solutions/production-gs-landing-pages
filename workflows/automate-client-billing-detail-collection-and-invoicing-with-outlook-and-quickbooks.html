Automate Client Billing Detail Collection & Invoicing

Streamline your invoicing process by automatically collecting missing billing details from clients and generating invoices in QuickBooks.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the common problem of chasing clients for missing billing details before you can send an invoice. Instead of manual back-and-forth emails and data entry, it automates the entire flow from request to invoice delivery.

When you need to invoice a client but lack their billing name or address, this system sends an automated email via Outlook requesting the information. The client submits details through a simple form, and the workflow automatically creates or updates their record in QuickBooks, generates the invoice, and sends it directly to them.

Perfect for freelancers, consultants, service providers, and small businesses that invoice regularly. It eliminates manual data copying between apps, reduces errors, and speeds up your billing cycle by days.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger the Request

You manually start the workflow by filling out a simple form with the client's email, invoice amount, description, and product/service name. This form can be accessed via a public URL, allowing you to trigger invoicing from anywhere without opening n8n.

Step 2: Send Billing Detail Request Email

The workflow sends a pre-written email via Outlook to the client, politely requesting their billing name and address. The email template can be customized to match your brand voice and include specific instructions.

Step 3: Collect Client Information

The client submits their billing details through a hosted form. The system captures their name and address securely, ready for processing.

Step 4: Add or Find Client in QuickBooks

The workflow checks if the client already exists in QuickBooks. If not, it creates a new customer record automatically. If they exist, it uses the existing record, ensuring data consistency.

Step 5: Generate Invoice

Using the submitted billing details and selected product, the system creates a complete invoice in QuickBooks with proper line items, amounts, and descriptions.

Step 6: Send Invoice to Client

The invoice is automatically emailed to the client using QuickBooks' native email interface, completing the cycle without any manual intervention.

What You'll Need

  1. A Microsoft Outlook account with credentials configured in n8n.
  2. A QuickBooks Online account with OAuth2 credentials set up.
  3. Your products/services listed in QuickBooks with exact names matching your workflow dropdown.
  4. Basic familiarity with n8n to import and configure the workflow.

Pro tip: Ensure product names in your QuickBooks item list exactly match those in the workflow's dropdown selection. This prevents invoice creation errors.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file using the button above.
  2. Import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import feature.
  3. Configure your Microsoft Outlook and QuickBooks Online credentials in the respective nodes.
  4. Update the product dropdown in the "Enter Client Details" node to match your QuickBooks items.
  5. Select the appropriate tax code in the "Create A New Invoice" node.
  6. Customize the email message in the Outlook node to reflect your brand voice.
  7. Test the workflow with a sample client email to ensure everything works smoothly.
