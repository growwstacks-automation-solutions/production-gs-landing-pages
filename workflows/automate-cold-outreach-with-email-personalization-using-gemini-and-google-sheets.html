What This Workflow Does

Cold outreach remains one of the most challenging yet essential sales activities, with response rates often below 2% for generic emails. This n8n workflow solves this by automating personalized email campaigns powered by Gemini AI's natural language processing.

The system pulls prospect data from Google Sheets, analyzes each contact's digital footprint using Gemini AI, and generates highly tailored emails that reference specific pain points, company news, or mutual connections. It then manages the entire outreach sequence with automated follow-ups while tracking engagement metrics.

How It Works

1. Prospect Data Import

The workflow connects to your Google Sheet containing prospect information. It reads contact details, company information, and any available context about each lead's potential needs.

2. AI-Powered Personalization

Gemini AI analyzes each prospect's LinkedIn profile, company website, and other public data sources to generate personalized email content. The AI crafts opening lines that establish relevance and value propositions tailored to the recipient's role.

3. Email Sequence Execution

The system sends initial emails and schedules follow-ups based on your predefined sequence. Each message maintains personalization while building on previous interactions.

4. Response Tracking

All replies and engagement metrics are automatically logged back to your Google Sheet, providing real-time campaign analytics without manual data entry.
