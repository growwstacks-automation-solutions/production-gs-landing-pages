What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the cumbersome process of handling data consent disputes into a streamlined, AI-assisted workflow. When a customer submits a request to access, modify, or delete their personal data (common under GDPR, CCPA and other privacy regulations), the system automatically validates the request, logs it in Google Sheets, generates an appropriate response using GPT-4o, notifies relevant teams via Slack, and maintains a complete audit trail.

Manual processing of these requests typically takes legal and compliance teams 30-60 minutes per case. This automation reduces that to under 5 minutes of human review time while ensuring consistent compliance with regulatory requirements. The system handles the entire lifecycle from initial receipt through resolution documentation.

How It Works

1. Complaint Receipt & Validation

The workflow triggers when a new consent dispute arrives via email or webform. GPT-4o analyzes the unstructured complaint text to extract key details like the requester's identity, data types involved, and requested action. The AI validates whether the submission contains all required elements for processing.

2. Data Normalization & Logging

Valid complaints are standardized into a consistent format and logged in Google Sheets with timestamps, status flags, and categorization. The sheet serves as both a processing dashboard and compliance audit trail, with columns for all required regulatory documentation points.

3. AI-Powered Response Drafting

GPT-4o generates a context-aware response draft that includes all legally required elements while maintaining a professional tone. The AI references previous similar cases from your historical data to ensure consistency. Responses are automatically saved to Google Drive as PDFs for record-keeping.

4. Team Notification & Escalation

Slack notifications alert your compliance team about new disputes, with severity indicators for urgent cases. The workflow includes escalation paths for complex situations requiring human judgment, with all context automatically passed to the assigned team member.

5. Resolution Tracking

As team members complete actions in Slack or email, the workflow updates the Google Sheets record with resolution details and timestamps. Automated follow-ups ensure no case falls through the cracks before regulatory deadlines.

Pro tip: Train GPT-4o on your past consent dispute resolutions to make its suggestions more aligned with your company's specific compliance posture and communication style.
