What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the tedious process of publishing content across multiple social media platforms simultaneously. It solves the challenge of maintaining an active presence on TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook without spending hours manually uploading and scheduling posts.

By leveraging Blotato's API integration capabilities, the workflow pulls content from your source (like Google Drive or Dropbox), formats it appropriately for each platform's specifications, and publishes according to your schedule. This eliminates platform hopping and ensures consistent branding across all channels.

Example of automated content publishing across multiple platforms

How It Works

1. Content Ingestion

The workflow begins by retrieving your prepared content from a cloud storage location. This could be raw video files, images, captions, and metadata stored in an organized folder structure.

2. Platform-Specific Formatting

Each piece of content gets automatically adapted for different platforms. Videos are reformatted to optimal aspect ratios, captions are adjusted for character limits, and hashtags are optimized per platform algorithms.

3. Scheduling & Publishing

The system publishes content immediately or schedules it for optimal engagement times. Advanced workflows can even stagger posts across platforms to maximize reach throughout the day.

Pro tip: Add a human approval step before publishing if your content requires compliance checks or brand alignment verification.
