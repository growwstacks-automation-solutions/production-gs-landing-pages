What This Workflow Does

This automation helps businesses proactively retain customers by identifying at-risk accounts before they churn. It analyzes customer behavior patterns using AI to predict which customers are most likely to cancel their subscriptions or stop purchasing.

When high-risk customers are identified, the workflow automatically generates personalized retention emails with special offers (delivered via Stripe coupons) and sends them through through Gmail. This creates a seamless, timely intervention system that reduces churn without requiring manual effort from your team.

How It Works

1. Customer Data Analysis

The workflow pulls customer data from your CRM or database, analyzing metrics like login frequency, support ticket volume, and purchase patterns to identify risk patterns.

2. AI Risk Scoring

OpenAI evaluates each customer's data point to generate a churn risk score (low/medium/high). The AI considers historical patterns and current behavior trends.

3. Coupon Generation

For customers flagged as high-risk, the system automatically creates personalized discount coupons in Stripe with appropriate discount levels based on customer lifetime value.

4. Email Personalization

The workflow crafts customized email content using customer-specific details and the generated coupon code. Emails are sent through Gmail with your business branding.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for SaaS companies, subscription businesses, and ecommerce stores with recurring revenue models. Customer Success Managers, Growth Marketers, and Retention Specialists will benefit most from implementing this automation.
