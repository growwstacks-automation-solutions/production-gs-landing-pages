What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered customer support automation transforms how you handle incoming support requests by combining the power of Gmail, Google Sheets, your ERP data, and GPT-4o artificial intelligence. It automatically processes customer inquiries, provides instant responses using your business data, and only escalates complex issues to human agents.

The system eliminates repetitive support tasks while ensuring customers receive accurate, personalized responses 24/7. By integrating with your existing ERP and knowledge bases, the AI assistant can answer questions about order status, product details, account information, and more - all while maintaining your brand voice and communication standards.

How It Works

1. Email Capture and Analysis

The workflow monitors your support inbox (Gmail) for new messages. When a customer email arrives, the system extracts key details like the sender's email, subject line, and message content. It then classifies the inquiry type and determines the appropriate response path.

2. Data Retrieval from ERP and Sheets

For inquiries requiring business data (like order status or inventory checks), the system connects to your ERP and Google Sheets to retrieve real-time information. This ensures all responses contain the most current data available.

3. AI Response Generation

GPT-4o analyzes the customer's question along with the retrieved business data to generate a personalized, accurate response. The AI is trained on your brand guidelines and past support interactions to maintain consistent tone and quality.

4. Response Review and Delivery

Before sending, the system can optionally flag certain responses for human review based on confidence levels or topic complexity. Approved replies are sent through your Gmail account with proper formatting and attachments if needed.

5. Ticket Logging and Follow-up

All interactions are automatically logged in Google Sheets for reporting and analysis. The system can schedule follow-ups for unresolved issues or send customer satisfaction surveys after case closure.
