What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually reviewing every job application that arrives in your inbox. For hiring managers and recruiters, sorting through dozens (or hundreds) of resumes to identify qualified candidates is one of the most repetitive parts of their job.

The workflow automatically processes incoming applications by extracting candidate data from CVs/emails, scoring applicants based on your criteria using AI, and organizing everything in Airtable for easy review. It eliminates manual data entry while ensuring no applications fall through the cracks.

How It Works

1. New Application Detection

The workflow monitors a designated Gmail label for new messages containing attachments (typically resumes/CVs). When a new application arrives, it triggers the automation process.

2. Data Extraction

The system extracts key information from both the email (job code, application date) and the attached resume. Using AI parsing, it identifies candidate name, contact details, work history, education, skills, and other relevant qualifications.

3. AI Scoring

Each applicant is automatically scored based on your predefined criteria (e.g., years of experience, specific skills, education level). The AI weights different factors according to your priorities and flags top candidates.

4. Airtable Organization

All extracted data and scores are automatically added to your Airtable base as a new candidate record. The record includes the original resume attachment, parsed data fields, and calculated match score for easy comparison.
