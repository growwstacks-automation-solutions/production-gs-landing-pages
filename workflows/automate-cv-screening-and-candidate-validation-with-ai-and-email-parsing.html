What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the tedious process of screening job applicants by combining AI analysis with automated validation checks. It transforms your recruitment pipeline by instantly evaluating incoming CVs against your job requirements, verifying candidate information, and sorting qualified applicants.

The system parses CVs from emails or application forms, extracts key information using AI, cross-references candidate details with external databases, and automatically moves qualified candidates to the next hiring stage while politely rejecting others. This reduces manual screening time by 80-90% while improving consistency in your hiring process.

How It Works

1. Email Parsing & Document Extraction

The workflow monitors your recruitment inbox for new applications. When a candidate email arrives, it automatically extracts attachments (CVs) and parses the email content for candidate details.

2. AI-Powered CV Analysis

Each CV is processed by AI to extract key information like skills, experience, education, and certifications. The AI compares these against your predefined job requirements to generate a match score.

3. Automated Candidate Validation

The system verifies candidate information by checking professional profiles, validating credentials, and confirming contact details. This reduces the risk of fraudulent applications.

4. Smart Candidate Routing

Based on the AI scoring and validation results, qualified candidates are automatically moved to your interview pipeline while others receive polite rejection emails. All data is logged in your ATS or spreadsheet.
