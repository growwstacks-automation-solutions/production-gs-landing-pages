What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of staying current with rapidly evolving AI developments. It combines Perplexity Sonar Pro's advanced search capabilities with Telegram's efficient messaging platform to deliver:

1) Daily summaries of key AI research papers and breakthroughs
2) Curated industry news from trusted sources
3) Personalized alerts based on your team's focus areas
4) Properly cited references for deeper exploration

How It Works

1. Content Aggregation

Perplexity Sonar Pro scans 50+ AI research sources and news outlets using customized search queries. The AI identifies emerging patterns and significant developments.

2. Intelligent Filtering

The system applies relevance scoring based on your predefined criteria (technical depth, business impact, novelty). Only high-value content proceeds to summarization.

Pro tip: Configure different Telegram channels for different team functions (research, product, executives) with tailored content filters.

3. Concise Summarization

Perplexity generates 3-5 sentence summaries of each key development, maintaining technical accuracy while improving readability. Citations link to full sources.

4. Scheduled Delivery

The workflow compiles digests and sends via Telegram at optimal times for your team's timezone, with options for urgent breaking news alerts.

Three frequent pitfalls: 1) Overloading digests with too many items 2) Failing to verify AI-generated summaries 3) Not allowing user customization. Avoid these by setting clear content guidelines, implementing human review steps for critical updates, and providing subscription options for different interest levels.

Another mistake is treating all sources equally. The system should weight content by credibility - peer-reviewed research higher than blog posts. Also ensure proper error handling when APIs change. Build in manual override capabilities for when the automation misses important context.

  • Limit digests to 5-7 key items maximum
  • Implement weekly quality assurance checks
  • Provide easy feedback mechanisms for subscribers
