What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of creating and publishing culturally appropriate Hindu festival posts on X (formerly Twitter). It solves the challenge of maintaining consistent, high-quality religious content while saving hours of manual research and posting time.

The system combines Google Gemini's factual accuracy with GPT-4o Mini's creative variations to generate diverse post content. It automatically checks the Hindu calendar, creates multiple post variations, selects the most appropriate one based on your criteria, and publishes it at optimal times - all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Festival Date Verification

The workflow first checks a reliable Hindu calendar API to identify upcoming festivals based on your configured location preferences. This ensures posts are always timely and relevant to your audience's regional traditions.

2. AI Content Generation

Using both Google Gemini and GPT-4o Mini, the system generates multiple post variations for each festival. The AI models use carefully crafted prompts to ensure culturally sensitive, informative, and engaging content that respects religious traditions.

3. Content Selection

The workflow evaluates generated posts against your predefined criteria (length, tone, keywords) and selects the most appropriate version. You can configure filters to automatically reject certain types of content or require specific elements in every post.

4. Automated Publishing

Selected posts are automatically formatted with appropriate hashtags, mentions, and media (if configured), then published to your X account at optimal times. The system can also create draft posts for human review if preferred.
