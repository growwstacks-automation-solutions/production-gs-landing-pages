What This Workflow Does

Managing your inbox can feel like a full-time job. Some emails deserve an instant response, others need thoughtful handling, and many don't need a reply at all. This workflow takes that weight off your shoulders by combining AI intelligence with human oversight, so you spend less time sorting and more time focusing on what matters.

This workflow does that decision-making for you. With the help of Google Gemini, it reads each incoming email, categorizes it, and then either replies instantly with a warm, professional message, prepares a draft reply for you to review, or does nothing if the message is irrelevant. How It Works

This intelligent email automation workflow follows a systematic process to handle incoming messages efficiently while maintaining human oversight where needed.

Step 1: Gmail Trigger Captures New Emails

The workflow starts by monitoring your Gmail inbox for new messages. It can be configured to trigger on specific conditions like emails from certain senders, containing particular keywords, or arriving during business hours. This ensures you're only processing relevant communications.

Step 2: AI Analysis and Categorization

Each incoming email is sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis. The AI evaluates the content, intent, and urgency, then categorizes it into one of three paths: instant reply (for routine inquiries), draft creation (for sensitive or complex topics), or ignore (for newsletters and promotional content).

Step 3: Intelligent Response Generation

Based on the categorization, the AI generates appropriate responses. For instant replies, it crafts complete, professional responses immediately. For draft emails, it creates suggested replies that maintain your tone and style while flagging them for your review before sending.

Step 4: Automated Action Execution

The workflow then executes the determined action: sending immediate replies, saving drafts to your Gmail drafts folder, or archiving irrelevant emails. All actions are logged for transparency, and you receive periodic summaries of automated activity.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this email automation workflow in your n8n environment:

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance using the "Import from File" option.
  2. Configure Gmail connection: Set up OAuth2 credentials for your Gmail account in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Add Gemini API key: Obtain your Google Gemini API key and add it to the AI node credentials in the workflow.
  4. Customize categorization rules: Adjust the prompt in the AI node to match your specific email types and response preferences.
  5. Test with sample emails: Run the workflow with test emails to ensure proper categorization and response generation.
  6. Activate the workflow: Once testing is complete, activate the workflow to begin automated email processing.
