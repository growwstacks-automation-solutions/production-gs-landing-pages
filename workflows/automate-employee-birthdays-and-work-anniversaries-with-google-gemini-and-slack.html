What This Workflow Does

This automation replaces manual employee celebration management with a seamless system that automatically recognizes birthdays and work anniversaries in Slack. It eliminates the administrative burden on HR teams while ensuring no employee milestone goes unnoticed.

The workflow combines Google Gemini's natural language generation with Slack's messaging capabilities to create personalized, authentic celebration posts. Unlike basic bots that send generic messages, this system crafts unique announcements tailored to each employee's role, tenure, and achievements.

How It Works

1. Daily employee data check

The system automatically checks your employee database each morning for any birthdays or work anniversaries occurring that day. It verifies employment status to ensure only current team members are recognized.

2. Personalized message generation

Google Gemini creates customized celebration messages by analyzing the employee's role, tenure, recent accomplishments, and any available personal interests. The AI avoids generic templates, producing messages that feel genuinely personal.

3. Slack channel posting

The workflow posts the generated message to your designated Slack channel, tagging the employee and including appropriate emoji reactions. Optional features include scheduling celebration threads at optimal engagement times.
