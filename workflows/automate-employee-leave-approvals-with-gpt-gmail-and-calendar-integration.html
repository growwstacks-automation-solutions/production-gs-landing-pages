What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the manual, time-consuming process of employee leave approvals into a streamlined digital workflow. It handles the complete lifecycle from request submission to final resolution, reducing HR workload while improving response times and policy compliance.

The system uses AI to evaluate requests against company policies, team schedules, and historical patterns. Approved leaves automatically block calendars and notify teams, while rejections include policy references and alternative suggestions - all without human intervention for standard cases.

How It Works

1. Leave Request Submission

Employees submit requests via email using a standardized template. The system extracts key details like dates, leave type, and reason using natural language processing.

2. AI Policy Evaluation

GPT analyzes the request against your company's leave policies, considering factors like accrued time, blackout periods, and department coverage needs. It provides an approval recommendation with confidence scoring.

3. Automated Calendar Integration

For approved requests, the system automatically creates calendar events marking the employee as out of office. It checks for scheduling conflicts and adjusts meeting invites if needed.

4. Notification System

Both employees and managers receive tailored notifications. Approvals include calendar details, while rejections explain policy reasons and suggest alternatives.
