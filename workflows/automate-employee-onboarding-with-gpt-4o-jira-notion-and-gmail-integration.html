What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your employee onboarding from a manual, error-prone process into a streamlined digital experience. When a new hire is added to your HR system, the workflow automatically creates their Jira account, generates a personalized Notion onboarding checklist using GPT-4o, and sends a tailored welcome email through Gmail.

The system reduces HR administrative work by 60-80% while ensuring every new employee receives consistent, role-specific onboarding materials. AI personalization means engineering hires get technical documentation while sales teams receive product training - all automatically tailored based on their position and department.

How It Works

1. New hire trigger

The workflow activates when a new employee record appears in your HR system or when a hiring manager submits an onboarding request form.

2. Jira account creation

The system automatically provisions Jira accounts with appropriate project access based on department and role permissions.

3. AI-powered Notion checklist

GPT-4o analyzes the new hire's role, department, and seniority to generate a customized 30-60-90 day onboarding plan in Notion with relevant documentation links.

4. Personalized welcome email

The workflow crafts and sends a warm welcome email through Gmail containing all access details, first-week schedule, and links to their Notion onboarding hub.

Pro tip: Add a Slack integration to notify team leads when their new team member's onboarding is complete, creating immediate connection opportunities.
