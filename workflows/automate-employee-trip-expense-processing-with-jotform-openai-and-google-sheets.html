What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the tedious manual process of employee expense reporting into a streamlined digital workflow. When employees submit trip expenses through Jotform, the system automatically processes receipts, categorizes expenses using AI, and logs everything in Google Sheets for accounting.

The workflow eliminates manual data entry errors while ensuring compliance with company spending policies. Finance teams gain real-time visibility into travel expenditures without chasing paper receipts or deciphering handwritten notes. Employees benefit from faster reimbursements and mobile-friendly submission options.

How It Works

1. Employee submits expense form

Team members complete a customized Jotform with trip details, attaching receipts and expense documentation. The form captures all necessary fields like dates, amounts, vendors, and expense categories.

2. AI processes and categorizes expenses

OpenAI analyzes submitted receipts and form data, extracting key information with high accuracy. The AI identifies expense categories, flags potential policy violations, and standardizes data formats for accounting.

3. Data syncs to Google Sheets

Processed expense details automatically populate a master Google Sheet, creating an audit-ready record. The system timestamps entries, assigns unique IDs, and maintains version history for compliance.

4. Approval notifications trigger

Managers receive automated alerts when reports require approval. The system can route approvals based on amount thresholds or department hierarchies, with all communication logged in the spreadsheet.
