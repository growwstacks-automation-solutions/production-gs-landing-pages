What This Workflow Does

Customer support teams waste countless hours answering the same basic questions repeatedly—"What are your hours?", "How much does it cost?", "Where are you located?". This WhatsApp automation bot solves that problem by providing instant, accurate responses 24/7.

The workflow operates in a continuous monitoring loop, analyzing incoming WhatsApp messages for predefined keywords related to common inquiries. When it detects relevant keywords, it automatically sends the appropriate information. For questions it doesn't recognize, it gracefully directs users to human assistance, ensuring no customer gets stuck.

This isn't just about saving time—it's about improving customer experience. People expect immediate responses on messaging platforms, and this automation delivers that consistency while freeing your team to handle more complex, value-added interactions.

How It Works

The bot follows a logical sequence that mimics how a human support agent would triage messages, but with machine speed and consistency.

Step 1: Message Monitoring & Capture

The workflow continuously monitors your connected WhatsApp Business account for new incoming messages. Each message is captured with its metadata (sender, timestamp, message content) and prepared for analysis.

Step 2: Keyword Analysis & Detection

The system scans each message for keywords you've predefined in your knowledge base. Common categories include operational queries (hours, location), pricing questions, product availability, and basic troubleshooting terms. The detection can be configured for exact matches, partial matches, or even synonyms.

Step 3: Response Matching & Delivery

When a keyword match is found, the workflow retrieves the corresponding pre-written response from your database or configuration. This response is then sent back to the customer via WhatsApp, typically within seconds of their original message.

Step 4: Fallback Handling

If no keywords are matched, the workflow follows a fallback path. This can include notifying a human agent via email or Slack, providing a link to your help center, or asking the customer to rephrase their question—ensuring no inquiry goes unanswered.

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this automation running in under 30 minutes with these simple steps:

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import function.
  2. Connect WhatsApp: Configure the WhatsApp trigger node with your Business API credentials or through a compatible service like Twilio.
  3. Set up your keywords: In the "Edit Fields (Set)" node, map your common customer questions to the appropriate response messages.
  4. Customize responses: Update the response messages in the Switch node branches to match your business voice and information.
  5. Configure fallback: Set up where unrecognized questions should go—email notification, Slack channel, or help desk ticket.
  6. Test thoroughly: Send test messages from different numbers to ensure keyword detection works correctly and responses are timely.
  7. Activate the workflow: Turn on the workflow and monitor initial interactions to fine-tune any keyword matches or responses.
