What This Workflow Does

This automation solution eliminates the manual work of tracking financial transactions by automatically extracting payment details from your Gmail, categorizing them using AI, logging them in Notion, and sending you real-time Telegram alerts. It transforms your inbox into a powerful financial monitoring system without any spreadsheets or manual data entry.

The system specifically targets transaction confirmation emails from banks, payment processors, and vendors. It extracts key details like amount, date, payment method, and purpose, then structures this information in your Notion finance database. For unusual or large transactions, you'll receive instant Telegram notifications for quick review.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously scans your Gmail for transaction-related emails using smart filters to identify bank notifications, payment confirmations, and invoices while ignoring irrelevant messages.

2. AI-Powered Processing

GPT analyzes each email to extract structured transaction data, categorize the expense/income type, and flag any unusual patterns or potential duplicates based on your historical data.

3. Notion Integration

Extracted transactions are automatically logged in your Notion finance database with proper categorization, tags, and links back to the original email for verification.

4. Telegram Alerts

You receive instant Telegram notifications for important transactions, with options to approve, reject, or flag for review directly from the alert message.
