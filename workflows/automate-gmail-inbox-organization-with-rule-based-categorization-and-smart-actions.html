What This Workflow Does

Email overload is a universal productivity killer. Professionals waste hours weekly manually sorting through messages, often missing critical communications buried in the noise. This n8n workflow solves that by automating your entire Gmail organization process.

The system continuously monitors your inbox, applying intelligent rules to categorize emails into logical groups like Work Correspondence, Purchase Receipts, Newsletters, and more. Beyond simple filtering, it can trigger smart actions like forwarding urgent messages, creating tasks from client requests, or compiling weekly digests from newsletters.

How It Works

1. Continuous Inbox Monitoring

The workflow checks your Gmail inbox at regular intervals (configurable from 5 minutes to hourly). It processes only new, unread messages to avoid duplicate actions.

2. Multi-Layer Categorization

Each email passes through a decision tree analyzing sender domains, subject line keywords, content patterns, and attachment types. Messages are tagged with appropriate categories based on your predefined rules.

3. Smart Action Execution

Based on categorization, the system performs actions like moving to folders, starring important messages, sending auto-replies, or creating tasks in connected apps. Critical alerts can bypass normal processing for immediate attention.
