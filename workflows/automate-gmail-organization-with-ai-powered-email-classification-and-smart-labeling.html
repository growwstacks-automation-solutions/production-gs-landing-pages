What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered n8n workflow automatically organizes your Gmail inbox by intelligently categorizing incoming emails and applying appropriate labels. It eliminates the manual work of sorting through hundreds of messages while ensuring important communications don't get buried in the noise.

The system analyzes email content, sender information, and contextual clues to determine the most relevant category for each message. It can distinguish between customer inquiries, internal communications, newsletters, receipts, and other common email types - then file them accordingly with color-coded labels.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your Gmail account. It captures the full message content including subject, body, sender details, and attachments.

2. AI Classification

An AI service analyzes the email content to determine its category. The system considers keywords, sentiment, sender reputation, and your past labeling behavior to make accurate predictions.

3. Label Application

Based on the AI's classification, the workflow applies the appropriate Gmail label and can optionally move the message to designated folders. You define the categories and corresponding labels during setup.

4. Priority Handling

For emails classified as urgent or high-priority, the workflow can trigger additional actions like notifications, task creation, or forwarding to specific team members.
