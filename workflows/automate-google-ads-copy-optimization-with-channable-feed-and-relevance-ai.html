What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of continuously testing and optimizing Google Ads copy for ecommerce businesses. By connecting your Channable product feed with Relevance AI's analysis capabilities, the workflow automatically generates data-driven ad variations that outperform generic copy.

The system analyzes historical performance data to identify which product attributes, emotional triggers, and messaging frameworks resonate most with your target audiences. It then generates dozens of optimized variations for ongoing A/B testing, freeing your marketing team to focus on strategy rather than manual copywriting.

How It Works

1. Product Data Extraction

The workflow pulls your complete product catalog from Channable, including titles, descriptions, categories, and attributes. This structured data serves as the foundation for AI analysis.

2. Performance Data Analysis

Relevance AI evaluates your historical Google Ads metrics (CTR, conversion rates, quality scores) to identify patterns in what converts best for different product categories and audiences.

3. AI Copy Generation

Using the product attributes and performance insights, the AI generates multiple ad copy variations for each product, testing different combinations of features, benefits, and emotional triggers.

4. Automated Deployment

The top-performing variations are automatically pushed to your Google Ads account through the API, while underperforming ads are paused or optimized.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run monthly for continuous optimization. The AI improves its recommendations as it gathers more performance data over time.
