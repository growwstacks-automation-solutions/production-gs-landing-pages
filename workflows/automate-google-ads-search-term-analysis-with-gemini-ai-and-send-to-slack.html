What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and time-consuming task of manually analyzing Google Ads search term reports. Instead of spending hours each week sifting through thousands of search queries, this workflow automatically processes your data using Gemini AI to extract meaningful insights and delivers them directly to your team in Slack.

The system identifies high-performing search terms to capitalize on, flags irrelevant queries wasting your budget, groups related terms for better campaign structuring, and surfaces emerging trends in customer search behavior. It transforms raw data into actionable recommendations that help optimize your PPC campaigns more effectively.

How It Works

1. Fetch Google Ads search term data

The workflow starts by connecting to your Google Ads account via API to retrieve the latest search term report. It pulls data including search queries, impressions, clicks, costs, and conversions for your specified date range and campaigns.

2. Process data with Gemini AI

Gemini AI analyzes the search terms to categorize them by intent (informational, commercial, navigational), identify irrelevant queries, group similar terms, and highlight performance patterns. The AI evaluates context beyond just keywords to understand true search intent.

3. Generate actionable insights

The system compiles the AI analysis into clear recommendations: negative keywords to add, high-value terms to bid up, new keyword opportunities, and campaign structure improvements. It prioritizes findings by potential impact on your ROI.

4. Deliver to Slack

Final insights are formatted into an easy-to-digest Slack message with visual highlights of key findings. The message includes direct links to make the recommended changes in Google Ads, enabling quick action by your team.
