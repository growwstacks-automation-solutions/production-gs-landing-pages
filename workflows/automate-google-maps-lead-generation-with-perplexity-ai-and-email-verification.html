What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually finding and qualifying business leads from Google Maps. Traditional prospecting requires hours of searching, copying data, and verifying contact information - tasks that are perfect for automation.

The workflow automatically discovers businesses matching your criteria on Google Maps, extracts their details, enriches the data with Perplexity AI insights, and verifies email addresses before saving everything to Google Sheets. This creates a ready-to-use lead list with validated contact information, saving sales teams countless hours of manual research.

How It Works

Step 1: Google Maps business search

The workflow starts by searching Google Maps for businesses based on your specified location, industry keywords, and other filters. It extracts key details like business name, address, phone number, and website.

Step 2: Data enrichment with Perplexity AI

Each business profile gets analyzed by Perplexity AI to extract additional insights. The AI identifies key services, customer review trends, and other valuable information to help personalize your outreach.

Step 3: Email discovery and verification

The system attempts to find contact emails through website scraping or pattern matching. All discovered emails then go through a verification process to confirm they're active and deliverable.

Step 4: Organized lead storage

Verified leads with all enriched data get saved to Google Sheets in a structured format. The sheet includes columns for original data, AI insights, verification status, and timestamps.
