What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your manual candidate screening process into an efficient, error-free system. It connects your Google Sheets applicant tracker with communication tools (Gmail), project management (ClickUp), and scheduling (Calendly) to create a seamless hiring workflow.

The system automatically evaluates applicants against your criteria, moves qualified candidates to the next stage, schedules interviews, and keeps all stakeholders informed—all without manual data entry or repetitive administrative tasks.

How It Works

1. Candidate data collection

The workflow begins by pulling applicant information from your Google Sheets tracker, where all applications are initially recorded. It checks for new entries at regular intervals.

2. Qualification screening

Using your predefined criteria (education, experience, skills), the system evaluates each candidate and assigns a qualification score. Only candidates meeting your threshold proceed to the next stage.

3. ClickUp task creation

For qualified candidates, the workflow creates a task in ClickUp with all relevant details, moving them into your interview pipeline. Hiring managers get automatically notified.

4. Interview scheduling

The system sends qualified candidates a personalized email with a Calendly link to book interviews, eliminating scheduling back-and-forth emails.
