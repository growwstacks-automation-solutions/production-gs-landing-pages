Automate HR celebrations with Google Gemini, Sheets & Chat for team milestones

Automated HR assistant that scans for employee milestones and posts AI-generated celebration messages to Google Chat

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how companies recognize employee achievements by automatically detecting milestones in HR systems and generating personalized celebration messages. It solves the common problem of inconsistent or forgotten recognition, which can negatively impact employee morale and retention.

The workflow connects Google Sheets (containing employee data and milestones) with Gemini AI to craft thoughtful messages, then posts them to Google Chat channels where teams collaborate. This creates visible, timely recognition without manual HR intervention.

How It Works

1. Milestone Detection

The workflow regularly scans your Google Sheets HR database for upcoming anniversaries, promotions, certifications, or other configured milestones.

2. AI Message Generation

For each detected milestone, Gemini AI creates a personalized celebration message referencing the employee's specific achievement and contributions.

3. Team Notification

The generated message automatically posts to designated Google Chat spaces, ensuring public recognition where colleagues can join the celebration.

Pro tip: Configure different message templates for various milestone types to maintain appropriate tone and formality.
