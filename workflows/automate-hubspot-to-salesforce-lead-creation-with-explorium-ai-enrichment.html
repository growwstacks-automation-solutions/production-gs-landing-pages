What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical sales efficiency problem: incomplete prospect data. When new contacts enter your HubSpot CRM, they often lack essential information like verified email addresses, direct phone numbers, company details, and professional background. Sales teams then waste valuable time manually researching each prospect before they can begin meaningful outreach.

The workflow automatically bridges this data gap by enriching HubSpot contacts with AI-powered intelligence from Explorium, then creating fully-formed, sales-ready leads in Salesforce. It transforms raw prospect information into actionable intelligence without manual intervention, ensuring your sales team always has complete information for personalized, effective outreach.

By automating this entire process, businesses can reduce lead research time by 70-80%, increase lead qualification accuracy, and accelerate sales cycles. The automation works continuously in the background, ensuring every prospect that enters your system receives immediate enrichment and seamless transfer to your sales team's primary platform.

How It Works

The workflow follows a sophisticated multi-step process that maximizes data enrichment while maintaining reliability and data quality.

1. Real-Time HubSpot Trigger

The automation begins when a new contact is created or updated in HubSpot. The workflow instantly detects this event and captures all available prospect information, including name, email, company, and any custom fields you've configured.

2. Initial Prospect Matching

The contact data is sent to Explorium's AI matching engine, which compares the information against billions of data points to identify the correct individual and company profile. This step ensures you're enriching the right person with accurate, verified information.

3. Bulk Data Enrichment

Once matched, the workflow performs parallel enrichment across multiple data dimensions. It gathers professional email addresses, direct phone numbers, company information (size, industry, funding), technology stack, social profiles, and professional background—all in real-time.

4. Data Transformation & Preparation

The enriched data is processed, formatted, and mapped to Salesforce field requirements. This includes handling data type conversions, flattening nested structures, and applying any business logic or validation rules specific to your sales process.

5. Salesforce Lead Creation

The final step creates a new lead record in Salesforce with all enriched information properly categorized and ready for sales engagement. The lead includes source tracking, enrichment timestamps, and complete contact intelligence for immediate outreach.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation running typically takes 30-60 minutes with the right access credentials.

  1. Download & Import: Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance using the workflow import function.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up three credentials in n8n: HubSpot (OAuth2 or App Token), Salesforce (OAuth2), and Explorium (Header Auth with your API key).
  3. Map Data Fields: Review the field mappings between systems and adjust any custom fields specific to your Salesforce lead layout.
  4. Test with Sample Data: Activate the workflow and test with a few existing HubSpot contacts to verify enrichment quality and Salesforce creation.
  5. Go Live: Once testing is successful, the workflow will automatically process new contacts in real-time.
