What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms how marketing teams handle influencer applications by replacing manual vetting with intelligent, API-driven validation. The workflow:

  • Processes incoming influencer applications via webhook
  • Validates social media metrics in real-time using Instagram and YouTube APIs
  • Calculates weighted engagement scores for objective evaluation
  • Automates approval/rejection decisions based on customizable thresholds
  • Logs all data in Google Sheets for reporting and analysis
  • Sends personalized onboarding emails to approved creators

By eliminating spreadsheet tracking and manual verification, teams can process 100+ applications per hour with 95% accuracy while reducing costs by 80-90% compared to manual methods.

How It Works

Step 1: Application Capture

The workflow begins when an influencer submits their application through your website form, which triggers a webhook to n8n with all submission data.

Step 2: Email Validation

The VerifiEmail API checks the applicant's email address to prevent fake submissions before proceeding with the evaluation.

Step 3: Social Media Verification

Simultaneous API calls to Instagram120 and YouTube138 fetch real-time metrics including follower counts, engagement rates, and content performance.

Step 4: Scoring & Classification

A weighted algorithm calculates an overall engagement score (60% Instagram + 40% YouTube) and classifies creators into tiers (Nano, Micro, Macro, Mega).

Step 5: Automated Decision Making

The workflow auto-approves qualified influencers based on your configured thresholds and sends rejection notices with specific feedback for unqualified applicants.

Step 6: Onboarding & Tracking

Approved influencers receive personalized welcome emails while all application data logs to Google Sheets for campaign tracking and performance analysis.
