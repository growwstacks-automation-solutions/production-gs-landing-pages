What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of matching incoming payments to outstanding invoices. Traditional manual reconciliation often leads to mismatches from bank fees, partial payments, or data entry mistakes. The workflow combines Mistral AI's pattern recognition with GPT-4's contextual understanding to achieve 98%+ matching accuracy.

By automatically syncing your accounting system with bank feeds, the system identifies paid invoices, flags discrepancies, and generates reconciliation reports. This eliminates hours of manual work while providing real-time visibility into cash flow. The AI continuously improves its matching logic based on your historical reconciliation patterns.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls open invoices from your accounting system while simultaneously importing bank transactions through secure API connections. All data is normalized into a consistent format for analysis.

2. AI Matching Process

Mistral AI analyzes transaction patterns while GPT-4 interprets payment references and memos. The system considers amount matches, date proximity, payment references, and historical patterns to determine probable matches.

3. Discrepancy Handling

When amounts don't match exactly, the AI identifies potential reasons (bank fees, partial payments, currency conversions) and suggests appropriate accounting adjustments. All exceptions are flagged for human review.

4. Reporting & Sync

Reconciled transactions are logged with match confidence scores. The system updates your accounting software and generates daily reconciliation reports with unmatched items highlighted.

Pro tip: For best results, train the AI with 3 months of historical reconciliation data to learn your specific payment patterns and common exceptions.
