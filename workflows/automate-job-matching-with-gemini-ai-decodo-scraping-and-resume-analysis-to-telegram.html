What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually searching job boards and matching qualifications. It combines Decodo's powerful job scraping with Gemini AI's contextual analysis to deliver highly relevant job opportunities directly to Telegram.

The system continuously monitors multiple job boards, analyzes new postings against your resume or candidate profiles using AI, and sends personalized matches with compatibility scores. This eliminates hours of manual searching while improving match quality by understanding context beyond keywords.

How It Works

1. Job Board Scraping

Decodo extracts structured job data from configured boards, filtering out duplicates and expired listings. It captures rich metadata including salary ranges, required skills, and company details.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

Gemini AI processes both the job descriptions and your resume/CV, evaluating multiple compatibility factors including skills alignment, experience relevance, and cultural fit indicators.

3. Smart Matching

The system calculates weighted match scores across different categories, prioritizing opportunities where your profile strongly aligns with the role's requirements.

4. Telegram Delivery

Formatted notifications arrive in Telegram with key details: match percentage, salary comparison, skills gaps, and direct application links. Users can interact with the bot to refine preferences.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits both job seekers and recruiters. Active candidates save 10+ hours weekly by automating their search. Recruiting agencies can process hundreds of applicants faster while improving placement quality. HR teams use it for internal mobility programs matching employees with open roles.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Decodo API credentials with your target job boards
  3. Set up Gemini AI API access in Google Cloud
  4. Create a Telegram bot via BotFather and insert your token
  5. Upload your resume or candidate profiles to the designated storage
  6. Adjust matching thresholds and notification preferences
