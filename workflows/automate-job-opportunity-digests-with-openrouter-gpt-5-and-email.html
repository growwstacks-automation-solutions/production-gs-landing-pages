What This Workflow Does

Finding relevant freelance, contract, or full-time opportunities in specialized communities like the n8n forum requires constant manual checking. You might spend hours each week scanning posts, only to miss time-sensitive opportunities or overlook projects that match your specific skills.

This intelligent automation solves that problem by continuously monitoring the n8n community job board, extracting new postings, analyzing them with AI to understand project requirements, and delivering a curated email digest straight to your inbox. It transforms reactive job hunting into proactive opportunity discovery.

The workflow doesn't just collect links—it provides context. Each opportunity includes an AI-generated summary highlighting key requirements, estimated scope, and client needs, allowing you to quickly assess fit without reading lengthy forum threads.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical four-step process to transform raw forum data into actionable intelligence.

1. Scrapes Recent Job Postings

The workflow begins by scraping the n8n community job board for postings from the last 7 days. It extracts job titles, descriptions, posting dates, client information, and direct links to each opportunity. This ensures you're seeing fresh, relevant opportunities without historical clutter.

2. Extracts and Structures Metadata

Each scraped posting is processed to isolate key information: technologies mentioned, budget indicators, project duration hints, and required experience level. This structured data forms the foundation for intelligent filtering and analysis in subsequent steps.

3. Analyzes with OpenRouter AI

Here's where the magic happens. Each job posting is sent to OpenRouter AI (using GPT-3.5-turbo, Claude, or your preferred model) with a custom prompt that asks for a concise summary of project requirements, client needs, and key takeaways. The AI identifies what matters most, saving you from parsing lengthy descriptions.

4. Delivers Formatted Email Digest

All analyzed opportunities are compiled into a professionally formatted HTML email. The digest organizes opportunities by recency, includes AI summaries, direct links, and key metadata. You receive a single, actionable email instead of scattered notifications.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your job monitoring automation running in under 10 minutes with these straightforward steps.

  1. Download and Import: Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import function.
  2. Configure OpenRouter Credentials: Add your OpenRouter API key in n8n's credentials manager. Select your preferred AI model (GPT-3.5-turbo recommended for starters).
  3. Set Up Email Sending: Configure your SMTP email credentials with sender and recipient addresses. Test the connection to ensure delivery works.
  4. Customize Date Range: Adjust the date filter node if you want to monitor opportunities from a different timeframe than the default 7 days.
  5. Test and Schedule: Execute the workflow once manually to verify everything works, then set up a schedule trigger (daily or weekly) for automated operation.
