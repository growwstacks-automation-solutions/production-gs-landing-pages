What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually posting job openings across multiple career sites and job boards. Instead of logging into each platform separately and copying/pasting job details, this workflow lets you manage all postings from a single Google Sheet.

The system automatically detects new job entries or updates in your spreadsheet, then uses BrowserAct to simulate human interaction with job board websites. This approach works even with platforms that don't offer API access, giving you comprehensive coverage across all your recruitment channels.

The n8n workflow showing connections between Google Sheets and multiple job boards

How It Works

1. Google Sheets as your control center

Your HR team or hiring managers add new job listings to a designated Google Sheet. The sheet contains all necessary fields like job title, description, location, salary range, and application instructions.

2. Automated triggering

The n8n workflow monitors your sheet for changes. When a new job is added or existing details are modified, the automation immediately begins processing the information.

3. BrowserAct posts to job boards

Using BrowserAct's browser automation capabilities, the workflow navigates to each job board's posting page, fills in the required fields with data from your sheet, and submits the listing - just like a human would.

4. Confirmation and logging

After successful posting, the workflow updates your Google Sheet with confirmation timestamps and direct links to each posted listing. This creates a complete audit trail of all your recruitment marketing activity.

Pro tip: Create separate tabs in your Google Sheet for different job types (full-time, contract, internship) to trigger different posting strategies.
