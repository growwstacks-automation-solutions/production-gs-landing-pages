What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of creating job postings by connecting form submissions directly to professional PDF generation. When HR teams or hiring managers complete a standardized job requisition form, the system automatically converts their responses into a branded PDF document with proper sections, formatting, and company branding.

The workflow then stores the final posting in your designated cloud storage folder, ready for distribution to job boards or your careers page. This ensures consistency across all postings while saving 30-45 minutes per listing that would normally be spent copying information between systems and formatting documents.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your job requisition form. The form captures all necessary details including job title, department, location, description, requirements, and compensation range.

2. Data Validation

The system checks for complete information and standardizes formatting (like consistent date formats or salary ranges) before proceeding. Missing or invalid data triggers an automated request for clarification.

3. PDF Generation

Using your company's branded template, the workflow generates a professional PDF with all sections properly formatted. The template ensures compliance with equal opportunity employment standards and includes your logo, colors, and standard disclaimers.

4. Cloud Storage

The final PDF is automatically saved to your designated Dropbox or Google Drive folder with a standardized naming convention. The system can also notify relevant team members that the posting is ready for review or distribution.
