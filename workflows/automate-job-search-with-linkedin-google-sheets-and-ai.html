What This Workflow Does

Job searching is time-consuming and inefficient when done manually. Most applicants waste hours scrolling through irrelevant listings, then more hours crafting generic applications that get ignored. The secret to landing interviews at top companies like Google, Apple, and Amazon is personalized, timely applications that directly address what each hiring team needs.

This automation transforms your job search from a reactive chore into a proactive system. It continuously monitors LinkedIn and other job boards for new postings that match your skills, automatically scores each role against your resume, generates a tailored cover letter using AI, and organizes everything in a Google Sheets tracker. Instead of "I'll apply later," you get a daily shortlist of high-signal opportunities with ready-to-send application materials.

Pro tip: Personalization increases callback rates by 3-5x. Recruiters immediately notice when your application speaks directly to their job description rather than sending the same generic resume everywhere.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a scheduled basis (daily or weekly) to ensure you never miss relevant opportunities while avoiding application burnout.

Step 1: Job Discovery & Collection

The system searches LinkedIn and configured job boards using your predefined criteria (job titles, locations, companies, keywords). It extracts complete job details including description, requirements, company information, and application links.

Step 2: AI-Powered Fit Scoring

Each job posting is analyzed against your resume using AI. The system scores the match based on skills alignment, experience requirements, and cultural fit, ranking opportunities from "perfect match" to "maybe later."

Step 3: Automated Cover Letter Drafting

For high-scoring roles, AI generates a personalized cover letter that references specific requirements from the job description and connects them to relevant experiences from your resume, creating immediate alignment.

Step 4: Centralized Tracking & Notification

All processed jobs—with their scores, cover letters, and metadata—are logged in Google Sheets. You receive a notification with the top 3-5 opportunities worth applying to immediately.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. LinkedIn account or access to job board APIs you want to monitor
  3. Google Sheets document for tracking applications and results
  4. AI service access (OpenAI, Anthropic, or similar) for scoring and content generation
  5. Your resume in text format for the system to reference during matching
  6. Notification channel (email, Slack, Telegram) to receive daily opportunity alerts

Quick Setup Guide

Get your automated job search system running in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure job sources by adding your LinkedIn credentials or job board API keys
  3. Set your search criteria including job titles, locations, companies, and keywords
  4. Connect Google Sheets and specify the spreadsheet where applications should be logged
  5. Add AI credentials and paste your resume text into the designated node
  6. Set the schedule (recommended: daily at 9 AM) and test with a manual trigger
  7. Review first results in your Google Sheet and adjust scoring thresholds as needed

Pro tip: Start with broad search criteria, then refine based on the quality of matches you receive. The system learns what constitutes a "good fit" based on your application decisions over time.
