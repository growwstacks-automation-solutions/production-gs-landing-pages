What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire lead enrichment process, transforming basic contact information into valuable, actionable intelligence. When a new lead comes in (from a form, CRM, or other source), the system automatically scrapes publicly available data, analyzes it with GPT-4o AI, stores enriched information in Airtable, and notifies your team via Slack.

The automation eliminates hours of manual research while providing your sales team with deeper insights about each lead. It gathers company information, social profiles, news mentions, and other relevant data - then uses AI to summarize key points and suggest personalized outreach strategies.

How It Works

1. Lead Trigger

The workflow starts when a new lead is detected from your chosen source (form submission, CRM update, or other trigger). Basic contact information like name, email, and company name are captured.

2. Web Scraping & Data Collection

The system automatically searches for and scrapes relevant information about the lead from public sources including company websites, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, and news sites. This gathers data points like company size, funding, recent news, and social media profiles.

3. AI Analysis with GPT-4o

All collected data is processed by GPT-4o, which identifies key insights, summarizes company information, detects potential pain points, and suggests personalized outreach approaches based on the lead's digital footprint.

4. Airtable Storage

The enriched lead data is automatically organized and stored in your Airtable base, creating a comprehensive profile that combines raw data with AI-generated insights.

5. Slack Notification

Your sales team receives an instant Slack notification with the lead's key information and AI-generated summary, allowing for timely, informed follow-up.
