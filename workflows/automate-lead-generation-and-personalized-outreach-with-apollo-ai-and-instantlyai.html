What This Workflow Does

This is a full-stack, AI-powered Business Development Representative (BDR) engine that automates your entire outbound sales process from start to finish. It replaces the manual, time-consuming tasks of lead research, data entry, email writing, and campaign setup with a seamless, intelligent automation.

The workflow connects Apollo for lead sourcing, Airtable as a central command hub, multiple AI services for enrichment and personalization, and Instantly.ai for high-volume email sending. It's designed for founders, agencies, and sales teams who want to scale their outreach without scaling their headcount or costs.

By automating up to 80% of a BDR's manual work, this system lets your team focus on what matters: having quality conversations with highly qualified leads. It's not just another email blaster—it's a smart, cost-efficient engine that ensures every message sent is relevant and personalized.

How It Works

The automation follows a sophisticated, multi-step pipeline designed to maximize efficiency and conversion rates.

1. Lead Generation & Sourcing

The workflow automatically pulls targeted lead lists from Apollo based on your ideal customer profile (ICP) criteria—like industry, company size, and job title. These raw leads are instantly sent to your Airtable base, creating a single source of truth for your campaign.

2. Deep AI Enrichment

For each lead, the system performs a multi-point enrichment. It scrapes the company's website for a business summary and key details, finds the prospect's most recent LinkedIn post to understand their interests, and searches for recent company news. This data provides the raw material for hyper-personalization.

3. Intelligent Lead Qualification

A dedicated qualification node evaluates each lead against your custom criteria set in Airtable. This step ensures a perfect ICP match before any further processing, preventing wasted spend on unqualified leads and protecting your email sender reputation.

4. Optimized Email Verification

Only after a lead passes qualification is their email address verified for deliverability. This sequence saves significant credit costs with services like Emailable, as you only pay to verify emails that are actually worth contacting—making campaigns roughly 30% cheaper than typical market tools.

5. AI-Personalized Sequence Creation

Using the enriched data, AI models craft a sequence of three highly personalized emails. Each email's subject and body are uniquely generated, referencing the company's specifics, the lead's recent activity, or relevant news. An integrated "remake" option allows for manual tweaks, ensuring brand voice alignment.

6. Campaign Launch in Instantly.ai

The fully enriched, qualified, and personalized lead list is seamlessly pushed to Instantly.ai. The workflow automatically creates a new campaign, sets up the sending sequence, and launches it—turning hours of manual setup into a fully automated process.

Quick Setup Guide

Importing and configuring this workflow is straightforward. Follow these steps to get your automated BDR engine running.

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your apps by setting up credentials for Apollo, Airtable, Instantly.ai, and your chosen AI provider in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Configure your Airtable base with the necessary columns for lead data, qualification status, and campaign tracking as outlined in the workflow.
  4. Set your qualification criteria in the "If" node based on your ideal customer profile (industry, size, role, etc.).
  5. Customize the AI prompts in the Code or AI nodes to match your brand voice and value proposition.
  6. Test with a small batch of 5-10 leads to ensure everything flows correctly from Apollo to Instantly.ai.
  7. Launch your first full campaign and monitor the results in Airtable and Instantly.ai's analytics.
