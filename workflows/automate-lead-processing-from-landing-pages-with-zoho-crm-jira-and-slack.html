What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of manual lead processing where marketing teams waste hours copying data between systems and sales teams lose opportunities due to delayed follow-up. It creates a seamless pipeline from your landing pages to your CRM and task management systems.

When a visitor submits a form on your landing page, the workflow automatically validates the lead data, creates a record in Zoho CRM, generates a corresponding task in Jira for your sales team, and posts a notification in Slack - all within seconds. For incomplete submissions, it intelligently routes just the available information to Slack for manual follow-up.

How It Works

1. Webhook Trigger

The workflow starts when your landing page form sends lead data via webhook. Common form platforms like HubSpot, Unbounce, and WordPress can all be configured to trigger this.

2. Data Validation

Essential fields like company name and contact details are verified. The workflow branches based on whether all required information is present.

3. Zoho CRM Creation

For complete submissions, a new lead record is created in Zoho CRM with all provided details mapped to the appropriate fields.

4. Jira Task Generation

A corresponding task is created in Jira with the lead details pre-populated, assigned to the appropriate sales rep based on your rules.

5. Slack Notification

Whether the lead is complete or partial, your sales channel in Slack receives an alert with all available information and links to the new records.
