What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow template deploys two specialized GPT-4o-mini AI agents to automate critical business functions: lead qualification and customer support. The system processes incoming leads through intelligent screening while simultaneously handling common customer inquiries with contextual understanding.

The lead qualification agent evaluates web form submissions, chat interactions, and email inquiries to score and prioritize prospects. Meanwhile, the customer support agent provides instant responses to frequently asked questions, order status checks, and basic troubleshooting - freeing your team for higher-value interactions.

How It Works

1. Lead Qualification Process

The AI agent analyzes incoming leads against your ideal customer profile criteria. It evaluates factors like budget, timeline, use case fit, and engagement level to assign a qualification score (Hot/Warm/Cold). High-potential leads trigger immediate notifications to your sales team.

2. Customer Support Handling

The chatbot agent processes support requests through multiple channels (website chat, email, etc.). It uses your knowledge base to provide accurate responses, escalating only complex issues requiring human intervention. The system logs all interactions for quality assurance.

3. Data Synchronization

Both agents sync their activities with your CRM and support ticketing system. This maintains complete visibility into customer journeys while ensuring no leads or support requests fall through the cracks.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits businesses receiving regular inbound leads and customer service requests, particularly ecommerce stores, SaaS companies, and professional service providers. Teams with limited sales/support staff will see immediate time savings, while larger organizations can use it to improve response consistency across channels.
