What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your lead management process by combining AI qualification with personalized follow-up sequences. When a new lead comes in through any channel (website form, chat, email, etc.), the system instantly evaluates their potential using Gemini AI against your ideal customer profile.

Qualified leads are automatically routed to your sales team with a scheduled Zoom meeting, while others enter a targeted Mailchimp nurture sequence. The entire process happens in minutes rather than days, ensuring no lead goes cold while saving your team 10+ hours per week on manual qualification tasks.

How It Works

1. Lead Intake & Initial Processing

The workflow triggers when a new lead is captured in HubSpot. Basic contact information and lead source data are collected, then passed to Gemini AI for initial analysis.

2. AI-Powered Qualification

Gemini evaluates the lead against your predefined criteria (budget, authority, need, timeline) by analyzing their responses, website behavior, and firmographic data. It assigns a qualification score and recommended next steps.

3. Automated Routing

Leads scoring above your threshold are marked as "Sales Qualified" in HubSpot and trigger a Zoom meeting invitation. Others are tagged for nurturing and added to the appropriate Mailchimp sequence.

4. Follow-up Execution

The system automatically sends personalized follow-ups based on qualification status - either scheduling a sales call or delivering educational content to move the lead further down the funnel.

Pro tip: Configure your Gemini AI model with specific examples of your ideal customers to improve qualification accuracy over time.
