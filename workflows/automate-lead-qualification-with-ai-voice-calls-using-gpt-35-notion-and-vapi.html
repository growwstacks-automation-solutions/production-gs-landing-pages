What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your website leads into qualified sales opportunities without manual intervention. When a visitor submits a contact form or shows interest, an AI-powered voice agent calls them immediately to qualify the lead, answer basic questions, and schedule a demo or meeting directly in your calendar.

The system uses Vapi's conversational AI to conduct natural-sounding phone calls, GPT-3.5 to handle complex questions intelligently, and Notion to track all lead interactions. It eliminates the need for sales reps to make cold calls or spend hours scheduling meetings, while ensuring no lead falls through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Lead capture from website

When a visitor submits a contact form or interacts with your website, their information is automatically captured and passed to the workflow. This triggers the AI calling sequence.

2. AI voice call initiation

Vapi's voice AI immediately calls the lead using a natural-sounding voice agent. The system uses your custom script and GPT-3.5 to handle the conversation intelligently.

3. Lead qualification conversation

The AI agent asks qualifying questions, provides basic information about your product/service, and determines if the lead is a good fit based on your criteria.

4. Meeting scheduling

For qualified leads, the AI agent offers available time slots and books the meeting directly into your calendar. The lead receives confirmation via email or text.

5. CRM update

All call details, qualification scores, and next steps are automatically recorded in Notion or your CRM for follow-up by your sales team.
