What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of slow, inconsistent lead follow-up that costs businesses valuable opportunities. When a potential customer submits your contact form, they often wait hours or days for a response—if they get one at all. This workflow eliminates that delay by creating an intelligent pipeline that captures, processes, and responds to leads within minutes while maintaining human quality control.

The system combines AI efficiency with human judgment: GPT analyzes the lead's message and generates a personalized follow-up email, then sends that draft to your sales team for approval. Once approved, the email sends automatically to the lead, and all details—including the draft, approval status, and timestamps—log directly to Airtable for complete visibility and reporting.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture & Trigger

The workflow activates when someone submits your n8n web form. It immediately captures all lead details: name, email, phone, company, and their specific message. This trigger happens in real-time, ensuring no lead sits unattended.

2. AI Email Draft Generation

OpenAI's GPT analyzes the lead's message and context, then generates a professional, personalized follow-up email. The AI references specific points from the inquiry, tailors the tone to match your brand voice, and creates content that feels genuinely human-written.

3. Human Approval Process

The draft email routes to your designated sales team member via email with "Approve" and "Reject" links. This critical step maintains quality control—your team can review, edit if necessary, or reject drafts that need revision before any communication reaches the prospect.

4. Automated Dispatch & CRM Logging

Upon approval, the workflow automatically sends the email to the lead via Gmail. Simultaneously, it creates a comprehensive record in Airtable including all lead details, the email draft, approval status, timestamps, and any notes. If rejected, it flags the record for manual follow-up.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Airtable account with a base and table configured for lead tracking
  3. OpenAI API key with GPT model access for email generation
  4. Gmail account or SMTP service for sending approved emails
  5. Web form (n8n's built-in form or your existing form with webhook capability)
  6. Approval email address where draft review requests will be sent

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your credentials in the workflow settings for Airtable, OpenAI, and Gmail.
  3. Configure your Airtable base with the required fields: Name, Email, Phone, Company, Message, Status, Email Draft, and Created On.
  4. Set your company details in the Workflow Configuration node—these populate the email signature.
  5. Specify the approval email address where draft review requests should be sent.
  6. Deploy the webhook by switching to Production URL and testing the Approve/Reject links.
  7. Publish your n8n form and submit a test lead to verify the entire pipeline works end-to-end.

Pro tip: Before going live, run 5-10 test submissions with different message types to refine your GPT prompts. Adjust the AI instructions to better match your brand voice and common inquiry types for optimal results.
