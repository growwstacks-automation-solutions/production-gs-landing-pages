What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of collecting business leads from websites and directories. Traditional lead generation requires hours of copying contact details, verifying information, and organizing data - tasks prone to human error and inconsistency.

The workflow automatically extracts leads using Scrapeless, cleans the collected data, and structures it in Google Sheets for immediate use. It handles validation, deduplication, and formatting so your sales team receives ready-to-use contact information without manual processing.

Scrapeless node setup for extracting website lead data

How It Works

1. Website Data Extraction

The Scrapeless node visits target websites and extracts specified data points like names, emails, phone numbers, and company details based on your configuration.

2. Initial Data Processing

Extracted data passes through filters that remove empty fields, detect duplicates, and flag potentially invalid entries for review.

3. Advanced Data Cleaning

The workflow standardizes phone number formats, validates email addresses, and normalizes company names using text processing functions.

4. Google Sheets Integration

Cleaned data automatically populates your specified Google Sheet with proper column headers and consistent formatting for immediate use.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to scrape multiple related websites simultaneously by duplicating the Scrapeless node with different URLs.
