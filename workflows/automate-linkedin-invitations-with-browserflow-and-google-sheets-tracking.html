What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates LinkedIn connection requests while maintaining human-like behavior patterns. It solves the challenge of scaling professional networking without triggering LinkedIn's anti-automation defenses or appearing spammy.

The system combines Browserflow for realistic browser interactions with Google Sheets for tracking responses. You can personalize invitations at scale, schedule sending at optimal times, and automatically log all connection activity.

How It Works

1. Data Preparation

The workflow pulls prospect data from your Google Sheet, including names, profile URLs, and personalized message templates. This ensures each invitation feels tailored rather than generic.

2. Browserflow Automation

Browserflow mimics human browsing behavior to visit LinkedIn profiles, click connect buttons, and send personalized messages. It introduces natural delays and randomizes actions to avoid detection.

3. Response Tracking

All sent invitations are logged back to Google Sheets with timestamps. Accepted connections trigger follow-up workflows while ignored requests can be flagged for manual follow-up.
