What This Workflow Does

Manual LinkedIn research and personalized email writing consume hours of valuable sales and recruitment time. This automated workflow solves that by creating a complete, hands-off pipeline that turns raw LinkedIn URLs into ready-to-send, highly personalized outreach emails.

The system automatically scrapes detailed profile information using Apify, enriches your lead database in Google Sheets, then uses Google Gemini AI to analyze career history and generate unique email content for each prospect. What used to take 15-20 minutes per lead now happens automatically in the background, allowing your team to focus on conversations instead of research.

Beyond saving time, this automation ensures consistency and depth in your outreach. Every email references specific career achievements, tenure, or transitions that demonstrate genuine research, dramatically increasing response rates compared to generic templates.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Lead Import

The workflow triggers on a schedule you set (daily, weekly, or real-time). It reads new leads from your Google Sheet where you've listed LinkedIn profile URLs. Only unprocessed leads move forward, preventing duplicate work.

2. LinkedIn Profile Enrichment

For each new lead, the Apify LinkedIn Scraper extracts comprehensive data: current position, company, "About" section, recent experience, skills, and education. This creates a rich profile beyond basic name and title.

3. Central Data Storage

All scraped career data is written back to your Google Sheet in a structured "Profile Data" column. This builds a searchable database of prospect information that improves over time and can be used for other automations.

4. AI-Personalized Email Generation

The complete profile is sent to Google Gemini via LangChain with a sophisticated prompt that acts as an expert B2B copywriter. The AI analyzes career patterns to identify unique hooks—like promotions, long tenure, or industry shifts—and crafts compelling opening lines.

5. Ready-to-Send Output

The AI generates both a personalized subject line and email body, which are saved back to your sheet. Your sales team now has campaign-ready emails that reference specific career details, making each message feel individually researched.

Pro tip: Add a "Status" column to your sheet to track which leads have been contacted and their response status. You can extend this workflow to automatically update this based on email opens/replies.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the download button above
  2. Create your lead sheet in Google Sheets with the required columns mentioned above
  3. Connect your Google Sheets account in all three Sheet nodes (update Document ID and Sheet Name)
  4. Add Apify credentials and ensure you have sufficient credits for scraping
  5. Set up Google Gemini in the LangChain node with your API key and preferred model
  6. Configure the schedule trigger based on your volume (start with daily to test)
  7. Add 5-10 test LinkedIn URLs to your sheet and activate the workflow
  8. Review the generated emails, tweak the AI prompt if needed, then scale up

Implementation note: Start with a small batch of 5-10 profiles to verify data quality and email tone before processing hundreds of leads. Adjust the AI prompt to match your brand voice after seeing initial results.
