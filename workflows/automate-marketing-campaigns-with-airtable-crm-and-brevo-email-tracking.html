What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your Airtable CRM and Brevo email marketing platform, creating a seamless marketing campaign management system. It eliminates manual data transfers between systems while providing real-time insights into campaign performance.

The workflow automatically segments contacts based on CRM data, triggers personalized email sequences, tracks recipient engagement, and updates customer records—all without manual intervention. This transforms static contact lists into dynamic, behavior-driven marketing campaigns.

How It Works

1. CRM Data Synchronization

The workflow continuously monitors your Airtable CRM for new or updated contacts. When changes are detected, it processes the data to determine appropriate marketing actions based on your predefined rules and segmentation logic.

2. Automated Email Triggering

Based on CRM data (like lead status, purchase history, or engagement level), the system triggers targeted email sequences through Brevo. Each email can be dynamically personalized using fields from the contact's CRM record.

3. Engagement Tracking

Brevo tracks all email opens, clicks, and other interactions. This engagement data flows back into Airtable, updating contact records with timestamped activity logs that inform future marketing decisions.

4. Lead Scoring & Follow-ups

The system automatically scores leads based on their engagement patterns and triggers follow-up actions—whether it's moving hot leads to sales or sending reminder emails to inactive contacts.
