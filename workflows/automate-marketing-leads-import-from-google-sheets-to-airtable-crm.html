What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring marketing leads from Google Sheets into your Airtable CRM. Marketing teams often collect leads through various channels (forms, events, campaigns) that initially land in spreadsheets, but sales teams need them in the CRM for proper tracking and follow-up.

The workflow automatically detects new lead entries in your designated Google Sheet and imports them into the correct Airtable base and table. It handles field mapping between spreadsheet columns and CRM fields, ensuring data consistency. You can configure it to run on a schedule or trigger instantly when new leads appear.

How It Works

1. Monitoring the Google Sheet

The workflow regularly checks your specified Google Sheet for new rows added since the last run. It identifies these new leads by tracking the most recent import timestamp.

2. Data Processing

Each new lead's data is extracted and formatted to match your Airtable CRM structure. The workflow can clean data (like standardizing phone numbers), merge fields, or apply conditional logic before import.

3. Airtable Record Creation

Processed leads are then created as new records in your Airtable CRM. The workflow checks for duplicates based on email or other unique identifiers before creating new entries.

4. Status Updates

After successful import, the workflow can update the Google Sheet with import status markers or trigger notifications to your sales team about new leads.
