Automate Marketing Revenue Attribution & ROI Analytics with Airtable and Slack

What This Workflow Does

Marketing teams often struggle to connect spend with actual revenue. They track leads, but not which leads convert into paying customers. This creates budget waste and missed growth opportunities.

This n8n automation solves that by linking closed deals in Airtable back to their original lead sources. It calculates true ROI—not just lead count—and delivers daily insights to Slack. You'll know exactly which channels drive revenue, which are underperforming, and where to allocate your next marketing dollar.

The workflow runs nightly, analyzing deals closed in the last 30 days. It matches each deal to its source, sums revenue, computes ROI and conversion rates, updates Airtable metrics, and sends a concise report to your team. This turns guesswork into data-driven decision-making.

How It Works

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates daily at midnight via n8n's Schedule Trigger. This ensures fresh data without manual intervention.

Step 2: Fetch Closed Won Deals

It queries your Airtable "Deals" table for records where Stage = "Closed Won" and Actual Close Date falls within the last 30 days. This isolates recent revenue-generating activity.

Step 3: Retrieve Lead Source Costs

The automation pulls cost-per-lead and total lead counts from your Airtable "Lead Sources" table. This provides the investment data needed for ROI calculation.

Step 4: Calculate ROI & Metrics (JavaScript)

A Code node processes each source: total revenue = sum of deals from that source; total cost = cost per lead × total leads; ROI = ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) × 100; conversion rate = deals closed / total leads × 100; average deal size = revenue / deal count.

Step 5: Update Lead Sources Table

The computed metrics are written back to Airtable, updating each lead source record with current ROI, conversion rate, and average deal size.

Step 6: Send Slack Report

A formatted message highlighting the top 3 performing sources is sent to a designated Slack channel, giving your team instant visibility.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtable base with "Deals" and "Lead Sources" tables (or equivalent structure).
  2. Slack workspace and a channel for receiving reports.
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with Airtable and Slack nodes configured.
  4. Basic understanding of your lead source costs and deal tracking process.
  5. Optional: Gmail node if you want to email reminders if the Slack report is missed.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure Airtable nodes with your base ID, table names, and API key.
  3. Set up Slack node with your webhook URL or bot token for the desired channel.
  4. Adjust the Schedule Trigger to your preferred time (e.g., 9 AM daily).
  5. Test the workflow with a few sample deals to verify calculations and output.
  6. Activate and let it run automatically—your first report will arrive the next day.
