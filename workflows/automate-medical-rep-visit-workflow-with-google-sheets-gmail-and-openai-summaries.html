What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the daily coordination headache for field-based teams like medical representatives, sales agents, and service engineers. Manual assignment distribution, reminder chasing, and report compilation consume hours of managerial time each week and lead to missed visits, inconsistent reporting, and delayed insights.

The workflow creates a complete closed-loop system: each morning it reads pending assignments from Google Sheets and emails personalized visit details to each representative. In the evening, it automatically sends reminders to those who haven't submitted updates. At night, it collects all form responses, uses AI to transform raw notes into structured summaries, and emails a consolidated report to management.

By connecting Google Sheets (your planning hub), Gmail (your communication channel), and OpenAI (your intelligence layer), this automation ensures nothing falls through the cracks while providing managers with clear, actionable insights without manual data processing.

How It Works

1. Morning Assignment Distribution

A scheduled trigger runs at your configured time (e.g., 8:00 AM). The workflow reads your Google Sheet containing the day's planned visits, filters for pending assignments, and sends a personalized email to each representative with their specific doctor/hospital details, objectives, and a link to submit their report.

2. Evening Reminder System

Another scheduled trigger activates in the late afternoon. It checks which representatives haven't submitted their visit reports and sends a polite, automated reminder email. The system updates the tracking sheet to indicate a reminder was sent, helping managers identify patterns of late reporting.

3. Nightly AI Summary Generation

The final scheduled process runs overnight. It collects all submitted Google Form responses, passes each through an AI agent that extracts key insights, normalizes data, and creates structured summaries. These are then compiled into a comprehensive daily report emailed to area managers and regional heads.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheet with columns for: Representative Name, Email, Visit Location, Scheduled Time, Status (Pending/Assigned/Completed)
  2. A Google Form for field representatives to submit visit reports
  3. Google Sheets API credentials (OAuth) configured in n8n
  4. Gmail API credentials (OAuth) for sending emails
  5. OpenAI API key for AI summarization (optional but recommended)
  6. Basic understanding of n8n interface for configuration

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets credentials to all relevant nodes
  3. Update the spreadsheet ID and sheet name in the Google Sheets nodes to point to your assignment sheet
  4. Connect your Gmail credentials and customize email templates with your branding
  5. Add your OpenAI API key if using AI summarization (otherwise disable those nodes)
  6. Adjust the three schedule triggers to match your team's working hours
  7. Test with a small group before full deployment

Pro tip: Start with just the morning assignment automation for the first week. Once your team is comfortable, add the reminder system in week two, and finally enable the AI summarization. This phased approach ensures smooth adoption.
