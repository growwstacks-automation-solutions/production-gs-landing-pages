What This Workflow Does

Sales teams waste valuable time manually researching leads before meetings. This automation solves that problem by automatically gathering intelligence about your prospects the moment they book a call. When someone schedules a meeting through Cal.com, this workflow instantly researches their LinkedIn profile using Bright Data, enriches their contact information, and generates a personalized meeting preparation document that's emailed to you and saved in your Airtable CRM.

The system transforms what was traditionally a 15-30 minute manual research task into an instantaneous, automated process. This means your sales team walks into every meeting fully prepared with relevant talking points, understanding of the prospect's background, and strategic insights—without spending any time on manual research.

How It Works

Step 1: Meeting Booking Trigger

When a prospect books a meeting through Cal.com, the workflow is triggered via webhook. The system captures all booking details including the prospect's name, email, company, and any custom fields (like LinkedIn URL) you've added to your booking form.

Step 2: Lead Enrichment & Research

If the prospect provided a LinkedIn URL, Bright Data automatically scrapes their profile for recent activity, job history, skills, and professional interests. This data is structured and prepared for analysis, giving you a comprehensive view of who you're meeting with.

Step 3: CRM Integration

The enriched lead data is automatically added to your Airtable CRM. The system creates or updates a contact record with all the gathered information, ensuring your sales team has a complete profile before the meeting even begins.

Step 4: Meeting Prep Generation

Using the enriched data, the workflow generates a concise meeting preparation document. This includes key talking points based on the prospect's background, potential pain points relevant to their industry, and conversation starters tailored to their interests.

Step 5: Delivery & Notification

The completed meeting prep is emailed directly to the sales representative and also attached to the CRM record. This ensures the right person has the information they need, exactly when they need it.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for sales teams, business development representatives, account executives, and consultants who conduct frequent prospect meetings. It's particularly valuable for B2B companies, agencies, and professional services firms where understanding a prospect's background before a meeting significantly impacts conversion rates. Teams using Cal.com for scheduling and Airtable for CRM will see immediate benefits, but the workflow can be adapted to other scheduling tools and CRM platforms.

What You'll Need

  1. Cal.com account with webhook access configured
  2. Airtable base with a contacts/leads table
  3. Bright Data API access for LinkedIn research (or alternative data enrichment service)
  4. Email service (Gmail, SendGrid, etc.) for sending meeting prep notes
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Pro tip: Add a LinkedIn URL field to your Cal.com booking form to maximize the enrichment potential. Make it optional but encourage prospects to share it for a more productive meeting.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure Cal.com webhook in your account settings to point to your n8n webhook URL
  3. Connect your Airtable credentials and map fields to match your base structure
  4. Set up Bright Data node with your API credentials and configure LinkedIn scraping parameters
  5. Customize the meeting prep template to match your sales team's preferred format
  6. Test the workflow by booking a test meeting and verifying the entire process works end-to-end
  7. Deploy and activate the workflow for ongoing automated meeting prep
