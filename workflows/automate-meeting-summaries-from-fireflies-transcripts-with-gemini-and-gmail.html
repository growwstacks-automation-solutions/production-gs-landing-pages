What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and time-consuming task of manually summarizing meeting notes. By connecting Fireflies.ai's meeting transcription with Google Gemini's AI capabilities, it automatically processes raw meeting transcripts into structured, actionable summaries delivered via Gmail.

The workflow eliminates hours of manual note-taking while improving documentation quality. It captures key decisions, action items, and discussion points that might otherwise be missed in traditional note-taking. The AI-generated summaries maintain consistent formatting and include all critical information attendees need for follow-up.

How It Works

1. Fireflies.ai captures meeting audio

The workflow begins when Fireflies.ai joins your scheduled meeting (via calendar integration) and records the conversation. Fireflies provides highly accurate transcripts with speaker identification and timestamps.

2. Transcript processing

Once the meeting ends, Fireflies makes the transcript available. The workflow automatically retrieves this transcript and prepares it for AI analysis by removing filler words and normalizing formatting.

3. Google Gemini analysis

The cleaned transcript is sent to Google Gemini, which identifies key discussion points, decisions made, and action items. Gemini structures this information into a professional summary with clear sections.

4. Email distribution

The polished summary is automatically sent via Gmail to all meeting participants and any other specified recipients. The email includes the summary as both formatted text and a downloadable attachment.
