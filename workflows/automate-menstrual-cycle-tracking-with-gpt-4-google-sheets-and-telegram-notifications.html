What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms menstrual cycle tracking from a manual process into an intelligent, personalized system. It combines Google Sheets for data storage, GPT-4 for AI-powered health insights, and Telegram for convenient notifications.

The workflow automatically analyzes cycle patterns, predicts upcoming periods with personalized recommendations, and delivers discreet notifications through Telegram. It eliminates the need for manual tracking while providing more accurate predictions and health insights than traditional calendar methods.

How It Works

1. Data Collection in Google Sheets

The system uses Google Sheets as a centralized database where users or healthcare providers can input menstrual cycle data. This includes start/end dates, flow intensity, symptoms, and other relevant health metrics.

2. AI Analysis with GPT-4

Periodically, the workflow sends the collected data to GPT-4 for analysis. The AI identifies patterns, predicts future cycles, and generates personalized health recommendations based on the individual's unique cycle characteristics.

3. Telegram Notification Delivery

The system sends automated Telegram messages with cycle predictions, symptom alerts, and health tips. Notifications can be scheduled based on the user's preferences - such as reminders before expected periods or ovulation windows.
