What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually searching for social media profiles across multiple platforms. When working with large contact lists in Google Sheets, researchers and campaigners often need to understand their contacts' social presence but face hours of repetitive lookup work.

The workflow automatically analyzes each row in your Google Sheet, using Gemini AI to intelligently search for matching profiles across LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and other platforms. It then enriches your spreadsheet with profile links, follower counts, and other public data - all without leaving your existing workflow.

How It Works

Step 1: Connect to your Google Sheet

The workflow begins by accessing your specified Google Sheet through n8n's Google Sheets integration. It reads the contact information you want to research (names, emails, or other identifiers).

Step 2: AI-powered profile search

Gemini AI processes each contact's information, using advanced algorithms to find probable matches across social platforms. It considers name variations, locations, and other contextual clues to improve accuracy.

Step 3: Results verification

The system evaluates potential matches based on multiple factors like profile completeness, activity patterns, and connection networks. Only high-confidence matches are returned to minimize false positives.

Step 4: Data enrichment

Verified social profiles are added back to your Google Sheet with direct links and relevant metadata. The workflow maintains your original data while appending new social insights in adjacent columns.
