What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your Notion CRM into a powerful onboarding engine that delivers personalized, timed communications through Gmail while keeping your team informed via Telegram alerts.

Instead of manually tracking signup dates and sending emails, this workflow runs daily to detect where each user is in their journey and triggers the appropriate action automatically - from welcome emails to trial expiration reminders.

How It Works

1. Daily Notion Database Scan

The workflow checks your Notion database every 24 hours to identify users at each onboarding stage based on their signup date.

2. Stage-Based Email Triggers

Five distinct email types are sent at strategic intervals (Day 0, 1, 3, 7 and trial expiry) with content tailored to where users are in their journey.

3. Telegram Team Alerts

For high-value actions like Day 7 follow-ups, the system simultaneously notifies your sales team via Telegram to take immediate action.

Pro tip: Use the configuration node to easily customize all email content and Telegram settings in one place.
