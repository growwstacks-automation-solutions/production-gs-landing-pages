What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your customer onboarding from a manual, time-consuming process into a seamless automated experience. When a new customer signs up or makes a purchase, the workflow automatically triggers personalized onboarding emails, schedules welcome calls in Google Calendar, and updates their contact record in HubSpot.

The AI-powered Gmail integration crafts context-aware email responses that feel personal while maintaining brand consistency. This eliminates the back-and-forth of scheduling while ensuring no new customer falls through the cracks during the critical first 30 days.

How It Works

1. New customer trigger

The workflow monitors your HubSpot CRM for new customer records or deal closures. When detected, it immediately initiates the onboarding sequence.

2. AI-powered welcome email

Using customer data from HubSpot, the system generates a personalized welcome email with relevant product information, next steps, and a call-to-action.

3. Calendar scheduling

The workflow checks your Google Calendar availability and sends the customer a scheduling link for their onboarding call. Once booked, it automatically adds the event to both calendars.

4. CRM updates

All interactions are logged in HubSpot, updating the customer's timeline and triggering any additional sequences based on their engagement.
