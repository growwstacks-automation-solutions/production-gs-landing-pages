What This Workflow Does

Manual news monitoring is inefficient, time-consuming, and often misses critical industry developments. This automation solves that problem by automatically scraping news articles from any website using AI-powered extraction and storing structured data in Google Sheets for analysis.

The workflow intelligently collects article titles, URLs, and categories, transforming unstructured web content into organized, searchable data. It runs on a schedule you define—daily, hourly, or weekly—ensuring you always have fresh intelligence without manual effort.

Whether you're tracking competitors, monitoring brand mentions, conducting market research, or curating content, this automation provides reliable, consistent data collection that scales with your needs.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates automatically based on your preferred schedule. You can set it to run during business hours, at specific intervals, or trigger it manually for ad-hoc research.

2. AI-Powered Scraping

Using ScrapegraphAI, the system intelligently navigates news websites and extracts structured data. The AI understands page layouts to accurately identify article elements, even on complex or JavaScript-heavy sites.

3. Data Processing & Formatting

Extracted data is cleaned, validated, and formatted for optimal spreadsheet compatibility. The system handles edge cases like missing fields, duplicate detection, and data normalization.

4. Automated Google Sheets Storage

Processed articles are appended to your Google Sheets with metadata including titles, URLs, categories, and timestamps. The spreadsheet becomes a living database of news intelligence.

Pro tip: Start with a single news source to test your setup, then gradually add additional websites. Implement rate limiting between requests to respect website servers and avoid being blocked.

Quick Setup Guide

Estimated time: 10-15 minutes

  1. Download the template using the button above and save the JSON file locally.
  2. Import into n8n by navigating to Workflows → Import from File in your n8n instance.
  3. Configure credentials for ScrapegraphAI and Google Sheets in the n8n credentials panel.
  4. Update the website URL in the ScrapegraphAI node to target your preferred news source.
  5. Test the workflow manually to verify connections and data formatting.
  6. Set your schedule in the Schedule Trigger node based on your monitoring needs.
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the initial runs to ensure proper data collection.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Google Sheet for this automation with pre-formatted columns. Consider adding filters, conditional formatting, or connecting to Data Studio for visualization dashboards.
