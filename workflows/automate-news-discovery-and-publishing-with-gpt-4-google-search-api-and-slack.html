What This Workflow Does

This automated system solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking industry news and sharing updates with your team. Marketing teams, PR agencies, and research departments often spend 10+ hours weekly scanning multiple sources, summarizing content, and distributing findings.

The workflow automatically discovers relevant news using Google Search API, processes articles through GPT-4 for intelligent summarization, then publishes polished updates to designated Slack channels. It maintains brand voice consistency while eliminating repetitive manual work.

How It Works

1. Automated News Discovery

The system queries Google Search API with your configured keywords and filters. It evaluates hundreds of sources in seconds, filtering out low-quality or irrelevant content based on your criteria.

2. AI-Powered Summarization

GPT-4 analyzes each article, extracting key points while maintaining original context. The AI identifies trends, highlights important statistics, and generates executive-style summaries tailored to your audience.

3. Smart Slack Distribution

Summarized content gets formatted with proper headlines, bullet points, and source links before being posted to your specified Slack channels. The system can tag relevant team members and include reaction buttons for feedback.
