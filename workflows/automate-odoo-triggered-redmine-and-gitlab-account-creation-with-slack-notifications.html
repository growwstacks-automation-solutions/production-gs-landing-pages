What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming and error-prone process of manually creating developer accounts during employee onboarding. When HR adds a new team member in Odoo, the workflow automatically provisions Redmine accounts for project management and GitLab accounts for version control, then notifies the IT team via Slack.

The system eliminates repetitive manual work while ensuring consistent account setups across your development tools. By integrating these critical systems, you reduce onboarding delays, maintain security standards, and provide developers with immediate access to necessary resources on their first day.

How It Works

1. Odoo triggers on new employee creation

The workflow monitors your Odoo HR system for new employee records. When triggered, it extracts relevant details like name, email, department, and job title to determine appropriate access levels.

2. Redmine account creation

Using the Redmine API, the system creates a new user account with project access based on the employee's department. Default permissions are applied according to your security policies, with options for custom role mapping.

3. GitLab account provisioning

The workflow then creates a GitLab account, assigning the developer to relevant groups and projects. SSH keys can be generated automatically or added later, depending on your security requirements.

4. Slack notifications

Finally, the system posts a detailed message to your designated Slack channel, informing IT staff about the new accounts. The notification includes usernames, temporary passwords (if applicable), and links to the newly created profiles.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to tag relevant IT team members in Slack notifications for faster response times when manual review is required.
