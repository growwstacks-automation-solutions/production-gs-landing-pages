What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation transforms your open house sign-in process from a manual, time-consuming task into a seamless lead management system. When visitors sign in via SignSnapHome at your open house, this workflow instantly captures their information, qualifies them based on their responses, and initiates personalized follow-up through Discord notifications, SMS via Twilio, and email—all within minutes of their visit.

The system automatically processes guest photos, converts them from base64 format, and displays them as thumbnails in team notifications. It intelligently scores leads as Hot, Warm, Medium, or Cold based on whether they have an agent, their interest rating, and buyer agreement status. This prioritization ensures your team focuses on the most promising prospects immediately while maintaining professional communication with all visitors.

Beyond basic data collection, this automation creates a complete lead management pipeline that eliminates manual data entry, reduces follow-up time from hours to seconds, and provides your entire team with real-time visibility into open house performance through rich Discord notifications that include all visitor details and custom form fields.

How It Works

1. Webhook Trigger & Data Processing

The workflow begins when a visitor signs in through SignSnapHome at your open house. The webhook trigger receives the POST data containing all form fields—name, contact information, photo, rating, and any custom questions you've added. The system immediately parses this data, separates standard from custom fields, and prepares it for intelligent routing.

2. Intelligent Lead Scoring & Categorization

Using JavaScript logic, the workflow evaluates each lead based on multiple factors: whether they already have a real estate agent, their provided interest rating (if enabled), and if they have a signed buyer agreement. Leads are automatically categorized into four priority levels with color-coded badges for instant visual recognition in team notifications.

3. Multi-Channel Notification & Follow-up

The system then executes parallel communication paths. First, it sends a rich Discord notification to your team channel with all visitor details, including their photo thumbnail. Simultaneously, it checks for a phone number and sends a personalized SMS via Twilio if available. If no phone is provided, it falls back to sending a professional HTML email. All messages are tailored based on the lead's category and include property-specific details.

Pro tip: Enable the optional rating field in your SignSnapHome form to automatically identify highly interested visitors (4-5 stars) as Hot leads for immediate personal follow-up.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this open house automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the workflow into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Create a Discord webhook in your team channel and update the HTTP Request node with your webhook URL
  3. Configure Twilio credentials in n8n if using SMS, and set your Twilio phone number in the SMS node
  4. Add SMTP credentials for email fallback if you want email capabilities
  5. Activate the workflow and copy the generated webhook URL from the Webhook node
  6. Configure SignSnapHome by adding the n8n webhook URL to your open house automation settings
  7. Test the system with a sample sign-in to ensure all notifications and follow-ups work correctly

Implementation note: The workflow automatically adapts to any custom fields you add to your SignSnapHome form—no modifications needed. Simply add new questions in SignSnapHome, and they'll appear in your Discord notifications automatically.
