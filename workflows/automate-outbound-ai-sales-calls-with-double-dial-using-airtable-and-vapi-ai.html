What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manual outbound sales calling. Instead of having your team waste hours dialing numbers and listening to ringtones, the system automatically pulls contacts from your Airtable database and initiates AI-powered calls through Vapi.

The double-dial feature intelligently redials unanswered numbers after a short interval, dramatically increasing your connection rates. When a prospect answers, the AI agent conducts the initial conversation using your predefined script, qualifies the lead, and logs all outcomes back to Airtable - complete with call recordings and transcripts.

How It Works

1. Contact List Preparation

The workflow begins by querying your Airtable base for contacts marked as ready for outreach. It filters out numbers on DNC lists and prioritizes contacts based on your criteria (like lead score or last contact date).

2. Initial Dial Attempt

Vapi AI places the first call attempt using your configured voice agent. The system waits for a set duration (typically 30-45 seconds) to determine if the call was answered or went to voicemail.

3. Double-Dial Logic

For unanswered calls, the workflow waits a predetermined interval (usually 2-5 minutes) before automatically redialing. This significantly increases answer rates by catching prospects who may have been temporarily unavailable.

4. AI Conversation Handling

When connected, the AI agent follows your conversation flow - introducing your offering, handling common objections, and either qualifying the lead or scheduling a callback. The system analyzes voice responses in real-time to guide the conversation.

5. Outcome Logging

After each call, the workflow updates Airtable with the call outcome, recording URL, transcript, and any collected information. It can trigger follow-up tasks like sending emails or assigning the lead to a sales rep.
