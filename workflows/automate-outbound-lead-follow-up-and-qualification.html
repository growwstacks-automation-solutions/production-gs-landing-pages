What This Workflow Does

Manual lead follow-up is time-consuming, inconsistent, and prone to human error. Sales reps spend hours crafting emails, tracking responses, and updating spreadsheets, while high-potential leads slip through the cracks.

This automation solves that by creating a hands-free lead outreach system. It pulls pending leads from Google Sheets, uses an AI agent to generate personalized email content, dispatches the emails via Gmail with unique tracking IDs, initiates follow-up sequences based on engagement, and updates your lead pipeline—all without manual intervention.

You get consistent, scalable outreach that works 24/7, ensuring every lead receives timely, relevant communication while your team focuses on closing deals.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a scheduled trigger, processing leads in batches to ensure efficiency and reliability.

Step 1: Lead Intake & Scheduling

A schedule trigger runs daily (or at your chosen interval) and fetches all leads marked as "Pending" from a specified Google Sheets tab. The workflow uses the "Get rows" node to retrieve only unprocessed records.

Step 2: AI-Powered Email Generation

For each lead, an AI Agent node crafts a personalized email subject and HTML body based on lead data (like company name, industry, or previous interactions). It uses an OpenAI chat model with a structured output parser to ensure consistent, brand-aligned messaging.

Step 3: Tracking & Dispatch

The system generates a unique tracking ID via an HTTP request to your tracking service (like a marketing automation platform). This ID is embedded into the email as a tracking pixel. The "Send a message" node then dispatches the personalized email through Gmail's API.

Step 4: Metadata Capture & Sequence Initiation

After sending, the workflow retrieves the email's Gmail metadata (messageId, threadId). It posts this data along with the tracking ID to your tracking API, activating a predefined follow-up sequence for that lead.

Step 5: Pipeline Update

Finally, the lead's status in Google Sheets is updated from "Pending" to "Done" (or "Contacted"), preventing duplicate outreach and giving your team real-time visibility into campaign progress.

Pro tip: Use the AI agent to tailor email tone based on lead source. For example, webinar leads might get a more educational tone, while demo request leads get a direct, benefit-focused approach.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities.
  2. Google Sheets API credentials with access to your lead spreadsheet.
  3. Gmail API access for sending emails from a dedicated sending domain.
  4. OpenAI API key (or alternative LLM provider) for the AI agent.
  5. A tracking service API (like your marketing automation platform) to generate tracking IDs and log engagement.
  6. A Google Sheets spreadsheet with columns for lead details (Name, Email, Status, etc.).

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets, Gmail, OpenAI, and your tracking API in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update the Google Sheets node with your spreadsheet ID and the specific sheet/tab name containing leads.
  4. Adjust the AI agent prompt in the "AI Agent" node to match your brand voice and email goals.
  5. Set the schedule trigger to your desired frequency (e.g., daily at 9 AM).
  6. Test with a single lead by setting the "Limit" to 1 in the "Loop Over Items" node, then activate the workflow.
  7. Monitor the first run in n8n's execution log and check your Gmail Sent folder to verify everything works.

Pro tip: Before going live, send test emails to yourself and team members to review formatting, personalization, and tracking pixel functionality.
