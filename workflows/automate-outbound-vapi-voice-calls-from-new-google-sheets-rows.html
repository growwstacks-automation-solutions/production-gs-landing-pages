What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually calling contacts added to spreadsheets by connecting Google Sheets directly to Vapi's AI calling system. Whenever new rows are added with phone numbers, the workflow automatically initiates personalized voice calls using your configured AI assistant.

Key capabilities include dynamically passing contact details from Sheets to calls, scheduling call timing, and logging results back to your spreadsheet. The system handles the entire outreach process without manual intervention while maintaining personalization through AI conversation.

How It Works

1. New Row Trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new entries. When detected, it extracts the contact details and prepares them for calling.

2. Data Processing

Contact information is validated and formatted for Vapi's API, ensuring proper phone number formatting and including any personalization fields.

3. Call Initiation

The workflow sends the contact details to Vapi which places the call using your configured AI assistant and phone number.

4. Result Logging

After the call completes, outcomes are recorded back to your spreadsheet including call status, duration, and any notes from the conversation.

  1. Import the template JSON into your n8n instance
