What This Workflow Does

This automation solution bridges the gap between your Go High Level CRM and voice communication by automatically initiating outbound calls when new sales opportunities are created. It eliminates manual dialing while ensuring immediate prospect engagement.

The workflow detects new opportunities in real-time, retrieves the associated contact details, and places a personalized call through Vapi's AI voice platform. This creates a seamless transition from digital lead capture to human-like voice interaction without sales team intervention.

How It Works

1. Opportunity Detection

The workflow triggers when Go High Level creates a new opportunity record, using webhooks to detect changes instantly without polling.

2. Contact Information Retrieval

It fetches the complete contact profile from GHL including phone number, name, and any custom fields that might be relevant for personalization.

3. Call Initiation

The system passes all relevant details to Vapi which places the outbound call using your configured assistant and phone number.
