What This Workflow Does

Manual lead response is slow, generic, and misses critical context. When a potential client fills out your contact form, you typically receive basic information but lack insight into their actual business needs. This creates delayed, templated responses that fail to engage.

This automation solves that by instantly transforming raw lead data into intelligent, personalized engagement. The moment someone submits your form, the workflow researches their company website, analyzes their business using AI, crafts a tailored response addressing their specific context, and logs everything in your CRM. What used to take hours of manual research now happens in seconds, 24/7.

The system maintains parallel tracking for appointment confirmations, ensuring your sales pipeline stays updated automatically. This isn't just an autoresponder—it's a complete lead intelligence system that gives you competitive advantage through speed and personalization.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture & Duplicate Check

When a new lead submits your contact form via webhook, the workflow immediately checks your Airtable CRM for existing records. This prevents duplicate outreach and ensures each lead receives appropriate follow-up based on their history.

2. Website Research & Analysis

The system visits the lead's provided website URL, scrapes publicly available content, and uses custom code to clean the HTML data. This extracts relevant information about their business, services, and target market.

3. AI-Powered Business Summary

OpenAI analyzes the cleaned website data to generate a concise, insightful summary of the lead's business. This includes their likely pain points, service offerings, and potential fit for your solutions.

4. Personalized Email Composition

Using the business summary and lead's stated goals, a second AI step drafts a highly personalized email. It references specific aspects of their business, asks relevant questions, and includes your contact details and value proposition.

5. CRM Logging & Tracking

All lead data, website summary, and email content are automatically logged in Airtable. A parallel flow monitors your inbox for booking confirmations and updates the CRM accordingly, keeping your pipeline current without manual entry.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtable account with a base and table configured for lead management
  2. OpenAI API access (GPT-4o recommended for highest quality output)
  3. Gmail account or Google Workspace for sending automated emails
  4. Website contact form that can send data via webhook (works with Typeform, Webflow, WordPress, etc.)
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Pro tip: Test the website scraping with several of your past leads' websites first. Some sites have complex structures or anti-scraping measures that might require adjustments to the code node for optimal data extraction.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Create an Airtable base with columns for: lead name, email, website, business summary, email sent status, and follow-up date
  3. Connect your Airtable, OpenAI, and Gmail credentials in n8n
  4. Update the webhook node URL in your website contact form settings
  5. Customize the OpenAI prompts with your specific email signature, phone number, and value propositions
  6. Run a test submission with a sample website to verify the scraping and email generation work correctly
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the first few automated emails for quality assurance
