What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually sending prescriptions to patients after consultations. Doctors typically write prescriptions by hand or print them, then staff must scan, email, or message them individually - a process that can take 5-10 minutes per prescription and often leads to delays or errors.

The workflow automatically detects new prescription entries in a Google Sheet, formats them professionally, and delivers them instantly via the patient's preferred channel (email or WhatsApp). It handles all the manual work behind the scenes while maintaining a complete audit trail in the spreadsheet for compliance purposes.

How It Works

1. Doctor enters prescription data

Medical staff input patient details, medication information, dosage instructions, and contact preferences into designated columns in a Google Sheet. The sheet serves as a simple interface that requires no technical training to use.

2. Automation triggers on new entries

The n8n workflow monitors the sheet for new rows or updates. When detected, it immediately processes the data while maintaining the original record for audit purposes.

3. System formats the prescription

The workflow structures the information into a professional prescription format with clinic branding, doctor details, and proper medication formatting that pharmacies can easily read.

4. Multi-channel delivery

Based on patient preferences marked in the sheet, the system sends the prescription either as an email attachment with instructions in the body, or as a WhatsApp message with the prescription PDF and key details in the text.

Pro tip: Add a column for "Preferred Pharmacy" to automatically CC the prescription to the patient's regular pharmacy, saving them an extra trip.
