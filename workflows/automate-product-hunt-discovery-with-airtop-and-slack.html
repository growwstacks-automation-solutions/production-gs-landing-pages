What This Workflow Does

Staying ahead of product trends requires constant monitoring of platforms like Product Hunt, which can consume valuable time. This automation solves that problem by continuously scanning Product Hunt for new launches and discussions that match your specific criteria, then delivering filtered, actionable updates directly to your Slack workspace.

The workflow combines Airtop's powerful monitoring capabilities with Slack's real-time collaboration features. It eliminates the need for manual checking while ensuring your team never misses important product announcements in your industry. For product managers, marketers, and founders, this means more time for strategic work rather than constant platform monitoring.

n8n workflow interface showing the Product Hunt to Slack automation steps

How It Works

1. Product Hunt Monitoring Setup

The workflow begins by connecting to Product Hunt's API through Airtop, which handles the authentication and data collection process. You configure specific filters like keywords, product categories, or minimum upvote thresholds to focus on relevant discoveries.

2. Data Processing and Filtering

Airtop processes the raw Product Hunt data, extracting key information like product names, descriptions, maker details, and engagement metrics. The workflow applies your custom filters to exclude irrelevant products and highlight the most promising discoveries.

3. Slack Notification Delivery

Filtered results are formatted into rich Slack messages with product images, direct links, and key metrics. Notifications are delivered to your specified Slack channel, where team members can immediately react, comment, or assign follow-up tasks.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated #product-discoveries Slack channel and use threads to organize discussions about each product. This keeps your main channels clean while preserving valuable insights.

This automation is ideal for product teams, startup founders, and investors who need to stay informed about industry trends. Marketing agencies will find it valuable for identifying partnership opportunities, while SaaS companies can use it to monitor competitors and complementary tools.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Airtop account with API access
  3. Product Hunt developer API credentials
  4. Slack workspace with webhook permissions
  5. Optional: Custom keywords or filters for your industry

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Airtop account in the first node
  3. Configure your Product Hunt search parameters (keywords, categories, etc.)
  4. Set up your Slack webhook URL in the final node
  5. Test the workflow with a manual trigger
  6. Schedule it to run automatically (recommended every 2-4 hours)
