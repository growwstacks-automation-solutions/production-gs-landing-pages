What This Workflow Does

Property maintenance management is notoriously time-consuming, with managers drowning in phone calls, lost requests, and vendor coordination headaches. This automation solves three critical pain points simultaneously: capturing tenant requests efficiently, intelligently routing them to appropriate vendors, and maintaining full visibility throughout the repair process.

The workflow begins when tenants submit maintenance requests through your Jotform. GPT-4o-mini analyzes each submission to categorize the issue (plumbing, electrical, HVAC etc.), assess urgency, and extract key details. The system then automatically creates a structured work order, emails the appropriate vendor with all necessary information, and initiates tracking - all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Tenant submits maintenance request

Tenants access a branded Jotform to report issues, upload photos/videos, and provide contact details. The form captures all essential information while ensuring consistent data structure for automation processing.

2. AI analyzes and categorizes request

GPT-4o-mini processes each submission to determine: issue category, priority level (emergency vs routine), required vendor type, and estimated repair complexity. This replaces hours of manual triage with instant, consistent classification.

3. System creates work order

The workflow generates a detailed work order with tenant information, problem description, AI assessment, and any media attachments. This becomes the single source of truth for the entire repair process.

4. Vendor notification and assignment

Based on the AI classification, the system emails the appropriate vendor with complete job details, expected response time, and SLA requirements. Vendors receive everything needed in one organized communication.

5. Status tracking and tenant updates

The automation monitors vendor response times, sends reminders if needed, and provides proactive updates to tenants. Upon completion, it requests tenant confirmation and closes the loop.
