What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually transferring customer and estimate data from sales tracking spreadsheets into QuickBooks. Sales teams often maintain prospect lists in Google Sheets, while accounting requires the same information in QuickBooks - leading to duplicate work and potential data discrepancies.

The workflow automatically syncs new customer records and quotes between systems, ensuring your accounting data stays current without manual intervention. Whenever your sales team adds a new prospect or project estimate to your Google Sheet, the system creates corresponding records in QuickBooks with all necessary details.

How It Works

1. Google Sheets Trigger

The workflow monitors your designated Google Sheet for new rows added. You can specify which columns contain customer details (name, email, phone) and estimate information (services, pricing, terms).

2. Data Validation

Before creating records in QuickBooks, the workflow validates that all required fields are populated and formatted correctly. Missing or invalid data triggers an email notification to your team for correction.

3. QuickBooks Customer Creation

Valid customer data gets pushed to QuickBooks Online as new customer records. The workflow maps your Google Sheet columns to the appropriate QuickBooks fields, including custom fields if needed.

4. Estimate Generation

For each valid estimate row, the workflow creates a professional QuickBooks estimate linked to the customer record. Line items, pricing, taxes, and terms are all transferred accurately.

5. Confirmation & Logging

The system logs all successful transfers and can optionally notify your team via email or Slack. Failed transactions are flagged for review with detailed error messages.
