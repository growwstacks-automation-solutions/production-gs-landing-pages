What This Workflow Does

Manual quote request processing creates bottlenecks that cost sales. Between form submissions, CRM updates, team notifications, and customer acknowledgments, valuable deals slip through cracks. This automation solves that by creating a seamless pipeline from initial contact to sales-ready opportunity.

The workflow triggers when a customer submits your Tally form. It immediately logs all submission details in Airtable as a new lead record, posts an alert in your designated Slack channel, and sends a professional acknowledgment email via Gmail. This eliminates manual copying between systems while ensuring no request goes unnoticed.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Capture

The workflow monitors your Tally form for new submissions. Each time a customer completes your quote request form, the workflow captures all field data including contact details, requirements, and any custom questions you've added.

2. CRM Record Creation

The submission data automatically creates a new record in your Airtable CRM base. The workflow maps form fields to appropriate Airtable columns, adds timestamps, and can apply initial tags or status markers (like "New Lead").

3. Team Notification

A formatted Slack message posts to your designated sales channel, tagging relevant team members. The notification includes key details pulled from the form and a direct link to the new Airtable record for quick access.

4. Customer Acknowledgment

The system immediately sends a personalized Gmail response confirming receipt of the request. This email can set expectations about next steps, include helpful resources, or provide preliminary information based on the submission.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to route different request types to specialized teams or apply custom response templates based on product interest.
