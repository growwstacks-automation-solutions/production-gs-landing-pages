What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually following up with real estate leads. The system initiates AI-powered voice calls to new leads, qualifies their interest level, then triggers personalized email campaigns based on their responses. Hot leads get immediate property details and scheduling options, while colder leads receive nurturing content.

By combining the personal touch of voice calls with scalable email automation, you maintain human-like engagement at scale. The workflow integrates with your existing tools to create a seamless lead management system that works 24/7, ensuring no prospect falls through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Lead Initiation

New leads from your website, Zillow, or other sources trigger the workflow. The system immediately initiates an AI call using natural-sounding voice technology to establish first contact.

2. AI Qualification

Llama AI analyzes the call responses to determine interest level, preferred property types, budget range, and timeline. This data categorizes leads as hot, warm, or cold for appropriate follow-up.

3. Multi-Channel Follow-Up

Based on qualification results, the system automatically sends tailored email sequences with property listings, neighborhood guides, or educational content. Hot leads receive immediate connection options to your team.
