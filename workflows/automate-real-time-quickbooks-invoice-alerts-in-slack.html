What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of financial visibility gaps in growing businesses. When invoices get paid or become overdue in QuickBooks, most teams either don't know immediately or waste time manually checking. This workflow bridges that gap by automatically pushing real-time invoice status updates to your Slack channels.

The system monitors your QuickBooks account for invoice changes and instantly notifies relevant team members in Slack. Whether it's a payment confirmation, overdue alert, or invoice modification, your entire team stays informed without needing to log into QuickBooks throughout the day.

How It Works

1. QuickBooks Invoice Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your QuickBooks account for new or modified invoices using the QuickBooks API. It identifies changes in payment status, due dates, amounts, or other critical fields that require team awareness.

2. Event Filtering

Only significant invoice events trigger notifications. The system filters out minor changes and focuses on important status updates like payments received, overdue notices, or large invoice modifications that impact cash flow.

3. Slack Notification Generation

For each qualifying event, the workflow generates a detailed Slack message containing customer name, invoice number, amount, due date, and payment status. The message includes a direct link back to the QuickBooks invoice for quick reference.

4. Channel Routing

Notifications can be routed to specific Slack channels based on criteria like invoice amount, customer, or payment status. High-value payments might go to a management channel, while overdue notices route to collections teams.
