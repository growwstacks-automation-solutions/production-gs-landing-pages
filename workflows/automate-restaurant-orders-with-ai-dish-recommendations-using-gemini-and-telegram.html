Process orders instantly, use Gemini AI to suggest personalized dishes, and send recommendations via Telegram—all automated.

What This Workflow Does

Manual restaurant order processing creates bottlenecks that slow down service and miss upselling opportunities. Staff juggle between taking orders, checking inventory, and communicating with customers—often leading to errors, delayed responses, and generic suggestions that don't increase order value.

This smart automation solves these problems by creating a seamless system that triggers when a new order arrives. It automatically extracts customer and dish details from Google Sheets, uses Gemini AI to analyze the order context and recommend complementary dishes or special offers, then sends personalized suggestions directly to the customer via Telegram. The entire process happens in seconds, ensuring timely, relevant engagement that enhances the dining experience.

How It Works

Step 1: New Order Trigger

The workflow activates when a new order is detected, whether from an online form, POS system webhook, or manual entry. This trigger captures all essential order details to begin processing.

Step 2: Extract & Format Order Data

Customer information and dish details are pulled from designated Google Sheets. The system organizes this data into structured formats, separating customer profiles from order specifics for efficient processing.

Step 3: Prepare Data for AI Analysis

Order details are cleaned and formatted to optimize AI comprehension. This includes standardizing dish names, quantities, and pricing information while maintaining context about customer preferences and order history.

Step 4: Generate AI Recommendations

Gemini AI analyzes the order context—considering dish combinations, customer history, seasonal ingredients, and kitchen capacity—to generate personalized suggestions. The AI can recommend complementary items, highlight specials, or suggest alternatives based on availability.

Step 5: Deliver Personalized Suggestions

Formatted recommendations are sent via Telegram directly to the customer. Messages include dish descriptions, pricing, and quick-response options, creating an interactive dining experience that feels personal rather than automated.

Pro tip: Configure your AI prompts to consider dietary restrictions and preferences stored in your customer database. This transforms generic suggestions into genuinely helpful recommendations that build loyalty.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Google Sheets with customer and order data in consistent columns
  3. Google Cloud API access for Gemini AI integration
  4. Telegram Bot Token created through BotFather for messaging
  5. Order trigger mechanism (webhook, form submission, or manual trigger)
  6. Basic understanding of your menu structure and customer data organization

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your restaurant:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n workspace
  2. Configure Google Sheets nodes with your spreadsheet IDs and OAuth credentials
  3. Set up Gemini AI with your Google Cloud API key and customize recommendation prompts
  4. Connect Telegram using your bot token and test message delivery
  5. Test with sample orders to ensure data flows correctly from Sheets to AI to Telegram
  6. Activate the workflow and connect it to your order intake system

Implementation note: Start with a small segment of your menu or customer base to refine the AI recommendations before scaling to full operations. This allows you to adjust prompts and messaging without overwhelming your system.
