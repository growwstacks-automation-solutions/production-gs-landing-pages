What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the tedious process of resume screening into an efficient, AI-powered pipeline. It solves the challenge of manually reviewing hundreds of applications by automatically processing, analyzing, and scoring each resume before integrating qualified candidates directly into your CRM.

The workflow captures resume submissions through JotForm, extracts text using Azure OCR, evaluates qualifications with GPT-4.1, and routes high-scoring candidates to Zoho CRM while providing feedback to others. This reduces screening time by 80% while improving candidate experience with timely responses.

Example of AI-powered resume analysis results showing skills extraction and scoring

How It Works

1. Resume Submission

Candidates submit resumes through your JotForm, which triggers the workflow automatically when new applications arrive.

2. Document Processing

PDF resumes are converted to images then analyzed by Azure OCR to extract all text content while preserving formatting.

3. AI Analysis

GPT-4.1 evaluates each resume against your criteria, scoring candidates and providing strengths/improvement suggestions.

4. CRM Integration

Top candidates (score >85) are automatically added to Zoho CRM with all extracted data, while others receive personalized feedback emails.

Pro tip: Customize the GPT prompt to emphasize specific skills or qualifications important for your open roles.
