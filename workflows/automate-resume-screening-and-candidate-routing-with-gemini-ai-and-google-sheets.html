What This Workflow Does

This workflow revolutionizes hiring by using Gemini AI to analyze resumes with human-like understanding, then automatically routes qualified candidates to the appropriate hiring managers or departments. It eliminates hours of manual resume screening while providing deeper insights than traditional applicant tracking systems.

The system evaluates candidates based on skills, experience, education, and cultural fit - not just keywords. It can identify transferable skills, career progression patterns, and potential red flags that basic screening tools miss. Results are logged in Google Sheets with clear scoring and categorization for easy review.

How It Works

1. Resume Submission

Candidates submit resumes through your existing application channels (email, forms, or ATS). The workflow monitors these sources for new submissions.

2. AI Analysis

Gemini AI processes each resume, extracting key qualifications, skills, experience duration, and education details. It evaluates how well the candidate matches your job requirements.

3. Scoring & Categorization

The AI assigns scores for technical skills, cultural fit, and overall match. Candidates are categorized by department, seniority level, or specific skill requirements.

4. Automated Routing

Based on predefined rules, qualified candidates are routed to appropriate hiring managers via email notifications or spreadsheet updates. Unqualified applicants receive polite rejection emails.

5. Reporting

All candidate data is logged in Google Sheets with analysis results, scores, and routing decisions for easy reference and reporting.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits HR teams, recruitment agencies, and hiring managers across all industries. It's particularly valuable for:
