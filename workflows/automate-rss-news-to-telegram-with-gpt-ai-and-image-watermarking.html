What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually curating and posting news content to Telegram channels. It monitors RSS feeds for new articles, enhances the content using AI to make it more engaging, adds branded watermarks to images, and publishes directly to your Telegram channel.

The workflow transforms raw news feeds into polished, on-brand content without manual intervention. It's ideal for news aggregators, content marketers, and businesses maintaining industry-specific Telegram channels who want to establish authority while saving significant time.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your specified RSS feeds for new content. You can configure multiple feeds and set custom polling intervals based on how frequently new content appears.

2. AI Content Enhancement

Each new article passes through GPT AI to improve readability. The AI summarizes lengthy content, adjusts tone for your audience, and extracts key takeaways while maintaining factual accuracy from the source.

3. Image Processing

Any images in the article automatically receive your branded watermark. The workflow maintains image quality while ensuring your logo or channel name appears consistently across all shared content.

4. Telegram Posting

The enhanced content publishes to your Telegram channel with proper formatting. You can customize message templates to include hashtags, channel links, or calls-to-action.
