What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of transforming RSS feed content into polished Medium articles with AI enhancement and human approval. It solves the challenge of consistently publishing high-quality content while maintaining editorial oversight.

The system pulls fresh content from your selected RSS feeds, uses Groq and Gemini AI to rewrite and enhance the articles, then routes them through a Slack approval system before automatically publishing to your Medium account. This creates a complete content pipeline that saves hours of manual work each week while ensuring quality control.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow begins by monitoring your specified RSS feeds at regular intervals. When new content is detected, it extracts the article text, metadata, and any relevant images.

2. AI Content Enhancement

Using Groq's fast inference and Gemini's advanced language capabilities, the system rewrites the content to match your brand voice, adds relevant context, and optimizes for Medium's audience. You can customize the AI instructions to match your specific requirements.

3. Slack Approval Workflow

The enhanced article draft is sent to a designated Slack channel where your team can review it. Approvers can make comments, request changes, or approve the article for publication with simple Slack reactions or commands.

4. Medium Publishing

Once approved, the workflow automatically formats the content for Medium, adds relevant tags and metadata, and publishes it to your specified Medium publication with the correct authorship and formatting.
