What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually converting RSS feed content into engaging social media posts across multiple platforms. By connecting Wallabag RSS feeds with AI content generation and GetLate scheduling, it creates a complete hands-off content pipeline.

The workflow automatically pulls new articles from from your configured RSS feeds, analyzes the content with AI to generate platform-optimized posts (considering character limits, hashtags, and formatting for each network), schedules them in GetLate, and tracks everything in Airtable, and posts at optimal times - all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Wallabag RSS feed for new content additions. When new articles are detected, they're immediately processed by the next steps.

2. AI Content Transformation

Each article passes through AI processing where the system generates multiple social media post variations optimized for different platforms (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, etc.), complete with appropriate hashtags and character limits.

3. Content Approval & Scheduling

The generated posts are stored in Airtable where you can optionally review them before they're automatically scheduled in GetLate for publication at optimal posting times across your connected social accounts.

4. Multi-Platform Distribution

GetLate handles the final delivery, posting each message to the appropriate platform at the scheduled time while tracking engagement metrics.
