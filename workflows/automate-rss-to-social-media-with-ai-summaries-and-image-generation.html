What This Workflow Does

Manual social media posting consumes valuable marketing time while inconsistent posting hurts engagement. This automation solves both problems by turning any RSS feed into scheduled, optimized social content.

The workflow monitors your chosen RSS feeds (blogs, news sites, industry publications), extracts new articles, generates concise AI summaries perfect for social platforms, creates relevant AI images, and posts directly to Facebook and Instagram. It logs everything in Google Sheets and sends Telegram notifications for complete visibility.

Instead of spending hours crafting posts, your social channels maintain consistent, high-quality content flow automatically. This is particularly valuable for content marketers, agencies, and businesses with regular blog output.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow starts by checking your configured RSS feeds at regular intervals. When new content appears, it extracts the full article text, title, and metadata for processing.

2. AI Content Optimization

Using OpenAI or similar AI services, the system analyzes lengthy articles and generates social-media-friendly summaries. These aren't just excerpts—they're rewritten to maximize engagement and click-through rates.

3. Visual Content Creation

Based on the article topic, AI generates relevant images using DALL-E or similar models. This creates unique visual content that performs 3-5x better than text-only posts on visual platforms like Instagram.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

The optimized content and images are published simultaneously to Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts via their Graph APIs, maintaining consistent messaging across platforms.

5. Logging & Notification

Every published post is recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps and performance data. A Telegram bot sends instant notifications so you're always informed without needing to check each platform.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. RSS feed URLs from your blog, news sources, or content platforms
  3. Facebook Developer Account with Page access tokens and Instagram Business Account connected
  4. AI API credentials (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini for text and image generation)
  5. Google Sheets for logging published content
  6. Optional: Telegram Bot token for notifications

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 high-quality RSS feeds rather than many low-quality sources. Quality input content leads to better AI summaries and higher engagement rates.

Quick Setup Guide

Import this template into your n8n instance and follow these steps:

  1. Import the workflow using the downloaded JSON file in your n8n editor
  2. Configure RSS Trigger by replacing the example feed URL with your target RSS sources
  3. Set up AI services by adding your OpenAI/Anthropic/Gemini credentials to the LLM nodes
  4. Connect social platforms by adding Facebook Graph API credentials and your Page/Instagram IDs
  5. Test with one article using the "Execute Workflow" function before enabling automation
  6. Schedule execution to run every few hours or daily based on your content volume

The workflow includes detailed sticky notes explaining each section. No credentials are stored in the template—you'll connect your own services during setup.
