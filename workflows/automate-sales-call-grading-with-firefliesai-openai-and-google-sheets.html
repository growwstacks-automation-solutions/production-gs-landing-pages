What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms raw sales call recordings into structured performance evaluations. It eliminates manual call reviews while providing consistent, data-driven feedback to your sales team. The workflow captures every customer interaction, analyzes conversation patterns, and generates actionable coaching insights.

By connecting Fireflies.ai's transcription with OpenAI's analysis capabilities, you'll automatically grade calls based on your specific sales methodology. Managers gain visibility into team strengths and coaching opportunities without spending hours listening to recordings.

Automated workflow analyzing sales call transcripts

How It Works

1. Call Capture

Fireflies.ai joins your sales calls (Zoom, Teams, etc.) and generates accurate transcripts with speaker identification. The AI notes key moments like objections, pricing discussions, and closing attempts.

2. AI Analysis

OpenAI processes the transcript using your predefined evaluation criteria. It scores elements like active listening, product knowledge, objection handling, and closing effectiveness on a consistent scale.

3. Results Compilation

The workflow organizes scores into structured data with timestamps for key moments. It calculates overall performance metrics and identifies patterns across multiple calls.

4. Dashboard Updates

All data flows into Google Sheets where pivot tables and charts automatically update. Managers see team performance at a glance while reps access personalized feedback.

Pro tip: Customize your evaluation criteria in the OpenAI prompt to match your sales playbook. Include examples of ideal responses for more accurate scoring.
