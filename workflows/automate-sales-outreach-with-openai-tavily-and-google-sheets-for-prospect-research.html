What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manual prospect research and outreach. Sales teams typically spend 60-80% of their time researching leads and crafting emails, leaving little bandwidth for actual selling conversations. The workflow automates the entire process from data collection to personalized outreach.

The system connects Google Sheets (your prospect list), Tavily (business intelligence search), and OpenAI (message generation) to create a seamless pipeline. It researches each company, identifies key decision-makers, analyzes relevant business context, and generates hyper-personalized outreach messages - all automatically.

How It Works

1. Prospect List Processing

The workflow begins by reading your Google Sheet containing prospect company names or domains. It validates each entry and prepares them for research.

2. Automated Company Research

Using Tavily's API, the system searches multiple business intelligence sources to gather key details about each company - including size, industry, recent news, and identified pain points.

3. Decision-Maker Identification

The workflow analyzes organizational structures to find the most relevant contacts for your offering, prioritizing those with matching job titles and decision-making authority.

4. AI-Powered Message Generation

OpenAI processes the research data to craft tailored outreach messages that reference specific company details, creating authentic personalization at scale.

5. Outreach Delivery

The completed messages are either saved back to your spreadsheet or sent directly via your preferred communication channel (email, LinkedIn, etc.).

Pro tip: For best results, provide the AI with 2-3 sample outreach messages you've sent manually. This helps it match your brand voice and communication style.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n account (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google Sheets with prospect list (company names/domains)

  4. OpenAI API key
  5. Email/LinkedIn account for sending outreach (optional)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the JSON template file
  2. Import into your n8n instance (Workflows → Import from File)
  3. Connect your Google Sheets account in the first node
  4. Add your Tavily and OpenAI API keys in the respective nodes
  5. Map your spreadsheet columns to the expected fields
  6. Test with 2-3 prospects to verify output quality
  7. Schedule the workflow or trigger manually as needed
