What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow completely automates the creation and publication of SEO-optimized blog content. It transforms simple prompts in a Google Sheet into fully-formed WordPress posts—complete with AI-generated articles, compelling titles, custom images, and search-engine-friendly meta tags.

For content marketers, SEO specialists, and business owners, this eliminates the most time-consuming parts of content production. Instead of spending hours researching, writing, and optimizing each post, you simply add a topic and keywords to your spreadsheet. The workflow handles everything else: generating human-like content that matches your brand voice, creating relevant images, and publishing directly to your WordPress site with proper SEO structure.

The system ensures consistency and quality while freeing up your team to focus on strategy and promotion rather than manual content creation. It's particularly valuable for businesses maintaining content calendars, agencies managing multiple client blogs, or anyone looking to scale their content output without compromising on SEO performance.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence that mimics what an experienced content creator would do manually, but at scale and with perfect consistency.

1. Trigger & Data Collection

The workflow begins by checking your Google Sheet for new content requests. Each row represents a blog post to create, containing the topic, target keywords, and any specific instructions. This sheet acts as your content calendar and briefing document.

2. AI Content Generation

Using DeepSeek or OpenRouter AI models, the system generates a complete article based on your specifications. It structures the content with proper headings, paragraphs, and formatting while naturally incorporating your target keywords. A separate AI call creates an engaging, click-worthy title.

3. WordPress Draft Creation

The generated content and title are sent to your WordPress site via its REST API. The workflow creates a draft post with the correct category, tags, and author assignment, ready for your final review before publishing.

4. Image Generation & Attachment

OpenAI's DALL-E or similar image AI creates a unique, relevant featured image based on the article topic. This image is automatically uploaded to your WordPress media library and set as the post's featured image—no manual downloading or uploading required.

5. SEO Optimization

Another AI analysis generates optimized meta titles and descriptions that balance keyword inclusion with click appeal. These are automatically added to the WordPress post's SEO fields (compatible with Yoast SEO, Rank Math, or similar plugins).

6. Tracking & Completion

The workflow updates your Google Sheet with the new post's URL, publication status, and generated metadata. This creates a complete audit trail and lets you track performance directly in your original planning document.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance using the download button above.
  2. Create your Google Sheet with columns for: Topic, Keywords, Target Word Count, and any specific instructions.
  3. Configure the Google Sheets node with your sheet ID and worksheet name in n8n.
  4. Set up WordPress credentials in n8n using an application password from your WordPress admin panel.
  5. Connect your AI services by adding API keys for DeepSeek/OpenRouter and OpenAI (for images).
  6. Test with a single row in your sheet to verify the complete flow from prompt to published draft.
  7. Schedule the workflow to run daily or weekly based on your content calendar needs.
