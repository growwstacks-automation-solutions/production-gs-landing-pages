What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming manual process of compiling SEO reports by automatically combining data from Google Search Console and Google Analytics 4 into organized Google Sheets reports. Marketing teams often spend hours each week copying and pasting data between platforms, leaving less time for actual optimization work.

The workflow extracts key SEO metrics like keyword rankings, impressions, click-through rates, and combines them with GA4 conversion data to show the full customer journey from search to conversion. This gives marketers a comprehensive view of what search terms are actually driving business results, not just traffic.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from Search Console

The workflow first connects to Google Search Console API to pull performance data for your selected properties. It gathers metrics for queries, pages, countries, and devices based on your configuration.

2. GA4 Metric Integration

Simultaneously, the system retrieves conversion data from GA4, matching URLs from Search Console with corresponding engagement and conversion metrics in your analytics.

3. Data Transformation

The workflow cleans and formats the raw data, calculating additional useful metrics like ranking changes week-over-week, conversion rates from search, and value-per-click estimates.

4. Google Sheets Population

Finally, the processed data gets written to designated Google Sheets in an organized structure ready for analysis. The template includes pre-built formulas for common SEO calculations.

Pro tip: Add a final step to automatically email the report to stakeholders or generate Data Studio visualizations for easier consumption.
