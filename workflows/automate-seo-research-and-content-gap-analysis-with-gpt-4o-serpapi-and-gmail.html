What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manual SEO research by combining real-time SERP data analysis with AI-powered content gap identification. It automatically analyzes competitor rankings, identifies content opportunities your site is missing, and delivers actionable insights directly to your inbox.

The workflow pulls live Google search results through SerpAPI, processes them with GPT-4o to identify patterns and gaps, then compiles the findings into an easy-to-digest report sent via Gmail. This eliminates hours of manual research while providing data-driven content strategy recommendations.

How It Works

1. Keyword Input and SERP Data Collection

The workflow starts with your target keywords list. SerpAPI fetches current ranking data for each keyword, including top-ranking URLs, featured snippets, and related questions.

2. AI-Powered SERP Analysis

GPT-4o analyzes the SERP data to identify common themes among top-ranking pages, content formats performing well, and gaps where your content could potentially rank.

3. Content Opportunity Scoring

The AI evaluates each opportunity based on competition level, search volume estimates, and alignment with your existing content to prioritize recommendations.

4. Report Generation and Delivery

The system compiles findings into a structured report highlighting specific content gaps and strategic recommendations, then emails it to your designated address.

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your SerpAPI account in the credentials section
  3. Add your OpenAI API key
  4. Configure the Gmail node with your sender credentials
  5. Add your target keywords to the input spreadsheet
  6. Test with 2-3 keywords before running full analysis

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run monthly to track content gap changes over time and identify emerging opportunities.
