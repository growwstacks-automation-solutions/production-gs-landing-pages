What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automatically identifies customers who have abandoned their Shopify carts and sends them personalized WhatsApp reminders with direct links to their abandoned products. By leveraging Rapiwa's WhatsApp Business API integration, you can recover lost sales with highly effective mobile messaging that gets 3-5x more engagement than traditional email reminders.

The system monitors your Shopify store for abandoned carts, waits for a configured period (typically 1-3 hours), then sends a friendly WhatsApp message that includes the customer's name, product images, prices, and direct links back to their cart. This removes friction from the recovery process and makes it easy for customers to complete their purchase.

How It Works

1. Shopify detects abandoned cart

The workflow starts when a customer adds items to their cart but leaves your Shopify store without completing the purchase. Shopify's webhooks trigger the automation when a cart meets your configured abandonment criteria (usually after 1 hour of inactivity).

2. Customer data is processed

The workflow retrieves the abandoned cart details including products, quantities, prices, and customer contact information. It formats this data into a structured message template and verifies the customer has opted in to WhatsApp communications.

3. Personalized WhatsApp message is sent

Using Rapiwa's WhatsApp Business API integration, the system sends a personalized message that includes product images, direct links back to each item, the total cart value, and optionally a limited-time discount offer to incentivize completion.

4. Follow-up logic handles responses

The workflow can be configured to send a follow-up reminder after 24 hours if the customer doesn't respond, or to track when they complete their purchase to avoid sending unnecessary reminders.
