What This Workflow Does

For e-commerce businesses, manually posting every new product to social media is time-consuming and error-prone. This automation solves that by instantly promoting new Shopify products across Instagram and Facebook the moment they're published.

The workflow listens for new products in your Shopify store, transforms product data into polished social media content using AI, posts to multiple platforms simultaneously, logs everything to Google Sheets for tracking, and sends confirmation messages to your team via Discord. It ensures consistent branding, saves hours per week, and eliminates the risk of forgetting to promote new inventory.

How It Works

1. Trigger on New Shopify Products

The workflow activates automatically whenever a new product is added to your Shopify store. It captures all product details including name, description, price, images, and URL.

2. AI-Powered Content Generation

Using GPT-4.1-Mini (or your preferred AI model), the system generates engaging captions and 10 relevant hashtags tailored to your product and brand voice. This replaces manual copywriting.

3. Multi-Platform Publishing

The workflow posts to both Instagram and Facebook simultaneously. For Instagram, it creates a media container, waits for processing, then publishes. For Facebook, it uploads the product image with the generated caption directly to your page.

4. Centralized Tracking & Notification

Every post is logged to Google Sheets with timestamps, product details, and platform responses. A summary notification is sent to your Discord channel, keeping your team informed in real-time.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompt in the workflow to match your brand's specific tone—whether professional, casual, humorous, or inspirational—for consistent messaging across all posts.

What You'll Need

  1. A Shopify store with products
  2. Facebook Business account with connected Instagram
  3. Facebook Graph API access tokens
  4. OpenAI API key (or alternative AI service)
  5. Google Sheets for logging
  6. Discord webhook URL for notifications
  7. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Shopify connection with your store credentials in the trigger node.
  3. Set up Facebook/Instagram API by adding your access tokens and page IDs in the configuration nodes.
  4. Connect AI services by adding your OpenAI API key to the LLM node.
  5. Link Google Sheets by authorizing access and specifying your tracking spreadsheet.
  6. Add Discord webhook URL for team notifications.
  7. Test with a product by creating a test item in Shopify and verifying the automated posting.

Pro tip: Start with a private test product in Shopify to verify the workflow works correctly before enabling it for your entire catalog.
