What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the problem of emotional, inconsistent, and time-consuming crypto trading. Manual traders often miss opportunities, make impulsive decisions, or lack systematic risk management. The SOL/USDT Multi-Timeframe AI Market Analyzer and Trader automates the entire process while keeping you in control.

It functions as your 24/7 trading assistant that analyzes Solana market data across multiple timeframes, uses AI to determine optimal trading positions, sends you Telegram messages for approval, and executes trades through your AFK Crypto wallet. The system manages risk with automatic stop-loss and take-profit orders, then notifies you of results.

By combining AI precision with human oversight, you get the speed and consistency of automation without sacrificing control over your capital. This is particularly valuable for active traders who want to participate in crypto markets without being glued to screens all day.

How It Works

1. Hourly Market Analysis Trigger

The workflow activates every hour to check current market conditions. This regular schedule ensures you never miss significant price movements while avoiding excessive trading frequency that can increase costs.

2. Multi-Timeframe Data Collection

It fetches SOL/USDT price data from Crypto Compare across three timeframes: 1-minute (for immediate trends), 5-minute (for short-term momentum), and 1-hour (for broader direction). This multi-perspective analysis provides stronger signal confirmation.

3. AI Market Analysis & Decision Making

Google Gemini AI processes the market data to determine sentiment (bullish/bearish/neutral), recommended position (LONG/SHORT/HOLD), optimal stop-loss and take-profit levels, and provides a confidence rating with reasoning. The AI considers patterns humans might miss.

4. Portfolio Balance Check & Risk Calculation

The system checks your AFK Crypto wallet balance via API and calculates position size based on a conservative 1% risk per trade. This automated risk management prevents overexposure and protects your capital.

5. Telegram Approval with Trade Details

You receive a Telegram message containing all AI insights, proposed trade details, and "Approve/Decline" buttons. This human-in-the-loop approach gives you final say before any capital is committed.

6. Automated Trade Execution

If approved, the workflow instantly executes the trade via AFK Crypto's swap API. The system handles all technical details including slippage tolerance and transaction confirmation.

7. Live Monitoring & Result Notification

Once a position is open, the workflow continuously monitors SOL price. When stop-loss or take-profit conditions are met, it automatically closes the position and sends a Telegram summary with P&L and updated balance.
