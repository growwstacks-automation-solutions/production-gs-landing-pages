Automate Systematic Literature Reviews with AI

Free n8n workflow that uses GPT-4, Gemini, and Qdrant to extract, evaluate, and categorize research papers from Google Drive with audit trails in Airtable.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Systematic literature reviews are essential for evidence-based research but notoriously time-consuming and prone to human error. This automation transforms that process by intelligently screening, evaluating, and categorizing research papers using AI. It downloads papers from your Google Drive, extracts their content, evaluates them against your specific inclusion/exclusion criteria using LLM agents (GPT-4 and Gemini), and routes them appropriately.

Papers that meet your criteria are sent to Qdrant vector stores with Gemini embeddings for semantic search, creating a searchable knowledge base. Excluded papers are moved to a separate folder for review. Every decision is logged to Airtable with PRISMA-compliant justifications, providing complete audit trails for research transparency and compliance.

How It Works

1. Paper Collection & Extraction

The workflow monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new research papers. When papers are added, it extracts text content using document processing nodes, preparing the full text for AI evaluation while preserving original formatting and metadata.

2. AI-Powered Evaluation

Extracted papers are sent to LLM agents (GPT-4 and Gemini) configured with your specific inclusion/exclusion criteria. The AI evaluates each paper against these criteria, determining relevance and providing detailed justifications for its decisions.

3. Intelligent Routing & Storage

Based on the AI evaluation, papers are routed accordingly. Included papers are processed with Gemini embeddings and stored in Qdrant vector databases for semantic search. Excluded papers are moved to a separate Google Drive folder for manual review if needed.

4. Audit Trail & Reporting

Every decision, along with the AI's justification, is logged to Airtable. This creates a PRISMA-compliant audit trail that documents the entire screening process, essential for research transparency, publication requirements, and compliance.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Drive credentials with access to your research paper folders
  2. Airtable base and table configured for decision logging
  3. OpenAI API key (for GPT-4 evaluation)
  4. Google Gemini API credentials (for embeddings and additional evaluation)
  5. Qdrant instance (supports up to 3 collections for different research categories)
  6. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)

Pro tip: Keep detailed descriptions of your inclusion/exclusion criteria in the sticky notes inside your workflow. This makes it easy to update your research parameters and ensures the AI agents have clear guidelines.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Drive, Airtable, OpenAI, and Google Gemini in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Set up your Qdrant instances and update the workflow with your collection names and connection details.
  4. Customize the AI prompts in the LLM nodes to match your specific research criteria.
  5. Test with sample papers to ensure the evaluation and routing work correctly before processing your full library.
  6. Schedule or trigger the workflow to run automatically when new papers are added to your Google Drive folder.
