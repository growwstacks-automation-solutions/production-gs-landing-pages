What This Workflow Does

Manual task deadline management is inefficient and error-prone, often leading to missed deadlines and last-minute scrambles. This workflow automates the entire reminder process by connecting your Google Sheets task list with Gmail to send timely notifications.

The system checks deadlines daily and automatically sends email reminders at three strategic intervals: Today (urgent), 3 days before (warning), and 7 days before (early notice). This gives team members ample warning while preventing deadline surprises.

How It Works

1. Google Sheets Integration

The workflow connects to your Google Sheet containing task details (name, description, assignee, deadline). It reads this data daily to identify upcoming deadlines.

2. Deadline Calculation

The system calculates which tasks are due today, in 3 days, or in 7 days. This categorization ensures appropriate timing for each reminder.

3. Personalized Email Generation

For each qualifying task, the workflow generates a personalized email reminder with all relevant details pulled from your spreadsheet. The email tone adjusts based on urgency.

4. Gmail Delivery

Reminders are sent through your Gmail account with professional formatting and clear subject lines indicating urgency level.
