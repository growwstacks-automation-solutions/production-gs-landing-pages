What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually uploading TikTok videos by connecting your Google Sheets content calendar with TikTok through Blotato. It automatically posts videos from Google Drive to TikTok according to your scheduled dates and includes all your prepared captions and hashtags.

The workflow eliminates the need to manually transfer files, copy-paste captions, or remember posting schedules. It ensures consistent content delivery even when you're busy with other tasks, helping maintain your TikTok presence without daily manual work.

How It Works

1. Content Planning in Google Sheets

Your team plans TikTok content in a shared Google Sheet, including video file names, captions, hashtags, and scheduled posting dates. The sheet serves as your centralized content command center.

2. Video Storage in Google Drive

All finalized TikTok videos are stored in a designated Google Drive folder. The automation will access these files when it's time to post, pulling the correct video based on your content calendar.

3. Automated Posting via Blotato

Blotato acts as the bridge between your Google Workspace and TikTok. It handles the secure transfer of videos and metadata, formatting everything correctly for TikTok's API while maintaining compliance.

4. Scheduled Execution

The workflow runs at your specified intervals, checking the Google Sheet for videos due to be posted. When it finds scheduled content, it automatically processes and publishes to TikTok.
