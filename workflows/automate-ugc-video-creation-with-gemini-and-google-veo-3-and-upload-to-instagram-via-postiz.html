What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating authentic-looking user-generated content (UGC) videos for social media marketing. Instead of hiring creators, scheduling shoots, and editing footage, this workflow uses AI to generate professional-quality videos in minutes and publishes them directly to your Instagram account.

The system transforms a simple product image into a complete Instagram post: Google Gemini analyzes the image and writes both a cinematic video prompt and engaging caption, Google Veo 3 generates the actual video, and Postiz handles the upload and scheduling. What traditionally takes days of coordination now happens automatically with consistent quality and brand alignment.

How It Works

1. Image Analysis & Creative Briefing

The workflow starts with your product image. Google Gemini acts as a creative director, analyzing visual elements and generating a detailed 8-second commercial scene description that captures the product's essence and desired emotional impact.

2. Parallel AI Task Execution

The creative prompt splits into two simultaneous AI processes. One stream generates the video using Google Veo 3's advanced video synthesis, while the other creates Instagram-optimized caption copy tailored to your target audience's preferences and platform best practices.

3. Asset Distribution & Publishing

The generated video saves to Google Drive for archival while simultaneously uploading to Postiz's social media management platform. The AI-written caption merges with video metadata, and the complete package publishes to your connected Instagram account on your preferred schedule.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for e-commerce brands, digital marketing agencies, social media managers, and content creators who need consistent, high-quality video content without the production overhead. It's particularly valuable for businesses selling physical products, subscription services, or digital offerings that benefit from visual demonstration and social proof.

Teams managing multiple product lines or frequent promotional campaigns will see the greatest time savings, as the system can generate variations for A/B testing different video styles, captions, and posting times with minimal additional effort.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Gemini API access with permissions for both text and video generation models
  2. Postiz account with connected Instagram Business profile
  3. Google Drive storage for video asset management
  4. Product images in accessible URLs or cloud storage
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with HTTP request capabilities

Pro tip: Start with your best-performing product images to establish baseline performance metrics before expanding to your full catalog. Track engagement rates between AI-generated and traditionally produced UGC to validate the approach with your audience.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Import the template JSON into your n8n workspace. 2. Configure Google Gemini credentials with access to both text (gemini-2.5-pro) and video (veo-3.0-generate-preview) models. 3. Replace the sample image URL with your first product image. 6. Test with a single execution before enabling scheduled automation.
