What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of producing authentic-looking user-generated content (UGC) videos at scale. Traditional UGC requires finding and managing real creators, which limits output and consistency. Our AI-powered workflow generates complete videos - from scriptwriting to final edits - while maintaining the imperfect authenticity that makes UGC effective.

Brands using this system can produce dozens of platform-optimized videos weekly without filming. The workflow combines Sora 2's realistic video generation with OpenAI's natural language capabilities and Gemini's format optimization - creating content that performs 3x better than stock videos while costing 80% less than human-created UGC.

How It Works

1. Content Brief Generation

The workflow starts by analyzing your product details, target audience, and campaign goals to generate video briefs. OpenAI processes this data to create varied content angles that match real UGC patterns.

2. AI Scriptwriting

GPT-4 transforms briefs into natural-sounding scripts using actual customer review data as reference. The AI mimics human speech patterns, including imperfections and platform-specific slang that boosts engagement.

3. Sora 2 Video Generation

Sora's advanced AI generates realistic video footage matching the script. The system creates authentic-looking "user" appearances, natural movements, and product demonstrations that avoid the uncanny valley effect.

Pro tip: Feed Sora 2 with your actual customer photos (with consent) to generate more authentic-looking "testimonials" that match your demographic.

4. Multi-Platform Optimization

Gemini AI reformats each video for different social platforms - adjusting aspect ratios, lengths, captions and CTAs. The same core content becomes vertical 9:16 for TikTok, square for Instagram, and landscape for YouTube.
