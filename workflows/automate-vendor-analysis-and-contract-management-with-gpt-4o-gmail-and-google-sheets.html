What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms how businesses manage vendor relationships by automating the collection, analysis, and reporting of vendor performance data. It eliminates manual spreadsheet updates and subjective contract reviews by leveraging AI-powered analysis through GPT-4o.

The workflow automatically gathers vendor pricing data from multiple sources, analyzes delivery reliability metrics, extracts key contract terms from emails and documents, and generates comprehensive vendor scorecards in Google Sheets. This gives procurement teams data-driven insights to make better sourcing decisions.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Data Collection

The workflow runs on a schedule to pull vendor pricing data from configured sources like supplier portals, invoices, and purchase orders. It standardizes this data into a consistent format for analysis.

2. Email Contract Processing

When new vendor contracts arrive via Gmail, the workflow extracts the attachments and uses GPT-4o to identify key terms like pricing structures, SLAs, termination clauses, and renewal dates.

3. Performance Analysis

The system compares actual delivery times against promised SLAs, calculates fulfillment accuracy rates, and flags any consistent performance issues across vendors.

4. Automated Reporting

All analyzed data gets compiled into easy-to-read vendor scorecards in Google Sheets, with color-coded performance indicators and AI-generated recommendations for improvement.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to alert your team when vendor performance drops below thresholds or when contracts are nearing renewal dates.
