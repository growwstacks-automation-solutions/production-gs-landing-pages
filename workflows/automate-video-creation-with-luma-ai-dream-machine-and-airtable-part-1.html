Automate video creation with Luma AI Dream Machine and Airtable (Part 1)

This n8n workflow automatically generates AI videos using Luma Dream Machine when new records are added to Airtable. Perfect for content teams needing to scale video production without manual work.

JSON · Zapier compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling video content production without proportional increases in time or costs. When teams need to create dozens or hundreds of videos—for social media, product demos, or training materials, manual creation becomes impractical.

The workflow connects Airtable (where video requests arequest system with Luma AI Dream Machine's video generation capabilities. New records in Airtable automatically trigger the creation of customized AI videos, with results saved back to your database."

How It Works

1. New record in Airtable

The workflow monitors a specific Airtable base for new records containing video parameters like script, duration, and style preferences.

2. AI Video Generation

Luma AI Dream Machine processes the request using the provided parameters, using the provided prompts to generate a video according to the specifications.

3. Automatic Delivery

The completed video URL is written back to the original Airtable record, and optional notifications can be sent to stakeholders.
