Automate Webinar Registration with Zoom & KlickTipp

Free n8n workflow to capture form submissions, enrich contacts with webinar details, and eliminate manual registration work.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual webinar registration is a time-consuming bottleneck that leads to errors, delayed confirmations, and missed opportunities. Every time someone fills out your KlickTipp landing page form, someone on your team has to copy their information, register them in Zoom, send the join link, and update your CRM. This process doesn't scale and creates a poor attendee experience.

This n8n workflow solves that problem by creating a complete automated pipeline from form submission to confirmed registration. When someone submits a KlickTipp form, the system instantly fetches their webinar choice, retrieves the correct Zoom webinar details, enriches their contact record with join links and session times, and applies segmentation tags. The entire process happens in seconds, ensuring attendees receive immediate confirmation while your team focuses on higher-value activities.

How It Works

1. KlickTipp Form Trigger

The workflow starts when a visitor submits a webinar registration form on your KlickTipp landing page. The outbound webhook sends the registrant's information including their name, email, and selected webinar topic (e.g., "Beginner" or "Expert" session).

2. Dynamic Routing Logic

A switch node examines the webinar selection and routes the registration to the appropriate path. This conditional logic allows you to support multiple webinar types from a single form, making your marketing campaigns more flexible and scalable.

3. Zoom API Integration

For each webinar path, the system connects to Zoom's API using OAuth2 authentication. It retrieves the recurring webinar details including the unique join URL, next occurrence date and time, duration, and host information. This ensures attendees always get the correct link for their chosen session.

4. Contact Enrichment & Segmentation

The workflow updates or creates the contact in KlickTipp, writing the Zoom join URL, session timestamp, registration ID, and duration into custom fields. It also applies topic-specific tags (like "Zoom webinar Beginner" or "Zoom webinar Expert") for immediate segmentation and follow-up campaigns.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with API access
  2. KlickTipp account with landing page forms and outbound webhooks configured
  3. Zoom Business/Enterprise account with API access and OAuth2 credentials
  4. Custom fields in KlickTipp for webinar join URL, start timestamp, registration ID, and duration
  5. Segmentation tags in KlickTipp for different webinar topics

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure the KlickTipp trigger node with your webhook URL from your landing page form settings.

3. Set up Zoom OAuth2 credentials with webinar:write:registrant scope and connect the Zoom nodes.

4. Update the webinar IDs in the switch node paths to match your actual Zoom webinar IDs.

5. Map the Zoom data fields to your KlickTipp custom fields in the update contact nodes.

6. Test with a real form submission and verify the contact is updated with all webinar details.

Pro tip: Create a separate KlickTipp automation campaign for each webinar tag to send targeted reminder sequences. For example, "Beginner" attendees might get basic preparation content, while "Expert" attendees receive advanced materials.
