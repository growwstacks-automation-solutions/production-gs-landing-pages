What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the tedious manual process of creating weekly Meta Ads performance reports. Marketing teams often spend hours each week extracting data, analyzing metrics, and formatting presentations - time that could be better spent optimizing campaigns.

The workflow automatically pulls your Meta Ads data, processes it through Claude AI for intelligent insights, formats the results using GoMarble MCP's visualization tools, and generates a polished Google Slides presentation ready for executive review - all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from Meta Ads

The workflow begins by connecting to your Meta Ads account through the API, pulling key performance metrics for your selected campaigns, ad sets, and ads. It gathers data on impressions, clicks, conversions, spend, and other critical KPIs.

2. AI-Powered Analysis with Claude

Raw data gets sent to Claude AI which identifies trends, highlights significant changes, and provides narrative explanations for performance fluctuations. Claude adds contextual insights that transform numbers into actionable business intelligence.

3. Visualization with GoMarble MCP

The analyzed data then flows into GoMarble MCP which automatically generates professional charts, graphs, and visual representations of your advertising performance. This step ensures data is presented in the most impactful way.

4. Slide Deck Creation in Google Slides

Finally, the workflow populates a Google Slides template with the visualized data and AI-generated insights, creating a complete presentation deck with consistent branding, proper formatting, and executive-friendly layouts.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run automatically every Monday morning so reports are ready before your weekly marketing meeting.
