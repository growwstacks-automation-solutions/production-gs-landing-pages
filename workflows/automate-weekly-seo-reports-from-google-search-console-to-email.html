What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of gathering SEO performance data from Google Search Console and delivering it directly to your email inbox on a weekly basis. It eliminates the manual work of logging into Search Console, exporting data, and compiling reports.

The workflow fetches key metrics including clicks, impressions, click-through rate (CTR), and average position for your tracked pages and queries. It formats this data into an easy-to-read report that gets sent automatically to your designated email address every week.

How It Works

1. Connect to Google Search Console

The workflow authenticates with your Google Search Console account using OAuth to access your site's performance data.

2. Fetch SEO performance data

It retrieves the last 7 days of search performance metrics for your configured properties, including clicks, impressions, CTR, and average position by page and query.

3. Format the data into a report

The raw data gets transformed into a clean, readable format highlighting key trends and changes from the previous week.

4. Send via email

The formatted report gets delivered to your specified email address with a clear subject line indicating it's your weekly SEO performance update.
