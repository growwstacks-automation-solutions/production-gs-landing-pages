What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually sending welcome emails to new subscribers, which becomes time-consuming as your audience grows. The workflow automatically detects new Mailchimp subscribers, generates personalized discount codes, and sends professionally designed welcome emails through Gmail.

By combining Mailchimp's audience management with Gmail's delivery infrastructure, you maintain brand consistency while ensuring reliable email delivery. The discount codes serve as powerful conversion tools, turning interested subscribers into paying customers immediately after they join your list.

How It Works

1. New subscriber detection

The workflow constantly monitors your Mailchimp audience for new subscribers. When someone joins your list, their details are immediately captured by the automation.

2. Discount code generation

For each new subscriber, the system creates a unique discount code tied to their email address. This prevents code sharing while making each customer feel valued.

3. Email personalization

The workflow merges subscriber data with your email template, inserting their name, discount code, and any other relevant personalization fields.

4. Automated sending

Gmail delivers the perfectly timed welcome email immediately after subscription, when engagement is highest. Delivery logs are recorded for tracking purposes.
