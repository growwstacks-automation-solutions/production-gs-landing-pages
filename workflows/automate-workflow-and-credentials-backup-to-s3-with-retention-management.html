What This Workflow Does

This n8n template solves the critical business problem of protecting your automation investments by creating reliable, automated backups of workflows and credentials. Manual backups are often forgotten, leaving companies vulnerable to data loss that can disrupt critical business processes.

The workflow automatically exports all workflows and credentials from your n8n instance daily, stores them securely in S3-compatible storage, and manages retention according to your policies. This ensures you always have recoverable versions while optimizing storage costs through automated cleanup of older backups.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Backup Trigger

The workflow runs on a schedule you configure (default daily) to initiate the backup process. This ensures consistent protection without manual intervention.

2. Workflow Export

All workflows are exported from your n8n instance as JSON files, preserving the complete automation logic, nodes, and configurations.

3. Credential Backup

Sensitive credentials are securely exported with additional encryption, maintaining security while ensuring recoverability.

4. S3 Storage

Backups are transferred securely to your S3-compatible storage bucket with proper folder structure and naming conventions for organization.

5. Retention Management

The workflow automatically applies your retention rules, deleting older backups while preserving recent and periodic historical versions.
