What This Workflow Does

Responding to Requests for Proposals (RFPs) is a critical but painfully slow sales activity. Each RFP contains dozens of technical, financial, and compliance questions that require accurate, consistent answers drawn from across your organization. Manual response processes take days, involve multiple departments, and often result in missed deadlines or inconsistent messaging.

This n8n workflow automates the entire RFP response lifecycle. It accepts an incoming RFP document, uses AI to extract all questions, queries your pre-loaded company knowledge base via OpenAI Assistants, and generates a complete answer document ready for human review. What traditionally took 20-40 hours of manual work can now be accomplished in under an hour, giving your sales team a decisive competitive advantage.

The system maintains your brand voice, ensures answer consistency across proposals, and allows your team to focus on strategic review rather than manual compilation. It's particularly valuable for businesses responding to multiple RFPs monthly, where speed and accuracy directly impact win rates.

How It Works

1. RFP Submission & Document Processing

The workflow begins when a buyer's RFP document is submitted via webhook, email, or file upload. The system accepts PDFs, Word documents, or text files. An initial AI agent scans the document, identifies all questions (including those embedded in paragraphs or tables), and extracts them into a structured list.

2. Question Analysis & Categorization

Each extracted question is analyzed for intent and categorized (technical, financial, compliance, etc.). This categorization helps route questions to the most relevant sections of your knowledge base and ensures appropriate answer tone and detail level.

3. Knowledge Base Query via OpenAI Assistant

The workflow loops through each question, posing it to your pre-configured OpenAI Assistant. This assistant has been loaded with your company's marketing materials, technical specifications, case studies, compliance documents, and past successful proposals—essentially your entire institutional knowledge.

4. Answer Generation & Validation

The AI generates accurate, context-aware answers based on your knowledge base. The system can be configured to validate answers against specific criteria (word count, required inclusions, compliance references) before accepting them into the final response.

5. Document Assembly & Delivery

All validated answers are compiled into a professionally formatted response document (Word, PDF, or Google Doc). The completed document is then delivered to your sales team for final review, with optional notifications sent via Slack, email, or your CRM.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this RFP automation in your business:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure OpenAI credentials in the AI nodes and set up your Assistant with your knowledge files.
  3. Set up your trigger (webhook for automated submission or manual file upload).
  4. Test with a sample RFP to ensure question extraction and answer generation work correctly.
  5. Connect output nodes to your preferred document format and notification channels.
  6. Run a pilot with your next real RFP, keeping human review in the loop initially.
  7. Refine and scale based on feedback, adding more knowledge sources and customizing answer templates.
