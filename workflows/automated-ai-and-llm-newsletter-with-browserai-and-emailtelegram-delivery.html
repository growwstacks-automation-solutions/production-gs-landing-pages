What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the expensive and time-consuming problem of creating and distributing newsletters. Instead of paying for premium newsletter services or manually compiling content, this automation leverages BrowserAI to gather, curate, and format content automatically.

The system generates personalized newsletters by analyzing subscriber preferences and interests, then delivers them via email or Telegram. This eliminates manual work while providing tailored content that keeps subscribers engaged.

How It Works

1. Content Collection

BrowserAI automatically scrapes and collects relevant content from specified sources based on predefined topics or keywords. The AI filters out irrelevant information and identifies the most valuable pieces.

2. Content Curation

The system uses LLM capabilities to summarize articles, extract key points, and rewrite content in a consistent voice. It can personalize content based on subscriber segmentation data.

3. Newsletter Assembly

The workflow compiles the curated content into a professionally formatted newsletter template, complete with images, headings, and proper spacing for optimal readability.

4. Delivery

The completed newsletter is automatically distributed to subscribers through their preferred channel - either email or Telegram. The system tracks opens and engagement for future optimization.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. BrowserAI account with API access
  3. Email service provider (like SendGrid or Mailchimp) or Telegram bot
  4. Content sources to monitor (RSS feeds, websites, etc.)
  5. Subscriber list with preferences if personalizing content

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your BrowserAI credentials in the designated node
  3. Configure your content sources and keywords for scraping
  4. Set up either your email service provider or Telegram bot integration
  5. Test with a small subscriber group before full deployment

Pro tip: Start with broad content categories, then refine based on which topics generate the most engagement from your subscribers.
