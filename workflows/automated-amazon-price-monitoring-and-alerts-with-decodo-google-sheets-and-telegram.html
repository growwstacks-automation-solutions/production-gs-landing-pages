What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking competitor prices on Amazon. Retailers often spend hours daily checking prices across multiple products and competitors, risking missed opportunities when prices change unexpectedly. The workflow automatically monitors specified Amazon product pages at scheduled intervals using Decodo's reliable price tracking API.

When price changes are detected, the system logs them in Google Sheets for historical analysis and sends instant Telegram alerts to your team. This enables real-time competitive response rather than delayed reactions that can cost sales. The solution helps maintain optimal pricing strategies without constant manual oversight.

How It Works

1. Scheduled price checks with Decodo

The workflow starts by connecting to Decodo's API to fetch current pricing data for your specified Amazon products. Decodo handles all the complexity of Amazon's anti-scraping measures while providing clean, structured data outputs.

2. Price comparison logic

Each new price check compares current prices against your stored baseline prices. The system calculates percentage changes and absolute differences, applying your configured thresholds for what constitutes a significant change worth alerting about.

3. Google Sheets logging

All price data gets recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps, creating a searchable historical record. Conditional formatting highlights significant changes, while formulas can calculate average prices, trend lines, and other analytics.

4. Telegram notifications

When prices cross your defined thresholds, the workflow sends formatted Telegram messages to your specified chat or group. These alerts include product details, old/new prices, change percentages, and direct links to the Amazon listings.
