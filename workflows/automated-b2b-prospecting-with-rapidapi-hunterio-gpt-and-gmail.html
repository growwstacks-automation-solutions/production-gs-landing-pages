What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of B2B lead generation by combining multiple tools into a seamless prospecting system. It automatically discovers potential clients in your target market, verifies their contact information, and sends personalized outreach emails - all without manual effort.

The workflow eliminates the repetitive tasks of searching business directories, scraping contact info, and writing individual emails. Instead, it delivers qualified leads directly to your inbox with AI-crafted messages that outperform generic templates in response rates.

How It Works

1. Business Discovery via RapidAPI

The workflow starts by querying business directories through RapidAPI to find companies matching your ideal customer profile. You can filter by location, industry, company size, and other criteria to build targeted prospect lists.

2. Email Verification with Hunter.io

Each discovered business is checked against Hunter.io's database to find valid email addresses for key decision makers. The system verifies deliverability and enriches contact profiles with job titles and social links.

3. AI Personalization with GPT

GPT analyzes each company's website and available data to craft hyper-relevant email content. The AI generates unique value propositions based on the recipient's industry, pain points, and business context.

4. Automated Gmail Delivery

Verified contacts receive personalized emails through your Gmail account with optimal send timing. The system tracks opens and replies while maintaining healthy email sending limits to protect your domain reputation.
