What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating consistent, high-quality blog content for WordPress websites. Instead of spending hours researching, writing, and designing images for each post, you simply input keywords and let AI handle the entire process.

The workflow automatically researches topics using Wikipedia for factual accuracy, generates well-structured articles with GPT-4, creates custom featured images with DALL-E, and publishes everything as drafts to your WordPress site ready for final review. What typically takes 4-6 hours of human effort is completed in 2-5 minutes with consistent quality and SEO optimization.

How It Works

Step 1: Keyword Input & Structure Planning

The process begins when you submit keywords through a simple form. The AI analyzes these keywords and creates a logical article structure with chapters, determining the optimal flow for reader engagement and SEO performance.

Step 2: Wikipedia Research & Fact Checking

The workflow automatically queries Wikipedia for relevant, factual information about your topic. This ensures your content has accurate data points, historical context, and reliable information that builds credibility with readers and search engines.

Step 3: AI Content Generation with GPT-4

GPT-4 generates comprehensive, well-written content for each chapter of your article. The AI maintains context awareness between sections, creates smooth transitions, and optimizes the writing for both readability and search engine visibility.

Step 4: Custom Image Creation with DALL-E

Based on the article content, DALL-E generates unique featured images tailored specifically to your topic. These aren't generic stock photos—they're custom visuals that perfectly complement your article's subject matter.

Step 5: WordPress Integration & Publishing

The completed article with its custom image is automatically uploaded to your WordPress site as a draft. The content includes proper HTML formatting, SEO elements, and is ready for your final review before publishing.

Pro tip: Use this workflow to maintain a consistent publishing schedule even during busy periods. The AI-generated drafts can be reviewed and published in minutes rather than hours, keeping your content calendar on track.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your environment:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure WordPress credentials in n8n's credential manager with your site URL and application password
  3. Add your OpenAI API key to the credential system for GPT-4 and DALL-E access
  4. Update the settings node with your specific WordPress URL and preferred content parameters
  5. Test the workflow with sample keywords to ensure everything connects properly
  6. Activate the workflow and share the form URL with your team for regular use
