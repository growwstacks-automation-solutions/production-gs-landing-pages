What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered business intelligence workflow solves the critical challenge of turning raw data into actionable insights without manual analysis. It automatically collects key performance indicators (KPIs) from your MSSQL databases, applies GPT-4's analytical capabilities to identify trends and anomalies, then delivers customized reports through your preferred communication channels.

For business owners and executives drowning in data but starving for insights, this automation provides daily/weekly/monthly intelligence briefings without requiring technical expertise or manual report generation. The system can be configured to monitor specific metrics relevant to your industry and business goals.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from MSSQL

The workflow connects to your MSSQL database using secure credentials, running pre-defined queries to extract the latest KPI data. This can include sales figures, customer metrics, operational stats, or any other quantitative business indicators stored in your database.

2. GPT-4 Analysis

The raw data is sent to GPT-4 with carefully crafted prompts that guide the AI to analyze trends, identify significant changes, and highlight potential areas of concern or opportunity. The AI adds contextual understanding that typical BI tools lack.

3. Insight Generation

GPT-4 generates executive summaries in natural language, explaining what the numbers mean for your business. It can compare current performance to historical data, identify seasonal patterns, and even suggest potential actions based on the findings.

4. Multi-Channel Delivery

The finalized insights are distributed through your preferred channels - email, Slack, Microsoft Teams, SMS, or any other communication platform integrated with n8n. Critical alerts can be prioritized and formatted differently from routine reports.

  1. An n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)

  3. OpenAI API key for GPT-4 access
  4. Destination channels configured (email, Slack, etc.)
  5. Basic understanding of your key business metrics
