What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually copying call summaries from Chorus AI into HubSpot CRM. Sales teams often waste 15-30 minutes per call documenting discussions, action items, and insights. This workflow captures all key call data automatically, ensuring your CRM always reflects the latest customer conversations.

The solution transfers complete call transcripts, talking points, sentiment analysis and action items directly from Chorus AI to the corresponding contact record in HubSpot. It maintains data integrity while eliminating human error in the process. Managers gain real-time visibility into team activity without manual reporting.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New Call Completion

The workflow monitors Chorus AI for completed calls. When a new call ends and Chorus generates the summary, the automation immediately captures all available data including participants, duration, and AI-generated insights insights.

Step 2: Match Call to CRM Contact

The system identifies the correct HubSpot contact using email addresses or phone numbers from the call participants. It handles both existing contacts and new prospects, creating CRM records when needed.

Step 3: Enrich CRM Fields

Key call insights are mapped to the most useful HubSpot fields. This includes adding notes to the contact record, updating deal stages, logging follow-up tasks, and enriching the timeline with call highlights.
