What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation transforms your recruitment process by automatically handling the initial screening of candidates. It monitors your HR email inbox for new applications, extracts key information from resumes using OCR technology, analyzes candidate qualifications with GPT-4, and makes data-driven recommendations about who to shortlist.

The system then takes action based on its analysis - updating your recruitment tracking sheet, notifying your team via Slack about promising candidates, and sending personalized responses to applicants. This eliminates hours of manual screening work while ensuring every candidate receives timely, consistent communication.

How It Works

1. Application Intake

The workflow monitors your designated HR email inbox for new messages containing resume attachments. It identifies these as new candidate applications and triggers the screening process.

2. Resume Processing

Attached resumes are processed through OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract all text content. This converts PDFs and image-based resumes into analyzable text while preserving formatting and structure.

3. AI Evaluation

GPT-4 analyzes both the resume content and any cover letter text against your predefined job requirements (stored in your Google Sheet). It evaluates skills match, experience relevance, and other criteria to generate a comprehensive assessment.

4. Decision Making

The system produces a structured recommendation including a fit score, strengths/weaknesses analysis, and suggested action (shortlist or reject). All data is logged in your recruitment tracking system for review.

5. Automated Actions

Based on the evaluation, the workflow automatically sends personalized responses to candidates and alerts your team via Slack about strong matches that require human review.
