What This Workflow Does

Manual cold email outreach is a time-consuming, error-prone process that often leads to inconsistent messaging and missed follow-ups. This automation solves that by turning your Google Sheets lead list into a personalized, scheduled email campaign engine.

The workflow reads a list of prospects from Google Sheets, filters out contacts already marked as "SENT," randomly selects an email template from a library to add variety, personalizes the content with the lead's name, sends the email via your SMTP service, and then logs the send status and timestamp back to the sheet. It operates in a loop with built-in delays to maintain good sender reputation.

This transforms a multi-hour manual task into a fully automated, hands-off process that runs in the background, ensuring every lead receives timely, personalized communication while giving your team full visibility into campaign progress.

How It Works

The automation is built as a sequential process that mimics a human sales rep's actions but with perfect consistency and at scale.

Step 1: Fetch & Filter Leads

The workflow starts by reading your "Leads" Google Sheet. It pulls columns for Name, Email, and a "Send Status." An "IF" node immediately filters the list, skipping any row where the status is already "SENT." This prevents duplicate emails and allows you to pause/resume campaigns easily.

Step 2: Select a Random Template

It then accesses a second "Templates" Google Sheet containing your pre-written email subjects and bodies. A Code node randomly picks one template from the pool. This introduces A/B testing and variety automatically, preventing your outreach from sounding robotic or repetitive.

Step 3: Personalize & Send

The selected template and lead data are merged. An "Edit Fields" node replaces placeholders like `[Name]` with the actual lead's name from the sheet. The personalized email is then dispatched through your configured email service (Gmail, SendGrid, Mailgun, etc.).

Step 4: Log Activity & Loop

After sending, a "Wait" node introduces a configurable delay (e.g., 2 minutes) to space out emails and avoid spam flags. Finally, the workflow updates the original Google Sheets row, marking the "Send Status" as "SENT" and recording the timestamp. It then loops back to process the next unsent lead until the list is complete.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets document with two tabs: one for your leads (columns: Name, Email, Send Status, Time Sent) and one for your email templates (columns: Subject, Body).
  2. Google account credentials set up in n8n to allow access to these Sheets.
  3. SMTP email service credentials (e.g., Gmail SMTP, SendGrid, Mailgun, Amazon SES) configured in the "Send Email" node.
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.

Pro tip: Use a dedicated sending domain and warm up your IP address with your email service provider to maximize deliverability before launching large-scale automated campaigns.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Prepare Your Sheets: Create your "Leads" and "Templates" Google Sheets with the required column structure.
  2. Import the Template: Download the JSON file from this page and import it into your n8n workspace.
  3. Configure Google Sheets Nodes: Update the "Google Sheets" nodes with the correct Sheet IDs and tab names for your documents.
  4. Set Up Email Credentials: In the "Send Email" node, add your SMTP connection details and set your "From" address.
  5. Adjust the Wait Time: Modify the "Wait" node delay (e.g., 120 seconds) to control the send rate and avoid being flagged as spam.
  6. Test with a Small Batch: Add 2-3 test leads with your own email to verify personalization and sending works correctly before running on your full list.
  7. Activate the Workflow: Turn on the workflow and let it run. Monitor the "Send Status" column in your Leads sheet to see progress in real-time.
