What This Workflow Does

Content creators and marketers face a constant challenge: staying ahead of trends while producing relevant, timely content. Manual research is time-consuming and often misses emerging opportunities. This workflow solves that by automating the entire trend-to-idea pipeline.

It continuously monitors Google Trends for queries related to your industry, identifies those with the strongest growth, fetches current news articles about those trends via Firecrawl, and then uses Claude AI to generate actionable content suggestions. The result is a monthly report that transforms raw data into editorial opportunities, helping you optimize your content calendar with high-potential subjects.

How It Works

Step 1: Trend Analysis

The workflow starts by querying Google Trends for searches related to your predefined topics. It examines search volume over the last month and ranks queries by growth percentage, filtering out geo-localized searches to provide a global perspective.

Step 2: Article Collection

For each trending query, the system searches Google News for recent articles. Firecrawl then scrapes the full content of these articles, extracting text for deeper analysis. This ensures suggestions are grounded in current coverage.

Step 3: Content Generation

Claude AI analyzes the collected articles alongside trend data. It identifies key angles, gaps in coverage, and unique perspectives, then generates personalized content suggestions tailored to your audience and brand voice.

What You'll Need

  1. A SerpAPI account for accessing Google Trends and Google News data.
  2. A Firecrawl API key for scraping article content from news results.
  3. Access to Google Sheets (for the tracking template).
  4. An AI model API key (Claude, OpenAI GPT, or any preferred LLM provider).
  5. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Duplicate the provided Google Sheets template and add your monitoring topics in the Query column.
  2. Configure API credentials in n8n for SerpAPI, Firecrawl, your AI model, and Google Sheets OAuth2.
  3. Import the downloaded JSON template into your n8n workspace.
  4. Update the "Get Query" node with your Google Sheets URL.
  5. Adjust language and location settings in SerpAPI nodes if needed (default is French/France).
  6. Set the schedule trigger to your preferred frequency (default is monthly on the 1st at 8 AM).
  7. Run the workflow once to test, then let it automate your content research.
