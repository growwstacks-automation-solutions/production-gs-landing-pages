What This Workflow Does

Corporate training programs often suffer from disorganized request processes, inconsistent prioritization, and poor tracking. This automation transforms that chaos into a strategic advantage by intelligently processing training requests through Jotform submissions, analyzing them with GPT-4, and maintaining perfect records in Google Sheets.

The system achieves 100% completion tracking and reduces training costs by 30% through AI-powered recommendations. It automatically categorizes requests by skill type, department needs, and budget impact while enforcing approval workflows and compliance requirements.

How It Works

1. Employee submits training request

A Jotform collects structured data including requested skills, business justification, urgency level, and preferred format. The form enforces consistent data entry with dropdowns and validation rules.

2. GPT-4 analyzes and categorizes

The AI evaluates each request against company skill matrices, identifies potential internal mentors, and suggests alternative learning resources. It flags high-priority requests that align with strategic initiatives.

3. Automated approval routing

Requests route to appropriate managers based on department, cost thresholds, and employee development plans. The system sends reminders for pending approvals and tracks response times.

4. Google Sheets tracking

All approved training populates a master spreadsheet with completion deadlines, budget codes, and expected outcomes. The sheet generates department-level dashboards and budget forecasts.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits HR teams at growing companies (50-5000 employees) that need to scale their learning programs strategically. It's particularly valuable for:
