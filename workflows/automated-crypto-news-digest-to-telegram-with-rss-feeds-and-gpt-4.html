What This Workflow Does

Staying updated with cryptocurrency news is time-consuming but critical for investors, traders, and community managers. Manually scanning multiple sources, filtering noise, and creating summaries can consume hours each day. This automation solves that problem by creating a fully automated news pipeline.

The workflow collects the latest crypto news from multiple RSS feeds (CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, Decrypt, etc.), filters and deduplicates articles, uses OpenAI GPT-4 to analyze and select the most important stories, translates and formats them into digestible summaries, and posts automatically to your Telegram channel or group. It runs on a schedule and ensures all messages fit Telegram's character limits.

How It Works

Step 1: RSS Feed Collection

The workflow begins by polling configured RSS feeds from major cryptocurrency news sources. It checks for new articles published within the last 24 hours, ensuring you get fresh content without duplicates.

Step 2: Filtering & Deduplication

Articles are filtered based on relevance and deduplicated using title and content matching. This prevents your audience from seeing the same story multiple times from different sources.

Step 3: AI Analysis & Selection

An OpenAI agent acts as a "Crypto Analyst," reviewing all collected articles to identify the most important market-moving events. It selects the best article for each major topic and extracts key insights.

Step 4: AI Formatting & Translation

Another OpenAI agent serves as an "SMM Editor," writing styled digests in your preferred language (default: Russian) with Telegram-compatible HTML formatting. It creates engaging summaries with proper emphasis and structure.

Step 5: Message Preparation

Long texts are automatically split into safe chunks ≤ 4096 characters to comply with Telegram's message limits. The workflow ensures complete delivery even for lengthy digests.

Step 6: Telegram Posting

The final digest is posted automatically to your configured Telegram channel or group. The workflow handles authentication and formatting, delivering professional-looking updates to your community.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (Cloud or self-hosted version ≥ 1.107.4)
  2. OpenAI API credentials with access to GPT-4o-mini or GPT-4.1-mini
  3. Telegram Bot Token created via @BotFather
  4. Telegram channel/group ID where the bot has posting permissions
  5. RSS feed URLs for your preferred crypto news sources (defaults included)

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your automated crypto news digest running in under 10 minutes:

  1. Import the template into your n8n workspace using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure Telegram node by opening the "Post to Group" node and entering your chat ID (e.g., @your_channel or numeric ID).
  3. Connect credentials for OpenAI and Telegram in n8n's credential management.
  4. Adjust scheduler (optional) to change how often the workflow runs (default: every 3 hours).
  5. Test manually by executing the workflow once to verify everything works.
  6. Activate the workflow to run automatically on your chosen schedule.
