What This Workflow Does

This automated trading bot combines institutional trading concepts with AI-powered analysis to execute cryptocurrency trades systematically. It implements ICT (Inner Circle Trader) methodology and Smart Money Concepts to identify high-probability trading opportunities while minimizing emotional decision-making.

The workflow integrates GPT-4o for advanced market analysis, processing news sentiment, interpreting complex chart patterns, and assessing risk parameters. By connecting directly with Coinbase's API, it can execute trades with institutional-grade reliability while maintaining proper risk management protocols.

How It Works

1. Market Analysis Phase

The bot continuously monitors cryptocurrency markets using ICT methodology to identify liquidity pools, institutional order flow, and optimal trade locations. GPT-4o enhances this analysis by interpreting unstructured data like news events and social sentiment.

2. Signal Generation

When the system identifies a high-probability setup based on ICT concepts (like fair value gaps or order block reactions), it generates a trade signal. The AI component validates this signal against multiple confirmation criteria before proceeding.

3. Risk Assessment

Before any trade execution, the bot calculates optimal position sizing based on account balance and current volatility. It sets stop-loss levels according to recent market structure and implements maximum risk-per-trade limits.

4. Trade Execution

The system connects to Coinbase's API to place orders with precise timing. It manages the entire trade lifecycle - from entry to scaling out positions - according to predefined rules that adapt to changing market conditions.

5. Performance Tracking

All trades are logged with detailed metrics for later analysis. The AI reviews performance data to suggest strategy optimizations and detect when market conditions may require adjustments to the trading approach.
