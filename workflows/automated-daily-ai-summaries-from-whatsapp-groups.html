What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of extracting valuable insights from your WhatsApp group conversations by generating AI-powered daily summaries. It solves the common problem of information overload in active group chats where important discussions, decisions, and action items can get buried in hundreds of messages.

The system processes all messages from specified WhatsApp groups, uses AI to identify key themes and action points, then delivers concise executive summaries to your preferred destination (email, Slack, or other platforms). This transforms chaotic group chats into structured business intelligence that helps teams stay aligned without reading every message.

How It Works

1. Message Collection

The workflow connects to your WhatsApp account via the WhatsApp Business API or third-party integration tools to fetch all new messages from specified groups at scheduled intervals (typically once per day).

2. Conversation Processing

All messages are compiled into a single conversation thread, with metadata like sender names and timestamps preserved. The system filters out common noise like greetings, reactions, and forwarded messages to focus on substantive content.

3. AI Analysis

Using OpenAI's GPT or similar AI models, the workflow analyzes the conversation to identify key discussion points, decisions made, action items, and important questions raised. The AI is configured to focus on business-relevant content while ignoring casual chatter.

4. Summary Generation

The system generates a structured summary with sections for key discussion topics, action items (with owners and deadlines when mentioned), decisions made, and follow-up questions. The format is optimized for quick scanning by busy professionals.

5. Delivery

The final summary is sent to your preferred destination via email, Slack, or other communication channels. You can configure multiple delivery methods and customize the formatting for each recipient type (executives vs team members).
