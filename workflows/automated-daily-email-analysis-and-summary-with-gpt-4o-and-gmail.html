What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the modern professional's biggest productivity drain - email overload. The system connects to your Gmail account, processes all incoming messages through GPT-4o's advanced natural language processing, and generates a structured daily digest highlighting key information.

Instead of spending hours sorting through your inbox each morning, you receive a single organized report containing action items, important updates, and summarized conversations. The AI identifies urgent requests, extracts deadlines, and even categorizes messages by project or priority level.

How It Works

1. Email Collection

The workflow connects to your Gmail account via n8n's Google node, fetching all messages from the past 24 hours. It can be configured to process specific labels or skip certain senders.

2. Content Analysis

Each email is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o API where the AI identifies key elements: action items, decisions needed, important dates, and core messages. The system maintains thread context across related messages.

3. Summary Generation

GPT-4o compiles the analyzed data into a structured Markdown report with clear sections for different message types. The summary preserves all critical information while removing redundant content.

4. Delivery

The final digest is delivered via your preferred channel - email, Slack, Notion, or any other connected platform. The template includes multiple output options.
