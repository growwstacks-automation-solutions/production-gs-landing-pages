What This Workflow Does

If your team spends hours every day manually sorting through emails, responding to routine inquiries, and routing messages to the right departments, this automation is your solution. The workflow transforms your email inbox from a time-consuming administrative task into an intelligent, self-managing system that operates 24/7.

Using GPT-5's advanced text classification capabilities, every incoming email is instantly analyzed for content, context, and intent. The system then routes each message to specialized AI agents designed for specific functions: customer support, sales lead handling, finance inquiries, or internal communications. Each agent has access to your company's knowledge base via Pinecone vector database, ensuring accurate, consistent responses that reflect your business policies and information.

This isn't just simple email filtering—it's a complete email management ecosystem that learns from your communications patterns, provides instant responses to common questions, escalates complex issues with full context, and keeps your team informed through real-time notifications on platforms like Telegram. The result is faster response times, reduced human workload, and consistently professional communication.

How It Works

The automation follows a sophisticated multi-stage process that mimics how an efficient human team would handle email, but with AI speed and consistency.

1. Intelligent Email Classification

When any email arrives in your connected inbox (Gmail, Outlook, etc.), GPT-5 immediately analyzes the subject line, sender information, and full content. The AI doesn't just look for keywords—it understands context, urgency, and intent to accurately categorize each message into predefined categories like Customer Support, Sales Opportunities, Finance, or Internal Communications.

2. Specialized Agent Routing

Based on the classification, the email is routed to the appropriate AI agent. The Customer Support Agent accesses your Pinecone knowledge base of FAQs and policies to draft immediate responses. The Sales Agent prepares personalized replies using your product/service information. The Finance Agent distinguishes between payment and receipt inquiries, routing them to appropriate teams.

3. Knowledge-Based Response Generation

Each agent queries your Pinecone vector database—which stores your company knowledge as semantic embeddings—to find the most relevant information for crafting responses. This ensures answers are accurate, up-to-date, and consistent with your business voice and policies.

4. Action Execution & Notification

Agents execute appropriate actions: sending drafted replies, labeling emails, creating tasks in other systems, or notifying team members via Telegram for human review when needed. The system provides complete transparency about what actions were taken and why.

What You'll Need

  1. Email Account: Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP-compatible email service
  2. Telegram Bot: Created via BotFather for team notifications
  3. OpenAI API Access: For GPT-5 text classification capabilities
  4. OpenRouter Account: Alternative AI model access for specialized agents
  5. Pinecone Database: Vector database to store your company knowledge base
  6. n8n Instance: Self-hosted or cloud version to run the workflow

Pro tip: Start by automating just one email category (like customer support) to test the system before expanding to all categories. This lets you refine the AI classification and agent responses with minimal risk.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this sophisticated system running requires some initial configuration, but the template handles the complex logic once connections are established.

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance using the "Import from File" option.
  2. Configure Email Connection: Set up the Gmail/Outlook trigger node with your email credentials and specify which inbox to monitor.
  3. Connect AI Services: Add your OpenAI API key to the classification node and configure OpenRouter for agent responses.
  4. Set Up Pinecone: Connect your Pinecone database and upload your company knowledge (FAQs, policies, product info) as vector embeddings.
  5. Configure Telegram: Add your Telegram bot token and specify which team members or channels should receive notifications.
  6. Define Categories & Agents: Customize the email categories and agent instructions to match your business needs.
  7. Test & Activate: Send test emails to verify classification accuracy and agent responses, then activate the workflow.
