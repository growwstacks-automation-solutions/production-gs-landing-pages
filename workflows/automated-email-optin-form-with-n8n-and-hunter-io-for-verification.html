What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the costly problem of building clean email lists without paying for expensive marketing platforms. Traditional email collection tools charge monthly fees while still allowing fake or invalid addresses to enter your database - damaging deliverability and wasting marketing resources.

The workflow creates a self-hosted alternative that captures email signups through any form, instantly verifies them using Hunter.io's enterprise-grade validation, then routes only confirmed addresses to your CRM or email service provider. This eliminates bounce rates while saving $20-$300/month compared to commercial solutions.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Capture

The workflow triggers when a new submission comes from any web form (Google Forms, Typeform, custom HTML forms, etc). It extracts the email address and additional form data like name or preferences.

2. Hunter.io Verification

Each email passes through Hunter.io's verification API which checks: domain validity, mailbox existence, role-based address patterns, and deliverability confidence scores. Invalid emails receive immediate feedback.

3. Conditional Routing

Verified emails proceed to your mailing list while unverified submissions trigger customizable actions - either quarantine for review or instant rejection notifications to the submitter.
