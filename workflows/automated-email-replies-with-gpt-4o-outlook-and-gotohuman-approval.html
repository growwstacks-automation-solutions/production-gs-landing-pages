What This Workflow Does

This automation solution tackles the time-consuming process of email management by combining AI efficiency with human oversight. It reads incoming Outlook emails, generates context-aware responses using OpenAI's GPT-4o, then routes these drafts through a human approval system before sending.

The workflow specifically addresses the challenge of maintaining quick response times while ensuring quality and appropriateness in business communications. By automating the drafting process but keeping human review, it strikes the perfect balance between efficiency and control.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Outlook inbox for new messages, filtering for today's emails and excluding those from your own address.

2. AI Draft Generation

Using OpenAI's advanced language model, the system analyzes each email's content and generates a tailored response based on your predefined guidelines and tone preferences.

3. Human Approval Routing

All AI-generated responses are sent to the GoToHuman node, which creates a review task for designated approvers in your team.

4. Conditional Sending

Only approved responses are automatically sent back via Outlook, while rejected drafts are flagged for manual revision or alternative handling.
