Automated Financial Tracker: Telegram to Notion with AI

Transform invoice photos into organized financial records with AI-powered automation

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual work of tracking business expenses by creating a seamless pipeline from receipt capture to organized financial records. Simply snap a photo of an invoice in Telegram, and the system automatically extracts all key details using AI, logs them in your Notion database, and provides summarized reports.

The workflow combines the convenience of mobile receipt capture with the power of AI data extraction and the flexibility of Notion's database system. It eliminates manual data entry while providing better visibility into your finances through automated weekly spending reports.

How It Works

1. Receipt Capture via Telegram

The workflow triggers when you send an invoice photo to a designated Telegram chat. This makes it easy to capture receipts on the go using your mobile device's camera.

2. AI-Powered Data Extraction

Google Gemini AI analyzes the invoice image to extract key details like vendor name, date, items purchased, quantities, prices, taxes, and totals. The AI also attempts to categorize each expense.

3. Notion Database Integration

Extracted transaction data is automatically logged in your Notion database with proper field mapping. Multiple items from a single invoice are split into separate records when needed.

4. Confirmation & Reporting

You receive a summary of the recorded transaction in Telegram. Weekly reports with spending breakdowns by category are automatically generated and sent to your preferred chat.
