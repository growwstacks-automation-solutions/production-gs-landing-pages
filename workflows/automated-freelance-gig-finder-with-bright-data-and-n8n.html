What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming problem of manually searching multiple freelance platforms for new gigs. Instead of checking each site individually, this workflow automatically scans platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer.com for opportunities matching your specific skills, rates, and availability.

The system uses Bright Data's web scraping capabilities to extract job postings while avoiding detection, then filters them through n8n based on your predefined criteria. Matching gigs are delivered to you via email or your preferred notification channel, ensuring you're always among the first to apply.

How It Works

1. Platform scanning

The workflow uses Bright Data to scrape multiple freelance platforms simultaneously, collecting all new job postings that meet your basic criteria (like job category or minimum budget).

2. Advanced filtering

n8n then applies your custom filters to these results - removing spam, excluding low-budget gigs, and prioritizing projects matching your exact skillset and preferred working hours.

3. Notification delivery

Qualified opportunities are formatted into a clean report and sent to you via email, Slack, or your preferred communication channel with direct links to apply.

Pro tip: Set this workflow to run every 2-4 hours during peak hiring times to catch new gigs as soon as they're posted.
