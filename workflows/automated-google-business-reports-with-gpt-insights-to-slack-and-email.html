What This Workflow Does

Marketing agencies often charge hundreds per month for "Google Business Pulse" dashboards that show visibility, reviews, and engagement trends. This workflow provides the same functionality for free by automating the entire reporting process.

It pulls data from Google Business Profile, compares weekly and 12-week trends, runs the numbers through GPT for insight summaries, then delivers polished reports via Slack and email - all without manual intervention. The system acts as your AI marketing analyst working 24/7.

Sample report output with AI-generated trend analysis and recommendations

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow starts by pulling company/location details from a Google Sheet, then queries the Google Business API for impressions, actions, CTR, and reviews across three time periods: current week, previous week, and 12-week average.

2. Trend Analysis

Code nodes calculate percentage changes and append results back to Google Sheets for historical tracking. This creates a growing dataset for longitudinal analysis.

3. AI Insight Generation

Three separate GPT prompts analyze different aspects:

  • Impressions analysis (visibility trends)
  • Reviews analysis (sentiment and volume)
  • Overall performance summary

4. Report Distribution

Formatted messages go to Slack using Blocks for rich formatting, while email recipients receive branded "Weekly Google Business Pulse" reports with all insights and key metrics.
