What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the universal problem of email overload by applying AI intelligence to your Outlook inbox. Instead of manually sorting through hundreds of messages, the system automatically analyzes each email's content and context to determine its category, priority, and destination.

The GPT-4.1 agent understands nuanced business communications, distinguishing between sales inquiries, support tickets, internal updates, and personal messages. It can route emails to appropriate team members, flag urgent requests, and even suggest responses - all without human intervention.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow starts by monitoring your Outlook inbox for new messages using Microsoft's API integration. It captures the full email content including subject, body, attachments, and metadata.

2. AI Analysis

Each email gets processed by the GPT-4.1 agent which evaluates multiple factors: the sender's importance, message urgency, content keywords, and historical patterns. The AI assigns category tags like "Sales Lead" or "Customer Support".

3. Automated Routing

Based on the AI's classification, the workflow moves emails to designated Outlook folders, forwards messages to relevant team members, or triggers follow-up actions in connected systems like your CRM.

4. Priority Flagging

The system applies Outlook importance flags to critical messages and can send mobile alerts for time-sensitive communications that require immediate attention.
