What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and often uncomfortable process of chasing unpaid invoices. By connecting your Google Sheets invoice tracker with email communication, it automatically sends polite but effective payment reminders that adapt their tone based on each customer's payment history.

The system intelligently escalates communication for chronic late-payers while maintaining positive relationships with reliable customers. It eliminates manual follow-up tasks, reduces accounting team workload by 60-80%, and typically improves collection rates by 15-25%.

How It Works

1. Daily Invoice Status Check

The workflow automatically scans your Google Sheets invoice database each morning, identifying invoices that have crossed their due date without payment.

2. Customer Payment Pattern Analysis

For each overdue invoice, the system reviews the customer's historical payment behavior to determine the appropriate communication tone - friendly reminder, firm notice, or urgent request.

3. AI-Powered Email Composition

Using predefined templates adjusted by AI, the system generates personalized email reminders that reference the specific invoice, amount due, and payment options.

4. Automated Follow-Up Sequence

If payment isn't received within set timeframes, the workflow automatically sends progressively stronger reminders while logging all communication in your tracking system.

Who This Is For

This automation benefits any business that sends invoices and experiences late payments, particularly:

  • Freelancers and consultants with irregular payment cycles
  • B2B service providers with net-30 or net-60 terms
  • Small businesses without dedicated accounts receivable staff
  • Companies wanting to improve cash flow without damaging client relationships

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets invoice tracker with columns for due dates, payment status, and customer email
  2. n8n account (free or paid)
  3. Email service provider (Gmail, SMTP, or transactional email API)
  4. Basic understanding of your payment terms and late fee policies

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets account and identify your invoice tracking sheet
  3. Configure your email service provider credentials
  4. Customize the reminder templates and escalation schedule
  5. Test with a few sample invoices before activating full automation

Pro tip: Start with gentler tones than you think necessary - you can always adjust escalation rules later. The goal is effective collection while preserving customer relationships.
