What This Workflow Does

Manual invoice processing is a time-consuming, error-prone task that plagues e-commerce businesses. Every time a customer places an order, someone must extract order details, calculate taxes and shipping, format a professional invoice, save it somewhere organized, and email it to the customer. This workflow eliminates all that manual work by automating the entire process from Shopify order to customer delivery.

When a new paid order arrives in Shopify, this automation instantly generates a beautifully formatted PDF invoice with your branding, saves it to a designated Google Drive folder for record-keeping, and emails it directly to the customer—all without human intervention. The system validates payment status, extracts complete order data, applies proper calculations, and maintains organized digital records while providing immediate professional communication to customers.

How It Works

1. Order Trigger & Validation

The workflow activates when Shopify sends a webhook notification for a new order. It first validates that the order is paid and contains all necessary information before proceeding, preventing unnecessary processing of incomplete or unpaid orders.

2. Data Extraction & Processing

Complete order details are extracted including customer information, billing/shipping addresses, line items with SKUs and quantities, taxes, shipping costs, discounts, and payment method. This data is structured and prepared for invoice generation.

3. Professional Invoice Creation

Using a customizable HTML template with your branding, the system generates a professional invoice document. The template includes your logo, company details, invoice numbering, and all order information in a clean, readable format suitable for accounting and customer records.

4. PDF Conversion & Storage

The HTML invoice is converted to a high-quality PDF file with a descriptive filename (e.g., "invoice-ORDER_NUMBER.pdf") and automatically saved to a designated "Invoices" folder in Google Drive. The folder structure can be organized by date, customer, or other parameters.

5. Customer Notification

A professional email with the PDF invoice attached is sent to the customer, confirming their purchase and providing immediate documentation. The email template can be customized with personalized messages, tracking information, or additional resources.

6. System Response & Logging

The workflow sends a proper response back to Shopify confirming processing and logs all actions for auditing purposes. Error handling ensures failed steps are captured and can trigger alerts for manual review if needed.

What You'll Need

  1. A Shopify store with webhook permissions configured
  2. Google Drive account for invoice storage
  3. Email service (Gmail, SMTP, or transactional email provider)
  4. HTML to PDF conversion service (various API options available)
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  6. Basic understanding of your invoice requirements and branding

Pro tip: Start with a simple invoice design and gradually add complexity. Test with a few real orders first to ensure all data fields map correctly before full automation.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template into your n8n workspace
  2. Configure Shopify webhook to trigger on new paid orders
  3. Connect your Google Drive credentials and specify the target folder
  4. Set up your email service with appropriate templates
  5. Configure your HTML to PDF conversion service (API key or self-hosted)
  6. Customize the HTML invoice template with your branding
  7. Test with a real order and verify all steps execute correctly
  8. Activate the workflow for continuous automation
