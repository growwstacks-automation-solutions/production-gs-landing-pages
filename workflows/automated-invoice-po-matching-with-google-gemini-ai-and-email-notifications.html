What This Workflow Does

This automated invoice processing solution eliminates manual data entry and verification for finance teams. The workflow intelligently matches incoming invoices with purchase orders using Google Gemini AI, flags discrepancies, and automates approval workflows based on predefined business rules.

By connecting Google Drive, Google Sheets, and email systems, the solution creates a seamless accounts payable process that reduces processing time by up to 80% while improving accuracy. Key features include automatic data extraction from PDF invoices, AI-powered exception handling, and configurable approval thresholds.

How It Works

1. Invoice Capture

The workflow monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new invoices. When a new file is detected, it automatically extracts key data including vendor details, invoice numbers, dates, and line items.

2. AI-Powered Matching

Google Gemini AI compares extracted invoice data against purchase order records in Google Sheets, identifying matches and flagging discrepancies like quantity or price variances.

3. Approval Routing

Based on configurable rules (e.g., amount thresholds), the workflow either auto-approves matched invoices or routes exceptions to the appropriate manager for review via email notifications.

4. Payment Processing

Approved invoices trigger payment processing workflows while maintaining a complete audit trail in your accounting system.
