What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually extracting job listings from various sources and publishing them to your WordPress site. HR teams and recruiters often waste hours each week copying information from emails, documents, and job boards into their careers page.

The workflow uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to intelligently process incoming job listings, Jina AI to extract structured data from unstructured sources, and OpenAI to generate polished, SEO-optimized job descriptions. The result is a fully automated pipeline that publishes professional job posts with consistent formatting and branding.

How It Works

Step 1: Source Monitoring

The system continuously monitors configured sources like Google Drive folders, email attachments, or web pages for new job listings. It can handle PDFs, Word docs, emails, and web content.

Step 2: AI-Powered Processing

Jina AI extracts key information (job title, location, requirements) from any format. RAG then retrieves relevant company information to enrich the listing before OpenAI generates the final description.

Step 3: WordPress Publishing

The formatted job post is automatically published to your WordPress site with proper categories, tags, and metadata. The workflow can include approval steps if needed.

Pro tip: Configure the AI to match your company's writing style by providing sample job descriptions during setup.
