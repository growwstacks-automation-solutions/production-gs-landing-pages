What This Workflow Does

This automated job finder agent helps streamline your job search process by continuously monitoring multiple job boards and filtering opportunities based on your specific criteria. Instead of manually checking dozens of websites daily, this workflow automatically scans for new postings that match your qualifications, experience level, location preferences, and other key parameters.

The system can integrate with various job platforms and recruitment sites, collecting relevant listings in one centralized location. It eliminates the repetitive task of manual job searching while ensuring you never miss a potential opportunity that matches your profile.

The workflow's filtering interface showing job criteria configuration

How It Works

Step 1: Data Collection

The workflow connects to various job board APIs and scrapes relevant websites to gather job postings. It can be configured to monitor specific companies, industries, or geographic locations.

Step 2: Filtering Process

Each collected job posting is evaluated against your predefined criteria such as salary range, required skills, company size, remote work options, and other important factors.

Step 3: Notification System

Qualified job opportunities are then forwarded to your preferred notification channel - email, Slack, SMS, or any other communication platform you use.

What You'll Need

  1. A self-hosted n8n instance (community nodes required)
  2. API keys for any job platforms you want to integrate
  3. Clear criteria for your ideal job position
  4. A notification channel (email, Slack, etc.) configured in n8n

Pro tip: Start with broad filters and gradually narrow them down as you understand which opportunities truly match your goals.

Modern automation tools can achieve 85-95% accuracy in matching jobs to specified criteria when properly configured. The key is starting with clear parameters and refining them based on initial results. Most false positives come from overly broad filters rather than technical limitations.

A healthcare professional might start by filtering for "registered nurse" positions but then add location, shift type, and certification requirements to narrow results. The system learns from your feedback - marking positions as relevant or irrelevant improves future matching accuracy through simple machine learning techniques.

  • Start with broad filters then narrow
