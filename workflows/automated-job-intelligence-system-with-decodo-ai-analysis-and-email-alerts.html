What This Workflow Does

Job searching is time-consuming and emotionally draining. Manually checking dozens of company career pages daily leads to missed opportunities and decision fatigue. This automated intelligence system transforms your job search from a reactive chore into a proactive, strategic process.

The workflow acts as your personal recruitment assistant. It systematically monitors target companies' career pages, extracts new job listings, uses artificial intelligence to evaluate their relevance to your specific skills and career goals, and delivers personalized alerts for only the most promising opportunities. Meanwhile, it maintains a comprehensive database of all discovered positions for tracking and analysis.

How It Works

1. Daily Monitoring Trigger

The system activates automatically each morning, scanning for new opportunities while you focus on other priorities. This scheduled trigger ensures consistent coverage without requiring manual initiation.

2. Company Data Retrieval

It pulls your target company URLs from a Google Sheets database. This centralized list allows you to easily add or remove companies as your search focus evolves, maintaining flexibility throughout your job hunt.

3. Web Scraping with Decodo

Using the Decodo API, the workflow extracts structured job data from career pages. This handles JavaScript rendering, anti-bot measures, and page structure variations that would break simpler scraping approaches.

4. AI-Powered Analysis

Each job listing passes through OpenAI's models to identify company names, positions, seniority levels, required tech stacks, and cultural signals. The AI assigns relevance scores (1-10) based on your predefined criteria.

5. Duplicate Detection & Filtering

The system compares new listings against your historical database to eliminate duplicates. Only genuinely new opportunities proceed, preventing notification fatigue from seeing the same jobs repeatedly.

6. Priority Alert Generation

Jobs scoring above your threshold (default: 8/10) trigger immediate email alerts with key details and direct application links. Lower-scoring opportunities get logged for later review without interrupting your day.

7. Comprehensive Database Update

All discovered jobs—whether high-priority or not—get stored in Google Sheets with timestamps, scores, and analysis details. This creates a searchable history of market opportunities.

What You'll Need

  1. Decodo API Credentials: For reliable web scraping of career pages (free tier available).
  2. OpenAI API Key: To power the AI analysis of job relevance and requirements.
  3. Google Sheets Access: With edit permissions for your company database and job tracking sheets.
  4. Gmail or SMTP Credentials: To send personalized email alerts for high-priority opportunities.
  5. n8n Instance: Self-hosted or cloud account to run the automation workflow.

Pro tip: Start with 5-10 target companies to test the system. Once configured correctly, scale to 50+ companies with minimal additional setup time.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file using the button above.
  2. Import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import function.
  3. Create a Google Sheet with two tabs: "Companies" (URLs) and "Job History" (tracking).
  4. Connect your Decodo and OpenAI API credentials in n8n's credentials management.
  5. Configure the Gmail node with your sending email address and authentication.
  6. Add 3-5 company career page URLs to your Google Sheets "Companies" tab.
  7. Test the workflow manually to verify data extraction and email delivery.
  8. Activate the schedule trigger for daily automated monitoring.
