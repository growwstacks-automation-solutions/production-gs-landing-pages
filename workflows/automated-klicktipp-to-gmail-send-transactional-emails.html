What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of sending transactional emails from KlickTipp to Gmail, eliminating manual work while ensuring timely, personalized communication. When a contact completes a specific action in KlickTipp (like signing up, making a purchase, or reaching a milestone), the system automatically triggers a tailored email sent through your Gmail account.

Transactional emails have 8x higher open rates than promotional emails, yet many businesses still send them manually. This automation ensures every customer receives immediate, consistent responses to their actions while saving your team hours per week on repetitive email tasks.

The workflow connects KlickTipp triggers to Gmail actions with customizable logic in between

How It Works

1. KlickTipp Trigger

The workflow monitors your KlickTipp account for specific contact actions like form submissions, tag additions, or sequence progress. You can configure which events should trigger emails based on your business needs.

2. Data Processing

When the trigger activates, the workflow collects all relevant contact data from KlickTipp including custom fields, tags, and interaction history. This information becomes available for personalization in the email content.

3. Conditional Logic

Optional filters let you send different emails based on contact attributes. For example, you might send a VIP welcome to high-value leads while sending a standard version to others, all from the same trigger event.

4. Gmail Integration

The processed data flows into your Gmail account where it populates a pre-designed email template. The system sends the message with your official email address, maintaining brand consistency.

Pro tip: Use Gmail's "Send mail as" feature to make automated messages appear to come from your business address rather than a generic automation account.
