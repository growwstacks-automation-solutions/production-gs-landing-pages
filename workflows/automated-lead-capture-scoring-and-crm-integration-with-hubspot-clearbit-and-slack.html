What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation system solves the critical business problem of manual lead processing, which causes delayed follow-ups, missed opportunities, and inconsistent qualification. When leads come from various sources—website forms, landing pages, events, or partnerships—they often sit in spreadsheets or email inboxes for hours or days before anyone reviews them.

The workflow automatically captures leads from any source, validates contact information, enriches profiles with company intelligence, scores them based on your ideal customer criteria, and instantly routes qualified prospects to your sales team while nurturing lower-priority leads. It eliminates manual data entry, ensures immediate engagement, and applies consistent qualification standards to every lead.

How It Works

The automation follows a sophisticated multi-step process that transforms raw contact information into actionable sales intelligence.

1. Lead Capture & Validation

The workflow begins by capturing lead data from webhooks, forms, or API connections. It immediately validates email addresses, phone numbers, and required fields, rejecting incomplete or invalid submissions automatically.

2. Data Enrichment & Intelligence

Valid leads are sent to Clearbit for automatic enrichment, which adds company information, industry classification, employee count, revenue estimates, and technographics. This transforms basic contact details into comprehensive prospect profiles.

3. Intelligent Scoring & Qualification

The system applies your custom scoring rules based on job title, company size, industry, geographic location, and engagement signals. Each lead receives a numerical score that determines its priority level and next steps.

4. Smart Routing & CRM Integration

High-scoring leads are automatically created as contacts in HubSpot with enriched data, assigned to appropriate sales reps, and tagged with their qualification score. The CRM becomes instantly populated with ready-to-contact prospects.

5. Instant Notifications & Follow-up

Sales teams receive immediate Slack notifications with lead details, score, and enrichment data. Automated follow-up sequences can be triggered based on score thresholds, ensuring no qualified lead goes unnoticed.

What You'll Need

  1. Active HubSpot account with API access for contact creation and management

  3. Slack workspace with appropriate channel access for notifications
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation running typically takes 30-45 minutes. Follow these steps to implement it in your environment.

  1. Download and import the workflow JSON into your n8n instance using the import function
  2. Configure API credentials for HubSpot, Clearbit, and Slack in n8n's credential management
  3. Set environment variables for channel IDs, API URLs, and any custom endpoints
  4. Customize scoring logic in the Code node to match your ideal customer profile
