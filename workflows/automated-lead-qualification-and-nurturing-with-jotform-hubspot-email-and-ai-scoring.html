What This Workflow Does

Manual lead qualification is a bottleneck for sales teams. Prospects fill out a form, then wait hours—or days—for someone to review their submission, assign a score, update the CRM, and send a follow-up. This delay kills conversion rates and wastes sales time on administrative tasks.

This automation solves that by turning a JotForm submission into a fully processed, scored, and nurtured lead in seconds. It captures the lead data, runs an intelligent scoring algorithm based on email domain, company size, budget, and timeline, categorizes the lead as Hot, Warm, or Cold, logs it to HubSpot and Google Sheets, alerts the sales team via Slack for hot leads, and sends a personalized follow-up email—all automatically.

The result: your sales team spends zero time on data entry, responds to hot leads instantly, and your marketing team automatically nurtures warm and cold leads with tailored content. You eliminate human error, ensure consistent follow-up, and convert more leads.

How It Works

Step 1: Capture Form Submission

The workflow starts when a prospect submits your JotForm lead capture form. It instantly pulls all submitted data—name, email, company, phone, company size, budget range, and timeline.

Step 2: AI Lead Scoring

An AI scoring node evaluates the lead based on predefined rules. Email domain quality (corporate vs. personal), company size, budget indication, and urgency each contribute points. The lead receives a score from 0–100.

Step 3: Route by Score

Based on the score, the workflow splits the lead into three paths: Hot (75+ points), Warm (50–74), or Cold (0–49). Hot leads trigger immediate sales alerts; warm and cold leads go to marketing nurture sequences.

Step 4: Update CRM & Spreadsheet

All leads, regardless of score, are simultaneously added to HubSpot CRM with custom properties (lead_score, lead_tier, etc.) and logged to a Google Sheets tracker for reporting and analysis.

Step 5: Notify & Follow Up

Hot leads generate a Slack notification to your sales channel with full details and notes. A personalized, urgent follow-up email is sent via SMTP. Warm and cold leads receive a different, educational email designed to move them up the qualification ladder over time.

Pro tip: Adjust the scoring thresholds in the workflow to match your ideal lead profile. If you prioritize budget over timeline, increase the budget point weight.

Quick Setup Guide

Import and configure this template in about 20–30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Connect your JotForm trigger by selecting your lead capture form and mapping field names.
  3. Review the AI scoring node and adjust point values for email domain (25), company size (30), budget (25), and timeline (20) to match your priorities.
  4. Set up HubSpot CRM node with your credentials and create custom properties: lead_score, lead_tier, budget_range, company_size, timeline.
  5. Connect Google Sheets and ensure column headers match the output data.
  6. Configure Slack notifications for your sales and marketing channels.
  7. Customize email templates in the “Generate Personalized Email” node—add your calendar links, case studies, and value propositions.
  8. Enter SMTP credentials and sender information in the Send Email node.
  9. Test the workflow with a dummy form submission to verify scoring, routing, CRM update, Slack alert, and email send.
