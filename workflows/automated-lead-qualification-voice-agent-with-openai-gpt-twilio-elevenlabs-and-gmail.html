What This Workflow Does

This automated lead qualification voice agent transforms how businesses handle inbound leads by using AI to conduct natural-sounding phone conversations. Instead of making sales teams spend hours on initial qualification calls, this system handles the first touchpoint automatically.

The workflow combines OpenAI's GPT for intelligent conversation, ElevenLabs for realistic voice synthesis, Twilio for phone call infrastructure, and Gmail for follow-up communication. It can ask qualifying questions, assess lead fit, and route promising leads to your team while filtering out unqualified prospects.

How It Works

1. Call Initiation

The system automatically dials new leads using Twilio's voice API. This can be triggered by form submissions, CRM entries, or other lead sources.

2. AI Conversation

OpenAI GPT generates natural responses based on the lead's answers, while ElevenLabs provides human-like voice synthesis. The conversation follows your predefined qualification script.

3. Qualification Scoring

The AI analyzes responses to determine lead quality based on your criteria (budget, timeline, needs match, etc.). This happens in real-time during the call.

4. Follow-up Actions

Based on qualification score, the system either schedules a human follow-up (via Gmail/calendar) or sends a polite rejection with alternative resources.
