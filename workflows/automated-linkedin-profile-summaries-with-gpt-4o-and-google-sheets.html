Transform LinkedIn profiles into AI-generated professional summaries for recruiters and sales teams

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually reviewing LinkedIn profiles during recruitment or sales prospecting. By connecting Google Sheets, LinkedIn's API, and OpenAI's GPT-4o, it transforms raw profile URLs into concise, structured professional summaries.

The workflow automatically extracts key information like work history, education, and skills, then uses AI to generate human-readable summaries that highlight the most relevant qualifications. This enables recruiters and sales teams to quickly assess hundreds of profiles while maintaining consistent evaluation standards.

How It Works

1. Profile URL Collection

The workflow monitors a Google Sheet for new LinkedIn profile URLs added by your team or generated from searches.

2. Data Extraction

Using LinkedIn's API (via RapidAPI), it pulls structured data including work experience, education, and skills sections from each profile.

3. AI Summarization

GPT-4o processes the raw profile data to generate a professional summary focusing on career trajectory, key achievements, and relevant skills.

4. Results Delivery

The AI-generated summary is written back to your Google Sheet alongside the original URL for easy review and sharing with hiring teams.
