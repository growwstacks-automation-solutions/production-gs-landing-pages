Automated marketing performance email reports with Google Sheets & Outlook

This n8n workflow template automatically pulls marketing data from Google Sheets, calculates key performance metrics, and sends beautifully formatted HTML reports via Outlook

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually compiling marketing performance reports. It connects Google Sheets (where your raw campaign data lives) with Outlook email to automatically generate and distribute professional performance reports.

The workflow calculates key metrics like open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates, then formats them into visually appealing HTML emails. Recipients get consistent, accurate reports without anyone spending hours copying data between spreadsheets.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction

The workflow pulls raw campaign data from specified Google Sheets, including metrics like impressions, clicks, and conversions across all your marketing channels.

2. Metric Calculation

Key performance indicators are automatically calculated, including ROI, cost per acquisition, and engagement rates. The system can handle complex formulas that would be error-prone if done manually.

3. Report Generation

The data transforms into a professional HTML email template with charts, trend analysis, and performance highlights. The template can be customized to match your brand guidelines.

4. Scheduled Distribution

Reports send automatically via Outlook to your specified recipients on a set schedule - weekly, monthly, or after each campaign concludes.

Who This Is For

This automation benefits marketing teams, agencies, and business owners who need to:

  • Share performance updates with executives or clients

Pro tip: Use conditional formatting in your Google Sheets to flag underperforming campaigns automatically. The workflow will highlight these in the email reports.
