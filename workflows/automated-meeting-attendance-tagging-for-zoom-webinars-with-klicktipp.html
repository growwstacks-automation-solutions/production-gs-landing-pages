What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking webinar attendance and updating marketing systems. When hosting Zoom webinars, knowing who attended and for how long is valuable data that often gets stuck in spreadsheets or requires manual tagging in email marketing platforms like KlickTipp.

The workflow automatically captures Zoom webinar attendance data as soon as a session ends, matches attendees with existing contacts in KlickTipp, and applies appropriate tags based on their participation level. This enables immediate, targeted follow-up campaigns without any manual data processing.

The workflow listens for Zoom webinar events and processes attendance data

How It Works

1. Webinar completion trigger

The workflow activates when Zoom sends a webinar.ended webhook notification. This ensures processing only happens after all attendance data is finalized.

2. Attendee data extraction

The system retrieves the full list of participants along with their join/leave times, calculating total attendance duration for each person.

3. KlickTipp contact matching

Each attendee's email address is used to find their corresponding contact record in KlickTipp. The workflow handles cases where contacts may not yet exist in the system.

4. Smart tagging application

Based on configured rules (like minimum attendance duration), the workflow applies relevant tags to categorize attendees in KlickTipp for segmentation.

Pro tip: Set different tag thresholds (like "Attended 50%" vs "Attended 90%") to enable more granular follow-up campaigns.
