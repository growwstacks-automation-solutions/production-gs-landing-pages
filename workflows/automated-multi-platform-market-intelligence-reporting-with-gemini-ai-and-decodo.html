What This Workflow Does

Businesses waste countless hours manually gathering online insights across platforms. Marketing and strategy teams need fast, structured visibility into what customers are saying and what competitors are promoting across regions and platforms. This workflow automates that process end-to-end.

It continuously monitors multiple platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Google) across selected regions, extracts and cleans live data through Decodo's advanced web intelligence engine, uses specialized AI agents to analyze raw text and structure it into key insights, and delivers a clear, ready-to-read daily report directly to your inbox—no dashboards, no manual effort required.

How It Works

1. Multi-Platform Data Collection

The workflow begins by connecting to Decodo's web intelligence API, which handles the complex task of gathering clean, compliant data from social platforms and search engines across your specified regions. This eliminates the technical challenges of web scraping and data cleaning.

2. Regional Segmentation & Processing

Data is organized by region and platform, then processed through n8n's workflow engine. The system handles rate limiting, error recovery, and data normalization automatically, ensuring you get complete datasets even when individual API calls encounter issues.

3. AI Analysis & Insight Generation

Google Gemini AI agents analyze the collected content, identifying key themes, sentiment patterns, and emerging trends. The AI doesn't just summarize—it structures insights by business impact, highlighting opportunities and risks specific to your markets.

4. Automated Report Delivery

Structured insights are formatted into a professional email report with clear sections: executive summary, platform breakdowns, regional highlights, and actionable recommendations. The report delivers at your chosen frequency (daily, weekly) without manual intervention.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for marketing teams, competitive intelligence analysts, product managers, and business strategists who need regular market insights but lack the time for manual monitoring. It's particularly valuable for businesses operating in multiple regions, companies tracking fast-moving industries, and teams that need to stay ahead of competitor movements and customer sentiment shifts.

Startups can use it to validate market opportunities, established companies can monitor brand perception, and agencies can provide added value to clients through regular intelligence reports.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Decodo API access for web intelligence data collection
  3. Google Gemini API key for AI analysis and summarization
  4. Email service credentials (Gmail, SMTP, or email service provider) for report delivery
  5. Clear monitoring objectives including target regions, platforms, and search topics

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 key regions and 1-2 platforms before expanding. This lets you validate the insights quality and refine your search parameters without being overwhelmed by data volume.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance through the workflow import function.
  2. Configure credentials: Set up your Decodo, Google Gemini, and email service credentials in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Define your regions: Edit the "Set – Regions" node to list your target markets (e.g., "North America", "Europe", "Asia-Pacific").
  4. Set search parameters: Modify the search topics in the workflow to match your business interests and competitor names.
  5. Test and schedule: Run a manual test to verify data flow, then schedule the workflow for daily or weekly execution based on your needs.
