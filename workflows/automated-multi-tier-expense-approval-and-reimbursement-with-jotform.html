What This Workflow Does

Manual expense processing is a time-consuming bottleneck that frustrates employees and burdens finance teams. This automation transforms that entire process from a bureaucratic nightmare into a seamless, policy-driven system. It captures expenses through Jotform, validates them against your company rules, and intelligently routes them for approval based on amount thresholds.

The system auto-approves compliant small expenses instantly, sends mid-range expenses to managers via Slack notifications, and escalates large expenses to finance directors—all while maintaining complete audit trails in Google Sheets and sending professional communication emails. What typically takes 2–3 weeks now happens in 72 hours with 90% less manual work.

How It Works

1. Expense Submission & Capture

Employees submit expenses through a Jotform with receipt uploads, expense details, and categorization. The workflow captures this data and normalizes it for processing.

2. Policy Validation Engine

Built-in logic checks each expense against your company's rules: category limits, amount thresholds, receipt requirements, and compliance standards. Violations are flagged immediately.

3. Intelligent Routing Logic

Based on the expense amount and compliance status, the workflow determines the approval path: auto-approval for small compliant expenses, manager approval for mid-range, or director approval for large amounts.

4. Notification & Approval Collection

Approvers receive Slack notifications with expense details and can approve/reject directly. The system tracks responses and timelines for complete accountability.

5. Audit Logging & Communication

Every action—submission, validation, routing, approval, and reimbursement—is logged to Google Sheets with timestamps. Automated emails keep employees informed at each stage.

What You'll Need

  1. Jotform account (free tier works) for expense submission forms
  2. Google Sheets for audit trail and expense database
  3. Gmail/Google Workspace for automated email communication
  4. Slack workspace for manager and director approval notifications
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation

Pro tip: Start with conservative auto-approval limits ($50-100) and increase them as you build confidence in the system. Include a link to your expense policy in all automated emails to reinforce compliance.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Create a Jotform with fields for employee details, amount, category, date, description, and receipt upload
  3. Connect your Google Sheets, Gmail, and Slack credentials in n8n
  4. Update the Google Sheet ID and Slack channel IDs in the workflow nodes
  5. Customize the policy validation rules in the "Validate Policy" node with your company's expense limits
  6. Test with sample expenses for all scenarios (auto-approve, manager, director, rejection)
  7. Activate the workflow and share the Jotform link with employees
