What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of identifying commercially viable technologies from NASA's extensive patent portfolio. Each year, NASA releases hundreds of patents with potential applications across industries, but manually evaluating them is time-consuming and requires specialized expertise.

The workflow automatically scans NASA patent databases, analyzes technical content using OpenAI, validates market potential through Google search, and organizes qualified leads in Notion. It assigns each patent a commercialization score based on predefined criteria like market size, technical readiness, and competitive landscape.

How It Works

1. Patent Discovery

The system queries NASA's patent database API to identify newly released patents matching your technology focus areas. It filters results based on keywords, classification codes, and release dates.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

OpenAI processes patent abstracts and claims to extract key technical concepts, potential applications, and commercialization barriers. The AI evaluates each patent against your scoring criteria to generate an initial viability rating.

3. Market Validation

The workflow performs automated Google searches to identify existing products, competitors, and market demand for each technology. This real-world validation helps refine the commercialization score.

4. Notion Organization

Qualified leads are automatically added to your Notion database with all relevant metadata, scoring details, and source links. The system creates structured records ready for review by your tech transfer team.
