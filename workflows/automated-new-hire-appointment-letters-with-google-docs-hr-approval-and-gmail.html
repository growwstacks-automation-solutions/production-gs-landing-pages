What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming and error-prone process of manually creating and sending appointment letters during hiring. HR teams often waste hours drafting similar documents, chasing approvals, and managing version control across email threads.

The workflow automatically generates personalized appointment letters by merging candidate data with your Google Docs template. It then routes the document for HR approval via email or Slack notification before final sending through Gmail. This maintains human oversight while eliminating 90% of manual work.

How It Works

1. Trigger from your applicant tracking system

The workflow starts when a candidate reaches the offer stage in your hiring pipeline. It pulls all necessary details like name, position, start date, and compensation details automatically.

2. Generate document from template

Using your pre-approved Google Docs template, the system creates a new document with all candidate-specific information merged in. Conditional logic handles different job types or compensation structures.

3. Route for HR approval

The generated document is sent to designated HR approvers via email or Slack with a direct approval link. Approvers can review and request edits if needed before finalizing.

4. Send to candidate

Once approved, the system converts the document to PDF and sends it via Gmail with your standard hiring communication. All documents are saved to Google Drive with proper naming conventions for records.

Pro tip: Add a signing step using DocuSign or HelloSign integration to complete the digital hiring package.
