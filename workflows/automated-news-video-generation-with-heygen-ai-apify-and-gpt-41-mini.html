What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-intensive challenge of creating daily video content for social media and marketing channels. Instead of manually scripting, filming, and editing videos, this workflow automatically scrapes news content, generates engaging scripts using AI, and produces professional videos with synthetic avatars—all without human intervention.

The system is designed for content creators, marketers, and businesses who need to maintain consistent video output across platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn. It turns hours of manual work into a fully automated process that runs on schedule, delivering ready-to-publish video content daily.

How It Works

1. Content Acquisition

The workflow begins by scraping the latest newsletter or article content using Apify's web scraping capabilities. It extracts clean, structured text from sources like Morning Brew, industry blogs, or RSS feeds, preparing it for AI processing.

2. AI Script Generation

GPT-4.1 Mini analyzes the scraped content and creates a concise, engaging 40-60 word script optimized for short-form video. The AI identifies key stories, adds conversational hooks, and structures the content for maximum viewer retention.

3. Video Production & Polling

The script is sent to HeyGen's API, which generates a video featuring your selected AI avatar and voice. The workflow includes intelligent polling to check completion status, ensuring videos are ready before proceeding to distribution.

This template is ideal for digital marketing agencies, content creators, social media managers, news organizations, and businesses with regular content updates. What You'll Need

  1. HeyGen API Key – For video generation and avatar access
  2. OpenRouter API Key – For GPT-4.1 Mini script writing

  4. n8n Instance – Self-hosted or n8n.cloud account
  5. Content Source URLs – Newsletters, blogs, or RSS feeds to scrape

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your automated video pipeline running:

  1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your API credentials in the credential manager
  3. Select your preferred HeyGen avatar and voice in the workflow settings
  4. Update the web scraper node with your target content URLs
  5. Test the workflow with a manual trigger, then schedule it for daily execution
  6. Connect output nodes to distribute videos to your social platforms or CMS

Pro tip: Create multiple workflow versions with different avatars and aspect ratios for each social platform. One script can produce landscape videos for YouTube/LinkedIn and vertical videos for TikTok/Reels simultaneously.
