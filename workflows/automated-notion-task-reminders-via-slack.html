What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of tasks getting lost or forgotten in Notion databases. By automatically sending reminders to Slack, it ensures important deadlines and action items stay top-of-mind for your team. The workflow checks your Notion database for tasks approaching their due dates and sends targeted notifications to the responsible team members via Slack.

Teams using this automation typically see a 30-50% reduction in missed deadlines and a significant decrease in the time managers spend chasing updates. The system works by querying your Notion database at regular intervals, filtering for tasks that need reminders, and formatting personalized messages that include all relevant task details.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Notion Database Query

The workflow begins by querying your Notion database on a schedule you define (daily, hourly, etc.). It looks for tasks that meet your reminder criteria, typically those with approaching due dates that haven't been marked complete.

2. Task Filtering and Processing

Each task is evaluated against your configured rules. You can set conditions like "remind if due within 24 hours" or "only for high priority tasks." The system extracts relevant details like assignee, due date, and task description.

3. Slack Message Generation

The workflow then generates personalized Slack messages for each task. It can tag the responsible team member, include the due date, and provide a direct link back to the Notion task for quick access.

4. Notification Delivery

Finally, the reminders are sent to the appropriate Slack channels or direct messages. You can configure whether to send all reminders at once or space them out throughout the day.

Pro tip: Add a "Last Reminded" date property to your Notion database to prevent duplicate reminders for the same task.
