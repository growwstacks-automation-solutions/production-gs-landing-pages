What This Workflow Does

Dental and healthcare clinics lose over 50% of potential patients due to slow response times. When someone submits an online inquiry for teeth whitening, Invisalign, or any service, they expect immediate acknowledgment. Manual processes mean leads go cold while staff are busy with patients.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless patient intake system. It captures form submissions, stores them in Google Sheets for tracking, uses AI to generate warm, personalized welcome messages, and instantly notifies both the patient and your clinical team. The result is faster engagement, higher conversion rates, and more booked appointments without increasing administrative workload.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture Trigger

When a new patient submits information through your website form, Google Form, or booking widget, the workflow automatically triggers. The patient's details—name, contact information, service interest, and any notes—are captured instantly.

2. Centralized Storage in Google Sheets

All lead data is written to a Google Sheet that acts as your simple, powerful CRM. Each inquiry creates a new row with timestamp, source, and patient details. This gives your team a single dashboard to track all potential patients and measure conversion performance.

3. AI-Personalized Response Generation

Using GPT-3.5 or similar AI models, the system generates a context-aware welcome message. It references the specific service inquired about, uses a friendly professional tone, and can include practice-specific information about next steps, insurance, or preparation instructions.

4. Instant Patient Notification

The personalized message is sent immediately via email (or SMS if configured) to the patient. This happens within seconds of their inquiry, dramatically increasing the likelihood they'll book while still actively researching.

5. Internal Team Alert

Simultaneously, your front desk or treatment coordinator receives an alert with the patient's details and the service requested. This enables prompt follow-up for scheduling while the AI response maintains immediate engagement.

Pro tip: Configure the AI to ask a qualifying question in its initial response, like "Would you prefer a morning or afternoon consultation?" This increases response rates and speeds up booking.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google Sheets setup with appropriate columns for patient data
  3. Google Forms or website form connected to the workflow
  4. Gmail or another email service for sending notifications
  5. OpenAI API key for AI message generation (or alternative AI service)
  6. Basic understanding of your clinic's consultation workflow

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.

2. Connect your Google Sheets account and select/create the spreadsheet where patient data should be stored.

3. Configure the trigger to match your form submission method (Google Forms, webhook, etc.).

4. Add your OpenAI API key to the AI agent node and customize the prompt to match your clinic's voice.

5. Set up the email nodes with your clinic's Gmail or SMTP details for patient and staff notifications.

6. Test the workflow with a sample submission, then activate it for live patient inquiries.

Implementation note: Start with a simple welcome message and basic data capture. Once working smoothly, add conditional logic for different services, urgency flags for specific symptoms, or integration with your calendar system.
