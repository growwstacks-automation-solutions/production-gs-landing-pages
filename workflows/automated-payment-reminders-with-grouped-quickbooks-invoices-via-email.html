Automated QuickBooks Payment Reminders

Group unpaid invoices and send professional reminders automatically

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the time-consuming manual process of chasing late payments by automating payment reminders for QuickBooks Online users. It fetches unpaid invoices, groups them by customer, and sends professional reminders with a clear breakdown of all outstanding balances.

Businesses using this automation typically see a 30-50% reduction in days sales outstanding (DSO) while maintaining positive customer relationships through consistent, professional communication.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs on a schedule you set (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) to check for unpaid invoices in QuickBooks Online.

2. Invoice Retrieval

It queries QuickBooks for all unpaid invoices within your specified date range, filtering by status and amount due.

3. Customer Grouping

A code node processes the invoice list to group multiple unpaid invoices by customer, calculating total amounts due.

4. Email Template Generation

The system generates a clean HTML email for each customer showing their invoice details in a table format with payment instructions.

5. Automated Delivery

Reminders are sent to each customer's billing email address with your branded template and payment links.
