What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and error-prone process of manually filling out tax forms like IRS W-9s and other PDF documents. Businesses that work with contractors, freelancers, or vendors often need to collect this information repeatedly, wasting valuable time on manual data entry.

The workflow automatically populates PDF forms with structured data from your CRM, HR system, or other business applications. It ensures all required fields are completed accurately, reduces compliance risks, and eliminates the need for printing, scanning, or manual signatures in many cases.

Automated population of W-9 form fields from structured data

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow begins by collecting structured information about the individual or business needing to complete the form. This can come from various sources like web forms, CRM systems, or HR platforms.

2. PDF Template Selection

The system identifies which PDF form needs to be filled (W-9, 1099, or other tax documents) based on your business rules and the collected data.

3. Automated Field Mapping

Using PDF.co's powerful API, the workflow maps each data field to the corresponding location in the PDF form, ensuring accurate placement of all information.

4. Form Generation and Delivery

The completed PDF is generated and can be automatically delivered via email, saved to cloud storage, or integrated with your document management system.
