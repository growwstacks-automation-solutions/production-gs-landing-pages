Automated Phone Interview Evaluation with Vapi, GPT-4o & Google Sheets

Transform phone interviews into structured candidate evaluations with AI. This workflow analyzes conversations, scores candidates against your criteria, and logs results automatically - saving hours of manual review.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming and subjective nature of phone interview evaluations. Traditional methods require recruiters to manually review notes and score candidates, which is inconsistent and inefficient. The workflow captures Vapi call transcripts, analyzes them with GPT-4o against your specific criteria, and saves structured evaluations to Google Sheets.

By automating the evaluation process, you gain consistent scoring across all candidates while eliminating hours of manual work. The AI identifies patterns and insights that humans might miss, providing deeper analysis of communication skills, technical knowledge, and cultural fit based on actual conversation data rather than gut feelings.

How It Works

1. Call Recording & Transcription

Vapi records the phone interview and generates a complete transcript. The system captures every question and response with timestamps, speaker identification, and conversation flow.

2. AI Analysis with GPT-4o

The transcript is sent to GPT-4o with your evaluation rubric. The AI scores each criterion (technical skills, communication, etc.), provides justification for scores, and highlights notable responses.

3. Results Compilation

The workflow structures the AI's evaluation into a standardized format, calculating overall scores and creating summary insights about each candidate's strengths and weaknesses.

4. Google Sheets Integration

All evaluation data is automatically logged in your Google Sheet, including scores, analysis, and direct quotes from the interview. This creates a searchable database of all candidates.

Pro tip: Add a step to notify recruiters via email/Slack when high-scoring candidates are identified to accelerate your hiring process.
