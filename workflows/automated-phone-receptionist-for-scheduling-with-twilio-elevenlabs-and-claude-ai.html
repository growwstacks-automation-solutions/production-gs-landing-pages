What This Workflow Does

This automated phone receptionist solution eliminates missed calls and scheduling bottlenecks by handling incoming calls 24/7 with AI. When customers call your business number, the system engages them in natural conversation to collect appointment details, check availability, and confirm bookings—all without human intervention.

The workflow combines Twilio's telephony infrastructure with ElevenLabs' realistic voice AI and Claude AI's conversational intelligence to create a seamless caller experience. It's particularly valuable for service businesses like salons, clinics, and consultancies that receive frequent scheduling requests via phone.

Sample conversation flow between caller and AI receptionist

How It Works

1. Call reception with Twilio

Twilio answers incoming calls and initiates the conversation flow. The system detects caller intent through DTMF tones or voice responses.

2. Natural language processing with Claude AI

Claude AI interprets caller requests, extracts key details (date, time, service type), and maintains context throughout the conversation.

3. Voice response generation with ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs converts Claude's text responses into natural-sounding speech using customizable voice profiles that match your brand.

4. Calendar integration

The system checks your connected calendar (Google Calendar, Calendly, etc.) for availability and books appointments directly.

5. Confirmation and follow-up

After booking, the system sends SMS/email confirmations to both the caller and your team with appointment details.
