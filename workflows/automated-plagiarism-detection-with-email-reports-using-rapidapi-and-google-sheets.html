What This Workflow Does

This automated plagiarism detection system solves the critical business problem of content originality verification. Manual plagiarism checks are time-consuming, inconsistent, and difficult to document. Our workflow automatically scans submitted content using RapidAPI's plagiarism detection services, logs results in Google Sheets for audit trails, and sends email alerts when potential issues are found.

Educational institutions, publishers, and marketing teams can process hundreds of documents daily with consistent quality control. The system flags content with similarity percentages above your threshold, identifies matching sources, and creates permanent records of all scans. This protects against legal risks while saving 80-90% of the time spent on manual verification.

How It Works

1. Content Submission Trigger

The workflow activates when new content arrives via webhook, email attachment, or form submission. Supported formats include Word documents, PDFs, and plain text. The system extracts the textual content for analysis while preserving the original file for reference.

2. API-Powered Plagiarism Check

The core functionality uses RapidAPI to connect with leading plagiarism detection services. The API scans billions of web pages, academic papers, and publications to identify matching content. You can adjust sensitivity thresholds to balance thoroughness with false positives.

3. Results Logging & Analysis

Detailed scan results automatically populate a Google Sheet with timestamps, match percentages, source URLs, and highlighted passages. This creates a searchable database of all verifications for compliance and quality assurance purposes.

4. Smart Alert System

When matches exceed your defined threshold, the system sends customized email alerts to relevant stakeholders. Notifications include the original content, matched sources, and similarity scores for quick review. Optional Slack or Teams notifications can be added.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n instance
