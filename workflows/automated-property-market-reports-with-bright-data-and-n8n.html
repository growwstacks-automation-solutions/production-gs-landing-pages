What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manual property market research by automatically collecting, analyzing, and reporting real estate data from multiple sources. It combines Bright Data's web scraping capabilities with n8n's workflow automation to deliver comprehensive market reports without manual data entry.

Real estate professionals spend 10-15 hours weekly compiling market data from disparate sources. This workflow reduces that to minutes by automatically tracking listing prices, inventory levels, days on market, and neighborhood trends - delivering formatted reports to your inbox or CRM on schedule.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

Bright Data scrapes property listings from configured sources (Zillow, Realtor.com, local MLS feeds) using custom selectors for price, features, and market metrics.

2. Data Processing

n8n nodes clean the data, remove duplicates, calculate derived metrics (price/sqft, YoY changes), and standardize formats across sources.

3. Report Generation

The system compiles data into PDF or spreadsheet reports with visualizations, highlighting key trends and anomalies for quick decision-making.

4. Distribution

Completed reports are emailed to stakeholders, saved to cloud storage, or pushed to CRM systems like Salesforce or HubSpot automatically.

Pro tip: Configure price change alerts to get notified immediately when properties in your target areas adjust their asking price.

Who This Is For

This automation delivers the most value for real estate agents, brokers, investors, and developers who need current market data to make informed decisions. Property managers can track rental market trends, while lenders can monitor collateral values across neighborhoods.
