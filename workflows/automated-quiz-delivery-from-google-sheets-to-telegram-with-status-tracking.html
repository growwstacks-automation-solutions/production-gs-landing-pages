Automated Quiz Delivery from Google Sheets to Telegram

Schedule and deliver interactive quizzes automatically with status tracking and refill notifications.

What This Workflow Does

Manually posting quizzes to your Telegram community is time-consuming and prone to errors. This automation solves that by connecting Google Sheets directly to Telegram, transforming your spreadsheet into an automated quiz delivery system. Each quiz question becomes an interactive poll delivered on schedule, with automatic status tracking to prevent duplicates.

The workflow reads pending quizzes from your Google Sheet, posts them as Telegram polls one by one, marks them as completed, and even sends notifications when you're running low on content. This ensures your community receives consistent engagement without manual intervention, perfect for educators, community managers, and content creators.

How It Works

1. Sheet Monitoring

The workflow regularly checks your Google Sheet for new quiz entries marked as pending. It reads the question, multiple-choice options, and any metadata you've included in your structured spreadsheet format.

2. Quiz Processing

Each pending quiz is formatted as a Telegram poll with the correct question structure and answer options. The system ensures proper formatting and handles any special characters or emojis you've included in your questions.

3. Telegram Delivery

The formatted quiz is posted to your specified Telegram group or channel as an interactive poll. Members can vote directly within the chat, and results are visible to everyone based on your privacy settings.

4. Status Tracking

Once successfully posted, the workflow updates the quiz status in your Google Sheet from "pending" to "completed." This creates an audit trail and prevents the same quiz from being sent twice.

5. Refill Notifications

When no pending quizzes remain, the system sends a notification to your admin channel, prompting you to add more content. This ensures you never run out of engagement material unexpectedly.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google account with Google Sheets access
  2. A Telegram bot token (created via @BotFather)
  3. The chat ID for your Telegram group or channel
  4. A separate notification channel ID for refill alerts
  5. A Google Sheet structured with quiz questions and options
  6. Basic understanding of n8n or similar automation platforms

Pro tip: Structure your Google Sheet with clear columns: Question, Option A, Option B, Option C, Option D, and Status. Use emojis like 🟨 for pending and ✅ for completed to make tracking visually intuitive.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets account with appropriate permissions
  3. Replace the placeholder sheet ID with your actual Google Sheet ID
  4. Set up environment variables for your Telegram bot token and chat IDs
  5. Test with a single quiz entry to verify the connection works
  6. Schedule the workflow to run at your preferred frequency
  7. Monitor the status column in your sheet to track delivery
