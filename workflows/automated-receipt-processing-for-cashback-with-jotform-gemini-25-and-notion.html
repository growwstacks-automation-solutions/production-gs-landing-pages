What This Workflow Does

Manual receipt processing for customer rewards, cashback programs, and loyalty points is a time-consuming, error-prone administrative burden. Employees waste hours each week reviewing submitted receipts, calculating eligible amounts, verifying totals, and updating spreadsheets—delaying customer rewards and creating frustration.

This automated workflow eliminates that entire manual process. When a customer submits a receipt through your Jotform, the system instantly extracts the data using AI, calculates the reward based on your business rules, validates the transaction, logs it in your Notion database, and sends confirmation emails—all within seconds instead of days.

The solution transforms your reward program from an administrative headache into a competitive advantage. Customers get instant gratification, your team focuses on strategic work instead of data entry, and you maintain perfect audit trails of every transaction without spreadsheet errors.

How It Works

1. Customer Submission via Jotform

Customers upload their receipt through your branded Jotform. The familiar form interface makes submission easy, while Jotform securely captures the image file and customer contact information. The workflow triggers immediately upon submission.

2. AI-Powered Receipt Analysis

The system sends the receipt image to OCR and AI services. Google Gemini analyzes the receipt content, identifying the merchant, date, total amount, and line items. The AI applies your business rules to determine eligibility and calculate the exact reward amount.

3. Validation & Business Logic

Conditional logic nodes verify the receipt meets all requirements: minimum purchase amounts, eligible merchants, valid dates, and non-duplicate submissions. If anything fails validation, the workflow routes it for manual review or sends an automated explanation to the customer.

4. Database Logging & Notification

Approved transactions create detailed records in your Notion database with all extracted data, calculated rewards, timestamps, and status. Simultaneously, the system sends personalized confirmation emails to the customer and internal notification to your team—all fully automated.

What You'll Need

  1. Jotform account with a form configured for receipt submission (file upload field required)
  2. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with webhook capabilities
  3. Google Gemini API key for AI receipt analysis
  4. Notion account with a database prepared for transaction logging
  5. Email service (Gmail, SMTP, or transactional email provider) for notifications
  6. Optional OCR service for enhanced receipt text extraction if needed

Pro tip: Start with a small test group of customers before full rollout. This lets you refine your validation rules and ensure the AI correctly interprets your most common receipt formats before scaling to all users.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance using the "Import from File" option.
  2. Configure credentials: Set up credentials for Jotform (API key), Google Gemini, Notion, and your email service in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update node settings: In the Jotform Trigger node, paste your form ID. In the Notion node, add your database ID. Update email addresses in the Email nodes.
  4. Set up webhook: Copy the webhook URL from the Jotform Trigger node in n8n and add it to your Jotform's webhook integration settings.
  5. Test thoroughly: Submit a test receipt through your Jotform and verify the entire flow—from AI analysis to Notion entry and email confirmation.
  6. Activate workflow: Once testing is complete, activate the workflow in n8n and share your Jotform link with customers.
