What This Workflow Does

This automated resume review system transforms your hiring process by using AI to analyze and summarize candidate qualifications. It eliminates the tedious manual review of hundreds of resumes by automatically extracting key information, scoring candidates against your job requirements, and presenting organized results in Google Sheets.

The system leverages OpenAI's natural language processing to understand resume content contextually - identifying relevant skills, experience levels, education credentials, and other qualifications. It then generates concise summaries and scores for each candidate, allowing recruiters and hiring managers to focus their attention on the most promising applicants.

How It Works

1. Resume Submission

Candidates submit resumes through your existing application channels (email, ATS, or form submissions). The system monitors these sources for new documents.

2. AI Analysis

Each resume is processed by OpenAI, which extracts and analyzes key information including work history, skills, education, and certifications. The AI evaluates how well the candidate matches your predefined job requirements.

3. Data Organization

The extracted information is structured and added to your Google Sheet, with columns for candidate scores, key qualifications, and AI-generated summaries. The sheet becomes your centralized review dashboard.

4. Notification

Your team receives alerts when new candidates are processed, with direct links to their analyzed profiles in the sheet. The system can also flag top candidates automatically based on your scoring thresholds.
