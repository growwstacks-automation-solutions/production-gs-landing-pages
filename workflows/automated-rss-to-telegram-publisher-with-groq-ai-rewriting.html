What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining active Telegram channels with fresh, engaging content. Instead of manually copying RSS feed items or forwarding raw content, the workflow automatically processes each new post through AI rewriting before publishing to your Telegram channels.

The system monitors multiple RSS feeds simultaneously, detects new posts, processes them through Groq's AI to rewrite and optimize the content, then formats the output for Telegram with proper formatting, hashtags, and link placement. This creates native-looking Telegram posts that perform better than simple RSS forwards.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your configured RSS feeds for new content at regular intervals. You can add multiple feeds from different sources that are relevant to your audience.

2. New Post Detection

When new content appears in any monitored feed, the workflow identifies it and extracts key elements like title, description, and publication date. It checks against previously processed items to avoid duplicates.

3. AI Content Rewriting

The core content passes through Groq's AI engine which rewrites it according to your specifications. This can include summarizing long articles, adjusting tone for your audience, adding relevant hashtags, or optimizing for mobile reading.

4. Telegram Post Formatting

The rewritten content gets formatted specifically for Telegram with proper message structure, link placement, and media attachments if available. The workflow handles Telegram's API requirements automatically.

5. Automated Publishing

Finally, the formatted post gets published to your designated Telegram channel(s). You can configure multiple channels and customize posting schedules if needed.
