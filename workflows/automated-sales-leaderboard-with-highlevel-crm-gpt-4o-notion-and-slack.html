What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of stagnant sales performance data by creating a dynamic, AI-enhanced leaderboard system. Instead of weekly manual spreadsheet updates, it automatically pulls the latest HighLevel CRM data, processes it through GPT-4o for insights, updates a Notion dashboard, and shares motivational updates in Slack.

Sales teams using this system typically see 20-40% faster response times to performance trends, as the AI identifies patterns humans might miss. The automated recognition in Slack keeps morale high, while the Notion integration turns raw numbers into actionable coaching opportunities.

How It Works

1. CRM Data Extraction

The workflow starts by pulling the latest sales metrics from HighLevel CRM, including deals closed, revenue generated, and activity metrics. It runs on your preferred schedule (daily/weekly/real-time).

2. GPT-4o Analysis

Raw data gets processed through GPT-4o to identify trends, streaks, and coaching opportunities. The AI generates personalized insights like "John is on a 5-day closing streak - suggest doubling down on his top performing vertical."

3. Notion Dashboard Update

The system updates a centralized Notion dashboard with current rankings, historical trends, and AI recommendations. This becomes the team's performance command center with linked playbooks and resources.

4. Slack Notifications

Curated leaderboard updates get posted to Slack with @mentions for top performers. The AI tailors messaging style based on team culture - from competitive hype to collaborative encouragement.

Pro tip: Configure different Slack channels for real-time wins (general) versus weekly deep dives (sales-team). The AI will adapt its tone accordingly.
