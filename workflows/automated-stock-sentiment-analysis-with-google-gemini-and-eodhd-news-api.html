What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow transforms how investors and traders analyze market sentiment by combining real-time financial news with advanced AI analysis. It continuously monitors the EODHD News API for stock-related articles, processes them through Google Gemini's powerful natural language processing, and delivers actionable sentiment scores.

The system eliminates hours of manual news monitoring and subjective interpretation. Instead of scanning dozens of sources, you receive quantified sentiment metrics that help identify emerging market trends before they're reflected in stock prices. This gives you a competitive edge in making data-driven investment decisions.

How It Works

Step 1: News Aggregation

The workflow pulls the latest stock market news from EODHD's comprehensive financial news API, filtering for your specified tickers or market sectors. This ensures you only analyze relevant content without information overload.

Step 2: AI Processing

Each article is processed by Google Gemini, which evaluates the language, tone, and context to determine positive, negative, or neutral sentiment. The AI considers nuanced factors like sarcasm, uncertainty, and comparative statements that simple keyword analysis would miss.

Step 3: Sentiment Scoring

The workflow assigns a numerical sentiment score (-1 to +1) to each article and calculates aggregate scores for each stock. This standardized metric allows easy comparison across different companies and time periods.

Step 4: Alert Generation

When sentiment scores cross predefined thresholds (either positive or negative), the system triggers alerts through your preferred notification channels, ensuring you never miss significant sentiment shifts.
