What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual work of sending purchase confirmations and follow-up emails after Stripe transactions. When a customer completes payment, the workflow automatically tags them in KlickTipp based on their purchase details, then triggers appropriate email sequences - receipts, download links, onboarding content, or upsell offers.

Ecommerce businesses waste hours each week manually processing these communications. This n8n workflow eliminates that repetitive work while ensuring every customer receives timely, personalized emails that improve their experience and increase repeat purchases.

The workflow connects Stripe payment events to KlickTipp email sequences

How It Works

1. Stripe payment trigger

The workflow monitors Stripe for completed checkout events, capturing key details like customer email, purchase amount, and product information.

2. Customer tagging in KlickTipp

Based on the purchase data, the system applies specific tags in KlickTipp - like "Premium Customer" for high-ticket buyers or "Product X Buyer" for specific items.

3. Email sequence activation

These tags trigger pre-built email sequences in KlickTipp, sending immediate confirmations followed by timed follow-ups tailored to the purchase type.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to segment customers by purchase value - sending VIP treatment sequences to high-value buyers while maintaining standard confirmations for others.
