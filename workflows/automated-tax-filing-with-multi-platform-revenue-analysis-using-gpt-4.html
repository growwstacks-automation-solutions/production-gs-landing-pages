What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the painful, time-consuming process of manually aggregating revenue data from multiple platforms and preparing it for tax filing. Ecommerce businesses, SaaS companies, and digital service providers often struggle with revenue scattered across Stripe, PayPal, Shopify, and bank accounts - each with different reporting formats and tax implications.

The workflow automatically pulls transaction data from all connected platforms, normalizes it into a consistent format, then uses GPT-4 to intelligently categorize each transaction according to your tax jurisdiction's rules. It generates ready-to-file reports while flagging any transactions that need manual review.

How It Works

Step 1: Multi-platform data collection

The workflow connects to your Stripe, PayPal, Shopify, and bank accounts via API to pull all transaction data for the period. It handles authentication, pagination, and error recovery automatically.

Step 2: Data normalization

Raw data from each platform gets transformed into a standardized format with consistent field names, currency conversion, and timezone normalization. This creates an apples-to-apples comparison across all revenue sources.

Step 3: AI-powered tax categorization

GPT-4 analyzes each transaction's description, amount, platform, and historical patterns to assign the correct tax category. The AI considers your specific business type, location, and any custom rules you've configured.

Step 4: Report generation

The system compiles all categorized transactions into formatted tax reports ready for your accountant or tax software. It highlights potential issues, calculates totals by category, and can even generate narrative explanations for unusual transactions.
