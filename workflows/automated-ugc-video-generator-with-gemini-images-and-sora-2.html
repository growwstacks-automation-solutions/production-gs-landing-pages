What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of creating authentic-looking user-generated content (UGC) videos at scale. Traditional UGC requires hiring influencers or collecting customer videos, which is time-consuming and expensive. This automation combines Google's Gemini for realistic product images with OpenAI's SORA 2 for lifelike video generation.

The system produces videos that appear to be genuine customer testimonials or product demonstrations, complete with realistic human elements and environments. This is particularly valuable for e-commerce brands, digital marketers, and content creators who need to showcase products in authentic settings without the logistical challenges of traditional UGC production.

Sample output showing realistic product demonstration video

How It Works

1. Product Information Input

The workflow begins by accepting product details either from your e-commerce platform, spreadsheet, or manual input. This includes product names, descriptions, key features, and any specific messaging you want highlighted in the video.

2. Gemini Image Generation

Google's Gemini AI creates realistic product images in various settings. The system generates multiple angles and usage scenarios based on your product type, with options to specify backgrounds, lighting conditions, and human interactions.

Example product images generated by Gemini AI

3. SORA 2 Video Synthesis

OpenAI's SORA 2 transforms the static images into dynamic videos, adding realistic human movements, product interactions, and environmental effects. The system can simulate different user demographics, settings, and scenarios based on your target audience.

4. Post-Production & Delivery

The final videos are automatically edited with transitions, captions, and branding elements. The workflow can deliver videos directly to your CMS, social media platforms, or cloud storage with metadata tagging for easy organization.

Pro tip: For best results, provide detailed product descriptions and specify your target audience demographics to generate the most authentic-looking UGC videos.
