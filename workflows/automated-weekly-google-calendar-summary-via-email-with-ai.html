What This Workflow Does

This automation creates a comprehensive weekly summary of your Google Calendar events, enhanced with AI analysis, and delivers it directly to your email inbox. Instead of manually reviewing your calendar each Monday, you'll receive a neatly formatted overview that highlights key meetings, time commitments, and even extracts action items from event descriptions.

The AI component transforms raw calendar data into actionable insights, helping you prepare for the week ahead with better context than a simple list of events. It can identify your busiest days, flag important meetings requiring preparation, and even analyze how your time is distributed between different types of activities.

How It Works

1. Calendar Data Extraction

The workflow connects to your Google Calendar via secure OAuth and retrieves all events for the upcoming week. It can be configured to include specific calendars or filter certain event types.

2. AI Analysis & Processing

An AI model processes the calendar data to identify patterns, prioritize events, and extract meaningful information from descriptions. This goes beyond simple date/time reporting to provide contextual understanding of your schedule.

3. Email Composition

The system generates a well-structured email with categorized events, time allocation breakdowns, and highlighted priorities. The formatting is optimized for quick scanning and includes visual elements like progress bars for time utilization.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run Sunday evenings so you receive your summary before the workweek begins.
