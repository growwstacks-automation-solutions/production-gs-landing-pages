What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining a consistent LinkedIn presence without daily manual effort. Professionals and businesses often struggle to post regularly due to time constraints, leading to inconsistent engagement and missed networking opportunities.

The workflow automatically publishes your pre-written LinkedIn content from Notion on a daily schedule. It pulls drafted posts from your Notion database, formats them for LinkedIn's requirements, and publishes them at your specified times—all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Content Preparation in Notion

You maintain a Notion database with your LinkedIn post drafts, including scheduled dates, post text, and any relevant tags or categories. Each entry is marked ready for publication when finalized.

2. Daily Content Check

The workflow runs each morning to check your Notion database for posts scheduled that day. It verifies each post meets LinkedIn's content guidelines and extracts all necessary elements.

3. Content Formatting

The system automatically formats your Notion content for LinkedIn's platform, handling character limits, link formatting, and hashtag placement according to your predefined rules.

4. Automated Posting

Formatted posts are sent to LinkedIn at your specified times. The workflow confirms successful publication and logs the activity back in Notion for tracking.

Pro tip: Create a Notion template for your LinkedIn posts to ensure consistent formatting and include all required fields for smooth automation.
