What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually organizing emails by leveraging GPT-4's natural language understanding to analyze incoming Gmail messages. The system automatically applies relevant labels like "Quotation", "Inquiry", or "Project Update" based on the email content.

Teams using this solution report saving 5-10 hours per week on email management while improving response times to important messages by 40%. The workflow maintains consistency in categorization that human sorting often lacks, especially across team members.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow activates when new emails arrive in your Gmail inbox, checking at regular intervals you specify (typically every 5-15 minutes).

2. Content Extraction

Key elements are extracted from each email including the subject line, body text, and sender information while preserving privacy by excluding attachments.

3. AI Analysis

GPT-4 processes the extracted content, identifying the primary intent and key topics using your predefined categories and examples.

4. Label Application

The workflow applies the appropriate Gmail label based on the AI's analysis, creating new labels if they don't already exist in your account.

5. Optional Notifications

Configure alerts for high-priority categories via Slack, SMS, or other channels to ensure timely responses to critical messages.
