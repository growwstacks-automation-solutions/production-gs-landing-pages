What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missed invoice payments by automatically detecting incoming invoices in Gmail and creating corresponding reminders in Google Tasks. Manual invoice tracking is error-prone and time-consuming, often leading to late fees and strained vendor relationships.

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for incoming invoices, extracts key details like due dates and payment amounts, then creates organized reminders in Google Tasks with all necessary information. This ensures you never miss a payment deadline while eliminating manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously scans your Gmail inbox for messages containing invoices. It identifies invoices based on keywords, sender patterns, and attachment types.

2. Data Extraction

For each detected invoice, the system extracts critical information including vendor name, invoice number, amount due, and payment due date. It processes both email body content and attachments.

3. Reminder Creation

The workflow creates a corresponding task in Google Tasks with all extracted details. Tasks are automatically categorized and prioritized based on due dates.
