What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of missed invoice payments by automatically creating Google Calendar reminders for upcoming Stripe invoice due dates. Instead of manually tracking payment deadlines or risking late payments, this workflow ensures you always have visibility into upcoming receivables.

The system monitors your Stripe account for new invoices, extracts the due dates, and creates corresponding calendar events with configurable reminder notifications. This creates a visual payment tracking system that integrates seamlessly with your existing calendar workflow.

How It Works

1. Stripe invoice monitoring

The workflow regularly checks your Stripe account for new invoices using the Stripe API. It identifies invoices with upcoming due dates and extracts key details like amount, client name, and payment deadline.

2. Due date processing

For each invoice found, the system calculates appropriate reminder times based on your configuration. You can set multiple reminders (e.g., 7 days before, 3 days before) to ensure adequate notice.

3. Calendar event creation

The workflow then creates Google Calendar events for each reminder, including all relevant invoice details in the event description. These events appear in your calendar with configurable colors to distinguish them from other appointments.

4. Notification triggers

Google Calendar's native reminder system handles notifications according to your preferences (email, pop-up, or mobile push). This means you'll receive alerts through channels you already use daily.
