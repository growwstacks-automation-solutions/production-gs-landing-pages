AI News Digest Agent: Auto News Summarizer & Email Newsletter

Automatically fetch, summarize, and email news headlines using GPT-4, NewsAPI, and Gmail. Perfect for content creators, marketers, and businesses.

What This Workflow Does

This AI News Digest Agent solves a critical problem for content creators, marketers, and business leaders: information overload. Manually scanning dozens of news sources, identifying relevant stories, writing summaries, and distributing them to subscribers consumes 5-10 hours weekly. This automation eliminates that manual labor while delivering superior results.

The system creates an intelligent news curation pipeline that automatically fetches breaking headlines from NewsAPI, processes them through GPT-4 for intelligent summarization, and delivers personalized email digests to your subscriber list via Gmail. It transforms raw news data into actionable insights that keep your audience informed and engaged.

Beyond simple automation, this workflow adds value through AI-powered analysis. Instead of just listing headlines, it provides context, identifies trends, and explains implications—turning information into intelligence. Whether you're running a professional newsletter, keeping your team updated, or providing industry insights to clients, this system scales your content creation without scaling your workload.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a continuous five-step cycle that runs on your schedule—every 10 minutes, hourly, or daily—ensuring your audience receives timely, relevant information.

Step 1: Automated News Collection

A schedule trigger initiates the workflow at your preferred frequency. The system connects to NewsAPI using your API key and fetches the latest headlines based on your configured parameters: specific topics, geographic regions, languages, and trusted sources. This ensures you get comprehensive coverage without manual monitoring.

Step 2: Intelligent Content Curation

The workflow filters and prioritizes incoming stories based on relevance, recency, and source credibility. You can configure it to focus on specific industries, exclude certain publications, or prioritize breaking news. This curation layer ensures your audience receives only the most valuable information.

Step 3: AI-Powered Summarization

Each selected story is processed through GPT-4, which generates concise 5-bullet summaries, extracts key insights, and provides contextual analysis. The AI understands nuance, identifies implications, and presents information in an easily digestible format that adds genuine value beyond simple aggregation.

Step 4: Subscriber Management

The system connects to your Google Sheets subscriber database, retrieving names, email addresses, and optional segmentation data (like interests or industries). This enables personalized delivery at scale, ensuring each recipient receives content tailored to their preferences.

Step 5: Automated Email Distribution

Personalized emails are generated using professional templates and sent via Gmail. Each email includes the recipient's name, curated summaries, and clear calls-to-action. The system handles all delivery logistics, including scheduling, formatting, and tracking.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run during off-peak hours (like early morning) so your digest arrives in subscribers' inboxes at the optimal time for engagement.

What You'll Need

  1. NewsAPI account: Free tier provides 100 requests/day—sufficient for most use cases. Upgrade for higher volume.
  2. OpenAI API access: Required for GPT-4 summarization. Costs vary based on usage but typically run $1-5/month for regular digests.
  3. Google Sheets: For managing your subscriber list with columns for Name, Email, and optional segmentation fields.
  4. Gmail account: Configured with OAuth2 for automated email sending. Consider using a dedicated newsletter address.
  5. n8n instance: Either n8n.cloud (hosted) or self-hosted version. The workflow is compatible with both.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement your AI News Digest Agent in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for NewsAPI, OpenAI, Google Sheets, and Gmail in n8n's credential management.
  3. Set up your Google Sheet with subscriber data (Name, Email, optional Interest categories).
  4. Customize news parameters in the NewsAPI node: select countries, categories, and sources relevant to your audience.
  5. Adjust the GPT-4 prompt to match your preferred summary style, tone, and length.
  6. Test with a small group before full deployment to ensure formatting and content meet expectations.
  7. Schedule the workflow for your preferred frequency and time zone.

Pro tip: Start with a daily digest for the first week to gather feedback, then adjust frequency based on subscriber preferences and content volume.
