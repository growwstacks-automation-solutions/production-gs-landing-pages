What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical business problem: understanding what customers are really saying in their feedback. Most companies collect surveys through Jotform but then face the manual, time-consuming task of reading through every response to identify patterns, complaints, and suggestions.

The workflow automatically processes each Jotform submission through Google Gemini AI to perform two key analyses: sentiment detection (positive, neutral, negative) and topic extraction (identifying main themes and keywords). The structured results are then saved to Google Sheets for easy reporting, trend analysis, and actionable insights.

Instead of spending hours manually categorizing feedback, you get instant analysis that helps you prioritize customer issues, measure satisfaction trends, and make data-driven decisions about product improvements and customer service.

How It Works

1. Jotform Trigger Capture

When a customer submits any Jotform (feedback form, survey, registration, etc.), the workflow automatically triggers. It captures all response data including text fields, ratings, and customer information.

2. Data Preparation & Formatting

The raw Jotform data is cleaned and formatted into a consistent structure that Google Gemini AI can effectively analyze. This ensures the AI receives well-organized input for accurate sentiment and topic analysis.

3. AI-Powered Sentiment Analysis

Google Gemini evaluates the emotional tone of each response, classifying it as positive, neutral, or negative with a confidence score. It also extracts key phrases that contributed to the sentiment determination.

4. Intelligent Topic Extraction

The AI identifies recurring themes and keywords across responses. For example, it might detect topics like "Pricing Concerns," "Feature Requests," or "Technical Issues" along with relevant keywords and importance scores.

5. Results Consolidation & Storage

The sentiment analysis and topic extraction results are merged into a comprehensive dataset and automatically written to Google Sheets. This creates a searchable, filterable database of analyzed feedback.

What You'll Need

  1. A Jotform account with forms collecting customer feedback, surveys, or reviews
  2. Google Gemini API access (available through Google AI Studio)
  3. A Google Sheets spreadsheet for storing analyzed results
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation
  5. Basic understanding of webhook configuration in Jotform

Pro tip: Start with a specific feedback form rather than trying to analyze all forms at once. This helps you refine the AI prompts and output structure before scaling to multiple feedback sources.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Jotform webhook by copying the webhook URL from n8n and adding it to your Jotform form's integrations
  3. Set up Google Gemini credentials in n8n using your API key from Google AI Studio
  4. Connect Google Sheets by authenticating with your Google account and specifying the target spreadsheet
  5. Test the workflow by submitting a test form and verifying the analysis appears in your Google Sheets
  6. Customize AI prompts if needed to better match your specific feedback terminology and business context
