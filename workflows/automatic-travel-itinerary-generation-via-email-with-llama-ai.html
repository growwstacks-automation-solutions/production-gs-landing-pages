Transform email inquiries into personalized travel plans instantly with AI-powered automation. Save hours on manual itinerary creation.

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how travel agencies handle customer inquiries by instantly converting email requests into complete, personalized travel itineraries. Instead of agents spending hours researching and compiling travel plans, this AI-powered system reads incoming emails, understands travel preferences and constraints, and generates professional itineraries within seconds.

The workflow addresses the critical pain point of delayed responses in the travel industry. When potential clients email asking for vacation ideas, they typically wait hours or even days for a reply. With this automation, they receive a detailed, customized travel plan almost immediately—dramatically increasing conversion rates and customer satisfaction while freeing your team to focus on complex bookings and relationship building.

How It Works

The system follows a logical sequence that mimics how a skilled travel agent would process a request, but at digital speed and scale.

Step 1: Email Capture & Analysis

The workflow monitors your designated email inbox for new messages containing travel-related keywords. When a relevant email arrives, it extracts key information: travel dates, budget range, number of travelers, destination preferences, and special requirements.

Step 2: AI Processing with Llama

The extracted details are sent to the Llama AI model, which analyzes the request against travel databases and creates a structured itinerary. The AI considers factors like travel time optimization, budget allocation, seasonal activities, and local regulations to generate practical suggestions.

Step 3: Itinerary Generation & Formatting

The AI's raw output is formatted into a professional travel itinerary complete with day-by-day schedules, accommodation suggestions, transportation options, activity recommendations, and estimated costs. The system can incorporate your agency's branding and standard templates.

Step 4: Automated Response & Follow-up

The completed itinerary is emailed back to the customer with a personalized message. The system can also trigger follow-up sequences, add the lead to your CRM, or notify a human agent for high-value opportunities requiring personal touch.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for travel agencies, tour operators, destination management companies, and corporate travel departments experiencing high inquiry volumes. It's particularly valuable for businesses offering package tours, weekend getaways, or standardized travel products where itinerary creation follows predictable patterns.

Small to medium-sized travel businesses benefit most, as they often lack the resources for 24/7 response teams but need to compete with larger players on service speed. Solo travel consultants can use this to appear more established and responsive than their actual team size would suggest.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with basic configuration
  2. Access to an email account (Gmail, Outlook, or IMAP-compatible service)
  3. Llama AI API access or compatible AI model endpoint
  4. Travel data sources or APIs for real-time pricing/availability (optional but recommended)
  5. Basic understanding of your travel products and standard itinerary formats

Pro tip: Start with a limited scope—perhaps just weekend packages to your top three destinations. This lets you refine the AI's responses before scaling to your full product range.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance via the workflow editor.
  2. Configure email connection: Set up the email trigger node with your agency's inquiry email credentials and filtering rules.
  3. Connect AI services: Add your Llama AI API keys and configure the prompt templates to match your agency's voice and style.
  4. Customize itinerary templates: Modify the output formatting to include your branding, contact information, and standard disclaimers.
  5. Test thoroughly: Send test emails from different addresses to ensure the system correctly parses requests and generates appropriate responses.
  6. Go live: Once satisfied with test results, activate the workflow and monitor initial customer interactions for fine-tuning.
