What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of ineffective meetings by providing data-driven feedback. Managers waste hours manually analyzing meetings, while teams often repeat the same unproductive patterns without structured improvement.

The workflow automatically retrieves meeting minutes from Google Drive, applies AI analysis across multiple effectiveness dimensions, and delivers constructive feedback directly to Slack. It transforms subjective impressions into actionable insights that improve meeting culture over time.

How It Works

Step 1: Retrieve Meeting Minutes

The workflow connects to Google Drive using OAuth and retrieves specified meeting documents either by URL or search parameters.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Natural language processing evaluates speaking time distribution, decision clarity, action items, and sentiment patterns to calculate an effectiveness score.

Step 3: Feedback Generation

The system generates structured feedback highlighting strengths and specific improvement opportunities in Japanese or English.

Step 4: Slack Delivery

Personalized feedback gets delivered to designated Slack channels with @mentions for relevant participants.
