What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of lead information getting lost or delayed between capture and action. When potential customers submit information through your Google Form, this workflow instantly transforms their responses into actionable Trello cards in your sales pipeline.

The system eliminates manual data entry, reduces response times, and ensures no lead falls through the cracks. Each submission becomes a visual card in Trello with all relevant details pre-populated, ready for your team to follow up efficiently.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Form for new submissions. As soon as someone completes the form, the automation is triggered with all their response data.

2. Data Processing

The system extracts key information from the form responses, such as contact details, service interests, and any custom fields you've set up. It can also perform initial data validation and formatting.

3. Trello Card Creation

Using Trello's API, the workflow creates a new card in your specified board and list. The card title typically includes the lead's name and primary interest, while the description contains all relevant details from the form.

Pro tip: Configure custom labels in Trello based on form responses to visually categorize leads by priority, service type, or other criteria.
