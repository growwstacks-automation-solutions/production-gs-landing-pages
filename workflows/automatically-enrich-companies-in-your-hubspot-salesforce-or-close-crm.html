What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automatically enriches company records in your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce or Close) with valuable business data. It solves the common problem of incomplete or outdated company information in your CRM by:

  • Identifying companies that need enrichment based on your criteria
  • Pulling fresh data from CompanyEnrich or similar enrichment services
  • Mapping the enriched data to the correct CRM fields
  • Updating records while preserving existing good data

The automation runs on a schedule you set (weekly by default) and handles all the API calls, data transformations, and error handling automatically. What normally takes hours of manual research happens automatically in the background.

How It Works

Step 1: Identify Companies Needing Enrichment

The workflow checks your CRM for companies that meet your enrichment criteria - typically those missing key data points or not updated recently.

Step 2: Extract Company Domains

It extracts company domains from CRM records, even if stored in different fields across different CRMs.

Step 3: Enrich Data via API

Each domain is sent to CompanyEnrich's API which returns standardized company data including firmographics, technographics, and other valuable insights.

Step 4: Map Data to CRM Fields

The workflow maps the enriched data to the appropriate fields in your specific CRM, handling the differences between HubSpot, Salesforce and Close.

Step 5: Update CRM Records

Finally, it updates your CRM records with the new data while preserving any existing manual overrides or notes.
