What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually adding captions to videos. Burn-in captions (permanently embedded text) are essential for accessibility and engagement, but creating them manually can take 4-5 times the video duration. The workflow automatically transcribes audio, formats captions, and renders new videos with perfectly timed text.

By connecting json2video's powerful rendering API with Zapier's automation platform, this solution handles the entire captioning process without manual intervention. It's particularly valuable for content creators publishing across multiple platforms where native caption support varies.

How It Works

1. Video submission trigger

The workflow starts when a new video is added to your designated storage platform (like Dropbox or Google Drive). Zapier detects the new file and prepares it for processing.

2. Audio extraction and transcription

The system extracts the audio track and sends it to a transcription service. Advanced speech recognition converts spoken words into accurate text with proper punctuation and timing data.

3. Caption formatting

The raw transcript is automatically formatted into readable captions - splitting long lines, adjusting timing, and applying your preferred styling (font, size, color, position).

4. Video rendering with json2video

The formatted captions are sent to json2video's API which composites them onto your original video frames. The API handles precise text positioning, anti-aliasing, and background shading for optimal readability.

5. Delivery to destination

The final captioned video is automatically saved to your specified location and can optionally trigger notifications or additional processing steps.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add your logo watermark during the rendering process for additional brand consistency.
