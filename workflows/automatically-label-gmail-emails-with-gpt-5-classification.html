What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the universal pain point of email overload by applying AI intelligence to your inbox. Instead of manually sorting messages or creating endless filters, the workflow uses GPT-5 to analyze each email's content and context, then applies appropriate Gmail labels automatically.

For businesses, this means customer inquiries get flagged as "Urgent" based on sentiment analysis, internal communications are categorized by department, and newsletters are archived without cluttering your primary inbox. How It Works

Step 1: New Email Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your Gmail inbox. It checks for unread messages at regular intervals (configurable from 1-60 minutes).

Step 2: Content Analysis

Email text is sent to OpenAI's API where GPT-5 analyzes language patterns, sentiment, and intent. The model considers factors like urgency indicators, question phrasing, and emotional tone.

Step 3: Classification Decision

Based on the analysis, the workflow assigns one of four primary labels: Urgent Action, Follow Up, Reference, or Newsletter. Sub-labels can be added for specific projects or clients.
