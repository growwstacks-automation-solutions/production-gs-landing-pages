What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the common problem of disorganized email attachments by automatically processing incoming Gmail messages, analyzing their attachments with GPT-4o AI, and saving them to Google Drive with intelligent, descriptive filenames.

Instead of dealing with vague filenames like "Document1.pdf" or "Scan123.jpg", the system generates searchable names incorporating sender information, document content, and context from the email. This transforms your document management from chaotic to systematic without manual effort.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Gmail messages

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your specified Gmail label or inbox. It checks for attachments and filters messages based on your criteria.

2. Extract and analyze attachments

The system downloads each attachment temporarily and sends it to GPT-4o for analysis. The AI examines both the file content and the email context to understand what the document represents.

3. Generate intelligent filenames

GPT-4o creates descriptive filenames incorporating relevant details like sender, date, document type, and key content identifiers. For example: "2024-06-15_AcmeCorp_ServiceContract_v3.pdf"

4. Save to organized Google Drive folders

The renamed files are automatically saved to your specified Google Drive location, with optional subfolders based on date, sender domain, or other logical groupings.
