What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common but time-consuming problem for businesses: creating professional, consistent product and marketing images. Instead of manually editing each photo in Photoshop or hiring expensive designers, this workflow uses APImage's AI API to automatically replace backgrounds and adjust lighting based on your description.

The system takes any image URL (from your e-commerce platform, cloud storage, or submitted via form) and a text prompt describing the desired background. The AI intelligently separates the subject, generates a new background that matches your description, and applies realistic lighting and shadows to create a natural-looking composite. The processed image is then downloaded or sent to your preferred storage destination.

This transforms what used to be a 15-30 minute manual editing task into a seconds-long automated process, enabling businesses to scale their visual content production without increasing costs or timelines.

Example transformation: Product photo background replaced from plain white to professional studio setting with proper lighting.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence that mimics what a human designer would do, but automated and scalable.

1. Input Collection

The process begins with a form trigger where users submit an image URL and background description. This can be replaced with automated triggers from e-commerce platforms, cloud storage events, or database updates for fully hands-off operation.

2. AI Processing

The image and prompt are sent to APImage's AI endpoint. The model performs three key operations simultaneously: precise subject segmentation, background generation based on your description, and lighting adjustment to match the new environment.

3. Quality Output

The AI returns a high-resolution image with the subject perfectly integrated into the new background. The system preserves fine details like hair, transparent materials, and complex edges that traditional tools often struggle with.

4. Delivery & Storage

The processed image is downloaded and can be automatically routed to Google Drive, Dropbox, your CMS, or directly to your e-commerce platform. Metadata can be added, and notifications sent to relevant teams.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in under 10 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Get your APImage API key from their dashboard and add it to the HTTP Request node.
  3. Test with a sample image by running the workflow manually with a product photo URL and background description.
  4. Customize the output by adding nodes to save processed images to your preferred storage service.
  5. Automate the trigger by replacing the manual form with webhooks from your e-commerce platform or scheduled runs.
