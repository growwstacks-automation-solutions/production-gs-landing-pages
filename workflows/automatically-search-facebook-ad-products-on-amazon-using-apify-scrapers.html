What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for ecommerce sellers - tracking competitor products advertised on Facebook and analyzing their performance on Amazon. Many successful Amazon products are heavily promoted through Facebook ads, but manually monitoring these connections is time-consuming.

The workflow automatically extracts product information from Facebook ads using Apify's powerful scraping tools, then searches for matching products on Amazon. This gives sellers real-time competitive intelligence about pricing, reviews and demand without manual research.

How It Works

1. Facebook Ad Scraping

The workflow starts by scraping product data from Facebook's Ad Library using Apify actors. It extracts product names, descriptions, images and engagement metrics from targeted ad campaigns.

2. Data Processing

Raw ad data is cleaned and standardized to create searchable product queries. The system removes duplicates and focuses on ads with clear product offerings rather than brand awareness campaigns.

3. Amazon Product Search

Each product identified from Facebook ads is automatically searched on Amazon. The workflow captures key metrics like price, review count, star rating and fulfillment method for competitive analysis.
