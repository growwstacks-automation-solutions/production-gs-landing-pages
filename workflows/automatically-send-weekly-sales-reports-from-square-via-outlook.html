What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming task of manually compiling and distributing weekly sales reports from Square. Retailers, restaurants, and service businesses using Square for payments often waste hours each week extracting transaction data, formatting reports, and emailing them to managers and stakeholders.

The workflow automatically connects to your Square account, pulls the relevant sales data for the specified period, formats it into a professional report, and sends it via Outlook to your designated recipients. It runs on a set schedule (typically Monday mornings) to ensure timely reporting without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates automatically every week at your specified time (usually early Monday before business hours). This ensures reports are ready when your team starts their week.

2. Square API Data Pull

The system securely connects to Square's API using your credentials to retrieve transaction data. It can filter by location, date range, payment type, or other parameters you specify.

3. Data Processing

Raw transaction data gets transformed into meaningful metrics: total sales, average order value, payment method breakdowns, refunds, and week-over-week comparisons. The system calculates these automatically.

4. Report Generation

The processed data gets formatted into an easy-to-read report with visual elements like charts and tables. You can customize the template to match your brand's look and feel.

5. Outlook Email Delivery

The completed report gets attached to an Outlook email and sent to your predefined distribution list. The email includes a summary of key findings and any custom messaging you've configured.

This automation benefits any Square merchant who needs to regularly share sales performance data with:
