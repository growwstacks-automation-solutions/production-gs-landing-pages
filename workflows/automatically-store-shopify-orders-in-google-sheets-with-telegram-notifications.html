What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking Shopify orders across multiple systems. Every time a new order comes in, the workflow automatically records all order details in a Google Sheet and sends an instant notification to your Telegram account.

E-commerce businesses waste countless hours copying order information between systems. This workflow eliminates that manual work while reducing errors and providing real-time visibility into your sales. The Google Sheet becomes a searchable database of all orders, perfect for accounting, customer service, or inventory planning.

How It Works

1. Shopify triggers on new orders

The workflow starts whenever a new order is placed in your Shopify store. It captures the complete order data including customer information, products, shipping details, and any custom fields.

2. Data transforms for Google Sheets

The raw Shopify data gets formatted into columns that match your Google Sheet structure. You can customize which fields to include - from basic order info to custom metafields.

3. Google Sheets update

The formatted order data gets appended to your designated Google Sheet. Each order creates a new row with timestamp, making it easy to sort and filter your sales history.

4. Telegram notification

Finally, a concise order summary gets sent to your Telegram account (or group). The message includes key details like order value, customer location, and products.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to highlight high-value orders or VIP customers in your Telegram notifications for priority handling.
