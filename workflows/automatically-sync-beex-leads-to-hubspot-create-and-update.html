What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of disconnected lead generation and CRM systems. When leads come into your Beex platform, they often need to be manually entered into HubSpot, creating delays and potential data errors.

The workflow automatically syncs new Beex leads to HubSpot as contacts, and updates existing HubSpot records when lead information changes in Beex. This ensures your sales team always has access to the most current lead data without manual intervention.

Example of Beex lead data automatically appearing in HubSpot

How It Works

Trigger: New or Updated Beex Lead

The workflow activates whenever a new lead is created or an existing lead is updated in your Beex account. This real-time trigger ensures no lead falls through the cracks.

Step 1: Check for Existing HubSpot Contact

The system first checks HubSpot to see if a contact already exists with the same email address. This prevents duplicate entries in your CRM.

Step 2: Create or Update HubSpot Record

Depending on whether the contact exists, the workflow either creates a new HubSpot contact or updates the existing one with the latest Beex lead information.

Step 3: Error Handling and Logging

The system includes robust error handling to retry failed syncs and logs all synchronization activity for troubleshooting and auditing.
