What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common productivity problem: task overload. When new items flood into your Todoist, you waste mental energy deciding what's important, when to do it, and how to phrase it clearly. This workflow eliminates that friction.

It acts as an intelligent assistant that monitors your Todoist for new tasks created in the last 5 minutes. For each task, it uses GPT-4.1-mini to analyze the content, rewrite it into a clear, actionable statement, assign a priority level (P1-P4) based on impact and urgency, and suggest a realistic due date that considers your existing workload.

The result is a consistently organized task list where everything is clearly defined, properly prioritized, and scheduled without overbooking your calendar. You get the benefits of a personal productivity coach without the manual effort.

How It Works

The workflow runs on a schedule you control (every 5, 15, or 60 minutes) and follows a precise sequence to transform raw tasks into organized action items.

1. Scheduled Trigger & Task Collection

A Schedule Trigger node initiates the workflow at your chosen interval. It then queries your Todoist account via the API to fetch all tasks created within the last 5 minutes. This ensures only fresh items are processed, avoiding redundant work on older tasks.

2. AI Analysis & Triage

Each new task is sent to the OpenAI GPT-4.1-mini model with specific instructions. The AI evaluates three key dimensions: business impact, urgency, and risk. It calculates a priority score and converts vague descriptions into imperative statements (e.g., "Research competitors" becomes "Compile a competitive analysis report with 5 key insights by EOD Friday").

3. Workload-Aware Scheduling

Before setting due dates, the workflow fetches your current open tasks from Todoist. The AI considers this existing workload when suggesting deadlines, preventing unrealistic scheduling. It aims to distribute work evenly and respect your capacity.

4. Task Update & Completion

The improved description, assigned priority, and calculated due date are pushed back to Todoist, updating the original task. The workflow logs all changes for transparency and continues to the next new task until all are processed.

Pro tip: The AI instructions (system prompt) included in the template are fully customizable. You can adjust the prioritization formula, description style, or scheduling rules to match your specific work patterns.
