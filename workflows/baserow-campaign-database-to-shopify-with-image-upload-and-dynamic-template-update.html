What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges your Baserow campaign database with your Shopify store, eliminating manual updates and ensuring consistent branding across all marketing channels. The workflow automatically syncs product details, promotional content, and high-quality images from Baserow to Shopify while maintaining proper categorization and metadata.

Beyond basic data transfer, the solution includes dynamic template updating capabilities. When your marketing team makes changes to campaign templates in Baserow, the workflow detects these modifications and applies them to your Shopify storefront automatically. This enables real-time campaign testing and iteration without developer involvement.

How It Works

1. Baserow Data Extraction

The workflow begins by querying your Baserow campaign database for new or updated records. It retrieves all relevant campaign details including product information, promotional copy, pricing adjustments, and image references.

2. Image Processing

Referenced images are downloaded from Baserow, optimized for web display, and converted to Shopify's required formats. The workflow handles resizing, compression, and proper file naming conventions automatically.

3. Shopify Product Updates

Product listings are updated with the new campaign information, including modified descriptions, temporary pricing, and inventory allocations for promotional items.

4. Template Application

The workflow applies the designated campaign template to relevant product collections and pages. Conditional logic ensures the right template version is used based on campaign parameters like audience segment or promotion type.

5. Verification & Reporting

After updates are complete, the workflow verifies all changes were applied correctly and sends a summary report to your team. Any errors trigger automatic retries or notifications for manual intervention.

Pro tip: Use Baserow's collaborative features to have your marketing team review and approve campaign changes before they go live on Shopify.
