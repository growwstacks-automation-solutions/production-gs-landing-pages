What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually reviewing stacks of resumes for job openings. Instead of spending hours reading each application, you can upload up to 20 resumes at once and let Google Gemini AI analyze them automatically.

The workflow extracts key information like skills, experience, education, and qualifications from each resume, then organizes the analyzed data into a structured Google Sheet. This gives you an instant, sortable overview of all candidates, allowing you to quickly identify the most qualified applicants based on your specific criteria.

How It Works

1. Resume Upload

Upload multiple PDF resumes (up to 20 at a time) to a designated folder or directly into the workflow. The system accepts standard resume formats and can process them in batches.

2. AI Analysis

Google Gemini AI reads each resume, extracting and categorizing key information. It identifies skills, work history durations, education levels, certifications, and other relevant qualifications based on your configured criteria.

3. Data Organization

The extracted information is structured into consistent columns in Google Sheets, with each candidate's data in a separate row. This creates a standardized format for easy comparison across applicants.

4. Results Delivery

The completed analysis appears in your Google Sheet, ready for review. You can sort, filter, or add your own notes alongside the AI-generated data to streamline your hiring decisions.
