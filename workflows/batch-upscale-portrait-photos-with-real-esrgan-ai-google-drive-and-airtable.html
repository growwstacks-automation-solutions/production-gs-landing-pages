What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually enhancing portrait photos at scale. Many businesses need to improve image quality for professional directories, marketing materials, or client presentations, but traditional editing requires specialized skills and significant time investment.

The workflow automatically retrieves portrait images from Airtable records, processes them through Real-ESRGAN's advanced AI upscaling technology, and stores the enhanced versions in Google Drive while updating the original records. This eliminates hours of manual work while ensuring consistent, high-quality results across all images.

How It Works

1. Retrieves portrait photos from Airtable

The workflow starts by querying your Airtable base for records containing portrait images that need enhancement. It filters for new or unprocessed photos based on your specified criteria.

2. Processes images through Real-ESRGAN AI

Each identified image gets sent to the Real-ESRGAN AI model, which analyzes and enhances the photo. The AI increases resolution while preserving facial features, reducing noise, and improving overall clarity.

3. Stores enhanced versions in Google Drive

The upscaled images are automatically uploaded to your designated Google Drive folder with standardized naming conventions. The system maintains your organizational structure while adding the enhanced versions.

4. Updates Airtable records

Finally, the workflow updates each processed record in Airtable with the new Google Drive link to the enhanced image. This creates a complete audit trail and makes the improved photos immediately available to your team.
