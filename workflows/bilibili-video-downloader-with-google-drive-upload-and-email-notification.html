What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually monitoring and downloading videos from Bilibili, China's premier video sharing platform. Businesses targeting Chinese markets or analyzing competitor content often need to systematically capture videos for market research, competitive intelligence, or content repurposing.

The workflow automatically downloads specified Bilibili videos through API integration, uploads them to a designated Google Drive folder for secure storage and team access, then sends email notifications with video details to relevant stakeholders. This eliminates hours of manual downloading and organizing while ensuring no important content is missed.

How It Works

1. Video Identification

The workflow starts by identifying target videos either through direct Bilibili URLs or by searching for specific keywords/channels. You can configure it to monitor particular creators or search terms relevant to your business needs.

2. Automated Download

Using Bilibili's API, the system downloads the video files in your preferred quality and format. The automation handles authentication and follows platform guidelines to ensure compliant downloading.

3. Google Drive Upload

Downloaded videos are automatically uploaded to your specified Google Drive folder with organized naming conventions. The workflow creates structured folders by date, channel, or other metadata for easy retrieval.

4. Notification System

Customizable email alerts are sent to your team with video details including title, source URL, and Drive link. Notifications can include additional metadata like view counts or upload dates when available.
