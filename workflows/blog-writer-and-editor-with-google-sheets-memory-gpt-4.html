AI Blog Writer with Google Sheets Memory

Automate blog writing and editing with GPT-4, store results in Google Sheets, and track session history for consistent content creation.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of inconsistent, time-consuming content creation by providing an intelligent AI assistant that writes and edits blog posts while maintaining memory across sessions. Instead of starting from scratch each time, the system tracks your content history, preferences, and previous outputs in Google Sheets, creating a growing knowledge base that improves with every use.

The workflow enables content teams, marketers, and business owners to produce high-quality written material at scale without sacrificing brand voice or consistency. Whether you need to generate new blog posts from scratch, rewrite existing content for different platforms, or maintain a coherent content series, this automation handles the heavy lifting while you focus on strategy and refinement.

How It Works

1. Content Request & Session Tracking

You provide a topic or paste existing text through a chat interface. The system assigns a unique session ID that tracks all related content requests, maintaining context across multiple interactions for coherent content series.

2. AI Processing with Context Awareness

The workflow checks Google Sheets for previous content in the same session, providing the AI with historical context. GPT-4 then generates or rewrites content based on this context, ensuring consistency with previous materials.

3. Structured Output & Storage

The AI returns structured content including the blog body, suggested headlines, and key takeaways. All outputs are automatically appended to Google Sheets with session metadata, creating a searchable content library.

4. Quality Control & Branching Logic

The system includes conditional logic that can trigger different actions based on content quality, session length, or specific requirements. This might include sending content for human review, adjusting AI parameters, or stopping generation when quality thresholds are met.

  4. Basic understanding of your brand voice and content requirements

Pro tip: Start with a small sample of your best existing content in Google Sheets to train the AI on your brand voice. Include examples of headlines, body copy, and calls-to-action that match your style.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure OpenAI credentials with your API key in the n8n credentials manager
  3. Set up Google Sheets connection using OAuth2 and select your target spreadsheet
  4. Prepare your Google Sheet with column headers: Session ID, Row Count, Output, Timestamp
  5. Test with simple prompts to verify the workflow connects properly to all services
  6. Adjust parameters like temperature and max tokens to match your content style
  7. Implement quality checks by adding conditional nodes for content review thresholds
