What This Workflow Does

This automated Brand DNA Generator solves the challenge of manually researching and documenting a company's core brand identity. Marketing teams often spend weeks collecting brand assets, analyzing messaging, and creating guidelines - a process this workflow completes in minutes.

The system gathers data from multiple online sources, uses AI to identify key brand patterns and elements, then organizes everything into a structured Notion database. This creates a living document of your brand identity that updates automatically as your online presence evolves.

How It Works

1. Brand Input Collection

A JotForm collects basic brand information including company name, website, social media profiles, and key competitors. This triggers the automated research process.

2. Online Presence Scanning

The workflow uses Google Search API to scan your website, social profiles, and mentions across the web. It collects text content, visual elements, and engagement patterns.

3. AI-Powered Analysis

Gemini AI processes the collected data to identify your brand's visual style, tone of voice, core values, and unique differentiators. It compares these against competitors to highlight what makes your brand distinct.

4. Notion Organization

The analyzed brand elements are automatically structured into a Notion database with sections for visual identity, messaging framework, brand voice guidelines, and competitive positioning.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for marketing agencies managing multiple client brands, startups establishing their identity, and growing businesses needing consistent branding across teams. It's particularly valuable for companies with distributed teams who need centralized brand guidelines.
