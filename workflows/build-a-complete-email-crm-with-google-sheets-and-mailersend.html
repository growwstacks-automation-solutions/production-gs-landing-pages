What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow transforms Google Sheets into a fully functional email CRM system by integrating with MailerSend. It eliminates the need for expensive marketing platforms like HubSpot or Customer.io by leveraging tools you likely already use. The automation handles contact management, email sequencing, and engagement tracking - all from a simple spreadsheet interface.

Businesses can save thousands annually on CRM software while maintaining complete control over their customer data. The workflow automatically sends personalized emails based on spreadsheet updates, schedules campaigns in advance, and logs engagement metrics back to your Sheets for analysis. It's particularly valuable for startups, small businesses, and teams that need lightweight but powerful email automation.

How It Works

1. Contact Management in Google Sheets

Your Google Sheet serves as the contact database, with columns for email addresses, names, segmentation tags, and other relevant customer data. The workflow monitors this sheet for new or updated contacts.

2. Triggering Email Sequences

When new contacts are added or specific conditions are met (like a date field reaching today), the workflow triggers appropriate email sequences through MailerSend's API. You can configure different email templates for various customer segments.

3. Personalization and Dynamic Content

The system pulls data from your spreadsheet to personalize each email. Merge tags insert customer names, custom fields, or other relevant information into the email content for higher engagement.

4. Engagement Tracking

MailerSend tracks opens, clicks, and other engagement metrics, which the workflow then logs back to your Google Sheet. This creates a complete history of customer interactions directly in your CRM.

Pro tip: Use separate tabs in your Google Sheet for different contact segments (leads, customers, inactive users) to easily manage distinct email sequences for each group.
