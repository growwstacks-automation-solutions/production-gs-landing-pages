What This Workflow Does

This template solves a common business problem: employees and customers struggle to find answers in extensive documentation, wikis, or knowledge bases. Searching through PDFs, help articles, or internal guides is time-consuming and often yields incomplete or outdated information.

The workflow creates an intelligent documentation expert using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology. Instead of a generic AI that might guess or hallucinate answers, this system becomes a specialist on your specific content. It processes your documents, understands their context, and provides precise, sourced answers to questions in natural language.

Think of it as hiring a librarian who has memorized every page of your manuals and can instantly provide the exact information needed, complete with references to where it came from. This eliminates support bottlenecks, accelerates onboarding, and ensures consistent information sharing across your organization.

How It Works

The system operates in two distinct phases: knowledge preparation and intelligent querying.

1. Document Processing & Knowledge Indexing

The workflow first ingests your documentation from various sources (websites, PDFs, wikis). It breaks down large documents into manageable chunks, creates numerical representations (embeddings) of each chunk's meaning, and stores these in a searchable vector database. This process happens once or whenever documents are updated.

2. Intelligent Question Answering

When a user asks a question, the system converts the question into the same numerical format, searches the vector database for the most relevant document chunks, and feeds only those specific passages to the AI model (Google Gemini). The AI then generates an answer grounded exclusively in your provided content, preventing speculation.

3. Response Delivery & Context Management

The system maintains conversation context, allowing follow-up questions, cites its sources for verification, and can be integrated into chat platforms (Slack, Teams) or web interfaces. It learns from interactions to improve future retrievals.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement your documentation expert chatbot:

  1. Import the template – Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Gemini credentials – Add your Google AI API key to the three Gemini nodes in the workflow
  3. Set document sources – Modify the HTTP Request nodes to point to your documentation URLs or upload your documents
  4. Run the indexing process – Execute the manual trigger to process and index your documents (15-20 minutes for typical documentation)
  5. Test the chatbot – Activate the workflow and use the chat trigger URL to ask questions about your documentation
  6. Deploy to your team – Share the chat URL or integrate with your communication platforms
