What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms Google Gemini into a powerful personal assistant that manages your email and calendar through natural language commands. Instead of manually composing emails or creating calendar events, you can simply tell your assistant what needs to be done in plain English.

The workflow connects Google Gemini's AI capabilities with your Gmail and Calendar through MCP (Message Control Protocol), creating a seamless bridge between voice/text commands and actual productivity actions. It handles complex requests like scheduling meetings with multiple participants, sending follow-up emails, or finding available time slots in your schedule.

How It Works

1. Command Interpretation

Google Gemini processes your natural language input (either typed or spoken) and extracts the key intent and parameters. For example, "Schedule a 30-minute catch-up with Alex next Wednesday at 3pm" would identify the action (schedule meeting), duration (30 minutes), participant (Alex), and time/date.

2. Data Validation

The workflow checks your calendar for conflicts, verifies email addresses, and ensures all required information is present. If anything is missing or unclear, the assistant will ask follow-up questions before proceeding.

3. Action Execution

Once validated, the system creates the calendar event in Google Calendar and sends any necessary email invitations through Gmail. All actions are logged for reference and can include custom templates for different types of meetings or communications.
