What This Workflow Does

Manual analysis of customer support tickets is time-consuming and often misses critical patterns. This automation solves that by creating a comprehensive analytics dashboard that automatically processes, categorizes, and analyzes support tickets using AI. It transforms raw ticket data into actionable insights, helping support teams identify trends, prioritize urgent issues, and improve response times.

The workflow monitors your support channels, extracts key information using ScrapeGraphAI, organizes everything in Google Sheets for easy reporting, and sends real-time alerts to Slack when critical issues are detected. This means you can spot recurring problems before they escalate, measure team performance accurately, and maintain high customer satisfaction without manual data crunching.

How It Works

1. Ticket Collection & AI Analysis

The workflow begins by collecting support tickets from your chosen channels. Using ScrapeGraphAI, it analyzes ticket content to automatically categorize issues, detect customer sentiment, and assign priority levels based on urgency indicators and keywords.

2. Data Processing & Enrichment

Each ticket is processed to extract key metrics: response time, resolution time, satisfaction indicators, and issue categories. The system calculates sentiment scores and identifies whether tickets require immediate attention or can follow standard procedures.

3. Google Sheets Dashboard Update

All analyzed data flows into a structured Google Sheets dashboard with columns for timestamp, ticket ID, category, priority, sentiment score, response times, and status. This creates a living document that serves as your single source of truth for support analytics.

4. Real-Time Slack Alerts

When high-priority tickets are detected or performance thresholds are breached, automated alerts are sent to designated Slack channels. This ensures your team is immediately aware of critical issues without constantly monitoring dashboards.

5. Scheduled Reporting

The workflow runs on a schedule you define (hourly, daily, or weekly) to provide consistent, up-to-date insights. It can also generate summary reports highlighting key trends, top issues, and team performance metrics.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with community nodes enabled
  2. ScrapeGraphAI API access for intelligent ticket analysis
  3. Google Sheets account with API permissions to create and update spreadsheets
  4. Slack workspace with webhook or app permissions for sending alerts
  5. Access to your support system (help desk software, email, or ticketing platform)
  6. Basic understanding of your support workflow and key metrics to track

Pro tip: Start by automating analysis for your highest-volume support channel first. Once that's working smoothly, expand to additional channels. This incremental approach reduces complexity and helps your team adapt gradually.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for ScrapeGraphAI, Google Sheets, and Slack in n8n's credential management.
  3. Update the ScrapeGraphAI nodes with URLs to your support portal or ticket sources.
  4. Customize the Google Sheets template by updating the spreadsheet ID and sheet name to match your preferred structure.
  5. Set up Slack alerts by adding your webhook URL and defining which channels should receive notifications.
  6. Test the workflow with a few sample tickets to ensure data flows correctly through all steps.
  7. Schedule execution based on your support volume—start with daily runs, then adjust frequency as needed.
