What This Workflow Does

Traditional employee recognition programs often suffer from inconsistent participation, delayed acknowledgments, and manual tracking that consumes HR resources. This automation transforms peer recognition into a seamless, AI-enhanced process that boosts engagement while saving administrative time.

The system automatically processes Jotform submissions where employees recognize colleagues' contributions, uses GPT-4 to analyze the feedback and generate personalized appreciation messages, then logs all data in Google Sheets for real-time tracking and reporting. This creates a virtuous cycle where recognition happens immediately, feels more meaningful, and provides actionable insights.

How It Works

1. Peer Submission via Jotform

Employees submit recognition through a customized Jotform that captures the recipient, contribution type, and specific examples. The form includes validation to ensure complete data and can be branded with your company's colors and values.

2. AI Analysis with GPT-4

Each submission passes through GPT-4 which extracts key themes, assesses the significance of the contribution, and generates a personalized appreciation message. The AI can identify patterns like frequent recognition of specific skills or spot emerging leaders.

3. Automated Tracking in Google Sheets

The system logs each recognition with timestamps, categorization, and AI-generated insights in a structured Google Sheet. This creates a searchable database for performance reviews and enables real-time dashboards showing recognition trends across teams.

4. Notification & Reward Processing

Recipients automatically receive their personalized appreciation message via email or Slack. For significant achievements, the system can trigger reward workflows like gift cards or extra PTO days based on predefined rules.

Pro tip: Configure your Jotform to include dropdowns for company values or competencies to make the AI analysis even more targeted.
