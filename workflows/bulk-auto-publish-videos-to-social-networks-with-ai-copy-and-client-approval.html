What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of preparing and publishing video content across multiple social platforms. Marketing teams and content creators often waste hours manually uploading videos, writing platform-specific captions, and coordinating approvals before publishing.

The workflow automatically processes batches of videos from a Google Drive folder, generates optimized captions using AI for each platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, etc.), creates an approval queue for clients or managers, and schedules posts once approved. It eliminates repetitive manual work while maintaining quality control through the approval step.

Example of AI-generated platform-specific captions for the same video content

How It Works

1. Video file detection

The workflow monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new video files. When videos are added, it extracts metadata including file names, descriptions, and any existing tags.

2. AI caption generation

Using AI services like OpenAI or Gemini, the system analyzes each video's content and generates multiple variations of captions optimized for different platforms - shorter punchy versions for Instagram, professional tones for LinkedIn, etc.

3. Approval workflow

The system creates an approval task in your project management tool (like Trello or Asana) or sends an email to designated approvers with the video preview and generated captions. Approvers can request edits or approve with one click.

4. Scheduled publishing

Once approved, videos are automatically uploaded to the specified social platforms with their optimized captions at your predetermined schedule. The workflow logs all actions and sends completion notifications.
