This automation transforms the tedious process of manual resume screening into an efficient AI-powered pipeline. How It Works

1. Resume Ingestion

The system accepts resumes from multiple sources - email attachments, applicant tracking systems, or LinkedIn profiles. It extracts text while preserving formatting and structure, handling PDFs, Word docs, and even scanned documents through OCR.

2. Job Description Analysis

GPT-4 parses your job description to identify must-have skills, nice-to-have qualifications, and implicit requirements. It creates a weighted scoring matrix that goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand context and competency levels.

3. AI Matching & Scoring

Each resume is evaluated against the job matrix, with scores for hard skills, experience relevance, education, and soft skills indicators. The AI identifies transferable skills and equivalent experience that might be missed by human screeners.

Pro tip: For best results, include 3-5 "cultural fit" keywords in your job description that reflect your company values. The AI will look for supporting evidence in candidates' work history and project descriptions.

4. Shortlist Generation

Candidates are ranked by overall fit score, with explainable AI notes highlighting why each was selected. The system can automatically schedule interviews with top candidates or feed data into your ATS.
