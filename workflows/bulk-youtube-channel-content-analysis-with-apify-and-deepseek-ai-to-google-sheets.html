What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for content creators, marketers, and educators who need to analyze competitor YouTube channels at scale. Instead of manually watching dozens of videos, this workflow automatically extracts key data points from YouTube channels and processes them through DeepSeek AI to generate actionable insights.

The system pulls video metadata, transcripts, engagement metrics, and content patterns from target YouTube channels, then organizes everything into structured Google Sheets with AI-generated summaries, topic clusters, and performance trends. This gives you competitive intelligence without the manual research grind.

How It Works

1. Channel URL Collection

The workflow starts by accepting a list of YouTube channel URLs you want to analyze. You can input these manually or connect to a spreadsheet containing multiple channel links.

2. Apify Data Extraction

Apify scrapes each YouTube channel to collect video titles, descriptions, view counts, engagement metrics, and transcripts. This happens automatically for all videos in the specified date range.

3. DeepSeek AI Processing

The raw YouTube data gets sent to DeepSeek AI which analyzes content patterns, identifies recurring topics, extracts key themes, and generates executive summaries of each channel's content strategy.

4. Google Sheets Organization

All processed data gets structured into a Google Sheet with multiple tabs for different analysis dimensions - video performance, topic clusters, sentiment analysis, and competitive benchmarking.
