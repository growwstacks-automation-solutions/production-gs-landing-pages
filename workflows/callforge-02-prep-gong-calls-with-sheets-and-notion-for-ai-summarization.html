CallForge: AI Gong Sales Call Processor

Automatically extract, enrich, and prepare Gong.io call transcripts for AI-driven sales insights and summaries.

What This Workflow Does

Sales teams using Gong.io face a common challenge: they have hours of valuable call recordings and transcripts, but turning this raw data into actionable insights requires manual work that's time-consuming and inconsistent. CallForge solves this by automating the entire process of preparing Gong call data for AI analysis.

This workflow automatically extracts call recordings, metadata, and transcripts from Gong.io, removes duplicate entries by checking Notion, enriches the data with product information from Google Sheets and competitor insights from Notion, cleans up transcripts for optimal AI processing, and delivers prepared data to an AI call processor for intelligent summarization. The result is consistent, enriched call data ready for AI-powered insights without manual intervention.

CallForge workflow architecture showing the complete data pipeline from Gong extraction to AI delivery

How It Works

1. Gong Data Extraction

The workflow connects to your Gong.io account via API and retrieves new call recordings, complete with metadata including meeting links, duration, participants, and full transcripts. It filters for relevant calls based on your criteria and prepares the raw data for processing.

2. Duplicate Prevention

Before processing, the system queries your Notion database to check if the call has already been analyzed. This prevents redundant processing and ensures each call is only analyzed once, saving computational resources and maintaining data integrity.

3. Data Enrichment

The workflow pulls integration details from Google Sheets (product information, pricing, features) and competitor insights from Notion databases. It merges this contextual data with the call transcript, giving the AI system a comprehensive understanding of what was discussed.

4. Transcript Preparation

Raw transcripts are cleaned and formatted for optimal AI processing. This includes correcting common mispronunciations, standardizing terminology, and structuring the data to reduce prompt complexity, resulting in more accurate AI outputs.

5. AI Delivery

The enriched, cleaned transcript is delivered to your AI processing workflow (like OpenAI or Azure AI) where it generates structured summaries, identifies key insights, extracts action items, and provides coaching recommendations for sales teams.
