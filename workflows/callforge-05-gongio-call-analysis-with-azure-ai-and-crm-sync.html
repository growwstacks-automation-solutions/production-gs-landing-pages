What This Workflow Does

This workflow automates the analysis of sales calls recorded in Gong.io, using Azure AI to extract key insights and sync them with your CRM system. It solves the problem of manual, time-consuming call analysis by automatically identifying customer pain points, competitor mentions, key discussion topics, and action items.

The system processes Gong.io call transcripts through multiple AI agents specialized for sales, marketing, and product insights. It then structures this information and pushes relevant data to your CRM (like Salesforce) and collaboration tools (like Notion), while notifying relevant teams via Slack.

Overview of the AI-powered call analysis workflow

How It Works

1. Call Data Retrieval

The workflow starts by retrieving call transcripts and metadata from Gong.io. It normalizes the data and corrects common mispronunciations (like "n8n").

2. AI Analysis

Three specialized AI agents analyze each call:

  • Sales Agent: Extracts objections, pain points, competitors, and next steps
  • Marketing Agent: Identifies recurring topics and content opportunities
  • Product Agent: Captures feature requests and technical discussions

3. Data Processing

The workflow structures the AI insights and handles API rate limits gracefully, automatically retrying if Notion or other services hit limits.

4. CRM Integration

Key sales insights are pushed to your CRM system, creating detailed records of each call's takeaways directly in the opportunity record.
