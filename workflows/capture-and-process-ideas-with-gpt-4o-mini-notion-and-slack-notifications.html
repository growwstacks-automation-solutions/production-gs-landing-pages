What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of lost or poorly documented ideas within teams. When employees, customers, or partners submit ideas through any channel, the workflow captures them, enriches them with AI analysis, organizes them in Notion, and notifies relevant stakeholders via Slack.

The system transforms unstructured ideas into actionable items with clear titles, categorized tags, estimated impact, and other metadata. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures no valuable concept falls through the cracks due to disorganized processes.

How It Works

1. Idea Capture

The workflow begins when an idea is submitted through a webhook, form, or connected app. This triggers the automation to process the raw input, whether it's text, voice memo, or attached document.

2. AI Enrichment

GPT-4o-mini analyzes the submission to extract key components: a concise title, relevant tags, potential impact score, and a standardized summary. The AI also identifies related existing ideas from your Notion database.

3. Notion Organization

The enriched idea gets formatted into a new Notion page with all extracted metadata. The system automatically files it in the correct database with proper relations to other projects or categories.

4. Team Notification

Based on the idea's tags and content, the workflow determines which team members should be notified and sends them a formatted Slack message with key details and a direct link to the Notion page.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits any team that regularly generates and evaluates ideas: product managers collecting feature requests, marketing teams brainstorming campaigns, R&D departments tracking innovations, or HR teams gathering employee suggestions.

It's particularly valuable for distributed teams who need structured collaboration tools and managers who want to quantify and prioritize incoming ideas without manual processing.
