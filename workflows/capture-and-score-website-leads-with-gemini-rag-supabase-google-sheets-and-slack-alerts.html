What This Workflow Does

Most website chatbots are simple FAQ tools that miss sales opportunities. This automation transforms your website chat into a complete lead generation and qualification system. When visitors ask questions, an AI agent answers using your company knowledge base (RAG), but first requires their name and email—turning anonymous browsers into captured leads.

The system doesn't stop at capture. It analyzes conversations for buying signals, scores leads based on urgency and intent, syncs everything to Google Sheets as a lightweight CRM, and sends instant Slack alerts when high-potential leads appear. An automated scoring engine runs every 10 minutes, reviewing inactive conversations and assigning Hot/Warm/Cold statuses so your sales team knows exactly who to contact first.

How It Works

1. Identity-Gated Chat Conversation

The AI chatbot politely requires name and email before answering substantive questions. This converts anonymous traffic into identifiable leads immediately. Visitor identity is stored in Supabase with session tracking.

2. Knowledge-Based Answers via RAG

Using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with your Supabase vector store, the AI provides accurate answers from your documentation, product info, and FAQs. Cohere reranking ensures the most relevant information surfaces.

3. Lead Creation & Deduplication

The system checks if the email exists in your Google Sheets CRM. New leads create rows; returning leads update existing records with fresh conversation data, preventing duplicate entries.

4. Real-Time Intent Detection & Slack Alerts

When visitors ask about pricing, request demos, or express urgency, the system detects these high-intent signals and sends immediate Slack notifications to your sales channel with conversation context.

5. Automated Lead Scoring Engine

Every 10 minutes, the workflow finds conversations inactive for 30+ minutes. AI analyzes the dialogue, assigns a score (1-10) and status (Hot/Warm/Cold), then writes results back to Google Sheets for sales prioritization.

Pro tip: The scoring timer resets with each new message, so leads are only scored after conversation inactivity. This ensures you're scoring genuine interest levels, not interrupting active conversations.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Sheets OAuth2 credentials – For CRM integration
  2. Supabase account – For vector store (knowledge base) and identity storage
  3. Google Gemini API key – For AI chat capabilities
  4. Cohere API key – For RAG reranking improvement
  5. Slack OAuth2 credentials – For real-time sales alerts
  6. Postgres database – For chat memory (can use Supabase Postgres)
  7. A Google Sheet with columns: email, name, lead_score, lead_status, interests, conversation_summary, notes, last_message_at, score_after, scored, created_at

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Duplicate the template Google Sheet or create your own with the required column structure.
  2. Set up Supabase tables: Create chat_users (session_id, name, email), documents (vector store), and chat_memory tables.
  3. Connect all credentials in their respective n8n nodes (Google Sheets, Supabase, Gemini, Cohere, Slack, Postgres).
  4. Customize the AI agent prompts: Replace [AI AGENT NAME], [COMPANY], and [YOUR SERVICES] with your details.
  5. Populate your knowledge base: Upload your company documentation, FAQs, and product info to the Supabase vector store.
  6. Update the Google Sheet ID in the workflow if using your own sheet.
  7. Activate the workflow: The chat flow triggers on every message; the scoring schedule runs every 10 minutes.
The AI chatbot gates conversations behind identity capture, requiring name and email before providing answers.

Implementation tip: Test the full flow with a few conversations first: provide name/email → ask questions → wait 30+ minutes → verify the lead gets scored in Google Sheets. This confirms all components are working correctly.
