Capture and Store CRM Contacts with Telegram & Gemini AI

An intelligent Telegram bot that automatically extracts contact details from messages, voice notes, and business card photos, then stores them in your CRM.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious problem of manual contact data entry. Instead of copying information from business cards, transcribing voice messages, or reformatting contact details shared via chat, this AI-powered Telegram bot handles everything automatically.

The workflow creates an intelligent agent that engages users in natural conversation to collect complete contact information. It supports multiple input formats—users can send text messages, voice recordings, or even photos of business cards. The AI extracts relevant details, asks clarifying questions for missing information, validates data, and stores everything directly in your CRM or spreadsheet.

Beyond simple capture, the system includes duplicate detection by email, gives users options to update existing contacts, and provides confirmation summaries before saving. What typically takes 10-15 minutes of manual work happens in seconds with perfect accuracy.

How It Works

1. Telegram Message Trigger

The workflow activates when a user sends any message to your Telegram bot. This could be text, voice, or an image. The system immediately processes the input type and prepares it for AI analysis.

2. Multi-Modal Input Processing

Text messages go directly to the AI agent. Voice messages are transcribed using Google Gemini's speech-to-text capabilities. Images (like business card photos) are analyzed using Gemini's vision model to extract text and contact information.

3. AI Conversation & Data Extraction

The AI agent engages in a contextual conversation with the user. It identifies what contact information has been provided, asks intelligent follow-up questions for missing fields (like "What's their email address?" or "Which company do they work for?"), and structures the data into standardized fields.

4. Duplicate Check & User Confirmation

Before saving, the system checks your existing contacts (in Google Sheets or your CRM) for duplicates based on email address. If a match is found, it asks the user whether to create a new contact or update the existing one. The AI then presents a summary of all collected information for final confirmation.

5. CRM Storage

Once confirmed, the complete contact record—including name, email, phone, company, job title, and any meeting notes—is saved to your chosen destination. The template uses Google Sheets for simplicity but can be easily adapted to any CRM like HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive.

What You'll Need

  1. Telegram Bot Token: Create a new bot via BotFather on Telegram to get your access token.
  2. Google Gemini API Key: Obtain from Google AI Studio for AI conversation, transcription, and image analysis capabilities.
  3. Google Sheets Document: Create a spreadsheet with columns for: Full Name, Email, Phone, Company, Job Title, and Meeting Notes.
  4. n8n Instance: Either self-hosted n8n or n8n.cloud account to run the workflow.
  5. Webhook URL: Your n8n instance must be accessible via HTTPS for Telegram webhooks.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this contact capture automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and Import: Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Telegram Credentials: Add your Telegram bot token to the Telegram Trigger node.
  3. Set Up Webhook: Copy the webhook URL from the Telegram node and set it using: curl -X POST "https://api.telegram.org/bot{YOUR_TOKEN}/setWebhook?url={WEBHOOK_URL}"
  4. Add Gemini API Key: Configure Google Gemini credentials in the AI Agent, Transcribe Audio, and Analyze Image nodes.
  5. Connect Google Sheets: Add your Google Sheets credentials and update the spreadsheet ID in the Parameters node.
  6. Test and Deploy: Send a test message to your Telegram bot with contact information and verify it appears in your spreadsheet.
