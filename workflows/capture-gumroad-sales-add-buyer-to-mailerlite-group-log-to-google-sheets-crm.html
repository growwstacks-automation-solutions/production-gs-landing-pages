What This Workflow Does

Every time a customer purchases your digital product on Gumroad, you face a critical choice: manually copy their details into your email list and CRM—or automate the entire process. This workflow eliminates the manual grind by instantly capturing new sales, adding buyers to the appropriate MailerLite subscriber group, and logging complete transaction details to your Google Sheets CRM.

The automation ensures your email nurture sequences begin immediately, building customer relationships from the moment of purchase. Meanwhile, your sales data accumulates in a searchable, organized spreadsheet, giving you real-time visibility into what’s selling and who’s buying—without ever opening Gumroad’s dashboard.

How It Works

1. Trigger on New Gumroad Sale

The workflow activates the moment a sale is completed on your Gumroad store. It captures the customer’s name, email, product details, price, and any custom fields you’ve configured.

2. Create or Update MailerLite Subscriber

The buyer’s email and name are sent to MailerLite. If they’re already in your system, their profile is updated; if not, a new subscriber is created, ensuring your list stays clean and current.

3. Assign to Specific MailerLite Group

The subscriber is automatically added to a designated group (e.g., “Gumroad Buyers” or “Product X Customers”). This triggers your pre-built email automation sequences for onboarding, upsells, or content delivery.

4. Log Sale to Google Sheets CRM

All sale details are appended as a new row in your Google Sheets spreadsheet. Columns typically include date, customer email, product name, price, and status, creating a permanent, queryable sales record.

What You'll Need

  1. A Gumroad account with at least one product for sale.
  2. A MailerLite account with an API key and at least one subscriber group created.
  3. A Google Sheets spreadsheet set up with column headers for your CRM data.
  4. Access to n8n (cloud or self-hosted) to import and run the workflow.

Pro tip: Before activating the workflow, test with a small Gumroad sale or use Gumroad’s “test mode” to verify the data flow without affecting real customers.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the Gumroad trigger node with your access token from Gumroad’s developer settings.
  3. Set up the MailerLite nodes by adding your API key and specifying the target group ID.
  4. Connect the Google Sheets node with your spreadsheet ID and worksheet name.
  5. Activate the workflow and make a test purchase to confirm everything syncs correctly.
