What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring lead information from Typeform submissions to your HubSpot CRM. Every time a potential customer fills out your Typeform, their details automatically appear in HubSpot as a new contact record.

The workflow eliminates the risk of human error in data entry and ensures no leads slip through the cracks. It captures all form fields including name, email, phone number, and any custom questions you've added to your Typeform.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Typeform Submission

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Typeform. n8n instantly receives the complete submission data including all answers and metadata.

2. Data Processing

The workflow maps Typeform fields to corresponding HubSpot contact properties. It can handle conditional logic for different form paths and clean data before sending to HubSpot.

3. Create HubSpot Contact

The processed data creates a new contact in HubSpot with all relevant information. The workflow can also update existing contacts if the email matches.

Pro tip: Add a hidden field in Typeform to track the campaign source, then map it to HubSpot's UTM parameters for better lead attribution.
