What This Workflow Does

Every website form submission represents a potential customer, but manually processing each lead is slow and error-prone. This automation solves that by creating a seamless pipeline from your website to your sales team.

The workflow captures lead data via a webhook, validates it to ensure quality, optionally enriches it with additional company information, stores it in your preferred system (HubSpot for full CRM power or Google Sheets for simplicity), and instantly alerts your team via Slack. This turns a static contact form into a dynamic, intelligent lead routing system that works 24/7.

For business owners, this means no more missed opportunities, faster response times, and a consistently professional follow-up process without adding to your team's manual workload.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence to ensure every lead is handled correctly and promptly.

1. Webhook Trigger

Your website form sends a POST request to a unique n8n webhook URL whenever someone submits their information. This triggers the entire workflow instantly.

2. Data Validation & Normalization

The workflow first checks that all required fields (like email and name) are present and properly formatted. It cleans the data—trimming whitespace, standardizing phone numbers, and lowercasing emails—to maintain consistency in your records.

3. Optional Lead Enrichment

If enabled, the system can call external APIs like Clearbit or Hunter.io to append valuable business context to the lead, such as company size, industry, and social profiles, giving your sales team a head start.

4. Storage in Your Chosen System

You choose one path: either create/update a contact in HubSpot for full CRM management, or append/update a row in a Google Sheets spreadsheet for simple tracking. The workflow handles duplicates by checking if the email already exists.

5. Instant Team Notification

A detailed message is posted to a designated Slack channel, including the lead's information and a direct link to their new record in HubSpot or Sheets. This ensures immediate visibility and action.

6. Success Confirmation

Finally, the workflow sends a 200 OK response back to your website, allowing it to display a "Thank You" message to the visitor, completing a smooth user experience.

What You'll Need

  1. An active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. A website with a form that can send POST requests to a webhook URL.
  3. Either a HubSpot account (for CRM storage) or a Google account with Google Sheets access.
  4. A Slack workspace and credentials to post to a channel.
  5. (Optional) API keys for Clearbit or Hunter.io if you wish to enable lead enrichment.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure the Webhook: Activate the Webhook node and copy its unique URL. Paste this URL into your website form's submission action.
  3. Choose Your Storage Path: Enable either the HubSpot nodes or the Google Sheets nodes—you don't need both.
  4. Add Your Credentials: Set up credentials for Slack and your chosen storage platform (HubSpot/Sheets) within n8n.
  5. Test the Flow: Submit a test lead from your website and verify the data appears correctly in your CRM/Sheet and that the Slack notification is sent.

Pro tip: Start with the Google Sheets path for simplicity. You can always switch to HubSpot later as your sales process matures.
