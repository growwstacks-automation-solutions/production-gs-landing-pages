Categorise and label Gmail emails with GPT-4o and OpenAI

Automatically organise your Gmail inbox using AI. This workflow categorises every incoming email and applies Gmail labels, keeping only important emails in view.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the universal problem of email overload by using AI to intelligently categorize and label every incoming Gmail message. Instead of wasting time manually sorting emails or missing important messages in a cluttered inbox, the system automatically analyzes each email's content and applies appropriate labels.

The workflow leverages GPT-4o's advanced natural language understanding to go beyond simple keyword matching. It can recognize context, intent, and urgency in email content, then apply your predefined Gmail labels accordingly. This creates an automatically organized inbox where you can focus on priority communications while less important emails get filtered appropriately.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your Gmail inbox. It captures the full email content including subject, body, sender information, and any attachments.

2. AI Analysis

The email content gets sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o for analysis. The AI evaluates the message's purpose, urgency, and content type based on your predefined categories.

3. Category Determination

GPT-4o returns its classification decision along with confidence scores. The workflow applies business rules you've configured to finalize the appropriate label(s).

4. Label Application

The system applies the determined Gmail label(s) and can optionally move the email to specific folders or mark priority status based on your settings.
