Automate Email Triage: Categorize with AI & Get Instant Alerts

Free n8n workflow that uses Azure OpenAI to intelligently categorize incoming emails, logs them to Google Sheets, and sends Telegram alerts for urgent messages.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the universal business problem of email overload. Instead of manually sorting through dozens or hundreds of daily emails, this workflow uses Azure OpenAI to automatically read, understand, and categorize each incoming message based on its content and subject line.

Once categorized, the system logs every email with its classification, sender, timestamp, and key details into a Google Sheet—creating a searchable database of all communications. For high-priority categories you define (like "Urgent", "Sales Lead", or "Customer Complaint"), it simultaneously sends instant alerts to a designated Telegram channel or group, ensuring your team responds immediately to what matters most.

The result is a smart email triage system that works 24/7, reduces manual sorting time by 80-90%, prevents important messages from getting buried, and creates valuable structured data from unstructured email conversations.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger & Capture

The workflow starts by monitoring your email inbox (via IMAP or Gmail node). When a new email arrives, it captures the full message including subject, sender, body content, and attachments.

2. AI-Powered Categorization

The email content is sent to Azure OpenAI's language model with your predefined categories (like "Support Request," "Sales Inquiry," "Newsletter," "Urgent"). The AI analyzes the email's intent and content, then assigns the most appropriate category with a confidence score.

3. Data Logging to Google Sheets

Every email—regardless of category—gets logged to a designated Google Sheet. Each row includes the timestamp, sender, subject, category, AI confidence score, and a snippet of the email content. This creates a permanent, searchable record of all communications.

4. Conditional Telegram Alerts

Based on rules you set (e.g., "if category is 'Urgent' or confidence score > 90%"), the workflow sends formatted alerts to your Telegram channel. These alerts include key details and a direct link to the email in your inbox or the Google Sheet row for quick action.

5. Optional Follow-up Actions

The workflow can be extended to trigger additional actions based on category—like creating tickets in your helpdesk, adding leads to your CRM, or scheduling follow-up reminders—creating a complete automated email response system.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (self-hosted version required for community nodes)
  2. Azure OpenAI API access with a deployed model (like GPT-3.5 or GPT-4)
  3. A Google Cloud Project with Sheets API enabled and service account credentials
  4. A Google Sheet prepared with appropriate column headers
  5. Email account credentials (IMAP or Gmail with app password)
  6. A Telegram bot token and channel/group ID for alerts
  7. Basic understanding of n8n workflow configuration

Pro tip: Start with just 3-5 broad categories (like "Urgent", "Informational", "Sales") and refine as you see how the AI performs on your actual emails. You can easily add more specific categories later.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Email Trigger: Set up your IMAP or Gmail node with your email credentials and polling interval.
  3. Connect Azure OpenAI: Add your Azure endpoint, deployment name, and API key to the AI node.
  4. Set Up Google Sheets: Create your sheet with columns for timestamp, sender, subject, category, confidence, and content. Add your service account credentials.
  5. Configure Telegram Bot: Create a bot via @BotFather, get your token, and set up the Telegram node with your channel/group ID.
  6. Define Your Categories: Edit the AI prompt to include your specific business categories and examples.
  7. Test & Activate: Send test emails to verify categorization accuracy and alert delivery, then activate the workflow.
