Categorize support emails with AI and create tasks in Dart

Automatically turn incoming support emails into categorized, prioritized tasks in Dart—complete with AI-generated summaries, tags, and sender context.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your chaotic inbox into an organized task management system. Every incoming support email gets automatically analyzed by AI, categorized by type and priority, then converted into a fully contextual task in Dart with all relevant details preserved.

The system eliminates manual email triage while ensuring no request falls through the cracks. AI adds intelligent tags, summarizes key points, and extracts actionable items—saving support teams hours of repetitive processing time each week while improving response accuracy.

How It Works

1. Email capture and preprocessing

The workflow monitors your designated support inbox, fetching new messages as they arrive. It extracts all relevant content including sender information, subject line, body text, and attachments.

2. AI analysis and categorization

Using natural language processing, the AI identifies the email's primary category (billing, technical support, account management), urgency level, and any specific product/service mentioned. It generates a concise summary highlighting the core issue.

3. Task creation in Dart

The system creates a new task in Dart with all processed information—including the AI-generated category tags, priority level, summary, and full original email content. Tasks can be automatically assigned based on category or workload distribution rules.

4. Continuous learning

As agents handle tasks and provide feedback on categorizations, the AI model improves its accuracy over time. The system tracks resolution metrics to identify process bottlenecks.
