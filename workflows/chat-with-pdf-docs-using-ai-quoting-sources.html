What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms static PDF documents into interactive knowledge bases. Employees can ask natural language questions about contract terms, research findings, or technical specifications, receiving answers that include precise citations to the source material.

The system handles complex queries like "What are the termination conditions in our client agreement?" by analyzing the document's semantic meaning rather than just keyword matching. Each response pinpoints the relevant section, enabling users to verify information in context.

How It Works

1. Document Processing

The workflow first breaks down uploaded PDFs into manageable chunks while preserving structural relationships between sections. This preprocessing ensures the AI can understand document hierarchy during analysis.

2. Question Handling

Users submit questions through a chat interface (or integrated platforms like Slack). The system analyzes the query's intent and identifies potentially relevant document sections based on semantic similarity.

3. AI Analysis

Your chosen AI model processes the question against the document content, generating a concise answer while tracking which sections contributed to the response. The workflow supports multiple AI providers for flexibility.

4. Citation Generation

The system formats the answer with clear references to source pages or paragraphs. For legal or technical documents, this creates an audit trail showing exactly where information originated.

Who This Is For

This solution benefits legal teams reviewing contracts, researchers analyzing papers, compliance officers verifying regulations, and any knowledge workers who regularly extract information from lengthy documents. It's particularly valuable when accuracy and source verification are critical.

Pro tip: Start with high-value documents like client contracts or product manuals where quick information retrieval directly impacts business outcomes.
