What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious and time-consuming process of manually checking flight prices. Instead of refreshing airline websites or waiting for generic price alerts, this workflow actively monitors Amadeus's comprehensive flight data for your specific itinerary and price requirements.

When it detects fares that meet your criteria, it automatically sends you a detailed email notification through Gmail with all the relevant flight information. This ensures you can book immediately when prices drop, often securing savings of 20-40% compared to last-minute purchases.

How It Works

1. Daily Price Check

The workflow connects to the Amadeus API each day to fetch current pricing data for your configured routes. It checks all available airlines, including budget carriers that might not appear on aggregate sites.

2. Price Comparison

Your predefined budget thresholds and preferred travel times are compared against the latest fares. The system evaluates both direct and connecting flights based on your preferences.

3. Smart Notification

When a matching deal is found, the workflow compiles all relevant details - including departure times, airline, layovers (if any), and exact pricing - into a clean email format sent directly to your inbox.
