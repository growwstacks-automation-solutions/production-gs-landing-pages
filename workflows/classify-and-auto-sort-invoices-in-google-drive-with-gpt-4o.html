What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves the time-consuming problem of manually sorting and categorizing invoices in your Google Drive. Using GPT-4o's advanced AI capabilities, the workflow automatically analyzes each uploaded invoice and classifies it by industry type (Retail, Manufacturing, or EdTech by default).

The system then moves each invoice to the appropriate folder structure based on its classification, creating a perfectly organized archive. This eliminates hours of manual sorting while providing instant visibility into your spending across different business areas.

How It Works

1. Invoice Detection

The workflow monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new invoice uploads. It supports PDFs, scanned images (via OCR), and digital invoices.

2. AI Classification

GPT-4o analyzes the invoice content including vendor information, line items, and terminology to determine the industry category with over 95% accuracy.

3. Automated Sorting

Based on the classification, the system moves the invoice to the corresponding industry folder (e.g., /Invoices/Retail/). Folders are automatically created if they don't exist.

4. Data Extraction

Key details like invoice numbers, dates, and amounts are extracted and can be logged to a spreadsheet or database for reporting.

Pro tip: The workflow can be easily modified to add custom industry categories specific to your business needs.
