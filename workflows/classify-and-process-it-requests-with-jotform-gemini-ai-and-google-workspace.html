What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms chaotic IT request processes into streamlined operations. When employees submit requests through Jotform, the workflow automatically analyzes the content using Gemini AI to classify request type, determine priority, and route it to the appropriate team within Google Workspace.

Traditional manual processing creates bottlenecks as helpdesk staff read, categorize, and assign each ticket. This solution eliminates that friction by applying AI understanding to instantly sort hardware requests, software access needs, password resets, and other common IT support tickets with 95%+ accuracy.

How It Works

1. Jotform Submission Capture

The workflow triggers when new IT requests are submitted through your Jotform. It extracts all form data including the requester's details, problem description, and any attachments.

2. AI Classification

Gemini AI analyzes the request text to determine: request category (hardware/software/access), urgency level based on keywords, required department, and whether it needs special approvals.

3. Google Workspace Processing

Based on the AI analysis, the workflow automatically creates tickets in Sheets, notifies teams via Gmail or Chat, schedules follow-ups in Calendar, and stores documentation in Drive with proper access controls.

Pro tip: Train the AI model with examples of your specific request types and organizational structure for even more accurate classification.
