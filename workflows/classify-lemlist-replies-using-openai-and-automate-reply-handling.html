What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for sales teams - efficiently managing and responding to inbound email replies from outreach campaigns. Without automation, sales reps waste valuable time manually reviewing each response to determine appropriate follow-up actions.

The workflow automatically classifies every new reply in lemlist using OpenAI's natural language processing, then triggers the appropriate follow-up actions based on the response type. This includes tagging interested leads in your CRM, sending Slack notifications to your team, and processing unsubscribe requests.

How It Works

1. New Reply Detection

The workflow triggers whenever a new reply comes into your lemlist campaign, capturing all message details including sender information and content.

2. AI Classification

OpenAI analyzes the email content to categorize it as positive interest, objection, unsubscribe request, or other common response types.

3. Automated Actions

Based on the classification, the workflow executes appropriate follow-ups like CRM updates, team notifications, or sequence removals.
