What This Workflow Does

In cold email campaigns, personalized company names are critical for engagement—they're the second most frequently used variable after the recipient's first name. Yet lead lists are often filled with messy data: "TechWave Solutions LLC - Innovative AI Platform" instead of just "TechWave Solutions." These legal suffixes and taglines scream "automated template" and kill response rates.

This workflow solves that problem automatically. It connects to your Google Sheets lead database, uses AI to intelligently clean company names by removing legal entities, marketing fluff, and inconsistencies, then saves the polished results back to a new sheet. The result? Cold emails that reference companies naturally, as a human would, dramatically improving trust and reply likelihood.

Beyond just cleaning, this automation ensures consistency across your entire outreach database. Whether you're pulling leads from LinkedIn, CRM exports, or lead forms, every company name gets standardized to its cleanest, most professional form before hitting anyone's inbox.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical three-step process that transforms messy data into campaign-ready assets.

1. Google Sheets Trigger & Data Extraction

The workflow starts when you submit a Google Sheet URL via a simple form. It authenticates with your Google account, reads the specified sheet, and extracts all lead records. The system looks for a "Company" column specifically—this is where your raw, unformatted company names reside.

2. AI-Powered Name Cleaning

Each company name passes through an LLM node (GPT-4 Mini by default) with a carefully engineered system prompt. The AI understands context: it removes "LLC," "Inc.," "GmbH," taglines, extra descriptors, and standardizes capitalization while preserving the core brand identity. "AlphaBeta Technologies - Premier Cloud Solutions Provider" becomes "AlphaBeta Technologies."

3. Results Compilation & Storage

Cleaned names merge back with original lead data into a structured object. The workflow creates a new sheet within your original spreadsheet (named "Cleaned_Results" by default) and writes the enhanced records. This preserves your source data while giving you a clean, ready-to-use output for immediate campaign deployment.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in your n8n environment in under 15 minutes with these straightforward steps.

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n instance, go to Workflows → Import from File and select the downloaded template.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up your Google Sheets and AI model credentials in n8n's credentials management. Ensure Google Sheets has edit permissions for spreadsheets.
  3. Prepare Your Sheet: Create or identify a Google Sheet with your leads. Ensure there's a column with the exact header "Company" containing the messy names you want cleaned.
  4. Test Execution: Run the workflow once manually with a small test sheet (5-10 records). Verify the output in the new "Cleaned_Results" sheet matches expectations.
  5. Schedule or Trigger: Set the workflow to run on a schedule (daily/weekly) or trigger it via webhook when new leads are added to your system.
