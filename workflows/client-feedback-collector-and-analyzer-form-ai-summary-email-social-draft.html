What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow transforms raw customer feedback into actionable business intelligence. It solves the common problem of collecting feedback that never gets analyzed or acted upon - a critical gap for service-based businesses and product teams.

The automation captures feedback through your preferred form tool (Tally, Typeform, or Google Forms), processes it through AI analysis to identify key themes and sentiment, then distributes summarized insights to relevant teams via email while simultaneously creating social media-ready content highlighting positive feedback.

How It Works

1. Feedback Collection

The workflow triggers when a new form submission is received from your chosen feedback collection platform. It captures all response data including ratings, comments, and customer details.

2. AI Analysis

Using OpenAI's natural language processing, the system analyzes the qualitative feedback to identify common themes, sentiment (positive/negative/neutral), and key phrases. This transforms raw comments into structured data.

3. Insight Distribution

The analyzed data generates two outputs: (1) An internal email summary for your team with prioritized insights, and (2) Draft social media posts highlighting positive customer experiences (with customer permission).

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits service businesses, agencies, SaaS companies, and product teams that need to systematically capture and act on customer feedback. Marketing teams will appreciate the automatic social content generation, while product managers gain structured insights from user comments.
