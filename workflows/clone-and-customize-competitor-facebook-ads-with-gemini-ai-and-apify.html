What This Workflow Does

The Nano Banana Ad Cloning System automates the process of researching competitor Facebook ads and adapting them for your own products. Instead of manually analyzing ads and rewriting content, this workflow uses Apify to scrape competitor ad data and Gemini AI to generate customized variations that maintain proven structures while making the content unique to your brand.

This approach saves marketers hours of manual research while producing higher-converting ad creatives. By leveraging what's already working in your market, you can achieve better ad performance with less guesswork and testing.

How It Works

1. Competitor Ad Scraping

The workflow begins by using Apify to scrape publicly available Facebook ads from your specified competitors. It captures ad copy, creative elements, and engagement metrics to identify high-performing patterns.

2. AI Analysis & Adaptation

Gemini AI analyzes the scraped ads to understand their structure and messaging framework. It then generates multiple variations that maintain the effective elements while rewriting the content to match your brand voice and product specifics.

3. Ad Version Creation

The system produces several ad variations with different headlines, calls-to-action, and value propositions. These are formatted for easy upload to Facebook Ads Manager or your preferred ad platform.

4. Performance Tracking Setup

The workflow automatically tags each generated ad with tracking parameters, making it easy to compare performance against your original creatives and optimize based on real data.
