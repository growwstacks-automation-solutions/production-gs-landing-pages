What This Workflow Does

For travel businesses, collecting timely customer feedback is crucial for service improvement but often gets neglected due to manual processes. This automation solves that problem by systematically gathering trip feedback through email and organizing all responses in Google Sheets for easy analysis.

When a new customer completes their trip, the workflow automatically sends them a personalized feedback form via email. Their responses are then captured, processed, and stored in a structured Google Sheets database. This eliminates manual follow-ups, ensures consistent data collection, and provides actionable insights to enhance future customer experiences.

The system handles the entire feedback lifecycle—from initial request to data storage—freeing your team to focus on implementing improvements rather than chasing responses. It's particularly valuable for tour operators, travel agencies, and hospitality businesses that need to maintain high service standards through continuous customer input.

How It Works

1. New Customer Detection

The workflow monitors your customer database or booking system for new trip completions. When a customer finishes their journey, the system triggers automatically to begin the feedback collection process.

2. Automated Email Dispatch

A personalized feedback request email is sent to the customer at the optimal time—typically 24-48 hours after trip completion when experiences are fresh but travel fatigue has subsided.

3. Response Capture & Processing

When customers submit their feedback through the email form, the system captures all responses, including ratings, comments, and specific suggestions. The workflow processes multiple submissions efficiently through batch handling.

4. Data Organization in Google Sheets

All feedback is automatically organized into predefined columns in Google Sheets, creating a searchable, filterable database. Customer information, trip details, ratings, and comments are stored systematically for analysis.

5. Notification & Follow-up Triggers

Based on feedback content, the system can trigger appropriate follow-up actions—flagging critical issues for immediate attention or sending thank-you messages for positive reviews.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Google Sheets account with API access enabled for read/write operations
  3. Email service (SMTP or transactional email API like SendGrid, Mailgun, or AWS SES)
  4. Customer database or booking system that can trigger new trip completions
  5. Basic understanding of connecting APIs and configuring email templates

Pro tip: Start with a simple 3-question feedback form (overall rating, what went well, what could improve) to maximize response rates. You can expand questions later based on initial data patterns.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance using the import functionality.
  2. Configure Google Sheets node with your API credentials and specify the target spreadsheet and worksheet for feedback storage.
  3. Set up email service by connecting your SMTP or email API credentials and customizing the feedback request template with your branding.
  4. Connect trigger source to your customer database or booking system to detect new trip completions automatically.
  5. Test the workflow with a sample customer entry to verify email delivery, response capture, and Google Sheets updating function correctly.
  6. Activate the workflow and monitor initial responses to ensure all systems integrate smoothly before full deployment.
