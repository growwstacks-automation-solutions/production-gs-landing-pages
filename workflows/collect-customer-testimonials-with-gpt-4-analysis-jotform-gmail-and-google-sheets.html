What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the common challenge of collecting, organizing, and leveraging customer testimonials effectively. Most businesses struggle with testimonial collection being ad-hoc, time-consuming, and underutilized. This system transforms that process into a strategic marketing asset.

The workflow automatically collects testimonials through Jotform submissions or email responses, analyzes them with GPT-4 for sentiment and key insights, then organizes the processed testimonials in Google Sheets with categorization and scoring. This creates a ready-to-use testimonial library that marketing teams can easily search and pull from for campaigns.

How It Works

1. Testimonial Collection

The workflow monitors multiple input channels including Jotform submissions and designated Gmail inboxes. When new testimonials arrive, they're immediately processed to extract key customer and product information.

2. AI Analysis

Each testimonial is sent to GPT-4 for sentiment analysis, key phrase extraction, and summarization. The AI scores each testimonial for usefulness and categorizes it by product/service mentioned.

3. Data Organization

Processed testimonials are automatically logged in Google Sheets with all extracted metadata. The system creates multiple versions of each testimonial - full text, summarized quotes, and bullet-point highlights.

4. Notification & Follow-up

The workflow sends notifications to marketing teams about new high-quality testimonials and can automatically thank customers for their feedback with personalized responses.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for SaaS companies, e-commerce businesses, service providers, and any organization that relies on social proof. Marketing teams, customer success managers, and product managers will benefit most from having organized, analyzed testimonials at their fingertips.
