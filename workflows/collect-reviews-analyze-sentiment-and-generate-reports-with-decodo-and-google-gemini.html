What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire customer feedback lifecycle—from collecting reviews across platforms to generating actionable insights. It solves the challenge of manually monitoring multiple review sources while providing deeper sentiment analysis than basic star ratings.

The system extracts review data from provided URLs, uses Google Gemini's AI to analyze sentiment and key themes, then compiles professional reports with trends and actionable insights. Businesses save 10-15 hours weekly on manual review monitoring while gaining more consistent, data-driven customer intelligence.

How It Works

1. Review Collection

The workflow begins by scraping review links from your Airtable base or other data sources. It handles authentication and pagination to ensure complete data collection from each review platform.

2. Data Extraction

Using Decodo's powerful scraping capabilities, the workflow extracts structured review data including text, ratings, dates, and reviewer information. This creates a standardized dataset regardless of the original platform format.

3. Sentiment Analysis

Google Gemini processes each review, assigning sentiment scores (positive/neutral/negative) and identifying key themes, complaints, or compliments. The AI detects emotional tone and specific product/service mentions.

4. Reporting

The system generates comprehensive reports including sentiment trends over time, common complaint categories, and positive feedback highlights. Reports can be delivered via email, saved to Google Drive, or pushed to BI tools.

Pro tip: Configure alerts for negative sentiment spikes to enable rapid response to customer issues before they escalate.
