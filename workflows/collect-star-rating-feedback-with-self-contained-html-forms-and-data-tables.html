Automate Customer Feedback with Star Ratings

Capture lightweight customer satisfaction scores through a self-hosted HTML form, store responses automatically, and trigger thank-you pages—all within n8n.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manually collecting and organizing customer feedback is time-consuming and often leads to low response rates. This automation solves that by embedding a professional, responsive star-rating form directly into your website or product. When a customer submits their rating and optional comment, the data is instantly captured, stored in a structured n8n Data Table, and the user sees a branded thank-you page.

The system is entirely self-contained, requiring no external services or paid subscriptions. You maintain full ownership of the data and can easily attribute feedback to specific campaigns or user segments by passing URL parameters (like ?source=email_campaign). This turns sporadic feedback into a consistent, actionable stream of customer sentiment.

How It Works

The workflow uses n8n's native nodes to create a complete feedback loop without writing code.

1. Form Presentation

A GET webhook serves a clean HTML page with a 1–5 star rating interface and a text field for comments. The form is fully stylable to match your brand.

2. Data Submission & Processing

When the form is submitted (POST), the rating, comment, and any URL parameters are captured. A Set node structures the data, and a Data Table node stores each submission with a timestamp.

3. Confirmation & Storage

The user is immediately redirected to a success page. Meanwhile, their feedback is securely logged, ready for analysis or integration with other tools like CRMs or alerting systems.

Who This Is For

This template is ideal for SaaS companies, e-commerce stores, support teams, and product managers who need to measure customer satisfaction (CSAT/NPS) without relying on third-party survey tools. It's perfect for businesses that want to keep feedback data in-house, avoid per-response fees, and integrate ratings directly into their existing dashboards.

What You'll Need

  1. A running n8n instance (Cloud or self-hosted).
  2. Basic understanding of how to publish webhooks in n8n.
  3. Access to your website to embed the form URL or iframe.
  4. Optional: A custom domain if you want to brand the form URL.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n workspace using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Publish the webhooks – both the GET (form) and POST (submission) endpoints need to be activated.
  3. Customize the form text in the HTML node to match your company's voice and branding.
  4. Test the flow by visiting the form URL, submitting a rating, and verifying the data appears in the Data Table.
  5. Embed the form on your website, in product dashboards, or share the link via email after support interactions.
