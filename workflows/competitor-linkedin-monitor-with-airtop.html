Competitor LinkedIn Monitor with Airtop

Automatically track and summarize LinkedIn posts from competitor executives. Get AI-powered insights delivered to Slack weekly.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual competitor monitoring on LinkedIn is time-consuming and inconsistent. Marketing teams, sales leaders, and business strategists spend hours each week scrolling through competitor profiles, trying to catch important announcements about hiring, product launches, or strategic shifts.

This automation solves that problem by systematically tracking LinkedIn posts from key executives at your competitor companies. It uses Airtop's browser automation to visit each profile, analyzes up to 5 recent posts, generates AI-powered summaries of main topics and engagement levels, and delivers structured reports directly to your Slack channel. What used to take 5-10 hours weekly now happens automatically with zero manual effort.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates on a weekly schedule (configurable to daily or monthly). This ensures consistent monitoring without manual initiation.

2. Competitor List Retrieval

It reads a Google Sheet containing LinkedIn profile URLs of competitor executives you want to monitor. This centralized list is easy to update as your competitive landscape changes.

3. Browser Automation with Airtop

Using an authenticated Airtop browser profile, the automation navigates to each LinkedIn profile, bypassing login requirements and handling LinkedIn's dynamic content loading.

4. Post Analysis & AI Summarization

For each profile, it captures up to 5 recent posts. Airtop's AI analyzes the content, identifying main topics, sentiment, engagement metrics, and key takeaways.

5. Slack Notification Delivery

Formatted summaries are posted to your designated Slack channel, including competitor names, post counts, main topics identified, and engagement levels for quick review.

Who This Is For

This automation delivers value to multiple business functions. Marketing teams use it to track competitor content strategies and campaign launches. Sales leaders monitor hiring patterns that signal market expansion. Business strategists identify partnership announcements and funding rounds. Recruitment teams watch for talent movements. Startup founders keep tabs on larger competitors' strategic directions.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtop API Key (free to generate from their portal)
  2. Airtop Browser Profile authenticated to LinkedIn
  3. Google Sheet with competitor LinkedIn URLs (template provided)
  4. Slack Bot credentials with access to your target channel
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Pro tip: Start with 3-5 key competitors rather than trying to monitor everyone at once. Focus on CEOs, CTOs, and heads of marketing/product for the most strategic insights.
