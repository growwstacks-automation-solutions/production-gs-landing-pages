Competitor Price Monitoring Automation

Track competitor prices automatically with web scraping and get instant Telegram alerts

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the tedious and time-consuming task of manually checking competitor prices. It automatically scrapes competitor websites daily, compares prices against your records, and sends Telegram alerts when prices change.

The workflow helps ecommerce businesses, retailers, and pricing analysts maintain competitive pricing strategies without manual effort. By automating price monitoring, you gain real-time competitive intelligence while saving 5-10 hours per week previously spent on manual checks.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs automatically once per day at 8 AM (adjustable) using n8n's schedule trigger. This ensures regular monitoring without manual initiation.

2. Fetch Product List

It reads your master product list from Google Sheets containing product URLs and last known prices. This serves as the reference point for comparison.

3. Web Scraping

For each product URL, the workflow scrapes the current price using CSS selectors. Built-in delays prevent bot detection while ensuring reliable data extraction.

4. Price Comparison

New prices are compared against stored values, with percentage changes calculated. The workflow flags products where prices changed significantly.

5. Alert Notifications

When price changes exceed your threshold, formatted Telegram messages are sent to your specified chat with details of the change.

6. Data Logging

All price checks are logged to a separate Google Sheet tab with timestamps, creating a historical record for trend analysis.
