What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your booking process into a streamlined system that respects your business hours while syncing perfectly with Google Calendar. It eliminates the back-and-forth emails, manual calendar updates, and scheduling conflicts that plague service businesses.

The workflow acts as smart layer between your public booking form and Google Calendar, applying business rules before appointments are confirmed. It can integrate with your website or CRM via REST API, creating a complete scheduling solution without expensive software.

How It Works

1. Availability Check

The system first checks your Google Calendar for existing appointments and compares them against your configured business hours. It automatically blocks out unavailable time slots.

2. Business Rules Application

The workflow applies your specific rules - minimum notice requirements, buffer times between appointments, maximum bookings per day, or service-specific durations. These rules prevent overbooking conflicts.

3. Calendar Sync

Approved appointments are automatically added to your Google Calendar with all relevant details. The system sends confirmation emails with calendar invites and customizable reminders.

4. API Integration

  • Push booking data to your CRM
  • Trigger invoice generation
  • Update internal dashboards
