What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of identifying valuable networking opportunities at industry conferences. Professionals often waste time manually researching events and attendees, missing key connections that could advance their business goals.

The workflow automatically scans Eventbrite for relevant conferences in your industry, then uses ScrapeGraphAI to analyze attendee lists and speaker profiles. It delivers actionable intelligence about who to connect with and why, saving hours of manual research while increasing your networking effectiveness.

AI-generated networking insights from conference attendee data

How It Works

1. Event Discovery

The workflow queries Eventbrite's API to find upcoming conferences matching your specified criteria (industry, location, date range). It filters out irrelevant events and prioritizes those with high-value networking potential.

2. Attendee Intelligence

Using ScrapeGraphAI, the system analyzes publicly available attendee lists and speaker profiles. It identifies key decision-makers, potential partners, and industry influencers attending each event.

3. Connection Prioritization

The AI ranks contacts based on relevance to your business goals, shared connections, and mutual interests. It surfaces the most valuable networking opportunities you might otherwise miss.
