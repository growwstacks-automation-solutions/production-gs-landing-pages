What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of managing multiple daily form submissions scattered across different Google Forms. Instead of receiving individual email notifications for each submission or manually checking your spreadsheet, this workflow compiles all responses into one organized email recap.

The system connects directly to your Google Sheets (linked to your Forms), extracts the latest submissions, formats them consistently, and sends them into a professional Gmail message. You'll receive one comprehensive recap at your preferred time, making it easier to review all responses without missing any data.

The automation pulls data from Google Sheets and formats it for email formatting

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow checks your specified Google Sheet at scheduled intervals (typically once per day). It identifies new submissions since the last run using timestamps or row numbers.

2. Data Extraction

Key fields from each form response are extracted, response details, and any attachments are extracted from the spreadsheet. The system can be configured to include specific columns while excluding others.

3. Formattingting

Responses are organized into a clean HTML email template with consistent styling. You can customize the layout to customize the layout, include your branding, or highlight important fields.

The final email presents all submissions in a standardized, easy-to-read format

4. Delivery

The compiled email is sent via Gmail to your specified recipients. You can set multiple distribution lists or conditions based on the form data thresholds that trigger immediate alerts.
