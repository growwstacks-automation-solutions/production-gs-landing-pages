What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms manual contract review processes by leveraging AI to extract key clauses, assess risks, and verify compliance automatically. It eliminates hours of tedious document review while reducing human error in legal analysis.

The system connects to your document storage and email systems to automatically process incoming contracts, then applies AI-powered analysis to identify critical terms, potential risks, and compliance requirements. Legal teams receive prioritized alerts with actionable insights.

How It Works

1. Document Collection

The workflow automatically gathers contracts from configured sources including cloud storage, email attachments, and document management systems. Scheduled triggers ensure continuous monitoring for new documents.

2. AI-Powered Extraction

GPT-4.1 Mini analyzes each document to identify and extract key contractual elements like payment terms, termination clauses, liability provisions, and compliance requirements. The AI understands legal terminology and context.

3. Risk Assessment

Extracted clauses are evaluated against predefined risk parameters and your organization's compliance policies. The system flags potential issues like unfavorable terms, regulatory conflicts, or missing requirements.

4. Compliance Verification

The workflow cross-references contract terms with current regulations and internal policies, highlighting any discrepancies or areas requiring additional review.

5. Reporting & Alerts

Results are compiled into structured reports with risk scores and prioritized action items. Critical findings trigger immediate notifications to designated stakeholders.
