Conversational Lead Capture with Gemini 2.0 Flash AI

What This Workflow Does

Traditional lead forms capture minimal information, forcing sales teams to waste time qualifying unready prospects. This AI-powered solution creates dynamic conversations that feel human while automatically capturing detailed lead information in Google Sheets.

The workflow uses Gemini 2.0 Flash AI to conduct natural conversations with website visitors, asking follow-up questions based on their responses. It scores lead quality, categorizes interests, and structures all data for easy follow-up - reducing manual qualification work by 60-80%.

How It Works

1. AI Conversation Initiation

When a visitor engages with your chat widget or form, Gemini 2.0 Flash begins a contextual conversation. The AI asks qualifying questions in your brand voice, adapting its approach based on each response.

2. Lead Scoring & Classification

The workflow analyzes conversation transcripts in real-time, scoring leads based on buying signals, urgency indicators, and fit criteria you define. It automatically tags leads as Hot/Warm/Cold and categorizes their primary interest.

3. Structured Data Capture

All conversation details get parsed into structured fields in Google Sheets - contact info, pain points discussed, products mentioned, and calculated lead score. The sheet becomes your centralized lead database with enriched prospect data.

Pro tip: Train Gemini with sample sales calls to mimic your top performers' questioning style. This makes the AI conversations feel more authentic and effective.
