What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your VK presence with an AI assistant that handles both text and voice messages intelligently. When users send messages, the workflow first checks if it's voice or text. Voice messages get transcribed by Whisper, then both input types are processed by GPT-4 mini to generate context-aware responses.

The system maintains conversation history for continuity and can integrate with your CRM or support systems. It's particularly valuable for Russian-speaking audiences where voice messaging is popular, reducing response times from hours to seconds while maintaining natural, human-like interactions.

How It Works

1. Message Reception

The workflow monitors your VK messages via webhook or API polling. New incoming messages trigger the automation, whether they're text or voice attachments.

2. Voice Transcription

For voice messages, the audio file gets sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. The system automatically detects language (primarily optimized for Russian) and converts speech to text with timestamps.

3. Context Analysis

The workflow retrieves previous messages in the conversation thread to maintain context. This history gets formatted with proper speaker labels (User/Bot) for GPT-4's understanding.

4. AI Response Generation

GPT-4 mini processes the message (either original text or transcribed voice) along with conversation history. The AI generates a response that's appropriate for the context, with configurable personality traits and safety filters.

5. Response Delivery

The final response gets posted back to VK in the same conversation thread. The workflow logs all interactions for analytics and quality improvement.

Pro tip: For best results, provide GPT-4 with examples of ideal responses and a clear instruction prompt about your brand voice and common query types.
