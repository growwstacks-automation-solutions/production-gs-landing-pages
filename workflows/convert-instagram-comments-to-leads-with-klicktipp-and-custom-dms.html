What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms Instagram comments into qualified leads by connecting your Instagram account with KlickTipp email marketing software. When users comment on your posts (including specific trigger phrases), the system automatically captures their profile information, adds them to your KlickTipp subscriber list, and sends a personalized direct message with relevant follow-up content.

For businesses, this eliminates hours of manual comment monitoring while ensuring no potential lead slips through the cracks. The workflow maintains a human touch through customizable DM templates that reference the original comment, creating authentic engagement at scale.

The workflow captures comment data and routes it to KlickTipp while triggering personalized DMs

How It Works

1. Instagram comment monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your specified Instagram posts for new comments. You can configure it to watch for specific keywords (like "interested" or "how do I sign up") or process all comments.

2. Lead qualification

Each comment is analyzed for intent signals. The system extracts the user's Instagram handle, profile information, and comment text to determine the appropriate follow-up sequence.

3. KlickTipp integration

Qualified leads are added to your KlickTipp account with relevant tags based on their comment content. This triggers any email automation sequences you've set up in KlickTipp.

4. Personalized DM response

Within minutes, the commenter receives a direct message that references their specific comment and provides the next step - whether that's a link to schedule a call, download a resource, or view product details.

Pro tip: Use dynamic fields in your DM templates to insert the commenter's name and specific phrases from their comment. This personalization can increase response rates by 35%.
