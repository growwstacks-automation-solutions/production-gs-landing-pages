What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms LinkedIn post reactions into qualified sales leads by combining LinkedIn data scraping with AI-powered analysis. It solves the problem of manually researching and qualifying prospects who engage with your content by automatically:

  • Identifying users who reacted to your LinkedIn posts
  • Enriching their profile data with professional details
  • Applying AI classification against your ideal customer profile (ICP) criteria
  • Storing qualified leads in Airtable for sales follow-up

Pro tip: The workflow includes built-in safety measures to prevent LinkedIn account restrictions by using cookie-free scraping and strict rate limits.

How It Works

1. LinkedIn Reaction Scraping

Using Apify's specialized actors, the workflow safely scrapes profile URLs of users who reacted to your specified LinkedIn post without requiring your LinkedIn credentials.

2. Profile Data Enrichment

Each profile is processed to extract key information including job title, company, experience, and other professional details that help evaluate lead quality.

3. AI-Powered Qualification

An AI model analyzes each profile against your defined ICP criteria, scoring leads and providing reasoning for the classification decision.

4. Data Storage & Organization

Qualified leads are stored in Airtable with their classification score and AI reasoning, ready for sales team follow-up.

What You'll Need

  1. Apify account with API access (free tier available)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Apify API credentials in the HTTP Request nodes
  3. Set up Airtable OAuth2 authentication and select your base
  4. Add your LinkedIn post URL to the initial scraper node
  5. Customize the AI prompt with your ICP criteria
  6. Test with a small batch of profiles before full deployment
