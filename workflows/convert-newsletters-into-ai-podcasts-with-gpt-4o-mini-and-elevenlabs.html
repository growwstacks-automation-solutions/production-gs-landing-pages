What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of newsletter overload by transforming unread email newsletters into engaging AI-powered podcasts. Many professionals subscribe to valuable industry newsletters but struggle to find time to read them all. This workflow automatically processes incoming newsletters, extracts key insights using GPT-4o Mini, and converts them into natural-sounding audio using ElevenLabs' advanced text-to-speech technology.

The system preserves the newsletter's core value while making it consumable during commutes, workouts, or other times when reading isn't practical. It's particularly valuable for executives, investors, and knowledge workers who need to stay informed but have limited reading time.

How It Works

1. Newsletter Capture

The workflow monitors your designated email inbox for incoming newsletters. It identifies them based on sender, subject patterns, or labels you configure. Only unread newsletters matching your criteria trigger the automation.

2. Content Extraction

The system strips away email formatting, ads, and non-essential elements to isolate the core content. It preserves critical information like charts (described in text) and maintains article structure for coherent audio output.

3. AI Summarization

GPT-4o Mini processes the extracted text, identifying key points and transforming dense information into conversational language. The AI adds context markers like "The author makes three main points..." to improve audio comprehension.

4. Podcast Generation

ElevenLabs converts the AI-processed text into natural-sounding speech with appropriate pacing and emphasis. The system can apply different voice profiles based on content type (e.g., professional tone for business newsletters).

5. Delivery

The finished podcast is saved to your preferred destination - whether that's a podcast app, cloud storage, or directly to your phone. Optional notifications alert you when new episodes are ready.
