What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of needing Spotify tracks in MP3 format for business use. Many companies need specific music for presentations, videos, or client projects but struggle with Spotify's streaming-only format. The workflow automatically converts Spotify track URLs to downloadable MP3 files, uploads them to your Google Drive, and creates shareable links through Google Forms.

By automating this process, you eliminate manual conversion steps that typically require screen recording, audio extraction, and manual file management. The solution maintains an organized library of converted tracks while providing controlled access to team members or clients through Google Drive's permission system.

How It Works

1. Spotify URL Input

The workflow begins when a Spotify track URL is submitted, either through a form, email, or direct input. The system extracts the essential track information from the Spotify link.

2. Audio Conversion Process

Using audio processing services, the workflow converts the streaming track into an MP3 file. This happens through API calls that handle the technical conversion without requiring manual intervention.

3. Google Drive Upload

The converted MP3 file is automatically uploaded to a specified Google Drive folder. The workflow can organize files by date, project, or custom categories based on your needs.

4. Shareable Link Generation

Once uploaded, the workflow generates a shareable Google Drive link and can optionally send it via email or integrate with Google Forms for request-based distribution.
