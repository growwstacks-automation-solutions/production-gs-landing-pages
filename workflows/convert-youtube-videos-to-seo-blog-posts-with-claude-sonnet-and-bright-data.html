Convert YouTube Videos to SEO Blog Posts Automatically

Scrape YouTube videos by keyword, generate professional blog content with Claude AI, and publish to Blogger with full tracking—completely automated.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually repurposing video content into written blog posts. Content marketers, bloggers, and businesses spend hours watching videos, taking notes, and writing articles—this workflow eliminates that manual effort completely.

The system automatically discovers relevant YouTube videos based on your target keywords, extracts their titles and descriptions, transforms the content into SEO-optimized blog posts using Claude AI, publishes them to your Blogger account, and tracks everything in Google Sheets. What used to take 2-3 hours per article now happens automatically while you focus on strategy and promotion.

How It Works

1. Keyword Submission & Video Discovery

You submit a target keyword through a simple web form. The workflow uses Bright Data's YouTube scraper to search for videos matching that keyword, extracting video titles, descriptions, channel information, and engagement metrics.

2. AI Content Generation

For each discovered video, Claude AI analyzes the extracted information and generates a 600+ word blog post. The AI maintains a conversational tone, adds proper heading structure, and optimizes the content for search engines while preserving the original video's core message.

3. Content Cleaning & Formatting

The generated content is cleaned to remove any markdown formatting or AI artifacts. The system ensures the final output is publication-ready with proper paragraph breaks, readable formatting, and consistent styling.

4. Automated Publishing & Tracking

The cleaned blog posts are automatically published to your configured Blogger account. Each published article's URL is logged alongside the original video title in Google Sheets, creating a complete audit trail of your automated content production.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Bright Data account with YouTube scraping access
  3. Anthropic API key for Claude AI access
  4. Google account with Sheets and Blogger permissions
  5. A Blogger blog created and ready to receive content
  6. Google Sheet for tracking published articles

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Import the workflow into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure Bright Data credentials with your API key in the HTTP Header Auth node
  3. Set up Claude AI by adding your Anthropic API key to the AI model node
  4. Connect Google services by authorizing n8n to access your Sheets and Blogger accounts
  5. Update configuration nodes with your specific Blogger blog ID and Google Sheet ID
  6. Test with a single keyword to verify the complete flow works end-to-end
  7. Activate the workflow and let it run on your preferred schedule
