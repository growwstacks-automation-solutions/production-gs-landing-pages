What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms manual web research into a scalable, AI-powered process. It systematically crawls target websites, extracts relevant content using GPT-5 nano's advanced comprehension capabilities, and organizes the distilled answers into a structured Google Sheets database. The solution addresses three major pain points: the time-consuming nature of manual research, inconsistency in data formatting, and challenges in team knowledge sharing.

By automating the research pipeline, businesses can achieve 10-15x faster data collection with built-in quality controls. The workflow includes source validation, confidence scoring for AI-generated answers, and automatic timestamping - creating an audit trail for compliance-sensitive industries. Teams gain real-time access to centralized findings without waiting for human researchers to compile reports.

How It Works

1. URL Input & Crawling

The workflow accepts a list of target URLs or RSS feeds, then systematically crawls each page while respecting robots.txt restrictions. Advanced settings allow for depth control (single page vs. multi-page crawling) and content type prioritization (text, tables, or specific HTML elements).

2. AI Processing with GPT-5 nano

Extracted web content gets processed by GPT-5 nano with your predefined question set. The AI identifies relevant passages, synthesizes information from multiple sources, and generates concise answers while preserving source attribution. Temperature controls ensure balanced creativity vs. factual accuracy.

3. Google Sheets Integration

Structured outputs automatically populate a Google Sheet with columns for questions, answers, sources, confidence scores, and timestamps. Conditional formatting highlights high-priority findings, while data validation rules maintain consistency. The sheet serves as both database and collaboration hub.
