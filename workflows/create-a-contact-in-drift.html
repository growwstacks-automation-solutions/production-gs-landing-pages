What This Workflow Does

This automation template solves the common problem of manual contact entry in Drift, which leads to missed leads, inconsistent data, and delayed follow-up. When potential customers interact with your business through forms, chats, or other channels, their information often gets stuck in different systems or requires manual transfer to your conversational marketing platform.

The workflow automatically creates contacts in Drift whenever a trigger event occurs in your connected systems. Whether it's a new form submission, e-commerce purchase, webinar registration, or API call, this automation ensures every potential customer is immediately added to Drift with complete profile information, ready for personalized engagement.

By eliminating manual data entry, you reduce response time from hours to seconds, ensure no leads are forgotten, and maintain consistent contact data across your marketing and sales tools. The automation also handles data validation and formatting, so your Drift contacts always have clean, usable information.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence to capture, process, and create contacts in Drift with minimal configuration required.

Step 1: Trigger Detection

The workflow starts by monitoring your chosen trigger source. This could be a webhook from your website form, a new row in Google Sheets, a scheduled check of your CRM, or any other event that indicates a new contact should be created in Drift.

Step 2: Data Processing

Once triggered, the workflow extracts and processes the contact information. It validates email addresses, formats names consistently, enriches data with additional sources if configured, and prepares the contact object according to Drift's API requirements.

Step 3: Drift API Integration

The processed contact data is sent to Drift's Contacts API using secure authentication. The workflow handles the API call, including error checking and retry logic for reliable operation even if there are temporary network issues.

Step 4: Response Handling

After creating the contact in Drift, the workflow captures the response, logs the successful creation, and can trigger additional actions like notifications to your team or updates to other systems in your stack.
