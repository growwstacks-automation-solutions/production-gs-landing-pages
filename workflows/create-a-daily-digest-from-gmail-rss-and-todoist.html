What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the common productivity challenge of information overload by automatically gathering your most important updates from Gmail, RSS feeds, and Todoist tasks into a single, organized daily digest. Instead of constantly switching between apps to check for new emails, tasks, and news, you receive one comprehensive summary at your preferred time.

The system intelligently combines these disparate information sources into a clean, readable format that helps you start your day informed and focused. It eliminates the need for manual checking while ensuring you don't miss critical updates buried in your inbox or task manager.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow begins by connecting to your Gmail account, Todoist projects, and specified RSS feeds. It retrieves all new items since the last digest was sent, applying any filters you've configured to select only the most relevant content.

2. Content Processing

Each information source is processed to extract key details. Emails are stripped of signatures and formatting, tasks are organized by priority, and RSS items are filtered by relevance. The system standardizes the presentation of different content types for consistency.

3. Digest Assembly

The processed content is combined into a structured HTML template that clearly separates each information type. The template includes visual indicators for priority items and creates logical groupings (like organizing tasks by project or due date).

4. Delivery

The completed digest is sent via Gmail to your specified address at your preferred time. The subject line clearly indicates it's your daily digest, and the email is formatted for optimal readability on both desktop and mobile devices.

Pro tip: Schedule your digest to arrive 30 minutes before your planned review time to ensure it's ready when you're prepared to focus.
