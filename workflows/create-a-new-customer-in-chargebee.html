What This Workflow Does

Manual customer data entry into Chargebee is time-consuming, error-prone, and delays your billing processes. This automation template solves that problem by automatically creating new customer records in Chargebee whenever a trigger event occurs—such as a new signup on your website, a completed purchase in your e-commerce platform, or a new lead conversion in your CRM.

The workflow captures essential customer information including contact details, billing address, and subscription preferences, then creates a complete customer profile in Chargebee ready for invoicing and subscription management. This ensures your billing system is always synchronized with your customer acquisition channels, reducing administrative overhead and improving data accuracy.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger Event Detection

The workflow monitors your chosen trigger source—this could be a webhook from your website, a new record in your CRM, or a form submission. When a new customer signal is detected, the workflow captures all relevant data fields.

Step 2: Data Validation and Enrichment

Customer data is validated for completeness and formatted correctly for Chargebee's API. The workflow can enrich the data with additional information, check for duplicates, or apply business rules before proceeding.

Step 3: Chargebee Customer Creation

The validated customer data is sent to Chargebee's API to create a new customer record. The workflow handles all required fields and can include custom fields specific to your subscription business model.

Step 4: Confirmation and Error Handling

Upon successful creation, the workflow can trigger follow-up actions like sending confirmation emails or updating other systems. If creation fails, the workflow logs the error and can notify your team for manual intervention.
