Create a new member on Mailchimp

Automatically add contacts to your Mailchimp audience from any trigger source

What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual process of adding new members to your Mailchimp audience. Whether contacts come from website forms, CRM systems, ecommerce platforms, or event registrations, this workflow automatically adds them to your specified Mailchimp list with all relevant contact information and tags.

The solution reduces administrative workload while ensuring real-time audience updates. It handles field mapping, consent management, and can even trigger personalized welcome sequences based on the member's source or attributes.

How It Works

1. Trigger Setup

The workflow activates when a new contact is detected from your chosen source platform. This could be a form submission, CRM update, or purchase event.

2. Data Validation

Contact information is verified for completeness and formatting. The workflow checks for required fields like email address and handles any data transformations needed for Mailchimp compatibility.

3. Mailchimp Integration

Using Mailchimp's API, the contact is added to your specified audience. The workflow can apply tags, set custom fields, and manage subscription preferences based on your business rules.

Pro tip: Add a step to segment contacts immediately based on their signup source or attributes. This enables targeted welcome sequences from day one.
