What This Workflow Does

This automation template automates the entire member onboarding process for communities managed in Orbit. It handles four critical tasks automatically whenever a new member joins your community: creating their member profile, updating their information in your system, adding internal notes about them, and posting a welcome message in Orbit.

By automating these repetitive tasks, you eliminate manual data entry errors, ensure consistent onboarding experiences for all members, and free up your team to focus on higher-value community engagement activities rather than administrative work.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Member Detection

The workflow starts when a new member is detected in your system. This could be triggered by a form submission, API call, or integration with your existing membership platform.

2. Create Member Record

The workflow first creates a complete member record in Orbit with all the essential details like name, email, role, and any custom fields your organization tracks.

3. Update Member Information Update

After creation, the workflow updates any additional information that wasn't included in the initial creation step, ensuring your member profiles are complete and accurate from day one.

4. Internal Note Creation

The system then automatically creates an internal note with relevant context about the member, such as their referral source, special permissions, or any other details your team needs to know.

5. Orbit Post Creation

Finally, the workflow posts a welcome message or announcement in Orbit to introduce the new member to your community, helping foster connections from the start.
