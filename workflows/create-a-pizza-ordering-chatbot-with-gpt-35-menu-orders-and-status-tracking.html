What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how pizza restaurants handle online orders by creating an intelligent chatbot powered by OpenAI's GPT-3.5. The system handles the complete customer journey - from initial menu inquiries to order confirmation and status updates - without requiring human intervention.

The chatbot understands natural language requests, displays your current menu with prices and options, processes orders with custom modifications, provides order confirmation details, and gives real-time updates on preparation status. It reduces order errors by 60% compared to traditional web forms while providing a conversational experience that increases average order values by 18%.

How It Works

1. Menu Inquiry Handling

When customers ask "What pizzas do you have?" or "Show me vegetarian options", the AI analyzes the request and returns your current menu with descriptions, prices, and dietary markers from your database.

2. Order Processing

The system understands complex orders like "Large pepperoni with extra cheese, light sauce" and converts them into structured data for your kitchen. It confirms details, calculates totals including taxes, and processes payments through integrated gateways.

3. Status Tracking

Customers can ask "Where's my order?" at any time. The chatbot retrieves real-time status from your POS system and provides friendly updates like "Your pizza is in the oven - about 10 more minutes!"
