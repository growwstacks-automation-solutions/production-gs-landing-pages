What This Workflow Does

This automation streamlines the process of creating Outlook emails with attachments, eliminating repetitive manual work. Businesses often waste valuable time drafting similar emails, attaching files, and sending communications. This workflow automates these tasks with precision and consistency.

The solution handles the entire email creation process automatically. It drafts the email with your specified content, attaches the correct files from your designated source, and sends the communication at the right time. This ensures professional, error-free emails while freeing up your team's time for higher-value work.

How It Works

1. Trigger Setup

The workflow begins when triggered by your chosen event - this could be a schedule, form submission, or system event. The trigger determines when the automated email process starts.

2. Email Draft Creation

The system creates a new email draft in Outlook using your predefined template. It populates dynamic fields like recipient addresses, subject lines, and body content from your data source.

3. Attachment Processing

The workflow retrieves the specified attachment from your storage system (OneDrive, SharePoint, or local storage) and attaches it to the draft email. It can handle multiple file types and naming conventions.

4. Email Delivery

Finally, the system sends the completed email either immediately or according to your specified schedule. You can configure delivery timing based on business needs.
