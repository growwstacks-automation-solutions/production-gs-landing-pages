What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of staying updated with tech industry news by automating the entire curation process. It collects articles from multiple sources, uses AI to analyze and summarize key points, then organizes the distilled insights into a structured Notion database.

For startups, investors, and tech teams, this automation replaces hours of manual research with a daily digest that highlights only the most relevant developments. The AI doesn't just summarize - it identifies emerging patterns, connects related stories, and surfaces insights you might miss when scanning headlines manually.

Example of AI-processed tech news organized in Notion with summaries, tags, and priority ratings

How It Works

1. Source Aggregation

The workflow pulls articles from configured RSS feeds, API sources, and newsletters. It checks for new content at scheduled intervals (typically morning and evening).

2. AI Processing

GPT-4.1 Mini analyzes each article, extracting key points, identifying relevant technologies/companies, and generating executive summaries with consistent formatting.

3. Relevance Scoring

The AI assigns importance scores based on factors like novelty, your specified interests, and source credibility to prioritize what deserves attention.

4. Notion Integration

Processed articles populate a Notion database with standardized fields: summary, source, date, tags, and direct links for deeper reading.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run before your team's morning standup so the digest is fresh when work begins.
