What This Workflow Does

This automation system solves the expensive and time-consuming problem of creating authentic user-generated content (UGC) videos for marketing. Instead of hiring creators, scheduling shoots, and editing footage, you simply send a product photo through a Telegram bot. The workflow then uses AI to analyze the image, generate realistic video clips of people using your product, and combine them into a complete marketing video.

The system bridges the gap between product photography and engaging video content. It understands product context, creates natural usage scenarios, and produces videos that look like they were filmed by real customers on their phones. This authenticity drives higher engagement and conversions compared to traditional branded content.

By automating the entire production pipeline, businesses can generate dozens of UGC videos per day at a fraction of traditional costs. The workflow handles everything from initial image analysis to final video delivery, making professional-quality UGC accessible to businesses of any size.

How It Works

1. Input via Telegram

You send a compressed product image (and optional character reference) to your Telegram bot with a simple text instruction like "Create a 20-second UGC video showing someone enjoying this coffee maker." The bot acknowledges receipt and begins processing.

2. AI Image Analysis

An OpenAI agent analyzes the uploaded image to extract product details (brand, color, features) and any character elements. This understanding forms the foundation for generating contextually appropriate video content that matches your product's actual appearance.

3. Prompt Generation & Image Creation

The system generates natural, UGC-style prompts describing realistic usage scenarios. It then creates supporting images using AI models, ensuring the visual style matches authentic "phone-filmed" aesthetics with proper aspect ratios for social platforms.

4. Video Clip Production

Based on your requested video length, the AI creates a script and divides it into scenes. Each scene prompt is sent to video generation models that produce short clips (typically 8 seconds each) showing your product in authentic usage situations.

5. Video Assembly & Delivery

All generated clips are automatically stitched together into a seamless final video. The completed file is then sent back to your Telegram chat or can be routed to other destinations like Google Drive, Dropbox, or your content management system.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance using the workflow import function.
  2. Configure Telegram: Create a Telegram bot via BotFather, obtain your token, and add it to the Telegram trigger node in the workflow.
  3. Connect AI services: Add your API keys for OpenAI and your chosen video generation service in their respective nodes.
  4. Test with a simple product: Start with a straightforward product image and basic instructions to verify the pipeline works end-to-end.
  5. Customize outputs: Adjust video length, style preferences, and delivery destinations based on your specific marketing needs.
  6. Scale production: Once configured, you can process multiple images simultaneously by duplicating workflow instances or adding batch processing.
