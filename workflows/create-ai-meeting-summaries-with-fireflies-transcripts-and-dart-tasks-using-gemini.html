What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually taking meeting notes, writing summaries, and creating follow-up tasks. By connecting Fireflies.ai meeting transcripts with Gemini AI and Dart task management, it transforms hours of administrative work into an automated process that happens in minutes.

The system captures every word from your meetings through Fireflies.ai's accurate transcription, uses Gemini AI to analyze and summarize key points, documents decisions in a structured Dart document, and creates actionable tasks with all relevant context. This ensures nothing falls through the cracks while saving each participant 2-3 hours per meeting.

How It Works

1. Fireflies.ai captures meeting transcripts

Fireflies.ai automatically joins your scheduled meetings (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, etc.) and records complete, timestamped transcripts with speaker identification. The AI notes important moments like action items, questions, and decisions during the meeting.

2. Transcripts are processed by Gemini AI

The raw transcript is sent to Gemini AI, which analyzes the conversation to extract key points, decisions, and action items. Gemini creates a professional summary with proper context, distinguishing between main topics, side discussions, and action items.

3. Summary saved to Dart document

The AI-generated summary is formatted and saved to a designated Dart document, organized by meeting date and participants. This creates a searchable knowledge base of all meeting outcomes without manual documentation.

4. Follow-up tasks created automatically

The workflow identifies all action items from the meeting and creates corresponding tasks in Dart with deadlines, assignees, and links back to the original meeting recording and transcript for context.
