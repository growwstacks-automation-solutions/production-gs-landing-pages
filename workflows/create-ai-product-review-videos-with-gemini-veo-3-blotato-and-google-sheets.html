What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating engaging product review videos at scale. Traditional video production requires significant time, resources, and coordination between writers, videographers, and editors. For e-commerce businesses with hundreds of products, manually creating review videos becomes impractical.

The workflow automatically transforms product data from Google Sheets into professional video reviews using Gemini AI for script generation and Veo 3 for video creation. Blotato orchestrates the process, ensuring smooth data flow between systems. The result is a scalable solution that produces consistent, high-quality product videos with minimal human intervention.

How It Works

1. Product data input

The workflow begins with your product data in Google Sheets, including product names, descriptions, key features, and image URLs. Each row represents a product that needs a review video.

2. AI script generation

Gemini AI analyzes the product information and generates a natural-sounding review script. The AI considers product specifications, target audience, and your preferred tone to create engaging content.

Pro tip: Include brand voice guidelines in your Google Sheets template to ensure consistent tone across all AI-generated scripts.

3. Video creation

Veo 3 takes the AI-generated script and product images to create a professional video review. The platform automatically adds animations, transitions, and text overlays based on the content structure.

4. Output delivery

The completed videos are saved to your preferred storage location (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) with standardized naming conventions. The workflow updates your original spreadsheet with video URLs for easy access.
