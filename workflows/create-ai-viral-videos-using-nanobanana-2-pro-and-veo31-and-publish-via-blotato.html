How It Works

1. Content Generation

NanoBanana 2 PRO analyzes trending topics and generates video concepts based on your niche. The AI suggests scripts, visuals, and editing styles proven to engage your target audience.

2. AI Enhancement

VEO3.1 automatically improves the raw footage with professional transitions, color correction, and audio balancing. It can add captions, emojis, and effects tailored to each platform's style.

3. Multi-Platform Optimization

The system creates platform-specific versions of each video - vertical for TikTok/Reels, square for Instagram, and landscape for YouTube. It generates appropriate thumbnails and aspect ratios for each.

4. Automated Publishing

Blotato handles the distribution, scheduling posts based on when your audience is most active on each platform. It manages all the technical requirements like file formats and metadata.

Pro tip: Set up content buckets (educational, entertaining, promotional) in NanoBanana to maintain a balanced content mix that keeps audiences engaged.
