AI Shopping Assistant on Telegram with GPT-4.1 & Google Sheets

Automate customer support & order taking on Telegram with AI. Handle text/voice messages, answer product questions, and record orders automatically.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of fragmented customer support and manual order processing for e-commerce businesses. Instead of having team members constantly monitor Telegram messages, answer repetitive product questions, and manually record orders in spreadsheets, this workflow creates a 24/7 AI shopping assistant that handles everything automatically.

The system transforms your Telegram bot into an intelligent sales agent that understands both text and voice messages, provides accurate product information using AI-powered search, and automatically captures complete orders in Google Sheets. It maintains conversation context so customers feel like they're chatting with a knowledgeable human assistant, while you save hours of daily manual work.

How It Works

Step 1: Message Capture

The Telegram Trigger node monitors your bot for incoming customer messages—both text and voice notes. When a message arrives, it immediately starts the automation process.

Step 2: Voice Processing

If the customer sent a voice message, the workflow downloads the audio file and uses OpenAI's Whisper technology to transcribe it into text. This allows the AI to understand spoken questions just as well as typed ones.

Step 3: Intelligent Response Generation

An AI Agent powered by GPT-4.1-nano analyzes the customer's message, searches your product catalog using Pinecone vector storage, and generates helpful, accurate responses. The agent remembers conversation context across up to 8 messages for natural interactions.

Step 4: Order Recording

When customers provide complete order details (name, phone, address, product), the AI automatically appends the information to your Google Sheets order log. This creates a structured record without any manual data entry.

Step 5: Response Delivery

The Telegram Response node sends the AI's answer back to the customer, completing the conversation loop instantly.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (self-hosted or n8n Cloud)
  2. A Telegram bot created via @BotFather with API token
  3. OpenAI API access for GPT-4.1-nano and Whisper transcription
  4. Pinecone account with vector database containing your product information
  5. Google Sheets account for order storage
  6. Your product catalog prepared in a format suitable for AI search

Pro tip: Start with a small subset of your products in Pinecone to test the AI's response accuracy before uploading your entire catalog. This helps refine your product descriptions for optimal AI understanding.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the downloaded JSON file into your n8n workspace.
  2. Create your Telegram bot through @BotFather and add its token to n8n's Telegram credentials.
  3. Configure OpenAI, Pinecone, and Google Sheets API connections in n8n.
  4. Upload your product data to Pinecone with clear descriptions, prices, and categories.
  5. Set up your Google Sheet with columns for customer name, phone, address, and product category.
  6. Customize the AI Agent's system message to reflect your brand voice and support policies.
  7. Test by sending both text and voice messages to your bot.
  8. Activate the workflow and share your bot username with customers.
