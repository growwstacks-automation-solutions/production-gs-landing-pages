What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually creating invoices from client submissions. When customers complete your Typeform for services, products, or registrations, this workflow instantly generates a professional invoice with all necessary details.

The system eliminates human error in data transfer and ensures invoices go out immediately after submission. This accelerates your billing cycle while maintaining consistency in your invoicing process across all clients and transactions.

How It Works

1. Typeform submission triggers the workflow

When a client submits your Typeform with billing information, the workflow automatically captures all response data. The system validates required fields like client name, email, service details, and pricing before proceeding.

2. Data processing and invoice generation

The workflow extracts relevant information from the Typeform responses and structures it for invoice creation. It calculates totals, applies taxes if configured, and formats all values according to your business requirements.

3. Invoice delivery to client

The completed invoice gets sent to the client via email with your branding. The system can also store a copy in your accounting software or cloud storage for record-keeping. Optional payment links can be included for immediate payment.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits service providers, freelancers, consultants, and any business that bills clients based on form submissions. Educational institutions processing course payments, event organizers collecting registration fees, and healthcare providers billing for services will find this particularly valuable.

What You'll Need

  1. An active Typeform account with your client submission form
  2. Access to an n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)
  3. Your preferred invoicing system or template
  4. Client billing information collected in your Typeform

Pro tip: Structure your Typeform questions to match your invoice fields exactly. Use dropdowns for services/products and validation for email/address fields to ensure data quality.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the JSON template file
  2. Import it into your n8n instance
  3. Connect your Typeform account in the first node
  4. Configure your invoice template in the document generation node
  5. Set up your preferred delivery method (email, accounting software, etc.)
  6. Test with a sample Typeform submission
