What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your script ideas into polished Instagram Reels without manual work. It combines three powerful AI tools to handle the entire content creation process: Heygen for AI video generation, Submagic for automatic captions and effects, and Blotato for scheduling and posting.

The workflow solves the time-consuming process of manually creating, editing, and posting Reels content. For content creators, marketers, and agencies managing multiple accounts, this automation can save 5-10 hours per week while maintaining consistent posting schedules and professional quality.

How It Works

1. Script Input

The workflow starts by accepting your video script either through a direct input or from a connected document or spreadsheet. The text is formatted for optimal AI video generation.

2. AI Video Generation with Heygen

Heygen creates a realistic AI clone video from your script, complete with natural-looking lip sync and facial expressions. The workflow automatically sends your script and receives the generated video file.

3. Caption & Effects with Submagic

The raw video is then enhanced with Submagic, which adds professional captions, transitions, and effects automatically based on the audio content and timing.

4. Auto-Posting via Blotato

The finished Reel is automatically uploaded to your Instagram account at your specified posting time, complete with optimized captions and hashtags.
