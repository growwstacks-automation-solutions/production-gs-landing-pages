What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the massive time drain of creating regular video content for social media and marketing. Instead of spending hours scripting, filming, and editing, this workflow automatically turns viral content ideas into polished AI videos and publishes them across your social channels.

The system connects Sora 2 Cameos for AI video generation with Gemini AI for intelligent script writing, creating a complete pipeline that requires zero manual intervention once configured. It's designed for content creators, marketers, and businesses who need to scale their video production without increasing their team size or budget.

By automating the entire process—from content discovery to final publishing—you can produce 5-10x more video content while maintaining consistent quality and brand voice. The workflow also tracks performance and logs results, giving you data to optimize your content strategy over time.

How It Works

The automation follows a structured pipeline that transforms raw ideas into published videos through intelligent processing at each stage.

1. Content Collection & Analysis

The workflow begins by automatically gathering viral content ideas from your configured sources—whether that's RSS feeds, social media trends, or your own content calendar. Each idea is analyzed for hooks, themes, and video potential before being added to a production queue.

2. AI Script & Prompt Generation

Gemini AI examines each content idea and generates structured scripts optimized for Sora 2 Cameos. The AI considers your selected avatar character, brand voice, and video length requirements to create prompts that will produce consistent, on-brand videos.

3. AI Video Production

Generated prompts are sent to the Sora 2 Cameos API, which renders short video clips using your pre-configured avatar. The workflow monitors rendering status, handles retries if needed, and ensures video quality meets your standards before moving to the next stage.

4. Video Assembly & Finalization

Multiple AI-generated clips are automatically merged into a cohesive final video. The system can add intro/outro sequences, background music, and text overlays based on your configuration, creating a polished piece ready for distribution.

5. Multi-Platform Publishing

The finished video is published simultaneously to your connected social platforms—TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, or others. Publishing results, including views, engagement, and any errors, are logged back to your tracking system for performance analysis.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Sora 2 Cameos account with API access and configured avatar characters
  3. Google Gemini API key or compatible LLM access for script generation
  4. Social media accounts with publishing permissions (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
  5. Google Sheets or similar for content tracking and performance logging
  6. Content sources configured (RSS feeds, trend APIs, or your own idea database)

Pro tip: Start with one social platform and a single content source when testing. Once the workflow runs smoothly, expand to additional platforms and sources. This incremental approach helps identify and fix issues before scaling.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure Sora 2 Cameos credentials in the appropriate nodes, selecting your preferred avatar character
  3. Add your Gemini API key to the AI agent nodes and customize the prompt templates for your brand voice
  4. Connect your social media accounts using OAuth or API tokens for each platform you want to publish to
  5. Set up your content source—configure RSS feeds, API connections, or Google Sheets for idea input
  6. Test with a single idea to verify the entire pipeline works before enabling scheduled execution
  7. Schedule regular execution based on your content needs—daily, weekly, or custom intervals

The workflow includes comprehensive error handling and retry logic, but monitor the first few executions closely to ensure all connections work correctly in your environment.
