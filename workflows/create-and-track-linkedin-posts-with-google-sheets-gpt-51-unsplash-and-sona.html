Generate professional posts, select smart images, get email approvals, and publish directly to LinkedIn—all automated.

What This Workflow Does

Consistent LinkedIn content is essential for business growth, but creating daily posts consumes valuable time. This automation solves that by connecting your content planning in Google Sheets with AI-powered writing, intelligent image selection, and direct LinkedIn publishing.

The workflow transforms your content ideas into scheduled LinkedIn posts without manual intervention. It reads post briefs from Google Sheets, generates professional content using GPT-5.1, selects relevant images from Unsplash, sends drafts for email approval, and publishes approved content directly to your LinkedIn profile. Everything is tracked back to your spreadsheet for complete visibility.

How It Works

1. Content Planning in Google Sheets

You manage all your LinkedIn content ideas in a simple Google Sheet. Each row contains a topic, content type, tone, target audience, and status. When you mark a post as "Ready," the automation picks it up for processing.

2. AI Content Generation

GPT-5.1 reads your brief and creates a professional LinkedIn post with proper formatting, hashtags, and engagement hooks. The AI follows your specified tone and content type to ensure brand consistency.

3. Quality Validation & Image Selection

The system automatically checks character limits and hashtag requirements. It then selects the most relevant, high-quality image from Unsplash based on your post content, or uses your provided image URL if available.

4. Email Approval Workflow

Before anything publishes, you receive a formatted email preview with the complete post and image. You simply reply "approve" to continue or make edits in your sheet if changes are needed.

5. LinkedIn Publishing & Tracking

Once approved, the workflow posts directly to your LinkedIn profile using the appropriate API. It then updates your Google Sheet with the publication status and timestamp for complete tracking.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Account for Sheets integration and Gmail approval workflow
  2. OpenAI API Key with access to GPT-5.1 (paid plan required)
  3. LinkedIn Account with publishing permissions
  4. Unsplash API Key (free tier available)
  5. n8n Instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  6. Google Sheet with the required columns: Topic, Content Type, Tone, Target Audience, Image Link, Include Image?, Status

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the workflow template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect credentials for Google Sheets, OpenAI, Gmail, LinkedIn, and Unsplash
  3. Create your content sheet with the required column structure
  4. Test with one post marked as "Ready" to verify the entire flow
  5. Configure schedule (default: daily at midnight) to match your posting frequency
  6. Start batching content by adding multiple ideas to your sheet
