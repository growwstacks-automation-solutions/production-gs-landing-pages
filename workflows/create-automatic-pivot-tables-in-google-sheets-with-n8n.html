What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the time-consuming task of manually creating pivot tables in Google Sheets. Every time new data arrives in your spreadsheet, the workflow automatically generates two customized pivot tables that summarize and analyze your data.

Businesses waste countless hours recreating pivot tables whenever source data changes. This automation eliminates that repetitive work while ensuring your reports are always up-to-date with the latest figures. The workflow pulls campaign data (or any dataset) from Google Sheets, processes it through n8n, and outputs the analyzed results as new pivot tables in your spreadsheet.

How It Works

Step 1: Connect to Google Sheets

The workflow begins by authenticating with your Google account and accessing the specified spreadsheet. It reads the raw data from your source sheet where new information gets added regularly.

Step 2: Process Data in n8n

n8n receives the spreadsheet data and prepares it for analysis. The workflow includes logic to clean, filter, and structure the data appropriately for pivot table creation.

Step 3: Generate Pivot Tables

The workflow creates two distinct pivot tables with different configurations. You can customize what dimensions to analyze (rows, columns, values) and which calculations to perform (sums, averages, counts).

Step 4: Output to Google Sheets

The completed pivot tables get written back to your Google Sheets document in designated tabs. The workflow can run on a schedule or be triggered manually whenever you need fresh analysis.
