What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating consistent, high-quality LinkedIn content. Instead of manually designing carousels and writing captions each week, this workflow automatically generates professional posts using your brand's Figma templates and AI-powered content creation.

The system fetches your pre-designed Figma templates from Templated, generates engaging captions using GPT-4o-mini (or uses your static content), creates branded images with dynamic text replacement, publishes directly to LinkedIn, and sends success/failure notifications via Telegram. It's perfect for marketers, agencies, content creators, and businesses wanting to maintain a strong LinkedIn presence without the manual effort.

How It Works

1. Schedule Trigger & Content Preparation

The workflow starts on your chosen schedule (daily, weekly, or custom cron). It then either uses static content you've predefined or activates AI agents to generate fresh LinkedIn post content based on your topic, industry, and target audience.

2. Template Selection & Image Generation

The system connects to your Templated account to fetch available Figma templates. It selects the appropriate template based on your content type, then dynamically replaces placeholder text layers with your actual content while respecting character limits and design constraints.

3. LinkedIn Publishing & Notification

Once images are generated, the workflow authenticates with LinkedIn's API, uploads the carousel document, creates the post with your caption, and publishes it to your profile or company page. Success or error notifications are then sent to your Telegram channel for monitoring.

What You'll Need

  1. A free Templated account (templated.cometai.eu) with your Figma designs imported
  2. A LinkedIn Developer application with OAuth2 credentials and posting permissions
  3. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  4. Optional: OpenAI API access for AI content generation (or use static content)
  5. Optional: Telegram bot for workflow notifications

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 different Figma templates for variety. Create templates for quotes, statistics, tips, and announcements to keep your content visually diverse while maintaining brand consistency.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure Templated connection by adding your API key to the MCP client nodes.
  3. Set up LinkedIn OAuth in the HTTP Request nodes using your Developer app credentials.
  4. Customize content fields in the "Edit Fields" nodes with your brand messaging.
  5. Test with manual trigger before scheduling to ensure everything works correctly.
  6. Activate the schedule (start with weekly posts) and monitor initial results.
