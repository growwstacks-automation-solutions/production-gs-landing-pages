What This Workflow Does

Creating engaging, humorous video content for social media is time-consuming and expensive. This automation solves that by building a complete, hands-off production pipeline. It generates creative video concepts using AI, turns them into realistic short videos with Sora2, crafts engaging captions, and publishes them to your social channels on a schedule.

Imagine launching a weekly comedic series for your product without ever picking up a camera. The workflow handles ideation, video generation, and distribution, freeing your team to focus on strategy and engagement. It's perfect for brands that want to maintain a consistent, viral-ready presence on platforms like Instagram Reels and TikTok without the production overhead.

Beyond saving time, it enables rapid experimentation. You can test different humor styles, product placements, and hooks to see what resonates with your audience, using data to refine your content strategy automatically.

How It Works

The workflow orchestrates several cutting-edge AI and automation services into a seamless sequence.

1. Scheduled Trigger & Concept Generation

A schedule node initiates the process daily or weekly. An AI agent (using OpenAI) then generates a unique, cinematic video prompt. This isn't a simple description; it's a detailed scene setup including camera angles, lighting, and comedic timing, tailored to promote your specific product or brand.

2. AI Video Creation with Sora2

The generated prompt is sent to the Sora2 API via Wavespeed to create a 12-second, ultra-realistic video. The workflow includes smart polling to wait for video rendering completion, handling the asynchronous nature of AI video generation seamlessly.

3. Social Media Integration & Posting

Once the video is ready, it's uploaded to your connected Blotato account. A second AI agent crafts a platform-optimized caption complete with relevant hashtags. Finally, the post is published or scheduled automatically to your chosen social platforms.

Pro tip: Use the built-in Data Table node to log every generated prompt and video URL. This creates a searchable library of successful concepts you can reuse or remix later.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your automated reel factory running in under 30 minutes.

  1. Import the template into your n8n workspace using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials: Add your OpenAI, Wavespeed/Sora2, and Blotato API keys to the respective credential nodes.
  3. Customize the AI Agent: Open the "Video Prompt Agent" node and update the system prompt to reflect your brand's voice, product details, and desired humor style.
  4. Set your schedule: Adjust the Schedule Trigger node to run at your preferred time (e.g., every Monday at 9 AM).
  5. Test the workflow: Execute it once manually to verify video generation, caption writing, and posting work correctly.
  6. Activate & forget: Enable the workflow. It will now run autonomously, creating and posting content on your behalf.

Pro tip: Start with a weekly schedule to gauge content performance. Use platform analytics to see which AI-generated videos get the most engagement, then refine your prompts accordingly.
