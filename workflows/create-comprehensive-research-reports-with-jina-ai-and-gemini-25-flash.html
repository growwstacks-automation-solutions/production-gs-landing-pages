What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered research assistant automates the entire process of turning a simple question into a comprehensive, well-structured report. Instead of spending hours manually searching the web, reading articles, taking notes, and writing summaries, this workflow handles everything from initial query to final polished document.

The system leverages Jina AI's powerful web crawling capabilities to find and extract content from the most relevant sources, then uses Google Gemini's advanced reasoning to analyze, summarize, and synthesize the information into a professionally formatted report complete with proper citations. It's like having a dedicated research team that works in minutes instead of days.

Perfect for market researchers, content creators, students, analysts, and business professionals who need to quickly gather and process information from multiple sources while maintaining academic rigor and proper attribution.

How It Works

The workflow follows a sophisticated pipeline that mimics how expert researchers work, but at machine speed and scale.

1. Query Initiation & Web Search

You submit your research question through a chat interface or webhook. The workflow immediately passes this to Jina AI, which performs intelligent web searches to find the 10 most relevant and authoritative sources on your topic.

2. Multi-Source Content Extraction

For each search result, Jina AI reads and extracts the full textual content, cleaning it of ads, navigation, and other irrelevant elements. This ensures you get pure information from each source without manual copy-pasting.

3. AI-Powered Summarization

Each extracted article is processed by a Gemini-powered summarizer agent that identifies key points, main arguments, and relevant data while maintaining context from your original query. The system includes rate-limiting to ensure stable API usage.

4. Knowledge Synthesis & Report Drafting

All summaries are aggregated and fed to a specialized generator agent that writes a complete research paper with executive summary, introduction, analysis sections, and conclusion. It structures information logically and adds inline citations for every claim.

5. Quality Assurance & Final Polish

A final evaluator agent reviews the draft for citation accuracy, logical flow, academic tone, and markdown formatting. It corrects any inconsistencies and produces a publication-ready document that you can immediately use or further customize.
