What This Workflow Does

Google Alerts is essential for staying informed, but it creates notification fatigue. You receive dozens of emails daily, each containing multiple articles, leading to information overload where critical insights get buried in your inbox.

This automation solves that problem by acting as your personal research assistant. It systematically fetches unread Google Alerts emails, extracts article links, scrapes and cleans the actual content, then uses Google's Gemini AI to generate concise 2-4 sentence summaries of each piece. Finally, it compiles everything into a structured HTML digest and sends it to you at a scheduled time, marking the original alerts as read.

The result? Instead of spending 30-60 minutes daily scanning emails, you get a 5-minute executive briefing that highlights trends, competitor moves, and industry developments—all formatted for quick consumption and decision-making.

How It Works

1. Email Ingestion & Processing

The workflow connects to your Gmail account and searches for unread emails from Google Alerts. It extracts all article URLs from each email body, handling various email formats and link structures to ensure comprehensive coverage.

2. Content Extraction & Cleaning

Each article URL is fetched, and the main content is extracted while stripping away ads, navigation, comments, and other webpage clutter. This cleaning process ensures the AI receives high-quality text for summarization, improving accuracy and relevance.

3. AI-Powered Summarization

Google Gemini AI processes each cleaned article with specific instructions to identify key points, extract actionable insights, and condense information while preserving context. The AI is prompted to focus on business implications, trends, and competitive intelligence relevant to your industry.

4. Digest Compilation & Delivery

Summaries are organized by topic or alert category, formatted into a professional HTML email with clear headings, bullet points, and source links. The digest is sent to your specified email address, and all processed Google Alerts emails are automatically marked as read to keep your inbox clean.

What You'll Need

  1. A Gmail account receiving Google Alerts emails (works with Google Workspace accounts too)
  2. Google Gemini API access with an active API key (available through Google AI Studio)
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  4. Basic understanding of how to import JSON workflows and configure node credentials in n8n
  5. 10-15 minutes for initial setup and configuration

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 Google Alert topics to test the workflow before scaling to all your alerts. This lets you refine the AI prompts and output format based on actual results.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance using the "Import from File" option.
  2. Configure Gmail node by authenticating your Google account with the appropriate permissions to read emails and send messages.
  3. Add your Gemini API key in the Google Gemini node settings within the workflow.
  4. Update the recipient email in the "Send Email Digest" node to your preferred destination address.
  5. Test the workflow manually using the "Execute Workflow" button to ensure all connections work correctly.
  6. Set up scheduling by replacing the Manual Trigger node with a Schedule Trigger configured for your preferred time (e.g., daily at 7 AM).
  7. Monitor and refine the first few digests, adjusting AI prompts if needed to better match your information needs.
