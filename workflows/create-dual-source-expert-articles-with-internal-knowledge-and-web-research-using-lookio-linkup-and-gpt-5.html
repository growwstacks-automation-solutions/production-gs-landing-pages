What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of creating truly authoritative content by automatically combining your organization's unique internal knowledge with the most relevant, up-to-date information from the web. Many businesses struggle to produce content that stands out because they either rely solely on their own expertise (which may become outdated) or only use external sources (lacking unique perspective).

The template integrates Lookio for internal knowledge retrieval, Linkup for web research, and GPT-5 for content synthesis. It automatically researches topics, combines findings with your proprietary information, and generates well-structured articles that demonstrate both expertise and current relevance - saving content teams hours of manual research and writing time.

How It Works

1. Internal Knowledge Retrieval

The workflow first queries your internal knowledge base using Lookio to extract relevant proprietary information, case studies, and unique insights your organization possesses about the topic.

2. Web Research Phase

Simultaneously, Linkup performs web searches to gather the latest articles, studies, and data points from authoritative sources related to your content topic.

3. Content Synthesis

GPT-5 analyzes both knowledge streams, identifies connections between your internal expertise and external findings, and structures a comprehensive article that positions your organization as both knowledgeable and current.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to prioritize recent (last 6-12 month) research findings to ensure your content reflects the latest developments in your field.
