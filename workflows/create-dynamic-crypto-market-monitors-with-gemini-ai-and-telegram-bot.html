What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates cryptocurrency market monitoring by combining Gemini AI's analytical capabilities with instant Telegram notifications. It solves the problem of manually tracking multiple crypto assets across different exchanges, saving traders hours of daily market surveillance while providing more sophisticated analysis than basic price alerts.

The system continuously analyzes market conditions, identifies significant movements or patterns, and delivers actionable insights directly to your Telegram account. This allows traders to respond faster to opportunities without being glued to trading screens all day.

Data flow architecture from exchange APIs through Gemini AI to Telegram alerts

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls real-time market data from cryptocurrency exchange APIs, including price movements, trading volumes, order book depth, and technical indicators.

2. AI Analysis

Gemini AI processes this data stream, applying machine learning models to detect patterns, correlations, and anomalies that may signal trading opportunities or risks.

3. Alert Generation

When predefined conditions are met (price thresholds, volume spikes, etc.), the workflow generates concise market summaries with key insights and recommended actions.

4. Telegram Delivery

Formatted alerts are sent instantly to your Telegram account, with options for different message types (text, charts, or combined media) based on the alert priority.
