What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how e-commerce businesses create and publish promotional carousels across social platforms. Instead of manually designing each slide and scheduling posts, the workflow uses Gemini 2.5 AI to generate cohesive, brand-consistent carousels automatically based on your product data.

The system pulls product information from your e-commerce platform, analyzes it with AI to create compelling visuals and copy, formats the content into platform-optimized carousels, and publishes them according to your social media calendar. This eliminates the repetitive work while improving content quality and consistency.

The workflow connects product data with AI content generation and social publishing

How It Works

1. Product Data Integration

The workflow begins by pulling product information from your e-commerce platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.). It extracts key details like product names, descriptions, prices, and images that will form the foundation of your carousel content.

2. AI Content Generation

Gemini 2.5 processes the product data to create engaging carousel content. The AI generates slide-by-slide narratives, writes compelling copy in your brand voice, and suggests optimal visual layouts. You can customize the AI parameters to match your specific promotional goals.

3. Carousel Assembly

The system automatically assembles the AI-generated content into properly formatted carousels for each social platform. It handles image sizing, text placement, and slide sequencing according to best practices for maximum engagement.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

Finally, the workflow publishes the completed carousels to your connected social accounts based on your content calendar. It can distribute the same campaign across multiple platforms with platform-specific optimizations.

Pro tip: Set up separate workflows for different campaign types (product launches vs. sales) to maintain distinct messaging strategies while keeping the automation consistent.
