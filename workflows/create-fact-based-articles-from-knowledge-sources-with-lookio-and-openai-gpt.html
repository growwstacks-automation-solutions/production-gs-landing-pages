What This Workflow Does

Move beyond generic AI-generated content and create articles that are high-quality, factually reliable, and aligned with your unique expertise. This template orchestrates a sophisticated "research-first" content creation process. Instead of simply asking an AI to write an article from scratch, it first uses an AI planner to break your topic down into logical sub-questions.

It then queries a Lookio assistant—which you've connected to your own trusted knowledge base of uploaded documents—to build a comprehensive research brief. Only then is this fact-checked brief handed to a powerful AI writer to compose the final article, complete with source links. This is the ultimate workflow for scaling expert-level content creation while maintaining accuracy and brand alignment.

How It Works

This workflow follows a sophisticated, multi-step process to ensure the highest quality output:

Step 1: Topic Decomposition

You provide an article title and guidelines via the built-in form. An initial AI call acts as a "planner," breaking down the main topic into an array of 5-8 logical sub-questions that ensure comprehensive coverage.

Step 2: Fact-Based Research (RAG)

The workflow loops through each sub-question and queries your Lookio assistant. This assistant, pre-configured with your uploaded documents, finds relevant information and source links for each point, grounding the content in your verified knowledge.

Step 3: Research Consolidation

All retrieved question-and-answer pairs are compiled into a single, comprehensive research brief. This structured document contains all the factual material needed for the final article.

Step 4: Final Article Generation

The complete, fact-checked brief is handed to a final AI writer (e.g., GPT-4o). Its instructions are clear: write a high-quality article using only the provided information and integrate source links as hyperlinks where appropriate.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import: Use the download button above to get the JSON template, then import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure credentials: Connect your AI provider credentials to the two Language Model nodes in the workflow.
  3. Set up Lookio integration: In the "Query Lookio Assistant" HTTP Request node, paste your Assistant ID in the body and add your Lookio API Key as a Bearer Token credential.
  4. Test with a simple topic: Activate the workflow and use the built-in form to generate your first fact-checked article with a straightforward subject.
  5. Adjust prompts for your brand: Tweak the system prompts in the AI nodes to match your specific tone of voice and content requirements.

Pro tip: Start with a small set of well-organized documents in Lookio. Clear, structured source material produces the most coherent and useful research briefs for the AI writer.
