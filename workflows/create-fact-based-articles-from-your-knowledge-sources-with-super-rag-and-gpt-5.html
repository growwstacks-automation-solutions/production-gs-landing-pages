What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for businesses drowning in internal knowledge but struggling to create external content. Most companies have valuable expertise locked in Notion pages, Google Drive documents, PDFs, and internal wikis, but transforming that knowledge into polished articles takes enormous time and effort.

The workflow implements a sophisticated "research-first" approach to content creation. Instead of asking AI to write from scratch (which often produces generic or inaccurate content), it first breaks down your topic into logical sub-questions, researches each question against your actual knowledge sources, compiles verified information with source links, and only then generates a comprehensive, fact-checked article.

This process mirrors how expert content creators work but automates the heavy lifting. The result is authoritative content that reflects your unique expertise, includes proper citations, and establishes your brand as a thought leader—all produced at a fraction of the usual time and cost.

How It Works

1. Topic Decomposition

You provide an article title and guidelines through a simple form. An AI "planner" analyzes the topic and breaks it down into 5-8 logical sub-questions that need to be answered for a comprehensive article.

2. Knowledge Base Research

The workflow queries your Super assistant (connected to your Notion, Google Drive, PDFs, etc.) for each sub-question. It retrieves relevant information along with source URLs, ensuring every fact comes from your trusted knowledge base.

3. Research Consolidation

All question-and-answer pairs are compiled into a single, comprehensive research brief. This brief includes verified information, source citations, and the logical structure for the final article.

4. Article Generation

A powerful AI writer (like GPT-5) receives the research brief with clear instructions: create a high-quality article using only the provided information and integrate source links as hyperlinks where appropriate.

Pro tip: The key to success is having a well-organized knowledge base. Spend time structuring your Notion pages or Google Drive folders with clear titles and metadata—this dramatically improves the AI's ability to find relevant information.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. A Super assistant account with connected knowledge sources (Notion, Google Drive, etc.)
  3. API access to an AI provider like OpenAI (GPT-5 or similar)
  4. Your Assistant ID and API Token from Super
  5. Basic familiarity with n8n workflow configuration

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure AI credentials in the two Language Model nodes (GPT 5 mini and GPT 5 chat).
  3. Set up Super integration by adding your Assistant ID and API Token to the HTTP Request node.
  4. Test the workflow by activating it and submitting a test article topic through the form.
  5. Customize prompts to match your brand voice and content requirements.
