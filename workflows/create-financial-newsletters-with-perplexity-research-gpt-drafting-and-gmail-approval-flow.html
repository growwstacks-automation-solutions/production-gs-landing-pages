How It Works

1. AI-Powered Research

Perplexity AI scans financial news sources and research reports, identifying key trends and compiling supporting data. The system prioritizes emerging themes based on market impact scores and your firm's focus areas.

2. Intelligent Drafting

GPT structures the research into coherent narratives, applying your brand voice guidelines. The AI highlights unusual data patterns and generates multiple interpretation angles for editor review.

3. Compliance Review Flow

Drafts route automatically to compliance officers and subject matter experts via Gmail. The system tracks approvals, collects feedback, and only proceeds when all stakeholders have signed off.

4. Personalized Distribution

Final versions are tailored to recipient profiles, with technical depth adjusted for different client types. The workflow integrates with your existing email platform for seamless delivery.

Pro tip: Configure the GPT model to generate executive summaries alongside detailed analysis, allowing time-pressed readers to quickly grasp key insights.
