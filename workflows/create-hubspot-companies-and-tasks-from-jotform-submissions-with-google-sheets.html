Create HubSpot companies & tasks from Jotform submissions with Google Sheets

Automatically create or update HubSpot companies and generate follow-up tasks whenever a Jotform is submitted

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manual data entry between form submissions and CRM systems. When potential customers submit information through Jotform, sales teams typically waste hours copying details into HubSpot and creating follow-up tasks—a process prone to errors and delays.

The workflow automatically creates or updates HubSpot company records whenever a Jotform is submitted, while simultaneously generating properly assigned tasks for sales reps. Google Sheets serves as a flexible intermediary that can transform, validate, or enrich the data before it reaches HubSpot.

How It Works

Step 1: Jotform submission triggers the workflow

Whenever someone submits your configured Jotform, the workflow activates. It captures all form fields including company name, contact details, and any custom questions you've added.

Step 2: Data flows into Google Sheets

The submission details are written to a designated Google Sheet tab. This serves as both a backup and transformation layer where you can add formulas to clean data, merge fields, or flag submissions needing review.

Step 3: HubSpot company creation/update

The workflow checks if the company already exists in HubSpot by domain or name. If found, it updates the record; if new, it creates a company with all relevant details pulled from the form.

Step 4: Task generation in HubSpot

Finally, the system creates a follow-up task in HubSpot assigned to the appropriate team member. The task includes contextual details from the form and can be customized with due dates, priorities, and custom properties.
