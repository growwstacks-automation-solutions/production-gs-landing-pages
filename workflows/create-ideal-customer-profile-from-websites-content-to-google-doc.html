What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your website content into a structured Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) document in Google Docs. It analyzes your case studies, product pages, and blog content to identify common customer characteristics, pain points, and buying signals—creating a data-driven foundation for your marketing and sales strategies.

Traditional ICP creation requires manual research across multiple sources, subjective interpretation, and tedious documentation. This workflow eliminates those bottlenecks by automatically extracting and organizing customer insights from your existing web content, saving 10-15 hours per profile while reducing human bias.

How It Works

1. Content Analysis

The workflow scans your website's customer-facing pages, identifying frequently mentioned challenges, desired outcomes, and demographic indicators. It uses natural language processing to detect patterns across different content types.

2. Data Structuring

Key findings are categorized into ICP components: firmographics (company attributes), technographics (tools used), psychographics (decision motivations), and buying triggers. The system weights findings by content type—case study mentions carry more weight than blog references.

3. Document Creation

A formatted Google Doc is generated with sections for each ICP dimension, including direct quotes from your content as evidence. The document includes editable templates for sales teams to operationalize the findings.

Who This Is For

This workflow delivers maximum value for B2B companies with content-rich websites, particularly those in SaaS, professional services, and technology. Marketing teams use it to refine messaging, sales leaders deploy it for account targeting, and founders leverage it to validate product-market fit.

It's especially powerful for companies experiencing rapid growth where customer understanding hasn't kept pace with expansion, or for teams transitioning from broad outreach to account-based strategies.
