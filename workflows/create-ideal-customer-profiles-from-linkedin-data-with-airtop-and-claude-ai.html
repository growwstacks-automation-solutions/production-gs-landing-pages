Automatically analyze LinkedIn profiles to build detailed customer personas with AI-powered insights

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for B2B marketing and sales teams: accurately defining your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) based on real data rather than assumptions. By analyzing patterns across your best customers' LinkedIn profiles, you can identify common characteristics that predict conversion success.

The workflow extracts key details from LinkedIn profiles (job titles, industries, skills, etc.), processes them through Claude AI to identify patterns and insights, then structures the findings in Airtable for easy segmentation and targeting. This creates a data-driven foundation for your marketing campaigns and sales outreach.

How It Works

Step 1: Collect LinkedIn Profile Data

The workflow starts by gathering LinkedIn profile data from your existing customers or prospects. This can be triggered manually or automatically when new connections are made.

Step 2: Analyze with Claude AI

Claude AI processes the raw profile data to identify common patterns, extract key traits, and generate insights about what makes your best customers similar.

Step 3: Structure in Airtable

The analyzed data gets organized into Airtable with standardized fields for job titles, industries, skills, and other ICP-relevant attributes, creating a searchable database of ideal customer characteristics.

For example, your ICP might target SaaS companies with 50-200 employees, while your buyer persona identifies the specific job titles (like "Director of Growth") that champion your solution internally.
