Automatically sync new Shopify customers to your Mautic marketing automation platform

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring customer data between Shopify and marketing automation platforms. When a new customer signs up or makes a purchase in your Shopify store, their information often needs to be manually added to Mautic for marketing campaigns.

The workflow automatically creates a new contact in Mautic whenever Shopify detects a new customer. By default, it syncs first name, last name, and email address, but can be easily customized to include additional customer data points. This ensures your marketing team has immediate access to fresh leads without any manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Shopify triggers on new customer

The workflow starts when Shopify's API detects a new customer creation event. This could be from a new account signup or a guest checkout conversion.

2. Customer data extraction

The workflow extracts key customer information including name, email, and any other configured fields from the Shopify customer object.

3. Data transformation

Customer data is formatted to match Mautic's expected contact schema, ensuring proper field mapping between the two systems.

4. Mautic contact creation

The formatted customer data is sent to Mautic's API, which creates a new contact record and makes it available for segmentation and marketing campaigns.

Pro tip: Extend this workflow to include customer tags from Shopify as custom fields in Mautic, enabling powerful segmentation based on purchase behavior.
