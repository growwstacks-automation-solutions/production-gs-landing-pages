Automatically sync new Airtable records to Mailchimp subscribers

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of transferring new contacts from Airtable to Mailchimp. Whenever a new record is added to your specified Airtable base, the workflow automatically creates a corresponding subscriber in your Mailchimp audience with all relevant contact details mapped correctly.

The solution eliminates spreadsheet exports, CSV uploads, and copy-paste errors that plague manual processes. It ensures your email marketing lists stay perfectly synchronized with your Airtable CRM or lead database in real-time, improving deliverability and segmentation accuracy.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Airtable records

The workflow monitors your specified Airtable base for new records. You can configure it to watch specific tables or views, and even filter for only certain record types if needed.

2. Validate and format contact data

Before syncing to Mailchimp, the workflow validates email addresses and ensures required fields are present. It can also transform data formats (like splitting full names) to match Mailchimp's expected structure.

3. Create Mailchimp subscriber

The contact data is securely sent to Mailchimp's API, creating a new subscriber in your chosen audience. The workflow handles all field mappings between Airtable and Mailchimp automatically.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Airtable to track which records have been synced to Mailchimp, preventing duplicate submissions if the workflow runs multiple times.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for marketing teams, event organizers, course creators, and sales teams who use Airtable as their source of truth for contacts but need those contacts in Mailchimp for email campaigns. It's particularly valuable when:
