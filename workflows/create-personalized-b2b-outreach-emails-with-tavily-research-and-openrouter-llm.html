What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the creation of highly personalized B2B outreach emails by combining AI-powered company research with advanced text generation. It solves the challenge of scaling personalized outreach while maintaining quality that matches manual research.

The system first researches target companies using Tavily to gather relevant business context, then uses OpenRouter's LLM to craft tailored email messages that reference actual company developments, pain points, and opportunities. This results in outreach that feels informed and relevant rather than generic.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Target Company Details

The workflow begins by accepting a list of target companies with basic information like company name, website, and key contacts. This can be fed from your CRM, spreadsheet, or other data sources.

Step 2: Automated Company Research

Tavily Research analyzes each company to gather recent news, funding announcements, product updates, leadership changes, and industry trends. This creates the contextual foundation for personalized messaging.

Step 3: AI Message Generation

OpenRouter's LLM processes the research data to craft customized email content. The AI references specific company developments while maintaining your brand voice and including clear value propositions.

Step 4: Output & Delivery

The finalized emails are output in your preferred format (direct to email service, CRM, or spreadsheet). The system can also trigger follow-up sequences based on recipient engagement.
