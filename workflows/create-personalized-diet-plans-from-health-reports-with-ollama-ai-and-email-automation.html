What This Workflow Does

This automation solution transforms raw health data into actionable nutrition plans with minimal manual effort. When clients submit lab results or health questionnaires via email, the system automatically analyzes their biomarkers, restrictions, and goals to generate a fully personalized 4-week meal plan.

The workflow handles the entire process from data extraction to final delivery. AI identifies key nutritional needs from blood tests, body composition scans, or medical histories, then constructs meal recommendations that align with clinical guidelines while accommodating personal preferences and allergies.

How It Works

1. Health Report Submission

Clients email their health documents to your dedicated inbox. The system supports PDF lab reports, scanned forms, or structured questionnaires.

2. Data Extraction

Key health metrics are automatically extracted from documents using OCR and natural language processing. The system identifies cholesterol levels, vitamin deficiencies, food intolerances, and other nutrition-relevant data.

3. AI Analysis

Ollama AI processes the health data through clinical nutrition models to determine optimal macronutrient ratios, micronutrient targets, and dietary restrictions.

4. Meal Plan Generation

The system constructs a 28-day rotating menu with breakfast, lunch, dinner and snack options tailored to the client's caloric needs, preferences, and health objectives.

5. Automated Delivery

Completed plans are emailed to clients with supporting educational materials, shopping lists, and preparation tips. Follow-up reminders are scheduled automatically.

What You'll Need

  1. An email account for receiving client health reports (IMAP enabled)
  2. Ollama AI access or alternative nutrition analysis API
  3. SMTP email service for sending diet plans
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  5. Optional: Meal database or recipe repository

Pro tip: Create template meal components (breakfasts, lunches etc.) that the AI can mix-and-match based on nutritional profiles to maintain consistency while personalizing plans.
