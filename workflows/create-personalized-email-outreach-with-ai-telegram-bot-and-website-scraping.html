What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized email outreach while maintaining quality and relevance. Traditional email campaigns often fail because they lack personalization or require excessive manual work to customize each message.

The workflow combines three powerful technologies: AI-generated personalization, Telegram bot interactions, and targeted website scraping. Together they create a system that delivers hyper-relevant emails automatically while allowing prospects to request demos or information through simple Telegram commands.

How It Works

1. Prospect Data Collection

The workflow begins by scraping publicly available website data about your target companies or individuals. This could include recent blog posts, product updates, job openings, or news mentions that provide natural conversation starters.

2. AI Personalization Engine

An AI model analyzes the scraped data along with any existing CRM information to craft personalized email content. It identifies the most relevant talking points and structures the message to demonstrate genuine understanding of the recipient's situation.

3. Telegram Bot Integration

Recipients can interact with your outreach through a Telegram bot included in the email signature. Simple commands like "/demo" or "/schedule" trigger automated follow-up sequences while qualifying leads in real-time.
