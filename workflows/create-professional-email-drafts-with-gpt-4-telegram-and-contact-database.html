This intelligent automation transforms how you create business emails by combining AI-powered drafting with instant access to your contact database. The workflow solves three major pain points: 1) Time wasted drafting repetitive emails 2) Inconsistent messaging quality 3) Difficulty personalizing at scale. The automation flow from Telegram command to completed email draft

How It Works

1. Initiate with Telegram command

Send a simple command like "/draft_email [contact_name]" through Telegram. The bot authenticates your identity and captures your request parameters. This mobile-friendly trigger works anywhere, perfect for busy professionals needing quick email drafts on the go.

2. Retrieve contact details

The workflow queries your connected CRM or contact database to pull all relevant information about the recipient. This includes their name, position, company, past interactions, and any custom fields you've configured for personalization.

Pro tip: Structure your contact database with clear relationship fields (like "Client Since" or "Last Meeting") to enable richer AI personalization.

3. Generate draft with GPT-4

GPT-4 analyzes the contact details, your command context, and any style guidelines you've provided to compose a professional email draft. The AI maintains your brand voice while adapting to each recipient's specific context and relationship with your business.

4. Deliver completed draft

The polished email draft returns to you via Telegram or your preferred output channel (email client, Google Docs, etc.) for final review and sending. The entire process typically completes in under 30 seconds, compared to 5-15 minutes for manual drafting.
