What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your team's blog ideas into fully researched, AI-assisted content drafts automatically. When a content idea is posted in Slack, the workflow uses Perplexity AI to gather relevant research, stores findings in Pinecone for future reference, and compiles everything into a structured Google Doc ready for writing.

The system eliminates the most time-consuming part of content creation - the research phase - while ensuring every post is backed by credible data. Marketing teams can produce higher-quality content faster, with less manual effort and more consistent research standards across all writers.

How It Works

1. Slack Trigger

Team members submit content ideas through a designated Slack channel using a standardized format. The workflow captures the topic, target keywords, and any specific research requests.

2. Perplexity Research

The system queries Perplexity AI with the topic to gather current statistics, studies, and expert opinions. It evaluates source credibility and extracts the most relevant findings.

3. Pinecone Knowledge Base

All research findings are stored in Pinecone as vector embeddings, creating a searchable knowledge base. Before new research begins, Pinecone checks for existing relevant information.

4. Google Docs Compilation

The workflow structures the research into a Google Doc with proper citations, suggested headings, and key takeaways. Writers receive a notification when their research-backed outline is ready.
