How It Works

1. Research Phase

Perplexity AI scans current industry trends, academic papers, and news sources to gather relevant data points based on your specified topics. It identifies key statistics, emerging patterns, and credible sources to support your content.

2. Content Generation

OpenAI processes the research findings into structured LinkedIn post drafts. The AI suggests multiple angles, formats (listicles, how-tos, stories), and includes proper citations. It maintains professional tone while adapting to your preferred writing style.

3. Human Review & Enhancement

The system presents draft options to a human reviewer who selects the strongest direction, adds personal anecdotes, adjusts tone, and ensures factual accuracy. This step transforms generic AI output into content that reflects your unique voice and expertise.

Pro tip: Schedule content in batches during your review sessions. This workflow's efficiency lets you create a month's worth of posts in just a few focused hours.
