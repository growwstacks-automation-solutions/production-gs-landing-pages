What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of scaling high-quality, SEO-optimized blog content production. It automates the entire process from initial keyword research to final WordPress publication, leveraging Google Gemini and DeepSeek AI to generate drafts that follow SEO best practices while maintaining readability.

The system handles technical SEO elements automatically—including meta tags, header structure, and internal linking—while allowing for human review before publication. This creates an efficient hybrid workflow where AI handles the heavy lifting of initial content creation, and your team focuses on strategic oversight and final polish.

How It Works

1. Keyword and Topic Generation

The workflow begins by analyzing your target keywords or automatically generating relevant topics based on your niche. It evaluates search volume, competition, and relevance to your audience.

2. AI Content Creation

Google Gemini and DeepSeek collaborate to create comprehensive drafts. Gemini focuses on factual accuracy and structure, while DeepSeek enhances the content with semantic richness and natural language flow.

3. SEO Optimization

The system automatically applies SEO best practices—optimizing headings, adding semantic keywords, creating meta descriptions, and ensuring proper content length for your target keywords.

4. WordPress Integration

Final drafts are formatted for WordPress, with categories, tags, and featured images assigned automatically. The workflow can publish immediately or schedule posts based on your content calendar.
