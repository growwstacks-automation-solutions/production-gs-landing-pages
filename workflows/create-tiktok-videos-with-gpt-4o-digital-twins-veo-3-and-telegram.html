What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating TikTok content that genuinely resonates with your audience. Traditional content creation often relies on guesswork or generic trends, resulting in videos that fail to engage. Our workflow combines multiple AI technologies to produce data-driven TikTok videos backed by real audience insights.

How It Works

1. Trend Research Phase

GPT-4o analyzes current TikTok trends in your niche, identifying high-performing topics and formats. It cross-references this with your brand messaging to create relevant content angles.

2. Audience Feedback Collection

Digital Twins engage with your target audience through Telegram, gathering authentic reactions to potential video concepts. This feedback is analyzed to identify what resonates most before content production begins.

3. Script Generation

The workflow combines trend data with audience feedback to generate optimized video scripts. These include hooks, key messages, and calls-to-action proven to engage your specific audience.

4. Video Production

Veo 3 transforms scripts into polished TikTok videos complete with visuals, captions, and effects. The AI selects the most engaging formats based on performance data from similar content.

5. Publishing & Optimization

Completed videos are automatically posted to TikTok at optimal times. Performance data feeds back into the system to continuously improve future content.
