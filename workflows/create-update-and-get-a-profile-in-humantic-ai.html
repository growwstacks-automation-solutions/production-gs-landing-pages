What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire Humantic AI profile lifecycle for recruitment and sales teams. It solves the manual bottleneck of creating, updating, and retrieving personality profiles by connecting Humantic AI's API with your existing candidate or prospect data sources.

The template handles three key functions: automatically creating new profiles when candidate data enters your system, updating existing profiles with fresh information, and retrieving complete personality insights for your team to use in assessments or sales outreach. This eliminates hours of manual work while ensuring your team always has the latest personality data.

How It Works

1. Trigger profile creation

The workflow starts when new candidate or prospect data enters your system through webhooks, scheduled imports, or manual triggers. This could be from your ATS, CRM, or other sources.

2. Submit data to Humantic AI

The workflow automatically formats the data (like LinkedIn URLs or email addresses) and submits it to Humantic AI's API to generate a personality profile. It handles all authentication and API requirements.

Pro tip: Set up webhook triggers from your ATS to automatically process every new applicant, ensuring no candidates slip through without personality analysis.

3. Retrieve and store insights

Once Humantic AI completes the analysis (typically within minutes), the workflow retrieves the full personality profile and stores it in your preferred destination - whether that's your ATS, CRM, or internal database.

4. Schedule profile updates

The workflow includes logic to periodically check for and update existing profiles with new information, ensuring your personality insights stay current as candidates or clients evolve.
