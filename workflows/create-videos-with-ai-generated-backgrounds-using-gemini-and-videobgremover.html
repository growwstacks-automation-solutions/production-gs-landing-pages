What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating professional-looking videos with custom backgrounds without expensive editing software or stock image subscriptions. It combines AI image generation with automated video processing to create studio-quality results in minutes.

The workflow generates unique backgrounds from text descriptions using Google's Gemini AI, removes existing video backgrounds using VideoBGRemover, and composites the final video with your new AI-generated scene. This eliminates hours of manual editing while giving you unlimited creative possibilities.

Example output showing a professional talking head video with AI-generated office background

How It Works

1. Background Generation

The workflow sends your text prompt to Gemini's image generation API, which creates a custom background image in about 10-20 seconds. For example: "Modern office with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking a city skyline at golden hour."

2. Background Removal

Your original video is sent to VideoBGRemover's API, which automatically isolates the foreground subject and removes the existing background with high precision.

3. Video Composition

The workflow composites the cleaned video foreground onto the AI-generated background, centering the subject and adjusting lighting for a natural look.

4. Delivery

The final video is saved to Google Drive with a shareable link, ready for use in presentations, social media, or marketing materials.

Pro tip: Use specific, descriptive prompts for best results. Instead of "office," try "modern minimalist office with wooden desks, green plants, and large windows showing cityscape."
