How It Works

1. AI Ad Concept Generation

The workflow starts by analyzing current viral trends using NanoBanana's AI. It generates multiple ad concepts based on your target audience's engagement patterns and past high-performing content.

2. Creative Asset Production

Seedance AI transforms the winning concepts into complete ad creatives - generating images, videos, and captions optimized for each social platform's specifications and algorithm preferences.

3. Automated Quality Review

Before publishing, the system checks each creative against platform guidelines and your brand standards. It can automatically flag potential issues or route exceptional creatives for human review.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

The upload-post integration handles all platform-specific formatting and publishes the ads at optimal times based on your audience's activity patterns and past engagement data.

Pro tip: For best results, let the AI generate 3-5 variations of each winning concept. The system can automatically A/B test these and double down on the top performers.
