What This Workflow Does

Every content creator and marketer knows the struggle: brilliant ideas strike at random moments, but transforming them into polished, viral-ready Instagram Reels takes hours of brainstorming, scripting, and planning. This friction kills consistency and slows down your content engine.

This automation solves that by creating an instant content production assistant. You send a raw idea—via text message or voice note in Telegram—and within seconds receive a complete Reel scenario: three attention-grabbing hook variants, a structured 30-60 second script, an engaging caption, and specific visual filming concepts. It’s like having a professional scriptwriter and creative director on call 24/7, powered by GPT-4o’s understanding of viral psychology and content structure.

The system goes beyond simple text generation. It includes a memory context, so it learns from your conversation to make better suggestions over time. Optional Google Sheets integration logs every idea into a searchable content library, turning spontaneous inspiration into a strategic asset.

How It Works

Step 1: Idea Capture via Telegram

You interact with a dedicated Telegram bot. Send a text message with your Reel concept (“How to automate blog publishing”) or record a quick voice note when inspiration strikes during your commute. The bot accepts both input types seamlessly.

Step 2: Intelligent Processing & Transcription

Voice notes are automatically transcribed using AI. The core idea is extracted and enriched with context from previous conversations (thanks to a memory node). This ensures suggestions are personalized and aligned with your ongoing content themes.

Step 3: AI-Powered Script Generation

GPT-4o analyzes the idea against proven viral content frameworks. It generates three distinct hooks designed to stop the scroll within the first three seconds. Then it builds a complete script with Hook, Subtitle, Body, and a clear Call-to-Action.

Step 4: Comprehensive Output Delivery

The bot returns the full package: hooks, script, a complementary caption (not just repeating the video), and actionable visual concepts (e.g., “open with a time-lapse of sticky notes being organized”). All results are delivered back to your Telegram chat and optionally saved to a Google Sheet.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Create Your Telegram Bot: Message @BotFather on Telegram, follow the prompts to create a new bot, and save the provided token.
  2. Get Your OpenAI API Key: Sign into the OpenAI platform, navigate to API keys, and create a new key with GPT-4o access.
  3. Import the Template: Download the JSON file from this page and import it into your n8n workspace.
  4. Configure Credentials: Add your Telegram Bot Token to the Telegram nodes and your OpenAI API key to the GPT-4o nodes. Test each connection.
  5. Activate & Test: Turn on the workflow, message your bot with a test idea, and verify you receive a complete Reel scenario in response.
