What This Workflow Does

Creating engaging social media videos is time-consuming and expensive. Between scriptwriting, visual creation, editing, and publishing, a single 60-second video can take hours of work. This automation solves that problem by turning a simple concept into a complete, polished video ready for publication.

The workflow acts as your AI video production team. It generates viral-worthy ideas, writes cinematic scripts, creates photorealistic visuals using FalAI Flux, animates them with Kling, adds professional voiceovers, stitches everything together, and delivers the final video to your Google Drive. What used to require a team of specialists now happens automatically.

How It Works

1. Idea Generation & Script Writing

GPT-4 analyzes your chosen theme (like "space exploration" or "business productivity") and generates a compelling video concept complete with title, hashtags, and description. It then expands this into a detailed 12-scene script with specific visual directions for each segment.

2. Visual Creation Pipeline

For each scene, FalAI Flux generates high-quality still images based on the script's visual descriptions. The OpenAI Vision node then analyzes these images and creates specialized animation prompts, which FalAI Kling uses to turn static images into 5-second cinematic clips.

3. Assembly & Delivery

All 12 clips are automatically stitched together into a seamless 60-second video. A synchronized AI voiceover is added based on the script text. The completed video is converted to a file format and uploaded directly to your specified Google Drive folder for immediate access and publishing.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Import the JSON template into your n8n instance. 2. Add your OpenAI credentials to all GPT nodes. 3. Replace the placeholder FalAI API key in each HTTP Request node's Authorization header. 4. Connect your Google account for Sheets and Drive integration. 5. Specify your target Google Drive folder in the upload node. 6. Test with a simple theme to verify everything works.

The workflow is designed to run on a schedule (daily, weekly) or be triggered manually. Once configured, it will autonomously produce videos based on your chosen themes without further intervention.
