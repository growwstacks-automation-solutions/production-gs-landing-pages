Automate product creation through conversational AI - no coding required

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how your team creates WooCommerce products by combining the convenience of Telegram messaging with the power of Google Gemini AI. Instead of manually entering product details in WooCommerce's backend, your team can simply chat with a Telegram bot to create complete, SEO-optimized product listings.

The workflow solves three major eCommerce challenges: reducing data entry time, maintaining consistent product information quality, and enabling mobile-friendly product creation. By automating the translation of chat messages into structured product data, businesses can cut product setup time by 75% while improving listing quality through AI-generated content.

How It Works

1. Telegram Bot Interaction

The workflow begins when a user starts a conversation with your Telegram bot. The bot guides them through a structured questionnaire to collect all necessary product information including title, description, price, categories, and attributes.

2. AI Content Generation

Google Gemini AI processes the collected information to generate professional product descriptions, SEO metadata, and optimized titles. The AI ensures consistency across your catalog while adapting to your brand voice and product specifics.

3. Data Validation & Approval

The system validates the product data against your WooCommerce settings and can route it for manual approval if needed. Administrators receive Telegram notifications to review and approve products before they go live.

4. WooCommerce Product Creation

Once approved, the workflow automatically creates the product in WooCommerce with all details, categories, tags, and inventory settings. The system can handle simple products, variable products, and digital downloads.

What You'll Need

  1. A WooCommerce store with API access enabled
  2. A Telegram bot token (create via BotFather)
  3. Google Gemini API access
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  5. Basic understanding of WooCommerce product structure

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure the Telegram bot node with your bot token
  3. Set up Google Gemini API credentials in the AI node
  4. Connect the WooCommerce node to your store
  5. Test the flow by messaging your Telegram bot
  6. Adjust approval settings based on your team structure

Pro tip: Train the AI with examples of your existing product descriptions to ensure consistent brand voice across all auto-generated content.
