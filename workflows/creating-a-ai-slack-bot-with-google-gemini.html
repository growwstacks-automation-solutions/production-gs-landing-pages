What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow creates a fully functional AI-powered Slack bot that connects your team with Google Gemini's advanced AI capabilities directly within your Slack workspace. Instead of switching between applications or manually querying AI tools, team members can simply type a question or command in Slack and receive intelligent, context-aware responses in seconds.

The automation bridges Slack's communication platform with Google Gemini's powerful language model, enabling natural language processing, content generation, data analysis, and task automation without leaving your team's primary collaboration tool. It handles the complete flow from receiving Slack messages to processing them through AI and returning thoughtful responses.

Beyond simple Q&A, this bot can be extended to automate routine tasks like drafting emails, summarizing meeting notes, generating reports, answering policy questions, or even analyzing data from connected systems—all triggered through simple Slack commands or mentions.

How It Works

The workflow follows a streamlined process that makes AI assistance accessible to every team member through familiar Slack interactions.

1. Slack Trigger Setup

The workflow begins with a Slack trigger node configured to listen for specific slash commands (like /askai) or app mentions. When a team member uses the command or mentions the bot, Slack sends the message content to your n8n webhook endpoint. This establishes the real-time connection between Slack conversations and your automation.

2. Message Processing & Context Building

Once triggered, the workflow extracts the user's query, identifies the sender, and can optionally retrieve conversation history or relevant context. This step ensures the AI has necessary background information to provide accurate, personalized responses rather than generic answers.

3. Google Gemini AI Processing

The core intelligence comes from the Google Gemini node, where the user's query is sent for processing. Gemini analyzes the request, understands intent, and generates a thoughtful response. You can configure the AI's behavior—tone, response length, creativity level—to match your team's needs.

4. Response Formatting & Delivery

The AI's response is then formatted for optimal Slack presentation, potentially adding formatting, threading, or interactive elements. Finally, the workflow posts the response back to the original Slack channel or as a direct message, completing the conversation loop within seconds of the initial query.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement your AI Slack bot in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Create a Slack App in your workspace's Slack API portal and note the Bot User OAuth Token
  3. Enable Google Gemini API in your Google Cloud Console and generate an API key
  4. Configure the webhook trigger in n8n to receive Slack events (use ngrok for local testing if needed)
  5. Set up a Slack Slash Command pointing to your n8n webhook URL
  6. Enter your API credentials in the respective n8n nodes and activate the workflow
  7. Test with simple queries in Slack and refine the AI instructions as needed
