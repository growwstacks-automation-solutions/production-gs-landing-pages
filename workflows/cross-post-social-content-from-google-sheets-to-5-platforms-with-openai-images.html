What This Workflow Does

This automation eliminates the manual grind of social media management by turning your Google Sheets content calendar into scheduled posts across five major platforms. Instead of copying, pasting, designing, and posting individually, you manage everything from a single spreadsheet while AI generates custom visuals for each piece of content.

The workflow reads content marked "Ready To Post" in Google Sheets, distributes text to X and Threads, generates AI images based on your content, then posts those images with captions to LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram. It updates your spreadsheet to prevent duplicates and provides error handling for failed posts.

How It Works

1. Content Detection & Scheduling

A scheduled trigger checks your Google Sheet at regular intervals (daily, hourly, or custom). It looks for rows where the Status column equals "Ready To Post" and hasn't been processed before.

2. Multi-Platform Text Distribution

The workflow extracts your post content and immediately sends it to text-focused platforms (X/Twitter and Threads) using their respective APIs. This happens simultaneously to ensure consistent messaging timing.

3. AI Image Generation & Optimization

An AI agent analyzes your post content and creates a detailed image prompt. This prompt is sent to OpenAI's image generation API, which creates a unique visual that matches your message's tone and intent.

4. Image Hosting & URL Generation

The generated image is automatically uploaded to ImgBB (or your preferred image hosting service) to create a publicly accessible URL. This step ensures images load quickly on all social platforms.

5. Visual Platform Posting

The image URL and your caption are posted to LinkedIn, Facebook Pages, and Instagram via their respective APIs. Each platform receives properly formatted content with optimal image dimensions.

6. Status Updates & Error Handling

Your Google Sheet is updated to mark the content as "Posted" with timestamps. If any platform fails, the workflow logs the error and can retry or notify you while continuing with other platforms.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Duplicate the provided Google Sheet template and structure your content with columns for: Content, Status, Platforms, Image Prompt (optional)
  3. Configure credentials for all platform APIs in n8n's credentials management
  4. Test with one post marked "Ready To Post" to verify all connections work
  5. Adjust scheduling to match your posting frequency (daily, multiple times daily, etc.)
  6. Set up error notifications via email or Slack to monitor the automation
  7. Go live and let the system handle your social media posting automatically

Pro tip: Create a "sandbox" social media account for testing. Use this to verify image quality, formatting, and timing before connecting your main business accounts.
