What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming problem of manually reposting TikTok videos across multiple social media platforms. Content creators often struggle to maintain consistent posting schedules across different networks while keeping their branding unified.

The workflow automatically detects when you post a new TikTok video, downloads it without the watermark, stores it securely in Google Drive, and then reposts it to your connected social media accounts. This ensures your content reaches all your audiences without manual uploading to each platform.

How It Works

1. TikTok Post Detection

The workflow monitors your TikTok account via RSS feed, instantly detecting when you publish new content. This trigger eliminates the need for manual notifications.

2. Video Processing

Once a new video is detected, the automation downloads the high-quality version without TikTok's watermark, preserving your professional branding across all platforms.

3. Cloud Storage

The video is automatically uploaded to your designated Google Drive folder, creating a centralized archive of all your TikTok content for future repurposing.

4. Multi-Platform Distribution

Using Blotato's powerful automation capabilities, the workflow then posts your video to all connected social media accounts simultaneously with your preferred captions and hashtags.

Pro tip: Configure different caption templates for each platform to optimize engagement based on each network's best practices.
