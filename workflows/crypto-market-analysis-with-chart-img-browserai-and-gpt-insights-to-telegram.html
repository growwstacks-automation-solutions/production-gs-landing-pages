What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of staying updated with crypto market movements without spending hours manually tracking prices and news. It combines visual chart analysis with AI-powered market insights delivered directly to your Telegram.

The workflow automatically gathers crypto price charts for BTC, ETH, SOL and XRP using Chart-img, scrapes relevant market analysis with BrowserAI, synthesizes the information into concise reports, and delivers them on a schedule you control.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs twice daily (adjustable) to provide morning and evening market snapshots.

2. Chart Generation

Chart-img API creates 24-hour price charts converted to base64 for easy integration.

3. Market Data Collection

BrowserAI scrapes and summarizes the latest crypto news and analysis from trusted sources.

4. AI Synthesis

Key insights are extracted and formatted into concise, actionable reports under 60 words.

5. Delivery

Final reports with charts and analysis are sent to Telegram (easily adaptable to other channels).

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance

  4. Adjust the schedule to your preferred timing
  5. Test with a small set of coins before expanding
