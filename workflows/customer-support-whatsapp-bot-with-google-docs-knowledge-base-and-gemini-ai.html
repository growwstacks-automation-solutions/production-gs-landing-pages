What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of overwhelmed customer support teams and delayed responses on WhatsApp. When customers message your business WhatsApp number with questions about policies, hours, services, or FAQs, they often wait hours or days for a reply—if they get one at all.

The workflow creates an intelligent AI assistant that instantly answers these questions by reading your company's internal knowledge base stored in Google Docs. It combines WhatsApp Business Cloud API, Google Docs, and Gemini AI (or ChatGPT) to provide accurate, natural-language responses 24/7. Every conversation is automatically logged to Google Sheets for review and reporting.

Unlike basic chatbots, this solution understands context and dates. If your Google Doc says "Closed May 25 for 30 days," the AI will calculate and respond: "We're currently closed until June 24." This makes it perfect for gyms, restaurants, agencies, clinics, or any business with recurring customer inquiries.

How It Works

The automation follows a smart sequence that mimics how a human support agent would work, but instantly and at scale.

Step 1: WhatsApp Message Trigger

When a customer sends a message to your WhatsApp Business number, the WhatsApp Cloud API triggers the workflow. The system captures the customer's number, message content, and timestamp.

Step 2: Knowledge Base Retrieval

The workflow reads your designated Google Doc containing company policies, FAQs, service details, or schedules. This document serves as the single source of truth that the AI will reference.

Step 3: AI Context Preparation

The system combines today's date, the customer's question, and the relevant document content into a structured prompt. This context helps the AI understand what information is relevant and current.

Step 4: Intelligent Response Generation

Gemini AI (or your chosen model) analyzes the prompt and generates a helpful, natural response in your brand's tone. The AI references specific details from your document to ensure accuracy.

Step 5: Automated Reply & Logging

The AI's response is sent back to the customer via WhatsApp. Simultaneously, the entire conversation—question, answer, timestamp, and customer number—is saved to a Google Sheet for quality assurance and analytics.

Pro tip: Use a dedicated Google Doc for your AI knowledge base. Structure it with clear headings, bullet points, and date-specific information. The AI performs best with well-organized content.

What You'll Need

  1. WhatsApp Business Account: Access to WhatsApp Business Cloud API (Meta Developer account)
  2. Google Account: With access to Google Docs and Google Sheets for the knowledge base and logging
  3. AI Model Access: Gemini API key (Google AI Studio) or OpenAI API key for ChatGPT
  4. n8n Instance: Self-hosted n8n or n8n.cloud account to run the workflow
  5. Knowledge Document: A Google Doc containing your company's policies, FAQs, or service information

Quick Setup Guide

You can have this AI assistant running in under 30 minutes by following these steps:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your WhatsApp Cloud API credentials in the WhatsApp trigger node (requires Meta Business verification).
  3. Add your Google account to the Google Docs and Google Sheets nodes, granting access to your knowledge document.
  4. Paste your document ID into the Google Docs node (found in the document's URL).
  5. Configure your AI model by adding either Gemini or OpenAI API credentials to the AI node.
  6. Test the workflow by sending a WhatsApp message to your business number—you should receive an instant AI response.
  7. Monitor conversations in the connected Google Sheet and refine your knowledge document based on common questions.

Pro tip: Start with a limited knowledge base covering your top 10 most frequent questions. As the AI handles these successfully, gradually expand the document with more detailed information and edge cases.
