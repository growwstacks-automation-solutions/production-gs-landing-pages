Daily AI News Digest to LinkedIn Posts

Automatically curate AI industry news, summarize with GPT, and generate ready-to-post LinkedIn content daily.

What This Workflow Does

Staying current with AI developments is crucial for professionals, but manually researching and creating content consumes valuable hours. This automation solves that by building a complete AI news curation system that works while you sleep.

Every morning, it scans trusted AI news sources via RSS feeds, filters for the most relevant articles, uses OpenAI's GPT to generate insightful summaries, and crafts LinkedIn-ready posts in your preferred tone. The final content is emailed to you for quick review and posting, establishing you as a consistent thought leader with minimal daily effort.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger & RSS Collection

A schedule trigger runs daily at your preferred time (typically morning). It connects to multiple RSS feeds from AI research publications, industry blogs, and news sites, fetching the latest articles published in the last 24 hours.

2. Content Filtering & Processing

The workflow filters articles based on relevance keywords, removes duplicates, and extracts key information: titles, summaries, publication dates, and source links. This ensures only high-quality, relevant AI news moves forward.

3. AI Summarization with OpenAI GPT

Each selected article is sent to OpenAI's GPT model with custom instructions to create concise, engaging summaries. The AI adds context, explains why the development matters, and highlights potential implications for professionals.

4. LinkedIn Post Generation

Using your brand voice guidelines, the system transforms AI summaries into polished LinkedIn posts. It adds appropriate hashtags, formats for readability, and includes calls-to-action to encourage engagement and discussion.

5. Email Delivery for Review

The generated posts are sent to your email with clear subject lines and formatting. You can quickly review, make any tweaks, and copy-paste to LinkedIn—or connect directly via LinkedIn API for fully automated posting.

Pro tip: Customize the OpenAI prompts to match your specific expertise area (like "AI for marketing" or "machine learning engineering") to create more targeted, valuable content for your niche audience.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import function.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up your OpenAI API key and email credentials in n8n's credential management system.
  3. Customize RSS Feeds: Replace the default RSS URLs with your preferred AI news sources in the RSS Read nodes.
  4. Set Your Brand Voice: Modify the OpenAI prompt nodes to reflect your writing style, expertise level, and target audience.
  5. Test & Schedule: Run a test execution to verify everything works, then set your preferred daily schedule in the Cron node.
  6. Review & Post: Check your email each morning, review the generated posts, and share them on LinkedIn.

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 high-quality RSS feeds rather than many sources. This ensures focused, relevant content and helps you refine the AI summarization before scaling up.
