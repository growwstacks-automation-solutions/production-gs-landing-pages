What This Workflow Does

This intelligent news monitoring system automatically aggregates the latest AI developments from Google News and Hacker News, filters them for relevance, uses GPT-4 to generate concise summaries, and delivers them to your Telegram channel. It solves the problem of information overload in the fast-moving AI field by providing curated, digestible updates.

The workflow saves professionals hours each week by eliminating manual news gathering. It ensures you never miss important breakthroughs while avoiding the fatigue of scanning dozens of articles. The AI-powered summarization maintains context and technical accuracy better than basic extraction algorithms.

How It Works

1. News Aggregation

The workflow pulls RSS feeds from Google News (set to AI topics) and Hacker News simultaneously. It checks for new articles every 24 hours by default, though this frequency can be adjusted.

2. Content Filtering

Using keyword matching and basic NLP, the system filters out irrelevant stories and removes duplicates that appear across both sources. Only articles meeting your configured relevance thresholds proceed.

3. GPT-4 Summarization

Each qualifying article is sent to GPT-4 with instructions to generate a 3-5 sentence summary that preserves key technical details. The AI identifies main points, innovations, and implications while removing fluff.

4. Telegram Delivery

Formatted summaries are compiled into a digest and sent to your specified Telegram channel. The message includes original links for those wanting full articles. Urgent breakthroughs can be configured for immediate alerts.

Pro tip: Add custom keywords for your specific AI interests (like "LLM" or "computer vision") to further refine content selection.
