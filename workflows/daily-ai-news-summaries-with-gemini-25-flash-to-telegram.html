What This Workflow Does

Staying current with artificial intelligence developments is crucial for tech leaders, investors, and innovators—but manually scanning dozens of articles daily consumes valuable time and leads to information overload. This automation transforms you from a news consumer into an intelligence director.

The workflow acts as your personal AI analyst: it monitors leading AI publications via RSS, extracts full article content using Jina AI's reading capability, processes the information through Google's cutting-edge Gemini 2.5 Flash model to generate structured briefings, and delivers polished intelligence reports directly to your Telegram channel. Instead of spending 5-10 hours weekly reading, you receive 5-minute summaries that highlight market shifts, technical breakthroughs, and competitive movements.

This isn't just content aggregation—it's strategic intelligence automation. The Gemini model is prompted to identify implications, connections between stories, and actionable insights tailored for business decision-makers. You maintain awareness without the cognitive burden of processing raw information.

How It Works

Step 1: RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow begins by monitoring specified AI news RSS feeds. When a new article is published, it automatically triggers the entire process. This ensures you're always working with the latest information without manual checking.

Step 2: Article Content Extraction

Using Jina AI's advanced reading capabilities, the system fetches the complete article text—bypassing paywalls, ads, and formatting issues to obtain clean, structured content for analysis. This solves the problem of partial information from simple scrapers.

Step 3: AI-Powered Analysis & Summarization

Google Gemini 2.5 Flash processes the article with a specialized prompt designed for business intelligence. It extracts key developments, identifies market implications, highlights technical innovations, and formats everything into executive-ready briefings with clear sections and takeaways.

Step 4: Telegram Delivery

The finalized intelligence report is sent as a formatted message to your private Telegram channel or group. The markdown formatting ensures readability on mobile devices, making the briefing accessible wherever decisions happen.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run at 8 AM daily—your team starts each day with current AI intelligence before their first meeting.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation
  2. Jina AI API credentials for article content extraction
  3. Google Gemini API key from Google AI Studio (free tier available)
  4. Telegram Bot Token & Chat ID for message delivery
  5. RSS feed URLs for your preferred AI/news publications

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n canvas using the download button above.
  2. Configure credentials for Jina AI, Google Gemini, and Telegram in their respective nodes.
  3. Set your target Telegram Chat ID in the Send Message node (can be a private channel, group, or direct message).
  4. Test with a single article by manually triggering the workflow to verify formatting and delivery.
  5. Activate the workflow and set your desired schedule (daily, hourly, or real-time).

Pro tip: Start with one RSS feed, then duplicate the trigger node to add additional sources once the core workflow is running smoothly.
