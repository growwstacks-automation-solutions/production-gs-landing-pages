What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of inbox overload by automatically archiving older Gmail messages based on customizable rules. Instead of manually sorting through hundreds of emails, the workflow systematically moves messages that meet your criteria (like age, sender, or labels) to the archive while keeping them searchable.

Professionals waste an average of 3.1 hours weekly managing email clutter. This template eliminates that time sink while maintaining access to historical messages. It's particularly valuable for solopreneurs, consultants, and busy professionals who need to focus on important communications without deleting potentially useful messages.

How It Works

Step 1: Filter incoming messages

The workflow first identifies messages meeting your archiving criteria (default: older than 30 days). You can customize this to filter by sender domain, labels, or keywords.

Step 2: Apply archiving rules

Messages that match your criteria are automatically archived using Gmail's API. The template includes safeguards to prevent archiving starred messages or those with specific labels you want to keep visible.

Step 3: Generate activity reports

Optional reporting steps can send you weekly summaries of archived messages, giving you visibility into what's been moved without cluttering your inbox.

Pro tip: Combine this with Gmail filters to create exception rules. For example, keep all emails from clients in your inbox regardless of age, while archiving newsletters after 7 days.
