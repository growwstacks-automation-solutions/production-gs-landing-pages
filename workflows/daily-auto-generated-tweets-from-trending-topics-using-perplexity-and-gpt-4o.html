What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of maintaining an active, relevant Twitter presence without daily manual effort. It automatically researches trending topics using Perplexity's AI, generates engaging tweet drafts with GPT-4o, and posts them to your Twitter account on a daily schedule.

The system eliminates hours of manual content research and creation while ensuring your tweets participate in current conversations. It's particularly valuable for brands, marketers, and thought leaders who need consistent social visibility but lack time for daily posting.

How It Works

1. Trend Analysis with Perplexity

The workflow starts by querying Perplexity's API for trending topics in your industry or specified categories. Perplexity scans thousands of sources to identify emerging conversations with high engagement potential.

2. Content Generation with GPT-4o

Selected topics are sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o model, which crafts tweet drafts matching your brand voice. The AI considers your historical content style, preferred terminology, and audience preferences to generate authentic-sounding posts.

3. Automated Posting to Twitter

The finalized tweets are automatically posted to your Twitter account via n8n's Twitter integration. The workflow includes error handling and retry logic to ensure reliable delivery even if API limits are temporarily reached.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run during your audience's peak activity times for maximum engagement. Most brands see best results between 9-11 AM and 1-3 PM local time.
