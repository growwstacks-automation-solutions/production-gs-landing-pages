What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of calendar overload by transforming your raw Google Calendar events into an intelligently formatted daily digest. Instead of scanning through multiple calendar entries each morning, you receive a single, well-structured message in Telegram that summarizes your entire day's schedule.

The workflow leverages Claude 3.5 Sonnet's natural language processing to analyze your calendar events and present them in a more readable, prioritized format. It can highlight important meetings, group similar events together, and even add contextual notes based on event descriptions.

How It Works

1. Daily calendar event retrieval

Every morning at 6:00 AM, the workflow connects to your Google Calendar account and retrieves all events scheduled for the current day. It extracts key details including event titles, times, descriptions, and participants.

2. AI-powered formatting

The raw calendar data is sent to Claude 3.5 Sonnet, which analyzes the events and generates a human-readable summary. The AI can prioritize events, detect conflicts, and even suggest optimal preparation times based on meeting types.

3. Telegram delivery

The formatted digest is then sent to your specified Telegram chat or channel. The message includes all your daily events in a clean, mobile-friendly format with appropriate emojis and visual hierarchy.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompt to format the digest exactly how you prefer - some users add weather forecasts, commute times, or motivational quotes to their daily summary.
