What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of monitoring your competitors' Twitter (X) activity and delivering actionable insights straight to your inbox. Instead of manually checking multiple competitor accounts daily, this system collects, analyzes, and summarizes their tweets using AI-powered GPT-5-Nano technology.

The workflow helps you stay informed about competitor marketing strategies, product announcements, customer engagement tactics, and industry trends without spending hours scrolling through social media. It transforms raw tweets into concise, business-relevant summaries with key takeaways highlighted.

How It Works

1. X API Data Collection

The workflow connects to X's API to fetch the latest tweets from up to 10 competitor accounts you specify. It retrieves all tweets from the past 24 hours, including engagement metrics like likes, retweets, and replies.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

GPT-5-Nano processes the collected tweets, identifying key themes, sentiment, and strategic insights. The AI categorizes content types (promotions, announcements, customer service, etc.) and evaluates engagement patterns.

3. Executive Summary Generation

The system generates a structured report with: competitor activity overview, top performing tweets analysis, emerging trends detection, and recommended actions for your business based on competitor behavior.

4. Email Delivery

The final summary gets formatted into a clean, readable email and delivered to your specified Gmail address at your preferred time each morning, giving you competitive intelligence before your workday begins.
