What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming task of manually tracking cryptocurrency market movements. Instead of checking multiple exchanges and compiling data yourself, this workflow automatically generates a comprehensive daily report showing the top gainers and losers among the top 100 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization.

The system pulls reliable data from CoinGecko's API, processes it to identify significant movements, then formats the information into clear reports delivered via both WhatsApp and email. This ensures your team or clients receive timely market insights without any manual effort.

The automated workflow pulling data from CoinGecko and formatting reports

How It Works

1. Data Collection from CoinGecko

The workflow starts by querying CoinGecko's API to fetch the latest market data for the top 100 cryptocurrencies. It retrieves key metrics including current price, 24-hour price change percentage, and trading volume.

2. Identifying Top Movers

The system analyzes the price change data to identify the top 5 gainers and top 5 losers. It applies filters to ensure only significant movements are included, ignoring minor fluctuations.

3. Report Generation

The workflow compiles the filtered data into a structured report format. It includes cryptocurrency names, symbols, price changes, and relevant links for further research.

4. Multi-Channel Distribution

Finally, the completed report is sent simultaneously via WhatsApp for instant mobile notifications and via email for archival purposes and richer formatting options.

Pro tip: Customize the threshold for what constitutes a "significant" price movement based on your audience's needs. More active traders might want alerts for smaller percentage changes than long-term investors.
