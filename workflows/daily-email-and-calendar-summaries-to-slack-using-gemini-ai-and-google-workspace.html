What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of email and calendar overload by delivering concise, AI-powered summaries directly to your Slack workspace. Instead of constantly switching between apps and digging through threads, you get all the essential information in one organized notification.

The workflow connects Google Workspace with Slack through Gemini AI, which analyzes your emails and calendar events to extract key information. It identifies action items, highlights important details, and filters out noise - saving you hours each week while ensuring you never miss critical updates.

How It Works

1. Email Collection

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages matching your criteria (specific senders, labels, or keywords). It processes both new emails and entire threads to maintain context.

2. Calendar Event Processing

Simultaneously, it checks your Google Calendar for upcoming events, extracting details like attendees, agenda items, and attached documents to include in the summary.

3. AI Analysis

Gemini AI processes the collected information, identifying key points, action items, and decisions while filtering out redundant or irrelevant content.

4. Summary Generation

The system compiles the analyzed data into a structured Slack message with clear sections for emails and calendar events, prioritized by importance.

5. Scheduled Delivery

Summaries are delivered to your specified Slack channel at your preferred times (typically morning and afternoon), with options for urgent notifications.

Pro tip: Set up separate workflows for different teams or projects to keep summaries focused and relevant to each group.
