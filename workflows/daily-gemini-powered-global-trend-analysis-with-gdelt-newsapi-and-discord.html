What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms raw global news data into actionable intelligence using AI analysis. It solves the challenge of information overload by distilling thousands of daily news events into concise trend reports delivered directly to your Discord team channels.

The workflow combines GDELT's comprehensive news database with NewsAPI's real-time updates, processes them through Gemini AI for contextual understanding, and delivers prioritized insights to your team where they collaborate. It replaces manual news monitoring with automated, AI-curated intelligence.

How It Works

1. Data Collection Phase

The workflow first pulls structured news data from GDELT's global event database, supplemented by real-time updates from NewsAPI. It filters sources by credibility score and geographic relevance to ensure quality inputs.

2. AI Analysis Process

Gemini AI processes the aggregated news, identifying emerging patterns, sentiment shifts, and geopolitical correlations. The AI scores each trend's potential impact based on historical patterns and cross-referenced sources.

3. Report Generation

The system compiles findings into structured reports highlighting key trends, supporting evidence, and predicted trajectories. Reports include confidence scores and recommended monitoring priorities.

4. Discord Delivery

Final reports are formatted for Discord with interactive elements like threaded discussions and reaction-based prioritization. Critical alerts trigger @mentions for immediate attention.
