What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the modern problem of information overload by delivering curated, summarized news directly to your Telegram. Instead of wasting time scrolling through multiple news sites and YouTube channels, you receive one concise digest each morning containing the most important updates from your selected sources.

The workflow combines AI-powered summarization with multimedia content aggregation. It pulls articles from RSS feeds and videos from YouTube channels, processes them through natural language algorithms to extract key points, and formats everything into an easy-to-consume Telegram message with proper attribution and links to original sources.

How It Works

1. Source Aggregation

The workflow first checks all configured RSS feeds and YouTube channels for new content. It filters out any items you've already seen and applies your content preferences (like excluding certain topics).

2. AI Processing

Each news item gets analyzed by AI to extract the essential information. The system identifies key entities (people, organizations, locations), summarizes main points, and determines sentiment and importance scores.

3. Content Assembly

The processed items get organized into categories based on your preferences. The workflow creates a structured message with headlines, summaries, and direct links while maintaining a consistent format.

4. Telegram Delivery

Finally, the assembled digest gets sent to your Telegram account at your preferred time. The message includes proper formatting, emoji indicators for importance, and options to explore specific topics further.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits busy professionals, researchers, and anyone who needs to stay informed but lacks time for manual news consumption. Executives use it for morning briefings, marketers track industry trends, and analysts monitor multiple sectors efficiently.

Teams can adapt it for internal knowledge sharing - delivering relevant updates to different departments. Content creators repurpose the summaries for their own materials, while educators use it to stay current in their fields.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. A Telegram account and bot token
  3. AI service API key (like OpenAI or similar)
  4. Your preferred RSS feed URLs
  5. YouTube channel IDs you want to monitor

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your RSS feed sources in the "RSS Read" node
  3. Add your YouTube channel IDs to the "YouTube Trigger" node
  4. Connect your AI service API in the "AI Summarization" node
  5. Set up your Telegram bot credentials in the final delivery node
  6. Test with a small set of sources before full deployment
