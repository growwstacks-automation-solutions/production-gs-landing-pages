What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of information overload and negative news bias by delivering a daily digest of uplifting news stories directly to your inbox each morning. Instead of scrolling through depressing headlines, you'll start your day with curated positive content selected and summarized by AI.

The workflow leverages OpenAI's natural language processing to analyze news sources, identify positive stories, and generate concise summaries. This saves you to stay informed while maintaining a positive mindset—a proven productivity booster for professionals and entrepreneurs.

How It Works

1. News Source Aggregation

The workflow first gathers news articles from pre-selected positive news sources or mainstream outlets with specific keyword filters to identify uplifting content.

2. AI Content Analysis

OpenAI analyzes the collected articles, evaluating sentiment and relevance to identify the most positive and meaningful stories that match your preferences.

3. Summary Generation

The AI then generates concise, well-written summaries of the selected stories, extracting key points while maintaining an uplifting tone.

4. Email Delivery

Finally, the system compiles these summaries into a beautifully formatted email and sends it to your Gmail inbox at your preferred time each morning.

Pro tip: Customize the news sources and keywords to match your specific interests—technology breakthroughs, humanitarian wins, or scientific discoveries.
