How It Works

1. News Aggregation

The workflow pulls from 15+ verified Solana news sources including The Block, CoinDesk, and Solana Foundation announcements. It filters for Solana-specific content while ignoring general crypto market noise.

2. AI Analysis

GPT-4.1-mini evaluates each article's relevance, extracting key technical details, partnership announcements, and security alerts. The model is fine-tuned to understand Solana's unique architecture and ecosystem terminology.

3. Automated Categorization

Updates are classified by type (protocol change, dApp launch, security alert etc.) and assigned urgency ratings. The system learns from your manual adjustments to improve future categorization.

4. Weekly Summaries

Every Friday, the AI compiles a executive summary highlighting ecosystem trends, notable developments, and potential impacts. These are formatted for easy sharing with teams or investment committees.
