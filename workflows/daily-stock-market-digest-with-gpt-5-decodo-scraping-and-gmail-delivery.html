What This Workflow Does

Manually tracking stock markets, reading financial news, and synthesizing information consumes valuable hours every day. This automation solves that by creating your personal AI research assistant that works while you sleep.

The workflow automatically gathers real-time financial headlines from trusted sources like Bloomberg, CNBC, Reuters, and Yahoo Finance, processes them through GPT-5 for intelligent summarization, and delivers a polished daily digest directly to your email. It covers major indices, global markets, sector trends, earnings reports, and regulatory news—all formatted for quick reading.

Instead of spending 1-2 hours each morning scanning multiple websites and newsletters, you receive one comprehensive, AI-curated report that highlights only what matters most to your investment strategy. This isn't just automation—it's intelligent information filtering that gives you back your most valuable resource: time.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence that mimics how a human analyst would research, but at machine speed and scale.

1. Scheduled Trigger

The automation starts on a schedule you set (typically 8:30 AM weekdays). This ensures you receive your digest before the trading day begins, prepared with overnight and pre-market developments.

2. Data Collection & Scraping

The workflow uses the Decodo tool node to extract current financial headlines and data from configured sources. This replaces manual browsing across multiple websites with automated, structured data collection.

3. AI Analysis & Summarization

Raw data flows into the OpenAI Chat Model (GPT-5) node, where a custom system prompt instructs the AI to act as a financial research assistant. It analyzes trends, identifies key developments, and synthesizes information into coherent insights.

4. Memory & Context Management

A simple memory node maintains conversation context between workflow runs, allowing the AI to reference previous analyses and track ongoing market narratives for more coherent reporting over time.

5. Email Composition & Delivery

The final summarized report is formatted into clean HTML and sent via Gmail to your specified address with a consistent subject line like "Daily AI Market Digest." The email includes categorized sections, bullet points, and actionable insights.

Quick Setup Guide

Importing and configuring this workflow takes about 15 minutes if you have your API keys ready.

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials by clicking each credential node (OpenAI, Decodo, Gmail) and adding your API keys
  3. Set your email address in the Gmail node's "Send To" field where you want to receive the digest
  4. Adjust the schedule trigger to your preferred time (default is daily at 9 AM)
  5. Test the workflow by clicking "Execute Workflow" once to receive your first AI-generated market digest
  6. Activate the workflow to run automatically on your set schedule
