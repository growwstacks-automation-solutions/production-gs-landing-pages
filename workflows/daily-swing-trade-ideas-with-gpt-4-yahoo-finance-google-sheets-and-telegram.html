What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually screening stocks for swing trading opportunities. Traditional methods require traders to analyze charts, news, and technical indicators daily - a process that can take hours. The workflow automates this research by combining Yahoo Finance data with GPT-4 analysis to generate qualified trade ideas.

Each trading day after market close, the system pulls relevant stock data, applies your predefined screening criteria, and uses AI to score potential trades based on technical patterns and fundamental factors. The highest-probability ideas get logged to Google Sheets for record-keeping and pushed to your Telegram channel for immediate review.

How It Works

1. Data Collection from Yahoo Finance

The workflow starts by pulling end-of-day market data from Yahoo Finance's API. This includes price action, volume trends, technical indicators, and relevant news for your watchlist stocks or predefined sectors.

2. AI Scoring with GPT-4

The raw market data gets formatted into a prompt for GPT-4, which evaluates each potential trade based on your specified criteria. The AI considers technical patterns, sentiment analysis, and fundamental factors to assign a confidence score.

3. Google Sheets Logging

All analyzed trades - both high-probability ideas and borderline cases - get recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps. This creates an auditable history for performance tracking and strategy refinement.

4. Telegram Notifications

The system pushes only the highest-scoring trade ideas to your Telegram channel with formatted details. Each alert includes the ticker, entry/exit levels, position size suggestion, and the AI's rationale.
