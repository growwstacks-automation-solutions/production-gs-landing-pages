What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking and summarizing daily tech news. It aggregates articles from multiple premium tech publications via RSS feeds, processes them through AI-powered summarization, and delivers a polished daily digest to subscribers' inboxes.

For content marketers, tech companies, and knowledge workers, this eliminates hours of manual research while ensuring your audience receives the most relevant AI and technology updates in a concise, easily digestible format.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Aggregation

The workflow pulls in the latest articles from configured tech news RSS feeds at scheduled intervals. You can customize which publications to include based on your audience's interests.

2. AI-Powered Summarization

Using GPT-4o-Mini, the system analyzes each article, extracts key insights, and generates concise summaries that capture the essence while maintaining accuracy and context.

3. Content Formatting

The summarized content is organized into a professional email template with proper categorization, formatting, and branding elements to create a polished newsletter experience.

4. Automated Delivery

The completed digest is sent via Gmail to your subscriber list at your preferred time, ensuring consistent delivery without manual intervention.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n account (self-hosted or cloud)
  2. GPT-4o-Mini API access
  3. Gmail SMTP credentials
  4. RSS feeds from your preferred tech publications
  5. Subscriber list (can start with just your team)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your RSS feed sources in the HTTP Request nodes
  3. Add your GPT-4o-Mini API key in the AI node settings
  4. Set up your Gmail SMTP credentials in the email node
  5. Test with a small subscriber list before going live
  6. Schedule the workflow to run daily at your preferred time
