What This Workflow Does

If you're like most professionals, your Gmail inbox has become a digital graveyard of read emails that no longer need your attention but still create visual clutter and mental overhead. Manually sorting through hundreds of emails to decide what to archive is time-consuming and often gets deprioritized.

This intelligent automation solves that problem by combining Gmail with GPT-4's understanding capabilities. It automatically identifies emails that are both old AND low-priority, then moves them to your archive folder—keeping your primary inbox focused only on what matters while preserving all emails for future search.

The system doesn't just use simple age-based rules. It employs AI to understand the content and context of each email, making intelligent decisions about what truly deserves to stay in your main inbox versus what can be safely archived.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a scheduled basis (daily or weekly) to systematically clean your inbox without any manual intervention.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger & Email Retrieval

A schedule trigger initiates the workflow at your preferred frequency. The system then connects to your Gmail account and retrieves all read emails older than 45 days using a targeted search query.

Step 2: AI-Powered Classification

Each retrieved email is sent to GPT-4 with a carefully crafted prompt that asks the AI to classify the email as either "Actionable" (needs attention or follow-up) or "Archive" (can be safely moved to archives). The AI considers the email's content, sender, subject, and context to make this determination.

Step 3: Intelligent Archiving Decision

The workflow analyzes the AI's classification. If GPT-4 recommends "Archive" AND the email meets the age criteria, the system proceeds to archive the email. If the AI flags it as "Actionable," the email is left untouched in your inbox for your review.

Step 4: Safe Archiving Process

The workflow moves approved emails to your Gmail archive folder—not to trash. This means all emails remain fully searchable and accessible, just removed from your primary inbox view to reduce clutter.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above to get the JSON file
  2. Import into n8n by going to your n8n dashboard, clicking "Workflows" → "Import from file"
  3. Configure credentials by setting up Gmail (OAuth2) and OpenAI API connections in the credential management section
  4. Adjust parameters in the "List Old Emails" node if you want to change the age threshold or add additional filters
  5. Test the workflow by executing it once manually to ensure connections work and review sample classifications
  6. Enable scheduling by activating the Schedule Trigger node with your preferred frequency (daily/weekly)
  7. Monitor initially by checking the archived emails for the first few runs to ensure the AI is making appropriate decisions
