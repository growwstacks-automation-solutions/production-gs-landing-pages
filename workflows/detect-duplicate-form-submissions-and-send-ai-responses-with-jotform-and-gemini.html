What This Workflow Does

Duplicate form submissions create messy databases, waste time on manual cleanup, and frustrate both your team and your customers. This automation solves that problem completely by automatically detecting when the same person submits a form twice, removing the duplicate entry, and sending intelligent, personalized email responses.

The workflow connects Jotform with Google Gemini AI and Gmail to create a seamless system. When a new submission arrives, it checks all previous submissions by email address. If it's a duplicate, the system deletes it and sends a friendly "we received your duplicate" notification. If it's new, it sends a professional welcome confirmation. The entire process happens in seconds with zero manual intervention.

Beyond just duplicate prevention, this automation improves customer experience by providing immediate, relevant communication. The AI-generated emails adapt to your specific form type and industry, making responses feel personalized rather than generic. This is particularly valuable for event registrations, job applications, support tickets, and any high-volume form where data quality matters.

How It Works

Step 1: Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Jotform. It captures all form data including email address, name, and any custom fields you've configured. This trigger is the starting point that initiates the entire duplicate checking process.

Step 2: Historical Data Retrieval

The system fetches all previous submissions from your Jotform account to compare against the new entry. It filters these submissions to find active entries matching the new submitter's email address, creating a comprehensive view of that person's previous interactions with your form.

Step 3: Duplicate Detection Logic

A counting mechanism determines if matching submissions exist. If the count is zero, it's a new submission. If the count is one or more, it's a duplicate. This logic is precise and configurable—you can adjust what constitutes a duplicate based on specific fields.

Step 4: Intelligent Response Generation

For duplicates: The workflow deletes the redundant submission and triggers Google Gemini to generate a polite, context-aware email explaining the duplicate was detected and removed. For new submissions: Gemini creates a professional welcome email confirming receipt and outlining next steps.

Step 5: Automated Email Delivery

The AI-generated email is sent via Gmail with proper HTML formatting, making it mobile-responsive and professionally branded. The system logs all actions for transparency and includes fallback mechanisms in case any step encounters issues.
