What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching competitors' e-commerce technology stacks. The workflow automatically analyzes websites to detect their underlying platforms (Shopify, BigCommerce, etc.), payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), marketing tools, and technical frameworks.

By connecting website analysis with Google Sheets, you create a living database of competitor technology intelligence that updates automatically. This eliminates the need for manual detective work while providing more accurate and comprehensive data than human researchers can typically gather.

How It Works

Step 1: Input website URLs

The workflow accepts a list of website URLs from your Google Sheet or other data source. These can be competitors, potential partners, or market research targets.

Step 2: Analyze website technology

Using advanced detection methods, the workflow scans each website's code, APIs, and DNS records to identify telltale signs of specific e-commerce platforms and technologies.

Step 3: Classify findings

Detected technologies are categorized into logical groups: e-commerce platforms, payment processors, analytics tools, marketing systems, and technical frameworks.

Step 4: Update Google Sheets

The workflow writes all findings back to your Google Sheet with clear labels and categorization, creating an automatically updating competitive intelligence database.
