What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates procurement fraud detection and supplier compliance monitoring for organizations managing complex purchasing operations. By combining GPT-4o's advanced AI analysis with real-time Slack alerts, it helps finance and procurement teams identify suspicious patterns, policy violations, and compliance risks before they escalate into major issues.

The system continuously monitors procurement data streams, applies AI-powered anomaly detection, and delivers actionable insights directly to stakeholders' Slack channels. This replaces manual spreadsheet reviews and periodic audits with continuous, automated oversight that scales with your purchasing volume.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls procurement data from your ERP, accounting software, or procurement platforms. This includes purchase orders, invoices, vendor details, and approval records.

2. AI Analysis

GPT-4o processes both structured transaction data and unstructured documents (contracts, emails) to identify potential fraud indicators like unusual purchase patterns, split orders, or vendor conflicts of interest.

3. Compliance Checks

The system cross-references supplier activities against compliance requirements, SLAs, and regulatory standards, flagging deviations for review.

4. Alert Generation

When the AI detects high-risk patterns, it generates detailed Slack notifications with severity ratings, contextual analysis, and recommended actions.

5. Reporting

The workflow maintains audit trails of all findings and can generate periodic compliance reports for management review.
