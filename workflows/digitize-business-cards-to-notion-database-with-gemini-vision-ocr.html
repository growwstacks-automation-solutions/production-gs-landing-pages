Digitize Business Cards to Notion with AI OCR

Automatically extract contact details from business card images using Google Gemini Vision AI and save them to a Notion database. Eliminate manual data entry.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Every networking event, conference, or meeting leaves you with a stack of business cards. Manually typing each contact into your CRM or database is tedious, error-prone, and wastes valuable time. This automation solves that.

This n8n workflow uses Google Gemini's advanced Vision AI to read business card images, extract structured contact information (name, position, phone, email, company, address, website), and automatically save it into a Notion database. It turns a physical card into a digital record in seconds, with no manual typing.

Beyond simple OCR, the AI understands context—it distinguishes between a phone number and a fax line, separates personal and company emails, and correctly parses multi-line addresses. This creates clean, usable data ready for sales follow-ups, marketing segmentation, or partner management.

How It Works

1. Upload & Trigger

A user uploads a business card image (JPG, PNG) through an n8n webhook or form trigger. They can optionally tag the contact with a category like "Client," "Vendor," or "Partner." The workflow starts when the image file is received.

2. AI Vision Processing

The image is sent to Google Gemini Vision (PaLM). The AI model analyzes the photo, performs OCR (Optical Character Recognition), and intelligently identifies entities: it finds the person's name, job title, contact numbers, email addresses, company name, physical address, and website URL.

3. Data Parsing & Cleaning

Gemini returns raw text. A Code node processes this output, converting it into a clean JSON object. It removes extra spaces, formats phone numbers consistently, validates email patterns, and structures each field for database insertion.

4. Notion Database Entry

The parsed JSON is mapped to properties in your pre-configured Notion database (e.g., "Customer Business Cards"). The workflow creates a new page with all extracted fields filled. The contact is now digitally stored, searchable, and ready for use.

Who This Is For

This template is ideal for sales teams, marketing professionals, event managers, recruiters, and business owners who collect many contacts. If you attend trade shows, host meetings, or partner with other companies, this automation eliminates the post-event data entry grind.

It's also perfect for solopreneurs and freelancers who want a simple, centralized contact system without expensive CRM software. Notion acts as your free, flexible database, and this workflow feeds it automatically.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Gemini API key (accessible via Google Cloud).
  2. A Notion account with a database ready to receive contacts.
  3. A Notion integration token with write permissions to that database.
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.
  5. Business card images in JPG, PNG, or JPEG format.

Pro tip: Create a simple mobile form for team members to snap a photo of a card and upload it directly—no need to collect physical cards.

Quick Setup Guide

Import this template into your n8n workspace and follow these steps:

  1. Download the JSON file using the button above and import it into n8n.
  2. Configure Google Gemini by adding your API credentials to the "Google Gemini" node.
  3. Connect your Notion database in the "Notion" node—set the database ID and ensure your integration token has write access.
  4. Test with a sample image by uploading a business card photo via the Form Trigger node or webhook.
  5. Activate the workflow and start digitizing cards automatically.
