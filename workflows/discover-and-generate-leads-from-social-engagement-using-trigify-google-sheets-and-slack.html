What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation system solves the challenge of manually tracking potential leads who engage with industry content across social media platforms. Traditional methods require constant monitoring and manual data entry, which is time-consuming and often misses valuable prospects.

The workflow automatically identifies high-value prospects by monitoring social engagement through Trigify, organizes their contact information in Google Sheets, and alerts your sales team via Slack whenever a qualified lead is detected. This creates a seamless pipeline from social interaction to sales opportunity.

How It Works

1. Social Listening with Trigify

Trigify monitors specified social media accounts, hashtags, and keywords to detect engagement from potential leads. It tracks likes, comments, shares, and mentions that indicate genuine interest in your industry or offerings.

2. Lead Qualification

The workflow analyzes engagement patterns and user profiles to determine lead quality. It considers factors like job title, company size, engagement frequency, and content relevance to score each potential lead.

3. Data Organization in Google Sheets

Qualified leads are automatically added to a structured Google Sheet with their contact information, engagement history, and lead score. This creates a centralized database for your sales team to reference.

4. Slack Notifications

When a high-value lead is identified, the system sends an immediate notification to your sales Slack channel with the lead's details and a link to their profile. This enables quick follow-up while the engagement is still fresh.
