What This Workflow Does

Manual email hunting is a time-consuming bottleneck for sales and recruitment teams. This automation solves that by instantly discovering professional email addresses when you have just a person's name and company domain. It uses uProc's intelligent email discovery engine to find the most likely email format, verifies it, and then sends the result directly to your team's Telegram channel for immediate action.

The workflow transforms hours of tedious research into a fully automated process that delivers verified contact information in seconds. This means your team can focus on engaging leads rather than finding them, dramatically increasing the volume and speed of your outreach efforts while maintaining high data quality.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Lead Information

The workflow begins with a simple data input—first name, last name, and company domain. This can come from a manual entry, a connected spreadsheet, your CRM, or any other data source. The system is flexible enough to integrate with your existing lead capture processes.

Step 2: uProc Email Discovery

uProc's "Get Email by Domain, Firstname and Lastname" tool analyzes the input against known email patterns, corporate formats, and real-time mail server verification. It returns not just the email address but also a confidence score and verification status, giving you clear insight into data reliability.

Step 3: Telegram Notification Delivery

Once discovered, the email and its metadata are formatted into a clear, actionable message and sent to your designated Telegram group or channel. The message includes the email as a clickable link, allowing team members to immediately initiate contact or save the information to your CRM.

Pro tip: Connect this workflow to a Google Sheet of target accounts. It can process hundreds of contacts overnight, delivering a batch of verified emails to your team each morning, ready for that day's outreach.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Set up your uProc credentials by adding your email and API key from the uProc integration settings page.

3. Configure your Telegram bot by creating a new bot via @BotFather, getting the token, and adding it to the Telegram node credentials.

4. Update the "Set" node with test data (first name, last name, domain) or connect it to your preferred data source.

5. Execute the workflow once manually to test, then schedule it or trigger it from your lead capture system.

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of 5-10 contacts to verify everything works correctly before scaling up. Check the confidence scores in the initial results to understand uProc's accuracy with your specific target industries.
