What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow creates a Telegram bot that automatically downloads videos from Instagram and Facebook when you send it a link. Instead of using unreliable online downloaders or screen recording, you can now save any Reel or video directly to your device with a simple message.

The automation solves the common frustration of wanting to save social media videos for reference, content curation, or offline viewing. Content creators, marketers, and researchers often need to archive videos but face limitations with native platform features. This workflow provides a private, on-demand solution without requiring third-party apps or browser extensions.

The workflow processes links sent to your Telegram bot and returns downloadable video files

How It Works

1. Send a link to your Telegram bot

When you share an Instagram Reel or Facebook video URL with your bot, n8n immediately receives the message through Telegram's webhook integration.

2. URL validation and processing

The workflow checks if the link is a valid Instagram or Facebook video URL. It extracts the essential video identifier while filtering out invalid or unsupported links.

3. Video downloading

Using n8n's HTTP Request node, the workflow fetches the video file from the social media platform. It handles authentication and bypasses platform restrictions to access the media file.

4. File delivery

The downloaded video is sent back through Telegram as a downloadable file attachment. You receive it directly in your chat with the bot, ready to save to your device.

Pro tip: You can enhance this workflow by adding automatic cloud storage backup to Google Drive or Dropbox for all downloaded videos.
