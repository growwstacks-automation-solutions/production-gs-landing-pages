What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of saving Instagram Reels and videos for offline viewing or content repurposing. Instead of using third-party websites or apps that may compromise security or require manual steps, this workflow lets you download content directly through Telegram.

How It Works

1. User sends Instagram link to Telegram bot

When you come across an Instagram Reel or video you want to save, simply copy the link and send it to your configured Telegram bot. The bot recognizes Instagram URLs automatically.

2. System fetches video content

The workflow uses n8n to extract the video content from Instagram's servers. It handles authentication and bypasses Instagram's download restrictions through API calls.

3. Video is processed and sent back

Once downloaded, the video file is optimized (without quality loss) and sent back to you through Telegram as a downloadable file. The entire process typically takes less than 10 seconds.
