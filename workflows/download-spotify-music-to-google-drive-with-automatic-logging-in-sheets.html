What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of systematically archiving music from Spotify while maintaining detailed records of your downloads. Many music professionals, podcast creators, and audio enthusiasts need to maintain organized libraries of tracks they use across projects, but manual downloading and logging is time-consuming and error-prone.

The workflow automatically transfers specified Spotify tracks to your Google Drive storage while creating comprehensive logs in Google Sheets. Each entry includes metadata like artist, album, duration, and download timestamp, creating a searchable database of your audio assets. This eliminates manual file management while ensuring you always know what you've downloaded and where to find it.

How It Works

1. Spotify track selection

The workflow begins by identifying which Spotify tracks to download, either through manual selection or automated triggers like playlist additions. You can configure it to monitor specific playlists or receive track lists from other applications.

2. Secure download process

Using authorized APIs and services, the workflow securely retrieves the audio files while maintaining proper metadata. This ensures your downloads contain all the original track information for proper organization and copyright documentation.

3. Google Drive organization

Files are automatically saved to designated folders in your Google Drive, with consistent naming conventions based on artist, album, and track information. The workflow can create nested folder structures based on your preferences.

4. Automated Google Sheets logging

Each download triggers a new row in your designated Google Sheet, recording all relevant metadata along with the Google Drive file path. This creates a complete audit trail of your music library that's searchable and sortable.
