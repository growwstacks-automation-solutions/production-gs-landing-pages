What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time drain of repetitive email composition by intelligently drafting responses using AI. It monitors your Gmail inbox, identifies messages needing replies (while filtering out newsletters and notifications), and generates professional, context-aware drafts ready for your review.

The system preserves your unique communication style while handling the mechanical aspects of email composition. For businesses receiving dozens or support teams managing high volumes, this can recover 5-15 hours per week otherwise spent typing similar responses.

How It Works

1. Inbox monitoring and filtering

The workflow checks your Gmail every 15 minutes for new messages, applying filters to exclude automated emails (receipts, newsletters, notifications) that don't require personal replies.

2. Context analysis

For qualifying messages, the AI extracts key details - sender identity, request type, urgency indicators. This context informs the reply structure and content.

3. AI drafting

Using OpenAI's API (via OpenRouter for cost efficiency), the system generates a complete email draft matching the message context. The prompt includes your preferred tone, common response templates, and any available contact history.

4. Human review and sending

Drafts return to your inbox as unread messages with "[AI DRAFT]" in the subject. Review, make any necessary edits, and send as normal - all while the system learns from your adjustments.

Pro tip: Create different AI profiles for various email types - brief/technical for internal team, warm/professional for clients.
