What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves the critical challenge of getting sales leads to the right rep at the right time. Traditional lead routing often relies on simple rules or round-robin distribution, which can result in mismatches, delayed responses, and lost opportunities.

The workflow combines HubSpot's lead capture with GPT-4's analytical power and Airtable's flexible team management to create a dynamic routing system. It evaluates each lead's characteristics, compares them against your sales team's profiles and availability in Airtable, and makes intelligent assignment decisions that maximize conversion potential.

AI analysis of lead data against sales team profiles in Airtable

How It Works

1. New lead capture from HubSpot

The workflow triggers whenever a new lead is created or updated in HubSpot. It pulls all available lead data including form submissions, website activity, and CRM fields.

2. GPT-4 lead analysis

The lead information is sent to GPT-4 for intelligent analysis. The AI evaluates lead quality, intent signals, and ideal rep matching criteria based on your configured parameters.

Pro tip: Train GPT-4 with your ideal customer profiles and past conversion data to improve its matching accuracy over time.

3. Airtable team lookup

The system queries your Airtable base containing sales team data - including rep specialties, territories, current workload, and performance metrics.

4. Optimal assignment calculation

Using a weighted algorithm, the workflow calculates the best rep match considering factors like: skill alignment, geographic proximity, capacity limits, and historical performance with similar leads.

5. HubSpot update and notifications

The chosen rep is assigned in HubSpot, and notifications are sent via your preferred channel (email, Slack, etc.) with the lead details and AI's matching rationale.
