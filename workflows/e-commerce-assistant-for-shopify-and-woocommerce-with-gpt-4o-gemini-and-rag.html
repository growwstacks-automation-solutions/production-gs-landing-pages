What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow creates a powerful AI assistant specifically designed for e-commerce stores on Shopify and WooCommerce. It combines multiple AI models (GPT-4o and Gemini) with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology to provide intelligent, context-aware responses to customer inquiries.

The assistant can handle product recommendations based on customer preferences, answer frequently asked questions by referencing your knowledge base, provide order status updates, and even assist with basic customer support issues. The RAG component ensures the AI has access to your specific product catalog and policies, preventing hallucinations and improving accuracy.

How It Works

1. Customer Inquiry Processing

The workflow begins when a customer submits a question through your website chat interface. The system analyzes the query to determine intent and context before routing it to the appropriate AI model.

2. Dynamic Model Selection

Based on the query type, the workflow selects between GPT-4o (for complex reasoning) or Gemini (for product-specific recommendations). For knowledge base queries, it first retrieves relevant documents using RAG before generating a response.

3. Response Generation

The selected AI model generates a response tailored to your store's products, policies, and brand voice. The system can pull real-time product data from your Shopify/WooCommerce store to ensure accuracy.

4. Response Delivery

The final response is formatted appropriately and delivered back to the customer through your preferred chat interface (website widget, Facebook Messenger, etc.).
