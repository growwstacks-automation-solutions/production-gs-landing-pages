What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your weekly e-commerce bestsellers into engaging product videos without manual effort. It identifies top-performing products from your Algolia search index, generates professional 6-second videos using Google's VEO 3 AI, and updates your product listings with the new media assets.

The system eliminates the need for expensive video production teams by automatically creating cinematic product showcases that highlight your items with professional lighting, smooth transitions, and subtle motion effects - all tailored to your actual customer favorites.

How It Works

1. Bestseller Identification

The workflow queries your Algolia index to find the current top-selling product based on your custom ranking criteria (sales, views, inventory status).

2. Media Validation

It checks that the product has high-quality images available and skips any items that don't meet your visual standards.

3. AI Video Generation

Using Google VEO 3, the system creates a cinematic product video with professional lighting and smooth camera movements that showcase the item from multiple angles.

4. Storage & Distribution

The finished video gets stored in your media library and automatically attached to the product listing, ready for use across your website, social media, and ads.

What You'll Need

  1. Algolia account with product index
  2. Google Cloud account with VEO 3 API access
  3. Cloud storage bucket (Google or Supabase)
  4. Products with high-quality images
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Algolia credentials (Search API key + Application ID)
  3. Configure your Google Cloud credentials for VEO 3 access
  4. Set up your storage destination (Supabase recommended)
  5. Test with a single product before enabling the weekly schedule
