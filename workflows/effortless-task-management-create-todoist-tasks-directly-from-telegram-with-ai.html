Convert Telegram messages into organized Todoist tasks automatically with AI-powered parsing

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the common productivity challenge of task fragmentation across communication and task management tools. Many professionals capture tasks in Telegram chats (both personal and work-related) but then need to manually transfer them to Todoist, creating friction and potential missed tasks.

The automation bridges this gap by automatically converting Telegram messages into properly structured Todoist tasks. The AI-powered parsing understands natural language, extracting due dates, priorities, and project assignments from your messages without requiring rigid formatting.

How It Works

1. Telegram message trigger

The workflow monitors specified Telegram chats for new messages. You can configure it to watch personal chats, group conversations, or specific channels where task-related messages typically appear.

2. AI content analysis

An AI parsing step analyzes the message content to identify task components. It detects due dates (like "tomorrow" or "Friday"), priority indicators ("urgent", "important"), and project references ("for client project").

3. Task creation in Todoist

The parsed data automatically creates a structured task in your Todoist account with all extracted details. The workflow handles API authentication and proper formatting behind the scenes.
