What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered email assistant solves the daily burden of manual email triage and response drafting. For businesses drowning in customer inquiries, sales requests, and support tickets, it provides an intelligent automation layer that works 24/7. When a new email arrives in your Gmail, the system instantly analyzes its content, determines whether it's a quote request, order inquiry, or general question, applies the appropriate label for organization, and drafts a personalized, context-aware response using your company's knowledge base.

You receive a Telegram notification with a direct link to review and send the draft with one click. This transforms email management from a reactive, time-consuming task into a streamlined process where you only need to approve pre-written, accurate responses. The workflow maintains your brand voice while ensuring no email goes unanswered for hours.

How It Works

Step 1: Email Trigger & Capture

The Gmail Trigger node monitors your inbox every minute for new messages. It captures the full email content including sender, subject, and body, ensuring the AI receives complete context for accurate processing.

Step 2: AI Analysis & Categorization

The OpenAI Chat Model node analyzes the email content against your predefined categories (like "existing_order" or "quote_request"). It cross-references the inquiry with your Data Table knowledge base containing product details, pricing, policies, and FAQs.

Step 3: Response Drafting & Labeling

Based on the category and relevant knowledge base information, the AI drafts a professional, personalized response. Simultaneously, the Gmail node applies the appropriate label to the original email, keeping your inbox organized automatically.

Step 4: Notification & Human Review

The Telegram node sends you an instant notification with the drafted response and a direct link to the email. You can review, edit if needed, and send with one click, maintaining quality control while saving 90% of the drafting time.

What You'll Need

  1. A Gmail account with OAuth2 access enabled
  2. An OpenAI API account (GPT-3.5 or GPT-4)
  3. A Telegram bot token and chat ID for notifications
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  5. Your company information, pricing, and policies for the knowledge base

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect accounts: Set up OAuth2 connections for both Gmail Trigger and Gmail nodes.
  3. Configure AI: Add your OpenAI API credentials to the Chat Model node.
  4. Set up notifications: Create a Telegram bot via BotFather and update the token and chat ID in the Telegram node.
  5. Populate knowledge base: Create a Data Table called "Customer Support Knowledge Base" with your company information, response guidelines, product details, and FAQs.
  6. Test and activate: Send a test email to your connected Gmail and verify the categorization, labeling, and notification flow works correctly.

Pro tip: Start with a small set of email categories (3-5) and expand as you refine the AI's accuracy. Use the "Simplify" option turned OFF in the Gmail Trigger to ensure the AI receives full email text for better context understanding.
