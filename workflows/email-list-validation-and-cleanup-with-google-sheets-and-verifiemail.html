What This Workflow Does

Maintaining a clean email list is one of the most overlooked yet critical aspects of successful digital marketing. Dirty lists lead to high bounce rates, poor deliverability, damaged sender reputation, and wasted marketing spend. This automated workflow solves that problem systematically.

The template connects Google Sheets (where most businesses store their subscriber data) with VerifiEmail's professional validation service. It automatically fetches your subscriber list, validates each email in real-time, removes invalid entries, and creates a clean, organized spreadsheet of verified contacts—all without manual CSV exports or imports.

Beyond simple cleaning, it classifies emails into actionable categories: valid contacts to keep, invalid addresses to remove, and special cases that might need attention. This intelligence helps you understand your list quality and make better marketing decisions.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical, step-by-step process that ensures thorough validation while maintaining data integrity.

1. Trigger & Data Collection

The workflow can be triggered manually, scheduled weekly, or activated via webhook. It connects to your Google Sheets document and reads all subscriber data, including email addresses, names, subscription dates, and any custom fields you track.

2. Email Normalization & Preparation

Before validation, the system normalizes email formats—converting to lowercase, trimming whitespace, and standardizing domain formats. This preprocessing step catches simple errors and ensures consistent validation.

3. Real-Time Validation with VerifiEmail

Each email undergoes comprehensive verification: syntax checking, domain existence (MX record verification), mailbox availability, disposable email detection, and role-based address identification. The service returns detailed validation scores and reasons.

4. Intelligent Classification

Based on validation results, emails are categorized as "keep" (valid and safe), "remove" (invalid/disposable/role-based), or "tag" (requires review). Custom rules can be configured to match your specific business requirements.

5. Automated Cleanup & Organization

Invalid emails are automatically removed from the source sheet. Valid contacts are written to a new "CleanSubscribers" sheet with all their original data plus validation metadata. A detailed summary report is generated for review.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run automatically every Friday night. You'll start each week with a freshly cleaned list, ensuring optimal deliverability for your Monday campaigns.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting started takes just a few minutes. Follow these steps to implement email list cleaning automation today.

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect Google Sheets by creating credentials in n8n and selecting your subscriber spreadsheet
  3. Add VerifiEmail API key from your account dashboard (sign up at verifiemail.com if needed)
  4. Update sheet names in the workflow nodes to match your actual spreadsheet tab names
  5. Test with a small batch of 5-10 emails to verify everything works correctly
  6. Schedule automation to run weekly or trigger manually before major campaigns

Pro tip: Create a separate "Archive_Invalid" sheet in your Google Sheets document before first run. The workflow can move removed emails there instead of deleting them permanently, giving you an audit trail.
