What This Workflow Does

School emergencies require immediate, reliable communication with parents and staff. Traditional methods like phone trees or mass emails often delay critical information when seconds matter most. This automation solution ensures instant multi-channel notifications the moment an emergency is declared.

The workflow integrates with your existing systems to process emergency alerts, validate recipient lists, and deliver synchronized alerts via SMS, email, and push notifications. It handles contact list management, opt-out preferences, and delivery confirmation - all within a single automated process.

How It Works

1. Emergency Trigger

The system activates when receiving a webhook call or manual trigger from authorized personnel. Different emergency types (lockdown, weather closure, etc.) can be specified to tailor response protocols.

2. Contact Validation

Active directories are checked to filter only current students, parents, and staff. The system checks for opt-out preferences and updates bounced contacts automatically.

3. Notification Distribution

Pre-approved message templates are populated with emergency details and sent simultaneously across all configured channels. The workflow tracks delivery status and retries failed deliveries.

4. Confirmation & Logging

Delivery confirmations are aggregated in real-time. Administrators receive summary reports while the system logs all actions for compliance and audit purposes.
