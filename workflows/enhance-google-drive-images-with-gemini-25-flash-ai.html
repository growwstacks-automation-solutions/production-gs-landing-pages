What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for e-commerce businesses, marketing teams, and creative professionals: manually editing large batches of product images is time-consuming, inconsistent, and delays catalog launches. The workflow connects Google Drive—where teams typically store raw images—with Google Gemini's advanced AI capabilities to automatically enhance photos based on customizable instructions.

When new images are added to a designated source folder, the system detects them, converts them to the appropriate format, sends them to Gemini AI with your specific enhancement prompts (like "clean white background" or "improve lighting for product shots"), and saves the professionally enhanced versions to a destination folder. This creates a seamless pipeline from raw upload to publication-ready imagery.

How It Works

1. Image Detection & Collection

The workflow monitors a specific Google Drive folder for new image uploads. It filters files by type (JPG, PNG, etc.) and prepares them for processing. This trigger can be scheduled to run hourly, daily, or in real-time depending on your volume needs.

2. AI Processing Configuration

Each image is sent to Google Gemini AI with customizable instructions. You can set different prompts for various product categories—for example, "remove background and add subtle shadow for electronics" versus "enhance natural colors and texture for clothing fabrics."

3. Automated Enhancement & Quality Control

Gemini processes each image according to your specifications, performing tasks like background cleaning, lighting adjustment, color correction, and detail enhancement. The AI returns the improved image while maintaining the original's essential characteristics.

4. Organized Storage & Delivery

Enhanced images are automatically saved to a designated output folder in Google Drive, with consistent naming conventions and metadata. The workflow can also notify team members via email or Slack when batches are complete.

Pro tip: Create separate source folders for different product categories (electronics, apparel, home goods) and use different AI prompts for each to get category-optimized results automatically.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Drive account with images stored in organized folders
  2. Google Gemini API access (available through Google AI Studio)
  3. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  4. Clear enhancement requirements for your images (background style, lighting preferences, etc.)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n workspace.
  2. Connect your Google Drive credentials for both source and destination folders.
  3. Configure your Gemini API key in the AI processing node.
  4. Customize the enhancement prompts to match your specific image requirements.
  5. Test with a small batch of images to verify results match your expectations.
  6. Schedule the workflow to run automatically based on your image upload frequency.
