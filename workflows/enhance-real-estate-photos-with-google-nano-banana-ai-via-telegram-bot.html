Enhance real-estate photos with Google Nano Banana AI via Telegram bot

Automatically enhance property photos using AI through a simple Telegram interface

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common challenge for real estate professionals: quickly enhancing property photos to make listings more attractive. Instead of manually editing each image or hiring expensive photo editors, agents can simply send photos to a Telegram bot which automatically processes them through Google's Nano Banana AI.

The workflow dramatically reduces the time and cost associated with professional photo editing while maintaining high-quality results. It's particularly valuable for agents photographing multiple properties weekly, allowing them to focus on clients rather than image processing.

Example of AI-enhanced real estate photo showing improved lighting and clarity

How It Works

1. Photo Submission via Telegram

Real estate agents send property photos directly to your Telegram bot. The bot accepts single images or batches, making it easy to process entire property sets at once.

2. Automatic AI Enhancement

The workflow routes each image to Google's Nano Banana AI service which analyzes and enhances the photo. The AI adjusts lighting, corrects colors, sharpens details, and removes imperfections.

Telegram bot interface demonstrating the photo enhancement process

3. Enhanced Photo Delivery

The improved images are automatically sent back to the agent through Telegram, ready for immediate use in listings. The entire process typically completes in under 2 minutes per image.

Pro tip: Create separate Telegram bots for different agent teams or property types to maintain organization and tracking.
