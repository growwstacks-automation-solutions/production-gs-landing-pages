What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching companies for sales, recruiting, or market analysis. The workflow automatically searches the web for information about companies in your Airtable database, uses AI to extract and summarize key details, and enriches your records with structured insights.

Instead of spending hours per company on Google searches and manual data entry, this system can process hundreds of companies overnight. The AI identifies key information like funding rounds, executive changes, product updates, and competitive positioning - then presents it in an easily digestible format within your existing Airtable workflow.

AI-enriched company profiles showing key details extracted from web research

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on new or updated records

The workflow monitors your Airtable base for new companies added or existing records marked for updating. This ensures research happens automatically as your database grows.

Step 2: Web search for company information

The system performs targeted searches across multiple sources including news sites, company websites, and business directories to gather comprehensive information.

Step 3: AI analysis and summarization

OpenAI GPT-4o processes the gathered information to identify key details, remove duplicates, and generate concise summaries tailored to your specific needs.

Step 4: Structured data enrichment

The workflow maps the extracted information to specific fields in your Airtable schema, maintaining consistency across all records while preserving source links for verification.
