Enrich Company Data from LinkedIn via Bright Data & Google Sheets

Automate company data enrichment from LinkedIn using Bright Data's search capabilities and export results to Google Sheets

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching company information on LinkedIn. Sales teams, recruiters, and marketers often need to enrich their prospect lists with accurate LinkedIn data, but doing this manually for hundreds of companies wastes valuable time and leads to inconsistent results.

The automation connects Bright Data's LinkedIn search capabilities with Google Sheets to systematically discover official company LinkedIn pages and extract key firmographic data. It transforms your basic company list into an enriched database with verified LinkedIn URLs and additional insights - all without manual copying and pasting.

The workflow architecture showing how company names flow through Bright Data to LinkedIn and into Google Sheets

How It Works

1. Input Company List

The workflow begins with your spreadsheet of company names in Google Sheets. Each row represents one company needing LinkedIn data enrichment.

2. Search LinkedIn via Bright Data

Bright Data's search API queries LinkedIn for each company name, identifying the official company page while handling LinkedIn's anti-scraping measures.

3. Extract Profile Data

For each matching LinkedIn company page, the workflow extracts the canonical URL and optionally pulls additional firmographic data like employee count and industry.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to extract only the data points you actually need to avoid unnecessary API calls and keep your Bright Data costs optimized.

4. Output to Google Sheets

The enriched data flows back into your Google Sheet, adding LinkedIn URLs and other extracted fields as new columns alongside your original company names.
