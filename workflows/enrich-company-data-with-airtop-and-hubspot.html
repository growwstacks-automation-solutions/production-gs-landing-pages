What This Workflow Does

Sales and marketing teams waste countless hours manually researching companies when new leads come in. This automation solves that problem by instantly enriching company data the moment a contact enters your system. It takes basic information like an email domain or LinkedIn URL and transforms it into a complete company profile.

The workflow automatically extracts company details including name, tagline, headquarters location, employee count, and website. More importantly, it calculates an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) score based on factors like company size, technology usage, and industry fit. All this enriched data then syncs directly to HubSpot, giving your sales team complete context before they make their first call.

This isn't just about adding data fields—it's about turning raw leads into qualified prospects automatically. By scoring companies against your ICP criteria, you can prioritize outreach to the best-fit companies first, dramatically improving conversion rates and sales efficiency.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Validation & Domain Extraction

The workflow begins by validating incoming contact information. It extracts the company domain from email addresses and filters out personal domains (like Gmail or Yahoo) to focus only on business leads. If a LinkedIn URL is provided, it's used for more accurate enrichment.

Step 2: Data Enrichment via Airtop

Using the Airtop platform with authenticated LinkedIn access, the automation scrapes and analyzes company information. It gathers comprehensive data including company overview, location details, employee count, and technology indicators that help determine company maturity and fit.

Step 3: ICP Score Calculation

Based on the collected data, the workflow calculates an ICP score. This score considers multiple factors: whether the company uses relevant technologies, its size relative to your target, agency vs. product company classification, and geographic location. Higher scores indicate better-fit prospects.

Step 4: HubSpot Synchronization

All enriched fields—company name, website, LinkedIn URL, employee count, location data, and the calculated ICP score—are mapped to corresponding HubSpot properties. The workflow then creates or updates the company record in HubSpot, ensuring your CRM always has the most current and complete information.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtop API Key: Required to authenticate with the Airtop platform for data enrichment capabilities.
  2. Airtop Browser Profile: An authenticated profile with LinkedIn access to enable accurate company data scraping.
  3. HubSpot Integration: Your HubSpot account with API access configured for creating and updating company records.
  4. n8n Instance: Either self-hosted n8n or n8n.cloud account to run the automation workflow.
  5. Input Data Source: A consistent way to feed contact emails and/or LinkedIn URLs into the workflow, such as web forms, CSV imports, or existing CRM triggers.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the Template: Click the download button above to get the JSON workflow file.
  2. Import to n8n: In your n8n instance, create a new workflow and import the downloaded JSON file.
  3. Configure Airtop Credentials: Add your Airtop API key and select your authenticated browser profile in the Airtop nodes.
  4. Connect HubSpot: Set up the HubSpot connection with your API credentials and map the output fields to your HubSpot company properties.
  5. Test with Sample Data: Run the workflow manually with a test company email domain to verify enrichment and HubSpot updates work correctly.
  6. Automate the Trigger: Connect the workflow to your lead source—this could be a webhook from your website form, scheduled imports, or triggers from other systems.

Pro tip: Start by enriching your existing CRM contacts in batches before connecting to live lead sources. This helps you identify any mapping issues and ensures your historical data is complete before automating new leads.
