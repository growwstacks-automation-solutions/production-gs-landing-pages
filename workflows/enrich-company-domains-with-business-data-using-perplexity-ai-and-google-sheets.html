Enrich Company Domains with Business Data

Automatically research and append comprehensive business information to company domain lists using Perplexity AI and organize everything in Google Sheets.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual company research is one of the biggest time-wasters for sales, marketing, and business development teams. Spending hours searching for basic company information like address, employee count, revenue, and industry classification delays outreach and creates inconsistent data quality. This workflow solves that problem by automating the entire research process.

When you provide a list of company domains, this automation uses Perplexity AI's advanced research capabilities to find comprehensive business information from multiple sources. It then structures this data and saves it directly to Google Sheets, creating a ready-to-use enriched company database. The workflow intelligently batches domains to optimize API costs and includes error handling to ensure reliable operation.

How It Works

Step 1: Read Unprocessed Domains

The workflow starts by reading your Google Sheets document and identifying company domains that haven't been processed yet. It filters out previously enriched records using a simple "processed" column flag, ensuring you don't waste resources on duplicate research.

Step 2: Batch Processing for Efficiency

Domains are grouped into batches of 10 (configurable) to balance API cost efficiency with processing speed. This batching approach minimizes Perplexity AI API calls while maintaining reasonable processing times, making the workflow cost-effective even for large domain lists.

Step 3: AI-Powered Company Research

For each domain, the workflow sends a detailed query to Perplexity AI requesting specific business information. The AI researches current data from multiple sources, including company websites, business directories, news articles, and professional networks to provide verified information.

Step 4: Data Parsing and Structuring

The AI's natural language response is parsed into structured JSON format, extracting key business data points like complete addresses, phone numbers, employee counts, revenue figures, industry classifications, and LinkedIn URLs. This transformation turns unstructured research into organized, usable data.

Step 5: Update Spreadsheet and Mark Complete

The enriched data is written back to your Google Sheets document in the appropriate columns, and each domain is marked as "processed" to prevent duplicate work in future runs. The result is a continuously updated company database that grows smarter with each execution.

What You'll Need

  1. Perplexity AI API key – Available from their developer platform
  2. Google Sheets API access – OAuth2 credentials set up in n8n
  3. Google Sheets template – A spreadsheet with domain and data columns
  4. n8n instance – Self-hosted or cloud version
  5. Company domain list – Starting list of domains to enrich

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Perplexity AI credentials in the HTTP Request node
  3. Set up Google Sheets OAuth2 authentication for both Sheet nodes
  4. Update the Google Sheets document ID in the configuration
  5. Add your company domains to the "domain" column in the Data tab
  6. Execute the workflow manually to test with a few domains
  7. Once verified, add a Schedule Trigger for automated weekly enrichment

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of 5-10 domains to verify data quality before processing large lists. Adjust the AI prompt to focus on specific data points most valuable to your business, such as technology stack or funding information for tech companies.
