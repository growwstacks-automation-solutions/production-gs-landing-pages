What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of building comprehensive company profiles for go-to-market (GTM) research by combining Explorium's Master Company Profile (MCP) data with Claude AI's natural language processing capabilities. It solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching companies by automatically gathering, analyzing, and summarizing critical business intelligence.

The workflow pulls verified firmographic and technographic data from Explorium, enriches it with web-sourced signals, then uses Claude AI to generate insightful company summaries. This creates ready-to-use research briefs that help sales and marketing teams prioritize accounts, personalize outreach, and identify buying signals more effectively.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from Explorium MCP

The workflow starts by querying Explorium's API to retrieve standardized company data including industry classification, revenue estimates, technology stack, and predictive signals. This establishes a verified foundation for each company profile.

2. Supplementary Data Collection

Additional web sources are scanned to capture recent news, job postings, funding announcements, and other temporal signals that indicate company momentum or changing needs. This dynamic data complements Explorium's core dataset.

3. AI-Powered Analysis

Claude AI processes the aggregated data to identify patterns, highlight key insights, and generate natural language summaries. The AI assesses company growth potential, technology adoption trends, and other factors relevant to your specific GTM strategy.

4. Profile Delivery

The enriched company profiles are formatted into standardized reports and delivered to your CRM, sales enablement platform, or research repository. The workflow can trigger alerts when specific high-value signals are detected.
