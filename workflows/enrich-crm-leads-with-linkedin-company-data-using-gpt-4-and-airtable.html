What This Workflow Does

Sales teams waste countless hours manually researching companies on LinkedIn before reaching out. This creates inconsistent prospect data, delays outreach, and results in generic messaging that gets ignored. The problem isn't lack of information—it's the manual effort required to transform LinkedIn profiles into actionable sales intelligence.

This workflow solves that by automatically enriching CRM leads with detailed company intelligence from LinkedIn using AI. When a new lead enters your Airtable (or any CRM), it instantly scrapes their LinkedIn company page, uses GPT-4 to extract key insights, and creates 15+ personalized variables for your outreach. You get company overviews, products/services, funding information, recent posts, and market positioning—all formatted as natural language you can drop directly into emails.

The result is sales reps who can personalize every message at scale, reference recent company developments, and demonstrate genuine understanding of each prospect's business context. What used to take 20 minutes of manual research now happens automatically in seconds.

How It Works

1. Trigger & Data Collection

The workflow activates when a new lead is added to your Airtable base. It reads the LinkedIn company URL from your lead record and uses the HTTP Request node to fetch the public company profile page. This provides the raw HTML containing all available company information.

2. Content Processing

The HTML content is cleaned and structured, removing navigation elements and formatting while preserving the valuable company data. This prepares the information for AI analysis by creating a clean text representation of the company's LinkedIn presence.

3. AI-Powered Analysis

GPT-4 processes the cleaned content with specific prompts designed to extract sales-relevant intelligence. The AI identifies company overview, products/services, recent funding, employee count trends, recent posts and content themes, and market positioning—transforming unstructured data into structured insights.

4. Personalization Variable Generation

The structured insights are converted into 15+ email-ready variables written in natural language. These include personalized opening lines, relevant value propositions based on company size/industry, and specific references to recent company developments mentioned in their LinkedIn activity.

5. CRM Update

Finally, the enriched data is written back to your Airtable record, creating a complete prospect profile. Sales reps immediately see the AI-generated insights alongside the original lead information, ready for personalized outreach.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtable account (free tier works perfectly) with a base containing your leads
  2. OpenAI API key (GPT-4o-mini recommended for cost-effectiveness)
  3. LinkedIn company URLs stored in your lead records
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with webhook capabilities
  5. 5-10 minutes for initial configuration and testing

Pro tip: Use GPT-4o-mini instead of GPT-4 for this workflow. It's significantly cheaper and faster for data extraction tasks while maintaining excellent accuracy for company profile analysis.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure Airtable connection by adding your API key and selecting your base/table
  3. Add OpenAI credentials to both AI nodes in the workflow (use same key for both)
  4. Map your fields ensuring the LinkedIn URL field matches your Airtable column name
  5. Test with one record to verify data flows correctly from LinkedIn → AI → Airtable
  6. Set the trigger schedule (hourly/daily) or connect to webhook for real-time enrichment
  7. Monitor initial runs and adjust AI prompts if needed for your specific use case
