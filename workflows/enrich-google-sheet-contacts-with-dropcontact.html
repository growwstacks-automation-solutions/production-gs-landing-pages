Automate Lead Enrichment from Google Sheets

Find verified emails, enrich contact data with Dropcontact, and automatically add qualified leads to Lemlist campaigns—all in one automated workflow.

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead research is one of the biggest time-wasters in sales and marketing. Teams spend hours searching for email addresses, verifying contact information, and manually transferring data between spreadsheets and outreach tools. This workflow eliminates that manual process by creating a fully automated pipeline that transforms raw contact lists into enriched, verified leads ready for personalized outreach.

The automation pulls contact information from Google Sheets, uses Dropcontact to find and verify professional email addresses, enriches each contact with additional professional data, and then automatically adds qualified leads to your Lemlist campaigns. What used to take hours of manual research now happens automatically in minutes, ensuring your sales team always works with accurate, up-to-date contact information.

How It Works

Step 1: Read Contacts from Google Sheets

The workflow begins by reading your contact list from a specified Google Sheet. It processes each row containing basic information like names, companies, and job titles. The automation can be scheduled to run daily, weekly, or triggered whenever new contacts are added to your spreadsheet.

Step 2: Enrich Data with Dropcontact

Each contact is sent to Dropcontact's API, which performs several critical functions: it verifies email deliverability, finds professional email addresses when only basic information is provided, and enriches the contact with additional professional data like LinkedIn profiles, company information, and social media links.

Step 3: Filter and Qualify Leads

Based on the enrichment results, the workflow can filter contacts by specific criteria. You might exclude contacts with invalid emails, prioritize leads from target companies, or segment contacts by job title. This ensures only qualified, relevant leads proceed to the next stage.

Step 4: Add to Lemlist Campaigns

Finally, enriched and verified contacts are automatically added to your designated Lemlist campaigns. The workflow passes all the enriched data, allowing for highly personalized email sequences from day one. You can configure different campaigns based on lead quality, industry, or other segmentation criteria.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above
  2. Import the JSON file into your n8n instance
  3. Configure the Google Sheets node with your spreadsheet ID and range
  4. Add your Dropcontact API credentials to the Dropcontact node
  5. Configure the Lemlist node with your API key and campaign ID
  6. Test the workflow with a few sample contacts
  7. Schedule the workflow or set up a webhook trigger for automation

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of contacts (10-20) to test the workflow before processing your entire list. This helps you verify data quality and adjust any filtering criteria without using excessive API credits.
