What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually looking up company information using Dun & Bradstreet's business intelligence data. Sales teams, recruiters, and market researchers often waste hours copying DUNS numbers between systems and manually entering retrieved data.

The workflow automatically reads DUNS numbers from your Google Sheet, queries Dun & Bradstreet's Data Blocks API for comprehensive company profiles, and writes the enriched data back to your spreadsheet. It handles hundreds of records in minutes with perfect accuracy - eliminating human error in data entry.

How It Works

1. Trigger from Google Sheets

The workflow starts when you add new DUNS numbers to your designated Google Sheet. It can run on a schedule (daily/weekly) or trigger manually when you're ready to process a batch.

2. Fetch D&B Data Blocks

Each DUNS number is sent to Dun & Bradstreet's API which returns a standardized data block containing verified company information. The API handles rate limiting and data formatting automatically.

3. Transform and Map Data

The raw API response is processed to extract the most valuable fields for your use case - financial indicators, executive contacts, corporate hierarchies, and risk scores are structured into clean columns.

4. Update Google Sheets

The enriched data is written back to your original spreadsheet in the designated columns. The workflow can append new rows or update existing records based on DUNS number matching.

Pro tip: Combine this with CRM integrations to automatically update company records in Salesforce, HubSpot or other systems whenever your master sheet gets refreshed.
