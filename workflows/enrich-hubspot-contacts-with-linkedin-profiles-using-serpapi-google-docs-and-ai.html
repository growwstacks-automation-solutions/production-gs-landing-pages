What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of having incomplete contact information in HubSpot by automatically enriching records with professional data from LinkedIn. Many sales and marketing teams struggle with partial contact details that limit their outreach effectiveness and personalization capabilities.

The workflow connects HubSpot with LinkedIn (via SerpAPI) to fetch profile data, uses AI to extract relevant insights, and stores the enriched information back in HubSpot while maintaining a backup in Google Docs. This creates a complete 360-degree view of your contacts without manual research.

How It Works

1. Contact Trigger

The workflow activates when new contacts are created or existing ones are updated in HubSpot. You can also trigger it manually for batch processing of existing contacts.

2. LinkedIn Profile Lookup

Using SerpAPI, the system searches LinkedIn for profiles matching the contact's name and company. The API handles the complex search logic and returns structured profile data.

3. AI Data Extraction

An AI component processes the LinkedIn profile to extract key information like job titles, skills, education, and career history while filtering out irrelevant details.

4. HubSpot Field Mapping

The workflow maps the extracted LinkedIn data to corresponding custom fields in HubSpot, ensuring the information is stored in the right format and location.

5. Google Docs Backup

All enriched data is simultaneously recorded in a Google Sheet for audit purposes, creating a searchable archive of profile information.
