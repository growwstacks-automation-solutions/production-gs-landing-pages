What This Workflow Does

When prospects book calls through your Calendly, sales teams typically spend 15-20 minutes manually researching each lead before the meeting. This repetitive administrative work steals valuable selling time and often results in inconsistent or incomplete prospect information.

This automation solves that problem by automatically enriching lead data the moment someone books a call. It extracts the prospect's email from Calendly, filters out personal email addresses, enriches professional and company information using data services, and syncs everything to HubSpot—all before your sales team even sees the booking notification.

The result is a complete prospect profile waiting in your CRM, complete with company size, industry, job title, and other valuable context that helps sales reps conduct more personalized, effective conversations from the very first interaction.

How It Works

1. Calendly Booking Trigger

Whenever someone schedules a meeting through your Calendly link, the workflow automatically triggers. It captures the prospect's name, email address, meeting time, and any custom questions answered during booking.

2. Email Validation & Filtering

The system checks if the email is a business address (not Gmail, Yahoo, etc.) and filters out personal emails. This ensures enrichment resources are only used on qualified business prospects where company data is available.

3. Lead Enrichment Process

For business emails, the workflow queries enrichment services to gather company information (size, industry, location), professional details (job title, department, seniority), and additional contact data. Multiple sources may be checked for comprehensive coverage.

4. HubSpot CRM Sync

All enriched data is formatted and upserted to HubSpot as a new contact or company record. The system creates or updates existing records, adds custom property values, and can trigger HubSpot workflows for follow-up sequences.

5. Notification & Logging

The sales team receives a notification with the enriched prospect profile, and all activities are logged for tracking and optimization. Failed enrichments or data issues are flagged for manual review.

What You'll Need

  1. Calendly account with admin access to create webhooks
  2. HubSpot account with API access and custom property setup
  3. Lead enrichment service like Clearbit, Hunter.io, or similar (API key required)
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with webhook capabilities
  5. Business email domains list for filtering personal emails

Pro tip: Start with a limited set of enrichment fields (company name, industry, employee count) and expand as you validate data quality. Too much data can overwhelm sales teams—focus on what actually improves conversation quality.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure Calendly webhook in your account settings to point to your n8n webhook URL
  3. Add API credentials for HubSpot and your chosen enrichment service in n8n
  4. Map data fields between enrichment results and HubSpot custom properties
  5. Test with a real booking using a team member's business email to verify the flow
  6. Set up notifications for your sales channel (Slack, email, or internal system)
  7. Monitor initial runs and adjust filtering rules based on data quality
