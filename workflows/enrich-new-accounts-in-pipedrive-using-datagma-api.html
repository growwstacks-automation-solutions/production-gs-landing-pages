What This Workflow Does

Sales teams waste hours manually researching new companies that enter their CRM. Without proper data enrichment, reps can't quickly identify ideal customers, prioritize outreach, or personalize their approach. This creates missed opportunities and inefficient sales processes.

This n8n workflow automatically enriches new Pipedrive accounts using the Datagma API. When a new company is created in Pipedrive, the workflow instantly fetches comprehensive data about the company's industry, size, location, technology stack, and key decision-makers. It then updates the Pipedrive record with this enriched information and identifies whether the account matches your ideal customer profile (ICP).

The automation goes beyond simple data lookup—it intelligently processes the enrichment results, extracts email addresses for key contacts, and creates new person records in Pipedrive when ideal customers are identified. This turns raw company data into actionable sales intelligence in seconds.

How It Works

1. Pipedrive Trigger Detection

The workflow starts when Pipedrive detects a new company creation. The trigger node captures all company details including name, website, and any initial information your sales team entered.

2. Datagma API Enrichment

The workflow sends the company details to Datagma's enrichment API, which returns comprehensive data including industry classification, employee count, revenue estimates, technology stack, and social media profiles.

3. Data Processing & Structure Simplification

Item Lists nodes process the API response to extract specific data points and reformat complex nested arrays into simple fields that Pipedrive can handle without custom JavaScript coding.

4. Ideal Customer Profile Matching

An IF node evaluates whether the enriched company matches your defined ICP criteria (such as industry, size, or location). This automated qualification saves sales reps from manual evaluation.

5. Contact Information Extraction

For companies that match your ICP, the workflow queries Datagma for email addresses of key decision-makers (like heads of sales or business development) based on job titles and seniority.

6. Pipedrive Record Updates

The workflow updates the original company record with enrichment data and creates new person records for identified contacts, completing the full account enrichment cycle automatically.
