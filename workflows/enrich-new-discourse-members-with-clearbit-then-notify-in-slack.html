What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of identifying valuable community members among hundreds or thousands of Discourse signups. Without automation, sales and customer success teams waste time manually researching each new member or miss high-potential prospects entirely.

The workflow automatically enriches new Discourse member profiles with Clearbit's professional and company data, then sends targeted Slack notifications when high-value members join. This enables immediate, personalized engagement with prospects who match your ideal customer profile.

The workflow checks new Discourse members against Clearbit's database and routes high-value profiles to Slack

How It Works

1. New member trigger

The workflow monitors your Discourse community for new member registrations via the Discourse API. Each time a new user signs up, the workflow captures their email address and basic profile information.

2. Clearbit enrichment

The system sends the new member's email to Clearbit's enrichment API, which returns professional details (job title, role) and company information (industry, revenue, employee count). This data helps assess the member's potential value.

3. Priority scoring

The workflow applies your custom rules to score each member based on the enriched data. You might prioritize executives from enterprise companies, or target specific industries relevant to your product.

4. Slack notification

For members meeting your criteria, the system sends a formatted Slack message to your designated channel. The notification includes key details and a direct link to their Discourse profile for immediate follow-up.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Slack channel like #community-vips for these notifications to ensure they don't get lost in general team chatter.
