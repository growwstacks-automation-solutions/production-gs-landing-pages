What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the critical challenge of staying compliant with the EU's rapidly evolving environmental regulations. With over 200 new sustainability policies introduced annually, businesses struggle to manually track requirements that impact their operations, reporting, and supply chains.

The workflow automatically monitors official EU sources for new legislation, uses GPT-4o to analyze documents and extract key compliance requirements, then organizes the information in Google Sheets with deadline tracking. It creates actionable tasks for relevant teams and maintains a searchable knowledge base of regulatory obligations.

Automated tracking of legislation status and compliance deadlines

How It Works

1. Legislation Monitoring

The workflow scrapes data from EUR-Lex, the EU Official Journal, and policy tracking platforms. It filters for documents matching your configured keywords (like "CSRD", "ESRS", or "Ecodesign").

2. AI Analysis

GPT-4o processes raw legal texts to generate executive summaries, compliance checklists, and business impact assessments. It identifies affected departments and extracts critical dates.

GPT-4o extracting key requirements from complex legal documents

3. Centralized Tracking

All data populates a master Google Sheet with filters by regulation type, effective date, and business unit. Color-coding shows implementation status and urgency.

4. Task Automation

The system creates follow-up tasks in your project management tool (like Asana or ClickUp) with deadlines based on legislative timelines, assigning them to responsible teams.

Pro tip: Configure alerts for "golden periods" - the 3-6 month window after legislation passes but before enforcement begins, when most compliance prep should occur.
