What This Workflow Does

This automated system solves the challenge of staying current with rapidly evolving EU sustainability regulations and news. Businesses struggle to manually monitor dozens of official sources, often missing critical updates that impact compliance requirements and operational practices.

The workflow continuously scans RSS feeds from EU institutions, filters content using AI classification, generates executive summaries, and distributes curated updates through both email digests and Telegram alerts. This ensures decision-makers receive timely, relevant information without information overload.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Aggregation

The workflow pulls updates from official EU sources including the European Commission, Parliament, and specialized sustainability portals. It monitors multiple RSS feeds simultaneously, capturing new publications as they're released.

2. AI-Powered Classification

GPT analyzes each article's content to determine relevance, urgency and topic categorization. The system scores updates based on predefined criteria matching your business priorities, filtering out irrelevant content.

AI classification workflow showing topic identification and scoring process

3. Executive Summary Generation

For high-priority updates, the system generates concise summaries highlighting key requirements, deadlines, and business impacts. ElevenLabs optionally converts these to audio briefings for mobile consumption.

4. Multi-Channel Distribution

Curated content is delivered through scheduled email digests and real-time Telegram alerts. The system maintains a searchable archive of all processed updates for compliance documentation.

Pro tip: Configure different alert thresholds for various teams - immediate Telegram notifications for urgent compliance updates, daily digests for strategic trends.
