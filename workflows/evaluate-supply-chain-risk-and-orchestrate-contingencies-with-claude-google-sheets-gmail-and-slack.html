What This Workflow Does

Supply chain disruptions cost businesses millions in lost revenue, production delays, and customer dissatisfaction. Traditional risk management relies on manual spreadsheet tracking and periodic reviews, creating dangerous blind spots between assessments. This automation transforms that reactive approach into a proactive, intelligent system.

The workflow continuously monitors your risk register in Google Sheets, applies AI-powered severity scoring using Claude, and automatically routes each risk through appropriate response channels. Critical risks trigger immediate executive alerts with detailed AI assessments, medium risks go to operational teams for review, and low risks are automatically acknowledged and logged—ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

How It Works

The system operates on a intelligent tiered routing principle that matches response intensity to risk severity, eliminating manual triage bottlenecks.

Step 1: Data Retrieval & Initial Assessment

The workflow connects to your Google Sheets risk register, pulling new or updated risk entries. Each entry includes supplier information, risk type, potential impact, and detection date. Basic severity indicators are calculated based on predefined financial, operational, and compliance parameters.

Step 2: AI-Powered Risk Scoring

Claude AI analyzes each risk entry, considering unstructured context that spreadsheets can't capture—recent news about suppliers, geopolitical developments, industry trends, and historical performance patterns. The AI assigns a comprehensive risk score and provides reasoning for its assessment.

Step 3: Intelligent Tiered Routing

Based on the AI score, risks are automatically categorized into three tiers. Critical risks (high probability, high impact) proceed to multi-agent assessment with immediate notification protocols. Medium risks follow standard review paths, while low risks receive automated processing without human intervention.

Step 4: Notification & Response Orchestration

Critical risks trigger coordinated alerts: detailed assessment emails to executive teams via Gmail and urgent notifications in designated Slack channels with actionable summaries. The system creates audit trails, updates risk status, and can initiate contingency workflows based on predefined response plans.

Pro tip: Configure different Slack channels for different risk types (financial, operational, compliance) so subject matter experts receive only relevant alerts, reducing notification fatigue.

Quick Setup Guide

Import and configure this template in under 30 minutes to start automating your risk management immediately.

  1. Download and import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file, then import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import function.
  2. Connect Google Sheets: Authenticate with Google and point the "Read from Sheet" node to your risk register spreadsheet. Map the relevant columns (supplier, risk type, detection date, etc.).
  3. Configure Claude AI: Add your Anthropic API key to the Claude Model nodes. Adjust the prompt templates if you want to customize the assessment criteria for your industry.
  4. Set up notifications: Connect your Gmail account for email alerts and authenticate with Slack. Specify which channels should receive critical vs. low-risk notifications.
  5. Customize thresholds: Modify the "Switch" node conditions to match your organization's risk tolerance levels for what constitutes critical, medium, and low severity.
  6. Test and activate: Run the workflow manually with sample data to verify all connections work, then activate the schedule trigger for continuous monitoring.

Pro tip: Start with a simplified risk register containing 5-10 test entries before connecting to your full supplier database. This lets you verify scoring accuracy and notification delivery without overwhelming your team.
