What This Workflow Does

Manual vendor proposal evaluation creates bottlenecks in procurement processes. Teams waste hours reviewing PDFs to extract pricing, terms, and compliance details, and service levels. This workflow automates the entire evaluation chain - from document ingestion to decision routing.

The system uses AI analyzes proposal documents against your criteria, scores them objectively, and triggers appropriate next steps. Responses can range from automated acknowledgments to escalation alerts, all delivered through Slack or via webhook to your procurement system.

How It Works

1. Document Processing

The workflow ingests vendor PDFs from email attachments, cloud storage, or form submissions. It extracts text while preserving document structure.

2 AI Analysis

OpenAI evaluates the proposal against configured criteria like pricing compliance, pricing competitiveness, and risk factors. It generates a scorecard with key insights.

3 Decision Routing

Based on scoring thresholds, the workflow either auto-responds, requests clarification, or escalates for human review. All actions are logged for audit purposes.

4 Multi-Channel Response

Approved responses deploy via Slack notifications to your procurement team and/or webhook to update vendor management systems.

Pro tip: Configure different response templates for various score ranges to maintain professional communication while automating follow-ups.
