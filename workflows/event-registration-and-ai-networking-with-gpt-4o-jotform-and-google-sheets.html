What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of meaningful networking at large events. Traditional events often leave attendees overwhelmed with random connections or missed opportunities. Our AI-powered workflow transforms Jotform registrations into intelligent networking opportunities.

The system analyzes each attendee's professional profile, goals, and interests using GPT-4o. It then generates optimal 1:1 meeting suggestions, group networking matches, and even conversation starters. All matches are delivered automatically via email or integrated event apps, creating a seamless networking experience.

How It Works

1. Registration Data Collection

Attendees complete a Jotform with standard registration fields plus professional details. The form captures skills, goals, and networking preferences that feed the AI matching algorithm.

2. AI Profile Analysis

GPT-4o processes each submission, extracting key attributes and creating semantic profiles. The AI identifies complementary skills, potential collaborations, and shared interests across attendees.

3. Optimal Matching Algorithm

The system evaluates thousands of potential connections using multiple criteria. It prioritizes matches with the highest mutual benefit scores while avoiding obvious mismatches.

4. Personalized Outreach

Each attendee receives 3-5 curated connections with meeting suggestions. The emails include AI-generated icebreakers and mutual interest highlights to facilitate conversations.
