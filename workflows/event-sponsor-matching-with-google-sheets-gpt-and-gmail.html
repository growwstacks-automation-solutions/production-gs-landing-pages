What This Workflow Does

Event sponsorship is a high-value, high-stress process. Planners waste hours manually matching dozens of potential sponsors to Gold, Silver, and Bronze packages, often missing perfect fits or leaving revenue on the table. This workflow solves that by automating the entire matching and outreach process.

It connects your Google Sheets sponsor list with AI (GPT-4o) to intelligently score each sponsor against your package tiers based on budget, industry alignment, and stated goals. It then generates personalized email recommendations via Gmail, logs all matches for tracking, and even projects potential revenue increases—turning a chaotic manual task into a streamlined, data-driven revenue engine.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger & Data Collection

The workflow activates when you update your "Sponsors" tab in Google Sheets. It pulls in all sponsor details (company, industry, budget range, goals) and your "Packages" tab (tier names, prices, benefits). This keeps your single source of truth intact.

Step 2: AI Scoring & Matching

Each sponsor profile is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o with a custom prompt that evaluates fit against each package. The AI returns a compatibility score (e.g., 92% for Gold) and a concise reasoning paragraph, identifying the top 1-3 recommended packages.

Step 3: Personalized Outreach Generation

For each sponsor, the workflow merges the AI's recommendation into a professional, tailored email template. The email highlights why the specific package is an ideal fit, referencing the sponsor's industry and budget, and includes a clear call-to-action.

Step 4: Send & Log

The personalized email is sent via Gmail to the sponsor contact. Simultaneously, all match data—sponsor name, recommended package, score, and email status—is appended to a "Matches Log" sheet in your workbook, creating a complete audit trail and pipeline view.

This automation is designed for event professionals who manage sponsorship sales. What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets workbook with two tabs: "Sponsors" (with columns for Name, Industry, Budget, etc.) and "Packages" (Tier, Price, Benefits).
  2. An OpenAI API key (GPT-4o recommended for best results).
  3. Gmail account credentials (set up via OAuth 2.0 in n8n for secure sending).
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to import and run the workflow.
  5. Basic understanding of your sponsorship package structure and target sponsor profiles.

Pro tip: Start with a small test list of 5-10 sponsors to fine-tune the AI prompt and email template before rolling out to your entire list. Adjust the scoring criteria in the prompt to weight factors most important to your event (e.g., "budget alignment" vs. "industry relevance").

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n dashboard, go to Workflows → Import from file and select the downloaded JSON.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up the Google Sheets, OpenAI, and Gmail nodes with your API keys and OAuth credentials using n8n's secure credential management.
  3. Map Your Data: Update the Google Sheets node to point to your specific spreadsheet ID and sheet names. Ensure your column headers match the expected format in the workflow.
