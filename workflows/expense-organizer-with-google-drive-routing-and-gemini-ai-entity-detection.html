What This Workflow Does

Manual expense tracking is a time-consuming, error-prone process that plagues businesses and individuals alike. This automation eliminates the headache of receipt organization by creating a complete, AI-powered expense management system. It transforms chaotic paper trails and digital clutter into structured, actionable financial data.

The workflow automatically processes uploaded receipts, uses Google Gemini AI to extract key information like vendor names, dates, and amounts, then intelligently routes documents to organized Google Drive folders based on expense category. It simultaneously backs up to cloud storage and sends detailed email notifications—creating a seamless, audit-ready financial record system without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Form Submission & Receipt Upload

Users submit expenses through a simple web form, uploading receipt images or PDFs and selecting categories (Personal, Business, or Shared). The system accepts multiple file formats and prepares them for AI processing.

2. AI-Powered Data Extraction

Google Gemini AI analyzes receipt content, extracting structured data with remarkable accuracy. It identifies vendor information, transaction dates, amounts, tax details, and categorizes expenses based on content patterns—far surpassing manual data entry speed and consistency.

3. Smart Routing & Organization

Based on the categorized expense type, the system automatically routes receipts to appropriate Google Drive folders. It creates year/month directory structures, applies consistent naming conventions, and maintains separation between business, personal, and shared expenses.

4. Multi-Destination Storage

Documents are simultaneously stored in Google Drive for easy access and S3 cloud storage for backup and compliance. This dual-storage approach ensures data redundancy while maintaining organization across different storage systems.

5. Notification & Integration

Formatted HTML emails are sent with complete expense details and storage links. Business expenses can be automatically forwarded to accounting systems via email hooks, creating a bridge between receipt management and formal bookkeeping.

Pro tip: Configure the AI extraction to learn your most common vendors—it will become increasingly accurate at categorizing recurring expenses from familiar suppliers, saving even more time over manual methods.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Gemini API credentials for AI-powered receipt analysis
  2. Google Drive OAuth2 access for personal and/or business accounts
  3. Gmail OAuth2 credentials for sending notification emails
  4. S3-compatible storage (AWS S3, Wasabi, DigitalOcean Spaces, etc.)
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  6. Web form endpoint for users to submit expenses (included in template)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials for Gemini AI, Google Drive, Gmail, and S3 storage in n8n's credential management
  3. Update placeholder values in the workflow nodes with your specific folder IDs, email addresses, and bucket names
  4. Test the form submission with a sample receipt to verify AI extraction accuracy
  5. Validate folder creation in Google Drive and check notification emails arrive correctly
  6. Activate the workflow and share the form URL with your team or set up for personal use

Implementation note: Start with a small set of test receipts to fine-tune the AI extraction before rolling out to your entire team. The system learns from consistent data patterns, so initial calibration ensures long-term accuracy.
