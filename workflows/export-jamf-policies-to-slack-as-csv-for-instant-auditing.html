What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of auditing Jamf policies across your Apple device fleet. Instead of logging into Jamf Pro to check each policy individually, the workflow automatically exports all policy configurations (including triggers, scope, and frequency) as a clean CSV file delivered directly to your Slack channel.

IT teams use this to maintain compliance documentation, quickly verify policy changes, and identify configuration inconsistencies across device groups. The automated export creates an auditable trail without manual reporting, saving hours each month on compliance preparation and policy management.

How It Works

1. Jamf Policy Data Extraction

The workflow connects to your Jamf Pro instance via API and retrieves all policy configurations, including often-overlooked details like execution frequency and scope limitations.

2. CSV Formatting

Raw policy data gets transformed into a standardized CSV format with columns for policy name, triggers, scope, frequency, and last modified date—exactly what auditors need to verify compliance.

3. Slack Delivery

The formatted CSV uploads to your specified Slack channel with a timestamp and summary of changes since the last export, creating a searchable audit trail in your team's collaboration hub.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run automatically before compliance meetings so reports are always current without last-minute scrambling.
