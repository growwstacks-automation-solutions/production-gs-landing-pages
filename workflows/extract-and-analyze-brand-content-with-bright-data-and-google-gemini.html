What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the complete brand content monitoring process from data collection to AI-powered analysis. It uses Bright Data to extract brand mentions and discussions from across the web, then processes this content through Google Gemini for sentiment analysis, key theme extraction, and executive summaries.

The solution solves the challenge of manual brand monitoring which is time-consuming, inconsistent across channels, and limited in analytical depth. Marketing teams, PR agencies, and customer experience managers can use these automated insights to track brand perception, identify emerging issues, and measure the impact of campaigns or product launches.

How Bright Data extraction connects with Google Gemini AI analysis

How It Works

1. Content Extraction Phase

The workflow begins by using Bright Data's web scraping capabilities to collect brand mentions from configured sources. This can include news sites, forums, review platforms, and social media channels where your brand is discussed.

2. Data Processing

Raw extracted content is cleaned and standardized, removing duplicates and irrelevant noise. The system organizes content by source type and timestamp, preparing it for analysis while preserving original context.

3. AI Analysis

Google Gemini processes the content through multiple AI models simultaneously: one analyzing sentiment (positive/negative/neutral), another identifying key themes and topics, and a third generating concise summaries of longer discussions.

4. Insight Delivery

Final outputs are compiled into structured reports that highlight sentiment trends, emerging topics, and notable mentions. These can be delivered via email, saved to databases, or pushed to dashboards for team review.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to alert your team when sentiment scores drop below a defined threshold, enabling rapid response to potential PR issues.
