Extract & Analyze Google AI Overviews for SEO

Automatically capture Google's AI-generated search summaries, convert them to actionable data, and generate AI-powered SEO recommendations to improve your content strategy.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Google's AI Overviews are transforming how users find information, presenting AI-generated summaries directly in search results. For SEO professionals and content marketers, understanding what content gets featured—and why—is crucial. This workflow solves the manual, time-consuming process of monitoring these summaries.

It automatically extracts AI Overview content from Google Search via a browser extension, processes the HTML into clean markdown, stores everything in Google Sheets for historical tracking, and uses an LLM (like Gemini 2.5 Pro) to analyze your page content against the AI summary. The result is a batch of actionable SEO recommendations: what to add, what to clarify, and how to better align your content with what Google's AI prioritizes.

Instead of spending hours manually checking searches and guessing what the AI values, this automation gives you a systematic, data-driven approach to SEO in the age of AI search.

How It Works

1. Capture AI Overviews from Search

A browser extension (available for Chrome and Firefox) detects when a Google Search result page contains an AI Overview. With a single click, it captures the HTML of the summary and its cited sources, then sends this data securely via a webhook to your n8n instance.

2. Process & Clean the Data

The workflow receives the webhook payload and immediately converts the raw HTML into structured markdown. This step cleans the text, removes unnecessary formatting, and prepares it for analysis and storage. It also extracts metadata like the search query and timestamp.

3. Store in Google Sheets for Tracking

All extracted overviews, along with their metadata, are appended to a Google Sheet. This creates a living database of AI Overviews across your target keywords, enabling trend analysis over time. You can see which queries generate overviews, which sources are cited most frequently, and how summaries evolve.

4. Generate AI-Powered SEO Guidelines

For URLs you add to a designated column in the sheet, the workflow fetches your page content and compares it against the corresponding AI Overview. Using a configured LLM (via OpenRouter), it produces specific recommendations: "Add a section about X," "Clarify your explanation of Y," "Include more data from source Z."

5. Schedule & Automate Ongoing Analysis

A scheduler node triggers the workflow every 15 minutes (configurable), checking the sheet for new URLs to analyze. This creates a continuous feedback loop: new overviews are captured, stored, and analyzed, keeping your SEO strategy dynamically aligned with Google's AI output.

What You'll Need

  1. A running n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. A Google account with access to Google Sheets.
  3. An OpenRouter API key (or similar LLM provider) for the AI analysis step.
  4. The companion browser extension installed (Chrome or Firefox).
  5. A prepared Google Sheet with the required columns: extractedAt, searchQuery, sources, markdown, myURL, task, guidelines, key.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get the automation running in under 15 minutes.

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n workspace via the "Import from JSON" option.
  2. Configure Google Sheets OAuth in n8n credentials, then update the three Sheet nodes with your spreadsheet URL and sheet name.
  3. Add your OpenRouter API key as a credential and select the Gemini 2.5 Pro model in the LLM node.
  4. Install the browser extension from the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons site.
  5. Copy your n8n webhook URL into the extension's settings and test the connection.
  6. Activate the workflow and perform a Google search with an AI Overview. Use the extension to send data and verify it appears in your Sheet.
