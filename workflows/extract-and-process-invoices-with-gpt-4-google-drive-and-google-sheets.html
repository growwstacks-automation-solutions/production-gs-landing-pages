What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms manual invoice processing into a fully automated system. It monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new invoice PDFs, uses GPT-4 to extract all key information (vendor, amounts, dates, etc.), organizes the files into proper accounting folders, and updates your Google Sheets booking list.

The workflow eliminates hours of manual data entry while reducing errors. It handles the entire process from file upload to accounting records with zero human intervention after setup. The AI-powered extraction adapts to different invoice formats without requiring template configurations.

The workflow processes invoices end-to-end from Drive upload to accounting records

How It Works

1. File Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your specified Google Drive folder for new invoice PDF uploads. When detected, it immediately begins processing.

2. AI-Powered Extraction

Using GPT-4 with a structured output parser, the system extracts key fields including vendor details, invoice numbers, dates, line items, totals, and tax information.

3. Data Validation

Extracted data is validated against your chart of accounts and business rules. Any discrepancies trigger automatic alerts or fallback processes.

4. File Organization

Processed invoices are automatically renamed using a consistent format and moved to the appropriate accounting folder based on vendor or expense category.

5. Accounting Update

All extracted data is logged in your Google Sheets accounting record, ready for review or export to your accounting software.
