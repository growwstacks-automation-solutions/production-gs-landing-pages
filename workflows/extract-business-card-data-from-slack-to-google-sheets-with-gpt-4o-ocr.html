What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious problem of manually entering contact information from business cards into your CRM or contact management system. When team members snap photos of business cards and share them in Slack, this workflow automatically extracts all the key details using advanced AI OCR technology and organizes them neatly in Google Sheets.

The system leverages GPT-4o's powerful image recognition capabilities to accurately identify and categorize contact information even from imperfect photos. It handles variations in card designs, fonts, and layouts, converting visual information into structured data ready for your sales team or CRM import.

How It Works

1. Upload business card to Slack

Team members simply take a photo of a business card and upload it to a designated Slack channel or via direct message to your Slack bot. No special apps or formatting required - just snap and share like any other photo.

2. AI processes the image

The workflow detects new image uploads and sends them to GPT-4o's vision capabilities for analysis. The AI identifies text regions, understands their context (name vs. title vs. contact info), and extracts the data with high accuracy.

3. Data validation and formatting

Extracted information goes through automatic validation checks - verifying email formats, standardizing phone numbers, and checking for missing fields. The system can flag questionable entries for human review while proceeding with confirmed data.

4. Google Sheets integration

Validated contact details are appended as new rows in your designated Google Sheet, with consistent column formatting. The sheet serves as both a contact database and ready source for CRM imports or mailing list management.
