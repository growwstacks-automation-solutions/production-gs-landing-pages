Extract Business Emails from Google Maps Listings

Automate your lead generation by scraping contact information from local business listings. Perfect for sales teams, marketers, and agencies needing targeted outreach to specific industries or locations.

Download Template JSON · Zapier compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of finding business contact information for lead generation. Instead of spending hours searching Google Maps and visiting individual websites, the workflow automatically extracts business names, websites, and email addresses from Maps listings based on your search criteria (location, business category, etc.).

The extracted data gets organized into a clean spreadsheet or CRM-ready format, saving sales teams countless hours of manual research. You can target specific industries in precise geographic areas, ensuring your outreach hits the most relevant potential customers.

How It Works

1. Define Your Search Parameters

The workflow starts by taking your target location and business type (e.g., "plumbers in Chicago" or "IT services in Austin"). These parameters determine which Google Maps listings get scraped.

2. Extract Business Listings

The automation pulls all matching business listings from Google Maps, including names, addresses, phone numbers, and websites. This forms your initial lead database.

3. Scrape Websites for Emails

For businesses that list websites, the workflow visits each site and scans for email addresses in contact pages, footers, and other common locations.

4. Organize and Export Data

All collected information gets structured into columns with business name, location, website, and email. The final output can go to Google Sheets, your CRM, or other business tools.

Pro tip: Combine this with email verification tools to automatically filter out invalid addresses before importing to your CRM.
