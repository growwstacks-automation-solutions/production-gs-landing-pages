What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manual lead generation by automatically extracting business email addresses from Google Maps search results. Instead of manually clicking through listings and copying information, the workflow systematically collects verified contact details and organizes them in Google Sheets for immediate use in outreach campaigns.

The solution is particularly valuable for B2B sales teams, marketing agencies, and service providers who need to build targeted prospect lists quickly. By automating this repetitive task, businesses can focus their efforts on high-value activities like relationship building rather than data collection.

How It Works

1. Search Execution

The workflow initiates with a targeted search on Google Maps using your specified keywords and location parameters. This ensures the results match your ideal customer profile.

2. Data Extraction

For each business listing in the search results, the automation extracts available contact information including email addresses, phone numbers, and business names directly from the profile data.

3. Data Validation

Extracted emails undergo basic validation to filter out incomplete or obviously invalid addresses, improving the quality of your lead list.

4. Google Sheets Integration

Verified contact information is automatically appended to a designated Google Sheet, with columns for each data point (business name, email, phone, etc.) for easy sorting and filtering.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run weekly to maintain an always-updated prospect list without manual effort.
