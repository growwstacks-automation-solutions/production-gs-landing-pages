What This Workflow Does

Manual company research is one of the biggest time-wasters for sales and marketing teams. This automation solves that problem by automatically extracting comprehensive company data from LinkedIn and calculating an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) score to instantly qualify leads.

The workflow takes a company domain as input, locates its LinkedIn profile, extracts key business information including company size, industry, location, and technology focus, then applies your custom scoring criteria to determine how well the company matches your ideal customer profile.

Instead of spending hours researching individual companies, your team gets instant, structured data that can be directly imported into your CRM or sales platform, complete with a qualification score that helps prioritize outreach efforts.

How It Works

Step 1: LinkedIn Profile Detection

The workflow starts by taking a company website domain. It uses intelligent search techniques to locate the company's official LinkedIn profile. If you already know the LinkedIn URL, you can provide it directly to skip this step.

Step 2: Data Extraction via Airtop

Using your authenticated Airtop profile connected to LinkedIn, the automation extracts structured company data including name, tagline, website, location, employee count, industry classification, and company description.

Step 3: ICP Scoring Calculation

The extracted data is evaluated against your predefined ICP criteria. The scoring model considers factors like company size bracket, AI/technology focus, geographic location, and whether the company is an automation agency. Each factor contributes to an overall score.

Step 4: Data Merging and Output

All data components—verified LinkedIn URL, company profile, scale metrics, classification, and ICP score with justifications—are merged into a clean JSON object ready for integration with your existing systems.
