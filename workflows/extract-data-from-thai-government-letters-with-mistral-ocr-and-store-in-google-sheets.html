What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow template solves the challenge of processing Thai government correspondence by automatically extracting key information from letters and documents. Many businesses and organizations dealing with Thai authorities struggle with manual data entry from official communications, which is time-consuming and prone to errors.

The workflow uses Mistral OCR technology specifically trained for Thai script recognition to accurately convert scanned documents or PDFs into structured data. It then organizes this information into Google Sheets for easy access, analysis, and integration with other systems. This eliminates hours of manual transcription while ensuring data consistency.

How It Works

1. Document Input

The workflow accepts incoming Thai government letters through multiple channels - email attachments, uploaded files, or directly from scanning devices. It supports common formats like PDF, JPG, and PNG.

2. OCR Processing

Mistral OCR analyzes the document with specialized Thai language recognition capabilities. It identifies text blocks, tables, and key fields even in complex government document layouts.

3. Data Extraction

The system parses the OCR output to extract structured information like reference numbers, dates, sender details, and document content. It handles variations in Thai government letter formats.

4. Google Sheets Integration

Extracted data is automatically organized into predefined columns in Google Sheets, creating a searchable database of all correspondence with timestamps.
