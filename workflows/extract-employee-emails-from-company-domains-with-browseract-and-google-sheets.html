What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the time-consuming process of finding professional email addresses from company domains. It combines BrowserAct's human-in-the-loop scraping capabilities with Google Sheets for organized data storage, creating an efficient system for sales prospecting, recruitment outreach, and partnership development.

The solution solves the challenge of manual email research which typically takes 5-10 minutes per contact. By automating the initial discovery and pattern testing phases while maintaining human verification, it delivers verified professional emails at scale while ensuring data accuracy and compliance with email collection best practices.

How It Works

1. Input company domains

The workflow begins with a list of target company domains, which can be imported from a CRM, spreadsheet, or manually entered. These domains serve as the foundation for email pattern discovery.

2. BrowserAct automated search

BrowserAct automatically visits company websites and professional directories to identify common email patterns (e.g., first.last@company.com). It tests these patterns against known contacts when available.

3. Human verification step

The system presents potential email matches for human review before final confirmation. This critical step ensures data quality while still saving 80% of the manual research time.

4. Google Sheets integration

Verified emails are automatically organized in Google Sheets with relevant metadata (name, position, company). The spreadsheet serves as a living database that can connect to CRMs or outreach tools.
