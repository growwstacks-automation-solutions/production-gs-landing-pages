Extract Google Ads Creatives by Domain with SerpAPI

Automate competitor ad intelligence. Pull creatives from Google's Ads Transparency Center, filter by domain, and export to CSV for analysis.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual competitor ad research is time-consuming and inconsistent. Marketing teams waste hours each week visiting the Google Ads Transparency Center, copying creatives into spreadsheets, and trying to spot patterns. This process misses important trends and can't scale across multiple competitors or regions.

This automation solves that problem by systematically extracting ad creatives for any target domain using SerpAPI's Google Ads Transparency Center engine. It filters results to show only ads matching your specified domain, categorizes them by format (text, image, or video), and exports clean CSV files ready for analysis. What used to take hours now happens in minutes with consistent, structured data.

How It Works

1. Configure Domain & Region

The workflow starts by setting your target domain (like "example.com") and SerpAPI region code. This ensures you're collecting ads from the correct geographic market where your competitors are active.

2. Fetch Ads via SerpAPI

Using your SerpAPI credentials, the workflow queries Google's Ads Transparency Center for the first page of results (up to 50 creatives). The API returns structured data including ad copy, images, video links, and metadata.

3. Filter & Categorize

The automation filters results to include only ads with an exact domain match, eliminating irrelevant creatives. It then categorizes each ad by format—text ads, image ads, or video ads—for separate analysis.

4. Export to CSV Files

Finally, the workflow generates three CSV files in your /files/ directory: text_ads.csv, image_ads.csv, and video_ads.csv. Each file contains creative-level details ready for spreadsheet analysis or import into business intelligence tools.
