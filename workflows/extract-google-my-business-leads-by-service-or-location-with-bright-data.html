Extract Google My Business Leads by Service & Location

Automate local business lead generation from Google Maps. Find targeted prospects by service category and geographic area, remove duplicates, and export to Google Sheets—all without manual searching.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation from Google Maps is a time-consuming, repetitive task that drains sales and marketing resources. This automation solves that problem by systematically extracting Google My Business listings based on specific service types and geographic locations.

The workflow accepts a service category (like "coffee shop" or "plumbing"), state, and country as input. It then uses AI to generate relevant city lists, queries Bright Data's Google Maps dataset for businesses matching those criteria, processes the results in batches with proper rate limiting, removes duplicate entries, and exports clean, structured data directly to Google Sheets. What would take hours of manual searching and data entry becomes a fully automated process that runs in minutes.

How It Works

1. Input Processing & City Generation

The workflow begins with a web form where you specify the service type, state, and country. It uses Claude AI to intelligently generate a list of relevant cities within that state, ensuring comprehensive geographic coverage beyond just major metropolitan areas.

2. Search Query Creation & Categorization

Each city is paired with the service type to create optimized search queries. The service is automatically categorized to improve matching accuracy with Google's business classification system.

3. Data Collection via Bright Data

The workflow connects to Bright Data's Google Maps dataset, which provides structured access to Google My Business listings. It processes searches in controlled batches with built-in rate limiting and retry logic to ensure reliable data extraction without triggering anti-scraping measures.

4. Duplicate Removal & Data Cleaning

Results are analyzed for duplicates based on business name and phone number. The workflow maintains data integrity by removing redundant entries before final export.

5. Export to Google Sheets

Cleaned data is formatted and written to a Google Sheets spreadsheet, creating a ready-to-use lead list with all relevant business information organized in columns.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the downloaded JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Bright Data credentials with your API access
  3. Set up Google Sheets OAuth2 credentials in n8n
  4. Add your Anthropic API key for Claude AI functionality
  5. Test with a sample search (e.g., "Coffee Shop" in California)
  6. Activate the workflow and use the webhook or form trigger for submissions

Pro tip: Start with broad service categories and specific geographic areas to validate data quality before scaling to nationwide searches. Monitor the first few runs to ensure the AI-generated city lists align with your target markets.
