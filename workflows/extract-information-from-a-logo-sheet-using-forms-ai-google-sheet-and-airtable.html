What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually identifying and cataloging logos from product sheets. Marketing teams, brand managers, and retail analysts often receive spreadsheets or PDFs containing dozens of product logos that need to be identified, categorized, and recorded - a process that typically consumes hours of manual work.

The workflow automates this entire process: Users upload an image file through a form, AI services analyze the image to detect and identify logos, extracted data gets organized in Google Sheets for review, and finalized records sync to Airtable for long-term brand management. This eliminates manual data entry while ensuring consistent formatting across all logo records.

How It Works

1. Form Submission

Users upload logo sheet images through a simple web form. The form captures essential metadata like submission date, department, and any notes about the logo sheet contents.

2. AI-Powered Logo Recognition

The workflow sends the uploaded image to computer vision AI services that detect all visible logos. Advanced algorithms identify brand names even when logos appear in different orientations or partial views.

3. Data Structuring in Google Sheets

Identified logos and their positions get recorded in a structured Google Sheet. Each row represents one detected logo with columns for brand name, confidence score, coordinates, and extracted image crop.

4. Airtable Synchronization

Verified logo data syncs to Airtable where it joins your master brand database. Airtable's relational capabilities let you connect logos to products, campaigns, or partnership records.
