What This Workflow Does

This automation workflow solves the tedious problem of manually entering data from paper or digital receipts into your accounting or expense tracking systems. It uses optical character recognition (OCR) technology to automatically extract key information like vendor names, dates, amounts, and line items from receipt images.

The workflow can process receipts received via email, uploaded to cloud storage, or captured through mobile apps. After extraction, the data can be validated, categorized, and pushed to your accounting software, ERP system, or expense management platform, saving hours of manual work and reducing data entry errors.

How It Works

1. Receipt Capture

The workflow monitors designated sources (email, cloud storage, forms) for new receipt images. It supports common formats like JPG, PNG, and PDF.

2. Data Extraction

Using OCR and AI-powered parsing, the workflow identifies and extracts key fields including vendor, date, total amount, tax amounts, and payment method.

3. Data Validation

Extracted data goes through validation checks to ensure accuracy. The workflow can flag potential errors for human review when needed.

4. Data Export

Validated receipt data is formatted and exported to your accounting system, expense tracker, or database according to your specifications.
