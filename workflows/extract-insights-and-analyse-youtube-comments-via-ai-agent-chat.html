What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms raw YouTube comments into actionable content insights by leveraging AI analysis. It solves the problem of manually reviewing thousands of comments to understand viewer sentiment, frequently asked questions, and content preferences.

The workflow automatically extracts key themes, categorizes feedback, and identifies trending topics across your video comments. This enables data-driven content planning and helps creators align their videos with audience interests.

How It Works

1. Comment Collection

The workflow connects to the YouTube API to fetch all comments from specified videos or channels, including replies and timestamps.

2. AI Processing

Comments are analyzed by AI models to detect sentiment (positive/negative/neutral), extract key phrases, and categorize feedback into predefined topics.

3. Insight Generation

The system identifies frequently mentioned topics, common questions, and sentiment trends over time, compiling them into an easy-to-read report.

4. Notification & Storage

Final insights are saved to your preferred storage (Google Sheets, Notion, etc.) and key findings are sent via email or Slack notification.
