What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the tedious manual process of copying LinkedIn Sales Navigator search results into spreadsheets. Sales teams and recruiters often spend hours extracting contact information from multiple pages of search results, dealing with LinkedIn's pagination limitations.

The automation handles the entire process: it navigates through all pages of your Sales Navigator search results, extracts key contact details (name, title, company, location, profile URL), and organizes them neatly in Google Sheets. The auto-pagination feature ensures you capture every relevant result without manual clicking.

How It Works

1. Configure your LinkedIn search criteria

The workflow starts by accepting your Sales Navigator search parameters - these could be filters like industry, company size, job title, or geographic location.

2. Automatic pagination handling

The workflow automatically detects and clicks through all available pages of results, overcoming LinkedIn's default 10-page limitation that manual users face.

3. Data extraction and formatting

For each profile in the results, the workflow extracts standardized contact information while maintaining LinkedIn's terms of service compliance.

4. Google Sheets population

Extracted data is organized into columns in your specified Google Sheet, with automatic timestamping to track when leads were added.
