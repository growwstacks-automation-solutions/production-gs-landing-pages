Extract Marketing Testimonials from Feedback with AI

Automatically transform raw customer feedback into powerful, emotional testimonials using Google Gemini AI and Google Sheets. Save hours of manual review and editing.

What This Workflow Does

Customer feedback is gold for marketing, but extracting usable testimonials from lengthy survey responses, support tickets, or review forms is time-consuming and subjective. This automation solves that problem by using AI to instantly identify and refine the most impactful quotes from any feedback source.

The workflow listens for new customer feedback submissions (typically from Google Forms), analyzes the text with Google's Gemini AI to extract emotional, benefit-focused statements, saves the polished testimonials to a Google Sheet for easy access, and notifies your team via email. It turns hours of manual review into a fully automated process that delivers marketing-ready content.

Beyond just extraction, this system ensures consistency in testimonial quality and helps build a searchable database of social proof that can be used across websites, sales materials, case studies, and social media campaigns.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New Feedback Detection

The workflow activates whenever new feedback arrives in your connected Google Sheet. This could be from a customer satisfaction survey, post-purchase follow-up, or support ticket system. The trigger monitors a specific spreadsheet and watches for new rows being added.

2. AI Processing: Testimonial Extraction

Google Gemini AI analyzes the raw feedback text with a custom prompt designed to identify emotional language, specific praise, and quantifiable results. The AI filters out neutral or critical comments and extracts only the most compelling statements that highlight your product's benefits.

3. Data Enrichment: Context Addition

The extracted testimonial is enriched with metadata including the customer's name (if provided), submission date, product/service mentioned, and sentiment score. This additional context makes testimonials more valuable for segmented marketing campaigns.

4. Storage: Organized Repository

Polished testimonials are written back to a dedicated Google Sheet column, creating a growing database of marketing assets. The sheet can include columns for approval status, use case categories, and performance metrics for easy filtering and retrieval.

5. Notification: Team Alert

An automated email is sent to your marketing or sales team with the new testimonial, customer details, and suggested uses. This ensures valuable social proof is immediately available for campaigns rather than getting lost in spreadsheets.
