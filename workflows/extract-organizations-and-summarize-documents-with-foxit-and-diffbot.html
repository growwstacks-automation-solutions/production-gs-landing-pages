What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the tedious process of extracting key information from documents and generating concise summaries. It combines Foxit's powerful Extraction API with Diffbot's AI-powered summarization to transform unstructured documents into actionable insights.

The workflow solves the common business challenge of processing large volumes of documents manually. Legal teams, researchers, and business analysts often spend hours reviewing contracts, reports, and other documents to identify relevant organizations and extract key points. This automation reduces that time from hours to minutes.

How It Works

Step 1: Document Input

The workflow accepts documents through various input methods - email attachments, cloud storage, or direct uploads. Supported formats include PDF, Word, and text files.

Step 2: Foxit Entity Extraction

Foxit's API processes the document to identify and extract organizations, people, dates, and other key entities. This structured data is preserved for downstream use.

Step 3: Diffbot Summarization

The document content is sent to Diffbot's API which analyzes the text and generates a concise summary highlighting key points, conclusions, and relationships.

Step 4: Output Delivery

The extracted entities and summary are formatted and delivered to your preferred destination - CRM, database, email, or collaboration tools.
