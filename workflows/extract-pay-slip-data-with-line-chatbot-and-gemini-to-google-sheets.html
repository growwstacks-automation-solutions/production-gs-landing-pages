Extract Pay Slip Data with Line Chatbot & Gemini to Google Sheets

Automate pay slip processing using AI. Employees send slip images via Line Chatbot, Google Gemini extracts key data, and results auto-populate Google Sheets—no manual entry needed.

What This Workflow Does

Manual pay slip processing is time-consuming, error-prone, and delays payroll reconciliation. Employees email or message slips, administrators manually transcribe data, and mistakes inevitably occur during data entry. This creates compliance risks, frustrates employees with payment errors, and wastes valuable administrative time.

This automation solves that by creating a seamless, AI-powered pipeline. Employees simply take a photo of their pay slip and send it via Line Chatbot (or any messaging platform). The workflow automatically detects it's an image, sends it to Google Gemini AI for intelligent data extraction, parses key fields like employee name, payment date, amount, and tax details, then writes this structured data directly into a Google Sheets spreadsheet. The entire process happens in under 30 seconds with near-perfect accuracy.

How It Works

Step 1: Image Reception via Chatbot

The workflow listens for incoming messages on your Line Business account. When an employee sends a message, it immediately checks whether the content is text or an image. Text messages can be handled by an AI assistant for general queries, while images trigger the data extraction process.

Step 2: AI-Powered Data Extraction

The pay slip image is sent to Google Gemini 2.0 Flash model with a specific prompt: "Analyze this image and return a JSON response with these exact fields: Status, From, To, Date, Amount." The AI understands the document context, locates each field regardless of slip format or layout, and returns perfectly structured data—no coding or template matching required.

Example pay slip showing the key fields (Status, From, To, Date, Amount) that the AI model extracts automatically.

Step 3: Data Validation & Storage

The extracted JSON data is validated for completeness and format. The workflow then appends a new row to your designated Google Sheets spreadsheet, with columns pre-configured to match the extracted fields. Administrators get real-time visibility into all processed slips through the automatically updated spreadsheet.

Google Sheets automatically populated with extracted pay slip data, ready for review and analysis.

Step 4: Confirmation & Error Handling

Once data is successfully saved, the workflow sends a confirmation message back through Line Chatbot to the employee. If extraction fails or returns incomplete data, the system flags the item for manual review and notifies administrators—ensuring no slips fall through the cracks.

Pro tip: You can easily adapt this workflow for invoices, receipts, or any semi-structured document by simply modifying the AI prompt and Google Sheets column headers. The same automation structure works across document types.

What You'll Need

  1. Line Business Account: A free Line Business account to create your chatbot endpoint.
  2. Google AI Studio API Key: A free API key from Google AI Studio to access the Gemini 2.0 Flash model.
  3. Google Sheets Spreadsheet: A Google Sheets file with columns for: Status, From, To, Date, Amount (or your custom fields).
  4. n8n Instance: A self-hosted n8n installation or n8n.cloud account to run the workflow.
  5. Employee Communication: Brief instructions for employees on how to submit pay slips via the chatbot.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Line Webhook: In the Line node, add your Channel Access Token and Channel Secret from your Line Business account.
  3. Set Up Google Gemini: In the Google Gemini node, add your API key from Google AI Studio and verify the extraction prompt matches your needs.
  4. Connect Google Sheets: In the Google Sheets node, authenticate with your Google account and specify the spreadsheet ID and sheet name.
  5. Test with a sample pay slip: Send a test image through your Line chatbot and verify data appears correctly in Google Sheets.
  6. Deploy and communicate: Activate the workflow and share the Line chatbot QR code/link with employees.
