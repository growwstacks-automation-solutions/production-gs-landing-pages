What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious task of manually recording product details from competitor websites or marketplaces. Retailers, price comparison services, and market researchers often need to track dozens of product attributes across multiple sources. Traditional methods involve error-prone manual entry or expensive scraping solutions that break when websites change.

The workflow uses Dumpling AI's advanced computer vision to extract structured data from screenshots, then organizes it in Google Sheets for analysis. It handles variations in product layouts and can process multiple screenshots in batches. You'll get accurate product catalogs without writing code or maintaining web scrapers.

How It Works

1. Capture product screenshots

Take screenshots of product pages or listings from any website or app. The AI works best with clear images of product cards that show pricing and key details.

2. Upload to Dumpling AI

The workflow automatically sends screenshots to Dumpling's API which identifies and extracts product fields using computer vision models trained on ecommerce data.

3. Validate extracted data

Before saving to Sheets, the workflow checks for missing fields or low-confidence extractions. You can set validation rules for required fields like price or SKU.

4. Organize in Google Sheets

Extracted data populates predefined columns in your spreadsheet. The workflow can create new sheets for different product categories or add timestamped rows to existing datasets.
