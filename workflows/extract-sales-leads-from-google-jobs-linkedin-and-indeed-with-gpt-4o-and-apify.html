What This Workflow Does

Manual sales prospecting is time-consuming and often misses critical buying signals. This automation solves that by continuously monitoring job postings from Google Jobs, LinkedIn, and Indeed for your target companies. When a company hires for specific roles, it signals budget allocation, new initiatives, and pain points—perfect timing for sales outreach.

The workflow scrapes job data daily, filters for your keywords, uses GPT-4o to analyze each opportunity, and delivers AI-generated insights directly to your sales team. It identifies not just that a company is hiring, but why they're hiring, what problems they're trying to solve, and how your solution can help—complete with urgency scoring and draft outreach emails.

How It Works

1. Multi-Source Job Scraping

The workflow runs daily using Apify actors to scrape job postings from Google Jobs, LinkedIn, and Indeed simultaneously. It targets your predefined list of companies and captures details like job title, description, location, and posting date.

2. Data Normalization & Filtering

Job data from different sources gets standardized into a consistent format. The system then filters postings based on your target keywords (like "data engineer," "cybersecurity," or "HR software") to identify relevant opportunities.

3. AI-Powered Analysis with GPT-4o

Each qualified job posting gets analyzed by GPT-4o to extract: inferred pain points from the hiring need, strategic sales angles, urgency score (1-10), and personalized cold email drafts. The AI understands context beyond keywords.

4. Real-Time Alerts & Weekly Reports

Immediate Slack notifications alert your sales channel with AI insights. Every Monday, a comprehensive Google Sheets report summarizes hiring trends, opportunity volume, and AI-generated strategic recommendations for the week ahead.

Quick Setup Guide

Import this template into your n8n instance and follow these steps:

  1. Create a Google Sheet with four tabs: Target Companies, Raw Jobs, Qualified Leads, and Weekly Reports
  2. Connect your Apify credentials and configure the Google Jobs, LinkedIn, and Indeed scrapers
  3. Add your OpenAI API key and customize the AI analysis prompts for your industry
  4. Set up Slack webhook (optional) for real-time alerts
  5. Configure the schedule trigger to run daily at your preferred time
  6. Test with 2-3 target companies before scaling to your full list

Pro tip: Start with 10-15 high-priority target companies and 5-7 keyword variations. Monitor the AI's analysis quality for a week, refine your prompts, then expand. This ensures you're capturing relevant signals without noise.
