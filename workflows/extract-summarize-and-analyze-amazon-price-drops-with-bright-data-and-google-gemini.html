What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of monitoring Amazon price drops, extracting key data points, and analyzing them with Google Gemini's AI capabilities. It solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking competitor pricing while providing deeper insights than basic price tracking tools.

The system uses Bright Data's web scraping technology to reliably extract product prices, then applies natural language processing to analyze customer reviews during price changes. This gives ecommerce businesses a competitive edge by combining raw price data with sentiment analysis and trend predictions.

The automated workflow extracts prices, summarizes changes, and performs sentiment analysis

How It Works

1. Price Data Extraction

The workflow begins by using Bright Data to scrape Amazon product pages at scheduled intervals. It extracts current prices, historical price data, and availability status while handling Amazon's anti-scraping measures automatically.

2. Change Detection

Each new price check compares against previous data points to identify significant drops. The system calculates percentage changes and flags products that meet your defined threshold for notification.

3. AI Analysis

Google Gemini processes the raw data to generate insights like price trend predictions, competitor reaction analysis, and optimal repricing recommendations. It also analyzes customer review sentiment during price changes.

Sample output showing price trends and AI-generated insights

4. Notification & Reporting

The workflow delivers analyzed data through your preferred channels - email, Slack, or integrated directly into business intelligence tools. Reports include executive summaries alongside detailed metrics.

Pro tip: Combine this with inventory data to automate reorder decisions when prices drop below your target thresholds.
