Extract URL from an Email Address

Automatically parse and extract domain names from email addresses to enrich contact data, segment leads, and power smarter business workflows.

What This Workflow Does

Every email address contains valuable business intelligence hidden after the "@" symbol—the company domain. Manually extracting this information from hundreds or thousands of contacts is tedious, error-prone, and doesn't scale. This automation solves that problem by programmatically parsing email addresses to isolate and extract the domain portion, transforming raw contact data into actionable business insights.

The workflow takes any list of email addresses as input, processes them through intelligent parsing logic, and outputs clean domain data ready for integration with your CRM, marketing platform, or analytics dashboard. This enables businesses to automatically identify which companies are engaging with them, segment leads by industry, personalize communications based on organization type, and maintain cleaner contact databases without manual data entry.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Email Data

The workflow accepts email addresses from various sources—CSV imports, form submissions, CRM exports, or live API connections. It validates each email format and prepares the data for processing, handling edge cases like multiple @ symbols or malformed addresses gracefully.

Step 2: Domain Extraction Logic

Using n8n's function nodes and string operations, the workflow isolates everything after the "@" symbol in each email address. It then cleans the output by removing subdomains when appropriate, standardizing formatting, and filtering out personal email providers to focus on business domains.

Step 3: Data Enrichment & Validation

Optional enrichment steps can verify domain validity through DNS lookups, append company information from business databases, categorize domains by industry, or flag personal vs. corporate email addresses for different processing paths.

Step 4: Output & Integration

The cleaned domain data is structured into your preferred format and sent to destination systems—updating CRM contact records, populating marketing segmentation lists, feeding analytics platforms, or triggering follow-up workflows based on company identification.

Pro tip: Combine this domain extraction with company enrichment APIs to automatically append firmographic data like industry, employee count, and location—creating fully enriched lead profiles in seconds.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above—this gives you a JSON file ready for n8n import.
  2. Import into n8n by navigating to your n8n instance, clicking "Workflows" → "Import from file" and selecting the downloaded JSON.
  3. Configure your data source by updating the first node to point to your email list—this could be a CSV file path, Google Sheets connection, or webhook endpoint.
  4. Test with sample data by executing the workflow with a few test email addresses to verify the domain extraction works correctly.
  5. Connect your destination by updating the final node to send results to your CRM, database, or preferred output location.
  6. Activate the workflow and set it to trigger on schedule, webhook, or manual execution based on your use case.

Implementation note: Start with a small batch of test emails before processing your entire contact database. This lets you verify formatting and catch any edge cases specific to your data.
