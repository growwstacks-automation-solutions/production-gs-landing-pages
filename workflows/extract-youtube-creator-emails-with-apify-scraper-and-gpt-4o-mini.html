What This Workflow Does

Finding contact information for YouTube creators is time-consuming when done manually. This automation solves that problem by systematically extracting and verifying creator emails from YouTube channels at scale. It's designed for businesses running outreach campaigns, sponsorship programs, or partnership initiatives.

The workflow first uses Apify actors to scrape publicly available information from YouTube channels. Then GPT-4o Mini analyzes the scraped data to identify valid email addresses, filtering out personal emails and focusing only on professional contacts listed for business inquiries.

How It Works

Step 1: Channel Identification

The workflow accepts a list of YouTube channel URLs or search terms. Apify scrapers then visit each channel to collect available contact information from About pages, video descriptions, and linked websites.

Step 2: Data Processing

Raw scraped data is cleaned and formatted. The system removes duplicates, standardizes information, and prepares it for AI analysis while preserving source attribution for compliance.

Step 3: Email Extraction

GPT-4o Mini reviews the processed data to identify valid email addresses. The AI is trained to recognize business contact patterns and ignore personal or outdated emails, significantly improving data quality.

Step 4: Verification & Export

Extracted emails undergo syntax verification before being compiled into a spreadsheet or CRM-ready format. The system can optionally check domain validity and recent activity for higher deliverability.
