What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of maintaining consistent, timely responses to Facebook comments by automating the entire moderation process with AI. Manual comment management is notoriously time-consuming, often leading to delayed responses, inconsistent messaging, and missed engagement opportunities.

The system intelligently analyzes each comment using Gemini AI to determine sentiment and intent, crafts appropriate responses in your brand voice, documents interactions in Notion for future reference, and can even escalate complex issues to human team members. This creates a seamless loop where customer interactions are handled promptly while building a valuable knowledge base.

How It Works

1. New comment detection

The workflow monitors your Facebook page for new comments using n8n's Facebook trigger. Each new comment is immediately processed through the pipeline with metadata including post context, author details, and timing.

2. AI sentiment analysis

Gemini AI evaluates the comment's tone (positive, negative, neutral), identifies key topics, and assesses urgency. The system can detect nuanced language like sarcasm or rhetorical questions that simple keyword filters would miss.

3. Response generation

Based on predefined rules and your brand guidelines, the AI crafts a personalized response. For common questions, it can pull answers from your Notion knowledge base. For complex issues, it can tag appropriate team members.

4. Notion documentation

Every interaction is logged in Notion with the original comment, AI analysis, response given, and any follow-up actions needed. This creates a searchable customer service history and provides data for performance analysis.

5. Response posting

The approved response is posted back to Facebook, maintaining natural engagement while ensuring brand consistency. The system can be configured to require human approval for certain response types if desired.
