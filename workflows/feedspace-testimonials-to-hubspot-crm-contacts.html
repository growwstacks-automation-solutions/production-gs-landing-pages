Feedspace Testimonials to HubSpot CRM Contacts

Automatically sync customer testimonials from Feedspace to HubSpot contact records

What This Workflow Does

Manually copying customer testimonials from Feedspace to HubSpot CRM is a tedious, error-prone process that wastes valuable time. This n8n workflow automates the entire testimonial collection and syncing process, ensuring all customer feedback gets properly recorded in your CRM contact records.

The automation captures new testimonials as they're submitted to Feedspace, maps the data to the correct HubSpot contact fields, and adds them as notes or custom properties. This creates a centralized repository of social proof that your sales and marketing teams can leverage throughout the customer journey.

How It Works

1. Testimonial Trigger

The workflow monitors your Feedspace account for new testimonial submissions using webhooks or scheduled checks. Each new testimonial triggers the automation sequence.

2. Contact Matching

The system matches the testimonial to existing HubSpot contacts using email addresses, company names, or other identifiers. If no match exists, it can optionally create new contact records.

3. Data Transformation

Testimonial content gets formatted and enriched with metadata before syncing. This includes extracting star ratings, video URLs, or specific feedback snippets based on your configuration.

4. HubSpot Integration

The workflow updates the matched HubSpot contact with the testimonial data, adding it as a note, custom property, or activity record based on your preferred implementation.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to only sync testimonials meeting specific criteria (like minimum star ratings) to maintain quality standards in your CRM.
