What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually analyzing and categorizing keyword lists for SEO and content marketing. Marketing teams often collect hundreds or thousands of keywords in spreadsheets, but actually making sense of them requires hours of tedious manual review.

The workflow automatically fetches keywords from your Google Sheet, sends them to an AI classification service, and returns organized results with proper categorization. It can identify search intent (informational vs. commercial), product categories, geographic relevance, or any custom classification system you define.

The workflow showing keyword extraction from Google Sheets and AI classification process

How It Works

1. Fetch keywords from Google Sheets

The workflow connects to your specified Google Sheet and extracts the keyword list from your designated columns. It handles authentication and can process multiple sheets or tabs within your document.

2. Send keywords for AI analysis

Each keyword gets sent to an AI classification service (like OpenAI or a custom model) with your predefined categorization rules. The AI examines semantic meaning, word patterns, and context to determine the most appropriate category.

3. Process and validate results

The workflow checks the AI's classification confidence scores and can flag uncertain results for human review. It formats the output into a structured dataset ready for use in your marketing systems.

4. Return classified keywords

The final step delivers the categorized keywords back to your preferred destination - either updating your original Google Sheet, sending to a database, or pushing to other marketing tools.

Pro tip: Start with a small sample of manually classified keywords to train your AI model before processing large lists. This improves accuracy significantly.
