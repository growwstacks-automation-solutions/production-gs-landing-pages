What This Workflow Does

This automation solution eliminates the tedious manual process of copying ETF metrics from JustETF into Excel spreadsheets. Investment professionals and individual investors waste hours each week manually transferring performance data, expense ratios, and portfolio compositions between platforms.

The workflow connects directly to JustETF's data feeds, extracts the latest metrics for your selected ETFs, and formats them perfectly in Excel. With one click, you can refresh all your comparison tables with current market data, ensuring your investment decisions are based on real-time information rather than stale snapshots.

How It Works

Step 1: Configure your ETF watchlist

The workflow begins by reading your predefined list of ETF tickers or ISINs from a configuration file. You can maintain this list in a simple text file or directly in the workflow settings.

Step 2: Fetch live data from JustETF

Using n8n's HTTP request capabilities, the workflow queries JustETF's API endpoints to retrieve comprehensive metrics for each ETF on your watchlist. This includes performance history, costs, holdings breakdown, and risk metrics.

Step 3: Transform and clean the data

The raw API responses are processed to extract only the relevant metrics, convert values to consistent formats, and handle any missing data points gracefully. This ensures your Excel reports maintain consistent structure even if source data varies.

Step 4: Populate your Excel template

The processed data is inserted into your predefined Excel template at designated locations. The workflow maintains all your existing formulas, charts, and conditional formatting while updating only the underlying data values.
