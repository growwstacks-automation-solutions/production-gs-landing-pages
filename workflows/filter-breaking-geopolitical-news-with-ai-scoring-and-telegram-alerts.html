How It Works

1. News Source Aggregation

The system pulls from configured RSS feeds, API news sources and web scrapers, collecting articles in real-time across your regions of interest. Multiple verification steps ensure source reliability.

2. AI Content Analysis

Each article undergoes NLP processing to extract entities (countries, organizations, people), sentiment analysis, and urgency scoring. The system compares content against your predefined watchlist of keywords and risk factors.

3. Priority Scoring

A weighted scoring algorithm evaluates articles based on your configured priorities - whether that's specific regions, economic impacts, or security concerns. Only items crossing your threshold proceed to alerts.

4. Alert Delivery

Approved alerts format into concise Telegram messages with key details, source links, and severity indicators. The system can also trigger secondary actions like email digests or CRM updates.

Pro tip: Start with broader monitoring parameters, then refine based on which alerts prove most valuable. The system learns from your team's engagement patterns to improve relevance over time.
