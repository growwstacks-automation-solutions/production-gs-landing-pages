What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for businesses dependent on online reputation: how to systematically increase positive Google reviews while preventing negative ratings from damaging your public profile. Traditional review requests often result in mixed feedback posted directly to Google, where one-star ratings can linger for years.

The workflow creates a smart filtering system that intercepts customer feedback before it reaches Google. Happy customers (4-5 star ratings) are seamlessly directed to post their reviews publicly, boosting your star rating and visibility. Customers with concerns (1-3 stars) are guided to a private feedback form, allowing you to address issues offline and turn negative experiences into recovery opportunities.

By incorporating incentives and automated follow-ups, this system dramatically increases participation rates compared to manual requests. What typically requires hours of staff time each week becomes a fully automated process that works 24/7, consistently building your online reputation while protecting it from damaging public feedback.

How It Works

1. Customer Initiation via Telegram

Customers scan a QR code displayed in your business or click a link shared via email/invoice. This opens a conversation with your Telegram bot. The bot immediately checks if this is a new customer and sends a small thank-you incentive (discount code, digital gift, or special offer) to encourage participation.

2. Smart Review Routing

After a brief delay, the bot sends a link to your custom review filtering webpage. This page presents a star rating system. If the customer selects 4 or 5 stars, they're automatically redirected to your Google Business Profile review page. If they select 1-3 stars, they see a private feedback form that captures their concerns without posting publicly.

3. Automated Tracking & Follow-up

All interactions are logged in Google Sheets, including customer ID, first name, status (link sent, clicked, reviewed), and any private feedback. If a customer doesn't click the review link within 23 hours, the system automatically sends a polite reminder via Telegram, significantly increasing conversion rates.

4. Centralized Management Dashboard

The Google Sheet becomes your central reputation management dashboard. You can monitor review funnel performance, track customer sentiment trends, and create alerts for negative feedback that requires immediate attention. This data can be connected to other systems like your CRM or customer service platform.

What You'll Need

  1. A Telegram Bot: Created via BotFather (free, takes 2 minutes)
  2. Google Sheets API Access: Standard Google Cloud credentials
  3. Custom Review Filtering Webpage: A simple page that routes users based on star ratings (basic HTML/JavaScript)
  4. n8n Instance: Cloud or self-hosted version
  5. QR Code Generator: For creating physical/digital codes linking to your bot
  6. Google Business Profile: Already claimed and verified

Pro tip: Start with a small incentive (5-10% discount or free add-on) for first-time reviewers. This can increase participation rates by 300-500% compared to simple requests. Track which incentives work best in your Google Sheet and optimize over time.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Create your Telegram bot via @BotFather and save the API token.

2. Set up Google Sheets API credentials in your Google Cloud Console.

3. Create your review filtering webpage or use the open-source template linked in the workflow documentation.

4. Download and import this JSON template into your n8n instance.

5. Configure all nodes with your credentials, Sheet ID, and webpage URL.

6. Generate QR codes linking to your bot and display them at customer touchpoints.

7. Activate the workflow and test with your team before going live.

8. Monitor the Google Sheet for the first week to ensure everything flows correctly.
