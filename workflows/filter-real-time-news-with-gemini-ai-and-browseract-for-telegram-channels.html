What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of information overload by using Gemini AI to filter real-time news based on your specific keywords, then automatically distributing relevant updates to designated Telegram channels. It eliminates hours of manual news monitoring while ensuring your team only sees content that matters to your business.

The system continuously scans multiple news sources, applies AI-powered relevance scoring, and routes high-value updates to appropriate channels. This creates a personalized news feed that helps teams stay informed without wasting time on irrelevant content.

How It Works

1. News Source Integration

The workflow connects to RSS feeds, news APIs, or monitored webpages where your industry updates appear. Multiple sources can be combined into a single monitoring stream.

2. AI Content Analysis

Gemini AI evaluates each news item against your configured keywords, considering semantic meaning rather than just exact matches. It scores relevance and flags items needing human review when confidence is borderline.

3. Automated Distribution

BrowserAct formats approved updates with source attribution and sends them to pre-configured Telegram channels. The template includes options for adding custom headers, hashtags, and priority indicators.

Pro tip: Combine 2-3 negative keywords (like "opinion" or "rumor") with your main terms to improve filtering accuracy by 30-40%.
