What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching and qualifying LinkedIn leads. Sales teams often waste hours browsing profiles trying to identify good-fit companies, only to have inconsistent qualification standards across team members.

The workflow automatically searches LinkedIn for companies matching your ideal customer profile, analyzes each company using GPT-4 AI to assign a lead score based on relevance and potential, then exports the qualified leads to your Google Sheets CRM with all key information. This creates a consistent, scalable lead generation process that surfaces the highest potential opportunities first.

How It Works

1. LinkedIn Company Search

The workflow starts by searching LinkedIn for companies based on your specified criteria like industry, location, company size, or keywords. It can search by first-degree connections, groups, or public company listings.

2. AI-Powered Lead Scoring

Each company profile is analyzed by GPT-4 which evaluates multiple signals including technology stack, hiring patterns, funding status, and content engagement. The AI assigns a score from 0-100 based on how closely the company matches your ideal customer profile.

3. Data Enrichment

The system extracts key information from LinkedIn profiles including company description, employee count, recent posts, and notable employees. This data is structured for easy review in your CRM.

4. Google Sheets Export

Qualified leads are automatically added to your Google Sheets CRM with all relevant information. The workflow can create new rows or update existing records, with configurable thresholds for which scores trigger export.
