What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge in recruitment and sales prospecting: accurately identifying and verifying LinkedIn profiles. Manual profile searches consume valuable time and often lead to mismatches or incomplete information.

The workflow automatically matches Airtop contact records with corresponding LinkedIn profiles, then verifies key details like employment history, education, and skills. This ensures your team works with accurate, up-to-date professional information while eliminating hours of manual research.

How It Works

1. Contact Data Extraction

The workflow pulls contact details from your Airtop database including name, company, and email address. These become search parameters for LinkedIn profile matching.

2. Profile Search and Matching

Using LinkedIn's search API, the system finds potential profile matches and scores them based on name similarity, company alignment, and other professional indicators.

3. Verification and Validation

The workflow cross-references profile details with your original Airtop data, flagging any discrepancies in employment dates, job titles, or education for manual review.
