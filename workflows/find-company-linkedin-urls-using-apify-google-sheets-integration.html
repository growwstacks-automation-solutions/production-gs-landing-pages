Automatically populate Google Sheets with LinkedIn company URLs for recruiting and sales prospecting

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of finding LinkedIn company profiles. Recruiters and sales professionals often spend hours searching LinkedIn for company pages to build prospect lists. The workflow automatically matches company names from your Google Sheet to their LinkedIn URLs using Apify's powerful web scraping capabilities.

The solution eliminates copy-paste errors and ensures consistent data formatting. It handles hundreds or thousands of company lookups in minutes rather than hours. The results populate directly in your spreadsheet, ready for CRM imports or outreach campaigns.

Example output showing automatically populated LinkedIn company URLs

How It Works

Step 1: Prepare your company list

The workflow reads company names from a designated Google Sheet column. For best results, include location details when company names are common (e.g., "Acme Corp - Chicago").

Step 2: Apify searches LinkedIn

Each company name gets sent to Apify's LinkedIn Company Scraper. The scraper handles authentication, rate limits, and CAPTCHAs while searching LinkedIn's directory.

Step 3: Results validation

The system verifies matches using multiple data points. Uncertain matches get flagged for review while clear matches proceed automatically.

Step 4: Google Sheets update

Verified LinkedIn URLs write back to your spreadsheet in a designated column. The workflow logs all actions for audit purposes.

Pro tip: Add a "Last Updated" column to track when URLs were found. LinkedIn profiles occasionally change, so periodic refreshes ensure data accuracy.
