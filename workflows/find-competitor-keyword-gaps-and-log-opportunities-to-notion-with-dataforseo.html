What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manual competitor keyword research by systematically identifying content opportunities you're missing. It connects DataForSEO's powerful keyword data with Notion's flexible workspace to create a living document of strategic SEO opportunities.

The system automatically pulls your competitors' ranking keywords, compares them against your own rankings, and logs the most promising gaps directly into a Notion database. This creates an always-updated pipeline of content ideas that can drive organic traffic growth without manual research.

The automated workflow connecting DataForSEO keyword data with Notion content planning

How It Works

1. Competitor keyword data collection

The workflow starts by pulling your competitors' ranking keywords from DataForSEO's API. It gathers comprehensive data including search volume, keyword difficulty, and current ranking positions.

2. Gap analysis processing

The system compares the competitor keywords against your own rankings to identify gaps - keywords they rank for that you don't. It filters these by business relevance, search volume, and difficulty score.

3. Opportunity scoring and prioritization

Each identified keyword gap receives an opportunity score based on potential traffic value and relative difficulty. The workflow applies your custom weighting rules to surface the highest-value opportunities first.

4. Notion database population

Finally, the system creates or updates records in your Notion content calendar or keyword database. Each entry includes all relevant metrics and can trigger notifications for your content team.

Pro tip: Set this workflow to run monthly to catch emerging keyword trends while they're still fresh opportunities.
