What This Workflow Does

Link building outreach often fails because you can't find the right contact email. Many small blog owners use simple Gmail addresses like contact@domain.com or name@domain.com, but manually searching for these is time-consuming and error-prone.

This n8n workflow automates the entire email discovery process. It takes your list of target domains, generates potential email address patterns, and verifies which ones are valid using the EmailListVerify API. You get a clean, verified list of deliverable addresses ready for your outreach campaign.

The system handles everything from pattern generation to validation, eliminating hours of manual work and ensuring your outreach reaches real inboxes instead of bouncing.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Domain Lists

You provide a Google Sheet with your target domains. The workflow reads this list and prepares it for processing. You can include hundreds or thousands of domains—the system scales automatically.

Step 2: Generate Email Candidates

Based on common email patterns (firstname, contact, info, admin, etc.), the workflow creates potential address combinations for each domain. You can customize patterns to match your target audience's likely naming conventions.

Step 3: Verify with EmailListVerify

Each generated email address is sent to the EmailListVerify API for validation. The service checks deliverability, mailbox existence, and spam trap risks, returning a confidence score for each address.

Step 4: Output Verified List

Valid addresses are written back to your Google Sheet with their verification status. Invalid addresses are flagged, so you only pursue contacts with confirmed deliverability.
