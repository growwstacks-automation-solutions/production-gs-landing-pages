Find jobs on Indeed via Telegram using BrowserAct and Gemini

Transform your Telegram bot into an intelligent job hunting assistant with this BrowserAct and Gemini powered workflow.

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your Telegram messaging app into a powerful job search assistant. Instead of manually browsing Indeed.com for hours, you can simply send your job search criteria to a Telegram bot. The workflow then uses BrowserAct to scrape Indeed for matching jobs, applies Gemini AI to filter and prioritize the results, and delivers personalized job recommendations directly to your Telegram inbox.

The system solves three major pain points: First, it eliminates the time-consuming process of daily manual job searches. Second, it applies intelligent filtering to surface only the most relevant opportunities. Third, it delivers results directly to your preferred messaging platform, making job hunting more convenient than ever.

How It Works

1. Telegram Command Processing

When you send a job search request via Telegram (like "/findjobs Marketing Manager in New York"), the workflow captures your criteria and prepares it for processing. The system understands natural language queries, so you don't need to learn complex search syntax.

2. Indeed.com Scraping with BrowserAct

BrowserAct automatically navigates to Indeed.com, enters your search parameters, and extracts job listings including titles, companies, locations, salaries (when available), and application links. It handles all the browser interactions just like a human would.

3. AI-Powered Filtering with Gemini

Gemini AI analyzes the scraped job listings to prioritize the most relevant opportunities based on your historical preferences and the specific context of your current search. It can filter out irrelevant matches and highlight positions that closely align with your career goals.

4. Results Delivery via Telegram

The workflow formats the filtered job listings into easy-to-read messages with direct application links and sends them back to your Telegram account. You can configure the frequency of updates and the number of results per message.
