What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually checking multiple flight booking websites to find the best deals. By leveraging web scraping technology through Bright Data, it systematically gathers pricing information from various sources, compares them in real-time, and can notify you when prices meet your criteria.

Business travelers, travel agencies, and budget-conscious individuals all benefit from eliminating hours of manual price checking while ensuring they never miss a price drop. The workflow can be customized for specific routes, date ranges, and price thresholds that matter to you.

How It Works

1. Route Configuration

The workflow starts by accepting your desired travel routes, dates, and budget parameters. These can be provided manually or pulled from a spreadsheet/database.

2. Multi-Site Scraping

Using Bright Data's web scraping capabilities, the system simultaneously checks multiple booking platforms like Expedia, Kayak, and airline sites to gather current pricing data.

3. Price Comparison

Collected prices are normalized and compared against your specified thresholds. The workflow identifies the best available deal across all monitored platforms.

4. Notification Triggers

When prices fall below your target or when a new best deal is found, the workflow can trigger email/SMS alerts or update a tracking spreadsheet automatically.
