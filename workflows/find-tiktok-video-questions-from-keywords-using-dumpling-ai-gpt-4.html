What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for content creators and marketers: understanding what your audience actually wants to know. Instead of guessing topics or manually scrolling through thousands of TikTok comments, this workflow systematically uncovers the real questions people are asking.

It transforms unstructured social media data into actionable content insights. You provide a keyword (like "digital marketing" or "fitness tips"), and the automation finds relevant TikTok creators, extracts comments from their recent videos, and uses AI to identify the most frequently asked questions. The result is a clean list of audience pain points you can directly address in your content strategy.

This approach moves you from reactive content creation to data-driven content planning. You'll discover questions you never thought to answer, identify emerging trends before they peak, and create content that genuinely resonates because it's based on actual audience demand.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence that mimics what a skilled researcher would do, but at scale and speed impossible manually.

1. Keyword Input & Profile Discovery

The process starts with a simple form where you enter your target keyword. The workflow then uses the Dumpling AI integration to search TikTok for creators who regularly post content related to that topic. It identifies profiles with engaged audiences in your niche.

2. Video & Comment Extraction

For each discovered creator, the workflow fetches their most recent videos (typically the last 10-20). It then extracts all comments from these videos, gathering raw audience feedback and questions. A Python script cleans this data, removing emojis, special characters, and irrelevant noise.

3. AI-Powered Question Analysis

The cleaned comment data is sent to GPT-4 with a specific prompt designed to identify questions. The AI analyzes hundreds or thousands of comments to find recurring questions, categorize them by theme, and rank them by frequency. It distinguishes between genuine questions and casual remarks.

4. Structured Output & Storage

The final questions, along with their source videos and context, are saved to a Data Table in n8n. This creates a searchable database of audience insights that you can reference whenever planning content. The output includes question frequency, related video links, and sometimes sentiment analysis.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get this automation running in your n8n environment:

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n instance, go to Workflows → Import from File and select the downloaded template.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up credentials for Dumpling AI and OpenAI in n8n's credentials section. The workflow nodes will reference these.
  3. Test with a Keyword: Open the Form Trigger node and enter a test keyword (like "content creation"). Execute the workflow once to ensure all connections work.
  4. Review Data Table: Check the Data Table node to see the structure of the output. You may want to customize the columns based on what insights are most valuable to you.
  5. Schedule Regular Runs: Set the workflow to run weekly or monthly to continuously gather fresh audience questions as trends evolve.

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 broad keywords in your niche, then use the discovered questions to identify more specific long-tail keywords for deeper research in subsequent runs.
