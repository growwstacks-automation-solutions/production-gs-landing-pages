What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of gathering company news from FinnHub's financial API and delivering it directly to your Slack channels. It solves the problem of manually tracking news for multiple stocks by automatically fetching updates and distributing them to your team in real-time.

Financial analysts, investment teams, and business intelligence professionals spend hours each week monitoring news sources. This workflow eliminates that manual work by creating a daily digest of relevant company updates based on your watchlist of stock tickers, delivered right where your team collaborates.

How It Works

1. Configure Your Stock Watchlist

The workflow starts with your predefined list of stock tickers (like AAPL for Apple or MSFT for Microsoft) that you want to monitor. These can be easily updated as your investment focus changes.

2. Fetch News from FinnHub API

Using FinnHub's free API tier, the workflow automatically retrieves the latest news articles mentioning your tracked companies. The API provides headlines, summaries, source URLs, and publication dates.

3. Filter and Format Results

The workflow processes the raw API data to remove duplicates, format readable messages, and prioritize recent updates. You can customize the filtering logic based on your specific needs.

4. Post to Slack Channels

Finally, the workflow sends the compiled news digest to your designated Slack channel with proper formatting, source links, and attribution. The messages include actionable links for your team to explore further.
