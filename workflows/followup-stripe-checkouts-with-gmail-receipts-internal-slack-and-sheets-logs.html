What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual, time-consuming process of following up after customer payments. When a Stripe checkout is completed, the workflow automatically sends a professional receipt email through Gmail, logs the transaction details in Google Sheets for record-keeping, and notifies your internal team via Slack - all without any human intervention.

Businesses using this template eliminate the risk of missed receipts, reduce administrative overhead, and create a centralized system for tracking payments. The automation ensures customers receive immediate confirmation while freeing your team to focus on higher-value activities.

How It Works

1. Trigger on Stripe payment events

The workflow activates whenever a successful payment occurs in your Stripe account. It captures all relevant transaction details including customer information, payment amount, and product details.

2. Generate and send Gmail receipt

Using the transaction data, the system composes a professional receipt email with all payment details. The template can be customized with your branding and includes dynamic fields for personalized customer information.

3. Log transaction to Google Sheets

Key payment details are automatically recorded in a designated Google Sheet, creating a searchable transaction history. This serves as both an audit trail and a simplified reporting tool for your finance team.

4. Notify team via Slack

An internal Slack channel receives a notification with payment highlights, allowing your team to stay informed about new revenue without constantly checking Stripe. High-value transactions can trigger special alerts.
