What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves two common business challenges: tracking food delivery orders and managing meal expenses. It automatically retrieves order confirmation emails from food delivery platforms, extracts key details like order contents, delivery time, and cost, then organizes this information for easy tracking and reporting.

For businesses that provide meals for employees or track food expenses, manually recording each order is time-consuming and prone to errors. This workflow eliminates that manual work by automatically capturing all relevant data and making it available in your preferred notification system and expense tracking platform.

The workflow parses food delivery confirmation emails to extract order details

How It Works

1. Email Capture

The workflow monitors your email inbox for messages from food delivery services. When an order confirmation arrives, it triggers the automation process.

2. Data Extraction

Key details like restaurant name, order items, total cost, and delivery time are extracted from the email using text parsing techniques.

Order details are formatted for notifications and expense tracking

3. Notification Delivery

The workflow sends the order details to your team via Slack, Microsoft Teams, or another messaging platform, keeping everyone informed about incoming deliveries.

4. Expense Recording

Finally, the order information is added to your accounting software or expense tracking system, categorized appropriately for easy reporting and reimbursement.

Order costs are automatically recorded in your expense management system
