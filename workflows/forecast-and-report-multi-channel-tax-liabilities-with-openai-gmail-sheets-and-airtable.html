What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the complex challenge of managing tax compliance across multiple sales channels and jurisdictions. For businesses selling through e-commerce platforms, marketplaces, and physical locations, manually calculating tax obligations is time-consuming and error-prone.

The automation aggregates sales data from various sources, applies the correct tax rates based on customer location and product type, then generates comprehensive reports and forecasts. It leverages OpenAI to analyze trends and predict future liabilities, while maintaining all records in Airtable for audit purposes.

How It Works

Step 1: Data Aggregation

The workflow pulls sales data from connected platforms (Shopify, Amazon, Square, etc.), email receipts in Gmail, and spreadsheet records in Google Sheets. It normalizes this data into a standardized format for processing.

Step 2: Tax Calculation

Using jurisdiction databases and product tax codes, the system applies appropriate sales tax rates to each transaction. It handles special cases like digital goods taxes, food exemptions, and cross-border rules.

Step 3: AI Forecasting

OpenAI analyzes historical sales patterns, seasonal trends, and upcoming tax law changes to generate 30/60/90-day liability forecasts. These predictions help with cash flow planning.

Step 4: Reporting

The workflow generates formatted reports showing liabilities by jurisdiction, prepares payment reminders, and maintains an audit trail in Airtable. Reports can be automatically emailed to accountants or tax teams.
