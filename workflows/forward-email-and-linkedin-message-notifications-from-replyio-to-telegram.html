What This Workflow Does

Sales teams using Reply.io for outreach face a critical challenge: missing prospect responses buried in email or LinkedIn inboxes. Every missed message represents a lost opportunity and wasted marketing spend. This automation solves that by instantly forwarding all Reply.io notifications—whether from email or LinkedIn messages—directly to your team's Telegram channel.

The workflow creates a real-time alert system that ensures your sales team never misses a prospect response. When someone replies to your outreach campaign, the system captures the notification via webhook, extracts key details (sender, message content, response channel), and delivers it immediately to Telegram where your entire team can see it and respond quickly.

Beyond simple forwarding, this setup creates a centralized communication hub for your sales team. Instead of monitoring multiple inboxes, your team gets instant mobile notifications with all the context needed to continue the conversation, dramatically reducing response times and increasing conversion rates.

How It Works

This automation creates a seamless bridge between your outreach platform and team communication tool, ensuring no prospect response goes unnoticed.

1. Webhook Configuration & Subscription Setup

The workflow begins by establishing a webhook endpoint in n8n that listens for incoming notifications from Reply.io. Utility nodes automatically create the necessary webhook subscriptions in your Reply.io account for both email and LinkedIn message events. This eliminates manual API configuration and ensures reliable delivery of all response notifications.

2. Notification Capture & Parsing

When a prospect responds via email or LinkedIn, Reply.io sends a webhook payload containing the message details. The workflow receives this data, extracts the essential information including sender details, message content, timestamp, and which channel the response came through. This parsing ensures only relevant, actionable data gets forwarded.

3. Data Transformation & Enrichment

The raw notification data is then transformed into a clean, readable format optimized for Telegram. The workflow can add contextual information, format the message with clear labels, and even enrich the data with additional prospect information from your CRM if configured. This step turns raw webhook data into immediately actionable alerts.

4. Telegram Delivery & Team Notification

Finally, the formatted alert is sent to your designated Telegram channel or group. The message includes all necessary context for your sales team to respond appropriately. Optional features include tagging specific team members, adding priority indicators for hot leads, or creating follow-up tasks directly from the notification.

Pro tip: Create separate Telegram channels for different sales teams or regions. You can modify the workflow to route notifications based on the prospect's location or lead score, ensuring the right team member gets each alert.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this notification system in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the workflow template into your n8n instance
  2. Create a Telegram bot by messaging @BotFather on Telegram and save your bot token
  3. Obtain your Reply.io API key from your account settings (Documentation: https://apidocs.reply.io/)
  4. Configure the webhook nodes with your production URL from n8n
  5. Enter credentials in the HTTP Request nodes (Reply.io API key) and Telegram node (bot token)
  6. Test the workflow by sending a test message through Reply.io and verifying it appears in Telegram
  7. Activate the workflow and configure team access to your Telegram notification channel

Important: Use the production webhook URL from your n8n instance when configuring the utility nodes that create Reply.io subscriptions. Test webhooks won't receive actual notifications from Reply.io.
