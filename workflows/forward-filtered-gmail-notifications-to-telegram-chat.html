What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of missing critical emails in crowded inboxes by instantly forwarding only the most important messages to your team's Telegram chat. It monitors your Gmail account for specific keywords (like "Urgent", "Server Down", or custom phrases you define) and automatically sends those messages to a designated Telegram channel or group.

The workflow acts as a smart filter that ensures time-sensitive communications get immediate attention while less critical emails remain in your inbox for later processing. This is particularly valuable for teams handling customer support, IT operations, or any scenario where delayed responses to important messages could cause significant problems.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Gmail account for new messages matching your specified criteria. You can configure it to watch specific labels, folders, or your entire inbox.

2. Keyword Filtering

Each incoming email is scanned for your predefined keywords in the subject line or body content. The template includes examples like "Urgent", "Help needed", and "Priority", but you can customize these to match your business needs.

3. Telegram Notification

When a match is found, the workflow extracts key details (sender, subject, and relevant message content) and sends a formatted notification to your chosen Telegram chat. The notification includes quick-action buttons for common responses when applicable.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for support teams, IT departments, operations managers, and any professionals who need to ensure rapid response to critical communications. Remote teams particularly benefit since Telegram notifications are more visible on mobile devices than email alerts.

Businesses with on-call rotations, time-sensitive service level agreements, or distributed teams will find this workflow dramatically improves their response times while reducing the stress of constantly monitoring email.
