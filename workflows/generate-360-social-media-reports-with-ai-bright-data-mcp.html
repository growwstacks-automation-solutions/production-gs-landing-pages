What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manually compiling social media intelligence across multiple platforms. By combining Bright Data's Market Research Cloud Platform (MCP) with OpenAI's analysis capabilities, it delivers comprehensive 360° reports on individuals' or brands' social media presence.

The system extracts structured data from LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram profiles, then applies AI to analyze posting patterns, engagement metrics, content themes, and professional signals. What traditionally took hours of manual research becomes an automated report delivered to your inbox or CRM.

How It Works

1. Data Collection Phase

Bright Data MCP gathers profile information, post history, and engagement metrics from specified social platforms using compliant data collection methods. This includes both public profile data and post-level interactions.

2. AI Analysis Layer

OpenAI processes the collected data to identify key patterns: content themes over time, sentiment trends, engagement velocity, and professional reputation signals. The AI contextualizes raw metrics into business-relevant insights.

3. Report Generation

The system compiles findings into a structured PDF or Notion report, highlighting key persona attributes, influence metrics, and actionable recommendations for engagement or recruitment.
