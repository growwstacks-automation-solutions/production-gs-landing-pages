What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of creating video content manually. Instead of recording, editing, and uploading videos—which can take hours—this workflow transforms any text script into a professional AI avatar video using HeyGen and automatically publishes it to your YouTube channel.

Marketing teams, content creators, and educators can now produce consistent video content at scale. Whether you need daily news updates, product tutorials, training materials, or social media content, this automation handles the entire production pipeline from text input to published video without manual intervention.

The system maintains brand consistency by using your chosen avatar and voice, ensures timely publishing through automation, and dramatically reduces video production costs—from hundreds of dollars per video to just pennies.

How It Works

1. Text Input & Processing

The workflow begins when you send a text script and title via webhook or schedule. The system cleans and formats the text, removing unnecessary formatting and preparing it for video generation.

2. AI Video Generation with HeyGen

The formatted text is sent to HeyGen's API with your selected avatar ID and voice ID. HeyGen processes the request and begins generating a video with a digital avatar speaking your script with natural-sounding synthetic speech.

3. Processing & Quality Check

The workflow intelligently waits for video processing completion, periodically checking HeyGen's status. Once the video is ready and meets quality standards, it proceeds to the next step.

4. Automatic YouTube Upload

The completed video file is downloaded and uploaded to your YouTube channel with the specified title, description, and category. The system handles all YouTube API authentication and optimization settings.

5. Notification & Logging

Finally, the system sends confirmation notifications and logs the successful publication, providing you with the YouTube URL and performance metrics.
