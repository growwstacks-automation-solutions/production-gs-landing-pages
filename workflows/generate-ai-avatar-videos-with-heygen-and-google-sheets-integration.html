What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually creating personalized AI avatar videos at scale. Instead of individually generating each video in HeyGen, this workflow connects your Google Sheets data directly to HeyGen's API, automatically producing customized videos based on your spreadsheet content.

The system pulls text scripts from your Google Sheet, generates the corresponding AI avatar videos in HeyGen, then stores the video links back in your spreadsheet - creating a complete automated video production pipeline. This eliminates hours of manual work while ensuring consistent quality across all your video outputs.

How It Works

1. Spreadsheet Data Retrieval

The workflow starts by accessing your Google Sheet and extracting the text content you want converted into AI videos. This could be personalized messages, product descriptions, or any other script content.

2. HeyGen API Integration

Each text entry is sent to HeyGen's API where it's processed into an AI avatar video using your selected avatar, voice, and style settings. The workflow handles all API authentication and formatting requirements automatically.

<3>3. Video Link Storage

Once HeyGen completes video generation, the workflow captures the video URL and writes it back to your Google Sheet alongside the original text, creating a complete record of all generated content.
