What This Workflow Does

Manual inventory management is time-consuming, error-prone, and reactive. Businesses often face stockouts of critical items or tie up cash in excess inventory because they lack intelligent forecasting. This workflow solves that by transforming raw Airtable stock data into AI-optimized reorder recommendations.

It automates the entire inventory intelligence pipeline: fetching live stock levels, validating data integrity, calculating dynamic reorder points and safety stock using strict AI formulas, generating actionable summaries, and distributing insights via email and Slack. Invalid records are automatically logged for audit, ensuring data hygiene.

The result is a proactive inventory system that tells you what to order, when, and in what quantity—before you run out—saving hours of manual work each week and preventing lost sales from stockouts.

How It Works

1. Data Fetching & Validation

The workflow starts by retrieving all SKU records from your Airtable inventory base. It checks each record for a valid ID and structure. Valid items proceed to AI analysis; invalid ones are logged to Google Sheets for review, maintaining clean data.

2. AI-Powered Stock Analysis

GPT-4o analyzes each SKU using configured formulas: Suggested Reorder Point = Reorder Level × 1.2; Suggested Safety Stock = Reorder Level × 0.5. It then categorizes stock status as Critical, Needs Reorder, or OK based on current quantity versus these calculated thresholds.

3. Recommendation Consolidation

All AI-generated recommendations are merged into a unified JSON dataset. This ensures a complete view of all inventory items with consistent formatting, ready for reporting.

4. Multi-Channel Reporting

The system generates two tailored summaries: a detailed email for management with comprehensive insights and alerts, and a concise, emoji-friendly Slack message for the operations team. Both are sent automatically, ensuring the right people get the right information in their preferred channel.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtable access token – To connect to your inventory base.
  2. Azure OpenAI GPT-4o credentials – For the AI analysis engine.
  3. Google Sheets OAuth – To log invalid records for auditing.
  4. Slack API credentials – To send alerts to your team channel.
  5. Gmail OAuth – To email detailed summaries to management.

Quick Setup Guide

Import and configure this workflow in under 15 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Airtable, Azure OpenAI, Google Sheets, Slack, and Gmail in the n8n credentials panel.
  3. Map your Airtable fields in the first node to match your inventory base structure (ID, ItemName, SKU, QuantityInStock, ReorderLevel).
  4. Update recipient details in the Email and Slack nodes with your manager's email and team channel.
  5. Test with a manual trigger to run the workflow and verify recommendations are generated and delivered correctly.

Pro tip: Start with a subset of your most critical SKUs to validate the AI recommendations. Once confident, expand to your full inventory.
