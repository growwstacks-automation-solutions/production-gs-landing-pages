Automate Market Research with AI: NewsAPI to Notion & Slack

Free n8n template to monitor competitors, analyze industry news with AI, and deliver actionable insights to your team automatically.

What This Workflow Does

Manual market research is slow, inconsistent, and often outdated by the time it reaches decision-makers. This automation solves that by creating a continuous intelligence pipeline that monitors your industry landscape 24/7.

The workflow pulls real-time news from NewsAPI, scrapes competitor websites for updates, consolidates everything, and sends it to an AI (like GPT-4) for analysis. The AI generates structured reports highlighting trends, SWOT insights, and recommended actions, then publishes the full analysis to a Notion database and sends executive summaries to your Slack channel. If anything fails, error notifications keep your team informed.

This transforms what was a weekly 5–10 hour manual research task into a fully automated process that runs daily, weekly, or on-demand, ensuring your team always has the latest competitive intelligence without lifting a finger.

How It Works

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger & Data Collection

A schedule trigger (configurable for daily, weekly, or custom intervals) initiates the workflow. It simultaneously fetches industry news via the NewsAPI using your configured keywords and scrapes competitor websites for recent updates or announcements.

Step 2: Data Consolidation & AI Analysis

All collected articles, snippets, and webpage content are merged into a single dataset. This consolidated information is sent to an AI model (like OpenAI's GPT-4) with a detailed prompt instructing it to analyze the data, identify key trends, perform a SWOT analysis, and extract actionable insights.

Step 3: Report Generation & Distribution

The AI returns a comprehensive markdown report. The workflow then creates a new page in your specified Notion database, populating it with the full analysis for long-term reference and searchability. Simultaneously, a concise executive summary is posted to a designated Slack channel to alert your team immediately.

Step 4: Error Handling & Notifications

The entire process is wrapped in robust error handling. If any step fails—like an API timeout or a website being unreachable—the workflow catches the error and sends a clear notification to a Slack alerts channel, ensuring you never miss a failure silently.

It's particularly valuable for SaaS companies, consulting firms, agencies, and any B2B business where staying ahead of market shifts provides a competitive advantage. Teams using Notion for documentation and Slack for communication will see immediate integration benefits.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. OpenAI API key with access to GPT-4 or similar models for analysis.
  3. NewsAPI account (free tier available) to fetch news articles.
  4. Notion account and a database ready to receive report pages.
  5. Slack workspace with permissions to create incoming webhooks for your chosen channels.
  6. A list of competitor URLs and industry keywords for monitoring.

Pro tip: Start with a broad set of keywords and competitor URLs, then refine based on the AI's output quality. You'll quickly learn which sources yield the most valuable insights for your specific niche.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials: In n8n, set up credentials for OpenAI, NewsAPI, Notion, and Slack.
  3. Set your parameters: In the "Research Configuration" node, input your industry keywords, competitor URLs, and any source filters.
  4. Connect your destinations: Update the Notion node with your database ID and the Slack node with your channel webhook URL.
  5. Test and schedule: Run the workflow once manually to verify everything works, then set the Schedule Trigger node to your desired frequency (e.g., every Monday at 9 AM).
