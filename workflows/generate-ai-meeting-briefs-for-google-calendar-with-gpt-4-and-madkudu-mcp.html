What This Workflow Does

Walking into external meetings unprepared is a missed opportunity. This automation solves that by transforming your Google Calendar into an intelligent meeting assistant. It automatically researches every external attendee before your meeting, providing you with a concise, AI-generated brief that includes company context, relevant news, and strategic talking points.

The workflow checks your calendar hourly, filters out internal meetings, enriches external attendees using MadKudu's company intelligence, then uses GPT-4 to synthesize the data into actionable meeting preparation notes. These briefs are automatically added to your calendar as private events, giving you instant context without switching between tabs or doing manual research.

For customer-facing teams, this means consistently better prepared meetings, stronger relationships, and more productive conversations. You'll never again wonder "Who is this company?" or "What should I ask?" five minutes before a call.

How It Works

The automation follows a logical sequence to ensure you get relevant, timely intelligence before every external meeting.

1. Calendar Monitoring & Filtering

A Schedule Trigger runs hourly to check your Google Calendar for upcoming meetings. It identifies events happening within your configured window (typically 1-24 hours ahead) and extracts attendee email addresses. The workflow then filters out any meetings where all attendees are from your company domain, focusing only on external engagements.

2. Attendee Enrichment

For each external attendee, the workflow queries MadKudu's API to retrieve company intelligence—firmographics, technographics, funding status, growth signals, and ideal customer profile scoring. This data provides the raw material for understanding who you're meeting with at an organizational level.

3. AI Synthesis & Brief Generation

The enriched data is passed to GPT-4 with a carefully crafted prompt that asks for: key company insights, potential pain points based on their industry/tech stack, relevant conversation starters, and strategic questions to ask. The AI synthesizes this into a concise, readable brief formatted for quick scanning.

4. Private Calendar Integration

The generated brief is created as a new private calendar event in your Google Calendar, titled "Prep: [Meeting Name]" and scheduled for 15 minutes before the actual meeting. This ensures the information is exactly where you need it, when you need it, without cluttering your main calendar view.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in under 15 minutes with these steps:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the download button above.
  2. Connect your Google Calendar account via OAuth in the first node.
  3. Set your MadKudu API key as a workflow variable for secure access.
  4. Configure your OpenAI credentials in the AI Agent node.
  5. Add your company domain (e.g., @yourcompany.com) to filter internal meetings.
  6. Test with a single upcoming meeting by temporarily adjusting the schedule trigger.
  7. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically on your preferred schedule.
