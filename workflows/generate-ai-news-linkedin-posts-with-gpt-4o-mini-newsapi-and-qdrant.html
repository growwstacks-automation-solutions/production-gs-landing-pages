Automated LinkedIn content generator that fetches trending AI news, enhances with contextual knowledge, and creates engaging posts

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining an active, relevant LinkedIn presence in the fast-moving AI industry. Manual content creation is time-consuming, and keeping up with daily AI developments requires constant attention. The workflow automatically generates high-quality LinkedIn posts by combining real-time news with your stored knowledge.

The system fetches trending AI news from NewsAPI, enriches it with contextual information from your Qdrant vector database, then uses GPT-4o-mini to craft engaging LinkedIn posts. Each post includes proper formatting, relevant hashtags, and a natural conversational tone - saving you hours per week while keeping your content fresh and authoritative.

How It Works

1. News Gathering Phase

The workflow starts by querying NewsAPI for the latest AI industry news. It filters results based on your specified keywords, publication credibility, and engagement metrics. Only high-quality, relevant articles proceed to the next stage.

2. Knowledge Enhancement

Selected articles are processed through your Qdrant vector store to find related information. This might include your previous posts on similar topics, company whitepapers, or industry reports. The system creates a rich context package for the AI to reference.

3. Content Generation

GPT-4o-mini receives the news article and contextual data, then generates multiple post variations. The AI follows your brand guidelines for tone, length, and structure while ensuring each post offers unique insights rather than just summarizing the news.

4. Quality Control

Before publishing, the workflow includes validation steps to check for accuracy, appropriate tone, and compliance with LinkedIn's content policies. You can review suggested posts or set the system to auto-publish based on confidence scores.
