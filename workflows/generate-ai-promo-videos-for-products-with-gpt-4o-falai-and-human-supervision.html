What This Workflow Does

This automation streamlines the creation of promotional video content by combining AI generation with human oversight. It solves the challenge of producing engaging marketing videos at scale while maintaining quality control and brand alignment.

The workflow generates video scripts using GPT-4o, creates visual assets with Fal.ai's video generation API, then routes outputs for human review and approval before final delivery. This hybrid approach ensures AI efficiency while preserving creative control.

The workflow combines AI generation with human review steps

How It Works

1. Product information collection

The workflow begins by gathering product details from your ecommerce platform, CMS, or spreadsheet. This includes product names, descriptions, key features, and target audience information.

2. AI script generation

GPT-4o analyzes the product data and generates multiple video script variations optimized for different social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts).

Example AI-generated script variations for review

3. Human script selection

The generated scripts are sent to a human reviewer via gotoHuman interface. The marketer selects the best option or requests revisions before proceeding.

4. Video asset generation

Approved scripts are sent to Fal.ai's API which generates video clips complete with voiceovers, captions, and background visuals based on your brand guidelines.

Human reviewer evaluating AI-generated video clips

5. Final approval and distribution

Completed videos are routed for final approval before being automatically published to your social channels or saved to your content library.

  1. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)
  2. GPT-4o API access

  4. gotoHuman account for human review steps
  5. Social media or CMS connection for distribution

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your GPT-4o API credentials in the AI script generation node
  3. Connect your Fal.ai API key in the video generation step
  4. Set up your gotoHuman webhook for human review steps
  5. Connect your final distribution channels (social media, CMS, etc.)
  6. Test with sample product data and refine prompts as needed
