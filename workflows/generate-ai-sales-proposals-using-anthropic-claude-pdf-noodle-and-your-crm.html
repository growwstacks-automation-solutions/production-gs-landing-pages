What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of creating customized sales proposals by combining AI content generation with your existing CRM data. The workflow automatically generates professional, tailored proposals for each prospect by pulling relevant information from your CRM and transforming it into polished documents using Anthropic Claude's AI and PDF Noodle for formatting.

Sales teams waste hours manually assembling proposals from templates, copying CRM data, and formatting documents. This workflow eliminates that repetitive work while ensuring consistent branding and messaging across all client communications. The AI-generated content adapts to each prospect's specific needs and deal stage.

How It Works

1. CRM Trigger

The workflow initiates when a new deal reaches the proposal stage in your CRM, either through webhook or scheduled polling. All relevant deal and contact information is extracted.

2. Data Transformation

The raw CRM data is processed to extract key details needed for the proposal - company name, decision makers, pain points, proposed solutions, pricing tiers, and any custom fields.

3. AI Content Generation

Anthropic Claude generates the proposal content based on your predefined templates, injecting personalized elements from the CRM data while maintaining your brand voice and messaging.

4. PDF Assembly

PDF Noodle formats the AI-generated content into a professional PDF document with your company branding, including cover page, table of contents, pricing tables, and signature blocks.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for B2B sales teams, agencies, and professional services firms that regularly send customized proposals. It's particularly valuable for:
