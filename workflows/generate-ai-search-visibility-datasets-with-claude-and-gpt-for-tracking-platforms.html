What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves a critical modern marketing challenge: tracking your brand's visibility in AI search engines. As more people use ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity instead of Google, traditional SEO tracking becomes insufficient. This automation generates a comprehensive dataset of 50+ natural language search prompts specifically tailored to your company.

The system combines AI-powered company research with structured prompt generation to create monitoring queries that reflect how real users interact with AI assistants. It analyzes your website to understand your business, then produces categorized prompts covering comparison questions, feature inquiries, problem-solving queries, and industry-specific searches.

The output is a ready-to-use CSV file formatted for import into AI search analytics platforms like ALLMO.ai, giving you immediate visibility into how your brand appears across different AI models and queries.

How It Works

1. Company Research Phase

The workflow starts by collecting your company name and website URL through a simple form. GPT-5 Mini with web search capability then analyzes your site to extract key information including buyer personas, core features, value propositions, and industry positioning.

2. AI-Powered Prompt Generation

The extracted company data feeds into Claude Sonnet 4.5, which acts as a sophisticated prompt writing engine. Claude generates natural, conversational search queries that potential customers would actually ask AI assistants about your products or services.

3. Translation & Structuring

English prompts are automatically translated into German (or other languages you configure), doubling your tracking coverage. The workflow then merges both language sets with metadata tags for easy categorization and filtering.

4. Export & Implementation

After 2-5 minutes of processing, you receive a clean CSV file containing 50+ categorized prompts ready for upload to your preferred AI search tracking platform. Each entry includes company name, industry, query category, language, and target AI model.
