What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the massive time drain of creating short-form health video content manually. Instead of spending hours researching trends, writing scripts, editing footage, and uploading videos, this n8n workflow turns the entire process into a scheduled, hands-off system.

It pulls viral health topics from your data sources, uses Google Gemini to analyze and write engaging scripts, generates professional videos using Google's Veo 3 AI, and publishes them to your social platforms—all while logging everything in a Google Sheet for tracking. You get a scalable content engine that produces consistent, high-quality videos while you sleep.

How It Works

The workflow operates on a trigger-based schedule, processing batches of content ideas through a series of connected steps.

1. Content Collection & Management

The automation starts by reading a Google Sheet where you store potential viral health topics. Each row represents a video idea with status columns (pending, processing, completed). Only "pending" items are processed, ensuring organized batch operations.

2. AI Analysis & Scriptwriting

Each topic is sent to Google Gemini (or your chosen LLM) with tailored prompts for the health niche. The AI analyzes the idea, suggests a compelling hook, and generates a complete short-form video script optimized for platforms like TikTok, Reels, or Shorts.

3. AI Video Generation with Veo 3

The finalized script is passed to the Veo 3 API, which creates a complete video with visuals, text overlays, and voiceover. The workflow handles the API call, monitors the rendering job status, and retrieves the final video file once processing is complete.

4. Publishing & Tracking

The generated video is then published to your configured social platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.) via their respective APIs. Finally, the Google Sheet is updated with the video URL, publish status, and timestamp, closing the loop and providing a clear audit trail.

What You'll Need

  1. A running n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. Google Sheets API credentials to read/write to your content spreadsheet.
  3. Google Gemini API key for AI script generation.
  4. Veo 3 API access for video generation (or an alternative AI video service).
  5. API credentials for your target publishing platforms (e.g., YouTube, TikTok).
  6. A Google Sheet structured with columns for Topic, Status, Script, Video URL, and Publish Result.

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of 5-10 test ideas in your sheet before scaling. This lets you verify video quality, script tone, and publishing success without consuming large API quotas.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n dashboard, import this workflow.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up the required credentials in n8n for Google Sheets, Gemini AI, Veo 3, and your social platforms.
  3. Prepare Your Sheet: Duplicate the provided Google Sheet template. Add your list of health video ideas and mark them as "pending".
  4. Map Your Data: In the workflow, update the Google Sheet node to point to your spreadsheet ID and sheet name.
  5. Test & Activate: Run the workflow once in manual mode to test one idea end-to-end. Adjust AI prompts if needed. Then activate the schedule trigger for automated runs.
