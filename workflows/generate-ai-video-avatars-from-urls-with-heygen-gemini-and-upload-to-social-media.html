What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating consistent, engaging video content for social media. Instead of manually filming and editing, this workflow automatically transforms any URL—whether a news article, blog post, or product page—into a professional vertical short video featuring your AI avatar explaining the content.

The system fetches the webpage content, uses Google Gemini to generate a concise 30–45 second script with platform-optimized descriptions, captures dynamic background visuals from the page itself, composes and renders the video using HeyGen's AI avatar technology, and publishes the finished video to your social media channels through Upload-Post. It includes an optional human review step for quality control before publishing.

Pro tip: You can run this entire workflow using free trials of all the integrated services—no credit card required for HeyGen, ScreenshotOne, Gemini, or Upload-Post APIs.

How It Works

Step 1: URL Input & Content Extraction

The workflow begins when you provide any webpage URL. It fetches the content, extracts the main text, and identifies key information and featured images that will form the basis of your video.

Step 2: AI Script Generation with Gemini

Google Gemini analyzes the extracted content and creates a tight, engaging script optimized for short-form video. It generates not just the avatar's dialogue but also platform-specific descriptions for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

Step 3: Visual Background Creation

ScreenshotOne captures dynamic visuals from the webpage—either as an animated scroll-through video or a static featured image—creating the 9:16 vertical background that will appear behind your AI avatar.

Step 4: HeyGen Video Composition & Rendering

The workflow sends the script and background to HeyGen, which composites your chosen AI avatar speaking the script with the background visuals. You can choose between free split-screen layouts or paid clean cut-out avatars.

Step 5: Review & Publishing

Once rendered, the video can be sent for human approval (optional) before being automatically uploaded to your connected social media accounts via Upload-Post, complete with the AI-generated titles and descriptions.

  3. ScreenshotOne API key for capturing webpage visuals
  4. Google Gemini API access (chat model)
  5. Upload-Post account connected to your social media platforms
  6. Basic understanding of API key management and n8n credential setup

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for HeyGen, ScreenshotOne, Gemini, and Upload-Post in n8n's credential management.
  3. Set your variables in the "Set Input Vars" node: your target URL, background type (video or photo), and whether to use background removal (paid feature).
  4. Test with a simple URL like a blog post or news article to verify all connections work properly.
  5. Configure publishing platforms in Upload-Post and enable the review step if you want manual approval before videos go live.
  6. Schedule or trigger the workflow manually whenever you have new content to convert to video.

Pro tip: Start with the free split-screen layout in HeyGen to test the workflow before investing in background removal features. The free option still produces professional-looking videos perfect for social media.
