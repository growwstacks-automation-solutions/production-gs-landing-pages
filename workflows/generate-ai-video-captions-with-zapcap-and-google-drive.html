What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of adding accurate captions to videos stored in Google Drive using ZapCap's AI technology. It eliminates the need for manual transcription, which can take hours for long-form content, and ensures your videos are accessible to all viewers including those who watch without sound.

The automation triggers whenever a new video is uploaded to a specific Google Drive folder. It then sends the video to ZapCap for AI-powered caption generation, and finally saves the captioned video back to your Drive. This creates a seamless, hands-off process for content creators, marketers, and educators who regularly produce video content.

How It Works

1. New video detection

The workflow monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new video uploads. When a video file (MP4, MOV, etc.) is detected, the workflow automatically triggers.

2. File processing

The system retrieves the video file from Google Drive and prepares it for captioning by checking file size, format compatibility, and other parameters to ensure successful processing.

3. AI caption generation

The video is sent to ZapCap's AI engine which analyzes the audio track, generates accurate captions with proper timing, and overlays them on the video in your preferred style (font, color, position).

4. Result delivery

The captioned video is automatically saved back to your Google Drive in a designated output folder, with the original file preserved. You'll receive a notification when processing is complete.
