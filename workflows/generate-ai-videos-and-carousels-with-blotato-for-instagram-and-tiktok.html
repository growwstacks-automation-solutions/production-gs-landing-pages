What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating engaging video content for Instagram and TikTok. Social media managers often spend hours scripting, filming, and editing videos - this workflow automates 90% of that process using Blotato's AI video generation capabilities.

The system transforms your content ideas, product details, or blog posts into professional-quality short-form videos automatically. It handles everything from generating scripts to adding visuals, animations, and captions - then publishes directly to your social channels or saves to your content calendar.

Blotato's AI agent creating video content from text prompts

How It Works

1. Content Input

The workflow begins by collecting your content sources - this could be RSS feeds from your blog, product database updates, or manual content prompts you provide.

2. AI Video Generation

Blotato's AI analyzes the content and generates multiple video variations complete with visuals, animations, captions, and background music tailored for each platform's requirements.

3. Quality Review

The system can automatically review videos for quality standards or send them for human approval before publishing, ensuring brand consistency.

Example of an AI-generated video thumbnail created by Blotato

4. Platform Optimization

Videos are automatically formatted for each platform's specifications - square for Instagram posts, vertical for Reels/TikTok, with appropriate lengths and captions.

5. Publishing

The final step publishes videos directly to your connected social accounts or adds them to your content calendar for scheduled posting.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to analyze your top-performing content and use those insights to guide the AI's video creation for better engagement.

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Blotato API credentials in the workflow settings

  4. Configure your content sources (RSS feeds, databases, etc.)
  5. Set up approval steps if needed (optional)
  6. Test with sample content and refine your video templates
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor initial results
