What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the creation of AI-powered videos using HeyGen's platform combined with voice cloning technology. It transforms text scripts into professional-quality videos featuring realistic AI avatars speaking with customized cloned voices.

The solution addresses the time-consuming and expensive process of traditional video production. Businesses can generate personalized video content at scale for marketing campaigns, training materials, customer communications, and more - all without cameras, studios, or professional voice actors.

How It Works

1. Script Input

The workflow accepts text input from various sources (CMS, Google Docs, or direct entry) and prepares it for video generation.

2. Voice Cloning

Using HeyGen's voice cloning API, the system processes the text with your customized voice profile for natural-sounding narration.

3. Avatar Selection

The workflow selects the appropriate AI avatar based on your predefined templates or dynamic criteria.

4. Video Generation

HeyGen's rendering engine combines the script, cloned voice, and selected avatar to produce the final video output.

5. Distribution

Completed videos can be automatically delivered to various platforms including email, social media, or your content management system.

Pro tip: For best results, provide high-quality voice samples (minimum 30 minutes of clean audio) when setting up your voice clone.
