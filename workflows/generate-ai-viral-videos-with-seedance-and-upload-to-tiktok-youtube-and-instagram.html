What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually creating and distributing viral video content across multiple platforms. Content creators often spend hours generating videos, editing them, and then uploading separately to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

The workflow automates the entire process: generating AI-powered videos using Seedance, optimizing them for virality, and simultaneously publishing to all major video platforms. This eliminates repetitive tasks and ensures consistent content distribution.

How It Works

1. Video Generation Trigger

The workflow starts either on a schedule (daily/weekly) or when triggered by new content ideas from your CMS or spreadsheet.

2. AI Video Creation

Seedance AI generates videos based on your parameters - length, style, music, and trending elements that maximize shareability.

3. Automated Optimization

The workflow automatically adds platform-specific optimizations like captions, hashtags, and thumbnails tailored for each destination.

4. Multi-Platform Distribution

Finished videos are simultaneously uploaded to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels with appropriate formatting for each.
