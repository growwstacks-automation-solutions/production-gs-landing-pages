How It Works

1. AI Video Generation

The workflow triggers VEO 3 to generate new videos based on your predefined templates, scripts, or prompts. The AI analyzes current trends to create optimized content likely to perform well.

2. Content Review (Optional)

For quality control, the workflow can be configured to send generated videos for human approval before publishing. This step ensures brand consistency while maintaining automation benefits.

3. TikTok Upload Automation

Approved videos are automatically uploaded to your TikTok account with optimized captions, hashtags, and scheduling. The workflow handles all API connections and formatting requirements.

4. Performance Tracking

After publication, the workflow can track engagement metrics and feed this data back to improve future video generation, creating a continuous improvement loop.

Pro tip: Combine this workflow with a content calendar to strategically plan your TikTok themes and posting schedule in advance.
