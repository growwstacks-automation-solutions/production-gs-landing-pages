What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of consistently creating and distributing engaging tech news content across social media platforms. Traditional video production requires significant time, resources, and technical skills - this workflow eliminates those barriers using AI.

The system automatically generates professional-quality video content featuring AI avatars from Heygen, then schedules and posts these videos to your social channels via Blotato. It transforms text-based tech news into visually appealing video format without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Tech news content aggregation

The workflow begins by collecting the latest tech news from your specified sources. This could be RSS feeds, Google News alerts, or custom APIs depending on your configuration.

2. Script generation

The system processes the raw news content into natural-sounding video scripts optimized for AI narration. This includes formatting, summarization, and adding appropriate transitions.

3. AI avatar video creation

Heygen's platform generates the video using your selected AI avatar, voice, and background settings. The system handles all API calls and file management automatically.

4. Social media distribution

Blotato receives the finished videos and posts them according to your predefined schedule and platform-specific optimization rules.

Pro tip: Configure different AI avatars and voices for different types of tech news to create variety in your content.
