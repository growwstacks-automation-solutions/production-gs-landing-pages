What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms Jotform order submissions into professional PDF invoices automatically. It eliminates manual invoice creation by generating formatted documents with your branding, itemized charges, and payment terms. The system then saves copies to Google Drive for record-keeping and emails them directly to clients.

By connecting your form submissions to document generation and delivery, this workflow creates a seamless billing process. It handles everything from data extraction to final delivery, reducing administrative work while ensuring consistency across all invoices.

How It Works

1. Jotform submission triggers the workflow

When a customer completes your order form in Jotform, the workflow automatically begins processing. It captures all submitted data including customer details, items purchased, quantities, and pricing.

2. Data transforms into invoice format

The system structures the raw form data into proper invoice formatting. It calculates totals, applies taxes if configured, and incorporates your company branding elements.

3. PDF generation creates professional document

Using the formatted data, the workflow generates a polished PDF invoice. This includes your logo, contact information, invoice numbering, and all required legal elements.

4. Google Drive backup

Each invoice saves to a designated Google Drive folder with proper naming conventions. This creates an organized digital paper trail accessible from anywhere.

5. Email delivery to client

The system emails the PDF invoice directly to your customer with a professional message. You can customize the email template to include payment instructions or special notes.

Pro tip: Configure your workflow to send invoice reminders automatically by adding a delay node that triggers follow-up emails for unpaid invoices.
