What This Workflow Does

This end-to-end automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating and publishing engaging anime content for TikTok. Instead of manually designing wallpapers, animating them, and scheduling posts, this workflow connects multiple AI services into a seamless pipeline.

It starts by collecting your anime theme and style preferences (via form or schedule), then uses Groq's LLM to craft detailed image prompts. These prompts go to Flux AI through Pollination AI to generate unique anime wallpapers. The static images are then transformed into animated videos using Fal AI's Minimax Hailuo model, and finally posted automatically to your TikTok account via the GetLate API.

The entire process—which would normally take hours of creative work—runs completely hands-off. You get consistent, high-quality anime content delivered to your TikTok feed without daily manual effort, perfect for building an audience around anime aesthetics.

How It Works

1. Trigger & Input Collection

The workflow begins with an n8n Form Trigger that captures your anime topic and desired visual style. You can also replace this with a Schedule Trigger to run automatically at set intervals, pulling themes from a database like Google Sheets or Airtable.

2. AI Prompt Enhancement

Your simple topic (e.g., "cyberpunk samurai") is sent to Groq's GPT-OSS model, which expands it into a detailed, stylized prompt optimized for Flux AI image generation. This ensures the AI produces consistent, high-quality anime artwork matching your vision.

3. Anime Wallpaper Generation

The enhanced prompt goes to Pollination AI's Flux model, which generates a unique, high-resolution anime wallpaper. The workflow handles image formatting, sizing, and quality optimization for social media standards.

4. Video Animation

The static wallpaper is sent to Fal AI's Minimax Hailuo 02 Fast model, which applies subtle animations—like parallax effects, flowing hair, or glowing elements—turning the image into an engaging video clip perfect for TikTok's short-form format.

5. TikTok Auto-Posting

The final animated video, along with your caption and hashtags, is automatically uploaded to TikTok via the GetLate API. The workflow manages authentication, video formatting, and post scheduling, delivering content directly to your audience.

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure credentials for Groq, Pollination AI, Fal AI, and GetLate in their respective nodes.
  3. Test the Form Trigger by submitting a sample anime topic and style through the generated webhook URL.
  4. Review the first output—check the generated image, animation quality, and TikTok post draft.

  6. Monitor and refine your prompts based on which anime styles perform best on TikTok.
