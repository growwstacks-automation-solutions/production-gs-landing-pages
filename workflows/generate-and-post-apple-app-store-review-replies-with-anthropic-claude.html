What This Workflow Does

This workflow automates the process of responding to Apple App Store reviews by combining AI-generated responses with human oversight. It eliminates the need for manual review monitoring while maintaining quality control through a human approval step.

The system fetches new reviews daily, generates appropriate responses using Anthropic Claude AI, stores them in Google Sheets for team review, then posts approved responses back to the App Store. This saves developers hours per week while ensuring high-quality, personalized replies to user feedback.

How It Works

1. Daily Review Collection

The workflow checks the App Store Connect API each morning for new reviews across all your apps, collecting both the review text and rating.

2. AI Response Generation

Anthropic Claude analyzes each review's sentiment and content to generate a personalized, brand-appropriate response draft.

3. Human Review Process

All AI-generated responses are compiled in a Google Sheet where your team can review, edit, or reject suggestions before posting.

4. Approved Response Posting

In the afternoon, the workflow posts all approved responses back to the App Store through the API, with logging for tracking.
