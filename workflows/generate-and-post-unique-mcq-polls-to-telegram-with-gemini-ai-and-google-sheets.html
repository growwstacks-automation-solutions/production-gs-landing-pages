What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of consistently creating fresh, engaging multiple-choice questions for educational Telegram channels or study groups. Manual question creation is time-consuming and limits how frequently you can engage your audience. The workflow combines Gemini AI's content generation with Telegram's interactive polls to deliver automated quiz content.

The system pulls topic parameters from Google Sheets, generates unique MCQs through Gemini AI, formats them for Telegram's poll feature, and posts them on schedule. This creates a hands-off content pipeline that maintains engagement while freeing up hours of manual work each week.

How It Works

1. Content configuration in Google Sheets

The workflow begins by reading your question parameters from a designated Google Sheet. You specify topics, difficulty levels, question formats, and any special instructions for the AI. This sheet serves as your control panel for content generation.

2. AI question generation

Gemini AI processes your parameters to create unique multiple-choice questions. The AI ensures questions are coherent, answers are logically distinct, and explanations are accurate. You can specify the number of questions per batch and include model answers.

3. Telegram poll creation

The formatted questions are sent to your Telegram channel as interactive polls. The workflow handles all API calls to Telegram, ensuring proper poll formatting with the correct answer marked. Participants can vote and see results in real-time.

  1. Download the JSON template and import into your n8n instance
