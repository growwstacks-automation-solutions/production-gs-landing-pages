What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of consistently creating engaging short-form video content at scale. It transforms the manual, time-consuming process of video production into an automated pipeline that generates high-quality content tailored to your audience's interests.

The workflow begins by scraping trending topics and content ideas using Apify, then uses Gemini to generate compelling scripts and captions. Sora-2 creates the actual video assets, which are automatically formatted for different social platforms before being scheduled and published.

How It Works

1. Content Discovery

The workflow starts by scraping trending topics and high-performing content from various sources using Apify. This ensures your videos are based on proven, engaging subject matter.

2. Script Generation

Gemini AI analyzes the scraped content and generates optimized video scripts tailored to your brand voice and target audience preferences.

3. Video Production

Sora-2 transforms the generated scripts into visually compelling short videos, complete with appropriate visuals, transitions, and captions.

4. Publishing Automation

The final videos are automatically formatted for each social platform's specifications and published according to your optimal posting schedule.

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 video variations per topic to test which approaches resonate best with your audience before scaling up production.
