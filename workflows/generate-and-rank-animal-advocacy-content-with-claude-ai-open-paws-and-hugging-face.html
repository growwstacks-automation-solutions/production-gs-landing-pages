What This Workflow Does

Creating persuasive content for animal advocacy campaigns is time-consuming and often relies on guesswork. This workflow solves that by automating the entire content creation and optimization process. It generates five distinct versions of your advocacy content—whether for social media, emails, or blog posts—then uses specialized AI models to score each variation based on predicted effectiveness.

The system combines Claude AI's natural writing capabilities with Open Paws models trained on successful advocacy campaigns and Hugging Face's evaluation frameworks. Instead of spending hours writing and testing different messages manually, you get a ranked list of content options with performance predictions, allowing you to deploy the most persuasive messaging with confidence.

How It Works

1. Content Specification & Input

You provide the topic, desired tone, content type, and key messaging points. The workflow accepts parameters like "campaign against factory farming," "empathetic but urgent tone," and "social media post format."

2. Multi-Version Generation

The system calls a research and content generation subworkflow that produces five fully written content variations. Each version approaches the topic from a different angle—emotional appeal, factual emphasis, call-to-action focus, etc.

3. AI Scoring & Evaluation

Each content variation passes through a scoring subworkflow using Open Paws AI models trained on real-world social media and email marketing data from advocacy campaigns. The models evaluate persuasion effectiveness, emotional impact, and alignment with advocacy goals.

4. Ranking & Output

The workflow aggregates all content with their performance scores, creating a ranked list from most to least effective. You receive the content along with specific scoring metrics and improvement suggestions for each variation.
