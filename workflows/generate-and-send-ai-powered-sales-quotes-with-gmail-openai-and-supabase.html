What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your sales quoting process by combining AI intelligence with your existing email workflow. It automatically monitors your Gmail inbox for quote requests, analyzes the content using OpenAI's natural language processing, and generates professional, data-driven quotes in minutes rather than hours.

The system taps into your Supabase database of historical pricing data to ensure quotes are competitively priced while maintaining healthy margins. It handles the entire process from request detection to sending polished PDF quotes - all without manual intervention for standard requests.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring and Classification

The workflow continuously scans your designated Gmail folder or labels for incoming messages. Using AI classification, it identifies genuine quote requests versus general inquiries, filtering out spam and irrelevant messages.

2. Information Extraction

OpenAI's NLP models extract key details from the email: requested products/services, quantities, deadlines, and any special requirements. This data gets structured for the quoting engine.

3. Intelligent Pricing Calculation

The system queries your Supabase database for similar historical quotes, analyzing success rates and profitability. It applies your business rules (minimum margins, volume discounts) to calculate the optimal price.

4. Quote Generation and Delivery

AI composes a professional response with pricing breakdowns, terms, and next steps. The quote gets formatted as a PDF attachment and sent through your Gmail account with tracking enabled.

Pro tip: Configure the system to flag quotes exceeding 15% of your average deal size for manual review, balancing automation with oversight for high-value opportunities.
