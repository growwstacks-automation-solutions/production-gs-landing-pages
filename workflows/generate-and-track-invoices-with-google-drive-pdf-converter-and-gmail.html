What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the tedious manual process of creating, sending, and tracking invoices into a seamless digital workflow. By connecting Google Drive, a PDF converter, and Gmail, it eliminates hours of repetitive administrative work while reducing human error in financial documents.

The system automatically generates professional PDF invoices from templates, calculates taxes and totals, stores documents securely in organized Google Drive folders, and sends them to clients via Gmail with tracking capabilities. What typically takes 30+ minutes per invoice becomes a 30-second automated process.

How It Works

1. Template setup

The workflow begins with your Google Docs invoice template containing merge fields for client details, services, and pricing. This template maintains your branding while allowing dynamic content insertion.

2. Data integration

Client and transaction data flows into the system from your CRM, timesheets, or order management system. The workflow validates all information before processing.

3. PDF generation

The system merges data with your template, performs all calculations (taxes, discounts, totals), and converts the document to a professionally formatted PDF with your branding intact.

4. Secure storage

Each invoice saves to Google Drive with standardized naming (ClientName-Invoice#-Date.pdf) in year/month folders for easy retrieval and audit purposes.

5. Email delivery

The system sends the PDF invoice via Gmail with your standard messaging template, CC's relevant parties, and tracks delivery status.

6. Payment tracking

The workflow updates invoice status when payments are received and can trigger follow-up reminders for overdue invoices.

Pro tip: Add a payment link to your invoice template to enable one-click payments and further accelerate your cash flow.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Workspace account (for Drive and Gmail access)
  2. PDF converter service (like PDFShift or DocRaptor)
  3. Existing invoice template in Google Docs format
  4. Source system for client/project data (CRM, spreadsheet, etc.)
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the workflow JSON into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Drive and Gmail accounts in n8n
  3. Upload your invoice template to Google Drive and note the file ID
  4. Configure your PDF converter API credentials
  5. Map your data source fields to the template merge fields
  6. Test with sample data and refine formatting as needed
  7. Schedule the workflow to run at your billing frequency
