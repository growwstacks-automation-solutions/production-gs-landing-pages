This n8n workflow automates the entire production pipeline for creating viral "animal versus animal" battle videos. The system combines Flux AI's animal analysis capabilities with Creatomate's video templating to produce professional-quality content at scale.

How It Works

1. Animal matchup research

Flux AI analyzes animal traits and statistics to generate compelling matchups. The system considers factors like size, habitat, diet, and special abilities to create scientifically plausible yet entertaining battles.

2. Content generation

The workflow automatically generates scripts, voiceover text, and visual asset requirements based on the selected animals. AI ensures each video has engaging narration points and clear visual comparisons.

3. Video assembly

Creatomate templates assemble the video using stock footage, generated text, and effects. The system adds dynamic elements like stat comparisons, slow-motion sequences, and dramatic transitions automatically.

4. Multi-platform optimization

Finished videos are automatically adapted for YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram formats. The workflow adjusts aspect ratios, adds platform-specific captions, and optimizes video lengths for each channel.

5. Scheduled publishing

Videos are queued for publishing based on your optimal posting schedule. The system can stagger releases across platforms or coordinate simultaneous launches for maximum impact.
