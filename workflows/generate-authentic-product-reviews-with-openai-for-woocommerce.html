Generate Authentic Product Reviews with OpenAI for WooCommerce

Automatically create human-like product comments using AI to boost engagement, build social proof, and make your store pages look active.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Empty product pages are conversion killers. New products often sit for weeks or months without a single review, making potential customers hesitant to purchase. This creates a chicken-and-egg problem: you need reviews to get sales, but you need sales to get reviews.

This workflow solves that problem by automatically generating natural, varied product reviews using OpenAI's AI and posting them directly to your WooCommerce store. It fetches your products, analyzes their descriptions, creates context-aware comments in different tones (positive, neutral, even slightly critical), and publishes them with randomized reviewer details. The result is product pages that look active, trustworthy, and ready for real customer engagement.

Beyond just filling empty spaces, this automation kickstarts the social proof cycle. When visitors see that others are discussing your products, they're more likely to make a purchase and leave their own genuine review. It's a strategic tool for building momentum in your e-commerce business.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence that mimics how a marketing team might manually create sample reviews, but does it automatically at scale.

Step 1: Fetch Your Products

The workflow connects to your WooCommerce store via its REST API and retrieves all products (or specific categories you configure). It gathers product names, descriptions, prices, and other metadata to use as context for review generation.

Step 2: Build AI Prompts

For each product, the system constructs a detailed prompt for OpenAI. This includes the product details and instructions to generate a realistic customer review in a specific tone—varying between enthusiastic, balanced, questioning, or even slightly critical to maintain authenticity.

Step 3: Generate Reviews with OpenAI

OpenAI processes each prompt and returns a unique, human-like review. The AI is instructed to mention specific product features, use casual language, include minor imperfections in phrasing, and avoid overly generic praise that sounds fake.

Step 4: Format and Assign Details

Each generated review is paired with a randomized reviewer name and email address to create the appearance of different customers. The system can also assign star ratings that match the tone of the review (4-5 stars for positive, 3 for neutral, etc.).

Step 5: Post to WooCommerce

The completed reviews are posted back to your WooCommerce store as product comments/reviews. You can configure the workflow to post immediately, schedule them over time for natural distribution, or hold them for manual approval if preferred.

What You'll Need

  1. WooCommerce Store: A functioning WordPress site with WooCommerce installed and the REST API enabled.
  2. API Credentials: WooCommerce consumer key and consumer secret from your store's settings.
  3. OpenAI Account: An active OpenAI API key with available credits (GPT-3.5 or GPT-4).
  4. n8n Instance: Either n8n.cloud account or self-hosted n8n version 1.49.0 or later.
  5. Basic Configuration: Your store URL and preferences for review frequency and tone distribution.

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of products to test the generated reviews before scaling to your entire catalog. This lets you refine the AI prompts to better match your brand voice.

Quick Setup Guide

Getting this automation running takes about 15 minutes if you have your credentials ready.

  1. Download and Import: Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import function.
  2. Add Credentials: In n8n's credentials section, add your WooCommerce API keys (consumer key/secret) and your OpenAI API key.
  3. Configure Store URL: Replace the placeholder store URL in the HTTP Request node with your actual WooCommerce site address.
  4. Test with One Product: Run the workflow once in manual mode to generate a review for a single test product and verify it posts correctly.
  5. Schedule Automation: Once tested, add a schedule trigger to run the workflow daily or weekly for new products, or set it to process your entire catalog.
  6. Review and Adjust: Check the first batch of generated reviews and adjust the AI prompt in the workflow if needed to better match your desired tone.
