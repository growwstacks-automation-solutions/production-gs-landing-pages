What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually creating avatar videos from script libraries. Content teams often maintain dozens or hundreds of video scripts in spreadsheets, but converting each one into a video requires repetitive editing work. The workflow automatically processes new or updated scripts, generates professional avatar videos, and organizes them in your Google Drive.

By connecting Google Sheets with avatar video platforms through n8n, you can scale video production without increasing headcount. The system handles script processing, video generation, file naming, and organization—freeing your team to focus on content strategy rather than production logistics.

How It Works

1. Script Detection

The workflow monitors your designated Google Sheet for new or modified script entries. It can be configured to check specific columns containing script text, video titles, and metadata like avatar selection or voice preferences.

2. Video Generation

Each script is sent to your preferred avatar video platform (like Synthesia, D-ID, or HeyGen) via API. The workflow includes all necessary parameters like voice selection, pacing, and visual elements based on your spreadsheet inputs.

3. File Management

Completed videos are automatically saved to specified Google Drive folders with consistent naming conventions. The workflow can update your spreadsheet with video links and status markers for easy tracking.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for content teams at marketing agencies, e-learning platforms, and SaaS companies that produce high volumes of instructional or promotional videos. Social media managers creating daily content will benefit from the time savings, as will customer success teams building video libraries for common support questions.

Pro tip: Use different avatar personas for different content types (e.g., instructor for tutorials, friendly host for social media) by adding an "avatar style" column to your script spreadsheet.
