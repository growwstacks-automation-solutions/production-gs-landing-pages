Generate Beauty Brand Hashtags with AI

Automate high-quality hashtag creation using Gemini AI, live website analysis, and social media trend research. Perfect for beauty marketers and agencies.

What This Workflow Does

Beauty and skincare brands struggle with hashtag strategy—spending hours researching trends, analyzing competitors, and testing different tags, only to see limited reach and engagement. Manual hashtag research is time-consuming, often outdated, and misses platform-specific opportunities that could dramatically increase content visibility.

This n8n automation solves that by combining AI intelligence with real-time data analysis. It automatically scrapes your brand's website to understand your messaging, uses Gemini AI to generate contextually relevant hashtags, researches current trends via SerpAPI, and organizes everything in Google Sheets—creating a dynamic, always-updated hashtag library tailored specifically to your beauty brand.

The result is a consistent stream of high-performing hashtags that align with your brand identity while tapping into current social conversations, saving marketing teams 5-10 hours weekly while improving social media performance by 30-50%.

How It Works

1. Brand Input & Website Analysis

Users submit brand details through a simple form interface. The workflow then scrapes the provided website URL to extract key messaging, product descriptions, brand values, and target audience cues. This foundational analysis ensures generated hashtags authentically represent your brand.

2. AI-Powered Hashtag Generation

Google Gemini AI processes the website content alongside brand parameters (tone, audience, goals) to create initial hashtag sets. The AI considers beauty industry specifics—skincare ingredients, makeup trends, sustainability angles, and consumer pain points—generating both broad and niche tags.

3. Trend Research & Enrichment

SerpAPI searches current social media conversations, competitor hashtags, and trending topics in the beauty space. This real-time data enriches the AI-generated tags with what's actually performing right now, creating a balanced mix of evergreen and trending hashtags.

4. Organization & Delivery

Final hashtag sets are categorized by platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok), purpose (brand awareness, product launch, community building), and performance potential. Everything automatically populates a Google Sheet that serves as your brand's living hashtag library, ready for immediate use.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for beauty and skincare brand marketers, social media managers at cosmetic companies, digital agencies managing multiple beauty clients, e-commerce founders in the beauty space, and content creators focusing on beauty tutorials or product reviews. Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this hashtag automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Import the template – Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance via the workflow import function.
  2. Configure credentials – Add your Google Gemini API, Google Sheets, and SerpAPI credentials to the respective nodes in the workflow.
  3. Customize the form – Adjust the form trigger node to collect your specific brand parameters (tone, target audience, social platforms).
  4. Set up your Google Sheet – Create a new Google Sheet and note its ID, then update the Google Sheets node with this information.
  5. Test and activate – Run a test with your brand's website URL, review the generated hashtags, then activate the workflow for ongoing use.
