What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of creating high-quality blog content by combining AI research, writing, and publishing into one seamless workflow. It eliminates the manual work of gathering information, structuring articles, and uploading to WordPress - tasks that typically take 5-10 hours per post.

The system generates complete, SEO-optimized blog posts including featured images, proper formatting, and internal linking. It maintains human-like quality by using GPT-5's advanced language capabilities combined with Tavily's research API to ensure factual accuracy and depth.

How It Works

1. Topic Research Phase

The workflow begins by analyzing your content calendar or receiving manual input for the next blog topic. It uses Tavily to gather the latest information from authoritative sources, compiling research notes and key points.

2. Content Generation

GPT-5 processes the research to create a structured outline first, then expands it into a full draft. The AI incorporates proper citations, creates natural transitions between sections, and maintains consistent tone throughout.

3. Quality Assurance

Before publishing, the system runs automated checks for plagiarism, factual accuracy against source material, and readability scores. Optional human review steps can be added at this stage.

4. WordPress Publishing

The finalized content gets formatted with proper HTML tags, headings, and metadata. The workflow automatically publishes to WordPress with the correct category, tags, featured image, and SEO settings.

Pro tip: For best results, provide the AI with 3-5 example posts that represent your ideal content style. This helps maintain brand voice consistency across automated posts.
