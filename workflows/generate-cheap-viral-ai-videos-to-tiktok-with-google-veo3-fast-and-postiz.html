What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of creating and publishing viral TikTok videos using AI technology. How It Works

1. Content Brief Input

The workflow begins by accepting your content brief - this could be text prompts, product details, or campaign objectives. The system structures this information for optimal AI processing.

2. AI Video Generation

Google Veo3 processes the brief to generate multiple video variations. The AI selects appropriate visuals, animations, and effects based on your brand guidelines and current TikTok trends.

3. Performance Optimization

Postiz analyzes each generated video, suggesting improvements to captions, hashtags, and editing to maximize engagement potential. The system automatically implements these optimizations.

4. Approval & Scheduling

The final videos are queued for review. Once approved, they're scheduled for posting at optimal times based on your audience's activity patterns.
