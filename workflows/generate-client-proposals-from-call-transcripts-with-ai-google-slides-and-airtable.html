What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually creating client proposals after sales calls. Instead of spending hours listening to recordings and drafting documents, the workflow automatically analyzes call transcripts, extracts key details with AI, and generates polished proposals in Google Slides format.

The system identifies critical information like project requirements, pricing discussions, timelines, and client preferences from natural conversation. It then populates these details into pre-designed Google Slides templates, creating professional-looking proposals ready for review and delivery.

How It Works

1. Transcript Processing

The workflow begins by receiving call transcripts from your meeting platform (Zoom, Teams, etc.). It cleans the text and prepares it for AI analysis, removing filler words and normalizing formatting.

2. AI Content Extraction

Using natural language processing, the AI identifies and categorizes key proposal components: project scope, deliverables, pricing, timelines, and special terms discussed during the call.

3. Template Population

The extracted data automatically fills corresponding fields in your Google Slides proposal template, maintaining your brand's visual style while customizing content for each client.

4. Airtable Record Creation

Each generated proposal creates a corresponding record in Airtable, storing all metadata including client name, proposal date, value, and status for easy tracking and reporting.
