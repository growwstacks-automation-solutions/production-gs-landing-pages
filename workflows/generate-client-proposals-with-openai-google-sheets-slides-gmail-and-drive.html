What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how businesses create client proposals by combining AI content generation with Google Workspace productivity tools. The system automatically generates professional, tailored proposals in minutes instead of hours by pulling data from your CRM or intake forms, analyzing requirements with OpenAI, and assembling polished documents in Google Slides.

The workflow eliminates manual copy-pasting between systems while ensuring brand consistency across all client communications. It handles the entire process from initial data collection to final delivery, including automated follow-ups and version tracking. Sales teams report 3x faster proposal turnaround times and 25% higher conversion rates using this approach.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow begins by extracting client and project details from your preferred source - whether Google Forms submissions, CRM records, or spreadsheet data. This includes client name, project scope, budget range, and any special requirements.

2. AI Content Generation

OpenAI processes the collected data to generate proposal content tailored to the specific opportunity. The AI references your past successful proposals, brand guidelines, and industry best practices to create compelling, on-brand drafts.

3. Document Assembly

The system automatically populates a Google Slides template with the generated content, inserting client-specific details, pricing tables, and customized value propositions. All assets (logos, images, case studies) are pulled from designated Drive folders.

4. Review & Delivery

Completed proposals are saved to a shared Drive folder with appropriate permissions. The workflow notifies assigned team members for review via email, then automatically sends the final version to clients through Gmail with tracking enabled.

Pro tip: Create multiple template variations for different service offerings or client types. The workflow can automatically select the most appropriate template based on opportunity characteristics.
