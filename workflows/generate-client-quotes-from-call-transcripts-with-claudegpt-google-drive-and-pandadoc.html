What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually creating client proposals after discovery calls. Service providers often waste hours listening to call recordings, taking notes, and drafting quotes - delaying the sales cycle and creating bottlenecks.

The workflow automatically processes call transcripts (from Google Drive), extracts key discussion points using Claude/GPT AI, structures the information into a professional format, and generates ready-to-send proposals in PandaDoc. It captures pricing discussions, scope details, and custom requirements mentioned during the call.

How It Works

1. Transcript Processing

The workflow monitors a designated Google Drive folder for new call transcripts. When a new file is detected, it extracts the text content and prepares it for AI analysis.

2. AI Content Generation

Using Claude or GPT, the system analyzes the transcript to identify key elements needed for a proposal: project scope, pricing discussions, timelines, and specific client requirements. The AI structures this into coherent proposal sections.

3. Document Assembly

The workflow populates a PandaDoc template with the AI-generated content, inserting variables like client name, discussed services, pricing, and terms. It maintains your brand voice while customizing each proposal.

4. Approval & Delivery

The completed draft is saved back to Google Drive for review. Optionally, you can configure automatic sending to clients or route it through an approval process first.

Pro tip: Train your AI model with sample proposals to ensure outputs match your preferred style and include all necessary legal clauses.
