What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how businesses create SEO strategy reports by deploying an entire "AI SEO Agency" within your n8n instance. Unlike basic chatbots, it uses a hierarchical swarm of specialized AI agents that collaborate like a professional SEO team.

The system takes a simple prompt about your website or target keywords and produces comprehensive reports including technical audits, content gap analysis, backlink strategies, and local SEO recommendations - all powered by live SerpAPI data and analyzed by GPT-4 specialists.

How It Works

1. Initial Analysis

The Director agent receives your request and checks its memory for previous interactions with your business, ensuring continuity across reports.

2. Live Research

Using SerpAPI, the workflow performs real Google searches to gather current SERP data, competitor rankings, and featured snippet opportunities.

3. Specialist Delegation

The Director assigns tasks to six specialized agents: Technical SEO, Keyword Research, Content Strategy, Backlink Analysis, Local SEO, and Performance Metrics.

4. Report Synthesis

All specialist findings are compiled into a cohesive strategy document with prioritized recommendations and implementation steps.
