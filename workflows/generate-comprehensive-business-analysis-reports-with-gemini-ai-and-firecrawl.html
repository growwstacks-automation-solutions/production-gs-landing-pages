What This Workflow Does

This workflow revolutionizes business intelligence by automating the entire analysis process. Simply submit a website URL, and the system will:

1. Scrape the website content using Firecrawl to gather comprehensive data
2. Analyze the information using Gemini AI to extract key business insights
3. Generate a professional report including company overview, target audience personas, brand analysis, and customer journey mapping

How It Works

Step 1: Website Scraping

The workflow begins by using Firecrawl to extract all relevant content from the target website, including text, structure, and metadata.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Gemini AI processes the scraped content to identify and categorize key business information, including unique value propositions and competitive advantages.

Step 3: Report Generation

The analyzed data is formatted into a comprehensive business report with visual elements and executive summaries, ready for client presentations or internal use.
