What This Workflow Does

The Trend2Content workflow solves a critical challenge for content creators and marketers: consistently generating fresh, relevant content ideas that resonate with your audience. Instead of manually scouring social media for inspiration, this automation system does the heavy lifting for you.

By combining Apify's web scraping capabilities with Gemini's AI analysis and Google Sheets for organization, the workflow transforms raw social data into structured content suggestions. It identifies emerging patterns, popular formats, and engagement drivers specific to your niche - saving hours of research while improving content relevance.

How It Works

1. Topic Input

The workflow begins with a simple web form where you enter your content focus area (e.g., "sustainable fashion" or "SaaS marketing"). This becomes the seed for all subsequent analysis.

2. Social Media Scraping

Apify crawls multiple social platforms to collect recent and top-performing content related to your topic. It gathers posts, engagement metrics, hashtags, and comments to build a comprehensive dataset.

3. AI Analysis

Gemini processes the scraped data to identify patterns, extract key themes, and generate content suggestions. It analyzes sentiment, detects emerging terminology, and surfaces frequently asked questions or pain points.

4. Structured Output

The final insights are organized in Google Sheets with columns for content ideas, supporting data, suggested formats (blog post, video, etc.), and potential angles. This creates an actionable content calendar foundation.
