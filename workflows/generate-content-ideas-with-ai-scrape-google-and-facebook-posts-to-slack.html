What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the constant challenge of finding fresh, engaging content ideas by automating the entire discovery process. It scrapes trending posts from Google and Facebook, analyzes them using AI to identify patterns and high-potential topics, then delivers curated content suggestions directly to your Slack channel.

Content teams waste hours manually searching for inspiration across platforms. This automation eliminates that grind while providing data-driven insights you'd likely miss. The AI component examines engagement metrics, sentiment, and emerging themes to recommend topics with the highest potential resonance for your audience.

How It Works

1. Automated Content Scraping

The workflow begins by scraping Google News and Facebook posts based on your configured keywords. It extracts titles, engagement metrics, and relevant text from trending posts in your industry or niche.

2. AI Analysis & Pattern Detection

An AI model processes the scraped content to identify common themes, high-performing formats, and emerging topics. It scores each potential idea based on engagement patterns and novelty factors.

3. Curated Idea Generation

The system generates specific content suggestions based on the analysis, including potential angles, formats, and supporting data points. It avoids duplicate ideas by comparing against your content history.

4. Slack Delivery

Final content ideas are formatted and delivered to your designated Slack channel, complete with source references and performance metrics. Team members can immediately discuss, refine, or assign the ideas.

Pro tip: Configure separate keyword sets for different content types (blog posts, social media, videos) to get tailored suggestions for each format.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits content marketers, social media managers, and bloggers who need to consistently produce engaging material.   1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
