What This Workflow Does

The AI Chef Bot solves the time-consuming process of recipe development and food photography for restaurants, food bloggers, and meal prep services. Instead of spending hours crafting recipes and staging photoshoots, this automated Telegram bot generates complete recipes with ingredients, instructions, and professional-quality images instantly.

When users message the bot with a dish request (like "vegetarian lasagna" or "gluten-free chocolate cake"), the system leverages AI to create a customized recipe considering dietary restrictions, available ingredients, and cooking skill level. Simultaneously, it generates a restaurant-worthy image of the dish that's ready for menus or social media.

How It Works

1. User submits request via Telegram

Customers or staff message the bot with their desired dish, optionally including preferences like dietary restrictions or cooking time. The bot uses natural language processing to understand the request.

2. AI generates the recipe

The workflow connects to OpenAI's API to create a detailed recipe with precise measurements, step-by-step instructions, cooking times, and nutritional information. The AI considers flavor pairings and cooking techniques.

3. Food image creation

Using DALL-E or Stable Diffusion, the system generates a high-quality image of the finished dish with proper plating and styling. The image matches restaurant photography standards.

4. Delivery via Telegram

The complete recipe and image are formatted into an attractive message with clear sections and sent back to the user through the Telegram chat interface.

Pro tip: Train the AI on your restaurant's signature dishes and plating style to maintain brand consistency in generated content.
