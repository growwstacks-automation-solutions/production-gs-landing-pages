What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating high-quality, SEO-optimized blog content that meets Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) guidelines. Content marketers and website owners often struggle to produce enough authoritative content that ranks well while maintaining quality standards.

The workflow combines OpenAI's advanced language capabilities with WordPress publishing automation. It transforms simple topic inputs into fully formatted blog drafts with proper SEO structure, internal linking suggestions, and E-E-A-T optimized content that appears genuinely helpful and expert-authored.

How It Works

1. Topic Input Collection

The workflow begins by collecting your target keywords, topic focus, and desired tone through a simple form interface. You can specify word count, target audience, and any specific expertise points to emphasize.

2. AI Content Generation

OpenAI processes these inputs to create a draft that includes: SEO-optimized title options, meta description, headings structure, and content that demonstrates expertise while maintaining readability.

3. E-E-A-T Enhancement

The system automatically injects credibility markers like relevant statistics, expert quotes (when available), and proper sourcing to boost trust signals in the content.

4. WordPress Integration

The finalized draft gets pushed to your WordPress site as a draft post, complete with featured image suggestions, category assignment, and SEO metadata ready for your final review.

The key is providing detailed prompts that include your brand voice guidelines, target audience specifics, and content purpose. Specify conversational tone preferences, reading level, and any terminology to use or avoid. Feed the AI examples of your best-performing content as references.

For instance, a B2B software company might prompt the AI to "Write in a professional yet approachable tone for IT managers at mid-sized companies, avoiding jargon unless clearly defined, and including real-world implementation challenges." This yields more targeted, natural-sounding output than generic prompts.

  • Provide 2-3 sample paragraphs as style guides
  • Specify sentence length preferences
  • Ask for analogies relevant to your audience

AI-assisted content maintains human oversight at key stages - topic selection, outline approval, fact-checking, and final edits. Fully automated content publishes without human review, which risks quality issues. This workflow focuses on AI assistance, creating drafts that humans refine to ensure accuracy and brand alignment.

  • Automated content works best for low-stakes topics
  • Human review essential for regulated industries
  • Hybrid approach maximizes both quality and output
