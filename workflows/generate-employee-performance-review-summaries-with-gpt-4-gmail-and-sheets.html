What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered automation transforms the time-consuming process of compiling employee performance reviews into a streamlined, consistent workflow. It pulls feedback data from multiple sources (manager notes, peer reviews, self-assessments) stored in Google Sheets, analyzes the content using GPT-4, and generates comprehensive, professional summaries that highlight key strengths, development areas, and growth opportunities.

The system automatically emails completed review summaries to managers for final approval before distribution, eliminating manual data compilation while maintaining human oversight. By standardizing the review language and structure across your organization, it reduces bias and ensures all employees receive equally thorough, professional evaluations.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow monitors a designated Google Sheet where performance feedback is collected. This can include structured ratings, qualitative comments, competency assessments, and goal progress metrics.

2. AI Analysis

GPT-4 processes the raw feedback data, identifying patterns, themes, and notable insights. The AI is configured with your company's specific evaluation criteria and cultural values to ensure relevant, on-brand summaries.

3. Summary Generation

The system generates a draft performance summary for each employee, organized by competency areas with balanced strengths and development suggestions. The output follows your preferred template format.

4. Manager Review

Completed summaries are automatically emailed to managers via Gmail for final review and editing. Managers can make adjustments before the summaries are finalized.

5. Distribution

Approved summaries are sent to employees and stored in your HR system with a complete audit trail. The workflow updates tracking sheets to maintain process visibility.

Pro tip: Configure the AI to flag significant changes between review periods, helping managers identify growth patterns and stagnation areas.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for HR teams and people managers at companies with 50+ employees where performance reviews have become administratively burdensome. It's particularly valuable for organizations conducting 360-degree reviews, those with distributed teams, or companies implementing more frequent check-ins alongside annual reviews.
