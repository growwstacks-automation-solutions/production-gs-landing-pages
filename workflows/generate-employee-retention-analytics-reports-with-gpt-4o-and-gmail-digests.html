How It Works

Step 1: Data Aggregation

The workflow automatically pulls employee data from connected HR systems (like BambooHR or Workday), performance management tools, and engagement survey platforms. It consolidates this information into a standardized format for analysis.

h3>Step 2: Retention Risk Scoring

GPT-4o analyzes the aggregated data against historical retention patterns to calculate individual risk scores. The AI considers factors like promotion velocity, skills utilization, manager relationship quality, and compensation competitiveness.

h3Step 3: Report Generation

The system generates easy-to-digest reports with color-coded risk levels, trend analysis, and prioritized action items. GPT-4o writes natural language explanations of each employee's risk factors and recommended interventions.

h3Step 4: Email Distribution

Personalized reports are automatically emailed to relevant managers on a scheduled cadence. High-risk alerts can trigger immediate notifications outside the regular reporting cycle. h2Who This Is For
