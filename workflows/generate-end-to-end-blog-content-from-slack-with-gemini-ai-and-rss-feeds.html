What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms Slack into a powerful content command center, eliminating the friction between idea generation and published articles. It solves three major pain points: content teams wasting hours on research, inconsistent publishing schedules, and difficulty scaling quality content production.

The system monitors RSS feeds for trending industry topics, uses Gemini AI to validate content angles, generates draft posts with proper SEO structure, and routes everything through Slack for human approval before auto-publishing. One marketing team using this workflow reduced content creation time from 8 hours to 90 minutes per article while increasing output by 300%.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow continuously scans configured RSS feeds from industry news sources, competitor blogs, and content aggregators. It identifies trending topics using natural language processing to detect rising mentions and engagement signals.

2. AI-Powered Topic Validation

Gemini AI analyzes each potential topic against your content strategy parameters - audience interests, past performance data, and keyword opportunities. It scores topics and suggests the most promising candidates to pursue.

3. Slack-Based Approval

Validated topics get posted to a designated Slack channel where editors can vote, comment, or request adjustments. Approved topics automatically move to the drafting phase with all contextual feedback attached.

4. Automated Content Generation

Gemini AI creates complete drafts including headings, meta descriptions, and internal linking suggestions. The AI references your style guide and top-performing content to maintain brand voice consistency.

5. Final Publishing

Edited drafts get formatted for your CMS and published on schedule. The workflow can also distribute content to social media and email newsletters as part of an integrated content distribution strategy.
