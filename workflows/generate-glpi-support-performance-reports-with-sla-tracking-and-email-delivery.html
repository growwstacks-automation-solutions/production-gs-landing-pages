What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of generating comprehensive technical support performance reports from your GLPI system. It eliminates the manual effort typically required to collect ticket data, calculate SLA compliance metrics, format reports, and distribute them to stakeholders.

The solution provides IT managers and service desk leaders with consistent, data-driven insights into team performance. It tracks key metrics like ticket resolution times, first response rates, and SLA compliance percentages - all formatted in professional reports delivered automatically via email on your preferred schedule.

The automated workflow pulls data from GLPI, processes metrics, and generates formatted reports

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from GLPI

The workflow begins by connecting to your GLPI instance through its API. It extracts ticket data for the specified reporting period, filtering for relevant metrics based on your configured categories, priorities, and service level agreements.

2. SLA Calculation & Metric Processing

Raw ticket data gets processed to calculate SLA compliance percentages, average resolution times by priority level, technician workload distribution, and other key performance indicators. The system compares actual performance against your defined service level targets.

Example report showing SLA compliance metrics and performance trends

3. Report Generation

The processed metrics get formatted into professional reports with visualizations like charts and graphs. The template includes executive summaries, detailed breakdowns by ticket category, and trend analysis compared to previous periods.

4. Automated Distribution

Completed reports get attached to emails and sent to predefined distribution lists. The workflow can generate different report versions tailored for various stakeholders (executives, managers, technicians) with appropriate detail levels.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run on the last business day of each month to ensure stakeholders receive reports consistently by the 3rd of the following month.
