What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually researching local businesses for sales outreach. Traditional lead generation requires hours of Google Maps searches, website visits, and social media checks to compile basic contact information. Our workflow automates this entire process with AI-powered enrichment.

The system extracts business listings from Google Maps based on your location and industry filters, then enhances each record with verified email addresses, social media profiles, and AI-generated business summaries. All this enriched data automatically populates a Google Sheet, creating a ready-to-use prospect database for your sales team.

Google Maps business data extraction process

How It Works

Step 1: Google Maps Business Search

The workflow starts by querying Google Maps for businesses matching your criteria (location, industry keywords, ratings). It extracts names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and other public listing details.

Step 2: AI-Powered Data Enrichment

Each business record passes through multiple enrichment services that add emails, social links, and company descriptions. AI analyzes website content to generate concise business summaries highlighting services, specialties, and unique selling points.

AI enrichment adds emails, social profiles and business insights

Step 3: Social Profile Discovery

The system cross-references each business across LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram and Twitter to find official profiles. This helps sales teams understand the company's online presence and engagement style before reaching out.

Step 4: Google Sheets Integration

All enriched data automatically populates a Google Sheet with columns for each data point. The sheet serves as both a database and launchpad for outreach campaigns, with options to connect to email platforms or CRMs.
