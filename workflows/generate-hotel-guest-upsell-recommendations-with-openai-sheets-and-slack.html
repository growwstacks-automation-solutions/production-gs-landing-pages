What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how hotels identify and deliver upsell opportunities to guests. By connecting your guest data in Google Sheets with OpenAI's AI capabilities and Slack for team communication, it creates a seamless upsell recommendation system that works 24/7.

The workflow analyzes guest profiles, booking patterns, and historical data to generate personalized upgrade suggestions. Instead of relying on staff to remember upsell opportunities during busy check-ins, the system automatically surfaces the right offers at the right time through Slack notifications.

How It Works

1. Guest Data Collection

The system pulls guest information from your Google Sheets database, including booking details, preferences, and past behavior. This serves as the foundation for AI analysis.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

OpenAI processes the guest data to identify optimal upsell opportunities. The AI considers factors like room type history, special occasions, length of stay, and seasonal promotions to determine relevant offers.

3. Recommendation Generation

The system creates natural language upsell suggestions complete with personalized messaging. For example: "Recommend premium ocean view upgrade for the Smith family celebrating their anniversary - include champagne package."

4. Slack Notification

Recommendations are delivered directly to your hotel team's Slack channel, prioritized by conversion likelihood. Staff can act immediately during guest interactions or pre-arrival communications.
