What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation transforms your HR document creation process by combining AI with your existing Google Docs templates. When new candidate information is submitted (via form, email, or your ATS), the system automatically generates personalized offer letters and employment contracts using GPT-4.1-mini's natural language capabilities.

The workflow ensures all documents maintain legal compliance while adapting to each candidate's specific role, compensation package, and start date. What traditionally took HR teams 30-60 minutes per hire now happens in seconds, with consistent formatting and error-free outputs.

How It Works

1. Candidate data collection

The workflow begins when candidate information enters your system - whether from an application form, ATS integration, or manual entry. Key details like name, position, salary, and benefits are extracted automatically.

2. AI document generation

GPT-4.1-mini processes this data against your predefined templates and company policies to generate draft documents. The AI maintains proper legal language while personalizing each element to the candidate's specifics.

3. Google Docs creation

The generated documents are automatically created in Google Docs with proper naming conventions and folder organization. HR managers receive notifications with direct links to review and approve.

4. Final delivery

Once approved, the system can automatically email documents to candidates, upload signed copies to your HRIS, or trigger next steps in your onboarding workflow.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to flag any unusual terms or compensation structures for manual review, giving you AI efficiency with human oversight.
