What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation is one of the biggest time-sinks in sales and marketing. Researching companies, understanding their business, identifying decision-makers, and crafting personalized outreach can consume 10-15 hours per week per salesperson. This workflow eliminates that manual grind.

It automates the entire process: from capturing a company website via a simple form, to conducting deep research using Perplexity AI, generating a detailed ideal customer profile (ICP) with GPT-4o-mini, finding matching leads through Apify's data scraping, and creating genuinely personalized email pitches for each prospect. What used to take hours now happens in minutes with consistent, high-quality output.

The system is designed for B2B companies, agencies, and consultants who need to scale their prospecting without sacrificing personalization. It turns your sales team from researchers into closers.

How It Works

1. Form Submission & Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits a JotForm with a target company website. This could be your sales team entering a prospect, or it could be triggered automatically from other systems. The form captures the initial company information that kicks off the entire automation sequence.

2. Company Intelligence Gathering

Using the Perplexity AI Search API, the workflow conducts comprehensive research on the submitted company. It analyzes their business model, market position, recent news, and industry context. This creates a rich data foundation that most manual researchers would miss or take hours to compile.

3. Ideal Customer Profile Creation

GPT-4o-mini acts as an expert client acquisition manager, analyzing the company research to generate a detailed ideal customer profile. It identifies what types of businesses would benefit most from your services based on the target company's characteristics, creating a blueprint for lead matching.

4. Industry Classification

Another GPT-4o-mini instance specializes in industry classification, taking the ICP and returning a single, specific industry sector (like "healthcare," "call center," or "plumbing services"). This precise classification ensures the subsequent lead search targets exactly the right businesses.

5. Lead Discovery & Enrichment

Apify's Local Business Lead Generator searches for 5 matching businesses in the United States based on the industry classification. This provides actual contactable companies that fit your ideal customer profile, complete with business details that can be used for personalization.

6. Personalized Email Generation

For each discovered lead, Apify's Pitches Pro generates a custom email that references the company's specific context. The AI is instructed to focus on time-saving benefits ("Save 10+ hours per week"), maintain a professional yet approachable tone, and include soft calls-to-action that feel natural rather than pushy.

Pro tip: The workflow processes leads in batches, allowing you to scale from testing with a few companies to running hundreds of prospects through the system weekly. Each step maintains data quality and personalization at scale.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure the JotForm trigger node with your form ID (252802732808054 in the template) or replace it with your preferred form service.

3. Set up credentials for Perplexity AI, OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini), and Apify in n8n's credentials management.

4. Test the workflow with a sample company website to ensure all API connections work correctly.

5. Adjust the email tone and messaging in the Pitches Pro node to match your brand voice and value proposition.

6. Activate the workflow and connect it to your CRM or email sending system for complete automation.

Implementation note: Start with 1-2 test companies to verify output quality before scaling. The AI-generated emails may need slight tuning to match your specific service offerings and communication style.
