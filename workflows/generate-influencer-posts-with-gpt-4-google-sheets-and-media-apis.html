What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating consistent, on-brand influencer content at scale. Marketing teams and influencers spend 10-15 hours weekly brainstorming post ideas, writing captions, and formatting content for different platforms. Our workflow reduces this to minutes by automatically generating complete social media posts from product images, brand guidelines, and campaign briefs stored in Google Sheets.

The system uses GPT-4 to analyze brand voice and product details, then generates multiple post variations with optimized hashtags, emojis, and calls-to-action. It can create Instagram carousels, TikTok scripts, YouTube descriptions, and Twitter threads - all formatted correctly for each platform's specifications. The generated content maintains authentic engagement while ensuring brand compliance.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Brand Assets

The workflow begins by pulling product images, campaign briefs, and brand guidelines from your Google Sheets. This includes tone of voice preferences, key messaging points, and any regulatory requirements for disclosures.

Step 2: AI Content Generation

GPT-4 processes these inputs to create multiple post variations. It references past high-performing content to optimize engagement factors while maintaining brand consistency across all generated material.

Step 3: Media Processing

The workflow automatically resizes images for different platforms, adds branded overlays or watermarks, and can even generate simple video storyboards from text prompts using media APIs.

Step 4: Quality Review & Scheduling

Generated content gets organized in a review spreadsheet where you can approve, reject, or request edits. Approved posts are automatically scheduled via your preferred social media management platform.
