What This Workflow Does

This automation acts as your personal social media content strategist and designer. You send a single topic idea—like "5 tips for better time management"—via Telegram. The workflow then uses AI to generate a complete, 5-slide educational Instagram carousel, complete with matching caption, ready for posting.

It eliminates the manual, time-consuming process of brainstorming slide concepts, writing engaging copy, designing visuals, and ensuring brand consistency. Instead of spending hours per carousel, you get a polished piece of content in minutes, directly in your Telegram chat.

The system intelligently structures the content, applies your predefined brand style, and handles the back-and-forth with image generation APIs. It's perfect for entrepreneurs, marketers, and creators who need to maintain a consistent content calendar without a full-time design team.

How It Works

The workflow is a multi-stage pipeline that transforms a raw idea into a finished social media asset.

1. Trigger & Authorization

You send a message to your private Telegram bot. The workflow first validates that the message comes from your authorized Chat ID, ensuring security and preventing unauthorized use.

2. AI Content Strategist Plans the Carousel

An OpenAI agent, acting as a "Senior Content Strategist," takes your topic and your stored brand guidelines. It outputs a detailed JSON plan for 5 slides, each with a visual concept and text overlay.

3. AI Copywriter Crafts the Caption

Simultaneously, a second AI agent ("Instagram Copywriter") uses the carousel plan to write a compelling, SEO-friendly Instagram caption designed to boost engagement and reach.

4. Image Generation & Processing

The 5 visual prompts are sent to the Kie AI (or similar) image generation API. The workflow includes a smart polling loop that waits and checks until all high-resolution images are ready for download.

5. Delivery

The final carousel images and the generated caption are sent back to you via Telegram. You receive a complete, ready-to-upload content package.

What You'll Need

  1. A Telegram Bot Token (created via @BotFather).
  2. An OpenAI API Key for the AI text generation.
  3. An Image Generation API Key (like Kie AI) for creating the visuals.
  4. A running n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with support for AI Agent nodes.
  5. Your brand guidelines (colors, tone, style) to input into the "Set Brand Style" node.

Pro tip: Start with a simple, clear brand guideline in the setup. Define 2-3 primary colors and a descriptive mood (e.g., "professional and clean," "fun and energetic"). This gives the AI a strong foundation for generating on-brand visuals.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your automated content factory running.

  1. Create Your Telegram Bot: Message @BotFather on Telegram, create a new bot, and copy the API token.
  2. Configure the Trigger: Add your Telegram Bot Token to the "Telegram Trigger" node credentials. Send a test message and note your Chat ID from the workflow output.
  3. Authorize Yourself: Paste your Chat ID into the "IF" node to ensure only you can trigger the workflow.
  4. Connect AI Services: Add your OpenAI API key to the two AI Agent nodes. Sign up for Kie AI (or a similar service), get your API key, and add it as a Header Auth credential in n8n.
  5. Define Your Brand: Open the "Set Brand Style" node and replace the placeholder text with your specific brand colors, typography preferences, and aesthetic description.
  6. Activate & Test: Turn the workflow on. Send a topic like "Benefits of morning meditation" to your bot and watch the magic happen.
