What This Workflow Does

Manual invoicing is a time-consuming, error-prone process that delays payments and frustrates customers. This automation solves that by creating a seamless pipeline from order to payment. When a customer submits an order through your Jotform, the workflow instantly creates or updates their record in Xero, generates a professional invoice with correct pricing and taxes, emails it to the customer, and then manages intelligent payment reminders.

The system runs 24/7, ensuring no order gets missed and every invoice receives timely follow-up. It transforms what typically takes hours of administrative work into a completely hands-free process, allowing you to focus on delivering services rather than chasing paperwork.

How It Works

1. Receive Order Submission

The workflow triggers when a customer submits your Jotform order form. It captures all relevant data: customer details, selected products or services, quantities, special requests, and contact information.

2. Create or Update Customer in Xero

The automation checks if the customer already exists in your Xero contacts. If not, it creates a new contact with all provided details. If they exist, it updates their record with any new information, ensuring your customer database stays current.

3. Generate Invoice with Correct Items

Using the product/service codes from the form submission, the workflow matches them to your Xero item list and generates a complete invoice with proper line items, descriptions, quantities, prices, and tax calculations.

4. Send Invoice via Email

The completed invoice is automatically emailed to the customer as a professional PDF attachment with a clear payment request and your branding.

5. Store Invoice for Tracking

Key invoice details (ID, amount, currency) are stored in a database table for tracking payment status and managing reminders.

6. Send AI-Powered Payment Reminders

Each day at 8 AM, the system checks all outstanding invoices. Using configurable rules and AI analysis, it decides whether to send a reminder (with personalized messaging), wait, or mark the invoice as completed if paid.

Pro tip: Configure your reminder intervals based on your customer payment patterns. Common setups: first reminder at 2 days, second at 5 days, final at 10 days. The AI can personalize message tone based on invoice age and customer history.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Jotform (webhook), Xero (OAuth), and your email service.
  3. Map your product codes – ensure Jotform field values match exactly with Xero item codes.
  4. Create the data table in n8n using the provided column structure.
  5. Update reminder intervals in the "Add reminders config" node to match your payment terms.
  6. Test with a sample form submission and verify invoice creation in Xero.
  7. Activate the workflow and connect your live Jotform webhook.

Pro tip: Start with a test Xero organization and a small subset of customers. Run the automation for a week to ensure everything works perfectly before switching your main business over.
