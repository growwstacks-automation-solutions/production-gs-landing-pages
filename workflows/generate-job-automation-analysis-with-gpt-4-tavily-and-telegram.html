What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of analyzing job market trends using AI-powered research. It combines GPT-4's analytical capabilities with Tavily's web search functionality to generate comprehensive reports about automation potential in specific job roles or industries.

The system automatically delivers these insights via Telegram, making it ideal for recruiters, HR professionals, or business analysts who need to stay updated on evolving job automation trends without manual research.

How It Works

1. Trigger Setup

The workflow can be triggered manually or scheduled to run at regular intervals, depending on your analysis needs.

2. Web Research with Tavily

Tavily performs targeted web searches to gather the latest information about job automation trends from authoritative sources.

3. AI Analysis with GPT-4

The collected data is processed by GPT-4, which summarizes key findings, identifies automation potential, and provides actionable insights.

4. Telegram Notification

The final analysis is formatted and sent directly to your Telegram channel or private chat for easy access.
