What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you build and manage vocabulary flashcards for language learning. Instead of manually looking up words, writing definitions, and creating example sentences, this workflow captures phrases from your daily life (via Telegram), enriches them with AI-generated context, and organizes them into a structured Google Sheet ready for import into Anki.

The system addresses the friction points in traditional flashcard creation: time-consuming manual entry, inconsistent formatting, and lack of contextual examples. By automating the entire pipeline, you can focus on actual learning while maintaining a growing, organized vocabulary database that syncs seamlessly with spaced repetition systems.

Whether you're learning through immersion, textbooks, or conversation practice, this workflow ensures every new word or phrase you encounter can be instantly captured, enriched, and added to your study system with minimal effort.

How It Works

The automation follows a clear three-stage process: capture, enrich, and organize.

1. Capture New Vocabulary

When you encounter a new word or phrase while reading, watching media, or conversing, you simply send it to your Telegram bot. The workflow triggers instantly, capturing the text and preparing it for processing. You can also manually input phrases directly into the workflow if you prefer.

2. AI Enrichment with GPT-4

The captured phrase is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4 model, which generates a comprehensive definition, identifies multiple meanings if applicable, and creates relevant example sentences showing the word in context. This transforms a simple word into a rich learning card with proper context.

3. Organization & Sync

The enriched data—including the original phrase, definition, examples, and timestamp—is automatically appended to a Google Sheet. This creates a structured database of your vocabulary that can be easily imported into Anki using the Google Sheets to Anki add-on with a single click.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the download button above.
  2. Configure Telegram node with your bot token from @BotFather.
  3. Add your OpenAI API key to the GPT-4 node settings.
  4. Set up Google Sheets credentials using a service account with edit permissions.
  5. Test the workflow by sending a phrase to your Telegram bot.
  6. Install the Anki add-on (ID: 1871608121) and connect it to your Google Sheet.
  7. Click "Sync Decks" in Anki to import your new flashcards automatically.
