What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of consistently creating valuable lead magnets - the free resources businesses offer in exchange for contact information. Manual brainstorming is time-consuming and often results in repetitive ideas.

The workflow automatically generates fresh lead magnet concepts by combining your topic list with AI-powered suggestions from RapidAPI. It monitors a Google Sheet for new topics, processes them through the AI API, and returns formatted ideas ready for your content team.

How It Works

Step 1: Sheet Monitoring

The Google Drive trigger detects new or updated rows in your designated spreadsheet, initiating the automation whenever you add topics.

Step 2: Data Processing

The workflow checks each row for completeness, ensuring topics are present while the content field is empty before proceeding.

Step 3: AI Generation

Valid topics are sent to the Lead Magnet Idea Generator API which returns multiple format suggestions (checklists, templates, guides etc.) tailored to each topic.

Step 4: Results Logging

The AI-generated ideas are written back to your sheet with timestamps, creating an audit trail of when each was created.
