What This Workflow Does

Creating engaging LinkedIn carousels is time-consuming. Marketers and content teams spend hours writing captions, designing slides, and managing files. This workflow automates the entire process from raw text to published-ready carousels.

The system takes any LinkedIn post or text input, uses Mistral AI to generate 3 short, slide-ready caption pairs (title + subtext), renders them onto your brand templates, uploads the finished images to S3 storage, and delivers a complete markdown response with image embeds and download links. It eliminates manual design work while maintaining brand consistency.

How It Works

1. Text Input & AI Processing

The workflow starts with a chat trigger that accepts plain text. The AI agent cleans the input (removing stray formatting while keeping emojis) and produces structured JSON with exactly 3 caption pairs. Each title is limited to 5 words, with subtexts being 1-2 short, value-adding sentences.

2. Template Rendering

Edit Image nodes place the generated captions onto your chosen templates from Google Drive. The system handles font styling, size, and positioning automatically, ensuring professional design consistency across all slides.

3. Cloud Storage & Delivery

Rendered images are uploaded to S3 storage with safe filenames. The workflow then aggregates all images, generates public URLs, and creates a final markdown output with inline image embeds and individual download links—ready to copy and paste into LinkedIn.

This automation is ideal for content marketers, social media managers, solopreneurs, and agencies who regularly publish LinkedIn carousels. It's particularly valuable for teams producing educational content, product updates, or thought leadership pieces where consistency and volume matter. Businesses with established brand templates will see the most immediate benefit.

What You'll Need

  1. Mistral Cloud account for AI caption generation
  2. Google Drive with your carousel template images
  3. S3-compatible storage (like AWS S3 or Supabase Storage) for rendered images
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  5. Brand templates in PNG format with consistent aspect ratios

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Mistral Cloud, Google Drive, and S3 credentials
  3. Upload your brand templates to Google Drive (or update the workflow to use your template source)
  4. Test with sample text to verify caption generation and rendering
  5. Adjust font styles and positions in the Edit Image nodes to match your templates
  6. Deploy the workflow and integrate it into your content creation process

Pro tip: Create multiple template sets for different content types (educational, promotional, case studies) and modify the workflow to select templates based on content category. This maintains brand consistency while providing visual variety.
