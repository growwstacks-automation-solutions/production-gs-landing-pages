What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually creating marketing banners for social media, email campaigns, and digital ads. Marketing teams often struggle to quickly turn messaging into visually compelling content, especially when responding to real-time customer interactions on platforms like LINE.

The workflow automatically transforms text messages from LINE into professionally designed banners using Google Gemini's AI capabilities for content optimization and Nano Banana Pro's design automation. It eliminates the back-and-forth between copywriters and designers, allowing your team to produce more content in less time while maintaining brand consistency.

How It Works

1. LINE message trigger

The workflow begins when a new message arrives in your designated LINE channel. This could be a promotional copy from your marketing team, customer feedback worth highlighting, or product details needing visual treatment.

2. Content processing with Gemini

Google Gemini analyzes the message text to extract key selling points, identify emotional triggers, and suggest optimal headline formulations. The AI enhances raw text into marketing-ready copy optimized for visual presentation.

3. Banner generation with Nano Banana Pro

The refined content gets passed to Nano Banana Pro which applies your brand's design templates, automatically selecting layouts that best suit the message length and content type. It handles image selection, typography hierarchy, and color application.

4. Output delivery

The finished banner gets saved to your designated storage location (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) and can optionally be shared back to LINE or sent to your marketing team for review.
