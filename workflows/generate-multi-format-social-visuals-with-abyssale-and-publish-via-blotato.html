What This Workflow Does

Managing social media across multiple platforms is a constant battle against time and consistency. Each network demands different image dimensions—Facebook posts, Instagram stories, LinkedIn banners, Twitter headers—forcing marketers to manually resize, reformat, and re-upload the same content. This workflow eliminates that grind.

It automates the entire social media visual pipeline: from AI-powered product image generation to template-based design creation in Abyssale, to automatic publishing across eight different social platforms via Blotato. What used to take hours of manual work now happens with a single trigger, ensuring brand consistency and freeing your team for strategic tasks.

The system handles background removal, dynamic text insertion, format-specific resizing, and channel-aware publishing—turning a product idea into a live multi-platform campaign in minutes instead of days.

How It Works

This end-to-end automation connects AI image generation, design automation, and social media publishing into one seamless process.

1. AI Product Image Generation

The workflow starts by creating a product visual using AI. You provide a text description, and the system generates a high-quality image with a transparent background, ready for placement in your designs.

2. Template-Based Design in Abyssale

The generated image is automatically injected into your pre-configured Abyssale template. The system dynamically updates text elements (like headlines, captions, CTAs) and generates all required format variations—square posts, landscape banners, story dimensions—maintaining perfect brand alignment.

3. Smart Format Routing

A switch node intelligently routes each formatted visual to its corresponding social platform. Facebook posts go to Facebook, Instagram stories to Instagram, LinkedIn graphics to LinkedIn, ensuring each platform receives the optimally sized asset.

4. Multi-Channel Publishing via Blotato

Blotato nodes handle the authenticated publishing to each connected social media account. The workflow can include captions, hashtags, scheduling, and even platform-specific optimizations, all automated.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Abyssale account with API credentials and at least one template configured
  3. Blotato account connected to your social media platforms
  4. AI image generation API access (like AtlasCloud/NanoBanana or similar)
  5. Background removal service (often included with image generation APIs)
  6. Social media accounts you want to automate (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Pinterest, TikTok)

Pro tip: Start with just 2-3 key social platforms to test the workflow before expanding to all eight channels. This lets you refine your templates and posting strategy without being overwhelmed.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in your n8n instance in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the JSON template file into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure credentials for Abyssale, Blotato, and your AI image service in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Set your Abyssale template ID in the appropriate node to use your branded design.
  4. Connect Blotato nodes to each social media account you want to publish to.
  5. Test with a simple trigger (like manual execution) to generate and publish one visual.
  6. Add your production trigger—this could be a webhook from your CMS, a scheduled timer, or a form submission.
