Generate Multi-Platform Content from Forms Using AI

Transform form submissions into tailored blog posts, LinkedIn updates, and Facebook content instantly with Tavily Research and OpenAI.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for marketers and business owners: creating consistent, high-quality content across multiple platforms is time-consuming and resource-intensive. Manually researching topics, writing blog posts, crafting LinkedIn updates, and creating Facebook content can take hours for each piece.

The workflow automates this entire process. When someone submits a form with a content topic and target audience, the system automatically researches the topic using Tavily's web search API, then uses OpenAI to generate three different content pieces: a comprehensive blog post, a professional LinkedIn article, and an engaging Facebook post. All three versions are delivered via Slack for easy review and distribution.

This isn't just simple content generation—it's intelligent, research-backed content creation that produces platform-optimized material ready for immediate use, saving 5-10 hours of work per content piece.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits a simple web form. The form captures the content subject and target audience—just two fields that kickstart the entire automation process.

2. Web Research with Tavily

The system takes the submitted topic and performs real-time web research using Tavily API. It gathers current, relevant information from multiple sources, ensuring the generated content is accurate and up-to-date.

3. AI Content Generation

OpenAI processes the research results and creates three distinct content pieces. Each piece is specifically tailored for its platform: long-form SEO-friendly blog content, professional LinkedIn thought leadership, and engaging Facebook social content.

4. Aggregation and Delivery

All three content pieces are compiled into a single output and sent via Slack notification. This gives your team one place to review, edit if needed, and schedule the content for publication.

This template is ideal for content marketing agencies managing multiple client accounts, small business owners wearing multiple hats, marketing teams with limited resources, solo entrepreneurs building their personal brand, and any organization that needs to maintain an active content presence across blog and social platforms.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. OpenAI API access with available credits
  3. Tavily API key for web research functionality
  4. Slack workspace for content delivery
  5. A simple web form (can be built within n8n or connected from another service)

Pro tip: Start with a limited set of test topics to fine-tune your AI prompts before scaling up. This ensures the generated content matches your brand voice and quality standards from day one.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template and import it into your n8n workspace
  2. Configure the form trigger with your desired fields (content subject and target audience)
  3. Add your OpenAI API credentials to the chat model nodes
  4. Sign up for Tavily API and add your key to the HTTP request node
  5. Connect your Slack workspace and specify the delivery channel
  6. Test with a sample submission to verify the entire flow works correctly
