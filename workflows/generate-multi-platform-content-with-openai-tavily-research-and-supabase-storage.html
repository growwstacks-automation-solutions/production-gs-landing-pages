What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating consistent, high-quality content across multiple platforms while maintaining efficiency. Marketing teams often struggle to produce enough variations of content tailored for websites, blogs, and landing pages - this workflow automates that entire process.

By connecting AI research tools with content generation and storage systems, it transforms simple topic inputs into complete content packages. The workflow handles research via Tavily, drafts content using OpenAI, manages assets through Google Drive and NextCloud, then stores everything in Supabase - all with built-in error handling.

The workflow architecture showing integration points between services

How It Works

1. Content Trigger

Google Sheets monitors for new content requests with topics and associated image URLs. Each new row initiates the workflow.

2. Automated Research

Tavily's API conducts advanced searches, returning 3 relevant articles to inform the content creation.

3. AI Content Generation

OpenAI's GPT-4 creates three versions of the content: website copy, blog article, and landing page text - each optimized for their platform.

4. Asset Processing

Images are downloaded from Google Drive, uploaded to NextCloud, and public URLs are generated for embedding.

5. Database Storage

All content versions, metadata and image URLs are stored in Supabase for easy retrieval and management.
