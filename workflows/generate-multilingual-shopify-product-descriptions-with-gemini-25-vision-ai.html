What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating localized product descriptions for international Shopify stores. Manually translating product content is time-consuming, expensive, and often results in inconsistent quality across languages. The workflow uses Gemini 2.5 Vision AI to analyze product images and existing descriptions, then generates culturally adapted translations while maintaining SEO value.

By automating multilingual content creation, e-commerce businesses can expand into new markets faster while ensuring brand consistency across all language versions. The AI handles technical specifications, material descriptions, and marketing copy adaptations, reducing translation costs by up to 70% compared to human translation services.

How It Works

1. Product data extraction

The workflow pulls product information from your Shopify store including titles, descriptions, images, variants, and metadata. This serves as the foundation for AI-generated translations.

2. AI analysis and translation

Gemini 2.5 Vision AI processes the product visuals and existing content to understand context, materials, and key features. It then generates localized descriptions in your specified target languages.

3. Quality validation

The system checks translations for accuracy, cultural appropriateness, and SEO preservation before pushing to Shopify. You can configure approval steps for human review if needed.

4. Shopify integration

Translated descriptions are automatically added to your Shopify products as metafields or through the multilingual app of your choice, ready for international storefronts.

Pro tip: For best results, provide the AI with reference translations of 5-10 key product terms to maintain brand voice consistency across languages.
