What This Workflow Does

This innovative automation transforms your Spotify listening habits into personalized news digests. By analyzing the emotional tone of your recently played tracks, it creates AI-curated content that matches your musical mood.

The system fetches trending news articles related to your dominant listening emotion, formats them into visually appealing cards using APITemplate.io, and delivers them to your Slack channel. Perfect for content creators, marketers, or anyone who wants to automate mood-based information discovery.

How It Works

Step 1: Spotify Data Collection

The workflow starts by accessing your Spotify account through its API to retrieve your recently played tracks. This includes song titles, artists, and metadata that will be analyzed for emotional content.

Step 2: Emotion Analysis

An LLM (Large Language Model) processes the musical data to determine the dominant emotional tone. The system looks for patterns in song titles, lyrics (when available), and artist styles to categorize your listening mood.

Step 3: News Matching

Using the identified emotion as a keyword, the workflow queries Google News RSS feeds to find trending articles that match your current musical mood. The top relevant result is selected for card creation.

Step 4: Visual Card Generation

APITemplate.io transforms the selected news article into a visually appealing card format, combining the article content with design elements that reflect the detected emotion.

Step 5: Slack Delivery

The completed news card is posted to your designated Slack channel along with the original article link, creating a seamless mood-matched content delivery system.
