What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-intensive challenge of converting written news into engaging video content. Media companies, content creators, and marketing teams struggle to keep up with video demand across platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. Manual video production requires editing skills, equipment, and significant time investment for each piece of content.

The workflow automatically monitors RSS feeds from news sources, extracts the latest articles, and converts them into professional videos using HeyGen's AI avatar technology. Each video features a realistic digital presenter who narrates the news with proper lip-sync and expressions, creating content that appears professionally produced without human intervention.

Beyond basic conversion, the system handles formatting, timing, and API communication to generate videos ready for immediate publishing. This transforms your text-based content pipeline into a multimedia production engine, allowing you to reach video-first audiences with minimal additional effort.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow begins by checking your configured RSS feed for new articles. It can monitor multiple sources simultaneously and filter content based on keywords, categories, or publication dates. Each new item triggers the video creation process, ensuring your video content stays current with your text publications.

2. Content Processing & Script Preparation

The system extracts the article summary or full text, cleans the content for optimal AI narration, and formats it into a proper video script. This includes adjusting sentence length for natural pacing, removing incompatible characters, and structuring the content for clear visual presentation alongside the avatar.

3. HeyGen API Integration

The formatted script is sent to HeyGen's video generation API with your preferred avatar selection, voice type, and video dimensions. The API creates a realistic AI presenter video with synchronized lip movements and natural gestures, turning text into a professional video presentation in minutes rather than hours.

4. Output Handling & Distribution

Once the video is generated, the workflow can save it to cloud storage, send notifications, or trigger additional distribution steps. Optional extensions include automatic YouTube uploads, social media posting, or integration with your content management system for scheduled publishing.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance using the "Import from File" option in the workflow editor.
  2. Configure HeyGen Credentials: Add your HeyGen API key to the HTTP Request node's headers. Test the connection with a simple script to ensure proper authentication.
  3. Set Your RSS Feed: Replace the sample RSS feed URL with your actual news source. Verify the feed structure matches the expected format for proper content extraction.
  4. Customize Video Settings: Adjust the avatar ID, voice selection, video dimensions, and background settings in the API request body to match your branding requirements.
  5. Test with One Article: Run the workflow manually with a single news item to verify video generation quality and timing before enabling automated scheduling.
  6. Add Distribution Steps (Optional): Extend the workflow with nodes to upload videos to YouTube, share on social media, or notify your team via Slack/Email.
  7. Schedule Automation: Set up a Cron trigger node to check the RSS feed at your desired frequency (hourly, daily, etc.) for continuous automated video production.
