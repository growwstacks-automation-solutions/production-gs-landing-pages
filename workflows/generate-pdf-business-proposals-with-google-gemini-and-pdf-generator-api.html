What This Workflow Does

This advanced n8n workflow automates the entire business proposal creation process from initial lead capture to professional PDF delivery. By integrating Google Gemini's AI capabilities with PDF Generator API, it transforms raw lead data into polished, customized proposals in minutes rather than hours.

The system handles everything from content generation to formatting and delivery, reducing manual work by 80% while maintaining professional quality. It's particularly valuable for service businesses that need to respond quickly to RFPs or create frequent client proposals.

How It Works

1. Lead Data Collection

The workflow begins by gathering all relevant client information from your CRM, intake forms, or email communications. This data serves as the foundation for personalizing the proposal content.

2. AI Content Generation

Google Gemini analyzes the collected data to generate proposal sections including project scope, methodology, timelines, and pricing. The AI references your past successful proposals to maintain consistent tone and structure.

3. PDF Assembly

The PDF Generator API takes the AI-generated content and formats it into a professional document with your company branding, logo, and design elements. It automatically creates tables, inserts images, and applies consistent styling.

4. Review & Delivery

The completed proposal goes through optional human review before being sent to the client via email or a secure link. The system tracks opens and downloads to help you follow up effectively.
