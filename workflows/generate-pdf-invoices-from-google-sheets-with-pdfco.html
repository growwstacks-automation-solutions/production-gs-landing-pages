What This Workflow Does

Manual invoice creation is one of the most time-consuming administrative tasks for small businesses, freelancers, and service providers. This workflow eliminates that burden by automatically transforming your Google Sheets invoice data into professional, ready-to-send PDF invoices.

The system pulls customer details, line items, quantities, prices, taxes, and totals directly from your spreadsheet and generates beautifully formatted PDF invoices using PDF.co's template engine. What normally takes 15-30 minutes per invoice happens in seconds, with perfect accuracy every time.

This automation ensures consistency in your branding, reduces calculation errors, accelerates your cash flow by getting invoices to clients immediately, and creates a digital paper trail for accounting purposes—all without manual intervention.

How It Works

Step 1: Read Invoice Data from Google Sheets

The workflow starts by connecting to your Google Sheets document where you manage invoice information. It reads rows containing customer details, services/products, quantities, prices, tax rates, and any discounts.

Step 2: Format Data for Invoice Template

A Code node processes the raw spreadsheet data, structuring it into the exact format required by your PDF.co invoice template. This includes calculating subtotals, applying taxes, and formatting currency values.

Step 3: Generate Professional PDF Invoice

The structured data is sent to PDF.co, which merges it with your custom HTML invoice template to create a polished PDF document. The template includes your logo, company details, terms, and professional styling.

Step 4: Deliver or Store the Invoice

The generated PDF can be automatically saved to Google Drive, attached to an email sent to the client, or stored in your document management system—completing the entire invoicing process without manual steps.

What You'll Need

  1. A Google Sheets document with your invoice data (client info, items, prices, taxes)
  2. A PDF.co account (free tier available) for PDF generation
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  4. Google OAuth2 credentials connected to n8n
  5. PDF.co API key from your dashboard
  6. Your custom HTML invoice template uploaded to PDF.co

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect Google Sheets by creating OAuth2 credentials in n8n and authorizing access to your spreadsheet.
  3. Set up PDF.co by creating a free account, getting your API key, and creating your invoice template in their dashboard.
  4. Configure the workflow nodes with your specific spreadsheet ID, worksheet name, and PDF.co template ID.
  5. Test with sample data to ensure the PDF generation works correctly before automating your real invoices.
  6. Schedule or trigger the workflow to run daily, weekly, or whenever new invoice data is added to your sheet.
