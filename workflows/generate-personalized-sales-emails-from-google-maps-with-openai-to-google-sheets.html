What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation and cold outreach are time-consuming, repetitive, and often yield low response rates. Sales teams spend hours searching for local businesses on Google Maps, visiting their websites to understand their offerings, and then drafting generic emails that get ignored.

This n8n workflow automates the entire process. It transforms you from a manual researcher into an automated prospecting engine. You define your target (e.g., "coffee shops in Austin"), and the system finds the businesses, intelligently scrapes their website content, uses OpenAI to craft a unique email referencing specific details from their site, and logs everything—including the final email draft—into a Google Sheet for your team to review and send.

The result is a scalable system for generating qualified leads with personalized outreach at a fraction of the time and cost, allowing your sales team to focus on conversations that actually convert.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical, step-by-step process to ensure high-quality, personalized output.

1. Configure Your Search & Service

You start by setting parameters in a configuration node: your target search query (location + industry), how many leads to fetch, and key details about the service or product you're selling, including its unique value proposition.

2. Scrape Leads from Google Maps

The workflow triggers the Apify Google Maps Scraper actor. This tool acts like a super-fast, automated user, searching Google Maps based on your criteria and returning a list of businesses with their names, addresses, phone numbers, and website URLs.

3. Analyze Each Business Website

For each business that has a website, the workflow sends an HTTP request to fetch the site's HTML. It then extracts and cleans the main text content, providing raw material about the business's services, tone, and customer focus.

4. Generate AI-Powered Personalized Emails

This is the core intelligence layer. The scraped website text, along with your service details, is sent to OpenAI (like GPT-4). A custom prompt instructs the AI to analyze the business and draft a compelling sales email subject and body that connects their specific offerings with your service's strengths.

5. Consolidate & Store in Google Sheets

Finally, all the data for each lead—original listing info, scraped website summary, and the AI-generated email draft—is structured and appended as a new row in your designated Google Sheet. This creates a ready-to-use outreach database.

Pro tip: Use the website analysis step to filter leads. For example, you can add a node to check if certain keywords (like "organic" or "family-owned") are present on the site, allowing you to only pursue leads that match your ideal customer profile.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n Instance: You can use the free, self-hosted version or n8n.cloud.
  2. Apify Account & Actor: A free Apify account is required to run the google-maps-scraper actor. You'll need to input your Apify API token.
  3. OpenAI API Key: An account with OpenAI and an API key (uses GPT-3.5-turbo or GPT-4) to generate the email content. Costs are minimal per lead.
  4. Google Cloud Project & Credentials: A Google Cloud project with the Sheets API enabled, and a service account JSON key to allow n8n to write to your Google Sheet.
  5. A Google Sheet: Create a sheet with the necessary column headers (provided in the template) where the data will be saved.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this workflow running in your environment in under 30 minutes.

  1. Download & Import: Click the "Download Template" button above to get the JSON file. In your n8n dashboard, go to Workflows > Import from File and select the downloaded file.
  2. Set Up Credentials: In n8n's Settings > Credentials, create new credentials for Apify (API Token), OpenAI (API Key), and Google Sheets (OAuth2 or Service Account).
  3. Configure the Workflow Nodes:
    • Open the first "Configuration" node and input your search query, service name, and key selling points.
    • Open the "Apify Google Maps Scraper" node and select your Apify credential.
    • Open the "OpenAI" node and select your OpenAI credential. You can modify the system prompt here to change the email's tone or structure.
    • Open the "Google Sheets" node, authenticate with your Google account, and select the spreadsheet and worksheet you created.
  4. Test with a Single Execution: Use the "Execute Workflow" button on the canvas. Set the "Max Places" in the config node to 1 or 2 for a test run to verify everything works and the email output looks good.
  5. Schedule or Trigger: Once tested, you can add a Schedule Trigger node to run this workflow daily or weekly, or a Manual Trigger to run it on-demand whenever you need a new batch of leads.

Pro tip: Before a full run, test the website scraping on a few URLs manually to ensure the HTTP Request node is correctly parsing the content. Some sites with heavy JavaScript may require a more advanced scraper setup.
