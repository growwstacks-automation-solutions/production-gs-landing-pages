What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized outreach while maintaining quality. Traditional sales emails often feel generic, while truly personalized emails take too much time to research and write manually.

The workflow automatically generates highly relevant emails by combining LinkedIn profile data with Claude 3.7's advanced natural language capabilities. It pulls key details from LinkedIn profiles to create emails that reference specific interests, job roles, and mutual connections—resulting in 3-5x higher response rates compared to generic templates.

How It Works

1. Lead List Processing

The workflow starts by reading your Google Sheet containing lead names, companies, and basic contact information. Each row represents a potential prospect you want to reach out to.

2. LinkedIn Profile Lookup

Using Apify's Google search integration, the workflow finds LinkedIn profiles matching each lead's name and company. It extracts key profile details like job title, recent posts, skills, and mutual connections.

Pro tip: Add a "personalization score" column to your spreadsheet to prioritize leads most likely to respond to personalized outreach.

3. AI Email Generation

The workflow sends the LinkedIn data to Claude 3.7 via OpenRouter, with instructions to craft a natural, personalized email that references specific profile details while maintaining your brand voice.

4. Email Delivery

The final personalized email can be delivered through your preferred email service (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) or added back to your CRM for manual review before sending.
