What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of creating platform-specific social media content efficiently. Instead of manually adapting the same message for different networks (which often leads to generic, underperforming posts), it uses GPT-4o to automatically generate optimized versions for six major platforms.

The system understands each platform's unique requirements - from LinkedIn's professional tone to TikTok's casual video scripts. It maintains your core message while adjusting length, style, hashtags, and formatting to maximize engagement on each network. Marketers report 2-3x better performance compared to cross-posting identical content.

How It Works

1. Input your core content

The workflow starts when you provide your base content - this could be a blog post, product announcement, or key message. The system extracts the essential information and prepares it for platform adaptation.

2. AI analyzes platform requirements

GPT-4o evaluates the content against each platform's best practices, including character limits, media preferences, and audience expectations. It identifies the most effective way to present your message on each network.

3. Generates platform-specific versions

The AI creates six distinct versions: professional articles for LinkedIn, concise threads for Twitter, visual-first posts for Instagram, short scripts for TikTok, engaging stories for Facebook, and pin-worthy descriptions for Pinterest.

4. Quality checks and outputs

Each version undergoes automated checks for platform compliance, brand voice consistency, and engagement potential. The final outputs are delivered in your preferred format - ready for review, scheduling, or immediate posting.
