What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical pain point for construction businesses - the time-consuming process of manually creating quotes from customer inquiries. When potential clients submit information through your JotForm, this workflow automatically generates professional, detailed quotes and sends them via email while storing all data in your Supabase CRM.

The system eliminates manual data entry, reduces quote preparation time from hours to minutes, and ensures consistent, branded communication with potential clients. It captures all lead information in your CRM for follow-up and analysis, helping you close more deals efficiently.

How It Works

1. Customer submits JotForm inquiry

The workflow triggers when a potential client submits your construction inquiry form. It captures all relevant details including project scope, measurements, materials, and contact information.

2. Data validation and processing

The system validates the submission, checks for completeness, and structures the data for quote generation. It can calculate material costs, labor estimates, and project timelines based on your predefined rates and parameters.

3. Professional quote generation

Using the validated data, the workflow generates a polished PDF quote with your branding, project details, pricing breakdown, terms, and validity period. The template ensures consistency across all your quotes.

4. CRM record creation

Simultaneously, the system creates a complete lead record in your Supabase CRM, capturing all inquiry details, the generated quote, and timestamps for follow-up tracking.

5. Automated email delivery

The workflow sends the professional quote to the customer via email with a personalized message. It can also notify your sales team and schedule follow-up reminders.

Pro tip: Configure your JotForm with conditional logic to capture all necessary details upfront, reducing back-and-forth and making your quotes more accurate.

What You'll Need

  1. An active JotForm account with your construction inquiry form
  2. A Supabase account configured as your CRM
  3. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  4. Email service (SMTP or transactional email provider)
  5. Your quote template (PDF or HTML format)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your JotForm account in the trigger node
  3. Configure your Supabase credentials in the CRM nodes
  4. Set up your email service in the email node
  5. Upload your quote template and adjust any pricing calculations
  6. Test with sample form submissions and activate the workflow
