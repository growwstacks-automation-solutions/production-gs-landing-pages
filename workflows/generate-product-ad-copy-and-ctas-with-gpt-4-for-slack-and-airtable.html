What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves the time-consuming challenge of creating fresh, compelling marketing copy for new products. Instead of staring at a blank page trying to craft the perfect message, your team can instantly generate professional-quality ad copy and call-to-action phrases with a single click.

The workflow connects your product database in Airtable with GPT-4's advanced language capabilities through n8n. When new products are added or updates are made, the system automatically generates multiple variations of marketing copy tailored to your brand voice and product specifications.

How It Works

1. Product data collection

The workflow monitors your Airtable base for new product entries or updates. It extracts key details like product name, features, benefits, target audience, and any specific messaging requirements.

2. AI copy generation

GPT-4 processes the product information through carefully crafted prompts that maintain your brand voice. The AI generates multiple variations of ad copy including social media posts, email content, and landing page text.

3. Delivery to Slack

The generated copy is sent to designated Slack channels where your marketing team can review, approve, or request revisions. Team members can also trigger new copy generation via Slack commands.

4. Storage in Airtable

All generated copy versions are automatically logged back to Airtable with timestamps, creating a searchable repository of marketing content tied directly to each product.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Airtable account and specify the base/table
  3. Configure your Slack webhook and channel settings
  4. Set up OpenAI API credentials with your API key
  5. Adjust the GPT-4 prompt templates to match your brand voice
  6. Test with sample product data and refine as needed
