What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually creating product announcements and marketing content from technical specifications. Marketing teams often spend hours translating engineering notes into customer-friendly communications, leading to bottlenecks in product launches.

The workflow automatically pulls product information from your Notion database, processes it through AI models (GPT-5 Mini and Claude), and generates polished Google Docs ready for review. It maintains consistent formatting, brand voice, and messaging across all your product communications while saving 4-6 hours per announcement.

How It Works

1. Notion Database Trigger

The workflow monitors your Notion product database for new or updated entries. When a product manager marks an item as "Ready for Announcement," the automation begins processing.

2. AI Content Generation

The system extracts key product details and feeds them to GPT-5 Mini for initial draft creation. Claude then refines the content for readability and brand alignment based on your style guidelines stored in Notion.

3. Google Docs Creation

The final content is formatted into a new Google Doc with proper headings, bullet points, and styling. The document includes placeholders for images and gets shared with your marketing team for final review.
