What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the costly and time-consuming challenge of creating professional product mockups for ecommerce businesses. Instead of organizing photoshoots or manually editing images, the workflow uses AI to seamlessly composite your product images onto model shots or lifestyle backgrounds.

The system handles everything from background removal to realistic shadow generation and perspective correction. It can process hundreds of product variations in minutes while maintaining consistent lighting and styling across all images - something nearly impossible to achieve with traditional photography at scale.

How It Works

1. Product Image Preparation

The workflow begins by automatically processing your product images - removing backgrounds, adjusting lighting consistency, and preparing them for composition.

2. Template Selection

Based on your product category and desired aesthetic, the system selects the most appropriate mockup template from your library or generates new ones using AI.

3. AI Composition

Gemini 2.5 Flash analyzes both the product image and template to create a photorealistic composite, automatically adjusting perspectives, shadows, and lighting for seamless integration.

4. Quality Control & Export

Each generated mockup undergoes automated quality checks before being formatted for your ecommerce platform and delivered to your designated storage location.
