What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the tedious, time-consuming process of creating professional email signatures for employees. Instead of manually designing signatures in Word or HTML for each team member, this automation generates polished, on-brand signatures in both HTML and PDF formats from a simple data input.

The system pulls employee details (name, title, contact information) from your HR system or spreadsheet, applies your company's branding template, and delivers the finished signatures directly to employees via email. This ensures consistency across your organization while eliminating hours of repetitive manual work for HR teams.

How It Works

1. Data Input

The workflow accepts employee data through multiple methods: API calls from your HR system, spreadsheet uploads, or manual entry. Required fields typically include name, job title, department, phone numbers, and email address.

2. Template Application

Your pre-designed HTML signature template (with company logo, colors, and layout) merges with the employee data. The system handles responsive design adjustments to ensure proper display across email clients.

3. PDF Conversion

Alongside the HTML version, the workflow generates a print-perfect PDF copy of each signature. This serves as an attachment for formal communications and provides an archive-quality version.

4. Delivery

The completed signatures get sent to employees via their preferred method - either as a direct Gmail/Outlook configuration, email attachment with setup instructions, or through your internal communication channels.
