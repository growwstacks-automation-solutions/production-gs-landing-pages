What This Workflow Does

Manual prospect research is time-consuming, inconsistent, and often misses key connection opportunities. This automation solves that by using Claude AI to analyze both your profile and your prospect's digital presence—social media, company websites, publications, and networks—to generate a comprehensive research report.

The workflow verifies identities, uncovers genuine shared interests, identifies mutual connections, and produces actionable insights. It delivers a personalized HTML report with conversation starters, engagement tactics, and strategic recommendations, transforming generic outreach into meaningful connection building.

Whether you're fundraising, campaigning, sales prospecting, or networking, this system ensures every outreach is informed, personalized, and strategically aligned with the prospect's background and interests.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Collection

The workflow accepts prospector and prospect names, social media URLs, specific outreach goals, and any initial context. This data triggers the research process.

Step 2: Multi-Source Research

Using a linked multi-tool research agent subworkflow, the system simultaneously gathers data from LinkedIn, company sites, news articles, and professional databases. It cross-references information to ensure accuracy.

Step 3: AI Analysis & Insight Generation

Claude AI processes the collected data, identifying patterns, connection points, shared values, and potential engagement angles. It moves beyond raw data to generate strategic insights.

Step 4: Report Compilation

The insights are structured into a comprehensive HTML report containing verified background, key connection opportunities, suggested conversation starters, recommended outreach timing, and engagement strategy.

Step 5: Output Delivery

The final report can be saved locally, emailed directly to your team, or integrated into your CRM to enrich contact records and trigger personalized outreach sequences.
