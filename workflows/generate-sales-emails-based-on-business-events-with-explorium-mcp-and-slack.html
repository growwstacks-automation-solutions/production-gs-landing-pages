What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of timely outreach when important business events occur with your prospects. Traditional sales teams often miss valuable opportunities because they don't have real-time visibility into trigger events like funding rounds, leadership changes, or product launches.

The workflow connects Explorium's Market Change Platform (MCP) with Slack to create an automated system that detects relevant business events, generates personalized outreach emails, and delivers them to your sales team via Slack for immediate action. This ensures your team always reaches out at the most opportune moments.

The workflow automatically generates sales emails based on Explorium event data

How It Works

1. Event Detection

The workflow continuously monitors Explorium MCP for specified business events like funding announcements, executive hires, or product updates. When a qualifying event occurs, the workflow captures all relevant details.

2. Prospect Matching

Each detected event is matched against your CRM or prospect list to identify which accounts should receive outreach. The workflow verifies contact information and ensures no duplicate outreach occurs.

3. Email Generation

Using predefined templates with dynamic fields, the workflow creates personalized emails that reference the specific event. Templates can include variables like company name, event details, and personalized value propositions.

4. Slack Notification

The completed emails are sent to a designated Slack channel where sales reps can review them before sending. This human-in-the-loop approach maintains quality control while still automating the heavy lifting.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for B2B sales teams that want to capitalize on timely events but lack the resources to constantly monitor every prospect. It's particularly valuable for:
