What This Workflow Does

This automated system solves the time-consuming challenge of manually finding and qualifying B2B leads. Traditional lead generation requires hours of Google searches, data entry, and guesswork to prioritize prospects. Our workflow automatically discovers local businesses on Google Maps, extracts their contact information, scores their potential value, and organizes everything in Google Sheets for your sales team.

The scoring system evaluates multiple factors including business size indicators, online presence strength, and customer engagement signals. This transforms raw business listings into actionable, prioritized leads - saving 10+ hours per week while improving lead quality. The workflow runs continuously, ensuring your pipeline stays full with fresh, qualified prospects.

How It Works

Step 1: Targeted Business Discovery

The workflow begins by searching Google Maps for businesses matching your ideal customer profile. You can specify locations, industries, and other filters to focus on relevant prospects. The system handles pagination to collect complete results beyond the first page.

Step 2: Data Extraction & Enrichment

For each business found, the workflow extracts key details including name, address, phone number, website, and review data. It then visits company websites when available to gather additional signals like contact forms, about pages, and service descriptions.

Step 3: Lead Scoring & Prioritization

Each lead receives a score based on multiple factors: review count and rating, website quality indicators, business age (when available), and completeness of contact information. Higher scores indicate warmer leads that deserve immediate attention.

Step 4: Organized Output

All collected data flows into a structured Google Sheet with columns for each data point plus the calculated lead score. The sheet automatically sorts by score, putting your best prospects at the top. Conditional formatting highlights key opportunities.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run weekly. This maintains a fresh lead pipeline and catches new businesses as they appear on Google Maps.
