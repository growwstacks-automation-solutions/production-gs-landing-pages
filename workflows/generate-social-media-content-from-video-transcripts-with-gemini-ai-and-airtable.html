What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of repurposing video content across multiple social media platforms. Content teams often spend hours manually transcribing videos, extracting key points, and rewriting content for different platforms—a process that's repetitive and inefficient.

The workflow automatically converts video transcripts into complete social media content suites. When a new video transcript is added to Airtable, the system triggers an AI analysis that generates YouTube titles and descriptions, Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, Instagram captions, TikTok scripts, and relevant hashtags—all formatted for each platform's specific requirements.

Everything is organized in Google Drive with the original transcript saved as a text file, and all generated content is stored back in Airtable for easy review and scheduling. This turns what was traditionally a 3-5 hour manual process into a fully automated 5-minute workflow.

How It Works

1. Trigger from Airtable

The workflow starts when a new video transcript record is added or updated in Airtable. A webhook captures the RecordId and action type, initiating the content generation pipeline without manual intervention.

2. Data Retrieval and Organization

The system fetches the complete record from Airtable, including the video name and transcript text. It then creates a dedicated Google Drive folder in your /tutorials directory to organize all generated content, storing the folder ID back in Airtable for future reference.

3. AI Content Analysis with Google Gemini

Google Gemini 2.5 Flash analyzes the transcript and generates structured JSON output containing all social media formats. The AI extracts key insights, creates engaging hooks, formats content for each platform's requirements, and suggests relevant tags.

4. Content Storage and Organization

The original transcript is saved as a text file in the Google Drive folder for archival purposes. All AI-generated content is then updated back to the Airtable record, with Twitter threads properly joined with newlines for clean formatting.

5. Ready for Review and Publishing

The complete content suite is now organized and accessible. Your team can review, make minor adjustments if needed, and schedule the content across all platforms—saving hours of manual work while ensuring consistency.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtable account with a base containing video transcript records
  2. Google Gemini API access (available through Google AI Studio)
  3. Google Drive account with folder creation permissions
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with webhook capabilities
  5. Basic understanding of your social media platforms' content requirements

Pro tip: Before implementing, review your most successful social media posts to identify patterns. Feed these examples to the AI as context to ensure generated content matches your proven winning style.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Airtable credentials for both the webhook trigger and record operations
  3. Set up Google Gemini API connection with your API key from Google AI Studio
  4. Connect Google Drive and specify your /tutorials directory path
  5. Test with a sample transcript to verify all connections and output formatting
  6. Set up Airtable automation to trigger the webhook when new transcripts are added
  7. Review and refine the AI prompts based on your brand voice and content style
