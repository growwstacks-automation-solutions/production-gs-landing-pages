What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms AI video creation from a manual, time-consuming process into a streamlined workflow. It generates creative ASMR video concepts using GPT-5.1, produces high-quality clips with Sora v2, stitches them together via Cloudinary, and automatically posts the final video to Twitter/X with optimized captions.

By connecting these cutting-edge AI tools, you can maintain a consistent content pipeline without daily manual work. The workflow handles everything from concept generation to final posting while tracking progress in Google Sheets. Failed renders are automatically skipped, and the system retries until successful.

How It Works

Concept Generation Phase

GPT-5.1 generates 3 unique ASMR cutting scene prompts with unusual objects (like glass avocados or rainbow soap) and creates Twitter-ready captions with relevant hashtags. All concepts are saved to Google Sheets for tracking.

Video Production Phase

The workflow creates 3 separate Sora v2 video clips (8-12 seconds each) in parallel at 720x1280 resolution. It monitors rendering status, downloads completed videos, and uploads them to Cloudinary for storage.

Post-Production Phase

Cloudinary stitches the clips into one seamless video with Twitter-compatible encoding (H.264 baseline, AAC audio, MP4 format). The workflow then handles Twitter's chunked upload API process and posts with the AI-generated caption.
