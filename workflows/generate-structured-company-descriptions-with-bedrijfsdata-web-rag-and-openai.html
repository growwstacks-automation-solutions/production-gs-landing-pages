What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for sales and marketing teams: manually researching companies is time-consuming and inconsistent. The workflow automatically generates structured, high-quality company descriptions by combining live web data from Bedrijfsdata.nl with OpenAI's language models.

Simply provide a company domain or Bedrijfsdata ID, and the system retrieves relevant website content, search engine data, and business information to produce ready-to-use profiles. These include detailed company descriptions, product/service overviews, and target audience analysis—perfect for enriching your CRM, qualifying leads, or personalizing outreach campaigns.

The output is structured JSON that can be directly integrated with your sales tools, ensuring consistent data quality across your organization while saving 15-30 minutes of research time per company.

How It Works

Step 1: Input & Validation

The workflow accepts either a Bedrijfsdata.nl company ID or a direct domain input. It validates the input to ensure required information is present before proceeding, with dedicated error handling for invalid data.

Step 2: Data Retrieval via Bedrijfsdata RAG

Using the Bedrijfsdata.nl API, the system retrieves comprehensive web content about the company through their RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) Domain and Search endpoints. This includes website text, search results, and business intelligence specific to the Dutch market.

Step 3: Content Analysis with OpenAI

The retrieved content is processed through OpenAI's language models with carefully crafted prompts to generate structured outputs. The AI analyzes the information to create coherent company descriptions, product/service summaries, and target audience profiles in Dutch.

Step 4: Structured Output Generation

The workflow produces clean JSON with three main sections: company_description (1-2 paragraph summary), products_and_services (detailed offerings), and target_audience (ideal customer characteristics including industry, location, and company size).

Step 5: Integration & Error Handling

The structured data can be sent to your CRM (like HubSpot), database, or other business tools. The workflow includes comprehensive error handling for API failures, LLM errors, and integration issues to ensure reliability.
