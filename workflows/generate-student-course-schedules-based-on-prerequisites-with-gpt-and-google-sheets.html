What This Workflow Does

This automation creates optimized course schedules for students based on their academic history, program requirements, and course prerequisites. It reads student data from Google Sheets, uses AI to determine the best course selections, and writes the schedules back to a spreadsheet.

The system enforces institutional rules including prerequisite chains, term availability (Fall/Both), credit hour targets (15-17), and prevents duplicate or already-completed courses. It prioritizes major requirements while including general education courses as needed.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow begins by reading student records from a Google Sheet containing StudentID, Name, Program, Year, and pipe-separated lists of completed courses.

2. AI Decision Making

An AI scheduling agent analyzes each student's history against the course catalog to select exactly 5 courses that meet all requirements while progressing the student's degree path.

3. Output Generation

The selected courses are split into individual rows and appended to a schedule worksheet with the student ID for advisor review and student distribution.
