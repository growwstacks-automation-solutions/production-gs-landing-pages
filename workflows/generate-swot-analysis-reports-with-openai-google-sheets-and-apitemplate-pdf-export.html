What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms hours of manual SWOT analysis work into a streamlined process that delivers professional reports in minutes. By connecting OpenAI's AI capabilities with your business data in Google Sheets, it automatically generates comprehensive Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats analyses.

The workflow structures raw company data, applies AI categorization, generates narrative explanations for each SWOT element, formats everything into a polished document, and can even email the final PDF to stakeholders - all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

Pulls company information from your prepared Google Sheet template containing key business metrics, competitor data, and market insights.

2. AI Analysis

OpenAI processes the data to intelligently categorize information into the four SWOT quadrants while generating insightful commentary.

3. Report Assembly

Combines AI-generated content with your branding to create a structured document with introduction, analysis sections, and conclusion.

4. PDF Generation

Converts the assembled content into a professionally formatted multi-page PDF report ready for presentations or investor reviews.

5. Distribution

Optionally emails the final report to specified recipients or saves it back to your Google Drive for easy access.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the Google Sheet template included with this workflow
  2. Import the JSON file into your n8n dashboard

  4. Customize the report template with your branding
  5. Test with sample data and refine prompts as needed
  6. Schedule to run automatically or trigger manually
