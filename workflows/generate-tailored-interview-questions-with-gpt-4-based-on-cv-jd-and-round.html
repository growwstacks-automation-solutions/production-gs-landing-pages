What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered workflow revolutionizes interview preparation by automatically generating customized questions based on three key inputs: the candidate's CV, the job description, and the specific interview round. It eliminates the time-consuming manual process of crafting questions while ensuring each interview is precisely targeted to assess relevant skills and competencies.

The system uses GPT-4 to analyze textual inputs and create questions that progress appropriately through hiring stages - from initial screening to technical assessments and final cultural fit evaluations. This creates a structured, objective interview process that reduces bias and improves hiring quality.

The workflow analyzes CV and JD inputs to generate targeted interview questions

How It Works

1. Input Collection

The workflow begins by collecting three key documents: the candidate's CV/resume, the detailed job description, and specifications about the interview round (technical screening, cultural fit, etc.). These can be uploaded manually or pulled automatically from your ATS.

2. Document Analysis

GPT-4 processes the documents to extract key information - skills from the CV, requirements from the JD, and contextual understanding of the interview stage. The AI identifies relevant technical competencies, experience levels, and soft skills to assess.

3. Question Generation

Based on the analysis, the system generates 8-12 tailored questions per interview round. Technical rounds receive coding challenges or system design questions, while later stages get behavioral and situational questions aligned with company values.

4. Output Delivery

The finalized questions are formatted and delivered to the interviewer via email, Slack, or directly into your hiring platform. Optional scoring rubrics can be included for consistent evaluation.

Pro tip: For best results, provide detailed job descriptions that include not just technical requirements but also team dynamics and success metrics for the role.
