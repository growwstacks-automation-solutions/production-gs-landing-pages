How It Works

1. Product Image Input & Analysis

The workflow begins when a new product image is received, typically via Telegram upload, eCommerce platform webhook, or scheduled import. OpenAI's Vision API analyzes the image to identify product features, colors, materials, and potential use cases. This analysis forms the foundation for creating contextually relevant video content.

2. AI Script Generation

Based on the visual analysis, GPT-based AI generates a natural, conversational script in UGC style. The system considers your target audience demographics, brand voice preferences, and platform-specific best practices. You can customize prompts to emphasize different angles—educational, emotional, luxury-focused, or problem-solving narratives.

3. Video Creation with Sora 2

The analyzed image and generated script are sent to Sora 2 via the Fal API to create a vertical promotional video. The workflow specifies optimal parameters: 9:16 aspect ratio for mobile platforms, 4-12 second duration for short-form content, and appropriate motion settings. The system monitors generation status and handles retries if needed.

4. Output & Distribution

Once the video is ready, it's downloaded to your storage system. The workflow can automatically publish to social platforms via Blotato's API, schedule posts for optimal timing, or notify your team for review. Metadata including generated script, performance predictions, and platform-specific optimizations are saved for analytics.

Pro tip: For maximum engagement, generate 3-5 slightly different scripts per product and A/B test them. The AI can create variations focusing on different benefits (convenience, style, value) to see what resonates best with your audience.
