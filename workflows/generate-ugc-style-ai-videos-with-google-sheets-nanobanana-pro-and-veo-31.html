What This Workflow Does

This automation workflow solves the challenge of creating authentic-looking user-generated content (UGC) videos at scale. Traditional UGC campaigns require recruiting real users, collecting submissions, and manually editing footage - a time-consuming and inconsistent process.

The template automates video production by connecting Google Sheets (for script management), NanoBanana Pro (for AI voice generation), and Veo 3.1 (for AI video synthesis). It transforms spreadsheet rows into professional-quality videos with synchronized voiceovers and visuals, maintaining brand consistency across hundreds of variations.

How It Works

1. Spreadsheet Input

The workflow pulls video scripts, product details, and customization parameters from a Google Sheet. Each row becomes a unique video variation with its own script, tone, and visual style parameters.

2. AI Voice Generation

NanoBanana Pro converts the text scripts into natural-sounding voiceovers. The system can maintain consistent vocal characteristics across videos while adjusting pacing and emphasis based on the script content.

3. Video Synthesis

Veo 3.1 combines the generated voiceovers with AI visuals to create finished videos. The system can produce talking-head testimonials, product demos, or scenario-based content based on the input parameters.

4. Output Delivery

Completed videos are automatically uploaded to your preferred storage platform (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) and organized by campaign. Metadata and analytics can be logged back to your spreadsheet.

Pro tip: Create a library of video templates in Veo 3.1 first, then reference these template IDs in your spreadsheet for consistent styling across campaigns.
