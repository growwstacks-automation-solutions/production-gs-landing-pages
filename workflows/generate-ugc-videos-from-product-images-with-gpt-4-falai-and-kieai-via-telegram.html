Generate UGC Videos from Product Images with AI Automation

Transform any product image into authentic user-generated content videos using GPT-4, Fal.ai, and KIE.ai via Telegram

What This Workflow Does

Creating authentic user-generated content (UGC) is one of the most effective marketing strategies for e-commerce, but it's traditionally slow, expensive, and difficult to scale. This workflow solves that problem by automating the entire UGC creation process using advanced AI models.

When you upload a product image via Telegram, the system analyzes it with GPT-4 Vision, generates realistic UGC-style product images with Fal.ai, creates compelling video scenes with KIE.ai, and delivers the final videos back to you—all within minutes. What used to take weeks and thousands of dollars in influencer campaigns now happens automatically for any product in your catalog.

How It Works

1. Image Upload & Analysis

The workflow starts when you send a product image to a Telegram bot. GPT-4 Vision analyzes the image, identifying key product features, colors, materials, and potential usage scenarios. This analysis generates detailed descriptions that inform the subsequent creative steps.

2. UGC Image Generation

Using the analysis from GPT-4, Fal.ai's nano-banana model creates realistic product images with authentic UGC styling. These aren't sterile product shots—they're images that look like real customers took them in natural environments, complete with appropriate lighting, backgrounds, and context.

3. Video Scene Creation

The system generates three distinct video scenes following proven UGC patterns: a hook scene to grab attention, a product demonstration scene showing features and benefits, and a call-to-action scene encouraging engagement or purchase. KIE.ai's Veo3 model produces each scene with authentic human-like presentation.

4. Video Compilation & Delivery

Fal.ai merges the individual video scenes into a cohesive final video, adding smooth transitions and optimizing for social media platforms. The completed UGC video, along with the generated images, is sent back through Telegram, ready for immediate use in your marketing campaigns.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance – Self-hosted or cloud version
  2. Telegram Bot Token – Create a bot via @BotFather
  3. OpenAI API Key – With GPT-4 Vision access
  4. Fal.ai Account – For image generation and video merging
  5. KIE.ai Account – For video scene generation
  6. Product Images – Clear photos of your products

Pro tip: Start with your best-selling products first. The AI models produce better results with clear, well-lit images showing the product from multiple angles. Include context in your images—a shirt on a hanger works better than a flat white background.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure the Telegram trigger node with your bot token and set up webhooks.

3. Add your API keys for OpenAI, Fal.ai, and KIE.ai in their respective nodes.

4. Test the workflow by sending a product image to your Telegram bot.

5. Adjust the prompt templates in the Set nodes to match your brand voice and product style.

6. Activate the workflow and start generating UGC content on demand.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Telegram channel for your team to submit product images and receive generated content. This keeps everything organized and allows multiple team members to use the automation without technical setup.
