Generate Videos from Chat with Google Vertex AI (Veo3)

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms text descriptions into professional-quality videos using Google Vertex AI's Veo3 model. Simply provide a text prompt describing your desired video, and the workflow handles the entire generation process automatically. It's perfect for content creators, marketers, and businesses needing to produce video content at scale without video production expertise.

The workflow connects chat interfaces (like Slack or Teams) to Vertex AI's powerful video generation API. It includes prompt optimization, quality checks, and automatic saving to your preferred storage location. What normally takes hours of video editing can now be done in minutes with consistent, brand-aligned results.

How It Works

1. Submit Your Video Prompt

Type or paste your video description into your connected chat platform. For best results, include details about scenes, actions, style, and duration.

2. Automatic Prompt Optimization

The workflow enhances your input with best practices for AI video generation, ensuring Vertex AI interprets your request accurately.

3. Video Generation

Vertex AI's Veo3 model processes the optimized prompt and generates a high-quality video clip (typically 10-60 seconds).

4. Quality Check & Delivery

The system verifies the output meets standards, then delivers it to your specified destination (Google Drive, YouTube, CMS, etc.).
