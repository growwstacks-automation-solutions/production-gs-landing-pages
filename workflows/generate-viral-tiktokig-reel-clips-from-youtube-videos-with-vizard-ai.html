What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of repurposing long-form YouTube content into engaging short-form clips for TikTok and Instagram Reels. Content creators often struggle to manually identify the most viral-worthy moments in their videos, leading to inconsistent posting schedules and missed opportunities.

The workflow integrates Vizard AI's powerful video analysis capabilities with n8n's automation platform to automatically scan YouTube videos, detect high-engagement segments based on visual and audio cues, and generate perfectly formatted clips ready for TikTok and Instagram Reels. This transforms hours of manual editing work into a fully automated process.

How It Works

1. YouTube video input

The workflow begins by monitoring your YouTube channel for new uploads or can process existing videos from a playlist. It extracts the video URL and metadata to prepare for analysis.

2. AI-powered clip detection

Vizard AI analyzes the video content, identifying key moments based on visual excitement (scene changes, text overlays), audio spikes (laughter, emphasis), and content patterns (transcript analysis for key phrases).

3. Automatic clip generation

The system generates multiple clip options (typically 3-5 per video) in the ideal 9:16 aspect ratio for mobile viewing, with automatic cropping to maintain focus on the main subject.

4. Platform-specific formatting

Clips are automatically formatted with captions (extracted from the transcript), branded overlays if configured, and saved in the optimal resolution and format for each platform.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to save clips to a shared cloud folder with your team for review before posting, allowing for final human curation while still saving 90% of the editing time.
