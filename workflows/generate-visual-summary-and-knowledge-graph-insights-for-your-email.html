What This Workflow Does

This powerful n8n workflow transforms your email inbox into a strategic knowledge asset. It automatically analyzes Gmail content, extracts key concepts, and generates visual summaries that reveal patterns and connections you might otherwise miss. The system creates knowledge graphs that show how different topics in your emails relate to each other, helping you make better decisions based on your communication data.

Traditional email management focuses on organization and search, but this workflow goes further by providing cognitive insights. How It Works

1. Email content extraction

The workflow connects to your Gmail account and extracts text content from selected emails. It can process entire threads, focusing on the most recent messages or analyzing historical data based on your configuration.

2. AI-powered concept extraction

Natural language processing identifies key entities, topics, and relationships within the email content. The system categorizes information and weights concepts based on frequency and contextual importance.

3. Knowledge graph generation

The workflow builds a dynamic graph structure showing how extracted concepts connect. Stronger relationships appear as thicker lines between nodes, while isolated concepts may represent new opportunities or knowledge gaps.

4. Visual summary creation

Finally, the system generates easy-to-understand visualizations including concept maps, topic clusters, and trend charts. These summaries update automatically as new emails arrive that match your criteria.
