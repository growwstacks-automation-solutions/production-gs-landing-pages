What This Workflow Does

Animal rights organizations spend countless hours manually scanning legislation for relevant bills, trying to identify opportunities for advocacy before critical deadlines pass. This automated workflow solves that problem by systematically monitoring U.S. House bills, identifying animal-related legislation using AI, and generating weekly briefings with clear action steps.

The system pulls new bills daily, uses Gemini AI to analyze their content for animal welfare relevance, summarizes key provisions in plain language, and creates prioritized action items with deadlines. This transforms what was traditionally a labor-intensive research process into an efficient, automated system that keeps your team focused on advocacy rather than administration.

How It Works

1. Daily bill monitoring

The workflow automatically checks the U.S. House of Representatives website for newly introduced bills, capturing their full text and metadata. It filters for bills in committees most likely to handle animal-related legislation.

2. AI content analysis

Gemini AI scans each bill's text to identify animal welfare relevance, scoring bills based on keywords, provisions, and contextual understanding of animal rights issues. This goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand nuanced legislative language.

3. Actionable briefing creation

For relevant bills, the AI generates concise summaries highlighting key provisions, potential impact, and specific advocacy opportunities. It identifies deadlines for comments, hearings, or votes and suggests appropriate action steps tailored to your organization's focus areas.

4. Weekly distribution

The system compiles all relevant bills into a polished weekly briefing document, organized by priority level and action timeline. This gets automatically delivered to your team via email or shared drive in your preferred format.
