What This Workflow Does

This automation solution eliminates the tedious manual process of compiling weekly energy consumption reports. It connects directly to EnergiDataService API to pull the latest consumption metrics, formats the data into professional reports, and distributes them automatically via email and Google Drive.

Facility managers, sustainability teams, and operations leaders spend hours each week gathering energy data from multiple sources, compiling spreadsheets, and sending reports. This workflow automates the entire process, ensuring stakeholders receive accurate, timely reports without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Data Retrieval from EnergiDataService

The workflow begins by connecting to the EnergiDataService API to fetch the latest energy consumption metrics. It automatically handles authentication and pulls the relevant data points based on predefined parameters like time period, meter IDs, and energy types.

2. Data Processing and Analysis

Raw API data gets transformed into actionable insights. The workflow calculates week-over-week comparisons, identifies consumption anomalies, and computes cost implications based on current energy rates.

3. Report Generation

The system generates professional PDF reports with charts, tables, and executive summaries. Reports follow your organization's branding guidelines and include all key metrics decision-makers need.

4. Automated Distribution

Completed reports get saved to a designated Google Drive folder with proper naming conventions and timestamps. Simultaneously, email notifications with report links get sent to predefined distribution lists.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run every Monday morning to ensure stakeholders have fresh reports at the start of each work week.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for facilities management companies, corporate sustainability teams, property managers, and industrial operations with significant energy consumption. Any organization tracking energy usage across multiple sites or meters will benefit from automated reporting.
