What This Workflow Does

Creating high-converting video content is time-consuming and repetitive. You need to brainstorm ideas, write scripts, generate visuals, adapt content for each platform, and manually publish—often taking 5–10 hours per video. This workflow automates that entire process, turning a single idea into a ready-to-publish video campaign across 9 platforms in minutes.

It connects AI video generation (Google's VEO3) with multi-platform distribution (Blotato) through n8n automation. The workflow triggers when a new video idea is submitted, generates the script and video using AI, optimizes captions for each platform, and auto-posts to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Threads, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Bluesky—all while logging results for tracking.

How It Works

1. Idea Submission & Trigger

The workflow starts when a team member submits a video idea via Telegram. The trigger captures the concept, style preferences, and target duration.

2. Parameter Fetching & Script Generation

It pulls additional parameters from Google Sheets (brand voice, tone, keywords) and uses GPT-4 with a master AI prompt to generate a compelling video script tailored to the idea.

3. AI Video Creation

The script is sent to Google's VEO3 video generation model, which creates the actual video with visuals, motion, and audio based on the prompt. The workflow monitors rendering and downloads the final video file.

4. Caption Optimization & Platform Preparation

GPT-4o rewrites the caption for each target platform, ensuring platform-specific hooks, hashtags, and formatting that increase engagement rates.

5. Multi-Platform Distribution

Blotato posts the video to all 9 configured platforms simultaneously with the optimized captions. Each platform receives the correctly formatted version.

6. Tracking & Notification

The workflow logs the published URLs, performance data, and any errors in Google Sheets for analytics. It also sends preview links to Telegram for team review.

Pro tip: Use this template as a starting point. You can add approval steps (Slack/email), integrate with your analytics dashboard, or trigger based on trending topics from social listening tools.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n (self-hosted) with Community Nodes enabled
  2. Telegram Bot Token for idea submission triggers
  3. OpenAI API Key for GPT-4 and GPT-4o script generation
  4. VEO3 API access via Google AI Studio
  5. Blotato account with platform tokens for TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, etc.
  6. Google Sheets with column structure for ideas, styles, and tracking

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to import and configure the template in your n8n instance:

  1. Download the template using the button above and save the JSON file.
  2. Import into n8n by going to your n8n dashboard, clicking "Workflows", then "Import from file", and selecting the downloaded JSON.
  3. Configure credentials for each node: Telegram, OpenAI, Google Sheets, VEO3, and Blotato.
  4. Test the trigger by sending a test message to your Telegram bot in the format: "idea: [your video concept]".
  5. Review the output in Google Sheets and check that videos are posting to your test platforms correctly.
  6. Customize prompts in the AI nodes to match your brand voice and content strategy.
  7. Activate the workflow and set it to run automatically when new ideas are submitted.
