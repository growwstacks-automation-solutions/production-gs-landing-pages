What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of slow lead response times by creating a real-time alert system for sales teams. When a visitor submits a contact form, MadKudu instantly scores the lead based on company fit and engagement signals. High-scoring leads get enriched with Hunter's contact data, triggering a detailed Telegram notification to your sales channel.

The system eliminates manual lead qualification work, ensuring your team spends time only on prospects with the highest conversion potential. For marketing teams, it provides visibility into which lead sources and campaigns generate the most qualified opportunities.

The automated workflow path from form submission to sales alert

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates when a new submission arrives in your form platform (Typeform, Google Forms, etc.). The system captures all submitted fields including email, name, and any custom qualification questions.

2. MadKudu Lead Scoring

Submission data gets sent to MadKudu's AI engine which analyzes hundreds of signals including company attributes, engagement history, and form responses. The lead receives a score from 0-100 based on your predefined ideal customer profile.

3. Hunter Contact Enrichment

For leads scoring above your threshold (typically 70+), Hunter finds professional email addresses, direct phone numbers, and LinkedIn profiles. This enrichment helps sales personalize their outreach with verified contact methods.

4. Telegram Alert Generation

The system formats all lead details into a rich Telegram message including: lead score, company info, contact details, and a link to the original submission. Sales teams can click-to-call or email directly from their mobile devices.

Pro tip: Set different score thresholds for different lead sources. Demo request forms might use 60+ while newsletter signups could require 80+ to alert sales.
