What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you prepare for meetings by leveraging artificial intelligence to conduct background research automatically. Whenever a new meeting is scheduled in your Google Calendar, the system instantly springs into action. It analyzes the meeting details, participants, and context to generate a comprehensive briefing document that includes relevant background information, recent news, and contextual insights.

The workflow eliminates the hours typically spent manually researching meeting attendees, companies, and topics before important discussions. Instead of scrambling to prepare at the last minute, you receive a professionally formatted briefing delivered directly to your Slack channel before the meeting begins. This ensures you walk into every meeting fully prepared with up-to-date information and relevant context.

How It Works

Step 1: Calendar Event Detection

The workflow starts by monitoring your Google Calendar for new events. When a meeting is scheduled, it captures all relevant details including the meeting title, description, participants, time, and any attached documents or links.

Step 2: AI-Powered Research

Using SerpAPI, the system conducts comprehensive web research based on the meeting context. It searches for information about attendees, companies, topics, and recent developments. Azure OpenAI then processes this information, analyzing and synthesizing the data into coherent, actionable insights.

Step 3: Briefing Generation

The AI compiles the research findings into a structured briefing document. This includes executive summaries, key points, background information, and suggested discussion topics. The system formats this information professionally with clear sections and highlights.

Step 4: Slack Delivery

Finally, the completed briefing is delivered to a designated Slack channel or direct message. The message includes the comprehensive briefing along with quick access links and highlights, ensuring you have the information exactly where you need it before the meeting starts.

Who This Is For

This automation is perfect for sales professionals who need background information on prospects before calls, executives preparing for partnership meetings, consultants meeting new clients, and anyone who regularly participates in meetings requiring preparation. It's particularly valuable for teams that handle numerous external meetings weekly and need to maintain consistent preparation quality across all team members.

Pro tip: Configure the system to generate briefings 30-60 minutes before meetings to ensure information freshness while giving you adequate review time.
