What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of managing GetResponse subscriber lists. Instead of exporting CSV files, manually editing them, and re-importing changes, this workflow automatically fetches all your contacts and applies updates based on your business rules.

Marketers waste hours each week on list maintenance tasks that can be fully automated. This template provides the foundation for keeping your email lists perfectly synchronized with your other systems while eliminating human errors in data handling.

How It Works

1. Fetch all contacts from GetResponse

The workflow starts by connecting to your GetResponse account via API and retrieving your complete contact list with all associated fields and tags.

2. Apply your update rules

Based on your configured logic, the workflow processes each contact to determine needed updates. This could include adding/removing tags, updating custom fields, or changing subscription status.

3. Push changes back to GetResponse

The modified contact data is sent back to GetResponse through API calls, keeping your lists perfectly synchronized without manual intervention.
