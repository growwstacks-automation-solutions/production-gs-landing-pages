What This Workflow Does

Manually curating and posting daily content to engage your Telegram community is time-consuming and inconsistent. This automation solves that by delivering fresh, translated poems to your chat automatically every day.

The workflow connects multiple services: it fetches random poems from a public API, translates them into English (or your preferred language), and posts the formatted result directly to your designated Telegram channel or group. It runs on a schedule you control, ensuring your audience receives valuable content even when you're busy with other business priorities.

Beyond just poems, this template demonstrates a powerful pattern for automated content distribution that can be adapted for news digests, motivational quotes, industry updates, or customer notifications.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

A Cron node initiates the workflow daily at your specified time (default 10:00 AM). You can easily adjust the frequency to hourly, weekly, or custom intervals based on your engagement strategy.

2. Fetch Fresh Content

An HTTP Request node calls the Poemist API to retrieve a random poem with its author and title. This ensures you never run out of content or repeat the same material.

3. Automated Translation

The LingvaNex node translates the poem into your target language. This step can be modified to use other translation services or can be skipped if you prefer source-language content.

4. Format & Deliver

The Telegram node formats the translated poem with proper attribution and posts it directly to your configured chat. The message includes the poem title, author, and the translated text for a complete presentation.

What You'll Need

  1. An active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. A Telegram bot token (created via @BotFather)
  3. The chat ID of your Telegram channel or group
  4. A LingvaNex API key (free tier available) for translation
  5. Basic understanding of n8n interface for configuration

Pro tip: Before going live, test your workflow with a private Telegram group to ensure formatting looks perfect. Adjust line breaks and spacing in the Telegram node for optimal mobile reading.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance via the "Import from File" option.
  2. Configure Telegram: Replace the placeholder bot token and chat ID in the Telegram node with your actual credentials.
  3. Set Translation: Add your LingvaNex API key and verify the source/target language settings match your needs.
  4. Adjust Schedule: Modify the Cron node to trigger at your preferred time and frequency.
  5. Test & Activate: Click "Execute Workflow" to test one run, then toggle the workflow to "Active" when ready.
