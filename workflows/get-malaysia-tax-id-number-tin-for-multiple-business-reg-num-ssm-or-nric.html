What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually looking up tax identification numbers (TINs) for multiple Malaysian businesses. The workflow connects to Malaysia's Inland Revenue Board (LHDN) API to automatically retrieve TINs based on business registration numbers (SSM) or NRIC numbers.

Instead of manually checking each business one-by-one through the LHDN portal, this workflow processes batches of registration numbers simultaneously. It's particularly valuable for accounting firms, payroll providers, and businesses managing multiple entities.

>How It Works

1. Input Business Registration Numbers

The workflow accepts a list of SSM business registration numbers (SSM) or NRIC numbers as input. This can come from spreadsheet, database, or directly from your business systems.

2. API Requests to LHDN

Each registration number is processed through the official LHDN API to retrieve the corresponding tax identification number. The workflow handles authentication and proper API formatting.

3. Output Structured Results

The system returns clean, structured data showing which registration numbers successfully returned TINs and which ones need follow-up. Results can be saved to your accounting software, CRM, or back to your original spreadsheet.
