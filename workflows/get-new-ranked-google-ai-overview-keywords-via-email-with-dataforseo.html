How It Works

1. Scheduled keyword ranking checks

The workflow triggers weekly to query DataForSEO's API for your target keywords. It specifically checks which terms now generate AI Overview responses in Google search results.

2. New appearance detection

The system compares current results with historical data to identify newly eligible keywords - those appearing in AI Overview for the first time during the tracking period.

3. Data enrichment

Each keyword gets analyzed for estimated traffic potential, competitive difficulty, and relevance to your content strategy. The system can integrate with Google Analytics for performance context.

4. Report generation

The workflow structures findings into a digestible format, highlighting strategic opportunities. It creates both executive summaries for leadership and detailed sheets for SEO teams.

5. Automated email delivery

Final reports send to designated recipients through your email service. The template includes options for Slack alerts or CMS integrations to act on findings immediately.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to track competitor keywords too. Seeing their AI Overview appearances reveals content gaps you can fill.
