What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the frustrating manual process of copying leads from Apollo.io to your Airtable CRM. Instead of wasting hours exporting CSV files or switching between tabs to copy-paste contact details, this workflow automatically transfers qualified leads with all their associated data in one click.

The system filters leads based on your criteria (like lead score or tags) and structures them perfectly in Airtable with mapped fields, notes, and interaction history. Your sales team gets instant access to warm leads without any data entry work, allowing them to focus on what matters most - closing deals.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New qualified leads in Apollo

The workflow monitors your Apollo account for new leads that meet your qualification criteria. You can set filters based on lead score, tags, company attributes, or any other Apollo field.

2. Data extraction and formatting

The automation pulls all relevant lead details including contact information, company data, interaction history, and custom fields. It reformats this data to match your Airtable structure.

3. Duplicate checking

Before creating new records, the system checks Airtable for existing contacts to prevent duplicates. You can configure whether to skip duplicates or update existing records with new information.

4. Airtable record creation

The workflow creates perfectly formatted records in your specified Airtable base, with all fields mapped according to your CRM structure. Related data (like company info) is linked properly.

5. Notification and logging

Optionally, the system can notify your team via Slack or email when high-priority leads are transferred, and logs all activity for tracking purposes.
