What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually checking cryptocurrency prices and market data across multiple platforms. It pulls real-time spot market data directly from Bybit's API and delivers formatted updates to your Telegram channel or private chat, with optional AI-powered analysis using GPT-4.1-mini.

Traders and investors can receive customized market updates without constantly monitoring exchanges. The workflow eliminates the need to switch between apps and manually compile data, saving hours per week while ensuring you never miss important market movements.

How It Works

1. Fetching Bybit Market Data

The workflow connects to Bybit's REST v5 API to retrieve the latest spot market prices, trading volume, and other key metrics for specified cryptocurrency pairs. You can configure which coins and metrics to track.

2. Optional AI Processing

For premium users, the workflow can pass the raw market data through GPT-4.1-mini to generate human-readable summaries, identify trends, or highlight unusual market activity based on your preferences.

3. Telegram Delivery

The processed data gets formatted into clean, readable messages and sent to your configured Telegram channel or private chat. You can set delivery schedules (e.g., hourly updates) or trigger alerts for specific price movements.
