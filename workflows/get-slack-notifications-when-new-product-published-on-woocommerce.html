What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common challenge for ecommerce teams - staying instantly informed when new products go live. Without automation, someone must manually notify team members in Slack or email whenever new products publish, creating delays and potential oversights.

The workflow monitors your WooCommerce store for new product publications. When detected, it automatically sends a formatted Slack notification to your specified channel with key product details. This keeps your entire team synchronized without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. WooCommerce Product Trigger

The workflow starts by monitoring your WooCommerce store's product feed for new publications. It checks at regular intervals you configure.

2. URL Validation

An IF node verifies the product URL matches your store's structure (e.g., contains /product/). This prevents false triggers from test products or drafts.

3. Slack Notification

Validated products trigger a customized Slack message sent through your Slack bot to your designated channel. The message includes product name, price, and direct link.
