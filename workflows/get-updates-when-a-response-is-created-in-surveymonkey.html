What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of delayed response to survey feedback by creating instant notifications whenever someone completes your SurveyMonkey form. Instead of manually checking for new responses or waiting for scheduled reports, your team gets real-time alerts with the respondent's answers.

The workflow connects directly to SurveyMonkey's API to monitor for new submissions. When a response arrives, it can trigger multiple actions simultaneously - sending email alerts, posting to Slack channels, creating CRM tickets, or logging data to spreadsheets. This ensures no feedback slips through the cracks while eliminating repetitive manual checks.

How It Works

1. SurveyMonkey Trigger Setup

The workflow begins by polling SurveyMonkey's API at regular intervals (typically every 5-15 minutes) to check for new responses in your specified survey. The trigger captures all response data including answers, timestamps, and respondent metadata.

2. Response Processing

Each new response passes through filters that can be customized to your needs. You might configure rules to only notify for specific answer patterns (like low satisfaction scores) or from particular respondent segments.

3. Notification Delivery

The processed response data gets formatted into your preferred notification template and delivered through your chosen channels. Common setups include rich Slack messages with answer summaries or emails with direct links to the full response.
