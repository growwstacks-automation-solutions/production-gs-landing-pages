What This Workflow Does

Email overload is a universal productivity killer. Professionals spend hours daily sorting, reading, and responding to messages, often missing critical communications buried in the noise. This intelligent automation system solves that problem by creating your personal AI email assistant.

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox in real-time, uses Google Gemini AI to understand and categorize every email, sends instant Telegram notifications for important messages, and can even generate professional replies for your approval. It transforms your chaotic inbox into an organized, prioritized communication hub that works while you focus on actual business.

How It Works

The system operates on a continuous automation loop, processing emails as they arrive and taking intelligent actions based on their content and importance.

1. Real-Time Email Monitoring

The workflow checks your Gmail inbox every minute for new messages. When an email arrives, it extracts the sender, subject, body content, and any attachments for processing.

2. AI-Powered Classification

Google Gemini AI analyzes the email content and assigns it to one of three categories: Needs Immediate Reply (urgent business matters), Important Notification (bills, confirmations), or Low Priority (marketing, newsletters).

3. Smart Notification Routing

Based on the classification, the system takes different actions. Urgent emails trigger Telegram notifications with the sender and key details. Important notifications get summarized and logged. Low-priority emails are archived for later review.

4. Response Generation & Approval

For emails requiring replies, Gemini AI generates a professional, context-aware response. This draft is sent to your Telegram for quick review and approval before being automatically sent from your Gmail account.

5. Daily Digest & Reporting

Every morning at 8 AM, you receive a comprehensive Telegram summary of the previous day's email activity, including pending actions, response statistics, and priority items needing attention.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement your AI email assistant in under 30 minutes:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file.
  2. Configure Gmail credentials using OAuth2 authentication in n8n's credential management.
  3. Create your Telegram bot via @BotFather and add the bot token to n8n credentials.
  4. Set up Google Gemini API in Google Cloud Console and add the API key to n8n.
  5. Update all Telegram chat IDs with your personal chat ID (found via @userinfobot).
  6. Test with sample emails to verify classification accuracy and notification delivery.
  7. Activate the workflow and experience transformed email management immediately.
