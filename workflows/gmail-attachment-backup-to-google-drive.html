Gmail Attachment Backup to Google Drive

Automatically save email attachments from specific senders directly to your Google Drive

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the common problem of important email attachments getting lost in crowded inboxes. It automatically detects emails from specified senders, extracts all attachments, and saves them to designated folders in Google Drive.

Businesses receive critical documents via email daily - invoices, contracts, reports, and more. Manually downloading and organizing these files is time-consuming and error-prone. This automation ensures no important attachment gets overlooked while maintaining perfect organization in your cloud storage.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Gmail account for new messages from your specified sender addresses or labels. You can configure it to watch specific folders or your entire inbox.

2. Attachment Extraction

When a matching email arrives, the system automatically identifies all attachments regardless of file type (PDFs, images, spreadsheets, etc.). It preserves the original file names and formats.

3. Cloud Backup

Each attachment gets uploaded to your Google Drive in the folder structure you define. You can organize by sender, date, file type, or any other logical system that fits your workflow.

Pro tip: Set up subfolders by month/year to maintain chronological organization of your documents automatically.
