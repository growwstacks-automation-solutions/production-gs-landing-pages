What This Workflow Does

Manual email outreach is time-consuming and inefficient. Sales teams, recruiters, and business owners waste hours sending individual emails, tracking responses, and deciding who to follow up with. This workflow automates the entire process of multi-step email campaigns using n8n, Gmail, and Google Sheets.

You define a sequence of emails (like "Day 0: Introduction," "Day 3: Follow-up," "Day 7: Final check") and a list of recipients in a spreadsheet. The system sends the first email to everyone, then automatically checks for replies. Only people who haven't responded receive the next email in the sequence. This means you focus your energy only on engaged prospects while the system handles the repetitive follow-ups.

The workflow intelligently tags emails, skips weekends, and can be customized for different campaigns—making it perfect for sales outreach, recruitment, event invitations, or customer onboarding sequences.

How It Works

The automation follows a smart logic flow that mimics how a human would manage email campaigns but with perfect consistency and timing.

Step 1: Load Recipient Data

The workflow reads your contact list from Google Sheets, including personalization fields like name, company, and any custom data you want to include in your emails.

Step 2: Send Initial Email

Each recipient receives the first email in your sequence, personalized with their data. The email includes a hidden identifier that marks it as part of this specific campaign.

Step 3: Monitor for Replies

The system regularly checks Gmail for replies to the sent emails. When someone replies, their thread is tagged as "engaged" and they're removed from the follow-up sequence.

Step 4: Send Follow-ups (If No Reply)

After the designated waiting period (e.g., 3 days), the workflow sends the next email in the sequence only to recipients who haven't replied yet. This continues through your entire email sequence.

Step 5: Complete Campaign

Once a recipient replies or reaches the end of your sequence, their record is updated in Google Sheets. You get a clear view of who engaged and who didn't without manual tracking.

Pro tip: Use this workflow for multiple campaigns by duplicating it and changing the Google Sheet and email templates. Perfect for different product lines, job roles, or event types.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and save the JSON file to your computer.
  2. Import into n8n: In your n8n instance, go to Workflows → Import from file and select the downloaded JSON.
  3. Configure Google Sheets: Update the Google Sheets node with your spreadsheet ID and sheet name containing your recipient list.
  4. Set up Gmail: Connect the Gmail node with your account credentials and test the connection.
  5. Customize email templates: Edit the email content in the workflow to match your messaging, using the personalization fields from your spreadsheet.
  6. Adjust timing: Modify the wait times between emails to match your preferred sequence (default avoids weekends).
  7. Test with a small list: Run the workflow with 2-3 test email addresses to ensure everything works before scaling.
  8. Activate and schedule: Turn on the workflow and set it to run on your preferred schedule (e.g., daily at 9 AM).

Important: This workflow is designed for legitimate business communication, not spam. Always comply with anti-spam regulations (CAN-SPAM, GDPR) and include unsubscribe options in your emails.
