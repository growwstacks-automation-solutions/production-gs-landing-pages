What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the common problem of email overload by automating message categorization and response using AI. It intelligently processes incoming Gmail messages, classifies them into predefined categories using GPT-4o, and generates context-aware replies while logging all interactions in Google Sheets.

The system eliminates manual email triage, reduces response times, and ensures consistent communication. It's particularly valuable for customer support teams, sales professionals, and busy executives who receive high volumes of emails requiring categorization and timely responses.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your Gmail inbox. It captures the message content, sender information, and other relevant metadata.

2. AI Classification

Using GPT-4o's advanced natural language processing, the system analyzes the email content and assigns it to predefined categories (e.g., Support, Sales, General Inquiry). You can customize these categories based on your business needs.

3. Response Generation

Based on the classification, GPT-4o generates a personalized response tailored to the email content. The AI considers context, tone, and your predefined response templates to create professional replies.

4. Approval & Sending

Before sending, the workflow can optionally route responses for human approval (configurable). Once approved, it sends the reply through your Gmail account.

5. Google Sheets Logging

All processed emails and responses are automatically logged in Google Sheets, creating a searchable archive of communications with timestamps, categories, and response details.

Pro tip: Train GPT-4o with examples of your ideal responses to improve reply quality. Include common scenarios and preferred response styles in your initial setup.
