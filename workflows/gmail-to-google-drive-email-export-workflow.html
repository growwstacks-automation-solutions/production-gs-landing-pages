What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the common problem of manually saving important emails to your Google Drive. Many businesses need to archive client communications, legal notices, or important vendor emails for compliance or record-keeping purposes. Doing this manually is time-consuming and prone to human error.

The automation retrieves emails from specific senders in your Gmail account and exports them as PDF files to designated folders in Google Drive. You can configure it to run on a schedule (daily, weekly, etc.) or trigger it manually when needed. Each exported email maintains its original metadata including sender, date, and subject line.

How It Works

1. Email Retrieval

The workflow connects to your Gmail account using OAuth and searches for emails matching your specified criteria (sender, subject keywords, labels, etc.). It can process both new incoming emails and existing messages in your inbox.

2. Email Processing

Each matching email is converted to a PDF format while preserving all headers, body content, and attachments. The workflow can optionally add custom metadata or tags to each file for better organization.

3. Drive Storage

The processed PDFs are saved to your Google Drive in a structured folder hierarchy. You can configure the folder naming convention based on date, sender, or other email attributes.
