What This Workflow Does

This automation system solves a critical problem for financial analysts, investment firms, and market publishers: manually tracking gold prices, news sentiment, and macroeconomic indicators is time‑consuming, inconsistent, and often delayed. By automating the entire pipeline, you get AI‑powered forecasts delivered to your WordPress site (or other channels) every 6 hours, with optional Slack/email alerts.

The workflow fetches live gold prices from MetalPriceAPI, scans financial headlines via NewsAPI, pulls key indicators like inflation and interest rates from FRED, merges the data, runs AI sentiment and trend analysis using Perplexity Sonar‑Pro or OpenAI, generates a concise forecast report, and publishes it automatically. It turns hours of manual research into a scheduled, reliable intelligence engine.

For businesses publishing market insights, hedge funds monitoring asset movements, or analysts needing timely data, this system eliminates manual data gathering, reduces human bias, and ensures reports are always up‑to‑date—freeing up expertise for higher‑value strategic decisions.

How It Works

The workflow follows a clear, sequential pipeline designed for reliability and scalability.

1. Scheduled Trigger

A Cron node triggers the workflow every 6 hours (or any interval you set). This ensures regular, automated execution without manual intervention.

2. Data Collection

Three parallel HTTP Request nodes fetch live gold prices from MetalPriceAPI, relevant financial news from NewsAPI, and macroeconomic indicators (CPI, unemployment, Fed rates) from FRED. Each stream is cleaned and formatted.

3. Data Merging & Preparation

A Merge node combines the price, news, and indicator data into a single structured dataset. This unified view is essential for the AI to analyze correlations and trends across different data types.

4. AI Analysis & Forecasting

The core AI node (Perplexity Sonar‑Pro or OpenAI) receives the merged dataset. It performs sentiment analysis on news headlines, identifies price‑indicator correlations, and generates a short‑term forecast with confidence scores and trend directions.

5. Report Generation

The AI output is formatted into a professional market report—including key takeaways, predicted movement, confidence level, and recommended watchpoints. The template creates HTML‑ready content.

6. Publishing & Notification

The report is posted as a new WordPress article via the WordPress node. Simultaneously, Slack and/or Email nodes send alerts to your team with the summary and a link to the full post.

Pro tip: Adjust the Cron schedule based on your reporting needs—daily for newsletters, hourly for intra‑day alerts, or weekly for summary reports.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to import and configure the workflow in your n8n environment.

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Add credentials in n8n’s settings for each service: MetalPriceAPI, NewsAPI, FRED, OpenAI/Perplexity, WordPress, Slack, and Gmail.
  3. Adjust the Cron schedule to match your desired reporting frequency (e.g., every 6 hours, daily at 9 AM).
  4. Test each node individually to ensure API connections work and data flows correctly.
  5. Customize the AI prompt in the AI analysis node to focus on specific indicators or output format.
  6. Set up your WordPress post template—define categories, tags, and featured image settings.
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the first run. Verify the report appears on your site and notifications are sent.

Pro tip: Run the workflow manually once before activating the schedule to catch any credential or configuration errors.
