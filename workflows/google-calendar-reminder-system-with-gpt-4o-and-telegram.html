What This Workflow Does

Traditional calendar notifications often get lost in the noise of daily digital distractions. This n8n workflow solves that problem by transforming basic Google Calendar alerts into intelligent, personalized Telegram messages enhanced with GPT-4o's natural language processing.

The system doesn't just remind you about upcoming events—it analyzes meeting details to provide context-aware notifications. For example, it might remind you about a client call while also surfacing relevant notes from previous meetings or suggesting preparation materials based on the agenda.

How It Works

1. Calendar Event Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Google Calendar for upcoming events, checking at configurable intervals (typically 15-30 minutes before events).

2. AI Context Enhancement

GPT-4o analyzes each event's details—title, description, attendees, and location—to generate human-friendly reminders with relevant contextual information.

3. Telegram Notification Delivery

The system sends these enhanced reminders directly to your Telegram account, where they stand out from email clutter and can include quick-action buttons.
