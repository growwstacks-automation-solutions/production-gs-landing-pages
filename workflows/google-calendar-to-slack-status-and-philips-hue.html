What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common productivity challenge of unwanted interruptions during focus time or important meetings. By connecting Google Calendar with Slack status and Philips Hue smart lights, it creates a multi-channel availability signaling system that helps protect your concentration time.

When you're in a meeting (as marked in Google Calendar), the workflow automatically updates your Slack status to "In a meeting" and changes your Philips Hue lights to a designated color. This creates both digital and physical indicators that you're currently unavailable, reducing interruptions from colleagues or household members.

Example Slack status configuration based on calendar events

How It Works

1. Calendar Event Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Google Calendar for new or updated events. It checks event titles, durations, and metadata to determine appropriate status updates.

2. Status Logic Processing

Based on the calendar event details, the system determines whether to set you as "Available" or "Busy," and selects appropriate status messages and light colors for different meeting types.

3. Multi-Platform Updates

The workflow simultaneously updates your Slack status (including custom emoji and messages) while changing Philips Hue light colors in your workspace. All updates happen in real-time as events start and end.
