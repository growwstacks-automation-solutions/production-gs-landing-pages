What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation is time-consuming and inefficient. This workflow solves that problem by automating the entire process of discovering businesses through Google Maps and extracting their digital contact information from websites. It runs every 30 minutes to continuously build your prospect database without manual intervention.

The automation scrapes business data from Google Maps using Apify's Google Places crawler, saves basic information to Google Sheets, filters businesses with websites, scrapes each website using Firecrawl to extract emails and social profiles, and organizes everything into a structured database ready for outreach campaigns.

How It Works

Step 1: Google Maps Scraping

The workflow starts by querying Google Maps for businesses in your target location and industry using Apify. It collects business names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and ratings.

Step 2: Data Storage & Filtering

Basic business information is saved to Google Sheets. The workflow then filters to identify businesses that have websites, as these are prime candidates for contact extraction.

Step 3: Website Contact Extraction

For each business website, Firecrawl scans the site content to extract email addresses, LinkedIn profiles, Facebook pages, Instagram accounts, and Twitter handles using pattern recognition algorithms.

Step 4: Data Enrichment & Organization

All extracted contact information is combined with the original business data and saved in organized Google Sheets with clear columns for each data point, making it easy to import into your CRM or email marketing platform.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google Sheets account with OAuth2 setup
  3. Apify account with API access for Google Places scraping
  4. Firecrawl account with API access for website scraping
  5. Target criteria (location, business type, search terms)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Set up Google Sheets OAuth2 connection in n8n credentials
  3. Add your Apify and Firecrawl API credentials to n8n
  4. Copy the provided Google Sheet template and update the Sheet ID in all Google Sheets nodes
  5. Configure the Apify scraping parameters for your target location and business type
  6. Adjust the schedule trigger from 30 minutes to your preferred frequency
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the first run

Pro tip: Start with a small geographic area and specific business category to test the workflow before scaling up. This helps you refine your search criteria and ensure you're collecting relevant leads.
