What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation is time-consuming, inconsistent, and expensive. Sales teams waste hours searching Google Maps, visiting websites, and trying to find contact information—only to end up with incomplete or outdated data. This creates pipeline gaps and missed revenue opportunities.

This automation solves that by creating a complete, hands-off lead generation system. It discovers businesses in your target cities and industries, visits their websites, uses AI to extract verified contact information (including decision-maker details), and stores everything in your Airtable CRM—ready for outreach. What used to take 10+ hours per week now happens automatically while you focus on selling.

How It Works

1. City & Business Discovery

The workflow starts by reading your target cities from Airtable. For each location, it queries Google Maps Places API with your search term (like "SEO Agency" or "Commercial Plumber"). It retrieves businesses with names, addresses, and websites, then checks your database to avoid duplicates.

2. Website Analysis & Contact Extraction

For each business with a website, the system visits the site and intelligently looks for contact pages (Impressum, Contact Us, About). It cleans the HTML, removing navigation and ads, then sends the relevant content to OpenAI's GPT model. The AI identifies decision-maker names with titles, valid email addresses, and phone numbers in international format.

3. Data Validation & Enrichment

Extracted contacts go through validation filters—emails must contain "@" and follow common patterns, phone numbers are formatted consistently. Incomplete records (missing email) are filtered out. The system enriches each lead with the original search context (city and search term) for better campaign tracking.

4. CRM Population & Tracking

Finally, complete lead records—company name, address, website, email, phone, decision maker, and source data—are written to your Airtable base. The workflow marks processed cities as "complete" so you can safely restart or add new locations without re-scraping.

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 test cities to verify your Google Maps API setup and contact extraction accuracy before scaling to dozens of locations. Monitor your API usage to stay within free tiers.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file. In n8n, go to Workflows → Import from file and select the downloaded template.
  2. Configure Credentials: Set up credentials for Airtable (with access to your base), Google Maps (API key with Places API enabled), and OpenAI (API key).
  3. Prepare Your Airtable: Create a "Cities" table with columns: City, Country, Status. Add your target locations. Create a "Companies" table with columns for the extracted data.
  4. Set Search Parameters: In the "Execute Workflow" node, edit the search term to match your target business type (e.g., "Digital Marketing Agency").
  5. Test & Run: Execute the workflow for one test city. Check the extracted data in Airtable, then scale to all cities once verified.

Pro tip: Add a "Wait" node between city processing to stay within Google Maps API rate limits. Start with 2-second delays, then adjust based on your quota.
