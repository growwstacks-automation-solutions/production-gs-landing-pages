What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire proposal-to-payment lifecycle using AI-powered intelligence. It transforms how businesses handle contracts by reducing manual work, minimizing errors, and accelerating cash flow. The system processes incoming proposals, analyzes terms using AI, generates compliant contracts, tracks approvals, creates invoices, and manages payments—all without human intervention for routine cases.

Traditional contract management often involves disjointed systems, manual data entry, and delayed payments. This automation solves those pain points by creating a seamless digital workflow that connects your CRM, document systems, accounting software, and payment processors. The AI component adds smart analysis that would normally require legal review, saving both time and expensive professional fees.

How It Works

1. Proposal Intake and Analysis

The workflow begins when a proposal enters your system, either through email, web form, or API. The AI immediately scans the document, extracting key terms like pricing, deliverables, and timelines. It compares these against your standard contract templates and business rules to identify any discrepancies or special conditions needing attention.

2. Smart Contract Generation

Based on the analysis, the system generates a compliant contract draft incorporating the negotiated terms. For standard agreements, this happens automatically. For complex cases requiring human review, the workflow routes the document to the appropriate team member with highlighted areas of concern and suggested language.

3. Approval Workflow and Signatures

The system manages the entire approval chain based on your business rules. Contracts requiring executive approval automatically route to the right person based on deal size or other criteria. Once approved, the workflow sends the document for electronic signatures and stores the executed copy in your document management system with proper metadata tagging.

4. Invoice Creation and Payment Processing

Upon contract execution, the workflow automatically generates invoices according to the payment schedule. It integrates with your accounting system to create proper journal entries and sends invoices to clients through their preferred channel. The system then tracks payments, sends reminders for overdue invoices, and reconciles received payments against open items.

Pro tip: Configure your payment terms analysis to flag clients with consistently late payments, allowing you to adjust future contract terms or require deposits.
