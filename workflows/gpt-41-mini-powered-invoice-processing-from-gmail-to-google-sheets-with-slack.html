What This Workflow Does

This automation completely transforms your accounts payable process by eliminating manual invoice handling. It automatically extracts key data from invoices received via Gmail, logs them in Google Sheets, and routes them through Slack for team approval.

The system handles the entire pipeline: from PDF attachment processing to AI data extraction, record keeping, and approval workflows. It reduces invoice processing time from days to hours while eliminating human data entry errors.

How It Works

1. Invoice Email Capture

The workflow triggers when new emails with invoice attachments arrive in your Gmail. It identifies invoices based on subject lines or sender addresses you configure.

2. AI Data Extraction

PDF attachments are processed through AI to automatically extract vendor details, invoice numbers, amounts, due dates, and line items with over 95% accuracy.

3. Google Sheets Logging

Extracted data is structured and logged in your accounting spreadsheet with status marked "Pending Approval" and the raw invoice text preserved for reference.

4. Slack Approval Flow

A formatted Slack message is sent to your AP channel with key details and one-click Approve/Reject buttons. Approved invoices update automatically in Sheets.

What You'll Need

  1. Gmail account receiving invoices
  2. Google Sheets for invoice tracking
  3. Slack workspace for approvals
  4. n8n cloud or self-hosted instance
  5. OpenAI API key for AI processing (optional but recommended)

Pro tip: For best results, train the AI with sample invoices from your frequent vendors to improve extraction accuracy over time.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Gmail, Google Sheets, and Slack accounts
  3. Configure the email filter to match your invoice patterns
  4. Set up your approval Slack channel and test the workflow
  5. Optionally add OpenAI credentials for enhanced extraction
