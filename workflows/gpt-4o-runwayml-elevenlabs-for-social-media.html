What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the massive time drain of creating daily social media video content for travel brands, tourism boards, and content creators. Instead of spending hours scripting, filming, editing, and publishing, this workflow automatically generates polished 20-second destination highlight videos using AI.

The system pulls destination ideas from a Google Sheet, uses GPT-4o to create engaging scripts and visual prompts, generates images with Piapia AI, produces video clips with RunwayML, adds professional voiceovers with ElevenLabs, and composes everything into ready-to-publish videos. The final output is uploaded to Google Drive and logged for tracking—all without manual intervention.

For businesses, this means converting a 10-hour weekly content creation process into a fully automated system that produces consistent, high-quality video content while freeing your team to focus on strategy and engagement rather than production.

How It Works

1. Content Ideation & Script Generation

The workflow starts by reading destination ideas from your Google Sheet. GPT-4o analyzes each destination and generates both a compelling 20-second script and detailed visual prompts for image generation. This ensures the content is both informative and visually aligned with the destination's appeal.

2. AI Media Generation

Visual prompts are sent to Piapia AI to create stunning destination images. Simultaneously, RunwayML processes prompts to generate short video clips. The script is converted to natural-sounding voiceover using ElevenLabs' realistic text-to-speech. All assets are temporarily stored with unique identifiers.

3. Video Composition & Assembly

The CreateMate API assembles images, video clips, and audio into a cohesive 20-second video with smooth transitions and timing. The system applies consistent branding elements and ensures the final product meets platform specifications for Instagram Reels, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts.

4. Distribution & Tracking

Completed videos are uploaded to a designated Google Drive folder with shareable links. The workflow updates your tracking sheet with video URLs, generation timestamps, and status markers. Optional integrations can push videos directly to social media schedulers like Buffer or Hootsuite.
