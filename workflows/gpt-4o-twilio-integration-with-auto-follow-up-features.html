What This Workflow Does

Every missed call is a lost opportunity—a potential customer who reached out but didn't get through. Manual follow-up is slow, inconsistent, and often forgotten. This automation solves that by instantly detecting unanswered calls, analyzing the context, and sending personalized recovery messages via SMS and email.

The workflow turns a missed call into a proactive engagement. It uses AI to craft messages that feel human, logs everything for tracking, and alerts your sales team about high‑priority leads. This ensures no lead falls through the cracks, directly recovering revenue that would otherwise be lost.

How It Works

Step 1: Missed Call Detection

A Twilio webhook automatically triggers when a call goes unanswered. The workflow captures the caller's number, time of day, and whether it's within business hours or a weekend.

Step 2: Context Analysis & CRM Check

The system checks your CRM (if connected) for existing customer data and purchase history. This information helps determine the lead's value and priority.

Step 3: AI‑Powered Message Generation

GPT‑4o generates a personalized recovery message for both SMS and email. The AI considers the call context, customer history, and your brand tone to create a natural, effective follow‑up.

Step 4: Instant Follow‑Up Delivery

The workflow sends an apology SMS with a booking link immediately. An email version is also dispatched if configured. All interactions are logged to Airtable for full visibility.

Step 5: Sales Team Alert & Follow‑Up

Slack receives an alert with customer priority and context. After 24 hours, the system checks if the lead booked; if not, a second follow‑up SMS is sent automatically.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for service businesses, sales teams, agencies, and any company that receives inbound calls from potential customers. If you have a high call volume and struggle to follow up on every missed call manually, this workflow will save you time and recover lost revenue.

It's particularly valuable for businesses in consulting, coaching, home services, healthcare, and B2B sales where a quick response can make or close a deal.

What You'll Need

  1. Twilio account with a phone number capable of sending SMS.
  2. OpenAI API key with access to GPT‑4o for AI message generation.
  3. Slack workspace for sales team alerts (standard plan works).
  4. Airtable base for logging call interactions (free plan is sufficient).
  5. Email SMTP credentials (Gmail, SendGrid, or any SMTP provider).
  6. Booking link from Cal.com, Calendly, or your scheduling tool.
  7. Optional: CRM API access (HubSpot, Salesforce, or custom) for enhanced personalization.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Add your credentials for Twilio, OpenAI, Slack, Airtable, and your email SMTP.
  3. Configure your Twilio phone number in both SMS nodes and set up the webhook.
  4. Create an Airtable base with the suggested columns (Caller_Number, Customer_Name, Call_Time, Priority, etc.).
  5. Replace placeholder IDs in the Slack and Airtable nodes with your actual channel and base IDs.
  6. Update the booking URL with your Cal.com or Calendly link.
  7. Test the workflow with a missed call simulation to ensure everything flows correctly.

Pro tip: Start by disabling the optional CRM lookup nodes if you don't have a CRM integrated. You can add that layer later once the basic follow‑up is working smoothly.
