What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common business challenge of managing approval requests through chaotic email threads and manual spreadsheet tracking. The workflow creates a structured system that handles the entire approval lifecycle automatically.

When a request is submitted (via form, email, or other trigger), the workflow sends professional notification emails to approvers via SendGrid, tracks responses in Baserow, updates request status automatically, and notifies all stakeholders of decisions. This eliminates the need for manual follow-ups and status checks.

How It Works

1. Request Submission

The workflow triggers when a new approval request is detected. This could come from various sources like web forms, emails, or direct database entries.

2. Approver Notification

SendGrid sends branded email notifications to designated approvers with all request details and action buttons (Approve/Reject). Email templates maintain consistent communication standards.

3. Response Processing

The system monitors for approver responses, validates them against business rules, and records decisions in Baserow with timestamps and responder details.

4. Status Updates

Baserow records are updated in real-time with approval status. Conditional logic handles different outcomes like full approval, partial approval, or rejection scenarios.

5. Stakeholder Notifications

All relevant parties receive automated updates about the approval decision via SendGrid, including next steps or required actions based on the outcome.

Pro tip: Configure Baserow views to show pending approvals by department or priority level, giving managers instant visibility into their approval queue.
