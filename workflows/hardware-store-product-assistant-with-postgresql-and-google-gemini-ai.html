Hardware Store Product Assistant with PostgreSQL & Google Gemini AI

What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered workflow transforms how hardware stores handle customer inquiries by combining PostgreSQL product data with Google Gemini's advanced natural language processing. It acts as a 24/7 virtual product expert that understands technical hardware questions and provides accurate recommendations from your inventory.

The system reduces staff workload by handling common product questions like "What size drill bit for concrete?" or "Which paint is best for outdoor metal furniture?" while maintaining your brand voice. It integrates with your existing product database to ensure recommendations reflect current inventory and pricing.

How It Works

1. Customer Query Processing

The workflow receives customer questions through your preferred channel (website chat, SMS, or email). Google Gemini AI analyzes the question's intent and extracts key product parameters.

2. Database Lookup

The system queries your PostgreSQL database using the extracted parameters, searching across product specifications, categories, and inventory levels.

3. AI Response Generation

Google Gemini synthesizes the database results into a natural language response, including relevant product recommendations, alternative options, and technical advice when appropriate.

4. Response Delivery

The finalized response is sent back through the original communication channel, complete with product links or reference numbers for easy purchasing.
