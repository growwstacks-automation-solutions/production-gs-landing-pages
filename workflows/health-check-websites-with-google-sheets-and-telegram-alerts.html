What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical business problem of website uptime monitoring. Instead of manually checking websites manually or relying on expensive monitoring services, this workflow automatically tests your URLs at regular intervals and alerts your team immediately when issues are detected.

The system pulls your website list from Google Sheets, checks each URL's availability and response status, then sends instant Telegram notifications if any sites are down or returning errors. This ensures your team knows about outages before customers report them.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs at intervals you configure (every 5 minutes, hourly, etc.) to perform fresh website checks.

2. Google Sheets Integration

It reads your list of URLs to monitor from a designated Google Sheet, allowing easy maintenance without technical skills.

3. Website Health Checks

Each URL is checked for HTTP status codes, response times, and basic availability. Failed checks trigger alerts.

4. Telegram Notifications

When issues are detected, detailed alerts are sent to your Telegram group including the problematic URL and error details.
