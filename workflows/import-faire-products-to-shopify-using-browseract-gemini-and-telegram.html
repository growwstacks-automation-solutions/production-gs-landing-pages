What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually importing products from wholesale platforms like Faire into your Shopify store. Dropshippers and e-commerce store owners often spend 15-20 minutes per product copying details, downloading images, writing descriptions, and entering data—a process prone to errors and inconsistency.

The automation acts as your virtual product sourcing assistant. You simply send a Faire product URL to a Telegram bot. The system then handles everything: scraping product details, processing images, enhancing content with AI for better SEO and conversions, and creating the complete product listing in Shopify with proper variants and pricing. It even handles CAPTCHA challenges by pausing and alerting you, then resuming automatically once solved.

How It Works

1. Receive & Classify Request

You send a Faire product link to your dedicated Telegram bot. An AI agent immediately analyzes the message to determine if it's a valid product import request or general conversation, ensuring the workflow only triggers for actual products.

2. Scrape Product Data

BrowserAct executes a background browser session to visit the Faire URL and extract the product title, description, price, variant options, and all image URLs. This happens without you needing to manually copy anything.

3. Handle CAPTCHA Challenges

If the scraper encounters a CAPTCHA, the workflow pauses and sends you an alert on Telegram. Once you solve the CAPTCHA manually, the workflow automatically resumes scraping from where it left off.

4. AI Content Enhancement

Google Gemini AI rewrites the scraped title and description to be SEO-friendly, sales-oriented, and formatted in HTML. It optimizes for search visibility while maintaining persuasive marketing language.

5. Create Shopify Product

The workflow creates a new product in your Shopify store, sets the pricing and SKU based on scraped data, uploads all product images, and publishes the listing—ready for sales.

What You'll Need

  1. BrowserAct Account with the "Product Importer Bot for Shopify" template saved.
  2. Shopify Store with an access token granting write_products permission.
  3. Telegram Bot created via BotFather with its API token.
  4. Google Gemini API Key for AI content enhancement.
  5. n8n Instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow.

Pro tip: Before going live, test the workflow with 2-3 products to ensure your pricing markup logic and AI prompt settings produce the exact listing format you want. Adjust the AI system prompt to match your brand voice.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials in n8n for Telegram, Shopify, BrowserAct, and Google Gemini.
  3. Update the BrowserAct workflow ID in the relevant node to point to your saved "Product Importer Bot for Shopify" template.
  4. Test the Telegram trigger by sending a Faire product link to your bot.
  5. Activate the workflow and start importing products with a simple message.
