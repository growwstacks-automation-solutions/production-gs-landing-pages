What This Workflow Does

Manual company research on Indeed is painfully slow and inconsistent. Recruiters, market analysts, and founders spend hours clicking through profiles, copying information, and trying to make sense of scattered data. This workflow automates the entire process, turning days of research into minutes of automated intelligence.

The system extracts company profile information from Indeed using Bright Data's Web Unlocker, sends the raw HTML to Google Gemini for intelligent summarization, and stores structured insights directly into Airtable. What used to take hours per company now happens automatically, delivering AI-enhanced summaries that highlight key information you'd likely miss manually.

Beyond simple data extraction, this workflow transforms raw company pages into actionable intelligence. It identifies company size, culture indicators, hiring patterns, and strategic positioning—information that helps you make better decisions about partnerships, investments, or talent acquisition.

How It Works

1. Trigger & Data Collection

The workflow starts by pulling company page URLs from your Airtable database. Each URL represents a company you want to research. The system can process dozens or hundreds of companies in a single run, scaling far beyond manual capabilities.

2. Web Scraping with Bright Data

Bright Data's Web Unlocker accesses Indeed company profiles reliably, bypassing anti-bot measures that would block simple scrapers. It extracts the complete HTML content, including company descriptions, employee reviews, benefits, and culture information that's valuable for analysis.

3. AI Processing with Google Gemini

The raw HTML is sent to Google Gemini with carefully crafted prompts that extract specific insights: company size, hiring trends, culture indicators, growth signals, and competitive positioning. Gemini transforms messy web content into structured, digestible summaries.

4. Storage & Notification

Structured summaries are saved back to Airtable with timestamps and source URLs. The system can also trigger notifications via webhook to Slack, email, or other platforms, alerting your team when new intelligence is available.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompts in Google Gemini to extract industry-specific insights. For investment research, focus on growth signals and market position. For recruitment, emphasize culture and employee satisfaction indicators.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtable account with a base containing company URLs to research
  2. Bright Data account with Web Unlocker access (free trial available)
  3. Google Gemini API key or access through Vertex AI
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  5. Webhook endpoint (optional) for notifications to Slack, Teams, or other platforms

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Bright Data credentials in n8n's credentials section using the Header Auth method with your Web Unlocker token.
  3. Add your Google Gemini API key to the Google Gemini (PaLM) API account credentials.
  4. Connect your Airtable base using a Personal Access Token, pointing to the table containing company URLs.
  5. Test with a single URL first to ensure all connections work, then scale to multiple companies.
  6. Customize the AI prompts in the Google Gemini node to extract the specific insights most valuable to your use case.

Pro tip: Start with 5-10 company URLs to test the workflow end-to-end. Monitor the summaries for quality, then adjust the AI prompts before scaling to hundreds of companies.
