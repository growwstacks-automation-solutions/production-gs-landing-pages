What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually searching job boards and screening listings. It combines BrowserAct's web scraping capabilities with Gemini AI's natural language processing to intelligently match Indeed job postings against your specific criteria, then delivers qualified opportunities directly to Telegram for immediate review.

Recruiters and job seekers waste an average of 12 hours per week on repetitive search and screening tasks. This workflow automates 80-90% of that process by analyzing job descriptions for cultural fit, required skills, and compensation signals that go beyond simple keyword matching. The AI evaluates listings with human-like understanding but at machine speed.

How It Works

1. Automated Indeed Search

BrowserAct performs targeted searches on Indeed using your configured parameters (job titles, locations, salary ranges, etc.). It extracts structured data from search results including job titles, companies, locations, and full descriptions.

2. AI-Powered Matching

Gemini AI analyzes each job description against your ideal candidate profile. It scores listings based on skills alignment, cultural indicators, and other qualitative factors that traditional filters miss.

3. Telegram Notification

Matches meeting your threshold are formatted into rich Telegram messages with direct apply links. The bot can include AI-generated summaries highlighting why each job matches your criteria.
