What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates comprehensive research on Indonesian stocks by combining Sectors App's financial data API with Gemini AI analysis and Tavily's web search capabilities. It solves the challenge of manually gathering and interpreting IDX market data, which is particularly time-consuming due to language barriers and fragmented local information sources.

The system generates structured research reports that include fundamental analysis, sector comparisons, news sentiment scoring, and AI-generated investment theses. For asset managers covering Southeast Asia, this automation can reduce research time per company from 4-6 hours to under 30 minutes while improving consistency across analyses.

How It Works

1. Data Collection Phase

The workflow begins by pulling structured financial data from Sectors App API for specified Indonesian stocks. This includes balance sheets, income statements, and key ratios formatted for analysis. Simultaneously, Tavily searches for recent Indonesian-language news articles and regulatory filings related to the target companies.

2. AI Processing Stage

Gemini AI processes the collected data, translating Indonesian content when needed, extracting key financial insights, and identifying relevant market trends. The AI is configured with prompts specific to Indonesian accounting standards and market conventions.

3. Analysis & Reporting

The system generates a comprehensive report comparing the target stock against sector peers, highlighting valuation discrepancies, growth potential, and risk factors specific to the Indonesian market context. Reports include both quantitative metrics and qualitative insights.
