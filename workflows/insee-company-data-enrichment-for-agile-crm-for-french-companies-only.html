What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of maintaining accurate French company data in Agile CRM. Many businesses struggle with incomplete or outdated records, requiring sales teams to manually verify information from multiple sources. The workflow automatically connects Agile CRM with France's official INSEE business database to enrich company profiles with verified data.

By implementing this solution, businesses ensure their CRM contains the most current official information for French companies, including legal addresses, government identification numbers, and business status. This eliminates manual research, reduces errors, and improves the effectiveness of sales and marketing efforts targeting French organizations.

How It Works

1) Extract company records from Agile CRM

The workflow begins by retrieving all company entries from your Agile CRM account. It focuses on records marked as French companies or those needing data verification. The system extracts key identifiers including company names and existing CRM record IDs.

2) Query INSEE OpenData database

Each company name is used to search France's INSEE business registry through their official API. The workflow handles variations in company naming conventions and performs fuzzy matching to account for minor discrepancies between CRM entries and official records.

3) Validate and enrich CRM records

Verified matches trigger updates to the corresponding Agile CRM records. The system adds INSEE-provided data including SIRET numbers, legal addresses, establishment dates, and business activity codes. Unmatched records are flagged for manual review.
