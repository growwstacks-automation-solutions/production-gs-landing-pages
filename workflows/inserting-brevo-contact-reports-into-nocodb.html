What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually exporting Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) contact reports and importing them into NocoDB. Marketing teams often waste hours each week compiling campaign performance data across multiple spreadsheets before analyzing results.

The workflow automatically extracts key engagement metrics from Brevo - including email opens, clicks, unsubscribes, and campaign-specific interactions - then structures this data for seamless insertion into your NocoDB tables. This creates a unified marketing performance dashboard that updates in real-time.

How It Works

1. Retrieving Brevo Contact Reports

The workflow connects to Brevo's API using your credentials to fetch the latest contact engagement data. It can be configured to pull reports for specific campaigns, date ranges, or contact segments.

2. Data Transformation

Raw Brevo data is processed to match your NocoDB schema. The workflow calculates summary metrics, normalizes field names, and formats timestamps to ensure clean data insertion.

3. NocoDB Record Creation

Processed data is sent to your NocoDB instance via API, creating new records or updating existing ones. The workflow handles field mapping and maintains data relationships between tables.
