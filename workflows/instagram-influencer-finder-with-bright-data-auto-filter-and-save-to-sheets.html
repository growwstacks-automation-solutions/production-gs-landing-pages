What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching Instagram influencers for marketing campaigns. It automatically scrapes Instagram profiles using Bright Data's powerful web scraping capabilities, applies your custom qualification filters, and exports vetted influencer profiles directly to Google Sheets.

The system eliminates hours of tedious profile browsing by programmatically evaluating engagement rates, follower authenticity, content relevance, and other key metrics. You'll receive a continuously updated database of pre-qualified influencers ready for outreach, sorted by your priority criteria.

How It Works

1. Profile Scraping with Bright Data

The workflow initiates by scraping Instagram profiles matching your target hashtags, locations, or competitor followers. Bright Data handles the complex data extraction while avoiding rate limits.

2. Automated Qualification Filtering

Each profile undergoes multi-stage validation checking engagement rates, follower growth patterns, and content consistency. The workflow applies your custom scoring algorithm to prioritize high-value influencers.

3. Google Sheets Integration

Qualified influencers export to your designated Google Sheet with all relevant metrics. The template includes pre-formatted columns for contact details, performance stats, and outreach status tracking.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the JSON template into your n8n dashboard
  2. Connect your Bright Data credentials in the HTTP Request node
  3. Configure your filters in the Function nodes
  4. Link your Google Sheets account and select destination
  5. Test with sample hashtags before full deployment
  6. Schedule regular runs (weekly recommended)
