What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of delayed lead responses that plague many businesses. When potential customers reach out via email, every minute counts - research shows response times under 5 minutes can increase conversion rates by 8x compared to responses after 30 minutes.

The workflow monitors your Gmail or SMTP inbox for new messages matching lead criteria. When detected, it immediately notifies designated team members via email with all relevant details, while simultaneously sending a personalized acknowledgement to the lead. This creates a seamless handoff that keeps prospects engaged from first contact.

How It Works

1. Lead Detection

The system continuously scans incoming emails for triggers like specific subject lines, sender domains, or form submission patterns that indicate a sales inquiry.

2. Team Notification

Identified leads instantly trigger customized alerts to your sales team with the lead's contact info, message content, and any attached files - all formatted for quick review.

3. Auto-Response

Simultaneously, the lead receives a personalized reply confirming receipt of their inquiry and setting expectations for follow-up, helping build trust immediately.
