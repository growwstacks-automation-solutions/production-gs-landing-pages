What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation transforms your interview scheduling process by eliminating manual coordination. It monitors your Google Sheet of candidates, automatically books interview slots in Google Calendar, and sends professionally written email invitations using GPT-4.

The system ensures no same-day interviews are scheduled and maintains a conflict-free calendar. By automating these repetitive tasks, recruiters save 5-7 hours per week while delivering a superior candidate experience.

How It Works

1. Candidate Monitoring

The workflow checks your Google Sheet every minute for new candidate entries, extracting their name, email, and position details.

2. Calendar Slot Selection

It scans your Google Calendar for the next available interview slot (defaulting to Mon/Wed/Fri at 3 PM) and reserves it automatically.

3. AI Email Composition

GPT-4 generates a personalized email invitation based on the candidate details, ensuring professional tone and formatting.

4. Automated Dispatch

The system sends the email via your Gmail account with the interview details and calendar invite attached.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Workspace account with Calendar and Sheets access
  2. Google Cloud Platform project with Calendar API enabled
  3. Gmail account with OAuth2 credentials
  4. Azure OpenAI API key (GPT-4 recommended)
  5. Google Sheet formatted with candidate details

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Google Calendar for interviews to keep your personal schedule separate.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Calendar, Sheets, and Gmail accounts
  3. Configure your Azure OpenAI API credentials
  4. Set your preferred interview days/times in the workflow settings
  5. Test with a dummy candidate entry in your sheet
