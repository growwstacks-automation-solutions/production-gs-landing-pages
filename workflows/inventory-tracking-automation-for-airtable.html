What This Workflow Does

Manual inventory tracking creates costly errors and consumes valuable time. This automation solves both problems by connecting your Airtable inventory database with sales channels, supplier systems, and alert mechanisms.

The workflow monitors stock levels in real-time, automatically updating quantities as sales occur or new inventory arrives. When products reach predetermined reorder points, it triggers notifications to your team or directly to suppliers.

Example inventory tracking dashboard showing stock levels and reorder alerts

How It Works

1. Inventory Data Collection

The workflow pulls product data from your sales channels (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.) and updates your Airtable base automatically. Each sale reduces the available quantity while new shipments increase it.

2. Threshold Monitoring

Custom rules monitor inventory levels against your predefined minimum quantities. The system can account for lead times and seasonal demand fluctuations.

3. Alert Generation

When stock dips below thresholds, the workflow triggers email/SMS alerts or even creates purchase orders automatically through connected supplier systems.

Pro tip: Set your reorder threshold to account for supplier lead times plus average weekly sales during that period.
