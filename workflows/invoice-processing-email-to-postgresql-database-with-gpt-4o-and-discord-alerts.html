AI Invoice Processing: Email to Database with GPT-4o & Discord Alerts

Automatically extract data from PDF invoices in email, save to PostgreSQL using AI, and send instant Discord notifications. Eliminate manual data entry.

What This Workflow Does

Manual invoice processing is one of the most time-consuming and error-prone tasks in business operations. Employees waste hours each week opening emails, downloading PDF attachments, manually entering data into spreadsheets or accounting systems, and then notifying relevant team members. This workflow eliminates that entire process through intelligent automation.

The system monitors a dedicated email inbox for incoming invoices, automatically extracts key information using AI, stores the structured data in a PostgreSQL database, and sends instant notifications to your team via Discord. What used to take 15-20 minutes per invoice now happens in seconds with perfect accuracy, giving your team back valuable time while improving financial visibility and control.

How It Works

Step 1: Email Monitoring & PDF Detection

The workflow continuously checks your designated email inbox using IMAP protocol. When a new email arrives with a PDF attachment, it automatically identifies the document as a potential invoice based on sender patterns, subject lines, or attachment characteristics.

Step 2: AI-Powered Data Extraction

The PDF attachment is processed through GPT-4o or similar AI model, which reads the document content and extracts structured information including invoice number, vendor details, invoice date, due date, line items, subtotals, taxes, and total amount. The AI understands context and can handle various invoice formats and layouts.

Step 3: Database Storage & Deduplication

Extracted data is formatted into structured JSON and saved to PostgreSQL database tables. The system checks for duplicate invoices based on invoice number and vendor combination, preventing accidental double-processing. Company information and invoice details are stored in separate but related tables for optimal data organization.

Step 4: Team Notification & Alerting

Once processing is complete, a formatted summary of the invoice is sent to a designated Discord channel (or alternative chat platform). The notification includes key details and a status indicator, allowing your accounting team to review processed invoices at a glance without checking multiple systems.

Pro tip: Configure the AI extraction prompt to match your specific invoice formats and required data fields. The more examples you provide in the prompt, the more accurate the extraction becomes for your particular vendors and invoice styles.

What You'll Need

  1. A dedicated email account for receiving invoices (Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP-compatible service)
  2. PostgreSQL database instance (local, cloud, or managed service like AWS RDS or Supabase)
  3. OpenAI API key or alternative AI provider with document processing capabilities
  4. Discord server with webhook permissions (or alternative notification channel like Slack, Teams, or Telegram)
  5. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud version) to run the automation

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure email credentials in the IMAP node with your invoice-specific email account details.
  3. Set up PostgreSQL connection with your database host, credentials, and ensure the required tables exist.
  4. Add your AI API key in the OpenAI/Language Model node configuration.
  5. Create a Discord webhook and paste the URL into the Discord notification node.
  6. Test the workflow by sending a sample invoice PDF to your monitored email address.
  7. Activate the workflow and set it to trigger automatically on a schedule (every 15-30 minutes).
