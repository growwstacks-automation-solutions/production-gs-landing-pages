Automate Invoice Processing from Gmail to Google Sheets

Save hours of manual work by automatically extracting invoice data from emails to a structured spreadsheet

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of downloading invoice attachments from Gmail, extracting key details, and entering them into spreadsheets. It automatically monitors your Gmail for new invoices, saves PDFs to Google Drive, and uses AI to extract structured data into Google Sheets.

The workflow runs continuously in the background, processing new invoices as they arrive. It handles variations in invoice formats and extracts consistent data fields including invoice numbers, dates, amounts, and vendor information - eliminating human errors from manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow checks your Gmail inbox every 5 minutes for new messages with attachments matching your invoice criteria (specific senders, subject lines, etc.).

2. Attachment Processing

When a matching email is found, the workflow downloads any PDF attachments and saves them to a designated Google Drive folder with consistent naming.

3. AI Data Extraction

Using OpenAI's natural language processing, the workflow analyzes each invoice to extract key details regardless of the invoice format or layout.

4. Spreadsheet Updates

The extracted data gets added to your Google Sheets invoice log, with columns for date, vendor, amount, due date, and a link to the original PDF.

Pro tip: For best results, train the AI with examples of your typical invoices to improve extraction accuracy for your specific use case.
