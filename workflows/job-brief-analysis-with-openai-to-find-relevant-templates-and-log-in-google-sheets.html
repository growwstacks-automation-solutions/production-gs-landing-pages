What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the analysis of job briefs using OpenAI's natural language processing to intelligently match job descriptions with your relevant n8n templates. Designed for recruiters, hiring managers, and operations teams, it eliminates hours of manual template searching by instantly suggesting the most appropriate templates based on the job requirements.

The workflow automatically logs all analyses in Google Sheets, creating a searchable database of job briefs and their matched templates. This provides visibility into your hiring patterns, helps identify frequently used templates, and ensures consistent application of your matching criteria across all job descriptions.

How It Works

1. Job Brief Input

The workflow accepts job briefs through multiple channels - email attachments, web forms, or direct API calls from your ATS. It extracts the full text content while preserving formatting and structure.

2. OpenAI Analysis

OpenAI processes the job description to identify key elements: required skills, experience levels, job responsibilities, and industry-specific terminology. The AI compares these against your template library's metadata and content.

3. Template Matching

Using weighted scoring algorithms, the system ranks your n8n templates by relevance to the job brief. Templates exceeding your confidence threshold are flagged as matches, with the top 3 suggestions highlighted.

4. Google Sheets Logging

Every analysis is recorded in your designated Google Sheet, including the original job brief, matched templates, confidence scores, and timestamps. This creates an auditable trail and enables data-driven hiring decisions.
