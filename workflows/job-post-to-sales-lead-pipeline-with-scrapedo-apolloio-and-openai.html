What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical business problem: identifying companies that are actively growing and ready to buy. When businesses hire for specific roles—like "Senior Data Engineer" or "VP of Marketing"—they're investing in new capabilities. This workflow automatically detects these buying signals from job boards, enriches the company data, finds decision-makers, and creates personalized outreach.

Instead of spending hours manually searching job sites and LinkedIn, this system runs continuously in the background. It transforms raw job postings into actionable sales leads with contact information, company context, and AI-generated personalized messages. The result is a steady stream of qualified prospects who are currently investing in areas relevant to your services.

How It Works

1. Job Post Scraping

The workflow starts by scraping job postings from Indeed using Scrape.do's reliable web scraping API. It uses residential proxies and JavaScript rendering to bypass anti-bot measures, ensuring consistent data collection without getting blocked.

2. Company Data Enrichment

Each company name from the job posts is sent to Apollo.io's B2B database. This returns comprehensive company intelligence including LinkedIn profiles, industry classification, employee count, location data, and company descriptions.

3. Decision-Maker Identification

The system searches Apollo.io for key contacts at each company based on configurable title filters (CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Marketing, etc.). It retrieves email addresses, LinkedIn profiles, and direct phone numbers when available.

4. AI-Powered Personalization

OpenAI generates unique outreach messages for each contact. The AI references the specific job posting, the contact's role, and company context to create messages that feel genuinely tailored rather than templated.

5. Centralized Lead Storage

All enriched leads with personalized messages are saved to Google Sheets, creating an organized database ready for your sales team. The sheet includes tracking columns for outreach status and follow-up dates.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the workflow into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure credentials for Scrape.do, Apollo.io, OpenAI, and Google Sheets
  3. Update the job search URL in the Scrape.do node with your target criteria
  4. Adjust title filters in the Apollo People Search node for your ideal contacts
  5. Test with a manual trigger to verify each step works correctly
  6. Activate the schedule trigger to run weekly or daily based on your needs
  7. Monitor your Google Sheets for new leads and response rates
