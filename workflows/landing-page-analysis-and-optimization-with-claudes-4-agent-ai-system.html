What This Workflow Does

This professional n8n workflow transforms landing page audits from time-consuming manual work into a powerful, automated lead generation machine. It analyzes any landing page URL through four specialized AI agents and delivers a comprehensive, $2,000-value audit report in under 90 seconds.

Perfect for digital marketing agencies, freelance consultants, and SaaS companies, this system showcases your expertise while capturing high-quality leads. Instead of spending hours on manual analysis, you can offer instant, professional-grade audits that demonstrate value and create natural upsell opportunities for your optimization services.

How It Works

The workflow operates as a sophisticated AI analysis pipeline that mimics a team of expert consultants working in perfect coordination.

Step 1: Data Collection & Initial Processing

The workflow starts by collecting the landing page URL and additional context from a web form. It fetches the page content, extracts key elements, and prepares the data for analysis by all four AI agents simultaneously.

Step 2: Four-Agent Parallel Analysis

Four specialized Claude AI agents analyze different aspects concurrently: the Design Critic evaluates UX/UI and visual hierarchy; the Copywriter assesses messaging and emotional triggers; the SEO Specialist audits technical elements; and the Growth Strategist synthesizes findings for business impact.

Step 3: Synthesis & Scoring

The Growth Strategist agent compiles all analyses into a unified scorecard with A-F grades across six dimensions. It creates prioritized action items ranked by impact versus effort, and provides conservative, realistic, and optimistic conversion lift estimates.

Step 4: Professional Report Delivery

The system generates a beautifully formatted HTML email report with all analyses, branded for your business. It includes the comprehensive scorecard, top 5 priorities, strategic recommendations, and a natural call-to-action for your optimization services.
