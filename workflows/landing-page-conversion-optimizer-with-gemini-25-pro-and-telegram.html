What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how businesses optimize their landing pages by combining AI analysis with instant notifications. It solves the time-consuming manual process of reviewing conversion elements by providing instant, data-driven suggestions.

The workflow takes any public landing-page URL, extracts the content, and uses Gemini 2.5-pro's advanced language understanding to evaluate conversion potential. It then sends prioritized improvement suggestions directly to your team via Telegram, creating a continuous optimization loop without manual intervention.

How It Works

Step 1: URL Submission

The workflow starts when you provide a public landing page URL. This can be triggered manually or automatically when new pages are published or manually as needed.

Step 2: Content Extraction

The system scrapes all visible page content including headlines, body copy, CTAs, forms, and trust elements while preserving the structure.

Step 3: AI Analysis

Gemini 2.5-pro evaluates the content against conversion best practices, identifying strengths, and psychological triggers. It generates specific recommendations ranked by potential impact.

Step 4: Telegram Notification

The system formats the analysis into clear action items and sends them to your designated Telegram channel or group, enabling immediate team discussion and implementation.
