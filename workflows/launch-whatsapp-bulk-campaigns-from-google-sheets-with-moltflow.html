Launch WhatsApp Bulk Campaigns from Google Sheets

Automate personalized WhatsApp marketing to hundreds of contacts directly from your spreadsheet. Create groups, send campaigns, and track results—all in one click.

What This Workflow Does

Manual WhatsApp marketing is time-consuming, error-prone, and doesn't scale. Sending individual messages to hundreds of contacts wastes hours better spent on strategy. This automation solves that by connecting your Google Sheets contact list directly to WhatsApp via MoltFlow.

The workflow reads contact data from your spreadsheet, creates a targeted group in MoltFlow, launches a bulk send job with built-in anti-spam delays, and provides a progress report. What used to take a full day of manual copying, pasting, and sending now happens automatically with one click.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger & Data Collection

You execute the workflow when ready to launch a campaign. The automation connects to your specified Google Sheet and reads all contact data including phone numbers, names, and optional custom message overrides.

Step 2: Contact Validation & Formatting

The workflow validates phone numbers, formats them correctly for WhatsApp, and prepares the data for MoltFlow's API. Invalid entries are filtered out and logged for review.

Step 3: Group Creation in MoltFlow

A custom contact group is automatically created in your MoltFlow account containing all validated contacts. This group can be reused for future campaigns or updated dynamically.

Step 4: Bulk Send Launch

The campaign is launched through MoltFlow's bulk send API. The system handles message delivery with intelligent delays between sends to comply with WhatsApp's rate limits and protect your number from being flagged as spam.

Step 5: Progress Monitoring & Reporting

After a 30-second delay, the workflow checks the campaign status and generates a summary report showing messages sent, failed, and remaining. This data can be logged back to your Google Sheet for record-keeping.

What You'll Need

  1. A MoltFlow account with a connected WhatsApp session (scan QR code to link your number)
  2. A Google Sheet with columns for: phone (digits only), name, and message_override (optional)
  3. A MoltFlow API key with custom-groups:manage, bulk-send:manage, and bulk-send:read scopes
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with Google Sheets OAuth2 credentials configured
  5. Basic understanding of how to import and activate n8n workflow templates

Pro tip: Test your campaign with a small group of 5-10 contacts first to ensure formatting and personalization work correctly before sending to your entire list.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets (OAuth2) and MoltFlow (API Key with Header Auth, header name: X-API-Key).
  3. Update the Google Sheet node with your spreadsheet URL and sheet name.
  4. Edit the Code node to replace "YOUR_SESSION_ID" with your actual MoltFlow session ID.
  5. Customize the default message template in the workflow to match your brand voice and campaign goals.
  6. Execute the workflow in test mode with a few contacts, then activate for full campaigns.
