What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire lead qualification and outreach process by combining AI-powered analysis with personalized email generation. When a new lead comes in, the system automatically researches the company using Firecrawl, analyzes the lead's profile with OpenAI, and generates a hyper-personalized outreach email ready for your review or automatic sending via gotoHuman.

The workflow solves the challenge of slow, generic lead follow-up that plagues many sales teams. Instead of sending templated emails or spending hours researching each lead manually, this system delivers personalized, context-aware outreach at scale - helping you stand out in crowded inboxes while saving hours per lead.

The workflow automatically processes new leads through research, analysis, and personalization steps

How It Works

1. Lead Capture

The workflow triggers when a new lead is added to your CRM or database. This could be from a form submission, LinkedIn connection, or any other lead source you connect to n8n.

2. Company Research with Firecrawl

Firecrawl automatically researches the lead's company, extracting key information from their website, news mentions, and other public sources to provide context for personalization.

3. AI Analysis with OpenAI

OpenAI processes the lead's information along with the company research to identify key talking points, potential pain points, and personalized icebreakers for your outreach.

4. Email Generation

Using your email templates as a base, the system generates a completely personalized email draft incorporating the AI's insights about the lead and their company.

5. Delivery via gotoHuman

The final personalized email can be sent directly through gotoHuman or routed to your team for review before sending, depending on your preferences.

Pro tip: For best results, provide the workflow with 2-3 sample email templates in different styles (casual, formal, problem-solving) so the AI can match the tone to each lead's profile.
