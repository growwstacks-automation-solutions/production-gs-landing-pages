What This Workflow Does

This automation system solves the time-consuming challenge of manually collecting business contact information from Google Maps listings. Sales teams, marketers, and recruiters often spend hours searching for and recording potential leads from local business directories.

The workflow automatically scrapes publicly available email addresses from Google Maps business listings based on your search criteria (location, business category, etc.), then organizes the collected data into a structured Google Sheets spreadsheet. This creates a ready-to-use lead database for your outreach campaigns without manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Google Maps Search Execution

The workflow begins by programmatically searching Google Maps based on your configured parameters like location, business type, and radius. It simulates a user search to retrieve relevant business listings.

2. Email Extraction Process

For each business listing found, the system scans the available contact information to identify valid email addresses. It uses pattern recognition to distinguish emails from other text and verifies their format validity.

3. Data Organization in Google Sheets

All collected emails along with associated business information (name, address, phone) are automatically formatted and added to a Google Sheet. The system creates structured columns and avoids duplicate entries.
