What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of efficiently processing and prioritizing incoming leads from web forms. Traditional manual review processes create bottlenecks, causing delays in responding to hot prospects while wasting time on unqualified leads.

The workflow connects Jotform submissions to Gemini AI for instant analysis, scoring each lead based on urgency, budget indicators, and buying signals. High-priority leads trigger immediate Slack notifications to your sales team with all relevant details, while lower-scoring leads get routed for follow-up sequences.

How It Works

Step 1: Form Submission Trigger

When a visitor submits your Jotform, the workflow automatically captures all response data including contact information, answers to qualifying questions, and any uploaded files.

Step 2: AI Analysis Processing

Gemini AI evaluates the submission using natural language processing to detect buying signals, urgency indicators, and fit scoring. The AI assesses factors like budget mentions, timeline specificity, decision-maker involvement, and problem severity.

Step 3: Priority Scoring

Each lead receives a score from 0-100 based on conversion potential. The workflow applies your custom weighting to different factors (e.g., budget = 40%, timeline = 30%, problem fit = 30%) to calculate the final score.

Step 4: Smart Notification Routing

Based on the score threshold you set, high-priority leads (typically 80+) generate instant Slack alerts to your sales channel with the lead's details, score, and key qualifying factors highlighted.
