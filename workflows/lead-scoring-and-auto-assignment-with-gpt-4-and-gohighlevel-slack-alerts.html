AI-Powered Lead Scoring & Assignment for GoHighLevel

Automatically score, categorize, and assign new leads using GPT-4. Tag contacts in GoHighLevel and get instant Slack alerts for hot prospects.

What This Workflow Does

Manual lead qualification is slow, inconsistent, and misses opportunities. Sales teams waste hours reviewing unqualified leads while hot prospects go cold waiting for a response. This creates bottlenecks, uneven workload distribution, and lost revenue.

This automation solves that by bringing AI-powered intelligence directly into your sales process. Whenever a new contact is added to GoHighLevel, the workflow instantly analyzes them using GPT-4. It evaluates their profile, engagement, and intent to generate a score (1-100) and category (Hot, Warm, Cold). The lead is then automatically tagged in GoHighLevel and assigned to the appropriate sales team member. If it's a hot lead, a Slack notification fires immediately so your team can respond within minutes, not days.

How It Works

Step 1: Trigger on New GoHighLevel Contact

The workflow listens for new contacts added to your GoHighLevel account. It captures the contact's full details, including custom fields and recent activity.

Step 2: Fetch & Prepare Lead Data

It consolidates the contact's information—name, email, company, source, notes, and any previous interactions—into a structured format ready for AI analysis.

Step 3: AI Scoring with GPT-4

The core AI node sends this data to GPT-4 with a custom prompt. The AI evaluates the lead based on your defined criteria (budget, authority, need, timeline) and returns a numerical score, a category, and a brief reasoning explanation.

Step 4: Tag & Assign in GoHighLevel

Based on the score, the workflow applies tags like "Lead Score: 85", "Category: Hot", or "AI Qualified". It then updates the contact owner in GoHighLevel, assigning it to the sales rep responsible for that score range or territory.

Step 5: Instant Slack Alert for Hot Leads

If the lead is scored as "Hot" (e.g., above 75), a formatted message is sent to a designated Slack channel. The alert includes the lead's name, score, category, and a link to their GoHighLevel profile for immediate action.

  1. A running n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. GoHighLevel account with API access enabled.

  4. A Slack workspace with permissions to create an incoming webhook.
  5. Basic understanding of your lead scoring criteria (what makes a lead hot, warm, or cold).

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure GoHighLevel: Add your GoHighLevel API URL and key in the "Workflow Configuration" node. Set the correct user IDs for assignment.
  3. Set up AI: Enter your OpenAI API credentials in the "AI Lead Scoring" node. Review the prompt to ensure it matches your business definitions.
  4. Connect Slack: Create a Slack incoming webhook and paste the URL into the "Slack Notify Hot Lead" node.
  5. Adjust thresholds: Optionally modify the score thresholds in the IF nodes (e.g., change "Hot" from >75 to >70).
  6. Activate & test: Turn the workflow on. Add a test contact in GoHighLevel to verify the full cycle—scoring, tagging, assignment, and notification.

Pro tip: Start with a small sample of past leads to calibrate your AI scoring. Feed it 10 known "won" leads and 10 "lost" leads. Adjust your prompt until the AI correctly scores them, then roll out the automation to all new leads.
