What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of lead leakage in sales pipelines. When potential customers submit web forms, there's often a dangerous delay before sales teams respond. Manual processes lead to missed opportunities, duplicate efforts, and inconsistent follow-up.

The workflow automatically captures new leads in HubSpot CRM while simultaneously notifying relevant team members via both Gmail and Slack. This multi-channel approach ensures no lead falls through the cracks, whether your team is checking email, collaborating in Slack, or working directly in HubSpot.

Workflow diagram showing the sequence from form submission to CRM entry and notifications

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates when a new lead submits any connected web form. It captures all form fields including contact details, company information, and custom questions.

2. HubSpot CRM Integration

Lead data automatically syncs to HubSpot, creating or updating contact records. The system maps form fields to corresponding HubSpot properties and applies appropriate tags or lead statuses.

3. Multi-Channel Notifications

Simultaneously, customized alerts deploy to both Gmail inboxes and Slack channels. Notifications include key lead details and direct links to the HubSpot record for immediate follow-up.

Pro tip: Configure different notification templates for various lead sources to provide context-specific guidance to your sales team.
