LinkedIn Content Factory: Auto-Generate Posts with GPT-5 & DALL·E

Transform your LinkedIn strategy with a complete AI-powered content pipeline—from idea to published post in minutes.

What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the biggest challenge for busy professionals and businesses: maintaining a consistent, high-quality LinkedIn presence without spending hours each week on content creation. Instead of using basic AI post generators that just spit out text, this system orchestrates the entire content lifecycle.

It captures content ideas and briefs in Google Sheets, uses GPT-5 to generate unique LinkedIn-ready posts, creates custom images with DALL·E based on the content, stores everything with proper audit trails, and prepares posts for publishing—all while avoiding duplicate content through smart deduplication logic.

The result is a complete content factory that turns half-baked ideas into polished, engaging LinkedIn posts with visuals in minutes, not hours.

How It Works

1. Idea Capture & Briefing

Content ideas are logged in Google Sheets with details like target audience, goals, key angles, and tone. This serves as your content calendar and briefing system.

2. Smart Deduplication

The workflow checks new ideas against previously generated content using fuzzy matching to avoid repetition. It ensures every post feels fresh and original to your audience.

3. AI Content Generation

GPT-5 takes the brief and generates LinkedIn-optimized posts with proper formatting, hashtags, and engagement hooks. The AI maintains your brand voice while producing high-quality copy.

4. Custom Image Creation

DALL·E or similar image AI creates unique visuals based on the post content. This step dramatically increases engagement rates compared to text-only posts.

5. Storage & Organization

Final text and images are uploaded to Google Drive with shareable links. Google Sheets maintains a complete audit trail showing the journey from idea to final asset.

6. Ready for Publishing

The output is a complete LinkedIn post package: compelling text, custom image, and all metadata—ready for your review and scheduling.

  2. OpenAI API key for GPT-5 access

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Set up credentials for OpenAI, Google Sheets, Google Drive, and your image AI service
  3. Duplicate the Google Sheet template and note its ID for configuration
  4. Create a Google Drive folder for storing generated images
  5. Test with a single idea in your Sheet to verify the entire pipeline works
  6. Adjust prompts and settings to match your brand voice and content style
  7. Schedule the workflow to run daily or weekly based on your content needs
