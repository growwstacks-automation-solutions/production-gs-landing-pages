What This Workflow Does

Manual LinkedIn job hunting and outreach is a time-consuming, repetitive process. Recruiters, sales teams, and business developers spend hours scrolling through listings, identifying decision-makers, and crafting personalized messages—only to see low response rates.

This n8n workflow automates the entire pipeline: it scrapes LinkedIn job postings via Apify, extracts hiring manager details, generates AI-crafted referral messages using Gemini, and drafts personalized outreach in Gmail. It turns days of manual work into a fully automated, scalable system that runs on schedule.

The result is a consistent, high-quality outreach stream that fills your recruitment or sales pipeline without constant manual effort. You get structured data in Google Sheets, ready-to-send email drafts, and a repeatable process that adapts to your target roles and companies.

How It Works

Step 1: Automated Job Data Collection

A scheduled trigger initiates the workflow daily. Apify actors scrape LinkedIn for job postings based on your criteria—role, location, company. The data is parsed and split into structured fields: job title, company, location, poster info.

Step 2: Decision‑Maker Discovery

Another Apify actor pulls LinkedIn profiles of relevant decision‑makers (hiring managers, department heads). The data is filtered by city or company name, ensuring you target the right individuals.

Step 3: AI‑Powered Message Generation

Gemini 2.0 Flash analyzes the job and profile data to generate concise, personalized referral messages. The LLM chain follows rules: tone professional, length under 100 words, include specific references. A structured output parser formats the message for consistency.

Step 4: Data Storage & Email Drafting

All scraped job and contact data is appended to Google Sheets for tracking and analysis. Anymailfinder (or similar) fetches professional email addresses from LinkedIn profiles. Finally, the Gmail API drafts the outreach message in your inbox with a pre‑filled subject and body, ready for your review and send.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self‑hosted).
  2. Apify account with credits for LinkedIn scraping actors.
  3. Google Sheets API access and a spreadsheet for data storage.
  4. Gmail API enabled via Google Cloud Console.
  5. Gemini API key from Google AI Studio.
  6. Anymailfinder, Hunter.io, or similar email discovery service.

Pro tip: Use a dedicated Google Sheet for each campaign (e.g., “Software Engineer Roles – March”). This keeps data organized and makes analysis easier.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure Apify actors with your target job criteria (role, location, company size).
  3. Set up Google Sheets – create two sheets: one for jobs, one for contacts.
  4. Add your Gemini API key in the LLM Chain node.
  5. Connect Gmail and your email discovery service (Anymailfinder/Hunter).
  6. Test with a small batch before scaling up.
