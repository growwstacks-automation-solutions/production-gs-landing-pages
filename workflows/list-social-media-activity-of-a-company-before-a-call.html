What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually researching a company's social media presence before sales calls. Sales professionals know that being prepared with recent company updates, product launches, or leadership changes can make conversations more relevant and impactful.

The workflow automatically gathers and organizes social media activity from multiple platforms, presenting key insights in an easily digestible format before your scheduled calls. It eliminates hours of manual research while providing more comprehensive data than any individual could collect manually.

Sample output showing recent social media activity organized by platform

How It Works

1. Trigger from calendar or CRM

The workflow starts when a new sales call is scheduled in your calendar or CRM system. It extracts the company name and any available context about the meeting.

2. Social media platform queries

The system simultaneously queries multiple social media platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.) for recent activity related to the company. It uses official APIs to ensure data accuracy and completeness.

3. Data analysis and prioritization

Advanced filters analyze the collected data to surface the most relevant posts - product announcements, leadership changes, funding news, and highly engaged content that indicates company priorities.

Automated data collection and processing flow

4. Report generation

The system compiles findings into a concise report, highlighting key insights and suggesting potential conversation starters based on the company's recent social media activity.

5. Delivery before meeting

The completed report is delivered to your inbox or CRM at a configured time before the meeting (e.g., 1 hour or 1 day prior), ensuring you have fresh insights when you need them.
