What This Workflow Does

Job postings contain valuable signals about company growth, new initiatives, and specific needs that most businesses overlook. This n8n workflow automates the process of capturing these hiring signals and routing them to your sales and marketing systems.

The automation monitors job boards or your specified sources, extracts key company and role information, then sends enriched lead data to both lemlist for personalized outreach campaigns and Hubspot for CRM tracking. This creates a closed-loop system where hiring signals directly fuel your pipeline with highly qualified leads.

How It Works

1. Job Posting Monitoring

The workflow begins by scanning specified job boards or RSS feeds for new postings matching your target criteria. It can monitor general boards like Indeed or niche industry-specific platforms.

2. Data Extraction & Enrichment

Key details are extracted from each posting including company name, job title, department, required skills, and location. The workflow then enriches this data with additional company information from sources like LinkedIn or Clearbit.

3. Lead Qualification

Using your configured rules, the system scores and qualifies leads based on factors like job volume, department focus, and seniority level. Only the most promising signals proceed to the next steps.

4. Dual System Integration

Qualified leads are simultaneously sent to lemlist to trigger personalized email sequences and to Hubspot to create/update contact records. All contextual hiring data is included for hyper-targeted messaging.

Pro tip: Configure your lemlist templates to reference specific job requirements or skills mentioned in the postings for maximum personalization impact.
