Low stock & sold out watcher for Shopify

Automatically monitor product inventory levels and receive alerts when items running low or sell out completely. Never miss a sale due to stock issues again.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation continuously monitors your Shopify store's inventory levels in real-time, alerting you when products reach critical stock thresholds or sell out completely. It eliminates the need for manual inventory checks and spreadsheet tracking, providing instant visibility into your inventory status.

The workflow helps prevent lost sales from unexpected stockouts while optimizing your purchasing decisions. By automating these checks, you can focus on growing your business instead of micromanaging inventory levels.

How It Works

Step 1: Connect to Shopify API

The workflow first establishes a secure connection to your Shopify store's API to access product inventory data. This happens automatically on a schedule you set (hourly, daily, etc.).

Step 2: Check Inventory Levels

It scans all your products (or specific collections you specify) comparing current stock against your predefined thresholds for low-stock threshold.

Step 3: Trigger Alerts

When products dip below your set threshold or sell out, the workflow triggers customizable alerts via your preferred notification channel (email, Slack, SMS, etc.).

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for:
