What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized outbound sales calls while maintaining compliance and respecting prospect timezones. Traditional cold calling is time-consuming, expensive, and often ineffective due to poor timing and lack of personalization. The workflow automatically initiates AI-powered calls from leads in your Google Sheet, with intelligent scheduling based on each contact's location.

The Retell AI agent handles initial conversations using natural-sounding voice interactions, qualifying leads before escalating warm prospects to your sales team. This approach increases call volume while improving answer rates and conversion metrics compared to manual outreach.

How It Works

1. Lead list processing

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new leads added to specific columns. Each row should contain contact details including phone number, name, and any relevant personalization data for the call script.

2. Timezone detection

Before initiating calls, the system determines the likely timezone for each lead based on their phone number prefix or additional location data in your sheet. This ensures calls only occur during appropriate business hours.

3. AI call initiation

The Retell AI agent places calls using a pre-configured conversation flow that can include personalized elements from your Google Sheet data. The agent handles initial qualification questions and objections.

4. Result logging

Call outcomes (answered, voicemail, not interested, etc.) and any collected information are automatically recorded back to your Google Sheet, creating a complete audit trail of all outreach attempts.
