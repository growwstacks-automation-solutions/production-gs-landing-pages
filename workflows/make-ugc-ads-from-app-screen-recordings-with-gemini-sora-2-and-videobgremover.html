Make UGC Ads from App Screen Recordings

Automatically generate authentic-looking user-generated content ads using Gemini AI, Sora 2, and VideoBGRemover

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating authentic-looking user-generated content (UGC) video ads at scale. Instead of hiring actors or relying on actual users, it transforms your app screen recordings into professional UGC-style ads using AI.

The workflow analyzes your screen recording with Gemini AI to generate a compelling ad script, creates hyper-realistic AI actors with Sora 2, and seamlessly composites them over your app footage using VideoBGRemover. The result is scroll-stopping ads that look like real user testimonials.

How It Works

1. AI Analysis

Gemini AI examines your screen recording and generates a UGC-style script including hook, problem statement, solution demonstration, and call-to-action - all tailored to your app's features.

2. Actor Generation

Sora 2 creates a photorealistic AI actor delivering the script naturally, with appropriate emotional expressions and gestures matching your brand style.

3. Video Composition

VideoBGRemover extracts the actor from their background and composites them picture-in-picture over your app screen recording with proper lighting and perspective matching.

4. Final Output

The completed video is saved to your Google Drive, formatted for platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook with optimized dimensions and file size.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Gemini API key (for video analysis)
  2. FAL AI API key (for Sora 2 access)
  3. VideoBGRemover API key
  4. Google Drive account (for final video storage)
  5. App screen recordings in vertical format (9:16 recommended)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template file
  2. Import into your n8n instance
  3. Add your API keys to environment variables
  4. Connect your Google Drive account
  5. Test with a sample screen recording (4-12 seconds ideal)
  6. Adjust composition settings as needed

Pro tip: For best results, use screen recordings with minimal UI clutter and clear feature demonstrations. The AI performs best when it can easily identify your app's core value proposition.
