What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manual contact form processing that consumes valuable staff time. When potential customers submit inquiries through your website form, this workflow instantly captures their information in an organized Google Sheet, notifies your team via Slack, and sends an automated acknowledgment email through Gmail.

The system eliminates the risk of lost inquiries buried in email inboxes while ensuring prompt responses. It creates a seamless customer experience while freeing your team from repetitive data entry tasks. Businesses using similar automations report saving 5-10 hours per week previously spent managing form submissions manually.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates when your contact form receives a new submission. The system captures all submitted fields including name, email, phone number, and inquiry details.

2. Google Sheets Logging

All submission data automatically populates a designated Google Sheet with timestamp. This creates a searchable database of all inquiries that never gets buried like emails might.

3. Slack Notification

Your team receives an immediate Slack alert in your chosen channel with key submission details. This ensures prompt awareness without requiring email monitoring.

4. Gmail Response

The system sends an automated acknowledgment email confirming receipt of the inquiry. This sets customer expectations while your team prepares a detailed response.

Pro tip: Customize the automated email template to include your average response time and links to relevant FAQs. This reduces follow-up inquiries by 30%.
