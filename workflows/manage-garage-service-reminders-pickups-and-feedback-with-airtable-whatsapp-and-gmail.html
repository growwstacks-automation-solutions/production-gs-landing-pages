What This Workflow Does

Garage and auto service businesses handle much more than just vehicle repairs. They manage complex customer communication cycles: scheduling reminders, pickup notifications, feedback requests, and repeat service prompts. When handled manually, these tasks lead to missed appointments, delayed pickups, unhappy customers, and lost revenue.

This free n8n workflow template delivers a fully automated, end-to-end customer communication system. It connects Airtable (your service database), WhatsApp (for instant messaging), and Gmail (for formal email communication) to create a seamless, professional experience for every customer touchpoint.

The automation ensures timely, consistent, and personalized communication at every stage of the service lifecycle—from the initial reminder to post-service feedback—without requiring any manual intervention from your staff.

How It Works

The system consists of four interconnected workflows that run automatically based on schedules and triggers.

1. Scheduled Service Reminders

Runs daily at 9 AM. It queries your Airtable base for upcoming, due-today, or overdue services. For each matching record, it generates and sends personalized reminders via WhatsApp and email, then marks the reminder as sent in Airtable to prevent duplicates.

2. Instant Pickup Notification

Triggers automatically when a service status in Airtable changes to "Done." It immediately notifies the customer that their vehicle is ready for pickup, sending a confirmation message through both WhatsApp and Gmail, and updates the record to track the notification.

3. Automated Feedback Collection

Activates when the service status becomes "Delivered." It sends a polite feedback request with a link to a simple form, encouraging customers to share their experience. This step only runs once per service to avoid spamming.

4. Next Service Reminder

Also runs daily, checking for upcoming next-service dates. It sends reminders for overdue services, services due today, and services scheduled within the next 7 days, helping to secure repeat business and maintain vehicle health.

Quick Setup Guide

Get your automation running in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Duplicate the provided Airtable base template and populate it with your customer and service data.
  3. Connect your Airtable, Twilio, and Gmail accounts as credentials in n8n.

  6. Activate the workflows and let the automation handle your customer communication.
