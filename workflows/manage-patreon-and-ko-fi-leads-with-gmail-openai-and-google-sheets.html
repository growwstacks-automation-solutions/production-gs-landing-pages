What This Workflow Does

This automation system solves the time-consuming challenge of manually managing communications with your Patreon and Ko-fi supporters. For creators juggling content production with community engagement, manually responding to each new supporter, tracking pledges, and maintaining records can consume 10+ hours weekly.

The workflow automatically processes new supporters from both platforms, generates personalized thank-you messages using OpenAI, sends them via Gmail, and logs all details in Google Sheets. This creates a seamless supporter management system that operates in the background while you focus on creating.

How It Works

1. New Supporter Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Patreon and Ko-fi accounts for new supporters or pledge changes. When detected, it extracts key details like supporter name, pledge amount, and reward tier.

2. AI-Powered Message Generation

OpenAI analyzes the supporter's details to craft a personalized welcome message that matches your brand voice. The AI considers pledge amount, reward tier, and any available supporter notes to create contextually appropriate content.

3. Automated Email Delivery

The generated message is automatically sent through Gmail using your branded email address. The system includes open-tracking and formats the message with your standard signature and styling.

4. Data Logging & Tracking

All supporter details and communication records are automatically logged in Google Sheets, creating a searchable database with timestamps. This becomes your single source of truth for supporter management.
