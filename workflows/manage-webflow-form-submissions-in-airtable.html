What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of Webflow form submissions getting lost in email inboxes or requiring manual data entry. When potential customers, job applicants, or website visitors submit forms on your Webflow site, this workflow automatically creates structured records in your Airtable base.

The solution organizes submissions by form type (contact requests, applications, etc.) into separate tables with all field data preserved. It maintains a complete history of submissions while eliminating the risk of human error during manual data transfer. Businesses using this automation report saving 5-10 hours per week previously spent copying form data between systems.

How It Works

1. Webflow Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Webflow site for new form submissions through the Webflow API. Each time a visitor submits any form, the automation captures all field data including hidden fields and timestamps.

2. Data Processing and Validation

Before transferring to Airtable, the workflow cleans and validates the submission data. It checks for required fields, formats dates consistently, and can apply basic spam filtering rules you define.

3. Airtable Record Creation

The processed form data gets mapped to the appropriate Airtable table based on your configuration. The workflow creates new records with all submission details while maintaining relationships between tables when needed.
