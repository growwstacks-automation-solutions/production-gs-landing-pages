What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually compiling marketing reports from multiple data sources. Marketing teams often spend hours each week extracting data from Google Analytics, formatting it in spreadsheets, creating presentation slides, and emailing reports to stakeholders.

The workflow automatically pulls key marketing metrics from Google Analytics, organizes the data in Google Sheets for analysis, generates presentation-ready slides in Google Slides, and sends email alerts with the completed reports. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures consistent, timely reporting across your organization.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from Google Analytics

The workflow begins by connecting to your Google Analytics account and pulling specified metrics like traffic sources, conversion rates, user demographics, and campaign performance. You can customize which metrics are collected based on your reporting needs.

2. Data Processing in Google Sheets

Raw data from Google Analytics is automatically formatted and organized in Google Sheets. The template includes pre-built formulas to calculate key performance indicators (KPIs) and visualize trends over time.

3. Slide Deck Generation

The processed data is then used to generate a professional slide deck in Google Slides. The template maintains your company branding and includes dynamic charts that update automatically with new data.

4. Report Distribution

Completed reports are automatically emailed to specified recipients on your schedule (weekly, monthly, etc.). The email includes both the slide deck and a link to the live spreadsheet for deeper analysis.
