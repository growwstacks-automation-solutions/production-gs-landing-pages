What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered workflow revolutionizes your recruitment process by automatically analyzing candidate CVs against job descriptions. Using Google's Gemini AI, it extracts key skills, qualifications, and experience from both documents, then provides an objective comparison with a fit score and detailed feedback.

The system eliminates hours of manual CV screening while reducing human bias in the hiring process. It generates professional reports that highlight matches, gaps, and overall suitability - giving hiring managers actionable insights to make better decisions faster.

How It Works

1. Input Collection

The workflow accepts CVs and job descriptions from various sources - email attachments, ATS integrations, or direct uploads. It extracts text content while preserving formatting and structure.

2. AI Analysis

Gemini AI processes both documents, identifying key entities like skills, job titles, education, and experience duration. It understands context rather than just keywords - recognizing that "5 years in marketing" equates to "senior marketing experience."

3. Comparison Scoring

The system creates a weighted match score based on your priority criteria (e.g., technical skills weighted higher than education). It highlights both strong matches and potential gaps in the candidate's profile.

4. Report Generation

A detailed PDF report is created with visual comparisons, fit percentage, and narrative feedback. The report can be customized to emphasize your organization's specific hiring priorities.

5. Distribution

Reports are automatically emailed to relevant stakeholders or saved to your HR system. The workflow can also trigger follow-up actions like interview scheduling for high-scoring candidates.

Pro tip: Configure different weightings for various roles - technical positions might prioritize specific skills, while leadership roles may emphasize management experience.
