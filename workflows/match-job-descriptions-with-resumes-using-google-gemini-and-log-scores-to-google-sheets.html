What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manual resume screening by leveraging AI to objectively compare candidate resumes against job descriptions. Recruiters and hiring managers often spend 5-7 minutes reviewing each resume - this workflow reduces that to seconds while providing standardized scoring.

The system ingests resumes (PDF, DOCX, or text) and job description URLs, extracts clean text, then uses Google Gemini's advanced natural language processing to generate compatibility scores across multiple dimensions. These scores are automatically logged to Google Sheets with timestamps, creating an auditable record and enabling easy candidate comparison.

How It Works

Step 1: Input Collection

The workflow accepts candidate resumes through multiple channels (email attachments, form submissions, or direct uploads) along with the relevant job description URL. This flexibility allows integration with your existing recruitment pipelines.

Step 2: Text Extraction

Resumes and job descriptions are processed to extract clean, formatted text. The system handles various file formats and removes headers, footers, and formatting that could interfere with analysis.

Step 3: AI Analysis

Google Gemini analyzes the extracted text, comparing skills, experience levels, education, and other relevant factors. It generates match percentages for each category plus an overall compatibility score.

Step 4: Data Logging

Scores are automatically recorded in Google Sheets with candidate identifiers, analysis timestamps, and detailed breakdowns. This creates a searchable database of all applicants with consistent evaluation metrics.
