AI Resume to Job Matching with Gemini & Decodo

Automatically analyze candidate resumes, match them to open positions using AI, and deliver ranked shortlists—saving hours of manual screening.

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming problem of manual resume screening by intelligently connecting candidate profiles to relevant job opportunities. It takes candidate intake data—including resumes, summaries, and LinkedIn profiles—then uses Gemini AI to extract skills and experience while Decodo enriches the data with additional profile insights.

The system analyzes the enriched candidate data against job requirements, ranks matches based on relevance scores, and generates polished HTML reports delivered directly to recruiters' inboxes. This eliminates hours of manual review while ensuring consistent, bias-reduced candidate evaluation based on actual qualifications rather than just keyword matching.

For businesses handling high-volume recruitment, this workflow can process dozens of candidates simultaneously, providing ranked shortlists within minutes instead of days. It's particularly valuable for agencies, corporate talent teams, and hiring managers who need to quickly identify top candidates from large applicant pools.

How It Works

Step 1: Candidate Intake & Data Collection

The workflow begins when a candidate submission arrives via webhook from your application form or ATS. It captures the resume file, candidate summary, and optional LinkedIn URL, then parses this information into structured data for AI processing.

Step 2: AI-Powered Resume Analysis

Gemini AI processes the resume content, extracting key skills, years of experience, education details, project accomplishments, and technical competencies. The AI understands context and semantic meaning, going beyond simple keyword matching to identify transferable skills and relevant experience.

Step 3: Profile Enrichment with Decodo

When LinkedIn URLs are provided, Decodo scraping gathers additional professional data including certifications, publications, recommendations, and career progression details. This enriched profile creates a more complete candidate picture than resumes alone can provide.

Step 4: Job Matching & Ranking

The system compares analyzed candidate profiles against job requirements from your database or Decodo's job listings. It calculates match scores based on skill alignment, experience relevance, location preferences, and role-specific criteria, then ranks candidates from most to least suitable.

Step 5: Report Generation & Delivery

An HTML report is automatically generated showing top matches with scores, key matching factors, and candidate summaries. This report is emailed to designated recruiters or hiring managers, complete with actionable insights and next-step recommendations.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Google Gemini API credentials for AI analysis (available through Google AI Studio)
  3. Decodo account and API access for profile enrichment and job data
  4. Gmail or SMTP credentials for report delivery
  5. Candidate intake source (web form, ATS export, or email parsing configured)
  6. Job database or feed containing current open positions with requirements

Pro tip: Start with a small batch of historical resumes and job descriptions to test and calibrate your matching criteria before running live candidates. This helps you refine scoring thresholds and ensure the system aligns with your hiring team's evaluation standards.
