What This Workflow Does

Meal planning is more than just deciding what to eat—it's about managing costs, reducing waste, and maintaining nutritional balance. For home cooks, small restaurant owners, and health-conscious individuals, this process can consume hours each week and lead to significant food waste and budget overruns.

This comprehensive automation template transforms Google Sheets into an intelligent kitchen management system. When you add a new menu item or recipe, the workflow automatically extracts ingredients, calculates costs, suggests recipes for leftovers, provides nutritional analysis, and can even create social media content about your healthy meals. It's like having a personal chef, nutritionist, and accountant working together in your spreadsheet.

Pro tip: This workflow is particularly valuable for meal prep businesses and small restaurants where ingredient costs directly impact profitability. Automating these calculations can reveal hidden cost savings of 15-30%.

How It Works

The workflow uses AI agents and Google Sheets integration to create a seamless kitchen management system.

1. Recipe Input & Analysis

You trigger the workflow by adding a new dish or recipe. A "Menu Agent" AI analyzes your input, extracts all ingredients, and determines current market prices based on your configured database.

2. Cost Calculation & Logging

The system calculates unit costs, quantities needed, and total recipe cost. All this data is automatically appended to your "Recipe List" Google Sheet with timestamps for historical tracking.

3. Leftover Management

A "Leftovers Agent" identifies unused ingredients from your planned dish and suggests three creative recipes to utilize them. This reduces food waste and inspires new meal ideas.

4. Nutritional Analysis

A "Nutritionist Agent" generates diary-style entries with dietary advice, highlighting key nutrients, potential deficiencies, and personalized suggestions based on the meal composition.

5. Content Creation & Sharing

Finally, a "Post Agent" transforms the nutritional analysis into engaging social media content, ready for sharing with your audience or keeping as a personal food journal.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under an hour:

Pro tip: Start with a simple test recipe like "vegetable stir fry" to verify all connections work before adding your full recipe database.

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance using the import functionality.
  2. Create your Google Sheet with three tabs: "Recipe" (for cost tracking), "Leftovers" (for recipe suggestions), and "Diary" (for nutritional notes).
  3. Configure Google Sheets nodes by adding your Sheet ID and selecting the correct tab names in each append operation.
  4. Set up OpenRouter credentials in all AI Agent nodes using your API key from the OpenRouter platform.
  5. Test with a simple recipe by triggering the webhook with a dish name like "chicken salad" and verify data appears in all three sheets.
  6. Customize AI prompts to match your dietary preferences, cost calculation methods, and nutritional focus areas.
  7. Build your ingredient database gradually by adding prices as you use ingredients—the system becomes more accurate over time.
