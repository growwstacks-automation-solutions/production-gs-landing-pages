What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the tedious process of converting paper medical records into organized digital data. It uses Mistral's advanced OCR technology to extract text from scanned documents, then structures the information in Google Sheets for easy access and analysis.

Healthcare providers waste countless hours manually transcribing patient records. This solution eliminates that bottleneck while reducing errors that occur during manual data entry. The system handles everything from patient intake forms to physician notes, creating searchable records that integrate with your existing systems.

How It Works

1. Document Upload and Processing

The workflow triggers when new medical documents are uploaded to your designated cloud storage folder. Supported formats include PDFs, JPEGs, and scanned images of handwritten or typed forms.

2. OCR Text Extraction

Mistral's OCR API analyzes each document, converting visual information into machine-readable text. The system identifies key data fields like patient names, dates, diagnoses, and treatment codes with high accuracy.

3. Data Validation and Structuring

Extracted information passes through validation rules to ensure accuracy. The workflow maps data to standardized fields in your Google Sheets template, maintaining consistent formatting across all records.

4. Secure Storage and Access

Finalized records are saved to your Google Sheets database with timestamps and source references. You can set up row-level permissions to control access while meeting HIPAA compliance requirements.

Pro tip: Create a master patient index in your first Google Sheet tab with hyperlinks to individual patient records for quick navigation.
