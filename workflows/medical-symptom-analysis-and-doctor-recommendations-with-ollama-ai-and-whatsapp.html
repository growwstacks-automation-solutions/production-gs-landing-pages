What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical healthcare challenge of providing immediate, preliminary medical guidance to patients before they can access a doctor. When someone experiences symptoms, they often face long wait times for appointments, uncertainty about symptom severity, and difficulty finding the right specialist. This workflow bridges that gap by offering an AI-powered symptom checker available 24/7 through familiar channels like WhatsApp or web forms.

The system analyzes described symptoms using medical AI models, matches them against known condition patterns, and provides intelligent doctor recommendations with contact details. It creates a structured log of each interaction for follow-up care while reducing the administrative burden on healthcare staff. For healthcare providers, this means better patient triage, reduced no-shows for inappropriate appointments, and more efficient use of clinical resources.

How It Works

1. Symptom Input Collection

The workflow begins when a patient submits symptoms through a configured channel—this could be a WhatsApp message, web form, or chat interface. The system captures all relevant details including symptom description, duration, severity, and any pre-existing conditions the patient chooses to share.

2. AI-Powered Symptom Analysis

Using Ollama or similar AI models trained on medical literature, the system analyzes the symptom patterns against thousands of documented condition profiles. The AI considers symptom combinations, temporal patterns, and severity indicators to generate preliminary condition matches with confidence scores.

3. Doctor Database Matching

The workflow queries a connected doctor database (Google Sheets, CRM, or healthcare API) to find specialists appropriate for the identified conditions. It filters by location, availability, specialty, and insurance acceptance to provide relevant recommendations.

4. Multi-Channel Response Delivery

Personalized recommendations are delivered via the patient's preferred channel—WhatsApp for immediate mobile access, email for detailed information, or both. The message includes suggested doctors with contact information, next steps, and appropriate disclaimers about seeking professional medical care.

5. Interaction Logging & Follow-up

Every interaction is logged with timestamps, symptoms analyzed, conditions matched, and recommendations provided. This creates valuable data for healthcare analytics and enables automated follow-up messages to check on patient status after recommendations.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this medical symptom automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Download and import the JSON template file into your n8n workflows section
  2. Configure AI credentials by setting up Ollama or your chosen medical AI model connection in n8n
  3. Connect your doctor database by updating the Google Sheets or API nodes with your specialist information
  4. Set up communication channels by configuring WhatsApp Business API and email service credentials
  5. Customize medical protocols by adjusting condition matching thresholds and escalation rules for urgent symptoms
  6. Test thoroughly with sample symptom cases across different severity levels before going live
  7. Implement compliance measures including disclaimers, data privacy safeguards, and audit logging

Pro tip: Start with a limited pilot for non-urgent symptoms only, and gradually expand as you validate the AI's accuracy and your escalation procedures. Always include clear disclaimers that this provides preliminary information only, not medical diagnosis.
