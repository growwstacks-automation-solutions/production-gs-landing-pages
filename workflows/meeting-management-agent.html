Meeting Management Agent

Let clients book, reschedule or cancel meetings directly through Telegram - automated 24/7

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow transforms Telegram into a powerful meeting management assistant, allowing clients to book, check availability, reschedule, or cancel appointments through simple chat commands. It eliminates the endless back-and-forth emails typically required for scheduling while providing 24/7 booking access to your clients worldwide.

The automation handles the entire process: checking calendar availability, sending confirmation messages, updating your calendar, and even sending reminders. It's particularly valuable for solopreneurs, consultants, and agencies who need to manage multiple client meetings efficiently without hiring administrative staff.

How It Works

1. Telegram Command Processing

The workflow starts when a user sends a command like "/book" or "/reschedule" to your Telegram bot. The system authenticates the user and presents available time slots based on your calendar rules.

2. Calendar Availability Check

The bot checks your connected calendar (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.) in real-time to determine available slots, respecting your working hours, buffer times between meetings, and any blackout dates.

3. Booking Confirmation

Once the client selects a time, the bot sends a confirmation message with meeting details and adds the event to your calendar. It can also collect additional information through a simple form if needed.

4. Automated Reminders

The system automatically sends reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before the meeting, significantly reducing no-show rates. Clients can cancel or reschedule with simple commands.
