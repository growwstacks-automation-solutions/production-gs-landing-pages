This automation transforms Amazon from a marketplace into a strategic intelligence platform. How It Works

Daily Market Scan

The workflow runs automatically at 6:00 AM UTC daily, capturing fresh competitive data before your team starts work. It targets your specific product categories or market niches, ensuring you monitor relevant competitors rather than generic market noise.

AI-Powered Analysis

ScrapeGraphAI intelligently extracts pricing, product details, and market positioning from Amazon listings. Unlike simple scraping tools, it analyzes competitor descriptions, feature highlights, and review patterns to provide strategic insights beyond raw data.

Competitive Intelligence Processing

The system compares extracted data against your products and pricing benchmarks. It identifies where competitors are undercutting you, where you have pricing advantages, and detects new competitor listings that might threaten your market position.

Keyword Opportunity Identification

The workflow analyzes competitor listings to identify high-value keywords they're using successfully—and keywords they're missing. This reveals SEO opportunities you can leverage to improve your own Amazon search rankings.

Strategic Insights Generation

Based on the collected intelligence, the system generates actionable recommendations for pricing adjustments, listing optimizations, and competitive responses. These insights help you make data-driven decisions rather than guesses.

Automated Reporting

All intelligence is formatted into comprehensive market reports and delivered directly to Google Docs. Reports include competitor pricing tables, trend analysis, opportunity summaries, and recommended actions—ready for team review.

What You'll Need

  1. ScrapeGraphAI API credentials – For intelligent web scraping and AI analysis capabilities.
  2. Google OAuth2 access – To automate report generation and delivery to Google Docs.
  3. Amazon product URLs or categories – To target your specific market niche for monitoring.
  4. Proxy rotation service (optional) – For large-scale monitoring across many product listings.
  5. n8n instance – Either self-hosted or using n8n cloud to run the automation workflow.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template – Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure ScrapeGraphAI – Add your API key in the credentials section for intelligent scraping.
  3. Set up Google Docs integration – Connect Google OAuth2 and specify the destination document.
  4. Customize Amazon targets – Replace sample URLs with your actual product categories or competitor listings.
  5. Test with limited products – Run the workflow on 2-3 products first to validate data accuracy.
  6. Schedule daily execution – Set the schedule trigger to run at your preferred time (default 6:00 AM UTC).
  7. Review initial reports – Check the generated Google Docs for formatting and insight quality.

Pro tip: Start with a narrow focus—monitor 3-5 key competitors rather than entire categories. This ensures data quality and manageable insights before scaling up.
