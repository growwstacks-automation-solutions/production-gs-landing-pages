What This Workflow Does

This automation solution continuously monitors seller activities and content for compliance violations using GPT-4o's advanced natural language processing. It replaces manual review processes with AI-powered detection that identifies policy violations, prohibited items, and deceptive practices with greater accuracy than keyword-based systems.

When violations are detected, the system automatically generates detailed incident reports, notifies compliance teams via Slack, and escalates critical issues through email alerts. The workflow maintains complete audit trails and can integrate with existing case management systems to streamline enforcement actions.

How It Works

1. Content Ingestion

The workflow pulls seller listings, product descriptions, and marketplace content from your ecommerce platform or CRM system. It normalizes the data for analysis and applies initial filters to prioritize high-risk items.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

GPT-4o evaluates each piece of content against your compliance policies, detecting violations that would escape traditional rule-based systems. The AI understands context, identifies disguised prohibited items, and flags deceptive marketing practices.

3. Alert Generation

Confirmed violations trigger real-time alerts routed to the appropriate teams. Slack notifications include violation details and suggested actions, while email escalations are sent for high-priority issues requiring immediate attention.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for marketplace operators, ecommerce platforms, and any business managing third-party sellers. Compliance teams, risk management professionals, and operations managers will benefit most from the automated monitoring and alerting capabilities.
