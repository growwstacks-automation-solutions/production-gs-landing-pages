What This Workflow Does

This automation synchronizes Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) between Monday.com and Jira to eliminate manual tracking and provide real-time visibility into progress. It automatically calculates variance between planned and actual progress, updates dashboards, and shares reports with stakeholders via Slack and email.

By connecting these platforms, teams can maintain alignment between strategic objectives (tracked in Monday.com) and execution (tracked in Jira epics) without manual reconciliation. The workflow runs on a schedule you define (daily/weekly) and only alerts stakeholders when variances exceed your thresholds.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates on your chosen schedule (typically daily or weekly) to fetch the latest OKR data from Monday.com.

2. Extract Key Results

Identifies all active Key Results in Monday.com and extracts their linked Jira epic keys for progress comparison.

3. Fetch Jira Progress

Retrieves current status, completion percentage, and last updates for each linked Jira epic via Jira's API.

4. Calculate Variance

Compares Monday.com target progress with actual Jira progress, calculating variance percentages and flagging risks.

5. Update Monday.com

Pushes actual progress metrics back to Monday.com boards, updating status indicators ("On Track", "At Risk", etc.).

6. Generate Reports

Creates formatted variance reports with owner assignments, progress trends, and recommended actions.

7. Distribute Alerts

Sends reports via Slack to teams and via email to executives, with priority tagging based on variance severity.
