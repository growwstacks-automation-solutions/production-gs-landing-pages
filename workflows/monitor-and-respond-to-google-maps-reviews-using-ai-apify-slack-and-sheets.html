What This Workflow Does

This workflow automates reputation management for physical stores (restaurants, retail, clinics) by monitoring Google Maps reviews, analyzing them with AI, and drafting professional replies. It acts as a 24/7 customer support assistant, ensuring you never miss a negative review while saving hours of manual writing time.

The system checks for new reviews daily, categorizes them by sentiment, drafts context-appropriate responses, alerts your team about critical feedback, and logs everything in Google Sheets for tracking and reporting.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Review Monitoring

The workflow runs on a daily schedule (configurable) to check for new reviews using Apify's Google Maps scraper.

2. Duplicate Prevention

Each review is checked against your Google Sheets database to ensure you only process new feedback.

3. AI Analysis

An AI agent analyzes the review text to determine sentiment, extract key points, and categorize the feedback type.

4. Response Drafting

Based on the star rating and sentiment, the AI drafts a professional, brand-appropriate response template.

5. Team Alerting

Negative reviews (<4 stars) trigger immediate Slack alerts to your customer service channel with review details.

6. Data Logging

All review data, AI analysis, and draft responses are logged in Google Sheets for record-keeping and reporting.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Apify account for Google Maps scraping
  3. Google Cloud Platform with Sheets API enabled
  4. Slack workspace for alerts
  5. OpenRouter or OpenAI API key for AI analysis
  6. Google Maps URL of your business location

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Create a Google Sheet with required columns (reviewId, publishedAt, reviewerName, stars, text, ai_summary, ai_reply)
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets, Apify, Slack, and your AI provider in n8n
  3. Edit the CONFIG node with your Google Maps URL, Sheet ID, and business name
  4. Set up Slack channel alerts for positive and negative reviews
  5. Test with sample reviews to verify AI response quality
  6. Schedule the workflow to run daily (or more frequently if needed)

Pro tip: Customize the AI's system message in the AI Agent node to match your brand voice - formal, friendly, or humorous depending on your business personality.
