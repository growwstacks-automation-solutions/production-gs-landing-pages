What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates comprehensive brand reputation monitoring by analyzing sentiment across multiple digital channels including news sites, social media platforms, review sites, and forums. Using GPT-4's advanced natural language processing, it detects emerging crises, tracks sentiment trends, and alerts your team via Slack and Gmail when potential reputation threats are identified.

The system eliminates manual monitoring efforts while providing more accurate and timely insights than human reviewers. It classifies mentions by severity, identifies influential authors, and can even suggest response templates based on the context of negative sentiment.

How It Works

1. Data Collection from Multiple Sources

The workflow pulls brand mentions from configured sources including Twitter, Reddit, news APIs, and review platforms. It normalizes the data into a standardized format for analysis.

2. AI-Powered Sentiment Analysis

GPT-4 analyzes each mention to determine sentiment (positive/neutral/negative), emotional tone, and potential crisis indicators. The AI understands context and sarcasm better than basic sentiment tools.

3. Threat Classification

Mentions are categorized by severity level (minor complaint, emerging issue, full crisis) based on content analysis, author influence, and spread velocity.

4. Real-Time Alerting

When critical issues are detected, the system immediately notifies designated teams via Slack channels and Gmail with relevant context and suggested actions.

5. Reporting and Trends

The workflow generates daily and weekly reports showing sentiment trends, top issues, and response effectiveness metrics.

Pro tip: Configure different alert thresholds for weekends/holidays when response teams may be smaller. The workflow can automatically adjust sensitivity based on time/day.
