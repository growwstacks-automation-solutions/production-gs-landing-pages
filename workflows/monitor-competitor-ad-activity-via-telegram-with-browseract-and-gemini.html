What This Workflow Does

Manual competitor research is time-consuming and often incomplete. This automation solves that by creating a 24/7 intelligence agent that monitors your competitors' advertising activity across platforms like Facebook and Google. Simply send a company name to your Telegram bot, and within minutes you receive a comprehensive analysis of their ad strategy, including active campaign count, key messaging hooks, and a strategic verdict on whether you should advertise now or wait.

The workflow transforms raw ad data into actionable business intelligence. It's like having a senior marketing analyst working for you around the clock, but without the six-figure salary. For e-commerce brands, agencies, and digital marketers, this represents a significant competitive advantage—you're no longer guessing what works but seeing exactly what your competitors are testing and scaling.

How It Works

1. Receive Command via Telegram

You send a natural language command like "Check ads for Nike" or "Spy on Higgsfield" to your Telegram bot. The workflow triggers immediately, parsing your request to extract the target company name.

2. Extract Brand with AI Agent

A Google Gemini AI agent processes your message, intelligently identifying the specific brand or company you want to investigate, even if your request includes additional context or casual language.

3. Scrape Ad Data with BrowserAct

The BrowserAct node executes a background task using the "Competitor Ad Activity Monitor" template. It searches public ad libraries (Facebook Ad Library, Google Ads Transparency Center) for active campaigns from your target competitor.

4. Analyze Strategy with Senior Marketing Analyst AI

A second AI agent, acting as a senior marketing analyst, reviews the scraped data. It counts active ads, identifies winning hooks and creatives, detects scaling patterns, and determines if the competitor is aggressively advertising or winding down.

5. Deliver Strategic Report to Telegram

The bot sends a formatted HTML scorecard directly to your Telegram chat. This includes the ad count, best-performing ad copy, key insights, and a clear strategic verdict—"ADVERTISE NOW" if they're scaling (opportunity to compete), "WAIT" if they're inactive (save your budget), or "MONITOR" for ongoing observation.
