What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for digital marketers and business owners: staying updated on competitor advertising strategies without manual monitoring. The workflow continuously scans Meta's Ads Library for new creatives from specified competitor pages, automatically logging them to Google Sheets and sending Telegram alerts.

Traditional competitor analysis requires daily manual checks of the Ads Library, which is time-consuming and easy to forget. This automated solution ensures you never miss a competitor's new ad campaign, while building a historical database of their advertising strategies for trend analysis.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Ads Library checks

The workflow runs at regular intervals (daily or weekly) to check Meta's Ads Library for new creatives from your specified competitor pages. It compares against previous scans to identify only new ads.

2. Data extraction and formatting

For each new ad found, the workflow extracts key details including the creative (image/video), ad copy, call-to-action, and landing page URL. This data is structured for consistent logging.

3. Google Sheets logging

All new ads are logged to a designated Google Sheet with timestamps, creating a searchable historical record. The sheet organizes data by competitor and date for easy analysis.

4. Telegram alert generation

When new ads are detected, the workflow sends formatted Telegram messages with the creative preview and key details. Team members receive these alerts instantly on their mobile devices.
