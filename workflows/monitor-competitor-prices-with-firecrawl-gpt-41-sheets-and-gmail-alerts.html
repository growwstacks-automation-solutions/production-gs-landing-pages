What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow continuously tracks competitor pricing across e-commerce websites, analyzes the data using AI, and alerts your team about significant price changes. It eliminates manual price checking while providing actionable intelligence to optimize your pricing strategy.

The system combines Firecrawl's web scraping capabilities with GPT-4.1's natural language processing to extract and interpret pricing data accurately. Results are logged in Google Sheets for historical analysis while immediate alerts are sent via Gmail when competitors adjust prices.

How It Works

1. Competitor Website Crawling

Firecrawl systematically scans competitor product pages at scheduled intervals, extracting current pricing information along with product details. The crawler handles dynamic pricing elements and JavaScript-rendered content.

2. AI-Powered Data Interpretation

GPT-4.1 processes the raw scraped data to normalize pricing formats, identify matching products across different sites, and flag promotional pricing versus permanent changes. This eliminates false positives from temporary sales.

3. Spreadsheet Logging

All pricing data is automatically recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps, creating a historical record for trend analysis. The spreadsheet includes calculated fields showing price differentials and percentage changes.

4. Alert Triggers

When prices change beyond configured thresholds (either dollar amount or percentage), the system immediately sends email alerts through Gmail with details about the change and competitor involved.
