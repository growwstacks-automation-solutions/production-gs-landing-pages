Monitor content trends across social media with AI, Slack and Google Sheets

Automatically track emerging trends across platforms with AI-powered analysis delivered to your team via Slack and organized in Google Sheets

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of manually tracking social media trends across multiple platforms. Marketing teams often waste hours scrolling through feeds trying to identify emerging patterns, while valuable insights get buried in notifications or spread across different tools.

The automation continuously monitors social platforms, uses AI to analyze content patterns and sentiment, then delivers organized trend reports to your Slack channels while maintaining a searchable database in Google Sheets. It transforms raw social data into actionable business intelligence without manual effort.

How It Works

1. Social media data collection

The workflow connects to various social media APIs to gather posts, hashtags, and engagement metrics based on your configured keywords, competitors, or industry terms.

2. AI-powered trend analysis

Using natural language processing, the system identifies emerging themes, sentiment shifts, and viral content patterns across the collected data. It categorizes trends by relevance and potential impact.

3. Slack notifications

Significant trends are automatically formatted and posted to designated Slack channels with priority tagging. The system can @mention specific team members for urgent trends requiring immediate attention.

4. Google Sheets organization

All trend data is systematically logged in Google Sheets with timestamps, platform sources, and analysis metrics. This creates a searchable historical record for strategic planning and performance comparison.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for marketing teams, social media managers, content creators, and business owners who need to stay ahead of industry trends. Agencies managing multiple clients will particularly benefit from the automated reporting and historical tracking capabilities.
