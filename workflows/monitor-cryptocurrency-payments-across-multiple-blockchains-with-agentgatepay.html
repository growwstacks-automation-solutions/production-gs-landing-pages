What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow template solves the challenge of tracking cryptocurrency payments across multiple blockchains when using AgentGatePay for autonomous AI agent transactions. Traditional manual monitoring of blockchain transactions is time-consuming and error-prone, especially when dealing with multiple chains simultaneously.

The automation continuously scans specified blockchain networks for payments to your AgentGatePay wallet addresses, categorizes them by origin, and triggers appropriate actions when payments are detected. This enables your AI agents to autonomously pay for resources while giving you full visibility into all transactions.

How It Works

1. Blockchain Network Monitoring

The workflow connects to blockchain nodes (either your own or public RPC endpoints) to scan for new transactions. It supports Ethereum, Polygon, and other EVM-compatible chains that AgentGatePay works with.

2. Payment Detection

For each detected transaction, the system checks if the recipient address matches any of your registered AgentGatePay wallets. It verifies payment amounts against expected values when applicable.

3. Event Processing

Valid payments trigger downstream actions like updating internal accounting systems, notifying stakeholders via email/Slack, or releasing digital goods/services automatically.
