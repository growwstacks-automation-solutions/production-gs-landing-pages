What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the monitoring of data breaches using the Have I Been Pwned service. It continuously checks whether your email addresses, domains, or passwords appear in new data breaches and sends immediate alerts when compromised credentials are detected.

The solution solves the critical problem of delayed breach discovery, which often leaves sensitive accounts vulnerable for weeks or months. By automating the monitoring process, you reduce response times from days to minutes, significantly improving your security posture against credential stuffing attacks and account takeovers.

How It Works

1. Scheduled breach checks

The workflow runs on a schedule you define (hourly, daily, etc.) to check Have I Been Pwned's breach database. It can monitor multiple email addresses or domains simultaneously through API calls to the service.

2. Breach detection

When the workflow finds a match in breach data, it extracts detailed information including the breach name, date, and types of data exposed (emails, passwords, credit cards, etc.). This context helps prioritize response actions.

3. Alert notifications

Upon detecting compromised credentials, the workflow triggers customizable alerts through your preferred channels (email, Slack, SMS, etc.). Notifications include all relevant breach details and recommended actions.

Who This Is For

This workflow is essential for security teams, IT administrators, and developers responsible for protecting user accounts and company data. It's equally valuable for individuals who want to monitor their personal email addresses and passwords.

Businesses with multiple employees or customer accounts benefit most, as manual monitoring becomes impractical at scale. The automated solution ensures consistent protection across all accounts without requiring individual users to check breach statuses themselves.
