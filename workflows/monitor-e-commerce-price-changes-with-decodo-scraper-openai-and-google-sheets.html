What This Workflow Does

This automated price intelligence system helps e-commerce businesses stay competitive by continuously monitoring product prices across multiple competitors. It combines web scraping technology with AI analysis to provide actionable insights rather than just raw data.

The workflow runs three times daily to capture pricing changes throughout the day. It extracts pricing data using Decodo Scraper, analyzes the information with OpenAI to identify strategic patterns, logs everything to Google Sheets for historical tracking, and sends categorized alerts when significant price changes occur.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Triggering

The workflow activates automatically at 9 AM, 3 PM, and 9 PM to provide comprehensive daily coverage of pricing changes.

2. Product Configuration

Global settings define which products to track, competitive thresholds by category (premium/default/budget), and who should receive alerts.

3. Web Scraping

Decodo Scraper extracts real-time pricing data from configured e-commerce sites, handling anti-scraping measures and dynamic content.

4. AI Analysis

OpenAI processes the scraped data to identify pricing patterns, compare against thresholds, and generate strategic recommendations.

5. Data Logging

All price data gets recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps, creating a searchable historical database for trend analysis.

6. Alert Classification

The system categorizes alerts as CRITICAL (major price drops) or STANDARD (minor fluctuations) based on configured thresholds.

7. Notification Delivery

Targeted email alerts are sent to appropriate team members with the price change details and recommended actions.
