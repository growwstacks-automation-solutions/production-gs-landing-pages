What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious task of manually checking Egyptian gold prices and currency exchange rates throughout the day. For businesses dealing with precious metals or foreign exchange in Egypt, staying updated with market fluctuations is crucial but time-consuming.

The workflow automatically scrapes reliable financial sources for current Egyptian gold prices (typically 24K, 21K, 18K) and EGP exchange rates against major currencies. When prices meet your predefined thresholds, it sends beautifully formatted Telegram notifications to your preferred chat or channel.

Example Telegram notification showing formatted gold and currency prices

How It Works

1. Price Data Collection

The workflow connects to trusted Egyptian financial data sources to fetch the latest gold prices per gram and current exchange rates. It handles the website scraping and data extraction automatically.

2. Threshold Checking

Configure your price thresholds for notification triggers. You can set absolute values (alert when gold hits 2,000 EGP/gram) or percentage changes (notify if USD/EGP moves 1% from last check).

3. Telegram Notification

When thresholds are met, the workflow sends a clean, readable message to your Telegram account or channel. The notification includes all relevant prices with clear labels and optional emoji indicators.

Pro tip: Create a dedicated Telegram channel for price alerts and invite your team members or clients to stay informed together.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for Egyptian gold traders, jewelry businesses, currency exchange offices, import/export companies, and individual investors. Anyone who needs to track these financial metrics regularly will benefit from the automation.
