What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves the common business problem of email overload and delayed responses. Instead of manually checking your inbox and crafting replies, this workflow continuously monitors your email via IMAP, analyzes incoming messages with AI, and sends contextual, professional acknowledgments automatically.

The system provides immediate value by ensuring every email receives a prompt response, filtering out spam and automated messages, and keeping you informed through real-time Telegram notifications. It transforms your email management from a reactive, time-consuming task into a proactive, efficient system that enhances customer communication while freeing up your team's time.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring & Initial Alert

The workflow polls your IMAP email account at regular intervals, checking for new messages. When an email arrives, it immediately sends a detailed Telegram notification containing the sender, subject, and timestamp, ensuring you're instantly aware of all incoming communications.

2. Intelligent Filtering & Processing

Each email passes through smart filters that identify and exclude automated messages, newsletters, and spam. Legitimate emails proceed to the AI analysis stage, while filtered messages are logged without generating auto-replies, preventing unnecessary AI processing.

3. AI Analysis & Response Generation

Using the Ollama local AI model, the system reads the email content, identifies the main topic or purpose in 2-4 words, and generates a professional acknowledgment response. The AI is prompted to create consistent, concise replies that thank the sender and promise a personal follow-up.

4. Automated Reply & Confirmation

The AI-generated response is sent back to the original sender via your SMTP server with a "Re:" subject prefix. Simultaneously, a second Telegram notification confirms the auto-reply was sent and includes the AI-generated content for your review.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud) with AI/LangChain nodes enabled
  2. IMAP email account credentials (Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP-compatible service)
  3. SMTP server access for sending emails (your email provider's SMTP or a service like Postfix)
  4. Telegram Bot API credentials (created via BotFather) and your Chat ID
  5. Ollama installed locally or on an accessible server with the llama3.1 model downloaded
  6. Basic understanding of n8n credential management and workflow activation

Pro tip: Start with a dedicated email account for testing before connecting your primary business inbox. This allows you to refine AI responses and filtering rules without affecting real customer communications.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Download and import the workflow JSON file into your n8n instance using the import function.
  2. Configure credentials for IMAP (email receiving), SMTP (email sending), Telegram API, and Ollama connection in n8n's credential management.
  3. Update node parameters including your Telegram Chat ID in both notification nodes and verify email addresses in the SMTP node.
  4. Test the workflow by sending a test email to your monitored inbox and verifying Telegram notifications and AI responses work correctly.
  5. Activate and monitor the workflow, reviewing initial AI responses for quality before full deployment.
