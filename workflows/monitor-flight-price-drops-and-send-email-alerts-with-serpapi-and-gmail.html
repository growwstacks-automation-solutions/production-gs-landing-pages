What This Workflow Does

Manually tracking flight prices is a time-consuming and frustrating process. Travelers often spend hours checking multiple websites, only to miss price drops or book at suboptimal times. This creates financial waste and travel planning stress.

This automation solves that problem by creating a hands-off monitoring system. It continuously checks flight prices for your specific routes and travel dates using SerpAPI's search capabilities. When prices drop below your predefined threshold, it automatically sends you an email alert via Gmail, enabling you to book immediately at the best available price.

The workflow transforms a reactive, manual chore into a proactive, automated advantage. Instead of constantly checking prices, you set your parameters once and receive notifications only when actionable opportunities arise.

How It Works

1. Schedule & Configuration

The workflow begins with a scheduler that runs at your preferred intervals (daily, hourly, etc.). You configure your travel parameters: origin airport, destination, travel dates, and target price threshold.

2. Price Data Collection

Using SerpAPI, the automation queries flight search engines to gather current pricing data. It extracts the lowest available fares for your specified route and dates, including airline, flight times, and total cost.

3. Price Analysis & Comparison

The workflow compares the retrieved price against your target threshold. If the current price is equal to or lower than your target, it proceeds to the notification stage. If not, it waits for the next scheduled check.

4. Alert Generation & Delivery

When a qualifying price drop is detected, the automation composes a personalized email alert with all relevant details: price, airline, travel dates, and booking links. It then sends this alert directly to your inbox via Gmail.

Pro tip: Set your price threshold 10-15% below current market rates to catch genuine deals while avoiding minor fluctuations that don't represent significant savings.

What You'll Need

  1. SerpAPI Account: Required for accessing flight price data through search engine results.
  2. Gmail Account: Or another email service configured in n8n for sending alerts.
  3. n8n Instance: Either self-hosted or n8n.cloud account to run the workflow.
  4. Clear Travel Parameters: Specific origin, destination, dates, and target price.
