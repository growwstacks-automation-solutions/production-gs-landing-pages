What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the time-consuming problem of manually tracking search engine rankings for your website and competitors. It connects SerpApi (a Google search results API) with Google Sheets to create a powerful SEO monitoring system that runs automatically on your schedule.

The workflow checks Google search rankings for your specified keywords and domains, then logs the positions in a Google Sheet with timestamps. This creates a historical record you can analyze to measure SEO progress, identify ranking drops, and track competitor movements.

How It Works

1. Keyword and Domain Setup

The workflow starts with your predefined list of keywords and domains to track in a Google Sheet. You can easily add or remove targets without modifying the workflow itself.

2. SerpAPI Rank Checking

For each keyword, the workflow queries SerpApi to get current Google search results. It scans the results to find your specified domains and records their positions.

3. Data Processing

The workflow processes the API response to extract ranking data, including position, URL, and search snippet. It can handle cases where your domain appears multiple times in the results.

4. Google Sheets Logging

All ranking data gets written back to your Google Sheet with timestamps, creating a searchable history. The sheet can include additional metrics like position changes from previous checks.
