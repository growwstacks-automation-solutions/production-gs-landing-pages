What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the challenge of manually tracking dozens of job boards to identify growing companies. Marketing agencies, recruiters, and B2B service providers waste hours each week scanning job sites for hiring signals that indicate business expansion.

The workflow automatically monitors specified marketing job boards using Bright Data's web scraping capabilities, then applies GPT-4o's AI to analyze job postings for growth signals. It identifies companies posting multiple marketing roles, detects expansion patterns in job descriptions, and compiles actionable intelligence reports.

How It Works

1. Automated job board scraping

The workflow uses Bright Data to scrape marketing job boards at scheduled intervals. It handles authentication, pagination, and anti-bot measures to reliably extract job postings without triggering blocks.

2. AI-powered opportunity scoring

GPT-4o analyzes each job posting to determine: company growth stage, department expansion patterns, budget signals in benefits/requirements, and strategic direction hints from position descriptions.

3. Intelligent opportunity alerts

The system generates prioritized alerts when it detects strong growth signals, such as multiple senior marketing hires or geographic expansion mentions. These get delivered to your preferred notification channel.
