Medical Equipment Monitoring Automation

Automatically track equipment usage and send maintenance alerts via WhatsApp and email

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution helps healthcare facilities maintain their medical equipment efficiently by automating usage tracking and maintenance alerts. The workflow reads equipment data from a Google Sheet daily, identifies devices needing maintenance or calibration, and sends notifications to technicians via WhatsApp and email.

By automating this process, healthcare organizations can reduce equipment downtime, ensure compliance with maintenance schedules, and free up staff time from manual tracking. The system creates an audit trail of all maintenance activities and escalates critical issues to supervisors when needed.

How It Works

1. Daily Equipment Check

The workflow triggers automatically every morning at 6 AM to review all equipment records. It checks each device's usage hours and maintenance history against predefined thresholds.

2. Data Processing

The system analyzes the Google Sheet data to identify equipment approaching maintenance intervals or overdue for calibration. It categorizes alerts based on urgency levels.

3. Alert Distribution

Technicians receive WhatsApp messages for immediate attention and follow-up emails with detailed instructions. Supervisors get escalated alerts for critical issues.

4. Status Updates

The workflow updates the Google Sheet with new maintenance schedules and logs all alert activities for compliance tracking.
