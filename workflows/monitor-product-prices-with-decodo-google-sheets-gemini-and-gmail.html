What This Workflow Does

Manual price tracking across e-commerce platforms is time-consuming, error-prone, and impossible to scale. This automation solves that by creating a fully automated intelligence system that monitors competitor pricing 24/7.

The workflow combines web data extraction (via Decodo), spreadsheet management (Google Sheets), artificial intelligence (Google Gemini), and automated notifications (Gmail) to create a complete price monitoring solution. It reads your product list and target prices from a spreadsheet, scrapes current prices from competitor websites, uses AI to accurately extract pricing data, compares it against your targets, and sends immediate alerts when opportunities arise.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Data Retrieval

A Schedule Trigger automatically pulls your product tracking list from Google Sheets at regular intervals. This list includes product URLs, competitor links, and your desired price thresholds.

2. Web Data Extraction with Decodo

The Decodo node processes each URL, capturing the full webpage content. This ensures you get complete data, not just surface-level information that simple scrapers might miss.

3. AI-Powered Price Identification

Google Gemini AI analyzes the webpage text to intelligently identify the product name and current price. This AI approach handles complex page layouts, promotional text, and dynamic content that would break traditional scraping methods.

4. Data Cleaning & Validation

A JavaScript node uses regular expressions to clean and validate the AI's output, ensuring consistent formatting and removing any extraneous information before comparison.

5. Smart Price Comparison & Alerting

An If node compares the extracted live price against your target price from the spreadsheet. If the price meets or falls below your threshold, the workflow automatically sends a detailed alert via Gmail to your designated team members.

Pro tip: Set up separate Google Sheets for different product categories or competitors. The workflow can read from multiple sheets, allowing you to organize your monitoring by business unit or region.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Google Account for Sheets and Gmail integration
  3. Google Gemini API key (available through Google AI Studio)
  4. Decodo credentials for web data extraction
  5. Google Sheet with columns for Product URL, Competitor URL, and Target Price

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Prepare your spreadsheet: Create a Google Sheet with columns for Product Name, Product URL, Competitor URL, and Desired Price.
  2. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  3. Configure credentials: Connect your Google Sheets, Gmail, Gemini API, and Decodo accounts in n8n's credentials management.
  4. Update node references: Point the Google Sheets node to your specific spreadsheet and worksheet.
  5. Test with one product: Run the workflow manually with a single product to verify all connections work correctly.
  6. Activate scheduling: Set your preferred monitoring frequency (hourly, daily, etc.) and activate the workflow.
