What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the critical challenge of maintaining optimal solar energy production by providing continuous monitoring and instant alerts. Solar installations often experience performance drops due to equipment issues, shading, or weather conditions that go unnoticed without constant supervision.

The workflow automatically checks your solar production data every 2 hours, compares it against expected output levels, and triggers alerts through both Slack and Gmail when anomalies are detected. Simultaneously, it maintains a comprehensive log in Google Sheets for historical analysis and reporting.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow connects to your solar monitoring API or inverter system to fetch current production metrics including kWh output, system status, and efficiency percentages.

2. Performance Evaluation

Retrieved data is compared against configurable thresholds you set based on your system's capacity and historical performance patterns.

3. Alert Generation

When production falls below expected levels, the system creates detailed alert messages containing the current output, expected output, and percentage difference.

4. Multi-Channel Notification

Alerts are simultaneously sent to a designated Slack channel for immediate team visibility and to Gmail for formal documentation and manager notifications.

5. Data Logging

All measurements are timestamped and recorded in Google Sheets, creating an auditable history for performance analysis, maintenance scheduling, and reporting.
