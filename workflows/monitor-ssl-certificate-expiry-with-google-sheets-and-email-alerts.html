Monitor SSL certificate expiry with Google Sheets and email alerts

Automatically track SSL certificate expiration dates across your domains. Get proactive email notifications and maintain a centralized Google Sheets log of all certificates with remaining days until expiry.

Download Template JSON · Zapier compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of SSL certificate expiration which can cause website outages and security warnings. When certificates expire unexpectedly, businesses face immediate downtime, lost revenue, and damaged credibility with visitors seeing browser security alerts.

The workflow automatically checks SSL certificate validity periods across all your domains, logs expiration dates in a centralized Google Sheet, and sends email alerts well before certificates expire. This eliminates manual tracking spreadsheets and prevents last-minute renewal scrambles that risk service interruptions.

How It Works

1. Domain List Configuration

The workflow begins by reading your list of domains from a Google Sheet or configured array. This centralized management point lets you easily add or remove domains without modifying the automation itself.

2. SSL Certificate Checking

For each domain, the workflow queries the SSL certificate information using free API endpoints. It extracts the expiration date, issuer details, and remaining validity period with cryptographic verification.

3. Expiry Threshold Evaluation

The system compares each certificate's remaining days against your configured warning thresholds (typically 30, 15, and 7 days before expiration). Different alert levels can trigger different notification actions.

4. Google Sheets Logging

All certificate data gets recorded in your designated Google Sheet with timestamps, creating an audit trail. The sheet automatically sorts certificates by expiration date for prioritized renewal planning.

5. Email Alert Generation

When certificates approach expiration thresholds, the workflow sends formatted email alerts to your IT team with all relevant details. Notifications include direct links to renewal portals when available.
