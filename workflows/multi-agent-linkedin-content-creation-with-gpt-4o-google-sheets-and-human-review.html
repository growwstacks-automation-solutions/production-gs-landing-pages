What This Workflow Does

This production-grade content engine solves the challenge of maintaining a consistent LinkedIn presence with high-quality, technically authoritative content. Many professionals struggle to post regularly while maintaining depth and authenticity in their content. This workflow combines the efficiency of AI with human oversight to create a scalable content pipeline.

Unlike generic AI content generators, this multi-agent system assigns specialized roles to different AI instances - one for research, another for drafting, and others for optimization and formatting. The human review step ensures all content aligns with your personal brand and expertise before scheduling.

How It Works

1. Content Ideation

The workflow begins by analyzing trending topics in your industry and suggesting content ideas based on your expertise areas. These ideas are logged in Google Sheets for easy team collaboration.

2. Multi-agent Generation

Different GPT-4o agents work in sequence - first researching the topic, then drafting content with appropriate technical depth, and finally optimizing for LinkedIn's algorithm and readability.

3. Human Review & Editing

Generated drafts are sent to a Google Sheet where you or your team can review, edit, and add personal anecdotes. This ensures the content maintains your authentic voice.

4. Scheduling & Publishing

Approved posts are automatically formatted for LinkedIn and scheduled according to your optimal posting times, creating a consistent presence without daily effort.

Pro tip: Use the Google Sheet to track which types of content perform best, creating a feedback loop that improves your AI agents' output over time.
