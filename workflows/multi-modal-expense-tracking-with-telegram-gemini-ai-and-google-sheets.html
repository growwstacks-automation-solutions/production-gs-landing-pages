Multi-modal expense tracking with Telegram, Gemini AI & Google Sheets

Automate your bookkeeping by capturing receipts via Telegram, extracting data with AI, and logging to Sheets - eliminating manual data entry

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Traditional expense tracking creates friction that leads to procrastination and disorganized finances. This automation solves three core problems: 1) Eliminates manual data entry by using AI to extract information from receipts, 2) Removes app switching by letting you log expenses directly from Telegram, and 3) Automatically organizes everything in Google Sheets for reporting and tax purposes.

The system handles all common expense scenarios - from restaurant receipts and travel invoices to digital subscriptions and mileage tracking. Simply send a photo, PDF, or even a voice note describing the expense to your Telegram bot, and the workflow will process it through Gemini AI to extract key details before logging the structured data to your designated Google Sheet.

How It Works

1. Expense Capture via Telegram

Users forward receipts or expense details to a dedicated Telegram chat. The system accepts photos of paper receipts, PDF invoices, or even voice messages describing expenses. This happens in the messaging app people already use daily, removing the friction of switching to a dedicated expense app.

2. AI Processing with Gemini

Each submission gets processed by Gemini AI which extracts: date, amount, vendor, category, payment method, and any tax details. The AI handles variations in receipt formats and can even interpret handwritten notes or partial images. Uncertain items get flagged for review while clear data proceeds automatically.

3. Google Sheets Integration

Structured expense data gets logged to your designated Google Sheet with columns for all key fields. The workflow can create monthly tabs automatically, apply conditional formatting, and even add calculated fields like tax breakdowns or mileage rates. Formulas reconcile totals while preserving the original receipt images as references.
