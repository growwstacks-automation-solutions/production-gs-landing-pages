What This Workflow Does

Content creators and marketers spend hours manually extracting insights from YouTube videos and reformatting them for different social platforms. This process is repetitive, time-consuming, and limits your ability to scale content production.

This automation solves that by monitoring your YouTube channels, using AI to extract and summarize transcripts, then generating platform-optimized content for LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Threads, and Instagram automatically. It turns one video into multiple ready-to-publish posts, complete with tone adjustments for each platform's audience.

The workflow centralizes everything in Google Sheets for easy tracking and collaboration, sends previews via Telegram for quick approval, and prevents duplicate processing by checking existing records. It's like having a content assistant that works 24/7.

How It Works

1. YouTube Channel Monitoring

The workflow starts by reading your YouTube channel URLs from a Google Sheet. It extracts channel IDs, then uses an RSS reader to check for new videos periodically. This ensures you never miss fresh content from your monitored channels.

2. AI-Powered Transcript Processing

When a new video is detected, the workflow calls the Supadata API to extract the full transcript. This AI service converts speech to text accurately, even with background noise or multiple speakers. The transcript is then passed to OpenRouter AI for summarization and analysis.

3. Multi-Platform Content Generation

The AI generates four distinct content versions from the summarized transcript: a professional LinkedIn article (≤1300 characters), a punchy X/Twitter thread (≤280 characters per tweet), a conversational Threads post, and an Instagram caption focused on visual storytelling. Each version is optimized for platform-specific engagement patterns.

4. Centralized Management & Notification

All generated content, along with video metadata, is written back to Google Sheets for easy review and editing. A Telegram bot sends you a preview of the content for quick approval before publishing. The workflow tracks processed video IDs to avoid duplicates on future runs.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompts in the workflow to match your brand voice. Adjust the tone parameters for each platform—more professional for LinkedIn, more conversational for Threads—to maximize engagement.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (self-hosted version recommended for community nodes)
  2. Google Sheets account with OAuth2 credentials for reading/writing
  3. Supadata API key for YouTube transcript extraction
  4. OpenRouter API key or alternative AI service for content generation
  5. Telegram Bot Token & Chat ID for content preview notifications

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials for Google Sheets, Supadata, OpenRouter, and Telegram in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Update the Google Sheet ID in the workflow to point to your tracking sheet.
  4. Adjust the schedule trigger to match your desired monitoring frequency (daily, hourly, etc.).
  5. Test with one YouTube video to ensure all connections work and content generation meets your standards.
  6. Review the Telegram preview and adjust AI prompts if needed before enabling full automation.

Pro tip: Start by monitoring just one or two YouTube channels. Once you're satisfied with the content quality and workflow reliability, add more channels gradually.
