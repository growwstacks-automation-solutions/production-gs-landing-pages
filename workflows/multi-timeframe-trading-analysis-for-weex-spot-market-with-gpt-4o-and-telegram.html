What This Workflow Does

This advanced trading automation system combines WEEX spot market data with GPT-4o's analytical capabilities to generate multi-timeframe trading insights. The workflow continuously monitors price action across different chart intervals (15m, 1h, 4h, daily), processes the data through AI models, and delivers formatted trading signals directly to Telegram.

The system acts as a 24/7 trading assistant that identifies potential setups based on technical patterns, volume analysis, and market sentiment. Unlike basic alert bots, this solution provides contextual analysis explaining why a setup might be significant, along with suggested risk management parameters.

How It Works

1. Data Collection from WEEX API

The workflow begins by pulling real-time and historical spot market data from WEEX's API endpoints. This includes candlestick patterns, order book depth, recent trades, and volume profiles across multiple timeframes simultaneously.

2. Multi-timeframe Analysis

Collected data undergoes technical analysis across different time horizons. The system correlates short-term price action with longer-term trends to identify high-probability setups where multiple timeframes align.

3. AI Processing with GPT-4o

Technical data feeds into GPT-4o which interprets patterns, assesses market conditions, and generates human-readable analysis. The AI considers traditional indicators (RSI, MACD) alongside unconventional pattern recognition.

4. Signal Generation & Formatting

The workflow structures AI insights into actionable trading signals with clear entry points, stop-loss levels, and profit targets. These include annotated charts and concise bullet-point summaries.

5. Telegram Notification Delivery

Final signals push to configured Telegram channels or private chats with proper formatting. Traders receive mobile alerts with all necessary information for quick decision making.

Pro tip: Configure different Telegram channels for different timeframes (e.g., #15m-alerts for scalping, #daily-setups for swing trading) to keep your trading organized.
