What This Workflow Does

This advanced research agent combines multiple automation tools to support high-impact decision-making for animal advocacy organizations. It serves as a reusable component that can be integrated into larger automation systems.

The workflow performs real-time Google searches using Serper API, extracts content with Jina AI, conducts semantic searches across the Open Paws knowledge base, and routes analysis through OpenRouter's multiple AI models. This creates a comprehensive research system that would otherwise require manual coordination of multiple tools.

How It Works

1. Search Execution

The workflow initiates with Google Search queries through Serper API, retrieving the most current web results on your research topic.

2. Content Extraction

Jina AI and Scraping Dog tools extract and clean content from search results, preparing it for analysis while handling anti-scraping measures.

3. Knowledge Base Lookup

The system queries Open Paws DB - an animal advocacy-specific knowledge base - to surface relevant facts and historical context.

4. AI Analysis

OpenRouter routes content through appropriate AI models (GPT-4, Claude, etc.) based on the analysis task, providing summaries, sentiment analysis, or other requested outputs.
