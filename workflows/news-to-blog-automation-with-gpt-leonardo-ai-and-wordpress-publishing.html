What This Workflow Does

This end-to-end automation solves the constant pressure to produce fresh, engaging blog content. It turns the manual, time-consuming process of news monitoring, article writing, image creation, and publishing into a seamless, hands-off system.

The workflow continuously scans multiple news sources for trending headlines, filters them for relevance, then uses advanced AI to generate original blog articles in your brand's voice. It doesn't just copy news—it creates substantive, well-structured content that provides genuine value to your readers. Finally, it publishes the complete package (article + custom AI-generated image) directly to your WordPress site and logs everything for tracking.

For content marketers, bloggers, and media companies, this represents a fundamental shift from reactive content creation to proactive content automation. You maintain editorial control while eliminating 90% of the manual work typically involved in daily publishing.

How It Works

The automation follows a sophisticated multi-stage pipeline that ensures quality and relevance at every step.

1. News Aggregation & Filtering

The workflow begins by fetching the latest headlines from configured news sources like Google News and GDELT. It merges results, removes duplicates, and applies your custom filters to select only the most relevant topics for your audience. This ensures you're automating content around subjects that actually matter to your readers.

2. AI-Powered Content Creation

Selected headlines are sent to GPT with your specific instructions for tone, style, and structure. The AI doesn't just summarize—it expands on the news with background context, analysis, and actionable insights tailored to your niche. You can specify word count, formatting preferences, and even include calls-to-action that match your marketing goals.

3. Visual Asset Generation

While the article is being written, the workflow simultaneously triggers Leonardo AI to create a custom image based on the article topic. You define the visual style (photorealistic, illustration, abstract) and Leonardo generates unique artwork that complements the content, avoiding generic stock photography.

4. Automated Publishing & Tracking

The completed article and image are formatted into a WordPress post with proper categories, tags, and SEO metadata. The workflow handles the entire publishing process, then logs the publication details to Google Sheets for performance tracking and content calendar management.

Pro tip: Configure the AI to write in the style of your top-performing existing content. Provide 2-3 examples of your best articles as reference to ensure consistent brand voice across all automated posts.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this automation running in under 30 minutes with these straightforward steps:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure credentials in n8n for OpenAI, Leonardo AI, and WordPress with appropriate API permissions.
  3. Customize news filters to match your niche—set keywords, exclude irrelevant topics, and define quality thresholds.
  4. Set your brand voice in the GPT node instructions. Include examples of your writing style for best results.
  5. Define image style preferences in the Leonardo AI node (realistic, artistic, specific color schemes).
  6. Test with a single execution to verify all connections work before enabling scheduled automation.
  7. Set your publishing schedule—daily, multiple times per day, or weekly based on your content strategy.

Important: Start with a conservative publishing frequency (once daily) and monitor quality for the first week. Adjust AI instructions based on the output before scaling up volume.
