What This Workflow Does

Professionals today subscribe to dozens of industry newsletters covering finance, legal updates, market trends, and regulatory changes. While valuable, these emails create inbox overload, often going unread despite containing critical information. This workflow solves the newsletter overwhelm problem by automatically processing subscription emails, extracting key insights using AI, organizing the data in Google Sheets, and delivering concise daily briefings.

The system transforms hours of reading into minutes of actionable insights. Instead of skimming or skipping newsletters, you receive distilled versions highlighting only the most relevant information. The Google Sheets integration creates a searchable knowledge base, while scheduled email briefings ensure you never miss important updates.

How It Works

1. Newsletter Identification

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for incoming newsletters using sender addresses, subject line patterns, and labels you define. It filters out personal and non-newsletter emails to focus only on subscription content.

2. AI-Powered Summarization

Each identified newsletter passes through an AI processing step that extracts key points, identifies trends, and generates concise summaries. The AI preserves critical data like numbers, dates, and names while eliminating redundant content.

3. Google Sheets Organization

Summarized content gets logged in Google Sheets with metadata including source, date, keywords, and the full summary. This creates a structured knowledge base that's searchable and filterable by topic or date range.

4. Scheduled Briefing Delivery

At your preferred time (typically morning), the system compiles summaries from recent newsletters into a single briefing email. You can customize the format, grouping summaries by topic or priority level.

Pro tip: Train the AI by reviewing initial summaries and flagging any missed points. The system learns your preferences over time for increasingly accurate results.
