What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates community monitoring and reporting for Nostr and Damus platforms by combining AI-powered analysis with multi-channel notifications. It helps community managers, moderators, and project owners stay informed about important conversations, trends, and potential issues across decentralized social networks.

The system scans Nostr events and Damus posts, applies AI analysis to categorize content, detect sentiment, and identify key topics, then delivers summarized reports via both Gmail and Telegram. This saves hours of manual monitoring while ensuring you never miss critical community developments.

Configuring the Nostr community node in n8n workflow editor

How It Works

1. Nostr Event Collection

The workflow connects to Nostr relays using your private key to fetch events from specific channels or authors you want to monitor. It filters content based on your configured criteria (keywords, hashtags, etc.).

2. AI Analysis & Categorization

Each collected event is processed through AI services (like OpenAI) to extract sentiment, detect key topics, and classify content into predefined categories (support requests, feedback, announcements, etc.).

3. Report Generation

The system compiles analyzed data into digestible reports highlighting trends, notable posts, and community sentiment. It can generate daily summaries or trigger alerts for urgent matters.

AI analysis results showing sentiment and topic detection

4. Multi-Channel Delivery

Final reports are automatically sent to both your Gmail inbox as formatted emails and to Telegram channels or groups as instant notifications, ensuring you receive updates through your preferred channels.

Pro tip: Configure Telegram bot commands to request on-demand reports when you need immediate insights between scheduled deliveries.
