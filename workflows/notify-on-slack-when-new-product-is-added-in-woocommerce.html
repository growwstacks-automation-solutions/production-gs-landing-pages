Notify on Slack when new product is added in WooCommerce

Get instant Slack notifications whenever new products are added to your WooCommerce store

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical communication gap in e-commerce operations. When new products are added to WooCommerce, multiple teams need to be informed immediately - from marketing to customer support to fulfillment. Manual notifications are time-consuming and often get missed in busy workdays.

The workflow automatically detects new product additions in WooCommerce and sends detailed Slack notifications to your designated channels. This ensures your entire team stays synchronized about new inventory without requiring manual updates or checking the admin dashboard constantly.

How It Works

1. WooCommerce Trigger

The workflow starts when a new product is created in your WooCommerce store. The n8n workflow polls your WooCommerce API at regular intervals to detect new additions.

2. Product Data Extraction

The workflow retrieves all relevant product information including name, description, price, SKU, and featured image. This ensures your team gets complete context about the new product.

3. Slack Notification Formatting

The product data is formatted into a clean, readable Slack message. The workflow can include product images, pricing details, and direct links to the product page for quick access.

4. Channel Delivery

The formatted message is sent to your specified Slack channel(s). You can configure different channels for different product categories or departments if needed.

Who This Is For

This automation is essential for any WooCommerce store with multiple team members who need product updates. It's particularly valuable for:

  • E-commerce managers overseeing product launches
  • Marketing teams planning promotions
  • Customer support teams preparing for inquiries
  • Warehouse staff managing inventory
  • Multi-department businesses where product information needs wide distribution

Pro tip: Create separate Slack channels for different product categories and route notifications accordingly. This keeps discussions organized and prevents notification overload.

What You'll Need

  3. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
