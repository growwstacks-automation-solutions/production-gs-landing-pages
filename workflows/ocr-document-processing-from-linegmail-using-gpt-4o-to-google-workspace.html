What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious problem of manually processing documents received through digital channels like LINE messaging or email. Instead of downloading attachments, copying text, or retyping information, the workflow automatically extracts text using OCR (Optical Character Recognition), processes it with GPT-4o AI for understanding and summarization, then saves the structured results to Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, or Drive).

Businesses receiving invoices, application forms, contracts, or other documents via digital channels can process them instantly without human intervention. The AI component adds intelligent analysis - extracting key data points, summarizing lengthy documents, or categorizing content - turning raw documents into actionable business information.

How It Works

1. Document Capture

The workflow monitors your LINE account or Gmail inbox for new messages containing document attachments. When a new document arrives, it's automatically captured for processing.

2. OCR Text Extraction

Image-based documents (scans, photos, PDFs) go through OCR processing to extract all text content. The system handles various formats including JPG, PNG, PDF, and more.

3. AI Analysis with GPT-4o

The extracted text is sent to GPT-4o for intelligent processing. Depending on your needs, this can include summarization, data extraction, categorization, or sentiment analysis.

4. Google Workspace Integration

The processed results are saved to your Google Workspace - creating organized Docs, adding rows to Sheets, or storing files in Drive with proper naming and folder structure.

Pro tip: Configure different processing rules for different senders or document types. For example, invoices can be parsed into spreadsheet columns while contracts get summarized in Docs.
