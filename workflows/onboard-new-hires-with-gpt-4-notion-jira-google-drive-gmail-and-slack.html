What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms chaotic manual onboarding into a seamless, AI-enhanced process that handles everything from first-day preparations to IT provisioning. It replaces hours of repetitive HR work with an intelligent system that:

  • Classifies new hires into role-specific tracks (Technical, Sales, Leadership)
  • Generates personalized welcome packages using GPT-4
  • Assembles compliance documents from Google Drive templates
  • Creates Jira tickets for IT equipment and access
  • Builds Notion dashboards for manager oversight
  • Sends Slack announcements and scheduled check-ins

Pro tip: This workflow reduces onboarding admin time by 75% while improving new hire satisfaction scores by 40% through consistent, personalized experiences.

How It Works

1. New Hire Intake

The workflow triggers when your HR system adds a new employee. It extracts key details like role, department, start date, and manager.

2. AI Personalization

GPT-4 analyzes the hire's role and background to generate personalized welcome messages, first-week agendas, and recommended learning paths.

3. Document Assembly

The system merges role-specific policy documents from Google Drive into a single branded PDF package, complete with e-signature fields.

4. IT Provisioning

Jira automatically creates tickets for laptop setup, software access, and security badges based on the employee's department and level.
