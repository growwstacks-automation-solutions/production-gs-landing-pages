What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-intensive process of creating YouTube Shorts by combining three powerful AI tools. It generates complete Shorts packages including scripts, voiceovers, and visuals with a single trigger. Content creators and marketers can produce professional-quality Shorts in minutes rather than hours.

The workflow connects GPT for script generation, ElevenLabs for natural voiceovers, and Leonardo AI for stunning visuals. It handles the entire production pipeline automatically, allowing you to focus on strategy and distribution rather than manual content creation.

How It Works

1. Script Generation

GPT analyzes your topic and generates an engaging 30-60 second script optimized for YouTube Shorts. The workflow includes prompts to ensure the script follows best practices for short-form video.

2. Voiceover Creation

ElevenLabs converts the script into a natural-sounding voiceover. You can customize voice characteristics to match your brand personality, from tone to pacing and emotion.

3. Visual Generation

Leonardo AI creates complementary visuals based on the script content. The workflow includes parameters to maintain visual consistency across your Shorts while keeping content fresh.
