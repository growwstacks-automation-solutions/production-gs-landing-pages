What This Workflow Does

Creating consistent, engaging social media content is time-consuming and often leads to creative burnout. This automation solves that by leveraging OpenAI's language models to generate tweet ideas and drafts based on your specified topics and hashtags.

The workflow automatically produces social media content, stores it in Airtable for team review and approval, and creates a structured content calendar. A JavaScript snippet manages topic rotation and hashtag generation, ensuring varied and relevant content that aligns with your marketing strategy.

Instead of spending hours brainstorming and writing tweets, you get a steady stream of pre-generated content that your team can review, edit, and schedule in minutes. This transforms social media management from a daily chore into a streamlined, scalable process.

How It Works

1. Topic & Hashtag Management

A JavaScript node handles your content topics in the form of hashtags or keywords. You can define multiple categories, and the system rotates through them to ensure content variety and coverage across your focus areas.

2. AI Content Generation

OpenAI receives the topics and generates tweet drafts following your brand guidelines, tone, and length requirements. You can customize prompts to match your specific voice—professional, casual, humorous, or educational.

3. Structured Storage in Airtable

Generated tweets are sent to Airtable as draft records. Each entry includes the content, associated hashtags, generation date, and status fields. This creates a centralized content repository that your entire team can access.

4. Review & Approval Workflow

Team members review drafts in Airtable, make edits if needed, and mark them as approved. Approved content can then be scheduled for publication or exported to your social media management tools.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for marketing teams, content creators, social media managers, small business owners, and agencies who need to maintain an active social media presence without the daily time investment. It's particularly valuable for businesses posting 3+ times per week across platforms.

If you struggle with content consistency, experience writer's block, or want to scale your social media efforts without hiring additional staff, this workflow provides the infrastructure to generate quality content systematically.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. OpenAI API key with GPT-3.5 or GPT-4 access
  3. Airtable account with a base and table ready for content storage
  4. Basic understanding of your brand voice and content pillars
  5. List of topics/hashtags relevant to your audience

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n workspace
  2. Configure OpenAI node with your API key and adjust the prompt for your brand voice
  3. Set up Airtable credentials and map the fields to your existing base structure
  4. Customize the JavaScript node with your specific topics and hashtag rotation logic
  5. Test the workflow with a manual trigger to ensure content generation and storage work correctly
  6. Schedule execution (daily or weekly) based on your content needs

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 core content pillars and expand as you refine your prompts. Save successful tweets in a "best performers" Airtable view to train future AI generations.
