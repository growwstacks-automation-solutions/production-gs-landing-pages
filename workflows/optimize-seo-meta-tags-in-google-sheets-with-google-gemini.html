What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually crafting SEO-optimized meta tags for websites. It connects Google Sheets with Google Gemini's AI capabilities to analyze content and generate compelling meta titles and descriptions that improve search visibility.

The workflow automatically processes spreadsheet data containing page URLs or content, sends it to Gemini for optimization, then returns AI-generated meta tags that balance keyword relevance with readability—all while maintaining consistent character limits and formatting requirements.

How It Works

1. Spreadsheet Input

The workflow reads your Google Sheet containing page content or URLs that need meta tag optimization. This can include product pages, blog posts, or service listings.

2. AI Analysis

Google Gemini processes each entry, analyzing the content against current SEO best practices and competitor data to determine optimal keyword placement and messaging.

3. Tag Generation

The AI generates multiple meta title and description variations that stay within Google's recommended character limits (60 for titles, 160 for descriptions) while incorporating target keywords naturally.

Pro tip: Use conditional formatting in Google Sheets to visually flag meta tags that exceed character limits or lack primary keywords.

Who This Is For

This automation benefits SEO specialists, content managers, and marketing teams managing large websites. It's particularly valuable for e-commerce sites with thousands of product pages, publishers with extensive content libraries, or agencies handling multiple client websites.
