What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms recruitment by combining AI assessment with operational efficiency. It addresses three major pain points in modern hiring: the overwhelming volume of applications, inconsistent candidate evaluation, and lack data-driven insights during screening.

The system automatically processes incoming applications through multiple AI models (GPT-4 and Claude), then compiles structured assessments in Google Sheets while alerting teams via Slack. This creates a candidate evaluation pipeline that operates at scale.

How It Works

1. AI-Powered Resume Screening

Incoming applications are processed through Claude for initial qualification checks against configured criteria, with GPT-4 performs deeper behavioral and cultural fit analysis.

2. Structured Assessment Output

AI generates standardized scorecards evaluating technical skills, communication quality, and red flags - all formatted for Google Sheets.

3. Real-time Hiring Team Coordination

Slack channels receive categorized alerts (urgent hires, diversity candidates, potential mismatches) while Sheets becomes single source of truth for team decisions.
