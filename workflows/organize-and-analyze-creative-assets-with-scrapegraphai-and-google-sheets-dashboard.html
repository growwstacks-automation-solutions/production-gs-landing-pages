What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of managing growing libraries of creative assets while maintaining brand consistency. Marketing teams, creative directors and brand managers often struggle with disorganized files, inconsistent tagging and manual compliance checks.

The workflow automatically processes uploaded assets through ScrapeGraphAI for intelligent analysis, generates comprehensive metadata, checks brand guidelines compliance, organizes files systematically, and maintains a real-time Google Sheets dashboard. It eliminates hours of manual work while improving accuracy and discoverability of creative assets.

How It Works

1. Asset Upload Trigger

A webhook endpoint activates when new creative assets are uploaded to your storage system, kicking off the automation process.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

ScrapeGraphAI examines each asset to extract colors, text content, visual style elements and generates descriptive tags automatically.

3. Brand Compliance Check

The workflow compares analyzed assets against your brand guidelines for colors, logos, typography and other standards.

4. Automated Organization

Files are renamed and organized into logical folder structures based on type, usage context and other metadata.

5. Dashboard Update

All asset information is recorded in a Google Sheets dashboard that serves as a searchable catalog for your creative team.
