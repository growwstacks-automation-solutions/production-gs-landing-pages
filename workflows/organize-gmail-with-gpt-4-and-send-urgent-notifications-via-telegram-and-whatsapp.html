What This Workflow Does

This automation solves two critical email management problems: disorganized inboxes and missed urgent messages. It uses GPT-4 to intelligently categorize and prioritize your incoming emails while simultaneously monitoring for time-sensitive communications that require immediate attention.

When urgent messages arrive, the system bypasses your inbox and sends instant notifications directly to your preferred messaging platforms (Telegram and WhatsApp), ensuring you never miss important communications even when you're not actively checking email.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages, checking both the inbox and specified labels for incoming communications.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

Each email is processed by GPT-4 which evaluates the content to determine importance, urgency, and appropriate categorization based on your predefined criteria.

3. Smart Organization

The system automatically applies labels, moves messages to appropriate folders, and flags priority items based on the AI's analysis of each email's content and context.

4. Urgency Detection

Messages identified as urgent trigger instant notifications sent simultaneously to both Telegram and WhatsApp, with customizable alert messages.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for busy professionals, executives, customer support teams, and anyone who receives high volumes of email where critical messages might get lost in the noise. It's particularly valuable for:

  • Business owners managing multiple communication channels
  • Remote teams coordinating time-sensitive projects

Pro tip: Train GPT-4 with examples of what constitutes "urgent" in your specific context to improve notification accuracy.

What You'll Need

  1. A Gmail account with API access enabled
  2. OpenAI API key for GPT-4 access
  3. Telegram bot token and chat ID
  4. WhatsApp Business API access or Twilio account
  5. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your Gmail connection with OAuth credentials
  3. Add your OpenAI API key in the GPT-4 node settings
  4. Set up Telegram and WhatsApp webhook connections
  5. Define your urgency criteria and notification preferences
  6. Test with sample emails and activate the workflow
