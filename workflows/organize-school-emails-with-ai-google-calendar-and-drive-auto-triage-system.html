What This Workflow Does

School administrators and teachers often drown in a flood of emails from students, parents, and staff - with critical information mixed with routine communications. This workflow solves that problem by automatically analyzing each incoming message using AI, then taking appropriate actions based on the content.

The system can identify event invitations and add them to Google Calendar, detect assignment submissions and file them in the correct Drive folder, recognize permission slips needing approval, and categorize other communications for efficient processing. This eliminates manual sorting while ensuring nothing important gets missed.

How It Works

Step 1: Email Retrieval

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages matching school-related criteria (specific senders, subject patterns, or labels). It pulls these emails for processing while leaving personal messages untouched.

Step 2: AI Content Analysis

Each email gets analyzed by AI to determine its purpose and content type. The system looks for event dates, assignment details, document attachments, and other key information - classifying messages into categories like "Calendar Event", "Document Submission", or "General Announcement".

Step 3: Automated Triage

Based on the AI classification, the workflow takes appropriate actions: adding events to calendars with all details pre-filled, saving attachments to organized Drive folders, forwarding specific message types to relevant staff, or applying labels for follow-up.

Pro tip: For best results, train the AI classifier with examples of your common email types during initial setup to improve accuracy.
