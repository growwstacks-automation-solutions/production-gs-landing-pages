What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the tedious process of manual invoice data entry by combining Nanonets' powerful OCR technology with Excel export capabilities. It transforms unstructured invoice data (PDFs, scanned images, emails) into structured, actionable information ready for accounting systems.

The solution eliminates the most time-consuming aspect of accounts payable processing—manual data transcription—while reducing human errors that lead to payment delays and reconciliation issues. Businesses processing just 50 invoices monthly can save 20+ hours of administrative work while improving data accuracy.

How It Works

1. Invoice submission and capture

The workflow accepts invoices through multiple channels—email attachments, cloud storage, or direct uploads. Supported formats include PDF, JPG, PNG, and TIFF files containing invoice data.

2. Nanonets OCR processing

Each invoice is sent to Nanonets' AI-powered OCR engine which identifies and extracts key fields like vendor details, invoice numbers, dates, line items, and totals with over 95% accuracy.

3. Data validation and formatting

Extracted data undergoes validation checks—verifying numerical calculations, detecting missing fields, and standardizing formats. The workflow can flag anomalies for human review when configured.

4. Excel export and organization

Validated data is structured into Excel columns with proper formatting for dates, currencies, and calculations. The system can create separate sheets by period, vendor, or department as needed.
