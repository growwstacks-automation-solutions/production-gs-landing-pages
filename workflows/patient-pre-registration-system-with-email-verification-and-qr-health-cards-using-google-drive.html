What This Workflow Does

Manual patient check-in processes create bottlenecks, data errors, and frustration for both staff and patients. This automated pre-registration system eliminates those pain points by handling the entire digital intake journey automatically.

When a patient submits their appointment information through your website form, this workflow instantly validates their email address to ensure deliverability, generates a professional digital health card with a scannable QR code for quick reception check-in, stores the card securely in Google Drive with organized patient ID naming, and sends personalized notifications to both the patient and your reception team via email and Slack. All this happens in under 20 seconds, turning what was previously 15-30 minutes of manual work into an instant, error-free process.

The system creates a complete audit trail in Google Sheets, providing valuable analytics on patient flow, verification rates, and appointment patterns. This isn't just about saving time—it's about creating a professional, modern patient experience that reduces no-shows, improves data accuracy, and lets your clinical staff focus on care rather than paperwork.

How It Works

1. Patient Data Capture & Validation

The workflow begins when a patient submits their pre-registration form on your website. A webhook captures all essential details: name, email, phone number, appointment date/time, symptoms, age, and gender. The system immediately validates required fields and cleans the input data to ensure consistency.

A unique patient ID is generated using timestamp and random components (format: PAT-{timestamp}-{random}), creating a reliable tracking identifier that prevents duplicates and enables easy record retrieval.

2. Email Verification & Quality Control

Before proceeding, the workflow uses the VerifiEmail API to check the patient's email address for RFC compliance, valid MX records, and filters out disposable or spoofed email addresses. This crucial step prevents failed communications and ensures your clinic can reliably contact patients for reminders, follow-ups, and results.

If verification fails, the system gracefully handles the error by notifying your reception team via Slack with the patient's phone number, allowing manual follow-up while maintaining the workflow's integrity for valid emails.

3. QR Health Card Generation

The system creates a unique verification URL and generates a scannable QR code using a free QR Server API. This QR code becomes the patient's digital check-in token—reception staff simply scan it upon arrival to instantly pull up their complete file.

A beautiful, responsive HTML health card is built featuring a modern gradient design with the patient's information grid, symptoms section, appointment details, and embedded QR code. The HTML is then converted to a high-quality PNG image (900x1200px) using HTMLCSSToImg API, ensuring perfect display on any device.

4. Secure Storage & Multi-Channel Notification

The generated health card PNG is uploaded to a designated "Patients record" folder in Google Drive, organized by patient ID for easy retrieval. The system then sends a professionally formatted email to the patient containing their health card as an attachment, appointment confirmation, Google Drive backup link, and preparation instructions.

Simultaneously, a real-time Slack message alerts your reception team with patient details, verification status, and the Drive link. All patient information, timestamps, verification results, and file links are logged to Google Sheets for comprehensive tracking and analytics.

5. Webhook Response & Completion

Finally, the workflow returns a JSON response to your website form with the patient ID, confirmation message, and Drive link, providing immediate feedback to the patient and completing the automated loop in under 20 seconds total.

What You'll Need

  1. VerifiEmail API account for email verification (free tier available)
  2. HTMLCSSToImg API account for converting HTML to images

  4. Slack workspace for team notifications
  5. Google Sheets for logging and analytics

Pro tip: Start with the free tiers of VerifiEmail and HTMLCSSToImg APIs—they typically offer sufficient capacity for small to medium clinics. Upgrade only when your patient volume justifies it.

Quick Setup Guide

2. Configure credentials for VerifiEmail API, HTMLCSSToImg API, Google Drive, Gmail, Slack, and Google Sheets in n8n's credentials management.
