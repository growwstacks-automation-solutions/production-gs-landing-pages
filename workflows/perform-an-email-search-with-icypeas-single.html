What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates single email address verification using Icypeas, an email validation service. It solves the critical business problem of maintaining clean contact lists by instantly checking whether an email address exists and can receive messages before you add it to your database or send communications.

Invalid email addresses waste marketing resources, hurt sender reputation, and create inefficiencies in recruitment processes. This template provides real-time validation that integrates seamlessly with your existing systems, preventing bad data from entering your workflow and ensuring your messages reach actual recipients.

How It Works

Step 1: Input email address

The workflow begins by receiving an email address from your chosen trigger source. This could be a web form submission, CRM update, or manual entry through n8n's interface.

Step 2: Connect to Icypeas API

The workflow authenticates with your Icypeas account credentials and sends the email address to their verification API endpoint. The connection is secured with HTTPS encryption.

Step 3: Process verification results

Icypeas returns detailed validation data including whether the address exists, if the mailbox can receive mail, and whether it's a role-based address (like info@ or support@). The workflow interprets these results.

Step 4: Output validation status

The final step delivers a clear validation status that you can route to other systems. Typical outputs include "Valid", "Invalid", "Risky", or "Unknown", along with specific reasons for the classification.
