What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow transforms research queries into publication-ready HTML content by combining Perplexity AI's web research capabilities with GPT-4's content generation. It automates the entire process from initial research question to formatted output, saving content teams hours of manual work while improving consistency and coverage.

The system is particularly valuable for businesses needing to produce large volumes of accurate, well-researched content quickly. Marketing agencies, publishers, and e-commerce sites using similar automation report 3-5x increases in content output with better SEO performance and factual accuracy.

How It Works

1. Research Phase

The workflow starts by sending your query to Perplexity AI, which scours the web for current, relevant information. Unlike standard search, Perplexity returns synthesized answers with proper citations, saving hours of manual research.

2. Content Generation

GPT-4 then structures the research into coherent content, applying your preferred tone, style guidelines, and formatting rules. The AI can produce anything from blog posts to product descriptions while maintaining brand voice consistency.

3. HTML Conversion

The final step automatically converts the content into properly structured HTML with headings, lists, and semantic markup. This ready-to-publish output integrates seamlessly with most CMS platforms.

Pro tip: For best results, provide clear instructions about tone, target audience, and content structure in your initial query. The more specific your guidelines, the better the AI can match your needs.
