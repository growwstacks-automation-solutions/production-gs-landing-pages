What This Workflow Does

This template solves the problem of digital fragmentation. Most professionals juggle multiple apps for communication (Telegram/WhatsApp), email (Gmail), scheduling (Google Calendar), and task management (Google Tasks). Switching between these apps wastes time and creates mental overhead.

The workflow creates "Jackie"—a personal AI assistant that lives in your Telegram messenger. Instead of opening four different apps, you simply message Jackie. Ask "What's my day look like?" and get a consolidated summary of unread emails, upcoming calendar events, and pending tasks. Send a voice note saying "Remind me to call John tomorrow at 3 PM" and Jackie creates the task automatically.

It transforms your messaging app into a command center for your digital life, saving 30+ minutes daily on routine checks and data entry while ensuring nothing slips through the cracks.

How It Works

The automation follows a clear sequence that mimics how a human assistant would process requests:

1. Telegram Trigger & Input Processing

The workflow starts when you send a message to your Telegram bot. It immediately detects whether the input is text or a voice message. For voice inputs, it automatically transcribes the audio using OpenAI's Whisper API, converting speech to text before further processing.

2. AI Assistant Processing

The transcribed or text message goes to Jackie (powered by OpenRouter's language model). Jackie analyzes the request, checks conversation history for context, and determines which tools to use based on your query—whether you're asking about emails, calendar, tasks, or a combination.

3. Google Services Integration

Based on the AI's analysis, the workflow connects to your Google account through secure OAuth2 authentication. It can: fetch unread emails with sender/subject/summary details; retrieve calendar events for specific dates (filtering out irrelevant future items); and both create new tasks and retrieve existing ones from your Google Tasks lists.

4. Response Generation & Delivery

Jackie formulates a natural, helpful response using the gathered information, current date/time context, and your conversation history. The response is sent back through Telegram in clean Markdown format, making it easy to read with proper formatting for lists, dates, and priorities.

What You'll Need

  1. Telegram Bot Token: Create via @BotFather on Telegram (free)
  2. OpenAI API Key: For voice-to-text transcription (Whisper API)
  3. OpenRouter API Key: For the AI language model processing
  4. Google OAuth2 Credentials: Enable Gmail, Calendar, and Tasks APIs in Google Cloud Console
  5. n8n Instance: Self-hosted or n8n.cloud account
  6. Basic understanding of webhooks (for Telegram bot setup)

Pro tip: Start with a separate Google account for testing before connecting your primary account. This lets you verify permissions and functionality without risking your main data.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to deploy your personal AI assistant in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download & Import: Click the download button above to get the JSON file, then import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Telegram Bot: Create your bot via @BotFather, get the token, and paste it into the Telegram trigger node. Set up the webhook URL from n8n.
  3. Set Up API Credentials: Add your OpenAI, OpenRouter, and Google OAuth2 credentials to their respective nodes in the workflow.
  4. Test Basic Functions: Send a text message to your bot first to verify the Google connections are working properly.
  5. Enable Voice Processing: Once text works, test voice messages to ensure transcription is functioning.
  6. Customize Responses: Adjust the AI prompt in the OpenRouter node to match your preferred communication style.
  7. Activate & Use: Turn on the workflow and start messaging your new assistant!
