What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation solves a critical e-commerce challenge: providing personalized shopping assistance at scale without increasing support staff. Traditional online stores force customers to navigate complex menus or wait for email responses, leading to abandoned carts and lost sales.

The workflow creates a hybrid AI assistant that handles two distinct scenarios seamlessly. For product searches, it understands natural language queries like "red handbags under $100" and fetches matching items from WooCommerce. For general inquiries, it answers store-related questions by retrieving information from your documents using RAG technology.

By combining product discovery with instant customer support, this automation transforms your WooCommerce store into a 24/7 conversational shopping experience that increases conversions, reduces support tickets, and builds customer loyalty through personalized interactions.

How It Works

1. Chat Interaction & Intent Detection

When a customer sends a message through your chat interface, the workflow first analyzes their intent using OpenAI. The system determines whether they're searching for products or asking general questions about your store policies, hours, or services.

2. Product Search Path

If the customer wants to find products, the workflow extracts specific parameters like keywords, price ranges, SKUs, or categories. It then queries your WooCommerce store with these filters, checking stock availability and returning relevant matches with product details, images, and pricing.

3. General Inquiry Path

For store-related questions, the workflow uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with documents stored in Google Drive. It searches through your FAQ documents, policies, and information sheets to find relevant content, then generates accurate, context-aware answers using OpenAI.

4. Response Delivery

The final step formats and delivers the response back through your chat interface, whether that's a website widget, messaging platform, or customer support dashboard. The entire process happens in seconds, providing instant assistance that would normally require human intervention.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this personal shopper chatbot in your WooCommerce store:

  1. Download and Import the template JSON file into your n8n instance using the import workflow feature.
  2. Configure Credentials by adding your WooCommerce API keys, OpenAI API key, and Google Drive authentication in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Prepare Documents by uploading your store FAQ, policies, and product information to a dedicated Google Drive folder.
  4. Update Node Settings in the workflow to point to your specific WooCommerce store URL, Google Drive folder ID, and preferred OpenAI model.
  5. Test the Workflow with sample queries to ensure product searches and general inquiries return accurate results.
  6. Connect to Chat Interface by configuring the webhook or trigger node to receive messages from your preferred chat platform.
  7. Deploy and Monitor the workflow, then gradually expand its capabilities as you see positive results.
