What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized onboarding for creator partnerships. When working with dozens or hundreds of content creators, manually researching each one's social presence and crafting tailored welcome messages becomes incredibly time-consuming.

The workflow automatically enriches new creator contacts in HubSpot with multi-platform social data from influencers.club, then uses GPT-4 to generate personalized onboarding sequences based on the creator's niche, audience demographics, and content style. This ensures every creator feels valued from day one while saving your team hours of manual research.

How It Works

Step 1: New creator contact captured in HubSpot

The workflow triggers when a new creator contact is added to your HubSpot CRM, either manually or through a form submission. Essential details like name, email, and primary social handle are captured.

Step 2: Social data enrichment from influencers.club

The system queries influencers.club's API to fetch comprehensive social media data for the creator, including audience demographics, engagement rates, content themes, and performance metrics across all their platforms.

Step 3: GPT-4 generates personalized content

Using the enriched social data, GPT-4 crafts a customized welcome message, suggests relevant collaboration opportunities, and creates a tailored onboarding checklist based on the creator's specific strengths and audience.

Step 4: Automated HubSpot sequence initiation

The personalized content is added to HubSpot and triggers a multi-touch onboarding sequence, scheduling emails, tasks for your team, and follow-ups based on creator engagement.
