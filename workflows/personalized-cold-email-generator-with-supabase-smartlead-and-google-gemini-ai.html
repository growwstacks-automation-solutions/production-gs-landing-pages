What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates personalized cold email outreach at scale by combining Supabase for lead storage, Google Gemini AI for personalization, and Smartlead for email delivery. It solves the challenge of sending mass emails that still feel personal and relevant to each recipient.

Traditional cold email campaigns often suffer from low response rates because they lack personalization. This workflow solves that by dynamically generating unique email content for each lead based on their profile data stored in Supabase, resulting in higher engagement and conversion rates.

How It Works

1. Lead Data Retrieval

The workflow starts by fetching lead data from your Supabase database, including contact information and any relevant personalization fields (like company name, industry, or past interactions).

2. AI Personalization

Google Gemini AI analyzes each lead's data and generates personalized email content tailored to their specific context. The AI can incorporate references to mutual connections, relevant industry trends, or personalized value propositions.

3. Email Delivery

The personalized emails are sent through Smartlead, which handles deliverability optimization, tracking, and follow-up sequences. Smartlead's infrastructure ensures your emails land in inboxes rather than spam folders.

4. Response Tracking

The workflow logs responses and engagement metrics back to Supabase, allowing you to refine your targeting and messaging over time based on actual performance data.
