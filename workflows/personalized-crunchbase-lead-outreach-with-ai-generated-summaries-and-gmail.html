What This Workflow Does

Manual lead generation from platforms like CrunchBase is time-consuming and inconsistent. Sales teams spend hours researching companies, crafting personalized emails, and following up—only to reach a fraction of their potential market. This creates bottlenecks in your sales pipeline and causes missed opportunities with high-value prospects.

This automation solves that problem by creating a seamless pipeline from CrunchBase to your inbox. It automatically identifies target companies based on your criteria (industry, funding stage, location), enriches the data with AI-generated company summaries, crafts personalized outreach emails, and sends them via Gmail at optimal times. The system also tracks responses and can trigger follow-up sequences, turning what was a 10-hour weekly task into a fully automated process.

How It Works

1. Target Identification

The workflow queries CrunchBase's API using your predefined filters—such as companies that raised Series A funding in the last 90 days, operate in your target industry, or are located in specific regions. This ensures you're only reaching out to qualified, timely prospects.

2. AI-Powered Enrichment

Each identified company's data is passed through an AI model that generates a concise, insightful summary. This might highlight recent news, leadership changes, or product launches—giving you talking points for personalized outreach that demonstrates genuine research.

3. Email Personalization & Sending

The system merges company data and AI insights into your email templates, creating uniquely personalized messages for each recipient. These are then sent via Gmail with optimized timing (avoiding weekends, targeting business hours) to maximize open rates.

4. Response Tracking & Follow-ups

When recipients reply, the workflow can categorize responses (positive, negative, requesting more info) and trigger appropriate next actions—like scheduling a meeting, sending additional materials, or adding to a nurture sequence.

What You'll Need

  1. CrunchBase API access – You'll need an API key from CrunchBase to query company data
  2. Gmail account – Or another email service provider (SendGrid, Mailgun, etc.) configured in n8n
  3. AI model access – OpenAI API key or access to another LLM service for generating summaries
  4. n8n instance – Either self-hosted or n8n.cloud account
  5. Target criteria – Clear definition of your ideal customer profile (industry, company size, funding stage, etc.)

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials for CrunchBase API, your AI service, and Gmail in n8n's credentials management
  3. Set your target filters in the CrunchBase node—adjust industry codes, funding ranges, and geographic parameters
  4. Customize email templates with your messaging, branding, and call-to-action
  5. Test with a small batch by limiting the initial run to 5-10 companies to verify everything works correctly
  6. Schedule automation to run daily or weekly based on your outreach volume needs

Pro tip: Start with broader targeting, then refine based on response rates. Track which company attributes (specific industries, funding amounts, employee counts) generate the highest engagement, and adjust your filters accordingly.
