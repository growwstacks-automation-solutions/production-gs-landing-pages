What This Workflow Does

Manual cold outreach is time-consuming and often ineffective. Sales teams spend hours researching each lead on LinkedIn and Crunchbase, then crafting personalized emails—only to see low reply rates because the messaging feels generic.

This n8n workflow automates the entire process: it pulls lead data from your list, enriches each contact with LinkedIn profile and company details plus Crunchbase insights, uses Google Gemini AI to generate a tailored email draft, and then runs a quality-check “judge” agent to ensure the output is approved before writing it back to your database.

The result is a scalable, AI‑driven outreach engine that produces highly relevant emails for every lead, saving 5–10 hours per week per salesperson while increasing engagement through genuine personalization.

How It Works

Step 1: Fetch & Enrich Leads

The workflow starts by reading your lead list from a Data Table (like Airtable or Google Sheets). It filters rows where the email hasn’t been drafted yet. For each lead, it triggers parallel enrichment tasks via RapidAPI: scraping LinkedIn profiles, LinkedIn company pages, and Crunchbase company data. The raw JSON results are stored back on the row for AI to use.

Step 2: AI Draft Generation

A “Creative Outreach Agent” powered by Google Gemini takes the enriched data—job title, company info, funding stage, industry—and combines it with your predefined “About Me” block (your role, company, value proposition). It outputs a structured email subject and body tailored to that specific person.

Step 3: Quality Assurance (Judge Agent)

Before anything is saved, a second “Outreach Email Judge” AI agent reviews the draft. It checks for tone, relevance, and compliance with your guidelines, returning either “APPROVED” or “REVISE” with brief feedback. Only approved drafts proceed.

Step 4: Write-Back & Ready for Send

Approved email subject and body are written back to the same Data Table row, flagging the lead as ready for human review or automated sending via your email platform (like Gmail or Outlook). The loop continues until all pending leads are processed.

What You'll Need

  1. A lead list in a Data Table (n8n’s built‑in table or integrated with Airtable/Google Sheets) with columns for email, name, title, LinkedIn URL, company site, etc.
  2. RapidAPI account with access to the “cold‑outreach‑enrichment‑scraper” service for LinkedIn and Crunchbase data.
  3. Google Gemini API key configured in n8n’s Google Gemini Chat Model node.
  4. Your “About Me” block—a short description of you/your company that the AI uses to contextualize drafts.
  5. Basic n8n familiarity to import the JSON template and set up credentials.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Create a Data Table (or connect your existing one) with the required columns: email, first name, last name, title, LinkedIn URL, company LinkedIn, Crunchbase URL, and fields for the scraped JSON outputs.

3. Add your RapidAPI key and Google Gemini API credentials in the respective nodes.

4. Replace the sample “About Me” text in the AI agent node with your own persona.

5. Test with a single lead row to verify enrichment and drafting work correctly.

6. Schedule the workflow (with a Cron trigger) or run it manually whenever you add new leads.

Pro tip: Use the “Wait” nodes strategically to avoid overloading the enrichment APIs. Adjust batch sizes based on your API rate limits.
