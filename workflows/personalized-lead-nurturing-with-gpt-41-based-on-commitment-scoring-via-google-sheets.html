What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you nurture leads by automatically sending personalized onboarding and follow-up emails based on each lead's commitment level. It eliminates manual email writing while dramatically improving engagement through AI-powered personalization.

The system scores leads based on their stated commitment in Google Sheets, then crafts and sends perfectly tailored emails. High-commitment leads receive detailed onboarding, while lower-commitment leads get nurturing content designed to increase their engagement.

How It Works

1. Lead Data Collection

The workflow triggers when new lead data is added to your Google Sheet, including their goals and self-reported commitment level (1-10 scale).

2. Commitment Assessment

An automated check determines if the lead's commitment score is 8+ (warm lead) or below 8 (colder lead), routing them to the appropriate email path.

3. AI-Powered Email Creation

GPT-4.1 generates completely personalized emails based on the lead's specific goals and commitment level, with different tones and content for warm vs. cold leads.

4. Automated Email Delivery

The system sends the tailored email through Gmail and logs all activity back to your Google Sheet for complete tracking.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Sheets with lead data (email, name, goals, commitment score)
  2. Gmail account for sending emails
  3. OpenAI API key for GPT-4.1 integration
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Pro tip: Start with a small test group of leads to refine your email templates before scaling to your full list.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets, Gmail, and OpenAI credentials
  3. Customize the email templates in the OpenAI nodes
  4. Test with sample data to verify everything works
  5. Connect to your live Google Sheet and activate the workflow
