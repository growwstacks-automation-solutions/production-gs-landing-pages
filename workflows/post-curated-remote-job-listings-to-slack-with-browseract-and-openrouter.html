What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming problem of manually curating remote job opportunities for communities, teams, or job boards. Instead of spending hours each day scanning multiple websites, this workflow automatically gathers listings from various sources, intelligently filters them using AI, and posts only the most relevant opportunities directly to your Slack channel.

The system acts as your personal job curation assistant, working 24/7 to ensure your community receives timely, high-quality job alerts. It eliminates spam, irrelevant entry-level positions, and mismatched opportunities while highlighting roles that genuinely fit your community's profile.

By automating this process, community managers, recruiters, and career-focused groups can provide consistent value to their members while reclaiming 10-15 hours per week previously spent on manual curation tasks.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger & Profile Configuration

The workflow begins on a schedule you set—daily, weekly, or custom intervals. It reads your configured profile including desired skills, locations, salary ranges, and target job sites.

2. Multi-Source Job Scraping

Using BrowserAct, the system scrapes the latest job listings from your specified sources like Dice, Indeed, and specialized remote job boards. It gathers comprehensive data including job descriptions, requirements, salaries, and company information.

3. AI-Powered Filtering & Analysis

Each job listing passes through an AI agent (OpenRouter/OpenAI) that evaluates it against your profile criteria. The AI normalizes salary data, scores relevance, filters out low-quality descriptions, and identifies mismatches that simple keyword searches would miss.

4. Content Formatting & Enrichment

The AI then creates structured summaries for top matches, including "Why This Fits" analysis that explains why each job aligns with your community's profile. This transforms raw listings into valuable, actionable opportunities.

5. Automated Slack Delivery

Finally, the curated job digest gets formatted into professional Slack posts and delivered to your designated channel. The posts include clear application instructions, salary information, and relevant details—all automatically.

What You'll Need

  1. Slack Workspace with appropriate permissions to create bots and post to channels
  2. BrowserAct Account with the Job Board Aggregator template configured
  3. OpenRouter Account or compatible LLM API credentials (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
  4. n8n Instance (cloud or self-hosted) with webhook capabilities
  5. Target Job Sources identified and accessible via scraping

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and Import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Credentials for Slack, BrowserAct, and OpenRouter in n8n's credential management
  3. Update Profile Variables in the "Add a Resume" node with your specific skills, locations, salary expectations, and target websites
  4. Set Your Slack Channel in the "Send message to Slack channel" node with the appropriate channel ID
  5. Adjust Schedule in the trigger node to match your preferred frequency (daily, weekly, etc.)
  6. Test and Activate the workflow with a manual run, then enable it for automated operation

Pro tip: Start with broader filters and gradually refine them based on the quality of matches you receive. Monitor community feedback for the first few weeks to fine-tune your criteria.
