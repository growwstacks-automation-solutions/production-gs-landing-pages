What This Workflow Does

Food waste is a massive problem for restaurants, costing the industry billions annually. Traditional inventory management relies on guesswork, leading to over-ordering, spoilage, and lost profits. This automation solves that by bringing AI-powered precision to your kitchen operations.

The workflow analyzes your historical sales and waste data, combines it with external factors like weather and local events, and generates daily predictions for food demand. It then creates optimized ordering recommendations, logs everything to Google Sheets for tracking, and sends email reports to your management team. This turns reactive waste management into proactive, data-driven decision making.

How It Works

1. Daily Trigger & Data Collection

Each morning, the workflow automatically activates and fetches your latest sales and waste data from Google Sheets. It pulls historical patterns to establish baseline consumption for each menu item.

2. Data Preparation for AI Analysis

The raw data is cleaned, organized, and formatted into a structure that AI models can process effectively. This includes normalizing quantities, categorizing items, and identifying trends.

3. AI Forecasting with Gemini

Google's Gemini AI analyzes the prepared data along with contextual information (day of week, season, weather forecasts) to predict tomorrow's demand for each food item with remarkable accuracy.

4. Recommendation Generation

The AI doesn't just predict—it recommends. It calculates optimal order quantities, flags high-risk waste items, and suggests menu adjustments based on predicted popularity and shelf life.

5. Reporting & Notification

All predictions and recommendations are written back to Google Sheets for historical tracking. A concise summary email is generated and sent to your kitchen manager and purchasing team for action.

Pro tip: Start with 3 months of historical data for decent predictions. After 6 months, the AI will understand your restaurant's unique patterns and seasonal variations, increasing accuracy to 90%+.

  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this food waste prediction system in your restaurant:

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Connect Google Sheets by authorizing access to your waste tracking spreadsheet
  3. Configure Gemini AI by adding your API key to the AI node settings
  4. Set up email notifications with your preferred email service credentials
  5. Test with sample data to verify predictions and email delivery work correctly
  6. Schedule daily execution to run each morning before purchasing decisions are made
  7. Review and refine based on actual vs. predicted results over the first few weeks

Implementation note: The system becomes more valuable over time. Don't expect perfection on day one—focus on getting the data flowing. Within 2-3 weeks, you'll see patterns emerge and accuracy improve dramatically.
