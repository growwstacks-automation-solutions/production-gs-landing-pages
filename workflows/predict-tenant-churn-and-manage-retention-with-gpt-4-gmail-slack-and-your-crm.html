What This Workflow Does

This AI-powered automation system helps property managers predict tenant churn before it happens and implement targeted retention strategies. By analyzing data from your CRM, email communications, and tenant interactions, it identifies at-risk tenants and automates personalized retention workflows.

The system evaluates multiple risk factors including payment patterns, maintenance request frequency, communication sentiment, and lease renewal timing. GPT-4 processes unstructured data like email exchanges to detect subtle signs of dissatisfaction that traditional metrics miss. Automated alerts in Slack ensure your team takes timely action.

How It Works

1. Data Aggregation

The workflow pulls tenant data from your CRM, including lease terms, payment history, and maintenance records. It simultaneously analyzes email communication patterns and sentiment through Gmail integration.

2. Churn Risk Scoring

GPT-4 processes the aggregated data to assign each tenant a churn risk score (low, medium, high). The AI model considers both quantitative metrics and qualitative factors from communication analysis.

3. Automated Alerts

High-risk tenants trigger immediate Slack notifications to your property management team with recommended actions. The system includes templated responses but allows for personalization.

4. Retention Workflows

Based on the risk level and tenant profile, the system automatically schedules follow-ups, generates personalized lease renewal offers, or initiates maintenance check-ins through your preferred channels.

Pro tip: Combine this with your existing tenant satisfaction surveys for even more accurate predictions. The AI can correlate survey responses with behavioral patterns to refine its risk models.
