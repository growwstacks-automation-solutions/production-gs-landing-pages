What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the costly problem of unexpected vehicle breakdowns for fleet-based businesses. Instead of waiting for a warning light or a complete failure, this system proactively identifies potential issues by analyzing real-time sensor data (telemetry) alongside the vehicle's complete service history.

It transforms raw data from your fleet management software into actionable intelligence. By merging current performance metrics with past maintenance records, the workflow uses AI to detect subtle anomalies and patterns that signal impending failures. The result is a prioritized list of maintenance actions, allowing you to schedule repairs during planned downtime, reduce emergency costs, and maximize vehicle uptime.

How It Works

The workflow is a scheduled, end-to-end analysis pipeline that turns data into decisions.

1. Data Collection & Merging

The process starts on a schedule you set (e.g., daily). It first fetches live telemetry data—engine diagnostics, GPS location, fuel levels, error codes—from your fleet management API. Simultaneously, it pulls the complete historical maintenance record for each vehicle from your database. These two datasets are then merged to create a comprehensive profile for each asset.

2. AI-Powered Anomaly Detection

The merged data is sent to an Anthropic Claude AI agent specifically prompted to act as a predictive maintenance analyst. The AI examines the combined dataset, comparing current readings against historical baselines and known failure patterns to identify deviations that could indicate developing issues.

3. Prioritization & Alert Generation

Another AI agent evaluates the detected anomalies. It calculates an urgency score based on safety risk, potential cost of failure, operational criticality of the vehicle, and parts availability. The workflow then formats the findings into clear maintenance tickets and, for high-priority issues, generates immediate alerts for your maintenance team.

4. Logging & Distribution

Every analysis run is logged with a timestamp and findings for audit and compliance. Finally, prioritized work orders are routed to the appropriate team channels (like Slack or email) or directly into your maintenance management system, closing the loop from data to action.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this predictive maintenance system running in your environment in four steps:

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Credentials: In the n8n workflow, add your Anthropic API key and set up the HTTP Request nodes with the API endpoints and authentication for your telemetry and maintenance systems.
  3. Map Your Data Fields: Adjust the "Merge" and "Set" nodes to match the specific field names (like 'vehicle_id', 'engine_temp') used in your data sources.
  4. Set Schedule & Outputs: Configure the Schedule Trigger to your desired frequency (e.g., every 6 hours). Then, connect the final nodes to your preferred notification apps (Slack, Email) or database to log the results.

Test the workflow with a single vehicle ID first to ensure data flows correctly before enabling it for your entire fleet.
