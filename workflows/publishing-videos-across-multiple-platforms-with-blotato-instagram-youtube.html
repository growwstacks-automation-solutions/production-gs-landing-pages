What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the time-consuming challenge of manually uploading videos to multiple social media platforms. It automates the distribution process using Blotato's API to simultaneously publish your video content to up to 9 different platforms including Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, and more.

The workflow includes both scheduled publishing capabilities and real-time triggers, allowing you to either queue up content in advance or publish immediately when new videos are added to your content management system. This eliminates hours of repetitive uploading while ensuring consistent cross-platform presence.

How It Works

1. Video Content Trigger

The workflow begins when new video content is detected in your source system. This could be triggered by a new file in your cloud storage, a database entry, or through direct API calls from your CMS.

2. Content Processing

The video file and metadata (title, description, tags) are prepared for distribution. The workflow can automatically generate platform-specific variations like different aspect ratios or trimmed versions as needed.

3. Multi-Platform Distribution

Using Blotato's API, the workflow simultaneously publishes the video to all configured platforms. Each platform receives the appropriately formatted content with customized metadata for optimal engagement.

4. Status Monitoring

The workflow tracks the publishing status across all platforms and provides a consolidated report. Failed uploads are automatically retried according to your configuration.
