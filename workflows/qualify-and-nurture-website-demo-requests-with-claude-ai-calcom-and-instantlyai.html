Qualify & nurture website demo requests with Claude AI, Cal.com and Instantly.ai

An intelligent automation workflow that processes website demo requests, qualifies leads using AI-powered analysis, and automatically nurtures prospects

What This Workflow Does

This intelligent automation system transforms how businesses handle website demo requests by combining AI-powered lead qualification with automated nurturing. It solves the common problem of sales teams wasting time on unqualified leads while letting promising prospects slip through the cracks.

The workflow automatically processes incoming demo requests through Claude AI for instant qualification, schedules qualified leads via Cal.com, and initiates personalized email nurturing sequences through Instantly.ai. This creates a seamless, automated pipeline from first contact to scheduled demo.

How It Works

1. AI-Powered Lead Qualification

When a demo request is submitted through your website form or email, Claude AI instantly analyzes the content. It evaluates factors like company size, job title relevance, expressed pain points, and buying signals to assign a qualification score.

2. Automated Scheduling for Qualified Leads

For leads meeting your qualification threshold, the workflow automatically creates a Cal.com booking link tailored to the prospect's timezone. It sends this along with personalized confirmation materials, eliminating scheduling back-and-forth.

Pro tip: Configure different qualification thresholds to route enterprise leads to your senior sales team while sending SMB leads to junior reps or automated demos.

3. Intelligent Nurturing Sequences

Instantly.ai kicks off targeted email sequences based on the lead's score and characteristics. These nurture tracks provide value-added content, case studies, and gentle reminders leading up to the scheduled demo.
