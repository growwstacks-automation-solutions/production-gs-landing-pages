What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually qualifying and contacting B2B leads. It combines AI-powered lead scoring with personalized outreach at scale, creating a seamless pipeline from prospect identification to first contact.

The workflow pulls lead data from Apollo, analyzes it using Groq AI to determine fit and intent, then crafts and sends tailored emails through Gmail while logging all activity in Google Sheets. This eliminates hours of manual research and follow-up while improving response rates through hyper-relevant messaging.

How It Works

Step 1: Lead data collection

The workflow retrieves new leads from Apollo.io, gathering firmographic data like company size, industry, technologies used, and contact details. It filters out unqualified prospects based on your predefined criteria.

Step 2: AI-powered qualification

Groq AI analyzes each lead's profile to assign a qualification score. The AI examines job titles, company signals, and engagement history to predict which prospects are most likely to convert.

Step 3: Personalized email creation

The AI generates customized email content referencing the lead's specific role, company challenges, and industry trends. It maintains your brand voice while adapting messaging for maximum relevance.

Step 4: Automated outreach

The workflow sends emails through Gmail with optimal timing and follow-up sequences. It tracks opens and responses while avoiding spam triggers through natural send pacing.

Step 5: Activity logging

All outreach data automatically updates in Google Sheets, creating a searchable record of contacts, email content, timestamps, and response status for performance analysis.

Pro tip: For best results, train the AI with examples of your most successful outreach emails to maintain consistent messaging style and tone.
