What This Workflow Does

Real estate brokers and teams face a constant flood of leads from websites, property portals, email inquiries, and open houses. Manually sorting through these to identify serious buyers from casual browsers wastes precious time and causes hot prospects to go cold. This automation solves that by acting as your 24/7 lead qualification assistant.

The system automatically collects leads from all your sources, enriches them with AI-powered analysis using Anthropic's Claude, scores each lead based on buying intent, budget, and urgency, then instantly routes the hottest prospects to your most appropriate agents. It transforms chaotic lead management into a streamlined, intelligent process that ensures no opportunity slips through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Multi-Source Lead Collection

The workflow triggers on a schedule (every 15-60 minutes) and simultaneously fetches new leads from your MLS portals, CRM systems, email inboxes, and website forms. It aggregates all inquiries into a unified dataset, eliminating manual data entry across platforms.

2. Parallel AI Enrichment

Each lead is processed through Anthropic Claude AI agents that analyze the inquiry text, property details mentioned, communication patterns, and explicit/implicit signals. The AI assesses buying intent (1-10), budget confidence, timeline urgency, and property type preferences.

3. Intelligent Scoring & Prioritization

Based on the AI analysis, each lead receives a composite score. The system applies your business rules to categorize leads into priority tiers: Hot (immediate contact), Warm (follow-up today), or Cold (nurture sequence). Custom thresholds ensure alignment with your sales process.

4. Smart Agent Routing

High-priority leads are instantly routed to available agents based on specialty (luxury, first-time buyers, commercial), geographic territory, or performance metrics. The system includes fallback routing if primary agents are unavailable, ensuring zero delay.

5. Tracking & Optimization

All lead data, scores, routing decisions, and subsequent agent interactions are logged to Google Sheets for performance analysis. This creates a feedback loop where the system learns which lead characteristics actually convert in your market.

Pro tip: Start with conservative scoring thresholds and adjust based on your first 100 processed leads. Most teams find they've been underestimating certain lead signals that AI identifies consistently.
