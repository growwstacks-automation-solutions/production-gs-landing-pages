What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical business challenge of efficiently qualifying and routing sales leads at scale. By combining Mistral-Saba AI analysis with Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM) scoring, it eliminates manual lead sorting while improving conversion rates.

The system automatically processes incoming leads from all channels, evaluates them against your ideal customer profile using both AI and rule-based scoring, then routes them to the most appropriate sales rep or team based on territory, specialization, and workload.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow aggregates lead data from multiple sources including web forms, email interactions, and CRM activities. All data is normalized for consistent processing.

2. AI Scoring

Mistral-Saba AI analyzes behavioral patterns and historical conversion data to predict lead quality. The AI model improves continuously as it processes more outcomes.

3. MCDM Evaluation

Leads are scored across weighted criteria like budget, authority, need, and timing (BANT) to create a composite qualification score that reflects your specific sales priorities.

4. Smart Routing

The system assigns leads to reps based on territory rules, expertise matching, and balanced distribution. Urgent high-potential leads get prioritized automatically.
