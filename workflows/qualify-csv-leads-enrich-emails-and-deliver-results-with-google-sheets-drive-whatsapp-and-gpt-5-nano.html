What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms messy lead spreadsheets into actionable, personalized outreach in minutes. It takes raw CSV data containing potential customer information, verifies and enriches contact details using AI, qualifies leads based on your criteria, then delivers them through your preferred channels with tailored messaging.

The system eliminates hours of manual data cleaning while improving lead quality. Sales teams receive fully vetted contacts with complete profiles, prioritized by conversion potential. Marketing gains accurate segmentation data, and management gets real-time visibility into pipeline generation.

How It Works

1. CSV Processing

The workflow begins by ingesting your lead CSV files from any source - website forms, event registrations, or purchased lists. It standardizes formatting, removes duplicates, and flags incomplete records for review.

2. AI-Powered Enrichment

GPT-5-NANO analyzes each lead's existing data to predict missing information. It verifies email addresses, appends professional details from public sources, and scores lead quality based on your ideal customer profile.

3. Automated Qualification

Leads are categorized using your business rules (budget, authority, need, timeline). The system creates separate lists for immediate follow-up, nurturing, or disqualification - all documented in your Google Sheets CRM.

4. Multi-Channel Delivery

Qualified leads automatically receive personalized WhatsApp messages with follow-up tasks created in Google Drive. The workflow syncs all interactions back to your master sheet for complete visibility.

Pro tip: Configure the AI to analyze lead responses and automatically adjust qualification scores over time based on engagement patterns.
