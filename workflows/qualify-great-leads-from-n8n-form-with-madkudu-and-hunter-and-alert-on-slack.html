Automate Lead Qualification with MadKudu, Hunter & Slack

Analyze and prioritize incoming leads automatically with real-time Slack alerts for high-quality prospects

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually qualifying leads from web forms. When potential customers submit information through an n8n form, the workflow automatically analyzes them using MadKudu's predictive scoring, enriches contact data with Hunter, and sends Slack alerts for high-quality leads that meet your criteria.

By automating lead qualification, sales teams can focus their efforts on the most promising prospects while ensuring no high-value leads slip through the cracks. The workflow eliminates manual data entry and analysis, reducing response times from days to minutes.

The workflow connects n8n forms with MadKudu for scoring and Hunter for contact enrichment

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when a lead submits information through your n8n form. This captures all initial contact details and qualifying information.

2. MadKudu Analysis

Lead data is sent to MadKudu which analyzes hundreds of signals to generate a predictive score indicating lead quality and fit.

3. Hunter Contact Enrichment

The workflow uses Hunter to find and verify professional email addresses and additional contact information for the lead.

4. Qualification Check

Based on your predefined criteria (like MadKudu score thresholds), the workflow determines if the lead qualifies for immediate follow-up.

5. Slack Notification

For qualified leads, a detailed Slack message is sent to your sales channel with all relevant information for immediate action.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure the n8n form trigger with your form's webhook URL
  3. Add your MadKudu and Hunter API credentials
  4. Set up the Slack webhook for your notification channel
  5. Adjust qualification thresholds in the "If" node to match your criteria
  6. Test with sample lead data and activate the workflow
