What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical "speed-to-lead" challenge for insurance agencies. When a potential client submits their information, every minute of delay dramatically reduces conversion chances. This workflow eliminates that delay by instantly triggering an AI-powered voice call to qualify the lead, analyze their needs through natural conversation, and automatically generate and send a tailored insurance proposal.

The system acts as a 24/7 virtual sales assistant that never sleeps, ensuring no lead goes cold. It captures detailed qualification data that human agents often miss, creates comprehensive records in your CRM, and handles both qualified and unqualified leads with appropriate follow-up actions—all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture & CRM Logging

When a prospect submits their details through your website form, the workflow immediately creates a new record in Airtable with their name, contact information, and submission timestamp. This ensures every lead is captured in your central database from the very first moment.

2. AI Voice Qualification Call

Within seconds of lead submission, the workflow triggers Vapi.ai to place a call to the prospect. The AI agent introduces itself as your representative, asks qualifying questions about insurance needs, budget, timeline, and specific coverage requirements, and conducts a natural conversation just like a human agent would.

3. Conversation Analysis & Routing

Once the call completes, Vapi sends the full transcript and analysis back to n8n. The workflow evaluates the conversation quality and whether specific insurance types were identified. Based on this analysis, leads are automatically routed down qualified or unqualified paths.

4. Qualified Lead: Proposal Generation & Delivery

For qualified leads, GPT-4 analyzes the conversation transcript and generates a personalized "Insurance Blueprint" email proposal. This email addresses the prospect's specific needs discussed during the call, includes relevant coverage recommendations, and maintains your agency's professional tone. The proposal is sent via Gmail, and the Airtable record is updated with "Qualified" status and call summary.

5. Unqualified Lead: Team Notification

If the AI determines the lead isn't qualified, the workflow updates the Airtable record with "Unqualified" status and the reason for disqualification. It simultaneously sends a Slack notification to your sales team with the call summary, ensuring they're aware without needing to manually review every interaction.

  3. Airtable Base: With a leads table containing specific columns for tracking
  4. Gmail Account: Or another email service for sending proposals
  5. Slack Workspace: For internal team notifications (optional but recommended)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance using the downloaded JSON file
  2. Configure credentials for Airtable, OpenAI, Gmail, and Slack in n8n's credentials management
  3. Set up your Airtable base with columns for: Name, Email, Phone, Status (Qualified/Unqualified), Insurance Type, and Call Summary
  4. Create your Vapi assistant with a system prompt tailored to your insurance products and qualification criteria
  5. Update node parameters to point to your specific Airtable base, Vapi assistant ID, and Slack channel
  6. Test with a controlled lead by submitting test data through your form and verifying the entire flow works correctly
  7. Go live by connecting your actual lead capture form to the workflow trigger
