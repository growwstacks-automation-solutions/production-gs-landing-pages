What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms conversational leads from Gemini chat into qualified prospects in your CRM system while continuously improving its own performance. It solves the challenge of manually processing chat-based leads and maintaining consistent qualification standards.

The workflow captures lead details from natural conversations, scores them based on your criteria, logs them to Google Sheets, and alerts your sales team in Slack - all while analyzing its own effectiveness to suggest improvements.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture Phase

The AI agent engages prospects in natural conversation through Gemini chat, extracting key qualification details like budget, timeline, and needs without rigid forms.

2. Qualification Processing

A custom JavaScript node analyzes the conversation to score leads based on your predefined criteria (budget, industry, decision-making authority).

3. Data Logging

All lead details and qualification scores are automatically recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps for complete tracking.

4. Team Notification

High-potential leads trigger instant Slack notifications to your sales team with all relevant context for immediate follow-up.

5. Self-Optimization

The system regularly reviews conversion rates and chat patterns to suggest improvements to your qualification criteria and prompts.
