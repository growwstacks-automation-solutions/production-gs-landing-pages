Automatically evaluate and categorize incoming leads using AI-powered analysis

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually evaluating incoming leads by automatically analyzing them using OpenAI's GPT-4. When new leads are added to your Google Sheets, the workflow instantly assesses their quality based on your predefined criteria.

The system evaluates factors like company size, industry relevance, budget indicators, and engagement signals to determine whether each lead is sales-ready or needs further nurturing. This eliminates hours of manual research and gut-feeling judgments from your sales process.

How It Works

1. New Lead Detection

The workflow monitors your Google Sheet for new rows added, triggering whenever sales teams or marketing automation tools add potential customers.

2. Data Preparation

Key information from the lead record is extracted and formatted into a clear prompt for GPT-4, including all relevant qualification criteria your team uses.

3. AI Analysis

OpenAI's GPT-4 evaluates the lead against your business's specific qualification standards, considering both the explicit data and implied signals in the information.

4. Results Processing

The AI's assessment is parsed and added back to your spreadsheet with clear qualification status and notes explaining the decision.
