What This Workflow Does

Real estate agents waste countless hours manually sifting through unqualified leads—people who aren't serious buyers, have unrealistic budgets, or aren't ready to purchase. This AI-powered automation solves that problem by instantly analyzing and scoring every property inquiry that comes through your website forms.

The workflow acts as your 24/7 virtual assistant, evaluating buyer readiness based on budget, location preference, timeline, and property requirements. It separates serious prospects from casual browsers, ensuring you focus your valuable time on leads most likely to convert into sales.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

When a potential buyer submits their details through your property inquiry form (Typeform, Google Forms, Webflow, etc.), the workflow automatically activates. It captures all submitted information including contact details, budget range, preferred locations, and timeline.

2. AI Lead Analysis & Scoring

GPT-4o analyzes the submission, extracting structured data and generating a lead score from 0-100. The AI evaluates factors like budget alignment with local market prices, timeline urgency, location specificity, and completeness of information provided.

3. Qualification Logic

Leads scoring 70+ are automatically marked as "qualified" and routed for immediate follow-up. Scores below 70 can be stored for later nurturing or flagged for review. You can customize these thresholds based on your specific market and business rules.

4. Automated Follow-Up Actions

For qualified leads, the system automatically creates a record in your Airtable CRM with all analyzed data and sends an email notification to your sales team. This ensures hot leads receive attention within minutes, not hours or days.

Pro tip: Configure different follow-up sequences based on lead score ranges. High-scoring leads (85+) might trigger immediate phone call alerts, while medium scores (70-84) could receive automated email sequences before human contact.

Quick Setup Guide

1. Import the template into your n8n workspace using the downloaded JSON file.

2. Configure your form trigger by replacing the sample webhook with your actual form submission endpoint.

3. Connect your credentials for OpenAI, Airtable, and your email service in the respective nodes.

4. Customize the AI prompt in the Information Extractor node to match your specific qualification criteria.

5. Test with sample data to ensure the scoring and CRM integration work correctly.

6. Activate the workflow and start automatically qualifying incoming leads.

Pro tip: Start with conservative scoring thresholds and adjust based on your first 50-100 leads. Track which scores actually convert to refine your AI's accuracy over time.
