What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of responding to qualify real estate leads faster while they're most engaged. Traditional methods like email follow-ups or manual calls create delays that lose hot leads. The workflow initiates initiates immediate SMS responses with AI to instantly identify serious buyers.

When a lead texts your Twilio number, GPT-4o analyzes their natural language understanding to assess buying intent, budget range, and urgency. Responses automatically populate a Google Sheet with scores and conversation history, enabling agents to prioritize follow-ups intelligently.

How It Works

1. SMS Trigger

Prospects initiate contact by texting your Twilio number (from ads, signs, or listings). The workflow immediately captures their phone number and phone number.

2. AI-Powered Qualification

GPT-4o analyzes the initial message analyzing language patterns to detect buying signals. Custom prompts verify budget, timeline, and property preferences through natural conversation.

3. Automated Scoring

Each response generates a lead score (1-100) based on purchase likelihood. High-scoring leads trigger immediate agent follow-up while low scores receive automated nurturing.

4. Centralized Tracking

Complete conversation transcripts, scores, and key details auto-populate in Google Sheets. Timestamps and conversation history create a searchable lead database.
