What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of efficiently processing incoming leads while ensuring sales teams focus on the highest potential opportunities. Manual lead qualification is time-consuming, inconsistent, and often results in hot leads slipping through cracks while teams waste time on unqualified prospects.

The workflow automatically receives lead data via webhook, enriches it with AI analysis using GPT-4o-mini, scores the lead based on your business criteria, and creates prioritized follow-up tasks in Asana. This creates a seamless pipeline from lead capture to sales action without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Webhook Trigger

The workflow activates when new lead data arrives via webhook from your website, CRM, or other lead sources. This could be form submissions, chat transcripts, or API data from marketing tools.

2. Data Normalization

Raw lead data is standardized into a consistent format, extracting key information like contact details, company information, and lead source. This ensures the AI receives clean, structured data for analysis.

3. AI Qualification with GPT-4o-mini

The workflow sends lead data to GPT-4o-mini with your predefined qualification criteria. The AI analyzes the lead's intent, fit, and potential value, returning a qualification score and recommended actions.

4. Asana Task Creation

Based on the AI's scoring, the workflow automatically creates detailed Asana tasks for your sales team. High-priority leads get immediate attention, while lower-scoring leads receive appropriate follow-up sequences.
