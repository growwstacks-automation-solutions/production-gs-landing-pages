What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of cold, untimely outreach to dormant leads by transforming reactive sales into strategic re-engagement. It continuously monitors Crunchbase and NewsAPI for trigger events like funding announcements, leadership changes, or product launches that indicate when a previously uninterested lead might now be receptive.

When a trigger event occurs, the workflow uses Hunter to verify contact information, then Claude AI to generate hyper-personalized emails that reference the specific event while positioning your solution as relevant to their new situation. The emails are sent through Gmail with optimal timing and tracking.

How It Works

1. Trigger Event Detection

The system continuously scans Crunchbase for funding rounds, leadership changes, and expansion plans while monitoring NewsAPI for relevant company announcements. It filters for only the most impactful events that signal buying intent.

2. Contact Verification

Hunter.io checks and updates contact information to ensure deliverability. The workflow verifies each lead still holds the same position and identifies the best contact method (direct email vs. LinkedIn).

3. AI-Personalized Drafting

Claude AI analyzes the trigger event context and your previous interactions to craft emails that feel human-written. It incorporates specific details about the event while maintaining natural conversation flow.
