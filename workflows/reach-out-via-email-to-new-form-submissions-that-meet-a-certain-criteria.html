Automated Lead Follow-Up for Form Submissions

Filter, enrich, and automatically email qualified leads from your forms—no manual review needed.

What This Workflow Does

Collecting leads through forms is essential for business growth, but manually reviewing each submission to determine which leads are worth following up on consumes valuable time that could be spent on actual sales conversations. This process often involves checking email domains, researching companies, and qualifying based on size or industry—tasks that are repetitive and prone to human error.

This n8n workflow automates the entire lead qualification and follow-up process. It triggers whenever a new form submission arrives, immediately filters out personal email addresses (like Gmail or Yahoo), enriches the lead data with company information using Clearbit, checks if the company is B2B and has significant size (500+ employees), and automatically sends a personalized email via Gmail to qualified prospects. The result is a fully automated system that ensures only high-potential leads receive immediate, personalized attention while saving your team hours of manual work each week.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your n8n form (or any integrated form platform). It captures all submission data including name, email, company, and any custom fields you've configured.

2. Personal Email Filter

Before wasting resources on enrichment, the workflow checks if the submitted email uses a personal domain (Gmail, Hotmail, Yahoo, etc.). Personal emails are routed to a "do nothing" branch, while business emails proceed to qualification.

3. Company Data Enrichment

Using Clearbit's API, the workflow enriches the lead with verified company information: employee count, industry, location, and company type. This turns a simple email address into a complete business profile.

4. Qualification Logic

An "If" node applies your business rules: Is this a B2B company? Does it have 500+ employees? Is it in your target industry? Only leads passing all criteria move to the final step.

5. Automated Email Outreach

Qualified leads receive a personalized email via Gmail with their name, company information, and relevant messaging. The email can include specific offers, scheduling links, or next steps based on what they expressed interest in.

Pro tip: Customize the qualification criteria to match your ideal customer profile. For B2B services, 100+ employees might be sufficient, while enterprise solutions might require 1000+. Adjust the workflow to filter for your specific thresholds.

What You'll Need

  2. Clearbit API key for company data enrichment (free tier available)

  4. Form platform (n8n forms, Typeform, Google Forms, etc.) with webhook capability

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure the Clearbit node with your API key from clearbit.com.

3. Set up the Gmail node with your email account credentials (OAuth recommended).

4. Adjust the "If" node conditions to match your qualification criteria (employee count, company type).

5. Customize the email template in the Gmail node with your messaging and branding.

6. Test the workflow with your own business email to verify enrichment and email delivery.

7. Once verified, activate the workflow and connect it to your live form submissions.

Pro tip: Start with conservative qualification criteria and gradually expand as you validate what types of leads convert best. You can also add a step to log all processed leads to a spreadsheet for performance analysis.
