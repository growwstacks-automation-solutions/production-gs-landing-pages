What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually researching property leads and entering them into your CRM. Real estate investors and agents typically spend hours searching county records, verifying owner information, and manually inputting data - this workflow eliminates those repetitive tasks.

The system combines BatchData's comprehensive property database with their skip tracing service to automatically identify high-potential leads, gather verified contact information, and route everything to your CRM. It transforms what was traditionally a 30-45 minute manual process per lead into an automated sequence that runs in the background.

How It Works

1. Property Search Criteria Setup

The workflow begins by defining your target property parameters - this could include location filters, equity thresholds, absentee owner flags, or other investment criteria. These parameters are saved as variables for reuse across multiple searches.

2. BatchData API Integration

The system connects to BatchData's API to pull property records matching your criteria. It retrieves detailed information including tax records, mortgage details, and ownership history - all the data points that help qualify a lead.

3. Skip Tracing Execution

For each qualifying property, the workflow automatically requests skip tracing through BatchData's service. This process locates current phone numbers, email addresses, and mailing information for property owners - especially valuable for finding absentee owners.

4. CRM Entry & Lead Routing

The enriched lead data is formatted and pushed to your CRM system. The workflow can create new lead records, tag them appropriately, and even trigger initial follow-up sequences based on lead type (absentee owner, tax delinquent, etc.).

Common questions about real estate lead generation and skip tracing

Skip tracing is the process of finding property owners' contact information when traditional methods fail. It combines public records, data aggregators, and proprietary databases to locate phone numbers, emails, and mailing addresses.

This technique is particularly valuable for finding absentee owners or inherited properties where owner information isn't readily available through standard MLS searches. Modern skip tracing services can achieve 70-90% accuracy rates for phone numbers and emails.

  • Essential for finding non-marketed properties
  • Dramatically increases direct outreach success rates
  • Most cost-effective when automated through APIs

BatchData provides comprehensive property data including owner information, mortgage details, tax records, and neighborhood demographics. Their skip tracing service enhances this with verified contact details.

By automating access to this data through APIs, investors and agents can quickly identify motivated sellers, absentee owners, and other high-potential leads without manual research. For example, you could automatically find all properties in a zip code with 50%+ equity and absentee owners - then have their contact information pushed to your CRM.
