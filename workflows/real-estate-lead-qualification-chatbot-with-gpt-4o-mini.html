What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates real estate lead qualification using GPT-4o-mini powered chatbot. It engages website visitors in natural conversations to assess their buying intent, budget range, timeline, and property preferences. The AI analyzes responses to assign lead scores and automatically routes hot leads to your CRM while disqualifying unqualified prospects.

The system solves the challenge of manually qualifying hundreds of real estate leads by providing instant, consistent qualification 24/7. It reduces response time from hours/days to seconds while capturing structured lead data that integrates with your existing sales tools.

How It Works

1. Chatbot Engagement

The workflow triggers when a visitor interacts with your website chatbot. GPT-4o-mini handles the conversational flow, asking strategic questions to assess lead quality.

2. Intent Analysis

The AI analyzes responses to determine buying signals like urgency, budget alignment, and property requirements. It scores leads based on predefined criteria.

3. Automated Routing

High-scoring leads are instantly pushed to your CRM with all collected data. Marginal leads receive automated follow-up sequences, while unqualified leads are politely disengaged.
