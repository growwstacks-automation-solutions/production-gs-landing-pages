What This Workflow Does

This automated system solves the critical challenge of information overload in crypto trading. It continuously monitors top crypto news sources, processes content through GPT-4o for sentiment analysis, and delivers distilled insights directly to your Telegram. Instead of manually scanning dozens of sites, you get real-time, AI-powered market intelligence in a single channel.

The workflow identifies emerging trends, potential market-moving events, and shifts in trader sentiment before they're fully priced in. For active traders and crypto funds, this provides a competitive edge by surfacing actionable signals from the noise of daily crypto coverage.

How It Works

1. News Aggregation

The workflow pulls from authoritative crypto news APIs, filtering for relevant coverage based on your configured watchlist of coins and topics. It checks sources every 5 minutes for breaking developments.

2. AI Processing

Each article undergoes GPT-4o analysis to determine sentiment (bullish/bearish/neutral), extract key entities (coins, projects, people), and generate a concise summary. The AI weighs language nuances and contextual factors for accurate assessment.

3. Priority Scoring

The system scores each piece based on sentiment strength, source credibility, and relevance to your portfolio. High-priority alerts trigger immediate notifications while lower-priority items get batched.

4. Telegram Delivery

Formatted alerts arrive in your private Telegram channel with sentiment indicators, key takeaways, and links to original sources. The bot supports threaded discussions for team collaboration.
