What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of leads falling through the cracks when they need to be manually sorted and assigned. When a visitor submits a Webflow form, the workflow instantly enriches their data using Datagma, then routes them to the most appropriate Calendly booking link based on their profile and needs.

The system eliminates guesswork in lead assignment by using objective criteria like company size, industry, geographic location, or indicated product interest to determine the ideal next step. This ensures high-value leads get prioritized attention while still providing appropriate paths for all inquiries.

How It Works

1. Webflow form submission trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new form submission comes through your Webflow site. It captures all form field data including name, email, company, and any custom questions you've included.

2. Datagma lead enrichment

The submission data gets sent to Datagma which appends valuable firmographic data points like company revenue, employee count, technographics, and intent signals. This enrichment happens in real-time, typically within 1-2 seconds.

3. Routing logic evaluation

The workflow evaluates the enriched lead data against your predefined routing rules. For example: "If company size > 500 employees, route to enterprise sales Calendly; if healthcare industry, route to specialist consultant; else default to general inquiry calendar."

4. Calendly booking assignment

Based on the routing logic outcome, the lead receives an email with the appropriate Calendly booking link. The system can also notify your sales team about high-priority leads via Slack or email alerts.

Pro tip: Create separate Calendly event types with customized booking pages that speak directly to each lead segment's needs and questions.
