What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the challenge of manually monitoring public transport schedules and communicating delays to staff and passengers. The workflow automatically scrapes transport authority websites for schedule changes using ScrapeGraphAI, detects delays, and instantly notifies operations teams via Microsoft Teams while maintaining comprehensive records in Dropbox.

Transport companies waste significant resources manually checking schedules and communicating delays. This workflow eliminates that inefficiency by providing real-time monitoring and automated notifications, ensuring faster response to service disruptions and better record-keeping for performance analysis.

The workflow automatically checks schedules, detects delays, and triggers notifications

How It Works

1. Schedule Data Collection

ScrapeGraphAI automatically extracts the latest schedule data from transport authority websites at regular intervals, even when no API is available. The scraper is configured to identify schedule changes and delays based on your specific route monitoring needs.

2. Delay Detection

The workflow compares scraped data against expected schedules, applying your configured thresholds to identify significant delays. Custom logic filters minor variations while catching important disruptions that require notification.

Pro tip: Configure different delay thresholds for peak vs. off-peak hours to avoid unnecessary alerts during typically congested periods.

3. Teams Notification

When delays are detected, the workflow posts detailed alerts to designated Microsoft Teams channels. Notifications include affected routes, expected delay duration, and alternative options when available. The system can @mention specific team members based on the route or severity.

4. Dropbox Record Keeping

Every delay event is logged in Dropbox with a timestamped record containing all relevant details. These records are organized by date and route for easy reference, creating a searchable archive for performance reporting and regulatory compliance.
