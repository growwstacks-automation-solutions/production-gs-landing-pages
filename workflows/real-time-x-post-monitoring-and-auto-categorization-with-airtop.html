What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually monitoring X (formerly Twitter) for relevant conversations and leads. Businesses often miss valuable engagement opportunities because they can't efficiently track mentions, keywords, or industry discussions in real-time across multiple accounts.

The workflow automatically scans X based on your configured search parameters, extracts high-signal posts that match your criteria, and organizes them into categorized records in Airtop. This creates a structured pipeline for social media engagement, lead generation, or market research without constant manual checking.

How It Works

1. X Search Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors X using the platform's search API, tracking specified hashtags, keywords, or account mentions based on your configuration.

2. Content Filtering

New posts are analyzed against your criteria (engagement thresholds, sentiment, keywords) to filter out noise and surface only the most relevant conversations.

3. Data Enrichment

Each qualifying post is enriched with metadata including author details, engagement metrics, and sentiment analysis to provide context for prioritization.

4. Airtop Categorization

The system automatically creates structured records in Airtop, categorizing posts by topic, urgency, or custom tags for easy follow-up and team collaboration.
