What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automatically monitors your Clockify account for new time tracking events and sends you real-time notifications whenever they occur. It solves the problem of manually checking Clockify for updates or missing important time entries that need approval or follow-up.

The workflow connects Clockify with your preferred notification channels (email, Slack, Teams, etc.), ensuring you're immediately aware of new time entries from your team. This is particularly valuable for managers, HR professionals, and project leads who need to stay on top of employee time tracking.

How It Works

1. Clockify Trigger Setup

The workflow begins by connecting to your Clockify account using API credentials. It periodically checks for new time entries based on your configured polling interval.

2. Event Filtering

New time entries are filtered based on customizable criteria like project, user, or date range. This ensures you only receive notifications for relevant events.

3. Notification Delivery

The workflow formats the event data into a clear notification and sends it through your chosen communication channel. You can customize the message format and included details.
