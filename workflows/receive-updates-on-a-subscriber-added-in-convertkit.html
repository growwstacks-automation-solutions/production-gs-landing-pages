What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of manually monitoring your ConvertKit subscriber list. Instead of constantly checking your dashboard, you'll receive instant notifications whenever someone new joins your email list. The workflow detects new subscribers in real-time and sends alerts through your preferred notification channel.

For content creators, coaches, and online businesses, this means never missing an opportunity to engage with new audience members. You'll know immediately when someone shows interest in your content, products, or services, allowing for timely follow-up that can significantly improve conversion rates.

How It Works

1. Trigger: New ConvertKit Subscriber

The workflow starts when someone subscribes to your ConvertKit email list. The n8n automation polls ConvertKit's API at regular intervals to detect new subscribers.

2. Data Extraction

The workflow retrieves key details about the new subscriber, including their email address, name, subscription source, and any tags applied. This information is formatted for clear notification delivery.

3. Notification Delivery

Based on your configuration, the workflow sends the subscriber information to your preferred notification channel. Common options include email, Slack, SMS, or even adding to a CRM.

Pro tip: Configure your notifications to include the subscriber's referral source so you can track which marketing campaigns are performing best.
