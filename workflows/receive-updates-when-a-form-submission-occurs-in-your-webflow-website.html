What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves a common problem for Webflow website owners - missing important form submissions. Many businesses lose potential leads because they don't have a reliable system to notify them immediately when someone fills out a contact form, application, or other submission form on their Webflow site.

The automation monitors your Webflow forms in real-time and triggers notifications through your preferred channel (email, Slack, SMS, etc.) whenever a new submission comes in. This ensures you can respond to inquiries quickly, improving conversion rates and customer satisfaction.

How It Works

1. Webflow Form Submission Trigger

The workflow starts by connecting to your Webflow account and monitoring specified forms for new submissions. It checks at regular intervals (configurable) to detect any new entries.

2. Data Extraction

When a submission is detected, the workflow extracts all form field data including the submitter's name, email, phone number, and any custom fields you've set up in your Webflow form.

3. Notification Processing

The workflow then formats this information into a clean, readable message and sends it through your chosen notification channel. You can customize which fields are included and how the message is structured.

Pro tip: Set up different notification rules for different forms - urgent inquiries might go to your phone via SMS while newsletter signups could just go to a Slack channel.
