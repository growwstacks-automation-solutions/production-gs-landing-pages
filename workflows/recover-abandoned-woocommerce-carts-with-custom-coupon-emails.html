What This Workflow Does

Abandoned carts represent significant lost revenue for WooCommerce stores - industry averages show 70% of shopping carts are abandoned before purchase. This workflow automates the recovery process by identifying abandoned carts and sending personalized coupon emails to bring customers back.

Instead of paying for expensive plugins or manual follow-ups, this n8n workflow connects directly to your WooCommerce store via API, monitors cart activity, and triggers customized email sequences with discount incentives tailored to each customer's abandoned items.

How It Works

1. Cart abandonment detection

The workflow checks your WooCommerce store hourly for carts that have been inactive for a set period (typically 1-24 hours). It identifies the customer, their cart contents, and the total value of abandoned items.

2. Coupon generation

For each abandoned cart, the system automatically generates a unique coupon code with parameters you configure (percentage or fixed discount, expiration date, minimum purchase requirements).

3. Personalized email delivery

The workflow sends a customized email to the customer containing their unique coupon code and a reminder of what they left in their cart. Email templates can include product images, personalized recommendations, and urgency elements.

Pro tip: Set your coupon expiration to 48-72 hours to create urgency while still giving customers time to return.

4. Conversion tracking

The workflow tracks which coupons are redeemed and calculates your recovery rate. This data helps optimize your email timing, discount amounts, and messaging.
