What This Workflow Does

Every missed business call represents a potential lost opportunity. Studies show that up to 85% of callers won't leave a voicemail, meaning you could be losing valuable leads without even knowing it. This automation solves that problem by creating a systematic approach to capture and follow up on every missed call.

The workflow integrates Twilio's call tracking with Slack notifications and Google Sheets logging. When a call goes unanswered, it automatically logs the caller's information, notifies your team in Slack, and creates a record for follow-up. This ensures no potential customer falls through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Twilio detects a missed call

The workflow starts when Twilio identifies an unanswered incoming call. Twilio's webhook sends the call details including caller number, time, and duration to n8n.

2. Data gets logged to Google Sheets

The workflow creates a new row in your Google Sheet with all relevant call details. This serves as both a backup record and a centralized tracking system for your sales team.

Pro tip: Add columns for follow-up status and notes to track your team's outreach efforts.

3. Slack notification sent to your team

The workflow posts a formatted message in your designated Slack channel with the caller's details and a direct link to the Google Sheet row. This ensures immediate visibility for your sales or support team.

What You'll Need

  1. An active Twilio account with phone numbers configured
  2. A Slack workspace with permissions to post messages
  3. A Google Sheet prepared with column headers for call data
  4. n8n instance or account to run the workflow

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Twilio account credentials

  5. Test with a missed call to verify all steps execute correctly
