What This Workflow Does

Missed appointments cost service businesses significant revenue and create scheduling headaches. This automation solves the problem by instantly detecting no-shows in your Calendly bookings and triggering a coordinated response.

The workflow monitors your calendar for appointments where the attendee didn't show up, then simultaneously alerts your team via Telegram while sending a polite follow-up email to the client. This dual approach ensures staff can take immediate action while maintaining professional communication with clients.

How It Works

1. Calendly Integration

The workflow connects to your Calendly account via webhook, monitoring all scheduled events in real-time. When an appointment time passes without the attendee joining (based on your video meeting platform or physical check-in system), it flags the event as a no-show.

2. Instant Staff Notification

Within minutes of a missed appointment, the system sends an alert to your designated Telegram group or channel. This allows your team to immediately assess the situation and decide on next steps, whether that's reaching out personally or letting the automated follow-up proceed.

3. Client Follow-Up

Simultaneously, the workflow triggers a personalized email to the client who missed the appointment. The message can include options to reschedule, reminders about cancellation policies, or other appropriate follow-up based on your business rules.

Pro tip: Customize the Telegram alert to include key client details like booking notes, previous attendance history, and lifetime value to help staff prioritize follow-ups.
