What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the manual, time-consuming process of registering volunteers in SinergiaCRM. Nonprofits and volunteer organizations often struggle with efficiently capturing volunteer information from web forms and getting that data into their CRM system without duplicate entries or manual data entry errors.

The workflow automatically processes new volunteer form submissions, checks for existing contacts in SinergiaCRM, creates new records or updates existing ones, establishes proper relationships, and notifies both the volunteer and your team about the successful registration. This eliminates manual data transfer and ensures your CRM stays updated in real-time.

How It Works

1. Form submission trigger

The workflow starts when a volunteer submits your web form (Google Forms, Typeform, etc.). All form data is captured including contact details, skills, availability, and other relevant information.

2. CRM record check

The system queries SinergiaCRM to check if this volunteer already exists in your database, typically matching by email address or phone number to prevent duplicates.

3. Create or update contact

Based on the check, the workflow either creates a new volunteer record in SinergiaCRM or updates an existing one with the latest information from the form submission.

4. Relationship management

The automation establishes proper relationships in SinergiaCRM, tagging the volunteer with appropriate categories (skills, interests, availability) and linking them to relevant campaigns or programs.

5. Notification system

Both the volunteer and your team receive confirmation notifications - the volunteer gets a welcome/confirmation message while your staff gets an alert about the new registration.

Pro tip: Add a step to automatically assign volunteers to specific teams or projects based on their skills and availability marked in the form.
