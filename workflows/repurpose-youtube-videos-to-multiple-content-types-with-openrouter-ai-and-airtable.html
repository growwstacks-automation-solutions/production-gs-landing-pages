What This Workflow Does

Content creators, marketers, and businesses invest significant time producing YouTube videos, only to see that content sit idle after publication. This workflow solves that problem by automatically transforming your video investment into multiple revenue-generating assets.

The automation extracts transcripts from your YouTube videos, uses AI to analyze and rewrite the content for different formats and platforms, then organizes everything in Airtable for easy management and distribution. What used to take hours of manual writing and formatting now happens automatically, allowing you to scale your content presence across blogs, social media, email newsletters, and tutorials simultaneously.

By maximizing the ROI of every video you create, this system helps establish thought leadership, improve SEO through content expansion, and maintain consistent audience engagement without proportional increases in time or resources.

How It Works

1. Video Processing & Transcript Extraction

The workflow starts by retrieving YouTube URLs from your Airtable database. It processes only videos marked as "selected" while intelligently filtering out content scheduled for deletion or already processed.

2. AI-Powered Content Analysis

Using OpenRouter AI models, the system analyzes video transcripts to identify key concepts, main arguments, and actionable insights. The AI understands context and extracts the most valuable information for repurposing.

3. Multi-Format Content Generation

Based on your configuration in Airtable, the workflow generates specific content types: detailed blog posts optimized for SEO, engaging LinkedIn articles, Twitter/X threads, email newsletter content, step-by-step tutorials, and even new video scripts with hooks and conclusions.

4. Smart Organization & Distribution Ready

All generated content is matched by source video URL and organized in Airtable with clear labels and categories. The system creates ready-to-use assets that your team can review, schedule, and publish across all your marketing channels.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud version)
  2. OpenRouter API key for AI content generation
  3. Airtable account with personal access token
  4. YouTube videos with available transcripts (public or unlisted)
  5. Basic Airtable setup with the provided table structure

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 existing YouTube videos to test the workflow before processing your entire library. This helps you refine the AI prompts and output formats to match your brand voice perfectly.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Create your Airtable base using the provided table structure
  3. Configure API credentials for OpenRouter and Airtable in n8n
  4. Add 1-2 YouTube URLs to your Airtable and mark them as "selected"
  5. Run the workflow manually to test the complete process
  6. Review the generated content in Airtable and adjust AI prompts if needed
  7. Schedule the workflow to run automatically for new videos
