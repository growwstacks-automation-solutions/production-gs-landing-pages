What This Workflow Does

This automation solution streamlines the process of gathering comprehensive intelligence about organizations by combining cutting-edge AI analysis with verified public data sources. It's designed for professionals who need to quickly understand an organization's legal standing, financial health, public perception, and regulatory compliance.

The workflow automatically pulls data from multiple authoritative sources including CourtListener for legal cases, LegiScan for legislative tracking, and various OSINT web sources. It then processes this information through GPT-5 and Gemini AI models to generate structured reports with key insights, risk assessments, and relationship mapping.

How It Works

1. Data Collection Phase

The workflow begins by querying multiple public databases simultaneously. It searches CourtListener for any legal cases involving the target organization, checks LegiScan for relevant legislation, and scans OSINT sources for mentions in news articles, regulatory filings, and public records.

2. AI Processing Stage

Collected data is then processed through GPT-5 and Gemini AI models. The AI identifies patterns, extracts key entities (people, locations, dates), and assesses sentiment from news coverage. It cross-references information across sources to verify accuracy.

3. Report Generation

The system compiles findings into a structured report including executive summary, risk assessment, key relationships, and recommended areas for further investigation. Reports can be output in multiple formats (PDF, Word, or database entries).
