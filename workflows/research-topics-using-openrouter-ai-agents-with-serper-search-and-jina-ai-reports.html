What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the problem of time-consuming manual research by automating the entire process of gathering, analyzing, and synthesizing information on complex topics. Instead of spending hours searching websites, reading documents, and compiling reports, you provide a research prompt and receive a comprehensive, verified report within minutes.

The system orchestrates multiple AI agents that perform strategic web searches, extract content from discovered sources, apply analytical thinking tools, synthesize findings, and verify the report against retrieved documents to detect and correct hallucinations. It's designed for researchers, analysts, and knowledge workers who need accurate, timely intelligence without the manual effort.

Whether you're investigating market trends, competitor activities, regulatory changes, or industry developments, this automation delivers the insights you need with factual accuracy and analytical depth that raw search results can't provide.

How It Works

1. Research Prompt Input

The workflow accepts a research prompt via webhook or sub-workflow call. This prompt defines what you want to investigate—for example, "Research the current state of cage-free egg commitments among major food service companies." The system uses this prompt to guide the entire research process.

2. Strategic Web Searching

Using the Serper API (Google search), the workflow performs multiple strategic searches to gather comprehensive information. It doesn't just do one search—it executes several queries to ensure coverage from different angles and sources, maximizing the quality and diversity of information retrieved.

3. Content Extraction & Analysis

The Jina AI reader extracts full content from discovered URLs, going beyond snippets to get complete articles, reports, or documents. This content forms the evidence base for the research. The OpenRouter AI agents then analyze this content, applying "thinking" tools for strategic analysis and planning.

4. Report Synthesis & Verification

The AI synthesizes findings into a coherent research report, organizing information logically and highlighting key insights. Crucially, the system then verifies this report against the retrieved documents to detect hallucinations—claims not supported by evidence. If fabricated content is detected, it automatically retries with corrections.

5. Output Delivery

The final output is a verified, source-grounded research report delivered in your preferred format. The workflow includes automatic retry logic if the AI doesn't use its tools properly or produces empty responses, ensuring reliable output every time.
