What This Workflow Does

This automated legal research system solves the challenge of manually tracking regulatory changes across multiple sources. Legal professionals typically spend hours each week monitoring CourtListener for case law updates, checking LegiScan for new legislation, and conducting supplemental web searches - often duplicating efforts across team members.

The workflow consolidates these research tasks into a single automated process that delivers comprehensive reports. It retrieves relevant case law from CourtListener, tracks legislative changes via LegiScan, enhances findings with AI-powered analysis through OpenRouter, and supplements with curated web search results - all formatted into organized briefing documents.

How It Works

1. Case Law Retrieval

The workflow queries CourtListener's API for recent decisions matching your specified legal topics, jurisdictions, and date ranges. It filters results by relevance score and citation count to prioritize impactful cases.

2. Legislation Monitoring

LegiScan integration tracks bills and statutes related to your compliance areas. The system identifies newly introduced legislation, amendments, and status changes across federal and state levels.

3. AI Analysis

OpenRouter processes the collected legal materials through specialized AI models trained on legal texts. It generates executive summaries, highlights conflicting interpretations, and flags potential compliance risks.

4. Web Research

The system supplements official sources with curated web searches for legal commentary, expert analyses, and real-world implementation examples. Results are filtered for authority and relevance.

5. Report Generation

All findings are compiled into formatted briefs with executive summaries, key takeaways, and source citations. Reports can be delivered via email, saved to cloud storage, or integrated with case management systems.
