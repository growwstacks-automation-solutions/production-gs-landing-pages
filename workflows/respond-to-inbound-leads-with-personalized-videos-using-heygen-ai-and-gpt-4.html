What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you respond to inbound leads by instantly sending personalized video messages generated with AI. When a new lead comes in (from a form, CRM, or email), the workflow uses GPT-4 to create a tailored script mentioning their specific needs, then Heygen AI generates a natural-looking video with synchronized lip movements.

The system eliminates manual video recording while maintaining authentic personalization at scale. Leads receive a human-like video response within minutes, dramatically improving engagement rates compared to templated emails or delayed human responses.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture

The workflow triggers when a new lead is detected in your CRM, form submission tool, or email inbox. It extracts key details like name, company, and inquiry details for personalization.

2. AI Script Generation

GPT-4 analyzes the lead data and generates a natural-sounding video script tailored to their specific context. The AI incorporates your brand voice, product details, and relevant pain points.

Pro tip: Customize the GPT-4 prompt template to emphasize your unique value proposition and common customer objections you address.

3. Video Generation

Heygen AI transforms the script into a realistic video using your chosen avatar and background. The AI handles lip-syncing, facial expressions, and voice modulation automatically.

4. Delivery & Tracking

The personalized video gets sent via email or SMS, with tracking to monitor opens and engagement. All interactions sync back to your CRM for follow-up.
