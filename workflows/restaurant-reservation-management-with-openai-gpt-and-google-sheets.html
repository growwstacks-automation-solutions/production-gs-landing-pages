What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how restaurants handle reservations by using AI to interpret customer messages. Instead of staff manually entering details from phone calls or emails, the system automatically extracts key information like party size, date, time, and special requests from natural language inputs.

The workflow checks real-time availability in your Google Sheets reservation calendar, confirms bookings automatically, and sends personalized confirmation messages. It reduces no-shows by 30-40% through automated reminders while building a customer database for targeted marketing campaigns.

The AI analyzes incoming messages to extract reservation details

How It Works

1. Message Processing

Customers send reservation requests via your website chat, SMS, or messaging apps. The AI identifies key details even when phrased differently ("Table for 4 at 7" vs "Can we dine Friday evening with 4 people").

2. Availability Check

The system checks your Google Sheets reservation calendar in real-time, accounting for table configurations, existing bookings, and staff availability rules you've configured.

3. Confirmation & Follow-up

For available slots, the system sends personalized confirmations with reservation details. It automatically adds reminders 24 hours before the booking and follows up afterward for feedback.

Pro tip: Train the AI on your restaurant's specific terminology (like "booth" vs "table") to improve accuracy over time.
