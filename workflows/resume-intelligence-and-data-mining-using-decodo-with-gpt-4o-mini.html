What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms unstructured resume data into actionable hiring intelligence. It solves the critical pain point of manual resume screening, which consumes 23+ hours per week for the average recruiter. By combining Decodo's document processing with GPT-4o-mini's contextual analysis, the workflow extracts standardized candidate profiles from diverse resume formats.

The system automatically identifies key qualifications, skills, experience levels, and potential red flags. It outputs structured data ready for integration with your ATS or HR analytics platform, enabling data-driven hiring decisions at scale. For high-volume recruitment teams, this can reduce screening time per candidate from 5-7 minutes to under 30 seconds.

Structured data output showing extracted candidate qualifications and skills

How It Works

1. Resume Ingestion

The workflow accepts resumes in multiple formats (PDF, DOCX, etc.) from your preferred source - email attachments, cloud storage, or direct ATS integration. Decodo's document processing engine converts all formats to standardized text while preserving layout context.

2. AI-Powered Data Extraction

GPT-4o-mini analyzes the processed text to identify and categorize information. It understands contextual relationships between sections, distinguishing between current vs. past roles, education levels, and skill proficiency indicators.

3. Qualification Scoring

The system compares extracted data against your job requirements template, scoring candidates on must-have and nice-to-have qualifications. Custom weighting adjusts for role priorities.

4. Structured Output

Final data exports as standardized JSON including work history timelines, skill matrices, education verification flags, and compatibility scores. This feeds directly into your HR systems or triggers next-step automations.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to flag candidates who meet 80%+ of requirements but come from non-traditional backgrounds - this helps surface hidden talent while maintaining quality standards.
