What This Workflow Does

Manual resume screening is one of the most time-consuming parts of recruitment. This intelligent automation solves that by combining AI efficiency with human judgment. The workflow automatically processes incoming resumes, extracts key information, scores them against your specific job description using OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini, and then intelligently routes candidates for human validation when needed.

Instead of replacing recruiters, it augments them—handling the tedious first-pass review while ensuring human oversight for critical decisions. The system creates a consistent, bias-reduced evaluation process that scales with your hiring volume without compromising on quality.

By integrating gotoHuman for validation, you maintain control over which candidates move forward while automating 80-90% of the screening workload. The result is faster hiring cycles, better candidate matches, and more strategic use of your recruitment team's time.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical pipeline that mimics—and improves upon—traditional screening processes.

1. Resume Ingestion & Processing

The workflow begins by receiving resumes through your preferred channel—email attachments, ATS webhooks, or uploaded files. It extracts text from PDFs, Word documents, or other formats, normalizes the content, and structures it for analysis.

2. AI-Powered Analysis & Scoring

Using your predefined job description as the benchmark, GPT-4o-mini analyzes each resume against key criteria: skills match, experience relevance, education, certifications, and cultural indicators. The AI generates a numerical score (0-100) and a detailed summary explaining the rating.

3. Intelligent Routing Logic

Based on configurable thresholds, the workflow automatically categorizes candidates: high-scoring applicants proceed directly to the next stage, low-scoring ones are rejected with templated feedback, and borderline cases are sent for human validation via gotoHuman.

4. Human-in-the-Loop Validation

For candidates in the "review needed" zone, the system creates a structured task in gotoHuman with the resume, AI summary, and scoring breakdown. Your team reviews these cases and makes the final call, with their decision automatically fed back into the workflow.

5. Action & Integration

Once decisions are finalized (by AI or human), the workflow triggers appropriate next steps: sending personalized emails to candidates, updating your ATS, scheduling interviews, or logging analytics for continuous improvement.

Pro tip: Set your AI confidence threshold at 75% initially. Candidates scoring above this proceed automatically, those below 50% are auto-rejected, and the 50-75% range goes for human review. Adjust based on your hiring volume and role criticality.

Quick Setup Guide

Get this workflow running in your n8n instance in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the JSON template using the button above
  2. Configure OpenAI credentials in n8n with your API key
  3. Install the gotoHuman community node (@gotohuman/n8n-nodes-gotohuman)
  4. Set up your gotoHuman connection with API credentials
  5. Create a review template in gotoHuman with Resume, Summary, and Rating fields
  6. Map your job description into the workflow's "Set" node
  7. Test with sample resumes to verify scoring and routing logic
  8. Connect your actual resume source (email, webhook, or upload trigger)

Important: Before going live, run the workflow with 10-20 real resumes to calibrate your scoring thresholds. Different roles and seniority levels may require different cutoff points for optimal results.
