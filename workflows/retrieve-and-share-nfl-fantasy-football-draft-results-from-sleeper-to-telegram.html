What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves a common pain point for fantasy football commissioners and league members who use Sleeper for their drafts. After completing your fantasy draft on Sleeper, the workflow automatically retrieves all draft picks and sends them to your Telegram group or channel.

Manually copying and sharing draft results is time-consuming, especially for leagues with many teams and rounds. This automation ensures everyone in your league gets the complete draft results instantly in your preferred Telegram chat, formatted clearly for easy reference throughout the season.

How It Works

1. Trigger on Sleeper draft completion

The workflow monitors your Sleeper league for draft completion. Once the draft finishes, it automatically pulls all draft picks including player names, positions, teams, and which fantasy team selected them.

2. Format the draft results

The raw draft data is processed into a clean, readable format. You can customize how the results appear - by round, by team, or with additional stats like projected points.

3. Send to Telegram

The formatted draft results are sent to your specified Telegram chat or channel. The message includes all relevant draft information in an organized layout that's easy for league members to reference.
