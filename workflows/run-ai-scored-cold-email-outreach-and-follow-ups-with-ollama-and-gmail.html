What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized cold outreach while maintaining quality. Traditional tools either require manual review (slowing down campaigns) or send generic blasts (hurting response rates). The workflow uses Ollama's AI to score each email's personalization, value proposition, and call-to-action clarity before sending through Gmail.

Key features include automatic follow-up sequencing based on engagement, real-time performance analytics, and AI suggestions for improving underperforming messages. Sales teams using this system typically see 2-3x higher response rates compared to manual outreach.

How It Works

1. Prospect List Processing

The workflow ingests CSV leads or connects to your CRM, enriching data with LinkedIn info or company details when available.

2. AI Content Scoring

Ollama analyzes draft emails against 12 quality metrics including personalization depth, value clarity, and readability.

3. Automated Sequencing

Based on scores and engagement, the system triggers tailored follow-ups (case studies, social proof, or direct asks) at optimal intervals.
