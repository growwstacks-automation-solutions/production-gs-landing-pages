What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow creates a powerful AI sales assistant that combines GPT-4o-mini's natural language understanding with real-time data from your Google Sheets CRM. It solves the challenge of providing instant, accurate responses to customer inquiries while maintaining context across conversations.

Traditional sales teams struggle with delayed responses, inconsistent information, and repetitive questions. This automated solution ensures customers get immediate answers about products, pricing, and availability while freeing your team to focus on high-value interactions.

How It Works

1. Customer Inquiry Processing

The workflow receives customer messages through your preferred channel (web chat, WhatsApp, etc.). GPT-4o-mini analyzes the intent and extracts key details like product names, customer IDs, or specific questions.

2. Google Sheets Data Lookup

The system queries your Google Sheets CRM based on the extracted parameters. It can retrieve product details, pricing tiers, inventory levels, or customer purchase history to inform the response.

3. Context-Aware Response Generation

GPT-4o-mini crafts personalized responses incorporating both the customer's query and the CRM data. The memory function ensures follow-up questions reference previous interactions for continuity.

4. Conversation Logging

Each interaction is recorded back to Google Sheets, creating a complete history of customer engagements that your sales team can reference during follow-ups.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. OpenAI API access for GPT-4o-mini
  3. A Google Sheets document with your product/CRM data
  4. Google Cloud credentials with Sheets API enabled
  5. A messaging platform webhook (optional for live deployment)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your OpenAI API credentials in the GPT node
  3. Connect the Google Sheets nodes to your CRM spreadsheet
  4. Set up your preferred messaging channel webhook
  5. Test with sample queries and refine prompts as needed
