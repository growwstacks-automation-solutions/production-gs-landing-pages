What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how sales teams research prospects by combining data from Apollo.io (a sales intelligence platform), Linkup AI (for relationship mapping), and LinkedIn (for social verification). Instead of manual research that takes 15-20 minutes per prospect, this workflow automatically compiles a complete 360° view of each contact in seconds.

The system goes beyond basic contact enrichment by analyzing professional networks, identifying mutual connections, extracting key insights from LinkedIn profiles, and preparing personalized talking points for outreach. This level of automation was previously only available to enterprise sales teams with dedicated research staff.

How It Works

1. Prospect Data Collection

The workflow starts by pulling prospect information from Apollo.io, including name, company, role, and any available contact details. Apollo serves as the foundational data source with its comprehensive B2B contact database.

2. LinkedIn Profile Enrichment

Using the prospect's name and company, the workflow searches LinkedIn to verify the profile and extract additional professional details. This step ensures you're working with current, accurate information and captures valuable context about their career path.

3. Relationship Mapping with Linkup AI

Linkup AI analyzes the prospect's LinkedIn connections to identify mutual contacts, shared alumni networks, or other relationship bridges. This intelligence helps craft warmer outreach messages by referencing common connections or experiences.

4. Personalized Insight Generation

The system compiles all gathered data into a structured profile with actionable insights for outreach. It highlights potential conversation starters based on career transitions, published content, shared groups, or other engagement triggers.
