What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of information overload by automatically saving and summarizing articles shared in Telegram channels to your Notion workspace. Instead of manually copying links or reading entire articles, the workflow uses GPT-4o to extract key insights and organize them in a searchable knowledge base.

The system captures the original article URL, publication metadata, and creates an AI-generated summary that highlights the main arguments, key data points, and conclusions. This transforms scattered Telegram content into structured, actionable knowledge that's easy to reference later.

How It Works

1. Telegram Trigger

The workflow monitors specified Telegram channels for new article links. When detected, it extracts the URL and any accompanying context from the message.

2. Article Processing

The system fetches the full article content, cleans the HTML, and identifies the main text while removing ads and navigation elements.

3. AI Summarization

GPT-4o analyzes the article and generates a concise summary that preserves the core ideas and key supporting points in your preferred format and length.

4. Notion Integration

The workflow creates a new Notion page with the original link, summary, and relevant metadata organized in your specified database structure.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to add custom tags based on article content or channel source for better organization in Notion.
