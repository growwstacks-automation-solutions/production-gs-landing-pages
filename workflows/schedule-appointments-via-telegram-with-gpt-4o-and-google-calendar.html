What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually scheduling appointments through messaging apps. When clients message your Telegram bot with booking requests, GPT-4o intelligently interprets their preferred dates and times, checks your Google Calendar availability, and confirms the appointment—all within the chat interface.

The system handles natural language variations ("next Tuesday around 2pm", "the 15th in the morning"), timezone conversions, and sends automated calendar invites. It reduces scheduling back-and-forth by 80% while maintaining a personalized client experience.

How It Works

1. Telegram Message Trigger

The workflow activates when a user messages your Telegram bot with a scheduling request. The bot identifies booking-related keywords and phrases to initiate the process.

2. GPT-4o Natural Language Processing

GPT-4o analyzes the message to extract: requested date/time, service type, duration, and any special requirements. It converts vague time references ("next week") into specific time slots.

3. Google Calendar Availability Check

The system cross-references extracted time preferences with your actual calendar availability, suggesting the nearest matching slots if the requested time is booked.

4. Confirmation & Invite

Once a time is agreed upon, the bot sends confirmation details and automatically creates a Google Calendar event with both parties invited. Reminders are scheduled based on your business rules.

Pro tip: Configure your Telegram bot to recognize cancellation requests too. The same workflow can automatically remove calendar events when clients need to reschedule.
