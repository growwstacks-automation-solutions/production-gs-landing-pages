What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually posting food photos to Pinterest with engaging captions. It connects your Google Sheets content library with AI-powered caption generation and Pinterest scheduling, creating a complete hands-off content pipeline.

The workflow identifies high-performing food photos from your spreadsheet, generates SEO-optimized descriptions using GPT-3.5's understanding of food trends and Pinterest algorithms, then schedules them for publication at optimal times. This eliminates hours of repetitive work while ensuring consistent, high-quality content output.

How It Works

1. Google Sheets content extraction

The workflow scans your designated Google Sheet for new food photos marked as ready for publishing. It pulls image URLs, metadata like recipe names or ingredients, and any specific caption instructions you've included.

2. AI caption generation

Each photo gets processed through GPT-3.5 with customized prompts tailored for food content. The AI analyzes visual elements (when available) and your provided metadata to create Pinterest-optimized descriptions that include relevant keywords and hashtags.

3. Quality control checks

Before scheduling, the system can optionally send generated captions for human review or apply content guidelines to ensure brand consistency. This step prevents any AI-generated text that doesn't match your voice or standards.

4. Pinterest scheduling

Approved pins get scheduled according to your predetermined calendar or performance-based timing rules. The workflow handles all Pinterest API requirements including image formatting, board selection, and link attachments.
