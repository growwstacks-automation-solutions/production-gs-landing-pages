What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually posting social media content across multiple platforms. Marketing teams often waste hours each week copying content from spreadsheets to social platforms, checking schedules, and ensuring posts go live at optimal times.

The workflow automatically pulls approved posts from your Google Sheets content calendar and publishes them to Twitter and Instagram at their scheduled times. It marks completed posts in your spreadsheet and can handle text posts, image attachments, and platform-specific formatting.

How It Works

1. Spreadsheet Setup

Your team adds social posts to a shared Google Sheet with columns for content, platform, scheduled date/time, and status. Posts marked as "Pending" will be queued for publishing.

2. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs at regular intervals (e.g., every hour) to check for posts due to be published. It filters the spreadsheet for rows with status "Pending" and scheduled times within the current window.

3. Platform Routing

Each post is routed to the appropriate social platform (Twitter or Instagram) based on the platform column in your spreadsheet. The workflow formats the content appropriately for each platform's requirements.

4. Post Publishing

The workflow publishes the content to the designated social platform at the exact scheduled time. It can attach images from URLs stored in your spreadsheet and handle hashtags, mentions, and links appropriately.

5. Status Update

After successful posting, the workflow updates the status column in your Google Sheet to "Published" and adds the actual publish timestamp. Failed posts are marked for review.
