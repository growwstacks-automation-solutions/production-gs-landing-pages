What This Workflow Does

Procurement teams often struggle with tracking dozens of purchase orders and ensuring timely follow-ups with suppliers. Manual tracking leads to missed deadlines, late payments, and strained supplier relationships. This workflow solves these challenges by automating the entire follow-up process from tracking to execution.

The system monitors your Airtable purchase order database for upcoming and overdue actions. Using AI analysis, it prioritizes which suppliers need immediate attention based on order value, contract terms, and historical data. It then creates Google Calendar events for follow-ups while notifying your team via Slack and sending polite reminders to suppliers through Gmail.

How It Works

1. Airtable PO Monitoring

The workflow regularly checks your Airtable purchase order base for records approaching their follow-up dates. It identifies overdue POs and those needing pre-delivery confirmation based on your configured thresholds.

2. AI Prioritization

An AI module analyzes each flagged PO using multiple parameters: order value, supplier reliability score, contractual penalties, and current inventory levels. This creates a priority score determining which follow-ups require immediate action.

3. Calendar Scheduling

High-priority follow-ups get scheduled as events in Google Calendar with all relevant PO details. The system blocks appropriate time slots based on estimated follow-up complexity and includes all necessary context for your procurement team.

4. Team Notifications

Slack notifications alert your procurement team about upcoming supplier actions. Messages include quick action buttons to confirm completion or escalate issues. For critical path items, the system can ping multiple team members.

5. Supplier Communication

Polite, templated emails automatically go to suppliers through Gmail, requesting status updates or confirming next steps. The system tracks responses and updates Airtable accordingly, creating a complete audit trail.
