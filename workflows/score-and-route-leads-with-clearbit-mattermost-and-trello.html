What This Workflow Does

Manual lead qualification is a major time sink for sales teams. This automation solves that by creating a hands-off pipeline that captures, enriches, scores, and routes every new lead instantly. When a lead submits a form, the workflow pulls additional company data from Clearbit, calculates a custom score based on job title, industry, and company size, and then takes action.

High-scoring "hot" leads trigger an immediate alert in your Mattermost (or Slack) channel with all key details, prompting quick follow-up. Simultaneously, a Trello card is created in a "Hot Leads" list. All other leads are added to a "New Leads" backlog in Trello for later review. This ensures your team spends time on the most promising prospects while maintaining a complete audit trail of every inquiry.

How It Works

The workflow is triggered on a schedule (e.g., every 15 minutes) to check for new form submissions.

Step 1: Fetch & Enrich Leads

The workflow starts by pulling unprocessed leads from your form backend or CRM API. Each lead, typically just an email and name, is then sent to Clearbit's enrichment API. Clearbit returns valuable firmographic data like company name, industry, employee count, and technology stack.

Step 2: Calculate Lead Score

A custom JavaScript code node analyzes the enriched data. It applies weighted points to different attributes—for example, points for being in a target industry, having a senior job title, or working at a company of a certain size. The output is a numerical score (e.g., 0-100).

Step 3: Route Based on Score

An "IF" node checks if the score meets your defined threshold (e.g., 70+). If it does, the lead is deemed "hot" and the workflow branches to send a Mattermost alert and create a Trello card in a priority list. If not, it simply creates a card in a standard backlog list.

Step 4: Notify Team & Create Tasks

For hot leads, a formatted message is posted to your designated Mattermost channel, tagging the sales team. A detailed Trello card is created with the lead's score, enriched data, and a link to the original submission. This card becomes the central place for all follow-up activity.

What You'll Need

  1. An active n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted).
  2. A Clearbit API key (free tier available).
  3. A Mattermost or Slack workspace with a channel for lead alerts.
  4. A Trello board with lists for "Hot Leads" and "New Leads".
  5. A lead capture form (like Typeform, Google Forms, or a custom webhook) that can deliver JSON data.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the Template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure Credentials: Add your Clearbit, Mattermost, and Trello API credentials in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Set Environment Variables: Define your Trello board/list IDs and your lead score threshold in n8n's settings.
  4. Connect Your Form: Update the first HTTP Request node with the endpoint URL and authentication for your form provider.
  5. Test & Activate: Run the workflow once with a test lead to verify alerts and Trello cards are created correctly, then activate the schedule trigger.

Pro tip: Start with a conservative scoring threshold. You can adjust it higher or lower after reviewing the first 20-30 scored leads to see if the automation correctly identifies your actual "hot" prospects.
