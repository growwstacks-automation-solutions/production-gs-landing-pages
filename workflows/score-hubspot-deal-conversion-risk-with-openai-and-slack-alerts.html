What This Workflow Does

Sales teams often struggle to accurately predict which deals will close and which might stall. Traditional forecasting relies heavily on gut feeling and manual probability estimates, leading to missed warnings and surprise losses. This workflow automates objective deal risk assessment by analyzing multiple data points in your HubSpot CRM.

Using OpenAI's natural language processing, the system evaluates deal health based on: communication patterns, engagement history, deal progression speed, and contextual signals in notes/emails. It generates daily risk scores for all active deals and sends Slack alerts when high-risk opportunities are detected, enabling timely interventions.

How It Works

1. Daily HubSpot Deal Extraction

The workflow starts by fetching all active deals from HubSpot each morning. It retrieves both structured data (deal stage, amount, close date) and unstructured content (notes, email threads, meeting summaries).

2. AI Risk Scoring

OpenAI processes the deal information through a custom prompt that evaluates multiple risk factors. The model considers: communication frequency changes, urgency signals, competitor mentions, stakeholder engagement patterns, and historical conversion rates for similar deals.

3. Priority Flagging

Deals are categorized into risk tiers (High/Medium/Low) based on score thresholds you configure. The system compares each deal's current score to its historical trend to detect meaningful changes.

4. Slack Notifications

For deals crossing into High Risk territory, detailed Slack alerts are sent to your sales channel. Alerts include: risk score change magnitude, key reasons for the change, and recommended next steps based on similar deals that successfully recovered.
