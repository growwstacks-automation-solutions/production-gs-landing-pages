What This Workflow Does

Manual LinkedIn prospecting is time-consuming and inconsistent. Sales and recruiting teams waste hours evaluating profiles, trying to guess which leads match their Ideal Customer Profile (ICP). This automation solves that by automatically scoring individual LinkedIn profiles against your specific ICP criteria using Airtop's enrichment capabilities.

The workflow extracts key data points from LinkedIn profiles—including job title, seniority, company information, technical depth, and AI interest—then calculates a weighted score based on how well each prospect matches your target profile. This transforms subjective gut-feel evaluation into data-driven prioritization.

Instead of spending 15-20 minutes researching each prospect, your team gets instant ICP scores that help them focus on the highest-potential leads first. The enriched profile data and calculated score can then be routed to your CRM, triggering personalized outreach sequences for hot leads while automatically disqualifying poor fits.

How It Works

This n8n workflow connects LinkedIn data with Airtop's AI-powered enrichment to deliver actionable ICP scores in seconds.

Step 1: Trigger & Input

The workflow starts when a new LinkedIn profile URL is submitted—either manually, via a form, or automatically from your lead list. The system accepts the profile URL and prepares it for analysis.

Step 2: Profile Enrichment with Airtop

Airtop extracts structured data from the LinkedIn profile, including full name, job title, employer, company LinkedIn URL, location, connections, followers, about section, and more. This creates a comprehensive data foundation for scoring.

Step 3: ICP Scoring Logic

The workflow applies your custom scoring criteria to the enriched data. You define weights for different attributes: AI interest (beginner 5 points to expert 35 points), technical depth (basic 5 to expert 35), and seniority level (junior 5 to executive 30). The system calculates a total score out of 100.

Step 4: Results & Routing

The enriched profile data plus the calculated ICP score is output in clean JSON format. You can then route high-scoring leads to immediate sales outreach, medium scores to nurturing sequences, and low scores to monitoring lists—all automatically.
