What This Workflow Does

Sales and marketing teams waste countless hours manually researching LinkedIn profiles to determine if someone fits their ideal customer profile. This subjective process leads to inconsistent prioritization, missed opportunities, and inefficient resource allocation.

This automation solves that problem by automatically scoring individual LinkedIn profiles against your specific ICP criteria. It extracts relevant data, evaluates AI interest levels, technical expertise, and seniority, then calculates a comprehensive score that tells you exactly how well a prospect matches your ideal customer.

The workflow transforms subjective gut-feel assessments into data-driven decisions, ensuring your sales team focuses on the highest-potential leads while maintaining consistent evaluation standards across your entire organization.

How It Works

1. Profile Input & Trigger

The workflow accepts LinkedIn profile URLs through a form trigger or can be initiated from another workflow. This flexible triggering mechanism allows integration with various lead sources, whether from web forms, CRM exports, or manual entry.

2. Data Extraction & Enrichment

Using Airtop's enrichment capabilities, the workflow extracts comprehensive profile data including job title, company information, location, connection count, about section content, and professional background details.

3. Multi-Dimensional Scoring

The system evaluates three key dimensions: AI interest level (beginner to expert), technical depth (basic to advanced), and seniority level (junior to executive). Each dimension receives weighted points based on your business priorities.

4. Composite Score Calculation

Points from all dimensions are combined into a total ICP score out of 95. The scoring algorithm applies your specific weighting preferences to ensure alignment with what actually converts for your business.

5. Structured Output Delivery

The enriched profile data and calculated scores are formatted into clean JSON output, ready for integration with your CRM, database, or notification systems for immediate action by your sales team.

What You'll Need

  1. Airtop Account & LinkedIn Connection: An active Airtop profile connected to LinkedIn for data extraction capabilities.
  2. n8n Instance: Self-hosted n8n or n8n.cloud account with API credential configuration.
  3. ICP Scoring Criteria: Clear definition of what constitutes your ideal customer across dimensions you want to measure.
  4. Lead Source Integration: Method to feed LinkedIn profile URLs into the workflow (forms, CSV imports, or API connections).
  5. Output Destination: CRM, database, or notification system to receive scored profiles for sales team action.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download & Import: Download the template file and import it into your n8n instance using the workflow import function.
  2. Configure Airtop Credentials: Add your Airtop API credentials to the Airtop node in the workflow settings.
  3. Customize Scoring Weights: Modify the scoring logic in the Airtop node prompt to match your specific ICP criteria and point allocations.
  4. Test with Sample Profiles: Run the workflow with 3-5 known customer profiles to validate scoring accuracy against your expectations.
  5. Connect Lead Sources: Set up your trigger mechanism—either a web form, scheduled import, or API endpoint for receiving profile URLs.
  6. Configure Output Destinations: Connect the workflow output to your CRM, database, or notification system for scored lead delivery.
  7. Monitor & Refine: Review initial scoring results and adjust weights as needed based on real conversion data.

Pro tip: Start with broader scoring criteria and refine over 2-3 weeks as you gather data on which scores actually correlate with conversion. The most effective ICP scoring evolves with your understanding of what drives customer success.
