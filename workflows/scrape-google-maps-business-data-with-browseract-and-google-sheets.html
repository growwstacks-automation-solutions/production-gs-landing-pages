What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of collecting business information from Google Maps. Marketing teams, sales professionals, and researchers often spend hours copying data like phone numbers, addresses, and reviews from Google Maps listings.

The workflow uses BrowserAct's AI-powered web automation to intelligently scrape Google Maps data, then structures and saves the information directly into Google Sheets. It handles the entire process from search to data extraction to spreadsheet organization automatically.

How It Works

1. BrowserAct navigates Google Maps

The workflow starts by launching BrowserAct to visit Google Maps and perform searches based on your specified parameters (location, business type, etc.). It mimics human browsing behavior to avoid detection.

2. Data extraction from listings

BrowserAct's AI identifies and extracts key business information from each listing - name, address, phone number, website, reviews, and other available details. It handles different listing formats automatically.

3. Data processing in n8n

The scraped data flows into n8n where it's cleaned, formatted, and organized. Duplicates are removed, addresses are standardized, and phone numbers are validated.

4. Export to Google Sheets

The processed data is then sent to your specified Google Sheet, with each business getting its own row and different data points organized into columns. The workflow can append to existing sheets or create new ones.
