What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manual lead generation by combining Google Maps scraping with email verification. Traditional prospecting requires hours of searching, copying data, and verifying contacts - this workflow handles all those steps automatically.

The system extracts complete business profiles from Google Maps including names, addresses, phone numbers, and websites. It then processes this data through Anymailfinder to identify and verify professional email addresses, creating a ready-to-use lead list for your sales or outreach campaigns.

How It Works

Step 1: Google Maps Data Extraction

The workflow starts by connecting to Apify's Google Maps Scraper. You configure search parameters like location, business category, and radius. The scraper then extracts all matching business listings with their complete public profiles.

Step 2: Data Cleaning and Organization

Raw scraped data gets processed to remove duplicates, standardize formats, and filter based on your criteria (like minimum ratings or years in business). The system organizes the information into structured fields for easy integration with your CRM.

Step 3: Email Finding and Verification

For each business website found, the workflow queries Anymailfinder's API to discover professional email addresses. The service verifies each address's validity and provides confidence scores, ensuring your outreach targets actual decision-makers.
