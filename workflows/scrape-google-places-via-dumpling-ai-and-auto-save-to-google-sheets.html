What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually collecting business information from Google Places listings. Marketing teams, sales professionals, and researchers often spend hours copying data from search results into spreadsheets. The workflow automates this entire process using Dumpling AI's advanced scraping capabilities and n8n's powerful automation platform.

When triggered, the workflow searches Google Places based on your parameters (location, business category, etc.), extracts all relevant business details through Dumpling AI's data extraction service, then formats and saves the structured data directly to your Google Sheets document. This creates a continuously updated lead database without manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Search Parameter Setup

The workflow begins by accepting your search criteria including location, business type, and any filters (minimum ratings, open now status, etc.). These parameters determine which Google Places listings will be processed.

2. Dumpling AI Data Extraction

Using Dumpling AI's scraping technology, the workflow accesses Google Places search results and extracts structured business data including names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, ratings, and review counts.

3. Data Cleaning & Formatting

The raw extracted data goes through processing steps to standardize formats (phone numbers, addresses), remove duplicates, and apply any quality filters you've configured.

4. Google Sheets Integration

Finally, the cleaned data is appended to your specified Google Sheet with proper column headers and consistent formatting. The workflow can create new sheets or update existing ones.
