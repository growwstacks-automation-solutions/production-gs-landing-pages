What This Workflow Does

Manual LinkedIn job research is time-consuming, inconsistent, and misses critical competitive insights. This automation solves that by systematically scraping job postings from your competitor list and organizing everything in Google Sheets for immediate analysis.

The workflow runs on a schedule, checks your target companies, launches Phantombuster scraping agents, waits for completion, fetches the results, and structures the data into clean, analyzable fields. What used to take hours of manual browsing now happens automatically while you focus on strategic decisions.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger & Company List Processing

Every Monday morning, the workflow activates and reads your "Companies Sheet" in Google Sheets. It filters for companies marked "Pending" to ensure you only scrape new or updated targets, preventing redundant data collection.

2. LinkedIn Job Scraping via Phantombuster

For each target company, the workflow launches your configured Phantombuster agent with the company's LinkedIn profile URL. It then waits 3 minutes (configurable) for the scraping to complete before fetching the output CSV link containing all job posting data.

3. Data Cleaning & Structuring

The raw scraped data undergoes transformation into standardized fields: Company Name, Job Title, Job Description, Job URL, Date Posted, Location, and Employment Type. This consistent structure enables reliable filtering, sorting, and trend analysis.

4. Google Sheets Population

Finally, each formatted job entry is appended to your "Job Results" sheet with a timestamp. This creates a growing historical database of competitor hiring activity that you can analyze with pivot tables, charts, or connect to BI tools.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow execution capabilities
  2. Phantombuster account with configured LinkedIn Job Scraper agent and API credentials
  3. Google Sheets with two sheets: "Companies" (with Status column) and "Job Results"
  4. Google Service Account or OAuth credentials for n8n to access your Sheets
  5. Target company list with LinkedIn profile URLs for scraping

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure Phantombuster: Add your API key and agent ID to the HTTP Request nodes.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: Set up authentication and update the spreadsheet IDs in the Google Sheets nodes.
  4. Set your company list: Populate your "Companies" sheet with target LinkedIn URLs and set Status to "Pending".
  5. Test and schedule: Run a manual test, then set the Schedule Trigger to your preferred frequency (weekly recommended).

Pro tip: Start with a small test list of 3-5 companies to verify data quality before scaling to dozens of targets. This helps you refine field mappings and ensure you're capturing the most relevant information.
