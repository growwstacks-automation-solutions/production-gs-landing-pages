What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire lead generation process by scraping local business directories using Bright Data's powerful web scraping tools, then enriching the data with OpenAI's AI capabilities. It solves the time-consuming problem of manually searching for and qualifying business leads, which typically takes sales teams 5-10 hours per week.

The workflow extracts key business information including company names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and categories from multiple directories. It then uses AI to analyze and qualify each lead based on your ideal customer profile, saving hundreds of hours in manual research while delivering higher-quality prospects than traditional methods.

How It Works

1. Directory Selection & Configuration

The workflow starts by connecting to Bright Data's scraping infrastructure and configuring target directories like Yelp, Yellow Pages, or industry-specific listings. You define search parameters including location radius, business categories, and filters to target your ideal prospects.

2. Automated Data Extraction

Bright Data's scrapers systematically collect business listings while handling proxies, CAPTCHAs, and anti-bot measures. The system extracts structured data including business names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and categories with 90%+ accuracy.

3. AI-Powered Lead Enrichment

OpenAI analyzes each business listing to estimate company size, identify key services, and extract decision-maker names when available. The AI scores leads based on your criteria and generates personalized insights for your sales team.

4. CRM Integration

Qualified leads automatically flow into your CRM or marketing system with proper tagging and categorization. The workflow can trigger follow-up sequences or assign leads to appropriate sales reps based on territory or specialty.
