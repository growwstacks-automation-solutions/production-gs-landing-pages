What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the time-consuming problem of manually checking multiple job boards for new opportunities. It automatically scrapes RSS feeds from your selected job sites, checks for duplicates against a Google Sheets database, and sends filtered alerts directly to your Telegram account.

For recruiters and active job seekers, this automation means no more refreshing boards or missing time-sensitive postings. The system works 24/7, scanning for matches to your criteria and delivering only relevant, non-duplicate opportunities straight to your phone.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Monitoring

The workflow begins by polling RSS feeds from your configured job boards at regular intervals. These could be general sites like Indeed or niche boards specific to your industry. Each feed URL can include search parameters like job titles, locations, or keywords.

2. Data Extraction

For each new posting found in the RSS feed, the workflow extracts key details like job title, company, location, salary (when available), description, and application URL. This structured data prepares each listing for processing in subsequent steps.

3. Duplicate Checking

Before sending any alerts, the workflow checks a designated Google Sheet that serves as your job posting history database. It compares the new posting's unique identifiers (usually a combination of title, company, and location) against existing records to prevent duplicate notifications.

4. Telegram Notification

Verified new postings get formatted into rich Telegram messages with proper line breaks, emojis for visual scanning, and direct application links. You'll receive these as instant notifications on your mobile device or desktop app.

5. Database Update

Finally, the workflow updates your Google Sheet with the new posting's details, maintaining a complete searchable history of all opportunities encountered. This record helps with tracking applications and analyzing job market trends over time.
