What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the challenge of keeping up with TikTok's rapidly changing trends while maintaining a consistent content pipeline. It combines web scraping, AI video generation, and publishing automation to create an end-to-end solution for content creators and marketing teams.

The system automatically scrapes trending TikTok content using Apify, analyzes the data to identify viral patterns, generates fresh AI videos with Fal AI that align with these trends, then prepares them for publishing through Google Suite integrations. This eliminates hours of manual research and content creation while ensuring your content stays relevant.

How It Works

1. TikTok Trend Scraping

Apify scrapes TikTok's trending page to extract viral hashtags, sounds, and video formats. The workflow filters this data based on your specified criteria (niche relevance, engagement thresholds, etc.) to identify the most valuable trends.

2. AI Video Generation

The scraped trend data is sent to Fal AI, which generates new video content matching the identified trends. The workflow can customize aspects like video length, style, and branding elements based on your preferences.

3. Content Review & Approval

Generated videos are automatically uploaded to Google Drive for review. The workflow can be configured to send approval requests via email or Slack before final publishing.

4. Automated Publishing

Approved videos are formatted for different platforms and scheduled for publishing through Google Sheets integration. The workflow maintains a content calendar and can distribute to multiple channels.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure your Apify API credentials in the TikTok scraper node
  3. Add your Fal AI API key to the video generation nodes
  4. Connect your Google account for Drive and Sheets integration
  5. Set your content preferences (video styles, posting schedule, etc.)
  6. Test with a small batch of scraped trends before full automation
