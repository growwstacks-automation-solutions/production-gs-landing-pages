What This Workflow Does

Manual CV screening is a time‑consuming, error‑prone bottleneck for HR teams. This automation solves that by instantly processing applicant submissions with AI, generating compatibility ratings, storing results in a centralized Google Sheet, and sending notifications to both HR and candidates.

The workflow transforms a multi‑hour manual review process into a minutes‑long automated pipeline. It ensures consistent, unbiased initial screening, provides data‑driven candidate rankings, and keeps everyone informed through automated emails. HR teams can focus on interviewing only the best‑fit candidates, accelerating hiring cycles and improving candidate experience.

How It Works

Step 1: CV Submission Trigger

Candidates submit their details and CV through a web form (like n8n Form Trigger). The submission automatically triggers the workflow, capturing applicant name, email, and CV file.

Step 2: PDF Extraction & AI Analysis

The CV PDF is processed to extract text. An AI model (like Google Gemini or OpenAI) analyzes the content against your job criteria—skills, experience, keywords—and generates a compatibility rating (e.g., 85% match).

Step 3: Results Storage in Google Sheets

The applicant’s details, CV text excerpt, and AI rating are appended to a Google Sheet. This creates a real‑time hiring dashboard where HR can sort, filter, and review all candidates.

Step 4: Automated Email Notifications

Two emails are sent simultaneously: a confirmation to the candidate thanking them for applying, and an alert to HR with the applicant’s details and AI rating for immediate review.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for HR teams, recruitment agencies, startups scaling their hiring, and companies receiving high volumes of applications. It’s especially valuable for roles where specific skills or experience thresholds are critical—tech, marketing, finance—and where unbiased, consistent screening is required.

Businesses that want to improve hiring speed, reduce manual workload, and create a more professional candidate experience will benefit immediately. Even solo recruiters can use it to manage applications without drowning in PDF reviews.

What You'll Need

  1. A n8n instance (cloud or self‑hosted)
  2. Google Sheets account & a prepared spreadsheet
  3. Email service (Gmail, SMTP, etc.) for notifications
  4. AI model access (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or similar)

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n workspace.
  2. Configure the Form Trigger node with your submission endpoint.
  3. Connect the Google Sheets node to your target spreadsheet.
  4. Set up your AI model node with your API key and screening prompt.
  5. Configure the Email nodes with your HR and candidate email templates.
  6. Test the workflow with a sample CV to verify ratings and notifications.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompt to match your specific job requirements. Include keywords, required years of experience, and soft‑skill indicators to get more accurate compatibility ratings.
