What This Workflow Does

This automation revolutionizes your hiring process by replacing manual resume screening with AI-powered candidate evaluation. When applicants submit information through Jotform, the workflow automatically:

  • Downloads and analyzes PDF resumes (even from private links)
  • Extracts key information from cover letters
  • Compares applicant qualifications against your Notion job descriptions using Gemini AI
  • Generates an objective "AI Fit Score" (0-100) and summary
  • Creates organized candidate pages in your Notion database
  • Alerts your team about top applicants via Slack

Pro tip: The AI scoring becomes more accurate over time as it learns from your hiring decisions. Consider reviewing scores periodically and adjusting the threshold.

How It Works

1. Application Submission

The workflow triggers when a candidate submits your Jotform application form. It captures all submitted data including resume files and cover letter text.

2. Resume Processing

The system automatically downloads PDF resumes (even from private links) and extracts all text content for AI analysis. No more manual PDF reading.

3. AI Comparison

Gemini AI compares the applicant's qualifications against your detailed job description in Notion, evaluating skills, experience, and other key factors.

4. Scoring & Filtering

Each applicant receives an objective score (0-100) based on job fit. The workflow can automatically filter out low-scoring candidates based on your threshold.

Example of AI comparing resume content to job requirements

5. Notion Integration

All applicant data is automatically organized in your Notion hiring database, with clear scoring and AI-generated summaries for easy review.

6. Team Notification

Your hiring team receives instant Slack notifications about top candidates, including their scores and key qualifications.
