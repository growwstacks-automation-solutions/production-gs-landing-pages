What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how sales and recruiting teams find business prospects by enabling natural language searches through Explorium's Market Contact Platform (MCP). Instead of struggling with complex filters and manual research, users can describe their ideal prospects in plain English and get automated, structured results.

The workflow connects Explorium MCP with n8n to process natural language queries, retrieve matching companies, enrich the data with additional firmographic details, and export the results to CSV or your CRM system. It eliminates hours of manual prospecting work while delivering more accurate, targeted lead lists.

Example of how natural language search results appear in the n8n workflow

How It Works

1. Submit natural language query

The workflow starts by accepting a natural language search phrase like "SaaS companies in Denver with 20-100 employees." This can be triggered manually or automatically from other systems.

2. Process query through Explorium MCP

n8n sends the query to Explorium's API which uses NLP to interpret the search intent and convert it into structured search parameters. The system understands business terminology, relative sizes, and geographic references.

3. Retrieve and enrich prospect data

Explorium returns matching companies with complete firmographic data including industry, size, technologies used, growth signals, and key contacts. The workflow can add additional enrichment from other data sources.

4. Format and export results

The final step formats the prospect data into a clean CSV file or sends it directly to your CRM, marketing automation platform, or internal databases for immediate use by sales teams.

Pro tip: Schedule this workflow to run weekly searches for new companies matching your criteria, ensuring your pipeline always has fresh prospects.
