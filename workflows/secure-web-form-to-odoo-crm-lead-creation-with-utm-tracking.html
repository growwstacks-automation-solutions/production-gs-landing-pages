What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of capturing web form submissions as properly attributed leads in Odoo CRM. Many businesses lose valuable marketing insights when form data arrives in their CRM without campaign tracking details. The workflow securely processes submissions from any web form, validates the data, enriches it with UTM parameters, and creates complete lead records in Odoo.

Unlike basic form connectors, this solution maintains data integrity through validation checks and preserves crucial marketing attribution that helps measure ROI. It handles all the technical complexities of API integration while providing clean, actionable lead data for your sales team.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Capture

The workflow begins when a visitor submits any web form configured to send data to the secure webhook endpoint. This could be a contact form, demo request, or lead capture form on your website or landing pages.

2. Data Validation & Processing

Each submission undergoes validation checks for required fields, email formatting, and spam indicators. The system extracts UTM parameters from the submission URL or hidden form fields to maintain marketing attribution.

3. Odoo Lead Creation

Validated data is transformed into Odoo's CRM lead format, mapping form fields to the appropriate Odoo fields. The workflow creates a new lead record via Odoo's API, including all UTM parameters as custom fields for reporting.

Pro tip: Use hidden form fields to capture additional context like page URL, visitor IP (for geo-tagging), and any custom parameters your sales team needs.
