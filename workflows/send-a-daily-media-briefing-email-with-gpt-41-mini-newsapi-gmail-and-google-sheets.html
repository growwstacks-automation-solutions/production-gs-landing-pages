What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking news coverage across multiple sources. Marketing teams, PR agencies, and executives spend hours daily scanning articles to stay informed about industry developments, competitor moves, and brand mentions.

The workflow automatically gathers relevant news through NewsAPI, processes articles with GPT-4.1-mini to extract key insights and sentiment analysis, then compiles everything into a polished email briefing delivered to stakeholders' inboxes each morning. It transforms raw news data into executive-ready intelligence without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. News Gathering

The workflow queries NewsAPI at scheduled intervals (typically overnight) using your configured keywords, topics, and preferred sources. It retrieves the most relevant articles published in the last 24 hours.

2. AI Processing

Each article passes through GPT-4.1-mini which performs three key functions: summarizes the content in 2-3 bullet points, evaluates sentiment (positive/neutral/negative), and extracts named entities (companies, people, locations).

3. Data Organization

Processed results log to Google Sheets for historical tracking, with columns for source, date, summary, sentiment score, and key entities. This creates a searchable archive of media coverage.

4. Email Composition

The system structures the top stories into an HTML email template, grouping by topic or sentiment. It includes headlines, summaries, source links, and a high-level trends analysis.

5. Scheduled Delivery

Gmail sends the formatted briefing at your specified time (typically 7-8 AM local time). Recipient lists can be managed in the Google Sheets configuration tab.
