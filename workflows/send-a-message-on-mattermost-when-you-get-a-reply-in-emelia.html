Get instant team notifications when leads reply to your outreach emails

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of missed email replies from potential customers by instantly alerting your entire sales team in Mattermost when a lead responds to your outreach. No more checking inboxes manually or delayed responses.

The workflow triggers when someone replies to your email campaign, extracts key information from the reply, and posts a structured notification to your designated Mattermost channel. This ensures your team can respond quickly while the lead is still engaged.

How It Works

1. Email Reply Detection

The Emelia Trigger node monitors your sent campaigns and activates when a recipient replies to any of your emails.

2. Data Extraction

The workflow parses the reply to identify the sender, their message content, and any attachments or links included.

3. Mattermost Notification

A formatted message containing all relevant details is automatically posted to your specified Mattermost channel, tagging appropriate team members.
