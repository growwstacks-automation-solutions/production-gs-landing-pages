What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of timely lead response by instantly notifying your sales team in Slack whenever a high-quality lead enters your Hubspot CRM. Manual lead monitoring often results in delayed responses, missed opportunities, and inconsistent follow-up processes.

The workflow intelligently filters new Hubspot contacts based on your predefined criteria for lead quality, ensuring your team only gets alerted about prospects worth immediate attention. This eliminates noise from low-priority leads while guaranteeing hot prospects never slip through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Hubspot contact monitoring

The workflow continuously checks your Hubspot account for new contacts using n8n's polling mechanism. It evaluates each new entry against your configured lead quality criteria.

2. Lead qualification filtering

Contacts are analyzed based on properties like lead score, company information, form submissions, or custom fields you specify. Only contacts meeting your high-quality threshold proceed to the next step.

3. Slack notification generation

For qualified leads, the workflow constructs a detailed Slack message containing key contact information, lead source, and a direct link to the Hubspot record for quick access.

4. Targeted channel delivery

The notification is posted to your designated Slack channel, optionally tagging specific team members or groups based on lead characteristics like territory or product interest.

Pro tip: Configure different Slack channels for different lead types (enterprise vs SMB) to streamline your sales team's focus.
