What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of consistently delivering fresh recipe content to your Telegram audience. Instead of manually sending recipes each day, this n8n workflow automatically selects and delivers one random recipe daily at your specified time.

The template comes pre-configured for vegan recipes but can be easily adapted for any dietary preference. It's perfect for food bloggers wanting to engage their audience, meal kit companies showcasing their offerings, or nutritionists providing clients with daily meal inspiration.

How It Works

1. Recipe Selection

The workflow connects to your recipe database or API source, randomly selecting one recipe that matches your configured filters (like vegan, gluten-free, etc.).

2. Formatting & Enrichment

The selected recipe gets formatted with images, ingredients, and instructions in Telegram-friendly format. You can customize the template to include your branding and calls-to-action.

3. Scheduled Delivery

Using n8n's scheduler, the workflow automatically sends the formatted recipe to your Telegram channel or group at your specified time each day.

Pro tip: Add a "Save Recipe" button to each message so subscribers can easily bookmark their favorites.
