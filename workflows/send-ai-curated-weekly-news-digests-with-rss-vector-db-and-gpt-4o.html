What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of staying current with industry news while avoiding information overload. It systematically collects, filters, and summarizes relevant content from multiple RSS feeds, then delivers polished weekly digests to your audience.

The two-stage process first uses vector database technology to identify semantically relevant articles based on their actual meaning (not just keywords). Then GPT-4o transforms these raw articles into concise, well-structured summaries with proper context and flow.

How It Works

1. RSS Feed Collection

The workflow pulls articles from configured RSS feeds at scheduled intervals. These can include industry publications, competitor blogs, or any news sources relevant to your niche.

2. Vector Embedding Generation

Each article is processed into vector embeddings - numerical representations that capture semantic meaning. This enables finding conceptually related content even when exact keywords don't match.

3. Semantic Filtering

The system compares article vectors against your defined topic vectors to identify the most relevant pieces. This eliminates irrelevant content more effectively than keyword matching alone.

4. AI Summarization

GPT-4o processes selected articles to extract key points, rewrite in consistent voice, add insightful commentary, and suggest connections between stories. The AI ensures each digest feels cohesive rather than just a collection of snippets.

5. Digest Assembly & Delivery

The finalized content is formatted into your chosen template (email, PDF, etc.) and distributed to subscribers on schedule. The system tracks engagement to continuously improve curation.
