What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of revenue visibility for sales teams. Traditional forecasting requires manual spreadsheet updates that quickly become outdated. Our AI-powered workflow continuously analyzes your HubSpot deal pipeline to:

  • Predict quarterly revenue based on deal stage and historical close rates
  • Identify at-risk deals needing intervention
  • Update forecasts in real-time as deals progress

Sales leaders receive Slack notifications about significant forecast changes, while finance teams get formatted Gmail reports. All data syncs to Google Sheets for historical analysis and board reporting.

How It Works

1. HubSpot Deal Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a deal is created or updated in HubSpot. It extracts all relevant deal attributes including amount, stage, close date, and custom fields.

2. AI Analysis

An AI model analyzes the deal against historical patterns to predict:

  • Probability of closing
  • Expected revenue amount
  • Potential close date variance
