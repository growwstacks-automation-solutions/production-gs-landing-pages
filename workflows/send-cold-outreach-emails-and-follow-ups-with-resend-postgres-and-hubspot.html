What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of scaling personalized cold outreach while maintaining human touch. Sales teams often struggle with manual follow-ups and CRM updates, leading to missed opportunities and inconsistent tracking.

The workflow handles the entire outreach sequence automatically: initial email sends, waiting 3 days for responses, triggering follow-ups for non-responders, and syncing all interactions to HubSpot. It reduces manual work by 80% while improving response tracking accuracy.

How It Works

Stage 1: Initial Email Send

The workflow pulls prospect data from your Postgres database and sends personalized emails via Resend's API. Each email includes tracking pixels to monitor opens.

Stage 2: Response Monitoring

For 72 hours, the system watches for replies. Any responses are automatically logged in HubSpot with the full email thread and marked for sales team follow-up.

Stage 3: Follow-up Sequence

Prospects who don't respond receive a tailored follow-up email referencing the original message. The system continues monitoring for another response window.

Pro tip: Use the workflow's A/B testing nodes to compare different follow-up timing (3 days vs 5 days) and message styles to optimize your response rates.
