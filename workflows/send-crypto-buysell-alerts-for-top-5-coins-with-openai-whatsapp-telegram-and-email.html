What This Workflow Does

Manual cryptocurrency monitoring is time-consuming, emotionally taxing, and prone to missed opportunities. This automation solves that by continuously tracking the top 5 cryptocurrencies, calculating objective trading signals based on price movements, and delivering instant alerts through your preferred communication channels.

The workflow fetches real-time price data from reliable sources like CoinGecko and Binance API, analyzes 24-hour percentage changes, and generates BUY, SELL, or HOLD signals. When significant price movements occur (typically above/below 2%), it triggers AI-enhanced analysis through OpenAI to provide context and reasoning, then sends human-readable alerts via WhatsApp, Telegram, and email simultaneously.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Price Monitoring

The workflow runs on a schedule you define (every 5 minutes, hourly, etc.) to fetch current prices and 24-hour change percentages for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other top cryptocurrencies from trusted APIs.

2. Signal Calculation & Filtering

Each coin's price change is evaluated against your configured thresholds. If the change exceeds your minimum percentage (default 2%), the workflow assigns a BUY or SELL signal based on direction, otherwise it's marked HOLD.

3. AI-Enhanced Analysis

For significant signals, OpenAI analyzes the market context, providing explanations like "Bitcoin showing strong upward momentum with increased volume" to help you understand why the signal was generated.

4. Multi-Channel Alert Delivery

The workflow sends formatted alerts through WhatsApp Business Cloud, Telegram Bot API, and email via SMTP/Gmail. Each message includes the coin, price, percentage change, signal, and AI insight.

5. Loop Processing with Rate Limit Management

Each coin is processed independently in a loop, with built-in wait/delay nodes to prevent API rate limiting and ensure reliable delivery across all notification channels.
