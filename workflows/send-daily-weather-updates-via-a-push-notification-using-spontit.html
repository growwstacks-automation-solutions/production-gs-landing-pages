What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the challenge of keeping distributed teams informed about weather conditions that impact their work. Instead of manually checking forecasts or relying on generic weather apps, this system delivers tailored weather alerts directly to employees' devices through Spontit push notifications.

The workflow automatically checks weather data at scheduled intervals, filters for conditions that meet your business criteria (like precipitation probability or temperature thresholds), and sends actionable alerts to designated recipients. This ensures field teams, event staff, or operations managers receive timely information without manual monitoring.

How It Works

1. Weather Data Collection

The workflow connects to a weather API to fetch current conditions and forecasts for your specified locations. It verifies data accuracy by cross-referencing multiple sources when available.

2. Condition Analysis

Custom logic evaluates whether the weather meets your alert thresholds. You can set parameters for temperature extremes, precipitation, wind speed, or other meteorological factors relevant to your operations.

3. Notification Trigger

When alert conditions are met, the workflow formats a concise message with essential details and sends it through Spontit's push notification system to your team's devices.

Who This Is For

This solution is ideal for businesses with weather-sensitive operations including construction companies, landscaping services, outdoor event planners, agricultural operations, and logistics providers. Any organization that needs to make daily decisions based on weather conditions will benefit from automated alerts.

Pro tip: Combine this with your scheduling system to automatically adjust shifts or reschedule outdoor work when severe weather is predicted.

What You'll Need
