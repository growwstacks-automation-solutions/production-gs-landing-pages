What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of feedback getting trapped in spreadsheets where team members might not see it. The workflow automatically monitors a Google Sheet for new feedback entries and posts them to a designated Mattermost channel, ensuring real-time visibility across your team.

By eliminating manual copying and pasting, this workflow saves teams hours per week while improving response times to customer or employee feedback. It maintains a complete record in your Google Sheet while making the information actionable through Mattermost's collaboration features.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Google Sheets

The workflow checks your specified Google Sheet at regular intervals for new rows or changes. You can configure it to watch specific columns that contain feedback content, author information, and priority levels.

2. Processing Feedback

Each new entry is formatted for Mattermost, with options to include metadata like submission dates, categories, or sentiment scores. The workflow can filter or prioritize certain types of feedback based on your criteria.

3. Posting to Mattermost

Formatted feedback is sent to your chosen Mattermost channel with proper formatting, @mentions for relevant team members, and optional threading to keep conversations organized.

Pro tip: Use different Mattermost channels for different feedback types (bugs, feature requests, praise) by adding a column in your sheet to specify destination channels.
