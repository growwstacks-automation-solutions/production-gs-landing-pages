What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical sales challenge of timely, consistent follow-up with leads and customers. Manual follow-up processes often fail because sales reps get busy, forget, or struggle to track all their outstanding communications. The workflow automatically sends personalized Gmail follow-ups to HubSpot contacts based on triggers you define.

By connecting your Gmail account with HubSpot CRM, you can create email sequences that activate when contacts reach specific stages in your pipeline, complete certain actions, or meet criteria you establish. All sent emails automatically log back to HubSpot contact records, maintaining complete communication history without manual data entry.

How It Works

1. HubSpot contact qualification

The workflow checks HubSpot for contacts meeting your specified criteria - this could be based on deal stage, lead score, last activity date, or custom properties. You configure the exact filters to match your sales process.

2. Email template selection

Based on the contact's properties and the trigger event, the workflow selects the appropriate Gmail template from your library. Templates can include personalized merge fields pulled from HubSpot contact data.

3. Automated sending

The system sends the email through your Gmail account while maintaining your normal sending patterns and limits. This helps emails appear more natural than bulk-sent messages.

4. Activity logging

Every sent email automatically creates an activity record in HubSpot linked to the contact, with details including the email content, send time, and any links clicked by the recipient.
