What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a common problem for marketing teams and social media managers: manually checking Instagram Insights every day to track performance. Instead of logging into multiple platforms and copying numbers into spreadsheets or chat messages, this workflow automatically collects your key Instagram metrics—follower count and total posts—and delivers them directly to your Mattermost team channel.

The workflow runs on a schedule (typically daily) to give your team consistent visibility into Instagram performance without any manual effort. It transforms raw social media data into actionable team updates, ensuring everyone from marketing to leadership stays informed about audience growth and content volume. This creates a culture of data transparency while saving 30+ minutes of manual reporting time each day.

Perfect for agencies managing multiple client accounts, e-commerce brands tracking social growth, or any team using Mattermost for internal communication, this automation turns sporadic manual checks into reliable, scheduled reporting that keeps your entire organization aligned on social media performance.

How It Works

The workflow follows a clean, logical sequence to gather, process, and deliver Instagram statistics to your team communication platform.

Step 1: Scheduled Trigger

A schedule trigger (like Cron) initiates the workflow at your preferred time—typically each morning before the team standup. This ensures fresh Instagram data is waiting in Mattermost when your team starts their day.

Step 2: Instagram Data Collection

The workflow connects to Instagram's API or a third-party service like Social Blade to fetch current metrics. It retrieves your total follower count, number of posts published, and can be extended to include engagement rates, reach, or other key performance indicators.

Step 3: Data Processing & Formatting

Raw numbers are transformed into readable insights. The workflow calculates changes from previous days, formats the data into clear messages, and prepares visual elements like emoji indicators for growth trends (📈 for increases, 📉 for decreases).

Step 4: Mattermost Delivery

The formatted Instagram report is sent to your designated Mattermost channel via webhook or bot integration. The message includes clear metrics, context about changes, and actionable insights—all formatted for easy team consumption.

What You'll Need

  1. An active Instagram Business or Creator account with access to Insights/API
  2. A Mattermost instance with webhook or bot integration permissions
  3. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with internet access
  4. Basic understanding of how to set up API credentials in n8n
  5. Optional: Social Blade or similar service account for enhanced Instagram metrics

Pro tip: If you're using Instagram's official API, ensure you have the necessary permissions for "instagram_basic" and "instagram_manage_insights" scopes. For team transparency, consider creating a dedicated "#social-stats" channel in Mattermost specifically for automated reports.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this Instagram-to-Mattermost automation in under 15 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Instagram credentials in the Instagram node—use either official API tokens or Social Blade integration
  3. Set up Mattermost webhook by creating an incoming webhook in your Mattermost instance and copying the URL
  4. Adjust the schedule to match your reporting frequency (daily, weekly, or custom intervals)
  5. Test the workflow with a manual trigger to ensure data flows correctly from Instagram to Mattermost
  6. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically according to your schedule
