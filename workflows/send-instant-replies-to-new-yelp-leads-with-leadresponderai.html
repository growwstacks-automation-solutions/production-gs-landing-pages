What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of responding quickly to Yelp leads - a key factor in converting inquiries into paying customers. When potential clients reach out through Yelp, they expect fast responses. Businesses that reply within minutes see significantly higher conversion rates than those who wait hours or days.

The workflow automatically detects new Yelp leads and sends personalized, immediate responses through LeadResponder.ai. This ensures your business never misses an opportunity to engage potential customers at the moment they're most interested in your services.

How It Works

1. New lead detection

The workflow constantly monitors your Yelp account for new lead notifications. When a potential customer submits an inquiry through Yelp, the system instantly captures all their contact details and message content.

2. Data processing

The lead information is formatted and prepared for response. The system can extract key details like the customer's name, phone number, email, and specific service requests.

3. Automated response

LeadResponder.ai generates and sends a personalized reply within seconds. The response can include your business information, next steps, and even schedule appointments automatically.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for service businesses that rely on Yelp for customer acquisition - particularly those in competitive markets where response time impacts conversion rates. Home services, healthcare providers, beauty professionals, and local contractors will benefit most.
