What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of medication non-adherence, which affects 50% of chronic disease patients according to WHO data. When a prescription is marked as sent in your pharmacy or EHR system, the workflow automatically initiates a sequence of personalized reminders through the patient's preferred communication channels.

The system handles complex scheduling needs - accounting for multiple daily doses, timezone differences, and varying medication schedules. It reduces administrative burden by eliminating manual reminder calls while providing audit trails of all communications for compliance purposes.

How It Works

1. Prescription Trigger

The workflow monitors your pharmacy management system or EHR for new prescriptions marked as "sent" or "fulfilled." This triggers the reminder sequence while pulling relevant patient and medication data.

2. Channel Selection

Based on patient preferences stored in your system, the workflow determines whether to send reminders via SMS, email, mobile app push notifications, or a combination.

3. Timed Reminders

The system schedules reminders according to dosage instructions, automatically adjusting for time zones. For example, a twice-daily medication would trigger alerts at optimal times based on the patient's local time.

Pro tip: Configure escalating alerts for critical medications - if the first reminder goes unacknowledged, follow-up messages increase in urgency.
