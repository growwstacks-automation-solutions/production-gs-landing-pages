What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually transferring invoice data between time tracking and documentation systems. When your team creates invoices in Clockify for client work, this workflow automatically sends that invoice data to your designated Notion database.

The solution eliminates duplicate data entry, reduces human error, and ensures your financial records stay synchronized across platforms. It's particularly valuable for agencies, freelancers, and professional service firms that need to maintain accurate billing records while keeping all stakeholders informed.

How It Works

1. Invoice Creation Trigger

The workflow activates when Clockify generates a new invoice, capturing all relevant details including client information, services rendered, hours worked, and amounts due.

2. Data Transformation

Key invoice fields are mapped and formatted to match your Notion database structure, ensuring clean, consistent data presentation.

3. Database Update

The processed invoice information creates a new entry in your specified Notion database, complete with all relevant metadata for easy filtering and reporting.

Pro tip: Use Notion's relation properties to connect invoices to client pages or projects for even better organization.
