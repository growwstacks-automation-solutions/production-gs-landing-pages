Send New WooCommerce Product Notifications to Slack, Gmail & Google Sheets

Automatically alert your team and log product details whenever a new item is added to your WooCommerce store.

What This Workflow Does

Manual product launch processes create communication gaps and data inconsistencies. When a new product goes live in WooCommerce, marketing might not know for hours, sales teams miss timely updates, and operations struggles to maintain accurate inventory logs across multiple spreadsheets.

This automation solves those problems by instantly detecting new WooCommerce products and distributing structured information to exactly where it's needed. It sends real-time Slack alerts to keep teams informed, dispatches professional email notifications to stakeholders, and automatically logs every product detail into Google Sheets for reporting and historical tracking—all without manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Product Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your WooCommerce store for new product creations. When triggered, it captures the complete product data including name, price, description, images, categories, and inventory status.

2. Data Standardization

A central formatting node processes the raw product information into a consistent structure. This ensures all downstream systems receive uniform data with proper formatting, extracted image URLs, and calculated values like tax-inclusive pricing.

3. Multi-Channel Distribution

The standardized product data flows simultaneously to three destinations: a designated Slack channel for team awareness, configured Gmail recipients for formal notification, and a Google Sheet that serves as a master product log for business intelligence.

Who This Is For

This automation is essential for e-commerce businesses using WooCommerce that want to eliminate manual coordination work. It's particularly valuable for marketing teams managing product launches, operations teams maintaining inventory records, sales teams needing immediate product knowledge, and business owners who want real-time visibility into new offerings without checking multiple systems.

What You'll Need

  1. A WooCommerce store with REST API access
  2. Slack workspace with permissions to create webhooks
  3. Google Sheets document with appropriate column structure
  4. Gmail account or SMTP service for email delivery
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file using the button above
  2. Import it into your n8n instance
  3. Configure the WooCommerce trigger with your store credentials
  4. Connect your Slack workspace and select the notification channel
  5. Set up Google Sheets and Gmail connections with OAuth
  6. Test with a sample product and activate the workflow

Pro tip: Create separate Google Sheets tabs or Slack channels for different product categories (like "New Apparel" vs "New Electronics") to help teams focus on relevant notifications.
