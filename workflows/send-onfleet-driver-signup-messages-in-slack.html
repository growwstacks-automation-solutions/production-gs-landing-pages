What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the visibility gap in driver onboarding by instantly notifying your team when new drivers sign up through Onfleet. Delivery businesses often lose valuable time between driver registration and activation because signups aren't immediately visible to dispatch managers. This workflow bridges that gap by sending real-time alerts to designated Slack channels.

The solution monitors Onfleet's driver registration events and transforms them into actionable Slack notifications. Each alert includes key driver details like name, contact information, and vehicle type - everything your team needs to begin the onboarding process without switching between systems.

How It Works

1. Onfleet triggers the workflow

The workflow activates whenever Onfleet registers a new driver in your system. It captures the complete driver profile including metadata you've configured in Onfleet's signup forms.

2. Data transformation

The workflow extracts relevant driver information and formats it for Slack presentation. This includes parsing timestamps to local time, structuring vehicle details, and applying your preferred notification format.

3. Slack notification delivery

Formatted messages are sent to your specified Slack channel with appropriate tagging (@mentions) of responsible team members. The notification includes quick-reference details and optional action buttons.

Pro tip: Add interactive buttons to your Slack notifications for instant driver approval or scheduling onboarding calls directly from the message.
