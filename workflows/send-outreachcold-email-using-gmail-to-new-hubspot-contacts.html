What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually sending cold outreach emails to new contacts in your HubSpot CRM. When new leads enter your system, they often require immediate follow-up to maximize conversion opportunities. However, manually tracking new contacts and composing personalized emails is time-consuming and prone to human error.

The workflow automatically identifies new HubSpot contacts that haven't been contacted yet, then sends them a personalized cold email through your Gmail account. It ensures no lead falls through the cracks while maintaining a consistent outreach cadence that can be customized to your sales process.

How It Works

1. New contact detection

The workflow regularly checks your HubSpot account for new contacts that haven't received any outreach yet. It filters out existing contacts and only processes fresh leads.

2. Contact information retrieval

For each new contact, the system pulls relevant details from HubSpot including name, email address, company, and any custom fields you want to reference in your email template.

3. Email personalization

Using the contact data, the workflow dynamically populates your email template with personalized fields. This creates a tailored message for each recipient while maintaining your brand voice.

4. Email sending via Gmail

The personalized email is sent through your Gmail account, appearing in the recipient's inbox as a regular email from your business. The workflow can be configured to send immediately or at optimal times.

5. Activity logging

After sending, the workflow records the outreach activity back in HubSpot, creating a complete history of your communication with each contact.
