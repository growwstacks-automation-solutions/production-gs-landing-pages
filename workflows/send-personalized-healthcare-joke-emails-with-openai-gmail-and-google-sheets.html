What This Workflow Does

Manual patient communication is time-consuming and inconsistent for healthcare providers. This automation solves that by creating a fully automated email system that sends personalized, healthcare-themed messages to your patient list daily. It combines AI-generated content with smart scheduling to maintain regular patient engagement without any manual intervention.

The workflow reads patient contacts from Google Sheets, uses OpenAI to personalize each email with the recipient's name and relevant content, sends emails through Gmail with proper rate limiting to avoid spam filters, and tracks which patients have been contacted—all while running automatically on a daily schedule. This transforms patient communication from a chore into a consistent relationship-building tool.

How It Works

Step 1: Daily Trigger & Contact Retrieval

Each day at your specified time (default 1 PM), the workflow activates and retrieves the next batch of patient contacts from your Google Sheets document. It intelligently selects only patients who haven't been emailed recently, preventing duplicate communications.

Step 2: AI-Personalized Content Generation

For each patient, the workflow sends the contact information to OpenAI's language model with instructions to create a personalized healthcare message. The AI incorporates the patient's name and generates appropriate, professional content that aligns with healthcare communication standards.

Step 3: Smart Email Delivery

The personalized emails are sent through your Gmail account with built-in rate limiting. The system adds random delays between emails (2-5 minutes) to mimic human sending patterns and avoid triggering spam filters or exceeding Gmail's sending limits.

Step 4: Tracking & Completion

After each email is successfully sent, the workflow updates your Google Sheets document with a timestamp in the "Emailed" column. This creates a complete audit trail of all communications and ensures the same patient won't receive duplicate emails in subsequent runs.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  2. A Gmail account with OAuth2 access configured for sending emails
  3. Google Sheets document with patient contacts (columns: First Name, Email, Emailed)
  4. OpenAI API key for generating personalized email content
  5. Basic understanding of how to import and activate n8n workflows

Pro tip: Start with a small test group of 5-10 patients to verify deliverability and content quality before rolling out to your entire patient list. This lets you fine-tune the AI instructions and ensure the tone matches your practice's voice.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template file into your n8n instance
  2. Configure credentials for Gmail (OAuth2), Google Sheets (OAuth2), and OpenAI (API key)
  3. Update the Google Sheet ID in the "Healthcare Contact List" node with your actual spreadsheet
  4. Customize the AI prompt in the "AI Email Generator" node with your practice details and preferred tone
  5. Test with sample data using the workflow's test mode to verify everything works correctly
  6. Activate the workflow and set your preferred schedule (daily recommended)
