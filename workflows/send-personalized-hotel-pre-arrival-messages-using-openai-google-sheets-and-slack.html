What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms generic hotel communications into personalized guest experiences. It automatically sends customized pre-arrival messages by combining guest data from Google Sheets with AI-generated personalization from OpenAI, then coordinates staff preparation through Slack.

The system eliminates manual message drafting while ensuring each guest receives timely, relevant information tailored to their stay purpose, preferences, and special occasions. Front desk staff save 2-3 hours daily previously spent on individual communications, allowing them to focus on higher-value guest interactions.

How It Works

1. Guest data collection

The workflow monitors your Google Sheets reservation database for new bookings or updates. It extracts key details like guest names, stay dates, room preferences, special requests, and loyalty status.

2. AI message generation

OpenAI analyzes the guest data to craft warm, personalized messages. The AI references past stays, special occasions, local events during their visit, and tailored recommendations based on room type and amenities.

3. Multi-channel delivery

Messages deploy via the guest's preferred channel (email, SMS, or WhatsApp) with consistent branding. Simultaneously, relevant staff receive Slack notifications about special requests or preparation needs.

4. Response handling

The system logs guest replies and questions back to Google Sheets, creating a communication history that staff can reference during check-in.
