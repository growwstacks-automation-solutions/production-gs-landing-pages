Automatically send contextual pre-meeting notifications using OpenAI, Google Calendar and Slack integration

What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the common problem of unprepared meetings by automatically generating and sending contextual information about attendees before meetings occur. It eliminates the manual work of researching participants and compiling background information, ensuring you're always prepared.

The system checks your upcoming calendar events, analyzes attendee information using AI, and delivers concise, actionable pre-meeting briefings directly to your Slack. This saves significant time while improving meeting outcomes through better preparation.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs hourly to check your Google Calendar for upcoming meetings within the next hour.

2. Attendee Analysis

For each meeting found, the system searches for recent communications and activity with each attendee to gather context.

3. AI Processing

OpenAI analyzes the gathered information to generate concise, relevant summaries about each participant and the meeting context.

4. Notification Delivery

The compiled briefing is formatted and sent to your Slack channel, providing all necessary context before the meeting begins.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompts to focus on information most relevant to your specific meeting types and business needs.
