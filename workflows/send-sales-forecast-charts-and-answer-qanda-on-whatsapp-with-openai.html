What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of making sales forecasts actionable for distributed teams. Traditional forecasting often creates static reports that sit in inboxes, requiring follow-up meetings to interpret. This workflow transforms forecasts into interactive conversations by delivering visual charts via WhatsApp with AI-powered Q&A capabilities.

The system pulls historical sales data, applies predictive algorithms, generates easy-to-understand visualizations, and enables natural language questioning about the forecasts. Sales managers can ask "Why is Q3 projected lower?" or "Which products are trending up?" and receive instant AI-generated explanations based on the underlying data patterns.

Example of AI-generated sales forecast delivered via WhatsApp

How It Works

1. Data Ingestion

The workflow connects to your Google Sheets or CRM to pull historical sales data, including product categories, regions, and time periods. It cleans and structures the data for analysis.

2. Forecasting Engine

Multiple statistical algorithms analyze trends, seasonality, and growth patterns. The system weights different models based on past accuracy to produce combined forecasts with confidence intervals.

3. Visualization

Key forecast metrics are transformed into easy-to-digest charts highlighting trends, variances from targets, and opportunity areas. Visuals are optimized for mobile viewing.

4. WhatsApp Delivery

Forecast packages are sent to predefined WhatsApp groups or individuals via the WhatsApp Business API. Messages include the visual summary and prompt for questions.

5. AI Q&A

OpenAI processes incoming WhatsApp questions about the forecasts, analyzes the relevant data patterns, and generates natural language responses with supporting evidence.
