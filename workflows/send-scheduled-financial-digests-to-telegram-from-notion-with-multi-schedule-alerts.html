What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of financial visibility for teams managing budgets in Notion. Instead of manually compiling reports or requiring stakeholders to log into Notion, it automatically formats key financial data from your databases and delivers it directly to Telegram at scheduled intervals.

The workflow supports multiple timing configurations - you can set daily cash flow updates, weekly revenue breakdowns, and monthly expense reports all from the same system. It transforms raw Notion database entries into clean, mobile-friendly financial digests with proper formatting, emoji indicators, and conditional highlighting based on your thresholds.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger Activation

The workflow activates at your predetermined intervals using n8n's scheduler. You can configure multiple schedules within the same workflow for different report types.

2. Notion Data Extraction

Connects to your specified Notion databases and retrieves current financial data based on your filters. This can include transaction records, budget vs actual comparisons, or revenue projections.

3. Data Transformation

Raw numbers are processed into readable formats - converting decimals to percentages, adding trend arrows, calculating month-to-date totals, and applying your custom formatting rules.

4. Telegram Message Composition

The system builds a structured message using Telegram's markdown support, creating clear sections for different metrics with appropriate emoji indicators for positive/negative trends.

5. Multi-Channel Delivery

Final reports are sent to designated Telegram channels or private chats. Different stakeholder groups can receive tailored versions of the same base data.

Pro tip: Create a "Financial Alerts Config" database in Notion to control scheduling parameters and message templates without modifying the automation.
