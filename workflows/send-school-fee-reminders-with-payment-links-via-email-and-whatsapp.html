What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow solves the common challenge schools face with timely fee collection. Manual fee reminders are time-consuming, often forgotten, and lack convenient payment options. Our solution automatically sends personalized fee reminders via both email and WhatsApp, including secure payment links that allow parents to pay immediately.

The system integrates with your student database to identify overdue payments, generates personalized messages with payment links, and sends them through parents' preferred communication channels. This reduces administrative workload while improving collection rates through convenient digital payments.

How It Works

1. Payment Status Check

The workflow first checks your student database or spreadsheet for upcoming or overdue payments. It identifies which parents need to receive reminders based on payment due dates and status.

2. Message Personalization

For each student with an upcoming or overdue payment, the system generates a personalized message including the student's name, amount due, due date, and a secure payment link. Messages are formatted appropriately for each communication channel.

3. Multi-Channel Delivery

Reminders are sent simultaneously via both email and WhatsApp, increasing the likelihood parents will see and act on them. The system uses your existing email service provider and WhatsApp Business API for delivery.

4. Payment Tracking

The workflow updates your records when payments are received through the provided links, preventing duplicate reminders and giving you real-time collection status.
