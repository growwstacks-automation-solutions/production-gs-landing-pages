What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of customers not receiving or seeing their order shipping notifications. Traditional email notifications often get lost in crowded inboxes or marked as spam, leaving customers uncertain about their order status.

The workflow automatically sends WhatsApp messages to customers as soon as their Shopify order is fulfilled, including all tracking information and estimated delivery dates. It intelligently formats phone numbers for international customers and ensures messages are delivered reliably through WhatsApp's high-engagement platform.

How It Works

1. Order fulfillment trigger

The workflow monitors your Shopify store for new fulfilled orders. When an order changes to "fulfilled" status, the automation is triggered and collects all relevant order and shipping information.

2. Phone number validation

The system checks the customer's phone number format and automatically corrects it to the proper international standard required for WhatsApp messaging. This ensures messages will deliver successfully regardless of how the customer entered their number.

3. Tracking information compilation

The workflow gathers all available shipping details including carrier name, tracking number, and estimated delivery date. It then generates a clickable tracking link for customer convenience.

4. WhatsApp message delivery

A personalized message is sent via WhatsApp Business API containing the tracking information and friendly order update. The message template can be customized to match your brand voice.
