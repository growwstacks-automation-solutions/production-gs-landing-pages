What This Workflow Does

Customer retention is the lifeblood of subscription businesses, yet manual renewal tracking often falls through the cracks. This automation solves that by creating a systematic approach to subscription renewals. It monitors your customer database for upcoming expirations, initiates timely reminders, and ensures your team follows up on every opportunity.

The workflow integrates your subscription data (from Stripe, Chargebee, or your CRM) with ClickUp for task management and your email system for customer communication. It eliminates spreadsheet tracking and manual email sending while providing full visibility into renewal pipeline.

How It Works

1. Subscription Date Monitoring

The system regularly checks your customer database for subscriptions approaching their renewal date. You can configure the look-ahead period (e.g., 30 days) based on your business needs.

2. ClickUp Task Creation

For each upcoming renewal, the workflow creates a dedicated task in your specified ClickUp list. The task includes customer details, subscription value, and any relevant notes from your CRM to help your team prepare.

3. Email Sequence Trigger

Personalized email reminders are sent to customers at strategic intervals before their subscription expires. The template includes merge fields for customer-specific details and can be adapted for your brand voice.

4. Renewal Status Tracking

The system updates ClickUp tasks based on customer actions—marking tasks complete when renewals happen or flagging them for follow-up when reminders go unanswered.
