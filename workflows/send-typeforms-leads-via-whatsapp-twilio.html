How It Works

1. Typeform Submission Trigger

The workflow starts when someone submits your Typeform. n8n monitors your Typeform account for new responses in real-time, capturing all submitted data including custom fields, timestamps, and respondent information.

2. Data Processing & Personalization

The workflow extracts key information from the submission—name, email, phone number, and any custom answers. It structures this data and prepares personalized message content, ensuring the WhatsApp message feels specifically crafted for that individual.

3. WhatsApp Message via Twilio

Using Twilio's WhatsApp Business API, the workflow sends a templated message to the lead's phone number. The message can include dynamic content from their submission, creating immediate relevance. Twilio handles delivery confirmation and compliance with WhatsApp's business messaging policies.

4. Success Logging & Error Handling

The workflow logs successful message deliveries and captures any errors (like invalid numbers or rate limits). Failed attempts can trigger alternative actions, such as sending an email fallback or creating a task in your project management system for manual follow-up.

What You'll Need

  1. A Typeform account with an active form collecting lead information
  2. A Twilio account with WhatsApp Business API access enabled
  3. WhatsApp Business approval for your message templates (required by Twilio)
  4. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation
  5. Phone numbers collected with country codes in your Typeform

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your Typeform account in the first node, selecting the specific form you want to monitor.
  3. Configure your Twilio credentials in the WhatsApp node, using your Account SID, Auth Token, and WhatsApp-enabled phone number.
  4. Customize the message template to match your brand voice and include relevant dynamic fields from the Typeform submission.
  5. Test the workflow by submitting your Typeform and verifying the WhatsApp message arrives correctly.
  6. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically for all future submissions.

Pro tip: Before going live, test with your team's phone numbers first. Ensure your WhatsApp message template is approved by Twilio and includes clear value—not just "thanks for submitting." Ask a question or offer next steps to encourage immediate replies.
