What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manually copying form submissions from Webflow to spreadsheets. Every time a visitor submits a form on your Webflow site, this workflow automatically captures all submitted data and adds it as a new row in your specified Google Sheet.

Businesses using this automation typically save 5-10 hours per week on data entry while eliminating human errors in transcription. The system creates a centralized, searchable database of all form submissions that can be easily analyzed, shared with team members, or connected to other business tools.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow monitors your Webflow forms for new submissions. When someone completes a form, Webflow sends the data to the workflow via webhook.

2. Data Processing

The workflow extracts and formats the form data, handling special cases like multiple-choice answers, file uploads, and conditional fields.

3. Google Sheets Update

The processed data gets appended as a new row in your designated Google Sheet, with each form field mapped to its corresponding column.

Pro tip: Add a timestamp column to track when submissions were received. This helps with response time monitoring and data analysis.
