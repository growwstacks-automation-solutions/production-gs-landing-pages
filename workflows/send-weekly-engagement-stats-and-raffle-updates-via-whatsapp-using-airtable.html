What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually compiling and sending weekly engagement statistics and raffle updates to participants. Businesses running loyalty programs, contests, or community engagement initiatives often waste hours each week creating and sending individual updates.

The workflow automatically pulls user data from Airtable, calculates relevant engagement metrics, and sends personalized WhatsApp messages at your scheduled time. It maintains excitement for ongoing programs while freeing your team from repetitive communication tasks.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates automatically at your specified day and time each week (e.g., every Friday at 5 PM). This ensures consistent, predictable communication with participants.

2. Fetch User Data

The system retrieves all relevant user records from your Airtable base, including contact information, engagement history, and current raffle entries. It filters for active participants who have opted into communications.

3. Calculate Engagement Metrics

For each user, the workflow calculates their weekly participation stats, points earned, leaderboard position (if applicable), and current raffle entries. This data gets compiled into personalized message content.

4. Generate Personalized Messages

Using template placeholders, the system creates unique WhatsApp messages for each participant. Messages include their name, specific achievements, and any raffle-related updates.

5. Send via WhatsApp

The workflow delivers messages through your connected WhatsApp Business account. Delivery status gets logged back to Airtable for tracking and follow-up.

Pro tip: Add conditional logic to vary message content based on engagement levels - for example, special encouragement for less active users or exclusive offers for top participants.

Compliance requires obtaining explicit opt-in consent before sending automated messages and providing clear opt-out instructions in every message. Store consent records in Airtable and honor unsubscribe requests immediately. Avoid sending sensitive personal data via WhatsApp and ensure your automation follows WhatsApp Business API policies.

Best practices include maintaining an up-to-date suppression list of users who have opted out and implementing double opt-in for new participants. Regularly audit your contact lists to ensure you're only messaging users who have actively consented to receive communications.

  • Document consent with timestamps and method
  • Include one-click unsubscribe in every message
  • Regularly review WhatsApp's acceptable use policies
