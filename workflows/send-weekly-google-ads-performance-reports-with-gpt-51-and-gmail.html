What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming task of manually analyzing Google Ads performance and creating weekly reports. Digital marketers and agencies often spend hours each week compiling data, identifying trends, and writing performance summaries - valuable time that could be spent optimizing campaigns instead.

The workflow automatically pulls your Google Ads metrics every Monday, processes them through GPT-5.1 for intelligent analysis, and delivers a polished performance report directly to your inbox. It highlights key metrics, compares week-over-week performance, identifies emerging trends, and provides AI-generated recommendations for campaign improvements.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Google Ads Data Fetch

The workflow triggers every Monday morning to pull your latest campaign performance data from Google Ads API. It collects metrics like impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions, cost per conversion, and ROAS across all your active campaigns.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

The raw data gets processed through GPT-5.1 which identifies significant trends, compares performance to previous weeks, and detects anomalies or opportunities. The AI contextualizes the numbers into meaningful insights.

3. Report Generation

GPT-5.1 structures the findings into a professional report format with executive summary, key takeaways, visual data highlights (using emojis and formatting for readability), and specific action recommendations.

4. Email Delivery

The completed report gets sent via Gmail to your specified recipients with a clear subject line and properly formatted HTML content. You can customize the email template to match your branding.
