What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of meeting coordination overhead. Professionals waste significant time each week manually checking calendars, compiling meeting lists, and sending preview emails to teams. Our workflow eliminates this repetitive task by automatically generating and sending a formatted weekly meeting schedule directly from Google Calendar to Gmail.

The system pulls all upcoming meetings for the specified time period (typically the next 7 days), formats them into an easy-to-scan email template, and sends it to designated recipients at your preferred time. This ensures everyone has visibility into the week's commitments without manual calendar checks or last-minute scheduling surprises.

How It Works

1. Calendar Event Retrieval

The workflow connects to your Google Calendar account and retrieves all events within your specified date range. It can filter by calendar, event type, or other criteria you define.

2. Data Formatting

Retrieved events are processed into a clean, standardized format. The system extracts key details like meeting titles, times, participants, and locations while applying your preferred formatting rules.

3. Email Composition

The workflow generates a professional email using your template design. It can include your logo, branding colors, and standard sections like "Important Meetings" or "Action Required" based on event labels.

4. Scheduled Delivery

The completed email is sent via your Gmail account at your chosen time (typically Friday afternoon or Monday morning). Recipients receive a consistent, well-formatted preview of their upcoming week.

Pro tip: Set up separate workflows for different departments or teams with customized templates that highlight the information most relevant to each group.
