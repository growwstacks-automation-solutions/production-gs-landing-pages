What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of timely engagement with new leads by automatically sending personalized WhatsApp welcome messages whenever a new contact is created in HubSpot. Many businesses struggle to follow up quickly enough with new leads, missing critical engagement windows in the first hours after initial contact.

The workflow listens for HubSpot webhook events, processes contact information, and triggers pre-approved WhatsApp message templates through Beex's API. This ensures compliant, branded communication that maintains human connection while operating at scale. The solution eliminates manual message sending while preserving personalization capabilities.

Webhook configuration in HubSpot to trigger the workflow

How It Works

1. HubSpot contact creation triggers the workflow

The workflow starts when HubSpot sends a webhook notification about a new contact being added to your CRM. The webhook includes all standard contact properties like name, email, phone number, and custom fields.

2. Contact data is processed and standardized

The workflow extracts relevant contact information and formats it for WhatsApp messaging. This includes validating phone numbers, extracting first names for personalization, and checking for any opt-out preferences.

Data transformation steps to prepare contact information

3. Appropriate Beex template is selected

Based on contact properties like lead source or tags, the workflow selects the most relevant pre-approved WhatsApp template from your Beex account. Different templates can be configured for different customer segments.

Pro tip: Create separate templates for different lead sources (website form vs event registration) to make messages more contextually relevant.

4. Personalized message is sent via WhatsApp

The workflow sends the message through Beex's WhatsApp Business API, inserting personalized fields like the contact's name. Delivery status is logged back to HubSpot for tracking and follow-up.
