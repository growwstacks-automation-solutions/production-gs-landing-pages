What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the costly problem of missed appointments by automatically sending WhatsApp reminders to clients based on their Google Calendar events. It eliminates manual reminder processes that consume staff time and often lead to human errors or forgotten notifications.

The system checks Google Calendar for upcoming appointments, filters events based on your criteria (like event titles or calendars), then sends personalized WhatsApp messages through MoltFlow's business messaging platform. You maintain complete control over message timing, content, and recipient lists while saving hours of administrative work.

How It Works

1. Calendar Event Trigger

The workflow monitors your Google Calendar for new or updated events matching your appointment criteria. You can specify which calendars to monitor and set filters for event titles, descriptions, or custom fields.

2. Reminder Timing Calculation

Based on your configured lead time (e.g., 24 hours before appointment), the system calculates exactly when to send each reminder. This ensures clients receive notifications at the most effective time to reduce no-shows.

3. Message Personalization

The template pulls relevant details from the calendar event - client name, appointment time, service details, and location - to create personalized WhatsApp messages. You can customize the message format and include variables for dynamic content.

4. WhatsApp Delivery via MoltFlow

MoltFlow's API delivers the messages through WhatsApp Business, ensuring high deliverability and compliance with messaging regulations. The system handles retries for failed deliveries and can include interactive buttons for confirmations.

Pro tip: Add a rescheduling link in your WhatsApp messages to let clients easily change appointments without calling your office.
