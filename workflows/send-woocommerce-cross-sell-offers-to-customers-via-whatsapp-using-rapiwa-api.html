What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manual cross-selling in WooCommerce stores by automatically identifying each customer's purchase patterns and sending targeted WhatsApp offers via Rapiwa API. It transforms your customer data into revenue-generating opportunities without requiring manual intervention from your team.

The workflow analyzes order history to determine which complementary products to recommend, then sends personalized WhatsApp messages with discount codes or limited-time offers. This approach achieves 3-5x higher conversion rates than email marketing while maintaining the personal touch customers expect from WhatsApp communication.

How It Works

1. Customer Purchase Analysis

The workflow queries your WooCommerce database to identify each customer's most frequently purchased items and common product combinations. It uses this data to build personalized product affinity profiles.

2. Cross-Sell Selection

Based on the customer's purchase history, the system selects the most relevant complementary products from your catalog. It applies business rules you define (minimum margin, inventory levels, etc.) to ensure profitable recommendations.

3. WhatsApp Message Generation

The workflow constructs personalized WhatsApp messages using Rapiwa's API templates. Each message includes the customer's name, recommended products based on their actual purchases, and a time-sensitive offer to encourage immediate action.

4. Compliance Check & Delivery

Before sending, the system verifies the customer has opted in to WhatsApp messages and checks against do-not-contact lists. Messages are queued for delivery during approved business hours to maintain compliance with WhatsApp's policies.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for WooCommerce store owners selling products with natural cross-sell opportunities: fashion retailers (matching accessories), electronics (compatible accessories), beauty products (routine bundles), or any business with complementary product lines.

It's particularly valuable for stores with 500+ monthly orders where manual cross-selling becomes impractical, or businesses seeing low email open rates wanting to leverage WhatsApp's higher engagement.
