What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the tedious process of SEO keyword research and filtering for e-commerce brands and content teams. It solves the challenge of scaling content production while maintaining strategic focus by systematically analyzing and prioritizing keywords based on your business objectives.

The workflow connects to popular SEO tools to gather keyword data, applies your custom filtering criteria (search volume, difficulty, commercial intent, etc.), and delivers prioritized keyword lists directly to your content team or CMS. This eliminates hours of manual spreadsheet work while ensuring consistency in your keyword selection process.

How It Works

1. Keyword Data Collection

The workflow pulls raw keyword suggestions from connected SEO tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner. It gathers comprehensive metrics including search volume, competition level, and CPC data for each term.

2. Automated Scoring

Each keyword receives a custom score based on your configured weighting for different metrics. The system applies your business rules to identify high-potential terms while filtering out irrelevant or overly competitive keywords.

3. Priority Categorization

Keywords are grouped into priority tiers (High/Medium/Low) based on their scores. The workflow can also categorize them by product category, buyer journey stage, or content type for better organization.

4. Output Delivery

The final filtered keyword lists are delivered to your designated systems - whether that's your content calendar, CMS, or team collaboration tools like Slack or Notion. Some workflows include automatic brief generation for writers.
