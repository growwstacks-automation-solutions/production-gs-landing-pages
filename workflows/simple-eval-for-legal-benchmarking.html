What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow template automates the evaluation process for legal benchmarking by extracting test cases from Google Sheets and analyzing the results. It solves the manual, time-consuming process of comparing legal performance metrics against standards or competitor data.

The workflow demonstrates a practical approach to information extraction evaluation, specifically designed for legal professionals who need to assess document processing accuracy, case outcome analysis, or other performance metrics. By automating this process, law firms can save dozens of hours per month on manual data comparison and reporting.

How It Works

1. Data Extraction from Google Sheets

The workflow begins by connecting to your Google Sheets document containing test cases. It automatically extracts the relevant data fields needed for benchmarking comparisons.

2. Test Case Processing

Each test case is processed through the workflow, applying your evaluation criteria. The system checks for accuracy, completeness, and other quality metrics you've defined.

3. Result Analysis

The workflow compares processed results against your benchmark standards, calculating performance scores and identifying areas needing improvement.

4. Reporting Output

Final evaluation results are formatted into a clear output that can be sent to team members, stored in databases, or integrated with other legal tech tools.
