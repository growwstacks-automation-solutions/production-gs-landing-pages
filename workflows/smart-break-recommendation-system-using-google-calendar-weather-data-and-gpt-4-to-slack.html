Smart Break Recommendation System

Automatically suggest optimal break locations based on your schedule, weather and preferences

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of wasted time between meetings for busy professionals. Instead of manually researching where to spend your break time, this system automatically analyzes your Google Calendar, checks local weather conditions, and uses AI to recommend the best nearby spots.

The workflow identifies gaps in your schedule, calculates travel time to your next appointment, and suggests weather-appropriate locations - whether it's a cozy café for rainy days or an outdoor terrace when the weather is nice. All recommendations are delivered directly to your Slack channel.

How It Works

1. Schedule Monitoring

The workflow checks your Google Calendar every 30 minutes to identify upcoming meetings and available break windows.

2. Travel Time Calculation

Using Google Maps API, it calculates how long it will take to reach your next meeting location from your current position.

3. Weather Check

The system pulls current weather data for your destination using OpenWeatherMap API to determine suitable activity types.

4. AI-Powered Recommendations

GPT-4 analyzes all the data and generates personalized recommendations for the top 3 spots to visit during your break.

5. Slack Notification

A friendly message with all recommendations is sent to your designated Slack channel for easy access.

What You'll Need

  1. Google Cloud account with Maps and Places APIs enabled
  2. OpenWeatherMap API key (free tier available)
  3. Notion account with a preferences database
  4. Slack workspace with bot permissions
  5. OpenAI API key
