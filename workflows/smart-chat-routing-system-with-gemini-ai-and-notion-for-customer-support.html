What This Workflow Does

Customer support teams waste countless hours manually triaging incoming queries, often routing tickets to the wrong departments. This AI-powered system eliminates guesswork by automatically analyzing each message's intent, urgency, and technical complexity using Gemini AI, then assigning it to the most qualified agent based on Notion's real-time team availability and expertise matrix.

The workflow reduces average first response time from hours to minutes while ensuring customers always reach someone who can actually solve their problem. For growing businesses, it eliminates the need for dedicated triage staff while maintaining (or improving) support quality as team size scales.

How It Works

1. Message Analysis with Gemini AI

When a new customer message arrives, Gemini AI evaluates its content to determine: query type (technical, billing, general), sentiment (frustrated, neutral, positive), complexity level, and any referenced products/features. This creates a detailed classification profile in under 2 seconds.

2. Agent Matching in Notion

The system checks your Notion database for agents with matching expertise who are currently available. It considers skill tags, current workload, language proficiency, and historical resolution rates for similar tickets.

3. Smart Assignment

The workflow assigns the ticket to the optimal agent while automatically creating a Notion page with all classification data and suggested knowledge base articles. If no ideal agent is available, it escalates based on your predefined rules (seniority, backup assignments, etc.).
