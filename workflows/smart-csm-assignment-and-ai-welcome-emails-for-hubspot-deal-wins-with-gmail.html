What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the critical handoff challenge between sales and customer success teams. When a deal is marked as won in HubSpot, it automatically assigns the right Customer Success Manager (CSM) based on your business rules (geography, product line, workload balancing, etc.) and sends a personalized AI-generated welcome email through Gmail.

The automation ensures no new customer falls through the cracks during the transition from sales to onboarding. It eliminates manual assignment processes and generic welcome emails, replacing them with intelligent routing and tailored first impressions that set the stage for successful customer relationships.

How It Works

1. HubSpot deal trigger

The workflow starts when a deal changes to "Closed Won" status in HubSpot. It captures all relevant deal and contact information including company details, contract value, and any custom properties you've set up.

2. Smart CSM assignment

Using your predefined rules (which you can customize), the workflow evaluates factors like customer location, product purchased, CSM workload, and specialization to assign the most appropriate Customer Success Manager.

3. AI-powered email personalization

The system generates a warm, personalized welcome email using AI that incorporates specific details about the customer's purchase, their business goals, and next steps. This goes beyond simple mail merge by creating truly contextual messages.

4. Gmail delivery and logging

The welcome email is sent through Gmail with the assigned CSM copied, and all details are logged back to HubSpot for complete visibility into the onboarding process.
