What This Workflow Does

Late payments are one of the biggest cash flow killers for small businesses and freelancers. Manually tracking overdue invoices and sending follow-up emails consumes valuable time and often gets neglected during busy periods. This creates a vicious cycle where forgotten invoices lead to revenue leakage and strained client relationships.

This intelligent invoice collection system solves this problem completely. It automatically monitors your invoice database in Google Sheets, calculates which invoices are overdue at specific intervals (7, 14, 21, 28 days), analyzes recent email conversations to avoid inappropriate follow-ups, and sends professionally crafted reminders using AI-powered personalization. The system maintains business relationships while ensuring payments are collected, potentially recovering $10,000 to $15,000 annually in previously forgotten revenue.

How It Works

1. Invoice Monitoring & Filtering

The workflow connects to your Google Sheets invoice database and runs on a daily schedule. It calculates days overdue for each invoice and filters them based on your escalation timeline—only processing invoices that have reached specific follow-up milestones.

2. Email History Intelligence

Before sending any reminder, the system checks your Gmail for recent conversations with each client. Using AI analysis, it determines if there's been relevant communication about the invoice within the last 72 hours, preventing awkward or redundant follow-ups during active payment discussions.

3. AI-Powered Message Generation

OpenAI analyzes the context and generates personalized email content. The system selects from four escalating template levels—from gentle reminders to firm collection notices—adapting tone based on how overdue the invoice has become while maintaining professionalism.

4. Automated Delivery & Tracking

Personalized emails are sent through Gmail with proper formatting, clear subject lines, and payment instructions. The system can create drafts for review or send automatically, and all actions are logged for complete transparency.
