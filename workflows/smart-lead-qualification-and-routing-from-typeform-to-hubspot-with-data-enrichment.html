What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of converting Typeform submissions into sales-ready HubSpot leads without manual processing. It eliminates the 60-90 minutes per day sales teams typically waste manually qualifying and entering leads by automatically enriching form data with firmographic intelligence, scoring leads based on your ideal customer profile, and routing them to the right sales rep or nurture sequence.

The system captures webhook submissions from Typeform, then layers on data from enrichment APIs to build complete prospect profiles. It evaluates each lead against your scoring criteria (like budget, authority, need, and timeline), then creates fully populated HubSpot contacts with all enrichment data while triggering appropriate follow-up sequences based on lead quality.

Visualization of the automated lead qualification pipeline

How It Works

1. Typeform Submission Capture

The workflow triggers instantly when a visitor submits your Typeform, capturing all response data through the webhook. It preserves hidden fields used for UTM tracking and page context, ensuring full attribution visibility.

2. Data Enrichment Layer

Using the submitter's email and company name, the system queries enrichment APIs to append professional details like job title hierarchy, company funding status, technographics, and social profiles. This transforms basic form data into comprehensive buying signals.

Pro tip: Configure fallback logic to handle incomplete form submissions - like using LinkedIn API when company email domains don't return enrichment data.

3. Dynamic Lead Scoring

Custom JavaScript nodes evaluate each lead against your ideal customer profile matrix. Points are awarded for positive signals (like "decision-maker" title or "enterprise" company size) and deducted for disqualifiers (like student emails or competitor domains).

4. Intelligent HubSpot Routing

Based on the calculated score and territory rules, the workflow creates HubSpot contacts with all enriched data, assigns owners, adds to appropriate lists, and triggers email sequences - all within 8-12 seconds of form submission.
