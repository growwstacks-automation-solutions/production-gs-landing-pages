What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of maintaining an active, engaging social media presence across LinkedIn and X (Twitter) without spending hours each week on content creation. It automates the entire process from idea generation to scheduled publishing.

The system generates AI-powered content tailored to your brand voice and industry, schedules posts at optimal times, and publishes them directly to your social accounts. It handles everything from hashtag research to platform-specific formatting.

How It Works

1. Content Generation

The workflow starts by analyzing trending topics in your industry and generating relevant content ideas using AI. It creates multiple post variations with appropriate hashtags and mentions.

2. Content Review

Generated content goes through quality checks to ensure it matches your brand voice. You can configure approval workflows where needed or let the AI handle everything automatically.

3. Platform Optimization

The system formats content differently for LinkedIn (longer-form, professional tone) and X (concise, conversational). It adds relevant hashtags and optimizes post length for each platform.

4. Scheduling & Publishing

Posts are scheduled based on your audience's most active times. The workflow handles all API connections to publish content directly to your LinkedIn and X accounts.

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 posts per week per platform, then gradually increase frequency as you refine your AI parameters.
