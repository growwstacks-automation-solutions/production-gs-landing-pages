Spotify Sync Liked Songs to Playlist

Automatically maintain a custom playlist that mirrors your liked songs collection

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common Spotify user frustration of having two separate places for favorite songs - your private 'Liked Songs' collection and any public playlists you create. Manually maintaining both is time-consuming and often results in outdated playlists.

The workflow automatically synchronizes your liked songs to a designated playlist, adding new favorites and removing songs you've unliked. This ensures your public playlist always reflects your current music taste without manual updates.

How It Works

1. Authentication with Spotify

The workflow begins by authenticating with Spotify's API using OAuth credentials you provide during setup. This grants secure access to your music library.

2. Fetching Liked Songs

It retrieves your complete list of liked songs, handling Spotify's pagination automatically to ensure all tracks are captured regardless of library size.

3. Comparing with Playlist

The workflow then fetches the current contents of your target playlist and compares it against your liked songs collection to identify additions and removals.

4. Synchronizing Changes

Newly liked songs are added to the playlist, while unliked songs are removed. The workflow maintains the original order of your liked songs in the playlist.
