What This Workflow Does

Construction site reporting is notoriously messy. Field teams submit handwritten notes, voice messages, or fragmented digital updates that lack consistency. This creates administrative bottlenecks, compliance risks, and lost visibility into project progress.

This AI-powered workflow solves that by automating the entire Daily Construction Report (DCR) lifecycle. It captures raw site inputs through a chat interface, validates them with AI guardrails against jailbreak attempts, standardizes the content using OpenAI's GPT models, distributes structured reports via Gmail, and logs Work Performance Data directly into Google Sheets for analysis and compliance.

The result is governance-ready documentation that aligns with PMBOK® execution standards, turning chaotic field communication into structured, actionable data.

How It Works

1. Field Input Capture via Chat

Site personnel submit daily reports through a simple chat interface (like Telegram, Slack, or a web chat). They can send text, voice notes, or photos—the system accepts natural language input just like talking to a colleague.

2. AI Guardrail Validation

Before processing, the workflow runs the input through AI guardrails that detect suspicious patterns, incomplete data, or potential manipulation attempts. This ensures report integrity and prevents garbage data from entering your system.

3. OpenAI Standardization

The raw notes are sent to OpenAI's GPT models, which extract and structure key information: project identification, field team details, activities performed, working hours, weather conditions, materials used, equipment status, safety incidents, and physical progress percentages.

4. Google Sheets Logging

The structured data is automatically appended to a Google Sheet, creating a searchable historical database of Work Performance Data. Each entry is timestamped and includes all standardized fields for easy reporting and analysis.

5. Gmail Distribution & Confirmation

The finalized report is emailed to project stakeholders (managers, clients, safety officers) while the field user receives immediate confirmation that their report was successfully processed and logged.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in your n8n environment:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n workspace using the import function.
  2. Configure credentials for OpenAI, Google Sheets, and Gmail in n8n's credentials management.
  3. Set up your chat trigger by creating a bot on your preferred platform (Telegram, Slack) and connecting its webhook to the workflow's webhook node.
  4. Customize the Google Sheet ID and tab name in the "Append to Sheet" node to match your destination spreadsheet.
  5. Adjust the AI guardrail prompts in the OpenAI nodes to match your specific validation requirements and report standards.
  6. Test the workflow by submitting a sample field report through your chat interface and verifying the structured output in Google Sheets and email.
  7. Deploy and activate the workflow, then train your field teams on the new reporting process.
