What This Workflow Does

Daily standup meetings are essential for team alignment but often become time-consuming rituals that interrupt deep work. This automated standup bot solves that by running the entire process asynchronously through Mattermost.

The workflow sends personalized messages to each team member every morning with three standard standup questions: what they accomplished yesterday, what they plan to do today, and any blockers preventing progress. Once responses are collected, it formats them into a clean summary and posts it to a designated team channel.

This eliminates scheduling conflicts, reduces meeting fatigue, and creates a searchable record of team progress—all while saving 10-15 minutes per person daily that would otherwise be spent in a synchronous meeting.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger & Configuration

A Cron node triggers the workflow daily at your specified time (e.g., 9 AM). The workflow first reads configuration settings from a separate "Standup Bot - Read Config" workflow, which contains team member lists, channel IDs, and question templates.

2. Personalized Message Distribution

Using the Mattermost node, the workflow sends direct messages to each team member with the three standup questions. The messages are personalized with the team member's name and include interactive buttons or formatted text for easy responding.

3. Response Collection & Processing

As team members reply throughout the day, a Webhook node captures their responses. The workflow parses each answer, timestamps it, and stores it temporarily while waiting for all responses or until a cutoff time.

4. Summary Compilation & Posting

Once responses are collected (or at a designated summary time), a Function node formats all answers into a clean, readable summary. The workflow then posts this summary to your team's Mattermost channel, highlighting accomplishments, plans, and blockers for easy visibility.

Pro tip: Extend this workflow by adding a "Reminder" branch that sends follow-up messages to team members who haven't responded by a certain time, ensuring complete participation without manual chasing.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this standup bot in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Mattermost credentials in the Mattermost nodes with your bot token
  3. Update the "Read Config" nodes to point to your configuration workflow ID
  4. Set the "Override Config" node to reference your override workflow
  5. Adjust the Cron trigger to your team's preferred standup time
  6. Test with a small group first, then roll out to the entire team
  7. Monitor for a week and tweak questions or timing based on team feedback

Implementation note: Start with a pilot group of 3-5 team members for the first week. This lets you iron out timing issues and response formats before scaling to the entire organization.
