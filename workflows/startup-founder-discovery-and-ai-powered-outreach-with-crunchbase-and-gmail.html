What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the time-consuming challenge of identifying and contacting startup founders at scale. It automatically discovers relevant founders on CrunchBase based on your criteria (industry, funding stage, location etc.), then generates personalized outreach emails using AI and sends them through Gmail.

The workflow eliminates manual research while maintaining personalization - a critical factor for successful founder outreach. It can process hundreds of potential leads per day while ensuring each message feels tailored to the recipient's specific startup and background.

How It Works

1. CrunchBase Founder Search

The workflow begins by querying CrunchBase's API with your predefined search parameters. You can filter founders by industry, company size, funding stage, location, and other criteria to target exactly the right audience for your outreach.

2. Data Enrichment

Each founder profile is enriched with additional context from their CrunchBase listing - company details, recent funding rounds, notable achievements, and other publicly available information that can inform your outreach.

3. AI-Powered Message Generation

Using the enriched data, the workflow generates personalized email content with AI. The system crafts messages that reference specific details about the founder's company and background, dramatically increasing response rates compared to generic templates.

4. Gmail Delivery

The final step sends the AI-generated emails through your Gmail account with appropriate timing controls to avoid hitting sending limits. The workflow can be scheduled to run at optimal times for maximum open rates.

Pro tip: For best results, customize the AI prompt template to reflect your unique value proposition and tone of voice before scaling your outreach.
