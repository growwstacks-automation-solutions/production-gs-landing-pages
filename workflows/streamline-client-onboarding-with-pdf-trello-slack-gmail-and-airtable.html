What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms chaotic client onboarding into a streamlined process. When a new client signs up, the workflow automatically captures their information, validates email addresses, generates personalized PDF documents, creates Trello cards for task tracking, adds records to Airtable, and notifies your team via Slack—all without manual intervention.

Traditional onboarding often involves copying data between 5+ systems, sending repetitive emails, and losing track of progress. This workflow eliminates those pain points by connecting your tools into one cohesive system that handles the entire process from first contact to first deliverable.

How It Works

1. Client Submission

The workflow triggers when a client submits information through your form, email, or CRM. It immediately validates the email address and checks for duplicate entries.

2. Document Generation

The system generates personalized PDF contracts, proposals, or welcome packets using the client's information. These are automatically attached to emails or made available through a client portal.

3. Trello Task Creation

For each new client, the workflow creates a Trello card with all relevant details and pre-populated checklist items for your onboarding process.

4. Airtable Record Sync

Client information is automatically added to your Airtable base with proper categorization and relationship fields to other records.

5. Team Notifications

Slack notifications alert your team immediately when new clients come in or complete key onboarding steps, enabling real-time collaboration.

Pro tip: Add a manual approval step before contract generation for high-value clients to ensure personalized attention where it matters most.

Who This Is For

This workflow benefits service businesses that onboard multiple clients per month, including agencies, consultants, law firms, and healthcare providers. It's especially valuable for teams using Trello for task management and Airtable as their client database.

The automation scales perfectly whether you onboard 5 clients or 500 per month. It eliminates the most time-consuming parts of onboarding while ensuring no client falls through the cracks.
