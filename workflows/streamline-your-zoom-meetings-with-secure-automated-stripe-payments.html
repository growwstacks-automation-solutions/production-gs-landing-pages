What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually collecting payments for Zoom meetings. It creates a seamless experience where clients can book and pay for your time in one step, while you avoid the hassle of chasing payments or dealing with scheduling conflicts.

The system handles everything from payment processing to meeting scheduling automatically. When a client completes payment through your Stripe checkout page, the workflow instantly creates a Zoom meeting, sends confirmation emails to both parties, and adds the event to calendars - all without any manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Payment Processing

Clients complete payment through your Stripe checkout page. The workflow verifies the payment status and captures all necessary details including email, name, and meeting preferences.

2. Meeting Creation

Upon successful payment, the system automatically generates a Zoom meeting with your preferred settings. It sets the correct date/time based on the client's selection and applies any meeting-specific configurations.

3. Confirmation System

The workflow sends a branded confirmation email to the client with their unique meeting link and details. Simultaneously, it adds the appointment to your calendar and sends you an internal notification with the client's information.

Pro tip: Customize the confirmation email template to include your branding, cancellation policy, and any pre-meeting requirements to reduce last-minute questions.
