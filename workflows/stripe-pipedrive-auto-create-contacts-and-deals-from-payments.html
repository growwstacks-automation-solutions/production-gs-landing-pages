What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your payment processing (Stripe) and sales CRM (Pipedrive). Whenever a customer makes a successful payment in Stripe, the workflow automatically:

  • Creates a new contact in Pipedrive (or finds existing one)
  • Generates a deal record with the payment details
  • Maps relevant payment data to custom CRM fields
  • Ensures your sales team has immediate visibility

Eliminating manual data entry between systems saves hours per week while reducing errors and ensuring no paying customer falls through the cracks in your sales process.

How It Works

1. Stripe Payment Trigger

The workflow activates when Stripe detects a successful payment event through its webhook system. This could be for one-time purchases or subscription signups.

2. Payment Data Validation

The system confirms the payment details with Stripe's API to ensure data accuracy and prevent duplicate processing of the same event.

3. Contact Lookup

Pipedrive is searched for an existing contact matching the customer's email address. If found, the workflow proceeds to deal creation.

4. New Contact Creation

For new customers, the workflow creates a complete Pipedrive contact record with name, email, and other available Stripe data.

5. Deal Generation

A new deal is created linked to the contact, populated with payment amount, product/service details, and custom field data from Stripe.

Pro tip: Customize the deal stage based on payment type - set subscription payments to "Recurring" while one-time purchases go to "Closed Won".
