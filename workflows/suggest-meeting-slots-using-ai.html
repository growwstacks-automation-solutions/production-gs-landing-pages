Suggest meeting slots using AI

Automatically detect meeting requests in emails and propose optimal times based on your calendar availability

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the tedious process of scheduling meetings by analyzing incoming emails and intelligently suggesting available time slots. It solves the common problem of wasted time spent manually coordinating schedules through endless email threads.

The system detects when someone requests a meeting in an email, checks your calendar availability, and responds with suggested time options - all without human intervention. This eliminates the back-and-forth typically required to find mutually available times.

How It Works

1. Email Monitoring

The workflow continuously monitors your Gmail inbox for new messages containing meeting request phrases like "schedule a call" or "set up a meeting".

2. AI-Powered Analysis

An AI model evaluates each email's content to determine if it contains a genuine meeting request, filtering out false positives with over 90% accuracy.

3. Calendar Integration

The system connects to your Google Calendar to check your real-time availability while respecting existing appointments and your working hours preferences.

4. Smart Time Suggestions

Based on your calendar openings and configured preferences, the workflow generates 2-3 optimal time slot suggestions tailored to the request.

5. Automated Response

The system replies to the sender with your available times, often within minutes of receiving their request, dramatically speeding up the scheduling process.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for consultants, recruiters, sales professionals, and service providers who handle multiple meeting requests daily. Teams that coordinate external meetings across time zones will particularly benefit from the reduced scheduling friction.

Executives and busy professionals who value their time will appreciate how this workflow eliminates calendar management overhead while maintaining a professional, responsive image.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. Gmail account with API access
  3. Google Calendar connected to your email
  4. Access to an AI service (like OpenAI) for text analysis

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance

  5. Set your working hours and meeting duration defaults
  6. Test with sample meeting request emails
