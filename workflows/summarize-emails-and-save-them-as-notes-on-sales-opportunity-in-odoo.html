What This Workflow Does

Sales teams waste countless hours manually reading through email threads, extracting key details, and copying them into their CRM. This creates data entry bottlenecks, risks missing critical information, and distracts from actual selling.

This n8n workflow solves that problem by automatically monitoring your sales Gmail inbox, using AI to intelligently summarize incoming emails, and creating structured notes attached to the corresponding opportunity in Odoo. When a new email arrives with a specific label (like "Sales Lead" or "Opportunity"), the workflow triggers, sends the email content to OpenAI for summarization, and creates or updates an Odoo sales opportunity with the AI-generated summary as an internal note.

The result is a fully automated pipeline that ensures every customer interaction is captured in your CRM without manual effort. Your sales team gets instant, actionable summaries of email conversations directly where they work—Odoo—while maintaining complete context of the customer relationship.

How It Works

The workflow follows a logical sequence that transforms raw email conversations into structured CRM data.

Step 1: Gmail Trigger

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for new emails with a specific label you define (like "To Process" or "Sales Email"). When a matching email arrives, n8n captures the full email content including subject, body, sender, and timestamp.

Step 2: Content Preparation

The email content is cleaned and formatted for optimal AI processing. This step removes signatures, disclaimers, and irrelevant formatting while preserving the core conversation. You can configure this to handle different email structures and languages.

Step 3: AI Summarization with OpenAI

The prepared email content is sent to OpenAI's API with specific instructions to extract key sales information: pricing discussions, next steps, objections, decision-makers, timelines, and requirements. The AI returns a concise, actionable summary in a consistent format.

Step 4: Odoo CRM Integration

The workflow searches Odoo for an existing opportunity matching the email subject or sender. If found, it adds the AI summary as a new note. If not, it creates a new opportunity with the email subject as the title and the summary as the initial note. The opportunity is automatically assigned to the appropriate salesperson.

Step 5: Notification & Logging

Optionally, the workflow can send a Slack or Teams notification to the sales team about the new note, or log the action to a spreadsheet for audit purposes. This ensures visibility and accountability.

Pro tip: Configure the Gmail trigger to watch for specific labels like "Sales/Open" or "Opportunity/Active" to ensure only relevant emails are processed, reducing noise and API costs.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get this automation running in your environment:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure Gmail node with your OAuth credentials and set the label filter to match your sales email labeling system
  3. Set up OpenAI credentials in n8n with your API key and adjust the prompt in the AI node to match your summarization preferences
  4. Connect Odoo node with your instance URL, database, username, and API key
  5. Test with a sample email by sending a test email with the configured label to your Gmail
  6. Activate the workflow and monitor the first few executions to ensure summaries are accurate and Odoo updates are working
  7. Refine as needed based on your team's feedback—adjust AI prompts, opportunity matching logic, or add additional processing steps

Pro tip: Start with a small test label and a few sample emails before applying this to your entire sales inbox. This lets you fine-tune the AI prompts and Odoo mapping without affecting live data.
