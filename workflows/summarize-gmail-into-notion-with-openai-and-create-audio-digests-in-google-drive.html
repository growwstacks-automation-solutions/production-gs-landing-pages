What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the common problem of email overload by intelligently processing incoming Gmail messages through OpenAI's AI models to create concise, actionable summaries. The workflow captures key information from lengthy emails and organizes it neatly in Notion, while optionally generating audio versions stored in Google Drive for listening during commutes or downtime.

Professionals receiving dozens or hundreds of emails daily can reclaim hours each week by letting AI extract the essence of each message. The system preserves important details like deadlines, action items, and contact information while filtering out redundant content and signatures. Audio digests provide an additional consumption option for busy professionals who prefer listening over reading.

How It Works

Step 1: Email Trigger

The workflow activates whenever a new email arrives in your specified Gmail label or inbox. It captures the full email content including subject, sender, body text, and attachments.

Step 2: AI Processing

The email content is sent to OpenAI's API where advanced natural language processing analyzes the text. The AI identifies key points, extracts action items, and generates a concise summary in your preferred format (bullet points, executive summary, etc.).

Step 3: Notion Integration

The structured summary is automatically added to your Notion database with relevant metadata like sender, date, and original subject line. You can customize the database structure to match your existing Notion organization system.

Step 4: Audio Conversion (Optional)

For selected emails, the system converts the text summary into natural-sounding speech using text-to-speech technology. The audio file is saved to your Google Drive in MP3 format, ready for playback on any device.
