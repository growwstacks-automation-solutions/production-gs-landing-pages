What This Workflow Does

Sales professionals waste valuable time manually researching companies before calls, often missing key insights buried in social media feeds. This n8n workflow automates the process by gathering and summarizing a company's recent social media activity into a concise briefing document.

The automation pulls data from LinkedIn, Twitter, and other platforms to identify trending topics, recent announcements, and engagement patterns. This gives sales teams instant context about what matters most to the prospect right now, helping them personalize conversations and demonstrate relevant expertise.

Automated compilation of social media data into a structured briefing document

How It Works

1. Trigger setup

The workflow activates when a new meeting is scheduled in your calendar. It extracts the company name and meeting participants from the calendar event.

2. Social media data collection

The automation searches multiple platforms for the company's official accounts and recent posts. It gathers metrics like engagement rates, post frequency, and trending topics.

Multi-platform data collection process

3. Analysis and summarization

Using AI processing, the workflow identifies key themes, sentiment trends, and notable content. It filters out irrelevant posts and focuses on business-relevant information.

4. Briefing document generation

The system compiles findings into a structured document with executive summary, key talking points, and recommended discussion angles tailored to your product/service.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for B2B sales teams, account managers, and business development professionals who need to quickly understand prospects before calls. Agencies managing multiple client accounts will find it particularly valuable for maintaining current knowledge across their portfolio.

Pro tip: Combine this with CRM integration to automatically attach briefing documents to contact records for future reference.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)
  2. API access to LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and other relevant platforms

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance
