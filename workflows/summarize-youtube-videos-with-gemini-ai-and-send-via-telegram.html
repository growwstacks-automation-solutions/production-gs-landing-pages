What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of information overload from lengthy YouTube videos by delivering concise, AI-generated summaries directly to your Telegram account. It's perfect for busy professionals, researchers, and content creators who need to stay informed but don't have time to watch hours of video content.

The workflow automatically extracts complete transcripts from YouTube videos, processes them through Google's Gemini AI to generate human-like summaries, and delivers the distilled insights via Telegram. You'll receive the key points from any video in a fraction of the time it would take to watch the full content.

How It Works

1. YouTube video input

The workflow accepts YouTube URLs through various triggers - manual entry, RSS feeds, or shared links from other apps. It validates the URL and prepares the video for processing.

2. Transcript extraction

Using Decodo's API, the system scrapes the complete video transcript along with metadata like title, duration, and upload date. This creates the raw material for the AI to analyze.

3. AI summarization

Google's Gemini AI processes the transcript to identify key themes, main points, and actionable insights. The AI is configured to maintain context while reducing content to 10-20% of its original length.

4. Telegram delivery

The final summary is formatted for readability and sent to specified Telegram channels or private chats. The message includes the video title, summary, and original link for reference.
