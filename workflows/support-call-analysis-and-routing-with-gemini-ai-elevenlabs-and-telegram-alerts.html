What This Workflow Does

Customer support calls contain valuable insights about product issues, customer sentiment, and urgent requests—but manually reviewing hours of audio recordings is inefficient and error-prone. This automation solves that problem by automatically analyzing call transcripts, extracting key information, and routing critical issues to the right teams.

The workflow uses Gemini AI to generate concise summaries of each call, ElevenLabs to convert urgent alerts into natural-sounding voice messages, and Telegram to notify support teams in real-time. This creates a seamless system where important customer feedback never gets lost in lengthy call logs.

How It Works

1. Call Recording Processing

The workflow starts when a new call recording is uploaded to your storage system. It automatically converts the audio to text using speech-to-text technology, preparing it for AI analysis.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

Gemini AI scans the transcript to identify sentiment, urgency level, product mentions, and customer emotions. It generates a structured summary highlighting key points and action items.

3. Priority Routing

Based on predefined rules (like mentions of "urgent" or "broken"), the system categorizes calls and routes them appropriately. Critical issues get immediate attention while general feedback is logged for later review.

4. Alert Delivery

For high-priority items, ElevenLabs converts the AI summary into a natural-sounding voice message delivered via Telegram to your support team's mobile devices, ensuring rapid response.
