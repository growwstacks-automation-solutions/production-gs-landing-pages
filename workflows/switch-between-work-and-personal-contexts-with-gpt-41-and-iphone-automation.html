What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the modern productivity challenge of mental context switching between professional work and personal activities. Many knowledge workers struggle with lingering work thoughts during personal time and vice versa, reducing effectiveness in both domains.

The workflow uses iPhone automation triggers combined with GPT-4.1's contextual understanding to create clean transitions between these mental states. When activated, it automatically adjusts your device settings, notifications, and even provides AI-generated context summaries to help your brain make the switch more effectively.

How It Works

1. Triggering the transition

The workflow activates based on iPhone automation triggers like location (arriving at/leaving work), time schedules, or manual activation through Shortcuts. This creates the initial signal to begin the context switch process.

2. AI-assisted mental transition

GPT-4.1 analyzes your current context and generates a brief summary of unfinished tasks or important thoughts, then provides closure suggestions. This helps your brain "bookmark" the current context before switching.

3. Environmental adjustments

The system automatically adjusts your iPhone settings - changing focus modes, notification preferences, and even app layouts to match your new context. This creates environmental cues that reinforce the mental transition.

4. Post-switch optimization

After the transition, the workflow provides a quick-start guide for your new context - whether that's showing your first work task or surfacing personal reminders, helping you dive straight into productive mode.
