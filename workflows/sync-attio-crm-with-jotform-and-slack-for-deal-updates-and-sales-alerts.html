What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of manual data entry between web forms and CRM systems. When potential customers submit information through your Jotform, this workflow instantly captures their details, creates or updates records in Attio CRM, and sends targeted Slack notifications to your sales team.

The solution eliminates the need for copying data between systems, reduces human error in lead processing, and ensures your team gets immediate alerts about new opportunities. It's particularly valuable for sales teams that need to respond quickly to inbound leads while maintaining accurate CRM records.

How It Works

1. Jotform submission triggers the workflow

When a visitor submits your Jotform (whether it's a contact form, demo request, or lead capture form), the workflow immediately activates. It captures all form fields including custom fields you've set up.

2. Data validation and processing

The workflow checks for required fields, formats phone numbers and addresses consistently, and ensures email addresses are valid before proceeding. This step prevents bad data from entering your CRM.

3. Attio CRM record creation/update

The system checks if the contact already exists in Attio CRM. If not, it creates a new record with all relevant details. For existing contacts, it updates their information and logs the new interaction.

4. Slack notification delivery

Customized Slack messages are sent to your designated sales channel, including key details about the lead and a direct link to their Attio CRM record. The notification can include priority flags based on form responses.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for:
