Sync Customer Email Subscriptions Between Shopify and Mautic

Automatically keep customer email preferences synchronized between your ecommerce store and marketing platform

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of maintaining consistent email subscription preferences between your Shopify store and Mautic marketing automation platform. Without synchronization, customers might receive emails they've opted out of or miss communications they want - damaging both customer experience and compliance.

The workflow establishes a two-way sync that instantly updates subscription statuses in both systems whenever changes occur. It handles all common scenarios: new subscriptions, unsubscribes, preference changes, and bulk updates. The automation runs in the background with zero manual intervention.

How It Works

1. Shopify Subscription Changes

The workflow monitors Shopify for any customer email subscription updates through webhooks. When a change is detected, it captures the customer details and new subscription status.

2. Mautic Contact Update

The system locates the corresponding contact in Mautic using the customer's email address and updates their subscription preferences to match the Shopify settings.

3. Reverse Sync from Mautic

Similarly, when a contact updates their preferences in Mautic, the workflow identifies the matching Shopify customer and aligns their subscription status.

4. Validation & Error Handling

Each sync operation includes validation checks to ensure data integrity and comprehensive error handling to manage API limits or temporary service interruptions.
