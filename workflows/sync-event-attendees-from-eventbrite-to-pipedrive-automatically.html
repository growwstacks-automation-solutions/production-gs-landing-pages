What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring event attendee data from Eventbrite to your CRM. Every time someone registers for your event, their contact information, ticket details, and event participation data automatically flows into Pipedrive as a new lead or contact.

The workflow eliminates spreadsheet exports, CSV uploads, and the risk of human error in data entry. It ensures your sales team has immediate access to warm leads while event excitement is still fresh, significantly improving conversion rates compared to delayed manual processes.

How It Works

1. New attendee trigger

The workflow monitors your Eventbrite account for new attendee registrations. Each time someone signs up for your event, the automation is triggered with their complete registration data.

2. Data transformation

Attendee information is formatted to match Pipedrive's expected structure. The workflow handles field mapping, data cleaning, and can apply conditional logic to categorize attendees based on ticket type or custom questions.

3. Duplicate prevention

Before creating a new lead, the system checks Pipedrive for existing contacts with matching email addresses. This prevents duplicate entries and ensures all event participation data is attached to the correct contact record.

4. Pipedrive lead creation

The attendee is created as a new lead in Pipedrive with all relevant event details. The workflow can assign the lead to specific team members, add tags, set follow-up tasks, or trigger email sequences based on your sales process.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Pipedrive to track which events each contact attended. This creates valuable segmentation data for future marketing campaigns.
