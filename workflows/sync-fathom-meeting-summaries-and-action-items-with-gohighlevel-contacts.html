Sync Fathom meeting summaries and action items with GoHighLevel contacts

Automatically transfer AI-generated meeting insights into CRM contact records for better follow-ups

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of valuable meeting insights getting lost between video calls and CRM systems. Fathom's AI-powered meeting summaries contain critical client information, action items, and discussion points that often remain siloed in meeting notes. Meanwhile, sales teams waste hours manually transferring these details into GoHighLevel contact records.

The workflow automatically syncs Fathom's meeting data - including summaries, action items, timestamps, and recordings - directly into corresponding GoHighLevel contact records. Action items become CRM tasks, summaries are added to contact history, and all data is preserved in a structured format for easy reference.

How It Works

1. Fathom detects completed meetings

The workflow monitors Fathom for new meeting summaries. When a meeting ends and Fathom processes the recording, it triggers the automation.

2. Meeting data is extracted and formatted

The workflow parses Fathom's structured output to identify key elements: summary, action items, participants, timestamps, and recording links.

3. GoHighLevel matches the contact

Using participant email addresses, the system finds the corresponding contact in GoHighLevel. If no match exists, it can optionally create a new contact.

4. Data syncs to CRM fields

The meeting summary is added to the contact's notes, action items become tasks assigned to relevant team members, and recording links are stored in custom fields.
