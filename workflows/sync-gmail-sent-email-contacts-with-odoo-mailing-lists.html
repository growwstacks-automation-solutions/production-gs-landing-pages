What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of valuable customer contacts being trapped in email conversations rather than your CRM system. Every time sales reps and customer support agents communicate with prospects and clients via email, those contacts often never make it into the company's official mailing lists.

The workflow automatically extracts recipient email addresses from your Gmail sent folder, validates them, and adds qualified contacts to your Odoo CRM mailing lists. This ensures every customer interaction contributes to your growing marketing database without manual data entry.

How It Works

Step 1: Scan Sent Emails

The workflow checks your Gmail sent folder at scheduled intervals (daily or weekly) to find new emails containing potential contacts.

Step 2: Extract Recipient Addresses

It identifies all recipient email addresses (To, Cc, Bcc fields) while excluding known internal domains and noreply addresses.

Step 3: Validate and Clean

Each address is verified for proper format and checked against your existing Odooogle's SMTP servers to confirm deliverability before being added to your system.

Step 4: Sync to Odoo

Validated contacts are then pushed to specified mailing lists in Odoo, with duplicate checking to prevent cluttering your CRM.

Pro tip: Create separate Odoo mailing lists for different departments (e.g. "Sales Contacts", "Support Leads") for better segmentation.
