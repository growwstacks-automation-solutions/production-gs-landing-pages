What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of manually extracting, analyzing, and reporting on CRM data. Sales teams often waste hours each week compiling reports when they should be selling. The workflow automatically pulls fresh opportunity data from HighLevel, organizes it in Google Sheets, analyzes trends with GPT-4o, and emails actionable insights to your team.

By combining CRM data with AI analysis, you get more than just raw numbers. The system identifies at-risk deals, highlights promising opportunities, and suggests follow-up actions based on historical patterns. This transforms your CRM from a simple contact database into an intelligent sales assistant that helps your team close more deals.

How It Works

1. HighLevel CRM Data Extraction

The workflow starts by connecting to your HighLevel account via API. It retrieves all active opportunities with their current status, value, and relevant details. The system only pulls new or updated records since the last run, ensuring efficient data processing.

2. Google Sheets Synchronization

All CRM data gets organized in a structured Google Sheet with separate tabs for raw data and summary views. The workflow maintains data consistency by updating existing records and adding new ones, creating a complete historical record of all opportunities.

3. GPT-4o Analysis

The AI examines the CRM data to identify patterns, risks, and opportunities. It evaluates deal progression, compares against historical close rates, and highlights anomalies. The analysis includes specific recommendations tailored to your sales process.

4. Email Report Generation

A formatted email compiles the key insights into an easy-to-digest morning briefing. The report includes summary metrics, highlighted opportunities, and prioritized action items. The workflow sends this directly to your inbox or distributes it to your entire sales team.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run at 7 AM local time so your team receives insights before their morning standup meeting.
