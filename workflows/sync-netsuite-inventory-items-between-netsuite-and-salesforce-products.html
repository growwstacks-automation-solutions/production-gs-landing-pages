Automatically export inventory records from NetSuite and sync them into Salesforce — creating or updating Products in real-time

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of keeping product inventory data synchronized between NetSuite (your ERP system) and Salesforce (your CRM). When inventory levels, product details, or pricing changes in NetSuite, this workflow automatically updates the corresponding records in Salesforce Products.

The solution eliminates manual data entry errors, ensures sales teams always have accurate product availability information, and maintains consistency across both systems. It handles both new product creation in Salesforce when items are added to NetSuite, as well as updates to existing product records.

How It Works

1. NetSuite Inventory Trigger

The workflow monitors your NetSuite inventory items for changes, either on a scheduled basis or triggered by specific events like stock level adjustments or new product additions.

2. Data Transformation

The system maps NetSuite inventory fields to their corresponding Salesforce Product fields, handling any necessary format conversions (like currency, units of measure, or categorization).

3. Salesforce Product Sync

For each inventory item, the workflow checks if a matching Product exists in Salesforce (typically by SKU or product code). If found, it updates the record; if not, it creates a new Product with all relevant details.

4. Error Handling & Logging

The system includes robust error handling to manage connection issues, validation errors, or mapping problems, with detailed logging for troubleshooting.
