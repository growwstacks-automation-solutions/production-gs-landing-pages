What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of losing potential buyers who visit your open houses but don't receive timely follow-up. Traditional paper sign-in sheets and manual data entry often result in missed opportunities, illegible contact information, and delayed response times that can cost you deals.

The workflow automatically captures every visitor's information from SignSnapHome's digital registration system, instantly creates a contact record in HubSpot, and triggers a personalized email sequence based on the specific property they visited. It eliminates administrative work while ensuring no lead falls through the cracks.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture Trigger

The workflow monitors SignSnapHome for new visitor registrations via Zapier's polling trigger. Each time a visitor signs in at your open house using the digital kiosk, their information is immediately processed.

2. CRM Contact Creation

The system creates a new contact in HubSpot with all collected details including name, email, phone, property interest, and custom fields like budget range or timeline. The contact is automatically tagged as "Open House Lead" and assigned to the appropriate agent.

3. Smart Segmentation

Based on the property visited and additional form responses, leads are categorized into different follow-up sequences. Hot leads who showed strong interest receive immediate personal contact while others enter nurturing workflows.

4. Automated Follow-Up

The system triggers a sequence of timed emails with property details, neighborhood information, and calls-to-action. The first email goes out within 5 minutes of registration while the experience is fresh in their mind.

Pro tip: Add an SMS confirmation immediately after sign-in to boost engagement - 98% of text messages are read within 3 minutes.
