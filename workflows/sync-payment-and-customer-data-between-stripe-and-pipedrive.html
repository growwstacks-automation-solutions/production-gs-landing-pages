What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of sales teams lacking visibility into customer payment activity. When payment data lives only in Stripe, sales reps waste time switching between systems or miss important financial signals that could impact customer relationships.

The workflow automatically combines Stripe payment records with corresponding customer data and adds them as notes to the appropriate organization in Pipedrive. This gives your sales team complete financial context right within their CRM, enabling more informed conversations and timely follow-ups.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Stripe payments

The workflow activates whenever a new payment event occurs in your Stripe account. This could be a successful charge, failed payment, refund, or subscription renewal.

2. Retrieve customer details

For each payment, the workflow pulls the associated customer record from Stripe, including contact information, billing details, and any custom fields you've configured.

3. Match with Pipedrive organization

Using email addresses or custom identifiers, the automation finds the corresponding organization in Pipedrive to ensure payment data attaches to the correct company record.

4. Format and add as note

The payment details and customer information are formatted into a clear note and added to the organization's activity timeline in Pipedrive, complete with timestamps and relevant metadata.

Pro tip: Customize the note template to include only the most relevant payment details for your sales team, keeping notes concise but actionable.
