What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for retailers operating both Shopify and WooCommerce stores: maintaining consistent pricing across platforms. Manual price updates are time-consuming and error-prone, especially with frequent promotions or inventory changes. The workflow automatically synchronizes product prices between your Shopify and WooCommerce stores while sending alerts when changes occur.

When a price changes in either system, the workflow detects the update, validates the new price against your business rules, then pushes the change to the other platform. Managers receive instant notifications about price changes for oversight. This eliminates pricing discrepancies that can confuse customers and damage your brand reputation.

How It Works

1. Price Change Detection

The workflow continuously monitors both Shopify and WooCommerce for product price changes. It can run on a schedule (hourly/daily) or trigger instantly when changes occur via webhooks.

2. Price Validation

Before syncing, the system checks new prices against your configured rules: minimum/maximum thresholds, acceptable change percentages, and margin requirements. Suspicious changes can trigger additional alerts.

3. Cross-Platform Update

Validated prices are pushed to the corresponding products on the other platform. The workflow handles SKU matching, variant pricing, and inventory status to ensure accurate updates.

4. Change Notification

Your team receives alerts via email, Slack, or SMS whenever prices sync between platforms. Notifications include before/after values and which team member initiated the change.
