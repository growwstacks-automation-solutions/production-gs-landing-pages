What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common problem of disconnected ecommerce and marketing systems. When customers purchase from your Shopify store, their information often gets stuck in Shopify without flowing to your CRM. This workflow bridges that gap by automatically creating HubSpot contacts for every new Shopify customer and updating existing ones.

By syncing these systems, you gain powerful capabilities like segmenting customers by purchase behavior, triggering personalized email sequences based on products bought, and maintaining accurate customer profiles that combine marketing interactions with order history. Businesses using this integration typically see 20-30% higher email engagement rates.

How It Works

1. New customer detection

The workflow monitors Shopify for new customer creation events, which occur when someone makes their first purchase or creates an account.

2. Data extraction

It pulls all available customer information including contact details, order history, and any custom fields you've set up in Shopify.

3. HubSpot contact creation

The system checks if the customer already exists in HubSpot using email as the unique identifier. If not, it creates a new contact with all relevant Shopify data mapped to corresponding HubSpot properties.

4. Data enrichment

The workflow can optionally enrich the contact with additional data like order value, product categories purchased, and customer lifetime value calculations.

Pro tip: Add a custom property in HubSpot to track "Customer Tier" based on purchase frequency or total spend, then use this for VIP marketing campaigns.
