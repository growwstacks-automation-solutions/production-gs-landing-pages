Sync Website Visitors from RB2B to Attio CRM via Slack

Automatically capture identified website visitors as sales-ready leads in your CRM with instant Slack notifications.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Every business website has anonymous visitors who could be potential customers. The challenge is identifying them and getting their information into your sales team's hands quickly. Manual processes mean missed opportunities and delayed follow-ups.

This automation solves that by connecting RB2B (which identifies website visitors) directly to your Attio CRM via Slack notifications. When a visitor is identified, their details—name, email, company, LinkedIn profile, and location—are automatically extracted from Slack, checked against existing contacts to prevent duplicates, and created as a complete record in Attio with an associated sales deal.

The result: your sales team receives instant, actionable leads in their CRM with zero manual data entry, allowing them to respond while the visitor's interest is highest.

How It Works

Step 1: Visitor Identification & Notification

RB2B identifies anonymous website visitors and sends structured notifications to a designated Slack channel with all available visitor details.

Step 2: Data Extraction & Processing

The workflow monitors the Slack channel, extracts the structured data from messages, and parses it into clean fields (name, email, company, etc.) for CRM ingestion.

Step 3: Duplicate Prevention

Before creating a new contact, the workflow searches Attio CRM by email address to check if the person already exists, preventing duplicate records and maintaining data hygiene.

Step 4: Contact & Deal Creation

For new visitors, a complete contact record is created in Attio with all available information. An associated sales deal is automatically generated, tagged as "RB2B Website Visitor" for proper lead source tracking.

Step 5: Existing Contact Management

If the contact already exists, their record is updated with new information, and deal creation is managed intelligently based on your sales process rules.

Pro tip: Configure RB2B to send notifications to a dedicated Slack channel used exclusively for this automation. This keeps the data clean and makes troubleshooting easier.

What You'll Need

  1. RB2B account with Slack integration enabled and configured
  2. Attio CRM account with API access (create an API key in settings)
  3. Slack workspace with appropriate bot permissions for the designated RB2B channel
  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  5. Basic understanding of your sales pipeline stages in Attio

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the Slack trigger to connect to your workspace and select the channel where RB2B sends notifications.
  3. Set up the Attio CRM node with your API credentials and test the connection.
  4. Customize the deal stages to match your sales pipeline in Attio (default is "RB2B Website Visitor").
  5. Test the workflow by having someone visit your website (or simulate a visitor) and verify the data flows correctly to Attio.
  6. Activate the workflow and let it run automatically—your sales team will now receive instant leads.

Pro tip: Start with a simple implementation, then iterate. You can later add lead scoring, automated email sequences, or additional data enrichment based on what works for your team.
