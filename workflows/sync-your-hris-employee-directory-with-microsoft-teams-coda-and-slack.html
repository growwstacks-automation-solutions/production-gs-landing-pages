What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of maintaining accurate employee information across multiple collaboration platforms. HR teams often waste countless hours manually updating directories in Microsoft Teams, Slack, and Coda whenever there are new hires, role changes, or departures.

The workflow automatically syncs your HRIS (Human Resource Information System) data with these platforms, ensuring everyone has access to current employee information. It eliminates duplicate data entry, reduces errors, and keeps your teams connected with up-to-date contact details.

How It Works

1. HRIS Data Extraction

The workflow connects to your HRIS system (like BambooHR, Workday, or ADP) through its API. It pulls employee data including names, titles, departments, contact information, and reporting structures on a scheduled basis.

2. Data Transformation

Raw HRIS data is cleaned and formatted for each destination platform. The workflow handles field mapping, data normalization, and any necessary transformations to match each system's requirements.

3. Multi-Platform Sync

The processed data is simultaneously pushed to Microsoft Teams (for the organization directory), Slack (user profiles), and Coda (employee database docs). The workflow handles updates, additions, and deletions across all platforms.
