Sync Zendesk tickets to Pipedrive contact owners

Automatically connect customer support tickets with sales contacts for better visibility between teams

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation bridges the gap between your customer support and sales teams by automatically syncing Zendesk tickets with the corresponding contact owners in Pipedrive. It solves the common problem where sales reps lack visibility into active support issues for their accounts, leading to awkward or poorly-timed outreach.

The workflow runs daily at 9 AM, collecting all Zendesk tickets and matching them with Pipedrive contacts based on email addresses. It updates each contact record with their latest support ticket status, priority level, and subject line - giving sales teams immediate context without needing to switch between platforms.

How It Works

1. Daily ticket collection

The workflow triggers automatically each morning to fetch all active and recently updated tickets from Zendesk. It filters tickets based on your configured criteria (such as status, priority, or tags) to focus on the most relevant cases.

2. Contact matching

Using the requester email address from each ticket, the workflow finds matching contacts in Pipedrive. It handles variations in email formats and can be configured to match additional identifiers if needed.

3. Data synchronization

The workflow updates custom fields in Pipedrive with the ticket details, maintaining a history of support interactions. Sales reps can see at a glance which of their contacts have open tickets and how urgent they are.

Pro tip: Add a custom view in Pipedrive to sort contacts by active ticket priority, helping sales reps prioritize their outreach effectively.
