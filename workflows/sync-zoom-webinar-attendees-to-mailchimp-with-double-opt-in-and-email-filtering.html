What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of manually transferring webinar attendees from Zoom to your Mailchimp email list while maintaining compliance with email marketing regulations. Many businesses waste hours copying and pasting attendee data or risk adding contacts without proper consent.

The workflow automatically captures new Zoom webinar registrants, filters out internal email addresses (like your own team members), and adds qualified leads to Mailchimp with a double opt-in process. This ensures only genuinely interested contacts who confirm their subscription enter your marketing funnel.

How It Works

1. Trigger on new Zoom webinar registration

The workflow activates whenever someone registers for your Zoom webinar, capturing their name, email, and registration details.

2. Filter out internal email addresses

Before processing, the system checks the registrant's email against your domain list to exclude team members or test accounts.

3. Add contact to Mailchimp with pending status

Qualified leads are added to your specified Mailchimp audience/list with a "pending" subscription status that requires confirmation.

4. Send double opt-in confirmation email

Mailchimp automatically sends its standard confirmation email, ensuring GDPR and CASL compliance for your email marketing.
