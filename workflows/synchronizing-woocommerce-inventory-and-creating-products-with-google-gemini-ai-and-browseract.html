What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves two critical ecommerce challenges: inventory synchronization and scalable product creation. It automatically syncs WooCommerce inventory levels across multiple sales channels while using Google Gemini AI to generate complete product listings from minimal input data. The integration with BrowserAct handles browser-based tasks like competitor price scraping or form submissions that traditional APIs can't accomplish.

For inventory management, the workflow reduces manual stock checks by automatically updating product quantities based on sales, returns, and supplier data. For product creation, it transforms basic product information into SEO-optimized listings complete with descriptions, tags, and variations - cutting product setup time from hours to minutes.

Automated inventory synchronization in action

How It Works

1. Inventory Data Collection

The workflow begins by aggregating inventory data from WooCommerce, supplier APIs, and other sales channels. It normalizes this data into a unified format, identifying discrepancies between systems.

2. AI Product Generation

For new products, Google Gemini analyzes product attributes and generates complete listings including SEO-optimized titles, detailed descriptions, and relevant tags. The AI can also create product variations based on color, size, or other attributes.

3. Browser Automation

BrowserAct handles tasks requiring browser interaction, such as scraping competitor prices, verifying product images, or submitting forms to marketplaces. This complements the API-based automation for complete coverage.

4. Validation & Publishing

The workflow includes approval steps where human reviewers can verify AI-generated content before publishing. Approved products are automatically added to WooCommerce with synchronized inventory levels.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to run inventory syncs during off-peak hours to avoid performance impacts on your live store.
