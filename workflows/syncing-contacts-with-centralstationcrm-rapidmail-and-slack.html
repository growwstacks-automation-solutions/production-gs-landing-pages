Syncing contacts with CentralStationCRM, Rapidmail, and Slack

Automate newsletter signups and team notifications when new contacts are added to your CRM

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of keeping your CRM contacts synchronized with your email marketing platform while maintaining team visibility. When a new contact is added to CentralStationCRM with a 'Newsletter' tag, the workflow automatically adds them to your Rapidmail email list and notifies your team in Slack.

The solution eliminates manual data entry errors that often occur when transferring contacts between systems. Marketing teams get immediate access to new subscribers, while sales teams maintain visibility through Slack notifications - all without any repetitive administrative work.

The workflow checks for new CRM contacts, processes them through Rapidmail, and sends Slack notifications

How It Works

1. Detecting new CRM contacts

The workflow regularly checks CentralStationCRM for new contacts tagged for newsletter subscription. Using n8n's scheduling capabilities, it can run every 15 minutes or hourly depending on your needs.

2. Processing contact data

Each new contact's information is formatted to match Rapidmail's requirements. The workflow handles field mapping, data validation, and error checking to ensure clean data transfer.

3. Adding to email lists

Validated contacts are automatically added to the specified Rapidmail list. The workflow can handle custom fields, tags, and subscription preferences during the transfer.

4. Slack notifications

Your team receives a Slack message with key details about each new subscriber. Notifications can be sent to specific channels or team members based on your preferences.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to include contact source information in Slack notifications to help your sales team prioritize follow-ups.
