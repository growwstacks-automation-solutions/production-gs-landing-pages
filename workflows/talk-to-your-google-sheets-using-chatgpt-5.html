What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow creates an intelligent data analysis chatbot that connects OpenAI's GPT-5 Mini model directly to your Google Sheets. Instead of manually sorting through spreadsheets or writing complex formulas, you can ask questions in plain English and get instant insights about your data.

The system automatically analyzes your spreadsheet structure, understands column relationships, and provides meaningful answers to business questions. It maintains conversation context for follow-up questions, making it feel like you're chatting with a data analyst who knows your spreadsheet inside and out.

Whether you're tracking marketing campaigns, sales figures, inventory, or customer data, this automation turns static spreadsheets into interactive data assistants that anyone on your team can use without technical training.

How It Works

1. Data Connection & Authentication

The workflow begins by establishing a secure connection to your Google Sheets using OAuth authentication. It reads your spreadsheet structure, identifies column headers, and understands the data types and relationships between different fields.

2. Natural Language Processing

When you ask a question, the AI analyzes your query to determine what information you're seeking. It identifies relevant columns, filters, calculations, and time periods mentioned in your question before extracting the appropriate data from your spreadsheet.

3. Intelligent Data Analysis

The system processes the extracted data through OpenAI's GPT-5 Mini model, which performs calculations, identifies patterns, and generates human-readable insights. It can calculate totals, averages, percentages, growth rates, and comparative metrics based on your question.

4. Context-Aware Responses

Each interaction maintains a conversation memory, allowing the AI to understand follow-up questions in context. If you ask "What were sales last month?" followed by "How does that compare to this month?", the system remembers the previous query and provides comparative analysis.

5. Formatted Output Delivery

The final step presents the analysis in a clean, understandable format. The workflow can deliver answers through various channels including chat interfaces, email summaries, or even updating other systems with the insights discovered.

What You'll Need

  1. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with workflow editing permissions
  2. Google Sheets account with the spreadsheet you want to analyze
  3. OpenAI API key with access to GPT-5 Mini or similar models
  4. Properly formatted spreadsheet with column headers in the first row
  5. Basic understanding of how to import and activate n8n workflows

Pro tip: Structure your Google Sheets with clear, descriptive column headers (like "Date," "Campaign Name," "Spend," "Conversions") for the best AI analysis results. The AI uses these headers to understand your data structure.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets account by setting up OAuth credentials in the Google Sheets node
  3. Add your OpenAI API key in the credentials section of the OpenAI Chat Model node
  4. Configure your spreadsheet ID and specify the sheet name or range you want to analyze
  5. Test the workflow by asking a simple question about your data and checking the response
  6. Deploy the workflow as a webhook or trigger to make it accessible to your team
