Task Deadline Reminders with Google Sheets, ChatGPT, and Gmail

Automatically remind team members of upcoming tasks with personalized messages—no manual work required

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the constant challenge of keeping teams aware of upcoming deadlines without manual follow-ups. It monitors a Google Sheet containing task assignments, identifies items due today or soon, and sends personalized reminder emails through Gmail with messages crafted by ChatGPT.

For project managers, this eliminates hours spent checking spreadsheets and sending reminder emails. The system works 24/7, ensuring no deadline slips through the cracks while maintaining a human touch through AI-generated messaging that improves team responsiveness.

How It Works

1. Google Sheets Integration

The workflow connects to your task management Google Sheet, scanning for rows where the due date matches today's date. It extracts task details, assignee emails, and any special instructions.

2. ChatGPT Message Generation

Each task's details are fed into ChatGPT, which generates a personalized reminder message. The AI considers the task's urgency, the assignee's history, and creates motivational language that improves compliance.

3. Gmail Delivery

The system sends the crafted message to the assignee's email with a clear subject line and friendly tone. You can configure it to CC managers or send Slack/SMS backups for critical tasks.

Pro tip: Add a "Priority" column in your Sheet to customize reminder frequency—high-priority tasks can get reminders 3 days, 1 day, and 1 hour before deadline.
