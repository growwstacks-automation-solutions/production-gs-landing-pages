Telegram shopping assistant: Amazon product search with Apify & OpenRouter AI

Automate Amazon searches to Telegram with AI-powered scraping

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms Telegram into a powerful shopping assistant by connecting Amazon product searches with AI-enhanced data processing. When users request products through Telegram, the system automatically scrapes Amazon using Apify, processes the results with OpenRouter AI, and delivers curated product recommendations back to the chat.

The workflow solves the problem of manual product research by automating price comparisons, review analysis, and feature summaries. Businesses can deploy this as a customer service tool or personal shopping assistant, reducing research time by 80% while providing AI-powered insights that improve purchase decisions.

How It Works

1. Telegram command triggers the workflow

Users send product search requests through Telegram messages or commands. The n8n workflow monitors these incoming messages and extracts search parameters like product categories, price ranges, or specific features.

2. Apify scrapes Amazon product data

The system sends the search parameters to Apify's Amazon Scraper, which handles proxy rotation and CAPTCHA solving while extracting product listings. It gathers titles, prices, ratings, and availability status while complying with Amazon's scraping policies.

3. OpenRouter AI processes the results

Raw product data gets sent to OpenRouter AI for enhancement. The AI summarizes reviews, compares similar products, and identifies key features. This transforms basic product listings into valuable shopping insights with personalized recommendations.

4. Formatted results return to Telegram

The workflow structures the enhanced product data into clear Telegram messages with images, pricing details, and direct purchase links. Users receive this within seconds of their initial request, creating a seamless conversational shopping experience.
