What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of creating and publishing video content across multiple social media platforms. Marketing teams often spend hours producing a single video, then additional time reformatting it for different platforms and waiting for approvals. This workflow streamlines the entire process from text to published video with human oversight.

The system takes a text input (like a blog post or product description), uses Fal.ai's AI to generate a professional video, routes it for approval via Telegram, then publishes the approved video to up to 5 social platforms simultaneously. It eliminates manual video editing, format conversions, and disjointed approval processes that plague content teams.

How It Works

1. Text Input and Processing

The workflow begins when you submit text content through your preferred channel (web form, email, API call, etc.). The system analyzes the text for key themes and visual cues to determine appropriate video elements.

2. AI Video Generation

Fal.ai's technology automatically selects stock footage, creates smooth transitions, adds background music, and generates voiceover narration if needed. The AI ensures the video matches your brand's style guidelines.

3. Telegram Approval

The generated video gets sent to designated approvers via Telegram bot. Approvers can view the video directly in Telegram and respond with a simple approve/reject button.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

Once approved, the system automatically adapts the video for each social platform's specifications (aspect ratio, length limits, etc.) and publishes according to your schedule. It handles all API connections and error recovery.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for marketing teams, content creators, and social media managers who need to produce video content at scale. E-commerce businesses can automate product videos, agencies can streamline client content production, and educators can quickly turn lessons into engaging videos.
