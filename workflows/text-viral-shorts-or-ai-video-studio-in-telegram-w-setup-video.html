What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms text messages into professionally formatted short videos ready for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels. It solves the time-consuming process of manual video creation by automating script-to-video conversion with AI-generated visuals, voiceovers, and captions.

The Telegram integration allows creators to submit video ideas from anywhere using their phone. The system then processes these submissions through an AI video pipeline that handles formatting, asset generation, and platform-specific optimization automatically.

How It Works

1. Text Submission via Telegram

Users send video scripts, captions or content ideas through a Telegram bot interface. The bot collects all necessary inputs including optional voice notes, reference links, and formatting preferences.

2. AI Content Processing

The workflow processes submitted text through AI services that generate complementary visuals, voiceovers, and animated captions. It analyzes the content to suggest optimal video structures based on trending formats.

3. Automated Video Assembly

An editing engine combines the generated assets into platform-optimized videos following best practices for short-form content. It adds transitions, effects, and branding elements automatically.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

Completed videos are formatted and uploaded to connected social platforms according to each service's specifications. The system handles aspect ratios, metadata, and hashtag optimization automatically.
