What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually tracking how your content performs in AI search engines like Perplexity. Traditional SEO tracking tools don't account for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), leaving marketers blind to how their content ranks in AI-powered search results.

The workflow automatically queries Perplexity for your target keywords, captures ranking positions of your URLs, logs this data to Google Sheets for historical tracking, and alerts your team via Slack when ranking changes occur. This gives you actionable GEO insights without manual checks.

How It Works

1. Automated Perplexity Search

BrowserAct automates searches in Perplexity AI for your configured keywords, simulating real user behavior to get accurate ranking results.

2. Ranking Position Extraction

The workflow parses Perplexity's results page to identify where your target URLs appear in the response, recording both presence and position.

3. Google Sheets Logging

Each search result is timestamped and logged to a Google Sheet, creating a historical record of your GEO performance over time.

4. Slack Alerts

When rankings change significantly (either improving or declining), the system sends real-time alerts to your Slack channel with details.
