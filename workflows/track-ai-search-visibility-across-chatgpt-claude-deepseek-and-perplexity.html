What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of tracking your clients' website visibility across leading AI search platforms including ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek, and Perplexity. As AI search becomes increasingly important for digital marketing, agencies need tools to measure and optimize their clients' presence in these emerging channels.

The workflow systematically tests key search queries, analyzes ranking positions, and compiles comprehensive reports showing how your clients' content performs across different AI platforms. This data helps you refine content strategies specifically for AI search engines—what we call AI Search Engine Optimization (ASEO).

How It Works

1. Query Input

The workflow begins by importing your list of target search queries from a spreadsheet or database. These should be the key terms your clients want to rank for in AI search results.

2. Multi-Platform Testing

For each query, the workflow automatically checks results across ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek and Perplexity using API connections. It records whether your client's website appears in the results and at what position.

3. Data Aggregation

All visibility data is compiled into a centralized dashboard showing performance metrics across platforms. You can see which AI search engines favor your content and identify optimization opportunities.

4. Scheduled Reporting

The workflow can be scheduled to run weekly or monthly, automatically generating PDF or spreadsheet reports showing visibility trends over time.
