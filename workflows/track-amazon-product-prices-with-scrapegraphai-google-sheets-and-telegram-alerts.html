What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking Amazon product prices. Instead of constantly checking product pages or relying on Amazon's limited price drop notifications, this workflow automatically monitors prices at regular intervals using ScrapeGraphAI, logs them in Google Sheets, and sends Telegram alerts when prices hit your target threshold.

The system is particularly valuable for ecommerce businesses tracking competitor pricing, dropshippers monitoring supplier costs, or consumers waiting for price drops on high-ticket items. It eliminates hours of manual checking while ensuring you never miss an important price change.

How It Works

1. Scraping Amazon Product Data

The workflow starts by using ScrapeGraphAI to extract current pricing information from Amazon product pages. This includes the product title, current price, original price (if on sale), and availability status.

2. Price Comparison Logic

Each scraped price is compared against your predefined threshold values stored in Google Sheets. The system checks whether the current price meets your criteria for triggering an alert.

3. Data Logging

All price data is recorded in Google Sheets with timestamps, creating a historical record of price fluctuations. This data can be used for trend analysis and future purchasing decisions.

4. Alert Notifications

When a price drops below your threshold, the workflow sends an instant notification via Telegram with key details about the product and price change. Multiple recipients can be configured to receive these alerts.

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n workspace
