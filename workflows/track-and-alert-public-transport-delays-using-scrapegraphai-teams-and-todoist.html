What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the critical business problem of transport delays disrupting operations. By automatically monitoring public transport schedules through web scraping, it provides real-time alerts when delays occur, helping teams proactively manage disruptions.

The workflow integrates ScrapeGraphAI to extract schedule data from transport websites, analyzes the information for delays, then simultaneously notifies your entire team via Microsoft Teams while creating actionable follow-up tasks in Todoist. This eliminates manual monitoring while ensuring consistent communication and task delegation during transport disruptions.

How It Works

1. Schedule Monitoring

The workflow uses ScrapeGraphAI to periodically scrape transport websites for schedule updates. It compares current departure/arrival times against expected schedules to detect delays.

2. Delay Analysis

When discrepancies are found, the system evaluates whether the difference exceeds your configured threshold (e.g., 5+ minute delay) before triggering alerts, preventing notifications for minor schedule variations.

3. Team Notification

Confirmed delays generate instant alerts in your Microsoft Teams channel, including details about affected routes, expected delay duration, and potential impact on operations.

4. Task Creation

Simultaneously, the system creates prioritized tasks in Todoist for relevant team members, such as rescheduling client meetings or adjusting delivery timelines based on the delay impact.
