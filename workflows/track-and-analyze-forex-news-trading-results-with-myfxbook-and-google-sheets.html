What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming manual process of tracking Forex trading performance after news events. By connecting MyFxBook with Google Sheets, traders can automatically:

  • Simulate trading results based on news alerts
  • Track high/low prices across currency pairs
  • Calculate points gained/lost per trade
  • Analyze which news events generate the most profit
  • Optimize take profit and stop loss levels

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow queries MyFxBook daily for updated high/low prices after trades are opened.

2. Price Analysis

It calculates multipliers based on currency pair decimals (e.g. 1000 for USDJPY) and determines if positions would be profitable.

3. Google Sheets Integration

All trading metrics are automatically recorded in Google Sheets for easy analysis and visualization.
