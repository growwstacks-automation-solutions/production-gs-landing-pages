What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the challenge of manual sales performance tracking by automatically collecting, analyzing, and visualizing key sales metrics with AI-powered insights. It eliminates spreadsheets filled with stale data and gives sales leaders real-time visibility into team performance.

The system connects to your sales data sources, processes information through AI analysis engines, and outputs structured insights into Google Sheets dashboards. This creates a single source of truth for sales performance that updates automatically, saving managers 5-10 hours per week on manual reporting.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The workflow pulls raw sales data from your CRM, email systems, and other sources on a scheduled basis. This includes deal stages, rep activities, conversion rates, and customer interactions.

2. AI Processing

An AI module analyzes the collected data to identify patterns, predict outcomes, and generate insights. It might detect that deals involving certain product features close faster or that specific email templates get better responses.

3. Google Sheets Integration

Processed data and insights are automatically organized into predefined Google Sheets templates. The system maintains historical records while highlighting current trends and anomalies for quick review.
