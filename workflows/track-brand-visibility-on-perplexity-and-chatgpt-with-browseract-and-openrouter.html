Automated workflow to monitor your brand mentions across AI platforms and optimize your Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) strategy

What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the emerging challenge of monitoring brand presence in AI-powered platforms like Perplexity and ChatGPT. As these tools become primary information sources for users, companies need visibility into how their brand is represented in AI-generated responses.

The workflow combines BrowserAct's automated browsing capabilities with OpenRouter's AI API gateway to systematically test how different prompts mentioning your brand generate responses. It captures this data for analysis, helping you identify misinformation, track visibility trends, and optimize your Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) strategy.

How It Works

1. Automated Query Generation

The workflow generates variations of search queries containing your brand name and relevant keywords. These simulate how real users might inquire about your products or services.

2. BrowserAct Execution

BrowserAct automatically submits these queries to Perplexity's web interface, capturing the full response including any cited sources. This mimics human interaction while operating at scale.

3. OpenRouter API Calls

Parallel queries are sent through OpenRouter to ChatGPT and other AI models, ensuring consistent testing methodology across platforms while handling authentication complexities.

4. Response Analysis

The system analyzes responses for brand mentions, sentiment, factual accuracy, and competitor comparisons. Results are structured for easy trend analysis.

5. Reporting & Alerts

Findings are compiled into reports highlighting significant changes or concerning responses, with optional alerts for urgent issues requiring intervention.
