What This Workflow Does

Crypto markets move fast, with narratives shifting hourly. Manually tracking which sectors are pumping—AI, DeFi, Gaming, or Memes—is time-consuming and prone to missing early signals. This automation solves that by systematically analyzing CoinMarketCap's top gainers, grouping tokens by narrative, researching the catalysts with Gemini AI, and delivering concise, actionable reports directly to your Discord channel.

It transforms raw market data into intelligence. Instead of just seeing that a token is up 50%, you learn why its entire sector is gaining momentum, what news is driving it, and whether the trend has legs. This gives traders, researchers, and fund managers a consistent edge in identifying rotation opportunities before they become mainstream.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Data Fetching

The workflow triggers on a configurable schedule (default: hourly). It calls the CoinMarketCap API to fetch the top 200 gainers sorted by 24-hour performance, ensuring you capture the most significant movements as they happen.

2. Rigorous Filtering

Not every pump matters. The workflow applies strict criteria: tokens must have gained >40%, have a market cap >$10M, and volume >$1M. This filters out micro-cap noise and focuses on substantial, liquid movements that indicate genuine sector momentum.

3. Narrative Grouping

Using CoinMarketCap's tagging system, tokens are grouped into sectors: AI, DeFi, Meme, Gaming, Layer1, Layer2, DePIN, RWA, and Infrastructure. When two or more tokens in the same sector meet the filter criteria, it signals a sector-wide narrative worth investigating.

4. AI-Powered Research

For each qualifying sector, the workflow constructs a research prompt for Gemini AI. The AI analyzes recent news, identifies catalysts, assesses sustainability, and provides a concise explanation of why the sector is pumping—turning data into insight.

5. Formatted Digest & Delivery

The workflow compiles a formatted report showing token performance, sector summary, and AI insights. It automatically splits long messages to respect Discord's character limit and posts the digest to your configured webhook, ensuring your team gets timely, readable intelligence.

What You'll Need

  1. CoinMarketCap API Key: Free tier provides 10,000 credits per month—more than enough for hourly monitoring.
  2. Google Gemini API Key: Available via Google AI Studio; used for sector research and analysis.
  3. Discord Webhook URL: Created in your server settings under Integrations → Webhooks.
  4. n8n Instance: Self-hosted or cloud account to import and run the workflow.
  5. Basic Configuration: 10 minutes to connect credentials and optionally adjust filter thresholds.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your crypto sector monitor running:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Create your API keys: Get free keys from CoinMarketCap and Google AI Studio.
  3. Set up credentials in n8n: Add CoinMarketCap as Header Auth (Header Name: X-CMC_PRO_API_KEY) to the "Fetch Top 200 Gainers" node. Connect Gemini credentials to the "Gemini Sector Research" node.
  4. Configure Discord: Paste your webhook URL into the "Send to Discord" node.
  5. Adjust filters (optional): Modify MIN_PERCENT_CHANGE, MIN_MARKET_CAP, and MIN_VOLUME in the "Filter Top Gainers" node if you want stricter or looser criteria.
  6. Activate the workflow: Turn it on and set your preferred schedule—hourly is recommended for active markets.

Pro tip: Start with the default filters, then adjust based on your risk profile. For conservative trading, increase the minimum market cap to $50M. For more aggressive early signal detection, lower the percent gain threshold to 30%.
