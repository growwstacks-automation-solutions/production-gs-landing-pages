What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow helps individuals track their daily emotional states and receive AI-powered analysis of their mood patterns over time. By combining GPT-4o's natural language processing with structured data storage in Data Tables and automated email reporting via Gmail, it creates a powerful self-reflection tool that requires minimal daily effort.

The system eliminates manual journaling while providing deeper insights than simple mood tracking apps. It identifies emotional trends, patterns tied to specific days or activities, and offers personalized recommendations based on your historical data - all delivered automatically to your inbox.

How It Works

1. Daily Mood Input

The workflow starts with a simple daily prompt where you select or describe your current mood. This can be done through various input methods (web form, mobile app, or even SMS).

2. Data Storage & Organization

Each mood entry gets timestamped and stored in a Data Table with additional contextual information (optional notes, energy level, activities). The structured format enables powerful trend analysis later.

3. AI Analysis with GPT-4o

At scheduled intervals (weekly/monthly), GPT-4o processes your mood history, identifying patterns, correlations, and providing narrative insights about your emotional wellbeing trends.

4. Automated Reporting

The system compiles the AI insights into visually appealing reports delivered directly to your Gmail inbox. Reports include charts, key takeaways, and personalized recommendations.
