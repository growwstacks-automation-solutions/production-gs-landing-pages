What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually tracking new product launches on Product Hunt, a time-consuming process that's critical for competitive intelligence and market research. The workflow automatically collects comprehensive product data including names, taglines, descriptions, maker profiles, and website URLs, then organizes everything in Google Sheets with website verification.

By automating this process, businesses gain a systematic way to monitor emerging competitors, identify partnership opportunities, and spot market trends without daily manual checks. The website verification step adds valuable quality control by filtering out inactive or spammy listings, ensuring your tracking focuses on legitimate products with web presence.

How It Works

Step 1: Fetch daily Product Hunt launches

The workflow connects to Product Hunt's API to retrieve the latest product launches. It captures all available metadata including upvote counts, maker information, and categorization.

Step 2: Extract website URLs

Each product's website URL is extracted from the launch data. This becomes particularly valuable for competitive research and potential partnership outreach.

Step 3: Verify website availability

The system checks each website URL to confirm it's active and accessible. This verification step filters out broken links and inactive domains.

Step 4: Organize data in Google Sheets

All verified product data gets structured and stored in Google Sheets, creating a searchable database of Product Hunt launches with timestamps.
