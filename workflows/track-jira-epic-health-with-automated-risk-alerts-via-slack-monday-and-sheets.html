What This Workflow Does

This automation solution solves the critical challenge of maintaining visibility into the health of Jira epics across multiple teams and projects. Traditional manual tracking methods often lead to late discovery of risks, missed deadlines, and reactive firefighting. The workflow automatically monitors key health indicators for all active epics, calculates risk scores, and distributes alerts through the channels your team actually uses.

By integrating Jira with Slack, Monday.com, and Google Sheets, it creates a comprehensive monitoring system that surfaces problems early while maintaining historical records. Project managers gain real-time insights without constant manual checking, while team members receive targeted notifications about issues requiring their attention.

How It Works

1. Continuous Jira Data Collection

The workflow runs on a scheduled basis (configurable from hourly to daily) to pull the latest data from your Jira projects. It examines each active epic for completion percentage, overdue tasks, blocked issues, recent activity, and other key metrics.

2. Health Scoring Algorithm

Each epic receives a calculated health score from 0-100 based on weighted factors you can customize. The template includes a default scoring model that emphasizes deadline proximity, unresolved blockers, and team velocity changes.

3. Multi-Channel Alert Distribution

Based on score thresholds you define, the system sends appropriate alerts: Slack messages for immediate attention, Monday.com board updates for visual tracking, and Google Sheets entries for historical analysis. High-priority alerts can tag specific team members or channels.

4. Historical Trend Tracking

All health scores and alert events get logged to Google Sheets with timestamps, creating a searchable record of project health over time. This data helps identify recurring issues and improve future planning accuracy.
