What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms how you track nutritional intake by eliminating manual data entry. Simply take a photo of your meal and send it via LINE messenger. The workflow automatically analyzes the image using Google Gemini's AI, extracts nutritional information, and logs it to a Google Sheets spreadsheet.

Traditional food tracking requires searching databases, estimating portions, and manual entry - a process that takes 5-7 minutes per meal. This solution reduces that to seconds while maintaining reasonable accuracy. It's particularly valuable for busy professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone following specific dietary plans who wants consistent tracking without the hassle.

How It Works

1. Photo Submission via LINE

Users send meal photos through LINE messenger, either to a dedicated account or via direct message. The system accepts both single images and multiple photos for complex meals.

2. AI Nutrition Analysis

Google Gemini processes the image to identify food items, estimate portion sizes, and calculate nutritional values. The AI references extensive food databases to provide calories, macros (protein, carbs, fats), and common micronutrients.

3. Data Logging to Google Sheets

The analyzed nutrition data automatically populates a Google Sheets spreadsheet with timestamps, meal descriptions, and nutritional breakdowns. The sheet organizes data for easy analysis and can connect to other health apps.
