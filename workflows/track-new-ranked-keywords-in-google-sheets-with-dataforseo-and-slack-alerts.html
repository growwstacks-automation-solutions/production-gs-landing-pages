Track New Ranked Keywords in Google Sheets with DataForSEO and Slack Alerts

Automatically monitor your SEO performance with weekly keyword tracking updates

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking keyword rankings by automatically monitoring your SEO performance weekly. It pulls fresh ranking data from Google via DataForSEO's API, compares it to previous results, and logs newly ranked keywords in an organized Google Sheet while alerting your team via Slack.

Instead of spending hours each week running manual searches and compiling spreadsheets, this workflow gives you consistent, scheduled updates without the busywork. It creates an audit trail of your keyword performance while ensuring you never miss important ranking changes that could impact your traffic.

Workflow steps connecting DataForSEO API to Google Sheets with Slack notifications

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow runs automatically on your preferred schedule (default is weekly) to check for new keyword rankings.

2. Data Collection

It pulls your target keywords and domains from a master Google Sheet, then queries DataForSEO's API for current ranking data.

3. Comparison Analysis

The system compares new rankings against previous results to identify newly ranked keywords.

4. Data Storage

Fresh snapshots are stored in Google Sheets while newly ranked keywords are logged in a dedicated tracking sheet.

5. Team Notification

A summary message is sent to Slack with key metrics about ranking changes for quick team review.

What You'll Need

  1. A DataForSEO API account
  2. Google Sheets with your keywords and target domains

  4. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted)

Pro tip: Use separate Google Sheets tabs for different domains or client accounts to keep data organized.
