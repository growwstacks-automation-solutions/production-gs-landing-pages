What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation helps SEO professionals and website owners identify new search queries where their pages appear in Google results but receive zero clicks. These "zero-click" queries represent untapped opportunities where your content ranks but fails to attract visitors.

The workflow automatically analyzes your Google Search Console data from the past 7 days, filtering for newly appearing queries with impressions but no clicks. This reveals emerging search trends and content gaps before competitors notice them, allowing you to optimize pages or create new content targeting these queries.

How It Works

1. Connect to Google Search Console API

The workflow authenticates with your Google Search Console account using OAuth, accessing your site's search performance data securely.

2. Retrieve query data from last 7 days

It pulls all search queries that generated impressions for your site during the past week, including click-through rates and positions.

3. Filter for new zero-click queries

The automation compares current data with historical records to identify queries that appeared for the first time in the last week and had impressions but zero clicks.

4. Organize and prioritize results

Results are sorted by impression volume and search position, highlighting the most significant opportunities where small optimizations could yield traffic gains.

5. Deliver actionable insights

The final output can be sent to your preferred destination - email, spreadsheet, or project management tool - with all relevant data for analysis.

Pro tip: Combine this data with your content calendar to prioritize optimizations for pages ranking for new queries but not converting impressions.
