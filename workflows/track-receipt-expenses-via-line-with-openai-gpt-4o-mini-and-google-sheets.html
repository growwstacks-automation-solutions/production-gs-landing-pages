What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking business expenses from paper receipts and digital invoices. Employees simply take photos of receipts and send them via LINE messenger, where AI instantly extracts all relevant financial data and logs it to an organized Google Sheet.

The system eliminates spreadsheet data entry errors while capturing more detailed expense information than traditional methods. GPT-4o-mini's advanced vision capabilities can read receipts in multiple languages, identify line items, calculate taxes separately, and even flag potentially fraudulent charges based on spending patterns.

How It Works

1. Receipt Submission via LINE

Team members send receipt photos or PDFs to your designated LINE business account. The workflow supports both individual messages and group submissions for team expense tracking.

2. AI Data Extraction

OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini analyzes each image to identify vendor names, transaction dates, amounts, taxes, payment methods, and expense categories. The AI can handle receipts in various formats and languages with human-level accuracy.

3. Google Sheets Logging

Structured expense data automatically populates your master Google Sheet with columns for all critical fields. The system timestamps each entry and includes the submitter's LINE profile details for accountability.

Pro tip: Create a LINE group chat called "Expense Reports" where your team can submit receipts and see real-time confirmations when expenses are logged.
