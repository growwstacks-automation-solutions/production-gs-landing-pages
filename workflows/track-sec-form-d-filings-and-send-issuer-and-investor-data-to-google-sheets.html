What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually tracking SEC Form D filings, which are critical for private market intelligence but notoriously difficult to monitor at scale. The workflow automatically checks for new Form D filings, extracts all relevant issuer and investor data, and structures it in Google Sheets for immediate analysis.

Investment professionals waste hours each week manually reviewing SEC filings, copying data between systems, and maintaining spreadsheets. This workflow eliminates that inefficiency by providing real-time, structured data about private capital raises - including issuer details, offering amounts, exemption types, and investor categories - all automatically organized in your preferred analysis format.

How It Works

1. Scheduled SEC EDGAR System Checks

The workflow runs at your specified intervals (daily recommended) to query the SEC's EDGAR database for new Form D filings. It uses official SEC APIs to ensure complete, up-to-date coverage without manual website scraping.

2. Structured Data Extraction

Each filing is parsed to extract all standardized data fields including issuer name, address, CIK number, offering size, exemption type (Rule 506b/506c), and investor details. The system intelligently handles variations in filing formats.

3. Google Sheets Integration

Extracted data is automatically appended to your designated Google Sheet with proper column formatting. The workflow maintains data integrity by checking for duplicates and validating regulatory identifiers.

Pro tip: Add a second tab in your Google Sheet with data validation rules to create a dashboard that automatically highlights large raises, first-time filers, or specific exemption types.
