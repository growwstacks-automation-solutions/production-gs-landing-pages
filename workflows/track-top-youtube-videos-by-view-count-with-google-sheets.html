What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious manual process of tracking YouTube video performance. Instead of checking analytics daily and copying numbers to spreadsheets, this workflow automatically collects view counts and other metrics from your YouTube channels and organizes them in Google Sheets.

The system helps content creators, marketers, and agencies identify top-performing videos, track growth trends, and make data-driven decisions about content strategy. By having historical data automatically compiled in one place, you can analyze what content resonates with your audience over time.

How It Works

1. YouTube API Connection

The workflow connects to YouTube's API using your credentials to access your channel's video data. It authenticates securely to pull the latest analytics for your specified videos or playlists.

2. Data Extraction

The system retrieves key metrics like view counts, likes, comments, and watch time for your videos. It can be configured to track specific videos, your most recent uploads, or your all-time top performers.

3. Google Sheets Integration

The collected data is automatically written to your Google Sheet in a structured format. Each run adds a new row with the current metrics and timestamp, creating a historical record of performance.

4. Scheduled Updates

The workflow can be scheduled to run daily, weekly, or at any interval you choose. This ensures your analytics data stays current without manual intervention.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for YouTube creators who want to track their content performance systematically. Marketing teams managing multiple channels will appreciate the centralized data collection. Agencies handling client YouTube accounts can use this to provide consistent reporting.

Businesses using YouTube for product demos or tutorials benefit from understanding which videos drive the most engagement. Educators and course creators can identify which lessons resonate most with students.
