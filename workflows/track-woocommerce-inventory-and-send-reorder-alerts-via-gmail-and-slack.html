What This Workflow Does

This automation continuously monitors your WooCommerce inventory levels and automatically sends alerts when products need reordering. It connects your ecommerce store with Gmail and Slack to ensure purchasing teams never miss critical stock updates.

The system tracks inventory changes in real-time, calculates when stock will run out based on sales velocity, and sends customized alerts with product details, current stock levels, and recommended order quantities. This prevents both stockouts (lost sales) and overstocking (tied-up capital).

How It Works

1. Inventory Monitoring

The workflow checks WooCommerce product inventory at regular intervals, tracking both stock quantity and sales rate. It identifies products approaching your predefined reorder thresholds.

2. Alert Triggering

When inventory drops below threshold levels, the system gathers product details including SKU, current stock, supplier information, and sales history to create a comprehensive alert.

3. Notification Delivery

Alerts are sent simultaneously to both Gmail (for formal records) and Slack (for immediate team visibility). Notifications include direct links to the WooCommerce product and reorder forms.

Pro tip: Set different threshold levels for fast-moving vs. slow-moving products to optimize your inventory turnover.
