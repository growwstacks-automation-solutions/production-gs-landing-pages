What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation solves the time-consuming challenge of manually reviewing and summarizing sales calls recorded in GoHighLevel. It automatically processes new call recordings through AI transcription services, generates concise summaries highlighting key points and action items, then attaches these directly to the relevant contact records in GoHighLevel as notes.

Sales teams waste countless hours reviewing call recordings or taking manual notes. This workflow eliminates that inefficiency while ensuring no important details are missed. The AI-generated summaries help managers quickly assess deal progress and coach reps based on actual call content rather than relying on self-reported notes.

How It Works

1. Detects new GoHighLevel call recordings

The workflow monitors your GoHighLevel account for newly completed call recordings, either through webhooks or scheduled checks. It captures the audio file URL and associated contact information.

2. Processes audio through transcription service

The call recording is sent to an AI transcription service (like OpenAI Whisper or AssemblyAI) that converts the audio to text with timestamps and speaker identification where available.

Pro tip: Configure the workflow to filter out very short calls (under 30 seconds) to avoid processing hang-ups or failed connections.

3. Generates AI summary of key points

The raw transcript is analyzed by an LLM (like GPT-4) that extracts important discussion points, action items, objections, and buying signals. The summary format can be customized for your sales process.

4. Attaches results to GoHighLevel

The full transcript and summary are added as notes to the contact record in GoHighLevel, with proper tagging for easy filtering. The workflow can also trigger follow-up tasks based on call content.
