What This Workflow Does

This n8n automation transforms YouTube videos into structured research assets by combining AI transcription with geographic intelligence. The workflow extracts audio from any YouTube URL, processes it through OpenAI's Whisper for accurate transcription, then uses GPT-4o-mini to analyze and summarize location-specific insights.

The final output is a neatly formatted Notion database entry containing the full transcript, key GEO findings, and structured metadata. This eliminates hours of manual work for researchers, marketers, and analysts who need to extract actionable insights from video content.

How It Works

1. YouTube URL Processing

The workflow starts by extracting audio from the YouTube video URL you provide. It handles various video formats and durations, automatically optimizing the audio for transcription quality.

2. Whisper AI Transcription

OpenAI's Whisper model converts the audio into accurate text, including speaker differentiation when available. The system automatically detects the language and applies appropriate processing.

3. GPT-4o-mini GEO Analysis

The AI scans the transcript for geographic references, contextualizing locations with relevant insights. It identifies patterns, trends, and notable mentions related to specific regions or cities.

4. Notion Database Integration

Structured data flows into your specified Notion database with consistent formatting. The automation creates rich-text entries with highlighted GEO insights, timestamps, and source references.

Pro tip: Use Notion's relation properties to link these summaries to your existing research projects or client databases for powerful cross-referencing.
