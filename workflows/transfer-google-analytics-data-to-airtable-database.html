What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually exporting and importing Google Analytics data into Airtable for analysis and reporting. Marketing teams often waste hours each week copying metrics between platforms, risking errors and outdated information.

The workflow automatically extracts session data from Google Analytics (grouped by your preferred dimensions) and transfers it to designated Airtable tables. This creates a live reporting system where analytics integrate seamlessly with other business data in Airtable.

How It Works

1. Google Analytics Data Extraction

The workflow connects to your Google Analytics account via API and retrieves specified metrics like sessions, pageviews, and bounce rates. You can configure the date range, dimensions (like source/medium or landing page), and metrics to match your reporting needs.

2. Data Transformation

Before transferring to Airtable, the workflow transforms the raw analytics data into a structured format. This includes converting dates to proper formats, calculating derived metrics, and applying any necessary filters or segmentation.

3. Airtable Integration

The processed data gets mapped to corresponding fields in your Airtable base. The workflow checks for existing records to prevent duplicates and can append new data or update existing entries based on your configuration.
