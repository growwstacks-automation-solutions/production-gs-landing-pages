What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of transforming quick event notes into professional LinkedIn content. Many professionals attend events but struggle to find time to craft thoughtful posts afterward. This workflow bridges that gap by automatically processing your Telegram messages into polished LinkedIn-ready content.

The system matches your notes with calendar events to add context, uses AI to refine the messaging, and stores everything in a database for future reference. Even if you don't post immediately, you're building a valuable library of your professional activities and insights.

How It Works

1. Telegram Input

Send a message to your Telegram bot following the format "Event Name: Your personal notes". This serves as the raw input for your LinkedIn post.

2. Calendar Matching

The system checks your Google Calendar for matching events from the past 7 days, pulling in details like date, location, and attendees to enrich your post.

3. AI Content Generation

Claude AI processes your notes and calendar data to create a professional post with proper structure and relevant hashtags while maintaining your authentic voice.

4. Database Storage

All generated content and source materials are saved to Supabase, creating a searchable archive of your professional activities and insights.
