What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of extracting value from the dozens of newsletters professionals receive but rarely have time to fully process. It automatically transforms Gmail newsletters into polished LinkedIn posts, helping you maintain an active professional presence without manual content creation.

The workflow identifies incoming newsletters, extracts their core insights using OpenAI's natural language processing, and reformats them into engaging social media posts. It preserves the original value while adapting the content for LinkedIn's professional audience and algorithm preferences.

How It Works

1. Newsletter Identification

The workflow monitors your Gmail inbox for incoming messages matching your newsletter subscriptions. It uses filters to distinguish newsletters from regular emails based on sender, subject patterns, or labels.

2. Content Extraction

Key content is extracted from the email body, removing headers, footers, and promotional sections. The system preserves the main article text while discarding boilerplate content that wouldn't be relevant for LinkedIn.

3. AI Processing

OpenAI analyzes the extracted content to identify key points, summarize complex information, and determine the most valuable insights worth sharing. The AI reformats the content using professional tone and LinkedIn-optimized structure.

4. Post Generation

The system generates a complete LinkedIn post draft including formatted text, relevant hashtags, and optional calls-to-action. You can configure length, style preferences, and inclusion of specific elements like quotes or statistics.

5. Review & Publishing

The generated post is saved to your preferred destination (Draft folder, Google Doc, or directly to LinkedIn via API). This gives you the option to review and edit before publishing while still saving the bulk of creation time.
