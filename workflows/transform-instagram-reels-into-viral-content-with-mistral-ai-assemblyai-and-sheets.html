What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the transformation of Instagram Reels into repurposable content using AI-powered analysis. It solves the time-consuming process of manually reviewing, transcribing, and extracting insights from your video content - a critical task for creators and marketers who want to maximize their content's reach.

The system automatically processes new Reels by extracting the audio, generating accurate transcriptions with AssemblyAI, analyzing content themes with Mistral AI, and organizing all data in Google Sheets for easy repurposing into blog posts, tweets, or future video ideas. This creates a searchable database of your best-performing content elements.

How It Works

1. Instagram Reel Detection

The workflow monitors your Instagram account for new Reels posts, either through scheduled checks or webhook triggers. When a new Reel is detected, it captures the video URL and metadata.

2. Audio Extraction & Transcription

The system extracts the audio track from the Reel and sends it to AssemblyAI for high-accuracy speech-to-text conversion. This creates a searchable transcript of all spoken content.

3. AI Content Analysis

Mistral AI processes the transcript to identify key topics, emotional tone, memorable quotes, and potential content angles. It generates actionable insights about what makes the content engaging.

4. Data Organization

All extracted information - including transcript, analysis results, and performance metrics - is automatically logged in Google Sheets with timestamps and categorization for easy reference.
