What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your long-form podcast content into bite-sized, engaging social media clips using Gemini AI. It solves the time-consuming process of manually identifying key moments, editing clips, adding captions, and publishing across multiple platforms.

The workflow automatically analyzes your podcast audio, identifies the most engaging segments, generates optimized captions and hashtags, creates formatted videos, and publishes them to your social channels. This ensures your podcast content gets maximum exposure with minimal effort.

How It Works

1. Podcast Content Analysis

The system processes your podcast audio file using Gemini AI to transcribe the content and identify key moments based on engagement potential, emotional tone, and topic relevance.

2. Clip Generation

AI selects the most shareable segments (typically 30-60 seconds) and automatically edits them into polished clips with proper transitions and audio balancing.

3. Caption & Hashtag Creation

Gemini AI generates compelling captions that hook viewers in the first few seconds and suggests relevant hashtags to increase discoverability.

4. Multi-Platform Publishing

The workflow automatically formats clips for each social platform (square for Instagram, vertical for TikTok/Reels) and publishes them according to your schedule.
