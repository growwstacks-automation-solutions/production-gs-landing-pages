What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious process of manually converting press releases into publishable articles. Marketing teams and media outlets receive dozens of press releases daily in various formats (PDF, Word, email body text) that need to be transformed into polished content while preserving key information.

The workflow automatically processes incoming emails with attachments, extracts the content regardless of format, uses AI to rewrite it in your preferred style, and delivers a ready-to-publish draft back to your team. It eliminates hours of manual reformatting while ensuring consistent quality and voice across all your published content.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow starts when a new email arrives in your designated Gmail inbox. It checks for attachments and filters out non-relevant file types.

2. Content Extraction

Depending on attachment type, it either extracts text directly from Word files or uses OCR for PDFs. For emails without attachments, it processes the message body.

3. AI Transformation

The raw content is sent to OpenAI where it's rewritten according to your predefined style guidelines and tone preferences.

4. Quality Assurance

The AI performs a self-assessment, comparing original and transformed content to ensure accuracy and completeness.

5. Delivery

The polished article is returned via email with both original and transformed versions for easy review.
