What This Workflow Does

Creating professional marketing images for products is expensive and time-consuming. Traditional methods require hiring models, photographers, and graphic designers—costing thousands of dollars and taking weeks to produce. Most small businesses and e-commerce sellers settle for basic product photos that don't convert well.

This AI automation workflow solves that problem completely. It transforms simple product photos into polished, professional marketing images featuring realistic models, contextual backgrounds, perfect lighting, and lifestyle scenes that help customers envision using your product. What used to require a full marketing team now happens automatically in minutes.

The system analyzes your product image, understands its context, and generates commercial-ready visuals suitable for social media ads, e-commerce listings, email campaigns, and website banners—all without any design skills or expensive software.

How It Works

1. Upload Your Product Photo

The workflow starts with a simple web form where you upload your basic product image. The system accepts common formats like JPG and PNG, and can handle multiple products in sequence for batch processing.

2. AI Analysis and Scene Generation

Google Gemini AI analyzes your product, identifies its characteristics, and creates a complete marketing scene. The AI considers product type, potential use cases, and generates appropriate backgrounds, models, and lighting that match commercial marketing standards.

3. Automated Image Enhancement

The system applies professional-grade enhancements: adjusting composition, optimizing colors, adding realistic shadows and highlights, and ensuring the final image meets advertising platform requirements for resolution and quality.

4. Download and Distribution

Your transformed marketing image is automatically prepared for download. The workflow can also integrate with your existing systems to directly upload to social media platforms, e-commerce stores, or content management systems.

  3. Basic Product Photos: Clear images of your products (the better the source, the better the result)
  4. n8n Instance: Either self-hosted or using n8n.cloud (free tier works perfectly)

Quick Setup Guide

Getting started takes just a few minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance
  2. Configure OpenRouter credentials in the "AI Marketing Image Generator" node with your API key
  3. Customize the prompt (optional) to match your brand's visual style preferences
  4. Test with one product image to ensure everything works correctly
  5. Scale up by processing multiple products or connecting to your e-commerce platform

The workflow includes clear comments at each configuration point, making adjustments straightforward even if you're new to automation.
