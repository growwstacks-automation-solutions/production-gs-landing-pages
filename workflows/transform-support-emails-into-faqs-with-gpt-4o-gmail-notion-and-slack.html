Transform Support Emails into FAQs with AI

Automatically convert customer support emails into structured FAQs, analyze sentiment, and alert your team—all with one n8n workflow.

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

Customer support teams drown in repetitive emails asking the same questions. Manual categorization, prioritization, and response creation consume hours each day. This automation solves that by creating a complete AI-driven support loop that transforms incoming emails into actionable knowledge.

The workflow monitors your support Gmail inbox, uses GPT-4o to analyze each message, creates structured FAQ entries in Notion, detects sentiment and urgency, alerts your team on Slack for critical issues, and sends acknowledgment emails to customers—all automatically. It turns reactive support into proactive knowledge management.

How It Works

1. Email Capture & Validation

The workflow polls your Gmail support inbox every minute for new messages. Each email is validated to ensure it contains complete data before proceeding to AI analysis. Invalid payloads are logged to Google Sheets for debugging.

2. AI-Powered Classification

Azure OpenAI GPT-4o analyzes each email to extract the core problem, categorize it (API, billing, UI, etc.), generate a 2-3 line solution, and flag whether it's a recurring issue. This creates structured data from unstructured email content.

3. Knowledge Base Creation

The classified information is saved as a new FAQ entry in your Notion database. Each record includes the problem summary, category, solution, and recurrence flag—creating a searchable knowledge base that grows with every support interaction.

4. Sentiment & Urgency Analysis

A second GPT-4o instance analyzes the email's tone to determine urgency (Low, Medium, High, Critical) and sentiment (Positive, Neutral, Frustrated, Angry). This intelligence helps prioritize responses.

5. Team Notification & Customer Response

Critical issues trigger immediate Slack alerts with email snippets and analysis. All customers receive automated acknowledgment emails with solution summaries, while the team focuses on high-priority cases.

Pro tip: Start with a dedicated support email alias like support@yourcompany.com. This keeps the automation focused and makes it easier to measure its impact separately from general correspondence.

What You'll Need

  1. Gmail account with API access (or a dedicated support email address)
  2. Azure OpenAI credentials with GPT-4o access
  3. Notion account with API integration and a pre-created FAQ database
  4. Slack workspace with app installation permissions
  5. Google Sheets for error logging (optional but recommended)
  6. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow

Quick Setup Guide

1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.

2. Configure credentials for Gmail, Azure OpenAI, Notion, and Slack in n8n's credential management.

3. Update the Notion database ID in the "Save FAQ Entry to Notion Database" node to match your actual database.

4. Set the Slack channel in the notification nodes to where your support team monitors alerts.

5. Test with a sample email by sending a support question to your monitored Gmail address and verifying the workflow creates a Notion entry and appropriate notifications.

6. Activate the workflow and let it run continuously to process incoming support emails automatically.

Pro tip: Create a "Sandbox" version first by duplicating the workflow and pointing it to test channels and databases. This lets you refine the AI prompts and categorization without affecting live support operations.
