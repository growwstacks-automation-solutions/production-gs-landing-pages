What This Workflow Does

Emergency departments face overwhelming pressure during peak hours, leading to delayed care for critical patients and burnout among triage staff. This AI-powered automation solves these challenges by intelligently processing patient intake information through multiple assessment stages before a clinician ever sees the case.

The system captures initial patient data through chat interfaces, web forms, or connected monitoring devices, then uses specialized AI agents to analyze medical history, current symptoms, and vital signs. It validates against clinical business rules, performs stability checks, calculates priority scores using established triage protocols, and determines appropriate care pathways—all within seconds instead of minutes.

By automating the initial assessment layer, hospitals can ensure every patient receives consistent, protocol-driven evaluation while freeing clinical staff to focus on complex cases and direct patient care. The system logs all interactions for audit compliance and integrates seamlessly with existing hospital infrastructure.

How It Works

1. Patient Intake & Data Collection

The workflow begins when patient information enters the system through chat interfaces, web portals, or connected medical devices. It captures symptoms, medical history, current medications, and vital signs, structuring this data for AI analysis while ensuring HIPAA compliance through encrypted transmission.

2. AI-Powered Symptom Analysis

GPT-4.1 processes the structured patient data, comparing symptoms against thousands of clinical patterns and medical guidelines. The AI identifies potential emergencies, flags concerning symptom combinations, and applies validated triage protocols like the Emergency Severity Index (ESI) to determine initial priority levels.

3. Clinical Rule Validation

The system cross-references AI recommendations against hospital-specific clinical rules and business logic. It checks for contraindications, validates medication interactions, ensures consent documentation is complete, and applies facility-specific protocols before finalizing priority assignments.

4. Priority Scoring & Pathway Assignment

Based on the validated analysis, the workflow calculates a comprehensive priority score and assigns patients to appropriate care pathways—immediate emergency care, urgent care, fast-track, or scheduled follow-up. It considers available resources, current department load, and specialist availability in real-time.

5. Notification & Integration

The system automatically notifies relevant medical teams via secure channels, updates Electronic Health Records (EHR), schedules necessary appointments or tests, and provides patients with expected wait times and next steps—all while maintaining a complete audit trail for compliance and quality assurance.

  1. Import the template into your n8n instance and review the workflow structure

  3. Customize clinical rules in the validation nodes to match your hospital's triage protocols
