What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of delayed lead follow-up. When potential customers submit a contact form, interest is highest immediately after submission. Traditional manual follow-up can take hours or days, resulting in lost opportunities and decreased conversion rates.

The workflow instantly connects JotForm submissions to Vapi AI, triggering a personalized outbound voice call via an AI assistant within seconds. The AI can greet the lead by name, reference their specific inquiry, answer preliminary questions, qualify their interest, and even schedule appointments—all without human intervention. This creates a seamless, immediate engagement that dramatically improves conversion rates while freeing your team from repetitive calling tasks.

How It Works

1. Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates automatically whenever a new submission arrives in your JotForm. It captures all form data including contact information, inquiry details, and custom fields.

2. Data Processing & Phone Formatting

An information extractor node processes the submitted phone number, ensuring proper international formatting (e.g., +391234567890) required by Vapi's telephony system. Additional form data is structured for personalization.

3. AI Call Configuration

The workflow configures the Vapi API call with your specific assistant ID, phone number ID, and API key. It maps relevant form fields to conversation variables so the AI can reference the lead's specific interests during the call.

4. Instant Voice Call Initiation

A POST request triggers Vapi to immediately place an outbound call to the formatted number. The AI assistant engages the lead with a personalized conversation based on their submission data.

5. Result Logging & Integration

Call results, duration, and conversation summaries can be logged to your CRM, database, or notification system, creating a complete audit trail and enabling further automation based on call outcomes.

Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to implement this automation in under 30 minutes:

  1. Download the template using the button above and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure JotForm trigger by adding your JotForm API credentials and selecting your specific form.
  3. Set up Vapi credentials in the HTTP Request node, adding your API key, assistant ID, and phone number ID.
  4. Map form fields to ensure phone numbers and personalization data flow correctly to the AI call.
  5. Test with a submission using your own phone number to verify the call triggers and sounds natural.
  6. Activate the workflow and monitor initial conversations to refine your AI script as needed.
