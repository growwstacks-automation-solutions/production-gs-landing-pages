Turn closed-won HubSpot deals into lookalike prospects with CompanyEnrich

Automatically identify companies similar to your best customers using n8n and CompanyEnrich's Similar Companies API

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the process of finding new prospects that closely resemble your most successful HubSpot customers. When a deal is marked as Closed-Won in HubSpot, the workflow automatically queries CompanyEnrich's Similar Companies API to identify businesses with matching characteristics.

Instead of manually researching similar companies or relying on basic firmographic filters, this automation systematically analyzes your best customers and generates a targeted list of high-probability prospects. The results can be added back to HubSpot as new contacts, companies, or deals for your sales team to pursue.

How It Works

1. Trigger on Closed-Won deals

The workflow monitors your HubSpot pipeline for deals that change to the Closed-Won stage. You can configure it to run immediately or on a schedule (daily/weekly).

2. Extract company details

For each won deal, the workflow retrieves the associated company record from HubSpot, collecting firmographic data like industry, employee count, and location.

3. Query CompanyEnrich API

These company attributes are sent to CompanyEnrich's Similar Companies API, which uses machine learning to identify businesses with matching characteristics.

4. Filter and process results

The workflow can exclude existing customers, apply geographic filters, and set similarity score thresholds to ensure high-quality matches.

5. Create new records in HubSpot

Finally, the matched companies are added to HubSpot as new contacts, companies, or deals based on your preferred workflow configuration.

Pro tip: Combine this with your sales engagement platform to automatically add these lookalike prospects to sequenced outreach campaigns.
