What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms your Google Sheets text ideas into professional 8-second videos using cutting-edge AI technology. Simply enter your concept in a spreadsheet, and the system automatically generates a video with Fal.AI's Veo 3 model, enhanced by GPT-4o's prompt optimization.

The workflow eliminates manual video production tasks, allowing marketing teams, content creators, and educators to quickly produce visual content without technical skills. You control whether videos include sound and can specify the aspect ratio directly in your spreadsheet.

How It Works

1. Input Your Idea

Enter your video concept in a designated Google Sheet along with preferences for sound and aspect ratio.

2. AI Processing

GPT-4o enhances your text prompt before sending it to Fal.AI's Veo 3 model for video generation.

3. Video Generation

Fal.AI processes your enhanced prompt and creates an 8-second video matching your specifications.

4. Delivery

The completed video URL is automatically added back to your Google Sheet for easy access and sharing.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON workflow file into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Google Sheets account using service account credentials
  3. Add your OpenAI API key in the designated node
  4. Configure your Fal.AI credentials in the workflow
  5. Make a copy of the template Google Sheet and share it with your service account
  6. Activate the workflow and start adding ideas to your spreadsheet
