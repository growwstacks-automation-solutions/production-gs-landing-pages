What This Workflow Does

This innovative automation bridges the gap between cosmic visuals and musical experiences by analyzing NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) through GPT-4o Vision and finding matching Spotify tracks. It solves the challenge of manually connecting visual art with music while demonstrating advanced AI integration capabilities.

The workflow automatically runs daily to fetch NASA's featured astronomy image, analyze its visual characteristics through AI, translate those findings into musical parameters, search Spotify for matching tracks, then share the paired content through Slack and Spotify playlists. This creates a seamless daily experience of discovering space imagery with complementary music.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates nightly at 10 PM through n8n's scheduler, ensuring fresh content is ready each morning.

2. NASA APOD Fetch

Connects to NASA's API to retrieve the current Astronomy Picture of the Day along with its description and metadata.

3. GPT-4o Vision Analysis

Sends the image to OpenAI's GPT-4o model which analyzes colors, composition, mood, and themes, outputting musical parameters.

4. Spotify Search

Uses the AI-generated parameters to query Spotify's recommendation engine for tracks matching the image's characteristics.

5. Creative Content Generation

GPT-4o creates a short poetic caption linking the cosmic image with the selected music.

6. Multi-Channel Distribution

Posts the paired content to designated Slack channels and adds the track to a Spotify playlist for archiving.
