What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common ecommerce challenge of creating valuable content around products without manual effort. When you add a new product to Shopify, the workflow automatically generates an SEO-optimized blog post by combining product data with AI-generated content and web research.

The system pulls product details (title, description, attributes), researches related topics using Perplexity's web search, then crafts a comprehensive blog draft with Gemini AI. The output includes proper heading structure, semantic keywords, and educational content that positions your store as an authority while driving organic traffic.

The workflow combines product data with AI research to create draft blog posts

How It Works

1. New product trigger

The workflow monitors your Shopify store for new product additions through webhooks or scheduled checks. When a new product appears, it captures all relevant details including title, description, variants, and metadata.

2. Perplexity web research

Using the product details as context, the system queries Perplexity to find related topics, common questions, and trending discussions about similar products. This ensures the blog content addresses real customer needs beyond just product specs.

3. Gemini content generation

The AI analyzes both the product data and research findings to generate a draft blog post. It structures content with an engaging introduction, problem/solution framing, product benefits, usage scenarios, and FAQ-style sections - all optimized for readability and SEO.

4. Google Sheets output

The final blog draft saves to a designated Google Sheet with separate columns for title, meta description, headers, and body content. This allows for easy review, editing, and batch publishing to your CMS.

Pro tip: Add a column in your output sheet for "Publish Status" to track which drafts have been reviewed and posted to your blog.
