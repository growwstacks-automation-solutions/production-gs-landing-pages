What This Workflow Does

Manual data entry from quiz submissions is a time-consuming bottleneck that leads to missed opportunities and delayed follow-up. This automation solves that problem by instantly transferring every Typeform quiz submission directly into your KlickTipp email marketing platform.

The workflow captures complete quiz responses including personal details, answers, and scores, then transforms the data to match KlickTipp's requirements. It automatically adds contacts to your subscriber list, maps quiz answers to custom fields, and applies intelligent tags for segmentation—all within seconds of submission.

This means you can trigger immediate welcome sequences, segment leads based on their quiz results, and personalize follow-up content without ever touching a spreadsheet. It turns your quiz from a simple data collection tool into a powerful lead qualification and nurturing system.

Workflow diagram showing how quiz data flows from Typeform through transformation steps to KlickTipp

How It Works

1. Typeform Trigger Captures New Quiz Submissions

The workflow activates whenever someone completes your Typeform quiz. It captures all submitted data including email, name, phone number, birthday, and every quiz answer with their responses.

2. Data Transformation and Validation

Phone numbers are formatted to international standards, dates are converted to UNIX timestamps, and quiz answers are mapped from multiple-choice values to readable text. Empty or malformed data is handled gracefully to ensure clean submissions.

3. Subscriber Management in KlickTipp

The cleaned data is added to your designated KlickTipp subscriber list. Personal details populate standard fields while quiz answers are mapped to custom fields you've created specifically for your quiz.

4. Dynamic Tagging and Segmentation

The workflow fetches existing tags from KlickTipp, defines new tags based on quiz responses, merges the lists, and applies appropriate tags to each contact. This enables immediate segmentation for targeted follow-up sequences.

What You'll Need

  1. Typeform account with a published quiz or form
  2. KlickTipp account with API access enabled
  3. n8n instance (self-hosted or cloud)
  4. Custom fields in KlickTipp created for your quiz questions
  5. KlickTipp community node installed in your n8n instance

Pro tip: Create your KlickTipp custom fields before setting up the workflow. Allow 10-15 minutes for them to sync with the API. If fields don't appear, simply reconnect your KlickTipp credentials in n8n.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance
  2. Connect your Typeform account to the trigger node
  3. Authenticate your KlickTipp account in the subscriber node
  4. Map Typeform fields to corresponding KlickTipp custom fields
  5. Test the workflow by submitting a test quiz response
  6. Activate the workflow and start capturing leads automatically
