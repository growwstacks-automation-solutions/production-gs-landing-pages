What This Workflow Does

This automated workflow transforms university admissions by combining Jotform data collection with GPT-4's analytical capabilities. It processes incoming student applications, evaluates academic credentials, assesses essay quality, and calculates scholarship eligibility—all without manual intervention.

The system standardizes evaluation criteria across all applicants, eliminating human bias while maintaining nuanced understanding of each candidate's potential. It automatically generates personalized responses and scholarship offers, while flagging exceptional cases for human review.

How It Works

1. Application Submission via Jotform

Prospective students complete digital application forms with transcripts, essays, and supporting documents. Jotform securely collects and structures all submission data for processing.

2. Automated Document Verification

The system validates academic records, checks for document authenticity, and converts international grading scales to your institution's standards.

3. AI-Powered Essay Evaluation

GPT-4 analyzes personal statements and supplemental essays for content quality, writing skills, and alignment with your program values—scoring each component objectively.

4. Comprehensive Candidate Scoring

A weighted algorithm combines academic performance, test scores, extracurriculars, and essay evaluations into a unified admission score.

5. Scholarship Matching

The system cross-references applicant profiles with scholarship criteria, automatically awarding eligible financial aid packages based on merit and need.

6. Notification & Follow-up

Automated emails deliver decisions, scholarship offers, and next steps—with personalized touches that maintain your institution's brand voice.
