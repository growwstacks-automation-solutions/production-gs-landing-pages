What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the time-consuming manual process of logging email interactions in HubSpot. It automatically:

  • Monitors your email inbox for new messages
  • Uses AI to extract key information from each email
  • Checks if the sender exists in HubSpot
  • Creates new contacts when needed
  • Logs each email as an engagement with proper context

Customer success teams using HubSpot report saving 5-10 hours per week by automating this previously manual process while improving data accuracy.

How It Works

1. Email Trigger

The workflow starts when a new email arrives in your connected IMAP inbox. It captures the full email content including headers and attachments.

2. AI Content Parsing

An AI service analyzes the email to extract structured data including:

  • Sender information
  • Key message content
  • Customer intent
  • Action items
  • Sentiment analysis

3. HubSpot Contact Lookup

The system checks HubSpot for existing contacts matching the sender's email address. If found, it retrieves the contact record.

4. Contact Creation (If New)

For new senders, the workflow automatically creates a complete HubSpot contact record using parsed email data.

5. Engagement Logging

Finally, the email is logged as a HubSpot engagement with all extracted context, linking it to the proper contact record.

Pro tip: Customize the AI prompt to extract industry-specific data points that matter most to your business.
