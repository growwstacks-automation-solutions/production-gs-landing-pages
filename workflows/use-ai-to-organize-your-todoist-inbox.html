What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common productivity challenge of an overwhelming Todoist inbox. Instead of manually sorting through dozens of tasks, the workflow uses AI to automatically assign priorities based on your predefined project importance levels.

The system analyzes each new task's content, project association, and due date against your priority rules. It then assigns an appropriate priority level (P1-P4) to help you focus on what matters most. This eliminates decision fatigue while ensuring critical tasks never get buried in your inbox.

How It Works

1. New Task Detection

The workflow monitors your Todoist inbox for new items. When a task appears, it captures all relevant details including the task name, description, project, labels, and due date.

2. Priority Analysis

An AI model evaluates the task against your configured project priorities. Higher-value projects receive greater weight in the scoring algorithm. The system also considers urgency signals from due dates and keywords.

3. Priority Assignment

Based on the analysis, the workflow assigns one of four priority levels (P1 = critical, P4 = low importance). These appear as Todoist priority flags, creating visual hierarchy in your task list.

4. Continuous Learning

The system tracks your manual priority adjustments to refine its algorithm over time. Frequent overrides in specific contexts automatically improve future recommendations.
