What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves a critical business problem: unverified, low-quality leads from contact forms. When someone submits a JotForm contact form, you often receive incomplete or invalid data—fake emails, missing professional context, or spam entries. Manually verifying each lead and researching their professional background is time-consuming and error-prone.

The automation automatically captures form submissions, validates email deliverability using Reoon's comprehensive verification API, filters out unsafe emails, and enriches validated contacts with professional data from Apollo (LinkedIn URL, job title, company name). All processed data is stored in Google Sheets, creating a clean, actionable lead database ready for sales outreach.

This eliminates manual data cleaning, ensures only high-quality leads enter your CRM, and provides sales teams with enriched context for personalized follow-up—all without human intervention.

How It Works

Step 1: Capture JotForm Submission

The workflow triggers automatically when a new form submission arrives in JotForm. It captures all form fields—name, email, phone, message—and prepares them for processing.

Step 2: Initial Storage in Google Sheets

The basic lead data is immediately recorded in a Google Sheet. This creates a backup and tracking point before validation begins.

Step 3: Email Verification with Reoon

The lead's email address is sent to Reoon's API for comprehensive validation. Reoon checks deliverability, spam trap risk, disposable email status, domain validity, and other quality metrics.

Step 4: Save Verification Results

The verification metrics (safe/unsafe status, risk scores) are saved back to the Google Sheet alongside the original lead data.

Step 5: Safety Filter

An IF node filters the workflow: only emails marked as "safe" by Reoon proceed to enrichment. Unsafe emails are logged but not enriched, protecting your database quality.

Step 6: Contact Enrichment with Apollo

For safe emails, the workflow queries Apollo's API to find professional information: LinkedIn profile, current job title, company name, and sometimes additional details.

Step 7: Final Update with Enriched Data

The enriched professional data is added to the Google Sheet record, completing the lead profile. The sheet now contains verified, enriched leads ready for sales action.
