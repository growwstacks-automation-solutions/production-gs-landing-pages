What This Workflow Does

This automation solves two critical challenges for event organizers: ensuring ticket authenticity and delivering exceptional fan experiences at scale. Manual validation processes often miss sophisticated fraud attempts while consuming staff time that could be spent enhancing the event itself.

The workflow coordinates two specialized AI agents - one focused on security validation and another dedicated to fan engagement. Together they automate 80-90% of routine ticketing operations while providing real-time insights into customer needs and potential issues.

How It Works

1. Ticket Purchase Validation

When a new order arrives via email or API, the system extracts key details and routes them through multiple verification checks. GPT-4o analyzes the purchase context while custom rules assess fraud risk factors.

2. Automated Fan Onboarding

Validated tickets trigger personalized email sequences with event details, venue guides, and optional merchandise offers. The AI tailors messaging based on purchase history and detected preferences.

3. Real-time Team Alerts

Suspicious transactions or VIP purchases generate Slack notifications with recommended actions. Staff can review flagged cases directly from the messaging interface.
