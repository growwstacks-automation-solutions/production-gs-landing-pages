What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of maintaining clean email lists in your Mautic marketing automation platform. When new contacts are added to Mautic, the workflow automatically validates their email addresses using OneSimpleAPI's verification service before they enter your marketing funnel.

Invalid email addresses waste marketing resources, hurt deliverability rates, and skew campaign analytics. This workflow prevents these issues by catching typos, disposable addresses, and inactive mailboxes immediately after contact creation. Validated contacts proceed normally while problematic addresses can be flagged or routed for manual review.

How It Works

1. New contact detection

The workflow monitors Mautic for new contact creation events through its API. Whenever a contact is added or imported, the system captures their email address and basic profile information.

2. Email validation

Each new email address is sent to OneSimpleAPI's validation service. The service performs multiple checks including syntax validation, domain verification, mailbox existence, and spam trap detection.

3. Result processing

The workflow receives a validation score and detailed report for each email. Based on predefined rules, contacts are categorized as valid, risky, or invalid. The system can update Mautic contact records with validation status.

4. Conditional actions

Depending on validation results, the workflow can trigger different actions: sending invalid contacts to a separate list, notifying sales teams about risky addresses, or allowing only validated contacts into email campaigns.
