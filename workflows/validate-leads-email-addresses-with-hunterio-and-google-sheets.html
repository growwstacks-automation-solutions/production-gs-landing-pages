What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of maintaining clean email lists for sales and marketing teams. Invalid email addresses waste outreach efforts, hurt deliverability rates, and skew campaign analytics. The workflow automatically verifies lead email addresses stored in Google Sheets using Hunter.io's professional verification API.

By running daily checks, it identifies deliverable addresses while flagging invalid, risky, or disposable emails. The system writes verification results back to your spreadsheet with confidence scores and status indicators, enabling data-driven decisions about which leads to prioritize.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow activates daily (or on your preferred schedule) to process new leads while re-checking existing ones based on your configured refresh rate.

2. Google Sheets Integration

Connects to your specified Google Sheet containing lead information with columns for email, name, company, and other relevant fields.

3. Data Preparation

Filters out blank entries and malformed email addresses before API submission to conserve verification credits.

4. Hunter.io Verification

Each email undergoes comprehensive checks including:

  • SMTP server validation
  • Mailbox existence confirmation
  • Disposable email detection
  • Quality scoring (0-100)

5. Results Processing

The workflow interprets API responses to categorize emails as:

  • Valid - Confirmed deliverable (with confidence %)
  • Risky - Possible issues (catch-all domains, etc.)
  • Invalid - Undeliverable addresses

6. Data Writing

Updates your Google Sheet with verification results, timestamps, and actionable status indicators while preserving all original lead information.

Pro tip: Combine this with your CRM integration to automatically segment leads based on verification status for targeted follow-up sequences.
