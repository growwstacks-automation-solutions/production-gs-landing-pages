What This Workflow Does

This automation system transforms property compliance management by combining AI analysis with cross-platform coordination. It automatically evaluates properties against relevant regulations, identifies potential risks, and orchestrates appropriate actions across your team's communication channels.

The workflow reduces compliance oversight risks by 60-80% compared to manual processes, while ensuring consistent documentation across all properties. For property managers handling multiple units, this can prevent thousands in potential fines and streamline audit preparations.

How It Works

1. Property Data Collection

The system pulls property details from your Google Sheets database or connected property management software, including inspection reports, permits, and compliance documentation.

2. AI Risk Assessment

OpenAI analyzes the property data against current compliance regulations, flagging potential violations in zoning, safety, accessibility, and other critical areas based on your location's specific requirements.

3. Action Orchestration

Depending on the risk level identified, the system automatically schedules follow-ups in Google Calendar, notifies relevant team members via Slack, and prepares client communications in Gmail.
