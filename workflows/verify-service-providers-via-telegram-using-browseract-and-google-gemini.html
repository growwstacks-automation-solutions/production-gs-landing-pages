What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually verifying service providers by combining Telegram messaging with BrowserAct's web scraping capabilities and Google Gemini's AI analysis. When you receive a service provider's name through Telegram, the workflow automatically researches their online presence, evaluates their credibility, and returns a detailed verification report.

The system eliminates hours of manual research by automatically checking business registrations, reviews, social media presence, and other digital footprints. It then uses Google Gemini to analyze the collected data and generate a risk assessment score with key verification points.

How It Works

1. Telegram message trigger

The workflow activates when a new message containing a service provider's name is received in your Telegram bot. The system extracts the business name and location (if provided) for verification.

2. BrowserAct web scraping

BrowserAct automatically searches multiple sources including business directories, review platforms, and social media to gather comprehensive data about the service provider. It captures registration details, customer reviews, service history, and other relevant information.

3. Google Gemini analysis

The collected data is processed by Google Gemini, which evaluates the provider's credibility based on patterns in reviews, consistency of information across platforms, and other trust signals. Gemini generates a detailed analysis with a reliability score.

4. Telegram response

The workflow sends back a formatted verification report through Telegram, highlighting key findings, potential red flags, and recommendations. The response includes clickable links to source materials for manual review if needed.
