What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical problem for marketing and sales teams: wasted time on unqualified leads and CRM pollution from invalid email addresses. Every time someone fills out your Jotform, they might provide a fake, mistyped, or temporary email. Manually checking each submission is tedious and error-prone.

The workflow automatically intercepts new Jotform submissions, validates the email address through ZeroBounce's multi-layer system, scores its quality, and only syncs genuinely qualified contacts to ActiveCampaign. It acts as an intelligent gatekeeper, ensuring your sales team only sees leads worth pursuing while maintaining clean data hygiene.

Beyond simple validation, it implements smart credit management to prevent API failures, categorizes every rejection with clear reasons, and creates audit trails in Google Sheets. This transforms your lead capture from a passive collection point into an active qualification engine.

How It Works

1. Trigger & Initial Verification

The workflow activates immediately when a new submission arrives in your Jotform. First, it checks if an email address was even provided—surprisingly, many forms are submitted with this field blank. If missing, the lead is flagged and routed to the rejection log with the reason "Email missing."

2. Credit Safety Check

Before making any external API calls, the workflow checks your ZeroBounce account balance. This prevents node failures and unexpected interruptions if credits run low. Insufficient credits trigger a graceful failover, logging the lead for later retry rather than breaking the automation.

3. Stage 1: Email Validation

The email undergoes ZeroBounce's primary validation, checking deliverability, domain existence, and mailbox status. Valid emails proceed directly to ActiveCampaign creation. Invalid ones are logged to Google Sheets with full validation details. "Catch-all" domains (which accept all addresses) proceed to secondary scoring.

4. Stage 2: Email Scoring

For ambiguous catch-all addresses, ZeroBounce's scoring API assigns a quality score (0-10) based on domain age, SMTP provider reputation, and other risk factors. High scores (≥9) are treated as qualified and synced to ActiveCampaign. Medium/low scores are suppressed but logged with their specific score for potential review.

5. Output & Synchronization

Accepted leads flow into ActiveCampaign with all their form data intact, ready for your sales sequences. Rejected leads populate a separate Google Sheet with comprehensive details—rejection reason, validation results, and scoring data—creating a complete audit trail without cluttering your CRM.

What You'll Need

  1. Jotform account with API access to trigger on form submissions
  2. ZeroBounce API key (get one from their member portal)
  3. ActiveCampaign account with API credentials for contact creation
  4. Google Sheets (or alternative data storage like n8n's Data Table node)
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the automation

Pro tip: Before going live, test with a small batch of existing leads to verify your scoring thresholds match your business's risk tolerance. You might adjust the "high score" cutoff based on your specific conversion patterns.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the JSON template into your n8n instance.
  2. Connect your Jotform trigger by adding your API key and selecting the target form.
  3. Configure ZeroBounce nodes with your API key and set your preferred credit warning threshold.
  4. Set up ActiveCampaign connection with your account URL and API credentials.
  5. Prepare Google Sheets with the recommended column headers or substitute with your preferred storage.
  6. Test with sample submissions to ensure validation, scoring, and sync work as expected.
  7. Activate the workflow and monitor the first few leads through both acceptance and rejection paths.
