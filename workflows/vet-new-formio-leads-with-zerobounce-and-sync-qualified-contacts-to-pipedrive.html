Vet new Form.io leads with ZeroBounce and sync qualified contacts to Pipedrive

Automated lead vetting workflow that validates email addresses and syncs qualified leads to your CRM

Download Template JSON · n8n compatible · Free

What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of low-quality leads entering your sales pipeline from Form.io submissions. It automatically validates each lead's email address through ZeroBounce's verification service before creating a contact in Pipedrive, ensuring your sales team only spends time on legitimate prospects.

The workflow eliminates manual data verification tasks that typically consume hours each week. By integrating three powerful platforms, it creates a seamless process that improves lead quality while reducing administrative overhead. Businesses using this solution typically see a 40-60% reduction in invalid leads and a 25% increase in sales team productivity.

How It Works

Step 1: Capture New Form.io Submissions

The workflow triggers whenever a new form submission is received in your Form.io account. It extracts all relevant contact information including name, email, phone number, and any custom form fields you've configured.

Step 2: Validate Email with ZeroBounce

Each email address is sent to ZeroBounce for comprehensive validation. The service checks for valid syntax, domain existence, mailbox activity, and spam trap risk. The workflow receives a quality score (0-10) and detailed validation results.

Step 3: Filter and Enrich Qualified Leads

Only emails meeting your minimum quality threshold proceed to the next step. The workflow can be configured to accept different score thresholds based on your business requirements. Validated leads are enriched with ZeroBounce's verification data.

Step 4: Create Pipedrive Contacts

Qualified leads are automatically created as contacts in Pipedrive, with all form data and validation results mapped to appropriate fields. The workflow can also trigger Pipedrive activities like tasks or follow-up reminders.

Pro tip: Configure different Pipedrive pipelines for different email quality scores to prioritize your sales outreach effectively.
