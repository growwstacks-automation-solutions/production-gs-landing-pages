What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a major content creation bottleneck: turning valuable video content into search-optimized blog articles. Most businesses record webinars, interviews, and presentations that contain goldmine insights, but manually transcribing and researching those videos takes hours—if it gets done at all.

The workflow automatically processes any video uploaded to Google Drive, transcribes it using Dumpling AI, extracts five key SEO keywords with GPT-4o, then conducts competitive research on those topics. Finally, it organizes everything into a structured Google Sheet ready for your content team.

Instead of spending 5-8 hours analyzing a one-hour video, this automation delivers research-backed blog outlines in under 10 minutes—with consistent quality every time.

How It Works

Step 1: Video Detection & Preparation

The workflow monitors a specific Google Drive folder for new video uploads. When a video appears, it downloads the file and converts it to base64 format for API processing. This trigger-based approach means you simply upload videos to a folder—the automation handles everything else.

Step 2: AI Transcription & Keyword Extraction

Dumpling AI transcribes the video with high accuracy, capturing spoken content verbatim. GPT-4o then analyzes this transcript, identifying the five most valuable SEO keywords based on search volume, relevance, and competitive landscape. The AI understands context, not just frequency.

Step 3: Competitive Research & Analysis

Using the extracted keywords, the workflow queries Perplexity AI and Google Search via Dumpling AI's research agents. It gathers what topics are trending around those keywords and finds real blog examples ranking for them. This gives you instant competitive intelligence.

Step 4: Structured Output to Google Sheets

All research gets formatted into clean, readable blocks and appended to your Google Sheet. Each row contains keywords, related topics from Perplexity, and actual blog post examples—creating an instant content brief for your writers.
