Voice-Controlled Expense Tracker with Siri & AI

Log and query your spending with simple voice commands. This n8n workflow connects Siri, AI, and Google Sheets to automate your personal finance tracking.

What This Workflow Does

Manually logging expenses is time-consuming, error-prone, and often forgotten. This automation solves that by letting you simply talk to Siri (or any voice assistant) to record or query your spending. The system uses AI to understand natural language, categorizes transactions, and updates Google Sheets automatically.

Whether you're driving and need to log fuel costs, at a restaurant recording a meal, or reviewing monthly budgets, this workflow transforms voice commands into structured financial data. It eliminates the friction between spending and record-keeping, making financial tracking effortless and consistent.

How It Works

1. Voice Trigger

You activate Siri (via Apple Shortcuts) and speak a command like "Log $50 for lunch" or "What did I spend on groceries this week?" The shortcut captures your speech and sends it as text to an n8n webhook endpoint via a secure POST request.

2. AI Processing

The n8n workflow receives your message and passes it to an AI agent (OpenAI, Gemini, or Ollama). The AI is prompted to determine if this is a recording request or a query, extract relevant details (amount, category, date), and structure the data appropriately.

3. Data Routing

Based on the AI's decision, the workflow either appends a new row to your Google Sheets expense log with the transaction details, or queries existing data to answer your question. The logic handles both expense and income entries with proper categorization.

4. Response Generation

The AI formulates a human-friendly response confirming the action taken or answering your query. This response is sent back through the webhook to your device, where Siri can read it aloud or display it on screen.

Who This Is For

This automation is ideal for busy professionals, freelancers, small business owners, and anyone who wants to maintain accurate financial records without manual effort. It's particularly valuable for people who are frequently on-the-go (salespeople, consultants, travelers) and need to log expenses immediately. Teams managing shared budgets can also adapt this for collaborative tracking with proper access controls.

What You'll Need

  1. An n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) with webhook capabilities
  2. Access to an AI service API (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, or local LLM)
  3. A Google Sheets spreadsheet for storing expense data
  4. An iOS device with Shortcuts app for Siri integration (Android alternatives available)
  5. Basic understanding of n8n workflow import and configuration

Pro tip: Start with a simple categorization system (5-10 categories max) before adding complex rules. The AI will learn your patterns better with clear, consistent examples.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download and import the template JSON file into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the Webhook node with a unique path and note the URL.
  3. Connect your AI service by adding API credentials to the AI Agent node.
  4. Link Google Sheets by authenticating and selecting your expense spreadsheet.
  5. Test the workflow manually by sending sample JSON to the webhook endpoint.
  6. Set up the iOS Shortcut using the provided instructions, replacing the webhook URL with yours.
  7. Activate and speak to Siri using your configured trigger phrase.
