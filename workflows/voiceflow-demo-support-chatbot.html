What This Workflow Does

This automation connects a Voiceflow AI chatbot to your backend systems, transforming conversational interactions into actionable business processes. Instead of letting chatbot conversations sit isolated, this workflow automatically creates support tickets in Zendesk, schedules follow-up meetings via Google Calendar, and aggregates conversation transcripts into Airtable for product team analysis.

It solves the problem of fragmented customer service where chatbot interactions don't seamlessly transition to human support or data collection. By automating these handoffs, businesses ensure every customer query is properly logged, escalated when needed, and analyzed for insights—without requiring manual intervention from your team.

Pro tip: This template is especially valuable for scaling customer support. As inquiry volume grows, automated ticket creation and scheduling prevent backlog and ensure consistent service quality.

How It Works

Step 1: Customer Lookup

The workflow begins by checking your database for existing customer records when a chatbot conversation starts. It returns relevant details if found, or a "NOT_FOUND" status for new inquiries, allowing the chatbot to tailor its response accordingly.

Step 2: Zendesk Ticket Creation

When a customer issue requires human intervention, the workflow automatically creates a support ticket in Zendesk with the conversation context, customer details, and priority level. This ensures no query gets lost between chatbot and human support.

Step 3: Meeting Scheduling

If the conversation indicates a need for a follow-up meeting, the workflow integrates with Google Calendar to check availability and schedule an appointment. It sends confirmation details back to the chatbot to share with the customer.

Step 4: Transcript Reporting

After each interaction, the workflow aggregates the conversation data and sends it to Airtable. This creates a structured record for your product team to analyze common pain points, feature requests, and customer sentiment.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Download the template JSON file and import it into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure the webhook node to receive triggers from your Voiceflow chatbot.
  3. Set up credentials for Zendesk, Google Calendar, and Airtable in n8n's credential management.
  4. Adjust database query nodes to match your customer data schema.
  5. Test the workflow with sample conversations to ensure tickets, meetings, and transcripts are created correctly.
  6. Deploy the workflow and connect your Voiceflow chatbot via the webhook URL.

Pro tip: Start with a limited test group of conversations before full deployment. Monitor the first 100 interactions to refine ticket categorization and meeting scheduling logic.
