How It Works

1. Content Extraction & Processing

The workflow starts by fetching website content via HTTP Request. It extracts the HTML body, converts it to clean Markdown, and removes navigation elements, ads, and other noise. This ensures only valuable content gets indexed.

2. Intelligent Chunking & Embedding

A Character Text Splitter divides content into optimal chunks (approximately 500 characters with 50-character overlap). Each chunk then gets converted into numerical embeddings using OpenAI's embedding model, capturing semantic meaning rather than just keywords.

3. Pinecone Vector Storage

Embeddings and their corresponding text chunks are stored in Pinecone, a specialized vector database. Pinecone organizes content by namespace, allowing separate knowledge bases for different websites, departments, or document types.

4. AI Chat Agent with Memory

When a user asks a question, the Chat Agent (powered by OpenAI or OpenRouter) queries Pinecone for relevant content. A memory buffer maintains conversation context, enabling multi-turn dialogues. The system prompt guides the AI to answer based solely on your content, reducing hallucinations.

5. Airtable Integration for Dynamic Data

For questions requiring live data (like billing status, inventory, or pricing), the workflow queries Airtable. This combines static knowledge from your website with dynamic information from your databases for comprehensive answers.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) combines AI with your own data, like website content or documents, to provide accurate, context-aware answers. Unlike standard chatbots that rely on general knowledge, RAG systems reference your specific content, reducing hallucinations and improving answer quality for customer support, FAQs, and internal knowledge bases.

This approach ensures the AI doesn't invent information or provide generic responses. Instead, it grounds every answer in your actual content, making it trustworthy and relevant to your specific business context and customers.

Pinecone is a vector database that stores content as numerical embeddings, enabling semantic search. When a user asks a question, Pinecone finds the most relevant content chunks based on meaning, not just keywords. This allows the chatbot to retrieve precise information from your website, leading to more accurate and contextually relevant answers than traditional keyword-based database searches.

For example, if a customer asks "How do I reset my password?", Pinecone will find your password reset instructions even if the exact phrase "reset password" isn't in the content. It understands synonyms, related concepts, and contextual meaning.
