What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms anonymous website visitors into qualified leads by combining real-time website intelligence with automated lead scoring. The workflow identifies companies visiting your site, enriches their data with valuable business insights, and automatically scores them based on predefined criteria.

Traditional lead generation requires manual research and guesswork about which visitors might become customers. This workflow eliminates that friction by automatically analyzing visitor behavior, company data, and engagement patterns to surface your hottest leads instantly.

How It Works

1. Visitor detection and tracking

The workflow begins by detecting website visitors through your existing tracking system. It captures key details like company name, pages visited, and engagement duration.

2. Company data enrichment with ScrapeGraphAI

ScrapeGraphAI automatically researches each visiting company, gathering intelligence like industry, company size, funding status, and recent news. This transforms anonymous visitors into known entities with valuable context.

3. Lead scoring in HubSpot

The enriched data flows into HubSpot where lead scoring rules evaluate each visitor based on factors like company fit, engagement level, and intent signals. High-scoring leads are flagged for immediate follow-up.

4. Real-time Slack alerts

When a high-value lead is identified, the system instantly notifies your sales team via Slack with all relevant details. This enables immediate, informed outreach while the lead is still engaged with your content.

Pro tip: Combine this with your CRM's automation features to trigger personalized email sequences when high-scoring leads are detected.

Effective lead scoring combines firmographic data (company attributes) with behavioral data (engagement patterns). Assign points for factors like company size match, page visits, content downloads, and time on site to create an objective prioritization system.

A perfect lead might be: Target industry (20 points), 500+ employees (15 points), viewed pricing page (10 points), downloaded case study (10 points), spent 10+ minutes on site (5 points) = 60 point lead flagged for immediate follow-up.

  • Start with 3-5 key scoring factors
  • Adjust weights based on what actually converts
  • Review and refine scoring monthly
