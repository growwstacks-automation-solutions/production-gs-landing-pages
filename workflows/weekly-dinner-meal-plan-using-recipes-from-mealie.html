What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the common challenge of meal planning fatigue by leveraging your existing recipe collection in Mealie. Instead of manually selecting recipes each week, this workflow automatically creates randomized meal plans while ensuring variety and considering your dietary preferences.

The system pulls from your Mealie recipe database (optionally filtered by specific categories like "Quick Meals" or "Vegetarian") and generates a structured weekly plan. This eliminates decision fatigue while maintaining control over your meal choices.

How It Works

1. Recipe Selection

The workflow queries your Mealie instance to retrieve available recipes. You can configure it to pull from specific categories or your entire collection.

2. Randomization

Using n8n's randomization functions, the system selects a specified number of recipes (typically 5-7 for weekly dinners) while avoiding duplicates from recent weeks.

3. Meal Plan Creation

The selected recipes are formatted into a structured meal plan and pushed back into Mealie as a new plan, complete with assigned dates and optional notes.
