What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the tedious, time-consuming process of manually compiling Facebook Ads performance reports for clients. Marketing agencies typically spend hours each week logging into multiple ad accounts, exporting spreadsheets, formatting slides, and writing analysis—only to repeat the process next week.

The workflow automatically gathers performance data from all connected Facebook ad accounts, applies AI-powered analysis to identify key trends and opportunities, then generates polished reports delivered directly to client inboxes via Gmail. It transforms what was a 4-6 hour weekly task into a fully automated process with smarter insights than manual reports.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

The system connects to your Facebook Ads Manager account(s) and pulls performance metrics for the specified date range (default is previous 7 days). It gathers campaign, ad set, and ad-level data including impressions, clicks, conversions, spend, and quality scores.

2. AI Analysis

An AI model processes the raw data to identify performance patterns, compare results against benchmarks, and detect significant changes. It highlights top-performing creatives, underperforming audiences, and optimization opportunities with specific recommendations.

3. Report Generation

The system compiles the data and insights into a clean, client-ready format. You can customize the template with your agency's branding and select which metrics to emphasize based on each client's goals.

4. Scheduled Delivery

Reports are automatically sent via Gmail at your chosen frequency (weekly by default) to designated recipients. The email includes the key highlights upfront with detailed data attached as a PDF or accessible via link.
