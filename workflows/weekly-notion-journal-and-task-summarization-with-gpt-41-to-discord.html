What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually reviewing weekly journal entries in Notion. Many professionals diligently maintain daily journals but struggle to find time to analyze them for patterns and insights. The workflow automatically collects your daily entries, processes them through GPT-4.1 for analysis, and delivers a concise summary to your Discord channel.

The AI-powered summary identifies key themes, emotional trends, productivity patterns, and action items from your week's reflections. This transforms raw journal data into actionable insights without requiring manual review. The weekly cadence ensures consistent reflection while preventing analysis paralysis from daily reviews.

How It Works

1. Scheduled Weekly Trigger

The workflow activates automatically every Sunday evening (configurable) to review the past week's entries. It connects to your Notion database using the official API with your provided credentials.

2. Journal Entry Aggregation

The automation queries your specified Notion journal database, filtering for entries from the past 7 days. It collects all relevant fields (text content, tags, mood ratings, etc.) and combines them into a single document for analysis.

3. GPT-4.1 Analysis

The compiled journal content is sent to OpenAI's API with a carefully crafted prompt that instructs GPT-4.1 to analyze for patterns, insights, and recommendations. The AI identifies recurring themes, emotional shifts, productivity blockers, and achievement highlights.

4. Discord Notification

The final summarized insights are formatted into an easy-to-read report and posted to your specified Discord channel. The message includes key takeaways, trends, and suggested action items for the coming week.
