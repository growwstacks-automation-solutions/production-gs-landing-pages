What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms WhatsApp into a powerful productivity hub by connecting it with OpenAI, Gmail, Google Calendar, task management, and expense tracking systems. Instead of switching between multiple apps, you can now manage your entire workflow through WhatsApp messages - drafting emails, scheduling meetings, creating tasks, tracking expenses, and getting AI-powered assistance with natural language commands.

The system handles the complex integrations in the background while providing a simple chat interface. For example, you can message "Schedule meeting with client X tomorrow at 2pm for 1 hour" and the workflow will check calendars, find availability, send invites, and confirm back via WhatsApp - all automatically.

How It Works

1. WhatsApp Command Processing

When you send a message to your designated WhatsApp number, the workflow first analyzes the text using OpenAI to determine the intended action (email, calendar event, task, expense, or information request).

2. Action Execution

Based on the identified intent, the workflow connects to the appropriate service: Gmail for emails, Google Calendar for scheduling, your task manager for to-dos, or expense software for tracking costs.

3. AI Enhancement

OpenAI improves all outputs - drafting professional email responses, optimizing meeting times based on participants' availability, categorizing expenses, or providing researched answers to business questions.

4. Confirmation & Follow-up

The system sends back WhatsApp confirmations of completed actions and can prompt for missing information when needed.
