What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the pain point of creating platform-specific content across multiple social media channels. Instead of manually reformatting content for each platform, you simply send a WhatsApp message with your core idea. The workflow then uses GPT-4 to generate optimized versions for each platform (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.), applies the right formatting and hashtags, and routes them through an approval process before publishing.

The system handles the entire content adaptation process automatically - from transforming your raw idea into polished posts to ensuring brand consistency across platforms. It saves hours per week by eliminating repetitive formatting work while maintaining your unique voice across all channels.

How It Works

1. WhatsApp Message Trigger

The workflow starts when you send a WhatsApp message containing your content idea. This could be text, voice note, or even an image with a caption. The system captures this input and prepares it for processing.

2. GPT-4 Content Generation

Your raw content is sent to GPT-4, which analyzes the message and generates platform-specific versions. For Twitter it creates concise threads, for LinkedIn it writes professional posts, for Instagram it suggests visual concepts with captions, etc.

3. Approval Workflow

The generated content is sent to a designated approver (could be you or a team member) via email or messaging app. The approver can review, edit if needed, and approve each platform's content with one click.

4. Platform Publishing

Once approved, the workflow automatically publishes to each social platform according to your scheduling preferences. It handles all the API connections and formatting requirements for each platform.

Who This Is For

This workflow is ideal for solopreneurs, content creators, and small marketing teams who need to maintain an active presence across multiple platforms but don't have time to manually create platform-specific content. It's particularly valuable for:

  • Consultants and coaches who share insights across professional and casual platforms
  • Small business owners managing their own social media
  • Agency teams handling multiple client accounts
  • Content creators who repurpose core ideas across platforms

Pro tip: Train GPT-4 with examples of your best-performing content to ensure generated posts match your brand voice and style.
