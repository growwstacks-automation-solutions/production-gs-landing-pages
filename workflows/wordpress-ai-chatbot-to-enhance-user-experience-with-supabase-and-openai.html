What This Workflow Does

This automation creates an intelligent AI chatbot for your WordPress site that leverages your existing content to answer visitor questions. By combining Supabase for data storage and OpenAI for natural language processing, the chatbot provides context-aware responses that improve user engagement and reduce support requests.

The solution implements Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), meaning the chatbot can reference your WordPress posts, pages, and products to deliver accurate information. Unlike generic chatbots, this system is specifically trained on your content, ensuring responses align with your brand voice and knowledge base.

How It Works

1. Content Indexing

The workflow periodically scans your WordPress site, extracting and organizing content into a searchable knowledge base stored in Supabase. This includes post titles, excerpts, categories, and other metadata that helps the AI understand your content structure.

2. User Query Processing

When a visitor asks a question, the system first searches your indexed content for relevant matches. This ensures responses are grounded in your actual published material rather than generating generic answers.

3. AI Response Generation

The workflow sends the user's question along with relevant content snippets to OpenAI's API. The AI synthesizes this information into a natural-language response that directly addresses the query while referencing your WordPress content.

4. Conversation Management

Supabase stores the chat history, allowing for contextual follow-up questions and maintaining conversation threads. This creates a more natural interaction flow compared to single-question chatbots.
