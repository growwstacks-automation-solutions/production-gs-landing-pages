What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming process of creating regular WordPress content. Instead of manually researching topics, writing drafts, and formatting posts for WordPress. Content teams often spend 3-5 hours per article when starting from scratch.

The workflow uses AI to transform simple keywords into complete WordPress posts in minutes. It handles the entire process: generating an outline, writing the content, optimizing for SEO, and formatting with proper headings. The result is publish-ready content that maintains consistency with your brand voice.

How It Works

1. Keyword Input

You provide 3-5 keywords or phrases that define your post's topic. These can be product names, industry terms, or target phrases. The system accepts input via form, spreadsheet, or direct integration with other tools.

2. AI Content Generation

The workflow sends your keywords to an AI writing assistant (like ChatGPT) which generates a draft article. The AI considers word count, tone preferences, and SEO requirements you've configured.

Optional steps can modify the draft—adding internal links, adjusting reading level, or incorporating specific product mentions. Some users incorporate human review at this stage before publishing.

4. WordPress Integration

The final content gets formatted with proper HTML tags and published to your WordPress site automatically. The workflow can set categories, featured images, and meta descriptions.
