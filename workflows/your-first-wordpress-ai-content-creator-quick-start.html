What This Workflow Does

This workflow solves the challenge of consistently producing high-quality blog content at scale for WordPress sites. Many businesses struggle with content bottlenecks - either lacking the time to create enough material or the budget to hire multiple writers. The automation generates draft content using AI, formats it according to your WordPress standards, and handles the publishing process.

By automating the initial content creation phase, you can focus your team's efforts on strategy and refinement rather than repetitive writing tasks. The workflow creates multiple reading level versions of each piece, making your content accessible to wider audiences while maintaining SEO value.

How It Works

1. Content Brief Generation

The workflow starts by creating detailed content briefs based on your target keywords and audience profile. These briefs include suggested outlines, research points, and tone guidelines.

2. AI Content Creation

Using your preferred AI writing tool, the workflow generates draft content in multiple reading levels. Each version maintains the core information while adjusting vocabulary and complexity.

3. Quality Assurance Checks

Automated checks verify originality, readability scores, and SEO optimization before content moves to the next stage. The system flags any potential issues for review.

4. WordPress Formatting

The workflow applies your preferred formatting standards - adding headings, featured images, meta descriptions, and proper HTML structure for WordPress.

5. Scheduled Publishing

Completed articles are either published immediately or scheduled according to your content calendar. The workflow can notify your team for final review when needed.

Pro tip: Start with 2-3 reading level variations per article, then analyze engagement metrics to refine your approach. Most blogs find their audience responds best to content at 8th-10th grade reading level.
