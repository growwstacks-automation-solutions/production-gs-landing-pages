What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow automates the entire process of gathering YouTube comments, analyzing them with GPT-4o's advanced AI capabilities, and delivering structured email reports. It solves the time-consuming manual process of reviewing hundreds or thousands of comments to extract meaningful insights about your audience's sentiment, frequently asked questions, and trending topics.

Content creators, marketers, and community managers can use this automation to save 5-10 hours per week that would otherwise be spent manually reading and categorizing comments. The AI-powered analysis provides deeper insights than simple sentiment analysis, identifying emerging themes, common pain points, and opportunities for content improvement.

How It Works

1. YouTube Comment Collection

The workflow begins by connecting to the YouTube API to fetch all comments from a specified video. It handles pagination automatically to ensure no comments are missed, even on videos with thousands of responses.

2. Data Preprocessing

Comments are cleaned and formatted for analysis, removing spam, duplicate entries, and irrelevant content. The system groups comments by thread to maintain conversational context for more accurate analysis.

3. GPT-4o Analysis

The cleaned comments are sent to GPT-4o for advanced natural language processing. The AI identifies sentiment trends, common questions, product feedback, content suggestions, and emerging discussion topics.

4. Report Generation

The workflow compiles the AI's findings into a structured report with visual charts, key metrics, and actionable recommendations. This includes sentiment distribution, top discussion topics, and notable comment highlights.

5. Email Delivery

The final report is formatted as an HTML email and sent to specified recipients on a schedule you configure (daily, weekly, or per video). The email includes both high-level summaries and detailed analysis sections.
