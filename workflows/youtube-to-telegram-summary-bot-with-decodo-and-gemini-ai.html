What This Workflow Does

This n8n workflow solves the universal problem of information overload from lengthy YouTube videos. It acts as your personal "TL;DW" (Too Long; Didn't Watch) assistant by automatically extracting key insights and delivering concise summaries to your Telegram account.

The system combines Decodo's video processing capabilities with Gemini AI's advanced natural language understanding to analyze video content, identify main points, and generate human-readable summaries. This eliminates the need to watch entire videos while ensuring you capture all critical information.

How It Works

1. YouTube Video Identification

The workflow monitors specified YouTube channels or playlists for new uploads. You can configure it to watch for specific keywords or only process videos matching certain criteria.

2. Transcript Extraction

Using Decodo's API, the system automatically retrieves the video transcript or generates captions if none exist. This text forms the basis for the AI analysis.

3. AI Summarization

Gemini AI processes the transcript to identify key themes, main arguments, and essential details. The AI applies context-aware compression to maintain meaning while reducing content length by 80-90%.

4. Summary Delivery

The polished summary gets formatted with timestamps for key sections and delivered to your Telegram account or channel. The message includes the original video link for reference.
