What This Workflow Does

This automation template transforms passive YouTube or Instagram viewers into active sales conversations on WhatsApp. It solves the common problem of lead leakage between platforms: someone watches your content, clicks your link, fills a form, but then gets lost in email follow-ups that they might never open.

The workflow creates a seamless bridge from video engagement to direct messaging. When a viewer submits a contact form on your WordPress landing page, the system instantly logs them in Google Sheets, adds them to FluentCRM with a "New Lead" tag, sends a series of warm-up emails, and finally delivers a personalized WhatsApp message via Whinta's API. This multi-channel approach ensures leads are engaged through the channels they actually use, dramatically increasing response rates.

For businesses selling coaching, courses, or services via video content, this automation turns viewers into conversations within hours—not days. It eliminates manual data transfer between systems and ensures every lead receives timely, personalized follow-up exactly where they're most likely to respond.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture from Video Content

Your YouTube or Instagram video includes a call-to-action driving viewers to a WordPress landing page with a Fluent Forms submission form. The form captures name, email, phone number, and optionally the video source.

2. Data Synchronization & Backup

The form submission triggers the n8n workflow. First, lead details are written to a Google Sheets spreadsheet for simple backup and visibility. Simultaneously, the contact is pushed to FluentCRM where they're tagged as "New Lead" and added to an email automation sequence.

3. Email Warm-Up Sequence

FluentCRM automatically sends a series of 1-5 emails introducing your service, building trust, and providing value before the WhatsApp outreach. This pre-conditioning increases the likelihood of a positive response when the direct message arrives.

4. WhatsApp Personal Outreach

One hour after the last email (or immediately after form submission if you prefer), n8n sends a personalized WhatsApp message via Whinta's API. The message references the lead's name and their specific interest, creating a human touchpoint that feels immediate and relevant.

5. Sales Team Notification & CRM Updates

The sales team receives notification of the new lead and can monitor WhatsApp replies directly. When a sale is closed, the CRM tag updates from "Engaged" to "Customer," completing the automation cycle.

What You'll Need

  1. WordPress website with Fluent Forms plugin installed
  2. FluentCRM (WordPress plugin) for email automation and tagging
  3. Google Sheets account for backup logging
  4. Whinta.com account with WhatsApp Business API access
  5. n8n instance (cloud or self-hosted) to run the workflow
  6. YouTube or Instagram channel with call-to-action driving to landing page

Pro tip: Before implementing, ensure your WhatsApp Business API account is verified and you have explicit consent language in your form. Compliance is critical for messaging automation.

Quick Setup Guide

  1. Import the template: Download the JSON file and import into your n8n instance.
  2. Configure WordPress webhook: Set up Fluent Forms to send form submissions to your n8n webhook URL.
  3. Connect Google Sheets: Create a spreadsheet and authenticate n8n with your Google account.
  4. Set up FluentCRM API: Add your FluentCRM API credentials in n8n and configure the "New Lead" tag.
  5. Integrate Whinta: Add your Whinta API key and sender ID in the WhatsApp node.
  6. Customize messages: Edit the email sequences and WhatsApp message template to match your brand voice.
  7. Test with a dummy lead: Submit a test form to verify the entire flow works before going live.
