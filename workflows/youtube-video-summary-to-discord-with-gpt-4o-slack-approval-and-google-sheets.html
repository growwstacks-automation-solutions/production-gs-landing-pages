What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms YouTube video monitoring into an intelligent content analysis pipeline. When new videos matching your criteria are published, the system automatically generates AI-powered summaries using GPT-4o, routes them through Slack for team approval, posts the finalized versions to Discord channels, and logs all activity in Google Sheets.

Content teams save 5-7 hours per week by eliminating manual video review while gaining consistent, searchable records of industry insights. The approval workflow ensures quality control, while the Sheets integration provides long-term trend analysis capabilities.

How It Works

1. YouTube Video Trigger

The workflow monitors specified YouTube channels or searches for keywords, triggering when new matching videos are published. It captures the video URL, title, description, and transcript automatically.

2. GPT-4o Analysis

The system sends the video transcript to GPT-4o with customized prompts to generate: key takeaways (3-5 bullet points), notable quotes, sentiment analysis, and relevance scoring based on your criteria.

3. Slack Approval

A formatted Slack message is sent to your designated channel with the AI summary and interactive Approve/Reject buttons. Approved summaries proceed to Discord, while rejected ones alert your team for review.

4. Discord Posting

Approved summaries are formatted with timestamps, speaker highlights, and key points, then posted to your specified Discord channels with the original video link. Threads are automatically created for discussion.

5. Google Sheets Logging

Every processed video is logged with metadata, summary, approval status, and analysis metrics. The sheet includes data validation and pivot table templates for trend analysis.
