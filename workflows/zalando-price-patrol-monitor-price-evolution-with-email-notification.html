What This Workflow Does

This automation solves a critical challenge for ecommerce businesses and savvy shoppers: tracking price fluctuations on Zalando products. Manually checking prices daily is time-consuming and impractical, especially when monitoring multiple products. This workflow automates the entire process, regularly checking your selected products and sending email alerts when prices drop.

The system captures comprehensive pricing data including current price, historical prices, discount percentages, and product availability. You'll receive detailed notifications showing price trends over time, helping you make informed purchasing decisions whether you're a retailer sourcing inventory or a consumer waiting for the best deal.

How It Works

1. Product selection and initialization

The workflow starts with your list of Zalando product URLs or category URLs you want to monitor. These are stored in a database for persistent tracking.

2. Scheduled price checks

At your specified intervals (daily by default), the workflow queries Zalando's API to fetch current pricing data for each product in your monitoring list.

3. Price comparison and analysis

Each new price check is compared against historical data. The system calculates price changes, tracks historical trends, and identifies significant drops.

4. Alert generation

When prices meet your configured thresholds (like a 10% drop or falling below a target price), the workflow generates a detailed email alert with product images, pricing history, and direct purchase links.

5. Data storage and reporting

All pricing data is stored historically, allowing for later analysis of seasonal trends and price patterns across your product catalog.
