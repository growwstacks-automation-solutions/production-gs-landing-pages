What This Workflow Does

This workflow transforms your Zoom meetings into actionable outcomes by automatically:

  • Retrieving Zoom meeting transcripts from the last 24 hours
  • Processing the conversation with AI (Claude 3.7 Sonnet)
  • Generating concise meeting summaries
  • Sending summaries to all participants via email
  • Creating ClickUp tasks for action items
  • Scheduling follow-up calls when needed

The updated version with Claude 3.7 and Think Tool delivers significantly better results, especially for longer meetings where context retention is critical.

How It Works

1. Meeting Data Retrieval

The workflow starts by fetching your Zoom meeting data through the Zoom API. It looks for completed meetings from the last 24 hours.

2. Transcript Processing

For each meeting, it retrieves the transcript and extracts the raw text content. The Think Tool helps maintain context throughout long conversations.

3. AI Summary Generation

Claude 3.7 analyzes the transcript, identifying key discussion points, decisions made, and action items. It formats this into a professional summary.

4. Email Distribution

The system sends the polished summary to all meeting participants via email, ensuring everyone has the same understanding of outcomes.

5. Task Creation

Action items are automatically turned into ClickUp tasks with assignees and deadlines based on the conversation context.

Pro tip: For best results, enable Zoom's automatic transcription feature and ensure all participants speak clearly.
